From what I am familiar with, your police district must be equipped with
the Lojack trackers.  More are supposedly being added regularly, I don't
know about Canada.  The sending unit itself is about the size of a
cigarette pack, hidden on the car.

Smart thieves are supposed to haul off your car to a remote area and
leave it there for a few days.  If it's still there (no Lojack present),
they then take it to the chop shop.  Though as Carol pointed out, you
must notify the police first that the car is stolen for them to activate
it.  It is not so much a deterrent as more a recovery system.
