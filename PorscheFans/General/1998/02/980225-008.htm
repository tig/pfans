Regarding VIN codes, the following applies, ( for '81 & later 911's)
VIN's consist of 17 digits

First three digits are the world producer code. Porsches' is WPO
The next three digits (4-6) are the"VDS" code
       Digit 4 tells which series
                A= 911 coupe
                E= Targa Cab. or Speedster
                 J= Turbo
       Digit 5 is engine
                 A= Canada (OK eh!)
                 B= USA
       Digit 6 is restraint type
                 0= active
                 1= passive
       R.o.W cars have "ZZZ" for digits 4-6
 
Digits 7 & 8 are the first 2 digits of the model type (ie 91=911, 93=930)
Digit 9 is a test digit ( doesn't stand for a darn thing!!!!!)
Digit 10 is designates the year 
        B=1981
        C=1982
        D=1983
Digit 11 indicates the manufacturing site. S=Stuttgart
Digit 12 is the third digit of the model #  (don't think I have to 'splain
that one!)
Digit 13 is the engine/body code
         0= R.o.W SC or Coupe
         1= R.o.W Turbo Targa
         2= USA SC or Coupe
         4= R.o.W SC or Carrera Targa
         5= Turbo Coupe-USA & Canada, 911SC or Cabriolet-R.o.W
         6= Turbo Targa USA & Canada, 911 SC or Carrera Targa-USA
         7= Turbo Cabriolet-USA & Canada, 911SC or Carrera Cabriolet-USA
Digit 14-17 are the 4 digit sequential serial number
