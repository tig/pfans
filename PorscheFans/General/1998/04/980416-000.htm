Hey everybody,  let's all pronounce this name more correctly. Fuchs, which is
German for fox, should be pronounced to ryhme with our word, "books" , or
"looks". I've heard some really creative pronunciations recently, some were
pretty funny, one was obscene!  If you look on the inside of a Fuchs wheel,
you'll see a little fox's head( pointy snout, ears, and whiskers).
I know, I know, the "ch" in Fuchs is actually a voiced velar fricative in
German, but we don't have that sound in English. Our "k" ,   a  voiced velar
stop, is the closest sound to it.  Now, I'll sleep better tonight.
