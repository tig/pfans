The decline of the tradition of flashing your lights as a greeting to 
other Porsches you encounter on the road is periodically a topic of 
discussion in the various Porsche online forums. Even a recent 
issue of Panorama had a note from the president of the PCA 
encouraging members to help revive this tradition.

In an effort to expand awareness of the tradition and encourage
Porsche owners to practice it, I have started a "Save the Flash!"
web campaign. If you manage a Porsche related web site, I encourage
you to visit this page:

    http://www.gate.net/~kopsis/dkessler/save_flash.html

To learn how your site can participate. Thanks, and keep those lights 
flashing :-)
