Hello William & P-Fans

	If you will have a car, drive over to Danville, Ca. Its near Berkley/Okland.
Go visit the Behring Auto Museum in Danville (ph#510-736-2278). There is lots
of cool old car there, I think the museum holds something like 150 cars! Also
in the complex is a shopping mall with the famous Blackhawk Auto Auctions in
walking distance. The Behring Museum is part of UC Berkley, and is one of the
finest auto museums in the USA. Only the MercedsBenz Museum in Germany
surpasses it for shear drop jaw awing cars!
