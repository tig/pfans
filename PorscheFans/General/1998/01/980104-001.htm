Hi Dirk,

Congratulations!

I've used Yokohama A008Rs and BFG R1s on the street for years. Both are
fine and I really like having the extra safety margin if things suddenly
get interesting. I even took my 951S skiing (literally) a couple weeks
ago when I drove it to Mt. Snow with used R1s. Anything short of that
they are just fine.

I'd stay away from Hoosiers on the street (Hooters if you are Scotty B.
White). They are reputed to have very thin walls. Speaking of Scotty,
I'll beat him to the punch and tell you that Kumhos would be a better
choice than A302s.

I've heard from everybody that the A302s are very noisy on the street.
They are great in the rain but they are slower than the other
performance tires in dry conditions.

Enjoy the new car,
