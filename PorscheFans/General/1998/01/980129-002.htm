> I am considering purchasing a Mity Mite Battery charger that directly and 
> permanently hooks into the cars battery.It only comes on when the 
> battery drops by one volt.  It then recharges the battery at 1.5 amps 
> until the voltage is back to normal.

  I talked to a elect. eng. friend about this a while ago when I was trying
  to figure out the best way to keep the battery charged in my 928.  He felt 
  that a 1 volt drop was too extreme before having the charger kick on.  The 
  1.5amp recharge rate wasn't a problem though.
  
  I know that a 928 will not start with a battery at 11 volts; not sure whether
  the 911/930 is as finicky.  I opted to use a Schumacher fully automatic
  starter charger (SE-5212A) that I just hook up to the jumper terminals 
  whenever I park the car.  This has been working great.  Check out 
  www.batterchargers.com:80
