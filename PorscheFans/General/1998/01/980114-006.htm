and a place of ideas that can be put into a phyisical action.  But as Tim had
stated before,  "And the off-road 4-wheel drive stuff was only to test and
refine their 4wd road cars." is incorrect.  In 1966, Vic Elford helped start

tires for this car and for those not familiar with the Paris-Dakar.  It is
14,000km or (8750 miles) of dirt, rock and sand in the middle of a desert.  I
wouldn’t worry about what Porsche is about to produce, for I’m sure they know
what they are doing.  But most likely, what ever they will produce will be the
best of their combined efforts.  As Porsche has stated, "when excellence was
expected".  If any one wants to see some neat pictures of the 959s, look for a
book by the name of "Porsche Legends"
