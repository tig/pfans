     Complete RENNTech stainless exhaust (includes headers, cats, resonator

     6.0 liter engine conversion (adds 96hp and 110lb/ft) (I think this
     includes labor): $30,450
     
     Want the 6.0 liter conversion that adds 159hp and 170lb/ft?  That one
     will set you back $49,900!
     
     Someone offered us a bargain price of $2K for a 3.08 ring and pinion
