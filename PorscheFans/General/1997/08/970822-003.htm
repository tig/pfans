A friend of mine had a fire in his nice, restored 911 last night.  The
battery had (?) shifted, and started a fire in the driver's side front
fender well and trunk area.  Please make sure your batteries are secure
and there are plastic covers or a lid or something over the terminals so
the battery can't short out.  I lost a good jacket beating out the
flames last night, rather not go thru this at any club meets again.  If
someone had not noticed the smoke the entire car and surrounding cars
could have gone poof.
