I am a member of the Porsche Club Canberra (ACT).  In Australia there is a
separate club for each state.  We publish a monthly newsletter and a
magazine about 4 times a year.  The newsletter just lists up-coming events
but the magazine has articles on the events, some techo and travel articles.
You could get most of the content of the techo articles from the net or more
widely circulated magazines so there would be no point in subscribing for
these but if you want to see one send me your address and I will see what I
can do about mailing you a back issue.
