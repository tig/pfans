	Neil, if it were me, I'd look at a 82 or 83 911SC. The engines on
	these cars are pretty much bulletproof when certain upgrades are
	done (update to Carrera tensioners, pop-off valve on the airbox,
	etc.). If you've got your heart set on a 911, be sure and get
	911 Performance Handbook by Bruce Anderson. He goes into
	great detail about what you can expect from the 911's, their
	problems and features, and which is the best to buy. Good luck!
