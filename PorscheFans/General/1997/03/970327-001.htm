>Does anyone know of an impact wrench with a feature like this:
>
>I want two separate torque settings, one for tightening, and
>another for loosening.

Try an Ingersol-Rand model 231.  Its been in production for
about 15 years and is used in many tire shops.  The last
Harbor Freight catalog I got had them on sale for $109.

Be careful not to confuse the ability to use more torque in one
direction than the other with the ability to accurately torque
a bolt.  Unless you have a special impact wrench similar
to the ones used in NASCAR, you need to manually torque
bolts and nuts (i.e. lug nuts).
