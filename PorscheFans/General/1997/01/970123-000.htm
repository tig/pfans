Answering David Rothenberg's question, I understand Porsche AG has been
trying to ban unauthorised usage of the crest for a few years but they have
not been internationally successful until now.  It is really an
intellectual rights and patent issue.  Genuine PORSCHE DESIGN apparels such
as expensive wrist watches and wallets have been in the market for a good
few years.  I think it is Porsche AG's intention to prevent unauthorised
Porsche products from downgrading its own.

The English guy that commented in Excellence (Feb 97 issue) may have tough
luck having his shop's crested products confiscated, but in the US and
Canada, these crested products are still on sale.
