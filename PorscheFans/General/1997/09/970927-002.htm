I can't believe that changing out a set of bearings and races could be
so easy!  If you have a similar car (no speed sensor on front wheels)
and need to adjust or change the bearings out I'd recommend you do it. 
It saves a lot of money and you will get to know the front end of your
car a bit better.  

I followed the Haynes 944 book instructions, had the races punched out
and new ones put in for $5.00 by a mechanic at a Marathon station and
voila!  The front end wobblies and noise are gone.    Ah well.   Anyway,
it is easy and fun, so just do it.
