The best english-language reviews I've seen thus far on the new 996 are in
the 10 September issue of Autocar and in the October 1997 issue of CAR.  Both
are brit mags. You can usually get CAR at Barnes & Noble or similar, Autocar
might be harder to find. 

The CAR review is by Georg Kacher. It's pretty lengthy but the gist of the
article is that the new 996 is really superb - sort of the best
characteristics of the "old" 911-993 series and a 928 (huh?) rolled into one.
It's much more refined and "civil" than the 911 - it appears Porsche's target
demographic for the 996 was grey-haired guys over 57 whose daily driver is a
Lexus. I passed the article on to my dad (61yrs, black E400 - close enough).
