Before anyone goes off and buys a Valentine "remote" detector, I should 
warn you that it's not exactly what it sounds like.

Since the Valentine looks both fore and aft, you can't very well hide
the thing in the front grille.  The remote only refers to the display. 
The detector still sits on your windshield, prominently displayed in the
middle of things.  They made the remote available so that cops behind you
couldn't see the little flashing lights at night. 

That said, the Valentine is still a great detector.
