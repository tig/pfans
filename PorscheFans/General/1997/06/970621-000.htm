    Yes... But if you don't want to damage your paint, it IS
    necessary to remove the bra when it gets wet.

>  What have other P-fans done with their bras?? Left them on ??

    Left them off... Unless I'm driving across the country or
    something.

    Leaving a bra on all the time -- even if it never gets wet --
    can cause problems... The bra will scratch your paint if it flaps
    or moves around even a little, and if the car's parked in the sun
    with a bra but no cover, it'll develop "tan-lines".  

    Leave it off unless you're taking a long drive... Or buy one of
    those semi-permanent adhesive-backed clear urethane bras that
    Those Guys Graphics (800 350-4897) sells.
