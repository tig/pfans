Yes, it's out.

Up-fixin is indeed a non-German term, probably coined while watching one too 
many episodes of Benny Hill.  What annoys me, though, is the whole title: 
Up-fixin der Porsche.  Any first-year student of German knows that "Porsche" 
here is the direct object, and so the title should read "Up-fixin *den* 
Porsche."
