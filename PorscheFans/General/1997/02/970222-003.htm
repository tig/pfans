Hi Andy,

I built my own press from an old diagram I found in Hot Rod Magazine.  
This one is real simple. I used channel Iron for the main frame and used
a couple of pieces of angle iron for the feet.  The whole thing is bolted 
together.  I used a 3 ton post jack.  The original diagram said that I could
use a 5 ton jack but I already owned the smaller jack.  I used some hood 
springs from a 914 for the return springs.  I used an old winch from a boat
trailer to raise and lower the press platform.  This press has been more 
than sufficient to do any press work on any of the P-Car pieces and once 
in a while 'merican iron type stuff.

Several accessories such as a bearing grab or splitter and a couple of 
pieces of 3/4" plate will be useful.  I keep a box of old bearings and 
assorted stubs of things that are useful for props next to the press.  
These things come in handy for the work.

Good luck.
