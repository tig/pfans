  I just called AVIS Car Rental in Frankfurt and they will rent 911's 
for 249 DM per day(at todays exchange rate = $156.60 day) with 500 
free km. So a week would be about $1096.00 depending on if you did 
not go over the 500 km. 
  I would make rental arrangements through AVIS in the U.S. to ensure 
that they have a 911 for you when you get here. They are hard to get 
according to the people in Frankfurt.
  Of course if you want to drive through Deutschland less expensively 
just rent a different car. You can rent from a Ford Escort up to your 
911. Just bring your VISA GOLD card!!
