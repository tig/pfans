I've seen some posts recently on the 911 list asking about transferring
regions.

If you want to transfer regions YOU must contact the Executive Secretary's
Office by phone or mail and request the transfer. Neither the losing or
gaining region can do this for you. You must do it yourself. If making the
request by mail, give your name, PCA membership Region, old Region and new
region.
