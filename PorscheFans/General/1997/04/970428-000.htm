in need of an SA90 or SA95 helmet in order to enter driver's ed and
autocross events with the local PCA chapter.

look like Darth Vader). It is EXACTLY THE SAME as the SA90 and SA95
models except the lining is not Nomex. They have not changed a thing
about this helmet in the last 12 years. Ask them yourself!

If I was required to wear Nomex clothes, shoes, and underwear to enter
these events, I could understand (to some extent) why the Safety Nazis
would require this additional expenditure.

this decision was made) was that helmets have some sort of inherent

styrofoam. As any garbologist will tell you, these do not decompose!

Don't get me wrong. I am all for safety when it has some useful purpose,
but I have to consider this a knee-jerk reaction.
