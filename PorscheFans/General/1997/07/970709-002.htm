According to an August 1970 Road Test Magazine article on a 1970 911S, the US
base price was $8775.  Add frieght ($148) and tinted glass and radio
(remember those days!) as it totalled at $9218.95.  Inflation was small in
those years.  A Road and Track test in March 1969 listed the price of a 1969
911S at $8750 on the east coast and $100 more on the west coast.  UK prices
could be different, but again, in those earlier days Porsche prices were more
uniform around the world.  Question is, what was the pound/dollar exchange
rate back then?  Probably around 2 to 1.
