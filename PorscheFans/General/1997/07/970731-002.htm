Responding to direct question by a Porschefan, may be of interest.

6" wide front wheels on any year 944, nothin' but trouble. All the 6" alloys,
because of their width, their positive offset, and tire limitation, causes
very unfavorable response on the front end of any year 944, turbos, and
non-turbos alike.  

Not only is dramatic understeer a problem, but braking is very unpredictable
and weak. Never run less than 7x15, or 7x16, etc.
