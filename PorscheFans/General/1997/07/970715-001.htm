If you are planning a trip to germany, the following might be
interesting for you:

At http://www.bmv.de (BundesVerkehrsMinisterium)
you can start a query for all roadworks on the german Autobahnen.
(Including length of the roadwork, what kind of work, start-date,
end-date, speedlimit, ...)

This service is only available in the german version, not in the english
one.
