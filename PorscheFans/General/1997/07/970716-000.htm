Anyone planning a Porsche Factory Tour should
be advised there is road construction on the Autobahn
just south of Stuttgart.  I'd recommend allocating an 
extra half hour to make it through the construction
and to find a place to park outside of the factory.
No, Porsche does not offer parking.

My wife, son, and I toured the factory June 16 and 
highly recommend it.  It's a must if you love Porsches
and are in the area.  The factory was in full gear
producing Boxsters and a few 911's.  Unfortunately, I
was unable to bring one home...this time.
