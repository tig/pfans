Mark,

What you want is the 75-77 Carrera tail, the 76-77 Turbo tail, or the
911SC Weissach tail. The only difference is the Turbo tail has a vent in
the horizontal surface. I personally like the tail with the vent. That is
what I have on my car.  This is my favorite whale tail....the tails with
room for intercoolers do not look right on a 911SC.

Also, these whale tails are fiberglass and are one piece (tail and deck
lid).
