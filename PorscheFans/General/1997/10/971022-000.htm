VRBA,

I drove a 924 Turbo and a 944 in the winter with no problems
whatsoever.  I tried the old GT+4 for a couple of years before I
switched to the Comp T/A m+s.  The Comp T/A was far and away the better
tire for winter and summer use.  I have been a ski instructor for 25+
years and spent 10 years in Summit County Colorado.  My 944 with the
Comp T/As always got me where I needed to go!  Then I spent the last 12
years in Michigan and had no problems here either!  The nice front /
rear weight bias of the 944 makes this combo safe and predictable!  The
924 Turbo was a little different, bucause of the turbo, but still never
left me stranded!

One of the ultimate snow tires is the Blizzaks.  I have used this tire
on several other vehicles and it is GREAT in the snow and ice.  I didn't
use it on my 944, but I know it would be very good in the winter.  Only
thing is, it cannot be driven in the summer like the Comp T/As. 

Hope this is helpful!
