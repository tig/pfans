On page 48 of the December Panorama the N0, N1, N2 and N3 are explained as
PorscheOriginal Equipment Approval Codes.  These designate tires as meeting
the special needs of a Porsche (for the street).  The article did not provide
specific definitions for each code.  I'd recommend you call your local Porsche
Service Department or Porsche Cars North America for more details.
