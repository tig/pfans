My 95 993 used a qt per 1000 miles until almost 5000 miles. My 96 C4S
did the same. Porsche fills with synthetic at the factory. This oil does
a better job of lubricating than regular  oils, therefore it takes
longer for the friction items to bed and seal. Use Mobile 1 and change
at 15,000 as Porshe suggests . Don't rely on the oil quanity gauge -
read gauge with engine hot, running at idle on level floor; and then
confirm reading by pulling the stick -engine still running - and
eyeballing the oil level on the stick. Don't fill more than 1/2 
distance between low and high marks.
