I can't speak for everyone, but I asked my friend, a p-car racing
engine builder, about using some of these "super" oil additives.  The
problem in a racing engine is that these additives, by design, attach
themselves to the various parts and pieces.  Since you can't control
this, it's hard to determine what clearances you need to use.  Just
using normal oil, he can set all the clearances in the engine to where
they need to be for optimum performance.  He does use a product
marketed under the brand name Mega Power.  He says this stuff treats
the oil, not the engine.  I don't know that much about it.  He's the
expert, so I just do what I'm told.

My $.02
