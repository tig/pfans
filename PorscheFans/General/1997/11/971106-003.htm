The Governor of California signed into law SB 42 on October 8, 1997.
The bill, sponsored by our great Senator Kopp, exempts all vehicles 1973
and older from any smog testing requirements.  This is the same
exemption that formerly applied to 1966 and older vehicles.  The law
takes effect on January 1, 1998.  Beginning in 2003, a "sliding
exemption" will apply to all vehicles older than 30 years. (i.e.-in
2005, it applies to model year 1975)

Also, new vehicles will not require smog testing until they are more
than 4 yrs old.  This because it was realized that 99% of vehicles were
passing anyway.

So, now we all have at least one reason to be optimistic about
government.
