Cardex, aka Certificate of Authenticity

It is the official original manufacturing specification (build sheet)
for your car.  It lists:

	- VIN
	- Model year and type
	- engine number
	- transmission number
	- exterior paint color name and code
	- interior color name and type
	- factory installed options

You might want one if you are concerned whether certain options on
your car are factory installed or installed by a Previous Owner.
Or if you are concerned about originality of the engine in the car...
