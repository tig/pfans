On Sat, 15 Nov 1997, Michael Hinsche wrote:

> I have recently noticed that the passenger side Mirror on my 1979 S.C is getting loose. Not from the door skin itself but from the pivot point. 
> 
> Automotion just recently advertised a special tool to tighten this joint, my question is this.
> Do I need to use this tool to do the job, and if anyone knows how to carry out this proceedure to advise.

I Had the same problem with my flag style mirrors. Remove the mirror at
the base from the door. There are 2 screws that are exposed when you swing
the mirror to do this. Now inspecting the innards find the large nut that
has a hex shaped hole. I jammed a screwdriver with a large wide flathead
into the hex and retightened. I had to push down hard while turning
to get the threads to catch, as there is opposite pressure from
the inner springs working against you. If you have some
locktite thread sealer, apply that before retightening to
prevent from re-loosening. The pivot point seems to unloosen
from too much swinging of the mirror, eg. when I wash the car
or turn away from my line of sight when I race in auto-x. Reassemble and
enjoy!
