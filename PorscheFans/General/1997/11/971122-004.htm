There was a test not long ago that proved what most in racing know: 
don't bother with the PTFE or other additives.  The only STP in the Indy
cars I used to work on was used as assembly lube.  Just keep your oil
clean, stick with a big name brand, and because you have a turbo, let
your engine idle for a minute or two after EVERY trip to let the turbo
cool down so it doesn't cook the oil in the bearing.
