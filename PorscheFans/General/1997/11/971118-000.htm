All this discussion about the flat six smoking when it starts should lead
us to consider the configuration of this engine.  Unlike your Honda or SUV
the cyclinders are laying down, they are horizontal guys, so the oil sits
near the rings in a little puddle after the car has sat for a while.  So
when we start them the concentration of oil allows some by the rings, and
voila! smoke.
Do we hear our 944 friends make this observation? No.  Their pots are upright.
