I use the Cool carbons for track events and stock metallic pads for the
street. The Cool Carbons have more stopping power, make more dust, and squeak
a lot. They also cost twice as much as metallic pads. The metallic pads have
plenty of stopping power for the street, are quiet, and not as dusty. - Just
my .02
