garage and filled it up with water. I looked for obvious leaks. there was
none. I let it run to warm up (checking the gauge constantly!) it was running
at normal operating temp and looked fine. Inside the engine bay there were
stains all over the passanger side (opposide side from were you fill the
coolant) from the coolant. Boy does that stuff make an awful smell, cooked
coolant, yuck! anyway, I couldnt see anything lose or burst. Oh yea, I didnt
mention there was no liquid that I could see whatsoever before I put the
water in today. ok, I went in the car and just took it up to about 2000rpm

the fan came on...then after a bit more time at idle, the temp shot up! I
immediatly shut off the car before the temp went into the red. ok, still no
visable sign of the leak, but I could see some water on the ground. I rolled
the car back a bit and there was now a huge puddle under the car. So, the
leak in on the passenger side. it is towards the bottom of the car, and this
time it just leaked out, but yesterday it shot up becuase the coolant was all
over the block and the passenger side of the engine bay. you can see all sort
of stains on the inside of the hood from it. I think I can even tell exactly

away, mostly highway. If I fill it up with coolant and take extra with me in
