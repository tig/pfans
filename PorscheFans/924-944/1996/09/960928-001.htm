Hi Nick, I havent been on highway 70, but when I picked up my car in Jun. 
this year from
Denver I headed home thru Wyoming. Took the normal route north on I25 to 
Cheyenne, then Casper. There I branched off onto Highway 20 heading west 
to Wind River Canyon. Then north to Worland. This is where the fun 
started, took Hwy.16 heading Northeast, thru Tensleep and the best part 
Powder River pass to Buffalo. Continued on hwy.16 to Gillette, then North 
on state route 59 thru Thunder Basin (a 45 minute straight shoot to Montana).

Ever been on those roads? Where are you?

BTW Montana was extra fun. Doing 100+mph easily on secondary highways 
with no traffic and never any cops. I topped out once at 152 mph! (where 
safe) Factory quotes 149 mph 
tops. The S2 at that speed was incredibly stable and didn't even feel like 
it was working up a sweat. I envy the chipped Turbo S', my friend saw 170 
mph in Montana too.

Guess I better start carrying a lower rad hose to the water pump. No 
small town would have that weird shaped thing.
