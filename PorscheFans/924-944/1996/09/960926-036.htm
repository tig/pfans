> Hi All;

> A few questions for 944/951 owners using adjustable shocks.  Is the
> adjustment from the top of the shock for rebound or compression? 

M030 option p-cars have factory yellow koni' adjustable for rebound only. 
From the top in the fronts only.

 Are the
> shocks on the 951 S cars adjustable for both?

No, see above. If Tokico', yes both adjust at the same time. You can buy 
or have Koni' modified to be double adjustable, but it aint cheap.

  If so, how is it
> accomplished? 

Pull the plastic piece off the top of the front shocks. Using the Koni 
plastic adjuster (or some vise-grips) turn clockwise? to stiffen. There's 
2 1/2 turns of adjustment.
