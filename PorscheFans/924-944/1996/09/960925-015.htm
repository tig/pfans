Locally, we have eliminated the drop the clutch and go by either havinga 
practice lap before the timed lap if time permits or the better solution 
is to require a 90 degree corner before the timing lights so as to 
minimize the amount of clutch damage or competitor burnouts.

Both of these are used by the Porsche clubs and the SCCA requires the 90 
degree corner as part of its Solo II (autocross) rules.

Hope this provides you some meaningful information.
