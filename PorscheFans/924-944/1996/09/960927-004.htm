My experience with all Koni adjustable shocks (i.e. those not
specifying a separately adjustable bounce and rebound setting)
is that the bounce and rebound are adjusted at the same time with
the same adjustment.

The Turbo S struts and shocks that I have seen have only a single 
adjustment (i.e. combined).
