Be sure to check the ignition wire.  (the one going from the center of the
distributor to the coil)  Mine received a few hits from the light assembly as
it was brought up and down, and thus had some cracks in it.  These cracks
allowed moisture in, and caused leakage.  Resulting in no operation if it was
really wet, and small backfires when it wasn't.
