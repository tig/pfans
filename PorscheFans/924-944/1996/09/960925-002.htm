I like to rotate the power window switches between the doors, to even out
the wear (since the driver's side gets the most use, and the passenger
side very little).  It is so simple, it only takes 10 minutes or so to do.
Haven't had a failure yet (knock on wood).. but the drivers side switch on
the '79 924 I owned ages ago did fail.  Note that that 924 switch lives on
as the electric hatch release on the 944's.  Trivia for the day.
