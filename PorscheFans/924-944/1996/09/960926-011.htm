Hi Brian and others;

1986 was the first year for the 951 in the U.S.  Although it is a nice car,
significant improvements were made in subsequent years culminating in the
Turbo S cars of 1988 and 1989.  I personally find the newer cars more
appealing with their refinements.  The price on the one your father is
looking at sounds reasonable, especially given all the new parts.
Considering the car is chipped, have the inspecting mechanic perform a
leakdown test to look for problems with the head gasket.  Remind your father
he is looking at a 10 year old sports car which may require considerable
care and feeding.  Good Luck.

Tracy Meyer
