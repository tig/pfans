> A few questions for 944/951 owners using adjustable shocks.  Is the
> adjustment from the top of the shock for rebound or compression?  Are the
> shocks on the 951 S cars adjustable for both?  If so, how is it
> accomplished?  TIA

Tracy,

The knob at top, according to the factory manual, says it is for "contraction
adjustment". Elsewhere in the same manual they show the adjustment which
requires the strut to be removed from the car and call that "rebound adjustment".

From this I say the in-car adjusters are for compression. From other references
in the manual I have concluded that the "S" units are double-adjustable. I have
the yellow Koni's which have only the single remove-from-car rebound adjustment.

Note that the top and bottom adjusters turn in opposite directions from each
other to firm up the respective adjustment.
