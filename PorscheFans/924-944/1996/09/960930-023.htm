that wasn't in your list of things you'd checked (I know, kinda like
asking "did you plug it in?").

.....
| - Where is this marked stud relative to the slot where the dust cap is 
| removed? Is it consistent? (the slot is between two studs)

You mark the stud after having the wheels balanced on the car (that is,
let the shop balance the wheels, then when you get home, take them off,
remember which stud had the lock in it, and paint the tip of that stud
with some touch-up paint.)  BTW: Be sure that you have the wheel lock on
the stud closest to the inflation valve -- that way, you'll be able to
easily mount the wheel with the rotating mass in the same place it was
when the wheels were balanced (I'd guess that the valve placement is
more important than wheel locks since it's at a larger radius from the
center of the wheel and about the same mass).
