>Hi, Jim

Has any work been done to the motor recently?

A typical problem occurs when the balance shaft pulleys are removed and
not replaced with the proper keyway aligned to the woodruff key on the
balance shaft itself.

The marks on the pulley are O (for ober - upper balance shaft) and U (for
uber - lower balance shaft).

If it feels like there is a vibrator in the shift lever, that's a likely
culprit...
