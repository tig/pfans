Some of you may recall that I mentioned a couple of weeks ago that I had
installed Hawk Blue (race) pads on my 87 951, and that I would report back
on my opinion of them.

Well, had the car at Sebring this weekend and I am *very* impressed with
these pads. More stopping power than Cool Carbons and not so finicky to bed
in. But, they are extremely dusty and noisy. (Noisy after they were heated
up once). These pads cost about $80 per axle from Racer Wholesale, and I
will definitely buy them again.

I have no interest in Hawk, etc.

Mike
