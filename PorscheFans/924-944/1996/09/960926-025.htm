>ps what is a talon ?

It is a car which was made by the Diamond Star Motors plant in Normal,
Illinois.  The cars were a collaboration of Mitsubishi and Chrysler 
Corporation.  They came with a 2.0L 4-cylinder turbo motor that was
virtually bullet proof.  Your balance shaft assembly is licensed from
Mitsubishi and is the same as found in the Talon, which are also notorious
for breaking timing belts.

The Talon has won 6 or 7 years in a row of its class in the SCCA World
Challenge races.  This was their last year though as Chrysler is 
stopping the sales of the Talon at the end of this year or next and is
putting its efforts into the Dodge Stratus touring car project.  The
Archer Brothers used to campaign the Talons.

I also had this happen to me the second day I had my car.  For me it
was a weak battery.  ABS takes a lot of power to operate and I guess the
computer shuts it down if the battery is too weak to operate it safely.
Mine actually shut off after about 10 minuts of driving and the voltage
gauge was back up where it should have been.  I was demonstrating the
power seats to a friend's mother and left the rear window defogger on
without the car on.  This car drains its battery pretty quick.  Next time
it happens, check the battery voltage and see if it was drained by 
something.
