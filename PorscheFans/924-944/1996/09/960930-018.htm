Wheel-Lock Position.  My information says the lock goes on the stud next to 
the valve stem.  I understand "proper" way to install the center cap is to 
have the point on the Porsche shield "aimed" at the valve stem.  This puts 
the point, lock, and valve stem in line.

Stud location for the wheel lock.  For p-cars having brake disks with top 
hats, I understand here that, without a paint mark on the stud, first try 
the stud opposite the 2 Phillips screws holding the disk.  It worked for me 
('87 944)
