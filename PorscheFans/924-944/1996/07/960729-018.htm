You sure the heat shield metal underneath the car is installed
and not rusted-out ?

There are a couple of such things underneath my 924, one is a
sheet-metal that is fixed between the exhaust pipe and the car
unibody. And another thing is heat-insulating blankets that are
pasted onto a couple of parts of the body underneath, closer to
the engine area and, near the clutch bell-housing.

If the car had any clutch or exhaust work done, these things
tend to take a one way ticket off the car, in the hands of
lesser mechanics. One so-and-so that my PO used to take the car
to left the heat cover of the catalytic off, so I am very
careful where I park, esp. in the fall with all that dry leaf
around. (That so and so also did some interesting things, like
install the gear-shift lever flipped over :)
