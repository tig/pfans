>With the key in spot 11 or engine running I can open the roof....arms go up but not 
>back..it looks like I have the two micro switches on the sun roof motor in the trunk 
>area set improperly....they have an adjustment of about 1cm when the screw that holds

>luck...they work...tested with VoH Meter...last attempt I dicovered the 3rd micro switch

>turns it moves a white plastic disc that activates the switches and where the switches

Well, the unwritten spec on adjusting the microswitches is to put them so that the 
drive stops when the roof is full open, or full closed, or the arms are fully retracted,
but not so that they strip out the gears.

I would say that your 3rd microswitch is the culprit. You might jumpering around it to 
see what happens.

Don't forget to try swapping out the relays (same pn, one for up and one for down.)
