>  For a competitive ITA CRX, here are my cost estimates:
>  Safety (roll cage, net, harness, etc.)        $1500-2000
>  Engine (balance, blueprint, overbore, port match, etc.)  $3500 including 
>  $1200 Quaiffe LSD
>  Suspension $1000 - $3000 depending on number of bushings replaced
>  Wheels/tires/misc  $2000
>  
>  How would these costs compare for a Porsche 944?

About the same I think. Wheels will be more if you go for the
Fuchs forged alloys which are the best available. I don't think
you need an LSD in a normal 944, not much power and
lots of traction. A factory LSD would be GREAT but very
hard to find in a cheap car. Too expensive to add either
factory or aftermarket LSD to a 944 I think.
