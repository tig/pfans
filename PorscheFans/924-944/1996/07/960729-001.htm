for a donation to a favorite charity ;-)

Along comes senior cop complete with large torch who proceeds to examine
english 944 and then starts talking loudly to assembled crowd who start
laughing at noil, noil seben jokes.

driving ban - as luck has it I bought a push bike the previous day.
