Hi Gang,

As the Auto-X chairman for Metro NY region, I am always looking for ideas 
to get more people to our events.  So last year I setup a season 
championship for our final event of the year and called it the "Metro 
Runoffs."  It featured the top ten drivers of the year as determined by a 
points system from the prior events.  These driver would all drive the same 
guest car at the Runoffs so the variability of car performance was not an 
issue.  May the best DRIVER win.

I was hoping to get something unusual for the guest car.  Not a Porsche, 
though, since it would put some drivers at an advantage or disadvantage.  
I had some connections for a Viper, a Volvo T5R and a Jaguar.  They all 
fell through.  I finally got a PCA member, Mr. Wrent A. Kar, to donate his 4 
door, automatic, NEON.

Bottom line is the winning driver in the NEON finished second overall at 
the event (though it was a tight course).  A '95 993 was FTD and '95 
bi-turbo was third.  The driver is always the most important part of the car.

Henry
