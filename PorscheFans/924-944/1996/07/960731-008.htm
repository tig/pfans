The reality is that there's _nothing_ like catching a glimpse of a car in a
parking lot out of the corner of your eye, being struck by it, and
thinking, "Now THAT's a great looking vehicle!" and then realizing it's
_your_ car. I do this all the time with my Porsche. Ten years after it left
the showroom, my 951 is still a stunning piece of art ... and one that I
can drive every day!

Case in point: last week I stopped in at the Golden Arches for some
sustenance (in a hurry ;-P), and the server (blonde sixteen year old
female) at the window said "Wow, that's a really nice car." Put a BIG grin
on my face (sure to receive flames galore for this posting ...). I ask you,
how many Neon drivers receive that comment? Porsche - there is no
substitute!

PS - Happens ALL THE TIME with the bike :-)))
