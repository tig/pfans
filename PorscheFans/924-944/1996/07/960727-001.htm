PorscheFans -- If this is any help, I had a supercharger on a late '85 944.
 The VDO pressure gauge hooked to the intake manifold usually read MINUS
8-10 psi under non-boosted cruising conditions.  In other words, it acted
like a normally aspirated engine sucking air. The stock casio mileage gauge
corroborated this reading.  So 0 psi was ambient pressure. I therefore
would assume that "1" on a turbo gauge is also ambient pressure - there
must be a vacuum sometime - even on a turbo.
