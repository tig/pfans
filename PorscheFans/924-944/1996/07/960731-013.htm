Both my '85 944's have been very marginal on battery voltage.

Both my cars ( uk spec ) have shown 11v with the headlights on, and
as a consequence my lights are quite dim. Turn on the heated rear window
with the wipers on, and you can see the wipers visibly slow up.

Quite clearly in the UK spec case the alternator is insufficient for the
task. I suspect that the later cars have larger alternators, or the ABS
would go nuts. I plan to find out soon, as winter driving in Germany by
the light of a miners lamp is not to be recommended.
