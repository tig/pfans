>4) I didn't know about differential lubricant .... I thought it was the same as

One and the same on a 944, but it's a manual tranmission fluid, sometimes called
hypoid gear oil.  And it's not the same as ATF.  On the 944, the diff and the
gear carrier share a single pool of lubricant.  Other cars are different.
