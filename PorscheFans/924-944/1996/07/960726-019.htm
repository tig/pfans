> Hi Folks, I was wondering if anyone had information in regards to the

> My 85/2 Rothmans Challenge 944 (8 valve) was equipped by the factory with
> the M030 suspension that featured adjustable threaded collars and Koni 
> dampers. I have been racing the car for the past few years and would like
> to replace the units as the collars are a bit scruffy.
>

It sounds like those are same struts use on the later cars with the 
M030 suspension. My car (89 Turbo) has the stock double adjustable 
(ride height and shock rebound) Koni. The ride height is adjusted by
threading the spring on the collar. If these are the same as your's
then a lot places sell the Koni units. Paragon Products in Texas
springs to mind. Brace yourself, because the font shocks are about
$400 a piece.
