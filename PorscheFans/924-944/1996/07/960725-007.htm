I assume we're talking about absolute manifold pressure, that is, it's at 
1.0 bar while sitting quietly at night in the garage.  Otherwise, boost 
pressures have really gone up since 1980 when P-cars used 0.7 to 0.8 bar
boost.  I've got my 931 set up at 1.0 bar boost now, or 2.0 absolute.  I guess
that with an effective intercooler and good electronic spark and fuel control, 
1.8 bar boost is not that far-fetched.  If things go wrong though....  =8-( )
