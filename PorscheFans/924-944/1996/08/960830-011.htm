I'm picking up the Carrera Cup 17" wheels today so I decided to take
a close look at the OZ 3-pce wheels on the car at this time before
I trade them in.  It turns out that they are HUGE.  The front's are
17x8.5 with 235/45R17 and the rear's are 17x10 with 255/40R17.  Wow,
I'm kind of downgrading by going to 17x7.5 and 17x9, but I think these
will rub at track days.

Boost pressure.  Hooked up the autometer boost gauge from my Talon
on the Porsche and found the boost pressure exactly where I expected it.
The boost builds up to 14psi, sometimes 15psi.  At high, high rpm's,
like 6500rpm right before the limiter, the boost starts to pull
down to around 11-13psi.  Stock boost for a 951(S) I believe is
10.5 psi, right?

I'm considering a newer, larger turbo for the car to bring the car's
performance one step above my Talon's (in a straight line that is)
and some added fuel.
