Doug:

The early 944's had a six point allen headed screw in the cam drive.  These
were absolutely impossible to remove because of the shallow depth required by
the rotor.  Had to drill off the head of the allen bolt every time to remove.
 Porsche superceeded this part to the 12 point style to allow more surface
area for removal.  Now you only have to drill out 75% of them.  All the
reseal kits come with a new bolt.  Go figure.
