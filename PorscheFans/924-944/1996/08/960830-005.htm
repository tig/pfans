>Am replacing 2 plastic gears in sunroof drive.  First had to 
>remove & replace drive motor and plastic wheel which acts as cam to 
>activate 2 microswithches.  Am not sure of correct position to place 
>the wheel and am concerned that activating sunroof control will just 
>strip plastic gears again .  Service manual does not even mention the 
>sunroof other than a wiring diagram which I am not sure is correct!
>Would appreciate any help.

Simple way to set it up would be to remove the gears, activate the sunroof
to its fully retracted position (the cable will be fully extended towards
the rear of the vehicle). At that point, reinstall the gears, with the arms
in the fully retracted position (so that the sunroof can be removed).

BTW, the factory service manual does have a detailed adjustment procedure
for the microswitches... Haynes does not.
