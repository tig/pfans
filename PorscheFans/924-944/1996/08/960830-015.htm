>I have an 83 944 and as you may know the rear end is raked up to meet bumper
>hieght regs.  Does anyone know how and what parts are needed to level out
>the car?  And maybe lower it as well.  The front doesn't seem to be a
>problem, there's enough spring kits around to solve that problem it's just
>the rear.

It is easy to lower the rear of the car by about 3/4 inch to one inch by
simply loosening the large eccentric bolt on the rear suspension (the 
plates that are driven by the torsion bars).  Trust me, the car will find
its lowest point on its own, and make a large noise while doing it...
