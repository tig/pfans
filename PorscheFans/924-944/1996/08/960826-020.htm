	Hi all,

	Well, I finally had a chance to crawl under my car this weekend
	to take a better look at things. Here's what I found:

	When I took out the inspection plug on the clutch, I couldn't 
	visually see any damage. I started the car and could it spinning.
	So, I took off the inspection plug at the back of the car where
	the shaft enters the transaxle. I saw the transaxle barely trying
	to spin. So, it ends up that the drive shaft is not spinning...
	so....does this confirm the clutch theory? I would assume that if
	it was the ring & pinion, then:

	a) The drive shaft would be spinning all the way up to the transaxle.
	b) The noises would be coming from the rear of the car.

	As it sits, the noise is coming from the clutch, and the driveshaft
	won't turn.
