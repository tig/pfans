The cracking will probably continue. 

The good news is that it is possible to fix it but in similar situations on
other cars, I have not had any luck with the aftermarket products available;
results just look amateurish. I would recommend that you find a local guy who
repairs and re-dyes vinyl and leather for the used car dealers. They can do
incredible things with their truckfull of tricks. One guy I talked to said "I
can guarantee that the dash will never crack in THAT spot again." Talk to a
dealer who sells higher-end used cars (Mercedes, Porsche, BMW). These guys
usually work out of their pickup trucks. 

To get at the cracks, it might be advisable to remove and reinstall the
windshield. Another job for another specialist working out of his pickup
truck.

Let us know what you find out.
