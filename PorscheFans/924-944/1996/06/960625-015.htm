I think the manual says 30K miles or something. I don't wait that long.

Tricks to fight gravity? You can use a hand pump which screws onto the
bottle of lubricant. Or a suction gun. What works just as good is a length
of 1/2" plastic tubing run out over the wheel with a funnel stuck in or
taped to the end. Warm the bottles of lubricant in a sink full of hot water
while you're draining the old stuff out. It will make a big difference when
you go to pour it down the tube.

Make sure you get the filler plug out before you pop the drain. That way you
won't be Porsche-less if you can't break it free.
