Hi Mark,


You might want to try a belt dressing on the power-steering and A/C Generator
belts.  This would be just to make sure that they are not squealing.
 Hopefully, these are the belts that are making the noise, not something else.


You might want to open the hood while the noise is present so you can locate
from which general area the squealing is coming from.

The other possibility is that there are some bearings that are making the
noise.  Belt rollers, water pump, alternator, they all have bearings.  If the
bearings are making the noise, you better replace whatever it is.
