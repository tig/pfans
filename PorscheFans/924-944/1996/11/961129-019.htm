>OK .. My question is that in the kit I have a gasket 951 104 374 02 which seems to
>fit a 944T 1986-88 as stated in the "Parts and Technical Reference Catalog". I was
>expecting the gasket 944 104 374 14 as stated in the same catalog. MY friend just
>bought a bit for a 1988 Turbo S and got exactly the same Head Gasket.

Know what I hate?  When someone asks a question, and I just know I've seen the
answer somewhere but cannot put my finger on it.  This was one of those; found the
answer in the '85 944 Turbo introduction Technik.  You can use the 951 head gasket
on normally aspirated cars, but not the opposite way 'round.  It is apparently
distinguished by a different fire ring and different silicone bead.  However, this info
is eleven years old, and both the aspiro and turbo parts have no doubt been
superseded several times.  I think you're OK, though.
