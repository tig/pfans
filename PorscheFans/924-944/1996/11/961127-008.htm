If you don't have an owner's manual, get one.  (from mail order or local
dealer).

If your fuses and relays are under the dash, the manual prints out the use and
function of each one.

If your fuses and relays are under the hood, by the driver's side (little black
box right next to the windscreen, opposite the battery.  The uses are printed
on the inside of the cover.  (just pop the clasps on the cover and take it off)

A common problem (well, common in the old style interiors) is a disconnected or
dirty switch. Pop the switches out and clean the connections (if you have the
old cosole (pre '85 1/2, which you don't), the switches can be pried out.  I
think the new console requires that you remove the console to get at the
connections (or see if you can reach them through the empty (or filled) slot
under the radio.

But, check the fuses first (easy check)
