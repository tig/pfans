Farzaan, 
I doubt if your  brake prop valve is misfunctioning. There really is nothing
in there but a spring but I guess it could fail, I just never heard of that
problem. 
BTW these cars have heavy front bias. In track situations you will wear out
your front brakes 4 to 5 times faster than the rear. If you replace the valve
make sure you get one from a 928 which will give you a bit more rear brake
bias and still not be dangerous.
