The default answer is that the fresh air blower is not serviceable.  However,
I had your problem with my own a number of years ago.  I tore down the
motor, cleaned everything, and lubricated with Bosch distributor grease,
which has the consistency of earwax.  I had tried lighter lubricants, first
lithium grease then wheel bearing grease, but they did not solve the
problem.  The distributor grease did the trick, applied to the bushings in
the motor case.  Hope you too have this luck!
