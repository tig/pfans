Could be lousy tires.  My 944S has lots of rubber, but does the same thing
when cornering.  It has the M474 sport suspension and is a bit stiff.  The
rear tires are Falken 225 X 50 16", and don't hold a candle to my normally
aspirated 944, standard suspension with the same size tires and wheels, but
have Perelli P7 tires.  My 944 (does have Turbo S sway bars front and rear)
is a far better handling car... Till I replace the tires on my S, that is.

What size and make rubber is on your car?  What type of suspension do you
have?  Look in the rear trunk, behind the carpet,  on the drivers side,
next to the spare tire.  The white sticker has all your options listed in
Porsche code.
