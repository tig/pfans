On Mon, 30 Dec 1996, Barry Lenoble wrote:

If you encounter stuck/stripped CV joint bolts, use this trick from _Poor
Richard's Rabbit Book_, or _How to Keep Your Volkswagen Alive_: 

Use a pair of Vise Grip locking pliers on the bolt *while* you have the
tool inside the bolt head, so the CV bolt doesn't get squashed all out of
shape when you squeeze the pliers.  The initial trick is to have the
inside of the bolt as clean as possible so you can get the 12-point tool
all the way in for maximum grip inside the head.
