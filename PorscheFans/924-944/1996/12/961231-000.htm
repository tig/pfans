I recommend getting the heads of those creatures sterilized!  Any dirt, or
grit left inside the head can also damage the tool, which in turn,
increases the chance of stripping out the fastener.

A small dental pick that I get free from my Dentist (that's the only thing
I get free, all the "discomfort" is paid for), works great.  I follow that
with a blast of Carb cleaner and a Q-tip to remove any remaining residue.
It also helps remove all the oils as well.  Keeps you from slip sliding
away.
