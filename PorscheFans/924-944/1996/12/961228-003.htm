> There exists locally a set of phone dial rims and tires from a 928,

>are beautiful.
>
>The owner says all 4 wheels are 7.5" wide.
>
>Will those wheels fit my 84 944  or will they require spacers other than
>the ones that came with my cookie cutters.
>
>Any advice pro or con will much appreciated.
>

Look on the inside of the wheels. On one of the spokes, you 
will see a stamping, ET 52 or ET 23.

If the stamping says ET 23, then you can use them on your 84 944.
If the stamping says ET 52, you will need not only wheel spacers,
but also longer studs (to extend through the spacers). Plan on 
spending about $10 per stud (remember that there are 20 of them
on your car.) plus about $50 per spacer.

At those rates, it doesn't look like such a good deal anymore...
