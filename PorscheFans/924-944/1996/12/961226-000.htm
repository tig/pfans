Glad to help.

The sun roof has a number of limit switches located on the driver's side of
the car under the carpet that runs from the edge of the rear hatch down to
the trunk floor.  That is, it is directly opposite the side of the fuel
filler cap.  You will have to pull the plastic snap on clips that hold the
carpet on to remove the carpet pannel.  Once removed, you should be able to
see a circular white plastic cam (wheel tucked up under the hatch lip where
only a monkey can reach it) which has a double switch assembly (two micro
switches).  Most likely one or more of those switches is out of alignment.
You can check the integrity of the switches with a continuity meter (ohm
meter) if they become suspect.

Those switches are a barrel of fun to adjust.  It isn't rocket science, but
patience and persistence will be required.

Good luck, let me know how you make out.
