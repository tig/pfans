Reason might not prevail here, for headgaskets blow in part because of
overheating and resultant flexing of the head, so perhaps yours needs to
warm up to flex enough.  And there is no such thing as white SMOKE from any
engine I ever saw.  Black is fuel SMOKE.  Blue is oil SMOKE.  White is
coolant VAPOR.  You should be able to smell the vapor.
