Regarding CV Joint Bolts:

It really helps a lot to clean out the 12-point star heads with a small screw 
driver or other suitable tool before you engage the special socket.  Then, what 
I do is tap the socket gently with a hammer to make sure that it is seated 
optimally, before I apply torque to remove the bolt.  With luck you won't ever 
strip a head.  When I haven't been careful enough, I've actually had to drill 
some out...what a pain...
