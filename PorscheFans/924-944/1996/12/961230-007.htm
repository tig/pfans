Hi Folks,

This morning I heard an interesting story from my mechanic.  As
some of you know, we've been water-logged here in the northwest.
One of the shop's clients drove his 944 through some high water,
and soaked the air filter.  One cylinder inhaled a mouthful, and
since water is incompressible, bent a rod!  Luckily his insurance
company paid the bill (water damage ...).

So be warned -- don't drive through water substantially above the
bottom of the skirt!
