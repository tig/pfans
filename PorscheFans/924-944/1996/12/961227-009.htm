At 03:45 PM 12/27/96 -0500, Marc Belanger wrote:
>I've been using 225/50-15's on 7 x 15 phone dials all four corners of my
>'86 944 with great success.  My friend with an '87 924S was
>wondering what would be the best performance combination for his car.
>Does anybody have any suggestions as to a good fit(i.e. wheels and
>tires) for a driver education weekend performance setup for the 924S?
>

7x15 inch wheels (with the 52 mm offset) should fit, but the tire size
might have to be limited to 215/60R15, in order to clear the (narrower)
fenders.

If he had access to a set of 7x15 wheels, he should try out what might
fit his car before he spends any money.
