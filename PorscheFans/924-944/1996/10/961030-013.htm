Farzaan -

Driveability with the Powerhaus setup is as good as stock with the only
exception being the exhaust odor.  I don't know if this is a result of the Mass
Air Flow chip set or the missing catalytic converter.  I don't suspect a fouled
O2 sensor, but I know I should check it anyway.

Initially, the car stalled while breaking hard into Big Bend at Lime Rock on
each lap.  The problem turned out to be a broken popoff valve.  The popoff and
boost control valves are relatively cheap and somewhat prone to failure.  A
simple vacuum tester can verify their health.

Good Luck,
