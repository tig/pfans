If it sounds like a groan, there is cavitation caused by air in steering
fluid. You can verify this by looking at fluid reservoir for  foaming fluid.
Air gets in through loose hose connections  from reservoir to mechanism (easy
fix), or old seals in mechanism itself. 
Occurs especially in cold weather when seals are shrunk. Happened on my  1987
924S at about 92k. Mechanic rebuilt, or at least reconditioned steering mech.
with Audi kit.  Temporary measure for bleeding air out of system is to turn
steering from full left to full right.   All in all, not a crippling problem
as long as fluid level is maintained.
