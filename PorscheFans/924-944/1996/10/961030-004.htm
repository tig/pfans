Hi, David,

Removing the speedometer from the instrument cluster requires removal of 
the steering wheel, the multifunction switch, and then the two screws at 
the top of the tach and fuel gauge pods.

Other than that, it's easy.

The odometer failure is most likely due to the nylon gear on the steel shaft
either being broken or having slid on the shaft. You will have to dismantle
the speedometer to correct this, or, as an alternative, good used speedometers
are available from reputable dismantlers...  :-)

Good Luck!
