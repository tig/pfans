> Can anyone give me any tips for removing / replacing the rear speakers
> in '86?  Removing the trim from the window seal is of a particular
> concern.  Also, any recommendations on a replacement radio?
 
I replaced my rear 6x9 speakers by removing a few of panel screws
so that I could bend the panel just far enough back to fit my
hand through.  Then, I took a very thin flat blade screwdriver 
and fitted it through the front grill opening to get to the four 
phillips head screws which hold the speaker, using my other hand 
to guide it to the screws.  You have to angle the driver through
the grille to reach the screws. 

I am told that you can break the panel by bending it, but I didn't 
have to bend it very far, and I didn't have to mess with the window 
seal.
