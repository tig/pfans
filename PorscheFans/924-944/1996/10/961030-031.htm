I got my headlight motor working perfectly.  I assumed that because 
the motor was getting current, the fuse was OK.  Wrong, there are two 
circuits, and the fuse was out in the circuit that closes the lights.  
They were installed correctly (some small amount of solace!!).  Moral 
of the story, check all fuses, and relays, duh! steve meltzer, 
re-resurrected '88 ts.
