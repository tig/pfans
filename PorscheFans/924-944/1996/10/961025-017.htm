Thanks to advice from the list, I replaced the coolant temperature 
switch that had gone bad on my 85.5 944.   After cleaning everything 
up, I tried to follow the procedures in the Haynes manual for 
bleeding air from the cooling system.

I started the engine, heater on, added fluid to the marks, opened the screw,
waited for the engine to reach operating temp, got some steam, etc. 
from the screw, waited until it seemed like just fluid, tightened the 
screw, and added some fluid to the reservoir.

Operating temperatures seem OK, fans work OK, but I don't have heat 
from the vents.  Arrgh.  I went back to the bleed screw, opened it, 
and nothing seemed to come out, no steam, nothing.  I made a test 
drive, including some idleing, and the engine temp seemed to be 
normal, but no heat.

Any advice appreciated.  Thanks!
