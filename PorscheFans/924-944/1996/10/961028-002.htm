There are differing opinions on snow tires, but I lived in Boston last year
(you remember, the blizard of the century) and I had Blizzaks on my wife's
Nissan Quest, and Goodyear GW's (which are available speed-rated) on my
Mitsubishi Galant VR4.

I'd never get Blizzaks for a Porsche. I'd get the Goodyear GW's.

P.S. I've been driving the car with the GW's year round, and they are
acceptable year-round tires for a "salt car."
