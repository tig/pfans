As I mentioned a couple of weeks back - I have had a chopped open throttle
switch on my car for nearly 2 years, and have had no associated moisture
problems.

I have had problems with water in the distributor cap - there is a plastic
plate behind the rotor arm, these get broken or removed allowing water
that gets on to the cam belt to be thrown into the cap - this will
certainly give your symptoms.

I am not certain, but I think you can disconnect the throttle switch and
the car will still run.
