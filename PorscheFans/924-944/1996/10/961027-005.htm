Y'all, Jerry W (Jerry944T@aol.com) has pointed out an oversight in my
previous posting, in which I suggested that the Fabcar a-arms were fine
for street use.  Not entirely true because of the lack of a rubber boot
around the ball joint, protecting the ball from the elements.  Thanks
for the correction, Jerry.

BTW, this is a concern in general with upgrades which include, for example,
heim joints in sway bar drop links.  You need to keep an eye on them, and
be prepared to replace them when play or other deterioration develops.  I
have double-heim droplinks front and rear on my car, and have had to replace
a couple of the heims over time.
