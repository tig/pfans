you wrote:
>Does anyone have an easy fix to allow heel and toe on my '88 TS?? I've 
>gotten the accelerator pedal to the right height, but it is about 
>1/4-1/2" too far to the right.  Since the pedals are setup differently 
>than on a 911, I am not sure if the "3rd foot" (or whatever that 
>overpriced device is called) will work.  There is no doubt in my mind 
>that this technique is quite useful on the track, so I'd appreciate 
>any assistance.  TIA. steve meltzer resurrected '88 944 ts

Sounds like you’re almost there.  You will need to disconnect the accelerator
actuator rod and remove the pedal.  Now, simply add about three or four
washers over the right hand pedal mounting stud.  Then, bend the accelerator
actuator rod to the left with either large channel locks or a web belt
wrapped around it.  Make sure the accelerator actuator rod is in alignment
with the pedal.  Re-install the pedal and enjoy.  I've done this mod myself
and it does help.  However, you may have to experiment with the exact number
of washers.
