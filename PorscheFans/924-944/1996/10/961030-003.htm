>My left side window motor has failed, and my right side window switch,

Hi, David,

Rebuilding the motor is not an option. Used motors are available from 
reputable dismantlers    :-)

Removing the window motor is as follows:

1) Remove inner door panel.
2) Mark the position of all the 10 mm bolt heads on the door shell.
3) Remove the bolts holding the window regulator, while holding the window
   glass so that it doesn't fall.
4) Remove the window glass through the upper part of the door shell.
5) Disconnect the regulator electrical connector.
6) Remove the regulator through the upper access hole (towards the rear of the
   door).
7) Remove the motor from the regulator scissors.
8) When reinstalling, adjust the regulator and the horizontal slider bar so
   that the window closes straight.

Good Luck!
