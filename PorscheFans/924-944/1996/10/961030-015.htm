There is only one rear semi-trailing arm for the 87 944-series cars, to
the best of my knowledge.  I put MY86 951 brakes on my MY87 944S some time
ago.  You need:

 - the callipers
 - the rotors
 - front spindles
 - mounting hardware

The mounting hardware includes spacers which I believe are for the rear
callipers.  You need four, p/n 999.025.131.02, list was $4.90 last I
checked.  I don't have the p/n's for the bolts and spring washers, but
you should definitely get them.

The hoses and lines are the same and need not be changed.  I should say
that the fiche called out different rear lines (rigid tubing) between the
rubber hoses and the callipers.  However, after obtaining the parts (one
side had to come all the way from Germany), I could see zero difference
between aspiro and turbo lines, holding them together.
