Hello Stephen;

I think upgrading to 17" wheels has been a wash.  The 17" wheels are
definitely heavier that the 16" forged  wheels from the factory, therefore I
feel the car accelerates a tad bit slower.  The 17" wheels have a slight
larger diameter than the 16" wheels so top end is slightly increased.  The
17" wheels are 7.5" wide in front versus 7" stock so I think it understeers
a little less.  The 9" rears have a shallower offset so the rear track it
increased a little.  I think the lower profile tires give me a better
traction on the track, but are stiffer on the street.  I did it mainly
because I liked the looks of the 5-spoke C2 Turbo wheels better than the
7-spoke design.  Your experience may vary.
