Most common leaks are from the two hoses as they enter the P/S reservoir.
Replace the solid band factory clamps with "Zebra" clamps, preferably with
rolled edges that won't cut the hose over time.  Porsche recognized that a
mistake had been made here and offered the clamps per a tech bulletin.

There is also a similar hose clamp on a banjo fitting on the P/S pump.  Same
problem, same solution.

Finally, the P/S pump will itself leak.  There is a big o-ring between its
case halves which is serviceable, if you can find a new o-ring.  If this is
the case for you, I'd ask Jay Pineau whether he can help.  He rebuilds
water pumps and advertizes in Panorama.
