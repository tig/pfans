At the suggestion of one of the members of the list (Thank you, Jim Pascha) I have just browsed 
through the above-named catalog received yesterday from my friendly Porsche parts manager.  It is 
an absolute necessity!  

The catalog has nice exploded diagrams, clear photos, current part numbers, helpful hints, update 
info, several tech bulletins and is generally loaded with good info for the paltry Porsche price 
of $9.95.  

Intended to supplement the Workshop Manual sets, it has no electrical diagrams and little text, 
but along with the owners' manual has enough info for many to do some repairs.  I'm sure there is 
not as much info as in the parts fiche, but it's a lot easier to read - nevermind skipping the 
expense and bulk of the special reader needed for the parts fiches. 

With actual part numbers, at least I won't have to pester the parts guy to find the part number 
when I shop around for a part. .
