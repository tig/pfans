Hi Tony
	It is possible for the fuel pressure regulator to fail intermitantly! It
happened to me! Sometimes my car ran great other times it ran like crap and
sounded like the fuel pump was about to explode! The FPR is cheap at $70 and
should be replaced even if just for a preventative measure. When it finally
fails, it will dump alot of fuel onto your hot engine! The FPR took care of my
problem. 
	As far as an eradic coolant gauge. Check the connections on the back of the
instrument pannel for the gas/coolant gauge. I had to replace my two gauges to
really fix the problem! The gauge costs about $100.
