My local p-dealer quoted 2 hours for a front wheel alignment, however after
fitting new shocks and track rod ends I needed a full wheel alignment check
and had it done by a good body shop. They spent 4 hours getting it right and
charged about half that Porsche would have. Porsche also quoted 2-4 hours to
include the full alignment. The final cost depends on the time to carry out
all the adjustments. It was money well spent.
