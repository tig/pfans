Just a little more stereo experience for your info.  I had an S2 with ADS
4x6 plate speakers in the doors.  Sounded great, but needed replacing
several times in the three years I owned the car( I don't listen to music
that loud either).  Also, to get good sound you really need to install 6"
or 6x9 in the rear storage bin lids.  Takes almost no storage space and
bounces bass off the glass very nicely.

Just my experience.
