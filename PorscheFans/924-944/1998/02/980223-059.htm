If memory serves well, you take this highly specialized tool called a
hacksaw!(really)  But I think the recommendation was to put two blades
adjacent to each other in it so you can make a wider slot, don't use one
blade. Then cut one slot on the middle of the pad on the short axis (not
length wise) at least halfway down through the thickness of the pad.  This
will let some gases from the pad vent. 

I have not done this myself, so please take it easy on me if it does not
return the results you expected.  I cannot find the original posts right
now or I would forward them to you.
