Why oh why do you guys not bring these cars up to B.C.???  Well, right now
is kind of bad with the exchange rate and all, but when things return to
normal, Turbo S's are worth a fortune up here.  There are so many buyers
waiting for a decent, non-abused car to show up at a dealer.

Prices for 944T's around B.C.:
89 944T, 44k km        $30,000 (US$21,400)
87 944T, 80k mi        $17,000 (US$12,100)
89 944T, 125k km       $21,000 (US$15,000)
89 944T, 120k km       $19,500 (accident damage listed on insurance)
88 944TS, 50k mi       $23,000 (US$16,428)

Keep in mind that we pay an additional 14% tax on top of those prices.

All of the above cars were decently maintained cars.  It's pretty hard to
find a really abused car here.  I've noticed that lately a bunch of 86's
have appeared that are not well taken care of, usually a fresh 16-year old
bought them, did a few too many clutch drops.  Facing a $3000 repair 
bill at the two decent shops around town, they put it up for sale
after swallowing the repair.  The only problem is, the car has decayed
around the driver with the leather in poor condition, etc.

If your car is low mileage, it will sell for a high price up here.  If it
has high mileage, don't bother because the price is likely the same as
down there.
