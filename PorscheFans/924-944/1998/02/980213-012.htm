You are technically correct. In 1988, Porsche offered two 944 Turbo
models: the 951 and the 951S. In 1989, they only made 951S-type cars.
They were called the 951. Go figure. Bottom line: '88 951S = '89 951.

However, many '89 951 owners refer to their cars as 951S because it it
actually much less confusing to do so. You aren't relying on the
listener to know this minutia.
