>     
>          5)  The oil pressure gauge stayed over 4 bar for the entire test 
>     drive (about 1/2 hour), even at idle.  Is this normal?  

Yes, the car will run right at 5 for the first 15 minutes or so.  Only then
when the engine is at idle it should go down to around 3 on the gauge.
This means you have reached a good warm running temperature, and that you
can now feel free to explore higher RPMs and more boost.

>He states he uses
>     Mobil 1 10W/30 motor oil.  

He should use 15-50 (unless you are in a much different climate.)

>Also when I looked at the oil filter it was much 
>     longer than the factory filter, and had a gray outer shell (I could not 
>     make out the name brand).
>     
>          6)  There was a ticking noise from the top of the engine.  The 
>     owner states it is the fuel system.  Is he correct?

Could be.  Try this also.  Pop the hood up while it idles.  Rev the engine
from under the hod and then quickly release it.  If you here some loud
clakity-clack noise from the cam cover you are hearing your belts slap it.
This means you need new belts ASAP (improper tension).  Of course, you
should get new belts anyway unless they were done recently and that it is
documented....
