question of the tube and bellhousing, she admitted she could not answer my 
question and said it was a question for their technical hotline.  Problem is, 
she could not give me the number for this tech hotline.  It's a service offered 
to dealers for a fee and is not made public.

I think I remember hearing (or reading) that the US 5-speed version of the 924 
NA for 1979 had the same tranny as the 5-speed 924 and 931 in 1980.  I suspect 
the european 5-speed 931 in 1979 has the same tranny also.  The $64,000 
question is, are the torque tubes also the same?  In the back of my mind I also
seem to recall some discussion about stronger gears in the turbo application.

Any info on this would be greatly appreciated.  Please also send a copy of your
