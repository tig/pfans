Not to take too much money out of Dr. Timmons' or Ken Myers' (I/O Port)
pockets:

You can make a camera mount very similar to the I/O Port design for about $30.
The actual mount is a Boden tripod head which can be purchased for less than
$25 mail order from a camera store like Camera World in Portland.  Comes with
the Sorbothane damper and everything.

Then you buy a muffler clamp and use a small piece of 3/16" steel to create a
mounting bracket.  I made one after buying the first when I ran two cars back
in '95. Took me about 30 mminutes to make.

Sold it for $40 at a swap meet when we started to run only the one car.

If you are not "handy" then buy one from Dr. Timmons.
