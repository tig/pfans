> If the stock order and serial number of a Porsche key is known, can

> Macray Co. in Wayne N.J.  Any help would be greatly appreciated.  Thanks.

In the US, if you supply a Porsche dealer with your VIN and your
registration or other proof of ownership, they will send the information
to PCNA to cut you a new key.  I did this with my '89.. I would think this
would apply to earlier models too.  Please verify with your dealer.  This
assumes the lock cylinders on your car are original.  Otherwise you will
have a non-functional key.  You can even order a new lighted key head.  If
you order this, ask for the blade style key.. as the standard molded 'VW'
style key will not fit.
