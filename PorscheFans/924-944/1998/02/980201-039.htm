If you have, or the previous owner has, replaced the windshield wiper
switch, there is a tab which must be removed before installing for the
intermittent wipers to work.  If this is the case, you need to remove the
steering wheel and turn signal switch to get at the wiper switch in order to
remove the tab.  I believe Mr. Haynes details the disassembly.   Its not a
big job.  Good luck.  Lee  larchie@lander.edu
