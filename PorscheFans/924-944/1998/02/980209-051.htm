Well, I got a chance to try and install the speedbleeders, two of the
ones I bought are the wrong size. I ordered:

2 part number SB7100 (for rear) and
2 part number SB8125 (for front)

The SB7100s were correct for the rear, and the fronts need the same part
number.  I'll be checking on Speed Bleeders return policy now. I'll keep
everyone posted...

The funny thing is that the front bleeders took an 8mm wrench and the
rears took a 7mm wrench, but every other dimension on them was the
same.... very odd.
