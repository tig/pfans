Dhruv, you have a worn synchronizer ring for second gear.  You can 
either have the transmission serviced, or you can live with it for a 
while by shifting a little more slowly.

If you continue to grind on engagement of 2nd gear, you will be wearing 
down and damaging the dog teeth on second gear, and the engaging teeth 
on its operating sleeve (AKA synchronizer hub).  Which means they will 
have to be replaced (not cheap) when you do get around to servicing the 
transmission.
