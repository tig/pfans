The dreaded red gear has a possibility of becoming misaligned again after
repair.  Fashion a spacer sleeve to fit between the gear and the housing
the shaft runs through.  I used the top 3mm of one of those  expandable
plastic sleeves used in drywall to give a screw something to bite into.
The size of the hole was perfect.
