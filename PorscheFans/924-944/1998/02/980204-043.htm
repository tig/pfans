	I have a 1986 951 with factory FUCHs 7"s & 8"s wheels. I have Dunlap D40M2's
225/50ZR16's on the front wheels and 245/45ZR16's on the rear wheels. My car

problems with rubbing at all. I would reccomend the 225 &245 set up.

wrong tire sizes!  Go with the 225/50 & 245/45's, these are the correct tire
sizes!
