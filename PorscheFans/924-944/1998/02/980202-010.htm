I have had the oppotunity to do about 5 or 6 belt changes in the last
few weeks.

I noticed a major change in belt construction.  Both the TRW and Gates
belts I have used now have a fairly substantial steel belt running
through them.

On the cars I did the belts on all had som kind of cotton-looking
reinforcement which was easily cut through with a pair of Dykes (side
cutters).

Anyway, I beleive this explains the lack of belt breakage stories of
late.

In general I use genuine belts because they are inexpensive and I like
the little card that comes with them reminding the customer to return
within 2-3000 miles for a retension.

At least one of these cars had been done in the last 8 months and still
had the older style belt.
