I simply took a razor blade and carefully cut & scraped the black paint
or trim away.  Cut it back to wherever the black gasket or the fram
begins.  Be very careful not to damage your rear window defog "wires". 
The end result of all this tedious work has been well worth it in my
opinion.  The glass hatch has a much "cleaner" look to it.  You will
hardly notice that the painted trim is not there.
