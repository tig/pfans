> I think your saying that the tapping test produces a broad spectrum

> This makes sense to me, however this method can still be used to
> check for some signal generation and whether or not that signal is

In his book, _Bosch Fuel Injection and Engine Management_,
Charles Probst gives a similar procedure for testing knock
sensors in Motronic systems, only he recommends revving the
engine to 2500rpm and checking the timing to make certain 
that it retards or "jumps out of its window" when the knock 
sensor is lightly tapped. He says that if it doesn't, then
the knock sensor is "most likely" bad.
