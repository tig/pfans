Walter, I wondered the exact same thing when replacing my tie rods.  My
local Porsche shop said to use a hammer on the end of the wrench instead of
just pulling on it.  I guess this simulates using an impact wrench and does
not put as much load into the steering gear.  Of course, you can't measure
the torque this way so it requires a guess as to what is tight enough.  The
locking feature on the tie rods seems pretty secure though.  I did this
several years ago and have had no problem with the rack since.  Good luck.
