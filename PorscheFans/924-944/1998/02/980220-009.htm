Ross, 
   There are actually two sets of microfiche which have been discussed.
Once set is a 944 factory workshop manual. The other is the 944 parts
manuals. The workshop manual is on 4" x 6" microfiche which most microfiche
readers will accept. The parts microfiche is on a larger microfiche (can't
remember the exact size) which normally requires a differenct reader.
However, some people (myself included) take the large microfiche and cut
them into sections so they can be used on a standard size reader. Many
places have the microfiche. You might try Europroducts (1-800-962-0911) or
(www.porsche-911.com).  You won't find the microfiche listed in the parts
on the home page but, drop them an e-mail and tell them what you want. They
respond very quickly. All of the microfiche are readily available and
Porsche dosen't care if they get sold to the customer. Your local dealer is
either uninformed or he's being untruthful. If you need anymore help
getting the microfiche let me know.
