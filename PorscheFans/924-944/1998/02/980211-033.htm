On the 944/968 there is a float gauge that shows when the oil is below the ½
way between two marks. It is a lock and hold switch so that cutting the
power for a few mins will not effect it.

 On a 944/68 when you are at the fill mark you are in trouble, the PCA
correct fill line is about ½ qt over the fill line, more if you are tracking
the car!  

So when that light goes on add a Qt.

And remember that all 944/68 motors use oil (make that consume). The spec
book will tell you what the valid range of oil consummation is.
