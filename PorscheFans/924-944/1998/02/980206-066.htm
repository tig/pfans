On Fri, 6 Feb 1998, Donovan Hebard wrote:

> A question for any electrical/stereo guys on the list, my system (all
> aftermarket) has developed a high-pitched whining sound which rises
> and falls with engine RPM.  Any ideas?

The easiest way to eliminate alternator whine is to make sure all your
components (head unit, amplifiers, equalizer, crossover) are grounded
together at one point on the body - preferably under a big bolt existing
on the body, or all at the negative battery terminal.  Otherwise,
alternator whine will be carried on the voltage difference caused by the
slight resistances between different ground points on the body.

Or, one of your existing grounds make be making bad contact.  Check all 
your connections.
