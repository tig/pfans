I changed the plugs in my 944 a month or so ago.
I decided the try NGK. I have found NGK to foul less than others
in 2 stroke engines and decided to try them in the 944.
I used whatever type was on the NGK conversion chart (I can't
remember the type)
The car runs well except seems to be using about 10% more fuel!
Hot days (ie the air cond) account for most of this but
I'll be switching back to bosch.
Has anyone else used the NGK's? They cost about the same.
I haven't found the fancy multi-electrode bosch plugs
the closest my local shop has is those plugs with the funny
electrode (spitfire) but at around $25 each that's waaaayyy
to much for me!
