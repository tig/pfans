John Asp et al:

The article(s) you are looking for are:

924 Bolt-on Bonanza: Richard Holderner, European Car, May 1996, Page   .
Air filter, exhaust, valve timing changes, big throat throttle body all
fail to increase power. Ignition timing advance yields a few hp. Results
using dyno. Head is power restriction.

924 Bolt-on Bonanza: Richard Holdener, European Car, July 1996, page 43.
Cylinder head is major power limitation. Installation of Eurorace =91big
valve' head, cam and extrude honing gained 15 hp dyno at rear wheels
with stock exhaust and 21 hp with 2 =BC inch exhaust. Replacing CIS with
Weber carburetors apparent increase of power but not dyno confirmed.
Interesting table of airflow through head comparing stock and modified.

Several members of this list (and the '924 list' kept by Vaughan Scott
) feel that the throttle body has indeed helped. I wish
someone with the mod had taken or would go backwards and take some
objective measurements to define the effect of adding the throttle body.
