I am the one who put in the response to the trans oil. I installed AMS
OIL gear lube in my 83 944 about 10 or more years ago. It is SAE 75W-90
made by AMSOIL Inc. Superior, WI 54880. I got it from a local AMS oil
dealer who ran a ad in a local paper. The shifting improved greatly -
especially when cold. I have received quite a few E-Mails since my
posting in the 944 group. I have owned Porsches for over 30 years and
think the current 944 is the best. I have one of the early version 944's
with the sport supension system etc. plus many upgrades since purchasing
from the original owner in '84.
