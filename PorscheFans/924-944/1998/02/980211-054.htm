Just FYI:

The latest 951(S) control arm part numbers are as follows:

944.341.927.031
944.341.928.031

These numbers supercede the old numbers:

951.341.027.031
951.341.028.031

My shop has not been able to get 944.341.927.031, so they called all over
the place including Germany and were told that they would have to use
951.341.027.031 until the parts supply runs out, then they would start
shipping the new 944.etc unit.

By visually looking at the new control arm, I did side-by-side comparisons.
This is what I noticed:

- the new arm has "webbing" I guess to make it stronger
- larger buildup of aluminum around the ball joint
- larger buildup of aluminum around the anti-roll bar mounting hole
- anti-roll bar mounting hole is enlarged on new unit
- the ball joint bottom is larger and hangs below the control arm hole (hard
  to describe;  the 951 unit sits flush at the bottom)

Otherwise, the dimensions are the same.  The new unit is stamped as a 1997
manufacture, the 951 part has a 1988 stamp.

Anyways, hope the mismatch parts don't affect the car in any way.
