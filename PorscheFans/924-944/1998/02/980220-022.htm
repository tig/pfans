Hey, you do know that you can make your 944 lamps remain in the "up"
position. Pop open the bonnet, look to the left side of the engine bay
just forward of the ignition coil to the left of the valve cover of the
engine, you'll see the headlamp motor. It should have a rubber weather
boot covering the top. Pull that off and rotate the knurled knob until
the headlamps are up. You can then conduct your work. If you already
knew about this, I apologize.......I didn't mean to insult your
intelligence.
