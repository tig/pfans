Having owned a 924, 944, 944S2 and now a 964 C4, I have to agree that the
944 series are better all around cars.  They ride smoother, get better gas
mileage, have a LOT more cargo room, cost less, etc, BUT the 911's are, in
my opinion, the best car in the world to 'drive'.  If you are on a twisty
country road or autocross track the 944's are great fun, but not the
complete experience that the 911's are.
