A reminder for someone who has been there.. be sure you get a low profile
or short allen key or socket.. at least with my '89 there isn't enough
clearance between the muffler and the transmission to the Sears allen
wrench (the L shaped type) in there.  I went to a junky little tool store
and found a 'VW transmission plug tool.' It is essentially an allen key,
but the 3/8 drive fits right inside it.. making it very short. $1.95. :)

The earlier 944's have somewhat smaller mufflers (judging from the 
underbody photo in the Haynes manual), so this may not be an issue.
