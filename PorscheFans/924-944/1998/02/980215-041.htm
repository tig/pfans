Tim, no you don't have to get all of the tappets done.  It should be easy
to isolate which one is the offending tappet (via listening with aid of a
screwdriver touching the cam carrier to your ear).  I had to replace a bad
tappet at about 100K miles and now have 170K.  The originals are still
quiet with the exception of a little noise for 2 - 3 seconds at cold start
up.  As I recall, one tappet was about $60 U.S., so replacing 8 of them is
significantly more expensive.  As for the cam/balance belts, unless you
just recently changed them I would suggest you put new ones in.  Also, if
the water pump and rollers are original this would be a opportune time to
replace them as well.
