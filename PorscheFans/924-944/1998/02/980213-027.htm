> I'm in the middle of a brake job on my 86 951 and am curious about what type
> of wheel bearing grease I should use.  The current grease is a pink-orange

That sounds like Redline synthetic. It's pinkish. Note that you should
not mix different types of grease. If you don't have Redline (or if it's
different stuff), then you should remove all the old grease, and use
fresh. I've used SWEPCO, Redline, and good ole whateverthelocalpartsplace
had for wheel bearings. I haven't noticed any difference.

> Also, am I nuts not to do the bearings while I'm replacing the rotors?  They
> seem to be in pretty good shape.

I dunno. I've reused the bearings for a while without any bad effects.
The outer bearing is a snap to do, and it's pretty cheap. The inner
bearing takes a lot more work.
