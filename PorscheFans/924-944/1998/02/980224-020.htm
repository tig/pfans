I have and 89 944T and a 95 993 C4.  I agree with you in that the 911
has the more classic lines and subjectively looks better.  The 993 is a

end is lighter and the steering is a little quicker.  The 993 has less

catalytic bypass and sport mufflers).  I can drive either car any time I
choose,  the 993 usually loses.  The 944 is more fun, I love it.  Maybe
if I had a twin turbo?
