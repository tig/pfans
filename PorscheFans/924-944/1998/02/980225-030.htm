Hello John & P-fans

	While I do not mean to belittle your mechanical ablility, have you checked
your clutch slave cylinder? Typically the slave will leak/weep and drip down
around to the oil pan. This looks like the rear main is leaking, when in fact
it is the slave! Also check the master cylinder, under the clutch/brake/gas
pedal area for leaks (are your floor matts wet?) It is far more common for the
slave/master to go than it is for the rae main. If it is indeed the rear main
seal, I would only replace it when also replacing the clutch. Lots of work! 

Good Luck
