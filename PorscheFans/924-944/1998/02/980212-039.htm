I earlier reported my purchase of a 1988 951S Limited Edition (Silver
Rose) last November.  The car had 1,200 miles and was kept in a curated
collection since original purchase before being traded in at a Porsche
dealer.  The car was in as-new condition (still is) and came with
elaborate after market stereo and security systems. My car probably can
be used as the benchmark for "flawless".  I paid $18,000 which included
a 30,0000 service, new belts and water pump and a 90 day warranty.  I
have put on 4,000 spirited weekend miles and the only problem was a
stuck boost gauge, repaired under warranty. I feel the asking price for
the '89 you are considering is fair, but nonetheless you should use my
purchase as comparable in negotiating a lower price if you can.

N.B.  I submitted details of my purchase to Excellence 3 months ago but
they have not mentioned it.
