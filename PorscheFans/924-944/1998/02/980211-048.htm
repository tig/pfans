The circuit is a simple bridge, but again it would only be valid at startup.
Once you get the oil moving all sorts of variable come into play. 

*	How hot it is effects how quickly it comes back down to pan.
*	How fast the motor is running effects pump flow rate

In an ideal system, you would keep the oil out of the pan (dry sump) and let
it be cooled ans then pumped though the motor dropped to pan then pumped out
to cooler.
