the leads coming out of the seatbelt receptacle were not attached anymore,
apparently having been "cut" possibly due to seat movement on the rails or
else the seat back.  When driving, I found that if I lifted my "butt" off

believe that there is some type of pressure (probably a micro switch)
sensor under the seat.  I have to pull out the seats in order to install my
competition harnesses anyway, so I will fix it at that time(probably by
disconnecting it entirely).
