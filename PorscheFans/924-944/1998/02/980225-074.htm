David Chou asked about clutch life on a 968
The 968 uses a "dual mass flywheel" to accomplish what the rubber centered
clutch did. Totally different animal.  The clutch is relatively easy to
replace on a 968 as the transmission does not have to be removed.  On the
other hand the flywheel cannot be resurfaced and if damaged must be replaced
spelled $ 1000.00 (firsthand experience thanks to P.O.)  Total life on the
clutch is probably about the same as a typical 944 depending on how it's
driven.
