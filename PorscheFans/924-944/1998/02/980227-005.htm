Mine does the same thing and I replaced all the rollers and water pump.  New
belts will make noise for the first 1000 miles or so, but mine has about 2000
on it.

The alternator belt should be tensioned to 9.5 on the 9201 tensioner gauge,
but you might be hearing a bit of bearing noise from somewhere.

Also, you need to retension those belts in about 1500 to 2000 miles.  If you
set your initial tensions right (4.0 for the cam belt and 2.7 for the balance
belt) you should be all right.

One trick I have used is an automotive stethoscope to pin point weird noises. 
It really works!
