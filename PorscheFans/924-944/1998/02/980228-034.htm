So I decided to take my freshly washed/scrubbed/polished/waxed 944 out for
a road trip up to Santa Barbara along Pacific Coast Highway today.  On the
trip I came across numerous other Pcars (over 10 I'd guess), only two of
which weren't 911's (A Boxter and 944).  If at all possible I tried to
wave, or flash, or some other little gesture of Pcar appriciation.  And
din't get a single response.  Notta one.  Most cases I was sure they say me
too.  Most of this was around the Santa Manica and Malibu area, so I
attribute it to the typical snobbiness  of the residents.  Especially
the 911 drivers, who as someone mentioned before, probably didn't even know
my 944 was a Porsche.  Nonetheless, it was a little disappointing.  Another
factor may be there are so many Pcars around here.  I even dare to say I
see more of them than Camaros and Vettes most days.

even bother.
