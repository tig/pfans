I had this problem with my '84 944 also.  In particular it seemed to happen
when it was really cold.  Not really knowing the problem, I replaced the
windshield wiper arm and control and the problem went away.  (I still don't
know what the problem was, so this might not be the fix).  You can try pulling
the wheel and removing the blinker/windshield wiper controls (they are clipped
together) and cleaning in the inside of the wiper assembly (PITA).  It might be
easier to simply replace it though, I remember it wasn't terribly expensive.
