 asked if a 944S would fit into a 8 valve 9444 anything is possible with money

Yes it is possible to put a 944S engine into a regular 8 valve 944. The
pistons are different.  You also have to change the control unit, dme wiring
and change wiring at the 14 pin connector.  
 Snap-on sell a long 6mm allen socket that will take out the cam box.
