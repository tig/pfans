Raminder,

I would begin by checking the thermostat in the water pump.  It may not 
be opening enough for sufficient coolant to flow through the radiator.

If the thermostat is not the problem, you likely have a clogged 
radiator or a waterpump that is not moving coolant.

I assume that you have properly bled the coolant.  Good luck,
