    I've had Wet Okole seat covers (from Performance) in my 87 944S daily
driver for close to a year and I'm still happy with them.  The fit is good
(seats must be removed to install well), and color has held.  These are made
of neoprene, like a wetsuit.  They do show dirt, and the wet suit shampoo I
bought is less than terrific. They are comfortable (they do add about 1/4
inch of padding).  If you order, consider telling them not to include their
logo on the headrest (tres tacky imho).  Someone claimed you could get them
with the Porsche logo which sounds nice. I shipped mine back and got the
plain (no logo).
