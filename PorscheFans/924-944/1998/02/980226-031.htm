Sheridan,
  There are TWO different sizes of brake shoes for
the 924 rear drums ... (we found out the hard way).
Before you start taking things apart, make SURE that
you have the "normal" size shoes (linings are available
just about everywhere) and not the oversized shoes/
linings . The backing plates are different, and the shoes
from one cannot be used on the other. The oversized
LININGS are availabe from a Porsche dealership, and have
to be rivited onto the old shoes.
