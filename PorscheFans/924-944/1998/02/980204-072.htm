Ron,
Since no one took question 3, I'll give it a try.  Back off the adjusting
screw until you can see clearance between it and the throttle valve lever.
Put a thin piece of paper between the screw and the lever.  With the valve
closed, turn the screw in till it contacts the paper.  Remove paper and turn
the screw in another half turn.  This should be very close to the factory
adjustment.  You are correct; the screw prevents the valve from damaging the
throttle body.
