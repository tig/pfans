Chris,
Loctite has a web page with lots of helpful info.
http://www.loctite.com

I used their search engine to find a data sheet on
Loctite 574.  It's a "flange sealant".  They have a 
toll free number on their web page for finding 
distributors.

I would suggest going to a bearing service to get the 
stuff (under "Bearings" in the yellow pages).  These 
guys sell or can get most any Loctite product.  If they
can't get 574, they can usually find you a substitute.
