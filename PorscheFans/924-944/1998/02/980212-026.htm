The blocks are different between the 951 and S2. The S2/968 engine has a
larger bore. Thus when a S2/968 crank is put in a 951 block the capacity
is only increased to 2.8L. The 968 turbo used the 968 block and crank
but with the 951 head. I don't know if it still used the plastic sump
from the standard 968 though. The mass flow sensor debate was thrashed
out last week, my conclusion is this. Porsche used flapper box on their
old cars now they use mass flow sensors on their new cars.
