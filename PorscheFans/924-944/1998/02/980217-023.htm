From the factory book (and from personal examination), the
88 'standard' turbo has an 18mm rear bar, the 88 turbo S
has a 16mm rear bar. 

The turbo S has larger rear torsion bars (23.5 vs 25.5), so I guess
Porsche thought it was OK to use a smaller rear sway.

Many turbo S people upgrade to a standard turbo 18mm bar. In fact, I 
have one that I'd be willing to sell for $75. It makes the car
turn in faster, and reduces understeer a good amount.
