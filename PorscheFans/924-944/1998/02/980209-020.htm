To David J, Kevin and Pfans,        I stand corrected, most of my knock
sensor experiences come from domestic products. However are you saying a
faulty knock sensor won`t cause excessive pinging? I think your saying
that the tapping test produces a broad spectrum of noise that even a
faulty sensor (out of tune) may respond to. This makes sense to me,
however this method can still be used to check for some signal
generation and whether or not that signal is reaching the connector at
the DME. I have read that most knock sensor failures are actually
misdiagnosed dirty connections. Because it is such a high impedance, low
current signal, it is critical that all its connections on the path to
the computer are very clean. Thanks for the feedback David J. and
apologies to Kevin.         Dave C.
