        On the subject of oil, I use Mobil 1 synthetic, 15w50 in my 85 1/2
944 that is primarily a track/club race car.  I wouldn't hesitate, however,
to use a good quality regular oil.  As far as tranny fluid, go with
synthetic.  I did a tranny rebuild after 40K miles using regular fluid; the
bearings and races were severely worn and pitted.  Since using synthetic, no
problems and no noise.  Mobil 1 makes as quality fluid as well as Red Line.
Hopefully they are available to you down under at less of an inflation rate
as your car.  Good luck
