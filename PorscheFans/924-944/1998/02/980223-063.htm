It sounds just like my right rear outer CV joint when it was acting up about 2
weeks ago. I pull all four Joints regreased them, replaced the boots ($6.95
from Zims) and reversed directions. The right outer was a little scored but
the others were fine. Just got back from a 700 mile round trip and no noises. 
Check out
http://www.NordStern.org/PCA_Articles/cvjoint.html
and
http://www.connactivity.com/~kgross/944faqcv.html

The later combined with the haynes manual make the job pretty easy.
