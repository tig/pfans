The last alignment I had done was by a reputable local
tire/brake/suspension shop. Before this alignment, my car always drifted
a little and never quite handled like it should.

I sprung for their $65 4-wheel alignment package (ok, so I'm a
cheapskate.) The tech spent three hours under my car cursing at it and
the entire nation of Germany, I think. He finally emerged from under my
car, covered in grease, and handed me the printout -- right on. 

The car handles significantly better now, considering that the
shocks/springs need replacement, and tracks perfectly. As for the shop,
they swore they would never, ever touch a Porsche 944 again. But I got a
cheap, good alignment, so I'm happy. I promised to send all my friends
there.  Essentially, I think that it all depends on the determination
of the individual working on your car.
