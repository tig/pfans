Jeff,

look at the boost hose that goes to your intercooler.  the one that
snakes in an S above your air cleaner.  Reach under it to the left of
the strap holding it to the air cleaner and see if when the lat time
someone replaced your waterpump/thermostat they left the hose clamp
screw at the top.  If so, chances are it has rubbed a hole in the boost
hose.  This is fairly common on the 951's with a less than perfect shop.


Also the cycle valve "blow off valve" can go bad over time and reduce
boost.  

In either event, A qualified mechanic would be able to notice either of
these as opposed to just say it doesn't seem as strong as other cars ;-)
