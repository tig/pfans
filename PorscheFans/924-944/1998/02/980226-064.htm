I just got 4 new tires and an alignment at NTB as well.  First of all,
anytime you get 4 new tire$ you should probably get the alignment at
least checked, (which NTB will do for free).  They show you a print out   
    

of where you should be and where you are.  I am not sure if they could
fudge this if they wanted to.  Anyway, get the alignment if you need it   
    

so your new tires wear correctly.  The car I just bought had uneven tire   
    

wear that looked like out of spec toe.  NTB "exotic" alignment for 944
was 79.00, and I got 10% off because I waited too long the last time I
got my other car aligned.
