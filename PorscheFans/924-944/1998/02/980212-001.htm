The Haynes manual does refer to the DME control unit as the DEE.  However,
I believe if you use the Haynes procedure for removing the control unit,
you'll never find it. The Haynes manual procedure addresses the location in
early 944s (as you can see by the fuse panel next to the control unit in
the photos). I believe your 85.5 has the DME in the same location as the
other late model 944s. If you will pull back the carpet at the front of the
passengers footwell, you'll find a wooden panel held in place by four
screws. The DME control unit is behind the panel.
