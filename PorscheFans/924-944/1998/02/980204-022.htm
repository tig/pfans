Another P-Fans was asking about how to remove the wire connectors from the
mirror plug. The way I removed them was to take needle nose pliers and try to
grab the connector from behind and give it a yank. All of them came out
sucessfully but one I had to solder back together.Be sure to spread the barbs
back out before you stick them back in the plug or they won't hold.
