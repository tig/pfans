Don't know why the knock sensor situation exists but make sure the catalytic
converter isn't clogged. Had a car once that had intermittent boost and it
turned out to be the cat which had partially melted down. Don't know why the
problem was intermittent but it was. Same thing happened to a friend where the
boost never exceeded 1.2 on the gauge. A new cat (actually a bypass) solved
the problem. It's worth a look. Good luck.
