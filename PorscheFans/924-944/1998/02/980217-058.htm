Interesting situation for those who are using lots of oil.  800 miles ago I 
switched from Valvoline 20W50 to Mobil 1 15W50.  I was adding 1Q of oil 
every 700 or so miles as my oil level light would come on.  So far in the 
800 miles I have used less than 1Q of oil with the Mobil 1.  Driving habits 
have not changed and the environmental conditions have been pretty 
consistent.  I expected to loose more oil when I switched as part of my 
problem is a valve cover leak, the other appears to be valve seals.  Nice 
surprise!
