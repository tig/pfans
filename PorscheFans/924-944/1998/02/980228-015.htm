My sunroof (87 944NA) exhibits the following behavior.

Key in POS 1: Rocker switch retracts arms in one direction but does not
return them.
Key in POS 2: Rocker Switch does nothing.

Go to back of car and reverse power plug on sun roof motor.

Key in POS1 : Rocker switch returns arms but does not retract them.
Key in POS 2: Rocker switch raises sun roof but does not lower.

Go to back and return power plug on motor to normal position.

Key in POS 2: Rocker Switch now will lower sunroof.

It appears that all functions work in regards to gears and cables but only
certain switch/power polarity combos work. There are two solenoids at the
back assembly - is one these the culprit? Swapping them did not reverse the
symptoms.
