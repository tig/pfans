Last summer I drove a 968 Cabriolet (I own a 944 S2
Cabriolet).  The 968 DOES indeed feel smaller.  Not
interior room, but driving the car, it feels smaller, and
LIGHTER.  I think you're right; some of this is visual --
the front of the car appears narrower from the driver's
seat. But that's not the whole story -- to me it felt
LIGHTER as well.  Hard to describe why, possibly turn-in
response. Possibly tighter suspension in general. (My
944 S2 is a '90 with about 50k mi at the time, the 968 had
about 30k mi and was, I think, a '94 model.  Neither has a
sport suspension.)

Any one else noticed this? (Coupes or Cabriolets)
