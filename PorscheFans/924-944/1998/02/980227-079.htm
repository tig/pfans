Steve, it sure sounds as if your mechanic was attempting to hose you.  Oil
seals do not just "let go" all of a sudden and drain the engine of oil. 
Even a few drops on the garage floor is nothing to be alarmed about.  These
type of seals can leak a little bit for a long period of time (my front
main seal has been for years).  Unless a few drops of oil on the floor
really bugs you, don't worry.  If you start to see what appears to be a
significant leak (say a spot greater than 1" diameter),  you should take a
closer look.  Bad seal leaks at the front of the engine can lead to oil
getting on the cam/balance shaft belts; not a good thing.
