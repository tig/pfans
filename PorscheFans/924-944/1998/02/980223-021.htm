The cross member is under the belly pan, so it's not a good choice for 
jacking.  I use either the side, centre jack pad (under the dimple in 
the rocker), or the front left or right jack point.  Put the jack 
stands under the frame rail - do not put them under the jacking point, 
too easy for them to slip off it.
