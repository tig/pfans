> Has anyone ever had a condensation buildup under the oil fillercap of a 1985

Hi Jason,
dont't panic! In the cold season this is quite normal. I bet you don't live
in Florida! This is condensate water from the exhaust gases. Here in
Germany i have this 'problem' from about October until April. (During the
winter, motor oil contains about 2% of water, but good oil can handle
this). But you may have a serious problem if the oil contains a visible
amount of water (all oil frothy 
brown). Best case this is a broken oil cooler seal, worst case, a broken
head gasket or broken cylinder head!
Wolfgang
