Bill,
     You asked about window tinting. Since you have the glass out of the 
vehicle, I suggest you do it yourself. The most difficult part of window 
tinting is doing it on glass that is installed on the vehicle. I tinted the 
passenger and driver's windows, and the rear quarter windows, but still have 
to re-do the rear hatch, and it was not too difficult. I used the lightest 
tint available. Having the glass removed from the vehicle makes the job VERY 
easy. If you need detailed instructions, let me know. The highest quality 
window tint film will run about ~$20 for enough to do the front windows and 
rear quarter windows. The local auto stores don't have the kits for curved 
windows in stock yet, so I haven't re-done the rear hatch yet..

Good Luck, let me know if you have any questions..
