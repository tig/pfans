I would suggest flushing the radiator and cooling system. Then change
the oil. DO this yourself and use clean catch pans(so you know the
contaminants came from the inside). When you drain the radiator look for
the rainbow of oil in the water. When you change the oil look for the
brown crap. Also look for the brown crap in the cooling system
reservoir, If you find any of the above is bad you probably have to do
the oil cooler reseal again. You can do this yourself, it is not really
that hard to do, but hard to get to. I just did mine. Write back if you
want some more tips.
