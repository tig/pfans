First thing that comes to mind is brakes.  The list price for the front 
calipers is $499.00 each, the front rotors list at $293.50 each and the 
rear rotors list at 207.80 each.  This makes the total for brakes alone 
$2000.60.

Also, the M030 front suspension wasn't just a different shock.  The front 
suspension was coil-overs and I believe (could be wrong though) the rear 
torsion bars remained the same with the addition of coil-over helpers.  The 
M030 on the 968 was a more substantial upgrade than the M030 option on the 
S2.

On my 93 968, I have installed the big brakes from a 89 951 (bigger pistons 
than the 968 M030 caliper, same pad size) and the M030 torsion bars. 
 Hopefully this season will see the suspension upgraded to full coil-overs 
on all four corners.  After which I guess the only thing left is a turbo 
:-)  (Yeah, like I need a 450 hp 968!)

I'm a little confused as to Matt's pricing the M030 and the wheels 
separately as the complete M030 package did include the 17" wheels.
