Hello Randall & P-Fans

	Sounds like there is still air trapped in your P.S. System. With the engine
running try turning the steering wheel from full lock to lock (ie all the way
from one side to the other) to bleed the system. Also check the feed line from
the resivoir to the PS pump and then from there(PS ump) to the steering rack.
Maybe you left a line loose. Any leaks? 
	
	One other note: the P.S. resivoir. When you have had a rack failure, the
metal particles will be inside of all the lines. Try flushing the system out
before installing the new parts. Also ALWAYS replace the PS resivoir; because
of the built in filter - traps the debrit. The resivoir is cheap and you will
never gets all the debrit out of the filter! BTW the 968 uses a cool looking
resivoir, the top half is clear to check fluid level at a glance, otherwise it
is the same part.
