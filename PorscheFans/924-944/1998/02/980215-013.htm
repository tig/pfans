I've never seen the splines damaged on half shafts, the drive shaft or 
the input shaft.  The symptoms you describe are consistent with one or 
more broken pinion gear teeth, and that is a common failure.  I did the 
math recently, per Alec Stokes book, on the peak loads a 944 
transmission put on the pinion gear, and the numbers are scary.  I 
wouldn't abuse the transmission with "burn outs" or whatever.
