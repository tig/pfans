This is for ROW rear foglights. There is a two position switch for
selecting front fogs or front + rear fogs. The switch is on the centre
console to the left of the cigarette lighter and the wiring (in mine
anyway) was behind the console (not connected). There is also an extra
relay needed. It is illegal to run rear fogs in Australia so I just
wired some extra brake lamps in there. Early model "Rabbit" fog switches
fit OK. Happy Birthday Lee!!
