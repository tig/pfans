The S box has hardened 1st and 2nd gears, a LSD and oil cooler. The
Quaife diff is a torsen type which transfers drive from the wheel to the
other as it looses traction where as a normal LSD just locks. Most
manufacturers use torsen type diffs now and they seem to be the way to
go. Although you could by a whole S box for the price of a new Quaife
diff.
