For years my local dealers told me the same story.  A few weeks ago
I happened by Automotion and discovered that they carry '968' blanks
which work perfectly.  They are a bit longer than the original keys,
but are otherwise identical appearance - including the supplier's
initials at the top.  I can look up the part # if anyone is
interested.

FWIW, there is a Volkswagen blank which substitutes for the
'unobtainable' alarm key too.  It took some prodding, but one
of the local dealers did confess this one to me.
