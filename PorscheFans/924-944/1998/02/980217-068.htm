Thore, the FAQ has a very good write-up on the procedure.  BTW, I would 
remove the transmission altogether, rather than trying to move the 
torque tube back with it still attached.

Yes, replace the pilot bearing, what you call the "needle bearing." 
 Also replace the throw-pit bearing guide tube, the throw-out bearing, 
clutch friction disk and pressure plate.  Check the release lever 
bearings, relube them.

I don't think you'll find any gaskets or seals needing replacement. 
 Take a look at the engine rear main seal, be sure it's not leaking.
