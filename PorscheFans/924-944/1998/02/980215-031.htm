Thanks to all who offered suggestions as to why my 951 was intermittently
missing, not starting hot, blowing smoke. Turns out the problem was a
corroded plug to the coolant sensor.

That replaced has the car running sweetly once more.

Seems the DME thought the coolant was cold even when hot, so the car was
running rich, which fouled plug/s and caused the missing and smoke.

Cheers, Gary
