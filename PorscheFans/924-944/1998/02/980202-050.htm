In order to get the retractors to "retract" into the roof, you must turn
the car off, and put the key into the first position on the ignition
switch - just turn it a little bit to the first click.  Then, operate
the sunroof switch and voila!  the retractors should retract.  Oh, make
sure the roof is in the down position before turning the car off!  Hope
this helps!
