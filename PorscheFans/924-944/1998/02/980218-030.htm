On Wed, 18 Feb 1998, Brian Burch wrote:

> Pardon my ignorance, but what is a 951?
A 951 is a 944 Turbo.  
A 931 is a 924 Turbo.
These are internal factory designations, the turbo part numbers start with
these numbers (ie 951.xxx.xxx.xxx),but you will not see 951 on the rear
end of a Porsche.  

This is Porsche talk, no need to apologize, sort of like a secret
handshake among Porsche owners.
