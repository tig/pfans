A couple of things to look for in the way of paint or possible wreck:

Overspray is usually most noticeable around the windshield/cowl area.  If
the painter wasn't very careful you can sometimes see tape lines around
trim, tail lights, etc.  Check the gaps in places such as the headlight
assemblies, doors, and panels.
