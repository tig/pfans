Frank:

The real secret to heel and toe is to get your brake pedal adjusted so that
when it's
about fully depressed it's parallel to your throttle. Then have the throttle
pedal widened and put some non-slip material on both pedals...viola...you'll
be shifting like a pro! 

I prefer  braking with the left part of my right foot and then rolling it
over slightly to blip the throttle. Practice on the street before you go to
the track. Adjusting your
pedals as I described above will make a world of difference. I have a size
13 foot and
had a hell of a time heel & toeing until the mods I made to the pedals.
