Paolo,
Disconnect battery, remove carpet and sound insulation material, and right
rear side panel trim.  Loosen plastic over on filler neck and remove.  Check
for gasoline, wipe away, and re-check.  You might only see where the gas is
leaking and not the hairline crack, often at a hose connection.  While
you're in there, check the valve for one-way operation.  The crack is a bear
to find.  Someone last month with a 944 for sale detailed his procedure
which seemed in his case to take some real master-sleuthing.  I think he
replaced the fuel tank breather system rather than trying some gas-resistant
epoxy.
