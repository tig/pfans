I installed a Canton/Mecca oil filter on my son's 951S.  This has a
replaceable element that comes out the TOP after you remove 4 stainless
bolts and take off the billet aluminum top with filter attached.  No oil
gets spilled and the elements last for many thousands of miles on the
street.  Price is roughly $90 and the elements are about $9 each.
