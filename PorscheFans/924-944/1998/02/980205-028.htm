To my knowledge the drain plug exists on all 2.5 liter 944 turbo blocks.
 (including the turbo S models).  The '89 944 did not have the drain plug, the
turbo did.

The drain plug is on the passenger side of the block.  It is as far back on
that side as it can get  (before the lip for the back side).  It is up toward
the top of the engine block.
