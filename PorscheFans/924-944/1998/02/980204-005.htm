Porsche recommend (or insists, actually) that the head nuts and the 
washers under them be replaced rather than reused.  My experience has 
been that they can be safely reused.  However, the fasteners do lose 
clamping force with each reuse, and in a turbocharged application I 
would personally be a little more inclined to replace rather than 
reuse.

I have to disagree with Chris Hanlon.  The angle torque method has 
nothing to do with stretch bolts.  The 944 head studs are not stretch 
bolts.  The angle torque method is simply a more accurate way to 
establish elastic (vs. plastic) bolt stretch.
