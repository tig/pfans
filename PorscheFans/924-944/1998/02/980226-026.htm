Hello to all,

 The rear axle nut on a 924 is the same as that of a Beetle
(VW Audi content) go to your local bug shop and ask for the axle nut
removal tool
this should run no more than 12$ US and really is the only effective way of
removing the 
rear nut. It is a piece of stock steel, 1/2 in thick with a cutout for the
axle nut, and another small 
cutout for a 1/2 in breaker bar. VW owners will swear by this tool for
their axle nut removal.
the use of a sledge hammer is usually required.(dont wince- I have seen
shops use this procedure.)
BTW, the brakes on a 924 are the same as a Type 3 VW.

Hope this helps,
