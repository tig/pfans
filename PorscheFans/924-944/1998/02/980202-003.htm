John,

The same switch went out on my 944.  The switch looked impossible to get to
and I was repeatedly warned about how fragile the headliner is.  So I found
the wires going to the switch and followed them until I found where they
plug into the harness.  I unplugged the switch from the harness and on the
harness side I made a jumper and plugged it into the harness.  This in
effect bypasses the micro switch in the sunroof but enables me at any time
to replace the switch and plug it back into the harness.  I doubt I'll ever
replace it but I can.

Good luck and if you decide to replace the switch, be careful of that
headliner.
