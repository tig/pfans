Hello P-Fans

	I thought that I would let the "collective know about Mitchell body shop
books. If your looking for a parts fische, but do not have access to a
microfilm viewer, try to get an old "Mitchell" body shop collision book. I do
not know why I did not think of this before? The "European" book will (there
are "Asian/American/Euro/Old-Cars) cover most Porsche models. These books have
most of the structural things (engine/trans/drivetrain/body/windows) parts you
might need, complete with part number, price, and install time (yea get real
on these labor times...).
	I think that Mitchell does have an web site, but I do not have an address.
Most body shops get these books updated every month or so. So time to get
friendly with your body shop manager or insurance adjuster!
