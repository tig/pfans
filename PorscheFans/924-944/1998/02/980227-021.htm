The M474 option is for 'sport shocks'. These are not the Koni gas
adjustable shocks you mentioned. I had them on my '84 944 and they
weren't that good. When I switched to the single-adjustable Konis, it
was like night and day. Try this upgrade before venturing down other
paths.

Paul Foster

BTW, changing the front struts is a royal pain in the ass and it can be
very dangerous. Unless you have a fairly complete home shop and you are
very handy with tools, I would not advise taking on this project. You
may become seriously injured as a result. The rears are a cinch.
