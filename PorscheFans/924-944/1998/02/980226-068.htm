> 
> Haynes manual shows you can adjust parking brake at the handle next to   
> the seat and at the brakes also.  I find my 86 does not have this   
> adjustment on the rear discs (I think).  My cable is fully adjusted at   
> the handle and does not hold the car.  Any suggestions?
> 
The emergency brakes are drum type with a conventional star-wheel adjuster.
The adjusted is accessed via a hole in the drum facing out toward the lugs.
You will have to rotate the drum so the hole is at the top and then use a
large screwdriver or brake adjusting tool to toggle the star. Haynes has
an acceptable write-up on procedure (although not as complete as in the
factory manual).
>
> Also, can someone tell me how to locate the wastegate for the turbo?  I   
> know where the turbo is.  Thanks!
> 
Climb under the car. Remove the metal splash pan. Look up under the rear of
the engine in front of the bellhousing on the passenger side. The rusty
rectangular chunk of metal with a diaphram housing on it is the wastegate.
