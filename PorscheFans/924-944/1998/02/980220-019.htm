    You can now convert your old sealed beam headlights to H4's on the
cheap. There are many suppliers

range from $19.95 for ones made in China to the Hella brand for $39.95. All
conversion kits contain the  7" Lamp (flat lense) and bulb. I did the 928
first because the headlights will stay up without the power on.  It took 40
minutes for me and I am not Mr Goodwrench. Not only do they look better but
they brighten up your night.
