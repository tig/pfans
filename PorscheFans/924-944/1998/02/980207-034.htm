my 924 did exactly the same thing (Re:Clutch/Torque tube judder). It was
very bad so i took it in and they said the torque tube was bent , it was
straightened and put back together. The shop said that some garages if
they aren't careful can accidentally put pressure on the tube and bend
it.
i originally thought that the clutch bearing may need lubing but it
seems not.
the bad side to the story is the judder occasionally returned only very
mild (compared to severe before), now however 8 months on i only get a
slight judder very rarely. so it seems to have gotten better by itself.
