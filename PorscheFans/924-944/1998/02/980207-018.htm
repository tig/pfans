Philip,
  You asked where to get a 6mm allen head socket for removing the head. I
bought a 6mm socket from sears, and a 6mm normal allen head wrench. I cut a
short piece off the allen wrench, and glued it into the socket. The diameter
of the socket was larger than the hole in the head, so I placed the socket in
a dril (6mm hex end in the chuck), and with the drill spinning the socket, I
used a cutoff tool on a dremel to grind the size of the socket down until it
was small enough to fit through the hole in the head. Worked great..
