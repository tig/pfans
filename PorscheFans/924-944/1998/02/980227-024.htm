The conventional wisdom says don't do it unless you own a rotor
manufacturing plant. Cross drilled rotors will crack and fail much
sooner. You can accomplish the same thing by slotting the rotors or
pads. Another alternative would be to modify your brakes to use the
993tt or 968 M030 'drilled' rotors which are actually cast holes...
