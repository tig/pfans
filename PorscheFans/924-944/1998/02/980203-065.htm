Anti-seize is certainly for aluminum, steel, titanium, and just about 
any other metal.  The important lesson is that, as you found, you need 
to torque to spec with the specified stuff on the threads.  In the case 
of flywheel bolts, I highly recommend you use red (high strength) 
Loctite, which will not interfere with torque and which will provide a 
little extra guarantee that things stay put.
