You wrote:
>My second question is: I tried to change my wheel bearings but could not
remove the grease cap.  Any hints??<

1. Remove the wheel involved.
2. Set a small wood block against the rear of the cap flange.
3. Use a hammer, and, hitting against the block, give the cap medium-firm
taps all the way around.
4. Repeat until the cap is loose enough to wiggle out with a pair of large
pliers. 
5. The left front wheel will have the speedometer cable attached to the
wheel bearing cap. (At least, it does on Series I 944s, up to mid-1985).
The cable end is secured with a circlip hidden under a dollop of silicone
sealer. Peel off the sealer and remove the circlip (with circlip pliers, of
course) before using steps 2-4 above.

Good luck......
