As promised, a post that announces the open registration for the PCA Zone 7 AX
School.  You can get the registration form by accessing the PCA Golden Gate
region web page via www.glenhhills.com.  This event always gets full almost
immediately, i.e, within a few weeks, if not days, after registration opens.
So if you are thinking about attending, go to the web page, get the form, fill
it out, and send it, along with yor check to the co-chair, Masuo.  Or send e-
mail to Masuo (address below) and he will send you the form via US Mail.

Anyone is welcomed to sign up and attend, as long as they are driving a
Porsche.   I cannot tell you how much fun this is-- you'll have to try it and
see for
yourself.  It is worth planning a mini-vacation around, if you live within a
reasonable distance of the SF Bay Area.  

Thanks again to Henry Watts, the original author of the post that follows.
For those of you who don't know Hank, he has written two books about cars.
One of them is called Secrets of Solo Racing, which is all about AX and solo
track event driving.  Great book-- you get one with your $98 entry fee for the
School.  Also makes an excellent gift.

Let's see some water pumpers out there!
