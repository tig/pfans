Club Sport is usually the lightweight version of the certain model.  Ie
there was the 968 and the 968 CS.  Through my limited knowledge on the
1988 944 Turbo S, I would say it is not the Club Sport by default.  All
the 944TS examples I have heard about were outfitted to be the most
luxurious of all (ie 6 speaker sound systems, full power seats, etc).  If
there is such a beast as a TS Club Sport, I would expect it to be lacking
lots of the options, ie no sunroof, no cruise, possibly no A/C, but I do
not know of a tell-tale sign.
