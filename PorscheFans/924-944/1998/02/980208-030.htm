Maybe I missed the point somewhere along the way, but I always assumed that
getting a Porsche would be a bit of a money sucking, high maintenance habit.
I didn't buy it because I thought it would be cheap, I bought it because I
wanted one, and I thought it would be fun, not just to drive, but to work on
and get to know.  I thought there was a lot of pride in ownership in having
one, and doing what work I can on my own makes it that much more a part of
me.  So far, my car has lived up to my expectations, and I wouldn't trade it
for another.

Two comments that I saw over and over on Caleb's page were the submarine
effect of the air intake, and oil cooler failures, neither of which I have
seen anyone on this list comment on in the 3 months of so that I have been
receiving it.  Anyone had similar problems?
