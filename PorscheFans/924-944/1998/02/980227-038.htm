Ramon,

You won't have any trouble using whatever "premium" unleaded gas you find
(usually 93 octane), unless the fuel is old (meaning it has had the chance
to accumulate too much water/moisture).  As long as you frequent a popular
gas station, their fuel deliveries are often enough so that there won't be
any problem.  I never used anything but normal "pump" gas in any of my
previous P-cars ('83 944NA, '88 944 Turbo S), even for "on-track" drivers
ed events, and will keep doing the same with my 968.
