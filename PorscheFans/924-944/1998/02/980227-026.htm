>> And does anyone know whether a '91 S2 can be retrofit with, say,
>> struts from an '88 or '89 Turbo without problems relative to clearance
>> of the Brembo brakes or interference with my 17 inch 993 Cup wheels?

No problem retrofitting the Turbo S unit to an S2 or a non-S turbo for that
matter. However, Koni is assuming you will be placing that unit on a car
that is already equipped with their ride height adjustable unit. Hence the
don't include the lower jam nut or lower spring perch. You will need to
source these items from Porsche or come up with an alternative. In addition
you will need to obtain a spring that is built for this type of shock/lower
perch.
