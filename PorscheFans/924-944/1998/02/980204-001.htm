'Pasha' trim refers to the early 1980's 'deformed' checker board pattern
usually seen in 924's, 928's, 944's and the odd 911 from that era.
Usually in brown/cream or black/cream.  I used to have a 944 with this
trim, but lost the car in an engine fire (car was written off) less than
a week after buying it.   :-(
A year later I replaced this car with another 944, Black with 'Porsche
Cloth' trim, and I've owned & enjoyed it for nearly two years now.  :-)
