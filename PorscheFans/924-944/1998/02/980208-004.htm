Chris,

You're right, the '86 Turbo has a different wheel offset than all other 944
Turbos and the wheels are not interchangeable. In 1987 they began offering
ABS and for some reason changed the offset. You can find the specifications
in the FAQ at PorscheFans web page, but as I recall the '86 is around 23mm
and the other about 53mm offset. Don't take these numbers to the bank --
it's just to confirm for you they are different and shouldn't be
interchanged with spacers.
