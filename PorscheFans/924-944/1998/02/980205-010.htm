I also had one of the early Autothority MAF kits installed.  My car
probably had 20M miles on it at time of installation and was stock
except for Autothority Stage 2 chips.  I did not notice an increase in
power or throttle response only an increase in noise.  The car hissed a
lot and to me felt slower than before.  I had it removed and sent back
to Autothority and was only out the money for installing and
deinstalling.  Maybe I got a bad unit, I don't know, but I wouldn't
spend the money again to find out.
