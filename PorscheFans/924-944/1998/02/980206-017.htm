	First off, let me say congrats on you new 944.  As for the hatch
rattle, I played this game for some time.  I think that I have finally
solved it though.  I took out the latch mechanism, two bolts from the
underside behind the carpet pannel, and gave them a good clean and lube.
But the most sucsessful thing that I did was, like you, adjust the posts.
The difference between latching well, or not at all can be 1 turn.  But
clean the actual latches first and see how that goes.  It may be a case of
the clamps that hook around the base of the post are stiff and not fully
closing.
