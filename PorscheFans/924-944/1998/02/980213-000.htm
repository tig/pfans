I have done several 700 - 900 mile /day trips in a 944 Turbo. The car
really does come into its own on such trips for me. You might find you
get a little back ache. I did find that I needed to take a can of oil or
else be prepared to buy oil en-route. Prolonged high speed turbo work
does use the stuff up.

I'd also ensure that you make sure your spacesaver spare wheel
compressor is present and functioning.

Go for it ....
