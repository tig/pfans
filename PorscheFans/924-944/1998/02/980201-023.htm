> A common problem on 944's is the door/window rattle, also light cracking
> on the outer door skin at the rear edge of the window.  The rattle will
> go away if roll the window up or down slightly, or if you press in on
> the door glass adjacent to the door lock button.
>
> A weld in the door subframe will crack, allowing the rattle to occur.
> If you pull the inner door panel off and press on the sheet metal close
> to the locking mechanism, you will find this broken piece.  Usually it
> it a spot weld that has failed.
>
> There are different ways to fix this problem.  The easiest, but maybe
> not the longest lasting, is to drill a small hole through the two pieces
> of metal that should be welded together. Screw in a small sheet metal
> screw, shortened for the proper length.  This fix can last quite a long
> time.  Other ways are to re-spotweld, weld, or epoxy the pieces back
> together.  I usually go for the screw method.
>
