Anyway, upon driving my 86 951 111,000 miles back to Dayton, OH from

After I let the pedal off, normal operation continued.  So, I ask, what   
causes this safety measure to kick in.  I experimented a little and it   
doesn't happen in the lower gears, only 5th gear, 90 MPH.
