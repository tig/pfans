Although I haven't tried it personally under the hood yet (only cleaning the
bilge on my diesel sailboat), I can recommend trying "Simple Green". Spray
it on, let it soak for about 20 minutes, spray again, soak 5 minutes and
then rinse. If the buildup is heavy, it might take another application or
two, but at least the cleaning agent isn't more toxic to the environment
than the oil accumulation.
