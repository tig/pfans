> Steve - the OEM part number you seek is 944.351.957.00 (at least according
> to the Automotion catalog), same as for the 944S and 924S.  Speed Bleeders
> did not supply a cross-reference part for that particular number, but I did
> receive a note yesterday from John Steczkowski saying that p/n SB7100 fit
> both front and rear on his 924S, so it is possible that the same might hold
> true for the 944.
> According to the Parts and Technical Reference Catalog PNA 000 147 A, the
> following cars use the same caliper, so it would stand to reason that the
> bleeders would also be the same:
>
> 924S (87-88) and 944/944S (84-88)
>         Front Left Caliper:     944.351.429.00
>         Front Right Caliper:    944.351.430.00
> 924S (87-88) and 944/944S (83-88)
>         Rear Left Caliper:      944.352.429.00
>         Rear Right Caliper:     944.352.430.00
>
> I suppose the only true way to tell if the bleeders are the same would be
> to physically compare the existing units on the car.  Who knows, there may
> be only a difference in the "hex" shoulder where the wrench goes.
>
> Again, I am only passing along information supplied by other sources, so YMMV.
