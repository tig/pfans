The 968 M030 rotors can be purchased from Porsche dealers.  They have a 965
part number.

They bolt right onto a Turbo S or '89 Turbo.  I only use the fronts, since
they are so expensive.  As a bench mark, I paid about $200 each from my
dealer.  If you are quoted a higher price, then you don't yet have the proper
relationship with your dealer.
