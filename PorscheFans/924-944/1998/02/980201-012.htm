	If I remember rightly the wheel lock keys can not be replicated by
Porsche. I have, in the past when keys have deserted me, enlisted the
services of a locksmith. The method to remove wheel locks favored by
most list members includes a hammer and cold chisel though. If you drive
carefully to a good locksmith he/she should be able to manufacture you a
key from a blank. This will take some time, but it should be cheaper
than buying new wheel locks from Porsche.
