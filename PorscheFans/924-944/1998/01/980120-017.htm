Hi Tom
I put the same speakers in the front and rear stock locations of my 944. =
The trick that I found was finding the correct grills. My local car =
stereo shop had some Advent grills that worked perfectly. In fact they =
knew exactly what I was looking for when they saw my 944 pull up! The =
grills cover all the exposed metal and used the stock mounting holes. =
The only modification needed was to remove some of the plastic screw =
housing from inside the grill so that the grill will sit flat. The =
speakers look great with these grills mounted in the door and no extra =
holes or modifications to the car needed. One word of warning - the =
Kappas need more power than the stock type radio can offer. They are =
just starting to sound good when the power runs out.
