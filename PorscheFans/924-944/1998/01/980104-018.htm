When my old battery had a cell die, I simply went down to Pep Boys and
asked for a battery for a 1984 Porsche 944, and they got me one off the
shelf. About $60 or so, sealed, maintenance free. The only problem was
that the terminals were slightly larger than they should be, so the
cables required a slight amount of force in attaching them.

I've had surprisingly good luck with buying "generic parts" - brake
pads, battery, filters, lights, etc. at Pep  Boys. All my other "little
bits" come from two import parts houses here in Santa Barbara, and odd
stuff comes from the Porsche, VW, or Audi dealer, or possibly mail
order.
