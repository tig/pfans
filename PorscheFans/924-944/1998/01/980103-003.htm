The Federal Trade Commission has issued complaints against STP Oil Treatment
and Slick 50 Engine Formula charging that the claims for improved engine
performance and reduced engine wear were deceptive.  STP apparently violated
the the order and had to pay penalities "among the largest sums the FTC has
ever obtained from a consumer-protection-order violation."  Slick 50, this
summer, has agreed not to claim performance, benefits or attributes of their
engine lubricants without "competent and reliable evidence."  See Consumer
Reports July 1996, page 10; October 1997, page 9,  If anyone is interested,
there is a fax available (#9486) from Consumer Reports (cost $7.75!) at tel
# 800 766-9988.
