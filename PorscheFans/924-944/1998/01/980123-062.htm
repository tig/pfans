Bill B., Lee. L., and List:

I made one of the harness guide devices that Lee referred to for about $5.00
from some webbing that I bought at REI (the outdoor products store).  It works
okay to keep the belts loctated on your shoulders if you use the Porsche high
back seats on the track.. You can also buy/make a sternum strap, to fasten
around the belts on your chest in case of a wreck.

All of this is to keep the belts from spreading and causing you injury in an
impact.  The best solution is to either buy a more appropriate seat, or cut
slits in your seats to have the belts go through.  A friend had to do this at
the Club Race at Las Vegas-- they wouldn't let him clear tech otherwise.  He
hated to cut his factory seats, but...he wanted to race...
