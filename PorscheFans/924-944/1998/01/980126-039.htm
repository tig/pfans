Ever find yourself wanting rear fog lights but don't want to pay $100+
just for one lens to hand off your rear bumper? Take a page from Volvo
- I found, after driving a few recently, that they incorporated the rear fog
lights into the rear lighting cluster. Funnier still, most of us happen to have
a space in our rear light clusters just waiting for a bulb!

 Why not just spend the $20-30 for the kit to insert bulbs into these
spaces, then use a more powerful bulb. It can be set with a relay and
additional wiring (if you'd like) to be operated by a dash switch, either
together or independently of your front fogs. Furthermore, if you want
even brighter rear lights, use the 50W halogen backup light bulbs
available at most parts places (but be careful, these may be too bright).

Now you've got stock-looking rear fog lights at a fraction of the
alternative!
