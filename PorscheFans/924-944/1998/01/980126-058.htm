Like most of you I wanted to keep the car looking stock. I did change the
front speakers to a two way Blaupunkt 4x6 and left the rears in place
because they are not easy to change. I also added a 4 channel Blaupunkt amp
and wired each speaker directly to the amp. The amp is mounted in the same
hold as the spare tire.

The sound quality is much better after adding the amp. It will not equal a
Bose system that could be found in a new Mercedes, but it also did not cost
as much.

The best part is the car looks original and the thieves are not interested.
