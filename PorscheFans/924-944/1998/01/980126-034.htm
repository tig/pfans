Reading from the infamous PNA 000 147 A Parts and Technical Reference Catalog:

944 (1983-85/1) p/n 944 106 239 02 Upper Radiator Hose w/o Bleeder Hole.

924S (1987-88) p/n 944 106 239 04 Upper Radiator Hose
