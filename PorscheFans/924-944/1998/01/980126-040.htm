Two things:

1.	DME relay is a known week spot (if it looks burnt replace it), if
not at least have a spare on hand
2.	You may have gotten water on your DME, it is right under the battery
tray in the passenger foot rest area, that will cause a similar effect.
	(to clean, just get some Printed Circuit Board cleaner and clean it
if you know what you are doing)
