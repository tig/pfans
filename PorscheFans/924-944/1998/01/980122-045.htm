> What does it mean : T=DCV?
This is the name of the German institute that gives cars a certificate
of approval, wich is neccesary in Germany to drive your car on the road.
All cars in Germany are checked for T=DCV again every year.

> And what is the date behind the "T=DCV"?
This means the certificate of approval is valid until this date.
