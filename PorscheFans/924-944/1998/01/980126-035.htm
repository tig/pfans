My '84 came with a Reno, an ARI controller and a 4x20watt Blau digital
amp. all installed from the factory. This Reno looked very different
than the later Renos, but a really nice stereo all the same. This unit
was stolen in '86, replaced by a Houston, which was stolen in '97!

Both the Houston and Reno have very clean harnesses. No mess of speaker
outputs or power cables. Just the one DIN cable to the Blaupunkt amp
mounted high up in the driver-side footwell, and a power and ARI plug
into the unit. Also came with a remote. The forward and reverse
music-search is fairly simple to use -- you press a button for the
search mode -- and the next time you hit FF or REW it will work in seach
mode.
