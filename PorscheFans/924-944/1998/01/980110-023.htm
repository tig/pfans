> How do you isolate the slight play from the wheel bearing from the ball joint.

The mechanic at the local Porsche dealership (MCL) had my car up on the
lift and I asked him to show me how he checked the ball joints. He took a
long 24" prybar (looks like an extra-long screwdriver) and placed the tip
between the balljoint and the piece it fits into. He applied leverage and
checked for slop/movement (fortunately none).
