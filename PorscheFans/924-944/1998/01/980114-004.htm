Hi P-fans,

I never got to know my Boges well since when I got the car, they were just
about to bust.  My fronts were replaced with Boges, but my rears were KYB.

KYB pro: Cheap.  $80 a pair.
KYB con: Stiff for one month, mushy after that.  

I still keep my front Boges Shocks, but am looking for new rears for a long
time now.  My .02 says no to KYB.  There aren't many options out there.
Tokiko has a 5point adjustment sport shock, but it cost MORE than KONI.
Most people said to go with KONI, so I probably will when I scrape up
enough $$$.
