My information is that in 1981 the first 924 Turbo (announced 1979) was
uprated from a 2 litre slant 4 engine outputting 170 BHP and 140 mph to
with a new turbo unit producingh 177 BHP and 143 mph. In the same year
the 924 Carrera GT (another turbo) arrived with 177 BHP and 143 mph. The
924S arrived in 1985 ('86 model year) with 150 BHP and 130 mph.

Boschen and Barth's Porsche Specials list 931 as supercharged 924.
