Quickest/cheapest solution? Buy a light-duty 90-degree drive for your 3/8"
drill (easily under $15), a short 1/2" drill bit (under $3) and spend 10
minutes drilling the head off the bolt. The bolt should easily twist out
by hand when the head comes off.
