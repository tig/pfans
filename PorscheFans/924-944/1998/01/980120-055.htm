The top end of the engine will make a very pronounced tapping noise 
when the oil has bled off the tappets.  Normally this does not happen 
quickly, but if you let your car sit for several weeks it can occur. 
 In your case, you may have lifters which have gone soft and are 
bleeding off far too quickly.
