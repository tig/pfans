Yes sir I have seen this problem, your guys are close it is a driver
(Injector) problem. The Brain box can only "drive" a limited amount of
current to the injectors, as resistance builds up in the wiring the
voltage drop is not enough to activate the injectors. When you remove
the load of one the voltage rise is just enough to do the job.

Most likely causes are:
1.	Injector ground (earthling)
2.	Corrosion in injector wires
3.	Corrosion in connector on firewall where injector leads come
through.
4.	And lastly but most likely, corrosion on edge connector of brain
(water drips from battery try onto brain)
