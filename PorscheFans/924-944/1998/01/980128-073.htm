Jay,
The 2/3 manifold was cracked at the flanges and in the center, like a wishbone
begining to split.  The 1/4 manifold was fine.  I don't know when it was
replaced, it's the updated manifold.  I'm concerned over how long it will hold
up because it wasn't done with stainless welding wire.  My fault.  I wrapped
them in header tape, and they look pretty good.  The manifold that was welded
was about 1mm wider, meaning the flanges were farther apart.  Took quite a bit
of prybar and back ache until I got the damn bolts in (I pulled the studs, and
replaced them with bolts.  In order to take the manifolds off with the engine
in is to pull the studs.  Then to replace the manifolds, you'ld have to
install the studs with the manifold on anyway.  

The manifolds came of relatively easily.  I used a floor jack and piece of
plywood to lift the engine a bit (If this is bad for the engine in any way
PLEASE tell me!).  This gave me enough clearance to pull the manifolds.

Good luck,
