If you can't locate one used, you might try the dealer.  New ones are not
available, but they sell 'refurbished' units.  These are units returned
for warranty repair to Blaupunkt, then resold by PCNA.  The price will be
reasonable, especially with the 15% PCA discount.  I checked last year for
a Reno replacement, it was about $300.  The Houston may be less, since it
is an older model. 

Consider a Reno if you can't find a Houston.  It has a logic controlled 
tape transport which is decent, but it has an excellent tuner section 
with good sensitivity and interference/multipath rejection.
