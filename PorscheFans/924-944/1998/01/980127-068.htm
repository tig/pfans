> I am considering to install a 1991 944S2 transmission in my 1988 944 
(8V).
>Are there any reasons why this would not work?

Yeah, you'll tear the teeth off the pinion gear sooner or later, 
probably sooner.  The 016 transmissions used in the aspiro eight-valve 
cars are somewhat marginal in design, esp. as the mileage piles up. 
 The AGx transmissions used in the 944S, S2, and 951 have substantially 
larger pinion gears, and wider (thicker) gearsets.

There was no cut-off year per se for improvements to the 016.  It 
enjoyed a lot of little changes over the years, including a shorter 
fifth gearset in the 1988 version.
