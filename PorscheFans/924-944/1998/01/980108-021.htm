Roxanne,

	I drilled a very small hole in the lenses to let it drain, I
could not get the goldfish food in the top, and they died.

	Two tricks to try:

1.	make sure the drains on the spoiler are clear, they look like
two rectangles on either side at base of glass & Spoiler
2.	Make sure the drains on the latches are connected and not
clogged. They are the tubes at the base of latches, you will have to pop
the rear carpet cove to see them. I have an air supply, so I just give
them a good blow. You might have to improvise.
3.	If all else fails, use a # 60 drill or smaller to make the light
drain out.

	Best of luck
