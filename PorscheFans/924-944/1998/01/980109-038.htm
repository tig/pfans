Just my experience with the Firestone Firehawk 690.
Good price, good performance on dry road and you will not spend money on
other tires so soon (they last for a long time). The problem is
aquaplaning. I don't know for sure if it's a 924 or a Firehawk problem.
Anyway, when rains hard, everybody goes faster than me. The next tires, I'm
considering are Pilot SX, but this ones are 50% more expensive. But, IMHO,
the Firehawk are a very good value, if you don't mind to slow down on rain
(at least, in my case). Maybe there are some differences between the 690
and SZ50.
