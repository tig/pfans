Something is warming up and failing. Buy a can of Cold Spray(yes there
really is such a thing) from an electronics hobby store. When the
problem happens again, try spraying the speed and reference sensors
located on top of the bellhousing. Also the DME box under the passenger
footwell. If this allows you to start the car more rapidly you have
found the problem.     Dave C.
