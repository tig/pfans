That "whoosh" is definitely pressure in the tank.  Franz (my '79 924) 
sometimes has a hot restart problem, especially after the 12 mile climb to 
my home at 6000' in the mountains.  Sometimes I can resolve it by opening 
the gas cap and then cranking it, but last summer I had a couple of 
episodes in which fumes continued to rush out for up to 15 minutes!  I 
could also hear what sounded like bubbling in the tank.  Could it be a form 
of vapor lock in which gasoline boils in the fuel lines around the engine 
and builds pressure in the system?
