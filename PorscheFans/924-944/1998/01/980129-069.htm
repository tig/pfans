You do not say if it is over boost or no boost. In any case the number
one boost problem is a vaccum or pressure line leak. If overboost is the 
problem check the line going from the wastegate. A crack here will cause
overboost then the computer gets in to the act and shuts down the party.
If no boost ails you check all vaccum lines also check the large intake
boot.
