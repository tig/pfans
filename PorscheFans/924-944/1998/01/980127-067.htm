The expense of the Brembo service kits certainly is true, at least it 
was a few years ago when I last checked.  Spending $100 a caliper would 
be a bargain - the prices I saw were closer to $150 - $200 depending on 
the piston diameters you had.  The reason for the cost was that a) 
Porsche would only sell the kits with new pistons included, and b) they 
forced Brembo not to make the kits available through any other 
channels.  (Porsche did a similar deal with Mahle which pushed the 
prices of some 911 p&c sets through the roof.)

More recently, I've seen Truechoice and Stillen both advertising Brembo 
repair parts and services.  I would check with them to see whether the 
seals-only kits can be had in the US.
