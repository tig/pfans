I have used Techron in all my cars for a number of years without any
downside.  In my 911 track car, Techron (IMHO) helped the #5 injector
regain it's proper spray pattern (compared to the other injectors).
Idle and power output were noticeably improved.

I use Techron about every 15K miles in all my P'cars -- after reading
the Porsche Tech Bulletin for my '87 951 recommending it's use.  Also,
Chevron is not a branded gas in my state (NH), so Techron is not a pump
fuel additive up here.

Another interesting data-point is that my '86 951 has not experienced
any fuel line deterioration as of yet - 114K miles.  I'll keep the list
posted if that changes...
