In a message dated 98-01-15 15:37:08 EST, you write:

<< Is this the "cycling valve"?  Do you have the part number? >>

No.  The cycling valve is located under the intake manifold between the 3/4
(or is it 1/2) intake runner and modulates the amount of pressure the
wastegate diaphram is supplied.  The cutoff valve (or turbo bypass valve or
turbo popoff valve) is located on the intake boot next to the coolant resevoir
and is about the size of a film canister and opens up to let intake air
circulate through the turbo when the throttle is closed.
