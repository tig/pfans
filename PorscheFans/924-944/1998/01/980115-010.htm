Here's a pump failure incident for you.  Late '85 944 in for change of
timing and shaft belts. Mechanic purposely overtightened the belts a bit
because he knew I drove hard. Without telling me. Screaming water pump
occured the next morning. Pump replaced.  No problem (no bad sounds, no
overheating problems) with the pump before belt replacement.  Could have
been a pump ready to go south anyway, but who knows.  Bottom line: be
careful with belt tension.
