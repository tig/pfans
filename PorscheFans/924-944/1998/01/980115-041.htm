Hello Manfred

	Chances are you have the original rotor or some has glued it on in place. You
will need to carefully crush the old rotor off with some pliers or vise-grips.
If you feel uncomfortable take you car to a compentent Porsche repair shop.
Make sure you use blue loctite to secure the rotor's set screw! The Germens
love to glue on the rotors from the factory, VW used this same method on their
16 Valve engines too!
