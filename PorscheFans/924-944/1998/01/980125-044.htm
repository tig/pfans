I HAD A WATER LEAK IN MY REAR FOOT WELL ON THE RIGHT SIDE. FIRST I
LOOKED AT THE PROVEBERIAL BATTERY BOX --- IT LOOKED OK.

I BLEW OUT I.E.- CLEANED MY SUN ROOF DRAIN HOLES--- REPLACED MY SUN ROOF
SEAL. DID NOT STOP THE LEAK. I FINALLY PULLED UP ALL CARPETING ON THE
PASSENGER SIDE OF THE CAR AND FOUND THE LEAK. IT WAS LEAKING FROM THE
BATTERY BOX SMALL CRACKS IN THE THICK RUBBERY COATING. I USED FIBERGLASS
FILLER AND SPRAYED UNDERCOATING OVER THAT. THEN REPAINTED THE ENTIRE
AREA. THAT SOLVED MY WATER PROBLEM IN MY REAR PASSENGER FOOT WELL. THE
WATER WAS RUNNING UNDER THE SOUND PROFFING MATERIAL IN TO THE REAR AREA.
