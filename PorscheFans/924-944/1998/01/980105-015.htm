Just be aware that if the main seal is leaking oil, there is a chance
that the clutch disc has been contaminated anyway. You'll be able to
notice this by a burning oil smell and/or slipping clutch. Once it's out
to repair the main seal, it'll be noticeable.  If it has been
contaminated, it should be replaced. I just wonder if, under your
warranty, they'll replace it too, free of charge since it failed due to
the leaking seal?  Just a thought..
