P-Fans,
This past summer while at a DE event, I walked back to my car after a run
session to find a pool of anti-freeze running away from it. As my car is a
turbo and it was just a pretty hard run (FTD, and for a water pumper!!), my
immediate thoughts went to head gasket. It wasn't. The hose for the heater
from the water pump had burst.
I was fortunate when a fellow 951 owner happened to have a spare and was
willing to share it!!! But during these winter months when so many of us
catch up on projects around the car, I thought I might share this experience
with everyone. I have now changed all the hoses and belts and feel quite
prepared (at least in this area) for next season.
If your car is more than 9 years old, it may be the preventive maintenance
that saves your track day or your next road trip from disappointment. By the
way, the burst hose was still soft, pliable and felt to be in good shape. I
found an entire set of hoses available from OG Racing. They are in Pano and
Excellence or have a web page at http://www.ogracing.com/ .
