The 944 has a central jack point located under the side rocker panels 
(denoted by a diamond stamped into the rocker) that accepts the factory 
spare tire jack.

Lifting at that point will lift the entire side of the car in fell swoop. 
 I use a hydraulic floor jack and a soft pad to do the same.

The four factory stubs located behind each wheel are not robust enough 
for jacking and best avoided.  Their true purpose is a bit of a mystery.  
I think they were factor lift/alignment points during assembly and never 
saw much stress.
