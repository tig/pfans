Chet,

A cracked rotor cap as well as a cracked distributor cap can cause both
problems you're having.  A cracked rotor cap can allow moisture in the
cracks and actually ground the rotor cap to the stud on the cam it is
attached to.  A cracked distibutor cap can allow moisture in the cracks to
short out between two plug wires.  Remember, water is a conductor of
electricity, a poor one but it is still a conductor.

Side note - major cause of battery failure is dirt on top of a battery.
The dirt holds moisture and over time it is a constant short between the
posts.
