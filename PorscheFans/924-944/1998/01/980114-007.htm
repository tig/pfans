Check all of your engine sensors: O2, engine temp, air flow meter, =
throttle switch, and aux air valve, as well as the thermal vacuum switch =
for the fuel pressure regulator. Look at your ignition system as well: =
cap, rotor, wires, etc. My 85 has a similar intermittent problem, where =
it all of a sudden develops a lumpy idle. Unplugging/replugging/cleaning =
the connections clears it up. Also check the flywheel sensors for proper =
ops. If you can run it long enough without tearing up the motor mounts =
under 'lumpy' conditions, do so, then let it cool and pull the plugs. =
This will tell you if the problem is with all cylinders, or one in =
particular.
