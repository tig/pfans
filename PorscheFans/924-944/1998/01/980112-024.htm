I've got a set that work reasonabley well in blocking brake dust from
accumulating on the exterior of the wheels. However, remember that this
also reduces the air flow around the brakes, making it easier to heat them
up. I think they're worth the money for a street-driven car, but I would
remove them before any extended spirited driving, i.e., driver's ed, Club
racing, etc.
