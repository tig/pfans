Clean with any good contact cleaner and protect with dialectric grease. I
use the stuff sold by Permatex. I have no idea what it is made from but I
strongly suspect it is a silicone of some sort.

Anyway, I use the stuff on all four of the cars I currently maintain and
have never had any contact corrosion problems. I put it on relay and fuse
lugs, temperature sensor connectors, AFM connectors, everything.
