Chris,

I've used a product called E-Z bleed that I got from AJ-USA and sells for
$44.50 in their current catalog. The "supply" side is gravity feed with a
nifty aluminum check valve which is just inverted and placed in your master
cylinder reservior. At the wheel, there is a hose which attaches to the
bleed valve at the caliper with a one-way valve that stops air from bleeding
back in. With the combination of the two units, one person can easily bleed
the brakes solo.

AJ-USA can be reached at 1-800-877-1911, e-mail at ajusa@ajusa.com or their
web page is http://www.ajusa.com .

They also have a few other interesting items including complete hose kits
with a 16-piece Turbo S set going for $103.95 or an genuine Porsche Tire
Inflator for $64.95.
