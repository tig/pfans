Friendly warning!

Don't try this with an 85.5 or later car.. If you push the clutch in with
the cruise engaged, your tach needle will instantly peg at redline!  It
happened to me, and the Owners Manual warns not to do this.  Its a stupid
design flaw.. Porsche should have installed a clutch switch in series with
the brake switch, which cancels the CC when the brake is depressed.  Or 
maybe there is a switch, and mine is defective.  :-)
