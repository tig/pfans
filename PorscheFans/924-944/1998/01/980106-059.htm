To the 924/944 and 928 Lists:

Thanks to Hank Watts for composing the notice.  For those of you whom may not
know Hank, he has written two books about cars.  One of them is called Secrets
of Solo Racing, which is all about AX and solo track event driving.  Great
book-- makes an excellent gift.

Anyway, I have attended the School described below, and for the last two years
served as an instructor.  Last year at the school, I had a student with a 993
turbo, which he let me drive!!!  This event always gets full almost
immediately (i.e, within a few weeks, if not days, after registration opens),
so stay tuned.  Although both Masuo and Jeff are 911 drivers, they are great
guys, and tremendous drivers.

At the school, we sometimes have some ex-pro and semi-pro racers helping to
instruct, along with the usual group of outstanding men and women from around
the Bay Area.  You'll get a lot of seat time for you money, and the
environment is safe and friendly.  Last year, we had some students who
traveled quite a distance to join us, and I believe that they found the
experience extremely worthwhile.

This School is a Porsche Club of America Zone 7 event, but anyone is welcomed
to sign up an attend, as long as they are driving a Porsche.

If you are interested, sign up immediately upon the opening of registration,
or else you may not make it in.  I will post a notice when official
registration opens.

- Mike Mitchell
