Stefan,
I haven't imported Porsche parts from the US, but I have imported other 
items and these are the taxes we pay in the UK:

New part not made in EU: 10% import tax + 17.5% VAT (also pay VAT on Import 
Tax)
New part made  in EU: 17.5% VAT
You are also supposed to pay the tax on what the amount costs in the UK.
If it's a used part you may not have to pay the VAT, I am not sureabout this 
though, any tax exports confirm ?
