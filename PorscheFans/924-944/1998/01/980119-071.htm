Hi P-fans!

Although reading a lot about this hydrolock nightmare, my personal
experience is not so bad with the stock air-intake system.
Yesterday I got in a heavy rainstorm an the german autobahn,
running around 200km/h while hitting a really deep puddle. Water
was spraying around everywhere, I couldn't see anything for a long
moment, lucky it was on a long straight.

Having read about hydrolock I got scared but the car was running
fine.

At home first thing was removing the cover of the air filter and
checking the filter and the hoses. The hose in the front wheel
housing was wet at the first one or two inches, but no water in
the filter housing.

Just my personal experience
