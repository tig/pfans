John, if you are fairly adept with tools then I would encourage you to
investigate your sunroof problem.  They're really not all that complicated,
although it would be a good idea to have the shop manual.  I'll bet it will
be pretty obvious what is wrong once you get the interior panels out of the
way.  However, you may find that the part you need is only sold by Porsche
as part of a larger assembly (the cable drive assy for example).  I was
able to buy a good used part (portion of the cable drive) from Kempton
Bros. (Tampa, FL) and so was able to avoid the high expense of buying the
entire assy new from Porsche.  Taking it apart will also allow you to
re-lubricate the cables and gears.  Mine had gotten pretty stiff due to
dried up lubricant.  It now works great.
