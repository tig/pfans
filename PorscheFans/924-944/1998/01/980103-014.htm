Dan,

	Porsche did make an "S" option for the 931. It was only a
suspension, brakes and cosmetic thing, no engine mods or power
upgrades. I do not have the option code for this item handy though
but look in the August 1980 issue of Road and Track, they do a road
test of an "S" optioned 931. Phone dials were not the wheels
included in the "S" option, they were 6"x16" flat disk forged wheels
styled like the (then) current 928 line.
