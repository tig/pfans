You are right to say:
> I have found a
> little plastic tab that sticks out, about 7 o'clock

That is the index mark for the bottom  pulley, which will have a "V"
notch (like the top pulley).  All is correctly lined up when:

- The engine is at TDC (#1 cylinder at Top Dead Center);
- The bottom balance shaft pulley "V" lines up with the tab at about 7
o'clock;
- The top balance shaft pulley "V" lines up with its index point.

Also, it is *much* easier to see/get to all this stuff if you jack up
the right side of the car...
Hope this helps..
