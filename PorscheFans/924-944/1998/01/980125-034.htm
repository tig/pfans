Simon,
Check the resistance of the pump itself by connecting your multimeter to the
terminals of the pump.  The pump should have very little resistance if the
motor is good--approximately 20 ohms.  Could it be bad out of the box?  Have
you checked the pump out of the car (i.e., using clean tubing only) to see
if it pumps on 12 v?  The windshield (windscreen) washer pump for 924, 944,
and 928 are all the same from 1977-87.  These pumps are generally quite
reliable.  Could it be that the screen filters, one way valves, or tubing
connectors are clogged?  Have you tried squirting water by hand or blowing
air to see if the system is functional?
