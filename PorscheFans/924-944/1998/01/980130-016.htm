I would say its more likely to be the wires to the solenoid, Can you
hear a large Click when you turn the key? If you can't then its probably
the starter solenoid or cables. All the starter cables go down past the 
exhaust pipe and can get brittle or in my old 924 melt and fuse.
( It engaged the start at about 60mph fun NOT! )

A word of caution. If you end up replacing the unit, make sure you get
a 924 NA starter, the 944 look the same (one is slightly larger than the
other) but the turn in the opposite direction :)
