Hi Bill,

I'm sure there's others more qualified than me, but my understanding
is that stock boost peaks about 1.9 on the in-car boost gauge. If the
car you were testing is only hitting 1.2 on the gauge, there is
definitely
something going on. The KLR box will limit boost to .2 bar (or 1.2 on
the
gauge) if it thinks the engine might be damaged. Possible causes could
be a loose vacuum hose, dirty contacts on the cycling valve or TPS
(throttle position switch). In my car, I had a bad TPS which would
stick as it warmed up.
