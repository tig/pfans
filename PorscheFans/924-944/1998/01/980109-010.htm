Hi Roxanne,

On my 90 S2, I found that the water was entering the lens through breaks
along the black separator that splits the lens length wise.  The fix was to
remove the lens, lay it flat so lens is up/horizontal, and run a bead or two
of superglue along the length of the black piece.

That was last summer and so far no leaks.  Now, what to do about the high
water mark inside the lens....
