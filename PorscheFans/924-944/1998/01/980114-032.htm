Hi, all,

I read somewhere the reason that factory pads are so soft and dusty. 
The DOT specifies a maximum (can't remember the exact number) allowable
brake pedal pressure that will produce a .5 G stop in the event of brake
booster failure.  The pads have to be reverse engineered to meet this
standard, hence they are too soft for a normally functioning brake
system.
