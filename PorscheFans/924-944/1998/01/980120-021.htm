On this cold knock subject-

A friend and I followed George's solid advice on this issue on the friend's
S2.  He couldn't figure out which lifter (tappet) was making the noise, so
he bought a fistful and figured we would replace those in the knocking area
and hopefully cure "the one".  We ended up leaving the suspect lifters
overnight sitting in Dixie cups after removing them from the engine- the
next morning the one which had leaked a big puddle into the bottom of the
dixie cup was clearly the culprit.

The message here- if you only feel like spending the cash for one lifter,
let them "leak down" overnight on a paper towel or something so you'll know
which one to replace.  Good luck, jeers also accepted at the above address....
