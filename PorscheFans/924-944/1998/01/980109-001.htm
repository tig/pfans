*I have an '81 924 which runs beautifully until it rains.   Then it
*misfires terribly and barely runs.  I have replaced the plugs, rotor,
*cap, ignition wires and coil but the problem is still there. I've even
*hosed down the engine but it only misfires under load, not at idle.

I had a similar problem with my Volvo 240 which also has K-Jetronic fuel
injection.  I did all the same things as you.  Finally tracked down the
problem to the fuel injector seals - cheap and very easy to replace.  I
change them routinely now every 12,000 miles.

Hope this helps.
