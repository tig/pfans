options under the PCA club racing rules for the 'stock' class are 16" or
17" wheels. The
fronts can be 7" to 8" wide and the rears can be 9" to 10" wide.

The obvious answer is to go to the widest and largest possible - 17x8 in
front and 17x10 in the rear, but I've heard some conflicting stories

makes 250 HP. Tires are not a problem since the better 'street' tires,
such as BFG R1s or Kumhos, are available in both diameters.
