Since some battery places don't have Porsche listed in their battery
replacement books, just specify a battery for a VW Golf or Jetta (Vento)
Diesel.  The actual battery size is Group 41.  Be sure to insist on this
size for proper fit into the battery tray and proper terminal hook-up.
I've had many unscrupulous dealers try to sell me "any old battery that
will fit" - even for my Jetta.. but I refuse every time.  Why do they do
this?
