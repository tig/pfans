I assume you have already checked/bled your clutch master and slave
cylinders.  
One guess for the clutch behavior you describe is that one or more of those
little clutch disk springs have been liberated and are intermittently
interfering with pressure plate operation.  I had this scenario occur on my
rubber donut clutch disk where a chunk of rubber was moving around in there
and sometimes binding up the p.p.  Finally, I guess the rubber really began
to come unglued (so to speak) and a large chunk permanently wedged itself
into the p.p. release spring.
