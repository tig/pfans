For the guy who is looking for the ac leak, the standard practice is to
partially pressurize the system while adding an ultraviolet dye. After
running system for a short time, a UV light is shined on the engine, and
any leak will shine under the light, thus showing where the leak is. I dont
know if this can be done at home, I would take it to a good shop. 
By the way, did you know that a cats sense of smell is many times greater
than any dog. Problem is they have no desire whatsoever to do any work.
