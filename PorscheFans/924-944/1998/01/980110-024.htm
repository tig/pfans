Casey, I would say your best bet would be to use heat.  I use a common
propane torch to carefully heat the area of the block around the bolt
thread.  Then grab the piece sticking out real tight with vise grips.  I
have successfully used this procedure on water pump bolts.  Make sure you
take all necessary safety precautions (goggles, keep away from flammable,
etc.)
