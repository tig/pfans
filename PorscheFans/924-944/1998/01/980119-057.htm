I've never experienced  this in car tires, but have numerous times with
Motorcycle rubber.  The manufacturer always replaces the tires, but if they
are mail ordered you lose the shipping and mount/balance.  On my Porsche I
tossed around mail ordering the tires, but discovered that the local tire
stores (I used Discount tire) matched the mail order prices once pushed and
prodded into it.  Try local before going through the hassle.  Just tell them
to match the price or you'll go elsewhere....they want your money.
