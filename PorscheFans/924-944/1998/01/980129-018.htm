The hose you speak of is for ventilation of the armour enclosure around
the timing & balance shaft belts (black plastic multi-part housing).  In
the factory manuals, I've read that the factory later felt that this
hose was superfluous.  If I understand the notes correctly, the hose may
be removed altogether as long as the holes are closed using plugs you
may find through your dealer.
