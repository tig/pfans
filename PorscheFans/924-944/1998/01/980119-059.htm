A Tip. Trying to remove those pesky allen head bolts?

	When trying to remove an almost stripped out allen head bolt (or any other
type fastener for that matter) try putting a dab of valve lapping compound in
the allen heads tip or tool end. The added friction will usually make a tough
job a snap (no pun intended!).
