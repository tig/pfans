The purpose of the wires in question is to send a signal to the DME to
boost the idle speed when the power steering is fully cranked to one
stop or the other, resulting in a stalled PS pump, and the resultant
load dropping the idle speed.

Unfortunately, it has been the cause of several wiring harness fires, and
resulting DME catastrophes (ask me how I know...)...

Anyway, my recommendation would be to leave the wires off... It seems to
have
been one of Porsches better ideas that was better off left undone...

Good Luck!
