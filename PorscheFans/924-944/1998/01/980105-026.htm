Farzaan Kassam wrote:
> 
> 1. How do you take the door panel off on a normal door 951 (not the big
>    speaker package door).  I have something rattling around in there I
>    want to take care of.
>
1. Remove screws holding bottom of armrest to panel/door.
2. Pull inside door release to expose phillips screw and remove the screw.
   Remove trim plastic held by the screw.
3. Remove phillips screws holding window switches to panel. Unplug switch
   assembly and remove.
4. Pop plastic covers off various phillips screws around top of panel and
   remove the screws. A very thin-bladed flat screw driver will remove the
   plastic caps without damage.
5. Remove the speaker grill and the speaker.
6. Start at the bottom of the panel and pop the plastic fastenters free from
   the door. Work around the lower part of the panel until all are free.
7. Grasp the armrest handle (still attached to the panel) and pull up to free
   the panel from the door.
8. You'll have to remove the plastic film water barrier to see anything. If
   you are careful you should be able to do so without tearing the plastic.
   When you re-assemble the door be sure the original adhesive still holds
   the plastic or use some new adhesive.

You'll probably find one or more of the window regulator screws loose. One-by-one,
remove, clean and replace with 241 (blue) Loctite to fix the problem for a
long time.
