You will find now for many things, the Canadian dealer price (after the
usual PCA club discount) can be the best price. It has been for every (yes,
*every*) part I've had to buy last year, especially when you take shipping
(afaik, no US "free shipping" parts place extends that offer to Canada),
exchange, brokerage, and GST (HST for you) into account.

E.g.:
951 euro fog/driving light assembly (no bulb or wiring) : $140-$170US from
the usual sources (+exchange, shipping, brokerage, GST) = $250-$300CDN
Local dealer price (euro fog/driving is the stock item in Canada) with PCA
club member discount: $140CDN (+GST +PST) =  $160CDN
