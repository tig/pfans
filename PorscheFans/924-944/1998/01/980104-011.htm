I've used Mobil 1 75W90 for a bunch of years in my 944 gearbox, with 
positive results in terms of usability (shifting) and wear.  Although 
it falls outside of Porsche's nominal recommendations, bear in mind 
that those recommendations were made before Mobil 1 (or any synthetic) 
hypoid gear lube came onto the market.

12.1 volts is low.  I've seen charts mapping values like this to a 
"percent charged" state.  I believe that 12.6 volts is the ideal static 
reading.  I would begin by checking the battery cells - look for fluid 
level, topping off with distilled water as needed.  Look for lots of 
crap in the cells, nothing you can do about that.  And check the 
specific gravity of each cell - you may have a bad cell or two.
