The crossover pipe for the 951s was changed to the two piece version half
way through the 1986 model year.  The two piece crossover pipe allows the
clutch to be replaced without disconnecting the exhaust at the turbocharger
inlet which requires the intake manifold to be removed (Usually an
additional 8 hour labor charge).  Rumor has that when some of the old style
944 turbo exhaust manifolds (without the expansion bellows) were replaced
under warranty or if a clutch had to be done due to premature failure, many
of the single crossover pipes were replaced by the two piece design at the
same time.
