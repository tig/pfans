Back a few years when I owned a 78 924, I had the same problem.

of the battery with the little flap) It took me a while to figure it out,
but what

leaves,
allowing water of any sort to fill the battery tray faster than it would
drain.
The vent for the fresh air was really low in conjuction with the battery
tray,
and as soon as the water level reached that height, it will flood into the
passenger
footwell.
