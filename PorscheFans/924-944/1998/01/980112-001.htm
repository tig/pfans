The kw figure of 102/5500 rpm is not !!!! the real power. In germany for
every modification on motor there has to be a paper called
"gutachten"-certificate for emmision and change of power. The figures of
this paper are filled in your "Fahrzeugbrief". This thing (this
certificate) is normally done once (for the prototype) - it costs a
lot... about 5000 DM (2800 $)..... It is like you buy a new car eg. a
Ford Ecord with 100hp. It shoul have 100hp.. some have 108 hp others
only have 94 hp but all are sold at 100hp....
Abgaskruemmer is not a performance exhaust system. It is the exhaust
manifold. But when it's printed there, it is a part of another
manufacturer, not genuine porsche.. The complete stuff means: You have a
modified head (grinded (?),polished, bigger channels, other valves..),
another camshaft (more degrees), another exhaust manifold, a thinner
head gasket (more compression).
