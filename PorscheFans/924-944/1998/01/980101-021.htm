	Sounds like your idle stabilizer is shot. With engine running, smack the idle
stabilizer with a H.D. screw driver. If the idle changes your idle stabilizer
is shot. The valve inside is sticking. Also do not for get to check the
obvious stuff, TPS throttle switch, air flow meter connector, and vacuum
leaks.
