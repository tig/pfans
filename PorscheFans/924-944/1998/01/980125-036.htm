Simon,
Crank no more than 12 seconds at one time.  Try cranking twice, wait one
minute, crank twice again, wait one minute, and the next crank should do it.
If not, try releasing the pressure in the tank by loosening then
retightening the gas cap--this sometimes works, depending on the cause of
the warm-start problem.  I don't know your model, but on some 924-944s, you
can take off the air cleaner, reach in and lift the air sensor to run the
fuel pump, on others you must disconnect an electrical connection beneath
the air sensor, others you cannot do at all quickly.
