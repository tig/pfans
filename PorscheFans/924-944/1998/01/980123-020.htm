Living in New York State, my 924 is on the road only from May through October. 
Storing it is a piece of cake!  First, Change the oil. Then put two bottles of
FI cleaner/water remove in and drive it for atleast ten miles letting it get
completely through the system.  Then fill up the fuel to the brim.  Lastly, park
the car on plastic drop cloths covered with laarge sheets of cardboard (vapor
barrier and absorbant).  Obviously I clean the whole thing inside and out.
Finally, take out the battery, cover the car, and walk away!  Six months later I
install the battery, check the tire pressure and off I go!
