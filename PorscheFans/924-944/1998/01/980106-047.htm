Chris,

If your turbo is like my N/A, the only good place to run the
wires is under the plastic trim piece at the lower door jamb.
I've got a big (4 gauge) power cable, CD changer cable, and
tons of speaker cable split between the two sides (along with
all the stuff the factory stuck under there). It's starting
to get a little tight in there now. Be careful pulling off
the trim, mine were very brittle with age and broke easily.
I bought one used for the right side from George B (thanks!)
and promptly broke the left one. Dammit! Hope this helps some.
