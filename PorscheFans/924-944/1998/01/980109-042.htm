Someone asked me if the option codes were different and what the
differences were for a pre-1994 clutch pack ltd. slip and a Torsen...

Option code is the same (220). Torsens use worm gears and do not wear out.
These gears effectively transmit power in only one direction.
Clutch packs use friction materials (like your manual transmission)and
will wear out. Torsen has no static pre-load and therefore does not have
continuous frictional drag. Works only when car is under load, therefore
it has a minimal influence on vehicle dynamics during braking. Silent
operation, especially in low-speed turns, such as when parking. No
special lubricant needed.
