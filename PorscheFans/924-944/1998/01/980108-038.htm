> To carry on the recent thread on driving style, I've a
> question about vehicle dynamics.  I've noticed that if I
> *abruptly* release the brakes after mild
> trail-braking, there is a slight *oversteer* reaction.
> This is counter-intuitive, as I would expect weight to
> transfer to the rear with an abrupt brake release and
> a consequent *understeer* reaction.
> 
Interesting. Sam Moses was writing in his column in AW some time back about
the same effect (happened to him when he spun a 993 with some Famous
Porsche Pilot in the right seat). The FPP attributed it to the front wheels
abrubtly contributing more cornering force because they were suddenly not
tasked with the braking force.

Not real intuitive but if you think about it kind of makes sense.
