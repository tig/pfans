Kevin,
The mounting threads of the antenna are pushed through the hole in the
fender.  The antenna is mounted (secured) with a screw (just one) through a
cylindrical bracket which goes into the wheel-well wall inside the fender.
The cable is threaded through the wheel-well through one of two holes and,
of course, goes to the center console.  Access to the screw holding the
antenna is easier if the front wheel is taken off, but this need not be
done.
