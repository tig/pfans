David Fasgold asked about a 77 4spd to replace the 79 5spd.

Yes with reservations. I think your 79 probably has the Porsche based
'Snail Shell' trans and the 77 the Audi based unit. If that is the case
you must change out the torsion bar carrier tube and the drive shaft
along with the trans (I think).

See: Drivetrain Components: Jim Pasha, Excellence, October 1994, page
109. Driveshaft, gearbox and half-shafts. Must read. Table of transaxle
applications and basic specifications.

You might want to see what it would take to repair the 5spd. Might not
be that bad.

Good luck, John Brown
