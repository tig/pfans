I have both types of jack plates - for the up to 85 and for the 85.5 and up
type 944s.  They are essentially round plates with welded surrounds and the
appropriate metal dimple or nipple and or a stirrup to engage the car's
jack point.  They sit nicely in the cup of a 2ton floor jack and alleviate
the need for a scrap 2X4.  If you have a turbo be forewarned that you need
a floor jack that will go 5" or lower for you to be able to use these
devices.  I personally do not think they are worth the $15 US most places
charge for them though. (Check Tweeks, Automotion etc.)
