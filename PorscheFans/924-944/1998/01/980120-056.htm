I don't believe you will need to remove the fan to change the switch. 
 Depending on your model and year, you may find it easier to do the job 
from the top or the bottom, and you may need to temporarily remove a 
radiator hose.  Other than that, the job is simple enough that an 
extensive FAQ write-up isn't needed.  Do replace the fibre washer on 
the sender with a new one, don't overtorque the sender - it's going 
into plastic or plastic with a brass liner.
