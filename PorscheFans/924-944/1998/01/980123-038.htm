This is an answer to the question of Gold Fuchs wheels.   My 1985/1
Black 944 came stock with Gold Fuchs in 7 and 8 by 15.  I have seen
several and they are usually on black or red 944's or 911s.  When I
tried to repaint them I was not able to find the stock color paint from
the local dealer.  I am ashamed to say I painted them a GMC champaigne
color.  They look good to me and I don't do concours, so I don't care. 
If anyone knows how to get the stock color let me know.
