In the early 944s if you "push the instruments out of the cluster" with
the cluster fully installed, you will break a white plastic tab holding
the cluster in place and aligning it. Each instrument also has a rubber
friction band around it, and it is difficult to push the instrument in
all the way even with the cluster upside down on a stable surface.
Reaching in behind the dash and trying to install the instrument will
leave it misaligned and prone to vibration, besides missing the plastic
tab to hold it in place.
