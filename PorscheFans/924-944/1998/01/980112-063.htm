Clark, I suspect that there are differences between the early and later
944s as far as this wiring is concerned.  The factory wiring diagram for
the early 944 shows 3 switches and 2 of them are mounted (stacked together)
at the aft end of the cable drive rail (I had to adjust these after
replacing a broken lift arm cable).  This pair is adjustable along the
track and determine where the cable will stop.  One of the switches, as you
said, is up in the header between the sun visors.  The only other switch in
the circuit is the one on the console with which you actuate the roof
(really doesn't qualify as a microswitch eh?).  I'm sorry if my info
confused anyone with a later model car.
