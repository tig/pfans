A guess on the cause for excessive oil consumption would be that the oil
control rings (below the compression rings on the piston) are frozen.  The
oil rings wipe off most of the oil that splashes up on the cylinder wall
during operation.  I have read that they  can freeze up on engines that sit
for an extended period.  Do you know how long the car sat before you
repaired it?  I think it is possible that you could have good compression
while having bad oil control if those oil rings are stuck.
