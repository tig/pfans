Look closely at the color. If it is light tan, it's champagne. If it's
nearly white, it's linen. If it is off-white toward the gray side, it may
be grey beige. Grey beige is usually found with black seats and a black
dash. Champagne is more often found with a brown or burdundy dash and brown
or burgundy seats. Linen can be found with either, but usually I've seen it
with similarly-colored (leather) seats.
