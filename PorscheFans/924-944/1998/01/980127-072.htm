	I found that the door rattle is the cable connecting the interior
handle to the latch mechanism rattling on the inner-framework of the door.
To solve this you do need to remove the door pannel, which I found to be
quite easy, as long as you are carefull with the clips.  I put a 3" piece
of foam around the cable, and it has been quiet ever since..
