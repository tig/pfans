		Mileage is not a major item with 944's, they have a few
basic flaws which I treat as consumables (see table), and of course the
birth year effects the price as well as the standard features. 

	I have found the '83 to be the best put together year ! It did not
have the neat dash, the better air flow, electric seats, or cast A-arms, but
it was very well put together, I think that year has held together better
than any other year.
