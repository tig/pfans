Shaun, first, I seriously doubt that you can repair the broken cable going
to the driver's side lifting arm.  I got a good used one from Kempton
Brothers (Tampa, FL).  Your set up sounds like mine in that the white box
with the gears had metal gears and on mine they were not stripped.  When
you get the replacement cable, assemble every thing (I suggest adding some
grease to the lifting arm boxes) and then before you try it, make sure the
lifting arms are raising the roof equal amounts on each side.  If not, you
may have to take apart the white box again and shift one or the other cable
relative to the gear.  After these are properly adjusted, you will then
have to make sure the limit switches are adjusted right.  (see my posting
from the other day).  It is a tedious job but it can be done.
