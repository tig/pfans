Michael,
	The Blauplunkt stereo has four holes in the corner into which a special
tool is inserted releasing the "locks" and allowing the unit to slide out.
Fortunately you can cut and bend a wire coat hanger into 2 "C" shaped tools
with the legs about 3 inches and the base the size of the distance between
the holes one end.

-----------------------------
|
|
-----------------------------
Insert the two tools into the four holes and wiggle it out.
