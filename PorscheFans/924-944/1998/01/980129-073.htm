It's perfectly OK to remove the teflon coating in the cylinders. Just make
sure that you buy replacement teflon coated piston rings.
What you don't want to do is use the teflon coated cylinders with the teflon
coated rings. This will result in an engine that is so slippery that it will
probably over rev when shifing.
