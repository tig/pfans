While out driving last weekend the outside temperature was about 40 deg C.
The fuel tank was full.  The result was the fuel pump became much louder
than normal and after a while fuel began to leak from the bottom of the
charcoal canister.  When I removed the fuel cap there was a great release of
pressure and the fuel leak from the charcoal canister stopped and the fuel
pump quietened somewhat.  Anyone else had this problem and if so what is the
fix?  My guess is a faulty breather somewhere.
