Yep, real easy...

Pop the black plastic ring off the switch (using a small screwdriver)
then you will see a hex ring surrounding the switch... Loosen that, an d
the switch falls into the body panel.

Open the driver door, remove the grille in the doorjamb and you can stick
your hand inside the body panel and fish out the switch... Remove the 
connectors and the switch is Free at Last!!!

Good Luck!
