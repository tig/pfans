In addition to the excellent step-by-step instructions Dan gave to the
list, I'll add one more:

On reinstallation, check that the main coolant hose from the radiator to
the pump has not slipped from the force of the engine being raised to
get to the mounts. After about twenty miles, if it is loose, the hose
will blow off the pump and you'll either have to get the car towed or
have an irate Significant Other who becomes stranded roadside.

I had both happen last month after replacing the motor mounts in my 86
951.... She has finally started talking to me again :-)
