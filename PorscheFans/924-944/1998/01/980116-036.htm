The glow means that your alternator is not producing enough power for your
load, so the question is your load too high or your alternator to low.

1. If your car is standard, then in most cases the brushes on your
alternator are down to the end of their life, they get consumed normally.
Nice thing is that on early cars they are field replaceable, check with you
local alternator rebuild or service group, should run about $15, and you pop
off the air box, and you can reach them.
2. Second alternative is you belt is lose, not cam shaft belt but the lower
ones, check to see it is correctly set.
