Actually the the 968 M030 front caliper is different than the 89 951 
caliper.  It uses the same pad and has the same swept area but the pistons 
are smaller than the 951 pistons.  Off the top of my head I don't recall 
the piston diameters. I have upgraded my 968 front calipers with those from 
a 89 951. When I purchased both the vibration dampers and the caliper 
rebuild kits for ease of ordering I specified the 968 M030 and the parts I 
received were too small and had to be returned for parts for a 89 951.
