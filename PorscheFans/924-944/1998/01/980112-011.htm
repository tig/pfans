local parts shops in Miami won't sell me the plastic connector alone. I
have to buy the door handle itself or rumage through the junk yards in
hopes to come across Porsche or VW door handle that is compatible and
simply remove that plastic joint and pay for that alone, if the boys at
desk will let me. Hope this helps.
