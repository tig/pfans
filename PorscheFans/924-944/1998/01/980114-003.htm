Robert:

I have autocrossed with 205/55/16 fronts and 225/50/16 rears, although on
street tires, and now with my 245/40/17 fronts and 275/40/17 rears (Hoosiers).
My Drivers Ed time has only been on the Hoosiers.  I can tell you that the
width of the contact patch with the 17" tires in these sizes is very different
than with the stock 16" sizes!  The 17" sizes mentioned do not rub at all on a
92 968.  If you are using the second set of wheels for track use only, why not
go all the way?  I'm pretty sure you will see a big difference.

I suspect that Matt Murray would be the expert on this subject.
