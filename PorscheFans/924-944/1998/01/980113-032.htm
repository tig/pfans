Engine temp sensor is located between #1 & #2 cylinders, drivers side =
under the intake manifold. From the front of the car it's just to the =
left and behind the air flow meter. The vacuum switch is between #3 =
cylinders, visible from above the manifold. One other thing to check is =
the auxiliary air regulator. This acts like a choke. Directions on how =
to check all of these wonderful little life improving devices is in the =
Haynes manual.
