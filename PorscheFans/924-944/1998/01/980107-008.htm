       You are correct.  You probably have air in your coolant system. If
you turn the ignition on, turn the heater controls to full heat, and vent
the coolant system, it should correct the problem. By the way, the 944
vents best when it has completely cooled. If you vent it while the system
is hot, it will develop air pockets as the coolant contracts during
cooling.
