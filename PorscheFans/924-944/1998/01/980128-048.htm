>Lee has provided me with information on the 968's 6-speed ratios. The
>following is a comparison of the 968's final drive ratios to the 951's
>final
>ratios with either the stock 3.38 or optional 3.89 ring and pinion:
>
> 968 -- 1st: 12.02  2nd: 7.56  3rd: 5.44  4th: 4.19  5th: 3.43  6th:
>2.94
>3.38 -- 1st: 11.83  2nd: 6.76  3rd: 4.73  4th: 3.48  5th: 2.80
>3.89 -- 1st: 13.61  2nd: 8.01  3rd: 5.44  4th: 4.00  5th: 3.22
>
>Next, the following performance test comparison data I'd put out earlier
>provides additional interesting information when comparing the above
>specs:
>
>3.89 Data:  0-30=2.0;  0-40=3.0;  0-50=4.1;  0-60=5.5; 0-70=7.1;
>0-80=8.8;
>0-100=13.6; & 1/4 mi=13.9/101
>3.38 Data:  0-30=2.2;  0-40=3.3;  0-50=4.3;  0-60=5.5; 0-70=7.3;
>0-80=9.1;
>0-100=14.1; & 1/4 mi=14.2/100
>
>Lastly, calculated speeds attained in each gear @6400rpm:
>
>968 --  1st: 35  2nd: 59  3rd: 83  4th: 103  5th: 128  6th: 156
>3.38 -- 1st: 37  2nd: 64  3rd: 94  4th: 130  5th: 162
>3.89 -- 1st: 33  2nd: 56  3rd: 83  4th: 113  5th: 140
>
