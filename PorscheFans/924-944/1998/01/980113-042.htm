Hi Richard,

M030 rear suspension is the same as standard 968 except for a
non-adjustable spring perch on the shock with a small barrel spring to
increase the rear spring rate.  Torsion bar diameter is same as stock
968 (25.4mm I think).  Rear sway bar is also different 19mm vs 16mm with
3 holes for adjustment.  Let me know if you have any more questions
