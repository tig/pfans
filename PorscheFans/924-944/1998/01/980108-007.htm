Michel,
        It sounds like there may be something else going on with your
timing belt.  With the hydraulic tensioners on the 968s, your belts should
not need the retension at 2000 miles that is required for the early 944s
without spring tensioners and the later model 944s with spring tensioners.
Your belt should still be inspected periodically (normally every 15,000
miles) but, if the hydraulic tensioner is working properly, your belt
should never need to be retensioned manually. It sounds as if your
hydraulic tensioner may not be working correctly.  Perhaps I have a
misconception about how the hydraulic tensioners on the 968s really work.
Can someone shed some light on this subject?
