    My 87 944S (116k miles) uses a lot, but slightly less, oil than you
describe, but similarly shows no other symptoms.  I don't even notice smoke
at any time.  The consumption does seem to rise precipitously with higher
speed driving.  Original posts suggested valve guides as the culprits.  I
have also seen many posts which describe leackage from the engine seals
which falls on the exhaust manifold thereby "disappearing."  I do notice a
very strong "sweet"  aroma in my car which oculd be associated with burning
oil.  I reassure myself with the thought of the constant stream of clean oil
I am circulating through the engine.
