I have been searching for a 968 model since I bought my car 2 years ago.
I FINALLY came across a kit on the web.  You actually have to buy it
from someplace in Japan.  It's a plastic model that you put together, so
be ready to paint/assemble 96 pieces!  I painted the car a gloss black
to match the color of my own car, and it looks quite good...much better
than the platic yellow that the model is orginally.  The kit is kind of
cool because it was obviously converted from 951 kit to a 968 kit.
There are still some 951 engine parts and seats included in the kit that
aren't used.  Heck, you could create your own, MODIFIED 968. haha.
Anyway, here's the address for the kit.  It's ~$20 + shipping (~$6 from
Japan).

http://www.iac.co.jp/~hlj/pages/hasegawa/hsgca12.html
