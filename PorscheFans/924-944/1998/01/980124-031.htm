If you're talking about the area behind the front passenger seat, I had
exactly the same problem.  Mine was leaking from around the rear hatch gasket
and dripping behind the side panel, under the carpet over the side wheel well,
under the seat and collecting in the wonderfully-designed reservoir behind the
passenger seat.  That sucker can hold ALOT of water!  Try SilGlide on the
gasket before you give in to replace it . . . . it worked for me.  Also, after
you sop up most of the water with towels, use a hair dryer to get the carpet
good and dry so it won't mildew.
