though the whole line, from 928 to 968, is now unfortunately dead, the
parts sharing concept (944 engine = 1/2 928 engine, etc.) adopted then is
the strategy that Porsche has maintained to stay in the black.  The only
two cars now in production share a lot of parts, for better or for worse.
