> You don't have to lift the motor much.
> 
> The trick to this job is to get the front up as high as you can
> *	Block and support the motor from underside (Nice block of wood
> on oil pan then floor jack)
> *	Remove the tires (front only)
> *	scribe the lower alignment arms
> *	unbolt the lower motor mount nut
> *	Unbolt the cross member bolts (4 - 2 each side)
> *	Unbolt the Steering rack from cross member
> *	Lower cross member down
> *	Unbolt the two upper motor mount bolts, and wigle the mount out.
