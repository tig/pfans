Yes, if you are trying to remove and change it from the top.  But if you 
work from below, all you need to do is remove the front right wheel 
(makes it easier to get under the car in that corner) and it is a lot 
easier to see and change.  A big socket the correct size also helps getting 
a grip on the switch to start with.  Remove the coolant by removing the 
bottom hose or opening the drain plug and then go to it.  Shouldn't take 
more than hour including installing coolant and bleeding the air out.
