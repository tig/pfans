The biggest thing to remember with a Porsche, be it a 924, 944, or 928, at
this price is that the price itself can be deceiving.

By that I mean you have to factor in the cost of maintenance for these
vehicles.  Its higher than most other cars that you could buy in this price
range.

One good place to start when looking at one of these vehicles is to read
the 944 FAQ which can be found at
 
http://www.connactivity.com/~kgross/944faq.html 

It will bring up many points for you to consider when looking to purchase
one of these vehicles.
