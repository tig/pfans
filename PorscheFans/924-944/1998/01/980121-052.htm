Mine was rattling when I purchased the car.  Checking from underneath revealed
that the shield had fractured at the edge of the mounting tab.  Removing the
oil filter showed that the other mounting tab was almost fractured through.

I thought of purchasing a new bracket and upper shield but rationalized that
the new parts would be no better.  I decided to repair and strenghten this
shield.

Removing it is only difficult, replacing it is terrible.  The shield is
actually three pieces: a lower shield, a upper shield with a mounting tab and
a bracket that bolts to the block and holds the two sheilds together.  The
shields are held to the bracket by three screws, two accessable from
underneath.  The bracket falls after removing the screws.  The lower shield
cannot be removed from the area where it is mounted.  The upper shield can
just squeeze past the oil filter housing.  (Cover the housing with plastic
after removing the filter to keep dirt out.)

It took one hour each night for three nights to replace the parts.  Good luck.
