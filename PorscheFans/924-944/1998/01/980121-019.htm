Mike,

I just replaced mine about a month ago.  It was a
pretty easy job.  It took me about 45 minutes (always
careful the first time).  I'm speaking from a 944 NA
perspective.  It may be the same or a little more
difficult for the 951.  Anyway.... e-mail me directly and
I'll give you details.  I did lower the fan housing w/o
disconnecting wires and the switch was easy to get
too.  However, you'll need a 29mm socket or wrench
or you can use a 11/4" or 11/8" (can't remember what
I used) standard.  The switch isn't in tight and you
want to be careful not to overtighten the new one!
