The Weltmeister shim for the wastegate is 3mm thick.  There are 3 (not
4) allen cap screws holding the diaphram to the valve.  Washers can be
used instead of the shim if you check the thickness and be sure that
they are exactLY the same (so no binding is put on the valve).  be sure
and do not let the inner diaphram spin when removing the valve from the
housing.
