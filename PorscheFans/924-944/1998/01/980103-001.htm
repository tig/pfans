In reference to getting a check valve from AutoAtlanta:  although they do
generally have excellent prices on 924 parts, be aware that their policies
of no returns after thirty days, refunds only after three months, 15%
restocking fees, and nonrefundable round trip shipping and handling fees are
strictly enforced.  I, too, used to get excellent service from AutoAtlanta,
but this year alone I have over $100 in parts which I am unable to return
for a merchandise credit.  According to the nice lady in customer service,
the sales rep. lose commissions when a parts are returned.

Can anyone suggest a parts supplier for 924's with more congenial customer
service?
