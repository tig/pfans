The 7-9 quoted hours to replace a water pump in the 951 can definitely
be a varied factor, as it is in all 924S/944/951/968 cars.  Normally,
when we replace water pumps, the labor is not that much greater to
replace all of the associated seals and o-rings.  The labor guides
usually list about 8 hours for the pump alone.  Replacing all of the
seals can add 1-2 hours, and if you replace the balance shaft housing
o-rings...that can add another 1-2 hours. It is a smart thing to do the
job properly the first time. If there is any question of leaks on the
oil seals or o-rings, I would say go ahead and replace them.

We usually use rebuilt water pumps and have had a very low failure rate
on them.
