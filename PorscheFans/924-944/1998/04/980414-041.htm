> At Saturday's DE I noticed that in each successive session my brakes seemed
> to feel more and more like my rotors were warping but when I got in the car
> and drove home, they seemed perfectly smooth. I still have plenty of pad and
> plenty of rotor. Could this just have been the ABS circuit causing the
> pulsing sensation?
> 
> How frequently should brake fluid be flushed and replaced when doing events.
> I am using a heavier duty brake fluid.
> 
I flush before each DE and once a year in the cars I don't track. I have
never experienced the pulsing (I don't have ABS) but one thing I would
check is rotor wear. The wear limits are not large (2mm total on a non-S
front rotor) and I suspect that most of the cars I see at the track need
new rotors for that reason alone. If you can feel a dramatic lip at the
outside edge of the rotor where the pad doesn't sweep you are probably
feeling 1mm of wear per side.
>
> How frequently should oil be changed when using the car for events. I am not
> using synthetic oil, but should I?
>
I use synthetic and change at 4K miles or 2 DE events, whichever comes first.
