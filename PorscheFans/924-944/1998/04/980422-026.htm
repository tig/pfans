To All 924 Owners:

Russ Bullock stated that the circle indentation was on the front of the
cam sprocket. It should be to the REAR. This tells me that the sprocket
may infact be installed backward. It does make alignment of the timing
marks more difficult.

If you remove the gear from the camshaft, please ensure that you
re-install it correctly. The gear must be removed to replace the front
cam seal or replace the cam.
