As far as I know (usual disclaimer), all 944NA and 924S models had
available an optional 3-speed automatic transmission to accompany the
standard 5-speed manual.
There was never an available automatic for the 951s (944 Turbo) or the 944S2.
The 968 model had available the optional 4-speed Tiptronic to go along with
the standard 6-speed manual.
