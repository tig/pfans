The 968 and the 88 & 89 951s also came with the 33 bar rear brake regulator.
On my 968 I changed to the 33 bar regulator with the stock calipers and it
did wonders for the braking balance.  Since then I have installed the M030
calipers and now feel the bias is a little too far forward again.  I am
going to try the 45 bar regulator.  Just waiting for it to arrive.

I am less familiar with the 86 951.  I think it has the same size calipers
and rotors as the stock 968.  If that is the case I would strongly suggest
changing to the 33 bar regulator.
