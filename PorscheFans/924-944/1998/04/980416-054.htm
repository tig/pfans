The console switches below the radio (85.5 and later) are non-illuminated,
except the red LED on the central locking switch.  The switch markings are
just painted on, as apposed to the transparent marked dash switches that
light up, such as the climate control, defogger, recirculation, etc. The 
window switches have painted on (actually silk screened) markings, so 
these can not be illuminated.
