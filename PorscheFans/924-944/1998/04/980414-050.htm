Keep in mind the relative simplicity of the system before pushing the =
panic button. There is a relay mounted under the rubber cover on the =
headlight motor itself, I suspect this could hang up causing your =
intermittent problem. Its about twenty bucks for the 924 setup, which is =
quite similar. In terms of preventive maintenance, be sure the axle pivot =
points are clean and lubed with a spot of lithium grease, be sure the =
headlight buckets themselves are able to move freely (the motor isn't =
THAT strong) and be sure the rubber covers for the motor and gearbox and =
relay are intact.
