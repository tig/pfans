Hi I have an 85 944 that had exactly the same problem, it would idle
slow-fast at about a one second cycle. The previous owner sold it real
cheap because he couldn't get it fixed . The car would sometimes idle
normally and other times idle about 400 rpm especially when the fans came
on. After several weeks of grief and a trip to the local agent (who also
couldn't help) I discovered that the rear lifting bracket on the motor was
loose causing voltage problems all over the motor. I found it by placing an
ammeter between the engine block and the battery negative terminal (mine
was showing 7 amps) with the engine running and the lights on. Any reading
indicates a poor earth.

PS my oil pressure is 5bar at 3000+rpm and about 2bar at hot idle.
