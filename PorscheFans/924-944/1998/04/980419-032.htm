It`s easy Bill.  Put dabs of contact adhesive around the perimeter of
the pan. Put the gasket in place on the pan so that it pickups up the
wet dabs of contact cement in the right places, pull the gasket off in
order to let the cement dry to a tack. Now when you place the gasket
carefully back on the pan, it will stick like poop to a blanket. It`s  a
good idea to use a little oil resistant silicone on the transition
surfaces of the block; bearing caps, joints, etc.      Dave C.
