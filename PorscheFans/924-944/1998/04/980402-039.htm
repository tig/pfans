A small note of caution from the most hated man on the internet:

For some boost guages (like the VDO 935 stle) the KLR line may be too
small, when used with a "T" for use with the boost guage.  For a "T" to
fit into the KLR line it has to be pretty small, an the larger boost guages 
have a fairly large volume when comared to the small sensor in the KLR.

I know that when I used that line in my 944turbo the guage was slow to
repond.  That slowness went away when I replaced the line with a larger
hose connected to the intake manifold directly, off one of the two
"octopus"hoses. You could also substituting a different banjo bolt
between cylinders 3-4 or tap the manifold and add a fitting.  I beleive
the 935 guage uses 1/4" NPT or something similar.

Just a thought from an overeducated theorist....
