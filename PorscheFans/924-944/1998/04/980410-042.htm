Subject:Found great way to remove hood badge
Date:Fri, 10 Apr 1998 11:17:18 -0700
A few people advised me to use a screwdriver or something similar to remove
the hood badge. This seems like a dangerous maneuver. Someone said to use
string...hm...pretty good, but the string slipped.
I used a small nylon tie wrap.  One on the top and one on the bottom
(lower).  Tie them together so each one is a separate loop and then simply
pull.  The sucker came right off!  I slipped each side under the badge so as
to push in the rubber gasket.  I bought a new one anyway.

Thanks for the advise.
