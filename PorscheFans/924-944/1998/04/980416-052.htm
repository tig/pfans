The recirculation button moves a flap inside the air box to seal off the 
cars interior from outside air, and recirculate the inside air.  The A/C 
will also close this flap when first turned on, until the climate control 
starts to regulate the interior temperature you preset.

The dash switch operates a solenoid, which controls vacuum to a servo
connected to the flap.  If you hear a click under the dash when you push
the switch, you have a problem with vacuum, the servo, or the linkage
connecting to the flap.  A hissing under the dash signifies a vacuum leak. 

No clicky.. problem with switch, wiring, or the solenoid.
