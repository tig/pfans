Heavy clutch is normal for all Porsches. In comparasion to other popular cars it's very heavy. But, assuming this in not normal even in PORSCHE I would like to recomend clutch cable inspection (replacement if necessary) and proper lubricating. The clutch release bearing and release bearing tube should be inspected as well. You can reach them removing inspection plate from clutch cover. Proper lubricating of release bearing tube can help, but be worn, clutch plate doesn't like to be lubricated !!! Think twice before decide of clutch plate or cover replacement - I have done it year ago - a lot of work (exhaust system and transmission nedd to be removed! ).
In addition I give You clutch part numbers for 924.
SACHS OEM
Clutch plate:1861 602 303
Clut cover (preasure plate):3082 025 531 (same as AUDI, VW 2.0 petrol)
Release bearing:3151 193 041 (same as AUDI, VW 2.0 petrol)
Clutch replacement set (all of above in one package): 3000 510 001
