	Just thought everyone might want to know about a New 996 Book available/thru
any Porsche dealer. The Book is called: "Porsche 911, The Evolution" by

model in detail! The book is a gorgeous lush - color bound book on Porsche
latest version of the venerable 911 series. It covers all of the various
designs that Weissach made in finally coming to the 996 production design.

testing, anto the production line.  From 356 to 911 early & late, 964, 993 and
onto the New 996! The full evolution of Porsche. It also has a neat chapter on
the GT-1 EVO and street model! Even rare hot and cold weather testing photos
of the R&D team. This book makes a nice addition to my Porsche book
collection. :-))
