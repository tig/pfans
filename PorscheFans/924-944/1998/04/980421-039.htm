The 968 squealed like a wounded pig until I got the OEM vibration dampers.
seems like most folks take these things out and never re-install them.  As
they have dual purpose; one, to dampen the pad vibration, and two, to make
certain the pads are pulled back from the rotors, it is critical the dampers
be replaced with every pad change.  With the dampers in place I rarely get
squeal, even from Pagid oranges.
