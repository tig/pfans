First of all 1 bar is a close approximation of 1 atmosphere.

1 bar = 1000 hPa  (hectoPascals)
1 atm (atmosphere) = 1013.25 hPa
14.696 psi (Standard Atmoshperic Pressure) = 1013.25 hPa

The 951 boost gauge does read absolute pressure so, 1 bar of 
boost is slightly less that 1 atm. 

Therefore, 1.7 bar (which someone asked about) indicated on 
the 951 boost gauge would actually  be 9.96 psi of boost 
(above standard atmospheric pressure).

Also, 2 bar would be 14.3 psi of boost above standard 
atmospheric pressure.
