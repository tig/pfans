Clark,
I've removed the cycling valve and reinstalled on two different 951s.
I've also removed the intake manifold for a different reason.  It is
much easier and quicker to remove the cycling(boost control) valve
leaving the intake manifold in place.  I'm not saying it is easy, the
clamps and the back allen bolt are difficult but can be done.  I left
the rear allen bolt out when reinstalling.
