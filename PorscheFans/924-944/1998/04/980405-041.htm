Steve,

There is nothing very complicated in removing the 944S intake manifold. 
 You basically removing fasteners until the job is done.  OK, because 
you insist, here are a few things to look out for.

When removing the white plastic pulley for the throttle cable, observer 
which washers go where on it.  Ditto on the bracket that holds the 
tension adjuster.

Don't drop any washers into the intake ports.  Observe whether there 
are washers under the feet of the injector rail (there aren't on your 
car).

Also... um, well, that's all I could think of.  With regard to 
installing the new gasket, it goes on dry, no sealant necessary or 
recommended.
