As a "rule-of-thumb" the oil pressure should be around 10psi per 1000rpm. When the Oil temp gets 
real hot the pressure will drop, also when the pressure drops the oil gets even hotter. 
An extra Oil Cooler or a larger one will help keeping the temp down. Also shimming the 
pressure rel. valve will increase the oil pressure some. I do not know the exact dimensions 
of the shims to use, but I can find out if desired.
