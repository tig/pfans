In addition to John Cheers message about ingintion rotor and cap I would like to recomend checking suppression cables. Problems with idling can occur also in case of air intake leak or fuel distributor malfunction. I will check the spark plugs for wetness after tries of start the engine. Dry plugs indicates fuel supply problems. In case of  ignition problems the plugs should be wet.
