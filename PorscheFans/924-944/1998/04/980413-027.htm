> My 1987 - 944S has developed an annoying problem with the cooling fan.
> After a drive, it will typically start or stay on for a few minutes
> after I shut down the car, which I believe it should be doing to help
> cool things.  However, in the last week when I shut off the engine off,
> the fan will continue to cycle on and off erratically, on for a split
> second to maybe a minute, and then off. There is no regular pattern
> either. (I noticed this morning before starting the car the fan still
> going on & off, after 12 hours sitting in the garage without use).
>
> I've pulled the fan relay, contacts are OK so I don't think this is the
> intermittent problem.

This very much sounds like a defective relay. To be sure: pull the
connectors off the temp. sensor at the radiator. If the problem persists:
replace the relay. If the problem is gone: replace the temp. sensor.BTW:
short cut the two connectors from the temp. sensor and the fan wil stay on.
