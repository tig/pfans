Anthony Novom asked about A/C losing freon charge

Sounds like the problem I have had a couple of times with my 924S. The
solution for me was very simple - use your A/C for at least 5 minutes
every month. Apparently that's all it takes to keep the seals lubricated
enough to keep the freon contained. Freon = A/C.

I was also told that converting the system to the non-freon type of  A/C
is not possible. The basic message was "Use it or lose it.".
