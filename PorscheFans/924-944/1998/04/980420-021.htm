> Is the turbo waterpump supposed to run for 30 seconds or so, or is it
> temperature regulated?  How should it act?

The turbo water pump should run for ~25 seconds after shut off
independent of coolant temp. It is possible that the pump is binding and
that loosening the hoses and adjusting things will free it up. Check the
FAQ for more on that subject.
