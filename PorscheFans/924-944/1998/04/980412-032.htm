David, the clutch and brake pedal pads can easily be replaced 
yourself. You can order pads from Automotion or Automobile Atlanta 
for $4 each. The old ones just pull off and then you slip the new 
ones on. When I pulled the old pads off I almost just left them bare, 
they look kinda cool and racy. I didn't try driving without pads but 
sitting in the car it felt like there was just as much grip as with 
pads. Since I had already bought new ones I went ahead and put them 
on.
