You may want to consider going to steel lug nuts. They will not come
loose at racing speeds as the alloys have been known to do (because of
the different heat properties of alloy vs. steel???). Anyway, PCA club
racing requires steel jug nuts so you may want to consider this option.

Whichever way you go, NEVER use locking nuts at the track. I wouldn't
even use them on a DE car. There are only a handful of cases where the
wheels were stolen from a Porsche while it was racing. ;-)
