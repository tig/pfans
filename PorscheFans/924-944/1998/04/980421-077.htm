If you decide to tint, it does come in different percentages of dark. I
personally think the lighter one looks better, and provides better
visibility. It does reduce the view out the back, but also reduces glare
and heat. The reason I tinted my car was half looks and half reduction of
heat and UV damage. Also, your AC doesn't have to work as hard. To me, the
darker limo tints on the side windows make you look like a criminal, 
and make it hard to get eye contact with that cutie that just pulled up
next to you.
