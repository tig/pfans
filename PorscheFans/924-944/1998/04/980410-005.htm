Matthew:

I believe your euro 78 has the Audi 4 speed, no? In which case you
should be able to put one of the later Audi 5 speeds in. But NOT the
Porsche 'Snail Shell' 5 speed. Different flange on the end of the torque
tube. Also, the turbos, at some point or all, used larger diameter
driveshaft and so the couplings are wrong and the clutch disc is
different. If you are in UK then, as I understand it, most of the 5
speeds available to you will be euro spec 'snail shell'?

In any case: Audi 4 to Audi 5, or Porsche 4 to Porsche 5, OK. But each
must be non-turbo because of the change in shaft diameter.
