Well I  found out where the KLR line passes through the firewall. There is a
small hole in the rubber that is part of the wire loom that comes through the
wall. Its a bear to feed the new line through but It is easy if you take some
wire, bend the end in a small loop and stick it into the line. The line
stretches enough to get the wire in. I sprayed the line with some silicone and
gently pulled it through the firewall.Presto!
