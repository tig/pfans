Richard,
      You've solved your own problem. The sensors you refer to are the
speed and reference
sensors. They send a signal to the DME to control ignition timing and
injector firing. Actually,
there is a third sensor which is different than those two sensors. It is
mounted in the same
block and is the Top Dead Center sender. The sensor which is bent probably
needs to be
replaced. The two sensors together are the same. The part number is 944 606
115 00.
Don't throw the old sensor away. You might be able to use it to make a tool
for setting the
sensor clearance. I'll have to give that to you later.
