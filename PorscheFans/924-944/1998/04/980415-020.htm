Jim
I added a rear wiper to my 86 944 and I recommend that you use the factory
switch. It makes the job easier. The wiring harness already includes all the
necessary wiring to hook up the wiper and its all color coded too! The 3
wires are as follows - ground, full time 12v, and 12 v power form switch.
The full time 12v is so that the wiper can return to vertical position after
the switch has been turned off.
