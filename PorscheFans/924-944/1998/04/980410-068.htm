Rox,

Yes, glass scratches can be successfully polished but you might want to check
with a good glass shop first to have them look at your windshield.  First, if
the scratch is too deep, it can affect the tempering of the glass . . . . .
tempered glass is under incredible stress and a deep scratch can put it in
danger of cracking.  Second, to get a good polish job you need to start with
coarse grit polishing compound (glass polish, not the kind you use on paint)
which is most likely aluminum oxide and graduate toward finer compounds.  It
takes a long time to really get a good polish on glass . . . . . maybe the
glass shop can do it faster and more economically than you can.
