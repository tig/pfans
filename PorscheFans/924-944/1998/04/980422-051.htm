Some of the general aviation aircraft we work on must have their exhaust
systems checked for leaks every 50 hours or so. This can be accomplished in a
couple of ways, but the easiest for a car is to get a good, powerful Shop-Vac
and put the hose over the exhaust side (so it blows rather than sucks) and
stick the other end in the exhaust pipe. Wrap it in duct tape so it
pressurizes and doesn't leak and get under the car with a spray bottle of
soapy water mix, spray it on and look for bubbles! If it bubbles, it leaks!
