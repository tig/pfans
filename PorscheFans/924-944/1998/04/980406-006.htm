The clutch is cable actuated. The thing to check is the free play and
the pedal stop. Maybe a po messed up the setting? Dont have the book
handy, check the Haynes. 23cm for free play? No time now, email me if
you dont have the Haynes. The adjustment in the cable is at the bracket
in the engine compartment beside and below the brake booster. If it is
hard about the only thing is to check the cable for binding, perhaps
some dry lube for it and same on the pedal friction points with white
grease. Otherwise, and with a car that old, you may need to pull the
clutch apart, clean, lube, and replace as neccessary. Just finished
mine. Works good
