Raising compression is not quite that simple.  Sure you can put in new
pistons and raise it but in order to do this and to still be able to burn
pump fuel other factors need to be considered, i.e., motronic programming
(emissions regs), combustion chamber design, cam profiles, etc.  Perhaps
someone out there knows if the latter two are at all different on the
European models.  Unless they really sell higher octane fuel over there,
there must be something different besides the pistons to allow the higher
ratio.
