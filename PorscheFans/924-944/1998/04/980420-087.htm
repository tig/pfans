Henrik, as for the boost gauge reading 1.0 whenever the car is on, you
will probably find a leak between the clear hose leading from the
intake manifold between cylinders 3 and 4 and the KLR box in the
passenger footwell.  If this hose is somehow cut or is leaking, the
KLR will be reading atmospheric pressure, which will result in no real
power for you.
