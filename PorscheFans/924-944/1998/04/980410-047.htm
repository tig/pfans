Ross, check that your fuel pressure regulator is working properly.  If
the regulator is not specifying the correct amount of fuel, you can
strain your pump and make it sing.  But don't let it sing for too long,
because it could actually hiccup and croak at any time after you start
hearing that whining sound.  So get that thing fixed as soon as you can.
It's a silly little thing that could leave you stranded.
