Jim,

It happened to me and it took me 5 seconds to verify 
that it was the switch by taking out the G10 relay and
measuring resistance between pin M1 to V1 and M2
to V2 (I'm not sure if these are the right pins, I don't
have the schematics in front of me but if you don't
have the manuals, let me know and I will look them up
tonight, if you can wait that long or somebody on the
list may be able to get the info to you sooner).  You
should read an open on both.  Anything other than an
open, your switch is bad.  On mine, one side read 3.2
ohms.  Its most likely your thermofan switch and it is
rather easy to replace.  I can give you more info on
replacement if you need it.
