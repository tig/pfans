Factory wear spec on page 30-16 for the 944 clutch disc:

Drive plate with riveted liner:
Relaxed thickness: 8.1 + or - 0.3mm
Relaxed wear limit:  6.3mm for symmetrical wear

The relates to about .205" new and .160" wear limit.

We rarely see 944 discs that are worn out...they usually split the
rubber hub.  Be sure and inspect the rubber hub for ANY cracking.  Also,
inspect the drive splines and the triangular shaped stop plate on the
back of the disc.  Any problems with any of these can be cause for
concern.
