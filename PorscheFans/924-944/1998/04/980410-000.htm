Jad,

Leather (and vinyl) can be successfully repaired to the point that the tear or
hole is undetectable . . . . . but only by a professional.  I've seen holes
the size of a quarter repaired so well that there is no indication of damage.
The kits have the ingredients but not the knowledge to really do a good job.
Kinda like handing you a scalpel and assuming you can do an appendectomy!
Anyway, I have enough experience with leather repairs to know that you need a
professional.  Check out the phone book and call around for leather upholstery
shops.
