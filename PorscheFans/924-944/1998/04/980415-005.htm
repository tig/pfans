Hi Jim, according to the diagram the rear wiper is fed from fuse 28 (25
amp) this connects to the blue wire on the wiper unit (parking circuit
feed) and also to one contact on the wiper switch. 
The black/green wire connects to the common terminal on the switch. 
The switch should be the change over type with the "off" contact connected
to earth to prevent arcing when the switch is opened.
The original circuitry doesn't use a relay so I wouldn't bother either,
also a 20 amp switch should be sufficient.
And yes brown is the ground.
