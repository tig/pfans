I had this problem too, on an '86 951 when it was nearly new; the fan
thermostat switch failed "on".  I'm having a similar problem of fan run-on
now so I'm replacing the switch yet again, for about $10-15.

The switch is located on the radiator, driver's side near the top.  Access
is best from the bottom of the car after pulling the pan(s).  It's hard to
reach with a tool - I used an open-end adjustable plier to loosen it and
then unscrewed it by hand.  The process dumps a bit of coolant so you'll
need to refill & carefully bleed the system afterwards.
