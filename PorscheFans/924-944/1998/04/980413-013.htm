Sounds like a thermostatic switch on the lower passenger side of the
radiator going nuts. Check it and replace it if needed before it fails in
the ON position and drains your battery. It has been reported you can use
the equivalant parts (3-pin temp switch)  from late (85-current) VW Golf
or Jetta, it is the same part.
