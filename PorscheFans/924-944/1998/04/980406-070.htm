I had a Europroducts chip in my 85.5 and it provided a modest but measurable
enhancement.  One difference between that chip and the APE chips is that it
was not EPA approved (it was marked "off-road use only").  I suspect there's a
little more power to be had, and less development expense,  if the chip burner
isn't getting the chip certified.  That may also be the case with the
Europroducts chip you bought.
