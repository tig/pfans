Hello fellow Pfans,
I recently signed on as a new member and have read many of the archives
for 924-944 and Racing. I recently purchased a `86 951 back in November
for 6500.00 repainted from Guards red to Alpine White with 16" Fuchs
rims. 
Let me describe what has happened since I purchased the @##$$% car.
The first day it rained the car flooded from every point imaginable,
sunroof real hatch seal and passenger compartment and rear lenses.
Secondly, every seal on the engine had to be done front upper, head
gasket lower oil sup gasket, new rack and pinion, manifold gasket.. you
name it.
Thirdly the clutch plate had to be replaced and now the exhaust is going
to be replaced soon as well. Brakes and suspension are on the way too.
Total repairs @$7500.00currently which is more than what I payed for it.
At the time I thought it wasn't a bad deal @ 6500 bucks, and I didnt the
luxury of having it checked out top to bottom, now in retrospect I guess
I should have. Anyhow I have owned 2 924's and a couple NA 944's but
never had the tantamount problems like the 951...... its a good thing I
love Porsches but they really put me through my paces @ times.
Anybody can share any  951 horror stories? perhaps I can be on the
lookout for future problems as well !!!
