Chris,

I have seen very few transmissions come through like the 924S you 
described, machined to take the transmission speed sender but with a 
plug in place.  The vast majority were not machined and cannot be 
easily upgraded.

Yes, if yours has a plug in the sender bore, it can to my understanding 
have the sender installed.  You need the plastic gear that presses onto 
the back of the differential, the plastic worm gear, and the sender 
itself.  it will take you a little longer than an hour to do the work, 
even if you do not count time removing and reinstalling the 
transmission.  You need to remove the gear carrier from the bell 
housing, then pull off the diff cover and remove the diff.
