Matt:

My .02. The stock boost gauge deals with MAP (manifold absolute
pressure). Thus 1.0 bar equals normal atmospheric pressure, 0 bar equals
absolute vacuum, and 2.0 bar equals 1 atmosphere or ~14.6 psi. The
comparison between Metric and SAE would be:

	-14.6	0	+14.6

	0	1	2

They are the same thing, just expressed differently.
