After talking to OG Racing this morning, I learned that the Camberballs only
work with the old style (pre 1985.5) strut plates, so late model cars need to
have the older plates retrofitted.  Since these are easy to find and cheap,
I'll go ahead and do it.  OG told me they usually tell folks about this when
they buy the parts, and they may well have told me, but it just didn't
penetrate my thick skull.

At any rate, OG promptly returned my call, were very friendly, and have
returned to my list of those in good graces.
