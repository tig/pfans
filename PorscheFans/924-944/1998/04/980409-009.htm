I found out something interesting regarding transaxles (only interesting to
a small group) A 924S transaxle can be converted to an 85 1/2 application
with just a small amount of effort. You need to add a plastic gear to the
driver side of the differential. You do need to remove the gear carrier and
the differential but since you don't have to mess with any of the shim set
ups you do not need to do all the gear lash set up. In fact I did the
operation in about 1 hour. (I do hate the smell of gear lube)
It would be interesting to know if this can be done to a pre 85 1/2
transaxle. Does anyone know if the threaded port for the speed sensor exist
in these transaxles?

If anyone wants more info let me know. (How many people could be
contemplating putting a 924S trans into a 85 1/2 or later 944na?)
