About Automotions new snap ring pliers:

I bought one of them and used it. 

Pro: I could measure off the separation between 
     the prongs because of the screw. So when I 
     found one of the openings in the snap ring the 
     second prong slipped in nicely into the other.
     I think the separation was ~0.9in.

Con: The prongs are detachable and work loose after
     some use, at least that is what happened in my
     case. Not only did it work loose but it found 
     the only crack in my garage floor and vanished :( 

     On the bright side, it didn't go somewhere where
     it might have done more damage.
