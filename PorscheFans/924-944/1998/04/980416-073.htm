Just use a generic class II bumper hitch. Mine is bolted to the bottom of
the bumper in multiple places across the length of the bumper, and then to
plates mounted on the inner and outer surfaces of the spare tire compartment.

Even though it is a class II hitch, I wouldn't use it for anything more
than a small utility trailer for tires and such. The bumper is aluminum and
quite weak.

Porsche does make a hitch for the 924/944 but it uses a metric ball and is
not available in North America. I've seen pictures and it appears to mount
to the bottom of the bumper and/or the bumper mounts (no bumper rams
outside of North America).
