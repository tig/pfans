change a door handle on a late model 944 you have to:

-- remove door panels
-- remove plastic water barrier
-- remove power door lock actuator mechanism
-- remove window
-- remove window guide rail

the
door latch linkage (or even see it).  The first three I did myself before
dropping off the car, but I didn't feel confident tackling the window.
