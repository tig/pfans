John:

I installed a Borla XR-1 race muffler (3") onto the stock exhaust
system (gutted cat).  I'm not sure about any increase in power as it
was done along with some other mods.  In terms of sound, it is much
louder than stock and sounds similar to an eight cylinder car at full
song; not tinny but VERY throaty.  I ended up putting a muffled tip on
to quiet things down a tad; it actually made the sound even deeper
with very little decrease in decibel level.  Please note that the
decibel level is not for everybody, just my personal preference... 
Price:  $200 plus installation.
