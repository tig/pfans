Throttle Position Switch (TPS).  It's about $50 and takes 5 min to install.
It's about 1/2 the size of a deck of cards and bolts on the other side of the
throttle body from the throttle linkage.  What happened when mine did this is
the little button in the TPS that tells the engine to reset everything to idle
conditions when you let off of the throttle all the way malfunctions.
