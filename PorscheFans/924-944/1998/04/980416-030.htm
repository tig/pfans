The turbo S cars, and the 88+ M030 cars have 25.5 mm torsion bars,
compared to 23.5 on the standard turbo, and 24 mm on the S2.

However, my car understeered a lot with the 16mm bar. Upgrading
to an 18mm bar was cheap and easy, and made the car handle
more neutral.
