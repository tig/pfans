	Ahhh, another mysterious rattle, well this one, (or atleast for
me) was simple.  The connecting steel cable that runs from the interior
door handle to the latch may be vibrating on the interior of the door
framework, and when you move the window, you move the cable out of the
way.
I wraped my cable in some bubble wrap and tape, so if it did decide to
vibrate against the door framework, I would not hear it.

	I hope this fixes your problem.
