> The climate control temperature servo works perfectly. But the air-vent

> split between the defrost vents and main (and side) vents. No air at the

It seems the servo for the footwell vents is stuck close.It's situated at
the right foot of the drivers side. It's hard to reach, but not impossible.
Remove the plastic cover that hides the servo's. Now you can remove the
servo. Before you disconnect the flap-lever: mark it's position on the servo
with some tipp-ex. While the servo is out: do not turn its axle.
Most probably the connector has one or more loose connections on the
circuitboard. Just resolder them, it worked on my car.
