Actually it is a precise method of adjustment for wheel bearings.  To be
more precise, you should not be able to move the washer with your finger
but should be able to move it with a flat blade screwdriver.  BTW, after
repacking the bearings, crank down hard on the spindle nut to seat the
bearings then back it off and adjust as above.
