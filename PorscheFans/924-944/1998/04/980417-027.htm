For me, the question is not whether they look good or not. It's the
protection they provide to all that paintwork exposed close to the road in
front. It can be really frustrating trying to get bugs, road tar and other
crap to come off. Using a bra can dramatically cut down the nastiness of
this cleaning chore. 

Also, for driver's ed track events, the bra can prevent the nose from
getting "sandblasted" by grit kicked up from the cars ahead of you. Make
sure, however, if you use it for this, that a clear path to the raditor and
front brakes is maintained.

Aside from the practical aspects, I think they look good, too.
