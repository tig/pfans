the little bolts that hold on the valve?  The R&R on the manifold only
takes about 30 minutes anyway (I have done it about five times now for
various jobs).  If you are replacing the valve, it's a great time to also
replace those rubber hoses under the manifold.  The dealership will not (at
least in my case) sell the rubber hoses individually, but you can replace
them with fuel injection hose.  The dealership will sell you the
valve/wastegate hose though.

customer instead of quoting him 4 hours of labor to replace the cycling
valve. (JMO).
