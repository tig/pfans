Alex,

Your almost there.  I'm assuming you've already removed the four 19 millimeter
bolts attaching the bellhousing to the engine.  The haynes manual is telling
you the correct thing to do.

Screw in an M8 bolt partway into the back of the release lever shaft.  This is
visible from underneath the clutch housing. (lower passenger side).  Make sure
the bolt pinning the shaft in, is removed.  This is a M6 bolt on the back of
the housing, near the M8 bolt you screwed in.  Once you remove this bolt, tug
at the M8 bolt you screwed into the shaft until the shaft comes free from the
clutch housing.  The housing should pull off.

Hope this helps!
