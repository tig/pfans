You might just need new valve stem seals.  The 924's, and Rabbits/Golfs 
from the same era suffer from valve stem seals that harden with age/heat, 
thus no longer doing their job.  '75 era Golfs suffered from premature 
valve guide wear, since they were so short.. not enough area to spread 
out the stress of valve operation, causing the soft brass guides to wear 
prematurely.  Not sure if this syndrome afflicts 924's.
