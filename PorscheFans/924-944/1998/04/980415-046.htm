David:

The "pulsing" you mention is typical of the ABS working properly...
Most DE events (I'm in Region 12) require a pre-tech inspection and
a common check-off is bleeding the brakes within the last 30 days.

Fluid change should occur 6-12 months on average.  I run the Super
Blue which is impossible to "boil" (in my opinion).  You certainly
should consider metal/braided brake lines...as swelling is a common
cause of failure, also.

I use synthetic because of temp. conditions.  I change every 2/2500
miles...but car is only for track.

I'm sure there is a differing of opinion...
