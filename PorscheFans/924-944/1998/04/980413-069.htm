From reading this list, and from experience, I wonder why anyone uses the
K&N filters as a performance enhancer.  The stock setup isn't overly
restrictive.  That filter element is pretty big.  It seems the bare K&N
filter cone just sitting out in the engine compartment just sucks in hot
air, instead of cool, dense air from the fender, which is what we want for
maximum charge. 

I do agree the K&N's are super as far as maintenance goes.. just clean 
and re-oil.  I would be inclined to update to the replacement element and 
retain the stock airbox setup.
