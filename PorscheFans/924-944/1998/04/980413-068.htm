There is a possibility that your drain hose in the pan under the AC
evaporator
has clogged and the water is accumulating there and sloshing. Good way to
check
is to run the AC on a humid day and make sure that there is a steady drip,
drip,
drip under the car.

If this gets bad enough, it will cause your AC to freeze solid under use and
become ineffective until it thaws again (been there, done that..)

OTOH, it could be that there is some air trapped in the heater core and the
coolant system needs to be bled...

Good Luck!
