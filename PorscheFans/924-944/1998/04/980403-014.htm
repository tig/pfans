Suggestions that I have seen on the list before are using GOOP Marine
Adhesive and Sealant or #U-400 urethane auto glass sealant (manufactured
by Essex 001-201-773-6300 from the UK).  Apparently 3M's Black Super
Weatherstrip Adhesive does not work well as it lacks flexibility once
installed. 

BTW, I have mentioned it before on the list and will do so again.  Do not
let the hatch raise and come to a stop by itself.  That stresses the hatch
and eventually leads to glass separation.  Always assist it and catch it
before it comes to a stop.  There have been articles on Pano and Excelence
(maybe both) on this.  Also keep the hinge pins lubricated.
