A useful tip I once heard (never tried, no need yet)

If you think there is air in your lines, try this.

W/car turned off, pump brakes to bleed off booster vacuum.
Next take brake fill cap off, and have someone press brake pedal in.
Have them quickly take their foot off the brake pedal.
Now here is the tricky part. If there is air in the line there may be
a"spurt of fluid that
rises up as the previously compressed air decompresses.

If you try this method beware the "Bubbling Fountain"!!
