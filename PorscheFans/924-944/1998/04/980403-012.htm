DON'T DROP THE CLUTCH.

The clutch disc on these cars is sort of fragile. There are two sets
of springs that provide dampening. The inner springs are large and strong.
The outer springs are small and fragile. If you are tough on the clutch
you will break one or more of the small springs. Then you'll have to
replace the clutch disc, which is NOT fun.

Otherwise, just keep the boost up! The brakes are GREAT, so brake late,
and get on the power soon.
