Chris:

Vanity Fair magazine has a spread on Jerry Seinfeld this month.  Apparently he
has 25 vintage Porsches.  His favorite is a green 911S (about 1973, I think).
He keeps them in an airline hangar and dotes on them quite a bit.

That poster of the red 951 is hanging on my garage wall.  It came unsolicited
in the mail about 1987 or so.  The best part is that you are looking at a
direct front view of the car, and the front plate is a vanity plate with the
recipient's last name (this was done with a sticker which looked like a
license plate).  I framed mine with a big lucite frame after getting it, but
put it away and forgot all about it.  When I got my first Porsche (92 968,
Guards red) in 12/96, I pulled it out of the basement and put it up on the
garage wall.  Destiny, you know...
