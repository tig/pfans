bad at what I would call an early age(mine at 47K miles).  Any 968
owner out there who has not had a starter problem?  My problem
was with the pinion gear/overrun clutch assembly being so gummed
up that it could not move in and out into position on that long shaft
that the 968 starter has.  I believe this is the same starter the newer
911's use, at least by the Automotion catalog.
