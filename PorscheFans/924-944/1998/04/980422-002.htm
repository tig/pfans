Philip,
If it's any good I would say buy it, my Porsche garage owner says, and a lot 
of people of this list
would agree with him, that the 951 is the best value Porsche you can buy. I 
can't believe that a 4 year older
911 will be any cheaper to run and may even cost more. The 951 as Tom Pultz 
said handles and performs better and
has better equipment i.e. ABS, A/C, etc. than an equivalent age 911. The 911 
will also cost considerably more.

Like any car and particularly a high performance car, regular maintenance is 
essential and will cost. I have had a 1987 951 for
almost 3 years and it has cost me 3400 GBP in maintenance bills, which are

I think despite the cost these cars are reliable and you have to remember 
that even a 1989 car is 9 years old and parts are going
to wear out from old age. I still think they are worth it and you will 
probably find the extra maintenance costs over an 'ordinary' car are
compensated by the low or nil deprecation.

I would say that GBP14K is a little bit espensive for a 89 951, that is the 
sort of price I would expect to
pay at  a dealer's but having said that if the mileage is genuine and it has 
a faultless service history then maybe it is worth
paying  a little over the odds.
