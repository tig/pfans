<< The mounts themselves are a pain to unbolt as the

FWIW, the first time I replaced motor mounts I worried the lower nut off this
way.  When I did them this past weekend, I found that it was MUCH easier to
drop the inside A-Arm mounting points.  It's simple to do, alignment can be
preserved as long as you don't loosen and move the eccentric on the rear
mount, provides easy access to the lower motor mount stud, and allows you to
carefully check the ball joint (no need to remove the ball joint stem from the
steering knuckle -- just let it hang free).
