Ryan, 

It is PNA 000147A, the Parts and Technical Reference Catalog for the
924/944/928 series up to 1988, very much worth having, IMHO.  Be sure not
to forget the A, without the A the PN becomes the 911 catalog.

Do not be surprised if the dealer quotes you about $49.50 for that PN. 
When these are ordered, they come in packs of 5, individually they cost
less than $10.  Try to explain to your dealer that it is really to their
advantage to order the pack and sell you only one catalog as you (and your
friends) will go back to the dealer to buy parts. If that fails, try a  
mail order source, ie Carlsen (800)523-2408, Don McGill or whatever you
fancy.
