The KLR computer is telling the cycling valve to send the full signal to
the wastegate because it is detecting a problem, or the cycling valve is
malfunctioning and not bleeding off any of the intake manifold signal.
You don't need to take your car to a garage yet.  Get some contact
cleaner from an electronics store, get the kind that is plastic safe.

Take off the connectors to the following and blast them with contact
cleaner and even clean them with a file if possible:
1. The air flow meter connector
2. The throttle position sensor connector
3. The cycling valve connector

Drive the car after that.  If it still doesn't go past 1.2 bar, send
another email and we'll work from there.
