You can replace the bulb without replacing the clock.  I used part 9995, =
bulb with holder, $3.78, from Tweeks.  Remove the glove box (be careful =
not to mangle the glove box light and switch at the top of the glove box =
when removing and replacing the box) and the cigarette lighter to access =
the back of the clock and the bulb.  Disconnect the wire connector from =
the bulb, grab the back of the bulb holder with needle nose pliers and =
twist the old bulb out (1/4 or 1/2 turn counter clockwise), twist the =
new bulb/holder in.  Even with everything removed, access is tight.  I =
found getting the cigarette lighter out of the way and then back in was =
the hardest part (need to remove and replace the large-diameter snap =
ring from the back of the lighter).
