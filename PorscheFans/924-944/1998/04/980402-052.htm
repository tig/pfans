  I have quite a few mods and am not finished yet.  Since my dyno I have
installeda ProFec Boost controller which I'm still trying to debug, and Nology

an aftermarket coil and MSD ignition I haven't installed yet but plan to as

time.  My other mods are the head work, E-H manifold, rising rate Fuel Pressure

starving for fuel and have since added the larger adjustable regulator.

  The ProFec is being troublesome.  When it was first installed it would boost
no lower
than 1.15 bar max.  I could adjust it up but not down.  I made some changes in
the way I
installed it and can now adjust it for pretty much any boost I want, but as
soon as it hits
max boost the boost drops back almost immediately to .5 bar or so.  I'm going
to reinstall
it the way I had it the first time and try to get tighter connections with
shorter lines between
the boostbolt and GReddy stepper motor through the boost control valve to
wastegate and
hopefully I will get more pressure to wastegate and it should open at a lower
boost.  If you
followed that you understand how the boost controls work pretty well, if you
didn't it doesn't
really matter.  I may end up having to go with an aftermarket wastegate but I'm
going to try
to get it to work with the stock one first.
