Hi all P-fan,
Recently I posted to the list regarding the re-start problem.
I've got it solved, this is things that I have done to the starter.
Remove the starter and service it, replace the solenoid,
the coil wire solder to the cable lug badly damage. Re-solder the wire to
the cable lug.
Install back the starter and solved my re-start or "hot start" problem
BTW removing the starter is really "pain in the neck"
