To all concerned about the switch replacement:

I recently performed this replacement myself.  I purchased only the snowflake
switch from the dealer, removed the climate control panel from the car,
desoldered the old switch, and soldered in the new one.  Not very difficult
and a heck of a lot cheaper than purchasing the whole control panel (which is
what the dealer suggested).

The dealer will sell you the switch separately, but it is not listed as a
separate part for the pre-'88 cars.  Just ask for the post '88 switch!

The solder job does take a lot of patience and some skill, but it can be done.
