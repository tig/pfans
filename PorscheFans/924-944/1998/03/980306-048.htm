The way it works on 86 951  NA is similiar

ther is a t'stat in radiator
it has two ranges
low range and high a wire goes to each plus a ground to t'stat
coolent gets hot and high or low range produces a 'ground'
on the appropriate wire.

wire goes to relay

the relay in fuse box (for a 951) 
has 3 yes 3 relays inside
#1) AC kicks this wich activates #2
#2) activates low speed if t'stat or #1 (AC) calls for it
   can run with key out of car!!! hint-hint
#3) High speed only if ignition on

Your trouble is probably that the t'stat in radiator
is bad or the wire from it to relay is shorted.
test by pulling the connector to tstat
and shorting pinst to gnd. with key on and off.
