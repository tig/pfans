Someone posted a few weeks ago that for the 944 good jacking points are
under the front wheel A-frame where it joins the frame of the car (rear
part of A), and for the rear under the rear shock absorber mount.

I tried the A-frame location and it worked fine. I put a jack stand under
the forward part of the A-frame. Then I removed the jack to use it to apply
pressure to a wrench. The jack stand (actually I put two of them under the
A) held there without a problem. I have not tried jacking the rear yet.

Notice that I'm talking about a 944, so if the 951 is different at the A,
this may not work.
