The bottom...I just did this last weekend.....a pain in the butt.  There
are two bolts in the bottom part and one at the top (fender).  Make sure
you remove the pump assembly from the tank.  Also, remove pan cover, and
loosening the power steering oil cooler and the brackets that hold those
hoses to the frame will gain you sorely needed thousandths of inches
clearance.  :-)
