Jason: before you start throwing money at the head gasket, try a new
expansion tank cap. My '84 had slow coolant loss without any visible
coolant leaks, and I was sure it was either the head gasket or the oil
cooler. A new expansion tank cap fixed the problem fast. The caps are
rated to 15psi, and when the springs/gaskets get old the system cannot
pressurize fully and starts overflowing coolant. Happens only on the
road when the car is hot so you won't see anything on the garage floor.
