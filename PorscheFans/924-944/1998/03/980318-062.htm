Lawrence,
The way it's sometimes done is to insert an air hose into the
fitting for the auxiliary air valve or idle speed stabilizer and
apply about 5 psi.  Soapy water in a spray bottle will work as a
leak detector.   You have to have the throttle open.  The difficutly
of this technique is plugging the air-flow sensor intake and the
exhaust tailpipe.  I don't know if the original post to this
question used this technique.
