I did this last year on my newly acquired 944. I only did the exhaust
side(skinflint ). For what its worth I would recommend doing both,
particularly if you are doing it yourself. The amount of stuff that has to
come off means that it makes sense and you know that both are in good shape.
I used genuine P parts, about £116 if I remember and they require the new
style bolts for the updated version. German and Swedish are a good
alternative and usually cheaper but they dont stock everything. Type 911, is
another good supplier if they have the part.
If you are doing this at home and need advice I'd be happy to help.
