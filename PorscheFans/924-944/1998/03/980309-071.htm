For Jim and all 951 pfans:

Per Jim's request (below) I visited Carlsen in Palo Alto today.  Their Porsche
Tool catalog still shows the KLR diagnostic tool as part number 9246 (order
number 000 721 9246).  My contact at the dealer told me that according to her
Porsche inventory computer, the tools are still available, at a list price of
under $35.  This is somewhat contrary to the information which Jim received
from some other source, as noted below.

The parts department at Carlsen is a very busy place.  If you call, please be
patient, the people there are very friendly and try to help everyone, it is
just sometimes hectic.  They will always try to match or beat a price that you
get from another dealer or aftermarket parts source.  The person I usually buy
from is named Linda, but Mike, Marty or Didi can help as well.  I'm sure that
there are many other dealers that provide the level of pricing and general
service that Carlsen does, but if you don't have such a dealer nearby, try
Carlsen.

- Mike Mitchell
