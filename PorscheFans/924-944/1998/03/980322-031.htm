       I just put everything back together after removing the turbo to
get to the oil trap. I started the engine then smelled gas. The crimp
connections on the fuel line were leaking. I guess because I have had
them disconnected for over a month the hoses dried out. I cut the
crimp at the metal line coming out of the wheel well with a air hack
saw. Great invention (watch out for sparks). Went to a auto store and
purchased some 3/8 in. fuel hose and 3/4 in heater hose and will make
up a new fuel line. The 3/8 hose goes into the 3/4 heater hose looks
just like factory. I will use two clamps at each end. Now on to the
next layer for the onion to see what else is wrong.
