I suspect the 968 feels different than the 951 because of the power curve.
The 951 is slower at low revs but picks up rapidly with the boost, while
the 968 starts off faster but has a flatter torque curve.  Even the new
engine chip won't replace the thrill of turbo boost, but it should reduce
the small overall power gap between the two cars.
