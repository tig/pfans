I have a rather new certificate from Porsche (Nov, 15th 1996) which
states what additional mods have to be done to the car when upgrading
to wider rims/tires. This certificate lists all (in germany) allowed
tire mods for all 944 models. (As Tom already said, 18" is a no-no)

When upgrading to 7.5x17,offset 65mm (225/45) and 9x17,offset 55mm (255/40)
you also have to install (among other things you have to check):
# 951.341.023.01 (rubber bearing)
# 951.341.431.02 (excenter bolt)
The listed parts are to be installed at the front suspension,not the rear.

When upgrading to 8x17,offset 52mm (225/45) and 9.5x17,offset 68mm (255/40)
you have to install the above parts and additionally a 17mm spacer for
the rear rims (#951.331.611.02)
