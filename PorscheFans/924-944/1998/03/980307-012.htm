Hi Everyone,

Just got my 94 968 back from the shop.  My oil level indicator problem
was NOT a faulty oil level sensor as I, and my mechanic had suspected,
but rather a faulty circuit board in the car's warning system.  That
warning light on my old 951 did occasionally do that if parked on a
severe incline, but never stayed on after I stopped the car, waited a
few minutes, and got a normal reading.

Just wanted to pass this on.
