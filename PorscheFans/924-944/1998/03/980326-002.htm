  Your heater flap bearing is probably shot. It is located under the
dash on the drivers side on the upper right hand side. It is a small
white nylon plastic piece that links the servo motor to the flap which
controls the amount of hot air mixing. Right now the relay is turning
the coolant valve on and off, so  you only get full hot or cold air.
Your local dealer should be able to find the part #, let me know if not
and I will dig it up.

Good Luck!
