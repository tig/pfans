Small addition to what DadMajor wrote: first open the fill plug.  You 
want to be sure you can open it before draining the fluid.  Better to 
drive to the shop with old fluid and a jammed filler, than no fluid at 
all!

Don't overtighten the plug on reinstall.  Everyone does, which is why I 
have a 17 mm allen on a 1/2" breaker bar handy.  Tightening torque is 
24 Nm, 17 ftlb, and that's plenty.  Really clean the bore and plug 
threads properly before reinstalling.  I find a lot of cases where 
debris on same has damaged the threads in the case bore or on the plug.
