JLM wrote about getting water behind the passenger seat. I had this problem
for a LONG time in my 83 944 and seem to have solved it by

1: Getting the car undercoated

2: Removing the battery and sanding down the rubberized coating in the battery
   tray with fiberglass epoxy resin and a new layer of rubberized coating.

So far, it's worked like a charm! ...I hated that smell of condensation after
the rain ended and the car sat in the sun...Try looking at your battery tray.

Good luck
