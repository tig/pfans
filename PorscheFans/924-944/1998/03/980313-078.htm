Finally, I've read about the usage of the "993 Compressor Bypass
Valve" for 951s.  What does this do?  Part #?  Price?

** It doesn't do anything if your 944 part is working correctly but many
are not.  If you take it off and can blow through it, it is bad and
boost is bleeding off through it.  The 993 part has a better diaphragm
but looks almost the same and does the same but will probably last
longer.  When you let off the throttle after boosting, the boost
pressure remaining between the turbo pump and the throttle body is bled
off through this valve otherwise it will try to go back through the
turbo which slows the turbine between gear shifts.  I don't know the
part number but it has been posted before.  Call PowerHouse and ask
David, he will know and they are fairly inexpensive.
