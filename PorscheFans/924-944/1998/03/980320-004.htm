There was a recent thread in the 928 list about this subject.   You
don't say how many miles on your engine, but it was suggested that
higher (say 80k miles and over) would be more likely to develop leaks
and not get so much of the advantages of synthetic.   I'll be changing
to synthetic at my next change, but I've re-ringed my engine and all
gaskets are new.   As an alternative, several manufacturers make a blend
of syth and dino - Castrol for one - and this may be a good alternative
to give you the best of both.

Hope this helps....good luck.   Be sure to advise what you decide and
what result you get.
