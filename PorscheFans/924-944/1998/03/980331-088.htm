Are you sure you want 120 ft-LB?  I believe you have an M14 x 1.5 mm 
nut that takes more like 77 Nm, which is around 52 ft-LB or so.

I use a 22 mm flare-nut style crowfoot socket on the M14 nut, and 
counterhold with either a 10 mm wrench or, if the top is buggered, a 
pair of vice grips.  The 10 mm ATF design is not very practical, an 
internal allen as used on other cars is a bit better.
