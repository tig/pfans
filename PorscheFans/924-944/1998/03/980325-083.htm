Peter:

I lowered my 1985.5 944 quite a bit (at least an inch) when I changed to
225/50/15's and shorter/stiffer Weltmeister front springs.  I noticed very
little difference in the ride quality, but a huge difference in the handling.
Many 944's are set too high in the rear from the factory to meet bumper height
requirements.  I don't know about the 968 in this regard.  At any rate--lower
away, within reason.
