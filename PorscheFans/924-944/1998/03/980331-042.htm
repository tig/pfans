All,
The 993 air bypass valve (also called turbo bypass valve) is identical to the
stock 951 valve except that the diaphram is reinforced w/ metal.  This
provides no performance gain over a properly function stock valve.  However, I
would guess that probably 90% or more of the original bypass valves out there
are beginning to malfunction or are badly malfunctioning.  All the oil
floating around in that system does a number on the rubber  diaphram/valve and
it sticks and/or leaks.
