Several of you wrote back regarding my comments on front spoiler repair (the plastic 
part on a 944NA) and the use of plastic epoxy...

My cracks were parallel to the car (front to back) and did not require the use of 
backing material. (I suppose your crack may vary). I was able to just but the edges  
together, and the rest of the spoiler structure held things in place.

I used an Epoxy designed for plastics (I couldn't believe how many are available) 
from Wal-Mart for about 5 bucks. It dries to a goopish white color. (I don't have 
the brand name...

It took paint well, although I didn't try sanding it...
