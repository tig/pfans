The new 18 mm wide belt is p/n 944.102.219.04, and Porsche has 
described it in Tech bulletin book L, group 1, number 9502, 7 June 
1995.  They advise that the old part is to be used until inventory is 
gone.  Since there have been very few problems with the old belt, that 
sounds fine to me, and I wouldn't go out of my way to locate a wide 
part.

The new belt is tensioned a little higher.  The spec is 3.5 +/- 0.5 
scale units.
