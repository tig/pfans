I am currently separated from my car and my car documents, so I cannot
check the details, but I had a very similar experience with my 88 951S.
After a while, I found out that there are more than one type of cigarette
lighter connectors, and not all of them are compatible with the 944
cigarette lighter bay. Some of them immediately cause a short circuit, blow
a fuse, and exactly the parts you mentioned will black out. This is also
backed by the fact that your voltage drops below 12V, which it should only
do during startup, and even then only if your battery is nearing
exhaustion.

I believe that the earlier intermittent operation of your dome light is not
connected to this problem.
