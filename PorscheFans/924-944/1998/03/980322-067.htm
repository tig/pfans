I just ran my first autocross with R1s that size in my 93 968.  The rear
is fine and the front rubs at full lock.  I would not recommend this
setup for street use.  Tire sizes from different manufacturers vary
quite a bit even if they are stamped identically and the BFG's are
already marginal for clearance.  If you are doing this for appearance,
you could get that filled wheel arch look by lowering the spring perches
on the front and dropping the ride height in the rear.  Just be careful
about setting the front too low (In my car, I have about 1" of tread
left on the collar.
