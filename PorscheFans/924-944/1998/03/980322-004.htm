You can only put newer 53mm offset wheels on yor turbo if you use 1" spacers.

Offsets as follows:

911 -89   10.8 in 7 or 8", 23mm 9" (extra 1/2" to inside), 23mm fits well
944 -86   23mm
944 87-   53mm except 9", which may be 53 or 60mm
928 all	  60mm 
964 (c2/4) 968, 993 narrow, 993tt/C4S/C2S front 53mm
993 tt/C2S/C4S Rear 40mm.

Spaceing difference:
911 -> Widebody flares, 1" front, 2" rear with 930 factory flares, stock suspension
911/944 early -> lae wheels 1" all around works well.

CAREFUL ON 944turbos - most 911 fuchs will not clear front calipers!
