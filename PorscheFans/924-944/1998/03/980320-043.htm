In some ways, the answer to your observation lies in the two lines above.
The 91 S2 has the "extended" aluminum control arms, the 84 has the steel
ones (unless you updated the suspension somehow).  The suspension is
slightly different and I believe the FAQ points to this difference as a
reason for the pre 85.5 cars being more susceptible to the 65mph shimmy.
