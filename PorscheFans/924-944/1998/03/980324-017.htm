Subject:Wheel shimmy problem goes away by itself
Date:Tue, 24 Mar 1998 09:31:59 PST

With the recent thread about the wheel shimmy, I thought I'd throw 
in my recent experience.

My car acquired a moderate shimmy at around 60-70mph after I put 
new AVS Intermediate tires on it.  I bought the tires from TireRack 
and had them balanced at NTW.  I re-positioned the wheel locks but 
it had no immediate effect.

After driving the car about 3000 miles on a daily basis and doing
nothing else, the shimmy has gradually disappeared.  All that remains
is a barely perceptible vibration in the steering wheel.
