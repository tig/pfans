Tom, 

I agree with Doug about the oil weights.  Also, about the overfill, the
oil capacity of the 951 was increased from 6.5quarts to 7.0 in MY 88.  The
difference in oil marks on the dipstick also went from 1.0l to 1.5l
starting in MY 88.  This data is according to the Technical Specs manual. 

Seems like Porsche realized it was good to overfill the cars, so they made
the overfill part of the spec.  Within the last month I thought there was
another post on the subject and it seems the overfill is not critical for
88+ cars, though keeping them at least to the max level on the dipstick
is.
