I have two large pine trees right next to my driveway, and when the sap
falls - which it invariably does on a clean car - I have found that warm
soapy water works. The warmth is important, and hot water works better. If
the sap has been there for a few days, it can only be chipped off, so I
check the car daily in the summer...

I try very hard to keep the sap off my timing belts, however.
