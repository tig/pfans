Jim,
    The lifting arms for the sunroof are raised and lowered by a set of
plastic gears (one for each lifting arm). If the sunroof motor is running
in both switch positions and the arms are not moving (or moving
erratically), the gears are stripped. The gears are relatively inexpensive.
Replacing them is a little tedious. Figuring out what caused them to strip
in the first place can be difficult without a shop manual. If you decide to
replace the gears yourself, let me know and I will give you as much
guidance as I can. I have repaired a lot of sunroofs but, quite frankly
there are still some things I don't understand about them. If you decide to
do it yourself, order two sets of gears. Sometimes I have stripped a new
set of gears trying to figure out what caused them to strip in the first
place.

Good Luck,
