On Fri, 13 Mar 1998, Tom Pultz wrote:

> I'll soon be taking my car on the track for DE and also plan to start
> autocrossing.  Therefore, I'm in the market for a helmet.  Any
> recommendations?  Full face?  Open face?  Fiberglass?  Kevlar?

For auto-x I prefer open face, it's lighter, gives best peripheal vision,
cooler (and your mouth exhales directly to the air, not inside your helmet
and easiset to put on eye/sunglasses. You're unlikely to ever collide into
something so hard that you would need the mouth protection.

If you're gonna be on the racetrack alot, some prefer the added safety net
of mouth protection. I still prefer open, as I have no plans for wheel to
wheel racing. Also when you you have to talk to someone, eg. outside of
the car or in-car to an instructor or student it's easier to be heard and
to hear.
