There are various hotels near Bremerton to stay at.  Paul, Mark and I
have decided to stay in the Holiday Inn in Port Orchard (about 15 minutes
away from the track).  The rates are very reasonable and they have ample
parking.  The nice lady (Rita) did warn me that they have a BMX convention
coming to town, so make sure and reserve beforehand as it's likely everything
will be sold out on friday night.

Holiday Inn's number is (360)895-2666.  We paid US$59 per night for two
nights and we can stick in four people in the room.  THe place sounds like
a motel, so I wouldn't expect a four course breakfast or anything :)
