As Chris pointed out, gas welders do have their place in the body shop. I
personally don't like welding with them but, they are invaluable for
leading. It is a dying art which few body shops offer as a service to their
customers. Most people don't even know it exists. I have a friend who
offers it as an option to customers when he does a wide body conversion.
You'll pay a premium to have it done but, if you want the best quality seam
on a wide body conversion, it's the only way to go.
