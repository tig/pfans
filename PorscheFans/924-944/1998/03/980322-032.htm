Don't do this.

Your repair may be good for low pressure systems, but the 944 and 944
Turbo are high pressure systems.  Eventually, you will end up with a
leak that will have catastrophic results...

This is not something to be cheap on... buy a new fuel hose from the dealer
with the right connections...
