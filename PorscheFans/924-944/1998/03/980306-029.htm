.... a roadside speed camera triggered by radar reflections from a
passing vehicle. If over a preset limited a flash photograph is taken of
the offending vehicle and, in theory, the Police then prosecute the
driver for speeding or jumping a red light.

In practise the larger proportion of the GATSO cameras are not loaded
with film (they still flash) as the Police find them expensive and
troublesome to load/unload film and process the results.

Ironically for fast drivers GATSO cameras are made by a Belgian firm run
by a certain Monsieur Gatsonides, a former racing driver.
