I "always" take anyone's (especially B&B's) exhaust HP claims with a grain of
salt.  I can believe on Honda's and Acura's there is a big jump in HP, but a
car as well developed as a Porsche, that feat is difficult.  The big reason to
change the exhaust would be asthetic.  Sound and looks.  That would naturally
be the muffler only.  I always frown when someone pulls the CAT on a car that
is mainly driven on the STREET.  Just being socially irresponsible.  For
crying out loud, it is a lousy 5-12 hp *claim*.  The 951 has plenty of power.
Spend the money on driver's, least of all an autocross.  People who have done
this can make more use of 100 hp than someone with NO CLASSROOM experience
with 300 hp ANYDAY.  I have actually seen people LOSE power when they switched
the exhaust.  I can go on forever on this, but most know what I am getting at.
