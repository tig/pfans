For some reason, many people are obsessed with larger wheels.  As far as I
know, there are 4 reasons to go with large wheels:

1.  looks (I think thats why 99% of the car owners buy them),
2.  to accomodate largest possible brakes (you shouldn't worry about this in 
    stock-class autocrossing),
3.  smooth ride (large tires span larger pot holes), 
4.  wasting money on expensive tires (check out prices for 17" and 18"
tires!).

Smaller wheels give you two benefits for autocrossing:

1.  light weight,
2.  lower gearing.

Therefore, I'd say use your Fuchs wheels for autocrossing and buy whatever
you like for the street.
