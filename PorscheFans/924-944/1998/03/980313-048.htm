Two questions:  (1)  Did the re-start problem occur just once?  (2)
Was the temp. gague in the red?
Before suspecting the ignition switch, temperature switch, or
hot-start problem, if the problem happened once or is intermittant,
the first thing to check for corrosion are the contacts on the
cooling fan relay and the cooling fan fuses.  If the problem remains
after cleaning the contacts, re-post and we can go from there.
