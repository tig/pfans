Michael,

	I had 215's on my front Borbet's, and they weren't even close to
rubbing.  225 should be fine but it seems at least one person had a
fitment problem otherwise Tire Rack wouldn't have said anything.  205's
would probably be a "waste" in the sense that you're supposed to be
upgrading in size, and the difference in width between the fronts and
rears would be very significant.  I'd go with 225's but if you're not
feeling lucky, compromise with the 215's.  By the way, I ran 245's in
the rear, and not the 255's.  Tire Rack only recommends 205/255 because
I think that's what 911 applications typically require.
