Stephen,
Currently the Performance Chip is the only upgrade that we carry for the
968.  Gains were on the lines of 8% to 10% across the board, with a 15
horsepower gain at the peak.  Product literature regarding it is currently
available via mail or fax.  Forward us either and we will be happy to send
it to you.  As for your warranty, you can certainly swap the chips as much
as your heart desires.  However, you should not ever have a need to do so.
Firstly, it can not legally void your warranty, at least not under U.S.
consumer warranty law.  Secondly, unless someone decided to open your
computer for some strange reason, they would never know that it was there.
To all known diagnostic equipment, including the Bosch Hammer, it looks
identical to the factory software.  Idle specifications are the same, as
well as any related codes.
