For the Turbo at least the cool brake kit does not require removal of the
fog/driving lights. The stock factory ducts inboard of the lights must be
replaced with new ducts with right-angle hose connection. This is pretty
quick and simple. However, the backing plates must be replaced, which is
a pain if you weren't already planning to a bearing re-pack.
