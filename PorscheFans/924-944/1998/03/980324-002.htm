As an example I just bought a USED Courbeau (sp) seat with the mounting
rails for $100. Keep in mind that most of the prices you see advertised
re seats do not include the extra $75+ for the seat rails.

One of the best deals to improve the steering wheel is purchase a used
Porsche 4 spoke wheel. It is of smaller diameter and so helps solve the
problem. $135 used. Again, the after market wheels leave out the part
about needing to also purchase horn ring adapters etc, whereas for $135
it fits right on.
