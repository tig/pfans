The HKS in my car is mounted on the driver's side window pillar just above
the dash. Although it does seem to "stare you in the face", it is not
obtrusive.
It is also much, much easier to check your boost in this position than it
would be in a dash mounted unit. I recall you are working with a K-27, Mass
Flow Sensor and APE II chip set-up. My car is likewise set-up and even with
only 15 lbs. of boost it would not be easy for me to glance down at the
center console as I row through the gears.
I beleive you will also find the guage to be extremely accurate and very
reliable.
