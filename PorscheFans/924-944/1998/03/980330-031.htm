> question:  are the calipers on the Turbo S larger, or are they the same?  I

The S2 has the same rear brakes (calipers and rotors) as the Turbo S and
the 928S4. The front brakes are marginally smaller, 11.75" rotors
instead of 11.97" rotors, and smaller calipers. Unlikely you will be
able to tell the difference except after a very long day on the track --
the Turbo S brakes probably cool better.
