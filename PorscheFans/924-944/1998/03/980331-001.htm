The following is a belated reply to your questions.  I have been off the list
for a while due to work monopolizing my life!

1.  The starter noise, as Alex Chiu described, is due to a malfunctioning
solenoid.  This happened to me about 55k.  The mechanic tried to fix it, but
eventually had to replace the starter with a rebuilt unit.

2.  The loud creak from the front end is the brake pads breaking free from the
rotors in front.   This happens to me when I get the pads wet and then park,
such as after washing the car.  I prevent it by driving the car a short
distance after washing, and applying the brakes firmly a few times to get rid
of the water.  It sounds alarming, but I don't think it indicates a
malfunction.
