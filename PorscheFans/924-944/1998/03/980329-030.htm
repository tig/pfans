Marshall,
You probably want to check the obvious first.  I.e., the radiator
fins for obstructions, the coolant level and bleeding procedures,
the oil level, and the battery ground strap for clean connections.
Is the heater valve leaking?  Has anyone plugged in the fan
backwards?  When was the cooling system last bled and flushed?  Are
you using phosphate free antifreeze and not antifreeze "safe for
aluminium engines"? Did you test the thermo switch and the
thermostat?  Assuming these are in order, a lower temperature thermo
switch could be installed and/or water-wetter used.  If these
suggestions are not satisfactory, then a complete cooling system
check is in order.
