Over the past few days there has been alot of discussion concerning the
upgrade to H4 headlights, and guess what, yesterday I blew a headlight. So

County, CA. looking for this upgrade kit. Well, we were finally succesful. A
Porsche only parts distributor had the upgrade kit for $80. This included the
two H4 bulbs 12V 60/55W and the two headlights. The diamond blue bulbs were

well. There is a definate resemblance to the new Porsche headlights. The
patterns are identical. 
The distributor that had the kit was Best Deal Porsche Parts in stanton, Ca.

hundreds of parts cars sprawled over their facility. Their number is
714-995-0081.
