I don't know if this warning applies to your car, but in
the owner's manual for my '84 944, it says that you should
not jack the car by the transaxle because it can cause
"severe damage" to the car.

I mentioned this once before on the list, and I received a
few replies from people who said that they had been jacking
from the transaxle for years with no problems.

Someone (I think it may have been Kevin Gross) said that on
my car, the transaxle hung from the chassis by a single
large bolt, and that it wasn't a good idea to support the
weight of the vehicle from this bolt.
