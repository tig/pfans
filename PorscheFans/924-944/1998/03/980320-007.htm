The DME doesn't know what gear you are in, as far as I know. 

The load on the engine in the lower gears is less due to the gear ratios
making it easier for the engine to accelerate the car. Even though you
may be at WOT in first and second gears, the engine is not at max load.
The oxygen sensor tells the DME how much fuel to inject to remain at the
optimum air/fuel ratio. Heat is energy, and fuel is burned to release
energy. Heat is what drives the turbo, less load, less heat, more load,
more heat, more boost!
