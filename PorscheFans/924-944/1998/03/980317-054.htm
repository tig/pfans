That about the third or fourth time I've seen an airflow sensor for sale that
says its a airflow mass sensor.  If it says mass, then it's not stock on a
944.  The volumetric airflow is measured by the airflow meter, and the DME
uses that and the temperature to APPROXIMATE the mass of air.  The mass
airflow meter reads the current required to keep a thin wire at a specific
temperature and directly calculates the mass from that value.  That said,
there's probably a dual-mass (flywheel on 911) thingy, or something similiar
