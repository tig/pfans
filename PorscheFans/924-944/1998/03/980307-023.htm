....a basic 944 (or 924) coupe with extra goodies was called a Lux and
sold in the UK. Eg;

- In October 1978 4-stud alloy wheels became standard on all 924s plus
cloth trim on the doors and better instrumentation. A 5-speed option was
also announced. Road noise was reduced through suspension improvements.
Throughout these early cars Porsche was constantly dickering with the
rear axle drive ratios to fine tune performance and economy. Porsche UK
announced its Lux package (alloy wheels, headlamp washers, rear wiper,
tinted glass).
