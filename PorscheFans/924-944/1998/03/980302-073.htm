Also wanted to mention that the drive to Kent from Burnaby is around 250km
each way (around 160 miles).  We drove down at 6:00am, arrived at 8:30am,
averaged 67mph including the start and stop.  Most times, we drove around
80mph.  The highway patrol in WA seems to be really cool, we were being
passed doing 80mph in the 70 zones by everything and all the drivers would
move into the right lane once finished passing.  Once into Canada, we picked
up the pace to around 85mph (familiar territory and all).

Anyhow, we travelled 320 miles in one day, autocrossed four runs and felt
no fatigue after, what a great car!
