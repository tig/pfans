     That is a good call, Rich.  Furthermore, IMHO, even with the wheels
     balanced well, things can still get screwed up when you put the wheels 
     on
     the car.  So my question to the original poster is "Do you have OEM 
     wheel
     locks?" If so, where is the valve stem located in relation to the 
     wheel
     lock?  Additionally, for the front wheels is the wheel lock installed 
     on
     the stud with the red dot on the end of it?
     
     The Porsche wheels locks (at least for my old 87 944S) are twice as 
     heavy
     as the accompanying lug nuts (25g vs 12g) and will unbalance the wheel 
     if
     not properly installed.  When installing Porsche wheels, align the lug
     hole that is adjacent to the valve stem with the painted lug.  Then
     install the lock on the painted lug.
     
     You get extra points if the point on the Porsche crest is closest to 
     the
     valve stem but that is only for looks, it will not affect the wheel
     balance.
