You need to pinpoint the source.  I would wash the engine (just hose 
down the existing anti-freeze), go for a short drive, and see where 
it's coming from.  It may be that one of the hoses leading to the block 
is cracked, loose, or otherwise not right.  There is one hose at the 
back of the engine, between it and the firewall, and a second hose at 
the front.

It is possible that you have either a head gasket problem or a cracked 
cylinder head.  Both are uncommon but not unheard of in the aspiro 
eight-valve cars.  Happy hunting,
