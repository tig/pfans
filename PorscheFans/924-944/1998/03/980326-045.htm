The heater/coolent valve on 944 is up against
the firewall just behind intake manifold.

Unlike US made cars the valve NEEDS vacuum to CLOSE

So if you are always hot the valve is not geeting
vacuum or  is stuck open.
tips:
1 check for vacuum at valve when temp to coolest setting defrost off
2 use vacuum pump to the valve to see if that closes it
3 check vacuum supply to dash from engine comp/
4 check opertion of electro-pnuematic servos
   just left of glove box.
5. remove valve and test/inspect
