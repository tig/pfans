Neil,

3M makes a product called quick fix adhesive, PN# 8155.  This stuff holds
like cement.  If you use this and do it yourself, be sure to get your
pieces lined up correctly as this adhesive dries quickly and sets like a
rock.  Once its cured completely, the area can be sanded and you can
procede with whatever painting may be necessary.  BTW, this stuff sells for
around $6.

Hope this helps.  My boyfriend did a similar repair on mine after a run-in
with a poodle.  I had him repaint the whole panel though because of some
cracking in the paint from the stress of the impact.
