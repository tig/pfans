I have an '87. I'm not sure of all of the differences, but the '87 had
bigger brakes and an updated crossover pipe. Also, most 87's have dual
air-bags and anti-lock brakes were available (not standard) for 87. Keep in
mind that the 86 was the first year for the turbo....I didn't see any other
responses to your question on the list, but I'm sure others probably know
more about the differences than I do. Having talked to some owners of 86's
that use them for drivers ed, the larger brakes on the 87 are a definite
plus on the track.
