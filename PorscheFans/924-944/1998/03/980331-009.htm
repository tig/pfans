Maurice,
     You do not have to remove the headliner to get to the front
microswitch. However, the sunroof does have to be removed from the car. If
your roof is not presently working you will have to retract the lifting
arms manually (at the sunroof motor) to remove the roof. Once the roof is
removed, there is a black plastic cover recessed into the headliner between
the sun visors. In the middle of the cover is another smaller plastic cover
which has to be gently pryed off (small flat tip screwdriver works well).
Once you get the smaller cover off, there are two Phillips head screws that
hold the bigger cover in place. Remove the screws and the microswitch is
behind the cover.

Hope this helps,
