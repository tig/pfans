Subject:Weltmeister harness bar review
Date:Sun, 01 Mar 1998 13:22:15 -0600
     Just received a Weltmeister harness bar ordered from Automotion on
their 20% off sale.

1.  The bar is a good 2" too short, too far, in my opinion, to try to
bend the "ears" to fit.

2.  The bar is one piece, which, in my understanding is not allowed in
SCCA stock class autocross.

3.  The bar uses the rear seat shoulder harness anchors which may exceed
the length of most harness systems if you are intending to utilize the
rear lap belt anchors to anchor your harness shoulder belts, as I was.

     So, it's on its way back to Automotion. Hope they'll help out with
the round-trip shipping since the item is, in my opinion, defective.
