There is a product available in the UK for repairing motorcycle
fairings, it is made by 'Three Bond' and is a kit consisting of a glue
to join the pieces, a plastic primer, woven matting to go behind the
repair and a glue accelerator. These kits produce a very strong / light
repair, which I have used in the past to remanufacture mounting points
for fairings etc.
