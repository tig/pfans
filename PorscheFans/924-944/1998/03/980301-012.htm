It is a common misconception the the 944 engine is simply half of a 928
engine. The 944 uses many of the design concepts from the development of
the 928. While many of the components have the same basic dimensions, very
few are actually interchangable (without machining). Also, the early 928s
had a displacement of 4.5L. If the 944 engine was simply half of a 928, it
should have been 2.25L instead of 2.5L.
