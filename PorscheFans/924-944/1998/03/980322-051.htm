While you can jump start a 944, you need to be especially cautious when
you connect and disconnect the helper battery...

A possible cause of the problem might be an alternator that is not
properly regulated to the 14 vDc charge, and the battery boiled out, so
the buffer capability of the battery is gone...

When you put 18 vDc into the circuit, and there is nothing to absorb it,
it goes into the DME and fries some of the circuitry... (sorry for the
technical terms...)
