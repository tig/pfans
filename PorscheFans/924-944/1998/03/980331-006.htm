Another possible problem with the clutch (and much cheaper to fix) is   
that the master cylinder needs to be adjusted at the pedal.  When I put   
the new master in my car it would do the same thing (go in fine, come   
about halfway up and I would have to pull it the rest of the way).  I   
fixed it by screwing in (or out, I forget) the master attachment.  Haynes   
was very helpful.
