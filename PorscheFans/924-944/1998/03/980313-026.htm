Full face helmets are, IMHO, a necessity if you plan to ever race in a car
that has a cage installed.  Have you ever seen what happens when a poorly
installed cage goes through the floor and digs into the pavement?  If you
do not have a full face helmet you are probably looking into dentures!

Since you are buying a helmet, protect your investment and buy a full face
one.  Even if a roll cage is not something that will happen, the
protection offered for other applications is still superior.
