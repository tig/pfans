As far as your power antenna is concerned,It is a very intelligent move to
replace the unit entirely as opposed to removing/replacing the mast. My
experience is that a tired motor will quit as soon as extensive testing is
over with the new mast. The radio can be turned over to the ignition switch
and will operate without a problem but the catch is that Porsche already
thught of this and for some reason left a spot where a relayt should go
without filling it in with sufficient electronics. Call up Dave Aase or george
with 944 ecology for more info. The relay should be located on the side of the
heater box and I beliee that all the post 85.5 cars had it.
