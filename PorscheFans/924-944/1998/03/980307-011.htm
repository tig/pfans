Ole,

According to the microfilm from Porsche:
If the 944 you saw is a 'S' ten the modelyear should be at least 1987. The
motortypenr. should say M44.40, the motornr. should be between
42H00001-50000. I don't have the motornrs. for the year you mention.
The old dash is also impossible.
You can recognise an 'S' engine at his/hers DOHC. The sparkplugs are at the
top of the cylinderhead, the 8V has its sparkplugs at the leftside of the
cylinderhead (right-side if you stay in front of the car).

Hope this helps.
