I think that Vaughan is correct.  The start problem is probably
unrelated to the fan.  Since the gagues work when the car won't
start, I would suspect that the brushes on the starter motor are
worn, causing intermittant contact with the armature.  If, when the
starter motor stopped, the brushes did not make contactat in that
position, then the motor would not turn on the next start-attempt.
When you turned the key to the start position, did you hear the
solenoid click but the starter motor not turn?
