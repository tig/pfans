Chris,

Check to make sure all the pulleys are straight, including the water pump
impellor.

If you have wear on the front edge of the belt (probably due to hitting the
front cam cover) check the timing belt pulley that is connected to the
crankshaft.  The lip of the pulley should be facing the front of the engine
(toward the balance shaft pulley).  If it is facing to the rear, it'll cause
the belt to ride a couple of mm off the camshaft pulley.
