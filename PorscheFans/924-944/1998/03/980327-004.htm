starts. Passee's only give the signal when they're comfortable, which
usually means at the beginning of a short straight. There seems to be an
every-wider spread of vehicle horsepower these days, and open passing
(instead of restricting passing to one or two main straights) works
reasonably well the few times I've tried it.
