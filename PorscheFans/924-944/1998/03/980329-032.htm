Khal,
I'm not sure what Tweeks sells, but it sound similar to others sold
by most of the catalog folks. If it is installed very carefully by
someone with experience, the results are good.  The problems to look
for are as a result of the improper weighting and straps on the
cover while the adhesive dries.  If there is any movement, the
following problems will result:  (1) the speaker covers won't fit,
(2) the defroster holes are partially blocked, and (3) the outward
ends can be cracked by a door closing.
