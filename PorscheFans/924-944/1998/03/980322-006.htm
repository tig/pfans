At the risk of starting a flame war, don't rule out the turbo :-)

When I started looking for a 944, I decided I definitely didn't want a used
turbocharged car.  But when I drove one, the decision was basically made.
They don't cost much more than a good S model and none of the maintenance
work I've done has been related to the turbo.  All of the mechanics that
I've talked to have said that the turbocharger itself it bulletproof as
long as the car is taken care of.

Having said that, I don't suggest that you buy a turbo.  For my driving
(mixed street/freeway, LOVE THOSE ONRAMPS), I like the standard turbo.  For
high speed driving, the Turbo S may be better.  But, for low speed grunt,
it seems like the S/S2 may be the way to go.
