The best way to check for LSD is to raise both rear wheels off the ground
and turn
one wheel in the forward direction. If the other wheel goes in the same
direction,
you have limited slip. If it goes in the opposite direction, you have an
open differential,
not limited slip.

I'm fairly sure that this also works with the Torsen / Quaife
differentials...

BTW, the limited slip was an option on 968 cars, not standard.
