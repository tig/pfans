Yep...

1) Remove Steering Wheel.

2) Remove Air Conditioning Grill and Instrument Cluster Surround.  There are
a couple of hidden
screws... don't force it...

3) Remove Instrument Cluster... disconnect cables.

4) Remove back of instrument cluster ... phillips head screws around the
perimeter of the printed circuit board.

5) Remove speedo from pc board (held to pc board by 4 screws.)

5) replacement is reverse of removal...
