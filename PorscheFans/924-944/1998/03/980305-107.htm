You need something that lets you pump the fluid up-hill and into the 
transmission.  There are various pump devices out there.  Some folks 
use a big syringe-like device.  Me, I take the transmission out, put 
new bearings, seals, and synchros in it, then fill it on the floor. 
 But I'm a bit of an overachiever.

For fluid, I like Mobil 1 synthetic 75W90.  If you have a limited slip, 
get a bottle of GM's Goodwrench LSD additive and add it.  Very good at 
eliminating low-speed, tight-turn chatter.

I would leave the shifter alone.  It's plenty short as it is.
