For everyone's information, Porsche Cars - NA has completed their move to
Atlanta. The phone number is still the same (1-800-PORSCHE). The new
mailing address is:

     Porsche Cars North America
     980 Hammond Drive - Suite 1000
     Atlanta, GA 30328

As far as I know, they still don't answer questions via e-mail. However,
they do answer questions submitted in writing or you can talk to one of
their technical people over the phone. Actually, the 800 number connects
you to some place in Maryland and they connect you to Atlanta. The phone
number in Atlanta is 770-290-3500. Some questions they will only accept
from an authorized dealer (i.e. verifying a part number is correct).
