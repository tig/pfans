It is primarily a matter of logistics, Rich.  Besides the 750 entrants,
there are a lot of non-entrant PCA (staff and workers) and non-PCA people
(vendors, factory personnel, etc.), which could bring the total number of
people participating to well over 1200-1300.  The Parade site has to be
able to provide adequate housing and banquet facilities, and don't forget
that it takes about 4 years to properly plan one of these events.
