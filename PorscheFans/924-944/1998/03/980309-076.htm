Likely an ignition problem.  One possibility is the rotor.  I had the
same symptoms recently.  I replaced the plugs, to little avail.  The fix
turned out to be replacement of what appeared to be a sound ignition
rotor.  It looked decent on the outside, but internally, it must have
been faulty.  Replacement of the rotor absolutely fixed my problem.  I
also replaced the cap, for good measure.
