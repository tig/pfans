Reg,

Chances are it's the master cylinder or the clutch slave cylinder.  Your best
bet (assuming you don't want to have to do things over real soon) is to
replace both cylinders and flush the system (including the brake fluid).
After that, make sure you flush both brake and clutch hydraulic fluid every
one or two years and you'll probably never have to replace the cylinders
again.
