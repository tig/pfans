FYI,
Minor cracks in our front plastic spoilers can be successfully repaired using Epoxy 
for plastics. I have found this is probably as strong (or stronger) than the 
original plastic, and takes paint very well. It also fills the gap up perfectly.
Saved me a lot of money, and looks good as new after some touch up paint...
