	The hatch rattle problem is easily remedied.  The bolts that come
out of the hatch go into two sets of locking jaws on either side of the
hatch.  Your's are to short if they rattle and release the hatch on their
own.  Loosen the jam nuts and lengthen the post by turning it.  Try 1/4
turn at a time.  If you make them too long then the jaws won't release the
posts all the way and your hatch won't open.

Note: These sometimes require seaonal adjustments.  At least mine has.
