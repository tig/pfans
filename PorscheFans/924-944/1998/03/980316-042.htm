This is a common misperception. In talking to a patrolman last year while
waiting for some maintenance to be done on the San Mateo bridge, I was told
that there are "radar certified" officers. In accordance with the changes
in the law, radar is allowed, but the officer must go through an extensive
training and certification process to use it. Although I didn't ask the
question, I came away with the impression that not many California Highway
Patrol Officers are radar certified.
