Young: I have been through one set D40M2s, one set Blizzaks and
(currently) one set  Pirelli P0s on my '84 944, three wheel alignments
(new shocks, new tie-rods, new turnstyle bearings), countless 4-wheel
balance jobs, a new steering U-joint and about 50,000 miles of 65mph
shimmy! The shimmy changes with road surface, goes away for a coupla
days, comes back with a vengeance, but never surfaces at more than 80mph
or less than 55mph. The only thing I have not tried yet is an on-car
balance, only because I have not found a place that can do it. Let me
know if you find such a place and if it helps.

My '91 S2 on the other hand does not show a trace of wheel vibration at
any speed, and I have not had the wheels balanced yet, go figure.
