> I had this problem about a year ago and it seems to have reappeared.
> My rear passenger wheel seems to creak under force.  By this I mean it
> seems to come from the wheel and occurs when I accelerate or engine
> brake, but not when I use the brakes or am coasting.  The creaking is
> discrete and proportional to engine speed.

Bad CV joints make a clicking noise that's louder under load and 
occurs typically once per >wheel< revolution.  If what you're hearing 
is proportional to >engine< speed, you can pretty much eliminate from
suspicion things downstream of the final drive (like the wheels).
