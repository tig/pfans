the wheel sharper, it just grabs the road and turns until it finally starts to
oversteer and it's like on rails now.  Before, it would start pushing first
and really hard to get the back to come out at all.  I think the new behavior
will be MUCH easier to go fast, but MUCHMUCH harder to save the back end when
it does go out at speed.   Luckily these traits are cumulative and should add
up to a MUCHMUCHMUCH more fun time on the track Sunday.
