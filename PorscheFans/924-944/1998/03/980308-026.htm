Eduardo,
I don't know if this trick will work on your S2, but if you
can manage to fit a very thin flat-blade screwdriver through
the speaker grille holes, then you may not have to remove the
panel.

What I did on my '84 944 was to remove the panel screws at the
bottom and front edge so that I could just fit my hand through
to reach the speaker, which is mounted with four phillips head
screws.   I removed these screws with a very thin flat-blade
screwdriver that I fitted through the speaker grille holes.
Be careful not to bend the panel too much or you could break it.

If you're not fanatical about appearances, you can punch small
holes in the panel in order to reach the speaker mounting screws
with a thin screwdriver.
