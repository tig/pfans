Hi Alex,

I had the same problem with my 93 968 at around 35000kms.  The problem
was caused by a cold solder joint on the starter solenoid.  This is the
solenoid for engaging the starter gear to the flywheel.  Get this fixed
before you ruin the flywheel as this is a big $$$ item.

and it 
has never happened when the car is warm or when the car has been sitting
dry.
>>

Don't know about this one here but I would check the ball joints and the
