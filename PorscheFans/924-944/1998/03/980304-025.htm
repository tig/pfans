Before you buy the cookie cutters, check the offset. I think that the
offset on cookie cutters is different than the offset on phone dials.
Phone dials are waaay positive, which is very unusual.  You may not be able
to fit the cookie cutters under the 924S fender wells.  Just FYI, if you
ever intend to autocross at an SCCA event, they don't allow more than 5mm
difference from stock offset in stock category.
