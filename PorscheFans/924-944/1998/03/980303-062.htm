Chris,

According to "THE PORSCHE FAMILY TREE" (copyright 1995 by PCA), all 944
models (at least U.S. models) from 1983 thru 1991 (which includes 944,
944T, 924S, 944S, 944S2) came from the Neckarsulm plant.  Beginning with
the 968 model in 1992, production was moved to Stuttgart.  This information
is confirmed in other pieceS of reference material I have, namely "THE
PORSCHE FACT BOOK" (PCNA, 1992 and subsequent editions).  I would tend to
believe that the same would hold true for all units, not just those
destined for the U.S.
