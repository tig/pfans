Why all the hype about dynoing a car? When I hook up a new aftermarket
goodie I hop in and take her up to about 3000 and let the cluth pedal
go.
Hp numbers are good to look at on paper but the proof is in how the car
feels. I have seen people hell bent on a specific hp number when in fact
they should be looking at the car as a whole; example less weight = more
speed, colder air on the intercooler means more power etc. These factors
along with common sense can mean more than that extra 5hp on the dyno.
So the next time you get the urge to dyno your car line up next to some
some unsuspecting prey and let her loose!
