Two Mustang  three wire sensors (not Bosch) are available from NAPA, they are:
OS 202 SB	for Ford, Merc 86-93
		Ford Truck 86-91	
		Lincoln 87-93
		will replace(E6SZ 9F472A)(E6TZ 9F472AA)(DY 575)(E7TZ 9F472A)(DY 605)
		(F03Z 9F472A)(DY 606)(F0SZ 9F472A)

OS 203 SB	Ford, Lincoln, Merc 84-87
		Ford Truck 86-87
		(59Z 9F472A)(E6TZ 9F472B)(0258 003 013)(DY 571)(E6TZ 9F472D)(DY 569)
		(E6TZ 9F472A)

These are inexpensive and while I bought the OS 203 SB, I have not as yet
installed it.  Your best bet is to take your old one to NAPA and see which
one matches yours.
They also carry a single wire P/N OS 201 SB.
