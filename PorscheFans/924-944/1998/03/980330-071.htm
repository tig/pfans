Well, this is certainly a new one on me...

Let it suffice to say that a proper functioning AC snowflake switch
will stay in (down) whether in a car or out of a car, plenty of freon,
low freon or no freon. (That is to say, there is no feedback from the
freon circuit at all - if the switch won't stay in, it is broken - fix
it...)
