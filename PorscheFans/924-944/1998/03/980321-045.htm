The "turbo" decal on the right fender was optional on all 944 turbo's. 
They came in black fade or silver fade.  They only go on the right
fender.  There was also a "Club Sport" decal that goes on the left
fender of the club sport cars.

I know this because Thursday I just installed the "Turbo" decal in black
fade on my 88 951 "S".  I think the black decal looks great on my silver
rose car.

The "Turbo" decal is about $98.00 from porsche (least that's what I
paid)

The "Club Sport" decal is about $198.00 ( for those of you with club
sport's)

If anybody wants to see what it looks like, e-mail me and I'll send you
a picture of the car from the right front and direct from the front, as
I just took some of it with the digital camera after installing the
decal!
