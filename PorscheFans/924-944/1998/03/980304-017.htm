The misadventure at the car wash reminds me of the admonishment I received
from my detailer recently. Always dust the finish before washing. Use a new
4" paintbrush. Lightly brush the entire surface, no water. Then, wash with
or without a mild detergent. The thinking among many detailers nowadays is
that spraying water or rubbing it around on a dusty surface is too closely
akin to sanding it.
