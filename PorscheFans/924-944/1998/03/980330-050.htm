Okay, I'm guilty as charged, the words "coil-over" make no sense in this
example. To be correct, I should have said "strut with adjustable spring
seat."

In a true coil-over application, the shock absorber is coaxial to the
spring and the spring is attached to the shock absorber. In fact, a
coil-over shock does not have to have an adjustable spring seat.
