So seldom do we hear about a good thing..  If your in the Seattle area
and need a good Porsche repair shop, you should consider "Continental
Motors" on Bothell way.  These guys are great.  I had no idea my low
mileage 83 944 would require a good friend other then me with a
diminishing check book.  Bellevue Porsche and Paul Weir (a great place
for used parts) have crappy service and charge astronomical repair rates
along with every other shop in the area.  I have trusted Continental
with the installation of water pump, Update kit, all belts, radiator,
engine mount and more and have been  delighted with the price and
service.  I recommend them with out hesitation.   The phone number is
206-362-5221.

If you have been looking for used parts through wrecking around Seattle,
you probably can't find anything.  Call Paul Weir for used parts.  They
have a great selection for 944/911.  Now you know the easy way!
