CABRIOLET HOODS

Fitting new windows to cabriolet hoods is a little specialised. AFN
charge 600 for hood fitting so it pays to look around. Try looking at
the following specialists :-

John Palwick    01252 702 492

Around 240 for supply and fitting of a rear window. He needs the car to
do it.

------------

Bob Williams    0836 554 347

250 inc VAT to fit a rear window. He'll come to the car, remove the
hood, return it with a new window in three days and refit the hood.

------------

More of a total hood overhaul at 400 + VAT but the hood is exhaustively
tested for leaks after the window is fitted.

Hood, trim and seat work at reasonable rates.  Replaces hood window for
