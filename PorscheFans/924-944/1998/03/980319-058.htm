Hello P-Fans

I personally like to use a can of CRC Brake-Kleen. It is important to use a
product the is 
NON-FLAMABLE!!! CRC is non-flamable!  I just spray around the intake system
where 
I suspect a vacuum leak. If the engine starts to miss, you have found the
vacuum leak!

Or you can always use a $10 vacuum gauge to measure the intake manifold
vacuum.
 The guage needle will oscillate instead of having a rock steady reading. If
the
needle is surging you know you have a leak. Now you just need to find it!
