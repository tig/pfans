> I have been considering a 1989 944S2 that a fellow nearby is

> 1. Can anyone tell me what I should pay for such a car?

$9K if it has maintenance records, +/- $1K depending on how well
maintained mechanically.

> 2. I know everyone here will tell me to buy it, but a friend of
>        mine who has been a Porsche mechanic for 20 years has
>        PROMISED me that this car will empty my wallet.  How
>        much can I realistically expect to spend on cost of owner-
>        ship per year?

The car has low mileage but will need belt job at 30K ($600), water pump
at 60K + belts again ($1000), clutch at say 60K ($1300). Check control arm
balljoints for play at full lock to lock, parts are $200/side + labor. New
cam cahin tensioner guides at 60K ($400 + labor). Read the 944 FAQ for
other common maintenance items. If the above is taken care of my
experience is you could spend $200-300/yr.

> 3. With this in mind, how do people feel about this idea: I buy
>        the car, but keep it garaged, pamper it, drive it only a
>        few times per year, but hold on to my little Mazda as my
>        daily driver so that I don't have to worry about reliability
>        or break down issues.  The hope here would be (a) to minimize
>        cost of ownership, and (b) to keep the car in terrific shape.

Cars are for driving, especially Porsches. I do keep a 2nd car for winter
and in case of S2 breakdown. But usually its left uninsured and is worth
less than $3K.

BTW the S2 is a great car and hasn't emtpied my wallet!
