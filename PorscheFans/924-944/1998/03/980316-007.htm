Interesting story.

I buy a few sets a month from Tire Rack (tires, wheels, or combos).

Here's how it goes:

"Hey Ray, it's Steve Timmins, I need some C2T wheels for an '89 944 turbo, with P7000SS
235/45+275/40"

Ray:  "OK. They'll be shipped today."

Two days later they are on my doorstep.

I have ordered with a variety of tires in a number of sizes and offsets.  I 
generally get the tires in 2 days, and if wheels/tires are purchaced together
I get mount/bal/valve stems free.  This goes for R1s.

I am a tirerack wholesale customer.  Several on the list have purchaced 
through me.  Since porsches generally only come in 2 offsets it's not a
real difficult task.

On the other hand, I deal with the same saleperson and I know what I want.
