Yes.  It's a Phoenix Gold brand circuit breaker.  I have one in my '95 968.
Mine was installed by the custom stereo shop installer.  If you do not have a
custom stereo system, maybe the previous owner did before they sold the car.
It protects the amplifiers, cell phone, stereo head unit, etc.  The way mine
is installed, I can disconnect the stereo system and most accessories without
disconnecting the starter and, more importantly, the airbag light.  The airbag
light comes on every time you disconnect the battery, and must be reset by the
dealer.
