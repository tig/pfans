David,
The most likely candidate is the clutch cable is binding.  This
could happen from dirt, rust, or gunk at the cable ends, but is more
likely the cable was moved or flops around when the pedal is
pressed.  Have someone look at it when you depress.  Other than that
check all moving hinges and levers from the pedal to the clutch
outboard lever.  Failing that, it sounds like a problem with the
release bearing in the clutch.
