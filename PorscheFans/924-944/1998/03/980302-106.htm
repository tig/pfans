> I have an 86 944 Turbo.  Every now and then, the cooling fan that goes stays
> on after the car is turned off doesn't shut itself off, killing my battery.  I
> asked automotion what is may be and was told it could be "4 or 5" different
> things.  Anyone have any input on this? 

Chances are good that it is the cooling fan switch that screws into the
left hand (driver's side) of your radiator.  I had the same symtoms
recently.  I've heard this is a common ailment in 944's.  Replacing this
switch solved the problem, in my case.  Unfortunately, access is
difficult.  You need just the right size socket and extension to get to
this thing.  Forget about using any sort of box end wrench.  You can
only get it with a socket, if the radiator is in the car.  The switch
itself is not terribly expensive, so go ahead and try it.  I suppose it
could also be the cooling fan relay.  I'd try the swith first.
