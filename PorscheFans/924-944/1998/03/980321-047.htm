This is in response to Richard Lukers' query  regarding 16" C2 wheels on his
924S.  I have an '87 924S and run those same wheels (6" & 7") with R-1's.
They bolt right on without any modifications or spacers, and the offset is
fine.  This is a common upgrade for the 924S, and there are at least two other
924S's frequently track driven in the NE with this setup.
