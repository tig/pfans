> Do the ROW cars actually have seperate instrument panel
> switch for front and rear fog lights?  Because that is

Yes, mine has. I can send a scan of the picture of my
owners manual. It looks exactly like in my car if any-
body is interested.
But the switches are not totally independent. The rear
fog light is only working if the front fog lights are
also turned on. Owners manual says so and reality shows
the same. The wiring scheme of Haynes shows why, the 
rear fog light switch gets its power from the front fog
light relay.
