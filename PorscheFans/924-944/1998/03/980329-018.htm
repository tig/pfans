Seeing that there are complaints about post length and relevance these
days, I'll just say they work "sorta fine" and if you're running in street
conditions and street levels of maintenance, the $40 extra is probably well
spent. Any wire using the "OEM" connector (made be Beru), which Magnecor
doesn't, is probably preferable.

Pfans on a budget with an ohmmeter can buy bulk wire and test and re-use
the connectors for a *much* cheaper ($20?) just-as-good-as-new solution.
