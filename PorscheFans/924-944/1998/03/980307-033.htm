Rob,
The turbocharger from a 968 Turbo may not be the best choice for a 951.
There are significant differences in the exhaust flow characteristics of a
951 (2.5L 8 valve) and a 968 (3.0L 16 valve). If you look at the efficiency
curves for turbochargers, the different areas of efficiency look like
islands. If the turbo is not correctly matched to the flow characteristics
of the engine, the turbo will not operate consistently at its maximum
effiency. Consequently, the car will perform poorly. Porsche did a lot of
research to pick the best turbocharger for the 951. I personally think
that, for street use, sticking with the 951S turbocharger or changing to
the 951S turbo if you have an early 951, is the best choice.
