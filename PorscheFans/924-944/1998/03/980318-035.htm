>Have an 86' 944 and need hood shocks

Hood and lid shocks are created differently.  Even though you order
by Porsche part # you might get an inferior product.  For example,
my hood shocks (for a 924) were made by Stabilus (Lift-O-Mat)
Germany and they will barely keep the hood up.  The rear hatch
shocks were made by Swag (Germany) and they had an open electrical
circuit (defroster).   These parts were sold by Auto Atlanta at a
similar price as the other big boys of the catalog-ilk.  The lesson
in this is to buy a brand known to be OEM or better.
