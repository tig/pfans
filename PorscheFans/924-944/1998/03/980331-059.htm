Dear Sam,

My snow flake switch failed recently too.  I purchased a used heat and A/C
control module from a newer car (an '89) for use in my '86 951.  I paid $100
for it, removed the knobs from the sliding controls, snapped off the face
plate (be careful, the tabs that hold it in place are rather small and are
succeptible to breaking), unplugged the old module (two large plugs with lots
of wires), plugged the new one in... and it works perfectly.  The alternative
is to purchase a new switch and solder it on.

Good luck.
