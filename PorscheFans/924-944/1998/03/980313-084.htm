Regarding your question about the difference in the USA and R.O.W power
figures it seems that the USA figures are measured by the American SAE
standard and the rest of the world by the German DIN standard.

example

1983 US 944 BHP/KW

Power DIN: 150/110
Power SAE: 143/105
