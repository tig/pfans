Some advice from a recent purchase of an 85 944.

Amicus2000


Know what you are buying and make sure you are aware of hourly rates 
from your local specialist.  You need to use a specialist because 
with out the product knowledge you could be in for a real shock when 
something really goes wrong.

I bought a cheap one and have spent about $5k US $7.5 New Zealand 
Reddies over the last six months on every thing from brakes to 
cooling systems, (water and oil) not to mention basic servicing, 
Cambelts and such like.

But hell all that does not really come in to it if you love the car, 
the Marque and the culture of it all. Just do it!!
