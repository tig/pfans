This is a problem that Porsche is aware of and in fact there is a TSB
for this. The noise you are describing is caused by some form of
resonance in the brake/strut assembly.  On  1993 and later cars, Porsche
added some weights on the top of the struts to counter this problem.  I
can look up the TSB later if you want.
