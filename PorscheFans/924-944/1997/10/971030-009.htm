	I used a prop stick until I could get two people to help me get
them in.  

		DO NOT, I MEAN DO NOT push the hood up higher to get them
to fit, you will push the hood into the windschreen and then, FUBAR!!

	What I did, was-	
		Attach the shock to the ball end, ie on the body.
		
		Have someone hold the hood and another person ready to
  		instert the pin
	
		Push like hell on the stut, you should be able to compress
		it about 1/2 way, ( just lean into it, with the ball end
  		mounted firmly, that will be fine, it take a fair amount
		of weight, so get you heaviest guy to do it. 

		Once you have it compressed, slowly release it and
		position it (attempt) ready for the guy to instert the
		pin.

		It may sound like a three ring circus, but I was gladly 
		suprised how easy it went with 3 pair of hands...

	Hope this helps..
