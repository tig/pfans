Hi Alex,

The CR-1's code should be in your CR-Manual that came with the car.  They
attach two cards to that manual with the code.

If you don't have that, and your car either came with the stereo from
Porsche, or was installed by the Porsche dealer, Porsche can look up the
code based upon your VIN number.  I don't know if they will release it
directly to you (how do they know you didn't just steal it out of a
Porsche).  You may have to ask your local Porsche dealer for the code.  They
can call PCNA and get it.

The customer service number for PCNA is 800 545-8039.
