Dear Matt & P-Fans
	The Federal Goverment mandates that the car manufacture guaranties the
emissions equipment last a certain amount of time, miles & years. On cars
built up to Model Year (MY) 1995, the federal emission warranty is 5/50 -
five years (from the cars in service date, not sold date, say if it was a
demo) or 50K miles, which ever comes first. For 1996 MY - cars and up I think
it is (?) - 7 years or 80K miles. 
	This warranty covers anything that can make a car fail the emission test
(tail pipe or the IM240 - dyno test). They will cover things like spark plug
wires, cap & rotor, spark plugs, catalytic convertor, egr system, ect....
