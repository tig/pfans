Minor correction here. According to the rules:

Section 6. Body / Chassis / interior
  G. Steering Wheels and shift knobs are free.

Also, all 951's in class F have the same minimum weight, 2899. While
the 86 951 may be the lightest 951, it isn't that hard to get any
951 down to the legal minimum. Change the seats to lightweight race
seats, remove the cat and muffler, remove the rear seat back (it
interfers with the roll bar), run with a 1/4 tank of fuel, and 
you'll be pretty close to (if not below) the minimum weight.
