Special to Myles Grant:
And, BTW, blinker fluid is supposed to be kept at "minimum" levels, unlike
other fluids in our water-pumpers.  If you are getting a build-up inside
your rear signal housings (not all that uncommon), remove the housings, dry
the areas thoroughly,drill a few small holes in the bottom sections for air
circulation and future drainage if necessary, and re-install the housings
using some silicone sealer around the edges to help prevent future moisture
invasion.  Also, make sure all of your drain tubes are clear and
free-flowing so that your rear hatch area doesn't fill up.
