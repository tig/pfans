Hi there:

I recently bought a 1977 Porsche 924 as my winter car and have several 
questions.

Is it normal for the engine temp to run so high while idling or 
traveling in the city. I've noticed the temp seems to rise to about 
the 3/4 mark on the gauge really quickly but once the car is moving 
the temp comes down to about the 1/4 mark. Is overheating a potential 
problem here ? The fan does come on when the car gets hot but is it 
normal for the engine to get so hot - my 911 does not get this hot 
ever.

Because of the age of the car does anyone have any knowledge of the 
effect of products such as Slick 50 etc ? I am considering putting it 
in the engine.

Does anyone have any experience with the wipers turning themselves on 
intermittently when the car makes hard turns ? - This could just be a 
loose wiring problem.

Also I sometimes find it very hard to shift into 1st gear ( I have the 
4-speed gearbox) and at one point put it in second gear at first and 
then pushed up to 1st gear, this worked for maybe a week and then 
second gear was hard to engage especially from third gear into second. 
Is there any type of lubricant that I should use to make shifting 
easier ?

Often there is a gasoline smell that enters the cabin - is this a 
byproduct of age i.e. pre catalytic converters ?

How does the heat/vent system work. I get hot defrosting when all 
three levers are pushed to the right but do not know how to get fresh 
air in the cabin from the vents or if there is a separate fan speed 
switch ( I doubt there is one) - How do the levers work ????

I know I did not buy a new car but these little quirks have me 
puzzled.

I have seen an ad in either Performance Products or Automotion 
advertising a grounding wire/sensor that eliminates the high 
temperature of the engine (due to inefficient grounding) and 
intermittent electrical glitches - does anyone have any experience 
with this.

Thanks to all who respond in advance. I love my 911 and wanted 
something to get me through the cold icy/salty winters of Toronto. My 
price on the 924 was very low and I believe I have a great winter car 
however the above are some of the anomalies I have been having. Any 
comments would be really appreciated.
