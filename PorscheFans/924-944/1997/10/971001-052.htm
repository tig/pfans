One advantage to the N/A 944 is the relative ease and lower cost of
maintenance compared to the 951.  Take a good look under the hood of both. 
If you fancy doing some DIY on your car (especially as a beginner), you
will find the N/A car to be much easier to get to things.  If you don't
work on it yourself then I would guess that labor charges for most jobs are
higher for the added parts that must be removed for access on the boosted
one.  Of course if money is no object, the afore mentioned advantage means
little (and you probably wouldn't be buying an 8 year old car.....).
