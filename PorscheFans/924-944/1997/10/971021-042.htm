About the stumbling and hesitation following a hot restart, before you 
spend $$$, it may be something as simple as dirty injectors. This has 
happened to my NA over the years and has always been fixed with can of 
BG 44K or Techon fuel injector additive. Put it in with 1/4 tank of 
fuel, drive it a little to get it through the system and let it set for 
a day or two. Then drive it hard.
