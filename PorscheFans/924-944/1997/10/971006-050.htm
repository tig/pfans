The vacuum line connecting the bypass valve to the three way connector
connecting to the fuel damper can come loose at either end.  This line is
probably the easiest to check and see.  Also check the lines from the cycling
valve to : intake boot, wastegate, intercooler tube.

The bypass valve is located on top of the intake boot.  It should have a vacuum
line coming out of top of it.
