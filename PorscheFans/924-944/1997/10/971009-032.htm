Although I haven't owned mine since new (bought it with 40K miles), I have
driven it for 125K and I can say that it has been quite reliable (85 944). 
Also, the all galvanized body is holding up excellently and the interior is
quite good compared to other high mileage cars I've seen.  If factory
recommendations on maintenance are followed then I believe they are
reliable cars.  However, they are probably less forgiving than say your
average Honda if regular maintenance is not adhered to.
