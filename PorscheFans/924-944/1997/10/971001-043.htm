Ezra,
I drove my 85.5 daily for six years in upstate NY (Albany area).   I'd put
Blizzaks on the track wheels and the car was a joy to drive in the slippery
stuff.   Only problem I ever encountered was with ground clearance after a
heavy snow, before the plows came through.
Also, the galvanized shell stood up well to the tons of road salt they spread
around here -- no rust anywhere on the car.
If I were you, I'd use the 85.5, or get something else that's cheaper to
maintain as a winter beater.
