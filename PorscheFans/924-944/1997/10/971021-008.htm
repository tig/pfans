Hello Gregor,

Yes, I did have to remove the steering wheel but it is a
piece of cake.  No problem!  Sequence of events:

1. Remove steering wheel pad (it just pulls off) and
disconnect the horn wire.
2. Remove the steering wheel (I believe 24mm).  The
manual says note the position of the steering wheel
prior to taking it off, but there is only one way to put it
back on, but I marked it anyway just in case.
3. Remove the 8 screws holding the vent flaps and
cluster trim.
4.  Remove the 4 screws which hold the instrument
cluster.
5. Pull cluster out and disconnect the three
connectors.
6. Install in reverse order.  Just make sure your
tripometer is positioned in it's hole and the vent flap
and cluster trim is positioned over it properly (you'll
know what I'm talking about when you get there)

Good Luck
