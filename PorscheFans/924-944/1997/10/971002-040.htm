I've ordered a clutch kit from Vertex, via e-mail. Found them to be very
good. As I didn't need the kit in a hurry I asked them to ship it first
class. Got an e-mail back the next day:

     I am sorry to inform you that I have just come from the post
     office and the shipping cost of that package is $120.00. Do you still
     want to send it first class?
     Let me know A.S.A.P.

I said no, just send it sea mail. They did, and charged just the actual cost.

That was good service.

BTW, I've since sold the 95.5 NA and bought a 951, so never got to put the
clutch kit on. If anyone in New Zealand wants one, lry me know!
