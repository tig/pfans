John, Porschefans;

Do not be so quick to jump to the Hoosiers if wear and expense are a major
concern.  The Hoosiers will wear out much faster than the R1s.  I do not
know how someone could wear out a set of R1s in 6 days.  I have had mine
for about 15 track days plus at least 1000 street miles and the right front
just recently wore out.  This is without alternating them left to right.
Must have been something wrong with them.

The Hoosiers are a faster and lighter tire, but it comes at the expense of
longevity.  Expect to spend more per tire and have to replace them more
frequently.
