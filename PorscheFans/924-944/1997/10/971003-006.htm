With all the recent talk about BAD parts suppliers, I would like to
recommend a VERY GOOD one--Imparts Ltd. in St. Louis. They are very
courtious and knowledgable AND they consistently have the lowest prices
for top quality parts--AND they get things to you quickly.

For my late '85 944, I have ordered from them: front brake disks ($49
ea.), Bosch rotor and distributor cap, a fuel filter, hood shocks (only
$15 each!) and a VERY nice beginners torque wrench ($25 with a carrying
case and two extentions).

Since, Imparts provides parts for Mercedes, BMW, Audi and  Porsche,
their free catalog doesn't list every little Porsche part you can
imagine--but they do have quite a lot at very reasonable prices.

I recommend that  everyone on this list check them out.

You can request a catalog on their web site:
http://speedy.daugherty.com/~helton/index.html
