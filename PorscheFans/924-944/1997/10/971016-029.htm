Hi,

Manged to have another good look up under the foot-well of my 952 this
weekend. Found the cause of the brake lights coming on when the clutch
pedal was depressed:

Seems that the bracket holding the switch for the brake light (just behind
the brake pedal) was mis-aligned. A slight touch of the brake pedal was
enough to close the switch and turn the brake lights on. And a slight
depression on the clutch was enough to move the pedal assembly (including
brake) and close the switch. The problem was solved by bending the bracket
holding the switch closer to the brake pedal so that the brake pedal had to
be depressed slightly further before the switch closed.

With that fixed, I shouldn't be giving the driver behind too much of fright!
