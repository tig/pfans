After re-reading your note, I see you were suggesting only adjusting the
balance shaft belt.  Although you can probably get away with this, I still
would not recommend it since if the balance belt is loose enough to jump a
cog, you may run the risk that it will be loose enough to prematurely fail.
 So what?  Balance shaft belt failures can take out cam belts on the way. 
It has happened.
