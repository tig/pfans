reasonably well on the track...Then it happened. I corded a front real quick
about 2 days. The cord was at the surface(no kidding!) but was above the
grooves. Then I heard others had similar problems. A friend went through a
set after a couple of days, ect.. We were also very good about heat cycling

changed, and or compounds changed...Who cares they won't be on my car
