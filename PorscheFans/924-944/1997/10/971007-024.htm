Folks,

A couple of months ago there was a thread on using
GM gear oil with a special limited slip differential
additive in order to help downshifts to 1st (particularly
nice for auto-x).

A couple of months ago I changed the gear oil in my 90 944 S2,
installed Mobil 1.  More recently I bought a tube
of Sta-Lube additive (intended for limited slip
differentials) and have to say it improved downshifts
to 1st and 2nd.  My shifter was never difficult to begin
with, but now it's astonishing.  Nice improvement
