Sounds like your crackcase vacuum is being lost somewhere.  You may also
have oil mist coming out of the oil fill cap.  I'm not sure how the NA 924s
achieve proper crankcase ventilation.  My 931 has an oil separator cup that
gets vacuum from the air filter box and sucks on the crankcase. [that may
send some parental blocking software into a tizzy]

Check for PCV system leaks first.  Then get a new dipstick.  My case vacuum was
being lost by a bad dipstick.  No oil coming out, just air going in.
