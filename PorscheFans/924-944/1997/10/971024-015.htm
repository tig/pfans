Hi, Paul,

I leave mine on my track car for two reasons:

1a) If I need to be towed in, I don't have to crawl out and install it.
There is no other place to attach
a tow rope to a 951.

1b) In the spring, I have seen drivers in six inches of water at the
entrance to the esses at Lime Rock.
I figure that if the tow eye is already there, I might get lucky and not
have to get too wet to get the rope
attached.

2a) If someone else needs to be towed in, I know exactly where the tow eye
is... e.g. at our last LRP event
(you were there) and the 951 lost the coolant hose, he didn't have a tow
attach point, and was holding
up the event while I sprinted with the tow eye in hand.

2b) If it is attached to the front of the car, it is not rattling around in
the cabin of the car, could be pretty
lethal in the case of a sudden change in momentum (i.e. crash).
"In the land of the blind, the one (tow) eyed man is king!"

Lastly, the eye screws directly into the bumper support, and will take at
least one ton of force, if used
properly, without damage to the car, or the tow eye...


Good Luck!
