I too had the floppy sun visor problem. The problem ended up being a weld
inside the visor that had broken free. No matter who tight you got the
adjustment screw the visor still flopped. 

I ended up removing the visor, using a razor to cut along the seem (closest
to the windshield) and opened the visor up. I took it apart, JB welded the
broken piece and bought new foam at a fabric store. After putting it all back
together, It looks good and has been functioning for the past two years with
no problem.
