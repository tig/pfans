Dear P-Fans & Barry
	When replacing the motor mounts in a 924S/944/951 with the updated ones
(part #951-375-042-04) there are longer bolts - to go with the updated
mounts. I believe there are three different lenghts of bolts available, but I
could only find part numbers for two sizes! 
	The bolts come in two sizes/lengths (8mm X 38mm - prt#900-378-024-02 and 8mm
X 42mm - prt#900-378-034-02) each motor mount uses two bolts (you have to
measure to see witch size you will need) and two washers (part#N-015-401-3).
	The bolts "drop-in from above the mount - which makes them a real pain in
butt to tighten! I found out that using a 13mm distributor ("L-shaped, bought
at SEARS) wrench up-side down works perfectly to tighten these damm bolts! 
								Good Luck, David Jalali
