As I am quite tall (1m90) I immediately replaced the
stock steering wheel in my 924S (380mm) with the
4 spoke wheel from a 944 (360mm). What you can also
do in a 924S is remove the spacers under the drivers seat.
They are about 1 cm thick. The wierd thing is that the
passenger seat does not have these spacers so what I have 
done is remove the spacers under the drivers seat and put
them under the passenger seat.
