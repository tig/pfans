Gregor, as a fellow 944 enthusiast and as someone who does the belt
changing (and tensioning) on his own car, I really would discourage you
from tampering with your timing & balance shaft belts unless you really
know what you are doing and have the correct tools.  The worst you can do
is MUCH worse than simply "slipping a cog" (can you say "bent valves?"). 
If your mechanic has double checked the tension and is sure it's correct
(you might want to ask him what setting he is using and see if that
corresponds to list knowledge) , I would suggest running the car for a few
thousand miles and letting the belt teeth wear in a little bit.  In my
experience they do quiet down to an acceptable level.
