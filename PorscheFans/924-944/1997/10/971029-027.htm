B. Clark, If you happen to have some 1/4 inch thick steel plate and a
section of gear teeth that happen to mesh with the flywheel and a welder,
you will then be able to make a facsimile of the tool in question.  I
bought mine from one of the advertisers in Panorama (I think it was
Automechanica but it was several years ago) and as I recall, it was not all
that expensive (maybe $50).  I have heard of some people jamming a big
screw driver between the ring gear teeth and clutch housing but you will
probably need  a helper to do it that way.  The flywheel lock tool makes it
a much easier job to remove the crankshaft bolt in my opinion.
