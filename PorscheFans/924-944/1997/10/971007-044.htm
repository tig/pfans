There's not much to check on the steering rack.  If your car has power 
steering and there is play, you should check the U-joints in the 
linkage from the steering wheel to the rack.  Also check the four bolts 
mounting the rack to the cross member.  Check the rack for leaking by 
pulling back the tie rod boots at each end of the rack.

Motor mounts need to be measured to tell if they are defective. 
 Defective ones show up as roughness in the engine which clears up 
above 1200 RPM.  If not corrected, this vibration can break the oil 
pick-up in the sump which is a major bummer.
