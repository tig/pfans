Tom's mechanic is a moron.  He is telling him wive's tales.  Spring 
rate has absolutely NOTHING to do with age or load placed on it at 
some point in its life, unless it is affected by physical damage or 
deformation.

Poorly designed springs can sag from loading after many years.  If 
you'll notice, one usually sees this happening on old stationwagons 
and Ford Granadas.  I don't think I've seen any P cars dragging their 
bumpers lately.

If you think about it, the shock is just a damper.  As such, it 
doesn't do anything to help support the car or prevent spring wear.
