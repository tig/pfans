Bob, the answer is most definitely yes; an over-tight cam belt puts higher
loads than designed for on the water pump bearings.  This scenario can
happen on any car with a belt driven water pump.  However, the bigger
threat with these cars is that a too-tight timing belt might break long
before the pump bearings get tired.  A broken timing belt means valves hit
pistons!  And your wallet will feel sick......
