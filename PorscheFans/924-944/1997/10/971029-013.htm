Hi  Marv & P-fans
	Regarding your intermittent 951's boost problem.  Two things to check. Boost
dump valve -  on the intake boot, to the right of the throttle body with a
vacum line going to th etop of the valve. Remove the boost valve -  and
pressure check to see if it holds boost pressure. Or. The ignition KLR box
could be going bad. When the ECU detects ignition knock, the ECU sets the
maximum boost to 1.2-1.3 Bar. If you turn the engine off, then restart it,
the ECU will reset to normal max boost 1.7 Bar stock (2.0 bar w/chips) the
ECU has no "hard" memory. Your ECU could be going bad? Can you swap out the
ECU with a freinds car?
