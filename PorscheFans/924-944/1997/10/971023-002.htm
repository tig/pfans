       I personally now own 2 931's, both circa 1981 and both are daily =
drivers. Since these are my #4 and #5 Porsche's, prior being a 74' 914, =

complain about maintenance costs on these cars. Since mine are 81's, I'm =
not sure if the 1980 model parts prices are really any higher but I do =

Just my $.02 no flames intended, just sticking up for my babies.
