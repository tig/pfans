Michael, When I last did my pump it was an updated pump and it did come
with the metal "safety" bracket that goes between the cam belt where the
teeth come closest to touching (on opposite sides).  Installation of this
bracket did require 2 new holes and some modification of the rear plastic
cover.  I found that a (hot) soldering iron worked quite nicely for making
these mods.

My pump came with a nylon plastic-looking cover that bolted on over the
extra hole in the pump.  It would seem that this should have been supplied
with your pump.
