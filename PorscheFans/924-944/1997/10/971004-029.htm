Finally: after enduring a number of months of "its coming" and "it'll be
out next week," porsche baby/child seats are available through porsche
dealers.  There are three different seats that are available: Baby-Safe
(upto 20 lbs, PNA-802-013-00, $150), Prince (20-40 lbs, PNA 802-015-00,
$180) and Zoom (37-100 lbs, PNA-802-017-00, $190).  NOTE: The video that
accompanies the seat states that the Baby-Safe is not suitable for use
in the back seat of a Porsche (The other two seats are), which is
contrary to what PCNA told me.  The seat actually fits in the back seat
(facing rear, as required by law), if the front passenger seat is pulled
up all the way.  The Prince fits perfectly!!!!
