To be more precise, Laguna Seca (near Monterey, CA) is south of San
Francisco. It's less than an hour from SF but at least three or four hours
north of LA. Judging by John's e-mail address he may well live in the Bay
Area.

Speaking of the race this weekend, is anyone else from the SF Bay Area
heading down to Monterey this weekend? The Exxon Supreme GT (GTS-1, -2, and
-3) is Saturday and the FIA World GT race, which includes cars such as
Porsche's GT1, McLaren, and Mercedes' new GT car, is Sunday. I'm heading
down from San Rafael (Marin county) both days. Tickets are still available.
See http://www.laguna-seca.com/ or http://www.professionalsportscar.com/
for more info.

Here's the provisional entry list for the FIA race, reprinted without
permission from the Professional SportsCar site. Of 42 entries, 17 are
Porsches, including at least two Werks cars. Good stuff. Don't miss the
Porsche contingent   in Saturday's race, either.
