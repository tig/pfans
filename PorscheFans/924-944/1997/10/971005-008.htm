Hi!  Go to Radio Shack and get a VOM meter with current measuring. 
Remove the positive battery terminal cable.  Following directions on the
meter (do not reverse bias!) hook the meter between the battery terminal
and the battery cable you disconnected.  Read the amount of current
draw.  This will tell you if there is a large current drain.  An alarm
will always draw current BTW.  Get a meter that reads in amps, not just
mA, ok?  Otherwise you'll damage the meter.
