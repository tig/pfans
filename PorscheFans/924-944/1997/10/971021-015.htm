Bill -

In the early years when I had my 924, I used to tow a small trailer all
the time with tires, jack, tool box and supplies to many events.  I had a
friend make a "T" shaped hitch by welding two pieces of 1/4" x 2" steel
together.  I then bolted to longer portion (top of the "T") to the
underside of the rear bumper after I drilled two corresponding holes in
the bottom of the bumper (out of site....out of mind).  It worked great
for many years including two Parades.  If you want more information, 
email me.  I may still have it around if you are interested and nearby, 
San Francsico Bay Area.
