If I were you I would remove the shift boot and top of the shifter and check
to see if the "c" clip ( or "e" clip as I have heard them referred to ) has
broken or come off of the shift linkage.  The purpose of the clip is to keep
the gear shift lever attached to the linkage once it has passed through the
hole in the linkage.  A little grease at this connection will also aid in a
smoother shift.  I hope this will help you out.
