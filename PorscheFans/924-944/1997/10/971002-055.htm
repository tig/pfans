In the meantime, if you have trouble with mail order, you can always call 
your credit card issuer and have a 'chargeback' issued for an item not 
received, or if you are unable to get a satisfactory replacement part 
shipped.  Upon being informed of this, some places will get off their 
butts and move, rather than being forced to deal with a chargeback.  Try 
this and give them a week, then charge the order back if you do not get 
any results.  In the US, you have the legal right to do this.  Don't put 
up with endless phone calls and lip service.
