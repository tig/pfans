James,

Before you buy a new relay or spend a lot of time searching for burnt
bulbs,

Find the relay
Activate the turn signal
Press, wiggle, shake the relay and see if it induces blinking.
If it does try re-seating the relay on the board.

I've seen the relays work loose from the board and cause the identical
problem.
