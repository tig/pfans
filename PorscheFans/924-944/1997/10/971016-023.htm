Mark, you may want to investigate a slip for the seat belt. We bought a
piece called a "saf-t fit" that consists of a nylon and velcro strap
type unit that adjusts the seat belt for children. My 5 year old uses it
whenever he sits in a front seat with an airbag. The unit relocates the
pivot or hinge points for the seatbelt so that it fits a child. It works
perfectly, keeps the kid back in the seat and with the seat back, keeps
them away from the airbag.
