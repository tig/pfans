I'll take a shot at this, since I know I used TIA recently, and don't mean to
be insulting (though I must admit, I'm definitely curious).

TIA - Thanks In Advance
IMO - In My Opinion
IMHO - In My Humble (or Holy, depending on your taste) Opinion
RTFM - Read The Bleeding Manual
RTFAQ - Read The FAQ (I'd have expected to see more of this around
here)
BTW - By The Way
WTF - What The Hey?
ROTFL - Rolling On The Floor Laughing
OTOH - On The Other Hand
AFAIK - As Far As I Know

There are plenty more, I believe they are tabulated in the Hacker's
Dictionary/Jargon File, or check out:

http://dante.creighton.edu/jargon/jargon.html - warning: very busy
