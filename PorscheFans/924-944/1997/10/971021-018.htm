> I heard somewhere that the rear ride height is adjustable on a 944.  Anyone
> have any ideas on how to adjust it, my 944 seems to ride a little higher in
> the rear than some of the others I have seen around my town.  as always, any
> help will be appreciated.

On the early 944 there are two eccentric cams in the rear that control
ride height. If you mess with these you will get a lot of negative
camber and accelerated tire wear, assuming you are not tracking the car.
With good torsion bars and shocks, these eccentrics need to be in the
highest position for for factory spec camber.
