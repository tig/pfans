Hello P-Fans
	 Any one out there that needs to replace their CV-Joints? Well you can use
Volkswagen Vanagon CV's - part #251-598-101. I used them on my '86 951, I
believe that they will fit all 944s up to '87 951's & NA's.  I replace all
four CV-joints two years ago and 50K miles with no trouble so far. 
	I also saw a note on brake pad recommendations. I like Jurid OEM pads,
Porsche part#964-351-939-02 or Jurid part #571348J. They squeal a little when
cold but really stop the car well. I've tried Repco (no dust -but they don't
slow the car down at all), Ferodo - Ok, but they seem to chew into the
rotors! I drove a friend car w/Hawk pads - WOW! They really stopped the car
but they were very noisy!

     Just my $0.02 worth, David Jalali.
