     The cheapest way(s) to get more horses outta your engine is to buy a 
     K&N air filter, this will increase the C.F.M. (some measurement of air 
     flow) into the engine by 59% (they have a website or you can order it 
     from performance products). Also, you can buy a throttle response cam 
     (under $25) which increases...ta da! your throttle respone. Those are 
     the cheapest and easiest methods I know of increasing power. If you 
     want the whole sha-bang you might want to consider buying the package 
     offered by performance products which includes the chip, air filter 
     and throttle response cam for around $450. Hope this helps.
