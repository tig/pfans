Hi P-Fans
	The 968 has "VarioCam" not "VarioRam"!  All 968's have VarioCam.
   The 993 has VarioRam. VarioRam was first used on '96 993 models and on.
The '95 993 did not have VarioRam. VarioRam made 15 moore Hp.
	VarioCam advances and retards the cam timing to decrease emissions at idle
and to boost HP at higher RPM's. The chain tensioner changes the amount of
"slack" in the chain effectivly changes the cam timing.
	VarioRam is a variable resonace intake manifold system. The intake manifold
runners change in length to boost hp and torque through out a wide RPM band.
Short runners for Hp at high RPM's and long runners for Hp and low RPM's.
