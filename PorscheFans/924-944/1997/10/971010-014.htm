idea, for as soon as opend the unit springs went flying everywhere, I mean
if it could possibly be designed with a spring, it was, and that spring
was now somehwere on my garage floor.  Needless to say, I found all the
springs, and with 3 pairs of hands I got the unit back together and it
does work..  I am not recomending that you perform this opperation, but it
can be done, for a whole lot cheaper than a replacement...
