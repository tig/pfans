The reason for the inaccuracies is the way the numbers are stated and the 
confusion when the "limited edition" and the Turbo S are stated. Also the 
"model year" for the US and other markets is different than the actual 
month/year the car rolled off the assembly line..

Here you"ll see what I mean.

How many 1988 limited edition(silver Rose Metallic) models Worldwide?
How many 1988 limited edition(silver rose metallic) models PCNA? Remember 
that "PCNA" also includes Canada!
How many 1988 limited edition(silver rose metallic) models USA?
How many, and what "different" color variations were there of the Turbo S 
(non silver rose metallic) produced in 1988? (this was not really 
publicised by Porsche because of the risk of affecting the limited 
edition status of the original production run.)
How many 1989 Turbo models worldwide? (this was the Turbo S, without the 
"S")
How many 1989 Turbo models PCNA? How many PCNA/USA?

The production information in "The Porsche 924/944 Book"p.167, by Peter 
Morgan conflicts with the letter I have and the "Porsche Buyers Guide" 
p.148, by Dean Batchelor.

The 944 Turbo S Sales Brochure states: "Only 1000 944 Turbo S models will 
be produced. Less than 500 will be made available in North America"

The Information Technik 944 Turbo S # WKD 495.021 states that the 
production run was limited to 1000 cars for the 1988 model year

I have also heard "rumors" that due to customer critism the
