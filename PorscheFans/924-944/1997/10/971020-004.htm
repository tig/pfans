Myles,

I too have suffered with the manifold breaking at the heat shield.
Apparently even Porsche realised that the design was junk and later
944T's manifolds have a 'concertina' in them to cover the thermal stress
problem. 
It must be possible to fit the later manifold to early cars as this is
what the shop did for me in exchange for an enormous wad of money.
Beware though, all sorts of bolts and brackets can break adding to your
grief not to mention it is a pig to fit. However I am assured that you
do not need to remove the engine.
