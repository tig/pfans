Michael: my '84 NA is pushing 103k and I recently had the (original)
clutch inspected (just a precaution, no problems). Measuring wear at the
peephole shows 40% of new spec, so the disc has worn 60%. At this rate I
will need a new clutch at 170k miles! Of course the rubber center is
likely to give much sooner than that, but driving it now, there is no
backlash on acceleration and minimal backlash on deceleration. The
clutch does not grab, slip (with the brakes on) or do anything else it
is not supposed to. Treat a 944 clutch well and it will behave like the
Energizer bunny!
