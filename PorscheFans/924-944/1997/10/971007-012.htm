Hi Bob,
   Camberball is actually a trade name for a specific product.  
They advertise in Pano and/or Excellence.
   I am familiar with both Camberball and Kelly-Moss.  My C2 
has Kelly-Moss and my wife's 944s has Camberballs.  Both work 
well.  The Kelly-Moss stuff is the best stuff out there in my 
opinion...if you need it.  The precision and adjustability is 
useful if you are a serious suspension tuner.  Otherwise, the 
Camberballs will do the job.  I have found no down-side woth 
using the balls.
   There are others but I have no experience with them.
