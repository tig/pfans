The dealer "Porsche" timing belt is stamped with a Pcar # and also says
"Gates". I understand from a post written by a "Porschephile" that Gates is
an OEM supplier to Porsche and the belt uses a HTD tooth profile (don't ask
me what HTD is an acronym for)  So since the local NAPA store also carries
Gates belts - You should look for the equivalent retail version of NAPA in
the UK.  BTW the timing belt is now sold by the dealer for $19CDN and the
balance shaft belt for $35CDN.-for 944NA and 951 (total 24 pounds sterling)
It may be worthwhile to mailorder from the US or Canada if the price
differential is this significant.
