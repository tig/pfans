Phil, it is my understanding that the oil pump must be removed to replace
the (crankshaft) front main seal.  This can be done with the engine still
in the car, but it would entail a lot of work since all of the
belts/rollers and cogs would have to be removed along with the plastic belt
cover.  I am pretty sure that the oil pan would need to be dropped as well
and doing that involves unbolting and lowering the front cross member
(unbolting motor mounts, disconnecting steering rack).  Mine leaks a little
too.  It will have to get a lot worse.....
