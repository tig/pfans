According to Factory Publication WKD 423 320 (aka "the little white book")
p. 66 states that "standard" steering wheel diameter is 380mm with
"optional extra" 360mm.  This section makes no distinction between 
944/924S.

What kind of steering wheels came stock in the '88 M030 924S?

If you are tall, I highly recommend installing either a 350mm or 320mm
MOMO wheel. My 924S was not tolerable for extended trips with the original
(380mm) fixed-position wheel.
