Hello Edward
		It sound like you ignition switch could be bad, only providing power to
coil durring cranking position. Next time the car will not "catch" try
putting a "jumper" wire from the battery's Pos terminal to 
the #15 terminal of the coil - this by passes the ignition switch. You could
also use a multi-meter to check for power at the #15 terminal durring an
atempted start-up. If you only have power durring cranking - the ignition
switch is bad!
