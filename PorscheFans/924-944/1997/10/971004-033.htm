> My new enamel porsche emblem for the horn pad just arrived with some
> other stuff I ordered from Performance Products.  How do I get the horn
> pad off?  Is it just clipped on and you can pull it carefully off or is
> there some hidden screws/bolts.

If your car is not airbag equipped, all you do is gently but very firmly 
grasp both sides of the horn pad and steadily pull towards you until it 
pops off.  Be careful when it comes off, as there is a wire going to the 
horn pad that is not very long and will get yanked off.  It can be 
unplugged after you have the pad loose.  Sometimes the plastic on the 
back of the pad can crack.. this is harmless.. just mend with some 
plastic adhesive before you replace it.

When you put the pad back on, push it back firmly, then test it by 
beeping the horn and seeing if it is sitting flush.  You may have to 
strike it with the palm of your hand to get it to seat properly.
