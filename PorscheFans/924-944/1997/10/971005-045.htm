One thing to watch out for--Kelly Blue Book values are somewhat
inflated.  Most banks use the NADA values to determine how much they
will loan for the car, and most dealers use NADA to figure trade-in
value.  For my 94 Z28, Blue Book said resale was 17,000, however, NADA
maxed out at about 15,500, with trade-in value about 12,000.  You can
usually find the little orange NADA books at most bookstores, or go to
your bank and ask them to run your car on the computer.
