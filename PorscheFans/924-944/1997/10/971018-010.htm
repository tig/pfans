If you stiffen either the front sway bar or the coil springs, yes, your car
will understeer.  The standard Weltmeister springs apparently do not lower
the car to the point at which the ball joints run out of misalignment, i.e.,
out of travel.  Check whether your particular car needs the spacers they
sell.

Note that I said "apparently".  Don't sue me.
