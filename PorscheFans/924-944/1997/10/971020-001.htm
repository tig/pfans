I have converted my 79 931 to a 81 model. It is straightforward except for
the electrics, you will need the wiring from your 82 model in the engine
room, all other parts: bellhousing, exhaust etc. are the same.

The difference between the turbos are the size of the turbine housing, it
is size 6 on a turbo-1 and size 4 on a turbo-2. This gives better  response
in the 2-3000 rpm area and lower max. power in the high end.
 I am not quite sure about the inlet side.

An interesting thing is, that if you take your turbo-2 turbo and fit the
turbine housing from your turbo-1 you have a Carrera GT turbo. It should
give around 10 HP more, but at the price of a slightly slower low end
pick-up.
