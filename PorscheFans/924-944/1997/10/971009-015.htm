When mine went out, my mechanic advised that the compressor on the 944's
was not a very good unit, was very expensive and he suggested replacing it
with a Sandan. It was cheaper for me to buy a new Sandan unit and have it
installed than a rebuilt Porsche compressor would have been. It has been in
for about 3 years with no problems at all.
