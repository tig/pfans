I was reading the thread about battery compartment repair and thought I'd
pass on some information about batteries.  The number one cause of battery
failure is from dirt on top of the battery.  The dirt gets moist and it
acts as a short between the post.  Sounds stupid but two of my battery
suppliers confirmed this.  The second cause is from vibration.  Just some
useless but maybe helpful trivia.
