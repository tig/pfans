Hi Chris,

If you replace only two cars on the car, you should replace them with the
same tire to maintain the handling characteristics.  Different models/brands
do their job differently.  Only replacing the tires at one end of the car
could (probably will) introduce some unwanted handling behaviors
particularly in an emergency situation (or time trials and autocrosses for
the rest of us).

Since the "good" tires are six years+ old, check them carefully for signs of
dry rot on the sidewall.  It will look like some minor surface cracking.  If
any  signs exist, you should replace those tires anyway.  Under load, those
tire could fail.
