here in Vermont, we have snow and ice on the roads in the winter. As such, the 
local road crews salt the roads, and occasionally spread sand on really icy 
days.  Why do I ask about this? My wife and I own an '87 Jeep cherokee that we 
drive in the winter, we are considering selling the Jeep and trying to find a 
cosmetically challenged (ie. cheap) 944 for winter use. I really don't want to 
use our '85.5 944 in the winter since they salt the roads in this area and it
