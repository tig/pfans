On Thu, 23 Oct 1997 ElTourista@aol.com wrote:

> This is a pretty generic question:  I want to replace my 944 stone gaurds,
> how do I remove the old ones?; I'm also looking for a good mechanic around
> Seal Beach, CA, any suggestions?

Carefully heat the area with a hair dryer (a professional heat gun is more
effective, but be careful not to overheat!).  Slowly and carefully peel
the old clear sheets off.  You may discover they come apart as you pull,
or the adhesive remains the paint.  Use solvent and ingenuity to get all
of the residue off without damaging your paint.  A soft plastic scraper 
or spatula can be helpful.
