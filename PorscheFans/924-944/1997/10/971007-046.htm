* I have the same looseness in mine(2-3 in. in any gear), except only 
horizontal. My mechanic
* that replaced my clutch last year said I was missing a bushing on the 
shift linkage at the trans.

There are no bushings on the shift linkage in the transmission.  I 
would check the two pinch bolts on the linkage on the transmission, the 
adjustment bolt in the same location (centers the gear lever), and the 
pin on the shift lever itself.  The latter is a wear item for many 
cars, and if worn you simply replace the whole lever.
