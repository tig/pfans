Your mechanic mistimed the balance shafts when s/he installed the belt.  
He may think he timed them correctly, but if he installed the sprockets 
upside down by mistake.. vibration.

The vibration will be worse if your motor mounts are worn as well.  Have 
the mechanic check this as well.
