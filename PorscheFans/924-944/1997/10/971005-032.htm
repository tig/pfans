Anthony, a while back I had to replace the joints which are located between
the end of the rack (on each side) and the tie rod.  I forget what the
proper name for them is.  They are basically spherical joints that allow
the tie rod to pivot where it meets the end of the rack (inside the boot). 
Mine had become sloppy and it was noticeable at the steering wheel.  And,
it was much cheaper and easier than installing a new rack.
