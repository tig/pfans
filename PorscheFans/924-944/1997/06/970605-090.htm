I recently read some comments on the list regarding Mobil 1 synthetic gear
oil; something to the effect that it did not meet GL-5 and was only for
hypoid gears etc....  This is what it says on the bottle:  Exceeds API
GL-5, GL-4 and GL-3, provides maximum protection for hypoid differentials,
manual gear boxes and all other automotive gears.  And it is fully
compatible with conventional gear oil.  I have used this oil over the last
110K miles (including about 25 hrs track time) and the car (N/A 944) now
has 160K total with only a balky 1-2 synchro.  Nor are there any leaks,
etc. from the transaxle.  No doubt there are other good synthetics but it
would seem the Mobil 1 'warning' was a bit off the mark.
