Eric, you symptom sounds like a classic case of the balance shaft being
out of time.  Has it been replaced or reset lately??  It can be easily
checked.  Even if the timing notches are in the correct position, I have
seen numerous cases where the gear was installed improperly on the
woodruff key, making the balance off even if the notches are positioned
correctly.  Usually this is the lower balance shaft.  It causes a
resonance/vibration at about 3,000 rpm and will go away around 3500-4000
rpm (depends on how far out the timing is).
