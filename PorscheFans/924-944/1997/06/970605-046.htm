What you have sounds like blown or missing oil bypass plugs.

Wow that sounds real bad, it is and easy to check and repair. The 944 head 
is shared with the 928 head (same basic part) there are two (2) oil galley 
openings in the head for the 928, when the head is put on a 944 the galley 
leads just back to the sump (slowly). When the head is installed the two 
(2) holes need to be plugged, you can do this by just removing the vale 
cover and they can be seen at the read of the head (not the front of the 
car the rear). If they are out you get the effect you talk about. Sometimes 
they just get blown out and can be found floating around the head.

Easy to check --- Easy to fix - Disaster if not repaired
