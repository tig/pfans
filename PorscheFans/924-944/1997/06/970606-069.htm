Steve,
I've been using my '83 944 for a daily driver since 1985.  It's only 
left me stranded one time in all those years.  Parts are expensive (but 
cheaper than they used to be) and labor is very expensive.  A friend who 
owns a 911 told that Porsche repairs come in multiples of $500 and 
usually start at $1000.  He's not far from worng.  I typically spend 
about $2000 a year for repairs.  

The car is showing it's age with stone chips and worn interior, but it 
still looks good for a 14 year old car.  And it's still great fun to 
drive.
