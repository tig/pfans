	A few people have asked me to tell them what I am finding in regards to
full-throttle engine power loss.  To reprise the problem;  At full throttle
(full load) the power output drops sharply after a few seconds, so that
if you hold your foot on the floor, the engine rpm begins to drop off
rapidly, almost as if the car has gone to idle power.  If you hold your
foot down the power will surge occasionally as the rpms and speed continue
to fall.
Since the boost guage shows no marked surging or drops in pressure
I am almost certain this is not a waste gate or turbo problem.  The car is
in the shop now for an estimate.
For the record, I called my local porsche dealer on pricing
of turbo parts.
Wastegate = $2,000
Turbo = $4,000
