Guess I've been skipping some of these posts.  Anyway, the speedometer in pre
85.5 cars is cable driven off the front left tire (driver side).  If you pull
the wheel off, you should see a square metal piece protruding from the wheel
bearing cap.  It should have a c-clip holding it there.  If the cable end isn't
protruding out and you just have a square hole;  remove the cap, fish the cable
out and put it so it sticks out of the hole in the bearing cap, get a c-clip
and clip it on.

Good luck
