Rick, I'm not in my office, so I can't look this up to be sure, but your
fan running is probably a stuck a/c cooling fan relay.  Look in your
fuse box for such a relay and disconnect it and see if the fan stops. 
If it does, the contacts are probably welded shut.  Replace the relay,
but check  the wires going to the fan and see if there is some corrosion
on them or if they look as if they have been hot, causing too much
resistance at the relay.
