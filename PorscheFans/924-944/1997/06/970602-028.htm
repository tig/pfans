You're right the jack goes wher the jack stands do, but theat isn;t the only
point jou can jack or support the car from. In front, place the jack stands
under the rear A-arm at the point it attaches to the body. In back, you can
put the jack stands under the large round torsion bar cap just forward of
the rear wheel on either side.

Safety, I try to support the car only on two jack stands and two wheels,
never on four jack stands, and I leave the jack up and engaged for good measure.
