I drove down to Atlanta for COMDEX in our AudiV8Quattro, since I was
forced to sell the 944turbo last week (someone offered me WAY too much money

can get to a 4-door Porsche.  Then again, I'm not exactly impartial!

Guess that's why I drove 800 miles each way even though I could have flown
at UD expense.
