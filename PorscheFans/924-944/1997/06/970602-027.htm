If your car has been lowered, the suspension geometry changes just enough to
wear them rapidly. The fix is to rebuild them, or to replace them with
racing parts made by Fabcar. To get the name of the rebuilder, call Tom
Charlesworth, Porsche Club of America's 944 tech guru, in Tulsa, Oklahoma at
(918) 664-8714.

RE: the hatch. Try moving the whole hatch & frame gently from side to side
while you depress it slightly. Sometimes this frees the slide latch & lets
it move back. Also, the release cable for one side or the other may be
broken, letting one side release but not the other.
