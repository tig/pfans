Front to rear endplay on the crankshaft should be .010 inch (that's 
ten one thousandths of an inch) not half an inch.

Tom Charlesworth has a broken crankshaft that he is fond of showing
around at tech sessions from a woman who continued to drive with
a broken rubber center clutch. Seems that the stresses of banging
the stops finally broke the crank...

If you have half an inch of play, your engine is going down for the
count, just a matter of days or weeks...
