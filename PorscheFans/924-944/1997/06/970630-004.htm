You might have the typical loose brake pad rattle. Here's the test.
Drive over a section of road that causes the rattle. Now drive
over it again, using your left foot to apply light pressure on
the brake pedal. If you don't hear the noise when your foot is on
the pedal, your rear pads are a little undersized, and are rattling
in the brake calipers. You can make some shims from an empty soda
can to solve the problem.

I first heard of this problem and solution from Jerry Weger.
