> Has anybody steamed/pressure cleaned there engine?  What kind of
> results did you get?  Is there another way of getting the engine to that
> brand new (or close to it) look?  
> 
Drive the car until the engine is warm, not hot. Spray everything in sight
with Simple Green, taking care near electrical connections, the fuse box
and the accessories (A/C compressor, alternator, etc.). Let soak for 15
minutes or so. Rinse with a high-pressure stream from your hose, taking the
same care with the electical bits and the accessories. Repeat as necessary.

If you have one of those zillion PSI pressure washers you can substitute
that for the garden hose, but the normal hose will produce a good improvement.
