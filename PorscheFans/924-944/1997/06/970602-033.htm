Someone (sorry I deleted it) recently posted a note saying they had just
switched to Mobil 1 Synthetic Gear Lubricant in a 75W90 weight.  From =
what
I just read on the Mobil web site, this fluid is *not* recommended for =
any
performance application specifying a GL-4 lubricant.  Since GL-4 is
specified for 944s, I would say this Mobil 1 gear lube is the wrong =
choice
and may cause damage.  Mobil 1 is really a GL-5 lubricant designed mainly
for hypoid gear sets and differentials.  It contains sulfur.

If you want the low temp advantages of a synthetic fluid I would =
recommend
Redline MTL or MT-90 as they are both GL-4 specification.
