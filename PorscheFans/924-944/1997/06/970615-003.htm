Hi Joern,

In the book "Porsche 944" by Julian MacNamara, the highlighted car is an
'84 944 LUX. Apparently it is not a myth! 

Two of the features he mentioned that were not a part of my '84 944 were
headlight washers and "long range driving lights recessed into the front
bumpers which also serve as flashers in daylight conditions when the
main lights are retracted." The latter may have been a standard feature
on Euro 944s, but I don't think headlight washers were offered in the US
in '84. I've also never seen a 944 in the US that is the same color. It
is somewhere between bronze and silver, almost linen metallic.
