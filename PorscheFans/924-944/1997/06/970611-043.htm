I am not sure of all the differences and some of the ones that I am
listing may in fact be part of the M030 option.  The principle behind
the Club Sport was to provide the customer with a car that they could
easily prepare to Race, AutoX etc.  The CS was available with a number
of options all of which is designed to save weight or make the car more
'race ready'.  The standard fitment / options that I know of (for cars
delivered in Australia) were;

*	No Back seat
*	Race Seats
*	Cloth trim 
*	Cross drilled / Ventilated brakes (928 GTS)
*	No air conditioning
*	No Radio
*	Mechanical window winders
*	Light weight wiring harness
*	Stiffer springs (I would expect that these are part of M030)
*	Ride height adjustable shocks.(spring seats)
