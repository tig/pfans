>Hello All,
>	I remember reading on t his list that there was some special tool required
to remove the radio unit on the 944.  Is this tool really necessary?  If
not, does anyone know the technique?  Thanks in advance.

There is a tool, if you're talking about the one needed to remove the
stereo's chassis from its retaining sleeve in your dash.  There are 4 pins
at the corners of your stereo's face, and all must be depressed for the
stereo to be released.  You can fashion your own tool for accomplishing this
using a coat hanger or by delicately manipulating 4 finishing nails.  Hope
this helps...
