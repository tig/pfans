IMHO the best tires on the market today are Bridgestone S0-2s. You WILL
notice the difference. Excellent in the wet, I just drove to work in
pouring rain with standing water on the road. Absolutely no sign of
aquaplaning. My car is a 951s with 225 & 245. You will have to be doing
something very illegal or stupid ( believe me I done both ) to achieve
anything other than more grip and a tightening line in the dry. I have
put ~7000 miles on mine since March with no adverse wear problems. I see
no reason why these tires wont last 25-30K miles. Very cool tread
pattern too.

Richard.
