Riff,
      When I bought my '86 944, the RF amp was disconnected. On my car, the 
power lead to the RF amp is a black wire which runs along the antenna 
connection. At the radio end, it should be connected to the switched output 
from the radio so that it received 12 volts when the radio is turned on. The 
actual RF amplifier is located above the passenger footwell on the right hand 
side. I noticed significant improvements in reception with the booster turned 
on, but it probably isn't as good as a WIP.
Good Luck!
Ezra
