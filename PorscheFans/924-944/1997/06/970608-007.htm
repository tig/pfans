There are quite a few widebody kits.

They seem to come in three flavors:

DP/Lemans style:  One peice nose with rounded blunt shape, box fenders

"Holbert" DP style - Looks like a 924 on steroids, large boxed fenders
with open backs, squared off rear vents (like 935)  Similar to the old IMSA Monza
Separate fenders and nose peice.  These were derived formt he original Holbert Motorsports moulds.

"Widebody" - this looks like a 944 or 944t with lareger fender bulges.  3" wider per side
or so.

are GT Racing, AIR, and IFC.
