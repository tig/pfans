I have seen various threads on power lock acting strange or not working at
all.
In my case they often recycle up and down several times and may or may not
open/close both doors in sequence.

Tonight I took off the door panel to look for broken plastic rods and the
other oft quoted causes and guess what I found...

The window rear vertical channel guide is fasted to the top of the door
(near the lock button) by a spot weld. Well mine has pulled out and the
channel is loose and I believe is cycling the micro switch!

I'll get this welded back (after disconnecting the DME) and report back if
it helps.
