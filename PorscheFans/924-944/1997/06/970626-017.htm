Personally, I have no problem with images. However, considering 
how fragile the PorscheFans servers appears to be, I don't
think sending images around is a good idea. For those of you

bandwith (space) on the system, and put a heavy load on the

it will ask you for it, and youcan then mail it directly. Better yet,
find some web page and put it there.
