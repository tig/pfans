Yes, but it won't do it by itself!

Turn your ignition key to the first position (detent) where nothing
happens.  Now push the sunroof button to "raise" the roof.  The rear arms
will actually retract into the roof.  Flip open the two levers up front.
Push up on the back of the sunroof and you can lift it up and back.  There
is a toung that slips into the front of the sunroof that holds it in place,
so you need to slide it backward by a few inches.  The first time will be a
little awkward, so get a helper to give you a hand.  I guess you should do
this from out side the car with the hatch open.

I am so used to doing it that I can do it from the inside of the car
without leaving my seat, but I always have the rear seats down so it is an
easy stow behind my head.  Just be careful not to scratch it.
