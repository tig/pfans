You can use just about any empty 1/2 or 1 gallon plastic jug (milk, washer
fluid, etc) in the same way to refill the coolant or washer reservoirs, and
you have a built-in handle.

How about using an old 35mm plastic film cannister as a postage stamp roll
holder.  Remove the top, cut a slit about 1/8" wide completely down one
side, insert a roll of postage stamps with one stamp sticking out, replace
the top, and "voila".  Just don't attempt to submit this idea to "Hints
from Helouise", as I already got that $5 check many years ago.
