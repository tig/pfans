As a further note on the subject of windscreens for the 944/968 cabs, 
the Porsche part # is 00004320250 (it is the same part for both the 944 
and 968).  They retail for about $435 and Sonnen Porsche sells them for 
$385.  However, you can get them from any Porsche dealer; however, I 
don't believe that most dealers are familiar with this item since there 
is not too much of a call for them.
I recently installed on on my 90 944S2 cab and it did make a great 
difference in wind noise and wind buffeting.  A much more enjoyable ride 
at freeway speeds.
