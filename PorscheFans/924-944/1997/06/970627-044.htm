Hey Lee,

Point well taken about over extending the hood and cracking the
windshield.  But I would also like to warn Peter about compressing the
strut against the inside of the fender with the hood side of the shock
attached.  Be careful not to let the strut slip as you try to lift on
the hood, while keeping the strut compressed and then attaching the
lower clip to the ball joint.  It may get away from you and scratch the
hell out of the top of your fender.  Also an expensive repair.  That
nearly happened to me, hence while I opted for my installation method.

I never experienced my hood getting near my windshield or the wiper arms
when fully extended on my '88 or '83.  Maybe I was lucky.  If your hood
does touch the windshield or wiper arms, I would have the hinges checked
to see why they are hyper-extending.

No disrespect intended, just another thing to be careful of when
changing hood shocks.
