Amazing...

Out of the top ten teams in Le Mans this year, 7 were Porsche teams.
The others were MacLaren (2) and Ferrari (1). 

Swedes were participants both in the first and second teams, for TWR
Porsche ex F1 driver Stefan Johansson and for MacLaren the good, but
largely unknown long distance specialist Anders Olofson. The Dane Tom
Kristiansson set the fastest lap.

Just in case someone was interested...
