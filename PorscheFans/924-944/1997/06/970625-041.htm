Ramzi --

I have a colleague in PCA w/ a 944S who likes his B&B muffler. Nice sound
and a gain of 8-9 hp is claimed. It's expensive relative to Bursch, etc. 
B&B has it listed at $1050, but Windward Performance sells it for around
$600-650 --  about the same price as the Porsche muffler. 

I once put a Bursch on a late '85 944. Fit was pretty good, but expect to
have to shop around for a good-fitting gasket to go between the cat pipe
and the cat-back.  If I remember correctly, the Bursch cat-back was one
piece.
