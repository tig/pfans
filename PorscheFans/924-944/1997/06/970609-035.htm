What kind of radio do you have?  I have found that many mid level to cheap 
aftermarket stereos have terrible tuner sections, sorry to say.  Make 
sure the antenna booster that is installed in your car is getting voltage 
from a switched lead on your unit.

Some stereos are much better at resisting multipath than others - I tried 3
different units before I found one that was satisfactory (Blaupunkt Ventura).

The stock Blaupunkt Reno found in many of these cars has an excellent 
tuner section as well. . better than the Ventura.  I only replaced mine 
as I wanted to play CD's.
