PCA CLUB RACE/ /DRIVER'S ED
LAS VEGAS MOTOR SPEEDWAY
SEPTEMBER 6 & 7, 1997

Mark your calendars now for the inaugural PCA Club Race & Drivers
Education at the new 2.5 mile F.I.A. road course at Las Vegas
Motor Speedway.  The road course uses the infield and banked oval
and offers speed, safety, good passing opportunities, and a
dynamite facility.  Fifth gear is the norm on the oval.  All the
pits are paved, and 60 enclosed garages are avialable to rent.

Accomodations will be available near the track as well as
downtown at the Imperial Palace for the night action.  We will
offer a  Drivers' Education group for up to 50 cars
for non racers who want a crack at this great track.

Applications have been mailed to licensed PCA Club racers.  
See our ad on page 61 on the June Panorama.  If you need 
more information or an application, visit the web page at:
