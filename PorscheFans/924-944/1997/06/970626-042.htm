Well it had to happen sooner or later....

Going to work today in my 83 944 and I stop to fillup with gas, start out
of the station and the horn goes off.  I tought that was strange and kept going,
turn again and the horn goes off several time, honk honk honk..  So people start
to look at me and Iam wondering what to do, so I pull over.  So I found that if
I turn the wheel to a certain spot the horn goes off, so I guess something is 
shorting the horn.  I even removed the horn pad and could not see a problem.

So I pulled the horn relay and headed off to work.   Looks like I get to remove
a steering wheel tonight....
