Hey guys,

Just for grins, check your cruise control actuator (passenger side ahead of
the battery . . . . there is a throttle-type cable going to it).  Mine
started clicking and it nearly drove me crazy until I found out what it was.
 It only clicked sometimes, at very low speeds during acceleration (ie. 5-8
mph), and only when I was going forward.  Finally, I found that it would also
click while in neutral with the clutch out and slightly elevated engine
speed.  I was able to observe the actuator with the hood open (while sitting
in the driver's seat) by looking under the trailing edge of the hood . . . .
.. that little actuator was clicking like crazy!  Since I rarely use my cruise
control (it's not too reliable as others have testified to) it doesn't bother
me.  Whenever it starts clicking I can make it stop immediately by either
switching the cruise control to "cancel" or "off" positions . . . . . it
stops every time.  I know this isn't the desired "fix" to the problem . . . .
.. I'd like to have someone answer that question.
