Hi Kevin,

You speedometer doesn't work because of the cable that goes to the LF
wheel. There is a square hole in the grease cap where it connects.
Sometimes, the c-clip comes off and it slips into the hole. Replacing
the cable is tedious if you haven't done so a few times before. My
mechanic charged me about $40 to do it including the new cable.
