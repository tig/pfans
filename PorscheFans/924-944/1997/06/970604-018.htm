If your deck is a stock blau, you neet 2 "keys" they look a wire "U" one
end goes into the top, the other the bottom. One key for left, one for
right. what it does is it spreads retaining clips in the din chassis,
one they are spread, you can pull the deck out.

You can use 2 large size paper clips made into a "U" slide them in place
and, pull your deck out ( you can't pull it with the paper clips, you'll
pull em out.)

Or you can go to a stereo shop that sells blau radios and talk an
installer loaning or giving you a set of keys (a set of keys comes with
every "din" blau radio, so they will probably give you for free)
