To remove old intake gaskets...razor blade off all you can of the old
gasket (put rags into the intake first).  Go to a tool company, hardware
store, etc. and buy a rotary bit that accepts a scotchloc abrasive pad
(like scotch brite).  These come in various configurations.  3M is a
brand name that sells these.  Put this bit in a slow or variable speed
drill (we use die grinders) and scour away, just like cleaning your pots
and pans in the kitchen.  Does'nt affect the aluminum.  Blow off the
residue, clean the mounting surface with brakllen or solvent...you are
ready to install the new gaskets.  Do not use sealant on the new
gaskets.
