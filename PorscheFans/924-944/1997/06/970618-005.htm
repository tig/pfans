I had this happen to my 89 951 too. I left it a little longer than a
should have and returned one day to find no tip or pipe back too the
muffler. I had it fixed by a exhaust shop who welded a new section of
pipe on and replaced the tip. In regards to after market mufflers I
spoke with my mechanic at length on Friday. He said unless you are
flowing more exhaust gases , ie bigger turbo and/or stroker kit, dont
bother because the distance between the exhaust ports and the turbo cant
be reduced like say a 930 can. I personally would rather have a
stainless exhaust. It is expensive, but you never have to worry about
your exhaust again.......ever.
