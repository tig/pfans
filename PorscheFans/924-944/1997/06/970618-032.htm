> Which is most reliable a plain 944, a 944 Turbo, or a 944S?
> 

	Michael, *generally*, in order of reliablility they are:

	1. 944
	2. 944S
	3. 951

	That said, there are some variances due to years. For instance,
	I would think a 1989 951 could easily be more reliable than a
	1984 944. Basically, though, the Turbo and the S model have more
	expensive parts than the standard 944. Probably the smartest thing
	to do is not to compare the types of cars overall, but to compare
	specific cars that you are looking at (I assume you're meaning to
	buy one of these) and then make an educated guess based on what
	your mechanic says about which specific car would be most reliable
	for you. Good Luck!
