Could be that your shocks have gone, or that your wheels are 
still unbalanced. Since the latter is the cheaper then check 
this first.

You could try swapping the front and rear sets - however I'm not 
sure about US spec cars and regs. In the UK this would be 
illegal as if you have different tyre widths the wider ones must 
be at the rear. If this cures the shimmying then try and find 
somewhere who'll balance the wheels on a rolling road. The 
advantage of this is that the "car" is balanced rather than the 
wheels, the disadvantage is that if you ever need to rotate the 
wheels then it'll probably go out of balance!
