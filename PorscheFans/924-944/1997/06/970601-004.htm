1. is there a correct amount of slack that the accelerator cable is
   supposed to have at idle?

Nothing specific, but there shouldn't be too much play in the cable 
between the point where it leave the sheath and wraps around the 
throttle cam.  Maybe 1 - 3 mm slack, if such is measurable.  Be careful 
to avoid too little slack or preload, because you can then damage the 
throttle at wide-open.

2. is the throttle arm the only adjustment to make in this regard

Where the cable leaves the bowden sheath, I believe your car has a 
threaded adjustment which can be used to control cable slack.
