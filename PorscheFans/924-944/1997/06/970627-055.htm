The Haynes manual does not, IMHO, stress the importance of making the
hub meticously clean before rebolting the rotor to the hub. This is most
important, as any left debris may (will, according to Murphy...) cause
the rotor to seat unsquared, and give symptoms very similar to a warped
drum. Also, it give no information about replacing bolts and nuts, wich
is good practice.
