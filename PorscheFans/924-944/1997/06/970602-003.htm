Kevin,
 My 944 had the same failure at 23k. The dealer questioned the symptoms and 
ended up replacing the sensors one by one looking for it.  After 2 weeks in 
the shop and everything but the Box, the zone rep ok'ed replacement and 
that was it.

I run and rebuild 914's and to quote MR. 914 at Auto Atlanta, the brain 
rarely fails, but quite a number of times we swap a box and the  problems 
go away.

The 944 "Brain" is an RCA 1800 based unit with over 24 I/O lines and a 
handful on ATD converters, each line requires an Isolated driver and things 
like that fail. Even an Optical Isolator can get tired, and head back to 
sleep.

After 10 - 12 years even solid state gets tired.
