Open the hatch and pull away the carpeting. You should see the lock
mechanism. If the two rods going from the center lock to the two pin locking
mechanisms that hold the hatch down are not moving, I guess you have to
change the mechanism. I guess you can see where the problem lies once you
take a look.

The springs on these things can be changed individually. Try AutoAtlanta,
Tweeks, Automotion or Performance Products..
