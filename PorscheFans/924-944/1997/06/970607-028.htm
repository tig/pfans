Porsche got new management in the early 90s to try to turn the company
around.  After about a year they decided to discontinue the front
engine, water cooled line in total and concentrate back on what brought
them their fame and fortune, rear engined, air cooled cars.  It was a
pure business decision that has brought Porsche back into the black, and
is now a very viable company.
