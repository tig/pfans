	I have this problem with my 87 944S.  I can fix it by reaching under the
dash to the right of the center console. There is a plastic housing there.
Grasp the housing and (gently) move it about.  Your fan wil come on and the
heat will stop blowing.  When you get real good you can do this while
driving. I achieved a more lengthy resolution by getting upside down with a
snake light under the dash and playing with all the electrical connections
(not recommended while driving).
