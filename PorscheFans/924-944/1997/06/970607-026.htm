Just got back from a Canadian government asset disposal sale (about once
every 2 months here). I picked up a used reader for $9 U.S.! Has high
(42X) and low magnifications, made by Bell & Howell and uses a 85Watt
halogen light source. They had other readers, mostly 50Watt. I saw one
with 150 Watt bulb but only had high magnification. My 85 Watter seems a
little dull when reading in daylight, but I don't have a real microfishe
yet to test.

Now I can order the 944 factory manual fische for around $18? Who sells
these for the least price? Can't wait to get a hold of them! Sure beats
spending $200-300 used for the hardcopy manual, even though they do have
superior photos.
