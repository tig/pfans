Hi again Rick,

        I have Metal Masters on my 951 now and they are the best street pads
I've ever used.  Better than factory pads, less dust, no squeal, and won't
hurt the rotors much more than stock pads will.  But make sure you do only
use them on the street.  They are semi-metallics, and I've been told that
they will melt when used on the track.
        Also, Metal Masters aren't made by Repco anymore.  Axxis now makes
Repco's old "Lucas Girling Metal Max" pads under the "Metal Master" name.
