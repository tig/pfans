Any of you guys out there who drink the sports drinks will find
that the top half of a quart-size Gatoraid or Poweraid bottle makes
an excellent funnel for the oil fill of a 944. It fits in the opening
without requiring a hand to hold it upright and the large diameter
spout allows the oil to be poured in as fast as it will go without
overfilling the funnel.
