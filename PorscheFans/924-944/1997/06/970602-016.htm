Michael - I have always found it easiest to jack up the car using the
jackpoint (which is also the center of gravity point) under the centerline
of the doors.  This raises the whole side (either left or right) of the car
- two wheels at a time.  Then it is easy enough to put jackstands under the
designated lifting points just behind the front wheels and just ahead of
the rear wheels.  I would presume that the 968 would have basically the
same jack/lift points as the 951.  Have you consulted your owner's manual?
