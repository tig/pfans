Jim,
    Other items you may consider replacing are:
 1) Water Pump 
    If you know the age and mileage on the current water pump, it is an 
    easy decision to leave it in, or replace it. 
    (See the FAQ: http://www.connactivity.com/~kgross/944faq.html )

 2) Rollers  
    You really can't diagnose these without some disassembly unless they 
    are really really shot. If there are a lot of miles on the vehicle,
    and you plan to keep it for a significant amount if time, you should
    probably just replace them. 
  
 3) Cam, Balance,and Crank Shaft seals
    If you can see that oil is seeping out from behind the cam and balance
    shaft housing, replace these. My Crank Shaft seal was leaking enough to
    see, so I replaced them all this spring.

Good Luck!
