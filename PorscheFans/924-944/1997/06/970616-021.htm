Everyone,

I am proud to say that my '84 944 is just about to turn 167,000 miles!

In the 3 years that I have owned it (no records from the PO) I have 
replaced the clutch assembly at 97,000, 2 sets of timing/balance shaft 
belts (1 includes a new head gasket, head resurfacing, and 4 new valves 
from the timing belt breaking), a set of front rotors, and 2 sets of 
pads.  I change the oil (converted to Mobil 1 6K miles ago) and he is 
running flawlessly with the exception of a contrary injector on the 
second cylinder.  It feels just as fast (a timed 137 MPH) as the day I 
bought it.  I have put a set of D40M2's in 225/50-15 on it, and I love 
the handling that has been created.  He also wears KYB's on all fours.
