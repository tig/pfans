>I just purchased my first Porsche!  It's a 1989 944 Turbo S in mint
>condition.
>
>I have a few questions for an experienced 944 Turbo S owner:
>
>1)  There is an intermittent rattle coming from the rear.  Its sounds
>like "clack clack" like something is hitting the back window.  I have
>checked everything I can think of inside the car including the jack.
>Any ideas?  Maybe something underneath?

Two things it could be: (1) the hatch glass tends to come un-glued and makes
an ungodly racket. They can be reglued by a competent glass shop. The hatch
must be removed to do it; (2) the rear brake pads will rattle if the pads
are aftermarket pads, especially Metal Masters. Test this by driving down a
bumpy road. If the rattling stops when you gently apply the brakes, its the
pads.

>2)  I notice that when I brake and going less than say 5 miles per hour,
>my steering wheel wants to turn right or left about 1/2 turn.  The
>brakes also squeak once in a while at slow speeds while braking.  The
>dealer said they were in good shape.  Any ideas? 
>
>3)  I was considering getting a Weltmeister chip to increase the
>horsepower.  Will this decrease the life span of the engine
>dramatically?  And how much difference can I expect?

Significant performance gains. I put a chip in my car at about 35,000 miles.
It now has 106,000 miles and the chip hasn't caused any problems yet.
