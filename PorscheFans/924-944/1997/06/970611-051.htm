Sounds like a ground problem to me.  The door switch works by providing 
a ground connection for the light when the door is open.(there is always 
a hot connection to the light)  I am guessing that somewhere in your 
ignition system there is a bad ground and when you open the door it is 
providing a connection. Electricity always follows the path of least 
resistance. Just a theory but it might be worth checking out.
