Sounds like a bad oxygen sensor. When you say "replace," do you mean with a
new oxygen sensor, or only that you reconnected the old one? If the latter,
you need to buy a new one.

The sensor definitely has an influence on your mixture at part-throttle and
idle, so it could be the cause of your surging problem. At WOT the mixture
goes full-rich anyway, and any input from the O2 sensor is ignored.
