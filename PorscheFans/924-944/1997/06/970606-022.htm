Here's my $0.01:

I use a gunk-engine-brite folowed by a shot from our power washer.

I use the "fan" pattern which results in only a few hundred PSI.

I'm fairly certain this will not harm the hoses, and definately not aluminum.

This gets of the major grime, and does not seem to cause harm, as the
pressure wash only cleans what you point it at.  We use luke-warm water
from the tap.

If you do this, watch out for decals/painted surfaces.  It'll peel a decal
if directed at it.

Just my own experience.
