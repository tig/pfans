I just got back from the first thing that never cost me and involved my
car. The local Porsche dealer MCL (Vancouver, Canada) had a free service
clinic sponsored by Porsche. It was great. My car was on the hoist for
about 30 minutes and I was able to go over the whole car with a mechanic
asking him questions. The mechanic knew a lot about 944's as he used to
race them. I learned more in that 1/2 hour than the last 6 months. He even
fixed a couple of small problems like a heat shield on backwards etc. for
free. It's obviously a fishing trip for my business but it worked. I'll
give them a try next time I get something done. The service manager did
seem a little disappointed that the only problem was a small oil leak I
already knew about. I heard him give one guy an estimate for several
thousand for the problems they found on his 911.

Kerry Brown
