Actually the information about the S2 I gained from 911 and Porsche
World. It was an old edition featuring the new 968's and a chance to win
one for a week. The 944 S2 in question was a SE model as it had SE
decals about 12 inches high along the sides. It also featured a
non-airbag three spoke steering wheel from a 930 S. It was sold at
considerable discount to make way for the new model. The 944 turbo SE
was a UK designation for 89 and 90 turbos not as you say a turbo S. The
turbo S was just that. 70 were imported to the UK in 88 and most were
silver-rose with studio check interior. The 924-968 club runs
independent of that magazine.
