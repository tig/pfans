Hi Bill
I have been using the Mityvac for some time now and I completely agree with
Marv that as a vacuum tool for brakebleeding it is not the way to go.
However,
If you use the pressure side of the Mityvac in combination with a spare
reservoir cap
it works perfect. You cannot overpressurize the reservoir and pressurising
the reservoir allows bleeding of two wheels. Then refill and pressurize
again
for the next two wheels or the clutch cylinder.
