Just picked up a plastic DIY assembly model 1:24 scale. Made in Italy by
Italeri, #653. Retail price was approx. $18 US, got it on sale less 25%.
It's fairly detailed, with undercarriage and engine components. Body is
already injection molded red. Phone dial chrome wheels. Accurate repro of
a euro model with side marker signal and full black rubber strip on rear
bumper.

The shop clerk said this manufacturer produced models better than US
companies. He also said there is available a 951 made in Japan by
Hasegawa, $6 more. I don't know the number.

Bought it at Wilkinson's Automobilia in Vancouver, Canada. They do
mailorder. Phone #604-873-6242, fax 604-873-6259. Tell 'em I sent you,
I was the guy in their for 6 hours this week going over all their books
and also got a nifty Porsche factory brochure of colors and trim for all
1991 Porsches, showing all possible exterior color and interior color
(dash and seats and carpet) combos.
