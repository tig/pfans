	Karen, why are you considering only those three pads? You have
	a huge variety available though mail order. Some other pads you
	might consider especially if you're doing track-type stuff are
	the Pagid Oranges, the Performance Friction pads, or the Cool
	Carbons. Bear in mind that track pads leave lots of dust and
	squeal a whole lot. You want to make sure that you find pads
	that are as rotor friendly as possible. Probably the best thing
	to do is try several of these pads out and find the ones that best
	fit you personally. Good luck!
