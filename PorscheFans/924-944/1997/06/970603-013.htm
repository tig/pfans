This sounds exactly like a throttle jack for the AC:

A sensor determines that the AC Compressor needs to go on, and turns on
the compressor clutch (the click). The additional load drags down the
engine until the throttle jack is engaged, at which point it does what
its supposed to by kicking up the throttle jack and your idle zooms to
1500.
