To my best information, the cylinders are originally Nikasil treated -
but, then, my information can be wrong. 

All aluminium cylinders have to be treated in some way for longevity,
and similar treatments to Nikasil exist. Biral comes to mind, also the
Nigusil (Italian) variety. Ferrari and BMW use the Nikasil process for
their aluminium cylinders - so Porsche may be in good company here. 

(And for those thinking 'BMW - aluminium cylinders???', think air, speed
and two wheels...)
