Our PCA region (Suncoast Florida) requires that the brakes be bled within 2
weeks of a drivers ed event. I'm pretty hard on brakes and even though I
use Super Blue brake fluid, the fluid gets hot, moisture accumulates in the
system, and by the next event, there are bubbles and moisture in the lines.
Once it gets hot, the moisture evaporates and you get MANY bubbles. Many
bubbles equals spongy pedal and loss of brakes. Too much risk and bleeding
the brakes is easy anyway.
