One trick used here in New Zealand is to use 3M reflective tape to make new
letters and numbers for the number plate. We have black letters on white
plates, with the white being reflective. In daylight the 3M tape (also used
to make road signs) looks black, but has the same reflectance as the white
on the plate.

I don't know if it works or not because ever since I've done my plates I
haven't been zapped! Murphy's Law :-)
