Chipping does help. The aftermarket chips delay the opening of the wastegate
so that more boost builds up before the wastegate releases it. Some of the
chip kits include shims for the wastegate which in effect increases the
pressure required to open it. None of them increases the output of the
turbo, just how long it stays on full boost before the wastegate opens.

There is a very noticeable, seat of the pants difference in a chipped and
non-chipped 951.
