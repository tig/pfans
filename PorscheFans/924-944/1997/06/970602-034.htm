I was getting some work done on my car today so I had them change the gear
oil. They used GM gear oil with a special GM additive for GM positrac rear
ends. I can't believe what a difference this made. Cold shifts are better
than it used to be fully warmed up. Once it's warm it's really slick. They
told me several other 944's and 911's are running this oil and additive for
autocrossing. It helps when shifting from 2nd to 1st.
