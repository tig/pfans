Hmm.. Well. There are actually 944s with three sets of lights. From top to
bottom they are something like this:

   Flippable (? :) headlights

   Driving lights in bumper

   Fog or driving lights in front spoiler/valance


The driving lights are supposed to come on immediately when you turn the
key. That is, if the switch is in the uppermost position. I guess you know
how the headlights work.. :)

As for the fog lights, I think they are actually driving lights. A 944 guy I
talked to had these on his '83, and they were lit at the same time as the
driving lights in the bumper. They were just as bright as far as I recall,
so I think they are driving lights.
