Of all the plastic covers I have seen for number plates the only ones
I've seen that would work have been made from "perspex" approx 10mm
thick with 10mm wide channels cut through them vertically with 10mm
spacing between them. Dimensions approximate. This has the effect of
being able to be viewed when looked at directly but "blanking out" the
numbers when viewed on an angle. These are cheap to make and are
effective against speed cameras, although if you are pulled over you
might have a bit of explaining to do.
