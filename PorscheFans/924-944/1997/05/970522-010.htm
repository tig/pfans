Wait until you get that wheel/tire combo on the car.  You will feel the
weight difference immediately in acceration and braking especially.  They
say that 10lbs taken off at wheel speed is like taking 3x10lbs off of the
car's body in terms of acceleration so taking 40lbs off at wheel speed
is like taking 40x3 = 120lbs taken off the car's body in terms of acceleration.
If you've ever run with a low tank of gas, you'll know that 120lbs can make
quite a difference to response and all-out acceleration.
