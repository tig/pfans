I have Bridgestone S-O2s on my 89 951. Excellent everywhere, wet, dry
etc. Fairly stiff sidewall as it has a big fat rim protector. RE-71O has
stiffer sidewall and more conventional tread pattern. I'm told S-O2  is
better tire of the two. I was also told by many different tire shops,
who could have sold me a more expensive tire, that this was the best for
any money. Excellent above 100mph, superb at 150.

Richard.
