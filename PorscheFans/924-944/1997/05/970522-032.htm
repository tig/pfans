These are the areas that would affect boost:
1. Cycling valve is not bleeding enough pressure so wastegate is seeing more
   pressure and opening at 0.8 bar.
2. The compressor bypass valve is leaking.  Your turbo is creating the 1.0bar,
   but the CBV is allowing it to leak back into the non-pressurized side of
   the turbo.  You can easily check this.  The CBV is the black cylinder next
   to the coolant bottle.  Take off the vacuum hoses and the intake hoses and
   see if you can see through it or blow through it.  It should be shut and not
   allow any air through.  Powerhaus said they fail every 40k and often need
   replacement.  My aftermarket unit will be arriving soon.  We were supposed
   to use the Talon one I got, but we used that on the hillclimb race car so
   Buschur Racing is sending me another one to test out.  My car will sound like
   a 962 when shifting gears.
3. The exhaust header is cracked and allowing exhaust energy to escape.
4. The wastegate is cracked and not making an effective seal.  Does the car feel
   laggy, really laggy?
5. There is an intake leak somewhere, perhaps a loose pipe.  Any wooshing noises
   under boost?

There are more like running bad gas that limits boost from knocking, etc, etc.
