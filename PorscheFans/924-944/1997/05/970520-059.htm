OE is 7.5 x 17 offset of 65mm and 9.0 x 17 offset of 55mm. Porsche does
list 8 x 17 offset of 52mm and 9.5 x 17 offset of 68mm, the rears using
17mm spacers. I've only seen these wheels on a 911 cup car. Wheels went
for about $954. and $202. for the spacer, all prices each (don't forget
studs and nuts, they're extra, too).

Check the offset on the wheels you wish to fit. You can get 245/40/17 on
the front and 275/40/17 on the rear.

The above info is from a service bulletin from PCNA for the 968.
