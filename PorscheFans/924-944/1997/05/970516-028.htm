Plain-ole cyanoacrylic (sp?) epoxy works very well, too. The last time
mine fell off was just before a driver's ed event at Lime Rock and I
borrowed some glue from the Skip Barber mechanics working on the school
cars. I kept expecting it to fall off and ruin my weekend, but it stayed
secure ever since.

I also found that the hinge gets rather hard and any sort of adjustment
makes it come right off. I played with the joint for awhile and got it
to turn much more freely before reattaching.

All in all it came off with the standard windshield epoxy 4 times over a
10 year period with 2 of the occurances due to other drivers not
realizing how fragile it was.
