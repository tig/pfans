Give a close look at the battery cable terminals. My '85 recently started
exhibiting much the same symptoms. After getting it fixed on the road twice,
I had to get it towed. The mechanic found that the battery terminal had
corroded around its inner core, and would interrmittently lose contact with
the actual copper wire itself. Impossible to see, until you start to take it
apart.
