All of the fast 944S2 in SCCA autocrossing are M030 cars except Jeff's.
(In this results you provided, I believe Rex Tener was co-driving Jeff's
car. Rex is no slouch either, but he finished nearly 1 1/2 seconds
behind Jeff. Bill Buetow is also a very good driver who has been
competing in 944s for years. His is definitely an M030.)

Jeff is one spectacular 2nd-generation autocrosser. I competed against
him in San Francisco for 2 years and watched him for another 2 years in
another class. He is married to possibly the best woman driver in the
country: Stacy (Lynd) Reitmeier. If you are ever on the left coast when
there is ANY autocross in the SF Bay area, show up and introduce
yourselves. I assure you they will be there!

I would strongly advise getting the M030 if you possibly can. With it,
you can adjust the ride height to whatever you want; one of the FEW
stock cars you can do this legally. (Hint: Lower is better.) Or, you can
go back in time, convince your father-to-be to buy a Porsche repair shop
and be involved in the sport, and then grow up hanging out at events
while your parents competed. Or you could do both and REALLY dominate!

Remember: practice, practice, practice...every day!
