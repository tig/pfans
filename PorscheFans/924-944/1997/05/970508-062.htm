I read your post about your turn signal problem.  I had the same problem
when I had a  240Z.  Your signals are miswired.  You will need a wiring
diagram and a Volt/Ohm meter.  It sounds as though you have a ground where
it doesn't belong.  There is also a chance that you have a problem with
your flasher relay, but I doubt it.  The flasher power goes through the
turn signal, (it overrides the turn signal).  Check your wires and see how
the power and ground route through the signal.  Good Luck.
