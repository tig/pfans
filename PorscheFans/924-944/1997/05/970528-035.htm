Malcolm, are you actually using leaded fuel ? I thought that UK had
switched to the less harmful natrium based additives many years ago?

But I think that you can use un'leaded' fuel without any harm. I have
for 7 years, and don't have any trouble with that. Valve seats are high
quality hardened inserts in the aluminium head, and valve seat problems
usually occurs in cast iron heads with no inserts.   

I run mine on unleaded 95 RON and never hear any pinging, but you may
want to be wary for the first tankfuls. Do not fill the tank more than
half full, so you can top up quickly with higher octane rated fuel if
any pinging occurs.

There is a switch on the DME box to retard timing 3 % if necessary.
