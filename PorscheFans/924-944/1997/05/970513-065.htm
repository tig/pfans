Another possibility for the rear end rattle is the shock absorber.  Not the
mouting, but the shock absorber itself.  Mine started rattling to the point
they almost sounded like popcorn popping back there.  I think it's the shaft
bumping against the cylinder bore.  I replaced mine and that took care of it.

The next rear-end rattle I had was the luggage cover.  When rolled up, like a
windowshade, it would rattle over bumps.

Just a couple more things you can check.
