I can't tell from the above what you are trying to fix, but if your problem
is excessively high idle, try the following: On the top of intake manifold
where it meets with the pipe from the intercooler there is a recessed
screw. This screw tends to back out, and when it does, the idle goes nuts
(that's a technical term). Mine has done it so often that I finally filled
the recess with silicone. If the screw is missing, you will obviously have
to replace it, but that may solve your problem.
