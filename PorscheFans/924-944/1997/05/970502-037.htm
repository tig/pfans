Shafts can sometimes be moved in and out of their cv joints. Its called
a floating axle, and is a feature (supposed to take away pressure loads
- and it does, too), The way to test them (aside from giving them a
hell-thy run) is to check if they have any noticeable play when rotated.
A plummers vice can be used, and it is highly unlikely that you can make
any damage this way. 
Any noticeable play is a bad sign, especialy on the powerful 951

Halshafts can be turned end-for-end and regreased to prolong their life.
An excellent FAQ is available on  these matters.
