Yehan,

>My 77' 924 makes a clunk sound when ever I release the cluch.

I would suspect either broken springs in your clutch disk, or perhaps a bad 
CV joint.  Easy to check: jack the rear with the car in gear, remove the 
inspection plug in the rear transmission case (um, assuming a 924 has one 
of these, if it's an 016 transmission you should), reach inside, grab the 
input shaft and check for free play.  If you got free play, it's the clutch 
disk.  If not, check the CV's, check rear wheel bearings, loose brake pads, 
etc.
