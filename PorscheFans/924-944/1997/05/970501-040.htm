When mine was having idling problems it was because the idle-speed
stabilizer was bad.  When I replaced it, it idled fine.  Likewise, if you
have removed the rubber boot between the air-flow sensor and the throttle
body, your car will also idle like crap.  Double check that everything is
tight.  If the computer doesn't get the right information about what's
going into the engine, it won't idle.  Since you replaced the belts I'll
remind you to double-check that small rubber hose from the cam belt cover--
it's easy to forget.
