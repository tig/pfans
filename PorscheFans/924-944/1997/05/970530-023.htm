also required to do so in a polite and courteous fashion.  Unless of
course, you're being an arguementative wanker and/or the situation
dictates a different response.  Then you will tend to reap what you sow,
since most officers deal with brainless and aggressive morons day in and
day out.

However, I am very surprised that the officer, upon seeing that you were
obviously sober, just didn't wave you through the checkpoint.  In my
opinion there was no justifiable reason for him to give you the hard
time that he did.  Understandably he may have had a bad day, or was a
new rookie (rookies tend to be a little too gung ho when they're fresh

you received.  If he's going to give you a ticket, or just give a
