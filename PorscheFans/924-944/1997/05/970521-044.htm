Ben,

The fuel pressure regulator is easy to locate:  there should
be an opening with two fuel lines coming into the engine bay
from the passenger side.  These two lines are rather large
and hard to miss.  One line leads to the fuel dampener and
the other to the regulator.  The regulator is the one closest
to the firewall.  As far as checking the regulator for
proper operation, I don't have a clue.  

Hope this helps a little at least.
