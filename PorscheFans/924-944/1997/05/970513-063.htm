Last summer several of us suffered from broken A/C compressor mounting
bolts, all around the same time.  In my case, the compressor was hanging by
friction and the belt only.  Also check to see that you haven't broken your
adjuster bars (one for power steering, one for A/C).  Other than that, a
bad motor mount can cause quite a shaking.  Hope this helps.
