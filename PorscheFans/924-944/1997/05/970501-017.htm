For those thinking about replacing their hood insulation, you can also
buy uncut insulation from JC Whitney for $20 -> $30 depending on what
type you buy. I bought the 1/2" mylar faced type. I think 3/4" thick
would also work. To clean the old liner off, I placed a large piece of
cardboard over the engine, and used a plastic scraper. I cut new pieces
that were roughly 1->3" larger than each opening. For each opening, I
pealed the backing off, stuck it in the center of the opening, and
trimmed around the edges with a pair of scissors. If you cut the edges
about 1/4" large, you can stuff the edges in, and it looks nice. Took me
about 1/2 hour for both sides. I also find it to be an improvement in
appearance, noise, and no more rotting foam !@#$ falling on the engine.
