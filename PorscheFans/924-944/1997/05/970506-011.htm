You have stumbled, as so many others have, on the 944 dilema.

Where will it end?  With 280HP available from a 944 turbo just from a
chip swap, I would imagine you'll be satisfied.  If not, you can go
into la-la land and do the major-mod thing, but it's been my experience
that a doubling of horsepower at the wheels will keep you happy for a
LONG time.

I saw your subject and though "Again?"  Yup!

The best and least expensive way to get more power out of a 944 is to
upgrade to a 951.

You might, in the back of your mind, think "what about a 911"...I can
tell you that you'll spend alot of money before you get the level of
touring performace avalable in a chipped 944 turbo, and you will never
again get the same amount of room or conveneince.  Having had several
of each, I can say that with some degree of confidence.

Last november we took the 911 off the street (essentaially) and now my
daily driver is again a 951.
