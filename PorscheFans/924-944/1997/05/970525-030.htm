        My 87 944S has the same problem.  I have found that it originates in
the mass of wiring and other stuff behind the dash up the right side of the
passenger compartment but close to the middle.  If you experience hot air,
try reaching up underneath dash from the driver's seat (I can do this while
driving) and grasping the plastic housing (blower I assume) and gently
moving it. This has almost always got the cold air to flow for me.  A more
permanent fix was achieved some time ago, by reaching up under there from a
prone position (not while driving!!) and checking all connections.
