> I have a 1987 924S and am experiencing a problem with the headlights.  The
> high beams work, the low beam on passenger side does not work.  My husband
> replaced the fuse and now only the high beams work.  (With the blow fuse

> He has replaced the dimmer switch, light switch and the headlights and this

This is a pretty long shot, but:

I had a similar problem on my 85.5 944 (same mechanical layout). It
turned out to be *worn out cables* on the right(passenger) side, and it
affected the low beam only. It was caused by cables breaking when
bending over and over, opening and closing the lid. 

All kind of strandge things will happen if the ground path to a lamp is
destroyed, currents go relatively freely thru' 65 W headlamp filaments,
so blown fuses can be very misleading in such a case. Your description
looks very much of a 'false ground' problem.        

Look for a broken brown ground cable at or near the lamp.
