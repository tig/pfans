At 12:44 PM 5/21/97 -0400, you wrote:

>- At all times, there appears to be a fan running in the vent .... a very
>slight breeze is always coming out (even when stopped and yes the fan knob
>is on the 0 position).  Is there some way to turn this off completely
>(could be quite bothersome in the winter)? 

No, this fan will always be blowing some air.  If you don't want to feel it,
then use the sliding levers to direct more air to the windshield and floor,
or close the dash vents themselves with the vertically sliding lever between
the 2 center vents and the horizontal sliding levers below the vents nearest
the doors.

>- If I use the center console button to lock the doors, and I then turn 
>off the car, the center console won't unlock the doors (I have to turn it 
>back on). Is that normal?

Yes, this is normal.  Just remember to unlock before turning off the car, or
don't lock your doors.  At least you can raise your windows with the car of
as long as you don't open your door!
