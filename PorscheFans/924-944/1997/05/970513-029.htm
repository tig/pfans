The comments about our "speed limit" are essentially correct.    The
actual verbeage is "Reasonable and Prudent", which allows county
JPs and MHP officers an awful lot of leeway.    At night the speed limit
is
set at 55 on secondaries, and 65 on the freeways (which are now
PAVED, by the way!).
    While conditions might be
perfect, blowing by one of our senior citizens with a 90 mph
RELATIVE speed doesn't endear you to the friendly villagers
and can get you turned in via cell phone.    And if you are passing
on a secondary at such speeds, the guy coming the other way
can be going even faster.

It is awfully nice to cruise at 80 without looking over your shoulder
all the  time.
