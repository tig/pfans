The 944 "self-tensioner" does not,repeat, does not tension the belt
proporly. The belt tension on the belt is cridicle and is very precise. That
is why there is a tool for it. If any doubts, and you have access to the
tool, set the tension with the "self-tensioner" and check it with the gauge.
I have repaired many 944's that the "self-tensioner" was used and the belt
broke. Those of you in dissagree-ment, good luck to you.
