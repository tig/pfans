>Is an '87 Porsche 944 a reliable car or a good choice for a first car?
>
I have an 87 944 na with 95000 miles (bought it with 70k).  I've driven at
driver's ed classes at Mid Ohio, Nelson Ledges, and Watkins Glen and have
autocrossed it a lot for three years.  I'd say the car is pretty reliable
although things will break when you use it hard.  I've seen quite a few
944s with over 100k miles and most of them look pretty good and run as good
as they look.
By the way, my 944 is not my primary car so when it breaks (once for a
clutch and water pump on the same day) it is not so traumatic......I can
still get to work, get groceries, etc.
