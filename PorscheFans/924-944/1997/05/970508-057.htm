I have been using OZ Racing Fittipaldi in 16x7.5 and 16x9 on my 944 for
about 3-4 years. I have not had any problem. Even with 225/50-16 and
245/45-16 tires there still seems to be enough rubber to prevent the
wheels from getting bent by holes in the road. Of course, I don't use
those wheels in the winter and early spring (pot hole season)-I put the
original 15 inchers on.
