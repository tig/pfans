>I have a 1980.5 924 NA and have a glitch in my turn signals.  When the car
>is operating the turn signals work as they should.  When the car (ignition)
>is off the turn signals will come on and glow steady if the turn signal
>lever is in either the right or left turn position.  Start the car and the
>signal will begain to function normally again.

This is a European standard on all Porsches - a parking light for nighttime
use.  I believe it is required in Europe that any cars parked overnight on
public streets have to have a parking light displayed for safety.  Just
remember to return your turn signal stalk to the neutral ("off") position
before exiting the car.  As for the other questions, sorry, but I can't
help.
