We've covered this quite a bit in the archives, but I guess they aren't
very easy to search.  But anyhow, Porsche has changed the thermostat to a higher
opening temperature to help emmissions.  I went through this exact thing you
did until my shop changed it back to an old 80C thermostats they had in stock.
All the new ones are 83C.  Someone recently went through this too and I think
he had the same results when he changed back to the low temp one.  When I called
Porsche to inquire, their response was, if it doesn't go into the red, it's
fine, and I believe them.  I've stopped worrying about the gauge moving around.

Someone can correct me if I'm wrong, but aren't all "new" pumps from Porsche
now rebuilds?  My new pump which came in a Porsche box with all new looking
parts was still a Porsche rebuild.  Apparently (this is from my shop), there

went through three aftermarket rebuilds.  As I said before, correct me if I'm
wrong.

They look the same, but the stamping says 83 instead of 80, so the mechanic
