> BTW. I do have the newer, updated "accordian" (sp?) type of headers.
> If I was to do this myself, how hard is it? I assume you should always

> bolts? Any special tricks?

If you aren't pulling the head at the same time, you'll have to remove
the studs from the head to get the headers out.

Removing the studs is only problematic if you break one.

Yes, replace the gaskets and donuts and probably the flange bolts since
they'll probably break on removal or installation. Other than the studs
it is no big deal.

If you pull the head you can just remove the nuts and get enough working
room to slide the headers off the studs as the head comes up.
