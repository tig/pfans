Awhile back, I asked about the effects on acceleration using a lighter
wheel/tire combination.  Since then I have aquired a set of Hoosiers tires
to put on stock '89 Turbo Forged 16" wheels.  For the sake of science, I
compared the weight of the 16" forged wheels/Hoosier tire package with the
17" C2 Turbo replica wheel/BFG R1 tire package.  I was quite surprised to
find that the 16" wheel/tire combo was about 10 pounds lighter per wheel
than the 17" combo.  I am sure this is due partly to lighter wheels and
partly to lighter tires.  I will let you know if it makes any difference on
the track after Memorial Day.
