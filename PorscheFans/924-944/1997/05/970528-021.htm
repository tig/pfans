Reference the msg of several days ago on the difficulty of closing the 
968 hood after putting on a Colgan Bra; I have found that the problem is
with the front not hood portion of the bra. Be sure the flap of the
front part that sticks into the engine compartment is folded under so
that it doesn't end up over the hood latch. I have a hole in my flap and
a bent hood latch to prove this ! You still have to slam the hood harder
than without the bra to get the latch to ketch. Also, the hood portion
will wear the paint off the inside edge of the fenders about 16 inches
from the front edge of the hood. A piece of plastic tape takes care of
that. Needless to say, I am somewhat disappointed with 
Colgan bras.
