Unbelievably, (at least in Eugene, Oregon) most auto glass shops will reglue 
mirrors for no charge.  I had to do it two years ago on my ex VW GTI.  I 
called several places for estimates - all free.  They will use their 
industrial winshield glue and clean/prep the surfaces properly.  I noted 
during the fixing that the glue did not resemble super glue 
(cyanoacrylate) and was in some sort of proprietary dispenser.
