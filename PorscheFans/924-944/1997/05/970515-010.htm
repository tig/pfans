Autothority stage two chips use a banjo bolt to control boost.  If your car
has never been chipped this is all you need. But, If you are replacing chips
on a modified car you need to check to determine if the recycling valve was
drilled out or if the wastegate was ever shimed.  If either one of these
modifications were done the value needs to be replaced and the shims removed.
 The stage 2 chips with these modifications will overboost your car (very
bad).
