loaded in the opposite direction.  This can make the noise stop as the =
CV's innards shift toward less worn faces.

a bad airflow meter.  The swept resistive element inside it can wear, =
and you may have a bad spot.  Or your problem could lie elsewhere.  This =
one needs some hands-on debugging.
