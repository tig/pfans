I got a VDO guage from Racer Wholesale. It reads to 15 psi, cost $26. plus
about 7.50 for a tubing kit.

There is a banjo bolt on the side of the intake manifold, right above the
rear most cylinder. There is a tube leading from that bolt which has a T
connector in it about 4 inches from the bolt. Replace the T connector with
an X connector and use the vacant position for the guage tubing.
