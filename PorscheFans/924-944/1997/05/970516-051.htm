Note of interest...  My hatch was rattling quite loudly (sounded like very 
loose bolts, with nuts about ready to fall off!).  I took the rear roof liner 
out of the car, and tightened all the hex-head bolts.  Only one turned, and 
then only about 1/8th of a turn.  It cleared the rattle up completely, and got 
rid of all the rubber-on-paint squeaking too (except on healthy bumps in the 
road), and quieted the ride noise, which must have been sneaking in around the 
rear hatch seal.  Not much adjustment, but an awful lot of benefit!
