Jeff, just jump the wires back together with a butt connector and tie
wrap the wiring out of the way and you should be set.  The wiring needs
to be a complete circuit to keep the light off.  When the rotor wears
into the plastic sensor block, the wire wears in two, opening the
circuit and interrupting the ground to the control unit.  No harm done,
just piece it back together.
