You can adjust the height the sunroof tilts up (until you hit the maximium
allowed by the arms) by adjusting the angle of the center mounted 'tongue'
that depresses the front micro switch.  You make the adjustment by removing
the two plugs near the center front of the sunroof looking up from inside
the car.  Once the plugs are removed, you can adjust two hex key bolts which
change the angle of the tongue.  I adjusted mine by tightening each until I
felt slight resistance while the roof was fully closed.
