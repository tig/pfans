There should be play in the wheel bearing when it is properly adjusted.  =
When you drive the car, the bearings warm up and take up the play.  If =
you overtighten them, then when they warm up there will be no clearance =
for lubrication between the rollers and the races.  Many more wheel =
bearings have been damaged by overtightening than undertightening.

It is very common for there to be what feels like a loose wheel bearing, =
which is in fact slop in the strut (shock).  The best way to classify =
this is to take the wheel off, place one hand at the shock/piston =
interface, and yank the brake rotor with the other hand.  You can them =
tell whether the strut is loose or it's in fact the wheel bearing.
