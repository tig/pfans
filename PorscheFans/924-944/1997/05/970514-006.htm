In reference to the question of Kevin Gross about Kumho track tyres, I have
experience with these tyres.
They are very, very good track tyres. They are used in the Porsche-Ferrari
challenge competition in Europe and I have used them a lot on my 944 racer
during training sessions. The difference in  lap time on the Zandvoort
track between Kuhmo's and medium compound slicks is 0.5 second and on the
longer Zolder track not more than 1 second.
