At 60K, assuming the oil and belts were done, I would change the following:
1. coolant
2. brake fluid (and bleed clutch hydraulics)
3. transaxle fluid
4. flush power steering system
5. repack CV joints
6. lubricate all moving parts (door hinges, headlight motors, hatch release 
   pins, etc.)

If you have a shop do it, it will be expensive, but worth it. If you do it
yourself, the parts are VERY cheap, but it will take a lot of time.
