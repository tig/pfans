Gary -
Answer to Question #1 - I don't know but I have the similar problem and for the time being I'll live with it. - The roof opens about 2 to 3".
Answer to Question #2 - the position of the micro switches determines this. If you fully retract the arms and then adjust the micro switch position until the motor dosent run you will be all set. This is why the micro switches are mounted in an elongated slot. If there is not sufficient range then you will need to remove the motor assembly and adjust the position of the cable be 'screwing' it in or out. The cable is actually threaded and twisting it clock wise or counter clockwise will shorten or lengthen it.
