Sometimes you can tighten up the heat shield by putting a big hose 
clip on each end. I did this on my Audi. The 924S cat is a different
shape, but might still be possible.

If it's not the heat shield but the cat itself, remove the cat, shake 
out all the loose broken-up honeycombe which is rattling around 
inside, and put it back on. The car will still pass emissions if it's 
fuel injection is correctly adjusted.
