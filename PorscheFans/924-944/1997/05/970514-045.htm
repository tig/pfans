jayjobes;

Even though the heater valve appears sound and the connecting cable is 
working properly, it can still leak water inside (from one side to the 
other) resulting in the excess heat that you have.  This has happened 
several times to me over the years with mine; the valves appear to be 
good for about 2 years tops.  Watch out for when they get old too as 
they jamb the dash control and break the plastic parts on same.
