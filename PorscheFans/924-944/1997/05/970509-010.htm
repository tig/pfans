>1) Maybe someone has experienced this and can lend some insight as to how
>to fix the problem.  I have a 1980 924, and after a day of heavy rain I
>can hear water sloshing around behind the dash board.  If I make a heavy
>turn I am libel to get a decent amount of water dumped on my feet.  Any
>idea as to how to get the water to drain properly, where the hole may be?

There are some drain holes in front of the battery tray and a similar place
on the other side of the car. There are some rubber flaps that tend to
collect leaves and other nasty stuff.

By the way, is your passenger side carpet soaked? If so, you may want to
consider covering the battery tray with pulyurethane or something. It's a
mess to work with, but it stops all leaks.

>3) My front defroster doesn't blow air with enough force.  Connecticut
>Inspection will not pass until the front defroster blows a piece of
>paper!  There is some air coming out of the vents now.  Any ideas on
>where the problem may be?

Take a look at the fan. If I remember correctly, you can get at it by
pulling all heater levers to the far left, then going to the engine
compartment and get off the cover from the odd-looking dome in the middle of
the car, near the windshield. When you get this off, pull the levers to the
far right, and something should pop up, and you should be able to see the
fan and its connections. Hope I remembered this correctly..
