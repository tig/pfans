Ed,

They don't fail, but they do soot up.  When that is the case, an idle =
stabilizer should be cleaned with a solvent such as brake cleaner.  My =
point was that they rarely fail altogether, requiring replacement.

Your stumble on decel could have a number of causes, including:

- bad basic mixture
- problems with the idle switch (in the throttle switch)
- aging lambda sensor

and a number of other possible causes.  If you want to chase this one =
yourself, I'd start with these three items and a clean idle stabilizer.  =
And I'd get a copy of Probst's book, lots of good troubleshooting info =
in there.
