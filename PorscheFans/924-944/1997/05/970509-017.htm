The entire door handle can be removed through the following steps, which
should allow further disassembly and lubing:

1) pry off the metal trim piece on the front part of the handle with a
flathead screwdriver, to expose the forward phillips-head screw, and
undo that screw.

2) open the door to access the other (large) phillips-head screw, and
undo it (like Larry mentioned).

3) the handle should then come completely out of the door, and can be
lubed, cleaned, etc. then.

BTW, these handles should be identical (except for the trim piece) to
the VW Rabbit et al. door handles, so if'n you've got a really bad one,
finding a cheap replacement at a junkyard should be very easy and
productive...
