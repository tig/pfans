roads are so populous, but I've never felt like I was in danger in this 
car.
The 944 has the brakes (and I've needed them) and handling covered. The 
real
danger is when I switch over to my girlfriend's Probe. I have to remind my-

people in the other cars you have to worry about. I think you'd have to be
pretty careless to lose it.
