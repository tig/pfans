Jeff writes: "is 12.5 psi equivalent to 1.8 bar?"


1 PSI =  0.068947572939 BAR
1 BAR = 14.503773773    PSI

So 12.5 PSI above atmospheric pressure would be ~0.862 BAR, and if we
assume 1 BAR atmospheric pressure, the absolute pressure would be 1.862
BAR, so you were fairly close
