My '88 944S came with 215/50 15 BF Goodrich Comp TA VR4s.
Despite their name these are in no way 4 season tires
(at least not if one of those seasons is Montreal winter!).
They are mid-priced, incredibly durable, provide moderate
dry traction with progressive breakaway, and are excellent
in the wet.
