I chaned my tacho 2 month ago.

1 remove the two screws at the top of the insrument holder. They are located
inside the cylinders of the rpm and the multi function instument.

2 Now you can pull out the plastic with all three instruments. The first
centimetres are easy, problem is to get it over the cover  of steering, turn
signal etc. You need to bend it a little, but somehow it works.

3 Now you can see the instrument lights cables etc. Remove the tacho axle,
cables and lights and press the complete insrument out to the front. There is
a rubber seal inside making little trouble. Don't turn it! There are some
plasics to prevent turning. 

Oh, I forgot: It's much easier with the steering wheel off.

I hope this will help you!

Carsten
