There are no holes in the 944 rear hatch glass.

Spoiler and rubber side pieces are secured by studs from the 
pieces going through the frame and being held in place by
round nuts (with flat screwdriver slots). I made a special tool
for removing them by grinding a slot in a large flat screwdriver.

The size of the frame (and, indeed the hole for the rear hatch in the
body) is exactly the same for 924, 944 and 968 cars.

Incidentally, the size of the spoiler for the 924S and the 924 Turbo is
smaller than the 944 and 944 Turbo. A 944 spoiler looks too large for 
a 924 body, kind of like a 911 Turbo spoiler on an early 911T...
