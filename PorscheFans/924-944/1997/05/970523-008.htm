For those following the thread on the cam chain tensioner replacement for the 944S 
(and S2) models, I went to the dealer today and (finally) found the part numbers 
for the "oil pipe" and the required "sealing rings".

The oil pipe is 944-105-167-03 and lists for exactly $20 (don't ask me how much I 
paid).

The sealing rings, and you'll need 3 not 4 like the fiche says, are part number 
900-123-115-30 and are $0.47 each. The technician told me that the fourth sealing 
ring shown on the fiche is incorrect since the 16V head is aluminum and the oil 
pipe is secured against it without such a ring.

Thanks to those that have helped me out with this so far!
