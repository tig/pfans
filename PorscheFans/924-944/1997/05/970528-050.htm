A few mail order catalogs I receive advertise a radar jammer called 
"Spirit II" from the Rocky Mountain Radar Co.  This unit adds noise to 
the received signal, then sends it back out, supposedly confusing the 
officer's radar unit to the point of not displaying a speed.

Several magazine articles over the past year point out that the unit is 
pretty much worthless (in case you are interested in obtaining one).

In any event, somehow this unit is not illegal or otherwise restricted by 
the FCC, otherwise it wouldn't be offered for sale by reputable outlets.
