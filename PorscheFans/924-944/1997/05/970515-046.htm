Daniel,

There are three nuts holding it on.  They are 13mm (I think) nuts.  Two near
the bottom of the tensioner, one at the top.  Remove the bolts, then carefully
pull the tensioner over the studs.

To replace, put tensioner on studs, tighten the 3 nuts.  13mm nuts usually
dictate 20nm of torque, however you might want to check for the proper torque.
