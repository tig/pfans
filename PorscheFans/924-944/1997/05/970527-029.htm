A VASCAR timing mark can be anything, any type of landmark. If the PD are
using a fixed speed trap there will often be white or yellow marks painted
along one side of the road, at right angles to the direction of travel.
Aerial spotters can use these too.  An officer in a cruiser can also follow
you, wait till you pass a landmark, start the VASCAR, stop it when he
passes the landmark (a mile marker or bridge) and he will have the speed
difference displayed between his car and yours.  Sneaky little buggers.
