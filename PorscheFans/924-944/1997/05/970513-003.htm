There are four different part numbers:

477.863.517A Left Hand Side, with inner square (17.5 mm rim breadth)
477.863.518A Right Hand Side (17.5 mm rim breadth)
477.863.517B Left Hand Side, with inner square (16 mm rim breadth)
477.863.518B Right Hand Side (16 mm rim breadth)

There are also suffixes for Black (05T), Brown (89S) and Burgundy (9MM)
for the larger (older) covers.
