Hello group,

I recently posted a request to find a solution to my feet roasting in a 924.
By mistake, I found the culprit.  The gromet that seals the hole the speedo
cable goes through had popped loose.  I replaced it and my feet are
comfortable again.

I also found a relatively inexpensive hop up for the 924.  I used to see ads
many years ago about the Weber big throat throttle body conversion.  I
finally broke down and got one.  BIG difference.  It has alot more usable
power right from idle and I can pass cars at highway speeds now without
shifting down into 4th.

Have a great day!
