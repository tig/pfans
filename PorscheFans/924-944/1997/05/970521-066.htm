My 89 turbo had a 930 clutch disc in it which never felt quite right, and
finally disintegrated while puttering down the road at 2000 rpm one day.  The
clutch was replaced with a 930 disc again on the recommendation of a local
shop who indicated that the stock disc springs will not stand up to the
torque of a chipped turbo.  It slipped at maximum torque for some time, but
seems to be holding OK now.  I've run two track events with no problem, but
it still doesn't feel right to me.
