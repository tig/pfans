Anders:

Sorry to dissapoint you but Frig-C is not a viable alternative to R-12,
unless you replace all seals and hoses (expensive). Tests by A/C seal
manufactures suggest marginal to poor compatability with anything other than
neoprene seals. As far as I know, GM is the only OEM that uses neoprene. And
not all their seals are neoprene due to it's problems with high underhood heat.

I don't really want to encourage this string, but think people considering
switching to Frig-C should be aware of the potential problems. Hence I have
posted this to the list.
