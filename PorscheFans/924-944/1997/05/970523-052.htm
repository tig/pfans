Yes, if you are speaking of a liquid line receiver-drier and not a 
suction (gas) line r-d.  A suction side r-d will only see gas if the 
system is charged and operating properly.  If over-charged, it will cause 
the refrigerant to turn back into a gas after the evaporator coil and 
possibly at the compressor or never if severely over-charged or possible 
other problems (like a stuck open expansion valve (txv)).  Guess 
this happens more on residential/commercial equipment than car systems.
