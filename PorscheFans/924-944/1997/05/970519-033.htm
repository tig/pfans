This might seem like a lame question with an obvious answer, but I'll ask
for experienced input anyway.  I've got an S2 that seems to be used on the
track with increasing frequency, and proportionately less on the street.

performance after adding stiffer springs (250+ lbs) and lowering the car

after these modifications?  If so, why? I'm a relative rookie on the track,
but I love hard driving. I'm within 3-4 seconds of my class time (1:05)
after my first outing on a track, and I figure I could find those seconds
with more experience -- w/out necessarily changing the suspension. 

Or not.
