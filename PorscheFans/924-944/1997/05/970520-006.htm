Richard,

What happens the 50% of the time when the 1-2 shift isn't smooth?
Resistance or grinding?  You may well have a synchro reaching the end of
its useful existence.  It's always possible that you have a problem with
the 1/2 operating sleeve or its ancillary hardware.

It is probably worth checking the linkage for adjustment and condition.
Mechanics have been known to bend the linkage when removing the
transmission, since getting the intermediate arm clear of the case is a
fiddle.
