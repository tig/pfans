This is great if you can find someone who'll do it for free. If not, you
can buy the glue yourself at any autopart store (esp. the larger
chain/supermarket type stores like Western Auto). The glue is
specifically for rear view mirrors. Just scrape the old glue off w/ a
razorblade, prep both surfaces with an alcohol swab, and glue merrily. I
did this on my parent's minivan, and the repair lasted for years until
they traded the thing in.
