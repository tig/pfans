> 1)  Any advice/experience on tire pressures in the R-1's?  I've only driven
> street tires in the past.

I just finished a track weekend at Buttonwillow in Central California.  I 
ran 26 front/27 rear cold (32/33 hot) all weekend with good results.  But 
I'm using 225/50x15 all around on my NA 944.  Also it will depend 
somewhat on your alignment.  I have quite a bit of camber on the front 
(since this car was setup for autocrossing) and the tires did show higher 
temperatures on the inside.  You can start around 28 all around and then 
find someone with a pyrometer and check your tires a couple times during 
the weekend.  Adjust accordingly.
