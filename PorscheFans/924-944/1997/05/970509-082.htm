okay here it is the way to adjust those pesky door handles:
1.  remove the door panel , not friendly
2.  with it off you can see the piece you will need.
2a. if you have the haynes manual find the page on door handle removal
3. if not the remove the handle, there is a white plastic piece that sort of dangles down from thetrigger mechanism, you will see it if the handle is out.
4. turn the white piece until it is more loose than previous. because of the now longer reach the trigger is tighter and worksMUCH better (before i coulndt get it open now my 5 foot tall 100 pound girlfriend can with ease.
5. disclosures. it is a pain in the a@@ the white piece doesnt want to come off and there isnt much room to work. while ou are in there lube all the pieces well to insure good working later.
