When I bought my '85 944, it, too, suffered from "that damn rear hatch
rattle". Adjusting the latch posts did not good, and the upper bolts were
socked down nice and tight.

At the advice of some forgotten but not unappreciated expert, I got under
the car and tightened the upper rear shock mount bolts. Presto! Rattle all
gone, for all time.

Why did this work? Who knows? Who cares? Like a lot of arcane Porsche
engineering, go figure.........

Good luck with the rattle.
