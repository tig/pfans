Simple answer: Yes. Assuming you are talking about a 944 Turbo (which I
inferred from the mailing list we are on!), you can run your car in
either A Mod or E Mod for Solo II. Most regional Solo Is have a catchall
group, such as ASR or Super Production, which should allow you to
'compete' (not that you will have a chance in hell against REAL race
cars.)
