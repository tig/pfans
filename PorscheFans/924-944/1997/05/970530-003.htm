One obvious thing to check before considering any expensive changes of
equipment is the engine grounding strap. Then, go hunting for loose
chassis (ground) connections before searching for detective components..

This very much sounds like a instrument 'false ground' problem. The
currents in instrument circuits are so small that they can make ground
(make connection with the negative pole) thru' other electrical
consumers, motor windings or similar high resistance paths. Electrical
current usually take the least resistive way, but do not hesitate to
seek out very strange paths in these cases.
