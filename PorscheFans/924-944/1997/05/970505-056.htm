An old trick for removing bolts with stripped heads - only if you have an
arc welder:

-Place a nut on top of the stripped head - can be the same size 
-With a small diameter welding rod, place  the rod through the nut and arc
the stripped head and begin to weld the stripped head to the inside of the
nut.

I know most of us don't have arc welders laying around but I've used this
method before and it works well.
