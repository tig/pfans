While I had the driver's seat out to adjust the hand brake cable, I
decided to investingate why the handbrake indicator was not illuminating
in the instrument cluster when the lever is up.  It turns out the bracket
supporting the switch under the lever had bent to the side, jamming the
switch.  8 years of the spring in the switch holding the plunger against
the lever gradually pushed the switch to the side.  It is made of very
soft metal, and it bent back with little effort. The switch itself is a 
cheesy VW style affair.

This minor problem was posted on the list some time ago, so I thought I'd 
post my experience.
