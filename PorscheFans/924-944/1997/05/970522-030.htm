It looks like I struck a chord (or a nerve) with a lot of pressurized Porsche
lovers.  Compared to Farzaan's numbers (16-18 psi), I'm not running much

Ray wondered why he wasn't getting over 1.2 bar.  I know why - I'll bet!  I
learned this by accident.  While unhooking the vaccuum line that runs from
the intake manifold to the KLR box, (for the purpose of "tapping in" for my
VDO guage) I removed a two-wire connector from a small cylindrical object
mounted below the intake manifold.  The wires were in a "T" connection.  Made
my hook up, fired up the car and took off out of the driveway.  I punched it
(the car was already warm) and was totally underwhelmed.  Couldn't get more
than 1.2 bar.  These car's aren't that quick at 1.2 bar.

Long story short, I'd forgotten to reconnect the wires on that cylindrical
piece (looks like some sort of solenoid, maybe).  Hooked the wires back up -
greased lightning!  Check your wires, Ray, and if they're hooked up, I bet
its the cylindrical part malfunctioning.
