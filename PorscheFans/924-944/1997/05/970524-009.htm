Can anyone advise me what's invloved in removing and replacing the cam =
cover (special tools and gaskets etc).  I want to check my tensioner =
before I find out the hard way that I have a problem!

Very simple.  Remove the spark plugs, remove the cover bolts with an =
allen socket.  However, to properly check the tensioner you need to =
remove it, check both ramps, and check the tensioner piston for =
"softness" just like a hydraulic lifter.  Any squish when you try to =
compress it with your fingers, toss it.

Be care not to overtighten the cam cover bolts on reinstall.  They are =
small, like M5, and get overtightened frequently.  Use a new gasket set =
if yours is hard, use a little case sealant at its four corners where =
the gasket is ribbed.
