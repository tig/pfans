Robert -
If you or anyone else wants to get a "Factory" document listing all of the
assembly specifications (build sheet) as originally shipped from Germany,
you can obtain one from PCNA in Reno by sending a copy of your title
listing the VIN registered in your name and a check for ?? (it was only $20
in 1993) and requesting a "Certificate of Authenticity".  It is a great
little piece of documentation to keep with all your other receipts
(especially in the case of an insurance claim or restoration process).  The
information they provide includes:

Model Year/Type		(mine was: 1988 944 Turbo S)
Engine Number		(mine was: 47J00580)
Transmission Number		(mine was:  AOR10117)
Exterior Paint Code/Color	(mine was F1F1-Rose Quartz Metallic)
Interior Material Color/Type	(mine was 03-Special Turbo S Multi-Color
Cloth)
Optional Equipment
		Mine had:	C02 - 49 State Emissions
			158 - Radio "Reno"
			454 - Cruise Control
			650 - Sunroof
			758 - Turbo S
