Someone please correct me if I am wrong.

I believe the M030 suspension includes a 30 mm front sway bar and a 19 mm
three way adjustable rear sway bar.  It also featured stiffer bushings.

The Turbo Cup suspension features stiffer front springs and rear coil-over
shocks - which are used in addition to the stock torsion bars.  The shock are
also different but I don't know exactly how.

The Turbo Cup suspension set-up should be used with the sway bars but the
bars are not part of the set-up.
