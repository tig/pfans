Menelaos,

The S2 you reviewed sounds like a bad example.  After driving an 86 951 for
a year, I found a low mileage S2 to replace it with (mainly for the airbags
and ABS).  The S2 definitely screams up to 4000 rpm faster than the 951.
After that, it's no contest; the turbo rules.  As other writers have
mentioned, the S2 a better street/every day driver than the turbo cars.
Additionally, I drove at least one 944S before buying either car.  The S2 is
much improved because of its better low end driveability.  The gains are
from the bigger displacement but also because the final drive ratio is short
(3.83:1 or something like that).
