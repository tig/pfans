> Porsche Fans:

> Time to replace my battery in my '87 944 NA. Any suggestions for the cheapest
> and biggest cca battery out there? Will any physical configuration battery
> work or do I need a special chassis battery for the unique hold down located
> at bottom of battery? I should go sealed because of potential corrosion from
> acid leaking from vented batteries; correct?

Any high quality group 41 size battery should work well.  Trying to
shoehorn a larger or odd size battery into that space is asking for
trouble.  The group 41 size will deliver in excess of 650 cold cranking
amperes, more than enough reserve to start the car in sub-freezing
termperatures, provided you have the correct viscosity oil in the
crankcase.  Whether the battery is sealed or not, I would remove the
battery at regular intervals and clean the area.
