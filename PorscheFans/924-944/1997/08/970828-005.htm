Here in NZ different vehicles are allowed different open road speed
limits. 100KPH ( 62.5 MPH ) is the open road maximum for cars, light
trucks, motorcycles and the like. 90 KPH ( 56 MPH ) for Heavy vehicles,
buses, etc. 80 KPH ( 50 MPH ) for school buses and all vehicles towing
trailers. And 70 KPH ( 45 MPH ) for Motorcyclists on a learner permit.
With this in mind surely a well trained driver in a nimble well
maintained vehicle should be allowed to driver at a higher limit. But
then how do you set standards for such a thing.
Telling people nothing but slow down does nothing other than to give
then more time to correct the mistake made in a dynamic situation.
Proper driver training, and attitudes, from a young age will prevent the
mistake being made in the first place. ( well hopefully.)
