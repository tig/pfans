Dean,

Sorry, easily fixed and Porsche do not go together.  The easy fix would =
be
to give it a whack and see if you could align it that way.  Getting to =
the
bolts is a PITA but since you asked.  Pull the wheel and open the access
door in the front of the fender well.  Remove the plastic finger nuts =
from
the running light and remove.  Take a 1/4" drive 10mm universal socket =
and
loosen the bolts holding the urethane nose to the fender.  Align, =
tighten
and reverse the procedure.  There is not much room to work through the
access panel so you probably would be better off removing the plastic
fender liner, I did.
