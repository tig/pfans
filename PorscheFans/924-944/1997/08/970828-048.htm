Note: All stainless steel spherical bearings are Teflon lined and have
static load capacities from 39,000 lbs to 137,000 lbs depends on size. 
Spherical bearings can be replaced between $30 - $60 each.  Because
spherical bearing do not compress and bind under load, it retain fluid
motion thought out compression and rebound stoke.  In addition, factory
casting and machining does not have the tight tolerance require for
spherical bearing conversion, therefore, machining is necessary for
proper fit.
