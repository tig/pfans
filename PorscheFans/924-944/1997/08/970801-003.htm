Try a higher octane fuel.  I have a car I leave in Mexico to tow my boat
which runs about the same with the key off as on.  It is the low octane
Mexican fuel (Pemex or as I call it "Pee Mix") that causes the run on.  You
may also have carboned up combustion chambers.  The carbon continues to
glow and ignites the fuel after the key is turned off.  There use to be an
additive to clean out the carbon.  If neither of these great ideas work,
let out the clutch as you turn off the engine.  Not correct P procedure but
will keep the babes happy until you get the problem fixed.
