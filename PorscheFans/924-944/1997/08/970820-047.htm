That's a pretty weird one.  Hmm.  There are two springs inside the 
gearbox which control the centring of the linkage (actually, the 
transverse rod which ends up locating everything else).  I think that 
what I would do, if as you suggest it happens consistently when the car 
is warmed up, is get the car warm.  Then jack the rear, check the 
linkage for binding and anything obvious.  Failing that, then 
disconnect the linkage at the rear: under the rubber boot there is an 
M8 pinch bolt which connects the intermediate rod to the linkage at the 
box.  Now see whether the linkage does or does not centre properly by 
operating it by hand.  You'll know whether the problem is within the 
gearbox, or is instead in the intermediate rod and forward.
