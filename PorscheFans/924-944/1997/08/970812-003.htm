True, the synthetic oil may not break down, but you still accumulate all 
of the combustion products over time, particularly from the Turbo.  These 
combustion products form corrosive acids as well.

I have said in the past, and will say again; the 3000 mile oil change is 
cheap insurance, but its your car, and your wallet.

>20,000 in a heavily laden oil delivery vehicle, we sent it off to be 
>tested, and
>showed no signs of breakdown.  Now I know that Turbo's generate a lot of
