The fact is that the simplistic model using a constant coefficient of friction
does not work when applied to a deformable tire in contact with a non-smooth
surface such as Tarmac, especially when you consider that the tire is in fact
rolling, and generating a slip-angle. Caroll Smith's "Tune To Win" explains
this stuff very well, showing curves for "grip" versus slip-angle, etc.
