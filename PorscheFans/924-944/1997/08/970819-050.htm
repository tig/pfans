     The splash pan (I call it undertray, but you can call it underwear if 
     you want to) provides some aerodynamic advantage, and it has vents to 
     channel air (and debris) to specific parts of the engine.  My first 
     944 had one, but my 951 does not (but's it's supposed to).  I was 
     worried at first, but my mechanics put my mind at ease and told me 
     that it was no big deal, especially since a lot of people take them 
     off anyway (for ease of maintenance access and questionably improved 
     cooling).  In fact, I've checked under every 944/951 I've come across 
     and I'd have to say that a third of them are running bare-bottomed.  
     So I wouldn't worry about it too much, but I guess it would be nice to 
     have one.  By the way, I also need the chin spoiler that goes right in 
     front of the undertray, so if anyone knows where I can get one, let me 
     know!
