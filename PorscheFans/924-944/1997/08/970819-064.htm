John, I would bet that the windage tray is only there as an aerodynamic aid
(as in reducing the drag coefficient).  It might possibly have some effect
on airflow through the radiator but I have driven my car with it removed
for a few days and never noticed any difference.  The other thing it
probably does is to keep splashing water from soaking the exposed
alternator and power steering belts.  You might hear some belt squeal in
wet conditions.
