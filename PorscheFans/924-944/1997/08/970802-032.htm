> My question is, I wonder if the car is chipped.  How would I check this?
>  Where is the computer located, how would I get to it, and what should I look

I believe all the chips raise the rev-limiter cutoff point by 400-500 rpm.
A stock 968 cuts out at 6700 (confirm this with your cabriolet), if your
coupe cuts out over 7000 rpm I suspect it's chipped. This was originally
suggested by another S2 racer I know when I was searching for my 944.
