I would be inclined to spend my money on one of those throttle cams that
seem so popular. If you sold your front seats and bought some light
weight buckets the weight saving would give you a better power to weight
ratio. You could, if you felt if it was worth the expense, have the head
polished and ported and a nice lumpy cam fitted. I don't know what the
DME would think of this but it would provide better breathing. If you
were a bit more serious you could have twin Webers with tuned trumpets
and extractors fitted also. I don't know what sort of power you could
make, but a 2ltr SOHC Ford ( sorry about using the F - word guys) can
make 175BHP with the Weber setup.
