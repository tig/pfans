Wesley,
       My first attempt was to make the gasket from an old truck inner tube. 
This did not work, it was too thick, and swelled with exposure to the P/S 
fluid. I found a cork/rubber composite gasket material at my local parts 
store. I think it was made by permatex. I used a can as a form and an exacto 
blade to cut the gasket out.   This material has worked well, it has not 
degraded, and does not fall apart. You can use stainless steel hose clamps 
from your local hardware store. I used extra wide clamps for added strength.
Good Luck!
          Ezra
