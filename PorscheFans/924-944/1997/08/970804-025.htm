Car:       1985 1/2 944, 90,000 miles, well maintained.    
Problem:   Intermittent, simultaneous failure of all dash lights/gauges
           and the brake lights.
Attempt:   Mechanic stated that he believed there to be a short in the
           fuse box. Solution: (a) Jerry rig repair or (b) replace
           the fuse box (@$800.00). I chose (a). Everything was fine
           on the way home from the shop. the next time the car was
           driven, the problem returned.
....
Any ideas?

You have a bad ground somewhere between your guages and the battery/car.
I experienced similar symptoms. My repair cost me $0.00 I began by
working my way back all the cables from the negative battery terminal.
After touching and moving each wire a bit, the problem disappeared. I
have seen it come back once in a blue moon, but I just pop the hood and
wiggle the wires. To be professional about it; undo all the wires, clean
all the contact points and fix them back tightly.
