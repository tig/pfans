I saw the plastic tail light in "Performance Products" magazine and
decided to order it.  Now, my '87 944 has a sharper back view.  If you
decide to buy it, plan on spending half-a-day installing it.  The wires
are a ten-minute job, the hard part is drilling the holes in the exact
place to mount the tail light - a template is provided(it might work for
you), but I ended up making my own and doing a little modification to
the tail light in order for it to fit JUST PERFECT.

I kept the template - the one I made.  If you should require some help,
I'd be happy to give you more detailed instructions and mail you the
template.  Good luck
