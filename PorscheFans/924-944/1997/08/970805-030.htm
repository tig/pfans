To my experience, the key to eliminating power steering fluid leaks is 
to replace the three solid-band original clamps with Zebra-style 
clamps.  Porsche agrees and issued a tech bulletin recommending this. 
 Two of the clamps are on the hoses at the base of the reservoir, the 
third is on the banjo fitting at the power steering pump.

I would certainly try this before replacing hoses or sanding down the 
bead on the nipples at the reservoir.  Also, use OE style clamps with 
rounded edges.  The flat hardware-store clamps can cut the hose.
