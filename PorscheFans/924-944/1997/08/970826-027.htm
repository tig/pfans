To Roger and anyone else offended by the lettering on their rear
reflector:
	I don't have a rear reflector on the back of my 944.  But, I believe
the lettering is just painted on.  If this is the case and you want to
remove it, just get some very fine (800-1000 grit) wet sandpaper and
wet-sand the offending lettering off.  After that has been accomplished,
purchase a rubbing compound or polish appropriate for plastic and rub
out the whole unit.  It should look like new.  Hope this helps.
