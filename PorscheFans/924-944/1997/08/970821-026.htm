I can't really trace back) that runs into it.

Is this an air conditioner?  I can't get close enough to see if it says

And while we're at it: I finally got everything back together after a
rebuild on my car.  Turned the key one position to check the electrics and
the oil pressure jumped immediately to ten bar.  So I figured (correctly)
that I had the wires backwards on the pressure sensor; I swapped them and
checked, it was okay (resting at zero bar).  Started cranking the engine
(pressure still at zero), no start.  Double-checked spark plug wires (3 &
4 were backwards - duh) and fixed them.  Cranked, not looking at pressure
gauge.  No start.  Cranked one more time, checked the gauge, it was at ten
bar.  Let go of the key and the pressure went back to zero bar.

My question: could this be a serious problem with oil blockage (like a
stuck oil filter or oil pickup hose) or is it more likely (my guess) that
I broke the pressure sensor somehow?
