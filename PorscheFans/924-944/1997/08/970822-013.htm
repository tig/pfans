Hey Jamie,
I don't know if there is anything wrong with any of these pumps.  I'm
starting to wonder if your fuel pump RELAY isn't on the fritz.  I think
your car is an '83, yes?  I think this failure is not unheard of in the
cars with the old fuse box under the dash.  I remember having rain water
get into my old 924, creep along the under side of the dash, and then
drip directly onto the box of fuses and relays.  Things got pretty
squirrely after a good rain.  

Should be cheap to investigate the relay.  Best of luck.
