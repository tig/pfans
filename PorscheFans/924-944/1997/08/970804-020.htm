THE MEANING OF CARRERA:
in terms of the Porsche Carrera models.
The Carrera PanAmericana was a famous long distance road race in
Mexico. The last one held in 1954. In 1954 Porsche's factory race
cars finished 1st and 2nd in class, 3rd and 4th overall behind 2 
large displacement Ferraris. To celebrate this win, Porsche created
the 356 Carrera. Needless to say, the name has been passed down a 
few times. So Carrera comes from that mexican road race....
