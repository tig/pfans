I think Bruce Anderson may have been referring to the life of turbochargers
in 930s, not 951s. I've read most of his technical articles from back
issues of Excellence (among other sources) and he does make the assertion

I know several 951 owners on this list have replaced turbos...has anyone
had to do so before 50k miles?
