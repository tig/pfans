Kieth, I did this a few years ago with an AGLA (Alan Gun Leather) kit.  It
did not fit all that well and the cross spoke pieces (a 4 spoke also) had
to be contact cemented on (they have some stitching for looks).  One of the
spoke pieces was not long enough and it took AGLA quite a while to send me
a replacement piece.  You could take your chances with another brand of kit
but if I were to do it again I think I'd pay the labor and expect a good
fit (or I'd have one).
