I suppose every state has slightly different standards, and the testing
equipment will vary from unit to unit, location to location, but here
are the results from my last two Illinois Vehicle Emission Tests for my
LATE '88 944 Turbo S.  Note that Illinois
doesn't test for O2 and now tests only for low idle conditions.  Also,
desired results are to be as low as possible except for CO+CO2% which
must exceed 6%:


	0220	01.20	06.00		(STATE STANDARDS)

	0063	01.14	15.79		idle


1996	0006	00.00**	15.21		idle

**I think that my cat converter had a hole in it and was
disintigrating, so it is possible that their test probe wasn't getting
a true reading, but they didn't question anything and I didn't
volunteer any information either.


Don't know if my readings are relevant to yours, but it's a start.
