A turbo S is a go faster 951. They produce 250hp and have strengthened
gearboxes, suspension and the like. They unfortunately don't have an 'S'
after the turbo badge on the rear. The 89 cars were the same spec as the
turbo S' but are not turbo S'. Only 1000 were built worldwide in 88 to
complement standard 88 951 production. Here are some chassis numbers to
help if you are in the market for one and fear you are being duped.
88 951 (220 BHP) 95JN150001-
88 951S (250BHP) 95JN50001-  ( I have also seen it printed as 97JN50001-
any help here?)
89 951 (250 BHP) 95KN150001-
