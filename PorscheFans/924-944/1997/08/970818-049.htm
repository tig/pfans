Excellent job of reading the wiring diagram... Yes, the ac/heater blower =
fan is indeed controlled
by routing the current through the 4 resistors (and their attendant =
overheating safety contacts).

Replacing the resistor block is not difficult at all. You can find the =
plug and the two Phillips head
screws for the block under that big plastic shroud right at the base of =
the windshield, under the hood.
Be careful with the shroud; the heat and age tend to make them brittle =
and want to crack and crumble=20
in your hands...
