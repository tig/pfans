Are you using premium fuel (91 octane in the US)?  Also, carbon buildup 
in the combustion chambers can cause hot spots that will ignite the 
air/fuel mix.  Someone may wish to verify here, but the fuel injectors 
should not dribble fuel after the engine is shut off.  Label them and 
bring them to your favorite shop for testing.  They may also have a handy 
fuel injector cleaning machine which will pump very powerful solvent 
through them to remove deposits.
