I have driven a wide variety of cars over the last 10 years.  Everything
from a 930, to an HKS modified 300ZXTT, to an NSX.  Although many of
those cars were extremely competent and fun to drive, only two stand out
as having an inherent "rightness" about them -- the 944 series and the
original M3.  Both posses a certain chassis magic that transcends their
shortcomings and makes them (in my mind) true over achievers.  If you
haven't driven an E30 M3, you really should.  Although I prefer the
Porsche, I can easily see how someone would opt for the BMW.
