FWIW there is no major difference as we think between synthetic and regular oil.
Oil molecules come in three major sizes, Small/Medium/Large.
Small molecules evaporate away in high temperatures.
Large molecules do not move (lubricate) in cold temperatures.
Medium does both OK.
So a regular oil does have these characteristics which may not be the best for
the motor. In synthetic oils, this is treated for all the molecules to pretty
much have the same structure (not by additives, but by refining). So they can hae
predictable behaviour based on the viscosity of the oil.
