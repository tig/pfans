Chances are the bulb is burned out.  You must remove the trim piece that
the clock mounts to.  This will give you access to the back of the clock
where the bulb will be rather obvious.  You twist the bulb to remove it
and replace.  Mine was about $.67, clearly the least expensive Porsche
part I have found if you don't have to pay for installation which will
take you about an hour.

I posted all the instructions some time ago but don't have access to
that email now.  Let me know if you need it and I will dig it up
tonight.

Good Luck. 

P.S. be very careful with the trim pieces, age makes the plastic very
brittle.
