Thanks to those who replied after my timing belt failed last week, I ran
a compression test last night and think I may have survived, all
cylinders are between 165 and 190 psi according to Haynes the tolerance

compression) it should survive, with a UK model which mine is it
shouldn't, but I guess that depends on what your valve clearances are,

tolerance.

open as far, therefore I didn't bend any valves. Am I talking rubbish or
does this make sense to anybody else.
