Hi, Ray,

Yep, that's the one...

To verify that this is the cause of the problem, remove the control arm =
and place the ball joint end
in a vise. If the ball joint compresses more that .005" or .010" (keep =
in mind that .030" is your spark
plug gap - this is not very much of a distance) you should replace the =
control arm...
