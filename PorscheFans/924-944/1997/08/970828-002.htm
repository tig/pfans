I had included a small diagram showing the terminals for the sensor
plug.  For some reason it did not get posted.  If you look at the sensor
plug the top  terminal (#1) is output signal, terminal 2, (lower left
terminal) is voltage and terminal 3 (lower right terminal) is ground.
If you look closely, you can see the numbers stamped next to the
terminals.
