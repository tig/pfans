You've got air into the clutch hydraulics.  Order an Eezi-bleed or a 
like positive-pressure bleeder.  When you've got air in the lines, it 
is generally helpful to jack the rear end of the car quite high in the 
air, to level the clutch MC so that trapped air can more easily travel 
out and down the line to the slave cylinder.
