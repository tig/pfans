I have just emerged from the basement after a rousing session of wheel
refinishing (I need a life).  I have a set of four bolt eight spoke wheels
from a 1980.5 924 that I am trying to polish.  Progress is slow but
promising.  I purchased a refinishing kit from The Eastwood Co. for about
$80.00 U.S.  The kit works great but the wheels have some pretty
inaccesable spots on them requiring some creativity to get to.  You can
contact Eastwood at  www.eastwoodco.com for information or to order.  They
sell a number of restoration products that may be of intrest to anyone
restoring a car of any kind.
