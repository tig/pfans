Howdy,
I have a 89 951 with16x7 & 9's
I had previously fitted D40 M2s in 225 & 245
I took them to the dump with about 50% tread left.
I now have Bridgestone S-02's in 225 & 245
These tires are IMHO as good in the wet as the D40s were in the dry. The
day I bought them it was raining heavily and I had a lot of fun out in
the hills. There is good grip from cold that was not evident in the
D40s. I have driven the S-02s hard and not managed to kill them. I have
put ~ 10k miles on them since March and I estimate at least 50% tread
left with none of the hardening that occurred with the D40s. I'm not
sure what winter weather is like in Ohio ( I live in New Zealand ) but
as long as you don't have a desire to go driving on ice or in the snow
you should check out this tire.

Richard.
