The 0-60 time quoted by the factory for the 968 coupe 6-speed is 6.1 sec.
 Car and Driver got 5.7 in 1992.  The car did not change mechanically over
its lifespan (92-95), so the time should not have changed.  The cab is
heavier and a little bit slower.  The tiptronic adds about a second (though,
if you shift as slowly as I do, a tiptronic is probably faster!).
