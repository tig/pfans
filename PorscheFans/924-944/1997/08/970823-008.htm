I disagree with all of you.

Here is the proper way to drain your battery:

I use a 1/8" drill. although many people have used an awl and it has 
worked just fine.  Whichever method you choose doesn't matter.  That's 
the beauty of this system.

Drill an 1/8" hole at the bottom of one corner of your battery. If you 
have chosen the awl method, use a mallet to drive the pointed end into 
the same corner.  

Now place the battery so the new hole is at the lowest elevation by 
shimming the opposite corner with a block of wood.  If you are in a hurry 
you can make another hole at the top of the battery.

Very soon all of the juice will flow out of your battery and it will then 
be fully drained.
