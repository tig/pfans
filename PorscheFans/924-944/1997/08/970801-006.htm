I read that Omni Magazine has awarded an individual for one of the best
theories this year.  It goes like this:

He observes that cats, when dropped, always land on their feet.  He further
observes that buttered toast always lands on the ground with the buttered
side down.  Since both have an affinity for the ground, he proposes to
strap a slice of buttered toast to the back of a cat.

He predicts that, when dropped, the "buttered cat" will hover just above
the ground spinning.  Now, he further postulates that a "buttered cat
array" could be built to create a levitation train.

On a smaller scale, perhaps we could use this as an engine?  All we need is
high octane Friskies at the pump.
