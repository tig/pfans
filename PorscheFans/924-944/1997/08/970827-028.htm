Linen refers to the color of the leather. I have a met blk 951 with
linen leather and polished wheels. As you can imagine keeping it clean
is almost as much fun as being audited. I used Mother's leather cleaner
to get the grime off, and Hide Food for the conditioning. Hide Food is
excellent and it will keep your leather in top shape.
