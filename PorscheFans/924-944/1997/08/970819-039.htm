This happened to my '85 944. Then it happened again........and again.......

Turns out that the battery terminal on the positive side had corroded from
within the cable-to-terminal sheath! It looked just fine on the exterior,
showed no corrosion powder around the battery post, and was nice and tight
to the touch. Inside, out of sight, solidly non-conductive. Dealer replaced
the terminal and, Presto! it all worked.

Take yours apart and see if that might be the cause. The positive terminal
is right under the spot where water runs down off the windshield. Covered
or not, this is a high-moisture spot.
