When multiple lights come on mysteriously all at once, it's usually a 
sign of a ground problem.  Where turning the steering wheel affects 
things, it's worth checking the ground wire from the steering rack to 
the cross member.  This is a short brown wire, underneath the car, and 
should be visible when you remove the belly pan.
