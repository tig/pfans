Here's how to approach the problem.

1. Disconnect battery negative and put an ammeter between the post and 
the lug.

2. Pull fuses one at a time from the fuse box(es) and see where the 
drain lies.

Once you've narrowed it down to a single fuse, it is usually pretty 
simple to fix the culprit.  Of course, if you have any aftermarket 
electrical stuff hacked into your car, look there first by all means - 
guilty until proven innocent.
