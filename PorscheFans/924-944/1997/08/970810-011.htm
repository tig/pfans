Terry,

I am familiar with the lag especially in the 944S(16 valves are much
better at high end while 8 vales...my understanding at least...will do
alittle better at low end).  Also, the Germans developed the Porsche for
quick highway response but slow controlled traffic driving(unlike the
Japanese which don't really have any highway system to drive their cars
fast.  They made them feel quicker off the line).  I drove a 944S for
awhile before getting my 951 and to overcome the low end lag you must go
hard on the clutch.  If you rev it to 4k+ and let the clutch out not too
fast while still giving it gas, the car actually appears quite quick off
the line.

Just my two cents,
