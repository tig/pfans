THROTTLE RESPONSE WHEEL/CAM
This piece eliminates the slow engine response to pedal pressure.  It
basically takes out the slack in the throttle cable, thus giving quicker
and smoother acceleration.  Takes about 20-30 minutes to install, and is
generally sold thru the catalog houses for about $US35.  To summarize - it
gives you more "perceived" power.  One of the first things I did to my old
'83 944 NA.
