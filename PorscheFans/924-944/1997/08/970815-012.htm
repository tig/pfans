I just went through the same process on my 89 951.  With lots of help
from this list I selected Metal Masters and they are grrreat!  Hardly
any squeal and very little dust.  Only modification needed was posted
here some time ago; take a hacksaw with two blades in it and saw a slot
in the pads so they let the gasses escape.  Look at the pads you are
removing and all will be self explanatory.  Got my pads from Vertex,
great customer service and very prompt.

Good luck!
