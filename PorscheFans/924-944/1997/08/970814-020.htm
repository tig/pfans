The book is correct. The cF does not change dependent on the shape of
the object, but the force to move it does. Take an eraser, stand it on
end, and move it across a table. Now, stand it on it's face and try the
same thing.

If you are really interested in vehicle dynamics I'd recommend "How to
Make Your Car Handle" by Fred Puhn, "Race Car Vehicle Dynamics" by
Milliken et al, or "Tune to Win" by Carroll Smith. The first is the most
basic and the least expensive. The last concerns itself primarily with
formula cars, but I just love Carroll Smith's writing style. RCVD is the
current 'gold reference' on the subject.
