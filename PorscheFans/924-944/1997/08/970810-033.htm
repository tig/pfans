Todd Leen wrote that he couldn't find the radiator drain plug.

On my '86 951 it is at the left bottom corner of the radiator, at the back
side.  It is a plastic plug with an o-ring seal, not a drain valve, and is
slotted to be removed with a screwdriver.

Also, I recently drained the radiator and left the plug off until the next
evening when I removed the block drain screw.  Only a few drops of coolant
dribbled out of the block drain hole.  I insured that it was not plugged.  I
believe by this that the block drains completely through the water pump.

Hope this helps, Claus
