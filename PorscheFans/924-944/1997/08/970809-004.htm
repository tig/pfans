Last night I realized one of my big mistakes in comparing the '86 951 I test 
drove with my '86 944. My N/A has the throttle response cam, whereas the 951 
must have had the stock throttle cam. This combined with the turbo lag gave me 
the impression that the low end was weaker. After looking at the owners 
manual, which shows the torque curves for both, and realizing my above 
mistake, I agree with Marv Debeque's analysis.
