Nick,
 The k26 (non 's')unit can be adjusted to (k27's') standards in about an
hour. The stock boost on the k26 is set from the factory at about half
of its potential. No, you don't need to replace the injectors. The stock
injectors will flow enough for about 17 lbs. of boost. 17 lbs alone will
place you in the high 200's. If this is not enough you will then need
the larger k27 unit.
