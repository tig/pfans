Was reading about reversing CV joints...  One would think it was a 
good idea, but when I've taken mine apart, the wear appears to be 
fairly uniform.  The problem I had last time is severe pitting on the 
pumpkin in the middle, I guess from water contamination or lack of 
grease.

I do know (at least from my Honda) that if your cage appears to be 
worn, replace the joint.  I had three fail catastrophically-the cages 
would break and let one of the bearings out.  Of course, front wheel 
drive doesn't help any...
