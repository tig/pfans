Thanks to everyone who responded to my question on how
long it takes to build up oil pressure. It turned out that there
was some dirt in the (very small) opening of the oil pressure 
sensor so I had pressure all along. After cleaning the thing
the pressure went right up to 4.5 bar at idle and the light went 
immediately. The engine is running well now (apart from the 
fact that I have 2 litres extra oil in the crankcase which gives 
a huge blue cloud whenever I get over 4500 rpm).
