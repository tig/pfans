I installed an Autopower roll bar in my '86 951 last week.  I removed the
plastic door sill trim and pulled the carpet up from the rear floor.  I
removed the fold-down rear seat backrest, loosened the plastic side panels
along the rear seat and pulled the carpet out from underneath to expose the
inner wheel well.

After the roll bar was fastened to the floor, I moved the carpet up to one
bar and guessed where the center of a hole would be in the carpet.  I cut
from that point to the edge of the carpet where I wanted the seam.  I then
cut from the starting point outward, one half of the bar diameter, in five
directions.

The carpet was then formed around the roll bar and the rear seatback was
reinstalled.  If I ever remove the roll bar, it may be possible to place tape
under the carpet seams and hide the cuts.
