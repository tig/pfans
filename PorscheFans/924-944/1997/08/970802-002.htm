> Hello,
> 	I have decided to replace all the shocks in my 1987 944S. I think they
> still have the original ones, and the car floats in sharp turns. So, any
> recommendations on shocks to use? 


	I replaced my factory shocks at 100K with yellow Konis and
	have never looked back. The ride difference between worn
	out, stock shocks and new Konis is tremendous. It will
	feel like you're driving a new car. The ride will be a bit
	rougher, though. You'll feel bumps you never felt before
	but the handling difference is well worth it in my
	opinion.
