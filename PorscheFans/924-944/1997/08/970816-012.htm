After working fine for 9 years my light recently snuffed out too.  Check your
fuse box.  Particularly the one labeled Clock.  Mine was blown.  Replacing it
fixed the problem.  Strangely enough the clock works without the fuse.  The
light, however, does not.

If this does not fix the problem drop me a note.  I know I can find the
schematic in the Factory Service Manual and may be able to find a fix too.
