I use the 8" unpowered Bazooka.. works terrific, tucks right behind the
back seat, and the roll up cover hides it away.  I use a 4 channel Alpine
amplifer.  Two channels are bridged to mono, which gives 150 watts to the
Bazooka.  The remaining channels feed the front speakers (50 watts each),
and the deck power (Blaupunkt CD, 20 watts) feeds the factory speakers I
left in the rear seat area.
