There are two ball joints, not clear from your message which if either 
you are talking about.  One is a little larger and is in the A-arm, at 
its end underneath the spindle (steering knuckle ) and strut (shock). 
 The other ball joint is at the end of the tie rod, the rod which goes 
from the steering rack to the steering knuckle.

In either case, if there is more than roughly a millimeter of play, 
replace the part.
