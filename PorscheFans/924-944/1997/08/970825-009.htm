Bob,please think seriously about taking the following advice.  I have
owned 951s for 10 years now and have blown them up quite
spectacularly....

Buy a VDO 935 boost guage.  They are expensive, but they read 2.0 bar
BOOST (gage, not absolute pressure) and are the size of a 911 clock
(3" or so).

The reason I say to do this is because they have a telltale which stops
at highest boost reading AND they will fit in the driver's side center
air vent without permanaent modification or adapter plates.  The heat/AC
will flow around the gage.

It should be plumbed into one of the two vaccume lines coming odd the
manifold.  DO not tap it into the line to the KLR as it is too small.
I know it's not supposed to matter but it does.

Expect to pay north of $150 for th eboost guage, butit's worth it as it looks way cool.
