The easiest way(besides the light on the dashboard) to check to see if you
have ABS is to open the fuse compartment and see if you have the ABS
Relay(a fuse in the slot marked ABS means absolutely nothing).  I think it
is something like spot G20 in mine.  If you don't have it and there is
plastic covering the slot where the relay goes(including unbroken tabs) 
then you're outta luck.
