I also removed the P/S reservoir and trimmed the seams. This was ~6kmiles ago, 
and still no leaks. I also made a gasket for the P/S reservoir lid, perhaps 
there is a stock gasket, but mine was missing. I also replaced the hose 
clamps. You might try finding some hydraulic fluid hose to replace the section 
in question, certainly would be less expensive than the OEM replacements.

Good Luck!

>I had a similar problem on my 87 944-S,  and based on the good advice 
>of a list member,  I removed the power steering resevior from the car, 
>thoroughly cleaned it, and sanded the little mold seam line marks from 
>the nipple ends on the bottom of the resevior with 400 grit sandpaper.
>
>The fine seam lines were apparently enough to cause the seepage.  Since
>this cheap/no money repair I have had no leaks.
