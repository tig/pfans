In the early 1970s, as a cost-saving measure, Porsche changed the standard
Targa top design to a rigid, non-folding panel, similar to the rigid roof
on the 914. The folding top was offered as an option. I seem to recall that
this lasted for only the 1973-1975 model years, after which they went back
to the folding top as standard equipment. I never personally saw one of
these tops. I have no idea how you would have stored the rigid panel top in
the car.
