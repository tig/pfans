Hey Fans,

Just a cautionary note.  It would be wise to make sure, especially if you
are getting them mail-order, that the H4s (headlight) and H3 (fogs) bulbs
that you buy (upgraded wattage or stock) are GERMAN such as Oxram?
or Hella.  (Tweeks is selling bulbs on sale that are made in Korea).  There
have been some nasty electrical problems (on older MBZ's in particular)
on cars equipped with cruise control when non-German bulbs were
installed as taillights.  Tended to blow out the cruise brain somehow
because the wattage or whatever was just a bit off (so, OK, I'm no
electrical wizard) :-).
