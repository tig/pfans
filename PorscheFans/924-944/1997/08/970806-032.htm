>I only see one listing for 88-89 turbos and don,t understand.  Are all
>88-89's turbo-s models or is the S version a limited production special
>those years with the standard also available?  I realize the S has
>extras like "tweaked" breaks, suspension etc.

Non S Turbo's had 217 HP, S's had 247 HP. In '88 you could buy either the
"normal" 217 HP Turbo or the 247 HP Turbo S. In '89 Porsche called the car
944 Turbo but they were all the 247 HP Turbo S spec. Therefore a '88 Turbo
may or may not be "S" spec whereas all '89's are "S" spec. Visual signs that
the car is a Turbo S  are:

Larger S4 front brakes
Forged Turbo S wheels, 7 & 9 by 16
Adjustable ride height front Sport Yellow Koni's
Limited slip and Transmission Cooler
