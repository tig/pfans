I have a suggestion.  Have someone (it is easy) build a blinker board to
drive the 2 LEDs.  Find the wires from the door to the stock alarm,
disconnect from the stock alarm, hook the blinker board to the  LEDs,
(make certain the polarity + - is correct so they don't burn out) hook
the blinker board to 12Vdc supply.  This will make the door LEDs blink,
all you will need to add is a switch to turn them on and off.  I'll
build the board for you if you wish, but someone local can do it and
Radio Shack has circuits to do this.  Easy!

The LEDs probably run off 1.5 - 3 volts or so, which is standard for an
LED.  I don't have the exact specs, go to Radio Shack (or equivalent) 
and take a look at recommended voltages for their LEDs.
