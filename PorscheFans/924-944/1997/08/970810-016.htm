>>This is an easy fix.  There are two allen screws, mounting the check strap
plate to the door.  Note they are not the cheesehead/triple square type that
mounts the check strap to the door jamb.  The allen screws are probably
loose or missing, causing the mounting plate to snap against the door when
you open it. 

Tighten/replace said allen screws.  At least that was the cause with my 944.

 -Gregor  944 2.7.<<
