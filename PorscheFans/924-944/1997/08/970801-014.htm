Greg,

As you probably know, airbags were standard on all '87 up 951's but a
special order might've been possible (another possiblity is not to
believe the PO).  The easiest way to tell is to look at the VIN - digit
6 should be either a 2 or an 0 and this is the # of airbags the factory
thinks your car has.  I dunno if this'd be different for a special order
so, especially if it's a 2, I'd also put in a call to PCNA @
1-800-545-8039 to double check with them about your specific car (you'll
need the VIN for this).
