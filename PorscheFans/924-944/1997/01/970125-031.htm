damaged tubes so they don't leak.   BUT they don't work
any more either.   If the radiator damage was extensive, you
might not have much of your radiator left working.  Consider
replacement.

Also, I wonder if your thermostat is working?   When the car 
is hot, is the radiator hot?   Is it all hot, or just part of it?
