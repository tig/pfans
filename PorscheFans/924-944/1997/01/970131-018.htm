	David,

	I have an opinion and no real data. But then again, I don't think 
	there is much in the way of real, verifiable data out there on
	this subject. I think sticking a new chip in a stock 951 and
	cranking the boost is a bad thing. I think it puts undue strain
	on the engine and has a definate effect on the longevity of 
	head gaskets and other parts. I think you can put a chip that
	increses boost in the car safely *provided* you modify the engine
	to tolerate the additional stress involved. This, however, is
	not cheap. Personally, I am always dubious of "quick fixes" and
	"miracle cures". In my mind, the words "cheap" and "easy" and
	"horsepower" are all mutually exclusive. Good luck.
