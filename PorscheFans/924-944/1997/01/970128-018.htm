Detonation?  A gentleman posted about pinging.

A car with a drilled cycling valve and wastegate shims accompanying aftermarket 
chips.  I have one ('87 951 with custom chips from Jon Milledge).

My boost gauge reads 2 bar at full boost (i.e., 1 bar of true boost in the 
manifold).  I just put in an aftermarket VDO and now realize that it makes 1.1  
- 1.2 bar of true boost.  My other car with the Autothority set up ('89 951) 
does not get this much manifold pressure (I think it gets to .8 or .9 bar only).

Anyway, Mr. Milledge suggested that I use colder plugs (5 heat range in the 
Bosch product lines) and race fuel on the track, where I am likely to use more 
of the available boost.  I have never heard ping or knock, and hopefully, with 
these precautions I never will.

I have heard that the knock sensors can go bad.  I believe that tests for this 
are described in the factory 944 Turbo service manual supplement.
