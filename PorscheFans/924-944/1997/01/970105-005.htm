Is there a way to adjust the headlight cover/bucket laterally?  When the
headlights are down, there is about 1/2 inch gap on the inward sides of the
headlights, while the outward sides are just about touching the fenders.



FYI, the pitch and yaw of the lights is adjusted by the bolts that hold the
base of the assembly to the frame.  You can move the base for and aft and it
causes the assembly to lift and lower the head lights.

The for/aft up/down of the buckets can be adjusted by loosening the bolts that
hold the headlight pivots on either side of the headlights and moving them up
and down.
