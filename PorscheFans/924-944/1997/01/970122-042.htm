Although you can never really change your oil too often, I do not think
this recommendation can be substantiated with real data.  Considering the
factory recommendations on oil change interval are over twice as long, I
see 3000 miles as extreme overkill, with the exception of 951s.  Oil is
cheap compared to a turbo replacement.

I have used Mobil 1 15W-50 in my '84 Audi 5000S since new and I routinely
go 10K miles or more between changes.  In almost 13 years and 166K miles,
the engine still uses no oil whatsoever in between changes.  The same can
be said for my '92 Civic Si.  87K and counting.
