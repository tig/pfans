I am thinking about adding oil protection to my '87 951.  Here is the overview 
of potential stuff:

(i)     Oil/air separator kit
(ii)    Second oil cooler
(iii)   '89 oil pan with custom baffling
(iv)    Electric Accusump

I have spoken with Mr. Milledge about this-- he provided some excellent advice.

have to pick a good place to run the lines (I don't really want them in the 
cockpit).

I believe that Mr. Woods and Mr. Milledge are old friends, and despite a

believe you could go too far wrong with any advice from either of them!
