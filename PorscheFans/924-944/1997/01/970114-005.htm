Hmmm, are you sure that you have a 1987 924S with a 1987 engine. If so, you
should have the infamous self tensioner on the timing belt, anyway...
That having been said, the belt tension should be set to 4.0 plus/minus 0.3
for a new timing belt and 2.7 plus/minus 0.3 for the balance shaft belt or 
a previously used timing belt.
