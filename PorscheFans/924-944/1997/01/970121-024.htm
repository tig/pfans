The wetness could be a result of a leak in the battery compartment.  It is
common for the battery tray to become corroded and leak water on the passenger
side.  You might want to sand the area and either repaint it or spray rubber
undercoating  on it.  Or simply get a false battery tray to put the battery in.


Water can also enter the car through the opening for the heater blower.  Make
sure that all passageways for water are not blocked.  If any of the drains for
that front area beneath the hood are blocked the water can backup into the
heater hole, and spill into the cabin (I know this for a fact on pre 85 1/2
cars, I don't know about the post '851/2 cars, but check anyway).

Hope you find out the source of your water.
