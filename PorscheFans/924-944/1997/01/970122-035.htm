I bought an 89 951 about a month ago.  Everything works, but it seemed
to me the clutch was a little stiff.  Today I got underneath the car
with the intention of bleeding the slave cylinder to rectify the
problem.  Once I got under there (where's the best place to put your

back in.

am a bit concerned as to what damage could have been done to my
clutch/slave cylinder.  I get the feeling with the slave cylinder being
so close to the opening I am going to be looking at replacement soon,
but I don't have a good feel as to what effect this could have on the
clutch.

Anyone know a good place to get this plug?  Dover DE doesn't have a
Porsche dealer within an hours drive.
