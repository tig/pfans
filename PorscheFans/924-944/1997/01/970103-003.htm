Andrew Reeves, M.D. wrote:
> 
> Hello to all.  I am a new list member, and also the new (and proud) owner of
> a fanatically maintained 1987 944-S.  But...there is a funny noise--low
> pitched--that comes from the right side of the car.  It depends upon wheel
> speed, not engine or gearbox speed.  It's worse when cornering to the left,
> but does increase with right-hand turns as well.  I checked and repacked the
> right front bearings...no problem, no change.  Any thoughts?  Things to check?
> 
Check the tire tread for excessive wear and or seperation, particuarly if
you have run the tire low on air or with too much negative camber at some
point. They can make noises even at very low wheel rotation rates (15MPH)
which will make you think about bearings.

Of course it could also be the rear wheel bearing.
