>     Regarding all the recent posts on service intervals, does anyone have 
>     knowledge or recommendations for limited use, low mileage cars?  I 
>     have 36k miles on an 88 924S.  I had the timing belt done at 27k,  
>     retensioned later and am well aware of the exiting opportunities that 
>     exist with a belt failure.  

Timing belts should be changed at about 30K miles or five or six years, 
whichever comes first, or at a good opportunity (e.g. doing the waterpump,
resealing the front of the engine, etc.)

Letting it go longer than 30K miles is like betting your paycheck on the 
lottery. You may end up winning, but chances are that you will lose it all...

>How about the water pump?  I've heard that

My expectation of the 944/924S water pump is in the 70-100K mile range.

My 83 944 received one at 75K miles.
