times without setting the alarm off. Alarm still works: when I crank the

sensor seems to be erratic.

This is an older alarm, no motion sensors here. The alarm is supposed to
activate when a door, hatch, hood or roof is opened, or if the car is
started. I figured the sensor on the door would be a switch, and the

left fender where the door hinges. Tried shorting this switch and it seems

I still can't figure out what triggers the alarm. The interior light comes

On the other hand when the alarm decides not to work and I open the door,
the interior light comes on and no alarm. All other switches in the
ignition, passenger door, hood, hatch and roof work fine. Anybody know
what triggers the alarm?
