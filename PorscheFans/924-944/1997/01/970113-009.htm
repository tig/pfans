Not to sound flipped, but for that kind of increase in hp, the best use of your 
money would be to sell yours and buy a Turbo(/S).  All the bolt on stuff won't 
do it unless you turbo/super/nitrous/chevy block.

Figure your car is worth $5-8,000 and a turbo is anywhere from $8-17,000 (plus 
a new chip, and eventually a head gasket), or an S2 (my favorite) for about 
$13,000.  IMHO a much better way to go.

(remember, if you up the hp, you will need to up the 
suspension/brakes/wheels/tires/electronics.   All these things, as an optimized 
package, are available direct to you from, Porsche in a '88 Turbo/S or '89 
Turbo or S2 (208bhp) w/M030)
