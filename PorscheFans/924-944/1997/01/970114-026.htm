Here's a funny (not) motor mount tale.... replaced the mounts last weekend
on my '87 944.  Was driving 2 days later when I noticed high temp gauge +
streams of antifreeze from the engine.  Checked it out, and somehow,
mysteriously, the upper expansion-tank-to-radiator hose was now laying
directly on top of the alternator fan, and had gotten a big hole chewed
into it!  The only thing I can think is, that when this hose burst last
summer, the motor mounts had already collapsed, and I positioned it with
adequate clearance for those collapsed mounts.  With the new motor mounts,
the engine must have raised up ~ 1-2cm, to allow the alternator fan to
nibble away at the hose!  I don't know how I missed seeing this...
