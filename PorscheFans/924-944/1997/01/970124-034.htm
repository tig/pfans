Re: John Christensen's fog/driving light query.

My Australian spec '86 951 has "euro" lights as standard.  I presume yours
would work the same as these:

Foglights can come on anytime as long as park lights are on (handy around
dusk or if visibility is not that good, as it allows you to "be seen"
without raising your headlights).  Foglight switch is two position only -
off or on.

The driving lights come on with the headlight high beams.  They are not
separately switched.  Fog lights can be on at the same time (putting out a
fair amount of candlepower out the front end!)

PS. My car has H4s as standard.
