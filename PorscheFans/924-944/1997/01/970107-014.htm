Hi Marianne,

The early 924 (Audi block engine) is notorious for vapor lock problems,
aggravated by a weak fuel pump.  Fewer vapor lock problems occur if your fuel
pump is supplying the proper pressure and volume of fuel.  Check your fuel
pressure and delivery volume and make sure it meets specs.

Another characteristic is if you miss getting it started on the first try when
warm, it takes quite a bit of cranking before the engine catches again.  Some
924 shops add a switch on the dash that opens the cold start value when
pressed.  You hold down the switch while starting a hot-warm engine.  This
helps speed the purge of the vapor from the fuel system.  I haven't heard of
anyone that found a complete solution to the warm start stumble.

Good luck!
