Hi Michael,

If you are a "reasonable" street driver, don't accelerate hard out of corners,
and stay off the tracks, you may get 20K miles out of the Dunlops.  If you
accelerate hard, the rears will wear out before the fronts.  If you add track
driving to your street driving, 4-5K miles will be your maximum life.

I haven't ever met a "reasonable" 968 driver, so ... find a friendly tire
store manager who realizes that you are a frequent buyer.  At least you will
get good service and a little bigger discount.
