     Raise the tail and rotate one wheel.  If the other wheel rotates the 
     same direction, that's limited slip.  If it rotates the opposite 
     direction, then it is not.
     
     If you pull the carpet off the back, and look next to the spare tire, 
     there should be a white sticker that lists all the option numbers (at 
     least it does on the 944).  My car (87 944S) did not list the LSD, so 
     I did the above test to confirm.
