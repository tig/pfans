Just a friendly reminder to those who live in wet climates (like myself 
in the Pacific Northwest US).  Check your trunk storage wells for water 
accumulation, and remove your valuables/speakers/stereo gear.

I am getting water entering the two rear storage wells on either side of
the trunk.  The water is draining out, and the drain hoses from the
sunroof and hatch pin locks appear to be routed and draining properly. 
However, there was enough of a puddle at the bottom of each well to (1)
short out one of my stereo amplifiers, and (2) soak the factory tool kit
and start it rusting.  Grrrr. 

The hatch seal seems to be intact, and I have no idea what to do, besides 
replacing it.  In the meantime, I am drying the amplifier in the kitchen 
oven!  ;-)
