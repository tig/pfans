Surya,
The screw is on the very top of the throttle assembly (where the throttle
cable attaches). It is used to control the idle speed. It is down inside a
hole so you need to look inside. If you plug the hole with your finger and
the idle speed changes, it is missing or leaking. The part number is
 951.110.919.00 according to George Beuselinck .
