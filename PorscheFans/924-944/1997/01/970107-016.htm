Hi Clark,

Disconnecting the power for a period of time results in the need for a on
board computer reset to clear the airbag fault indicator.  This is a normal
occurrence whenever the power is disconnected for a period of time.  It will
take the dealer about 15 minutes labor to fix it.  It requires reading the
computer fault memory, and then resetting it.  I don't know how or even if you
can do it yourself.
