Just had mine aligned during the Thanksgiving holidays with excellent
results. Here are the specs:

Front Toe= +.1 deg right and +.1 deg left (total +.2 deg)
Front Camber= -.5 deg
Front Caster= factory spec
Rear Toe= +.1 deg right and +.1 deg left (total +.2 deg)
Rear Camber= -.5  deg

These worked well after I adjusted my tire pressures (29 front; 34 rear).
This is set up for street driving and some light track duty (I stayed away
from radical changes beacuse I didn't want to have to change my tires too
often) I also wanted to stay away from 0 deg toe beacuse I've had poor
results, at speed, in the past  (not on this model car though). Total of
front align, rear align, rotate and four tire balance was $190 with tax.
