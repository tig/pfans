On Tue, 21 Jan 1997 modmech@juno.com wrote:

> I read somewhere that when the 944 gets to certain temperature that one
> and only one fan should come on and if it gets any hotter the other fan
> will kick in. If this is true does anyone know what would cause the first
> fan not to kick in. The only time my fans come on is together when the
> temp gauge touches the red, and when I turn on the A/C or defrost.

	Yeah, something's wrong somewhere. You say you can
	get both fans to come on eventually? If so, then you've got a
	faulty thermo-fan switch. Or, less likely, a bad connection.
	I don't rememeber the exact temps. but on my car, here's what
	happens: temp. reaches middle of the range between the two marks,
	first fan comes on, then temp. reaches second upper mark and
	second fan kicks in. Things should cool off now and second fan
	will shut off. If you turn on the a/c, both fans kick on 
	immediatly. Check your thermo-fan switch. Don't run this
	car hot! Good luck.
