changed, then change them immediately.

Keep the evidence so the next buyer will know when they need
to be replaced.

The belt service is:
1) change them now.
2) have them retensioned in 1500 miles from now.
3) have them retensioned in 15000 miles from now.
4) change them at 30000 miles from now.
