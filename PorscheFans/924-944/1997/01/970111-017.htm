efficient" oils) has a much higher ambient
temperature tolerance than dino oil. 68 deg. F versus 105 deg. F. I prefer
5W30

syrupy oil waiting to flow thru my engine. The synthetics superior high
temp breakdown protection means I can use the 30 instead of dino oil 50.
