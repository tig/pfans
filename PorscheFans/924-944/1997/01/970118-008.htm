Hi Mike,

This is a classic symptom of a fuel pressure loss.  Either your fuel pump
check valve or your fuel accumulator (or both) have failed.   When you turn
the engine off there is not enough pressure left in the system to keep the
fuel from flashing to vapor in the injector/supply lines.  The pump cannot
pump vapor through the injectors, so you won't start.

I would replace both.

Good luck.
