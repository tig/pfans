> I'm not accumulating water in the rear wells, but in the last week have
> started  getting rainwater under the passenger's side seat and into the
> footwell behind the passenger seat (83 944). Problem is it occurs any
time
> the car is in the rain, even when it's not moving... just sitting in the
> driveway is enough. Floorpan is tight, no holes.

Sounds like you have a leaking battery tray...This is a common problem,
caused by the battery slowly leaking acid onto the battery tray (under the
hood, just above the passenger footwell), which will eventually rust
completely through.  When it rains, it can cause a lake to form in the
front & rear passenger footwells.  The best fix is to get a new piece of
sheetmetal welded in, but you can get away with fiberglassing it for a
while.  However, fiberglass will usually start leaking again after a while.

Clint
