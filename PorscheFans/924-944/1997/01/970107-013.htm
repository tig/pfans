"Tracy Meyer wrote about a possible head gasket leak."

Have your cooling system pressure checked, you may have a slow leak, I did.

If you do decide it is the head gasket, go with the wide fire ring gasket and
Raceware studs.  You will have to double nut, heat the old studs and use a long
breaker bar to get them out.  Porsche sticks the studs in with some super
Locktite and heat is the only answer.  Have a Porsche knowledgeable machine shop
check and surface the head.  Have the valves checked, the exhausts on a turbo
take a beating.  If you do not need new guides and a valve job then at least put
in new seals.  

Slap the sucker back together and hit the road.  It should be Spring by then and
the driving will be great.

Jim Richmond
