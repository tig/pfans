Back in mid-November, we had a discussion about windshield wiper chatter,
possibly due to residue of Rain-X applied 13 years ago.  Andrew
 suggested cleaning the windshield with Strip It, a product
available at Wal-Mart, followed by Bosch wiper blades.   I've gone that route,
tested in some good rainstorms, and here's what happened:  First I installed
Bosch Micro Edge Excel blades ($9.99/pr at Autozone), which Bosch claims are,
among other things, supposed to reduce chatter due to use of a different rubber
for the edge and for the substrate.  These alone did reduce the magnitude of the
chatter, but not enough to satisfy me.  Then I bought the Strip It paste at
Wal-Mart (in the section where they sell wiper blades), and followed the
instructions. This did the trick.  If you look real close at the ends of the
blades as they wipe, they do quiver a little, but for all practical purposes,
the chatter is gone -- after 13 years of enduring it!.  One hint:  A little of
that Strip It goes a long way.
