The Wichita Region of PCA is getting ready to embark on the 2nd annual 24 
Hours TO Daytona. Last year we had 13 members attend the race and it looks 
like this year the number has increased to around 24 (coincidence?). Last 
year we rented one motorhome (of course, some flew) and drove straight 
through the worst ice storm of the winter (remember how cold it was?) to see 
some fabulous racing.

This year we're renting two motorhomes and planning the same road trip. Our 
infield spot is in the Green (I think) motorhome section, the one closest to 
the tunnel where the cars come off of the banking onto the grandstand 
straight.

Anyway, look for our PCA banner and stop in and say "hi."

World Grand Touring (GT) Championship will be racing at Sebring on Oct. 18 
and Laguna Seca on Oct. 26? You can check out the press release on IMSA's 
home page. Looks like another road trip in the motorhome.
