I am new to the list and a new and very happy Porsche 951 owner. My

when cruising. I also have heard a large rush of water under the
passenger side footwell when starting the car after it was cold. Both of
these problems started at the same time. Last, but certainly not
least...at 80mph in both 4th and 5th gear I have experience what my shop
calls misfiring. The car begins to buck as if the fuel supply has been
cut. It does not happen when cruising, only when accelerating.
Am I nuts? Or does this seem excessive for a car that checked out fine
at 71,000.
I am not implying any foul play on the part of my current shop. They are
reasonably priced, friendly, eager to please and I genuinely like them.

specializes only in Porsches and knows a great deal about 9441s.
Any recommendations, advice or suggestions would be greatly appreciated.
