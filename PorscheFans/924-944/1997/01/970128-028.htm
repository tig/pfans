As far as the cold start problem and the low rev's I would first try a fuel
filter.  It is the least expensive thing to replace.  Here is why I think
it could help.  When the car is cold it takes more fuel to keep it going. 
It takes less once warm so the clogged filter will have a lesser effect
once the car warms up.  The restricted fuel flow is causing the RPM lag. 
It takes more HP to get the same RPM's in higher gears, so thusly it takes
more gas.  If the filter doesn't do the trick look for other blockages in
your fuel delivery system.
