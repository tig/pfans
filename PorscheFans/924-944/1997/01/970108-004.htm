Web-Cam has a performance grind they can do by hard-welding the lobes on
your cam.  They want a little over $400 for the work, but my experience has
been that they set the standard by which the rest may be judged.  They are
very quick also.  Call them at 909-369-5144.  I have used them many times
and have always been impressed.
