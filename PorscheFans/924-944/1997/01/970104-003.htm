No.  A "951" is the factory model designation for a 944 Turbo.  This
number is on the sticker by the spare tire.  Similarly, a 931 is for a =
924
Turbo.  Thus, they are just shorthand ways of saying the same thing.

The 911 world has similar types of designations, with the earlier 911
Turbos being 930s.  A late model normal 911 is a type 993 and the =
previous
Carrera 2 version is a 964.  It can be confusing at times.
