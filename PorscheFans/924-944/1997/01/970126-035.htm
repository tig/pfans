On Sun, 26 Jan 1997 GARY_GOELKEL@HP-PaloAlto-om5.om.hp.com wrote:

> I have a 94 968 with the "Porsche CR-1" stereo.  The head unit was designed
> to control a 6 CD changer unit.  I checked with my local dealer, and they
> wanted $1500 for the changer.  Does anyone know what brand of changer works
> with the "Porsche" control head, and if the wiring harness for the changer is
> present in the car?

Remove your present stereo, and look at the labels to determine if it is a
Blaupunkt/Bosch, Panasonic/Matsushita or Clarion built unit.  If so, you
may be able to use the associated brand CD changer with the unit.  In the
United States, there is a stereo mail order outfit, Crutchfield, which can
help out with a phone call.  They sell CD changers to supply current model
Volkswagens with the 'Premium' sound system, and include a wiring adaptor
to allow the unit to plug into the head unit. Maybe the Porsche stereo is
similar enough that they can do the same.  1-800-955-3000 or http://
www.crutchfield.com.  There is an article in the current issue of
_European Car_ which details using an aftermarket CD changer with the VW
factory stereo.
