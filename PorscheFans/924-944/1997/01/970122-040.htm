Ville,

	These are for the GT, do not have any part info for the GTS/GTR
cars. As a comparison I will give the GT # as well as an '81 931 #.


> Does anyone know Carrera GT and/or GTS part numbers or specs for following
> parts:
> cylinder head
	GT: 	931.104.901.02
	931:	931.104.901.03

> intake valve
	GT:	931.105.401.02
	931:	931.105.401.02 (same in both cars)

> exhaust valve
	GT:	931.105.406.02
	931:	931.105.406.02 (same in both cars)

> camshaft
	GT:	931.105.021.01
	931:	046.109.021.    (last 2 digits are not listed)

> tappet (cam follower?)
	GT:	931.105.121.00
	931:	046.109.311.     (last 2 digits are not listed)

> valve spring(s)
	GT:	046.109.623 / 046.109.633   (outer/inner)
	931:	046.109.623 / 046.109.633   (outer/inner)

> fuel injector
	GT:	931.110.225.00
	931:	931.110.225.00  (same)

> fuel distributor
	GT:	931.110.907.02
	931:	931.110.907.02  (same!!)

> intercooler
 	GT:	931.110.033.04  (plus lots of little associated mounting/rubber bits)
