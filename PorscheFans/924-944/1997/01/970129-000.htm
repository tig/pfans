I had the same problem with my Bell 735. I solved it by moving the
detector up the windscreen, instead of behind the wipers. These things
generate microwaves of their own, and the waves were reflecting off the
wipers and causing false alarms. I haven't had a problem since (only one
ticket, too :-().
