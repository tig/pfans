Re: improved shifting

my '86 951's gearbox shifted very difficult, particularly in cold weather it 
was very difficult to go from 1 to 2 and 2 to 3,

my local mechanic suggested to replace transmission fluid (old one came out 
like heavy syrup, proably 10 years old) with a newer type of fluid and treat 
box with teflon: the result is unbelievable: smooth shifting for below 100 
bucks, even in cold weather, I felt it immediately and it improved after 60 
miles. the effect is fantastic .

my question: is there anyone with (long(er) term) experience with teflon 
treatment in this application and are there any downsides to this ??

so far I am thrilled, but never thought about future problems.
