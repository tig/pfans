     Dave,
     
     I just stuff paper towels under the filter before I remove.  If you 
     remove the plastic under pan it is easier to get the towels under the 
     filter from underneath.  I also remove the power steering fluid fill 
     tank to gain better access to the filter.
     
     I usually spill some oil, but I spend the time to clean it off.  
     That's another reason to remove the bottom pan. 
     
     Just use lots of paper towels and make a cradle for the oil to pool 
     into.  The towels will absorb it if you do it right.
