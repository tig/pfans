The problem with the sunroof becoming unlatched is most likely
because of a small micro-switch located in the front edge of the
sunroof.  It sits behind a small triangular plate right in the middle. 
The best thing to do is remove the sunroof and access this switch being
careful not to break the cover plate (lesson learned when I replaced
mine).  Just replace this small switch and you should be back in
business.
