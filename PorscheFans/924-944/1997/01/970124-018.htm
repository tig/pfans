Okay, I have recieved several threads along these lines.

I spoke with my brother in England and his car (Ford) does indeed use the rear
defroster as an antenna. The circuit to do this is relativly simple. It
isolates the DC power of the defroster and matches (tunes) the wires to the
middle of the FM band. (All you want is the RF signal). This is the simular to
the devices that you may have seen advertised which turn power cables in houses
into a TV antenna. (Those are always on and are 110 V).
