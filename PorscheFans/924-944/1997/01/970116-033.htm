	One time I had my sunroff off and had extended the arms that hold
the sunroof to the car. I had started the engine and they retracted. The
reason they retracted was that there is a micro switch at the center of
the opening. It is under a black square or rectangle plastic. Remove
you sunroof and press on the plastic to here if it clicks. If it does
it does not mean it works, but at least you found it. You will have to
take a meter and ohm it out. It may also be out of adjustment.
