On Thu, 23 Jan 1997, Michael wrote:

> The A/C compressor is out on my '82 924. Rebuild replacements are around
> $500. The compressor on my Jaguar XJ-S cost around $200. And the Jag A/C is
> much colder, in a bigger car, than the 924's ever was. Are there any A/C
> experts on the list with advice on changing the 924's compressor to the same
> type as in the Jag? 

Sadly, AC capacity is dependent on the size of the condenser and 
evaporator, as well as the compressor.  If it is a York piston type
compressor, you may be able to upgrade it to a rotary unit (Sanden) as 
used on the later cars.

You might find your AC runs colder with a new original compressor anyway, 
especially if the old one was tired and not compressing efficiently.
