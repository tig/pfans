I drive a 85.5 944 that I just put 195/65R15 Pirelli 210P Winters on. We
just had a great taste of winter in Vancouver with 34cm of snow in one day.
I found these tires to be great. I normally drive with V rated 16" all
season Goodyear's and could not climb any small hill, but will the
Pirelli's, hills are not a problem. The only problem with our cars is
ground clearance, too much snow and you become a plow.
