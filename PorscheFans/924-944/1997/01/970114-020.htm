Hi all 924/944 enthousiasts,

To adjust the timing belt on my 87 924S I don't need the tensioning gauge
P9201.

From the 1987 model year (H-serie), the tensioning has been semi-automatic,
eliminating the need for the fiddly gauge on the timing belt. The tensioner
clamping bolt is simply loosened, the engine manually turned over once or
twice to balance the tension along the whole belt and then retightened.

This is a good example of the continuous technical improvements from Stuttgart.

I'm not sure whether/when this modification has been applied to the US-version.
It might be interesting to check this out before hurting your knuckles.

Good luck!
