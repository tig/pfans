Use the small jack to raise the car enough to
put a coupld of 2 x 4 blocks under the nearest wheel, 
then use the larger jack to get under the car.  Most jacks 
are about the same height, and have difficulty gettin under
low cars.  I always have this problem with  911s, so I use
the normal jack pad to do the first lift, then go all the way
underneath later.  You can avoid one stage of this hassle 
by just driving onto the blocks to begin with.
