Ronald, 
  Just a note reference your possible 82 944 purchase. Dave made 
excellent comments concerning what to watch out for. However, 944's 
WERE made in 1982. These are ROW cars, NOT U.S. spec cars!! Now 
someone might have imported this car from Europe and done so when the 
EPA/DOT had a window for importing these "grey" cars without meeting 
their standards. OR they might have imported it and done all the 
necessary EPA/DOT conversions. If the car is in good shape it might 
be worth your while since these ROW 82 944's have a 10.6:1 
compression and 163 bhp. Definately faster out of the box than the 
143 hp U.S. versions.  My friend next door has an 82 944 and we both 
race slaloms with PCA-GR.
