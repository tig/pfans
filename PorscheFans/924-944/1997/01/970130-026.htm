The both the S2 and the TS have a lot more HP than the NA. If you're
working with a limited budget, look to the 944na. The S2 pulls stong over
the whole rev range. It's peak horsepower is comparable to the 944 Turbo
(not TS). The Turbo has to spool up to about 4k rpm before the fun starts.

If your friend is looking for a mostly street/occasional drivers ed car,
I'd recommend the S2; it's power is more useful in around-town driving. If
he's looking for the ultimate in performance, the Turbo S can't be beat.

I don't know much about the 911SC's, but they do have significantly less
power than the 944 Turbo and S2. Another obvious differenc is age. The 911
is 10-12 years older than the 944's.

Have fun shopping!
