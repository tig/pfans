Hi gang,
I have a fairly large collection of Porsche toys (about 3000 pieces) and a
good amount are of the 924/944 variety.  The models come as small as the
1:100 scale up to a 1:8 (I think).  Your best bet is to look for toy OR Train
shows in your paper.  I say Train shows because many times you'll find great
Porsche models that people were using in their train gardens and are selling
really cheap.  My favorite models are Majorette 1:24 pieces that are
decorated with the Turbo Cup series decals.
