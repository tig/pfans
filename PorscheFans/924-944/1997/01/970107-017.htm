Hi Marv,

Disconnecting the o2 sensor may cause the 951 to run slightly richer. 
Otherwise, you won't notice a difference.  In the early 951 days, a story
circulated stating that you could get more performance out of a 951 by
disconnecting the o2 sensor.  I tried it at the time, but couldn't notice any
difference.  It did raise the exhaust emissions very slightly.

I would be surprised if you could feel any difference during a test drive.
