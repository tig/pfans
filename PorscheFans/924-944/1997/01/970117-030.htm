The 951 instrument cluster is modular, with a couple of large multiple
plugs connecting all the inputs.   The instruments are tied together in
back by a flexible circuit board.  A problem such as yours is usually in
the connecting plug (bad contact) or a crack in the circuit board.  You might
be able to deal with the connector simply by unplugging and replugging.
A circuit board problem would require removal and repair by a VDO 
shop.   Your problem is not uncommon.
