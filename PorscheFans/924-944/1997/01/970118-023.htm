I just had my timing belts, rollers, and front end seals replaced. Quote
was originally 'about $500' depending on the condition of the rollers.
Actual came to $635 with all five rollers replaced. I'd say your estimate
is about right: $110 seal kit, $300 labor for mine.

A word of caution: Test drive the car immediately and 'listen' for any
vibrations that were not there before especially above 3000 RPM. I have
to take mine back Monday so they can orient the balance shaft correctly
this time. I understand this is a very common SNAFU.
