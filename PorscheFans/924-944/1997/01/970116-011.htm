	Replacement should be done every 30K with a retensioning at
	the 2K mark after the replacement. I don't use a dealer so I
	can't comment on that but belt replacement for me runs around
	$250 at my shop if I don't have to replace any of the rollers.
	The rollers run between $45 and $80 depending on which of them
	you're replacing and there are 5 of them. Hope this helps.
