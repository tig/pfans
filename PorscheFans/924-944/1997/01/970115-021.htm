I don't think there is any reason to install a lower temp thermostat in a car.
 The thermostat is there to keep your car running at an optimum temperature.  A
lower temp thermo would open sooner, it could prevent the engine from fully
warming up.  The biggest thing is to make sure that the thermostat *is* opening
up when your car gets warm.

I can see in race applications, you might want to remove your thermostat to
allow maximum flow of the water thru the engine, however for daily driving I
don't think it is neccessary.
