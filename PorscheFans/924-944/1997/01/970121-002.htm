ModMech@juno.com wrote:
I read somewhere that when the 944 gets to certain temperature that one
and only one fan should come on and if it gets any hotter the other fan
will kick in. If this is true does anyone know what would cause the first
fan not to kick in. The only time my fans come on is together when the
temp gauge touches the red, and when I turn on the A/C or defrost.>


I think the temp sensor on the radiator has two wires going to it. (?)
So it may be a two temp sensor.  Also make sure the fans are rotating 
correctly. Reading for the AUTOMOTION catalog: "After working on the
924 or 944 fan,make sure it is rotating in the correct direction. 
standing in fron of the car facing toward the rear,the fan should 
rotate clockwise, inadvertenly switching wires will cause the fan to 
run backwards, causing your engine to overheat, and possibley leading 
to expensive engine damage." Good luck
