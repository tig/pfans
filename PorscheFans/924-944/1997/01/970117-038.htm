Hi, Jim,

According to Betty Jo Turner in the November '85 issue of Panorama,

"The 944 saw significant improvement with the introduction of the so-called 85/2 in the 
spring of 1985.  Among the 42 listed improvements were suspension and engine changes 
(including a new crankshaft, new balance shaft, and modified combustion chamber), new 
transmission mounts and modified transmission case, a larger capacity gas tank, flush 
mounted windshield, integrated windshield antenna, new seat generation and a new dash 
with greatly improved heating and ventilation."

I hope this helps.
