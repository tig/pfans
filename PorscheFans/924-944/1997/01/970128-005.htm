A good place to start to enhance your 944's performance is Automotion which
has their web site.  If you have some spare change, buy a copy of
Excellence or European Car (assuming you are in N. America).  All you
questions should be answered. 

From my painful experience, I advise you to gather more info on the exhaust
system before you make a choice.  If you buy a wrong exhaust system (eg. an
universal one), you will alter the back pressure required by the engine and
hence trigger unwanted results.  In my case, I bought a high flow muffler,
resonator tip and replaced the cat. converter with a tube resonator.  Low
rpm throttle response is killed although high rpm is now smoother.
