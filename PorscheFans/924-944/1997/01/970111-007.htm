The Deltran SuperSmart Battery Tender I just bought for my 356 came with a harness 
that attaches permanently to the battery.  This has a quick-disconnect plug so that the 
Battery Tender can be plugged in easily for charging.  Obviously, they do not expect 
you to disconnect the battery ground strap before charging.

BTW, Deltran claims normal trickle chargers are not suitable for long-term use.  To 
quote, "Trickle chargers decrease current but increase voltage to upwards of 15.5 
volts and continue the excessive gassing action (the gassing point of a battery is 13.8 
volts) that is most detrimental to the life of batteries".  In any event, for long-term 
charging of lead-acid batteries, they remind you to check the water level periodically.
