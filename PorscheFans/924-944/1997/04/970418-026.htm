Thore,

          > Another thing, is it possible to jack only the rear or
          > front of the car up and place it on jack stands? Is this
          > safe?

Absolutely - I have only two jacks stands and won't buy two more until
I need to get both ends up.  Just make sure you chock the wheels that
are on the ground.  If you're raising the front, you might set the
parking brake (after it's up) in addition to chocking the wheels.  I
make sure the shift is in neutral when I'm raising the car (so as to 
avoid strain on the trani - but this may not be necessary).  Make sure
you understand where the jacking points are in the front - there's been
some confusion about this.  If you have the Haynes manual, look at  
their picture of
the underside of the front -- it will unambiguosly locate the front
jacking points for you.

Have fun!
