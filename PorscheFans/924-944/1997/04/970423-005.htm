Thanks to all who responded reference my question concerning using a 
motor flush prior to an oil change.  I think I will hold off using the 
motor flush.  Just took the throttle switch assembly off my 85.5 944na. 
 Could not believe the crud and gunk inside.  Cleaned it all up, 
(including the gunk inside the intake manifold), put it back on and my 
idle speed increased 500 RPM.  After adjusting the idle speed, the 944 
started quicker, ran smoother (didn't think I could get my 944 to start 
any quicker or idle any smoother) and noticed a big difference in low 
end torque and more power overall.  It even sounds better!!   

Just thought I'd pass along the information.
