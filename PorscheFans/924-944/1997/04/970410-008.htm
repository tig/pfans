        The problem with the lines is not that they work loose (at least I have 
        not seen this), but that they are susceptible to bursting caused by 
        rubbing against something or corrosion from brake dust.  I have had two 
        burst on my VW Jetta.  The lines were less than 2 years old.  If you do 
        decide to go with these lines you MUST check them on a regular basis for 
        wear and tear.  These line are not like the stock ones that are mostly 
        install and forget.
