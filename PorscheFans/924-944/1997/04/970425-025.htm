Carsten,

I'm not sure how similar my 951 is to your car, but both 944 variants that I
have owned activated the mirror heaters when the rear window "defogger"
switch was toggled on (when used to heat rear glass).  Your mirror heaters
may not be functioning properly (or you haven't been able to prove that they
work) if you're having problems with your defogger's circuitry, as you
suggested in your original message.  Within a minute of turning on my rear
glass heater, my side mirrors generate the tell-tale frost patterns on the
surface where the heat has cleared up portions of the glass at different
rates (near the heating elements themselves).  A very useful feature...
