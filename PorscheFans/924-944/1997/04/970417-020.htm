Hi Russ,

Most motor flushes are thin solvents that probably dissolve
some combustion deposits from the engine interior.   They end
up being pumped anyplace oil gets pumped.   With the 944
engines already "challenged" in the #2 rod bearing area, I
personally would not touch the stuff, but rather would do a 
couple of short time oil changes instead.    

If a customer ever asked me to flush his car with one of these
products I would direct him to K-Mart and wish him/her luck.

Dave
