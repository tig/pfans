Jeff,

	Wheel Bearings requite special Porsche brand screwdriver, Metric 
Alen, aand a pair of channel locks.

1. pop off dust cover (Pop is sorta like pry)

2. Using Metric Allen undo locking nut (insert allen & turn till easy  to 
move)

3. Tichten down untill the Screwdriver can only just move the washer 
under nut (to tight it will not move)

4. Lock nut by turning the allen the other way firmly

5. Replace dust cover (use special tool - Hammer !) keeping the cover 
in line.

Thats it turkey
