Here's a better way to do it.

rpm * 60 * 2pi * radius / 12 / 5280 / axle ratio / gear ratio = speed (mph)

3000 * 60 * 2pi * 14 / 12 / 5280 / 5 / 0.6 = 83 mph

going 83 mph.  (Not that I've ever gone that fast, of course.  *cough*)
