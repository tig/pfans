     I owned an immaculate 944S for 5 trouble-free years.  Nice car, with 
     lots of "extras" as standard, but rather disappointing from a 
     performance point of view, despite 190HP versus 165HP for the 8-valve 
     (I live in England, and I think we get a few more horses than the US). 
     
     I've driven a few 8-valve  944s, and the low-end torque was better thn 
     my S.  Yes, once above 4500 rpm the S picked up its skirts and flew, 
     but how often can you drive like that in everyday traffic.  I hardly 
     ever used 5th gear.  I was also never impressed by the brakes despite 
     renewing everything.  
     
     Last year I switched to a 3.0 S2, and the difference is stunning.  The 
     S2 is everything I wanted the S to be.  Loads of low-down torque and 
     brilliant (ABS) brakes.  Servicing costs are only slightly higher than 
     any other 944 (but with all those valves,don't break a cam belt......) 
      
     
     If you are thinking of upgrading from a 2.5 944 forget the S, find a 
     few more _ ($) and go for an S2.   .
     
     
     Mike
