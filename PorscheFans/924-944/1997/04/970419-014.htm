Main differences that I'm aware of:

Bigger S4 calipers on the front, cross drilled rotors front and back.
Adjustable ride height Konis on the front, rear Koni's had a barrell shaped
"helper"   spring that was used in conjunction with torsion bars.
30mm front sway bar (non-adjustable), 21?mm rear three position adjustable
rear sway  bar.
