Dave wrote:

> Finally the PO also replaced the Drivers side door, and whoever did the
> job did it terribly.  I am going to be taking it apart to fix it but I
> have some questions: 
> 	1) Which window switch is *supposed* to open the drivers side window
> the forward one or the rearward one?  I think mine might be backwards.
> 	
The switch closest to the driver is for the driver's side.
>
> 	2) My window vibrates if I close the door with it open, I assume the
> bracket inside is loose, is everything pretty accessible?  I've done
> this before, but not on a car with power windows so I imagine it will be
> somewhat different.
> 
The regulator assembly (the scissors mechanism which raises and lowers the
window) frequently is loose on these cars. Pull the interior cover off the
door and tighten the bolts. If you only want to do it once use some blue
Loctite on the bolts. Everything is easily accessible inside there.
>
> 	3) When I lock/unlock the drivers side door it doesn't unlock the
> passenger door.  The pwr lock switch on the centre console works
> correctly.  When I unlock the passenger side door it unlocks both, but
> only locks itself.  What is supposed to happen?  Any suggestions?
> 
There is a small plastic linkage between the lock motor and the lock
mechanism. It gets brittle with age and breaks. Easy and cheap to replace.
