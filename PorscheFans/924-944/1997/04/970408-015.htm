I assume that you've already applied liberal amounts of a penetrant
solution like WD-40, over a several day period??
If so, try this: Try to turn the bolt clockwise.  Be very careful to
control this-you only want to turn this a few degrees at most.  This
(sometimes) serves to break the corrosion free.  Good luck.
