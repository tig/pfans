Michael, you definitely will have to bleed the coolant system.  If you
don't have a pressure bleeder, run the car until it is at standard
operating temp with the heater on, open the bleeder screw on the upper
rad. hose on the engine block slightly.  If air is in the system, it
will hiss out.  Keep it open until you get a steady flow of water out
the bleeder.  Tighten it (not overtighten, the screw will break), and
check you coolant level.  It is a good idea before you start to put a
new crush washer under the screw.
