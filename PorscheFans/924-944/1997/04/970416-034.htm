The MAIN reason for sending this message is because of the statement you
made about blower belt breakage and the results of a broken belt.

engine and it will run fine, of course you lose about 70% of your power,
but it will run fine, and if it breaks under throttle , not a problem
either. A few years back I was running the Camaro for my NHRA license
,and during one of the passes about 3/4 track the belt snapped(you can
even see the belt flop accross the track on the video tape :) ), well ,
the result, ......
nothing, I slowed down , drove back to my trailer and replaced belt.
