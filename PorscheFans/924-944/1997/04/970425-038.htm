Joe, are you sure that the squeal was not just belt slippage from the
antifreeze being on thre ac/alt belt, and possibly causing the "smoke"
also.  Rinse the belt with water (won't hurt anything) let it dry and
start it up.  Also, check the tension of the belt and see if it is
loose.  One other thing, if it squeals constanly, is the compressor
clutch turning, or is it locked up (SEEN THAT MANY TIMES)?  The clutch
or compressor not turning will definitely cause a squeal and smoke.
