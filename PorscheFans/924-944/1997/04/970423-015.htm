> Folks,

> A recent posting (yesterday from Christopher Hanlon) suggested that
> a gas gauge that reads 1/4 tank below actual can be remedied by
> cleaning the contacts on the sending unit on top of the tank.
> I tried this last night, without success.  The contacts were
> bright and shiny but the gauge still reads low.  Is there an
> adjustment for this?

No adjustment for this, but you can remove the sender from the tank (see
the factory manual or Haynes for the procedure) and examine the
float/contact assembly for proper action (watch the gauge and move it up
and down).  From a posting I saw on the defunct Porschephiles list, one
can gently bend the contacts to make better contact with the resistive
strip, as these seem to wear or otherwise make poor contact over time. 
Please keep your fire extinguisher handy.  ;-)
