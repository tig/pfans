Hi Ben,

Blinker fluid, headlight fluid, horn fluid, all this stuff is the same 
and your car has an inexhaustable supply in the metal wiring. Electrons, 
a truely renewable resource. Prior to the Federal Coulomb  
Regulatory Bill,
there *was* a black-market in electrons, with some vendors claiming "low 
viscosity" additives etc.  But most of these claims don't stand up
under scrutiny.  And, like you, none of the unhappy consumers were  
able to
locate the filler cap.

If the blinkers don't work, AND the emergency 4-way blinker doesn't  
work,
it's probably the flasher (one of the modules in the relay/fuse
box).  Otherwise the turn signal switch.

T. Leen
