Try the instrumentpanel-back. The gauge is fitted with an screwed-up
two-pin contact. Remove the contact and clean surfaces with tape-head
cleaner.
It worked with my car. It also might be a loose resistor in the gauge. To
repair this, you have to spend some more time, but it can be fixed. Be
carefull while taking the panel apart.
