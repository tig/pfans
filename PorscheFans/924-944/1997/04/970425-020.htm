The "switch" is located in the right hatch shock. The left shock is used for
the defogger, the right one is on chassis (ground). If the hatch is open the
cable on the right shock is connected to mass and your interior and luggage
light is switched on (they always come together). When closed the outer
cylinder moves underneath the clip with the cable, opens the connection and
the light is out.  
No switch! Only a cheap trick.....
