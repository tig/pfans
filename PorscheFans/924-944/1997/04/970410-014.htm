Rich,

The fumes are most likely comming from the top of the tank. The problem is
that the tank has to be dropped. To check, undo the cover which encloses
the filler neck from the inside of the hatch. You can usually see any
signs of leakage on the hoses. If you get lucky it will be a hose that can
be replaced from the top.

Remember to use an appropriate sealant after you are done to seal up the
cover again. RTV I think it is called. The factory uses some nasty stuff
that looks and acts like tar.
