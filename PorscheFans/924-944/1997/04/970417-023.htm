For what it's worth, had exactly the same problem in my 83 944NA. Fuel pump 
relay, under the dash. If it's the same part in the 924, it's a VW/Audi 
part. Get the PN from Porsche then truck on over to the VW dealer and see 
if they have it. Saved me about $15. Not a big deal, but worth a try.
