Steve,


My '84 944 used to do this, and now my '89 951 is starting to do this.  To fix
my older 944, I cleaned the contacts to the sending unit in the gas tank.
 Underneath the rear carpet/sound deadener is a square piece of cloth that is
glued over the sender in the gas tank.  Remove this cloth, you'll see wires to
the sender.  Remove the wires, clean the contacts, reconnect them...

This may solve your problem.
