However, there may be a alternative cure: 

Electrical gadgets work on a concept of 'Contained Black Smoke - CSM'.
If the CSM leaks out of anything electrical it will cease to function,
witnessed by stereo amplifiers, toasters, silicon chips - whatever.

The cure is simple, just get watever component failed filled up with new
CSM. Sometimes, however, they are sealed, so you have to a buy a
complete preloaded CSM container.
