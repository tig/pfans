Hi Porschefans,

Here is the latest update on my 951 power loss. The mystery remains 
unsolved.

History : The car is 20hp down on power because somewhere something is 
preventing it to run with full turbo pressure. With the control line to 
the wastegate disconnected the car will run with full boost nicely.

Latest investigation : I have checked the cycling valve which controls 
the wastegate through the control line. All lines (control, return and 
pressure) check out ok. When I apply power to the solenoid there is a 
click and the valve opens (resistance of the solenoid = 35ohms). When I 
drive the car the solenoid never gets any power which means the valve 
remains closed limiting boost.

Question : Why is the control unit not sending any signal to the cycling 
valve? What can be the cause of this? All help very much welcome!
