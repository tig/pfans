I had a returning dying car problem in a 924S. Changing the fuelpump relay
didn't work. Because of tachometer cutout I expected an ignition problem.
It turned out to be an internal break in an electrical wire to the coil. If
you cannot find a solution, check the wires to the coil and the plus and
ground wires of the DME.
