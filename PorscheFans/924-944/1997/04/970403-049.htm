Uh, blinker fluid?  They blink, or rather do on most cars, via a
capacitor.  When it reaches its maximum, it discharges the current to the
light bulb.  If your lights come on and stay on, your capacitor is bad.
If they don't come on, there's either a wiring problem or a very bad
capacitor.  Might also be the switch, relay (there's a relay for the
blinkers, right?), or fuse.

Blinker fluid, though?
