I put up a question on the list some days ago:

....What are the most probable reason for a consumption of 2-3 
liters oil in 6000 miles...

I got a number of reassuring answers, all pointing out that this is not
a troublesome level of oil consumption.

This, I already knew...   :-)

Marv de Beque pointed out to me that oil consumption may even go as high
as 1 litre on around 700 miles before that alone warranted a engine
rebuild - according to the Porsche workshop manual.

Don Istook pointed out that if no other problems were seen i. e. fouled
plugs - then it was no big problem. 
  
Thank's to all who responded.
