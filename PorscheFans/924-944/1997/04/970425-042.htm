Tom, you had better check the tire specifications before you but.  Check
the rolling radius on the 225/60 15, and I'm sure that it will not be
what you want.  It will be a fairly tall tire.  the tire company should
have a spec on revolution per mile.  Check that with your current size,
probably 215/50 15, then figure the percentage difference.  This will
raise you gear ratios by that percentage, probably not what you want. 
the tires physically will probably fit, but will be very tall and I
think look unusual.  Also, you speedo/odo will be off the same
percentage.
