Your 944 Turbo will have two anti-roll bars, one at the front and one at the
rear. The "stock" (i.e., non-"sport option") suspension supplies two
relatively small-diameter bars that are replaceable with larger-diameter
bars from a variety of vendors. Weltmeister is a common and long-established
name. Call or e-mail Automotion (www.automotion.com) for prices.
Installation is best handled by a shop that knows how to balance the loads
side-to-side and front-to-rear.

Good luck and may you realize lower times!
