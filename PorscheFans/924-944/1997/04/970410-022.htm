I'll give my impressions of how the tires fit to give an idea of how
245/45R16 Kumhos fit on a 7" rim.  The fronts look fine.  The sidewalls
are not perfectly vertical, but they are nowhere near as angled as they
are on my 225/50R16 Hoosier on the same width rim.

The rear 265/45R16 is also narrower than my 245/45R16 Hoosier on the
same 9" wide rim.  I don't foresee any problems with sidewall flex,
etc.
