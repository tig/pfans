If you want anything of use on your Porsche, call PCNA.  I don't have the
number, but someone else on the list can supply it to you if you want it.
Give them the VIN of your car, and they can tell you the paint code (and
color, in English or German), options, date-of-manufacture if you lost
your "MANUFACTURED BY Dr. Ing. h.c. F. PORSCHE AG WEST GERMANY" sticker,
too.  I also wouldn't be too sure that LM1Y is your paint code, especially
if the dealer claims it doesn't exist.

> So, what color is it?  If I could further impose, I'd like the name in both
> german and english.

Stop by at a local auto parts store, like Auto Palace.  They should have
books lying around with paint codes.  Check under VW/Audi for the year of
your car.  You should be able to go by descriptive color alone (i.e., not
the paint code), since there really aren't many colors.  Then, go buy
yourself a German dictionary.

Mine is easy: Diamant Silber, or Diamond Silver.
