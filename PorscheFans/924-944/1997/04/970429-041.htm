At 11:01 AM 4/30/97 +0800, you wrote:

>Does anyone know how easy it is (and the costs involved) of replacing the
>rear rubber lip spoiler on a 944 with the latter "hard plastic" one as
>found in post 1990 944's and the 968. Is there a conversion kit available?

You have to trade in your 944 rear-hatch for a 924 one (without the rubber
lip), and then you can bolt-on one of the various spoiler kits that are
avaliable through distributor's like AIR.  They have both the standard 968
wing or the higher, adjustable wing from the 968 RS turbo.  You can paint
either to match body color, and they are pretty expensive (~thousand?).  You
should call AIR for exact pricing.
