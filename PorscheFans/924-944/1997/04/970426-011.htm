The shifter is guided sideways by springs inside the the gearbox itself.
Ideally, it is supposed to rest between 3-4. If it isn't, there is still
hope. The plate steel shift lever may gave too much play in the
connection to the shift rod. This can be inspected without too much
trouble, thru' the hole under the shift boot.
