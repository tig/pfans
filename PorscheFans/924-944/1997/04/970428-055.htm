For all sourcing the *cheapest oil filter*, please be aware that Porsche
944 utilizes a inverted filter position, There are many filters that
will fit the filter head, but some (most) do not have the non return
flap that keeps oil in the filter at rest. Porsche 'original' filters
are made by Mann, BTW. It is very common for independent Porsche
suppliers to use the Porsche part nr - this may not be necessarily
indicative of actually selling a OEM or original part. They do say in
the fine print 'The name of Porsche is only used for reference.. yak yak
yak'.
