>   Anyone have any advice as to the best method of keyway removal? I am
> replacing the seals on the balance shaft, camshaft, and crankshaft, they
> are in the way.
> 
>  Should I:
> 1) Grab onto them with vice grips and hope I don't round them off to the

> 2) grind a slot with the dremel tool so I can pry it off with a

>

Take a punch and hammer and give the key a little rap on the face perpendicular
to the shaft. If that doesn't pop it right out it will loosen it to allow vice
grips to pull it out. Worked for me anyway.
