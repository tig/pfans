The Hard plastic cage goes in between the ball, and the A-Arm housing. The 
Clip is covered with a hard caulk type substance in original arms.  The 
Rebuilt A-Arm I received back from Dynamic European Tech. had the entire 
"back" filled in with an epoxy, don't know if they used a clip. The end Cap 
from an early '85 replacement ball joint just fits in the smaller diameter 
region:

with epoxy or a metal washer in between the old and new.
