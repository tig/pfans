Todd,  

I have the Potenza S-O2s on my 87 951.  I have 205/55 and 225/50
and I think they are great.  Lots of grip in the dry and staggering
performance in the wet--especially under braking.  When it is really
pouring down though, they will hydroplane a bit.  I have no experience 
with these tires on the track.  They have also widthstood a top speed
run with no problems and are showing nice, even wear after about
4000 miles.
