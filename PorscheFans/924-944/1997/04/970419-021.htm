I had some of the same symptoms on the Boost and found that the vacuum 
line at the computer under the passenger floorboard had become loose. 
 There is a copper fitting that can be tightened by hand, check that.

As for the idle, mine jumped around until I got some contact cleaner 
and sprayed the inside of the connector to the throttle position 
sensor, all is well now.

Hope that works!!!
