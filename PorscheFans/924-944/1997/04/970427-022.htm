From ground to the heater cable is 12v unconnected and then when connected it
should be almost 0. Think about it!

My 83,944 once had a problem with the fuse box providing a resistance for the
rear defogger circuit.  Such that the fuse used to heat up, and the rear
defogger wasnt powerful enough.  I removerd the fuse and soldered in the two
wires of an external plastic fuse holder clip.  No trouble since (3 years).
