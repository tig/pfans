Hi, George,

It probably is the fuel pressure problem.  But if that's not it, your 
intake valve stems may have carbon deposits on them.  I remember reading 
about rough running when cold problems in Panorama about 10-12 years ago. 
 The deposits caused a lean mixture condition until the engine warmed.  
Porsche's official fix is to blow crushed walnut shells through the 
intake manifold to clean the valve stems!
