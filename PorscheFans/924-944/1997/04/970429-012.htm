        When you take off the balance belt, I will bet you a crate of beer 
     that the balance shaft WILL turn.
     
        There are marks on all the important pulleys; they are there for a 
     reason: use them.
     
        If you cannot see the marks, then you can make more marks with 
     white or silver paint to help you, but be careful not to wipe these 
     off.  The factory marks are pretty easy to see apart from the bottom 
     balance shaft, there is a little raised bit of plastic which has to 
     line up with the groove in the pulley, you have to do this one from 
     underneath.
