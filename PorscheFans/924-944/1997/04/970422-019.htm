I had the same noise. Thought it was the catalytic converter matrix breaking
up inside. Turned out to be a loose sensor tube running from the exhaust
pipe up to the engine bay. The top of the tube and its attachment are right
at the back ofthe engine bay to the left of hte engine as you look at it
from the front. The bottom hooks into the exhaust pipe just aft of the "Y"
that joins the two exhaust manifold outlets.
