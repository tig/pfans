The paper manuals are on infinite back order.  The CD ROM version does not
exist, but they are available on microfiche.

PNA 000 127  ==> Workshop Manual
??? ??? ???  ==> Turbo Supplement  <== Does anyone know this number???
PNA 000 111  ==> Tech Bulletins
WET 614 321  ==> Parts Microfiche (for 1985.5 to 1988)
WET 614 921  ==> Parts Microfiche (for 1989 to 1991)

You will need a reader that has at least 40X magnification.  The parts
microfiche is an oversize format (about 6" X 8").  The rest are 4" X 6".
