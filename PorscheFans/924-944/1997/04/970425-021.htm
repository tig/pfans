On Fri, 25 Apr 1997 Steve_Vojkovic@discreet.com.sg wrote:

> Am installing an aircon. system on my 944S. Was wondering if any readers
> who have the same model and air-con. can give me an idea as to how much
> power loss there is when the unit is operating, ie. is it very noticeable?
> Also do you guys remove the belt during winter, I've heard that this takes
> any drag away from the engine when not in use thereby giving you max.
> power.

The AC system takes quite a bit of power (estimated to be 10 hp or so) 
when the compressor is engaged.  However, when it is off, the drag is 
negligible, and its not worth the time to take off the belt.  To prove 
this, remove the belt and spin the AC pulley.  It will spin like a 
bicycle wheel.
