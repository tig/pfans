I have a MityVac. It's a vacuum pump that you connect to the bleed
nipple on the brake caliper or clutch slave cyl or ....

It works OK, but not great. I've found that it's good for changing
the fluid but not so great at bleeding air from the system. I think
it's because of the vacuum. It's tough to see when all the air is
gone because some air leaks through the threads on the bleed nipple,
and also from the connector on the bleed nipple.

The MityVac kit that I have also has adapters for checking any
component that uses a vacuum. I haven't had to do any of that.

The kit costs about $50.
