The vast majority of early 944s had the three spoke, but a few 4-spoke
wheels made it through.  Check out the famous/infamous comparison test Road
& Track did in 1983 with the 944, a 928, a Corvette, and a Ferrari 308.
The 944 with the old-style dash and the four-spoke wheel was used.  Not
surprisingly (to us, anyway), the 944 was voted the overall winner of the
bunch!
