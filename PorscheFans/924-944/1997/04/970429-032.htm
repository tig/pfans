The Turbo S clutch has two sets of springs.  One set counters the torque of the
engine on the wheels, the other counters the torque of the wheels on the
engine.

I don't know what the turbo cup clutch looks like, but if I were in need of a
clutch, I'd get the turbo cup clutch.
