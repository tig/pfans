>Ezra:

>You want to be careful not to put too much grease in the hub. The 
>factory specified 60 grams for a reason. If you load it up with 
>grease, the hub generates a lot of heat from the brakes, bearing friction, 
>etc. and will cause the grease to melt and form pressure in the form 
>of a gas. This pressure, if allowed to build, will blow out the wheel 
>seals. I've seen CV joint boots blown out because of too much grease 
>used in a racing application.
