For us hobby types, the pressure bleeders (Eezeebleed in particular) are
without a doubt the way to go, especially if you have trouble finding help
when you need it.  You fill a bottle with fresh fluid, hook it up to a
compressor or a partially-deflated tire with 10-20PSI, and have at it. The
only time you have to stop is if the bottle runs empty.
