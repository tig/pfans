> 
> This is a two part question:
> 
> 1)  How much play is acceptable in front wheel bearings?  I have a "wink"
> when grasping the top and bottom of the tire and wiggling, with the wheel off
> the ground.  Maybe 1/32".  Is this ok?

Those races should (ideally) have no noticeable play when checking on
the wheel in that fashion. A surefire way to verify hub play is to have
an assistant braking hard. This makes the hub quite solidly held via the
rotor and caliper. Any play felt at this time will come from other
components.    
> 
> 2)  If this isnt ok, how does one remove the *&^%$#@ hubcap?  Looks like
> another job for a special Porsche tool?

A carpenters hammer using those rounded 'thinghy for pulling nails'
works fine for me. Not excactly a 'Porsche tool' tho'. Put the claw
against the hubcap, the head against the hub and heave.
