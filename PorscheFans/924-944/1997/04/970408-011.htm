Recently someone asked about using a portable pump to change tire pressure
at races.  I spoke with a friend of mine who races very often and he said
he uses a 10lb capacity compressed air canister to change pressure quickly.
This is because the portable pumps are very slow to add a few lbs of
pressure. And mostly when you need to change pressure is when you are going
to grid.  If you can't change it fast enough, either you don't bother or
miss pregrid. I believe you can get this item from a motorsport specialty
shop.  Hope this helps.
