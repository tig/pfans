May I humbly recommend a fellow Porsche friend to invest in tools
appropriate to the very powerful and strong car he owns - now when a
reinvestment opportunity exists? 

I suggest using at least a 1/2" socketed tools for boltheads over 17 mm.
Even a 3/4" will not be overkill - as long as it is room for it.
Remember that when a thin breaker bar shaft flexes it will soften the
quick and snappy motion that is neccessary to release well seated bolts
and nuts.
