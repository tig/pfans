Kevin,

I just put a new stereo in and have the same deal.  Somewhere on the
internet, I acn't remember where I read the cure.
  There is a preamplifer for the antenna which receives it's power on
the small black wire harnessed with the antenna cable.  You must supply
12V to this black wire only when the radio is on or the antenna is
effectively disconnected.  Most stereos have a switched 12V wire output
avail. for amps etc. which can be used for this purpose. (on my pioneer
it is a blue wire)
  Also I found the preamp by accident when installing my alarm. It is
located under the dash between the glovebox and the inner fender at the
top and sure enough, the black wire goes to it!
