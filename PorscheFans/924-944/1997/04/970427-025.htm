STOP!! Before you spend big bucks tracking down that intermittent fan
switch problem, try  a $5 solution which worked for me.

Buy a can of tuner cleaner from Radio Shack and spray into the switch.
The rotate a few times to clean possible dirt and corrosion.

Worked like a charm on mine and also fixed a "bad" power window switch.

Also works wonders on unresponsive remote controls and cordless phones
spilled on once too often.
