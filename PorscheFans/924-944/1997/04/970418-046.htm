Chris, 

I hate to pop your bubble, but the gauge is showing anything BUT 
healthy charging.   In fact, it is showing only battery voltage.   A
good alternator should/must put out just over 14 volts, or the battery
will begin a slow decline.   12 volts says it isn't working at all, and
you are about to be stranded.
