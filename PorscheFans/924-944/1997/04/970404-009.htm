Try using about 30psi front and 28psi rear. You can also drop down to 28psi
front and  26psi rear. These are cold temps. You can bleed off air in 2 psi
increments once they warm up if you feel it is necessary. Since your tires
are 
, which is no problem, I would lean towards the higher 30 and 28. Tire temps
depend a great deal on conditions and driving style, not to mention set up
but you can't go wrong with these figures as a starting point.
