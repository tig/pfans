All 944s tend to have that front wheel shimmy. It's most egregious on cars
with manual steering and 16" wheels (like one of mine). I've heard that if
the car is aligned while a pre-load of about 40 pounds is applied between
the steering rod ends, that the problem can be minimized. On-the-car
high-speed balancing also helps, but shops that do it are rare as hen's
teeeth here in the Northeast. The last one in Albany faded away about a
decade ago.
