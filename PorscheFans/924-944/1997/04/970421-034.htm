Hi Nick, a good thing to try is use good quality body shop type masking tape
it comes in widths up to 3inches so you could use it the same way.  Another
good thing to do is to put car wax on before the tape or stickers, then do
not
buff it off - just tape or decal on top of it. Then the tape or sticker is
easier to
get off, but should stay on for a race or track event.
