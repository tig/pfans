I have used Champion, Bosch and NGK in my NA 2,5. I prefer the NGK plug,
and use BPR6ES. 

I am not sure about 'splitfires' but if that is a multi-(3 or 4) tipped
plug, chances are that the tips will mask the spark from the gas/air
mixture. The flamefront may (will) be uneven, and the engine slightly
less effecient. Multi tip spark plugs have been used in VW and Audi for
some years, but are not AFAIK recommended for 944:s, due to differences
in combustion chamber design, swirl, tip exposure and so on. 

I suggest that money could be equally well spent on changing plugs more
often, than on very expensive platinum plugs? The gain of Platinum plugs
lies mostly in the extended heat range, not in any better performance
(if the 'simple' plug is within its optimum range).  

The term 'YMMV' is unusually appropriate this time... I'd like to hear
other peoples experiences!
