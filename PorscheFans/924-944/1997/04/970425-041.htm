Christian, a 944 turbo intercooler will fit in the nose of a 924 turbo,
and will be an excellent choice.  We usually buy them for about $200-250
used.  You already have the open valance for air flow, but you can do
some real easy duct work to really make it work.  You can install a 944
radiator without too much fabrication, and the hoses are not diffucult
to retrofit.
