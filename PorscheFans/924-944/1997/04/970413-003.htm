>All those makes are good, but if you do go Redline it's not MTL for our
>944'. Should be Redline 75W90 gear oil.

Porsche specs GL-4 lubricant for the 944 which corresponds to Redline MTL or
MT-90.  MTL is a 75W80 lubricant and MT-90 is a thicker 75W90 version of
MTL. Porsce specs GL-5 lubricant for the 911 and 914 which corresponds to
Redline 75W90.
