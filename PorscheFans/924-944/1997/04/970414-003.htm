I currently use Mobil 1 synthetic, but have not experiemented with
Swepco or the Redline synthetic. Mobil 1 provides farily easy cold
shifting and doesn't get notchy towards the end of a hot track session.
The LSD likes the Mobil 1 much better than it liked the dino lube I
used before.

I change twice a year, before the Spring and Fall track events.

I might experiment with Redline when I change it next.
