Well, I installed the AutoThority Stage II chips in my 87 951 and
was amazed at the increased power.  I also raised the boost from
stock to 1.0 bar (and I know people who run in the 1.2 - 1.3
range on their track cars).

With the slight boost increase and the chips, my car's top speed
was increased by 12 mph (at least, that's where I was running out
of road).  The power increase is extremely noticable, especially
above 3500 rpm or so.  Third and fourth gear feel almost the 
same when the car is on full boost.


Anyway, that's my experience.
