Just a general note concerning packing wheel bearings.

There is a good description in the Haynes manual about how to pack wheel
bearings.  This is the same method I've used for years.  If you pack the
bearings correctly, there shouldn't be a need to use excessive grease in
the hub.  

Another method is buy a bearing packer at your local auto parts store - I
know NAPA carries them.  You simply place the "clean" bearing in the
packer, crank the lid down, then use a grease gun applied to the nipple on
the bearing packer.  JUST MAKE SURE YOU USE THE CORRECT GREASE IN THE
GREASE GUN!  This method packs a perfect bearing everytime!
