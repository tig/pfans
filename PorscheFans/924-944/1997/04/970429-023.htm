Ben:

I don't know about the color of your car, but I had good luck with 
Plasti-Kote import color paint. Got it at Discount Auto Parts or Pep 
Boys, I think.  They had a list in the store with the vehicle colors 
and the corresponding match.

PK makes a spray can version and a small touch up bottle version.  I 
used the spray to fix up my engine bay (can't drive my car to the 
paint shop-no motor :( ) and it matches perfectly.  If you use the 
spray, tho, be careful-it RUNS like hell if it's hot outside.  use 
THIN coats.

They have a web site, but I don't remember address.  Just search for 
it.
