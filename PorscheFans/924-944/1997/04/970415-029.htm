Hi, Mark,

I have no personal experience with either of these rotors, but from what 
I've read, cross-drilled rotors tend to develope cracks around the holes. 
Therefore, they are best left for racing cars where they are changed 
frequently.  Slotted rotors seem to be the way to go for street cars 
that see ocasional track use.  Of course, you'll want to get the opinion 
of someone who has actually used one or the other before deciding.  I 
hope this helps.
