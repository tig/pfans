  I too had the vibration in the shifter at 4000 rpm.  Turned out to be the
balance shaft belt. It was missing about ten teeth along the outside so only
the lower belt was out of time.

  Someone also mentioned a loud whirring sound after having the belts done.
Check the balance shaft belt idler pulley. If this is incorrectly set then
it can contact the belt as it runs over the top of the balance shaft
sprocket causing a very annoying noise. I know...
