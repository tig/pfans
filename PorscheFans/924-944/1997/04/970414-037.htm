On Apr 14, 10:14am, Jeff Mayzurk wrote:

I ran home over lunch...here is the procedure (abreviated) in "How to
Understand, Service & Modify: Bosch Fuel Injection and Engine Management",
Charles Probst.

While watching the bell housing window on the flywheel with a timing light, the
timing mark should be visible.  Rev engine to ~2500 rpm.  Lightly tap the
sensor.  The engine timing should retard, moving the timing mark over in the
window.  The mark may even move out of the window.  If the timing mark doesn't
do the specified behavior, then the sensor is probably bad.

Good luck!
