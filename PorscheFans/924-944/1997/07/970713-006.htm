Dana,

The o2 sensor is on the pipe coming from the turbocharger (on the driver's
side).


At first it seems rather far away from the exhaust valves, but it makes sense
to have it that far away.  It is at a place where all the exhaust waves are
practically cancelled out (which you want with a turbo), so the o2 sensor
receives a steady flow of exhaust.
