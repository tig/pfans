Hi Michael - If you got 170k out of the original unit why switch?? I
used a sachs clutch when I did mine last winter. The rubber hub does
have it's flaws, mine lasted 109k and 11 years (not too bad). On the
other hand the ring and pinions are fragile and I would be afraid of
transmitting any additional shock or vibration to the transaxle. Keep in
mind that the rubber center in the clutch disk is the only thing in the
entire drive line with any give in it. (I've got to do my ring and
pinion this winter.)
