I have been reading a lot lately on clunking at the rear end of the 944's.  I
had the same problem and spent many hours trying to track the problem down
(hatch, shocks, transmission, loose bolts/screws etc...).  I finally found the
problem; a loose parking brake cable inside the rear disk.  Everytime I hit a
bump or uneven pavement I got this noise.  I secured  the cable and, walla, the
noise is gone.

What a relief, no noise no more.  I do not know if this is a common problem but
it is easy enough to check and fix.  I told my shop about the fix since at the
time they were trying to track down a mysterious noise.  Turns out it too was
the parking brake cable.  Maybe they are indebted to me somewhat.

Hope this helps someone.
