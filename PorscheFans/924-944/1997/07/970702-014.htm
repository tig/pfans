> 	* This car has some strange moonroof / removeable roof. Is this normal?
> It's all factory and motorized with clips at the front to remove.
>

Yes it is a strange moonroof/removeable roof.  Doesn't really have much of a
purpose either (well, I don't think so).  To remove it, turn the ignition key
to the first indent (very important), push the up part of the button, wait till
the noise stops, unclip and remove.  (this unlocks the sunroof)  Installation
is reverse except you push the down part of the button to lock it in place.

DO NOT try to remove the sunroof any other way.
