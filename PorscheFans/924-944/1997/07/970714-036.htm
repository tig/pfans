I thought I'd post a note regarding my vibration at 4k rpm and above.
 Although I initially suspected the AMOCO, I had the car in today to the
dealer.  4 teeth were sheared off the balance shaft belt.  They replaced it
and checked the cam belt ($320), and the car is now smooth.
