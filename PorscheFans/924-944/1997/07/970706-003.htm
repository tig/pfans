harcore comments backed with dyno proof.

The following is REAL, these are not lies, I have the dyno numbers to
back what I say, so you K&N lovers can put that flame thrower down
right now!

1. Get rid of that K&N, all it does it allow fine dirt into your engine!
   It doesn't do anything for power.  Next time at the dyno, I'll be
   testing Windward's bolt on cone K&N and we'll see if that can redeem
   K&N.  Not a single HP, not a single lb-ft of Torque for the darn C$50
   filter!
2. If you want raw high numbers, use the boost bolt, if you want smooth
   power without dips and valleys, but without the super high numbers,
   use a drilled cycling valve.
3. For the smoothest power, the factory knows best with the turbo cup
   computers making the aftermarket chips look like some drunk programmed
   them.
4. Autothority's claims for HP and Torque are real, very real, almost
   conservative!
5. Our Weltmeister friend did not show and our B&B exhaust did not come
   in, so those will be another dyno day.
6. Next week we are testing the cat pipe.
