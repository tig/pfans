Steve,
When Mr. Haynes says do not remove the nut, I think he means loosen 
it most of the way, but do not take it off, so that when the rod-end 
finally lets go from the knuckle (under whatever means of persuasion 
you have chosen), things will not go flying in all directions.

Castelated just means a nut with a "square wave" serrated pattern on
its top surface. Don't worry about it.
