Ed -

Saw your note on poor radio reception with the new super tuner from Pioneer.
That "little black wire" that is a part of the antenna coaxial cable going to
the window is important to making the reception significantly better.  This is
connected to an antenna amplifier so it is critical to have it hooked up - and
this is simple to do.

Take the power antenna lead from your new super tuner and connect it to the
black wire that is a part of the car co-axial antenna cable.  The easiest way
is to strip the power antenna lead wire and slip it into the black wire
attached to your antenna co-axial cable and wrap electrical tape around it all
to hold it together.

Made a world of difference in my reception.  Went from poor FM and no AM
reception to clear reception on both.  Please excuse the verbiage regarding the
wire names, I think you know what I am talking about.
