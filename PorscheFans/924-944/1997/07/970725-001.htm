Thanks a million for your reply to my inquiries on 7/18.  A brief update:

I had John at Protosport temporarily fix the uncontrollable heat problem.  He
connected a different vacuum line to a defective selenoid and turned it off.
 Apparently this selenoid is stuck open and the coolant was flowing through
the heat exchanger all the time.  I don't know what the proper fix is but the
heat is off for now.

At an auto-x on Sunday I swapped my D40M2's (on phone dials) with my R-1's
(on fuchs).  At the end of the event I swapped them back.  Presto - no more
shimmy.  I wish everyone's fix was this easy.

I will remove the check valve in the windshield wiper fluid line and see if
that fixes the problem.
