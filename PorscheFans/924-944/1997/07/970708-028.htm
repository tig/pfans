Marv,
Recently, I had the coolant hose to what I believe is the heater control
valve "pop off" during hard acceleration in my '86 951.  I smelled
coolant big time.  I opened the hood and saw the hose off.  It is the
hose that connects to a box type device right below the rear intake
manifold tube on my 951.  Check this fitting to see if this hose is
tight on the fitting, and that the hose clamp is tight.  A loose fitting
here will leak coolant onto hot engine parts, and you will smell it. 
Also, while you are at it, check the hose leading from the heater valve
to the firewall.  I understand if this one goes, coolant could flow into
the bell housing, soaking the clutch- NOT GOOD.
