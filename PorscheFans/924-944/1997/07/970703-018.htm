I've heard of others attaching sub belts this way, but I think the sub
belt needs to be mounted at or behind your crotch to be totally
effective. Running the belt over the front of the seat is giving you a
decent amount of room to submarine.  I still think the best approach is
to use a 6-pt. system and attach the anti-subs to the same points you
mount the lap belt. Some organizations, such as the SCCA think you
should use different mounting points, but I simply don't see the need.
If you lose one of the lap belt mounting points, your antisub belt isn't
goind to do you *any* good!
