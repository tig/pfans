Hi, Morgan,

The expansion valve is directly below the heater blower fans.

To find it, open your hood, remove the thin, brittle cowl shroud
and remove the fan assembly. If you look straight down you will
see the AC evaporator and, to the passenger side, covered with 
a tar-like substance, the expansion valve!

Good Luck!
