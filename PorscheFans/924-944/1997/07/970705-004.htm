I would be most suspicious of the motor mounts.  There is a specification
for the height of the motor mount when measured from two points, but I do
not know what it should be.  Look at the passenger's side first.  You might
be able to compare the height of either side.  The passenger side usually
fails most often.  The link is the high heat that the exhaust produces
causes the motor mounts to break down.  Check your exhaust hangers too.


Does anyone on the list know what the normal height for these critters
(motor mounts) are?
