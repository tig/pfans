On Wed, 16 Jul 1997, James M. Lemanek wrote:

> I have an 1987 944
> When I depress the clutch pedal all works fine until I release the clutch. 
> The pedal does not return to the full up position. It returns about 60 mm 
> from its normal full up position. When I pull up the pedal it snaps to the 
> full up position.
> I looked under the dash and cannot see any obvious problems. I checked the 
> pre load spring adjustment and it measures 61 mm. The clutch engaging and 
> disengaging feels normal. Any advice would be appreciated.


	Have you done any work on the system lately (brakes, too)? It's
	possible that you might have somehow gotten air in the lines 
	because the behaviour you're describing can be caused by this.
	Bleeding the system is a very cheap fix compared to other
	alternatives so start there. FYI, 944's are notoriously finicky
	when it comes to clutch/brake bleeding.
