Subject:Intermittent idle problem ('89 951)
Date:Thu, 3 Jul 1997 08:51:01 -0700
My '89 951 idles normally when cold, but once it's warmed up I experience
an intermittent problem where the idle stays between 1500 and 2000 rpm.
When it happens, the idle doesn't drop low and then jump up to a higher
rpm; it slowly falls to a high idle. It's not consistent, except that a
blip of the throttle almost always causes a return to normal idle speed
(~950 rpm). I have this problem most frequently when lugging around in
first gear in city traffic, i.e., light throttle -- if I let off from a
higher rpm it usually idles normally.

I realize idle problems have been discussed to no end on this list, but
this problem doesn't seem like a typical failure case for the idle
stablizer/auxillary air regulator. Any suggestions?
