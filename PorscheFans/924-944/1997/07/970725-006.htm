Continuing 944 work stories:
For the benefit of San Diego area Porsche owners, there is a good new/used
BMW/Porsche parts place called Autobahn on Vernon Way in El Cajon with prices
lower than dealer's on some new factory parts as well. 444-2290.
For anyone attempting clutch/differential/brake work, you may need a 17mm
male hex wrench: you can get one for much less than the catalog prices at a
Volkswagen parts store - - lest we forget our heritage.
Lastly, although we've drained and replaced the power steering fluid twice on
my 944, the steering is still kind of heavy, especially at slower speeds. Any
opinions?
