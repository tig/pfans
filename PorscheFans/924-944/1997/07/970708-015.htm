The 968 is an excellent handling car.  I have had my '92 968 at the 
Mid-Ohio a few times, and it performed wonderfully.  It is hard to top 120 
in the back stretch (I tried it once and my instructor grabbed the dash as 
I hit the brakes into the turn).

It is a Tiptronic, which shaves even more time of the start.  As stated, it 
is easy to drive fast and very forgiving (much more than a 911).
