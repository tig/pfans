Hello,

This is the final chapter in my R12 to R134A conversion.

I now have nice cold A/C. 

In all, the conversion was very simple. I had some problems
caused by a defective conversion kit. Once I figured that
out, and fixed it, the conversion process took about 20 minutes.
The A/C is nice and cold, and I'm happy.

Special thanks to Doug Donsbach  and
Mike Lommatzsch  for informing me where
the low pressure switch is. Interestingly enough, I don't
think you need to do anything to the low pressure switch.
I think that when you add the first can of R134, the switch
will close and allow the compressor to operate.
