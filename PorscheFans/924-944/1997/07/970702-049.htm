For those seeing a possibility to soup up the (meager) original
Blaupunkt amplifier, this is a viable option. A word of warning, tho':

The DIN socket is configurated differently from the look-alike Japanese
variety. There are amplifiers readily available for both systems and
each can be utilized - WITH THE RIGHT ADAPTOR CABLE...

Beware, because using the wrong cable may (will!) destroy the amplifier,
regardless of make and standard.
