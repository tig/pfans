and I enjoyed driving it very much.  While on the way back to 
San Franscisco before passing the Golden Gate Bridge, traffic

traffic at 5mph on a 25 degree incline hill for about 20 minutes.
And here comes the nightmare......... White burning smell smokes
starting to come out from my 951.  First I thought it was the VW
in front of me but then after some stares from the cars around me
I realized my car was leaving behind a huge cloudy white smoke.  2 cars

At the same time, while releasing my clutch the whole #$#@@

again!!!  

Forturnately I was almost at the tip of the hill when all these
things happened. Then I basically avoided to engage my clutch and
let the car run at neutral while going downhill.  The smoke stopped
on the way going downhill.
