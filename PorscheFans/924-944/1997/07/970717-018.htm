5) Cleaned electrical connections to air flow sensor

7) Re-assembled air intake system
8) Started car
9) Car smoked 3 minutes, engine cleaning fluid burning off exhaust
   pipes.
10) Engine at operating temperature
11) Car exceeded 1.8 bar boost and speed limit 3 times within 10
    minutes, no engine roughness noted, no engine power loss.

        I'll try an extended run tomorrow, but so far the car is running
