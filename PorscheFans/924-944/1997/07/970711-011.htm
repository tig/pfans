David,
Replace the pump.  It's not that big of a job and a used pump can be picked 
up from Kemptons or one of the other used parts guys for less than $35.  The 
main benefit of the cooling pump is when the car is running it pushes cooling 
fluid to the turbo and when the car is run hard and turned off it will cycle 
on and off depending on the temp of the turbo.  A new turbo is very expensive 
and a very difficult job.
