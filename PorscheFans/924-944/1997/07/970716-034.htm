Hey P-Fans,
I never owned a 951 but I would be really surprised if Porsche made a
different sunroof for turbos and NA's. I think the early 924 had a metal
sunroof.  However, I know for a fact that both my '83 NA and '88 NA have
a heavy (when one person removes or replaces it), fiberglass sunroof.
The '83 was manual and the '88 is power.  

Regarding clutches.  The clutch on my '88 did the same thing.  I
adjusted the free play and the pedal returned all the way up on its own.
It started a short time later.  I found the clutch master leaking.  The
slave was not leaking, but I replaced them both and the clutch works
great.  Check for a small leak.  Any air in the system and it won't work
worth a darn.  For what it's worth.
