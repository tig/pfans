The clunking noises from the back of the car can be either the CV joints
and/or the differential. If the transaxle has over 100k miles on it and
depending on previous use,or abuse, it may be ready for a rebuild or
replacement. Rebuilt units run around $1100 (Vertex) and up, exchange. If not
corrected, the ring and pinion will eventually fail. The transmission itself
is pretty reliable, though synchros, of course do weaken a bit with high
mileage. Replacement axles run around $200 per side. I don't know how much
shop labor would be; I've always done my own work. The original diff in my 84
failed at about 85k miles, though I had been autocrossing and tracking the
car quite a bit. If you rebuild the CV joints yourself, new joints (need 4)
cost $60-$70 each. Good luck.
