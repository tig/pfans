Rod/other listmembers,

call PCNA Customer Commitment Group and give them your VIN#.  They will tell
you if your car has had any and all of the recall work done.  Also, ask them
to send you a letter if the recall work has been done and you don't have the
records from the PO.  This letter will help serve as proof if you resell
your car in the future.

I don't recall the injectors being replaced on my 86 951 when this recall work
was done by the PO.  Sorry but I don't have the # handy for PCNA CCG, but you
can call 1-800-PORSCHE and they can give it to you.
