I would like to back up Farzaans claim that K&N filters had no
measurable positive effect but just allowed fine dust acess.

The Porsche factory air filter are big enough for the car, but does
(ofcourse!) rely on not being clogged. Any K&N type filter will be
better than a seriously oil/dirt clogged factory filter.

Anyone not keen on saving wear on their engines may just remove the
factory filter and run without it, if they want 'free flow'. Backside
may be that depending on tuned intake length and pressure sensing
devices, it may even give *less* power.  

Now, its just too bad Farzaan doesn't have a non-turbo too in that dyno
setup. Would have been nice to see if and what could have been done
there.
