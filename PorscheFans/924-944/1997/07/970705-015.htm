The german S2 has 211hp while the US spec car has 208bhp.
(But i've been told that 211hp == 208bhp - so this seems
to make no difference)

German 944s have other direction indicators on the front fender:
The german is *behind* the front wheels and measures about 3*6 cm.
US 944s have their direction indicators *before* the front wheels
and are much longer ~15cm(?) (Saw them only on pictures)
