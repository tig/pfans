Hi, I''ve got 17 x 8 & 9 Hart C2-Turbo wheels in the standard late offsets
on my 87 944-S,  The tires are Dunlop SP-8000 in the 255/40-17 and
275/40-17 sizes.

I'll tell you that they do not fit without some minor modifications,  I had 
rubbing at the ten oclock and two thirty positions on each side and had 
to bend/grind back the inner lip over 1/8 inch at those positions.  They now
do not rub.  If you go with a custom offset 3pc wheel you could avoid this
problem.

Looking back, it was really no big deal to get them to fit, and it was well 
worth it because they look absolutely awsome on the car.  I wanted some
tires/rims to REALLY FILL OUT the wheel wells - These definitely DO!
