Jeff,
     The heater flap is controlled by a servo motor which is located on the 
upper right hand side of the driver's footwell. It is very common for the 
white plastic "bearing" to break. If you reach under the dash while in the 
driver's seat, you can reach it. The part number for the replacement is:

944.572.313.00,  85/2-88 944 microfiche in frame 8/13/00 (P/N from George 
Beuselinck)

Good Luck!
