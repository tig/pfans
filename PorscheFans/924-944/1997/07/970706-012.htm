I would check ALL the rubber hose connections. Typically, if the head
coolant riser (connects to a short rubber hose) has a leak, you will not
see and cooland, but will smell it, due to the coolant evaporating when
hitting the head. Head leak will manifest itself as bubbles in the
coolant.
