Well, it's up to you, really.  I'd either bite the bullet and rebuild 
what I have, or buy a good used box.  The only problem with the latter 
is, to quote Stan Hanks, you've already got a used transmission.  How 
good is another good used box really?

When you say "carrier", I assume you mean the open differential. 
 Despite visual appearance, I would absolutely magnaflux the pinion 
gear, and preferably the ring gear as well.  You may not see any 
damage, but you don't really know for sure.  You also don't know 
whether you've spun the pinion or diff bearings, yet.
