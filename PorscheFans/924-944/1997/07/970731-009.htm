Grassroots Motorsports did some analysis of ignition system enhancers a few
years ago.  They looked at improved wires (like Nology) and improved spark
plugs, among other things.  They used several methods to test the results of
upgrades, including a bench test.  It's been awhile since I read the article
but I believe they reached the conclusion that a weak ignition system can be
improved by wires and plugs.  However, if the ignition system is up to OEM
standards then there was virtually no power gain.

If true, this observation may explain the different results obtained by
readers who have experimented with Nology wires.
