1. Both fans should be running with A/C on. If only one, check fan relay or
fan motor itself. If neither fan is running, temp switch in radiator may be
bad.
2. Debris may be clogging the condenser and radiator; radiator should be
removed for cleaning.
3. To improve cooling performance, replace the 4-bladed fan blades with the
later 6-bladed version. Blades w/o motor have been available from Vertex.
