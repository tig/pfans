The question was  asked: How to disconnect the factory alarm?


Here is what I have done successfully:

Open the compartment under the passenger footwell which holds the DME. (This is
under the carpet in the sloping front.) You will find a blue plastic box about
4" by 3". (Not to be confused with the much larger metal DME)

This has 2 wiring harnesses attached to it. If you remove the smaller harness,
which has only 2 wires, the alarm is disabled. (do NOT remove the larger
harness with 6(?) wires or the ignition will not function.)

If you remove the 2 wires, the car runs fine in all other respects.
