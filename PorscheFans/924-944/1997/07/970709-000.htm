Anything's possible.  If you want to modify you can go as far as 9.0+10.5
with 275/315.  That will take a large hammer to the inner fenders and some grinding
to the camber adjusting bolts.

Normally 8.5+10.0 works well with 255/275.  Offset is approximately 65mm
(2.5")
and may require tweeking the front fenders.  All boides are different and 
some of this depends on how much camber you are running.  I have this setup
on my 951S.

It had better not rub under hard cornering.

You may or may not be able to just bolt thses on.  It depends on the
individual car.  If you are lucky they'll bolt on.  If not - be
prepared.

BFG Comp T/A now makes a R1 drag radial for the cornering impared.  Maybe 
a set of skinnies can be had for the front to minimize drag ;^}
