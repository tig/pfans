Hi Holger,

>I feel a little foolish asking this but how do I take of my electric
>sunroof on my Porsche 944. I don't have a manual but it sure looks as if
>it it possible from what I can see inside but I can't get it of.

-	turn right your ignition key into the *first* position
	(ignition itself must be off)
-	press the "open sunroof" side of the switch
	(you'll hear that something happens)
-	wait until sound from the motor stops
-	now your sunroof is unlocked and can be taken out
	after the two clamps over your head have been opened.

re-installing the sunroof is done vice versa.
