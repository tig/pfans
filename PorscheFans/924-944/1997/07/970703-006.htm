I wanted to convey information on some products I have recently used.  
1.  In the never-ending search for brake pads that do not squeal, I have
installed a set of Metal Masters/Mintex pads on my 88 951s.  After 5000
miles they do not squeal at all and produce very little dust.  Overall
very impressive for a street pad.  They do lack the bite of the carbon
pads, but IMO are fine for the street. 
2.  I have installed a set of stoneguards on the superfragile fog lights
on the front of my 951s.  They fit very well and carry a three year
warranty against breakage.  Overall impressed with ease of installation
and appearance.
Both of the above products were obtained from ZIMS at reasonable prices.
