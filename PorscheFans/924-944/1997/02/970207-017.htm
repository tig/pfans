I use a Recaro SRD for my Carrera that sees significant street and track
use.  This seat still has adjustable tilt and will tilt forward for access
to the back seat.  It has a little better side bolsters than a factory sport
seat (IMO), and more importantly, it has openings for shoulder harness and

comfortable than the factory seat even for street use.  "I liked it so much,
