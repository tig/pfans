My understanding of the manual is that the washer does not spin when you
adjust. It does have enough movement side to side. I tighten the bearing
and then move the washer to check the adjustment. If the nut is to tight
the washer will not move. I just make sure there is "drag" on the washer 
from side to side just before it stops moving. This seems to work for me.
