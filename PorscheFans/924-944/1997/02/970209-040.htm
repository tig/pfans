Paul,

Assuming these are the same as the Koni yellows, the front shocks should
be adjustable by a small tab on the top. They can be adjusted in the
car. The rears are adjusted by compressing fully until they engage, then
turning.

Both front and rear have about 2.5 turns adjustability, make sure you
don't turn them further than they want to go.

I'm not sure which direction you have to turn, but I think it's
anti-clockwise for the front and clockwise for the back to make them
stiffer.

About the springs. I have 260 lb in the front with 26mm torsion bars in
back. They seem to balance OK with the stock sway bars, but I some
oversteer. I would suggest heavier springs.
