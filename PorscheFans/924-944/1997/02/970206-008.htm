Has anyone had a 951 block rebuilt with new pistons and
a rebore ?

I have had a variety of advice, ranging from

1. Dont rebore or hone. Just fit new rings. Reboring will cause
more problems, due to special alloy finishing not thick enough

2. Rebore it ! These things will build up crankcase pressure, and
leak oil out of every seal.

3. Just put back original pistons and rings.

It is also interesting how many O-rings etc are not included in the
head and block set.

I had take the engine out to fix numerous oil leaks and to replace
bearings and all rubber, hoses, seals, gaskets, turbo and countless
other parts.
