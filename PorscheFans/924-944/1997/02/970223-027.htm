	Welcome Estonia! How many Porsche owners are there over
	there? To answer your question...these are readings taken
	from the dash guage so make sure it's working properly. It
	should never really drop below 2 even on the hottest of days.
	You most likely have a problem somehwre if yours is doing this.
	Even on the hottest of days in Texas, the lowest mine gets is
	about 2.5. This is when it's idling, btw. When you step on the
	gas, even a little bit, it should shoot straight to 4 or higher,
	the harder you step, the higher it goes. At 4 thousand rpm, it
	should be at or very near 5. For extended highway cruising, it
	is not unusual for it to come down to 4.5 or a little lower.
	Hope this helps!
