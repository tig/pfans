Wes Shew wrote:
> 
> While bleeding my brakes I made the error of allowing the brake reservoir
> fluid level get too low and sucked air in. Does this automatically mean I
> need to bleed the hydraulic clutch fluid? What are the symptons of air in
> the clutch system? I don't notice any difference when driving the car. TIA
> 


There is a barrier in the reservoir which will let you get the fluid level
really low for the brake master cylinder without uncovering the feed for
the hydraulic clutch master cylinder.  And you probably were not pumping
the clutch pedeal when you drained the reservoir, so if the clutch still
feels right don't worry about it.
