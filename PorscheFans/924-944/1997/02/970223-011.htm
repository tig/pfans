To continue on the battery thread, double check when you go to purchase
your battery, but I think all 944's use size 'Group 41' batteries (this is
in the US, I don't know if Europe uses the same numbering scheme).  If you
are looking for a specialty battery such as the Optima, don't assume they
just come in one size and compromise by having to modify your cables. Be
sure to tell them it is for a Group 41 application.  Interstate brand
batteries are top rated here in the states.  I have one, and even though
it is a conventional lead acid type, it has not leaked a drop from
anywhere.

Gregor - 89 944 2.7L with Group 41 Interstate battery.
