It really wouldn't be worth upgrading to a larger turbo without an increase of
engine displacement.  The larger turbo is going to increase the amount of lag.
 You might want to try a cup car turbo K-26 with the larger hot side housing.

Turbos are not easy to install or remove.

If you want more power you can increase the stroke of the engine to a total
displacement of 2.8L.  Contact powerhouse for info.
