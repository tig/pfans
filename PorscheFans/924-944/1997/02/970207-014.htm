Greg,

I also have a 1980 924 Turbo and have had a similar problem with my starter.
The problem is that the solenoid is binding and not engaging the starter gear
with the ring gear, nor energizing the starter motor.  It has a trigger that
will not energize the motor until the gears are well engaged in order to save
the gears from damage.  A new or rebuilt solenoid is all you probably need.
I just took my solenoid off, cleaned the old sticky lubricant out of the
solenoid cylinder, re-lubed with a little lithium grease and slapped her back
on.  It's been working for about 4 years since.
