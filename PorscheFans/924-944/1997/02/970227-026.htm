Yes, there are a couple of screws for adjusting each of the beams,
up-and-down and side-to-side.

First, park the car about 15 feet from a building with a light colored
exterior, at night, with the low beams on, and mark where the brightest
part of the beam is pointing (with a crayon or a piece of tape).

Then, using the adjusting screws, adjust it until you are happy with where
it is pointing.

Alternatively, take it to a shop and they can do it for you...
