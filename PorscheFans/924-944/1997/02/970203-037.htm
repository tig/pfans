My '80 931 had a similar fuel leak.  It was caused by a rust-through
on the bottom of the reserve well of the tank (it rusted from the
inside out).  I solved it by replacing the tank with a junkyard part.

I suspect that water got into the fuel at some point, and settled in
the lowest part of the tank.  Maybe it is a good idea to periodically
add some water remover to the fuel, as preventative maintenance...
