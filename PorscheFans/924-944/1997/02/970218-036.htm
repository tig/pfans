Thanks to all who offered comments on aftermarket exhaust for my 951.
After exhaustively (heh heh) considering all the possibilities, I've
decided to go with a Borla cat-back set-up.  A B&B is nice but would be
about twice the cost.  The car is almost entirely street driven so I
couldn't justify the added expense.   Now there seems to be other options
to consider, namely the exhaust tip itself.  I understand Borla has a tip
called the "inter cooler" or something like that, which has a metal circle
connected to the outer diameter of the pipe by a number of metal vanes.  Is
this thing for real or a gimmick?  I prefer a straight, round or oval
stainless steel tip.  Anybody have any suggestions?

Thanks again,
