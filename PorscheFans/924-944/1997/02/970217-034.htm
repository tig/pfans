Hello P-fans,
i think i discovered the secret of the magic hood lamp... Maybe i can help
someone: After searching for 20 minutes for a switch, i started to measure
the voltage of the hood lamp wire (i pierced it with a small needle).
And...more magic... there are ALWAYS 12 Volts on this line, regardless


whether the lamp is shining or not...?!?! Then i started to inspect the lamp
socket...and here comes the solution: Inside the socket there is a small
metal ball with shortens two contacts inside it when the hood reaches a
certain angle. There is no external switch! When the lamp doesn't work even
if the bulb is OK (and the contacts are not corroded, clean them first, and
tighten the mounting screw for good grounding), correct the lamps mounting
angle by bending the socket a little bit more up (as if the hood would open
wider). Good luck!
