	I have not done this on a 951 but on a 944 you can get the oil cooler out
without removing the header.  Drop the power steering pump and you can pull
the oil cooler out.  This save time but you will have the replace the power
steering fluid.  You  also need the alignment tool to put the oil cooler
back on.
