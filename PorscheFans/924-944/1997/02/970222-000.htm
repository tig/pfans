It is possible your starter solenoid (mounted on the starter) is sticky
or filled with gummy old grease.  The starter will not turn until the
solenoid has made its complete travel and the gear is engaged in the
flywheel. 

One can take it apart, examine, clean and lube with high temperature
grease as needed.
