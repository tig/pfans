>Subject: Market driven vs internal focus
>        Would someone can help me to identify the difference between a
>market driven company and an internal focus company?  Are they the two
>extremes of a continuum, or they are two aspects of the management manner.
---snip ---
1) Try Innovation and Entrepreneurship by Peter Drucker.

2) The ultimate customer oriented companies are those that build race cars.
The ultimate internal focus are; a) those who build what they enjoy
building, and b) most government organizations.

3) These two are not opposite extremes of a continuum.  Market Driven is at
12 o'clock, Profit Driven is at 6 o'clock, Technology Driven is at 3
o'clock, and Internal focus is at 9 o'clock.

Hope this helps, or at least encourages you to find a better answer.  Keep
us informed.

SAPICS HOSTS WORLD SYMPOSIUM
AT SUN CITY 3rd -6th JUNE 1997

SAPICS is proud to announce that its 19th international Conference -
the 1997 World Symposium - will host the biennial gathering of
world-wide professionals in the field of Manufacturing and Logistics.

With over 30 International; sponsoring "PICS" organisations the SAPICS
WORLD SYMPOSIUM 1997 will host the only dedicated Conference &
Exhibition of products and services committed to excellence
in the Total Supply Chain.

Sun City will be the venue for the 19th international conference &
exhibition and with over 35 "PICS" affiliates world-wide SAPICS
expects about 120 international delegates to attend the World
Symposium, particularly from the traditional markets of Europe and
Australia, with great interest being shown by some of the Pacific rim
countries, and the USA.

The World Symposium will be officially opened by the Premier of the
North West Province, Mr. Popo Molefe, and there will be over 20
international speakers who will include Eli Goldrat - Author of "The
Goal" and Hal Mather, President, Hal Mather Inc., both from the USA.
Tony Arnold - Author and Educationalist from Canada as well as Greg
Schlegal, 1997 APICS President, USA.

The 1997 World Symposium, Sun City, South Africa
3rd - 6th June 1997
