I did mine with Simple Green and a pressure washer (for the safe areas) and
just a hose for areas that are sensitive. With engine warm (not hot), cover
the distributor and the brake fluid reservoir with a plastic bag. Spray the
Simple Green everywhere, and let it sit for about 10 minutes. Take a tooth
brush to areas you can reach. Then rinse with the pressure washer or hose.
The first time, you may have to repeat the procedure. After that, you can
keep it fairly clean if you hit the dirty areas with the Simple Green about
once a month.
