Sounds to me like it could be the resister pack that runs the different
fan speeds. The fastest speed sends current around all the resisters;
that is why 4 and the defroster (which turns on speed 4) work ok. On my
car, speeds 1-3 failed to work 1 in three successive summers and finally
failed in December. The resistor pack fixed it.
