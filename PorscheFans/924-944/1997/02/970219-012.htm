My suggestion would be to replkace the Pads/Rotors yourself.

It is fairly easy and ALOT less expensive.

If you look in Excellence you'll find '87 rotors
for $ 66/58 F and R, and pads can be had for $100 = 375 including shipping.

(NOPI 800-227-6674)

Once you remove the wheel, all you do is remove the caliper (2 bolts,
one line clip) and two philips head scres (get an impact screwdriver)
and the rotors pop off.  On the '87 there is no bearing 
removal, the rotors are "Hats"

Buy an impact screwdriver (craftsman, $14) and save $625 for two hour's work.
