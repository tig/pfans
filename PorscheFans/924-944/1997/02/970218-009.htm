Good call (not permanently installing the kit)!  I did this on my last truck, 
and one night the cheap plastic cap they give you to screw onto the plastic 
tube you install in your heater hose cracked right down the middle, spraying 
hot coolant everywhere.  If you use the kit, I recommend removing the plastic 
tube and replacing it with a 4" length of copper tubing once you've finished.  
It's not much trouble to R&R with the plastic part when you actually go to 
flush the system the next time.
