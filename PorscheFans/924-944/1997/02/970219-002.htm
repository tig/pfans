Hi David,

I believe the word you are looking for is 'Bremsbelag' 
and not 'Bresemslag'.

Bremsbelag means 'Brake Lining'. 

I'm no expert, but the previous mail from Clark sounds
on the button. I know from experiences with Fords and
Renaults that the brake pads had a metallic strip which
made a circuit. If the brake pad wore through this strip
the circuit was broken and the Dash light would come on.

So if you mechanic just replaced the pads, then maybe he
forgot to reconnect the wiring to the sensor, or as Clark
suggests maybe he accidentally broke it.
