Regarding Max boost - The overboost condition should result in 1.1-1.2
bars, (16.2-17.6psi) depending on RPM.  The fuel cutoff should envoke
after 3 seconds.  Until the thee car will be rediculously fast.

That's my reading from a 935 style 2.0BAR (gauge, not absolute)
indicator, fed off the intake manifold, not post cycling valve.

Boost shoud be measured at the intake, off one of the two conections.
I used an Xtra-long banjo-bolt with three feeders (self-fabricated)
from the rear connector by #3 sparkplug.

Stock boost on a real guage is about .7 steady (gauge, not absolute).
As you know the 951 stock boost guage reads absolute pressure.  Using
the Autothorithy 2.7 kit with the standard boost bolt the boost hits .95
and themn tapers off to .7.  This canb be modified by drilling out the
weber carb main jet whcih they install into the crossover banjo bolt.
