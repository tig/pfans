My wife can do pads.  If you can unbolt a caliper you're done!  You will need to bleed the brakes.

Here's a comp-lete lisitng of tools 

19mm socket and combo wrench (unbolt caliper and wheels)

11mm line wrench (remove beake line, if you chose to do so, you should be able to get around this.  Remove the hard line from the flexible line NOT the caiper for ease of reinstallation)

Impact screwdriver (removes set-scres)

Pliers (for compressing retainer butterfly to remove brake pads)

I beleive that's it, other than bleeding the brakes, which requires a helper and some clear plastic tubing.

Enjoy!
