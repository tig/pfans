The hose your discribing goes to the turbo recycling valve ( not to be
confused with the turbo bybass valve).  This valve controls turbo boost.  The
dealers do not sell only this hose, they want to sell the hose and all of the
tubes connected to it for around $150.  You can very carefully cut the end
fitting off of the hose, reuse them and just replace the hose.  Any good
quality fuel line will work.  The hose sees approximately 15 # of pressure
and/or vaccum.
