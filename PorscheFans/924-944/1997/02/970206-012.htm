This is usually the most common cause of water in the 944 lens (usually 
only in the brake light section of the lens. This drove me crazy for 
several months!


It's coming it because the lens assembly is not properly sealed to the 
body.
You can buy generic sealer from an auto parts store, or you can buy the
stuff from Porsche, actually a VW part, as AKD.522.009.

Getting the lens off can be quite difficult, be careful it will 
eventually come loose.
