Try "Extend" from Permatex.  It goes on as a clear liquid and hardens to
a black, paintlike finish, which can be sanded, primed, etc.  The
cleaner the rusty areas before application, the better the result.  We
used this in the Rust Belt of Upstate New York several times with
positive results.
