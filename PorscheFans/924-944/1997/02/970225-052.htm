Hi Barry:

I cannot recall the Momo name for this seat.  Its minimal,
lightweight.  A fiberglass shell, covered with a velvety black
fabric, the Momo logo in red/yellow just behind the driver's head.
There is almost no padding, I used it with a 2" foam insert behind my
waist.  It holds you well, supports the upper torso, and it weighs
about 17 lbs.  Which was a 25 pound savings over my factory sport
seat with all its motors.  The adaptor bolts into the bottom of the
seat(four locations).  The assembly is adjustable fore/aft with a
slider actuated at the front between your legs.  Five cutouts for a
5-6 point restraint system.  The quality of the fiberglass and
metalwork on the adaptor is top-notch.  There is a predrilled hole in
the adaptor for a quick-release eye bolt if you want to use such.

Hope this is informative.
