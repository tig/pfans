>Can anybody please help me. I am in the UK and have owned a '79 924 for

>start problem. The car starts from cold after a few turns no problem. I
>t will start from hot (10-15 mins after stopping) straight away.
>
>If I leave it for 20 min to 2 hours, it rarely starts. If I turn it over
>constantly for a minute or two, it will eventually fire. When it does
>fire, it is clean and runs perfectly.
>

You have a common problem with the Bosch CIS systems, which is a loss
of system fuel pressure, usually caused by a leaking check valve or leaking
injectors, resulting in vapor lock in the fuel injector lines. 

Have your mechanic hook up the fuel pressure gauge setup and
watch it as you turn it off. Usual specs call for it to hold the pressure
for half an hour or so.
