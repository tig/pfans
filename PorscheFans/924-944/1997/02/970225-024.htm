>2) In the front middle of the sun roof, there is what appears to be a
>switch that is activated when the roof is closed.  What is the purpose   
>of this switch? 

This switch informs the car that your roof is in place.  There is no immediate 
reaction. (notice if you replace the sun roof with the car off, and then just 
start it, about 15 seconds later they engage... It is this front center switch 
that lets the car sense the presence of your roof.   Many people have problems 
with the contacts, causing the roof not to open or close.  You can clean them 
if this problem arises
