        To loosen the lock nut (the lower nut) I just use a punch and
hammer. The upper nut which actually supports the spring can be turned with
a so-called spring platform wrench.  An adjustable (fits different diameter
nuts) one costs $26.99 from  Pegasus Auto Racing Supplies p/n # 3106.  Call
1-800-742-0484.  Their catalog is great. Everybody into motorsports should
get one.
