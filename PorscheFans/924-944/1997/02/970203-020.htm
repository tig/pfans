Greetings, 

I wanted to thank everyone who responded to my queary about
the "bad noise" my 944S started making a couple of weeks ago.

It's gotten worse and now there is the occasional clunk to go 
with the rattle.  So it seems the clutch rubber center is the
problem.  I've been back to the shop and we checked the play 
in the drive shaft where it enters the transmission.  There    
is some play and there is some signs of rubber in the the 
clutch housing.  Time for a new clutch.  The car goes into 
the shop on Thursday.                                

Steve '87 944S in need of a clutch, again!
