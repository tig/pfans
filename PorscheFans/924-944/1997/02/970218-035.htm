Welcome, 
If you want to do PCA Racing you can't lighten the car past what is legal in
your class. You also can't add fiberglass or anything else to the body
panels. All bodywork must be stock. This applies to racing in the stock
classes. If you have an urgent need to rid yourself of a good deal of your
net worth then go for it and set your car up for the modified classes where
$$$$ rules.
In any event have fun!
