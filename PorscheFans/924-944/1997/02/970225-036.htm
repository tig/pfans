At 09:02 AM 2/25/97 -0800, Mike Lommatzsch wrote:
>I'm in the process of securing another set of wheels for my 944.  Can 
>somebody tell me the correct offset for the wheels?  I have 7x15 in the 
>front and 8x15 in the rear.  I have access to another set of 8x15 wheels 
>and would like to check the offset before purchasing.  I think these 
>wheels are for the 911 model though.

If your cars is 1986 or older, they use the 23 mm offset. The 8x15 fuchs
wheels have a 10.6 mm offset, and work just fine.

If your car is 1987 or newer, they use the 52 mm offset (but some of the 
wheels that fit range from 52 to 65 mm).
