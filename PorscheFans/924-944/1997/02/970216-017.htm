Just a quick wrap-up to the Weltmeister chip question concerning
improvements on a 87 944S.

From five respondents, the answer is basically "Don't bother". RPM limit
raised-yes, with small increase in mid-range driveability and pull, but
basically not nearly enough to offset the cost of the chip and possible
perils to the DME from improper installation and/or a blast of static.
