If all US 944 Turbos came with air and it could not be simply option
deleted by checking a box on the order form, then (as with US 3.2
Carreras) it must be left on the car.  This goes for compressor,
condenser, lines, etc.  The only removable component would be the belt.
Everything else must be there though we don't require that it be
functional.

Cars have been disqualified for lack of major A/C components.

Harry"
