>will not unlock. Has anyone ever had this problem and maybe know how to

Inside the driver's-side door, there is a plastic arm that connects the
electric mechanism to the lock unit.  The plastic piece tends to wear out and
disconnect at the ball joint.

On my 944, I removed the e door panel, reattached the plastic arm, and
everything worked fine for about a month until the unit presumably
disconnected again.  I'm going to replace the piece this Spring.
