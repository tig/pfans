I just put on the D40m2 tires on my 951 225 ft and 245 rear. These are great
tires so far. Very sticky and quiet. I also had falkins on. Even when i
went to buy the d40m2 (suggestions from everyone, thanks) the dealer tried
to switch me to the falkins. The falkins must have very good price margins.
Also the dealer (Wheel Works) matched the tire rack prices + shipping. I
saved about $40. I was a little worried about side wall flex on the front
tires because i have 225's on 7in rims. Everything seem o.k. Thanks again
for all the help!!
