  Hello all! Another new guy here. About the tire choices that Paul
Ingebrigtsen asked about...I.M.H.O. I think you have the perfect set-up to
use "R" compound tires(B.F.G R1's,Yokohama 008RS or A032R,etc...) at all
times. You don't drive many miles a year(most likely only in nice weather),do
3-4 track days a year(where the additional traction is FUN),and want a
cheaper tire.(The Mich. Pilots you mentioned,225/50/16,$220-ish @ Tire

wild. My friend Bill Buetow was getting much more than 5K miles with
Yokohamas. Either way enjoy your new tires,tell us how they work out.
