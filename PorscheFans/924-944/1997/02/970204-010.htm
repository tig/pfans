Roger,
      I have replaced the rear speakers in my 944, and had to remove the rear 
windows in order to remove the panels covering the speakers.  (The foam had 
rotted on the original speakers)  I used pieces of plexiglass to carefully pry 
the rubber moulding for the rear windows while removing the glass, and used a 
similer technique while installing.  Once you have the glass removed, you will 
have to peel the vinyl from the window openings, as it is glued.  I also took 
the opportunity to clean the rubber on the rear windows with the glass removed.
I am interested in what you did to place the larger speakers in your front 
doors.

Good Luck!
