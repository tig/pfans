>>
>>  6) Does the 944 use an antenna amplifier?  If so, what are the details
>>  (where is it, what wires "run" it, etc...)
The later 944s use an antenna in the windshield and an amplifier. You need 
to power the amplifier when the radio is switched on. I did it using the 
power antenna wire coming out of the stereo. The wire you need to connect 
is one the same harness as the antenna connection itself. The factory 
manual has a picture of it.
