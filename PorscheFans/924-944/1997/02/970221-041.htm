If the engine is putting out too much heat and is over-loading the cooling
system, then it seems like there's a large range of possibilities you could
explore, such as the ignition timing, possible exhaust restriction, and
abnormal accessory loads.  To make the job less time-consuming, you might 
want to restrict the initial scope of your investigation by accounting for 
the 1) intermittent nature of the problem, and 2) the problem appearing
out of nowhere.
