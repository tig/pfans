Actually, Porsche's prices have come down to earth on certain items (in
the US, I don't know about other countries).  In order to compete with
aftermarket parts retailers to gain market share on the most commonly
purchased parts, PCNA has created the 'Porsche Express' line of parts. 
Same quality, lower prices.  You should be able to get a catalog with
prices at your dealer. 

Some examples include belts, brake parts, body seals, foglight/body lenses, 
engine gasket sets, bearings, control arms, hoses, filters, clutch parts, 
CV joint stuff, cables, A/C parts, fuel pump, and cooling system parts.

I just got to purchase new AC and PS belts that has 'Porsche' printed them 
for the same price as aftermarket belts.  They used to be double in price.
