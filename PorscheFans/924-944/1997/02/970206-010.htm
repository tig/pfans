	Sometimes dirt and grit can get into the workings of the mechanism
	and cause it to hang. If you'll look in your owner's manual,
	there's a description of how to operate the system manually. I've
	found that if you try this, you'll often get the dirt out by 
	cranking on the thing when you lower it. Be prepared, if it comes
	loose, it'll close automatically and take your hand with it!
