>  After sitting for a couple months the pass side power window in our 89 S2
>goes down just fine but won't go up ;-( I tried both switches to no avail.
>Is this a common problem or is there something easy to look at?

Hi, Bob,

the passenger side window regulator circuit goes through both of the 
window switches (i.e. when one switch fails, they both are inoperative).

The easy way to see which one is the culprit is to swap each with the switch
for the driver's side window and test it there.

If all else fails, you can remove the passenger side door panel and 
apply power directly to the motor to raise the window...
