 Your battery problem could have been caused by those lights. When the
lights are on, the altenautor (spelling?) can not keep up with the drain of
the light. I just read a tip in the "Automation" catalog that when the
battery is drained (sometimes by storage) the altenator will not fully charge
the battery and could be ruined by always charging. Use an ohmvolt meter and
watch the battery. Good Luck.
