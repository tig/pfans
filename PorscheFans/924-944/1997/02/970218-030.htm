'82 through '86 NA 944s came with the same 2.5 litre 143 HP motor. '87 and
'88 motors had the increased compression (up from 9.5:1 to 10.2:1) and 158
HP. The '89 944 (the last 8 valve NA 944) had a 2.7 litre motor, 104mm
bore and a compression ratio of 10.9:1, putting out 162 horses and lots of
torque.

I don't know about "better dash layout." I have driven an 87 944 for 6
months and I have had my '84 944 for 1.5 years now and I much prefer the
early dash. Besides the non-generic aesthetics of the early dash, I find
it more logical and easy on the eyes. No manual-mania for finding the trip
meter reset button, no ugly digital clock and very logical vent controls. 
The 85.5> dash is just just...too Japanese! Am I the only list member that
prefers the boxy, full-of-character, early dash??
