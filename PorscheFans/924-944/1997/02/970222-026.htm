> Also, they said I had a radiator leak, but four months later have never had

	What a bunch of crap. I deal with this radiator thing
	all the time. Mine has little bitty leaks from time to
	time that my shop and I keep a close eye on. When we see it
	leaking, we crimped that spot again to make sure it's tight
	and this will stop the leak for 4-6 months. Bottom line; unless
	water is pouring out a big hole somewhere, you're ok so long
	as you watch the radiator religeously (you say you do) and
	fix those little leaks when they do crop up. Also, make sure you
	drain and flush the coolant when you're supposed to. The radiator
	can clog up otherwise...
