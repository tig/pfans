Watch out for Simple Green on these cars.  I think that it may stain the wheels 
permanently.  The PO of my '87 951 used the stuff on the silver forged "dish" 
wheels, and they have permanent streaks.

Every once in a while, I spray down my '87 engine with P21s wheel cleaner.  I 
start with a cold car.  I get everything wet, then spray on the P21s.  Then 
after going over a few areas with a gentle brush (e.g., toothbrush) I hose it 
off.  Worked great the 3-4 times I've tried it!.  MAKE SURE THAT YOU USE 
PLASTIC BAGS TO COVER KEY AREAS, LIKE THE DISTRIBUTOR CAP AND THE TURBOCHARGER 
WATER PUMP.  THESE THINGS PROBABLY DON'T WANT TO GET WET!

On a really dirty engine, I'd just do more brushing...
