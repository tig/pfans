"Question about powder coating an exhaust"

If you want a visit from your local fire department by all means have your
exhaust powder coated.  Burning your car up is probably the extreme, but the
least you will do is have a lot of smoke coming from under the hood until the
powder coat or enamel burns off.

If you can not stand the rust color, must be NA as the turbos are stainless,
have it bead blasted and paint it with a high temp exhaust paint.  It will burn
off but not as fast and with no smoke.  If you want to shoot some cash, send it
out to be ceramic coated.  This will last but is not overly attractive.
