I'll probably get flamed for this, but I've run BFG R-1's for the past 2
years at autocrosses and drivers eds, and I cannot run the recommended tire
pressures without being very loose. I run no more than 25 lbs all around.
Tire wear is not great (you get an interesting center groove) but the car
sticks. BTW my car is an 87 951. Some of the 911 guys run their R-1's at 20
to 22 pounds, so I'm not the only one that opts for low pressures.
