Colin wrote:
> I have a problem with the brake lights on my (RHD) '80 924. Basically,
> they won't come on unless extreme pressure is applied to the brake
> pedal.  

My '80 931 has brake lights that only come on with moderately hard
brake pressure.

Unlike many manufacturers who use a brake pedal position sensor to
activate the lights, Porsche uses a hydraulic pressure switch, mounted
on the master cylinder.  If it is a problem for you (like you're
afraid you'll be rear-ended), you can replace the switch.  Make sure
you pre-fill the new one with fluid, and re-bleed when you're done.

If your car stops as it should, then the brake booster is not
involved.
