another common problem with our water pumpers. 

Check the individual lenses in the tail light assembly, note there are 
five different separate lenses. Just use some silicon sealant RTV Dow 
Corning 732 or something similar and smear in the gaps between these 
individual lenses and you will stop the filling. 

PS wisest to drain the lenses first !!!!
