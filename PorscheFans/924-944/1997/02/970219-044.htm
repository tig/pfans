I would take a good look at all of your hoses. Maybe check the coolant,
stsrt the car, and take it for a boot. Then get inside the engine(or
underneath, or ontop) and really check everything out.

Pay close attention to the heater valve and related hoses, it is at the
back of the engine and who knows where leaking coolant would trail to from
there.

If it is any piece of mind, I had my Water Pump go and the coolant seems
to collect inside the timing belt cover and drip from the bottom of that.
