Regarding 951 8x16" wheels and the addn'l 1/2" inset:

You can run 'em on the rear of an earlier car, but only with 225 R1
rubber and it's VERY tight.  Not really worth it.

You may or may not know that the 245/45/16 BFG R1 is somewhat of an aberation:

Here are the BFG R1 tires:

		Theoretical 	------Acutal -------	
Tire	Aspect	Width	Dia	Tread	Section	Dia
205	55	8.1	24.9	7.3	8	24.8
225	50	8.9	24.9	8.4	8.8	24.8
245	45	9.6	24.7	8.9	9.2	24.5

It should be substantially wider than it is, so the 245/45/16 can be used in tight spaces
(like the front of a 951 or even 911 carrera, or rear of 911).
