Make DAMN sure the click click click is not the pinion bearing.  If it continues
you'll take out the R&P + all gears.

The CVs usually humm and the pinion clicks.  If the pinion bearing is
clicking you'll nee a new transmission CASE..not cheap.

Yes this has happened to me which is why my current pinion bearing is
pinnned (rather than press-fit) in place.
