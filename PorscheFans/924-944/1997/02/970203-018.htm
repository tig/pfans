I've had the 3 spoke steering wheel off my '83 944 several times.  It's 
a straight forward procedure. Disconnect the negative battery cable. 
Remove the horn pad starting at the loweroutside corners by pulling it 
off. Be careful of the horn contacts. Disconnect the horn wire. You 
might want to mark the shaft and wheel to assure proper realignment. 
Then use an appropriate socket on a 6" extension to remove the large nut 
on the shaft.  Then pull the wheel off the shaft. 

Installation is the reverse.  Don't forget the washer under the nut and 
remember to attach the horn wire.
