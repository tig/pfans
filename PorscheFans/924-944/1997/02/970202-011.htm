Farzaan, sorry to hear about the bath your car took.  I thought of something
you might want to look into.

Do you think the water reached the top of your transaxle?  I suspect you have a
vent up there (my 911 does).  If so, you might want to check your tranny
fluid.  If you got water in there, it won't just settle to the bottom.  All the
churning of the transmission will get you a really nasty water/oil emulsion.
It's really sticky, and will be hard to drain.

If you drain out some fluid and it looks like a dense foam, you've got a
problem.  You might even consider changing the fluid a couple of times to flush
out all of this mess.

Good luck!
