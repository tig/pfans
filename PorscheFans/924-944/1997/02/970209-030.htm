Hi, i just read about your problems,
i had a similar problem on my 944S2 about 1 year ago and it was easy
to fix: Probably the micro switch, which controls the motor stopping
at the 'closed' position, is loosened. This micro switch is found
near the sunroof motor, which is located at the driver's side of the
luggage compartment. You have to remove the carpet to get to it. You
have probably just to tighten the switches little screw at the correct
position (try out the position, where the sunroof locks properly).Don't
forget to fix the screw with some glue or enamel.
Good luck and greetings from Germany
