Marv just posted a nice write up on his sunroof info. Reading it only confirms that there are substantial differences between pre 87 and post 86 sunroof systems. So if you have been confused by any descriptions this may be why.
The basic theory between the two systems is the same but the execution is quite different. Also - beware that the change was actually made in late 86 so you may have the later style system in an 86.
