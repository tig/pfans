The 1980 924 S was a regular 924 with the 924 Turbo S Sport Suspension. It 
came with 5 spoke spider wheels, 4 wheel disk brakes, Koni Sport Shocks,
23.5 mm torsion bars, and the same sway bars as the 1980 924 Turbo S. This 
was known as the M471 option for the 1980 model year.

The factory did not make many of these cars, and because of that some
people consider them the best of the 2.0 ltr. 924's.
