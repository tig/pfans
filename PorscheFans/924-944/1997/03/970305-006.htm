On to your sun roof question - when you say that you have to hold down the button and nothing happens - is there any noise or complete silence? Also look at your sun roof motor and see if  you have a early or late style sun roof. The entire system was changed some time in 86 including the motor, track, cables and latches. The later motor looks very similar to a window motor and is smaller than the early style. The early style does not have any connectors in between the motor and the relays - it's hard wired.
