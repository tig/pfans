Hmm.. According to my sources, the 944GTR you are referring to is actually a
924 GTP Le Mans. 2.5l 944 engine, 410HP, 7th place overall, so I guess we
are talking about the same car. The chassis number is 924 006, and it is
clearly a rebuilt 924, at least the body is from a 924 (I have a picture
here). The Porsche museum in Stuttgart, where this car is located, has this
car on display as a 944 GTP, but all documents mention this car as a 924 GTP
Le Mans. (According to an article I have on the car, at least.)

BTW, it also won the price for the shortest time spent in the depot, with
only 56 minutes of 24 hours.
