Hi, CD.  I have a similar problem with my '88 944S sunroof.  When I
bought it about 6 months ago, the sunroof seemed to work fine.  About a
month afterwards, the racks that push the roof up and down started
slipping on their pinions.  Then I lost the ability to raise/lower the
sunroof altogether, but it seemed to be a motor control problem.  I
think the raise/lower switch on the dash is supposed to have different
functions depending on the position of the ignition switch.  Anyway, I
am still able to unlock the sunroof (detach the racks from the roof)
using the switch, so I can take the roof off and make it secure again,
which is all I really care about.  I keep meaning to take apart the roof
to check everything out but I haven't had time.  I don't really like the
way the roof just tilts up in the back, anyway; I just love being able
to take the whole huge thing off.
