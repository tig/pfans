Wow!  If I were you I'd look for another service shop!  An alignment would be
a 
waste of money with a worn suspension component. As for the motor mounts,
I'm not sure where the third one is, but if the two I know of are bad (the
one 
under the exhaust manifold usually goes first), you should replace them.
Rough idle is the telltale symptom, but it's not harmless. That vibration can
eventually cause components to break or come loose.  If you do return to the 
same shop, you might want to run any repair estimates they give you past the 
group to make sure you're not getting hosed.
