 If you are planning on carrying compressed air in your car I would 
STRONGLY reccomend you purchase a quality portable tan rather than 
using an "old freon cylinder". Freon is VERY VERY dry in storage, 
compressed air is relatively high in moisture content. You have no 
way of knowing when the interior of the thin walled "old freon 
cylinder" is rusting and getting weak, with potentially disasterous 
results. 
                    My $.02 worth
