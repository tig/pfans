Any balance pipe should join the left and right exhaust systems just in
front of the cat(s).  Since no flow will actually go through the pipe it
can be a smaller diameter for ease of installation.  It's only purpose is
to improve low end torque and quiet the exhaust, which it will do very
nicely.  I have one on my V-6 Capri and it made a huge difference in
noise.
