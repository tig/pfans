     Mark,
     
     I put Tweeks anti-squeal shims on my normally aspirated 87 944 after 
     going nuts with the squeaks.  End result was no squeals for me at all.
     
     I just replaced pads and rotors on my 89 951.  I did not see those 
     shims listed by Tweeks, so I found some goo that the previous owner 
     left in the glove box.  I clipped the top of the goo bad and spread 
     some on one caliper pad (on the back side!  Your brakes will 
     absolutely not squeal if applied on the front side, and you won't stop 
     too well either), rubbed the two pads together and pooped them in.  No 
     squeals yet.
     
     I also used a clean paper towel with carb cleaner on it to clean the 
     surface of the pistons were they contact the pads.
