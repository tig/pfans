>A question to those who have driven both of the above mentioned or similar
>models.  How would you compare them in terms of driving pleasure?

It depends on what you are really looking for.  If you want raw speed and
loads of horsepower, the 951 is the ONLY way to go, especially the '89.
However, due to expensive exhaust components and the nature of turbo systems
in general, the S2 is more reliable and will have lower repair costs.  If
this will be your primary car, I'd recommend the S2.  Good luck, no matter
what you choose.
