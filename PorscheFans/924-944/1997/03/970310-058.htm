> I also have a minor power steering fluid leak.  The leak seems to be coming out of the 
> canister or the two hoses under it.  Could that possibly be responsible???? Occasionally 
> I see fumes rising from the front right corner of the hood, and the area around the coil

	As far as the power steering leak goes, you've got a couple
	of possiblities. Number 1, that horribly engineered canister
	and rubber hoses are giving way. Replacement of these is
	pretty straightforward. Number 2, the power steering pump is
	giving way and leaking. Not too expensive but a pain. Number
	3 (pray it isn't this), the steering rack is going out. That's
	expensive. try to determine where exactly it's leaking from.
	That should tell you what you have to replace. Good luck!
