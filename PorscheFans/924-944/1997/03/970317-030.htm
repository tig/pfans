I'll take a shot in the dark at this one.  It seems that
it could be fuel pressure related.  You might check your
injectors (did the 84s have injectors?) to see if they
are leaking.  Also, check the fuel lines.  Since it always
starts on the second try, the system might have built up
enough pressure by then.  Can you stop the car and
then restart it immediately?  If so, how long do you have
to wait until you encounter the problem?
