     Rob,
     
     Reach under the lip of the fender wells.  The Turbo S had those wells 
     "rolled" to allow for a wider wheel/tire combination.  If you have a 
     944 now you will understand when you reach under and grab it.
     
     Also the '88 should sport the "S" after the Turbo insignia on the 
     back.
     
     Front shocks should be Koni with the adjustable perch.
     
     Sound system should have the 6 speaker system with four of the 
     speakers in the doors.  Two speakers are located in the armrests.
     
     Also get the VIN number and call PCNA.  They can tell you every 
     possible thing about the cars original specifications you would want 
     to know.
