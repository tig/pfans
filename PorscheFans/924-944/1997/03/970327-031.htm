Porsche alloys are pretty sensitive to chemicals so you have to be
careful.  The factory only recomend using P21S (found in Automotion)  to
clean wheels.  My father got streaks on his 928 rims when using some
chemicals to clean them and the only way he got them removed was by
completley redoing the rims.
