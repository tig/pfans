Hi Andrew -

I've installed and Optima battery in my 951. My experience may apply to
your situation. I used a battery with terminals on the side as well as on
the top. I laid it on its side with the top terminals pointing inboard. I
connected the negative lead to the (previously) top terminal. I had to
push the lead back thru the firewall a little. I connected the positive
lead to the side terminal into which I first screwed an adapter post. No
changes to the factory wiring or terminals were required.

Hope this helps.
