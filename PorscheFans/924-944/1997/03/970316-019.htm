Nick,
I often travel to events this way, especially when my kids come along.
I have THULE racks, and I use the basic rain gutter hooks that came with
them, though I had to bend them to change the angle a little so the door
would
close without pinching the rubber too badly. The rear hooks fit nicely in the

space over the rear quarter window.   Be sure to straddle the sunroof with
the 
towers so the weight rests on the strong part of the roof.  Have fun!
