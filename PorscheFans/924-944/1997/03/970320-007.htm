> I have been reading all the question about adjusting the belts and I was
> wondering about the automatic tensioner. After using the (famous) gauge
> what if it is not right. How do you adjust the string tension to the 
> correct value? Do you move it then tighten down the hold nut? Thx
> 
The automatic tensioner only applies spring tension when the retaining
nut is loosened. If this does not apply the correct tension to the belt
you can move the tensioner by hand or with a tool to apply the correct
tension. Of course you will tighten the nut a little to hold the tension
in place while you measure your measurement.
