     On my sunroof, with the ignition is position 1 and the button held 
     down, the 2 toothed arms withdraw from the sunroof, but the motor 
     doesn't stop.  What seems to happen is that when the toothed arms 
     "bottom",the slipping clutch on the motor operates and prevents the 
     gears stripping.  I don't know if this is the intended action.  If it 
     is, your problem may be that your slipping clutch is set too tight.
     
     Alternatively, I may have the same fault as you, and my lightly set 
     clutch is all that's saving my gears!
     
     It would be very interesting to know if other people's motors DO stop 
     when the arms withdraw.
