Going from 86-> 87 or 88 will get the following:
	Semi-Automatic timing belt Tensioner
	Airbags
	Larger front wheel bearings
	Possible ABS
	53mm offset wheels
	Anodized spare tire
	SUBTLE INTERIOR IMPROVEMENTS/chassis stiffening
	NO trans oil cooler unless special ordered.

87/88 -> turboS/89 will get you the following
	Sport seats (on some)
	Limited slip standard with larger thickness gears/Cooler
	larger turbo/247HP standard
	10 speaker audio standard
	larger sways
	25.5mm torsion/stiffer springs
	Stiffer suspension bushings throughout.
	Konis -w- front adjustable perches
	7 or 7.5_+9" forged alloys
	225/245 tires standard
	928S4 style big brakes and ABS standard

You can't upgrade a turbo to turboS for $3000.

On the other hand the price gap seems to be much closer to $5000+
unless you get a rose-metallic-quartz-plaid model, in which case the
price gap seems to be closer to $300 since the color and interior is
less appealing to many buyers.
