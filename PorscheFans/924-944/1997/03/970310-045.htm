Question :       Am going to replace the shocks on this 
car as I have no idea when it was done last. OEM OK or should I go 
for the expensive ones? Plan to dothe work myself... recommendations?

Reply :           With regards to the shock replacements, I replaced 
my front shocks with KONI adjustables, to allow for different driving 
styles i.e. My driving style and my wifes' driving style.  The shocks 
are top adjustable, and when adjusted to firm the car now corners
fantastically, and when set nearer to soft the car has a smoother ride
and a less aggressive tendancy to follow the seams.  The fitting of
these shocks, I feel, has greatly improved the handling od the car and
the wearing of the edges of the tyres (due to less body roll).

Hope this info is of interest
