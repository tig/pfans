The factory service manual says to make the measurement at the dme plug,
not at the sensor. In any case, by making the reading at the dme plug it
rules out the possibility of wiring being the culprit. Also, it's much
easier to crank the car and make the reading since the dme plug is sitting
on the passenger floor!
