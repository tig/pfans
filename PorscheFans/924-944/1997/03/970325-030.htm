Matt,
     I have not obtained the adjustment tool yet, but Jason Burkett
posted the Koni phone number today, 606-727-5028  As I understand it,
the tool looks like a U with pins sticking down.  The pins fit into the
round plate on top of the shock body where the piston enters.  You
rotate the round plate in one direction or another.

  I use a floor jack under the jack point below the doors to raise one
side at a time, and place jack stands under the Front and Rear jack
points.  I use wood blocks to protect the vehicle.  The aforementioned
jack points are mentioned in the Owner's Manual, Haynes manual, etc..  
if you need to locate them.
