Hi all;

There've been a number of posts about "will my Porsche last over 100000
miles".

I just thought I'd add my .02 worth.  This AM, I had my '84 944 inspected.
 It has
150,000 on the odometer, and never had anything done to it except the usual
(clutch, w/pump, cam belt, etc.).  Regular maintenance of course.  I was a
little concerned because I noticed some brittleness in the plug wires, but
figured I'd give it a shot.

I'm in the Dallas-Ft Worth TX area, where they have recently changed the smog
check portion of the inspection.  Used to be just an idle check.  Now in
addition they perform a 2500 RPM measurement.  At idle, I am allowed 120 PPM
HC and 1.2% CO.  I had 10 PPM HC and 0.00% CO.  At speed, (same allowances),
I got 13 PPM HC and 0.03% CO.

From these readings, I would assume that that means little/no blowby,
excellent performance from the "brain", etc.  Not too shabby for 150,000
miles I think....  Just thought I'd pass on my experiences.
