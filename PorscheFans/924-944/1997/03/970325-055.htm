shut the car door the back of the roof popped up. I opened the door reached

had retracted all the way into the car . This was quickly going from bad to
worse! I played with the switch and got the roof closed but stopped before
the fingers retracted all the way. I finally had gotten the roof closed and
secured, so I pulled the module back out. Unfortunately I'm right back
where I started,. With a sunroof that I can't use. Can someone please shed
some light on this for me.
