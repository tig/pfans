The sunroof motor unit has an adjustable clutch that prevents the motor
from stripping the gears once they have moved to their limits.  The
adjustment has a specification for just how tight to make it, measured in
inch-lbs.  I have never done this repair myself, but am only repeating the
info. given to me by the mechanic who did the repair for me.   He warned
me that my repair bill (ultimately $300) would be high due to the
difficulty of getting the clutch adjusted exactly right.  He said it
requires trial and error.  I hated to have to pay to get this done, but my
roof was stuck open, snow was on the way, and this is my commuter car!

Hope this helps.
