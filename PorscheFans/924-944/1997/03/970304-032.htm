I write in response...
I don't agree.  The downshift to first is really hard on the car, even with
a match rev downshift, and it's a pain in the butt.  The car can maintain enough
momentum that a downshift to 1st is not necessary.  The key I think is the
tire choice, a 17" 255/40R17 street tire is geared taller than stock and
also does not allow as much speed to be carried through.  If you are
running a 245/45R16 R-tire, then you can hold more speed through the corner
putting you into a better rev range and as with all turbo cars, you really have
to get on the throttle right after braking to get the boost up on the exit.
