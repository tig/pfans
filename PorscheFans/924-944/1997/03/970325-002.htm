661-2780) control arms on my 89 Turbo.  Since the Turbo has the M030

Eric guarantees his work on street or track for 3 years.  I am satisfied
with his work.  He also supplies Tweeks with their rebuilds.  Tweeks has
been very happy with his work with zero returns to date.
