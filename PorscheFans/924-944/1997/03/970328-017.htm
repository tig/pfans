Marv, thanks for the terrific post.  Through reading it, I discovered I
have a defective or unconnected microswitch (the one that controls the
limit for retraction for removal).  The motor strains against the little
clutch and continues to run if I hold the button down after the arms have
released the roof.  I previously thought this was normal. 

A few trivia notes to add.. the microswitch in the headliner not only
informs whether the roof is in place or not, it also shuts of the
windshield washers (I wonder why!).  Also, the ABS computer on later cars
will lock the sunroof if the arms are retracted, when the car starts to
move.  You will hear the arms lock at the same time the ABS motor does its
test cycle when the car just starts to roll after first starting out.
