  Water is a by product of combustion,
  That is why you must change oil more frequently
  when you only drive short distances. The Oil doesn't get
  hot enough to make  the water evaporate on short trips.
  Also that is why some people think they burn a half quart of oil
   on a long day trip. What is really happening is the oil finally
  got hot enough to make the water finally evaporated and
  that drops the oil level.
