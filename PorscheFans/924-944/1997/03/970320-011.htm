The CPU for the airbag is marked "Siemens" and it is located behind the glove 
box.  It is attached to the body of the car with special break away (aka 
"cheese head") nuts, which require a special tool.

I have just ordered this tool from Linda Grigsby at Carlsen in Palo Alto and 
will get it tomorrow.  The P/N is: 000 721 925 90.

The special fault connector is also located in the same area.  It is an 
unterminated plug with three female sockets.  Pin number two is the key item, 
according to the reset directions.
