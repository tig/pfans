For the 944S owners I would recommend the following:

944S Test Plan DME					WKD 494 121
944S Servicing, Assembling and Adjusting		WKD 493 521

The first book covers the DME, including the wiring diagram and tests for
each sensor.  The second book covers belt adjustment, engine removal,
torque charts and general service  procedure.

I bought mine used, at a swap meet but I am sure these should be available
from PCNA.
