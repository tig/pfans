Hi, Mark,

You need to use an antifreeze that doesn't contain phosphates.  Zerex Extreme 450 is 
okay.  For temperatures down to -13F, use 40% antifreeze/60% water.  For temperatures 
down to -31F, use 50% antifreeze/50% water.  I hope this helps.
