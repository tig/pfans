If I make the assumption that by "pegs" you mean it goes to the 4+ or
5 region, do not worry, it is supposed to do that.  You mentioned snow in
your email, so I do not know how warm or cold it has been where you
live.  However, on a nice, decent day with more than 50 F outside, after
running the car for a while (at least 30 minutes), especially if running it hard,
you should see the pressure needle drop to less than 4.  How much less 
depends on the oil circulating through your engine.
