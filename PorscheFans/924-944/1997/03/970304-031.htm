The hubs warm up from the rotors' heat, thus the wheels act like fins to the
hubs and dissipate heat.  One would need a pyrometer to see if the painted
wheels hold in more heat than the non-painted ones.  I am not sure though that
the heat transfer coefficients are high enough to the point that in non-racing
applications or autocrossing one would care.
