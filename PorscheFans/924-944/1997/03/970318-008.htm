Hi ya,
I have had this problem with several cars in my past; mainly with my 
1978 924.  What I *think* is the problem is that your not giving the 
fuel pump a chance to pressurize the fuel lines.  Please try this and 
let me know what you find:  Get in and insert the key.  Twist it to the 
RUN position (not the start position).  Now WAIT about 8 to 10 seconds.  
During this time you may or may not hear a hum from the fuel pump behind 
you (below the rear deck carpeting on the passenger's side).  Now start 
the engine.  I think it will start right up.
