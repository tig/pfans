Daniel,

Just had mine done by a local shop "Alberta Wheel Repair". They removed
the tires, plastic bead blasted the wheels, refinished the wheels,
polished the lips, and mounted and balanced the tires.

They did all of this on a set of 17" C2 turbo rims, and did them for
$400.00 canadian plus 7% Sales tax, end result $428.00 Canadian for
probably the nicest set of rims I have ever seen.

This seems to be pretty reasonable for this job, I have heard of other
shops charging a little more in the $450 u.s. range plus shipping.

My advice is to find a shop that can do the job locally, and properly the
first time.

Best of luck,
