At 08:45 PM 3/4/97 -0600, you wrote:
>My '89 951 has a horrible squeal from the brakes.  They squeak about 
>60% of the time and it is not necessarily tied to specific 
>environmental conditions ( I have already tried a harsh stop from 
>about 100MPH to get the glaze off but it did not help).  The PO had 
>the local dealership put new pads on it and I believe them to be 
>semi-metallic, heavy on the metallic.  These pads also seem to fade 
>when they really heat up.  Can anyone recommend some pads the first 
>and foremost don't squeak and second outperform OEM semi-metallics?
>
Try Metalmasters. They don't squeal much, they stop pretty well, and they
aren't real dusty. Be advised, however, that the rear pads may rattle, which
may cause you more anguish than squealing. If so, a thin *aluminum* piece of
sheet stock can be placed under the pad to stop the rattling.
