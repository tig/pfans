Starting in 1984, the sunroof was always an additional cost option on 944
cars.

Most cars that dealers ordered on spec had the sunroof. The cars that did
not have them were generally specially ordered by someone who needed 
and/or wanted it that way...

That having been said, my calculation is that 95% of the 944 cars on the 
road have sunroofs.
