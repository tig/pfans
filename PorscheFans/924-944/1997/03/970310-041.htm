I run my 944 against a well driven 924S.  He has more hp, it's an 88.  Mine is 
an 84 motored 83.  He has less drag.  I'm usually faster.  Stick is the issue 
as you suspect.  These chassis need a wide track up front. He has trouble with 
any larger than 7" rims and 1/4 in. spacers in the front.  This limits his 
tire size options.  I run 8" rims with a lot more rubber and plenty of 
clearance.  This is the downside of the 924S....your class rules may limit 
your rim size choice also.
