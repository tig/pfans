I worked in a law library for many years, fiche does not do pictures very
well at all, it is probably fine for diagrams, though some details (like
important parts numbers) may not be legible on printouts... another problem
is that if you can't find a decent fiche copier/reader, you won't get clean
photocopies (read- black text on dark grey pages)... also, if you did not
photocopy enough pages, you will probably have to get a loupe and a backlit
table for casual late night home viewing, or wait to go back to the public
library in the morning...

I'm not trying to rain on anyone's parade, I need these manuals too, but I
think fiche has very limited usefulness...
