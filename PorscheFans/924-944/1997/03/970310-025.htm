	The PO (and original purchaser) of my '86 951 bought a Porsche 944
Electric Saratoga Top for the car for $399.00 on 11/27/89.  I have owned
the car since spring, 1994 (it had 30,820 miles when I bought it). 
	 The top has been perfect for me- never leaks, squeaks, rattles, or
anything else.  It just does what it is suppose to- let in filtered
sunlight and go up and down.  A word of caution, though.  You may want
to keep your leather seats and dash extra cared for so the sun doesn't
dry out the leather.  (I also cover my seats with towles if I know the
car must be parked in the sun for any length of time.)
