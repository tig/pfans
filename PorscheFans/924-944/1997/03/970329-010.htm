Carl, I went through the same question. I live in San Jose California, we
have a tire works here. When I went to the Tire Works store to get a quote
I asked them if they would match the "Tire Rack" add. They actually had 
the add behind the counter. They matched the ad added shipping and I bought
the tires from them. I did have to pay sales tax and the tire rack I would
not have to. Also if I would bring in tire to a store I would have to pay
mounting. Over all I save about $20 a tire. Maybe you would be a better
negociater (and speller) than I am.  Good Luck.
