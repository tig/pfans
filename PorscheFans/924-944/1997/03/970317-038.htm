The only thing that I can suggest is that maybe when you reinstalled 
the steering wheel originally that you reversed the Airbag connector. 
 As I recall it is not keyed in any way so you could accidentally 
reverse it.  If you do take it apart yourself make sure you disconnect 
the battery and ground yourself to the chassis, one static discharge 
and you eat airbag.  You might also call your P dealer and ask if this 
is a possibility.
