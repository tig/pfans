I'm glad to see I'm not the only one!  I was wrestling w/ the same problem
when I bought my 951.  The solution is so simply, I'm embarrased to admit it
took me about three days to figure it out.

You can reverse the latch without removing it from the belt.  Simply fold it
over itself (double it to 1/2 the normal width) and ease it through the slot
in the latch, twisting as you pull.

It's difficult to describe (kinda like trying to describe a spiral staircase
with both hands in one's pockets), but esentially, you're turning the belt
over, in the groove of the latch.  Make sense?
