No point in aligning the front end with a bad ball joint it is a waste of 
money.  Bite the bullet and get it fixed, You may not need an alignment if you 
replace it.  Same deal with the motor mounts.  Rough idle is not the only 
result.  You could damage other parts from the resultant vibrations.   
Spend the $ now or spend even more later....... 
 
PS:  Get another shop to check it out.  They should not have recommended an 
alignment if you need a ball joint in the a-arm.......If this is the same shop 
checking out the motor mounts I'd get a second opinion on both fronts. Telling 
you that it'll only cause a rough idle is accurate.  I'd be concerned about 
their competence.
