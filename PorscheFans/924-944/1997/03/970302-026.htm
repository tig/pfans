Hi Spiros

On my 1990 C4, the Autothority chip gave a small improvement around 6000
rpm, but was somewhat worse than stock at rpm less than this. Net result
was a slower 30mph to 100mph time in 3rd gear. The chip did have a
modification that reduced the liklehood of the engine dying with the
lightweight flywheel it has, otherwise I would have gone back to stock. I
suspect that on the 964/993 normally aspirated cars, performance
improvements will be hard to come by with a simple chip change.
