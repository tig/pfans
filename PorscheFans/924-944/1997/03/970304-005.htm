On Mon, 3 Mar 1997 cwhanlon@eai.com wrote:

> Do most people with 944 turbos keep their cars in 1st?  Everytime I shift into
> second I end up redlining it at 60+ miles and hour in a slalom, way too fast to
> make any quick turns.  The next turn after a slalom usually is pretty tight and
> requires a down shift (into 1st), I'm wondering if it is better to avoid that
> awkward downshift into 1st, and just leave it in 1st...of course I've only been
> to one autocross, so I have little experience...

I've been autocrossing 10+ years and IMHO the 951 needs to be upshifted
into 2nd and downshifted into 1st for slower sections. Common beginner
mistake is to stay in 1st, believe me , it's slower! You most definitely
need to heel and toe downshift. If you're not proficient at it, practise
in your daily driving. Have fun.
