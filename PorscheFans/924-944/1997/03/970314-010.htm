There are a few items for you to look at.


First make sure your timing belt is aligned properly.  The cam shaft housing
timing mark for TDC should be aligned with the clutch bell housing mark.  If
these are aligned correctly you might want to check out the throttle position
sensor or the air flow sensor.

Also, there is a TDC sensor next to the inspection hole of the bell housing
(viewed from top of engine).  It is next to the speed sensor, you might want to
check this out (they both have wires going to them).

Good luck!
