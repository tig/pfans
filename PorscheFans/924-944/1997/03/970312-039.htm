 You are looking in the wrong place, shocks don't stop roll, roll is a
function of  weight transfer and spring rate.

Will Hartje

Right.  Shocks won't limit the total amount of body roll, they only affect
the rate at which you achieve the total amount of body roll your
springs/torsion bars and sway bars will allow.
