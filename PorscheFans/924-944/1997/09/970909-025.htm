Hi James,

Check under the hood (emission control sticker) and see if your car says
that it is certified for 49 states or all 50 states.  If it has a fifty
state emissions sticker, the only CA requirement is a smog test (everyone
has to get one every two years).  If it has a 49 state sticker, CA will
charge you $300 to bring the car into the state.  You will also need a smog
test.

I brought in an 86 951 with a 49 state sticker, and a 94 968 with a 50 state
sticker.  If I look at both smog tests, the 951 was cleaner than the 968.  I
had to pay the state $300 for the dirty 951.
