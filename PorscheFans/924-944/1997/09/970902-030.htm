Save time and aggravation. Buy a new bolt, a right-angle drive for your
3/8" or larger electric drill and drill the sucker out. A right-angle drive
suitable for the few times I will ever use it cost me under $20. You will
need a short bit to be able to get a good straight shot at the bolt. Use a
bit large enough so that it will self-center in the inside of the
triple-square bolt to get started.

The bolt drills easily and once the head comes off it will likely unscrew
by hand. Position a shop vac hose near the work if you're anal about metal
chips.
