If it is not an S model it will be bolts, and S will have allen screws.  If 
it is indeed a bolt get a 1/2 inch drive six point socket (a good brand) 
and a long breaker bar or cheater of some sort.  These things are tight! 
 Some wd-40 could not hurt but be careful what you spray it on.
