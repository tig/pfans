Thanks to Steve Gomberg's advise about removing the AC dashboard assembly, I
successfully replaced the switch today.  That complete circuit board assembly
was replaced by the PO six months ago, probably for the same switch problem.

When I went to the dealer to purchase the switch, the parts man could not
find it in the '86 microfiche.   The '89 and later cars use the switch
without the circuit board attached, as in my '86 951.  Luckily for me,
someone there knew this.  I had not seen the switch part number posted before
so here it is:  944.653.922.00.
