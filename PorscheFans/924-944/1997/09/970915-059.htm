Todd Sims Posted:


>What is the recomended cleaner for phonedials, also what to clean carpet
>with (coffee/coke stain) ?  thanks in advance..

Try P 21 S 

It is available at most speciality shops/Mail Order/Porsche /Mercedes/BMW 
dealers
A German product that works rather well.

NOTE: Once the wheels are totally clean (cleaner/ploish) apply a couple 
coats of wax.
This makes the P21 S work much better, it also is very safe for alloy 
wheels.

The stains can be removed with mild detergent and warm water. I have used 
Spray Nine, but be careful it is a rather strong cleaner.
