Did you happen to check to see that both filaments in the dual-filament
bulbs are intact?  Sometimes, when 1 of the filaments goes, it "freezes"
the action.  The turn signal relay is probably listed as the "flasher"
relay in the relay board in the engine compartment.  Consult your owners
manual or lid on the relay board for exact number and location.
