These lamps... I wouldn't get them unless you find a design that is
specifically set-up for your car, and I doubt that this can be found... 
You would probably need, 2 high voltage transformers that you need to
find a mounting place for, RF shielding (these things are incredibly RF
noisy), and the light output will probably not be as good as an H4 setup
with 100 watt bulbs.
