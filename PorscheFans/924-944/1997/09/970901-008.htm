The little plastic caps and o-rings for the injectors are available at any
Pep Boys store (or probably any autoparts store, for that matter).  
Many U.S.-built vehicles use Bosch injectors - I bought an injector 
seal and cap kit for a Buick Grand National, which uses Bosch injectors
of the same style as the 951.  I think it was about $8 for 6 caps and 12 
Viton O-rings.
