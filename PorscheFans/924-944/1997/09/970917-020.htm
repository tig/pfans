A grateful thanks to all of you who suggested solutions to my shimmy
problem.  The solution was the locking lug nuts.  As I had previously had a
full alignment done and had the wheels balanced multiple times, I went
straight to the locking nuts and put them adjacent to the stems as
suggested by several kind people. I also re-torqued the remaining nuts just
to be sure, and that was the end of the shimmy.  Jim
