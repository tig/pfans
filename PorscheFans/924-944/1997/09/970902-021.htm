OK.  I figured out what's wrong with my 82 931.  After hours of anger 
and frustration, my friend and I figured out that the car was leaning 
out intermittently due to some electrical fault to one of the pumps.  
The relay was hot enough to cook eggs on and the fuse blew every time 
I turned off the car, so we hard wired the fuel pumps to the battery 
with a cockpit mounted switch.  Now the car works fine.  

Also, without the relay or fuse in place, the frequency valve stays on 
continuously (car on or off).

Does this indicate a simple relay problem?  I'm hoping it's not wiring
!!
