I took apart the seat control on my 88 951.  When I first pulled it out, it 
would only move about an inch but with a little wiggling, the wires came 
out about another 3-4" inches.  If worse comes to worse, pull the seat.  
Slide it back, two bolts inside the rails, slide it forward, same thing at the 
back.  Disconnect the wires and get some slack from the bottom to work 
with.  These are pretty simple inside and not hard to take apart.
