I liked that, to me it looked like the car had been converted to no pop-up
headlights.  Similar to what was done with the late 300ZX.  The pop-ups
are cool toys, but given the choice I would rather not have them.  Cleaner

them ought to be cheaper than replacing a cracked Hella.  Plus, you can do

in my experience ignored, or could not see if I was too close (Nobody
ignored a flash from the 100W Hellas :-)) )
