Alyn,

Yes, you can change the seals on the cooler without removing the manifold. 
I just completed this myself some 2500 miles ago. You will need a seal kit 
and pressure relief vave alignment tool (most of the parts suppliers sell 
them for about $15 with the seals costing about $35). It's difficult and 
will try your patience, but if you persevere and stay calm, it should only 
take about two hours. But then most of the repairs on a Porsche are 
difficult and try your patience. Make sure you check the cooler for leaks 
while it's out.
