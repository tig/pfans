In fact, the 968 Turbo S (the factory's "ultimate" incarnation of performance in 
the 924/944/968 evolution) used an 8-valve head--essentially a modified version 
of the 951S motor. I seem to remember reading somewhere that Porsche believed 
the 8-valve to be adequate for this application.

PowerHaus in Arizona has done 968 turbo conversions using the factory 16V head. 
From what I hear they've experienced good results...does anyone own, or has 
anyone driven, one of these beasts?
