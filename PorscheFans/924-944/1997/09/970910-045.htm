Here's a little tip -
Everytime I change an automotive bulb (also useful for household bulbs in
high moisture areas - bathroom/kitchen/outdoor), I work a small amount of
"vaseline" onto the contacts/base to keep moisture/corrosion to a minimum.
Don't use a great deal - just enough to leave a light film.  You'll be
surprised at how easily the bulbs come out the next time you have to change
them.
