In response to this questions, the simple answer is no. Calipers will not
present a problem, but the offset of any fuchs will pusj it too far out.
Smaller 6" wont be a problem, but that is defeating the purpose, since the 6"
will perform badly on the car. The minimum width needed is 7", but if these
are used on all 4 corners, you will find a tendency to oversteer. 8" on the
rear will solve that problem, but now, clearance will be the new prob.

If you want a forged wheel, use the flatdish (928 style). Let us know if you
need further info.
