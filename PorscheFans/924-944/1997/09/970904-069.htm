The ULW is Ultra Light Weight.  They are a special version of the SP8000
that are about 4lbs lighter per tire.  The ULW in the olden days was 
considered outrageously expensive, but now in the day of Pzero and S-02,
the ULW is about the same and the normal version are actually "cheap".
