> I believe what you are referring to are the "lifting points" which are
> commonly used for a workshop hoist - and, they also can be used with a
> floor jack, although you will only be lifting one wheel at a time, whereas
> the jackport (under the centerline of the door, which also happens to be

> support the car on jack stands (here, again, with some type of padding).

While looking through an old issue of Christophorus, I ran across some

believe that I'll continue using the frame rails when I raise my car,
since certain jack stand pad shapes can distribute the weight unequally
