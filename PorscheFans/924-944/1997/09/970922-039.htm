	My 968 Workshop Manual says that the cup design wheels (5 spoke) fitted to
the 92 and 93 model years should have the locking nut on the color-coded
stud arranged at the opposite side from the valve. The book indicates this
is a departure from previous practice. (Incidentally, the crest is shown
pointing away  from the valve.)  As of MY 94 a different type of security
nut is used that is of the same weight as the wheel nuts.
