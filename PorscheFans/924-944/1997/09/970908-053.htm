First thing to check is the clutch hydraulics to see if the release fork is
traveling the required distance (as measured at the inspection port). 
Hopefully this is your problem.  If not, it could very well be that your
clutch disk rubber 'donut' has fragmented and the rubber pieces are
interfering with the pressure plate release action.  I experienced very
similar symptoms to Steve's and the latter scenario was the culprit.  Only
thing, I'm not positive that the 944S used the rubber center disk in 88. 
I'm sure someone on the list knows.
