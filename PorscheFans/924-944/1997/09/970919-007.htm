One other option for carrying tires to the track is to saw off the bracket
that holds spare tire and stick one in the well. You can get a 7" wheel in
there easily, and an 8" with 245/45s if you also remove the two lower
braces in the spare tire well. A bit drastic perhaps, but it frees up
space.
