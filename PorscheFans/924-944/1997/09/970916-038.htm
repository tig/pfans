First a piece of advice I picked up from Automotion.  Do not plane the
head.  Have it straightened on a head straightening bench.  The 924
Aluminum head is very close tolerance and planing it may be too much.  As
far as wear limits, check the Haynes manual.  It is fairly good about
limits.  Good luck!
