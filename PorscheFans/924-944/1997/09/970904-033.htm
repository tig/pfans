I installed a racing cutoff switch with removable key in the sheetmetal
above the driver's left foot, below the fusebox, behind the charcoal
canister. I brought the cables (bought at Pep Boys for about $20)  across
the top of the firewall in the engine compartment, through a notch cut in
the top of the fender. The installation is invisible inside and out, makes an
excellent theft-preventative (the post that accepts the key just protrudes
from the carpet, and is un-identifiable with it's cap on), and is incredibly
convenient. Of course, now I leave it in most of the time, since I replaced
my battery with a good one ;)
