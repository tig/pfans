If anyone else encounters the same problem, I made a little tool that did the
job nicely.  After brazing a bar onto a 22mm socket (standard practice, I
think), I cut away the top 1/2 of the socket, half-way around.  In essence, I
cut away 1/4 of the socket, which allowed me to turn the locknut, while
holding the rod very securely w/ a 10mm open end wrench.
