Bill -

Be careful of putting the amp in the same space as the subwoofer.  The main
concern would be heat dissipation since amps running subs can get quite hot.

I placed my amp in the cubby where the spare tire is.  Don't do the dumb
mistake of drilling randomly to mount the amp, I put a hole in my gas tank
(dumb, dumb, dumb).

Another good spot is under the passenger seat.  There is enough room for a low
profile amp.

Going through the firewall is a breeze.  To the right of the battery and a
little lower, there is a rubber "grommet"? that has a cable already running
through it.  Just pry it open a little bit and thread your amp wire at that
spot.  From there you can go either under the door sills or alongside the
cassette holder and under the carpet to the rear of the car.  I chose the
latter method since it is a cleaner and shorter run.
