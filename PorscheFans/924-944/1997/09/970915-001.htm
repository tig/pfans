I assume by closed end mufflers you mean the Supertrapp pipes, if so
these pipes have a virtually empty end can (Just a straight through
perforated tube). The closed end are bolt on disks with spacers between
and a large hole though the middle and a closed end cap, you can
add/subtract disks to your hearts content to alter noise/performance
(Very useful for the annual test.)
These pipes only work well on singles or twins. they don't flow enough
for a multi.
