One thing I should add to my recent post about modifying the throttle on
early 924's: after installing the nut-bolt pair, check the full range of
throttle motion to ensure positive return. If the bolt head is too large
or too close to the rest position of the primary throttle, the secondary
throttle cam can turn too far and cause the linkage to lock at full
throttle. Yes, I did figure this out _before_ starting the engine back
up.
