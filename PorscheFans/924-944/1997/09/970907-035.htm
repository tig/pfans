left.  The reason for the rotor wear limit is so that the pistons don't extend
themselves too far out of the caliper.  (although a thinner rotor can be more
susceptible to warping)

A mechanic once told me an old dirt track trick was to shim the brake pads so
that they could use thinner rotors (so they could save weight) on their cars.
