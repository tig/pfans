They are jacking points. However, I would not put jack stands under 
them because of the risk of their slipping off the round knobs.  I 
would (and do) use these points for the floor jack.  For jack stands, I 
use the frame rails at the front and the torsion bar mounting flange 
(i.e., the nice flat aluminum member) at the rear.  Alternatively at 
the rear, I use the torsion bar end caps.
