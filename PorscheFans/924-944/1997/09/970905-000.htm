Use a battery cover from an 85 or later Golf/Jetta/Vento.  They use the
same battery size (US designation Group 41), and it just clips over the
sides.  If your battery is slightly different than oem, it will just sit
on the battery without coming off.  Looks factory, and dresses up the
engine compartment. 

This is highly recommended, as it hides the battery terminals from the 
outside world (visible through the hood gap) and keeps rain from washing 
corrosive battery acid from the top of the battery down into the battery 
tray and firewall.

 -Gregor.  Battery cover from 85 Jetta in service on the 944 2.7.
