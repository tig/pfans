20W50??? Why don't you just run molasses? ;) Seriously, you will get a

I eat a quart of oil per day at the track and I appear to eat a quart
every 500-1K miles on the highway. There are no apparent leaks and the
rear is clean so I don't think it is being burned unless it is *real*
slow. Reports concerning 928S4s consuming a quart every 400 miles are
frequent. I suspect Porsche has recently concluded a deal with the oil
companies to eject it through a special opening whenever the car goes
over 40 mph. ;)

make a racket if you aren't used to them, but the lifters sound
completely differently. Listen to another 944 and see if it sounds the
same.
