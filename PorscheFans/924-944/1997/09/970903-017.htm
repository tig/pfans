<<     Does anyone have any recommendations on how to gain more fender 
     clearance on an 86 951. The trick is doing it without having to 
     repaint my fenders. I only need a small amount. Only the outside of 
     left rear tire rubs. And only on rare occasions. (I have 255s in
the 
     back) Still, its obviously not a good thing so I figured I'd look
into 
     fixing the problem. Any suggestions? (other than trading it for a 
     later model with rolled fender edges from the factory)>>

1) Jack up car.
2) Place aluminum baseball bat between fender and tire.
3) Carefully lower car.
4) Roll baseball bat over the tire to roll the fender edge. 

If you are careful you won't have to repaint the fender.
