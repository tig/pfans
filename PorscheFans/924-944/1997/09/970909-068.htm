Lots, but check the intake manifold gasket, lower injector o-rings, and

short boot next to the throttle body.

If the vibration is very noticeable at idle and clear up above 1200 
RPM, it's typically the motor mounts.

     7. Is there a mechanical heater regulator somewhere? It seems like

There is a heater control valve next to the oil filter, plumbed between

     9. Gas tank - smells like leaking while filled to the top. Any
     potential weak places?

Check the plumbing to the top of the tank, which is under the rear 
carpets.  Also check the filler neck by pulling back the interior 
carpet over it.

     10. Clutch pedal does not release itself to the end - the clutch
     itself works just great, but the pedal stops after that... Will
     bleeding help?

Yes, but you need to use a positive-pressure bleeder connected to the 
brake fluid reservoir.  Getting air out of the clutch hydraulics is a 
bit of a pain.  Try jacking the rear of the car quite high to level the 
clutch master cylinder, which is mounted at an angle that traps air.
