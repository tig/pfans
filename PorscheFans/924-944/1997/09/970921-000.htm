Jeff,
The screw is an idle speed adjustment, The o ring probably needs to be
replaced.

The hose you refered to is for cooling your altenator. The factory put
the altenator stuffed so tight you cant even see it. Try crawling under
the driver side fender (with the car jacked up a few inches) the alt is
back about even with the tires look straight up. I would try to place
this hose on. The altenator gets no air, this could be bad for the
diodes and brushes.
