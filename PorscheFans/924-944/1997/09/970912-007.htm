This is a pretty common problem. There's a gear in the speedo that breaks and
then you still have speed indication but no odometer. You need to pull the
speedo out and have it fixed in a speedo repair shop. I had to do the same
thing on my '84 when I bought it. I think mine cost 70 bucks about 2 years
ago and I also had the clear lens replaced. 

I think Haynes had a pretty good instructions for removing the speedo. One
note, those two things that look like screw covers above the speedo on either
side are NOT screw covers. Look a little under them and you'll see the
screws.
