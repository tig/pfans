In our '82, we've had two 100w Kenwood amps working in the rear cubbies for
over 15 years, no sweat. There's plenty of volume in the space to circulate
cooling air, and the metal around the cubby soaks off the heat.

The power leads were installed by the simple expedient of drilling a new
hole in the passenger-side firewall about 6" inboard from the battery
mount. After leads were installed, they were wrapped well in tape where
they pass through the firewall. Then, the hole was caulked to seal it. 

Hope this helps......
