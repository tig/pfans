Hello Richard,

Well the 944 Prototyp for Bodywork and Spoiler was the 924 Carrera GT,
presented at IAA '79.
Porsche runs with these 924 Carrera GT at Le Mans '80 with a 3 Car-Team.

For the engine they used the half 928 V8 engine, because they were
unhappy with the modified Audi engine and much more with the 924 Turbo
engine.
And in LeMans '81 runs a car under the label Porsche 924 GTP, but it was
the first 944 Turbo.
In '84 a Zero-Production 944 Turbo wins at Nelson Ledges (NL), Ohio,
USA. NL is a 24h roadrace for Production- and Prototype-Cars.
