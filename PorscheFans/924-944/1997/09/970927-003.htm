The factory wear limit for a 924 head is 139.55 mm (5.494 in.).  This is
measured at the rear of the head (drivers side) from the bottom of a
machined hole down to the cylinder head sealing surface.  I just
measured an uncut head and it measured out at 5.524"in.  We have being
having 924 heads decked for years.  If your head has gotten hot, be sure
you have it pressured tested before you have any work started.
