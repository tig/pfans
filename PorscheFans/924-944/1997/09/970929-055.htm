Marv,

I had this same problem with the cam shaft seal, I replaced it as a
preventative measure, however it developed a leak soon after replacing
(replaced it during the winter, and it leaked in the spring)

To get the most up to date part, order the seal from the dealership.  While the
two seal that I replaced had the same lip, the newest seal (dealer bought) had
a slightly different lip on it (hopefully better).

Make sure you lubricate the lip of the new seal with oil.  Without lubrication,
the lip can wear away quite quickly when the engine is spinning.

Hope this helps...
