>You'll wear out the inside edge prematurely.

This is not always true.  If you drive the car VERY hard, negative camber
will actually even out tire wear.  A car that corners hard regualarly will
wear the outsides of the tires first, negative camber will even this out.
That is the point of the negative camber, to keep the most rubber in
contact with the road (or track) at all times.
