Absolute easiest way I know of to tell an S from a non S is to look at the
brakes. If the front caliper is bigger than the rear, it's an S. The 928s4
caliper on the S is strikingly bigger than the non S as well as having a
thicker roter. The regular Turbo's calipers are the same size, front to
rear. Of course someone could have retrofitted the bigger brakes, so you
could also look at the tranny and see if it has an external oil cooler.(a
black plastic tube, coiled several times) Of course, this could be added as
well. 
I guess if your biggest worry in life is whether your car is one of the
best ever made, or one of the better than the best ever, your doing pretty
