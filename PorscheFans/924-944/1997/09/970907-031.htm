Ok here goes...

'84 NA 944 man A/C Cyclamen (maroon) metalic paint.
4 careful owners full logs and service history. 158,000 kms (approx 98750 mi)
With a little work to the stone chips on the front this could be a concours car.

-Car AUD$22,500 (USD$ $17,100) this was a little high but reflected history etc
-Insurance (60% no claim) AUD$740 per annum (USD$562)
-Minor service (due in about 2 weeks!) AUD$350 (USD$266)
        plus new cam belt AUD$178 (USD$135)
-Fuel AUD$0.78 per litre (USD$0.59/litre) (USD$0.16/gallon)
-Mechanics at porsche mechanic AUD$45-60 per hour (USD$34-46 per hour)
                (less for cash payment!)
-License is about AUD$300 (USD$228) per year.

Apart from the cost of the car I think our running cost are quite good,
except I use a lot of fuel, Australia is a BIG country and the 944 does
about 600 kms (375 mi) per week!
