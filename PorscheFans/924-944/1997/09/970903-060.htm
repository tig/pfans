You just need a Torx (T30?).

1) Disconnect the battery.
2) Wait 15 minutes to ensure no capacitive charge left in 
system.
3) Undo two Torx screws on airbag unit.  (one each side of 
wheel)
4) Lift airbag unit out (wiggle a bit if necessary)
5) Disconnect connector on back of airbag.
6) Remove steering wheel nut (24mm).
7) Lift off wheel (no puller required).
8) Contact unit is underneath and attached with 2 screws.  Read 
the manual regarding orientation before removing so you can put 
it back together correctly if removing.
