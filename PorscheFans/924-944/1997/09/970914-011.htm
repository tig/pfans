I have tested these plugs, and subjectively (extremely so), I did not
think they were any better than my usual NGK single electrode, rather
the contrary. (NA, 1985.5, 163 /10.8:1 euro version)  

The plug is a marketed in 4 or 5 grades, and the recommended grade for
944's (all of 'em) is the same for many other modern cars. I think this
plugs virtue will be longevity, not performance.

I think a three electrode similar plug is the recommended 968 plug, so
the concept will probably do no damage.
