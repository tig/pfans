I have the fisch - trust me, the catalog is ten times better. The 
fisch is hard to read and difficult to find what you want. The 
catalog is full of tech tips and other neat info. If you have a 924, 
944, or 928 - you gotta have one of these. It's cheap at twice the

that is available, complete with part numbers and detailed drawings.

my fische set for free (old set from local P-dealer) and found a 
reader at a local flea market (10 bucks) - but I wouldn't think of 
looking at those with this catalog available.
