I took my new '86 951 racecar (to be) to a BMW CCA driving school this last
weekend.  My car went down chipped, with R1s, Performance Friction pads, AP
600 brake fluids, roll bar w/ harnesses, and I had a BLAST!  To make a long
story short, the car was one of the fastest car there.  There were a few
supercharged E36 M3s, a Viper GTS, a ZR1, 2 E30 M3 racecars, etc. but the
951 held its ground, and was only passed two or three times in 4 hours of
track time.  I will be on the track soon again....
