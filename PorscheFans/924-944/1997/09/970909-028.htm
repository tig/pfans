Yes, it'll increase the already huge gap between temperatures at both ends
of the turbocharger even more, increasing the chances material fatigue and 
general wear.  I for one would not do anything with the exhaust portion of the
car which may lead to any increase exhaust temperature beyond the normal 
levels.

Turbochargers are mechanical wonders as they are.  Don't mess with them!
