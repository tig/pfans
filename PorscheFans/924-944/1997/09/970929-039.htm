Dear P-fans
    The aluminium washer is there to seal the drain plug to the oil pan. I
feel the aluminium washer (part #N-013-827-2 ) works best in sealing, the
coper one seems to leak. I'm not sure why? I know the oem Mann filters I use
come with the aluminium washers. 
    By the way, I like using the Mann "OC-75" filter (it is just a little
taller than the original filter (Porsche part #944-107-201-90)) and I feel it
has just a litter more filtering capacity. The Mann OC-75 is for 911 C-2
Turbo's 3.3L (320 PS.).
