	In reference to the "up/down" seat switches, I had the same problem and
attempted a fix as you have only to discover that the wiring does not
easily grant access which, give my ham-handed approach to things, rsulted
in a total breakage of the one switch.  Eventually, I contacted Kempton's
in Tampa (a salvage yard for Porsches) and bought a switch ($50?) - not the
whole assembly, just one switch.  It sat on the shelf until I found that,
to install my Wet Okole seat covers, I had to remove the front seats.  This
is not hard to do and, once the seat is sitting out of the car, I found
that the replacement of the switch was more or less a piece of cake.
