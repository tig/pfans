I also had this problem on my late departed 951. I thought it was shims
or pads, something simple like that. It turned out to be corrosion under
the stainless steel slider plates on the rear calipers was jamming one
side of the caliper. This was an expensive fix. Pull your wheels off and
have a look. If one pad is more worn than the other you have a new
problem. Go find someone that knows brakes, you don't want to find out
the hard way that you brakes aren't up to scratch.

Richard.
