PCA club racing doesn't specify ANYTHING about suspension settings. If
you want, you can run 5 degrees of camber, and lots of toe out. No one
will check for that. 

I quote the rules:

2. Suspension
   C.  Any suspension setting not requiring machining or modification
       of factory parts is allowed.

I think that you are allowed to use "Camber Balls" and other type
devices, but I don't know if they allow more chamber adjustment.
