     Saw your post to PFans list regarding front end shimmy. I had this 
     recently as well. 
     
     My vehicle has just returned from the shop having it sorted out. The 
     part that was replaced (and you haven't changed them yet) are the 
     track rods, the bearing at the end where the rod joins the steering 
     rack was worn. As soon as these were replaced and the tracking reset 
     the problem was gone.
