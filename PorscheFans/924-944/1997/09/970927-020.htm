Dear Mark
    I think what you are experiencing is detonation. If the DME detects
detonation, the DME will limit boost to 1.3bar on the dash gauge. If you turn
the engine off, then restart the DME will re-set to full boost untill
detonation is detected again. What ocatne are you using? How much boost are
you running? Max boost level should be 2.0 bar on the gauge(with chips, 1.7
w/o chips) .
    You might also check the CO Test pipe, located on the pass side firewall
area. Mine blew apart last winter on a cold morning while waxing it real good
(read: High boost levels), when I shifted I heard a blam! Then the rushing
air sound when ever I got on boost. Iterestinly enough, I could still attain
full boost - it just took awhile longer to attain full boost! That stainless
steel line is $145 so I simply removed the pipe and installed a nut cap to
seal the exhaust leak. I used a metric end nut adaptor Porsche Part
#928-110-475-01.   

						Good Luck
