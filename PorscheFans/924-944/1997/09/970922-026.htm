Regarding the recent front-end "shimmy" thread, somebody mentioned that 
the lock should be installed on the wheel stud with the "red mark" on 
the end of it, and that stud should be positioned close to the wheel
stem.

On my '84 944, there is no "red mark" on any of the studs on the front
of the car, and of course, my car has the infamous shimmy problem that
comes and goes at around 65mph.

So what do I do?  

Some background:  The wheel shimmy appeared after I bought new tires. 
I re-mounted the wheels and locks in the exact same positions as the 
original tires relative to the studs.  (The locks weren't closest to 
the stems).  After the shimmy appeared, I positioned the locks near 
the stems, and the problem has been relieved somewhat, but it's still 
there.
