In a 1985 944 I once owned we installed the following:

ADS 300i--front door speakers
ADS 200i--rear quarter speakers
ADS 7" subwoofer(s)--1 in each rear corner compartment cover

An ADS 642 Amp/Crossover (30wx4+sub power) took the signal from an
un-amplified Yamaha cassete/tuner head unit. 

This was a practical, clean sounding system that would also generate
respectable volume to compete with the wind and road noise.
