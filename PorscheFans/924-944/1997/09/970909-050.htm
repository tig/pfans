During this past weekends driving event, I spoke with the Alabama
region's Porsche technical expert, Bill Mitchell.  He mentioned the
width of the timing belt for the 944 has been increased.  He also
recommended buying the timing and balance shaft belts from a dealer to
ensure the most up-to-date version of the belt.  By the way, he has no
connection with the local dealer but has seen horror stories of belts
purchased from aftermarket suppliers breaking/separating after 10K
miles.
