For all of those experiencing 951 cutout caused by overboost, look for this
before tearing through your computers.  In the rear of the engine bay,
there is a small tube coming up from the exhaust, used for testing
purposes.  Through the years they will get rusty and can cut through the
wastegate hose, not allowing it to open.  Simple fix- REPLACE THE HOSE!!! 

I've known this to happen to 3 cars including one of my own.  Check it out
it just might be the problem.
