The beautiful phonedials on my '86 951 have all the counterweights
mounted on the outside of the wheel (not the edge between the wheel and
the tire, but rather the ~3" flat surface before that -- what's that
area called anyways?).  I thought it was odd when I first saw it, and
several ppl have commented on it as well; e.g., that they thought these
counterweights should've been on the inside (read: on the side where the
brake rotors are) instead of the outside.

They're little lead squares with their weight in grams and ounces
printed on it.  They're stuck to the wheel with a foamy/spongy kind of
doublesided adhesive tape.
