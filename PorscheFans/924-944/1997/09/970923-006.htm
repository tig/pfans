Hi,

  I've received several messages about renting a Porsche while 
visiting Germany. So I called AVIS today and here is the deal:

They rent new 993's.
Cost is 303.48 DM per day (~ $173.42).
You get 200 km free per day.
Cost is 1.57 DM for every km over the 200 km.
Cost for one week rental is 1517.00 DM.
You get 1000 km free. 
Cost is 1.57 DM for every km over the 1000 km.

PLEASE NOTE, that the above prices DO NOT include
the German Tax which is 15 %.

  As far as I know, AVIS is the only rental car company which rents 
Porsches here in Germany. 
  The phone number to reach them is: 0180-55577.  You must use the 
German country code when dialing this #. The country code for Germany 
is 49. Contact your operator if you have a problem connecting.
  
  Also, you MUST BE at least 30 years old to rent one, and have had a 
drivers license for at least 3 years. 

  Also, you MUST HAVE 2 credit cards (major ones like Am Ex, Diners, 
Visa or MC) with you when you sign up for the rental. 

   Hope this helps in the future for any Porsche drivers visiting 
Germany who wish to experience the Autobahn. Truly an experience 
you'll NEVER forget!
