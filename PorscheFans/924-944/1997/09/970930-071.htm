If your option sticker lists option 220, your car left the factory with 
an LSD.  Which is of course no guarantee that it's still there.  For 
that, jack the rear and turn one of the wheels by hand.  If the other 
rear wheel turns the same way, LSD.  Opposite means open diff.  No 
turning means broken pinion...
