Regarding the windshield replacement..  You have the right to a factory
replacement windshield, with costs covered by the insurance company.. 
expecially the folks with Sekuriflex internally coated winshields (not to
be confused with Sekurit, the actual manufacturer of the glass).  The
insurance company can not reduce the safety of your car to save on their
costs. 

Explain that the car's safety will be seriously compromised if your 
internally coated winshield is replaced with a plain one.

I haven't replaced a winshield since my Golf/Rabbit days, but when that 
fateful day arrived, I insisted on factory glass.  It took a few days for 
the insurance company to begrudgingly oblige.
