I read last week that someone on the list was looking at purchasing a Dino
Forza urethane steering wheel.  I just installed one, and the wheel was not
bad, but the hub kit was as cheap as could be... with an awful-looking logo
on the horn button and a real cheap casting.  I'd worry about gripping the
wheel too hard if I were a racer !  I'd recommend spending the extra bucks
for the MOMO.
