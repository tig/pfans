I don't mean to be "nit-picky", but I have to take slight exception to two
of the above items as being standard equipment on the original '88 TurboS.
I have a copy of the original window sticker(Moroni?sp?) from my recently
demised Limited Edition, and the M454 Auto Speed Control and M650 Removable
Sunroof w/ Elect Tilt were both extra cost options ($422 and $965
respectively) not included in the Turbo "S" option package ($5298).  Also,
FYI, the M490 Hi-Fi Sound System did not include a radio, but was only the
multiple speaker setup - the Reno Radio was $686 extra.
Just my $.025(as in litres).
