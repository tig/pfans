I've done this upgrade on an '87 944S.  You have to replace the front 
spindles with the appropriate part from an 87-forward 944 Turbo (non-S 
package ) or S2.  The rear hubs and semi-trailing arms are fine.  You 
of course need different rotors, and you should replace the calliper 
mounting bolts.  The rear callipers require the 4mm thick spacer 
washers which are original on the 944 Turbo for 88.

The upgrade makes a nice difference in stopping power and a huge 
difference in confidence when driving at the track.
