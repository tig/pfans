Robert,

I originally built a 1 cubic ft box to house my 8" MB Quart sub. I had
it in the back. I could close the cover ever it. It did impose somewhat
so I looked at alternatives. I ended up placing it in the right rear
fender well. I cut out a piece of particle board to fit over that area
with a hole for the sub. I sealed it up with caulking. The sound is
actually improved over the box, it can't be seen and no loss of luggage
space. The sub is very accurate and reasonably loud (enough for me) and
complements my 3 way MB Quarts in the doors. E-mail me if you want more
info.
