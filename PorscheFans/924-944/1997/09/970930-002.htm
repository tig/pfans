Roxanne,

Do me a favor and check your fuel pressure
regulator!  Unless you have a pressure gauge, you're
going to have a Porsche mechanic do it.

You will still get fuel until the pressure builds up (I
mean way up) and the engine will shut off as if you
have no fire!  Classic symptom of one gone bad -
after a few minutes, pressure gone, start engine, runs
great until pressure builds up and stalls!  Do this
enough times and you're going to end up replacing
your fuel pump!
