I have an amp running my front mids and tweeters and my rear full ranges
mounted in my drivers side rear storage bin. I is mounted to a custom cut
mdf board and then fastened to the body. Some have said they use velcro
but I opted for a more permanent installation.

On the board I have a power distribution block as well as a crossover
mounted on top of the amp. Ofset with some 3 1/2" bolts ant "t" nuts.

I then used some RTV to seal up the screw holes from under the body.

Just be careful to use appropriate sized screws and not to tap into
anything crucial!
