BTW,

I use white bearing lube on all my bushings and it does a pretty good job.
I clean and reapply every spring and in between that crawl under the car
and liberally spray power lube underneath. Not sure who makes it but it is
completely safe for rubber and plastics. Comes in a red and white labeled
can.

Other than that my weltmeister bushings have been pretty quiet.
