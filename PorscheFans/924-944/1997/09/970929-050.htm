	There is a Porsche Information Tech Book detailing the differences between
the two models, titled: "944 Turbo S" Information Technik, Part#WKD-495-021,
sells for $8.00 aprox 30 pages. I bought mine from "My-Porsche" on the Net.
There is also a neat "Tech-Spec Book" available that has all the technical
data for 951's: part#WKD-423-520, $8.00.

     The followingis what the book("944 Turbo S" Information Technik,
Part#WKD-495-021) states as the differences. Production is limited to 1,000
cars for 1988 model year.
	*Engine power 247 SAE HP
	*Sport running gear (M030)
*Stronger more powerful front brakes (simular to 928 S-4)
*manual trans w/stronger gears & limited slip
*Stronger clutch disc	 
*body paint in "Silver Rose Metalic" paint#P 823
	 color code F1F1
*Burgundy plaid interior cloth seats

The USA Turbo S cars have the folowing optional M-equipment as standard for
the Turbo-S.

M418 Protective side mouldings   M526 Door pannels in multicolor
M288 Headlight washers		  M437/438 Full power sears L&R
M030 Sport chassis		  M990 Full cloth seats
M593 ABS	   M490 Hi-Fi Sound pakage
M220 Limited slip diff.	   M454 Speed conrol/cruise
M685 Split rear seatback	   M650 removable elect/Sunroof
M425 Rear window wiper

	Here are my notes on the basic differences between the two models, for the
Turbo-S Model.
*Only avail in Rose Metalic for '88 MY (opional colors for"89 &up)
*Sport (read stiffer) suspension with adjustable spring perchases.
*7JX16 & 9JX16 Forged wheels with 225 & 245's tires
*Larger K-26 Turbo, max boost 1.82 bar absolute & differnt DME.
*Harder suspension bushings
*Reinforced front frame rails to the body shell (by the PS unit  	 
 and the coolant exp tank).
*Larger Brakes frt &rr with larger brake caliper pistons.
