I track my 968 hard and I have not had any brake cooling problems.  The =
only time I experienced significant fade was at Road America where after =
about 6 hard laps the brakes showed fade in Canada Corner.  (Not a good =
place!)  I use the Ford High Performance brake fluid as it has a higher =
dry boiling point than the ATE super blue and since its clear, it's easy =
to see if the fluid has become dirty or if there are bubbles in the =
line(s).  The Ford fluid is also less than half the price of the ATE so  =
it's cheap to change the fluid before every event.  Probably not =
necessary, but makes me feel good.

If you have the M030 sport suspension you already have the big front =
rotors and calipers.  If not you're still fine.  The brakes on this car =
are great.  Have fun!
