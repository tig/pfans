     Well here are my calculations on this subject. I've based the prices 
     on an exchange rate of 1.6USD = 1 GBP.
     
     Location: London, England.

     Gallon of Gas (UK Gallon) 4.92 USD.
     
     Cost of 1Yrs. insurance: 1216 USD.

     want to find out either ! 
     
     Prices outside London will probably be a bit lower esecially for
