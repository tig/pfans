The lights and fan are two of many items that are wired through the X
relay (G14 for 1989, check underneath the fuse cover of the central
electric panel to verify).  This is the relay that cuts power to the major
electrical conumers (fans, AC, headlights, radio, etc) when the key is in
the crank position to allow maximum current flow to the starter.  Remove
and replace the fuse to check for corrosion on the terminals.  If the
symptom pops up again, replace the relay.  If you are adventursome, you
can remove the relay cover to see if the contacts are worth cleaning up. 
You may find they are severely burned/worn away.
