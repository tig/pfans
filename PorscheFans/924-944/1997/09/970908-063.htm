To George's list, I would add the following.  If the triple-square 
socket doesn't work in the bolt head, try using a pair of vice grip 
pliers on it instead.  Use a pair with jaw teeth in good condition. 
 Clamp it to the head really, really, really tight, then turn.  This 
works well for this bolt and for any socket head bolt that's stripped, 
typically the CV joint bolts.

Because of this sort of problem, I generally like to replace the bolt 
every time it comes off, or failing that when there is any question as 
to the condition of its socket.
