My experience with Mobil 1 is that if your car has any - even *tiny* - =
oil leaks, they will become a geyser with Mobil 1.  This has to do with =
the low viscosity and low surface tension of synthetics - that's what I =
read anyway.

My advice is to crawl under your car.  If it leaks oil *anywhere* just a =
*minute* amount, that leak will turn into a river with Mobil 1.  =
Otherwise give it a shot.  If it starts leaking, change back to Dino =
Oil.
