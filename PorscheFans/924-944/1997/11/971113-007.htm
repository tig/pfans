IMHO, your best bet would be to look for an old set of "cookie cutter"
alloys in 7x15 size. They're around pretty cheaply (75-100 per wheel in
reasonable condition, less if they're rough) and the snows to go on them
aren't all that expensive. Look for Pirelli Winter P210s (more performance)
or P190s (more traction, noisier) in 215/60x15.
