I recently purchased some Texaco Havoline anitfreeze (product code 2353) that
was advertised as safe for aluminum engines.  I wrote the Texaco web site and
they responded that this product did indeed contain phosphates and that the
correct product for a Porsche water cooled engined would be Havoline Dexcool
Extended Life Anti-Freeze which contains no phsphates.

For what it's worth.
