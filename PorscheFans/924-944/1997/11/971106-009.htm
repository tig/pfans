	I too just replaced the tires on my 85.5 944 NA, also with 15inch
phone dials.  I put on some bridgestone RE71 225/50-15's.  I think think
that it looks realy slick, I was concerned at first that the tire would
not be large enough to fill the wheel whell, but that is not a problem.  I
like a 50 series tire over a 60 series, sure it gets a bit rougher, but I
dont feel that I have as much "tire roll (lateral)" as I did with the 60s.
	So my recomendation is go with the 50's all around, but then again
this is just my 0.02
