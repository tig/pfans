1) Check to see if there's a relay on the fuel pump circuit, and if it's
good. Also, try checking the circuit with a voltmeter, first at the pump
terminals, then back to the fuse block, both the inbound bus and the
outbound wire. Beyond that, I can't help.

2) The water may be coming from the rear hatch area, either a bad seal, or
plugged drains under the latch posts. Blow out the drains, and also blow
out the small drains where the spoiler meets the glass. That's where my
waterfall was coming from.

3) North Hollywood Speedometer is the shop to call about the tach. They can
probably fix it for a lot less than a new one. Look for their ads in
"Excellence" magazine, or PCA's "Porsche Panorama".

Good luck....
