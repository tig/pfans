If you have hydraulic motor mounts you can measure the height of the mount
to determine if its still good. Take a piece of wire and bend into sort of
a C-shape. Hold it up to the mount and place the ends at top and bottom of
the mount. Remove the wire and measure the gap between the ends. I dont
remember where I read this technique so i'm afraid I cant verify the
measurement but 61mm sticks in my mind. Maybe someone else knows for sure.
