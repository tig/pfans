Dave,
You WILL spend considerable $$$ on 944 maintenance/repairs that's a given.
Mine is now unofficially(so my wife won't raise hell) a hobby. As you well
know these are high end cars that are primarily 6-13 years old and
virtually all of them need TLC or more. Compared to the 924 the same
repairs on a 944 will be more expensive. Parts are probably a little higher
but labor will be significantly higher due to greater complexity. How much
is too much has to be decided individually based on your own comfort level.
A belt job at the dealer will usually run ~$400+extra for obligatory H2O
pump, clutch $1200+. If you can/are willing to do most of the of the work
yourself you can save a lot of $$$.
