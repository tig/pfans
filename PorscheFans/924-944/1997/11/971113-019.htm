>      I just purchased a '93 968 Tiptronic with 27k mi. and the brakes are
> very sensitive (grabs) at the very end of a stop.  My mechanic is telling me
> that I need to get new rotors in the front as well as new pads if I would
> like to get rid of the problem (glazing sp?).  It's about $400 for the front
> and I wondered if replacement is is the best solution, or if there are other
> solutions which might be less expensive.

It's easy to change rotors and pads on our cars ourselves. Got a great
deal on rotors & Perf. Friction. HP street pads from OG Racing in VA. They
advertise in Pano. For your car the parts should run $200 or less. Get a
second opinion and have the rotors thickness measured now, to see if you
can just have them machined to a clean fresh surface.

>  Also, it seemed odd to me that new
> rotors were required after only 27k mi, even if the car is 3.5 years old.

My rotors lasted 50K with lotsa auto-x racing.
