Nakarin,

This sounds like the boost is exceeding the safety curve.  When you
exceed this curve (i.e. boost at a certain rpm), the engine will do a
number of things.  One is to disable the fuel injection until the boost
and rpm come back down.

If you are running stock chips with a different boost bolt, you will
encounter this situation.  If that isn't the problem check for leaks in
the hoses going to and from the cycling valve.  The valve connects to
the wastegate, first intercooler pipe, and the intake boot.
