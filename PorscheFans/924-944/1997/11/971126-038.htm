The battery must be disconnected and reconnected in order for the clock
digits to flash and the buttons to be operable for setting time.  I recently
removed the two radio fuses to reset the clock but this didn't work.  Write
down your preset radio stations before shutdown.  This is the only way that I
can set my '86 951.
