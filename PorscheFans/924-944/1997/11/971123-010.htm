I forgot to mention that there are a host of other items that can affect 
top end speed.  Your car must be in top running order, no head wind, tire 
inflation is set correctly, tires themselves, and not running into a 
gaggle of bugs.

One trick used to sneak a few extra mph is turning those big sail like 
side view mirrors in toward the side windows to reduce drag.  I heard of 
one racing team waxing their car in a desperate attempt to give them an 
aerodynamic edge!
