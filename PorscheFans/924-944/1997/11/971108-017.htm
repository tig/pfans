I've chanched oil every 10.000 Km (6234 Miles) on every car I owned.
No problems what so ever. Try sticking to the same oil (type and brand).
This prevents the new oil from removing the oil-layer the previous oil
made. No harm is done but the first time the engine consumes more oil
than it did before and will afterwards. 
The various rubber components in the engine (for example seals) seem to
dislike different sorts of oil. They desintegrate faster.

Just my opinion.
