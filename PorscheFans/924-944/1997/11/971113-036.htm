Dear Steve & P-Fans
	I would suspect that the "heater control valve" either has a vacuum leak
(internally) or that the vacuum line going to the heater valve has come off
or not supplying vacuum to the unit. The heater control valve is located at
the rear of the engine, above the clutch bell housing. The valve is "T" into
one of the thumb size rear heater hoses, it has a round diaphragm with a
vacuum line attached. This valve is made of two pieces of plastic glued
together - they always leak coolant onto the clutch area. I would check this
ASAP! The space behind the engine is really tight - I would make the repairs
with engine cold.
