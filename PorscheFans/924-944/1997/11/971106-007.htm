Converting 924 brakes to 944 brakes is an easy, very worthwhile
conversion.

For the front, you will need from the spindle, rotor/hub combination,
and brake caliper

For the rear, you will need the rear hub, brake disc, backing plate with
parking brake mechanism, brake caliper, parking brake cable, and rear
wheel spacer (depends on the wheel you use) rear steel brake line from
the trailing arm to the caliper.

I would suggest installing stainless braided lines at that time on all
four corners.  the original master cylinder can be left alone.

You will now have the 5 bolt pattern, which will allow you to use late
944 telephone dial 7x15 wheel front and rear if you wish, or 6x15 924S
front wheels coupled with early 7x15 telephone dials in the rear (or
similar combinations.  I use 225/50 15 tires front and rear and it makes
a nice combination.
