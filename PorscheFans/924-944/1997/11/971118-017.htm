I had the same problem on my '84 944. It sounds like your cooling fan switch
(I think that's what it's called) has failed. It's a cheap part that screws
into the radiator and when the fluid reaches a certain temp. it activates the
second fan. You can find it on the driver's side lower part of the radiator.
It'll have two wires going into it. If you jumper these wires the second fan
will kick in. Just replace this part and you're in business.
