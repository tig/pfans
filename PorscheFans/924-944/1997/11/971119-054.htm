Porsche tells you to use M8 x 38 bolts with washers on the right hand 
side, when updating to the new style mounts.  You should also eliminate 
the 4 mm thick spacer washers formerly used.

On the left hand side, you are to measure the height of the foot of the 
bracket connecting the motor mount to the engine.  If it's 21 mm thick, 
use the same M8 x 38 bolts, and if it's 25 mm thick, use M8 x 42 bolts. 
 Lose the spacer washers here too.

The heat shield should not be removed.  In fact, Porsche added an extra 
one in 1986 to protect the oil pan gasket from catalytic converter 
heat.  It's a good idea to retrofit this to earlier cars.  The part is 
951.101.327.01, and you need two M8 x 20 bolts and washers to install 
it.
