Ok guys lets talk about reliability,

I drove an 83 - 944 from birth of car. It had problems, they were:
1.	Water pump  leaked at 75k
2.	Radio attena failed at 12k and 25k (after that I learned how to
rebuild them)
3.	Rear wheel bearing went at 85k
4.	Parking brake never really  worked
5.	The seat broke when it crashed at Watkins Glenn (so did the
whole back end of the car).

Then I got an 87 also from birth. It had the following

1.	Oil cooler seals leaked at 30k
2.	DME failed at 20k
3.	Water pump leaked at 80k
4.	Belt broke at 80k
5.	Clutch broke at 110k (Bad shift on track)
6.	Belt broke at 113k
7.	Parking brake still does not work


	Yes I ran the 83 on the track, lots of drivers ed. And don't run
the 87 other than daily driver. And when the 944 is in the ship I drive
the914 Track car around. No air, heat, radio, or power anything but
always runs and is great fun to drive on the street.

	So all in all I have been happy with both cars, but the 83 was
by far the better put together car.
