The cooling fan thermoswitch on my car is located halfway up the radiator
tank on the drivers (expansion tank side, for those in the UK and other
RHD countries) side of the car.  It faces towards the engine.  I haven't
replaced it, but it appears the cooling fan assembly needs to be removed
in order to access it.  Not too difficult, just awkward.  As another 
member posted, unplug it first to see if your fan(s) stop.
