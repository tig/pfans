HOW TO TIGHTEN FRONT THE WHEEL AXLE NUT

1. Release the drag from the brakes on the wheel disc, by slightly
spreading the brake pads with pliers.
2. Tighten the hub nut while letting the brake disc spin freely.
3. The inside washer (You do have a washer right?) will move JUST LIKE A
FEELER GAUGE under the nut when its just tight enough.
4. The wheel disc will STILL spin freely, with NO play.
4. That's how you tighten the lug nuts.
