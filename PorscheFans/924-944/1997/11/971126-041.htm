See if you cannot get your hands on Confor padding material for the 
roll cage.  This is the stuff the F1 guys have been mandated to use for 
the past couple of years.  I believe that Safety Devices carries the 
stuff.

I think you also asked about roll cage to the floor vs. to the frame 
rails.  Visualize your car on its roof with four (or six) legs of the 
cage sprouting through its floor pan.  This has happened.
