> I had a similar problem with my '89 944 NA.  I spent some time trying
> to hunt down the reason that my headlights would blink off and then
> back on from time to time.  Same symptoms, the lights stayed up, but
> off.  It turned out to be the ignition switch.  The little spring that
> returns the key from start to run apparently wasn't turning the lock
> cyclinder back enough, so a bump would sometimes kill the lights.  I
> still haven't replaced the ignition switch, I just turn the key back a
> little more from where it rests on its own.
