Sounds like the problem I had with my 1987 NA944.
It turned out to be the idle regulator. Once the car is hot it seems to
idle oke.

On my car the idle stabilizer was dirty, but on the inside.
Look at the FAQ at http://www.connactivity.com/~kgross/944faq.html
chapter 5.

I've cleaned mine. Maybe only cleaning the valve part will do the job.
In my case I've taken the whole stabilizer apart and cleaned the rotor
wich was very dirty.
No problems ever since.
