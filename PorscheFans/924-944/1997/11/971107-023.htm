equipment without them there!  Some may have seen this, but here in
Germantown, the cops have this huge electronic/digital billboard that has a
radar attached to it.  It shows the Speed limit on one side, and shows YOUR
speed as you drive by it.  Its ususally hooked up to a patrol car, but
officers are around--they just set it up here and there to 'scare' you.....

of "How high can you make the sign read" ;)  To make a long story short, we
got it to where the speedometer read about 167/168.  (I have the 180mph
speedo, of course)  The radar sign indicated 162.  So it was after all 5mph
optomistic----but I'll take anything over 160 anyday!
