Nick,

Take the cap off and bend the lip very slightly wider around the 
circumference.  This should give it a little more spring pressure and 
will keep the cap in place.

The hub-centric design of the factory wheels will, to my best 
understanding, keep the caps from coming all the way off of the hub.  I 
could be mistaken.

I would *not* drill a hole in the cap.  They are there to seal the 
wheel bearings from moisture.
