Dear Surya & P-Fans

	Check your X-Relay located inside the fuse box. The X-Relay switches off all
accessories during starting/cranking of the engine. This relay can  switch
off the head lights and the wipers, defroster, blower motor, ect...  Or the
ignition key contacts ( the electrical switch part) could be worn. Next time
this happens try wiggling the ignition key. The switch bellow the ignition
key tumbler could be worn. Last but not least - check the fuse for corrosion
- but this is unlikely since the high and low beams are on separate fuses. it
is not a bad idea to replace all the fuses in a older car ever few years. the
fuse blades will develop corrosion over time. replacement is the best
preventive maintenance.
