I think you're just hearing the turbo spool up and pump air.  Sometimes you
don't notice a noise for a long time.  Then when you finally notice it, you
can't "not" notice it, so it seems like it just appeared.  I'm lucky, my
mechanic is one of my best friends now.  He would almost pay me to have him
testdrive my 951.  You should have your mechanic test drive your car with you
so he can hear any noises, but It sounds to me like you're ok if the car's
running great.  The idle doesn't sound like a problem.  Sounds like it's set
a little low.  The idle adjuster screw is ontop of the intake manifold.  When
the car's at operating temp, bump your idle up a hair and see if that doesn't
clear it up.
