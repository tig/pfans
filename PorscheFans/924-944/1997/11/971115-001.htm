>I was wondering if anyone knows if a 924 (1978 European model) will run on
>unleaded petrol. It seems that the European Union will be banning leaded
petrol
>from the year 2000.

The PCGB Autumn magazine had an article fuel requirements. It says that the
76-79 924s can be run on super unleaded (98 octane) but use leaded for
every 4th fill! I think there may be some additives available which will
also replace the lead. 

The post 1980 924 can run purely on super unleaded.

I find that my 1988 944 runs better (no pinking) on super unleaded than
leaded (97 octane).
