928.361.016.00 is a 7JX16 wheel with 65mm offset. It will work on the 87up
944 cars with 52,3mm offsets.  (incidentally, I think this wheel was used
on the front of a 928. The rear was an 8JX16 - 52.3mm offset with p/n
951.362.116.01)     

>I need help.  Has anyone used 928 offset wheels on their 944?  Do the 928's

wheels with 65mm  offsets (p/n 928.362.114.20) without any problems.
