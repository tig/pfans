I would add to John Dunkle's very nice set of instructions a 
recommendation that you buy and use the line-up tool for the oil 
pressure relief bore.  If you do no use this tool, there is a fairly 
good chance that your oil pressure relief valve will bind and hang up, 
which is generally believed to be a bad thing.

There are two versions of the tool, the right one depends on your 
engine's year.
