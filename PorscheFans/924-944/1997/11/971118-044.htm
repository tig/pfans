Dear Mitch & P-Fans
	I'm not sure what the EA831 Project is/was. ?  My books explain that VW paid
Porsche to develop a series of economy cars that could also spawn off a
sports car project - this was in the early '70's. Porsche had two years left
in the contract, to finish the project, they were looking for a replacement
for the aging 911, but VW canceled the project before bringing the cars to
production. Porsche had hoped that VW would economy cars would be the
foundation for a all out sports car - a Porsche. This was the EA-266 project.
VW was paying the way for Porsche to build a 911 successor.  VW was paying
Weissach an enormous amount of money, first a few years earlier VW aborted
the 914 project, paying dearly. Now they canceled the EA-266 project. The EA-4
25 project became the 924 Porsche model. Which in turn became the 944/951
models.
