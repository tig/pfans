Ladies and Gentlemen. 
 
SCCA Classifications 
 
951: REGIONAL Racing Only if your lucky and have an ITE class (This is not a 
GCR endorsed class,  but a number of regions have added to create a home for 
Run-What-You-Brung,  no national standards).  You'll find a number of Old
SSGT 
, ex-World Challenge (Corvettes, ...) and other near race cars in this
class.  
Don't be surprised if your competitive one week and not the next.  The SSCA 
hates Turbo Cars,  too easy to cheat,  bad history in the SS classes.  This 
car will NEVER be classified on a national level for regional racing,  such
a 
shame... 
 
944 NA 2.5:  REGIONAL Racing Only in ITS.  This is a GCR specified class,  
universal rules for all Regions.   Yoou'll be running with 240Z-300ZX and
Gen 
II RX7s.   The 944 will be short on horse power at the LONG tracks (CRX will 
give you a run for your money),  short tracks can be a lot of fun,  the
engine 
mods are extremely limited.  Sorry no 2.7s 
allowed. 
 
The jist is:  If you own a 951 and it has a full Cage and External Ignition 
Cut off switch most regions will allow you to play in ITE if available. 
I've 
seen a number of GTS-PCS racers playing in this class and a couple of big 
dollar ex-WC Corvettes.  
 
I hope this add values,  and if not, Oh Well.... 
 
Happy Trails
