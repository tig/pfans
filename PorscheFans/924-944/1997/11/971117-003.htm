I had a similar problem with my 924 turbo.  I finally checked the wire (big
red)which runs from the alternator output to starter. I found that it had
broken half way through just as it goes into the connector on the
alternator, under the black shrink tape. Cut off the shrink tape and see. I
now have 14.2 volts at the battery! It looked like it fatigued maybe from
vibration. I put a new connector on in situ by buying a large "blind"
solder connector and drilling a hole on the end and filling it with solder.
It was the only way I could get access as the wire is quite short. hope
this helps from Oz.
