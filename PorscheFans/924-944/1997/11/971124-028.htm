DO NOT use your parking brake in the winter if you can at all help it. On 
almost all vehicles in the winter, you will have a problem with the parking 
brake freezing on, prohibiting your wheels from turning until the next thaw. 
If you can, use the transmission/engine to hold the vehicle on a level 
surface, or use wheel chocks(sp?).
