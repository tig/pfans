Bill, I wouldn't worry too much about that aluminum clutch housing.  It's a
pretty beefy casting and I'm sure you won't crack it.  However, you might
just want to invest a little ($20 - $50) in a flywheel lock.  This little
tool makes the job much easier and you won't even need a helper to keep
that screw driver in place.  I know these are available from several
suppliers in Pano or you might just be able to find something at Pep Boys
too.  Good luck.
