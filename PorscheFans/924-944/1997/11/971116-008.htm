Tom,

If you can find some local 944 owners who can recommend a good mechanic,
have the inspection done.  The car may have been perfectly maintained,
but even flawless cars can eventually have problems -- expensive
problems.  Treat the $100 expenditure as insurance and a little peace of
mind.  If in a few months, an expensive problem does occur, you'll kick
yourself for not having spent the money.  Besides, if they find a few
minor things wrong, it may even help you negotiate a few more dollars
off the price of the car.
