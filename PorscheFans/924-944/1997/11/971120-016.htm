According to the PCA Club Race Rulebook:

6. Body/Chassis/Interior
   F. Seats are free providing minimum weight of vehicle is met.
      Any ballast to meet weight must be places entirely in the
      driving compartment and must be securely bolted to the chassis.

So yes, one piece lightweight racing seats (like Kirkey, ButlerBuilt, 
etc.) are legal. Note that racing seats, with harness slots, are
STRONGLY recommended.
