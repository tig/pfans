Hi, Bob,

Actually, the 968 has a "Variocam" setup, which uses a chain tensioner that
changes the valve cam overlap to increase torque at various points in the
curve.

The 968 did not use the "Varioram" setup, but did use a redesigned intake
manifold
with a bypass passage, allowing a kind of pressure charging provided by
pressure
vibrations caused by the engine's intake rhythm.
