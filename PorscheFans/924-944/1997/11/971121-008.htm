I don't have info on European Turbos, but here is the data on U.S.
models (from PCA Parade Competition Rules):


MODEL	1st	2nd	3rd	4th	5th	R&P


944S2	3.50	2.06	1.40	1.03	0.78	3.87

('89-'91)


944T	3.50	2.06	1.40	1.03	0.83	3.38

('86-'89)


And, just for comparative purposes, the '87-'88 944S was identical to
the 944T except for R&P being 3.89
