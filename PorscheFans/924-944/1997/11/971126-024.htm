When considering an out-of-state car purchase, maybe you could try this
list to ask for the assistance of a local P-fans member to check out the
car and give their opinion. Offering $20 might help? Before I bought my S2
sight unseen, I had the reassurances via e-mail of other S2 owners who had
competed against it at races. IMHO an autocrossed Porsche owned by a
nationally competitive or hardcore racer, has a better chance of being in
better overall condition/maintenance than one owned by Joe Consumer. YMMV.
