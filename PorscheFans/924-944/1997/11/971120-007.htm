Hi there Porschefans:

I had such great response from you guys before I figured I would try 
again before going to a mechanic.

Question:

On my '77 924 I have great difficulty engaging 1st and 2nd gear when 
the car is cold. Once it warms up though everything is perfect.
Has anyone had this problem ? At first the Porsche mechanic said it 
might be the 1st and 2nd gear syncros ( high $ to fix) but with the 
temperature factor I am thinking it may be the transmission/gearbox 
oil which is not lubricating the 'box.

Also when I turn right the oil light and handbrake light come on. I 
have heard 924's have "dodgy" electrics but this is giving me concern. 
The dipstick reading says the oil level is normal so is this another 
electronic gremlin I live with ?
When driving I also get the battery light coming on and off for no 
reason, sometimes it begins with a glow then slowly turns red, other 
times it is fully red and this is driving at a fairly decent speed 
with not electrics on other than the lights and sometime the heater 
fan. The battery light disappears and appears for no reason. I do not 
think it is battery or alternator related as I live in Toronto Canada 
and have got superb cold cranking amps in the cold weather. Anyone 
experienced this ??
