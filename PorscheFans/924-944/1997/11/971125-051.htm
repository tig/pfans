Jeff,

My impression is that the foam is to prevent abrasive metal-to-metal contact
between the strut bearing carrier and the inside of the strut tower, the point
being to avoid introducing a rust point underneath the tower.

I had some thin plastic foam sheet, about 1/8" thick, that I cut to fit the
bearing carrier (plus a little overhang). The foam I used was similar to the
kind of stuff used for weather sealing house windows, etc.
