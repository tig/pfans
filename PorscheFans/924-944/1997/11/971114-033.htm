|Well, providing you weren't planning any hijinks in the snow.....sure. The
|point to remember is that skinnier snow tires tend to cut down through the
|snow to the pavement rather than toboggan up across it. The 15" wheels with
|60 or even 65 series snows will do this more effectively. Just make sure to
|match the  overall diameters to ensure good wheel clearance in the wheel
|wells.
|
|Also, be aware that roads in the winter, even if dry, are much colder, and
|therefore offer less contribution to traction, than they do in the summer.
|That, coupled with your snow tire's generally VERY DIFFERENT tread design
|and compound, should give you pause to think about taking those familiar
|on-ramps at the same pace you do in the summer.............
