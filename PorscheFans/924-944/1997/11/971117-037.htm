There is a typical noise made in fact by the gearbox, which may be what 
you are hearing.  If you mechanic says it's normal, and it is not 
something which is new and extremely nasty sounding, I wouldn't worry 
about it.  The typical noise sounds like chains across a grate, and is 
most common at idle and low speeds after the car has been thoroughly 
warmed up.
