Dear Jeff & P-Fans
	First, brake squeal is caused by the brake pad moving or vibrating in the
pad carrier.  You could try to use a lube (anti-seize, applied to the back of
the pad, the side that faces the caliper - not the rotor face!). I think
Porsche sells a gummy patch, that has an adhesive side to the back of the
brake pad's backing plate.  You can also buy these isolation pads from any
good autoparts store, cut to fit your barke pads. You might also try to slot
the pads to prevent the pads from glazing the rotors inturn vaibrating the
pad - and you know the rest.
