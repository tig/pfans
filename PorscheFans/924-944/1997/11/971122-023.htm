I do not know specific details about the Blaupunkt however I have seen many
radios act the way you descibe.  Electronic radios do wierd things when
they lose power or are subjected to volatage spikes.  Try disconecting the
ground cable from your battery and then turn the radio switch on for a few
minutes.  Turn the switch off and then reconnect the battery.  Often this
will reset the electronics.  Removing the radios fuse often will not work
as many modern car radios have more then one source of power.
