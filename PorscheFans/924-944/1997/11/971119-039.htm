Notice that 228 hp makes the specific power output of the S2 engine close
to that of the S.  228hp/2.990l = 76.25hp/l for the S2 SE vs 188hp/2.479l
= 75.84hp/l for the S. The US S2 specific output is 69.6hp/l, less than
the of US S.  This makes no sense that one would reduce the specific
output on a replacement model, except when you compare the 228hp rating to
the 217hp rating of the 951.  Can we have the NA motor be stronger than
the turbo one?  Hmmm.... 

The data I have used came from the Technical Specs manual.
