Steven,

The c-clip that connects the speedo cable to the front left wheel
bearing cap has probably come off or broken.  (I'm assuming you have the
early 85 car)

Jack the front left of the vehicle, remove the front left wheel.  You'll
see a bearing cap at the center of the wheel.  In the middle of the cap
is a small square hole.  If the end of the speedo cable is not sticking
out, then pop off the cap, thread the cable through the cap, then c-clip
so it stays put.

Hope this helps.
