Roxanne asked how to set the time on the radio. Well, I tried more times than
I can count, then one day resolved that I would not leave the car until I had
solved this puzzle. I pushed every combination I could dream of. Here is the
answer:
-Press the clock button. Time is displayed for several seconds.
-During this display period, press and hold down the ME button.
-Advance the time with the  toggle switch.
-Press left side to advance the "minutes" and right side for the "hours"

I find the radio defaults to the station frequency. Pressing the time button
displays the time for 8 - 10 seconds, then back to the radio frequency. If
anyone knows how to change the default, I would be interested.
