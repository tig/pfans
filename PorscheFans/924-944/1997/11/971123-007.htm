I think you are right, assuming a flat, level, and ideal rolling surface. 
 If the road ungulates, turns, or goes up hill, all bets are off.

My understanding is that the Turbos are rev limited for top speed, not 
drag limited.

If you know, to some reasonable accuracy, how fast you are going at 3,000 
RPM in fifth gear you can easily calculate your top end if you know where 
the rev limiter kicks in.  If you have your car chipped and the rev 
limiter is raised, add that to your calculations.
