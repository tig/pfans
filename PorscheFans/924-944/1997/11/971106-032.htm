Most of the cars that I've seen use a simple gear driven mechanism which
has little semblance of accuracy. I'm not sure about Porsche but needle
and dial speedos and tachs often use a magnet attached to the shaft
which spins creating a magnetic field. A coil attached to the needle 
reacts to the magnetic field causing the needle to move while a spring
pulls it back. The faster the magnet spins the greater the magnetic
field the greater the force pulling on the needle etc. As you can
imagine this system is far from precise.
