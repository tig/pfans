> I understand the 968 M030 rotors are "cross drilled" from the factory.  It
> seems like these rotors would fit a Turbo S since I think the 968 M030
> uses the same brakes.  Am I correct, and if so, would they be of any real
> benefit other than looking cool? :-)
Drilled rotors are supposed to let gases vent easier and be cooler. 
Temperature wise, not looks wise, that is :-)  If the size of the rotors
is the same, then switching to drilled rotors will lead to reduced swept
area (braking area) which is not a cool thing.  Maybe the TS brakes are so
huge that the difference would be negligible.  In general I thought that
rotors got bigger when manufacturers switced them to a drilled design to
compensate for the loss in swept area, but I could be wrong.
