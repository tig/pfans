Nice to hear from you, sorry about the circumstances.  

There was one response about fuel tank pressure.  I feel that is probally
the most likely.  Other things to check:

Plugged fuel filter

If you have 2 fuel pumps, maybe the one in tank is bad

Fuel suction line plugged.  Usually just a screen in the tank.

Here is a deviation from fuel system failure, check the coil.  Your coil
could be collapsing and they will generally only show up when hot.  Next
time it dies, check spark, should be strong and consistant.  I have had
them die before, and it happened just like you described.
