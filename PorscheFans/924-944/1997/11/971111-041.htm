Hi Bart,
1. Most likely a cambox gasket is leaking.
2. A new gasket is in order. If I remember rightly these need to be
fitted dry. ( Someone with a manual correct me if I'm wrong )
3. I had one of these go at the beginning of the year. More annoying
than anything else, BUT the leaking oil will leak on to other rubber
bits and perish them. *Your oil filter housing gasket and the rubber in
the steering rack may have been contacted by the oil too. Check your
belts for signs of any oil, there shouldn't be but a 15 minute check is
much cheaper than a $1500 bill. I had to replace my swaybay bushes at
the same time. Make sure you don't run low on oil and you are still
getting good oil pressure.

If you pop your hood and start your engine you should be able to trace
the flow of oil easily enough. I hope this helps.

Richard.
