Hello P-Fans
	Stabilant is an excellent contact cleaner - it actually will act like a
soldered wire connection when a drop is applied between two connectors. BUT,
do not use this stuff on low voltage circuits like O2 sensors, Mass Air Flow
sensors and the like. The manufacture suggest conventual cleaner (like Wurth
"OL")  on those type of circuits. Stabilant 22 is recommended by VW/Audi for
warranty repairs - but is also expensive at something like -  $30 for a 3 oz
bottle!
