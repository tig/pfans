Someone (I didn't see the address) wrote about mowing the air cond
switch in the 924S/944.
My 944 (84-old dash) has the clock where the a/c switch should be
and has a small rotary switch (with a outer ring that controls the
thermostat) in the little panel opposite the brake warning/pad/hand
brake warning lights on the center console). On my old 924 this
had an electric antenna control button but is blank (I think) on
most 944's I've seen.
It's not very ugly and having the clock sure makes the console
look a whole lot neater.
The switch looks like the same air-cond switch fitted to my
mum's old '81 mitsubishi sigma!

I think the system was fitted by the porsche dealer cause it seems
to be all genuine except thet left the clock in!
