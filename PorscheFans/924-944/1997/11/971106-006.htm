Stuart, to replace the flywheel, you will have to drop the transmission
and pull back the torque tube, but you will not have to drop the rear
suspension.  Be sure and inspect/replace the pilot bearing while you are
there.

You will not get any real benefit from going with a different injector
for your 1980 924.  The 1980's had a 55mm diameter sensor plate, whereas
the earlier 924's had an 80mm plate.  Drop me a note and I will chat
with you about real performance improvements for your 924.

Our 1980 924 that was the pole sitter at the 1982 SCCA runoffs in SSA is
now being turned into an ITA SCCA race car to rent out.  WE used to race
DP (full race) 924's in SCCA and a 924 Carrera GTR in IMSA, so we do
have a little knowledge about these cars.
