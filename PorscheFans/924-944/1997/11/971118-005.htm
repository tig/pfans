Personally, I do not like the factory Textar or Jurid pads. They squeek,
don't last all that long, make a lot of dust, and are expensive. For 
street/autocross/ light track use, I prefer Axxiss MetalMaster pads. 
They don't dust or squeek, stop well, and are cheap!

> I also have considerable road wander when driving streets with ruts but

I think it's the combination of wide wheels, and low profile tires.

FYI, unless you have turbo S, or aftermarket wheels, the stock 88 951
wheels are 7 + 8 by 16". Stock tires were 205/55 front, 225/50 rear.
Most people replace the tires with 225/50 front, 245/45 rear.
