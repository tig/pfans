now my 0.02 to the speedo - discussion. When my car was dynoed,
speedometer was checked, too.
It is about 9% optimistic. Not depending to the speed-level. 
Showing			Real
50 km/h			46 km/h
80 km/h			73 km/h
120 km/h		110 km/h
160 km/h		144 km/h     maybe test-failure
200 km/h		183 km/h
240 km/h		220 km/h

boost ~0.2 bar) but with WOT and 
many road (several km's) it came up to 260 km/h on the speedo => about
238 km/h real.
