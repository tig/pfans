> 	Word to the wise on contact cleaner from Radio Shack. I bought a can of
> contact (labeled "tuner") cleaner from them about a year ago. The damn
> stuff completely gummed up my receiver with some really nasty gunk.

Sorry to hear you got bitten by this -- the stuff labelled "tuner" cleaner
is actually intended to be used on TV tuners (back when they were
mechanical rotary switches).  The "gunk" is actually grease, intended to
lubricate the contacts in the rotary switch.  The stuff you actually want
is the can labelled "Cleaner Degreaser", catalog #64-3322 (no, I don't work
for RS, I just happen to have a can on my desk from a recent monitor tune-up).
