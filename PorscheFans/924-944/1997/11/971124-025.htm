Thanks to everyone who forwarded their suggestions and
comments about my fuel problem with my '77 924 that died last week. It
turns out the fuel pump has definitely died. I've got a replacement on
order. I suspect highly that this was killed by bad gas, because my rental
car started running rough after filling up at the same gas station! Looks
like water in the fuel, as I have used that gas station many times
previously without any problems. I think I'm gonna stop buying Amoco for
awhile... This, incidentally, was the same way my last car died, though
from a different gas station...

Other things that were suggested as possible causes were:

Vacuum in gas tank caused by clogged vent hoses.
Water in gas 
Loose intake boot after air flow sensor
Bad fuel pump relay/ broken wiring
Plugged fuel filter
Fuel suction line clogged
Bad ign. coil
Vapor lock

Thanks again, guys...
