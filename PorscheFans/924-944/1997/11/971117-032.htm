Insist on a genuine Porsche windshield.  There Is No Substitute. (tm). ;-)

Seriously, though.. the glass shop shouldn't care, but the insurance
company may balk.  Take your car to the dealer, and insist the insurance
company pay the bill.  You have the right to factory original parts, to
maintain the original safety parameters designed into the vehicle. 
Afermarket glass reduces the originality of the car, should you be
interested in maintaining resale value or keep it concours.  Serious
buyers look for matching factory glass all around.  My .02.
