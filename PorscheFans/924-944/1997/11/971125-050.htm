I noticed a post that mentioned rattling coming from the tranny area at idle.
 Please note that there is a transmission coupler in the front area of the
tranny that couples it to the central shaft.  These rascals can come loose,
or may not be properly torqued after a service (e.g., a clutch job).  One
sign is rattling. If you have a rattling or squeaking noise back there, pull
off the rubber cover and check the coupler for proper tightness.

I carry an extra coupler to track events, as I found mine loose on one
occasion.
