Wow, been-there done that!

Few things:

1.	Convert to AAA Plus (covers 100 miles per tow)
2.	You may have had a belt slip, and then it's O-S******, but your
shop can spot that fast. 
3.	We fought chasing the same symptom on my 944 for 4 weeks Shop
changed DME relay, looked at fuel regulator, sensors, etc..
4.	Turned out to be corrosions in DME and fire wall connections.
(they cleaned both with electrical cleaners and it has not failed yet)
5.	BTW: it ended up costing about $400 to find the problem, and 3 -
tows (I have AAA Plus)
