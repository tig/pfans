I would not use sealant.  The seals themselves are fine.  Any sealant 
you use will only require clean-up next time the job gets done, which 
is any even worse pain that the manifold re-seal already is.

Great rule-of-thumb a local mechanic (Bill Pfister) once taught me: 
always do the job assuming you're going to be the next guy who has to 
work on the same parts.  Which is why I buy my anti-seize by the quart!
