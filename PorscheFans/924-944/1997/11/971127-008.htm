Do you feel slop when you get on and off the gas?  It may be that 
you've shattered the rubbber centre to your car's clutch.  When this 
happens, there are hard metal stops in the friction disk which will get 
you home.  However, they are not designed to last forever and if you 
trash them, you will be stranded.

Jack the car, put it in neutral, remove the inspection cover from the 
rear transmission bell housing (trapezoidal shape).  Reach in and try 
turning the drive shaft by hand.  If you get the substantial turning 
like at the wheel, it's the clutch.

Failing this, you may have a problem with your differential (unlikely), 
or you may have broken a pinion gear tooth or two (fairly common).
