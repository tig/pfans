I had mine, 89 951, to 270 ( about 168 mph ). There was still some left
unfortunately you run out of road very fast. I have found that there is
a loss in stability above 230kph ( 140mph ) with the lights popped up
that isn't there during the day. Not that you should run that fast at
night. I can't see how you can get 300 kph unless the rev limiter is
raised to another 500 rpm over APE chips. I have to agree with Farzaan,
a 951 doesn't 'tighten' up until you are above 160 kph ( 100 mph ).
