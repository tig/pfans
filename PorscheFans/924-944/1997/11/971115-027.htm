I disagree with KMR's recommendation of the Pagid pads, after having a 
ton of problems with them this season.  The first set I used in '96 was 
fine.  But subsequent sets have had consistent problems with material 
transfer to the rotors and with aggressive and uneven rotor wear.  I 
checked with a whole bunch of other folks who've used them, same 
problems.  Current solution for me is Hawk pads, and so far so good.

BTW, real racers don't mill rotors.  They throw them away.
