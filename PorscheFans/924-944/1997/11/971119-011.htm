> Your description very much sounds like a broken rubber centered clutch.

I agree with Paul.  The clutch has mechanical stops that will keep the
center from spinning when the rubber center expires.  Jack up the rear of
the car and remove the plastic access panel on the transmission.  My guess
is that you will be able to twist the transaxle shaft considerably with
your hand.  Does it also make the noise when you step on the gas pedal
after coasting or when you release the accelerator abruptly on 1st and 2nd
gear?  If so, the odds are against the clutch.
