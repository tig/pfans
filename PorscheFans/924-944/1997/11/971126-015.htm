Hi Pxhil (!)
It is important to know that the sunroof system was totally revised in
the mid to late 86 model year. The two systems have no interchangable
parts. Most folks seem to assume that if you have an 86 you have the
earlier style. The plastic gear that store want to sell you is the gear
that is contained in the sunroof gearbox located in the roof - a common
failure. I think you need a new motor! or better yet a good used motor.
If you have the later style I have a motor the I got to fix mine.(this
is how I found out about the 86 model revision!)
If you have any questions about the earlier type I have become quite
familiar with the system.
