Claus,

It's most likely the thermoswitch on the radiator. Pull off the
connectors and the fan should stop. Shortcut the (two) connectors and
the fan starts running again (Watch your fingers here!)
If you don't pull off the connectors or unplug the relay, this will
drain your battery.
