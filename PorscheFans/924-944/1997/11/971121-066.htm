A couple of weeks ago you might remember I posted that my gas mileage 
had dropped 2 to 3 mpg and I wondered if a missing oil cap o-ring 
might have been the reason. Well after a full tank with a new o-ring 
gas mileage is back to normal.:-) I estimate right at a 3 mpg loss 
without the o-ring. Im not surprised that it made some difference but 
that is more than I expected. I'm just glad it was nothing serious.
