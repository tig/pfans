A standard, Fisher-Price babyseat (I had one in my basement) will not fit the
contoured, rear bucket seats of a 968 coupe.  I do not know if Porsche sells
a special seat for this application.  My 3-year-old daughter is pretty large
for her age, and she fits perfectly in the seat itself, with the shoulder
belt to restrain her.  Interestingly, my 9-year-old son fits back there too
(just barely), and would rather ride in the rear of our 968 coupe than in the
rear of our Maxima!

The 968 cabriolet has no back seat at all, just a parcel shelf.
