>Do I need to use some special
>Porsche bolts on the top, or can I simply use 8x40 grade 8?
You'll need to use 2  8 X38mm bolts with washer on the right mount. For the
left mount you need to get under the car and measure the thickness of the
flanged part of the mount...the part that the bolts go through. If it is
21mm then you need to use 2  8 x 38mm bolts with washer...if it measures
25mm then you need to use 2  8 x 42mm bolts with washer. I would certainly
use Porsche hardware. The washers are the correct thickness and the mounts
are internally threaded so the bolts need to have the correct pitch.
