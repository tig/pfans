Hi, Dave,

Your Haynes manual doesn't even know what a 944S is...much less what the torque
spec is for one of the bolts holding the camshaft cover on the head...

The proper torque for those bolts on the cam cover is 7 lbft (10 Nm).

Whining of the gears tends to implicate one of the bearings rampant throughout
the transaxle...
