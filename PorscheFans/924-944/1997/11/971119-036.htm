Hi Vaughan;

Reading your post was like deja vu for me...  

In my 924 Turbo I once owned, the gas tank vent clogged up. What
happenend was that after about 1 hour of drive time, the engine would
die like it had run out of gas. After sitting about 1/2 to 1 hour, it
would fire back up and run briefly. If I left it overnight, well another
hour drive time...  Sound familiar?

Solution (short term) - loosen the gas cap.

Solution (long term) - Clean the vent hoses.

You'll know if this was the source of the problem when you loosen the
gas cap afetr it's just died. The "whooshing" sound of air going into
the tank in unmistakable :-)

Hope this is it for you....
