On Fri, 14 Nov 1997, Chris Rowe wrote:

> Can anyone tell me how to reset the trip meter in my 1990 944S2?

Verify in your owners manual.. but it should be the air vent louver just
adjacent to the instrument cluster.  Be sure the key is in the run
position, then press the textured part of the vent louver.  It is a switch
that triggers a solenoid in the instrument cluster to reset the trip
meter.. cleverly hidden, those engineers.
