Hi Bodo !

Looks like you got a new engine in an old car. Porsche
built the 931 in two versions for the european market:

'79 and '80	170 PS 
'81 and '82	177 PS

The main differnce is the electronic ignition control. With
this module the porsche guys where able to increase the
compression to get more power with less fuel.
You should be able to find the control unit right beside the
your accellerator inside your car. The engine body has a
lot of modifications to hold the needed sensors. In addition
the 177hp version uses different pistons.

It's a good idea to check if you really have a complete
177hp engine and not a customized 170hp version.

If you do have the VIN and/or the engine number 
I could tell you more about your car.
