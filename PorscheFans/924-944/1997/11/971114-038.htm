	Forget the Speedo, learn to use your other facilities, Hearing,
balance, touch. Our computers only have so much bandwidth per input
device (mind <- eye etc). So if you learn to use them all you will be

it is coming up to fast. When the tires are close to limits. When the
road surface changes

dancing)

wheel through your hand, the brakes from your toes, the clutch from the
toes.
		Fear - "it's gods way of letting you know there is a
lesson about to be learned.
		Excites - "What you feel when things are done right"
