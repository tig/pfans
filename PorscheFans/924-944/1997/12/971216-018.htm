Yesterday I got tired of watching the girlfriend struggle while
squeezing the door handle on the passenger side of my 85.5 944.  I took
the door panels off, thouroughly greased everything in there with a
toothbrush and grease, removed and adjusted the threaded clip thing that
comes from the outside handle to the mechanism (just look in there,
you'll see what I mean).  It took me a total of an hour to do both door,
and that's after adjusting the drivers side to far out on the threaded
thingy.  It popped off (after I had reassembled the door) and the
outside handle stops working....  Anyway, I went back in and threaded it
on again.  The doors now open with ease and as I said...piece of cake. 
A dozen or so phillips screws, a few plastic popmeoffs and the panel
lifted right off.  Don't know if the S2 is different, but I'd imagine
it's about the same thing.
