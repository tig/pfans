Dave,
     A very interesting post, I will be investigating and possibly performing 
some of these modifications. One suggestion for all of you out there that are 
considering cleaning your air flow meter. When re-gluing the cover in place, 
use a non-corrosive silicon sealant, do not use your garden variety which 
contains nasty acetic acid! I cleaned mine early this summer, can't say I 
noticed a difference, but worth checking. I used high temp gasket making 
sealant, can't remember which type. Also, to those considering the adjustment 
of the spring pre-tension in the meter, please read a good book on the topic 
first (Bosche Fuel injection..). This will alter your DME's reading of air 
flow, and decrease the air/fuel ratio making for a more rich condition...
