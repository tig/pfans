I assume you can find some black sticky tape?

The other method is to use the wires from the dead pad sensor and cut and 
electrically tie them together.  The brake warning light system requires 
the sensors to be electrically shorted in the normal condition.  When the 
sensors wear, they break the connection and trip the warning lamp.
