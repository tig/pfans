The 89' Turbo and Turbo S also has a baffle to help prevent oil from
sloshing away from the pickup and causing engine damage during high G
maneuvers. Have you test driven this 'S' yet? Is there actually a
noticable improvement in acceleration? The figures state that the 247 HP
88' Turbo S and all 89 Turbos 0-60 about .5 seconds faster than their
older 217 HP siblings. However, the stock KKK K26-8 turbocharger found
in the 247 HP models spools slower than the KKK K26-6 found in the 217
HP models, which means the extra 30 HP comes at a price in turbo lag.

Just something to think about,
