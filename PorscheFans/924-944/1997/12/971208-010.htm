I had this kinda thing happen with my '77 924. I only discovered it after
quite a while. The belt ended up rubbing through the metal timing cover
(after about 15kmi)! I now have a nifty little slot in there. The reason it did
this was because the rubber mounting for the bolt that holds the bottom
end of the camshaft cover on was worn out, and when I cranked down
on it after replacing the timing belt (preventative maintenance,
wouldn't you know) to get rid of all the noise I could, I brought the cover
in contact with the belt. I now have to find a replacement for this rubber
grommet/bolthole. (any suggestions?)
