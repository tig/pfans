Some of my friends have experienced this when they have used a low octane
fuel on their car(too low for the car that is). They have fixed the problem
with turning back to the high octane stuff. If your 944 is an european
model with a high compression ratio, Porsche recommends the gas to have a
minimum of 98 oct, atleast in the earlier 944s. The octane recommendation
is normally on the gas cap, or right next to it. You can also find this
info on the manual. 
  You could also try cleaning your injectors or something.. Hope this helps.
