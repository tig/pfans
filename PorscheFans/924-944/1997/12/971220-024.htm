>I happened to see BadBob951 reply to your 951 start/ running problem.  I
>had a similar thing happen, and I hope that your problem has the same
>cause as mine- real easy to fix!  Check the connectors for the
>crankshaft position impulse senders.  They are the two black rectangular
>Bosch-type connectors behind the intake manifold along with the round
>junction for the O2 sensor. They have a habit of coming loose, but are
>easily pushed back together.  If this is your problem also, you will
>want to figure out a way to replace the wire retaining clip if missing,
>or just tape together, etc...   Hope this helps- let me know if it
>works! Good Luck.   Ken  drken@htc.net
