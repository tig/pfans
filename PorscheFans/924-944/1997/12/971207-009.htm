Some time in MY 92 the "pivot bolt" on the tensioner was changed.  In the
earlier versions the tensioner arm had a tendency to freeze to the shaft of
the pivot bolt preventing the tensioner from applying proper tension to the
belt.  Mine froze at about 32,000 m.  I had all the belts replaced at the
time. PCNA covered the balance belts and part of the labor.
