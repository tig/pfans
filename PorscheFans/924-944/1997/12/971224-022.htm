> There are LOTS of 255's for 16" wheels. The problem is that they are
> all 50 series aspect ratio. That will make the sidewall too high, and

I've auto-xed and streeted on 255/50x16 and 245/45x16 BFG R1' on the
rears. You should have no rubbing problems with Stock 9" wheels. There
wasn't a big performance difference. If i had to choose I'd stick with the
245'. The 255' do fill the rear wheelwells and look cool, but does hike
the rear end up 3/4" (you could lower the rear ride height). THey should
last slightly longer because of the bigger diameter and more rubber. If
you can get a much better price, I'd say go for it. All IMHO.
