Hi, Kyle,

The fuel mileage gauge is driven directly from the DME.

It uses inputs from the speedometer (via the cruise control
sensor), and knows how much fuel it is kicking out through the
injectors, so does the calculation and sends the signal to the
mpg gauge...

When you are going less than about 20 mph, it reverts to a 
Gallons per hour reading...
