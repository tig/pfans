The Turbo S model was a limited edition run in '88, and became the standard
Turbo for '89. differences included 247 HP vs. 217 (larger turbo unit),
bigger brakes, tires, stiffer suspension, and, on the '88 series, a
"distinctive" paint job and interior (Op Art all over again!). There were a
few other detail changes, but thsoe were the big ones. The 'S' models are a
very desirable car to own, and theere is a wider choice of colors and
interiors if you can find an '89 for sale.
