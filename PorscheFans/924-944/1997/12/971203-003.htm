The chilton's labor guide lists 13.4 hours to replace a head gasket
alone.  My shop charges $50. per hour, so that equates to $670. for
labor alone.  A head set is approx. $100.  You would also add the cost
of oil, oil filter, coolant, new spark plugs, possibly belts, possibly
oil seals, etc, etc.  Also if the head is off, the valve guides/seals
need to be inspected.  

The real question, is of course, why does an engine need a head
gasket...is there a real problem that needs to be addressed??
