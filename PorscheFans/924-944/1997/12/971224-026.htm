Steve:

There must be a short to ground for the fuse to blow.  Since the AC compressor
clutch was not at fault, a wire, between the fuse and clutch, that
intermittently shorts to ground is probably the cause.  Try to trace the
clutch wire and find where it passes through sheet metal holes or rubs against
something.
