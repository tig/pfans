Hi Joseph,

Recently swapped the KPH speedo on my ex-German 85 944 for a MPH one.
Had similar problems to yourself.
These were solved by getting a friend to push the speedo cable towards
the engine bay bulkhead from inside of the engine bay, while I gently
pulled the instrument cluster. I managed to get 3 inches more cable that
way. Getting the speedo disconnected was a cinch then. Be sure that the
two speedo illumination bulbs are pushed back into their sockets in the
instrument before reassembling.
