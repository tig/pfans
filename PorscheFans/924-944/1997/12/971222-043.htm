Everyone,

I just put a set of Nitto 450's on my car, 205/50-15 in front, 225/50-15 
in back.  The effort to steer the car is greatly reduced, along with the 
improved turn-in (read oversteer).  It hasn't been much of a problem 
yet.  I did get to test the wet-weather traction right after I bought 
them...looks like controllable breakaway, along with reduced 
hydroplaning.  These were quite inexpensive considering what D40M2's 
cost...I also replaced the rubber bushings in my car with Weltmeister 
Delrin bushings...noticeable difference in response and ride.

Happy Holidays to all!

Michael
