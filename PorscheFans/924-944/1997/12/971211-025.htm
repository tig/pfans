Select an allen-head wrench of the suitable size for the spring retainer,
place the bent end in a vise and leave the straight end pointing at you.
Then, after placing the spring and retainer in position on the allen
wrench, hold the mirror housing and door mount in the correct positions and
"push" toward the vise.  You need to push fairly hard while you twist the
retainer into the mirror housing, so be sure the vise is securely mounted.
