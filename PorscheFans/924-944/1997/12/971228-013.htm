<< I have a 86 944 turbo. I have been hearing a ticking noise from the rear of
 the car that only occurs when the car is in gear and increases as i increase
 the speed. The car shifts and runs fine and iam not getting any noise or
 problems with the transmission. It also comes and gos.....any ideas.    >>

Sounds like your CV joints.  Common practice is to pull the shafts, repack the
joints, and swap sides with the axles keeping the outside CV joints on the
outside.  This reverses the rotation of the CV joint and allows it to wear on
a different surface, effectively doubling the life of the joint.
