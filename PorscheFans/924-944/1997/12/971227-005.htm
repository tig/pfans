Got this neat book for Xmas and don't recall seeing it mentioned before.
"Classics in Colour" series on the "Porsche 924 & 944" by Nigel Edwards.
Published 1992. ISBN 1 87200 476 8. British publication, 96 pages and lots
of photos. Text is very light. Writer is PCGB contributor and owns a 924
Carrera GT. Many of the cars pictured are RHD. I'd rate it 8/10 mainly for
the photos. Cost 12 British Pounds on the cover.
