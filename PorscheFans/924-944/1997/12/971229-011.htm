That's about all I can think of right now.  Don't worry, I'm not destroying
my street car, the little red beast is going to stay as a street car.  It's
still got way too low km to turn it into a dedicated track/race car.  I've
been debating the Talon vs. 944T thing for some time now.  With the Talon,
I get a brand new 400hp motor from day one for free, built by David Buschur
of Buschur Racing, but as tempting as that sounds, I hate how that car
requires so much suspension work to stop it from eating its front tires, and
as nice as 400hp sounds, the motor has a lot of weight to push/pull with the
AWD system in there.

But, the final decision maker was not so much lap times, but rather the
enjoyment of driving, the 944T is just a much more exciting car to drive
fast than the Talon on the track, both are about same for absolute lap times,
with the edge going to the Talon if the track is broken up or wet.
