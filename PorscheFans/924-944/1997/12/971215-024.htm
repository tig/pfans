Marc,  this may sound too simple but have you checked the coolant level
in the rad? If it gets really low, it stops circulating at idle or low
revs. Another possibility would be a loose impeller on the waterpump.
This is rare but I`ve seen it before. Either way I would take care of
this problem right away as any coolant circulation problem can lead to
catastrophic engine failure that the warranty provider could walk away
from(they`d call it owner abuse).       Dave C.
