Randall,

Try disconnecting the temp.sensor at the radiator. It's at the left, top.
Maybe hard to reach from above since you have a turbo.
If the sensor is fault, the fan wil stop. Shortcut the two wires and the fan
starts again.

Easy to replace. Do not overtighten the new one. That may destroy your
radiator.

Check if the cycling valve is plugged in. 
0.2 Bar is the minimum max boost that the DME can dial in.  This is
accomplished by supplying the full pressure signal from the intake to the
waste gate diaphram via the cycling valve.  If the valve is unplugged, it will
send the full pressure to the diaphram, and you should get .2bar
