Bob,
If the repack doesn't do it, instead of replacing the joints, I'ld recommend
that you swap the axles side to side keeping the outer and inner CV joints in
thier respective ends.  This will reverse the rotation and give the joints a
new wear surface.  I swapped mine about 30k miles ago and haven't heard
anything since (currently 145k).
