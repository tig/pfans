I have my doubts about this simple device too due to the lack of any
substantiating test data to prove that it works, but there is a somewhat
different device that is made for 4-barrel carbureted cars that does
indeed work.  It is a 1" carb spacer that has special swirl slots =
machined
into the edge of each hole (one slot per hole).  It imparts a swirl to =
the
mixture *after* the carb, not before it like the SpiralMax (tm).  In
actual dyno testing on a performance V-8 it improved torque and HP.
Driveability was also improved.  I don't recall the exact magazine that
did the test but it may have been Popular Hot Rodding.
