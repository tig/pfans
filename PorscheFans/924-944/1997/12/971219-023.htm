Hi Richard,

I also have an 83 944, and know how bad your wheels must look after 14 years
of road use and little care.
As a fervent admirer of the early US cars, I chose to have my wheels
refinished, to preserve the car's originality. It "only" cost $100 per wheel,
which is less than you will spend on "decent" used ones anyway, and the
results are SPECTACULAR. They really dress up the car, and give it a fuchs
look. If you have cookie cutter wheels, as I assume you do, DON'T switch. Send
them to RENNSPORT, in Roswell, Georgia. You'll be glad you did, and your car
will still be original.

Just my .02
