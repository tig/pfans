	I seem to recall that Porsche had a problem with the 
drain line from the carbon canister (located in the rear 
of the drivers side fender) to the under side of the car 
an freeze over. Porsche had a tech buletin on this. The 
tech buletin #8714 was for '87 944-S models. Models built
 after Vin #94HN453032 deleted this extra drain tube so 
it would not freeze over. The drain tube goes down to 
the plastic pannels bello wthe drivers seat area. 
	If you OVER FILL the gas tank it is possible to fill
 the carbon canister with gas instead of collecting fumes. 
In the summer this evaps fairly quickly but in the winter 
you might enjiy the fumes a little longer.
