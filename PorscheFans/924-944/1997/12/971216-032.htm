I would definitely not recommend craftsman for any type of socket that has a
bit in it (i.e. allen head sockets).  I've had a lot of trouble with the allen
head bits coming out of the socket.  Nothing is more irratating than having the
little bit come out and get stuck in the bolt.  Sears uses a pin to hold the
bits in the sockets.  The pin acts as a c-clip (surrounding the bit).  The pin
do not hold the bit in.

Find someplace that sells sockets that use a better bonding process.  A rule I
usually use is that if the bit wiggles in the socket, then pass.
