A friend of mine with an 89 951 just had a PRofec boost controller
installed in his car. The "brains" are mounted on the firewall, and it
looks as if the input to it is the output from the cycling valve.

Inside the car is a small unit which is the controller.It is mounted in the
space under the radio.

I rode in the car at the last drivers ed event at Sebring. In 3rd gear, the
guage went to 2.0 bar at about 3000rpm and stayed there to at least 6000.
It did not fall off at all. The car is seriously fast.

Anybody else tried one of these?
