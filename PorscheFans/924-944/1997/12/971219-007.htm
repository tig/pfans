Like many of the rest of this list my 944 heater control has a mind of
its own.
it works for a while then quits and I freeze. Some time back a list
member
mentioned that by pulling the vacuum line off the solenoid located to
the right
of the console and under the lip of the dash he could get full heat when
he 
needed it. I tried this and found it to work but the whistling of the
vacuum line
was irritating and the heat was too hot for cool days.

THE TIP: Pull the vacuum line from the solenoid. Install a flow control
used to
              regulate the air flow on aquarium air pumps. They are
available for 
              less than $2.00 US and provide full control from under the
dash.
              I recommend extending the line to the drivers side of the
console for
              convenience and safety.
