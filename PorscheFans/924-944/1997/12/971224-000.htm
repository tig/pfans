You may want to check the obvious items first.  Pull the connectors to both
the throttle position sensor and the air flow sensor and clean them with
contact cleaner and reconnect.  Drive the car and see if you are getting
more than 1.2 on the boost guage.  

Check all vacuum lines - especially the connections on the cycling valve
(hidden by manifold) and the one under the throttle. Any leaks in these and
the DME will retard/restrict boost to 1.2

If this fails to clear the problem than proceed to check all of the other
sensors to the DME. 

The above did the trick on my turbo - otherwise you will have to seek
knowledge from the "wise ones" on the list.
