	All of the talk lately about O2 sensors going bad made me think of the
following silicone product warning in an old VW tech bulletin. 

	dated: Nov, 1992

	Warning/Caution
	Do not use sealants, liquid gaskets or sprays that contain silicone on
engines 	with oxygen sensors. Silicone entering the air intake system is not
burned 	during combustion and can damage the O2 sensor!
	Always read the ingredients of the material you are using. It must not
contain 	silicone!

	Just becareful when using RTV, Liquid Gaskets and Sprays around your engine.
