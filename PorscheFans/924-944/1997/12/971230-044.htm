Farzaan, as for putting in a solid motor mount, I'm sure this would pose no
problem for a track car (unless a lumpy idle really bothers you).  I had
run with both stock mounts completely shot for a while and other than the
absence of a rough feeling idle it was not dramatically different after I
installed new ones.  I think the stock mounts with the hydraulic dampers
are really tuned to damp out the low frequency (i.e. low rpm) stuff.
