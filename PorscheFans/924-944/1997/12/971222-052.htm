The problem might be the voltage regulator in the alternator. this is a
$50 part that is easy to change - it screws into the back of the
alternator. You might check the battery voltage when the car is running.
If below 12V try a new regulator. And your mechanic is wrong - with
proper care your car should last another 200,000 miles.
