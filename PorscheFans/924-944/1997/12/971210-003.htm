I checked my owner's manual for the 93 968 and the recommended
replacement interval for the timing belt is 60000 miles.  The re-tension
check is done after 2000 to 2500 miles.  There is also a note that for
MY 1992 and earlier, the change interval is at 48000 miles.  Take this
information for what it's worth but I'm playing it safe.
