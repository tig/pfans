T.C., you might find this surprising but it is much, much easier to do the
C.V.'s on the 944 than on a (fwd) Honda.  I have done both and there is
much more involved with the front wheel drive cars with press fit parts and
special pullers required, etc.  On the 944, all you need is the special 12
point internal socket to get the bolts out (available from Snap On or most
good auto part stores) and maybe you will have to remove the muffler for
better access to the driver's side.  Take a look under there and see what I
mean.
