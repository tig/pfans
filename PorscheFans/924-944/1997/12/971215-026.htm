> Does anyone know what the spring rate came stock on a 951 S ? I liked the
> way the suspension felt on one I test drove. Torsion bar size? I beleive
> the front bar is 26.7 and the rear 18.05. Is this correct?
A request to the Delphic Oracle specializing on Porsches returned the
following information: (in other words I am referencing the Porsche
Technical Specs manual)

Coil Spring P/N 944.343.531.01, spring rate 28N/mm, inconstant wire
diameter 11.7-12.2mm.  This is listed as standard equipment on the M758
(ie 951S) model, used from 87-89 on M030 option.

Coil Spring P/N 951.343.531.03, spring rate 28N/mm, constant wire diameter
11.6mm.  Used for the 951 from model 1990.

Torsion bar: 25.5mm.

Sway bars: Front 26.8mm, tubular	Rear 16mm
