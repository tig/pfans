To all Dutchies on the list (and I know you are out there somewhere…),

I'm looking for some (first hand) info or experiences with Dutch Porsche
Clubs. Our P-family on the other side of the big ocean seem to have a lot
more fun than we do overhere (driver's ed., club-racing, autocrossing
etc.). I've visited the PCA Northstern region last summer and they had tons
of fun at the track! They really got me jealous and I wanna get some
track-fun too… after all, the way the police is checking now a days it's
not much fun on the public roads anymore (been to court for a speeding
ticket earlier this year and I can tell you it's definitely NOT funny –
driving 110km (or  70mi) to fast). Our once-a-year day at the
Zandvoort-track organized by Pon (the Dutch P-importer) is usually
overcrowded (over 100 cars) and I wasn't much impressed by snobs cruising
their brand new cars along the track while using their mobile phones either
(look mom, no hands… look mom, no brains!).
So, does any of you cheeseheads on this list know of a club where I can get
me some real adrenaline-rushes (there must be something else to do with our
Porsches than cruising along the wind mills on Sunday afternoon 8^)?

Let me know if you know…
