The simplest way I've found as a temporary fix, is to wedge a small piece of
non-corregated cardboard between the plug and the outlet. This keeps the plug
from popping back out. 

As a permanent fix, assuming there's room behind the plug, I'd just remove the
outlet and replace it with a new lighter/outlet assembly from an auto parts
store. They all seem to be about the same size and you probably wouldn't need
to modify the dash.
