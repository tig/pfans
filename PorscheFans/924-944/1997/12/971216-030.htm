BadBob951 wrote:
> 
> Secondly, regarding dyno testing.  Has anyone had any experience with the
> small accelerometer (sorry about the spelling) based dynos that are out there
> for under $200?  They are supposedly quite accurate.  You input the cars
> weight (I'm not sure about drag coefficient, I know the higher line dynos of
> this type allow multiple car parameters to be input), and using the
> accelleration it reads, can calculate your HP at any instant.  Additionally,
> it automatically will calculate your 1/4 mile and 0-60 time.
> 
I went halves with a guy here in the office on one. He used it to tune his
MR2 Turbo and subsequently paid for a "real" dyno run. The indicated HP
from the Gtech was 179HP and the dyno said 182HP. We haven't verified the
0-60MPH and 1/4 mile times but they are consistent.  It seems like the unit
is at least good enough to do before/after measurements, if not absolute
measurements.
