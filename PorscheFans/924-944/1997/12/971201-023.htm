	Hello P-Fans

	*Leave the CV joints on the same side of the car, just reverse
the drive
	shaft rotation! Simply flip over the drive shaft so that the
inner joint(by
	the trans) is now on the outer joint (by the wheel). 
	*I also flat rate the repacking of the CV Joints w/grease. I
remove the joint
	- but I do not disassemble! I simply wash off all the old grease
with
	CRC/BrakeKleen. I install a small clamp (or use a tie-wrap) on
the drive
	shaft, fill the CV boot with fresh grease - lots of grease, then
push/in the
	top of the big end of the CV boot onto the joint, and then
squeeze the grease
	thru the CV joint. Neat! Well maybe not so neat once your hands
are covered
	by grease!
