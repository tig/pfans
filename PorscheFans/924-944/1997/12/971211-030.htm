In the 944FAQ, I remember reading about the hatches not automatically
opening
when hitting the button.  Porsche tried fixing this problem repeatedly with
no
success.  Therefore, they changed their positioning and said that the manual
was wrong and the hatch is only a release mechanism.  It is not designed to
automatically lift.  I can hit the button and hear the latches let go.  Then
I
go to the back and lift the hatch.  Not as cool as if it lifted
automatically
but still quite functional.  Next I'll need to tie in a remote servo to it
so I
can unlatch it from a car alarm key fob.
