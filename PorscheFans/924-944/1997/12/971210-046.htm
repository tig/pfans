You need to let the p/s fluid warm up and lose some viscosity. Drive 15
minutes, leave the car running, uncap the p/s fluid reservoir and move
the wheel lock to lock. Do this until you stop seeing foam/froth
surfacing in the reservoir. Clean the rubber o-ring and the mouth of the
reservoir and re-cap.

Some "groaning" noise is normal when the fluid is hot, the car is
stationary and the wheel is being turned lock to lock.
