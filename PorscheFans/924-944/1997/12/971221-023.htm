I have a broken drive shaft on a ''79' 924 I wanted to know if I can take
the
shaft out of the tube and insert a shaft from another year as ' 79 is a
hard
year to find.

The 79 drive tube is not interchangeable with anything that I know of.  You
can buy a used unit from Automobile Alanta for about $850.  US.  A new tube
runs something like $1900.  If you can find a really good machiist you
could probably get a new shaft made but if you get someone who doesn't know
what they are doing it could be a real nightmare.
