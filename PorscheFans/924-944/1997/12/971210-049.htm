Fixing a clock light is one of the quickest and lowest cost repairs I 
have yet to find on a 951 or 944.  The bulb is availble at the dealer for 
a little over three dollars.  I bought two in case I broke one.  Remove 
the trim strip from the dash.  There are screws on the passenger door 
side, and under the climate control face plate.  There is also a screw 
that is hidden under the air sensor cover.  BE CAREFUL REMOVING THIS - 
THE FINS BREAK EASILY!  I 
used a quarter and turned it a half turn (I think counter-clockwise) to 
remove it. When this is done (I think I mentioned all the screws), pull 
the trim strip out and you will see the bulb on the clock.  Twist it 
counterclockwise from the base and pull out.  Put the new bulb in (Just a 
suggestion - make sure it works before you re-assemble.)  Place the trim 
strip back into the dash making sure the wires are where they are suppose 
to be and the rubber hose lines up with the air sensor and re-install the 
screws.

Total time of about a half hour and under 10 dollars.
