Yes, Klaus,
I've used this. Don't waste your time or money. You can find this at any
discount auto  parts store. Its pretty worthless as far as I'm concerned.
First you'll never get the color match right...you might think you have but
give it a couple of months. Then the grain patterns will leave a funky
pattern on the good areas adjacent to the crack. Finally after a few months
the adhesive inside the crack will separate from within the crack,
voila...extra cracks. Well you get the picture...not a pretty one. Anyway
I've given this product _way_ too much bandwidth. You should probably try a
dash cap. I'm waiting for warm weather to do mine.
