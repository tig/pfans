Are cars more safe with DRL's?  Are motorcyclists' injuries less severe when a 
helmet is worn?  Maybe, maybe not.  But, should we have our respective 
governments mandate safety features to drivers, or, should each driver have 
the right to choose the features they want?  If I want DRL's, I turn them on.  
If I don't want DRL's, I turn them off.  

I believe we should have the right to choose.
