hanger at Algeria in Africa. It was fogged in and they had nothing to
do. One said to the other, "Mon, have you got anything to drink?" "No
mon, but I hear you can drink jet fuel and that'll kinda give you a
buzz."  So, Paul and Stephen drank jet fuel, get smashed and had a great
time. The following morning, Paul woke up and figured his head would
probably start pounding as soon as he stood up.  But it didn't.  He felt
good.  In fact, he felt great - no hangover! Paul's phone rang, it was
Stephen.  Stephen asked, "Hey, how do you feel?" "I feel great!" replied
Paul. "I feel great too! You don't have a hangover?" said Stephen. "No.
