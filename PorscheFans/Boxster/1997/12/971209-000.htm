don't procrastinate - early birds get the rooms! ($79 + 9% room tax).

reserve. Call 1-800-634-6441 - ask for Reservation Agent Blue; refer to
Boxster Gathering, Porsche Club. You are responsible for all
room/incidental charges you incur. You can come early and get the same
rate; you can reserve just Friday, April 17, BUT YOU CANNOT reserve just
Saturday, April 18.
