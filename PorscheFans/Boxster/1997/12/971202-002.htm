Yesterday's post by Mike Jacobs was interesting -- Boxster bodies are
assembled , finished, and then sent to Stuttgart for installation of the
mechanicals.  This has been confirmed by other sources.

Every Saab convertible I have examined in the past year has been exceptional
in regard to panel fit and paint --at a time when the new 911's I looked at
had poorly fitted doors and paint which was not perfect.

My Boxster is a Stuttgart model.  I have yet to see a Valmet Boxster, but my
guess is that it will be well nigh perfect.  No reason at all to devalue a
Valmet Boxster, in my opinion.
