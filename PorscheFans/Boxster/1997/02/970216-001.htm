	Well, you get really good at waxing :) As I understand it,
	the main reason swirl marks get in the paint is from putting
	the wax on in circular motions. The wax should be applied with
	a back and forth motion with a *clean* 100% cotton cloth. Or your
	hand. If you have dirt on your cloth, hello scratch. If you have
	dirt in your cloth and you're putting the wax on in a circular
	motion, hello swirl scratch. Try mailing Larry Reynolds 
	(CarcareSp@aol.com) at Car Care Specialties and see what he says.
	He also offers some nifty products to keep your car in shape.
