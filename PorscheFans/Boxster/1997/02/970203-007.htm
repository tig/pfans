Let's just hope oil service is somewhat easier/cheaper than it is on a 993: 
90 minutes labor, good oil and two filters runs about $200.

The prevailing wisdom is sound: change the oil at 1k and thereafter every 
5-7.5k miles.  Use the best stuff you can buy (like Shell's Rotella T dino 
or AmsOil Racing synthetic).  Don't scrimp here.
