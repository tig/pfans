According to the Boxter Service Information Technical Book (part # WKD 499
721, about $25 from your dealer) the coolant temp. gauge is on top of the
right instrument pod with the cooling system light set on the right side of
the gauge. This warning light is lit continuously if the temperature is
above 120 C and flashes if the coolant is low or if there are engine
compartment blower faults.
