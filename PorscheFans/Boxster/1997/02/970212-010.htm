If You want the P38 package and P-16, you must do it this way.  Order P38 and
then order all the components of P16, but delete the 17" wheels.  You will
lose the package price of P16 ($160 savings) but you will get the Tecknic
suspension. The spec for the different suspensions are
front axle                           STANDARD            SPORT
Spring wire        mm        10.25...13.18       10.84...13.90
Spring coil          mm        90.50...186.5        91.68...186.5
number of coils   mm        4.05                             4.05
tube stabilizer      mm       23.1x3.4                     23.6x3.5
 
rear axle            
spring wire         mm         12.6                            13.30
spring coil          mm          148.5                          148.5 
number of coils                  4.55                            4.40
tube stabilizer    mm        18.5x2.5                19.6x2.6
 the car will sit 10mm lower with the sport suspension
If you have problems with the order call Contemporary Cars 407 6454-4222 and
talk to them. This setup took many hours and letters to Reno to get it to
work.
 If you do not plan to drive this car on the track I would save my money on
the suspension and just get ABD.  It is not needed for the street. Good luck.
