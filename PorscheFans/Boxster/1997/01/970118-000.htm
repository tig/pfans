According to my dealer in Oregon (Sunset Porsche-Audi) the P 38 option indeed
includes the wheels, ABD traction control as well as the stiffer shocks,
springs, and sway bars (quite a bargain!).  The hard tops are being sold with
the cars (and so they'll get taxed by the federal gov't), but I believe they
will be available later (at a higher cost).  Perhaps you can find another
buyer of a car your color and buy a hard top that way.  That sort of info
might be available from your dealer or the local PCA region. Good Luck!  I'm
getting an allocation with the former P 17 package (now known as P 64, I'm
told).  The actual process of ordering a car seems to have a bit to do with
circumstance.  I'm just pleased to finally be IN the pipeline.
