To add to the sport suspension thread:

I test drove a Boxster this last weekend up in Sacramento.  It was
fabulous!  Very quiet until you get it above 5000 rpms, very smooth.  It
had the stock suspension with 16" wheels.  My feeling is that the
suspension is a little soft on good roads.  For comparison, my '91 camry
is very comfortable after I upgraded its suspension (Eibach springs, KYB
struts, 16" wheels, 205/50 AVS-I's) and I felt the Boxster was as soft or
maybe a little softer.

IMO, if you live in an area with bad roads and lots of potholes, you might
prefer the stock suspension.  If you live in an area with good roads, then
don't be afraid of getting the 17" wheels and sport suspension.  I think
for California's central coast (where I live), the P38 will be perfect.
Can't wait to drive a Boxster down to Big Sur!
