I have the new turbo wheels 17" aftermarket for my track tires.  They are 7.5
and 9 inch and fit very well.  I elected to install the same size tires front
and rear. Also, I have a Technical Information page  from Porsche that says"
eighteen inch wheels are not approved for use on Boxsters. Use of eighteen
inch wheels on Boxsters under severe conditions may result in structural
failures of the body and/or suspension."
