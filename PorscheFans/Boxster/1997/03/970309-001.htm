I've been reading the apprehensive comments about the Boxster rear window.
When I restored my 356 I used a plastic polish to restore the original shine
to dash knobs, shifter handle, etc.  Polish is available in most areas from
companies doing plastic or plexiglas fabrication.  It comes in a small or
larger squeeze bottle, in a couple of grades: one to remove minor scratches,
one for final polish.  I've never used it on anything like a "soft" window,
but it should work, to one degree or another.
