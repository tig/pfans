Actually, if I read the owners manual correctly, Boxsters already have
a passenger side airbag disable switch.  It just may be hard to use...

The manual talks about a special infant seat that you can use in the
boxster.  It has a special attachment methnod that clips into a socket
under the seat (similar to a seatbelt).   When there is a clip in this
socket, the passenger airbag is automatically disabled.  Therefore, if
you had one of these clips, you could just insert it into the socket
under the seat to disable the airbag temporarily.

At worst case, it would mean you would have to buy the infant seat and
cut off the clip.

If anyone figures out a source for these clips, I would appreciate
it.  My son is two and although he is not in an infant seat, it seems
as if turning off the airbag might be preferable with the recent press
reports about children and airbags.  Anyone have a comment on this?
