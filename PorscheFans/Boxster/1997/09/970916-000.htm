     I had a similar problem instaalling my remote mount radar detector in 
     the front .
     The only path I found was drilling a tiny hole in the Plastic cover 
     above your left foot in the Cabin .
     From the trunk .. Remove the plastic cover to the drivers side of the 
     battery and right at the back there is a plasic cover about the size 
     of a paperback book. I called Porche looking for help on this and 
     their suggestion was not to install the Detector in the front ?
     I fed the wires down then sealed the wire with some silicon .
     This way I did not drill through any metal or any of the firewall .
     
     Good luck .. Let me know how this goes or if you find a better way ..
