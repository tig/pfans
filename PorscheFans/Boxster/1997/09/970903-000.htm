Traveled to a nearby antique village for lunch with a friend.
Parked the Boxster nearby, within eyesight of the restaurant.
Lots of pedestrians, shopping.  A nice, quiet afternoon.
Of course, the car gets its' fair share of lookers.  Some folks even stop in
their cars to catch a glimpse.

Two women, in their later years, stop to look.
They walk around it on the sidewalk side, checking out the labels to see what
it was.  Look in the windows, commenting to one another, pointing.

And then, to my surprise, one takes a picture of the other standing in front
of it, then trade places and take another picture!
