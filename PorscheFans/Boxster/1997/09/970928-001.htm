This is in reply to Bob Didner's request several days ago.  I also purchased
a Becker silverstone; the only one to get for our cars.  To get through front
trunk/firewall:  First, remove black plastic panels around the battery
(battery cover, left a/c air filter cover, right cover).  The latter 2
require a Torx (size 14?) screwdriver.  Then remove battery cabling, battery
hose, then battery.  The battery has cool integral handles which pop up on
top!  [Yes, I hear the Porsche batteries are not much improved with the 986.
 So, once out of warranty, a good hi-capacity Mopar Dodge battery was
recommended to me.] Next remove battery tie-down bracket.  Then there before
your smiling face is a black rubber plug in the firewall underneath the
battery area--pry it out with your fingers, and you're inside your car, in
the driver's side really close to the center console.  Good luck!
