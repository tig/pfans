     Jim, Pfans,
     
     After 2K miles with the P38 Tech Sport suspension (17" wheels, heavy 
     springs, shocks sway bars, active break differential) I would 
     definitely recommend it.  Last weekend I did 1.2K miles between 
     Northern N.J. and Richmond Va.  On smooth road, the ride is extremely 
     smooth.  On broken up pavement, the ride is busy (lots of small 
     pitches) but the driving position, (dead midway between the wheels) 
     results in little harshness, and no appreciable fatigue (even after 
     600 mi. of straight thru driving).
     
     I love the suspension, turn ins are lightning quick, no perceptible 
     lean in the fastest corners, track out is also extremely well 
     controlled with none of the 911s tendance to oversteer on track-out.
     
     If you have any thoughts of autocrossing or tacking you Boxster, I 
     would definitely recommend the P38.
     
     Enjoy,
     
     --Bob Didner
