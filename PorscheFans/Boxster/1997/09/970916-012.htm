Boxster out of over 50 cars was noticed to spin repeatedly and very
quickly.  The conditions were very wet.  Boxster was on good street tyres,

the driver expressed surprise about the behaviour.

The moment of inertia of the Boxster may be lower, and could contribute to
the high speed of the spin when it finally happens.
