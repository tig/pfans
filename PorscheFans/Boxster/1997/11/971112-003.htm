You asked if anyone is glad the bought a Boxster..  We have one of the first
Boxsters in the United States, (picked it up in NC on Jan 21).  The car is

and handles.

what to expect and what not to expect in a Porsche.  

Did you make a mistake in ordering one...In my humble opinion NO....but then
I am very biased.  Hope you enjoy your Boxster as much as we do ours.
