The secret on my '97 to easy removal is to push down on the top edge of the
windscreen above the latch, while actuating the latch. Then do the other
side. It comes out VERY easy. If you just pull on the latch it is VERY hard
and feels as thought it will snap off at any moment.

Maybe others can try this and confirm to the list if I've hit on the magic
technique!
