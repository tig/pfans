I have used most all detectors mentioned the past few days and in my opinion,
the Valentine 1 is WORST BY FAR!!!
In even its least sensitive mode, it will defeat the purpose of using a
radar/laser detector in the first place with its continuous littany of
multiple false alarms!
I say stick with a cordless Escort Solo.  It's totally convenient, performs
flawlessly, has tones and alerts FAR more programmable and intuitive than
Val1's or BEL's, has a suction cup windshield bracket FAR superior to Bel and
val1's chintsy, bulky brackets and you don't have to run tacky and obtrusive
wires all over the place--just clip it off when you go to a mall, etc; if you
want some extra sensitivity, get their optional power cord for about $20. so
you can plug the thing on those infrequent long freeway jaunts.
