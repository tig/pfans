Boxster's unprotected sides no different from many other autos,
including many P-----.  Purchase magnetic removable side protectors for
parking lot use, or construct your own.  I did, using triangular section
wood and self-adhesive magnetic tape (similar to flexible refrigerator
perimeter door seal material).  Paint body color, carry and install in
seconds if parked in risky area.
