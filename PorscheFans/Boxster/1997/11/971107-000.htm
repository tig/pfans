the extended oil change intervals on the Boxster, synthetic oil is an absolute
necessity.  Conventional oil should be changed every 3000 miles for adequate
performance.  A dealer service department that puts conventional oil in a Boxster
and tells you it doesn't make any difference is just trying to screw you by 
using cheap oil in your car.
