There is an excellent article on the Boxster and, more specifically, the
tiptronic in the July/Augst issue of "Classic Automobile Register". (This is
a new mag - the first issue had a picture of Jay Leno on the cover.)  Check
your newstand or Barnes and Nobel.

Also, the 2/97 issue of "Christophorus" (Porsche house magazine) has a
fasinating article on tire and wheel approval.  The bottom line - stay away
from 18" wheels on the Boxster as they can cause structural damage. After
reading this I have to believe the warning is not simply a marketing ploy, as
some have suggested.
