> Question for anyone who has gotten the "Boomerang". How does it install?
> Is drilling holes required?
Yes, drilling is required. The instructions clearly warn you to be very
carefull.  You have to drill holes in the car to mount the boomerang,
(the boomerang comes pre-drilled), and some of the holes have to be
drilled directly under the radiators and associated piping.  The
instructions recommend you put tape on your drill bit to prevent you
from drilling too far into the car.  Good luck.
