sponge" is povided to clean the stainless steel not rough up the paint!

The Boomerang installs with stainless steel screws set every
one-and-a-half-inches.  The drilling is done underneath the front bumper.  It
takes approx 15 minutes to install and cannot be seen unless you crawl
underneath your car.
