I have found an ideal product to safely and conveniently remove the four
airbag warning labels from the sunvisors.

It is called EASY-LIFT-OFF, made by Floquil-Plly S Color Corp, 4715 Sthwy
30, Amsterdam NY 12010.  I found it in a hobby shop.  It is said to be safe
on most plastics.  To do so, it works very slowly.

After draping the area against unwanted drips, I simply brushed the clear
liquid over the decal, and left it for about 10 minutes.  Then using a
semi-stiff hand brush, like a nail brush, with nylon bristles, and starting
at one edge, I was able to lift off the decal in one piece.  The decal
appears to be made of a rubberized material.  Then I used adhesive remover
to take the remaining glue off.  The car looks much better now.
