The July/August issue of Classic Automobile Register has a nice
pictorial and article about the 986.  It compares the car to the 550
Spyder.  Cool photo of James Dean in front of the Boxster.  I especially
enjoyed the pictorial because it features my car (i.e. silver with black
interior).  Also, the article also says:  "There will also be 175 to 200
examples of a new limited edition Turbo S with 450 horsepower for
upwards of $150,000.  All may have been sold by now."  Has anyone hear
this?

Does anyone think the 1997 986 has a shot at becoming a collectible as
the first year of production?  If so, you may want to reconsider
removing the "ugly" sun visor stickers.  A friend of mine that collects
Aston Martins told me that cars in "stock" condition always command a
premium, flaws and all.
