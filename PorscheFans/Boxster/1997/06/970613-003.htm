As far as I know but don't quote me on this, just about all tape heads
since the mid '80s can take a metal tape (as opposed to metal oxide).
Now cheaper portable and car units won't have the bias (record only) and
equalization switches for chrome/metal (the same eq), but on playback
all that does is give a little brighter treble response.  So you can use
those audiophile metal master tapes in the car.
