Berkeley,

Unless the tape deck comes with a switch for using metal tapes you won't
notice any important difference in sound quality. And a good metal tape
won't leave any more or less particles on the tape head versus a good
chrome tape. Probably the best thing you can do is to make sure you are
using high quality cassette tapes. Maxell, Sony, and TDK are three
examples of such. Using a high quality cassette tape of the proper type
for your deck is the smart way to go. Good luck!
