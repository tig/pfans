"Fans,

For all those worried about red-lining a NEW engine I found this
interesting paragraph on the Porsche web page!

http://www.porsche-usa.com/driving/911html/technology.html


"In the engine assembly shop, two people complete each 993 engine. By
contrast, each Boxster/New 911 engine is assembled from start to
completion by the same technician. Assembly and test steps are signed
off so that responsibility is established. Different tests are designed
to quality-check and validate various steps of the engine assembly
process. A 20-minute test measures horsepower and torque. Each engine is
run 2 minutes at redline. Engines are tested three times - on a
dynamometer,  once in the car on the "rolling road" test, and during the
final road test."
