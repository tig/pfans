The ineptness of many of the Porsche service departments is scary.

The heat shield was added to all five-speed Boxsters starting approx three
months ago.  It is located on the rear transmission support bracket to
protect the back of the shifter assy.  Germany (not prone to make changes on
a whim) obviously discovered some early shift failures and made this
important improvement.  

How do you know if your car has the heat shield?   Stick your head undreneath
the rear.  It's in plain view.  If you have an early Boxster have your
mechanic get the heat shield part number off a current edition Boxster and
order one - it's very simple to install (two screws).  

Be advised, however, that Porsche will not pay for upgrades to your car
unless it invovles a saftey issue.
