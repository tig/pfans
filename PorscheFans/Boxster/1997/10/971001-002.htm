Porsche is out with their winter tire tests available for your reading
in the latest Christophorus.  The Dunlop SP Winter Sport M2 tops the
list for their 16" recomendation with the Pirelli Winter 210 as another
recomended option.  For 17" on the Boxster they again list the Dunlop SP
but not the 210 Pirellis (something Porsche doesn't like about 17"
210's).  Sizes are exactly as you'll find printed in the owners manual.
I've enjoyed my 17" 210s on the 911 the last 2 winters but will give the
Dunlop SP's a good look-see. Any first hand comments from SP users would
be interesting.   Thanks,   Gregg
