Neal:

	I have the electric seats on my Silver/Black Boxster.  I was able to
give them a last recently.  On my way to the Oregon coast I was passing
through a wooded area that caused the temperature to drop.  The first
thing I did was turn the heater up to 80 but I was still cold, then I
remembered the heated seats!  It took less then a minute on high before
I felt toasty, I changed to the lower setting which was perfect. 

If you are going to be driving in an area that gets cool or cold make
sure you order the heated seats.
