You must gently push on both black levers at the same time.  Porsche has had
many problems with these levers breaking and will replace your windstop under
warrenty if they do break.  It takes about a month to get a new one.  Some of
the real early Boxsters have had their windstops recalled because of latch
failure also.

By the way they are now using a different manufacturer for the windstop and
the latches seem to be better constructed.  You might want to see if your
dealer will order you a new one.
