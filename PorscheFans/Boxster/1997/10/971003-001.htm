Before this list degenerates into the inevitable "I'm right - you're an
asshole" dialog that every list seems to eventually reach, I'd like to
propose a simple rule:

If you get the urge to post a note that is not about the Boxster
and/or says potentially unfriendly things about someone - don't.

Just send it to the original author if you must send it at all. I
realize that it is satisfying to get a larger audience for your opinion
on what you consider a sensitive matter, but this *is* a Boxster list
and we are (mostly) all grown-ups and can form our own opinions about
the value of other's ramblings.

If you find this note pisses you off for some reason, please just try
out my suggestion and mail your anger to *me* and not the list. Thanks.
