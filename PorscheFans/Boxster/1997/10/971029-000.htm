Took delivery Friday Oct 17 in Stuttgart... I recommend this to anyone 
buying a Porsche.

Broke the car in on the way to Luzern (CH), drove through the Alps 
Gotthard Pass and Nuffen Pass (2600m). The top did not go up and the 
seats kept us warm (bake or broil). Sport suspension is a pleasure. So 
far the car is living up to expectations !!

Stayed in Geneva (CH), then went to Torino (I) (visited ItalDesign). 
Parma, Firenze, San Gimignano, Sienna, Portofino, drove route 
Napoleon(F), to Saint Etienne (F), Geneva again and back to Stuttgart 
(D!)

4700km on the car, not a scratch, 20 rolls of film and an incredible 
blur.

An explanation from the factory on why custom paints are not available 
anymore: the paint shop works 3 shifts and the rest of the lines only 
2 shifts. Paint is the bottleneck for production so they tried to 
simplify the process.

Does anyone know how long it takes to deliver in California ?

The only logical conclusion to tourist delivery: I am going back to my 
dealer to order a Boxter S... This is just too much fun !
