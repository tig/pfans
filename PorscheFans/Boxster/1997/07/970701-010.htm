It looks like we are approaching $100 mark on this subject, but I still would like to
add my 0.02. I have P38 on my car. I've spent last weekend running autocross at
Candlestick Park in San Francisco. I have a video of my car on the track. All I can
say is the car has no body roll. Compared to other Boxster and 993 which were not
setup for the track, you can definitely see an advantage. I live in San Francisco, and
roads in most parts of the city are far from perfect. The only time I had doubts about
sport suspension, is when I was returning home after big dinner :-). So my advice,
if you ever plan to run you car on the track then get it. If all you are going to do is 
to cruise highways, then it is probably not worth it.
