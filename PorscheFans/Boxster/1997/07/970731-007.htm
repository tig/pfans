muddy and high end nice but not very crisp. The Becker unit seemed to user

the 6 speaker upgrade with in-dash CD but have deleted it. 

I will have a local place (Boca Raton) that has already worked on
approximately 8 Boxsters install a fine sytem for $700-1000.  Basically we're
talking in-dash CD, power amp, replace dash speakers, but woofer or

CD changer for much less than Porsche charges.  Several companies are coming
out with units or cables that can mate with the Becker in-dash cassette.
