I have had zero problems with the alarm system.  As someone has already
pointed out on the list, the instructions in the owners manual are way
too complex, and needlessly so.  Simply use the buttons on the key, once
to activate, once to turn off. With the top up, the alarm senses
movement in the cockpit, but turns this feature off when the top is
down.  A strange choice -> it's my understanding that other soft-tops,
like the mustang, have a cockpit sensor that works top up or down, an
approach I'd like to see Porsche take.  Makes leaving the car alone with
the top down feel like a more secure position.

Cheers, RC
