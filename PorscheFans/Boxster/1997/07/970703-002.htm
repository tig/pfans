I have a guards red Boxster with about 4k miles and haven't had a
ticket yet. (In this car.)  Only one ticket on my lime green Kawasaki
ZX-7.   However, I would bet that all things being equal, a bright red
car will be stopped more often than a silver car.  I have no actual
data, but I'm sure red catches the gendarms' eyes just like it does
ours.

However, I'll also bet that Speed Yellow will get more tickets than
Guards Red.  Just take the name: *Speed* yellow.  It is much less
common and easier to spot.  I remember a story someone told on the old
porschephiles mailing list.  They said they used to own a bright
yellow Corvette and that it made the cops "apoplectic".  He claimed to
have gotten two speeding tickets while stopped at traffic lights.
