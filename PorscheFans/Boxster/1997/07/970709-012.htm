dave,

big differences in horsepower (282 vs. 201), and straight line 
acceleration performance (i've seen magazines show sub 5.0 sec on the 
993 vs. 6.0/6.1 on the 986), and social cachet (median salary of a 993 
owner is roughly $250K vs. about half that for the typical boxster owner 
- although i realize your question implies a zero price differene for 
your case).  the 993 has something resembling a back seat.  for a 
driving comparison check out andrew potts' page at 
http://www.welding-units.co.uk/boxster (i'm pretty sure that's the link 
- i've got a link at my site if that's not correct) for a comparison 
from somebody who currently owns a boxster (over 10K miles) and has put 
a fair amount of time in the 993.  while i'd always wanted a 911, i've 
become convinced that the boxster will be more of a fun car.  certainly 
more unique at this point in time - heads don't turn too much for 993's 
but boxsters command a lot of attention (which could be a plus or a 
minus depending on how you look at it).
