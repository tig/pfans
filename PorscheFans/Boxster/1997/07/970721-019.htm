The San Diego Region "Windblown Witness" reports coolant leakage from a 
rubber T-fitting on the lower right side of the engine.  The report says 
that Porsche is aware of the problem and is under warranty replacing the 
T-fitting with an improved part.
     
Also a warning not to add any coolant to the Boxster except the type 
available only at ---you guessed it--- the dealer.  Says so in the manual.
