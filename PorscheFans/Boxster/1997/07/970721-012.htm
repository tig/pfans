I have had the same problems with one side of the top not closing in on
the channel / liner properly.  Well, this turns out to be a common
boxster problem that is EASILY fixed: There is a bracket, or clip of
sorts (it's black and slides) that controls the angle of the top in
reference to the 'cage'.  It took my Porsche dealer some 2 minutes to
fix this for good - note, that the first service person did not know
about this. I had a really nice guy that now works here (Santa Monica,
CA) - he actually is FROM Porsche in Germany (semi-retired here
apparently) and knew exactly and right away what the problem was.
