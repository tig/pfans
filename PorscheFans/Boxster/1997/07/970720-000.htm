rough pavement.

not seating in the channel when raised w/o maunual assistance. Comparing

change?  Can anyone post a suscinct solution?

my driveway.  Now I go up at an angle to avoid problem.  Porsche: Please
give us an extra 1/2 inch clearance here.  Will Boomerrang help this?
