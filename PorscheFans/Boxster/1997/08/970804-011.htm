Today I received a recall notice from Porsche:

"Porsche AG has determined that a problem relating to the wear of the
tension and deflectin rollers exists in certain 1997 model year
Boxsters.

On these models, the tension and deflection rollers for the engine
drive belt may have faulty seals.  Grease could leak out of the
bearings, which could cause the bearings to fail and destroy the drive
belt.  To correct this, we will at no cost to you, replace the
tensioning and both deflection rollers and check and/or replace the
tensioning roller lever and tensioning element."

Thought you all might be interested.  I wonder if everyone got this,
or only people with early Boxsters?
