After seeing the aluminum dails I decided to investigate putting them on as
an aftermarket part.  What I found surprised me.  The "aluminum" dials are
actually thin plastic with an aluminum colored coating.  As North Hollywood
Speedometers (the best known Porsche Speedo shop in the country) pointed out,
metal would prohibit light shining though.  Duh! 

Plastic or not, I still like the look and would like to know from those of
you who ordered this option if it was worth the $600.
