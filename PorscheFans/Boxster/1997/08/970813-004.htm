     All else is wonderful.  BTW, if anyone does any autox in 
     their Boxster, I have found that when I remove the spare

     The sound gets amplified by the car body, and you would 
     think that something important is falling off.   The bolt 
     can be removed, but doing that often would result in fraying 
     of the carpeting in that area.  Instead, I have taken a 
     block of insulation foam (about 1.5" thick) and use that as 
     a spacer over the bolt, holding it on with the hardware 
     which held the tire in place.  Works very well in removing 
     the distraction.
