include anything called dealer prep.  The only adder to my tab was $42(?) for

plate frames.  Also, I seem to recall that several years ago (10 or so) the
factory recommended against chroming wheels. 

Good luck - read your contract closely (preferably before you sign it) and
find a good dealer.
