Just to set the record straight -- the radio actually tracks the
vehicle speed.  I know this because my wheel speed sensors are flakey
and when the speedometer dies and reads zero, the radio volume goes
down.  This is probably the right thing (rather than tracking RPMs)
since wind noise probably dominates engine noise at speed.

I initially thought that the radio volume was tied to ambient noise
level (measured with a microphone) and was surprised to find it just
tied to speed.
