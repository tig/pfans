The way it worked for me was the following:

When the dealer had an available month (depending on specific dealer 
allocation as well as other factors) he allocated a car to me for that 
month.  At this point he ordered my car and handed me a printout of an order 
confirmation from Porsche complete with an order number and production 
month.  At this point I felt comfortable.  It took about 3 weeks after the 
end of my production month for me to receive the car (I imagine this may 
vary) .  Hope this helps.
