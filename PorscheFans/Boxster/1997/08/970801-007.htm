The lastest Boxsters (last three weeks) are arriving with additional heat
shields mounted on the rear transmission support bracket insulating the rear
shifter linkage on sticks, oil cooler on tips.

Porsche will not be retrofitting.  Those of you with early Boxster may want
to contact your dealer about mounting the part thereby obtaining what may
prove to be significant benefits (a part number is now available for the heat
shield).

Today I drove the Performance Products'  Boxster pictured in October's
"Excellence" (and to be featured in a future "Motor Tend" issue) - which many
are calling the best handeling car they've ever driven.  No argument from me.
 It's been lowered one-and-a- half inches, has B&B headers and exhaust,
TechArt body kit, TechArt metal pedals, and  TechArt streering wheel, 17"
 Antera wheels  and, of course,  the Boxster "Boomerang.  Wow!
