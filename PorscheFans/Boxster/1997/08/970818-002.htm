> How does it handle in erratic, broadside 40+ mph wind gusts? Just

I drive a stretch of road regularly which gets these kinds of winds -
there are often cars and camper trailers tipped over.  I found the
Boxster handles better with the top up in these situations.  It gets a
little squirrely, and much noiser with the top down - but at 70mph
it's still driveable.  It doesn't get 'caught' by the wind, like my
significant other's Isuzu Rodeo (yoiks!), just pushed around a bit.
I think the low profile helps here.
