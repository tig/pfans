I bought my Boomerang from Ogner Porsche in Woodland Hills, CA.  My dealer
installed it on my new bumper in 20 minutes.  Although they didn't charge me
because of other work I had done, they said they would normally ask $45.00 to
put it on.  (after seeing it, they are going to start carrying it)  

Since the steel bar is predrilled, installation was very simple - just a
matter placing the boomerang against the underside of the bumper, and
attaching it with stainelss steel screws.  I could have done it myself.   And
you can't see it from above.

I dealt driectly with Jeff Korda (a Porsche mechanic with alot of experience)
- a very smart guy and very pleasant to talk to.  No pressure at all to
buy...

By the way, before I put this on I called the Porsche line to get their
imput.  Off the record, the rep admitted they have had several reports of
front bumper damage and thought this was an excellent idea.
