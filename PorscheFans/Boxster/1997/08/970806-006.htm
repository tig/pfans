While on the phone today with Valentine Research, I asked about radar
usage and detector compatibility in France. They don't ship to Europe,
so they don't keep a record of frequencies used there. They did say that
some of the frequencies used in Europe are the same as in the U.S., but
that there could be some which are different, too.

Valentine suggested calling the National Motorists Association at (608)
849-6000 for better information.
