Looking for Boxster paraphernalia? Parts? Accessories? While I wait...
and wait... for delivery, I've started compiling a directory of web
sites that specifically (well, mostly) mention Boxster goodies. I began
with the October '97 issue of Excellence magazine. No web site
mentioned, not included. Here's the list. The text highlights their ad's
content. I'd appreciate any others you know about. Send them to:
vfarace@satmansys.com. Thanks for your help.

Porsdealer@aol.com -- Tailpipe extender, bra, caps
