General info to all of you re. the ugly sunvisor/dashboard labels:
These labels are a legal requirement (see your Code of Federal Regulations or 
Federal register) for all (repeat: all) manufacturers. Look at any brand-new car 
for model year 1997 (or it might be for MY 1998, I'm not sure) and you will see 
these gems. One of the reasons for making them so unattractive is to 
force/encourage manufacturers to develop "smart" airbag systems.
