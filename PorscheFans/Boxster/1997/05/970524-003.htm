I received several E-mail postings about my last message referring to Panorama
..  Pano is the national publication of the Porsche Club of America.  It alone
(I think) is worth much more than the annual cost of membership in PCA.
 Check out the links to PCA on the PorscheFans' Home page. There is a
national map showing Zones and Regions and some E-mail points of contact.
 They also publish collections of their technical articles in volumes called U
pfixin' der Porsche.  I have benefited enormously from these sources with the
previous Porsches I have owned.  Additionally, in Pano there are interviews,
news, and historic photos and articles on personalities, racing, vehicles,
etc.  I can't think of a single better source.
