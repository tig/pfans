FYI to any racing game fans out there:

Sony has just announced it will in fact be releasing its Playstation
Boxster racing game, 'Porsche Challenge', in the U.S. & Canada (NTSC
version). It was previously announced that the game wouldn't be crossing
the Atlantic, but apparently will now be coming out in August. The game
has been very successful in Europe, and the industry press has really
lauded its realism. Andrew Potts, whom some of you may know for his
brilliant Boxster website ( http://www.welding-units.co.uk/boxster ),
has it and gives it top marks for simulation & playability. 

Check out today's (5/30) news on the PsxPower website (
http://www.psxpower.com ) for more info.
