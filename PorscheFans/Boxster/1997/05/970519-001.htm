> What are the
> items/problems that some of you have noted once you got your Boxster

Make sure all the keys (all three) work.  One of mine didn't start the
car because the transponder for that key wasn't appropriately
programmed into the car.

I recently noticed a small elastic band with a plastic fitting at the
end was broken (the fitting was broken) inside the top.  I suspect it
was broken since I picked it up.  Look inside the top with the top up
-- especially around the back corners of the top -- to make sure
nothing looks awry.
