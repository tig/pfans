Yep, after waiting three years, I finally get my Boxster.  Overall, I'm
pleased with it (look, feel, performance).

Yesterday I hit 149 m.p.h. on my routine trip to the local food hall,
Porsches' advertised top end for the car.
I, of course, zipped through the five speed manual transmission with my
throttle foot flat to the floor in each gear.  Zero to sixty m.p.h. was
within the specification for the car (I forget right now.)
I drive the car at the very edge at ALL times (doesn't everyone??)
