stan,

i haven't driven one yet either, but i have spent a fair amount of time
sitting in the showroom model at our dealer.  i was concerned, and i'm only
6'1".  also, i'm pretty slender.  from a height perspective (or, more
importantly from a leg perspective, since i'm sure we'll never use the top
in san diego :-) if i push the seat all the way back it's a tad too much
for me.  if your extra two inches are all in your legs it might be rough.
if they're distributed a little more evenly (which they probably are) i'd
guess you'll do all right.  with the top up i actually had decent room.
again, assuming an even distribution of the two inches i'd say you're ok.
the seats, however, proved to be a pretty snug fit on me.  i like them a
lot actually, but i weigh about 170.  hope they work for you . . .
