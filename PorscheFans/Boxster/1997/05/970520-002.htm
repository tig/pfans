Make sure all three keys work.  There is a recall out there, replacing
the net on the storage area behind the seats (on top of the engine
cover) with a zippered cover.  I think all cars with the sport package
already came with the zippered cover, (mine did), but I got a recall
notice anyway, went to the dealer, only to find I was OK.  I did have
top alignment problems, other than that no complaints at all.
