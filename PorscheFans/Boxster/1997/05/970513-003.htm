After mentioning the Boxster "boomerange" in yesterday's posting I had eleven
 inquaries on my PC this morning!

Here's the scoop...

The boomeramg is a polished stainless steel bar (beautifully finished) that
is fitted under the front bummper to prevent scrapes from driveways and road
bumps.  

It takes apporx. 15 minutes to install (I had the dealer do it) and is not
visable unless you look underneath the car..

The cost is $299.00 and is shipped with all instructions.  The mechanic said
individuals can do the simple install themselves, if so desired.

I spoke with several Boxster owners who swear by it.  And after seeing two
Boxsters with front bumper scrapes I think this is a high priority option (By
the way, I am in no way affliated with the dealership  or recieve compesation
for this endorsement!)
