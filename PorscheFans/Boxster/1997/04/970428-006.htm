opinion but I think the standard interior is high quality and tastefully
understated.   The seats are mostly leather  - the dash, carpeting, and door
sides all seem first-rate. Spending $2,000 on a leather overlay seems like a
complete waste (also more prone to cracking from the sun) - especially in
black.
