I've had many Porsches. My comments are...

1. you will add your first quart of oil at about 3000 miles, not 800 like 
the 911

2. First oil change at 15000 miles....I'm doing mine at 7500 miles.

3. 30000 miles just plugs and oil. Everything else is self adjusting. A 911 
would cost about $900.00 to service. I'm bringing my own Mobil I to the 
dealer next week for a change.

As far as the chrome shifter and brake handel, they look great. I have 
ordered mine. I have the sills and the aluminum racing pedals. These are a must.
