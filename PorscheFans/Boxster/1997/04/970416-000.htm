I have a Boxster with the Tiptronic S transmission. In manual mode, I
find it as responsive as actually shifting a 5-speed. In fact, the feel
is so much like a manual transmission, my wife keeps after me to put it
back in D so it's smoother and less "race car" like. I'm very impressed
with the manual shifting features, but I confess and addiction (at least
in  commuting traffic) to the driving ease provided by a fully automatic
transmission.
