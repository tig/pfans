     I just confirmed with three dealers that all cars ordered with "X" 
     options (custom leather trim colors, etc.) would be delayed anywhere 
     from 2 to 6 weeks.  There was no explanation for the delays.
     
     I have also been told that once the dealer report has a VIN #, the car 
     is actually in country at one of the pre-delivery centers.  
     Apparently, Porsche does not tell the dealer the VIN # until the car 
     is nearly ready to be shipped in case the car is damaged.
     
     My car should be in sometime in late May or early June and I am 
     looking forward to it!!!
