 At San Diego National Tour this past weekend the two co-drivers of a 
Boxster, Josh Sirota and Michael Butler, came in the first two positions both 
days in AS.  There were 12 drivers entered in, I think, 4 M3's, 3 MR2TT's, a 
944S2, others.

So the car, as is (97 Boxster, totally stock except BFG-R1's), with the right 
drivers, IS competitive. Course was reported to be "very fast and lots of 3rd 
gear chicanes", so while low end torque is somewhat lacking, the car appears 
to have a good balance which, at least in SOLOII, can make it a winner.

For the record:  I am NOT one of the drivers - just have the same last name 
as one of them, so please no-one flame Josh for this "bragging".
