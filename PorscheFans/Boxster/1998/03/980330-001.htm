On the '98, there is a plastic cover at the end of each rail on the
floor on which the seat slides. These pull out toward the front,
revealing the seat rail. You need to slip the plastic clip up in there
so the hole in the clip catches the head of the bolt which attaches the
seat rail to the floor.
