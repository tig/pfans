Spring is here (maybe), time to take off the hard top and store it.  I'd
appreciate recommendations for a storage rack, I believe Porsche offers one
and there may be one or two after market units as well.  Also, someone
recommended the storage rack for the MB SL
Dealer called and cautioned not to fully raise soft top after long winter of
storage in boot (having used the hard top).  He said to open only partially,
allow top to stretch (ideally in sunlight), wait day or so, before fully
raising top.  Concern is that seams may rip loose if material isn't given time
to stretch.  Having owned many convets, I fully understand. With my 64 Austin
Healey 3000, if I leave top down for winter (stored in my heated garage), I
park it in the sun first nice day in spring and then I'm able to fully raise
and secure the top.
