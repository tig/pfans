Boy, have you asked THE question!  I'm a Boxster owner, and in my
opinion, it's the real thing.  It is a sports car with soul, and like
all porches, it has something the BMWs lack -- personality.  It's not
quirky, mind you, but it does command respect.  It's a fabulous
automobile.
I haven't driven the M Roadster, but I drove a Z3 and Boxster
back-to-back.  The Z is a beautiful car, but the Boxster is an animal!
I also drive a BMW 328is...and a '72 2002, so I'm not blind to the
virtues of the BMWs.  But I also drive a '70 911S...and the Boxster, and
in my book, the Porsches have soul.  Period.   Great, great machines. 
Pick the Boxster.
