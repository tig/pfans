A year ago I went to look at a Z3 2.8.  (Just for fun... my Boxster deposit
was in long before that!) The salesman told me the full leather option is only
for looks, smell and feel.  The partial leather is more durable, easier to
clean, and less likely to stain.  Subsequently, my Porsche salesman told me
the same thing and encouraged me to not get full leather.  I love Ferraris and
the leather in the cars requires some care and can look fairly beat up years
later if not maintained. 

So, it seem to be an issue of pleasure.  If you love the look and sensation,
get full leather.  If you want durability and ease of maintenance, then the
answer is would be to skip it.   Let us know what you decided so we can get
some sleep at night.  Good luck and most of all, have fun!
