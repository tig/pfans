A complete list of 1997 PCNA Technical Bulletins appeared in the
latest (January 1998) Porsche Panorama (pp. 12-13).  There were 54
bulletins in all.  The article says that PCNA will publish a book in
March or April of 1998 which will include all of the 1997 Boxster and
993 tech bulletins.  All tech bulletins and the upcoming book of
bulletins are supposed to be available at any dealer's parts counter.

If someone has a scanner with OCR available, this would be a great
thing to put up on the Web!
