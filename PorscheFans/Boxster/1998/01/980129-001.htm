     Mike Warner asked about anyone's experience with the Tip. Well I use 
     my Boxster as a daily driver in the Chicago area. As you may be able 
     to imagine rush hour traffic is terrible and the Tip is a blessing in 
     such conditions. I haven't really tested the abilty of the CPU to 
     compensate for stupidity (downshifting to 2nd @ 100mph) but it is fun. 
     The only thing that bothers me is that fact the the shifter is on the 
     stering column rather than center console as on the 911. This takes a 
     little getting used to on winding roads. I would rather have opted for 
     the stick, but I made a deal with my wife to get the car.
