I have all black w/ partial leather and its fine.  No problems with reflection
of the dash onto the windshield (which I heard can be a problem with the tan
interior).  True, it does not have the "rich" look of leather when one is up
close.  

However, several grand for leather interior? Not me. Personally, I'd
rather--and did--spend the money on other things.  

N.B.: Obviously if you are going for red or green interior I believe the point
is moot. One thing...if you live in a southern area (FL, AZ, etc.) my
salespersons cautioned the red leather might fade over time due to the hot
sun.
