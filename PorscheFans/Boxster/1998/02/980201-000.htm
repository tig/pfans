There is a Boxster Technical Bulletin, Front and Rear Hood Release 
Inoperative [grp5 1/96 5510 1/21/97], that describes how you can unlock the 
front hood to get to the battery when the battery is dead or disconnected. 
A summary of this bulletin can be found on the Boxster FAQ, at 
http://boxster.dyn.ml.org/~grr/faq.htm.
