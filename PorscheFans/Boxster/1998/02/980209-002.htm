Take a look in you manual.  Your car should never be towed it should
always be put on a flat bed.  If you think you might scratch you bumpers
getting your car on to a flat bed, just try dragging it behind a truck
at a 45 degree angle while going over bumps.  You should have roadside
service (comes with the car), and they tell you right over the phone
that they only send flatbed trucks out (I speak from experience, I had a
injector relay go out when my car was 3 weeks old).
