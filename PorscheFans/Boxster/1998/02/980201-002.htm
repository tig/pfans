the question of the trunks being mechanical ... the trunk is likely mechanical

central locking system... the central locking system locks the latches in
place... hence the latch will move but it wont go far enuff to open the
trunk... as far as the location of the emergency release for the front trunk it
is a security issue hence i will not disclose it here... but let me assure you
