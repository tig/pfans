> steering wheel.  (The difference is that the buttons rotate with the wheel

A bit of trivia: Formula One paddle shifters rotate with the wheel (I
think they even use buttons on the left/right steering wheel sections
now, too) but because the turning radius in these cars is so tight
(half-turn lock-to-lock?!) you wouldn't ever get in trouble confusing
the upshift/downshift controls. The road cars are, of course, much
different in regards to the turning radius, so a compromise had to be
made.
