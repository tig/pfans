Hi, Clark.

It's thick jute padding. Bill Perrone at The Parts Shop (714-894-3112, fax
714-894-8694) sells this and most other precut textile padding and sound
deadening kits for the trunk and engine compartment (tarpaper, jute/tarpaper
combinations, etc.) The gas tank pads are relatively cheap. They come precut
and are absolutely indistinguishable from original. You install them with
contact cement.
