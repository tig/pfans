Hi Steve.  The linkages are metal rods with ball joints on the ends.
Sometimes a worn balljoint can pop off.  There are 4 linkages on the 912.
One 3" from the pedal forward to the pedal quadrant, one 5' from the   
quadrant to the trans quadrant that runs through the tunnel, one 3' from   
the trans quadrant to the fan shroud quadrant, and one 8" from shroud q   
to carb linkage (runs up the back of the fan shroud).

the most likely culprit is the 3' trans linkage.  It is the only linkage   
that does not have a ball joint (at the trans quadrant) it is just a   
metal hoop that wears right through.  So get nder the car and look for it   
at the front of the trans on the drivers side.

Fix: Braise another piece of rod around the hoop.  I found this works   
really well as the brass acts as a bushing as well!
