I haven't seen any other postings on this, so here's some info for anyone
interested.

Aug 2 - 4, Marconi Auto Museum for Kids and VARA will be racing at the Tustin
Marine Airbase on a track layout that hasn't seen road racing in forty
(that's 40!) years.

The event is in conjuction with an annual air show and a sizable car show and
around 100,000 people are expected over the weekend.

You can expect lots of Porsches and other great cars - hope to see you there,
just look for one red and one green 912 (#'s 912 and 913) and stop in and say
hello.

BTW, Tustin and the Marine Air Base are near the I-5 and I-405 intersection
in Orange County.
