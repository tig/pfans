There's a special tool used to crimp the chrome bezels onto the instrument
case. The chrome bezels are throwaway items; once you take one off (I think
they have to be cut off) it's trash, you put a new one one. This is not the
sort of thing you can do yourself, and it doesn't cost that much to send them
out and let the pros do it. 

I like to use Palo Alto Speedometer. Just ask them to clean the glass and
face, and replace the bezel. Chances are your bezels have some rust pitting
anyway.
