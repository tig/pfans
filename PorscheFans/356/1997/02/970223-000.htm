Mark:

The PCA booklet "The Porsche Family Tree" indicates that *80131* is in the
engine serial number block for 1956 1600S (75 HP) engines.   The *702963*
engine is in the block from July 1962 onward 1600S (75 HP)engines.

Hope this helps...
