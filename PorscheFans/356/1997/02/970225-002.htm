Bryan,

Do the shift bushings! It's an easy job (you don't need to pull the 
engine and tranny) and the results are much improved shifting. I 
recently replaced the shifter and shift coupler bushings, put in short 
shift kit, replaced the reverse lock-out plate, and installed the heavy 
duty spring kit in my '68 912. There is virtually no slop in the shifter 
now and correct gear selection is much easier. I should have done this 
years ago.
