As far as valve cover finishes: I've seen plated finishes(dull metalic and
polished chrome) and black painted.  The dull plate seems original, the
black is probably too, and would be most efficient at dispersing heat.
Polished chrome looks good, disperses heat the worst.  Personal experience.
