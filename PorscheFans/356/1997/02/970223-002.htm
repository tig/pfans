Mark, according to Dr. Brett Johnson author of The 356 Porsche, A Restorer's
Guide to Authenticity, engine # 80131 is a 1956 356A 1600cc S, engine model
616/2, 3 RB (which means a 3 piece case and a roller bearing crank). It
should have 40PICB Carburetors, and produced 75 HP at 5000 RPM. Engine #
702963 is a 1962 356B/1600cc S, engine model,616/2, 3PB (which means 3 piece
case and plain journal bearing), 32 NDIX carburetors, and produced 75 HP at
5000 RPM. I can't help with the transmission.

Hope this helps,
