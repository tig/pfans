    Your understanding is completely WRONG!
   Jerry's Speedster did NOT have the Original Engine. It had a Heinz 57
engine, with Gross Mismatches. Thanks to the  Engine Data Base that now has
some 5,000 Engine Numbers, Date Stamps and the all-important Case Casting
Number it was easily determined that Jerry's engine had a pair of
 Main Case Halves made in  1959. The Third Piece  of Jerry's Case was from a
mid-1957.   And the  Speedster was  a 1958. Yes, it averages out to 1958, but
you can't do it that way. 
     You  surely wouldn' t know this, but the 1957 Third Piece used an
External Oil Booster Line that ran from the Oil Bypass Valve up past  the
fuel pump on the OUTSIDE of the Third Piece,  to  the Junction Block to
supply oil pressure to  the valve. But 1959 Main Case Halves are drilled  to
use an INTERNAL "oil booster line" that  fits INSIDE the Third Piece of the
1959 Engine. 
   Ergo, there was NO Oil Booster Line hooked up AT ALL! on Jerry's Engne!
Which may be part of the reason  why this mismatched pile of parts posing as
a Porsche engine had all of 16 PSI oil pressure. (Of course,  that  was AFTER
it had lost a fan belt 47 miles after being rebuilt by a Dealer in La La
Land. And Jerry didn't stop until BOTH lights came on on the dash.)
   The Second Under 356A crank needed to go  to Third Under. Not a nice
choice. The 356A rods had to be replaced- always-  there's no choice there.
The Original 356A Normal cam provokes no excitement. The 356A  Normal Cast
Iron Cam  Followers that break their heads off and destroy engines had to be
replaced by Ate 21411, 3000 or 3900 Steel Cam  Followers. Likewise the
MisMatched Case with LOTSA welded areas on the bottom was almost 2  thou out
and needed an align bore and  WASN'T THE ORIGINAL  ENGINE ANYWAY.
   Oh, and let us not forget  the severely flycut 356A heads still with  the
Brass Seats inside.
    And how about a New First- a fairly new Big Bore Kit with THREE different
kinds of Oil Control Rings! Wonder how/why that happened. This rebuild, you
may recall, was done by a DEALER!
