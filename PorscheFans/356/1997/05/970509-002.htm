Fellow PorscheFans,
This is almost your last change to register for the Roadrunner Regions 
annual Fiesta del Porsche in beautiful Santa Fe, New Mexico. This is a 
regional PCA event.

The event includes a concourse, autocross and rally and will be run from May 
23 through May 26. Hotel reservations will be tight and hard to find at this 
time, but it this event is worth trying.

The Concours will be Friday afternoon around the historic Plaza in Santa Fe. 
Friday evening will be the driver's meet and swap meet. The Driving Event 
will be Saturday and Sinday at the New Mexico Law Enforcement Academy track. 
Sundat evening will be the Concours and Driving Event Awards Dinner. Early 
Monday morning the TSD rally will take us through the beautiful mountains 
and mesas of northern New Mexico.

You may obtain additional by visiting http://www.swcp.com/~jungmann/rrr.html 
or email: jungmann@swcp.com (Registrar)
