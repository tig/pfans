To P-fans  from proud owner,

Last spring I was contacted by a small film company to ask if I would be
interested in renting my car ( a 1959 Conv. D ) for use in a film they were
making in my area. The film was to be called  'Love and Death on Long
Island'. Considering I had been working on restoring the car for the last 9
years and it wasn't quite finished yet I thought what could be a better
incentive ( I work better under time pressure anyway ). So off the car went
to the movies. 

Almost a year latter there was now news on the film until I got a phone
call one night. Guess what, the film is debuting at the Cannes film
festival and it is even up for some awards! When I checked out the festival
sight they even had the car in a picture for the film. So if anyone is
interested in seeing my labor of love, (even if it's just a cockpit view )
check out the following link.



Keep an eye out for the film too, the car plays a major role in the movie
with lots of on screen time.
