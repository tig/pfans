Kyle, 
no, it's not a VW motor, but some parts do work on the 912.  look for
Harry Pellow's "The Maestro's Little Spec Book".  It lists VW parts that
will work for the 912/356 motor.  The Maestro is controversial and his
writing style turns some people off, but if you're interested in low cost
VW parts, this book may help.  As far as catalogs and companies, there
are many.  If you haven't already, join the Porsche Club of America, it's
worth it if only for 'Panorama', the monthly magazine that will get your
parts search started.
