Dear Jeff:

When I got my '66 912 years ago it has the big bore kit and the
original, but worn out 022 distributor (with two piece points, bad
design but original).  I installed an 031 distributor that had had a
similar performance curve and one-piece points.  It cost more than the
009, which people used on 914s, but the performance was worth it.  Don't
know about the 050.

Regarding the points, I never eliminated them, just put a Delta CD unit
on to prevent the points from pitting and to boost the spark voltage.  I
now have a Delta CD on my 1968 912 Targa.
