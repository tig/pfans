Statewide 356 Gathering Near Austin (TX) Coming Up This Spring

Marble Falls in the Texas hill country is the location of an event which
will be held on the weekend April 4-6, 1997.

Marble Falls is near Austin and about 200 miles from both Dallas and
Houston, so it is well within reasonable weekend reach of out of state 356'ers.

Room reservations can be made now for those who may be interested, email for
details.

This is a preliminary notice, and more details will be posted as they become
available.
