Bob Langenbach asks

<< I have been told that this Porsche has existing anchors
 for 3-point belts.  Who can recommend where to get belts, and where are the
 built-in anchor points located in the body?  I live in the Seattle area.
  >>

Your car may be too early for the anchors. If you have them, there should be
a plastic disc on either side of the rear seating area, held in by a large
phillips screw. If you take that out you will find a threaded hole that was
intented for a seat belt anchor. You have to make your own hardware and find
your own belt material and latches. I use Simpson racing belts.
