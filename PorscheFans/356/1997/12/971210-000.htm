Re :Have restored a lovely 1965 SC Cab 4 years ago but continue to experience
chronic starting problems after running engine. Then stop for a moment-starter
just won't turn over engine! ( 6 volt system). Can only do a running (push)
jump start to ignite engine again! Or wait until engine cold again!


Most likely the starter bushing in the transmission housing is binding the
starter shaft.  This can be a temperature sensitive problem.  Bushings in
the starter can be worn out also which causes the shaft to misalign and
bind. So, remove starter, check bushings in starter, replace bushing in
trans. Should cure the problem but, given the location, its a non trivial
job.  I did it with the engine in once and never again.  Good luck.
