Unleaded fuel causes problems for valve seats not guides. No oil to 
cylinder head or high mileage causes worn guides. If guides are worn 
a properly ajusted valve will still be noisey. With the valve cover 
off you can wiggle a valve with a badly worn guide. The 356 valve 
seats are hard enough however to run unleaded fuel. How do you know 
you are "eating" guides? Bruce
