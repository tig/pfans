Greg,

	Sounds like fuel starving, and you seem to have checked just 
about all of the obvious sources, but you didn't mention fuel filters 
(one in tank, one in engine compartment) in your missive.  Don't know if 
you just didn't mention, or haven't checked this yet.  Another 
possibility is a clogged charcoal cannister (don't know if '68 912s had 
these) or a clogged fuel tank vent (do you hear a hiss when removing the 
fuel cap after driving a half hour or so?).

Anyway, just a few more obvious, inexpensive things to try before you try 
tearing down the Solexes.
