Do not drive the car until you can determine what is wrong. The 912 case has 
very limited clearances, and anything comming apart inside could potentially 
destroy the case, crank, and other VERY expensive parts. 
First thing to check is the oil screen. (Drain oil, remove screen from 
bottom of engine) look for little shiny metal shavings. IF they are copper 
colored then it's likely a bearing failure. Next, check valve play, as if 
you were adjusting all the valves (Any evidence of a broken/bent valve will 
be apparent there). E-mail me if you need additional help, and I can put you 
in touch with someone that can help.
