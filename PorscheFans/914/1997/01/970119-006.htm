Here's an OPINION:

If you're starting off intending to BUILD a 914-6, start with a
pair of cars of the same year; a 914 with a good frame, and a 911
with a good motor.

If you're starting off intending to OWN a 914-6, buy someone
else's project and finish it.  (If you buy the right project,
finishing it might mean inserting the ignition key.)
