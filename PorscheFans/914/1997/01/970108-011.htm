>> Until recently I also believed the way to pass was to lean until it 
>> almost won't run.  It turns out that the lean mixture results in 
>> unburned, or half-burned oxygen - CO.  You have to be near stoich 
for 
>> ideal emissions.  Too lean, you get CO; too rich, you get unburned 
gas 
>> - HC.  If you have both high CO and HC, then it is too lean and 
>> misfiring.
>> 
>> You can richen the mixture most easily by putting a 500 ohm 
>> potentiometer (rheostat, really) in series with the Cyl Head Temp 
>> sensor.  You can go lean by reducing fuel pressure.  I would set it 
up 
>> by ear, then pay a gas station for 20 minutes playing while their 
>> machine reads out the numbers.  At too lean, the rpm hunts up and 
down.
>> Get it just richer than that.
