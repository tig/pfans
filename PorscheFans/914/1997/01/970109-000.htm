Just a thought, but although the battery is very suspect, a failing
voltage regulator could be contributing by not fully charging the
battery. Last winter my 2.0 would barely start even with an almost new
battery. It turned out to be the VR, which was only delivering 11-12
volts. Replacing it fixed the cold starting problem.
