My understanding is that the 914 fuel line recall involved
replacing the original metal fuel lines with plastic lines.
The problem is that the fuel lines run underneath the
battery and battery acid could drip on to and corrode
the metal lines causing a great fire hazard.  The plastic
replacement lines are impervious to battery acid.

If you have plastic fuel lines, then your car has already
had that recall work done to it.
