Here's the problem with trying to piant the case. You will never be able
to keep the paint adhering to the case. Especially around the CV flanges
and the fill/drain plugs. The reason is that the Magnesium alloy is that
the case is made of is porous and is prone to weeping oil. The paint
will loose it's grip the same way that rust blisters paint from
underneath. Even if you beadblast/scrub/soak the case, it will never
stay clean if you put oil into it.
