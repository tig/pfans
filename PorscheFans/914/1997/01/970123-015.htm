According to my Stoddard catalog, there are quite a few parts that are
different between the early and late shifter. The shifter rod and coupling
in the shifter tunnel is different. There's a bracket on the early shifter
that isn't used on the later one. The shift lever (mount) and bracket is
different, also. Everything from the firewall back to the transmission is
different. The best bet is to grab the entire guts intoto and transplant
it into your earlier car.
