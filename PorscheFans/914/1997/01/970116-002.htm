The brake lines are "bubble" flared which you cannot make yourself. 
Must buy prefab lines w/ metric fittings.  I needed to couple 2 lines
and found that NOBODY sells a female metric bubble coupler... the
solution is waiting for you in any wrecking yard 914; there's a coupler
at the aft end of the tunnel, at the shift rod adj hole.... I put my
valve in the cockpit just behind the shifter.
