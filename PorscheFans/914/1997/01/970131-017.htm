	I cleaned out my injectors with spray can carb cleaner
	when I replaced the hoses last year.  I had the same thing happen
	upon start up.  Car started, ran like crap, turned out one
	bank was dead.  Solution was easier though.  I just had to
make up the electrical connectors.

	I had to use a bpwer source too, but mine was just a 1.5 volt
	battery.  I know there are resistors in line with the solenoid
and they are not continuous duty so I used the smallest voltage that
	would just make it work.  ALso I reinstalled them in the car
and ran gasoline through them right away.  No problems once
I remembered to plug them all in.
