Here's a thought (wow! a first from me....):

Are ducts/scoops/air channelling allowed in your class?  I think
Andy pointed out that there are a big dead spot of air right behind
the rear window.  Well, in some old VW&Porsche magazine I have somewhere,
there was an article about a 914 convertible.  Since the engine cover
grille was covered by the top, they added some side scoops (extending
from behind the door handles).  Apparently, this worked well, but made
the rear end all light, so they stopped doing it.  Why couldn't
you do the same, but duct the air over the rear lid, and fill the dead
spot?  You certainly get more cool air into the engine compartment, and
that might be worth something.....
