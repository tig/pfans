I'm no guru, but I've always heard that it was because the VW combustion 
chamber shape and valve layout was so bad, on top of the fact that the 
head temperature is much higher for air cooled engines than they are for 
water cooled. Unless the cooling system is enhanced, going to a high 
compression ratio or significantly increasing the displacment will mean 
higher temperatures and therefore shorter engine life.
