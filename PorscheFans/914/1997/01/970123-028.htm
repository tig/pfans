I'll be breif because this is so general to be nearly useless.

In theory you need never change sway bars at all, PROVIDED you have
maintained front and rear balance by upgrading the springing at both
ends by the same proportional amount. In practice it all depends on how
you drive hand how your car handles (or doesn't handle). What are your
plans for the front torsion bars?

In my road-going 75 2.0 with stock F/R sway bars I run 19mm front
torsion bars with 165-lb progressive rear coils. I would also feel OK
with 140 lb. rear coils, but I don't race.When I do venture out on the
track, I am happy with this set up.
