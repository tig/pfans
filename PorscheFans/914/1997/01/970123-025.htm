I second Gary's opinion to not go to carbs.  FI is where it's at.  

The motor mounts and whatnot might be a bit of a headache, if I remember
correctly.  The early 70-72 mounts are at the ends of the mount bar, and
the later mounts are in the middle between the motor and the bar.  However,
I'm sure the bar+mounts will bolt up to either case, since the cases are
identical. 

If you do the side-shifter conversion (highly recommended) then go with the
76 mounts+bar & linkage, and get the firewall adapter bushing from one of
the mail-order sources.

If it were my car, I would make sure I get the 2.0 FI and install it.  You
can keep the same fuel pump, but almost everything else is different, if I
remember correctly.  The injectors, MAP sensor, etc. are all different from
1.7 to 2.0
