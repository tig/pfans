A while ago someone commented on  the 914 having the aerodynamics of a 
brick.  I knew this wasn't true, but at the time I had no real 
evidence-until now.

The source:  Aerodynamics of Road Vehicles, Butterworths, 1986
	     Edited by Wolf-Heinrich Hucho

The cars:   914	 Cd: .34 to .35
            911 Carrera Cabrio  Cd:  .40 to .41
	    911 Carrera  Cd:  .38 to .39
	    928 S  Cd:  .38 to .40
	    944  Cd:  .35 to .36
	    944 Turbo  Cd: .33 to .34
	    924  Cd: .31 to .33		

Although the years aren't specified, the 914 has lower figures than the 
911, and 928.  

Just thought you'd like to know...
