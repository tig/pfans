Are you certain it's an exhaust smell? It could very well be burning
dust if your heat hasn't been used in a while. Possibly oil as well.

> The system looks like it is all in place, we replaced the full heater
> system a few years ago carefully, and while it smelled like this briefly
> after the heater parts were put in place then, the bad smell is not going
> away now. Again, all parts appear to be in place.

Do you mean you replaced the heat exchangers? Or just the heater ducting
under the engine. If you have new heat exchangers then maybe you're just
burning off the coating. If the heat exchangers are old, maybe they've
now rusted through and if so, REPLACE THEM IMEDIATELY. 
 
> 2. We had a new Bursch muffler put in about one month ago. I simply do

>    before the new muffler either.

Aha! What you are smelling is the Powder coating of the collector
burning off. This will last for a while untill the powder coating is
gone. Happened to me too.

> Any ideas what to look for? Is this a rotten heat exchanger? The muffler was
