What were the condition of the O rings? They may be leaky by now, so it 
sucks air instead of gas. There is an exact replacement in viton rubber 
at your local hardware store. I think it's a #81 or #85. Take the front 
cover to the store and find one that just fits (barely protrudes above 
the flat surface). I replaced mine, but haven't run the pump yet. I do 
know that an O ring that is too thick will disrupt the clearnace between 
the impeller and the casing. The pump needs a sandwich together tight. 
there should be no gap around the edges after you screw it back together. 
Hope this helps.
