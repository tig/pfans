To answer your question about your front nose piece, I have an original Kremer
K5 one-piece, I pulled out of the original bucks.  If you have Joseph Katz's
"New Dimensions in Race Car Aerodynamics", look on page 19, Figure 1-30.  That

pics can be found in the Jun 93 "Excellence Magazine", on pages 88 & 89.  I have

that pic, you'll notice that IMSA made him remove the bottom front horizontal
lip, the curling fences going up over the fender line, and they didn't let him

be generated by this nose and they trimmed Sam way back.
