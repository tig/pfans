Fred,  
 
The BFGR1s will outlast the Hoosiers and my personal favorites, the Goodyear 
GSCS by a factor of 2-3.  Look at the wear rating number on the tires.  GSCS 
is 30.  R1s are 60 or higher if I remember correctly.  Check that wear number 
on anything you're looking to buy.  The higher the number the longer wearing.  
Yoko AVS Z rated by comparison had a rating of 240 I think. 
 
I haven't heard anything positive on the new Yokos yet.  I think those who 
have 'em are still working out the pressures as I've heard some negative stuff 
but I don't think the sources understood tire temping and suspension 
adjustment very well.....
