>The only reason NOT to use a wider tire...

>A 225-45 might not be appropriate on a 5.5" rim; not enough

Agreed.  An assumption should be made that any application of a tire greater
than, say, 205/50-15 should be mounted on a rim wider than stock 5.5 inch rims.

Using a tire too wide for a given rim CAN BE dangerous.  The angle created
between the sidewall and rim will cause a weakened seal.  I'm sure the
physics guys in the group can add to this layman's list of reasons.
