I put new turbo tie rods on my autocross 914 and nad to remove the ball
joint. 1. Remove the castellated nut and cotter pin.  They are on the top
of the connection.  Be careful of the sealing bellows.  The easiest way to
do it is to use a special forked tool that slips into the space on the ball
joint.  Gently tap with a hammer until the joint pops.  This can be done
with an ordinary chisel if caution is used not to damage any of the parts.
