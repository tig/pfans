	Well, the VW Type IV heads, regardless of displacement, have the
exhaust ports (two per side) on the bottom--not the ends.
	If I remember correctly, the heads were stamped "040".  I think I
remember hearing "040 heads" referenced in someone's go-fast Type I (VW)
motor-building recipie.  My guess is that they're relatively hi-po Type I
heads.
	If you have a local Bug-specialty house, you could take the heads
over to them and ask.  You could also try on rec.autos.makers.vw.aircooled
for an answer.
