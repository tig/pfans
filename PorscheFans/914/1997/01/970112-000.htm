I don't know the German for ROZ and RON.  Research octane is higher, 
and more generous.  For practical purposes, Mot is a better guide.
I have a long, very technical article from a recent issue of Automotive 
Engineering on the history of gasoline since the 1930's.  Could make a

To measure MON in the good old days, they used to put the test gas in a

they do now, but I have a nephew who mixes and tests, so I could find 
out.
