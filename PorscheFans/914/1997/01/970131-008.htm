>   Anyone have recommendations on the high tech spiral wound type wires?

Yes. I use Super Trak's Turbo Mag brand from a mystery straight 6 engine. 
About 15$. If you look on the box, they show a magnetic core with 
spiral-wound wire around it. The wire is only 7mm, though, but it's good 
and cheap. The only other wires I've seen like it are in the Summit 
Racing catalog, but there you'll have to buy for a V8 (hey, have some 
spare wires). The magnetic core suppresses resonance in the square 
waveform of the spark pulse. Some wires say they are "mag" but they don't 
look like it to me. Hope this helps.
