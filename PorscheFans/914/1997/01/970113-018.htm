> With some nice weather this weekend, I took my 74 2.0 out for a drive.  I   
> noticed a minor fuel leak dripping in the vicinity of the #3 injector. I   
> could not tell exactly where the drip was originating.  After driving the   
> car for 5 minutes the leak went away (i.e. as the engine heated up).  Has   
> anyone seen this type of problem before?  Could the O-ring seal be   
> expanding as the temperature heats up?

It's the injector.  It expands as it gets hot, and the crack closes.

General note:  The injector closest to the pump seems to fail first.

> 
> I have a friend with a recently purchased 76 914 2.0.  He noticed that   
> the back wheels bow in slightly.  At what point does this become a   
> suspension problem?

It's supposed to be that way; a *slight* bow in at the top.

Worry when:
1:  Left and right are different,
2:  There is a sudden change in either side.
    (But if there is a sudden change, it will be the right side)
3:  The camber is more than slight.
