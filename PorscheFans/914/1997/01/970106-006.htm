I wrote one of my senators about clunker laws and emissions testing for
older cars. He forwarded my letter to the EPA for comment, and I finally
received the comments last week. The letter says that they set these
clunker guidlines as a totally voluntary way to get heavy polluter cars
off the road. The guidelines have a provision to enable parts to be
stripped from the car before it's crushed. Basically, epa says that they
intended to create a market for these cars that no one supposedly would
want. The letter also says that they encourage the upkeep and
maintainence on older vehicles.
