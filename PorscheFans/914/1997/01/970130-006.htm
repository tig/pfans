  You will notice the absence of any sort of date... there is a deadline 
date of May 15 for motel reservations, and that's it.  Note that entries in 
the events sections of the various web pages tend to hang around for 
awhile after the event occurs; not posting an event date, with the year 
means that no one, with the possible exception of the event organizers, 
has the slightest idea of when the get-together is supposed to take 
place.
