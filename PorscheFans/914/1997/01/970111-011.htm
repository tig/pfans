There are lots of good ways to mount an aux. cooler. The best location
for a cooler in the front bumper. Plumbing and exhaust are problems
though when mounting in front. Some people like to mount it on the
underside of the trunk next to the tranny. The easiest location is on
the engine lid. I have a 25row fan forced oil cooler mounted on the
inside of the engine lid. Works great! With the fan on, I have yet to
see temps above 210, and that's on a hot day, keeping the revs in the
fun zone. The fan makes a significant contribution. 

Good luck with your oil cooler.
