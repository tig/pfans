Timothy Gordon wrote:
> 
> Questions on pressures? I currently have Yoko A-008R (195x60x15) mounted on
> stock Fuchs. I an currently running Time Trials as well as Autocrosses. I
> have heard as low as 24 lbs and as high as 36 lbs. What is the best pressure
> for this type of use? I would like them to last as long as possible, but if
> performance dictates tire life then I guess thats the price one pays. Any
> info would be greatly appreciated. Also would these differ if I purchased
> 205x50x15?

Pressures are very important for tire life. Usually, the optimal
performance pressure would also be best for tire life. What kills race
tires is uneven tire wear and excessive heating. I used to run that size
A008Rs and I found that 28-32 lbs was a good range for these tires on a
914. This will vary from tire to tire and size to size. So your milage
may vary.
