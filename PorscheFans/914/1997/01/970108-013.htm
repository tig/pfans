 I read an article in european car about replacing the trailing arm

Don't need to do all of this.  Force lube such as soapy water between the
shaft and the bushings and twist them off by holding the outer mount in a
vice, and pulling on the trailing arm as you twist it on the stationary
shaft, or use a hydraulic press.  All burning them out does is make a mess,
stink up the neigborhood, and burns the paint off the trailing arm.
