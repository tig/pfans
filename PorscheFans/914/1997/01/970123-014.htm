>I'm thinking about stiffening up the suspension on my track '73 914 for   
>this year's season.  Presently it has just the stock components including   
>front and rear factory sway bars.  How high (stiff) can I go in the   
>springs before I need to consider changing the sway bars to a larger

This is not your answer, but remember that stiffer is not always better (at
least when your talking about your 914 suspension :) ).  If it were, we'd
all be removing our suspensions.

>Note:  I'm presently thinking of going to 140 lb adjustable springs (so I   
>can lower the rear: the front is already lowered).  I'll also be changing   
>to Koni adjustable shocks all around.

Check your regulations to make sure adjustable perches are allowed.  Some
classes do not allow this.

PS - I use 140 lb'ers.  My car will be a combination street/autocrosser.
140's seem to be the consensus for my application.  I've been told that
180's are OK for an autocross only car.
