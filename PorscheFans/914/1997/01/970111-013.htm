>just noticed that this unit has a notch that restricts the travel of the

>is less than good, clearances are so tight that rotor wears into the
>perlux module...had similar (but worse) experience w/ crane unit in 911
>distributor, one reputable suplier actually shipped me a "new" unit that
>had this wear already,  WHAT IF i bend the tab an allow full vac
>advance?

I don't think the restriction is intentional -- I think they simply made
the unit for some other Bosch distributor, and didn't know about the range
of the 914's advance and retard.  I usually grind that half-moon to be
larger so as not to limit advance and retard. Don't bend the tab -- grind
the base.

I've been able to get both of my units to work without rubbing against the
(non-rev-limiting) rotors -- it's tight, but it works.  On both units I
very carefully bent the pickup so that it is perfectly perpendicular to the
base -- this made a big difference.

my 914 folder!
