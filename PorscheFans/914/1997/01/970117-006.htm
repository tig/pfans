Gary Helbig wrote:
> 
> The only reason NOT to use a wider tire:
> 
> The tread width becomes so wide that the sidewalls have to
> pinch in to meet the rim.
> 
> A 225-45 might not be appropriate on a 5.5" rim; not enough
> sidewall to "span the gap".
> 
> FWIW:  Stock combinations have about 30mm of tread per inch
> of rim width.

Gary, 

	For one, a 225/45 will never fit under a stock rear fender. And I would
be sceptical that any tire shop would try to mount a 225/45 on a 5.5"
rim. Especially a modern race tire like the R1, the sidewalls are too
stiff and would likely pull the bead right off the rim. It's just a
dangerous proposition.
