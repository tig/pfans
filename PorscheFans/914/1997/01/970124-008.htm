I'll second Andy's comment.  From my experience in SCCA crew
susp setup you'll find that you lose some level of control and 
defeat the purpose of your shocks if you go too stiff on the springs.

We find that on most tracks (unless smooth as glass) a slighly
softer spring and a real stiff  anti-sway bar works best with a Porsche.

This experience is in racing but it probably applies.
