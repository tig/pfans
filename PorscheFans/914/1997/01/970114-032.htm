Stephen,
A diamond is 4 sided with longer flexible sides. The hex is 6 sided with
short ridgid sides. These extra sides are increasing the strength of the
material to keep flex from occuring without any increase in the total weight
of the hood. Varify by modeling  these geometric shapes from paper and you
will see that the hex will support more weight before collapsing  than the
diamond. This will explain your question.
