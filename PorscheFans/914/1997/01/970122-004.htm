Thanks to all who responded about the front swaybar installation, esp. Dirk
Gary, and Mike!

Quick recap:
I was trying to install a Weltmeister front swaybar, along with the optional
reinforcement plates for cars originally unequiped with swaybars.

Tip 1:
Get/use the optional triangular reinforcement plates (already got 'em); the
swaybar may rip holes in the body if these are not used.

Tip 2:
The reinforcements should be welded in place on the inside of the body.

Tip 3:
Make a photocopy of the instructions, and cut it out/use as a template by
gluing it to the car to mark/align drill points.

Thanks again; I'll probably be attempting the install soon.....
