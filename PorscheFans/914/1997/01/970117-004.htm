The only reason NOT to use a wider tire:

The tread width becomes so wide that the sidewalls have to
pinch in to meet the rim.

A 225-45 might not be appropriate on a 5.5" rim; not enough
sidewall to "span the gap".

FWIW:  Stock combinations have about 30mm of tread per inch
of rim width.
