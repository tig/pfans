The floors are convered with a bituminous sound proofing material that 
traps water and rusts out the metal. I took a heavy duty scrapper, 
sharpened it, and hammered my way around the floor to remove it. You'll 
mostlikely find rust, mostly in the long indentations across the floor, 
and arounf the pedal cluster area.
