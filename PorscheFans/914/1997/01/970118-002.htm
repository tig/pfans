> Anyone have a *new* fuel pump for sale? Car is a  1973 2.0L.

You can try and replace the two O rings. Remove the four bolts and you'll 
find 2 O rings, one on each side of the pump plate. Take the end cover to 
a hardware store and find an O ring that just fits inside the groove, and 
only protrudes a very small amount. I think it's a #81, or #85. You'll 
know you have the right one when you put it back together and the plates 
fit flush (no gap after tightening).
