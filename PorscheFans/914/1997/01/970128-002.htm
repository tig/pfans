I finally figured out a way to get my tranny case painted without having 
to take it to the blaster/striper/powder-coater. Since the surface of the 
case is a rough casting, brush strokes won't show, so I don't have to 
spray. That's a good thing, because the self etching primer that the pros 
use on alloys only comes in cans to be used in spray guns. So, once I 
throughly de-grease the outside of the case, I can apply the primer, then 
top coat with an epoxy paint to resist corrosion and chemicals. Iwas 
thinking either lime green or bright yellow.
