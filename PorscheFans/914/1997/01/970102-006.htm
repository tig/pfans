Is there any spark being produced by the ignition system?  Simple test:
pull one of the plugs, put the plug wire back on, and with a pair of
rubber-gripped pliers, hold the tip of the sparkplug next to a grounded
part of the engine compartment while someone else turns the car over.  I
usually hold it near the fan housing (preferably away from any fuel hoses).
This should produce a spark sufficient enough to see in the daylight.  If
not, search the elctrical system from there on back.

Sorry if this has been mentioned, but it's bitten me before.
