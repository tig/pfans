My preferred way to install new battery tray or rear decklid hinge 
bracket is 1/8" steel pop rivets every half inch.  They hold good if 
there is good base material, and it's easier to drill them out the next 
time rather than chisel or grind spot welds like the original.  Unless 
you can do spot welds, I recommend steel pop rivets.
