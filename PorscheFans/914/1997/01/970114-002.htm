> "I have a friend with a recently purchased 76 914 2.0.  He noticed
> that the back wheels bow in slightly.  At what point does this become
> a suspension problem? "
> 
>   Automobile Atlanta's tech tip says:  "If the right rear wheel is
> suddenly IN at the top and OUT at the bottom, the rear suspension
> console has broken and needs replacement."
> 
>   The usual cause for failure of this part is the battery acid rust
> problem; that is why they talk about the RIGHT side.  Inspect the
> area for rust, cracks, or obvious breaks in the metal.  If none are
> found, the problem may just be rear camber adjustment.  If you find
> the suspension mounting point to be weakened or broken, this
> means a welding job with the engine OUT, followed by a rear wheel
> alignment.
