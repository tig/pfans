I've heard from 914 friends that there is a square rubber bushing/gasket
between the caliper halves, (unobtainium brand), which, if compromised,
will render your caliper useless.  ie: have a backup used caliper in
reserve when you break the halves.

I have an operational right rear 70 914 caliper; reasonably priced.
