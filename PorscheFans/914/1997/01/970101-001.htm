Re:
> Can anyone tell me the piston size of a 9146 caliper ?? I know 
>the calipers on a 914 and 9146 look the same. Im told the only 
>difference is the piston size ?? Is this true ?? anyone know the 
>size ?? Thanks for the help, Don

914-4   Front   1.7"/42mm       Rear    1.3"/33mm
914/6   Front*  1.9"/48mm       Rear    1.5"/38mm

The front pads are the same size.

The BMW320i calipers have similarly "large" pistons (does anyone have a
spec?) but use a pad that's 40% larger.

You may want to check out my TechTip at: http://www.estinc.com/porsche/. It
covers a lot of ground on the whole 914 brake issue.


* Same as 911T, 911E '69-'74.
