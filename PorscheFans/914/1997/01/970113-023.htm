"Wasn't aware..."  Yeah, RIGHT!  It's probably been sitting *because*
it wouldn't pass smog.  And because of "bug" maintenance, it will be
a task to get it to pass.  (Mayby)

The standards are easier for the "pre-75" cars.  If it's running
right, and all of the hoses are in the right spot, it should
pass.  I have to take mine to someone who knows what he's doing,
the local "smug-pros" can't do it.
