Weltmeister kit goes through body.  The adapter kit that does not require
removing gas tank uses u bolts to tie in the mounts.  NOT recommended as the
sheet metal will fatigue and tear around the holes.  Best approach is to weld
in the swaybar reinforcements with the gas tank removed (not a hard job), and
do the job right.  Heavier baars only case craks if the reinforements are not
welded in.
