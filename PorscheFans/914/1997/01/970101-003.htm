I just bought "Porsche Chronicles" by the consumer guide people. They
discuss the 914/8 prototypes a little, and I discovered that they built
them in 1969, not later, as I thought. The first had the 300hp flat 8 and 
was clocked at 155mph. The second was detuned to 260hp, and was the 
present to Dr. Porsche on his 60th birthday. The second one had a fixed 
steel roof, like the 916, plus a sun roof! I thought this might be 
interesting enough to share with the list.
