> So what's a nifty "oil transfer" thing???
> 

It's a hand operated piston inside a closed cylinder with a plastic tube 
on the suction end. You stick the tube into your fresh oil, pull the 
piston up to draw oil into the cylinder, crawl under the car, stick the 
tube into the fill hole (after replacing the drain plug of course!) and 
push on the pison to transfer the oil into the gear case. Gotta have good 
muscles with cold 90 weight...
