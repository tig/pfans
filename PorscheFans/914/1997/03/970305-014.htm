Hi all,

	I had a bit of time today and caught Austrailian Sprint Boat racing on
SpeedVision. The best way to describe it is a combination of powerboat
racing (little boats, big V8s), autocross, and rally racing. A total
kick to watch.
	The 'course' is a serpentine trench that's been dug and filled with
water. At its widest it's about 50-60 ft wide, narrowest is about
15-20ft. The whole course winds around on itself producing multiple
crossovers. A run lasts about a minute. And there's a navigator pointing
the direction to go. These little boats were both powerful and agile!
I'd say there were about 10-15 turns for one run. What a cool idea! In a
really tight turn the boats manage to pretty much evacuate the trench of
the water behind the boat as the hull and motor throw the water into the
air and onto the banks.
	I just _had_ to pass that along.
