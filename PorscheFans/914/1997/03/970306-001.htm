Hey everyone, there's a great article in the March edition of Racer's 
World, a motorcycle racing magazine, about the cryogenic treatment of 
engine and transmission parts. Apparently, many of the top motorcycle 
racers have cyrogenically treated engines. I'm going to get the 2 good 
first speed drive gears for my transmission treated by Cryolabs 
(909)672-0693 in California. They have lots of experience with treating 
these types of parts. Basically, you have to send them clean, 
disassembled parts for treatment - they don't do any disassembly or 
cleaning. Thier prices seem reasonable, too - most of motorcylce engine 
for around 250$. A couple of small gears would be next to nothing. I want 
to get these gears treated because 1) they fail early, and 2) they are 
becoming unavailable new. Since they are high carbon, heat treated steel, 
the results shoudl be excellent.
