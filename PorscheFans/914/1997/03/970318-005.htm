that's been exposed to engine heat will have brittle insulation and 
corroded copper. The connectors (those tiny female spades and those 
special card edge) are not readily available, so you can't repair the 
harness yourself with a superior, modern wire. If you've ever cut the 
plastic tubing off the harness you'll know the meaning of grungy. I mean 
disgustingly gross ancient oil everywhere!
