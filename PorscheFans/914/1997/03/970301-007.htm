Ted's right about his assessment on car entry numbers - at last week's POC TT
and Cup Race at Buttonwillow, there was one 356, 5 or 6 early 911/912, and a
handful of 914s (not counting high-end 6 cyl cars). I only saw 2 of the 914s
in the cup race. Also, all of early 911/912s but one and the 356 were only
there to get time in on a new track for an upcoming VARA event and we all
left before timed runs.

Here's my usual pitch for Vintage Racing. The person that started this thread
could build a "starter" 912 for no more than the 914 he mentioned... and then
have about 100 vintage events to chose from around the country and still
enter PCA/POC/etc events whenever he desired.  The same isn't true for the
914 because most vintage organizations won't let it run.
