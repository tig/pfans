Well, a year or so ago, I had one of our distributors on a distributor
machine, and found out that the retard is ~10-12 deg and advance is ~5-6
deg if my memory is correct.  What I remember is that the retard was about
2x the advance.

Hope this helps.  The other way to quickly find out would be to have the
engine running, and use a vacuum pump and an advance timing light.
