Nick,

If you stand on the battery side of the engine compartment, it will be to
the left of the distributor.  Carefully remove the rubber sealing boot, and
then use a 24mm socket (pref deep or semi-deep) or a 15/16" if you don't
have metric.  You may have to remove the cap and pull it aside to find
clearance to get to it.  I use 2 wraps of teflon tape to help seal the
threads.  Don't overtighten. This sender is often the source of the oil
drops that end up on the bottom of the oil filter :)

cheers, Brian
