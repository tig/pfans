	Big line (green) is for manifold vacuum for  the vacuum retard.
The other  line that goes in the  top  (small)  is for the  vacuum
advance.  That  is the one  that  goes to the ported  vacuum  on the
throttle body  (along  with  egr, whic year is it?).
