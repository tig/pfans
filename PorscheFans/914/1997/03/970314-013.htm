The 10 or 12 piece chassis stiffening kit is something you
probably will NOT need for street use.  I know of racers out
there who may need it, but street tires with "hard" street
driving doesn't tax the 914 frame that much.

I'd do that shock/spring setup...hey....I did!!!  :-)

If you are considering flares, then you've got to fill them
with something...and wide wheels and tires cost $$$.
Fiberglass flares generally suck quality-wise and
steel 914-6 GT flares cost about $1000 or more for the
set.

I believe both the Chalon and the 914-6 GT can use
15x8 for the front and 15x9 for the rear...so get some prices
on those while you're at it.

you like.
