Dave,

	The button is on the right side of the master, and can be reached with
a long extension or screwdriver thru the wheel well. The button is
covered with a rubber boot. Push in on the boot untill it gives
noticeably. That should reset the switch. I would recomend using an
extansion rather than a screwdriver, you don't want to damage the boot.
