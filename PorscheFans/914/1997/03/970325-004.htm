Just got my gears back from CryoPro in Springfield, MO. Total cost was 
43$ for 2 pairs of first speed (4 gears). They charge 10$/# for misc. 
parts like these. Other parts like rods are a couple of bucks each. It 
takes a little over week round trip, since they go slow on the cool down 
and warm up phases of the process. I hope this makes a difference in the 
longevity of these increasingly hard to come by parts. They gave me a 
little promotional gift of a treated Bic razor, too. Nice people.
