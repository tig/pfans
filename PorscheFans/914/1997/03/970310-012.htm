          After pulling the engine out of my 914 a couple of times I
          came up with this method of removal. Disconect all the top
          of the engine stuff. Jack up the and then put jack stands
          under it. Remove the under car stuff (Clutch shift linkage .
          . .). Then I use a pair of bottle jacks and a lawn mower
          frame. The handled of the mower is parallel to the ground
          so I can slide it under the car. The frame is put under the
          engine tranny combo. O lower the car until it is a couple of
          millimeters above the frame. I start unbolt the front mount
          bar. This lowers the engine the rest of the way to the
          frame. Then I unbolt the tranny. You have to be careful here
          because it is higher then the front of the engine due to
          the incline of the car. I normal let it rest on my legs
          while I unbolt then lower it the rest of the way with my
          arms. Yes its heavey. Yes it hurts. Then I Lift the car up
          out of the way and pull the whole thing out.

          Nick
