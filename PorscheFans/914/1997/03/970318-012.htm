 Mike Nugent asked above:

Weber Carburators Manual  thru 1979 by Haynes shows this info on the 44-IDF
Manufacturer          Model Details      Capacity in CC   Carb. Type

Fiat                           124 Sport/Rally - 1997 CC - 44IDF 20/200/21/200
Fiat-Abarth              124 Sport/Rally - 1756 CC - 44IDF 20-21/200 
Ford                         Escort 2000RS - 1993 CC - 44IDF 40/41

So the shown CC is from 1756cc to 1997cc in a dual carburator configuration.

As an additional note:
The Porsche 914/6 shows 1991CC with a Weber 40IDTP 13C/1. 
The Weber 40IDF series support Lamborghini Urraco (USA) @ 2462 CC (8
cylinder & 4 carbs)
