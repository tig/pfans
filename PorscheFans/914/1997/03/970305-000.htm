Hi all.

We recently had the 73 914 2.0l in for pre-track-days top-to-bottom
going over at Powertech (Porsche shop) here in NJ. (wonderful place by 
the way, Keith and Mike are top-rate)

They replaced the gearshift gasket under the transmission, and while 
doing so they also replaced the trans fluid with Swepco. (Car has 121K;
engine was rebuilt at about 100K, but original trans.)

Here's the deal: With the Swepco the terrible 2nd gear synchro is quiet
and happy once the car warms up. The difference is simply amazing. We had 
changed the trans fluid about 10K miles ago with regular fluid, no 
difference then. The 2nd gear is still horrible until warm. I do not 
downshift into 2nd until about 5 minutes after running.

I am not associated with Swepco in any way...just thought others would 
rather replace fluid than think about rebuilds...
