Chris,

I took an old distributor cap and cut it in half.  I then painted the
cylinder numbers on the outside to make it easier after reinstalling a
distributor (for whatever reason) and setting the valves.  This way, I can
also easily see the action of the points.  Oh yeh, and I also made marks on
the fan when I reached my 'best guess' at each TDC with the engine out of
the car (so I could also easily see the pistons.

Hope this helps a little.
