Don,
just don't change anything untill the engine is back in!!!  You may be 
amazed how much the front end will drop when the engine goes in - remember 
the wonderful front-to-rear weight distribution of these cars.  Also, if you 
don't have adjustable spring perches on the back, you can't adjust the rear 
ride height so you'll want to adjust the front to match the rear.  I like to 
have my car sit just slightly (about 1/4") lower in the front than the back 
with a full gas tank.  That way, when the tank is empty, the front sits just 
about level with the back.

Just my thoughts....
