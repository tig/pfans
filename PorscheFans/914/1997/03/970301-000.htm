Well, it looks like new fuel injection harnesses are really, really NLA. 
It's certainly not a complex part - why doesn't someone start making them 
again?? (George - are you listening??). Any used one is going to be 
cruddy. 

I've determined that the little mini female spade connectors with the lock
tab on the back are called "slip spades," but I haven't found a source for
them yet. I don't have a clue for a source of the crimp connectors for the
card edge connector - I think Stephen mentioned that they were the same as
the ones used in old mainframe computers. Anyway - this looks like a
possible business oppurtunity for someone with the resources and knowledge
to find the connectors and remanufacture new ones. And please, use the
high temperature PVC insulation and *tined copper* wire. 

I'd gladdly pay a couple of hundred for a well made harness with high 
quality parts. I'm sure others would too.
