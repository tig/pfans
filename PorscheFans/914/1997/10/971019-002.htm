Jason Burkett at Paragon Products in Corpus Christi, Texas helped me with a
nice set up that was not terribly expensive.  He sells a 3" wide 5-point set
up by M&R that features a cam lock closure.  I also purchased a "headache
bar" from him so that I could mount the shoulder part of the harness.  This
bar fits into the two holes left by the stock seatbelt guides.  Pretty nifty
setup and it was relatively ($impson) inexpensive...pretty close to your
price range.
