At 7:47 PM -0500 10/3/97, Jim Sucharski wrote:
 I went to check it out, and it's
>stuck in neutral, and the shiftlever wont move except to take up the
>slack in the linkage. It's apparently locked up in neutral. The clutch
>seems OK. Where do you start on a problem like this. The linkage appears
>to be all right.  We'll tow it home tonight, but then what?
>

First, take a close look at every joint in the shift linkage, including the
one at the base of the shift lever. You want to eliminate that as a problem
before proceeding any further. Make sure you remove the boots and covers
and actually look at the linkage joint itself. If you're certain it is not
the linkage, then it can only be inside the transmission, which would
require removal, disassembly, repair, et al.
