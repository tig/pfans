First, you need some software. Either mIRC for pc's or Ircle for macs. They
both have web sites where you can download them. Once you've installed the
software, you have to pick a server to connect to. Choose one that ends in
....dal.net. Of course, you have to pick a nickname, like BigDave. If your
nick is use by someone else, though, you'll have to change yours. Once
you're on the server, type in /join #914chat, which should make a window
pop up. That's it, you're on.
