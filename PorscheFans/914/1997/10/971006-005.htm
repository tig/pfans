> I have had a bit of time in this area.
> 
> The points are binary, that is they work or don't. There are two of
> them
> and some times after cleaning the are not centered on the shaft. Pull
> the dist. And use a meter to see that they are both firing.
> 
> Check the wires that they plug into, yes they get dirty and break.
> 
> Once you have the wires checked  you can use a low wattage 12v light
> bulb (Flashlight style) to attach to injector plugs to make sure they
> are firing.
> 
> If the injectors are firing, and you KNOW the trottel body switch is
> good and properly adjusted, then I would have the injectors tested.
> 
> Most likely problem areas:
> 
> 1. Points not centered
> 2. Wire cracked or plugs dirty
> 3. Trottel switch dirty or out of adj.
> 4. One bad injector (not clogged just wearing out)
>
