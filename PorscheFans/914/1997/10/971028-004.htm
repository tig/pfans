It sounds like two different similar things to me.  Dirty connections,
pull the fusses and take some 300 grit sandpaper(or so) and some contact
cleaner and clean the end of the fuses and the clips they sit in.   Then
take the cleaner and spray the back of the light switch and turn it back
and forth.  I use lps#1 after that and work it a little more.  What you
are doing is cleaning the wiper and wire wound (?) resister in the
switch.  Of course this is all just conjecture, but these are good
practices on these old cars.  Good luck  Bill S.
