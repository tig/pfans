Okay, I found it -- the black one is:
    Autobooks 713,
    Porsche 914 Owners Workshop Manual
    by Kenneth Ball
    laminated hardback, 158 pgs,
    about the same dimensions as the Clymer book

This one covers the detail a bit different from the Haynes and Clymer. While
I am grossly unqualified to judge the mechanical areas, I have found the
electrical and bodywork sections to be good. The instructions are less terse
than Haynes. The illustrations are sometimes clearer, being line drawings
rather than photos (with occasionally hard to see detail) like used in
Clymer and Haynes.

As always, the best results come from combining two or more of these books.
