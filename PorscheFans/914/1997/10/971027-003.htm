One thing most have seem to forgot, Dirk mentioned it I think.  A
914(901) will not bolt into a VW chassis of any vintage.  You want need
and or desire a 911(901), most of the gearing will work in your
situation, except first is inbetween your current 1-2 combination.  No
big deal, ewxcept on really steep hills.

The 911(901) still has to be modified a little to fit the VW saddle
mount, and small modification to the front of the 911 mount.

Need more info - well I am currently installing one in a Ghia, it has
been a long time since I have worked on a 412.

>Torjus............All the 914s used a 7:31 ring and pinion with the 
>following
>gear sets:
>1st  - 11:34 or 0.32 (A)
>2nd - 18:34 or 0.53 (F)
>3rd - 23:29 or 0.79 (N)
>4th - 27:25 or 1.08 (V)
>5th - 31:22 or 1.41 (ZD)
>Second gear is a fixed gear but can be altered by some of the good 
>trans.
>builders.  The reast of the gears also have some options as to 
>different
>ratio choices.
