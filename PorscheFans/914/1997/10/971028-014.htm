Removing the 12 point hex bolt

I have had to remove tomany of these stinky little bolts and the easyest way
to remove those little stinkers are (1) to get the proper 12 point tool that
fits in to a 1/2" socket so that you can use a ratchet with it. (2)  in stead
of using a ratchet,,,,BUY YOUR SELF A IMPACT WRENCH!!!!!!  The impact wrench
will be a life saver, espeshaly if you are dealing with a old 914 that has
lots of old bolts that havent been broken in many yrs.  By placing the tool
in the 1/2" socket and placing the socket on to the impact wrench,  3 to 4
quick smacks and your bolt is loose.,,,,,though you do want to be carful not
to trash your tranny in the process, but i havnt had any problems with it
yet.
