Pete Wilson signed four bills designed to give some relief from 
Smog Check II inspections to motorists.The bills will go into effect on
Jan. 1,1998.They will:

1) Limit repair bills for low-income motorists ($27,000 and below) to 
$250.00 repair and create repair assitance program.

2) Pay for the low-income assistance program by using $300.00 smog fee 
paid by motorists moving cars into the state and by charging $4.00 
yearly for new cars exempted from inspections.

3) Exempt new cars from smog inspections for first four years.

4) Exempt from smog inspections 1974 and older cars :-) The current 
exemption is for 1966 and older cars.Beginning in 2003,vehicles
that are 30 or more years old will be exempt.

This is good news for alot of 914 owners,including me!
