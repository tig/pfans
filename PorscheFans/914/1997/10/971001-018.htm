Sounds to me like you might need to spend some time cleaning and
re-lubricating your window mechanisms, cleaning (or replacing) the "furry"
channel that the window rides in, and checking over the whole mess for
alignment/adjustment.  When I got my car the passenger side operated pretty
rough.  Major cranking required.  I pulled the armrest stuff and the panel
off and found a pretty dry situation.  Luckily all was fixed with some elbow
grease and some grease grease.  Those pot metal handles won't hold up if
it's dry, out of adjustment, or dirty.
