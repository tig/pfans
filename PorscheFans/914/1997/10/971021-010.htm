> Misfire or Erratic Idle
> - Confirm basic engine, intake, and ignition systems are in good shape
> as
> these are the most likely culprits of misses at idle.
> - Inspect temp sensor I (intake temp sensor ) and temp sensor II (
> cylinders temp sensor).
> - Check trigger points
> - check MPS for leaks from the intake manifold to the sensor and that
> the
> line is not collapsing under vacuum
> - Do a flow test and look at the spray pattern
> 
> If all checks out, you missed something basic, go back and check basic
> engine function.
> 
> Misfires at Cruise
> -- Confirm basic engine, intake, and ignition systems.
> - Preform a Fuel pressure - volume test, (Notice especially if there
> is a
> drop in pressure at the point of the miss fire.  (bad pressure
> regulator
> or fuel pump connections or pump.  Possibly dirt or containments in
> the
> fuel filter.
> - If pressure remains constant check the Temp sensor II ( cylinders
> head
> temp) and the throttle switch.
> - If these do not find the problem begin test procedure again,
> something
> was missed.
> 
> Misfire under load
> -- Confirm basic engine, intake, and ignition systems
> - Preform a Fuel pressure - volume test, (Notice especially if there
> is a
> drop in pressure at the point of the miss fire.  (bad pressure
> regulator
> or fuel pump connections or pump.  Possibly dirt or containments in
> the
> fuel filter.
> - If pressure remains constant check the Temp sensor II ( cylinders
> head
> temp), trigger points, and injectors.
> -if these are good test injection flow.
> - If these do not find the problem begin test procedure again,
> something
> was missed
> 
> Trailer hitching:
> (feels like you have a 17/8 ball on your car and towing a trailer with
> 2"
> hitch.)
> -- Confirm basic engine, intake, and ignition systems
> -Inspect circuits for the MPS sensor, temp sensor II, trigger points
> and
> injectors.
> - If all above test good then do a injector flow test
> - Also test the Throttle position switch.
> - If these do not find the problem begin test procedure again,
> something
> was missed
>
