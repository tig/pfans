The washers that should be used on 914 CV bolts are spiral lock washers
which before installed are concaved. They should be installed with the
convex side facing the head of the bolt. I don't feel that the "triple
square" bolts are any better than the allen bolts; I have always found
that the former are always much easier to strip and much harder, almost
impossible to torque to spec.
