Torjus............All the 914s used a 7:31 ring and pinion with the following
gear sets:
1st  - 11:34 or 0.32 (A)
2nd - 18:34 or 0.53 (F)
3rd - 23:29 or 0.79 (N)
4th - 27:25 or 1.08 (V)
5th - 31:22 or 1.41 (ZD)
Second gear is a fixed gear but can be altered by some of the good trans.
builders.  The reast of the gears also have some options as to different
ratio choices.
