> Does anybody know of a good 914 reference manual other than Haynes?  I
> saw a couple in the catalog, but would like the list opinion.

I really think the most authoritative way to go is to get the factory
manual on Microfiche from Stoddard, and then find a used office
equipment co. for a reader. The fiche costs  about $20, the reader $35
to $50. If you wanted to buy the same manual in hardcopy form from
Porsche, you're looking at $300+. Of course, the Haynes fits in the
trunk a little better.     Jim S.
