For those who've been trying to extract studs. Try this:

Cut a deep slot in the end of the stud and then use a screwdriver, and
see if you can get enough torque to break it free. This of course means
the stud is toast when you're done. How to cut the slot? Well I'd say
use a Dremel. What? You don't have one? Hmm, now _that_ is a problem. :)
