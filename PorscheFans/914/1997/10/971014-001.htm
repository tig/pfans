BTW - remember to put a jack under the motor - transmission junction to
catch it.

1.	disconnect the shift rod
2.	disconnect the positive battery line
3.	disconnect the throttle cable
4.	remove bolts holding brain and place brain on to motor
5.	disconnect and plug with golf tee the fuel lines
6.	disconnect ground strap on top of transmission
7.	unbolt 4 - bolts on motor mount bar & transmission mounts
8.	catch motor, Trans, & exsaust 
9.	have fun

Takes 60 min 1st time, 30 second, 20 from then on.

Dan (in&out in 20)
