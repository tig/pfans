> I believe that there is an adjustable stop behind the pedal. So, would I be
> correct in thinking I should move the stop in further and compensate for
> that when I take up the slack in the cable?

You want pedal down to be just far enough to open the throttle valve
-all the way-. So what I'd recomend, is setting the throttle pedal where
you think you might want it, and then adjusting the cable at the
throttle body. Then go back and see if the throttle has enough room or
if you've pulled the cable too tight.
