JJ -
You state that everything except the electronic ignition
module is new.  Let's assume that all the new parts are
working correctly -- at least nothing got worse when you
put the new stuff in.

The only old and questionable thing left (that I see) is the
Crane Cams module.  The first thing I would do would be to buy a set of
ignition points and install them in place of the electronic module. 
Mechanical points are cheap and should be easy to put in for a quick test. 
If your spark improves, suspect the electronic module.
