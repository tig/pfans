You know there are two things you can check real fast:

1.	Pull the connector to one of the two not firing injectors and
insert a 3 - 6 volt flashlight bulb in line (The voltage to the
injectors is low and has almost no power so you can hold the wires on
the bulb if need be or just wrap the wire around it to make contact.
		Then run the motor. If the bulb flashs you have power.

2.	If you have power pull the injector out (1 nut is all it takes)
and let it spray on a paper towel (with motor running). If you have gas,
check the valve clearance the input may not be opening.
