First of all, I would get some 16" rims. I wish I had. My 7 & 8x15"
Fuchs dont fill up the GT wheel wells enough. Having said that, I
would go with the BFG Comp/TA ZR, to get the 245s in back.I have Yoko
A509s, which I am very satisfied with. 225 is the widest in that tire,
in 15" rim. There is not alot of difference between the 225 and 245.
The biggest difference made will be how far out you can get the
wheels.You need to do some careful measuring to see how long to get
your hub studs, to use with your spacers. I use 20mm spacers in the
rear, and 23mm in front
