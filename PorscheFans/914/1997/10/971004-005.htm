Just tried out 2 new tools that I have to recommend.  After spending =
hours trying double nuts and wrenches, vice grips, and a propane torch, =
I looke through the Sears tool catalog for some way to remove the =
cylinder head studs.  There is a Craftsman stud removal tool that works =
like a charm.  You need about 3/4" of unobstructed access to the stud, =
so it won't work on everything (like exhaust studs).  While paging =
through, I also saw drag link sockets.  Basically a huge screwdriver =
blade on a socket.  The 9/16" one is good for oil relief piston caps, =
the screw that was holding down the triple ground, and other places =
where the biggest screwdriver I could find was way too small.
