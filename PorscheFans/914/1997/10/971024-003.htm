	Craig,

	Of course they are reversible, what I do is swap them side to
side this
	reverses the direction.

	Just follow simple directions:

	1.	Mark the inner side of the shafts with tape or
fingernail
	polish.
	2.	Un-bolt the CV-shafts from the trains and hubs.
	3.	Take the left shaft and move it to the right side and
install
	with marked side to the hub (wheel).
	4.	Repeat for other side (Yes you will need to drop them
both
	before reinstalling one.
	5.	Toque the bolts back to18 # or what ever the factory
setting is.

	What you will get when done is the inner CV will now be on the
outer of
	other side or car. 6 months later you can repeat the procedure
just
	moving the marked side to the inside.

	While I am doing this I usual PACK the joints with Mobil 1 sin
grease. I
	do this by just filling the whole joint and boot with grease.

	I now have 140k on the current set.

	Dan
