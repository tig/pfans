> I am looking for advice on a safety harness. This will be a street /
> autocross car. I want a 5 point set-up. I will be using sseats for now and a
> harness bar.I've seen set ups ranging from $90 to $250 each. I need 2 and
> would like to spend no more than $250 to $300 for the pair. Is the latch type
> of buckle a pain to use? the single button type seems nicer, but they usually
> cost  more. Any suggestions?

Yes they cost more, but they are more convinent to use. And, they're
generally more comfortable. A five point setup however will entail you
drilling a hole in the floor pan to mount the sub strap, and it will not
be at the right position when you use it. Get a double sub strap and
make sure it's LONG. Then you replace the regular lower seat belt bolts,
and use large eye bolts with locking latch mounts (where the belt
connects to the car, not the buckle). This way, teh belts can be removed
without undoing any bolts. And yes, they are perfectly safe. Ultimately
you'll want to get a seat. Try 'em on for size before buying. You want
it to fit right.
