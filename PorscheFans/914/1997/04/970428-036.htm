Paul,

The Pertronix may solve your problem, particularly if it is due to a worn
distributor. In that instance, because the shaft has developed some play,
the point gap and hence ignition timing will vary quite a bit over short
periods. Replacing the points with an optically triggered ignition reduces
the variation in timing caused by shaft wobble. Made all the difference in
the world in my '74 914.
