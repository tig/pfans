If you can't get a little dental mirror in there (with adequate light), you 
can try the "Braille" method:  What you're feeling for is a raised two-digit 
number in the casting.  Each number is about 1/2" high.   One number will be a 
"1", the other either a "7" or a "9."  Once you find the raised numbers, 
telling one from the other is easy.
