>... noticed that the spring-clip
>that retains the pushrod tubes is routed *underneath* the rocker arm

	Just to clarify Dirk's reply:
	The pieces that hold the rocker shafts onto the head have a
notch cut out of one side.  This notch should be pointing downward
when these pieces are installed, and the spring goes into the notches.
I use an Allen wrench (L-shaped) to hook the spring and move it around.
	The downward-pointing part of the spring should push against
the bottom lip of the pushrod tube, holding the tube in place.  It
should not be touching the pushrods once the rocker arms are installed.
