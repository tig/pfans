I got the 300+ psi from a past life installing air conditioning in
houses. I had no idea that it rated that well! The only drawback to
using soft copper would be the ease of which it can be crushed. I used
it on my old Baja Bug in SoCal for the roof mounted cooler and had no
problems. I would just be sure to armor it in wheel wells or anywhere
damage might occur.
