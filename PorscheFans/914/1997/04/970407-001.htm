At 8:04 PM -0800 4/6/97, Gerry O'Brien wrote:
>  Also does anyone know if 2 lt ss exchangers will work on a 1.7 .

	They should, but not with a stock 1.7/1.8 muffler.  You will
need to go to the 2.0 muffler, or one of the aftermarket ones that
fits on the 2.0.  If you find a 2.0 muffler, you'll need the different
muffler hanger thing too.  The aftermarket stuff varies, you may or
may not need the 2.0 hanger.
	Ya see, the main differences are that the 2.0 has a slightly
larger tube diameter (I think) and is straight all the way back, while
the 1.7/1.8 has a bend upward near the back of the pipes.
