I recently was able to solve the ever-present dilemma of how to get more
rubber under stock fenders quite inexpensively. I had a local custom
wheel shop cut the centers out of some stock 914 steels and weld them
into 15x7 rims. The magic numbers I used were 140mm backspacing for the
rears and 130mm backspacing for the fronts. This has allowed me to
comfortably fit 205/50/15's all the way around. They don't look too bad
painted black with chrome beauty rings on them (okay, I'm rationalizing
a little). It only cost me $40 per wheel for the work and these guys did
a beautiful job with a 360 degree weld holding the centers in. If you're
interested and don't know of a good custom wheel shop in your area,
contact Willamette's Custom Wheel Service in Portland, Oregon. Ask for
Chuck to do the work. The number is (503)-232-8141 (I get nothing for
this free plug, by the way). Good luck in making your 914 the way it
should have been in the first place...
