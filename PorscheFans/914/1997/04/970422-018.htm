OK, I've got here my Mark's Handbook with a table of ANSI B36.1.0-1967
"Allowable stress values for temperatures up to 650degF." For B88 annealed
seamless copper water tube, the max psi @ -20->100degF is 6,000, @200degF -
5,500, @300 - 4,750, and @400degF - 3,000. It's the same for the other 
copper tubing - B75 and B68 seamless copper tubing. I don't know what 
they sell in the stores, but this sure is plenty good enough.
