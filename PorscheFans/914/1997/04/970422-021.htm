          The most common set I have seen around me is 140LB springs
          with stock front torsion bars and 21mm front sway bar. So I
          would say getting the 19mm to full stiff is the best way to
          go. I don't think I know of any non track only cars with
          anything stiffer than stock front torsion bars.
