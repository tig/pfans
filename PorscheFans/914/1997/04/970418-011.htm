	I have different info from two different sources now, BTW.  The
Haynes book says the Euro 2.0 needs 98 RON Octane, which works out to
about 92-94 US Pump Octane.  However, I have what appears to be a non-US
owners manual from 74, and it says (I think it was) 94 RON Octane, which
is about 89-90 US Pump Octane.  So it seems likely that the Euro-spec
2.0 (8.0:1 CR) will run fine on mid-grade gasoline, not super.
	BTW, all the US-Spec non-1.7s should run fine on regular.
