If you are after a quieter 914, don't forget the engine lid. That flat
un-damped panel right behind your head resonates something fierce at
higher rpms. I found it added at least 50% of the "hot cam" sound at the
torque peak.

Dynamat might be too heavy, though, I used thin engine felt and was very
surprised by the change.
