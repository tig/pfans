Stock is 7.6 : 1, but I think the big variable here is the 63cc
combustion chamber, and whether or not the deck height is included in
the displacement.  In your initial calculation, you came up with a
difference of 42ccs (1971-1929), which divided by four equals 10.5ccs.
If I "reverse engineer" this number running under the suspicion that
it's the deck height displacement, then I come up with .060 in deck
height on every cylinder.  It seems a little high, but if you've got
..010 under the cylinder and .030 on top, then there's .040.  So, maybe
we're close.  Does anyone out there have the bore and stroke for a 2.0
914?
