I used solid aluminum lines from front to rear on my 914 to get oil up to
the front cooler.  As of yet, I have not had a problem with any leaks. 
Plus the aluminum is a great heat dissapaiter (sp.).  At the end of the
aluminum, I used flared fittings to go to aeroquip lines to the engine and
cooler respectively.
