Celibration 10 years Porsche 914 Club Holland

The Porsche 914 Club Holland proudly announces its tenth anniversary!

This is sufficient reason for us to celibrate ! We intend to do so in the
weekend of 24 & 25 Mey next.

In order to enable our friends from Germany and other European countries to
come to the festivities, we have chosen a site in the Province of Limburg,
namely in the village of Sevenum ( near Venlo).

Recreatiecentrum "De Schatberg" is the perfect place for our happening.Wide
green pastures invite us to bring many of our beloved Porsche 914 cars
together.As we have our disposal a new sprinting track, the chances that we
will bore ourselves are very small indeed !

The picturesque environment are the gently curved streets almost make it
impossible not to participate in the Rally Tour.

The bowling lanes and a homely restaurant guarantee a relaxed atmosphere in
the evening.

Playgrounds and swimming pools will keep the children more than busy all
day.

The large camping site and the nearby facilities to spend the night
cmfortably,offer proper places of rest for all participants.
