I think the real issue here is which conducts heat better, rust or paint? 
If rust conducts better, then forget about the rusty cylinders. If paint 
is better, then paint them before installing them. For what it's worth, 
Bob Hoover says paint the cylinders with rustoleum paint thinned to 50% 
and applied with a thin brush so you can get down between the fins.
