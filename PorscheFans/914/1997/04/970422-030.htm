This is in response to brian ng's 4/21 query: I ran my lines just as you
said- under the R rocker cover, over the front wheel inside the
wheelwell and back down to my bumper mounted cooler. I'm using blue
rubber-like 250psi, high temp hose the whole way. So far no problems. It
dissipates heat quite well and as long as I have it padded near any
metal edges I don't see any risk of it rupturing. I'm also not using a
thermostat. It takes a bit longer to warm up this way but my cooler
seems to be handling it well enough. My tweaked 2.0 used to get as hot
as 235 deg in the smmer when I ran it hard. Now it doesn't go over 190
deg. Hope this helps you (or maybe I'm insane to do it this way?).
