For those interested the calculations of compression ratio and 
displacement (and other automath), the recent issue of Racecar 
Engineering mentioned a web site you might want to visit:

http://devserve.cebaf.gov/~bowling/auto.html

You'll have to convert mm to inches (hint: divide by 25.5) for some of 
the calculations but I tried a few and they seem to be correct.

Oh, on the compression ratio page, you must specify the 'piston top 
volume' as a negative number for domed pistons.

Enjoy!
