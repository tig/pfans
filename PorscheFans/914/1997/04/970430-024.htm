This may be common knowledge to you gearheads already but 
I've just learned this trick this morning so I'll 
pass it along:

Use a can of carb cleaner, spray LIBERALLY around all
the hoses, injectors, manifolds, ... while the engine
is running.  If the idle changes or shuts down, you've
found your vacumn leak.  Do this while
the engine is warm since the cold start valve may
mask the problem.
