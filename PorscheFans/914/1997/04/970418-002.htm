Make sure they are popped open in the full "on" position if you're not 
going to use the thermostat. Pull up on the little lever that the cable 
attaches to until you feel a snap. It'll stay that way in the full open 
position then. You shoudl leave them in unless you have disposed of the 
stock oil cooler. The passenger side has a flap that directs air down 
through the cooler.
