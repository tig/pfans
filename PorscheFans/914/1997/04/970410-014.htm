Some time ago, someone on the list talked about using 944 seat belts. I
recently picked up a pair of belts complete with all hardware from an '86
944 Turbo. The only modifications I had to do was to grind a bit off the
locating pin on the back of the reel, and open up the hole for the pin in
the firewall. Otherwise it was a nobrainer. Works great and looks good.
BTW, the belts cost me $100 complete at my local yard.
