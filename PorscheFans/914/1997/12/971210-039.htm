I did a 5 lug conversion a couple of year back for my 914 race car.  Bought
a complete 911 front end (shocks, brakes, rotors, torsion bars, calipers
and EVERYthing) for $350.00.  I assume if you are in Southern California,
you can find the same deal easily; oh the 911 front is off of an SC.  Just
pop in the front and you have a 5 lug 911 front suspension.  
Rear - bought a pair of early 911 caliper ($200?) and made my own adapter. 
Took the hub to FAT Performance to redrill it into 5 lugs, then pop in (I
forgot which yr) vented rotors and put the calipers on them.  The car stops
on a dime.  But of course, it a race car and I didn't care about the
parking brakes.  I also have Beck body kits, so I can use as wide a
rim/tires as I like.  you can see the car at :
http://members.aol.com/ruf964/Nine14.htm
