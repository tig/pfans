I don't remember details of the front, but the firewall has holes I used
to route my power leads. If you remove the panel behind the seats you
will see a couple of raised round bumps under the felt pad. These bumps
have a hole in the center which is covered by the felt. After youpuch
out the hole, you have to snake the lead to it under the insulation on
the engine side. For that reason, I used a hole close to the top and ran
the lead down to the shift tunnel from there.
