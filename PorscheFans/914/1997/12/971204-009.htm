Garold Shaffer wrote:
> 
> Any tips on how to restore a faded top. Is there some sort of dye or
> something?
Yep, exactly. I found some in Germany called "Frost Schutz" and it looks
like thick liquid shoe polish or thin paint. It even goes on with a foam
applicator, like shoe polish, BUT it's not a wax and it lasts like
paint. It works great on the top, side vinyl and rubber bumpers. 

Recently I saw what looks like the same thing from Griot's Garage. I
don't have the name, but in the description they call it a dye. It is
NOT their Vinyl & Rubber dressing, which I also use.

When you apply it, rub it around with a damp cloth to work it into the
texture and to cut the shine. So applied, it looks just like a brand new
top (or bumper).
