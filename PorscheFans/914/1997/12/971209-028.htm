Yeah,

  Automobile Atlanta has a 5 lug conversion package, and I think it is
somewhere between 1000 and 1500 dollars.  It includes rotors, brake pads, and
everything you need, less the 5 lug rims.  So, if you are going to do it, it
would be finacially beneficial to do it when you need a brake job, that way
you are not replacing parts that dont need to be replaced anyway ( who would
want to throw away a good set of pads or rotors?)
     But that is pretty expensive, and I dont think its worth it cause you
wont get the money back when you sell the car.  If the reason you want to do
it is for a wider selection of better rims, I suggest you look in a VW mag
like VW TRENDS, because in the advertising section, I have seen that they have
really nice 5 star 4 lug rims for like 110 a piece.  They also have  914 2.0
alloys for 85 a piece.
