A late model hub (74-89) can be used on 914 trailing arms even though wheel
bearings in the late models are a different size than 914.  Simply use a
properly dimensioned spacer (fabricated with 4130 chromoly) with the longer
hub.  GP MOTORSPORTS, INC. has the proper spacers for $25/pair.
