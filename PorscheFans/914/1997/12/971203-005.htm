>2- I live in NH ("Live Free Or Die" state) where there are no smog checks.
>Is it relatively easy to de-smog the 1.8? If I do, any downside to
>reliability? Any idea to upside power gains?

	If the car has a smog pump:  Remove the pulley from the middle of
the fan.  Remove the pump and associated tubing and hoses, block off any
holes left by such.  (Two in each head, possibly one on the air cleaner,
maybe more?)
	Buy stainless steel heat exchangers (SSI's) from a private party,
or from GPR (800/321-5432) and a stock 1.7 or 1.8 muffler.  Be careful with
the studs holding the exhaust onto the heads, and with all the exhuast
fasteners in general.  Liquid Wrench is your friend.
	Save all the original parts (including catalytic converter, if
equipped).  They're expensive!
