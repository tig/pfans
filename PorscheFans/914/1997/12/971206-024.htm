> What's the difference between 2.0liter and 1.7liter heat exchangers?

The 1.7 heat exchangers kick up at the rear...other thatn that they are
identical. If you're running 1.7 exchangers, you have to get the 1.7
style exhaust...this might be the only possible area for a performance
compremise, but if there is one, it's probably not significant at non
race velocities. I cleaned/derusted my bursch  thoroughly with naval
jelly, wire brush, sandpaper, etc. and then coated with a VHT enamel,
satin black, three coats, and it's held up fine for about 6 months now.
