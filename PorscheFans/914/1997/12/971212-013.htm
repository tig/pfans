As Dirk said (12-10 posting) POR 15 is an
Isocyanate and is poisonous.  Well, I did
some checking and got an MSDS on the 
major component of POR 15:
4,4'-methylenebis(phenyl isocyanate).
This is not only poisonous but also a 
mutagen, y'know.... 3 legged kids kinda
stuff.  So, as with all chemicals, do not use
without proper personal safety equipment
including: a respirator (that fits YOU) with organic vapor/ 
acid gas canisters, latex gloves, goggles (goggles
today let you ogle tomorrow) and clothing you
don't mind throwing away.  And of course, the all
important.... use in a well ventillated area.
SAFETY FIRST, enjoyment of your project second!!
