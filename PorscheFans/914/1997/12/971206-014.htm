   I think that someone else already posted a better suggestion than
this one, but I would use an air or electric impact wrench.   There's
nothing quite like it for getting nuts off things like alternator
shafts and the like that are difficult or imposible to hold from
turning.

   The drawback is how do you tighten them back up?   There are
torque bars for impact wrenches, but I don't know if I would want to
re-assemble my suspension with an impact wrench... taking it apart is
a piece of cake, though.
