          Hey everyone,

          My name is Nick Cole, age 25, and I live in Bethesda MD a
          suburb of Washington DC. Yes I one of those people inside
          the beltway.  I work for a law firm (getting worse), but am
          not a lawyer (better?). My area of practice is international
          trade ie anti-dumping ITC 337 patent actions etc. I am
          single with no kids.

          The 914 habbit started on a lark. I had a CRX that needed a
          valve job and I was borred with it so I sold it. I say the
          914 on the side of the road and bought it that day because I
          liked the way it drove. It was a blast!! So I ended up with
          73 2.0 which looked good (fresh yellow paint) but had badly
          rusted rockers. I took the car to a good body shop and had
          the rockers repaired to the tune of $1500. Now the car is
          solid I drove it for 4 years. from 98,000-150,000 no
          problems. No FI no nothing. Lots of long trips too. The car
          regular went from DC to NY (twice a month). The winters were
          hard on the car as well as the miles so the engine came out
          and a rebuilt my first motor so far so good. When I spent
          all that time under the car getting the engine out I noticed
          rust form in the rockers around the repaired sections so
          $2,000 more rust was repaired in the rockers (with
          reinforcing clam shells).  No I had paid as much in rust
          repair as the car :). I decided it was time to buy something
          else for the winter. So I added a subaru wagon to the fleet,
          now the nine comes out to play in the sun only.

          The car has been running on the new motor now for 2 years
          now. So I am bored with it and have always wanted to convert
          to a six so when mike wanted to sell a 2.4 the engine I
          decided was ideal for my conversion desires I lept at the
          chance. It turned out to be a great leap. I got a 914-6 oil
          tank fly wheel mounting bar etc from him. so now all I have
          to figure out is how it all goes together. (How does the oil
          tank mount??) Anyway the long term prject is to rebuild the
          2.4 with S pistons E cams and webers. should be good 150-160
          hp I figure. Enough for the 914 anyway. So thats the plan
          and the intro an questions coments or advice is always
          welcome.
          Happy Holidays


          Nick
