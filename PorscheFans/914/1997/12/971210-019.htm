The case makes some difference, alum. T4 is heavier but stronger than the
lighter magnesium T1 case.  The big weight penalty is the T4 fan shroud
which is pretty heavy.  Don't have the book here so going on memory, but in
the T4 to T1 manual I seem to recall seeing the figure of 25 lbs heavier
for the conversion.  On the VW lists they talk about the T4 case as
'bulletproof'.

Don't have any figures for total weight of a T1 and a T4.

You can go nuts with the Berg parts.  A guy from New Zealand who is on the
VintagVW list built an engine from all Berg stuff. About like that 2110cc
engine.  With the money conversion rate and shipping he had about $10k just
in the engine, but it was his dream engine and he was tearing up the roads
in New Zealand.
