space.  I would like to find a good home for them (i.e. I would rather an

Cars have good glass, panels, suspension, seats;  Two transmissions (tail

bought the cars, but I simply don't have the time to make the switch.  The

getting in the accident.

Cars are in Lancaster, CA (in the Mojave Desert north of Los Angeles). If

My price: Best offer, you haul all three cars away.  I am not super
