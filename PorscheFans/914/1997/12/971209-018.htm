          It depends why you are doing the conversion. If it cruising
          all you need is the 4-5 lug adapters. If you want to do
          brakes as well as wheels its a whole other kettle of fish.
          You need to choose calipers rotors maybe even rear swing
          arms. It is probably easier for me to say what I did then
          list all the possiblities.

          Front: 85 911 struts (bilstiens)
                 85 911 Rotors
                 85 911 Calipers
                 All the new bearings and seals assocoated with this
          (which reminds me I still have to find the lugs for Dirk)

          Rear:   85 911 Rotors
                  85 911 calipers
                  85 911 bearings etc.

          (Note the means no emergency brake/parking brake you have to
          go to vellios to get those swing arms to have the 911 style
          park brake. I do note like not having an emergency brake and
          would say don't do this split the 914 rear calipers for the
          vented rotors so you have a parking brake or use 308 rear
          calipers)
