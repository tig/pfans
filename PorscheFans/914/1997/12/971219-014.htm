1. Check the hose between auxilary air regulator and intake air distributor
to see if it is loose, cracked or broken.
Do these below if above was not the problem.
2. Check these things in these order.
Throttle plate not moving freely in throttle body. Spray with carb cleaner
with engine off. Let sit for 30 minutes before cranking. Don't over spray,
about 5 seconds maximum. Sitting will desolve the lacquer build-up on warm
engine.
Check point gap for dirty contacts or gap set incorrectly.
Check spark plug gap.
Check distributor rotor, cam and shaft for excess wear.
Test coil and condenser.
Check timing.

One of these is probably your problem. Not correcting will only get worse.
