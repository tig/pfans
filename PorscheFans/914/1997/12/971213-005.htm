> knock offs (VW offset) with the above  mentioned HR-4's  on a basically

>         What are some good modern tires that work  on these cars.
>         I want performance, not longevity.  So f I'm down to two,
> Dunlop D60A2 in the above size and Michelin MXV4 in above  size.

Oh don't go the Michelin route if you're looking for a performance tire,
at least not the MXV4s. I've driven them, I don't like 'em. In a
185/65-15? I don't know of many if any performance tires that are made
in that size. 

>  I
> really don't care about  size,  except that the  ones on  there are
> already short enough.  I  get 22mph/1000rpm  meaning it allready  screams
> on the highway.  My original   intent was to get Comp  T/A Gen 3 in 205-50,
> but  I  don't know of anyone doing that or how bad  the  lower gearing
> would  be.  This    isn't for racing or just around town.  I do a lot
> of highway stuff too.   Please help.

A 205/50-15 is a good bit shorter than what you are currently on, so
maybe that's out. It would most likely be out anyway as the Mahles and
Rivieras I don't believe have the neccessary backspacing to fit a 205
under stock fenders. I'd say that if you're concerned more with
performance than longevity, then a 195/60-15 Yokohama A509 woudl be the
tire for you. About the same rolling diameter as a 185/65-15 and you
will notice a _considerable_ improvement of those HR4s you've been
running on.
