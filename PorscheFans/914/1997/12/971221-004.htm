At 12:59 AM 12/21/97 -0700, you wrote:
>I hooked up my VDO oil pressure gauge today, and the needle goes to
>maximum and stays there constantly. I ran a wire from the switch, to the
>S terminal, then an accessory feed to the positive terminal, and a
>ground wire to the ground terminal. The "S" terminal is putting out 8
>volts, so it doesnt seem to high. Any thoughts?

Make sure your gauge and sender are "matched". An aftermarket VDO gauge
should be matched the the corresponding aftermarket VDO sending unit. Your
gauge should be part #350040 and the corresponding sending unit should be
#360001 (Single Sender) or #360006 (Dual Sender for idiot light and gauge).
