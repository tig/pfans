I have built five 901 transmissions, two of which have different gear sets in
them.
When you rebuild one you can not just replace the syncro bands, you should
also 
replace the dog teeth and the sliders.  All these parts wear together and
replacing
just one of these parts would not help very much. To replace the dog teeth you
will need a press and a special tool, so you may have to take it to a place
who can do it.  My 2 cents.
