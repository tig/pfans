Hey all,

Dave makes a very good point on overall diagnostics. And Andy is quite
right about the flywheel. But let's not forget the simple fact that
these things are ADJUSTABLE!

There should be a small hex head on the nipple that the little hose fits
on and you can turn that to increase or decrease the "damping" effect it
has on idle return. These little valves are actually quite handy to use
when you DO have a lightened flywheel. You can increase the "damping" to
prevent stalling if you've got a moderately light flywheel.
