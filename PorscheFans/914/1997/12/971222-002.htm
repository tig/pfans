          Hi all,

          Just wanted to share my 914 weekend with people who might
          care. It was a a fantastic day in the washington DC area on
          Sat. 60 degrees and sunny. Time to take the 914 out. Drove
          all over the place through the twisties and all it was
          great. I got home and decided to take some advice. I have
          the 150lb springs in the back and the factory sway  bar. I
          removed the bar and the car felt great. There was a little
          more understeer but not as much as I thought there might be.
          But the big plus was acelerating out of corners. Wheel spin
          was greatly reduced. Also the car seemed much more settled
          in sweeping turns with uneven pavement. The tail tracked
          much better no skipping around. All in all very happy with
          the deletion.

          Nick
