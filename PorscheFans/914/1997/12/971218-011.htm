          Well I try to share my similar experiences with the group I
          don't know what has been tried. because I wasn't following
          the thread in my conversion world. Here goes: I had a
          similar problem and found that the spark plug hole in one of
          my cylinders was screwed up so the plug was not going all
          the way down into the combustion chamber. This caused all
          kinds of problems and I chased it for a couple months
          checking FI and Ignition with no luck. Even changed the
          brain. Did discover the spark problem until the engine was
          dissasembled. This made the engine hard to start & rough
          running until warm then most of the problems went away.

          Next weird problem came from the cylinder head temp sender.
          The engine would run perfectly until warm and then would
          just die. Let it cool off and it started fine. I traced the
          sensor wire through the harness and found some of the
          shielding had worn away and was grounding on the engine tin.
          A wrap with electrical tape and all is  well again. 

          Hope this helps 

          Nick
