Bernie,

I've run the 205/50s. They're great runners but you're speedo will be off
about 10 mph. A good compromise is to run 195/60 or 205/60s in a high
speed rating. That said, if cornering is real high on your priority list
go with the 50 series tires and live with the higher RPM. BTW the lower
profile tire also lowers the car 8-)).
