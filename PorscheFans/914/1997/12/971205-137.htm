Based on mention in a post several weeks ago, I have ordered from amazon.com
and read cover-to-cover "How to Rebuild your Volkswagen Air-Cooled Engine"
by Tom Wilson, ISBN 0895862255.

The book goes into great detail on teardown, assembly, adjustment, and, most
importantly, when not to tear down type-I, II, III, VW IV, and specific 914
type-IV engines.

This is the best $13.00 I've spent in a while!
