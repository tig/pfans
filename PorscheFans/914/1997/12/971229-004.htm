Since I had Friday off, I got out to the garage and did some of
the things I've been putting off for a month or so.  First on the
list was putting the canopy back on my pickup (it hangs in the
garage above the 914 when it's not on the truck.)  Well, gee, then

made it back home, with a silly grin frozen on my face (I didn't

and fresh motivation to get out there and fix all those niggly 
things that keep me from driving it more often.
