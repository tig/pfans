> Hi, I've got a '70 six with the factory steel flares.  I use four 8 x 15 911
> offset factory Fuchs wheels with no clearance problems at all, front or
> rear.  I am using 225/50 street tires.  It is lowered and does not rub at

> Given the clearance on my car I would think that you "might" be able to
> get away with a 9inch in the front, but the bigger problem is the rear.  You
> cannot use a standard 911 offset 9 inch rear wheel with the OEM flares.
