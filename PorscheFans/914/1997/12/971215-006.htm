would recomend it.
 
> Second, the list says don't goop the pushrod tube seals. The author says use

That's a bad one there I guess. I refuse to use RTV unless there's no
other alternative. The idea of little bitty chucks of the stuff getting
sucked into the engine and colgging a bearing oil passage are not things
I like to dwell on.
