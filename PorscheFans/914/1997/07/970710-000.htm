>I,m trying to decide about having my 74 1.8 rebuilt. The engine  has 85K
>miles and shows 120-125-125-125 compression. Doesn't seem to burn much oil.
>Was thinking about Big Bore P/c's but now getting worried about cost v power
>increase and possible problems with the fuel injection. Any thoughts?. 

Since you're in kali-fore-nigh-hey, stick with a stock rebuild. The
injection will function correctly (ie, pass smog) only with a stock
configuration. If it was me, I'd do some upgrades like a counterwieghted
crankshaft, upgraded oil pump, etc, but keep the stock displacement. The FI
also needs the stock valve sizes, since a change there would change the air
flow and effect the flapper box. Sadly, it looks like you're stuck with it
unless you want to spend big money for an approvable (smoggable) transplant.
