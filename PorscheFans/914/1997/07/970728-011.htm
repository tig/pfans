	The 914 factory wiring diagrams (and the copies of those
diagrams in Haynes, among other places) talks about the wires in
terms of the diameter of the wire element, in millimeters.  Most
places in the US only know about the "gauge" of the wire.
	Last October, someone posted a conversion table on the
rec.autos.makers.vw.aircooled newsgroup.  It went something like
this:

	 0.5  mm = 24 AWG
	 0.75 mm = 22 AWG
	 1.0  mm = 20 AWG
	 1.5  mm = 18 AWG
	 2.5  mm = 16 AWG
	 4.0  mm = 14 AWG
	 6.0  mm = 12 AWG
	35.0  mm = 4-6 AWG


	I don't know if these numbers are correct, though.  Can
anyone help out with this question?
	Thanks!
