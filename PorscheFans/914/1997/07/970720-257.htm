SB 42 (KOPP) makes any car 25 years or older smog exempt forever, with a
rolling exemption each year. It has passed the Senate with flying colors,
almost unanimously, and the Assembly Transportation Committee 11-0. However,
the appropriations committee has become a solid roadblock. Since the state
will be missing the $8 smog certificate fee, they feel they would be losing
millions of dollars (you bet). The bill was tabled on Wednesday (7/16) and
will die in committee if not out by 8/30. It has rescheduled for 8/28 or 29. 

We need to swamp the appropriations committee with telephone calls in favor
of this bill. Please call and make your voice heard.

names/numbers of committee members if you wish.
