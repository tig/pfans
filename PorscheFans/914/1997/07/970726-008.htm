James, that's not a glitch. That's a built-in FEATURE! I know, it's
annoying sometimes, but it really is a feature. Many european cars have
it. The idea is if you park on the side of a narrow street or country
road, you flick the turn signal lever to illuminate the lights on the
street side of the car. That way, other cars are less likely to
sideswipe you in the dark. Really, I'm not joking. No, really, I'm
serious. Come on, I mean it. Really...
