I use Plastikote engine enamel in the appropriate color (I'm not too
picky
about the exact shade of silver, etc.).  This paint will dry to the
touch
almost as quickly as lacquer, but for real durability you need to bake
it. 
I let it air dry for about 1/2 hour, then put it in the oven at 300
degrees
for an hour.  Just to satisfy myself that I have enough paint on it, I
do
this three times.  I try to do most of it when my wife is at work, then
stay out of the house myself while it cooks, 'cause it don't smell like
bread baking.  The smell only lasts for about 15 or 20 minutes each
time.
