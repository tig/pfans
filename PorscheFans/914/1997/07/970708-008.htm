Here is a reminder of the upcomming 914 rally on Table Rock Lake in Branson,
Mo.  All 914 fans are welcome whether you are a club member or not.  The
following is a copy of the posting from the Porsche 914 Club's Event page for
this years Mid-West Renegade festival.

The Porsche 914 Club's
Third Annual
Midwest Renegade Festival

The Porsche 914 Club will have it's third annual Mid-West Renegade Festival
in Branson, MO Sept. 11-14, 1997.  The facility for this event is the Rock
Lane Resort on Indian Point Rd, in Branson.  There is a block of 25 rooms
reserved for the Club with a special rate of $63 per night, one and two
bedroom condos are also available at different rates, be sure to say you're
with the 914 Club when making reservations.  Rooms should be reserved before
July 1, after that rooms may not be available.
Included in the registration fee of $70 per person are; registration mixer,
TSD rally on Friday, Saturday continental breakfast, Saturday night banquet
(which will be on the Ozark Mountain Zephyr train), Sunday breakfast buffet,
a tech session and quiz, special event T-shirt, door prizes, awards, and
more.  There is a late registration fee of $10 to encourage early
registration, and non-Club Members add $5 to the registration fee.  Meal only
fees are available for non-participants, call for more info.
Make your check or money order payable to: Porsche 914 Club

Send your registration fee to:
Mid-West Renegade Festival
c/o Diane Johnson
7905 Steppington Dr.
Plano, TX 75025
PH# (972) 517-1167 

I hope some of you can find the time to join this gathering of fellow 914ers.
 The Table Rock Lake area is part of the Diamond Lakes region and is a
wonderful recreation lake.  From fishing, swimming, jet-skiing, sailing
aboard the Lodges catamaran (capable of holding 40 people) to Silver Dollar
City or the famous Branson night life, this area can provide something for
everyone.
Feel free to call if you have questions.
