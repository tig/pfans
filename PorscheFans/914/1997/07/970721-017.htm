Easy and cheap !

Go to dealer and order the Factory manuals on Microfiche  ( they may have 
to look up the number) or call PCNA in Reno via 800-545-8039 and ask for 
the Parts people they will give you the part number. Cost is about $25 +/- 
$10. Then Go to local library store or office supply group for A/V 
equipment and buy a used microfiche viewer with 42X lenses  cost should be 
in the under $50. And your have your manual.

If anybody has a fiche scanner I have both the 44 & 14 shop manuals.
Or I might be persuaded to make them available on CD- for windows users
