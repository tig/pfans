Hi Dirk, I also do a lot of work in the heat, I'm in south east PA and we're
having the same stinking humidity that you guys have.  The heat index
yesterday was 104degrees, and I worked out in the garage from 7:45am
to 4:30pm with a lunch break only.

I have the fortunate position of working in a garage so I'm out of the sun,
but it gets plenty hot in the garage too.  I know it's a bad when the sweat
keeps dripping down on what I'm working on.  

What I have done is to buy one of those commercial type high volume fans
for the garage & outside.  It is still hot but the fan pointed on you will
make 
it somewhat more bearable.  We're not talking a cheap $19.00 fan but believe
me $50 or $60 is worth it.    

Drink a lot of water while you are working, and tie a wet towel around your
neck.  

Take care/keep cool.
