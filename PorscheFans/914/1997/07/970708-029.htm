Bill, I read your post about 944T prices. I have an 86 944T and can only
say... never buy one without service records. 86 Turbo prices seem to be
depressed right now as mine has complete records and thousands of
dollars of maintenance done, ie. belts, clutch, alternator, radiator
etc... and my hope is to sell it in the New Orleans market for $8000.
Now you can get a decent 83-85 944 for under 5k, the only thing is the
Turbo is like an amusement park ride and the regular is like my wife's
Honda... (no offense to the non-turbo guys out there.) A final thing to
remember is that a 5K Porsche is still a Porsche and unless you're doing
the maintenance yourself buy the best you can afford otherwise you'll
spend your inheritance correcting the previous owners mistakes.
