Mig welding on MGB

Use mild steel wire .025 dia.with tri-mix gas.(Yes,yes, I know that's
only supposed to be for stainless but it works like a dream on
mild).Tack weld every 4 inches or so cooling with compressed air(best)
or very wet cloth(good) as you go,then fill in with solid weld.
Forget the air-chisel! Drill your spot welds out with a solid carbide
purpose built thingy.Eisengefs (see Hemmings), sell the bees's knees and
vicar's knickers in this department.
