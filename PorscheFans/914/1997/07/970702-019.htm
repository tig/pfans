For 911 engines and 40IDA3C carbs, PMO recommends an
isolator much as you described.  This is to help prevent too
much heat transfer to the carbs which can result in boiling
the fuel in the float bowls.  Another common mod to the carbs
is to drill vent holes to allow vapors to escape.  PMO sells
a special jig for your drill to get the correct angle.

This isolator was used in addition to a gasket.
