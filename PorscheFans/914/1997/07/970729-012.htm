hey everyone,

After I finished the 5 bolt conversion I have been driving the car a
lot.
The engine is new with about 3,000 miles on it. I noticed the oil light
was
not coming on when the ignition was on but engine not running. Bad bulb
no
bulb works fine, so i figure bad sensor. Well I was driving on the hiway
at
about 3200 rpms last night and started to coast the oil light flickered
on
and off. as soon as the engine past 2700 rpms it went out. I tried it
again
the oil light would not come on so I started cruisin again. I had to
coast
and the light went on again. This time I did something dumb. I stepped
on
the gas and the light went out. So you need to coast down from over
3000rpms of 5min driving to get the oil light  to come on. it will go
off
at around 2700rpms coasting down  in gear o if you step on the gas and
finally there is no light on when the ignition is on and engine is not
running. Oh also there is a higher pressure oil pump installed.
