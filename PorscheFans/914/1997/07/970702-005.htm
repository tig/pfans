Having driven both, and currently owning both, I go for the 914 as a track car, for the following reasons:
1. Stock 944 class is very full
2. Cost to repair on 944 is a lot more than 914
3. If you bend body on 914, you can take all the good stuff off and swap to a new roller (Body) cheaply
4. You can do all the work on a 914 yourself with over the counter tools. (The belt adj. Tool for 944 is $350)
5. You can put 911 motor in 914 as big as your pocket book will buy, if you Feel the need for speed.
6. It is easy to tow (Flat tow is workable)

That's my $.02, plus we can always use another J stock racer
