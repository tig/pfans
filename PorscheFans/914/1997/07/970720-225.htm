The brake venting being discussed is for the rear calipers on a 914, only.
 The venting is mechanically adjustable because the emergency brake on a 914
moves the pads in the caliper.  The vening adjustment is an initial
adjustment.  Once set, the auto adjuster takes over for the life of the pads,
unless you remove them for some reason and have to reajust.
