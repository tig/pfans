I'm not an expert, but if this 914 was still fuel injected, it sounds for
all the world like one of the fuel injectors quit working.  I used to drive
a Honda Prelude SI (fuel injected) and one day, one of the injectors quit
working.  At idle, it was rough, but when trying to accellerate, it shook
something fierce.  Running an FI car out of gas might cause one of the
injectors to suck in something that would clog it.  You might be able to
flush the injector (pull the connector from each injector in turn to
determine which one isn't working) or you may have to replace the injector
($45 - $60 each US dollars).

Just my opinion.
