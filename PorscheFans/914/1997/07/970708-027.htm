John,

For about $275 H&H Specialties in MA has a great tow bar.  3/8" x 2 1/2"
stamped steel (stores in a bag in 10 minutes if you'd like), hardware
and mounting plates for your '14 included.  Uses a Fulton coupler, class
2, 2" ball.  You have to drill through your pan under the spare for the
mounting plates.  My car is slightly lowered, but I don't have to remove
the brackets for driving (street or track, but look out for potholes). 
Attaches to car with clevis pins and clears the front valence.

Maiden voyage over the 4th to the Glen: over 1000 miles at 75 mph and
better, no sweat.
