If you're ditching the fuel injection, then you can use the compufire coil
pack ignition for about 200$. You'll need a 009 distributor, also, for
about 45$. The compufire replaces the cap, rotor, coil, wires and points.
It has two coils on a slab with an umbilical to a circular part that mounts
on the distributor. Each coil has two ignition wires coming off of it (2
coils total). This is what I'm putting into my 914 someday.

0.050" plug gap - whoeee!!
