You forgot 15x6.5" wheels, the driver suit, spares (CV joint, wheel bearings,
FI parts, gaskets, etc.).  Oh, you'll also need an external oil cooler for a
2.0-$400 to $450 from BAT.  Also need a GOOD exhaust system, $600 or so.  

Numbers and class stickers-$50.

You also forgot plastic bushings for the suspension, $100, and monoballs for
the upper front strut mounts, $175.  After insalling all of those suspension
pieces, that car needs to be realigned and corner balanced, $200. How about
rain tires and wheels?  Do you have sway bars?  Do you have a trailer to haul
it?  Spare gas cans (you don't want to pay track prices for gas).

Don't forget the license fees, entry fees ($250 per race), your race physical
every two years, hotel and travel expenses.
