Switching over to the threaded collar adjustable perches is not a cheap
affair. Yes, it only fits Koni shocks, and it requires specially sized
springs. Unless you plan on prepping the car for competition and need to
corner balance the car, the perches are essentially a cash-extraction
device. It will be _alot_ cheaper to simply replace your worn out rear
springs. Why do you need more rake? How much rake do you already have?
And what is your ride-height now? Measured from the bottom edge of the
front and rear corners of the rocker pannel.
