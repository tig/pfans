Okay the thingy in the footwell passenger side is the OGR / CAT control
thingy.  It is a must have on california emissions equiped cars.  Can you
move it, hey anything is possible.  It connects way back in the back and
also to those lovely extra lights you have on the dash, each side of the
tach.  Is it worth moving, don't know.  

You may want to measure out a bazzoka tube with built in amp 6" unit and
tuck it infront of the passengers seat.  I like Brians woffer box, it is
a great idea.  But if you can not relocate this control, then that gives
you an alternative.
