In 1970, the 2.2 liter 911 had just come out, and the factory made racing
versions.  In fact the preparation instructions are in the same manual as the
ones for the 914/6 GT.  The early GT's ran in Group 4, and after 1000 were
produced ran in Group 3, I THINK!!  911s ran in Group 2 and Group 3; special
911S'  ran in Group 4, I believe.  I don't know if there were subclasses by
displacement or not.

There may have been some 2.0 liter 911s there, which should have been in the
same class.  If the #40 Sonauto GT beat any of the 2.2 liters, I don't know.
