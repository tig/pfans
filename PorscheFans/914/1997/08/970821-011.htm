I beat the hell out of those when taking my rusted to death parts (first
914 purchase) car apart.  Then found the problem.  Those are nuts welded to
the pedal assembly for the master cylinder.  You have to go under the car,
remove the steering rack protection plate then unbolt the MC mounting bolts
from that side.
