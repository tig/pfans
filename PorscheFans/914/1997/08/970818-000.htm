As I recall when a friend helped me do this, we used a large pipe wrench and
a large crescent wrench. We attached the pipe wrench to the end of the
torsion bar, and attached the crescent to the end of the pipe wrench at a 90
degree angle. Pushing on the crescent wrench made the torsion bar move, then
we used a large screwdriver to pry the bar over so that it engaged the
roller. It's a two person job, unless you have that special tool that a list
member came up with a while back.
