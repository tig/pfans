Car Guys  (AKA Keenly) do a very nice job, they draw from PCA instructors 
in the area and they are run well. They are not cheap but you get what you 
pay for.

I have taught for them and they are a good place to go.

If you are in the PA-MD-VA area you might try BSR's FATT program ( it runs 
once or twice a month on Fridays (Friday at the track).

The Potomac Drivers Ed program is one of the best at 1/2 to 1/3 the other 
for profit programs, in many cases the same instructors.
