        If your engine will not stop running when you turn the key to the
"off" position and only stop when you turn your lights off it is a bad
headlight switch. A bad headlight switch will sometimes "back-feed" power to
the ignition switch. It is a semi-common problem that is easily repaired
with a new or good used switch.
