Steve:

I can't talk about the fog lights, but changing the bulb might work, but
you should look for those trick high-output bulbs that give you the
light of a 80-100watt bulb with the heat and power drain of a standard
55W bulb. Otherwise you should probably also install a relay to take the
current load off of your headlight switch.

I found, though that by far the biggest gain was made by replacing the
old (Cibie Z) halogen headlight units with new ones. Just a little
pitting on the lens and some fogging of the reflector must have cut
light output by 50%. Do it and you'll wonder where the yellow went.
