Clark, my personal opinion is STAY AWAY from Falkens.  I put a set on an
87 Prelude a few years back and hated them.  Poor traction and within 3
months they started to develop irregularities across the tread and
sidewalls, making them impossible to balance and very annoying to drive
on.  I still like the Yokohama A-509s for street use.  They're sticky,
look good, and are only about $70 each.
