I put a V8 in mine.  You had better have lots of time and hair(to pull
out).  I'm not the greatest mechanic which I'm sure didn't help and I
did it on the cheap.
I would reccomend a small v8, I used a 283.  The selection of parts for
a chevy is the best and the least expensive.  They are also bulletproof
engines.  I went for a smaller displacement because I could get higher
revs and I didn't have to beef up the rear swing arms.  I can do 0-60 in
second gear in under 5 sec.  Let me know when you are ready to start and
I will tell you more of what I did if you like.
