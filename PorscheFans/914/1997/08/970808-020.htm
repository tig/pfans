I rolled my fenders a while ago to clear 220 55 390's.  I used a pair of
channel locks pliers and a piece of 1x2.  I put the 1x2 (pine) on the
outside and got the steel edge of the fender in a notch of the plier
jaws and turned it up. A little at a time all the way across.  It is
almost unnoticeable from the outside.  Hope this helps.  Bill S.
