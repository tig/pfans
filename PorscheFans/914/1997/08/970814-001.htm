> 1. I need a manual bad! Where can I get the best?

Two choices really. Factory workshop manual, about $350-$400 for a full
set. Haynes, about $10-20 depending opn who you buy it from. The Haynes
will cover all models.
 
> 2. My first tinkering was to figure out why the passenger door won't open
> from the outside. After taking apart the good door, I figured out I need a
> little round white plastic thing with two flat pivoting legs.

Easy, it's a cheap part available from most any P-car supply house.
Automotion, GPR, Performance Products, High Performance House,
Automobile Atalanta, Otto's, all of which are very well stocked parts
houses for us orphan P-car lovers.

lessons learned and all that...
