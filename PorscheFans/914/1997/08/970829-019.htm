Dave,

Take your socket to a bench grinder and smallify it's OD.  Make sure that
it is indeed an 8mm nut/bolt first.  Just drop an 8mm nut on top of the nut
you're trying to identify.  

Your A/Prepared (lightened, higher performance :) socket will still work
fine for 98% of 8mm stuff.  If you ever break it, then buy another one.  Or
if you're paranoid, go buy an 8mm socket from Sears and modify that.
