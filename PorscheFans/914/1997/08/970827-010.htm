Hey everyone,

The 914 has finished being upgraded to carrera spec brakes. In the end I
decided to go the vellios route and use his swing arms for the parking
brake. Any way, I went the activities around pebble beach and had a
great
time. I come back and can see the rotors through the fuchs are rusty
from
lack of use. I go for a 20-30 min around town drive and the front rotors
nice have a nice shine rears a dull one. I closer look ad the rears are
warm not hot and have some rust  left on them. I jack of the car and I
can't turn the rear wheels with even light pressure on the brake peddle.
So
am I being paranoid or is something wrong. Oh, I better descrbe the
brake
set up:

Carrera calipers front & rear

19mm master cylinder.

no proportioning valve just a T junction (This was done in college when
the
valve started leaking so I replaced it with the T junction only per AA
advice)

New fluid that when bleed seemed as memory serves to come out as well in
the rear as the front.

Nick
