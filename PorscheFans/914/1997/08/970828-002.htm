I have 2 BMW's, both with ATE brakes, just like the 914. However, the BMW's
have a nice low brake fluid warning light setup that the 914 doesn't have.
The BMW setup includes a float and a cap with a switch in it. When the float
goes down, the switch is actuated. I'm going to retrofit one of these into
my 914. The parts you need from any old BMW 2002 or bavaria are the float
and screen assembly, and the cap. You can also buy new ones from a BMW
dealer. One of those "all german car" mail order places like Mesa
Performance can probably help out too. Then I'm going to rig up a buzzer (or
maybe a small light) as a warning indicator.
