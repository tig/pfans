	Walker's right, Hoosiers do run wide. But a 225 might possibly fit on a
15x7 with 4 7/8 backspacing I think. 5 inch plus will not work, but
adding an appropriately sized spacer could do the trick. I managed to
squeeze 225/50-14 BFGs on 14x6 steel wheels (I happen to have 'em) under
the rear a while back, but that was _really_ tight. Less than 1/4 inch
for either fender wall. Anything more than a 7 inch wheel and forget it.
