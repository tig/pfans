Dion et al,

The 009 does not have a vacuum advance/retard.  The points breaker plate
does not move relative to the distributor housing.  Therefore, no clearance
mods need to be made to allow the Petronix unit to move back and forth. 
The stock distributor *does* require clearancing to fit the Pertronix unit.
