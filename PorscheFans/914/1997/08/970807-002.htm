tribulations, here's the latest:

temp sensor and the harness.  This took care of the lean-running problem
and made starting the car much easier.  I ordered a new sensor and will
install it next week when it gets here (along with new FI seals,
mainfold hoses, trigger points, contact points, condensor, etc.).

2.  My fuel pressure gauge should be here today, but I probably won't
have time to install it until tomorrow night - we've got our monthly PCA

3.  The car starts up fine, runs fine (still idles a little high -

sits for 15 minutes and I turn the key on and off several times to pump
fuel, it will start just fine and run for a while again.  Then, all of a
sudden, it lean-hunts about 3 times then craps out again.  Will a fuel
pressure gauge give me any indication if vapor-lock is occurring?  Maybe
I just don't have enough pressure in the lines.  Hopefully the gauge
will make this clear.

this or am I screwed on this count?
