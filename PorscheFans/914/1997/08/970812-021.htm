Most Dot 5 is silicone based and great for sub-zero temperatures where
water in the glycol kind might freeze. To use it, you must flush your
system completely of the glycol. Most Porsche experts do not recommend
it. Silicon makes it harder to get a firm pedal when bleeding because it
absorbs air bubbles.

Some exotic high-grade fluid is rated DOT 5, but glycol based. It's
interchangeable, but expensive.
