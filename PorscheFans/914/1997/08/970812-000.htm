The Porsche 914 Club's
Third Annual Midwest Renegade Festival 

Hay everyone.............August is here with September just around the
corner.  Anyone with interrest in attending this years rally should contact
Diane via e-mail or at the phone number listed below.  We have 30 plus 914ers
registered and would encourage anyone interrested to make their plans as soon
as possible.  There are plenty of rooms and condos still available. 
This is the third annual Mid-West Renegade Festival.  Our site is in Branson,
MO and the dates for the event are Sept. 11-14, 1997. The facility for this
event is the Rock Lane Resort on Indian Point Rd, in Branson.  This location
is right on Table Rock Lake.  The resort has all the outdoor amenities for
fishing, sailing, boating and tennis.  Golfers bring your clubs and schedule
yourselves for a T-time at one of several local sites. There is a block of 25
rooms reserved for the Club with a special rate of $63 per night. One and two
bedroom condos are also available at different rates, be sure to say you're
with the 914 Club when making reservations. 

Included in the registration fee of $70 per person are: Registration mixer,
Gimmick rally on Friday (great scenery and roads). Saturday continental
breakfast, "top-only" concour, tech. quiz and group tech. session, evening
banquet which will be on the Ozark Mountain Zephyr train. Sunday breakfast
buffet and awards meeting. We will have a special event T-shirt, many door
prizes, awards, and more.
There is a late registration fee of $10 to encourage early registration, and
non-Club Members add $5 to the registration fee. Meal only fees are available
for non-participants, call for more info.

We encourage everyone that has 914 interrests to come to the event. Car or
not, it will be fun, a great place to see some incredible 914s (4s, 6s and
8s) and meet some outstanding 914 fanatics from the Mid-West all over the
world.

If you are mailing your registration in please make your check or money order
payable to: Porsche 914 Club.
