Stephen,

Believe it or not, last week I picked up a Pertronix unit from the good ol'
boys at Summit Racing for my little flat four.  I actually find a lot of
stuff there for my car that they don't advertise in their Mustang/Camero-
heaven catalog.  The guys are pretty cool about it too, and are usually as
surprised as I am when they find these things in stock.  Plus they are
located less than ten minutes from my house.

Anyway, I installed the Pertronix unit with NO mods to my 009 distributor.
I kept looking for the 'grinding' portion I've always read about, but the
thing fit in there exactly like it was supposed to.  And like you, I
haven't either had the thing long enough, or couldn't feel any noticeable
difference in the performance of the engine.  But it is nice not worrying
about the points and condensor anymore!
