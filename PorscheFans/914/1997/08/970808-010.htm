>I need a little more height in the rear of my 74, and I dont want to
>lower the front. I was wondering if anyone knew of some spring height
>adjusters. I saw some in the Automotion catalog (pg. 10), but it said
>for Koni shocks only! I have KYB's, but I thought they were the same
>shape. I need about 1" more rake. I may go with the Koni shocks if I
>cant find any adjusters for the KYB's.

Your rear springs may very well be sagging. Replacement with new springs may
give you your inch back. The adjustable spring perch kit you've seen isn't
really made for your KYB units. The standard Koni units have a 3 position
ride height feature on them that would probably meet your needs rather than
going with the adjustable spring perch kit.  The kit is great for corner
weighting and more precise ride height adjustments, but I don't think you'll
probably need it in your situation.
