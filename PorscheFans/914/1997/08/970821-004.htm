At 10:42 AM 8/21/97 -0500, Craig Zaplatynsky-CCZ001 wrote:
>I know that collectively this list has a wealth of knowledge.  So that's why 
>I'm asking, for a strictly STREET set up will I see any handling improvement 
>by adding a factory sway bar to the rear only.   Will I only see an 
>improvement if I mount bars both front and rear ? 

The rear bar requires that a bracket be welded to the trunk floor. The
bracket is available from Stoddard. You also need different shock mounting
bolts that have a ball on the end.

I would not ever use just one bar. On a 914, you can get away with just a
front bar, but a rear bar alone would make the handling really weird. It's
worth it to pay someone to install the front and rear bars correctly.
