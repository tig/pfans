Earlier, Andrew (AEK@netcom.com) touted the advantages of
"black zinc" plating rotors.  And it made a lot of sense
to me.  The plating was blasted off instantly where the
brake pads made contact (which is a GOOD thing), but stayed
forever on the other parts (another good thing).

Cadmium is hard enough that you would have to selectivly
plate the rotors, and masking adds to the cost.
