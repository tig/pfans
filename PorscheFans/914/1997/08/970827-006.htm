Put the outputs into a 2 channel scope and then you can get some decent
information. (the ecu fires the injectors in pairs, so only a 2 channel
scope is required). Put the head temp sensor next to your soldering iron,
and the other two sensors in your hot cup of coffee. Take the spring off of
the throttle body and move it open and closed, and then take the manifold
pressure sensor and hook it up to your vacuum cleaner.
