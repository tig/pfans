I just painted my rear calipers with Silver Hammerite (spray) when I
rebuilt them and reinstalled with new pads. I painted the inside of the
disk (the hub) with black hammerite (brush). I then re-painted my gold
empi's eight spokes with black hammerite. Then I steel wooled the outer
"shiny" edge of the wheels. Looks Great!

Brake fluid will eat thru the hammerite if you get any drips while
bleeding, but if your careful it works great. I don't know if they have a
red--I know they have a brown/gold
