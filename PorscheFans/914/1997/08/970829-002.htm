I have been designing a replacement ECU for the D-Jetronic system.  The
original manifolding with throttle body and injectors will be used. The
pressure sensor will be replaced with a solid state MAP sensor.  A
modern resistive TPS will replace the throttle position sensor. The
system will be adjustable via a hand-held programmer or a PC with a
serial port.  The total system will be much smaller and lighter than the
original system.  It will also include fuel pump drive, and onboard
diagnostics.

As an option a direct fire ignition (no rotor) with adjustable timing as
a function of RPM,manifold pressure, and temperature is integral to
the ECU.  EFI points will not be required. Distributor modifications
will be done with a kit to lock the advance and replace the points with
non-contact sensors.

A prototype is nearly complete.  Firmware programming is in progress.

Is there any interest?  Right now it is a hobby thing.
