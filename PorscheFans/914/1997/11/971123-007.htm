I have seen this done. You need a complete 911 column and a 914 column,
cut the mounting brakets off both columns, weld the 914 brakets onto the
911 column. cut hole in dash for stock 911 ignition switch. figure out
wiring problems and your done....instant 914-6 column.

I really wanted to do this on my 914-6 conversion, but dont think I
will, just because of the cost.

Good luck,=20
