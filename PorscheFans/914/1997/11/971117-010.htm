I think its a good idea to check/clean the strainer every 2 or 3 oil
changes.  If the screen ever gets REALLY gummed of with stuff, it will
restrict your oil flow.  But more importantly, this is where big things
like chunks of disintegrating cam bearings and such will end up.  If you
never check it, you'll never know if something bad is happening inside
your engine.
