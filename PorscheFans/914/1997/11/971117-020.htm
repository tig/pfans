At 6:09 PM -0000 11/16/97, Todd Holyoak wrote:
>I am unable to open the rear trunk on my '72 914.  Pushing in the rear
>button on the latch doesn't seem to do anything.  i understand this is a
>fairly common problem. Is there some way I can get into the trunk in order
>to then repair the latch.

	The easiest things to do:
	From the engine compartment, push aside both of the plastic covers
that the targa top latches go into.  Shine a flashlight into one of them so
you can see, and use a socket on a LOOOOOOOONG set of extensions to unbolt
the latch from the body.
	Another option is to use a long piece of stiff wire or rod to push
on the catch to release the pin on the hood.

	Anybody have other suggestions?
