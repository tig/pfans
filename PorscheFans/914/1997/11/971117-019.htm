I've been reading on your cold stalling problem and it seems that your aux air
regulator is not the problem.  Have you tried testing the Temperature Sensor II
yet (the one that is mounted into the head near the #3 spark plug.  It is very
important in determining mixture depending on head temperature.  It changes
resistance from 2000 to 3000 ohms when cold to something less than 1000 ohms
when hot.

If you don't already have it - absolutely positively go to the following url
and print out Kjell Nelin's D-Jetronic Fuel Injection for the Porsche 914.  It
is a must have.

http://www.estinc.com/porsche/djet.html

Good luck!
