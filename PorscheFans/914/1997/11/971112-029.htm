I ran a set of KYBs on the rear of my 68' 911 for about 2-3 years. 
Worked just fine, it was a champ autocross car (seriously, I/it was 1990
Florida state SCCA champ for A Stock).  I tried Konis, but the damn
things kept developing thumps and nonlinear action; I disassembled one
and discovered some seal had disintegrated and plugged the port passages
(small fluid holes in some chunk of shock guts).  I tried rebuilding em
and that got worse, so I junked em & got away from Konis, went to KYBs.

I have a set of KYBs in my attic for 914 if ya wanna try em, cheap.  I'm
using Bilsteins on my 914s cause they have adjustable height spring
perches.
