>          I called a couple friends and AA and Trans Log about the
>          electric heaters in the rockers. I have got the following
>          responses. The heaters are designed for use in a VW bug or
>          Bus. There has to be some modifying done to the heat tubes
>          in the 914 to make them work. How hard is it. AA says its a
>          'pain', but of course they don't carry it :). The opinion of
>          the person who did is not hard, but tim consuming. Nobody
>          seemed to remeber a brand name or a phone number, but said
>          look in air cooled VW mags and you will find it. As for
>          effectiveness, you get instant heat like with heat
>          exchangers, they don't seem to overload the electric system,
>          the smell is diminished, but not gone because the location
>          of the fan pick for the heat in the engine. Also my friends
>          914 the main engine cooling fan is still used to blow air
>          over the heaters in the tubes so the heating system works
>          exactly the same as in a stock 914 but with a different
>          source for heat. My friends next idea is to use two electric
>          fans inside the car at the other end of the heater tubes to
>          suck the air in and a different air intake location for the
>          tubes.
