> << What is the best source for a mounting kit?
> -RJ- I'm working on a variation of the centermount (may be able to use your
> help here).  There are several other "cross-bar" mounts available that work
> fairly well for most projects.  I prefer the center mount as it eliminated
> any interferrence, or flex  problems.  It is also the closest to the factory
> mount and produces a very clean finished product.  The only down side is it
> will also be the most expensive when compared to the others.  Up side is the
> front mount from your S can be used to make the centermount which will help
> in cost.
> 
> << Does any one know if the oil tank from the 73 S will fit the 914?
> -RJ- The tank will not fit as is.  I have heard that it can be cut a modified
> to work but the rub is it doesn't line up with the factory mounting points,
> will require the internal tank baffles and line attachments to be altered.
> A suggestion would be to consider a factory tank.  They are available and can
> use the S oil filter console and filler neck.  I may know of a used one.
> 
> << Will the S brakes fit the 914 (72)?
> -RJ- Perfectly.  The only change you "may" have to make is the lower ball
> joint.  I think your "S" strut will have a wedge pin attachment and I'm not
> sure whether your '72 914 has this style or the earlier pinch type ball
> joint.
> 
> -RJ- Dave C. mentioned the rear 5 lug hub conversion.  Your right, the 911
