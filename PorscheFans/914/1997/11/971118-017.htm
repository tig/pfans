When you replace the strainer, replace the gasket.  This is a special =
sealing washer that sits under the bolt head.  The washer is round, like =
an 0-ring, but squishes flat when it's installed.  If you don't replace =
this gasket, you may be tempted to over-torque the bolt to fix that oil =
drip, and actually destroy the engine.
