Everything on the motor is VW, as far as I know, except the heads. The first
thing you need to do is replace all of your vacuum hoses. Also check your
fuel lines. Any good auto parts store should have the hose by the running
foot (meter in your case). That hose that keeps falling off is really
important, since it goes to the manifold pressure sensor (You guys don't
have the decel valve like we have here in the states). Once you have all of
your vacuum leaks taken care of, and if you still have problems, then start
checking your electrical grounds and fuel injection wiring. There could any
number of things causing this problem.
