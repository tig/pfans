Make a new bracket or get a new one.  Once the metal has been pulled and
straightened it will not have the same strength as it did originally. 
And they are not very strong to begin with!  You can easily make some
with some Steel channel and drill, a few bits and some time.  I used
3/16" wall on mine.  Used an original to make the pattern, and I now
don't fear the bolt head pulling or bending the plate. I have several
examples of the bolt pulled thru the plate.

Or you can fix yours and have them heat treated or weld in a
reinforcement plate to the original bar.
