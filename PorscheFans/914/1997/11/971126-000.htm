  > Also, a little more 914 trivia for all you know it alls out there. 
Why
did Porsche use the Getrag pattern (first over and back, and 5th out on a
limb).  Why not just a normal 5 speed pattern. WHAT GIVES?

Actually, the 901 gearbox shift pattern was designed for use on race
tracks. As you know 1st is only good for getting the car launched, after
that it's 2nd to 5th. Notice that 2nd thru 5th is a perfect "H" pattern,
a lot easier on the track to use all 4 of those gears. If you race your
914 you will grow to appreciate this feature.
