Gerald,

I'm running weber 40IDF and the setup works good.
What kind do you think you have? Dellorto?
They may just be clogged with junk. Without a good filter, I get little
specks
of rust from my gas tank. This can really screw up your carb.
But nothing a good cleaning and a new filter wouldn't fix.

Also, make sure you are NOT still using the original fuel pump that was
meant for FI.
Makes too much pressure for carbs. You need to get one of the pumps that
makes about 4psi, and get a pressure regulator to dial it back to 2.5 or 3.

IF the idle mixture is adjustable, like the webers, you need to set that
correctly.
Also make sure the carbs are balanced from side to side, (they pull the
same amount of air when you apply throttle).

After that, it sounds like idle jets (if they have em).
