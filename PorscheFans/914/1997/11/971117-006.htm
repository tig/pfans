1.	open motor hood and remove the 4 bolts holding the rear deck lid
to swing arms. (2 either side)
2.	Using a very long exstension or two unbolt the 2 bolts holding
the latch kit (10mm's)
3. 	Lift and done.

	After your done you will need to work on realinging the deck lid
a bit but it is perty streight forward.
