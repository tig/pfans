I have been looking at front shocks recently (though it's hard to tell if 
mine are really worn).  GPR pointed out that Bilsteins (front) are not 
really made for the 914, what other people sell is for the 911.  I guess 
they fit but not tuned for the 914.  I like my Bilsteins in the rear.  
Auto Atlanta likes the KYBs, but I would confirm that they are not also 
really for the 911.  Besides Konis, the only other available option that 
I am aware of is Boge.  By the way, KYBs are available locally through 
several places, the least costly I know of (in SF Bay area) is Auto Parts 
Club - 46.50 or so.
