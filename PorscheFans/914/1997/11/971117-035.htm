You can gain access into the trunk through the plastic covers in the =
engine compartment.  There is one on each side of the car.  Force these =
covers into the trunk.  You can reach into the trunk through these holes =
and unbolt the trunk lid from the hinges.  Then carefully lift the trunk =
lid up and unbolt the latch mechanism. Or try to actuate the trunk latch =
with a stiff bent coat hanger or something similar.  I had to do this =
about a year ago.  Its not easy and have a friend help you hold the =
trunk lid up.  Good Luck!
