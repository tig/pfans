over this one.....

Either Weber 40IDF or 44IDF should be good. I think 48IDA's are overkill.
If you have the PO's phone number, call him and ask him about the cam and
carbs. Otherwise, you're going to have to measure the lift at the rocker
arms to determine if the cam is stock. If the cam is not stock, then you
can't use the stock fuel injection. Any carburation problems can be solved.
First figure out what you have, then either get the haynes book for webers,
or contact Dellorto abou thier carbs.
