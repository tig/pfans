RE:914 Locked rear caliper - Wheel locked HELP!!

There are a couple aproaches you can employ the get your car rolling.  The
best way is to pull the wheel off and back off the adjustment screws on the
outboard and inboard calipers halfs.  These are the same screws used to
adjust the venting clearance.

I picked up a parts car last summer with a locked rear caliper, and used the
GBH methond.  GBH= great big hammer.  I took a GBH and smacked the wheel,
hard, right at the caliper, and this moved the caliper enough to allow the
wheel to turn.  This car had rusty steel wheels on it though, and I wouldn't
recommend this technique with your Fuchs!  You can also pull the wheel and
tap the caliper and rotor, this may be enough to push the pistons off of the
rotor.
