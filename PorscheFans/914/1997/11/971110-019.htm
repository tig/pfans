along with the wiper switch on my 914 with the combined unit from a 924.

electrical washer switch instead of our little spare tire air diverter
whatever thingie. I'm not good with proper names. Anyway I hope this
helps. At least in my area there are so many 924s in wrecking yards that
you can probably just pull a used one off a wreck without it setting you
back too much and it will probably be fully functional. One thing that
might pose a problem is that your earlier chassis has a slightly
different steering column than my '76's, which looks almost identical to
the 924's with its plastic housing and everything, so I don't know if
the switches from the 924 would be as much of a "bolt-in upgrade" as
mine was.
