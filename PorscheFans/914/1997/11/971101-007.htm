Steve and Terry,

    Here's what worked for me...
- slide the joiner piece, top/center to one side
- use something wide like a gasket scraper and gently/firmly pry from the
window side
- the chrome is mounted on little clips spaced at intervals around the
windshield...pry gently until the clip pops...then move on to the next clip

    You're gonna bend it a little somewhere along the line...stuff happens.
BUY NEW CLIPS.  This is the secret of getting the chrome back on the car.  I
used the black sticky stuff to set my windshield in.  Haven't used the
rubber gasket.  Black sticky stuff works pretty good.
