Pros & Cons -- Modified 914 vs. Beck Spyder kit (ignores aesthetic issues...
your call, I love 'em both!):

914 PRO:
-- Original.
-- Relatively sophisticated suspension/steering.
-- All weather capable.
-- Storage space.
-- Relatively low registration/licensing hassle
-- 914@porschefans web site!

914 CON:
-- Rust (rust!, rust!, rust!).
-- Poor engine access.
-- Limited chance that a sweetly modified car (steel flares, 6-cylinder.
   conversion, etc.) will ever recoup investment.
-- Requires complete bare metal teardown to achieve "as new" condition.
-- Parts prices.

BECK 550 SPYDER REPLICA PRO:
-- You won't see another one for a long time.
-- A "new car" that starts life fresh & clean.
-- Glass body won't rust.
-- Ultra lightweight = major performance from basic engine, brakes, etc.
-- Possibility of recouping investment if you decide to sell.
-- Spectacular access for maintenance.
-- VW (vs. Porsche) parts prices. 

BECK 550 SPYDER REPLICA CON:
-- Not original, just a replica/kit car.
-- Limited all-weather capabilities.
-- Almost no storage space.
-- VW suspension geometry, steering, etc.
-- No 914@porschefans website!
