For what its worth, I've got about two thousand miles on my 914 with a
3.6 ('91 DME, stock) with my original 901 tranny. I'm just as nervous as
everyone who says it can't be done, but remember the tranny's not worth
much, and its going to cost about $4,000 to put a G50 in (next step).
Furthermore, I understand 90% of the time when the 901 fails it is on
first gear, and with the huge torque of the 3.6 first gear is useless,
so I always use second. Even quick autocross starts are fine in second.
