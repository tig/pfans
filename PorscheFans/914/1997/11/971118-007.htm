Todd Holyoak wrote:
> 
> Also while I'm wating for my shop manuals to come in, I was wondering how
> to remove the seats.  The handle to slide the seats is broken off and I
> can't seem to see any bolts to unfasten to remove the seats.

Behind the seat and on the floor you'll find the mounting bracket. Each
side will have 2 small bolts (10mm head). Undo those bolts, -don't loose
the washers-, then lift the seat up and back to relaese the front catch
and out it goes, adjustment rails and all. Putting it back it is not too
big a deal, I've done it quite a bit, but it takes considerably more
patience to instal it than to remove it.
