Bill, you'll need a 914-6 oil tank. I've actually seen a 911 oil tank used
but it required butchering the rear trunk. A generic dry sump tank may also
be an option for you...perhaps a better option on a track car. The S brakes
won't bolt on to your strut. Best to switch to a 911 strut (anything that
used A or Carrera calipers).
