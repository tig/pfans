The ballast resistor goes between the + terminal on the coil and the 12V
supply. I think they are typically 1.4 ohms, 20->30 watts. You'll have to
get buddy-buddy with a parts guy and ask him to let you look at his parts
book. You want one with it's own little mounting bracket. They cost a few
dollars, not much. If you get the Pertronix, it'll have directions and
diagrams with it that will explain everything. Good luck!
