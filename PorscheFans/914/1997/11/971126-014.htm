>At 08:53 AM 11/25/97 -0800, Ben Thatcher wrote:
>>Talk to me about how you do this ball batting.
>
>I learned a couple tricks while doing the "ball-bat" thing myself.
>
>(1) I first went to the sporting goods store to buy a bat - $35.00.  
>NO WAY!
>
>(2) I went to the hardware store and bought a series of PVC pipes of 
>varying
>diameter - a few cents a piece.  (much better)
>
>(3) Start at either end of the wheel arch with a pipe size that just 
>*almost
>fits* toward the top of the arch (between the tire and the fender).
>Slowly start the car rolling, pulling the pipe across the fender.  
>Keep the
>pipe straight for an even pull.
>
>(4) When you get to the top of the arch *STOP*.  If you go past this 
>point,
>it will pull the lip out, instead of rolling it under.
>
>(5) Start the pipe from the other end of the wheel arch, and again 
>roll
>until you've reached the mid-point where you stopped in the other 
>direction.
>
>(6) Repeat the process with incrementally larger pipes until you've 
>reached
>the desired effect.
>
>BTW - We were able to do this without cracking any paint.
>
>Hope this helps!
>Jeff D.
