> Does anyone have a neat trick to get the front hood springs up
> a notch? My hood kind of droops, I need to make it tighter. My=20
> large screwdriver seemed a little dangerous for this job.


Use a piece of coathanger, and make a tool shaped like this _/\_   Then =
grab both ends of the coat-hanger wire with vise-grips, and hook the V =
part under the spring, and pull the spring and move it up a notch.  You =
may want some help for this part, as the spring is quite strong.  =
Holding the hood up with a broom handle helps too, because when you've =
got one side partially un-hooked, the hood can fall down.
