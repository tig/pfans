Well that all depends on what you want to do with the car. My general
response to that is "Do you ever intend to compete with car? If so,
decide on what class you want to compete it, and then READ THE RULES.
Then and only then, start your mods.". However, if you have no
intentions of competing, go for what ever you feel like. Just remeber
the race car builders decree...

"Speed is just a matter of money. How fast can I afford to go?..."

Keep in mind that _any_ engine transplant will wind up costing ALOT of
money. And it is never a simple bolt-in swap. Even if you were to just
go from a 1.7 to a 2.0, there will still be funny problmes that will
arise.
