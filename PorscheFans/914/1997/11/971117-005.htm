>I'm still working on this rocker arm devil. I have a partial solution, which
>I ordered parts for this morning. This solution gets rid of the spring
>between the rockers and replaces it with a shaft collar and some shims. This
>shoud reduce some of the friction in the valve train anyway. It's also a no
>brainer modification that anyone can do. The collar goes in the middle on
>the rocker shaft bewteen the rocker arms and then shims are added to each
>side to take up clearances. No, I don't know yet what the clearance should
>be. The collars are 14mm wide and the distance between the rockers is about
>18.1mm. So, you need about 4mm worth of shims (2 on each side of the
>collar). I ordered some 0.5mm shims also because I only measured one rocker
>arm set (ran out of time). They also have thinner shims too, down to 0.1mm.
>The thin shims goes towards the collar, the thicker ones go towards the
>rocker arms. I ordered the parts from M&MCC (that fabulous catalog that I've
>been raving about lately) for 18 bucks. The parts are:

I didn't need the 0.5mm shims, so you can probably just not order them. They
don't make 2mm shims in this diameter, so you have to use 2 of the 1mm shims
instead.
