I don't know about Vasoline, though I've heard of using it on the battery
terminals to cut down on buildup there.  I used a product called KoprShield
on all my terminals and connections when I cleaned them up.  Suppose to
protect them and be electrically conductive.  Got it from the Eastwood
catalog.  It looks and feels like copperbased anti-sieze but I can't verify
if it is the same or not.

I also used it on the bulb bases and sockets though watch out there.  I
fried the wire from the light switch up to the bulb for the parking lights
on indicator in the speedometer because I was a bit too generous with the
application on the small bulbs and the stuff shorted the base of the bulb
to ground. Nice smoke cloud out of the dash. :-(
