I've been running a pair of DellOrto DRLA 40's with Claude's Buggyworks
manifolds and linkage. Very nice setup. I did have some problems with
idle jet size. Tried a number of different sizes but I just wasn't
happy. Then I checked the float levels. They were significantly low
which make the carbs too lean. Don't forget to carefully set the floats
and then use the recommended jets as a starting point.
