I had a set of KYB's in my 914 a number of years ago, and in my opinion
they wern't the right shock for the car. The KYB gas-a-just shock has a
non-linear pumping action that stiffens the shock progressively over
time under higher frequency movement. The impractical result of which is
on the road, especially a freeway with rippled pavement, is that the
shocks will continually get stiffer and stiffer until the front of the
car is hopping over every bump and ripple on the ground. 

Nope, didn't like 'em. Can't say I'd recomend 'em...
