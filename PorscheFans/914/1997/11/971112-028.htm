The two designations that are most comonly refered to are 12A and 13B.
'12' and '13' being displacement designators, and 'A' and 'B' being
generation designators (I believe). 1200cc and 1300cc rotary engines.
The reason why they aren't mentioned in your book are that 12A and 13B
are Mazda designations for their engines that were found in the early
RX's.
