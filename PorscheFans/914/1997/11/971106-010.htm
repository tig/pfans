>   What's the best coil choice for a daily driver 914 2.0l w/FI?
>

Not to start yet another string of messages on coils but, get an Accel
Superstock coil (26$), a bracket for that coil (the oem one won't fit), and
a "universal" ballast resistor and mount them both off the engine. Put them
on the firewall near the ecu box and distributor. Also, replace your points
with a Pertronix. Any coil made by Bosch is going to be pathetic, in my
opinion. They all have internal resistors that heat the coil up, reducing
spark intensity because the resistance of the high voltage windings goes up
with heat. You want your coil to be cool. You racers should make an ice
bucket for your coils (insulate the coil from electrical shorts, of
course!). Cold coils make hotter sparks!
