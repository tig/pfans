The 550 kits have fascinated me from time-to-time. Beck used to sell a
really good kit, but the Brazilian company that made the fiberglas shells
stopped due to low demand. Thunder Ranch now makes the kit. These are
tube-frame with fiberglas skins using VW front and rear suspensions. The
drivetrain is VW also, but midengined like the original. 

I dont' see a need for a 914-to-550 conversion, the 914 is a much better
design in the first place. Save your money and just put a 911 six in your 914.
