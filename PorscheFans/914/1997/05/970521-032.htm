There are a great many cars out there that run without 'em and do fine.
There is an interesting pluss to having a _functional_ decel valve, and
that is if you run a lightened flywheel. The decell valve lets you
control how quickly the engine approaches idle. If you run a lightened
flywheel with no decel valve or other way of compensating, the engine
speed can very likely overshoot the idle speed on the way down and
stall.
