Keep the rake. The car will handle better. But if you really like the
level look then by all means do so then. The 3 position perch should
allow you to do that.

Even in simple spirited driving the rake will make a difference in
handling. Try it out. If you're that used to swapping springs, and have
a safe spring compressor, try one perch height for a while, then
another. Drive the same stretch of road and see what the difference is.
I think you'll find the rake suits the car better. And personally, I
think the car looks good with a rake! Not one of those floppy leafy
things either, I mean a good three tined pitchfork type thing! A really
sharp one too! The kind you can just go off and...and.. oh, sorry, wrong
