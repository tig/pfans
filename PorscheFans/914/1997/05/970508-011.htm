          I will leap into the wide tire advantage debate with my two
          cents worth. I was lead to believe by the truck tire experts
          I have worked with that wide tires control heat better so
          the can grip better. I narrow tire will over heat its tire
          compound faster than a wide tire because it has less area to
          dissipate the heat generated by the tires sliding across the
          pavement. The truck tire guys said wide tires real
          advantages only show up over narrow when the tire is rolling
          and slipping. They also pointed out that wide tires have
          several dissadvantages. Poor tracking in a straight line
          rolling resistance and wind resistance. Anyway thats the
          perspective from a truck tire guy

          Nick
