Stock tire is 165 SR 15. I have one, on my spare wheel (never used it
though, god knows how old it _really_ is). Measured from hub center to
tread, the radius is 12.5 inches.

205/50-15, my race tires. Measured from hub center to tread, the radius
is 11.4 inches.

Stock tire:

((12.5 in)(2)(pi))(1 ft/12in) = 6.545 ft circumference

(5280 ft/mi)(1 rev/6.545 ft) = 806.72 revs/mile

205/50-15s:

((11.4 in)(2)(pi))(1 ft/12in) = 5.969 ft circumference

(5280 ft/mi)(1 rev/5.969 ft) = 884.57 revs/mile

So:

(806.72 revs/mile)/(884.57 revs/mile) = .912

So your speedometer will read approx 9% higher with 205/50-15 tires.
