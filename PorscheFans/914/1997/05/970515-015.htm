A 2" "NO HUB" pipe coupling makes a  very effective and inexpensive ring
compressor for assembling 914 P&C and other small engines.  This pipe
coupling is constructed of a band of fluted steel with two worn drive
hose clamps attached.  The clamp is flexible and can be easily unscrewed
and opened up to remove it from the P&C assembly once the rings are
inside the cylinder.  These couplings are available at any plumbing
supply house, and some hardware stores, or e-mail me at MURPHYJ2@aol.com
and I will assist you.
