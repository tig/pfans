Is anyone else experiencing this?

Sometimes after I've been cruising along in 5th gear for a while (say 5-10
miles) and I try to shift back to 4th, the tranny seems to be stuck there
in 5th for a few seconds. I just keep the clutch in and pressure on the
shift lever and it finally lets go, but it's really a slow shift. It
doesn't always happen. Other times it shifts fine.

BTW, all of the other shifts work OK. No sticking. I'm currently using the
RedLine Gearlube (not the dreaded MTL). The sticking occurred before I put
the RL in. Another BTW, I'm going to be taking the RedLine out because it
seems to be causing a leak that wasn't there with dino lube.
