I was recently talking to a tech at DeMoore Tires in Atlanta. He said he
would never touch a BFG R1 because of it's "killer groove" problem. It
goes like this: R1 sidewalls are so rigid that instead of the whole tire
shifting over during a hard corner, the tire compresses instead, causing
the center to bulge out and thus causing increased wear in the center of
the tread and narrowing the contact patch. He said he's seen cars crash
during roadraces because of this. I'm currently running R1's and like
them for autox but will need a new set soon. This guy's trying to sell
me some Toyo RA1's or some Hoosiers instead. Has anyone out there ever
heard of or experienced this problem? TIA,
