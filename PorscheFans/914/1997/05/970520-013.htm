dry.
 
> Do you prefer to store them stacked up or standing up (roll of
> LifeSavers on end or on side), inflated or deflated, and what sort of
> covering or coating would be considered sufficient protection from the
> summer sun and the elements?  Would a blue tarp suffice? Should I be
> scouting for an inside spot for the winter?

If the tires are going to be outside or subject to UV or weather, keep
them in dark plastic trash bags. But you don't want it compleatly
sealed, too much collected moisture can also be bad. When I kept them in
my carport, I had them lifesaver style and it left some little glossy
flat spots on the rubber. So I keep them stacked now. Keep some plastic
between them or you'll find they glue themselves to each other. And keep
air in 'em.
