PMO is an excellent source for Weber parts.  Their only drawback is they
don't take plastic - COD or prepay only.  You can call them @ (310)
393-5423.  Usually jets are like $4 each.

I have to say, I'm curious about what you're going to do with 60 idles
though.  If your running Webers on a 914 or VW engine, the 50's should
be just right.  I tried a set of 45's and a set of 55's on my 914 2.0.
The 45's were too lean - engine 'hunted' and sputtered and the 55's were
too rich - too much gas smell while idling.  So I went back to the 50's.

Oh, and BTW, the jets for the 44 IDF's are the same as for the 40 IDF's.
