	I dunno...  If you look at how the factory did the original Sixes,
they did just about the minimum changes they could.  The interiors are very
similar between fours and sixes, and the main exterior change was the five-

sedly Porsche was pretty hard-pressed to keep the /6 under the 911 T's price
range, so they didn't go overboard with "Six-only" features.
