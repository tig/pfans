  At any rate, I need a general idea of how much a 914 should bend.  My trouble is represented by

Doc & Cy's which mounts on the front edge of the roll bar.  I started to wonder if the chassis was 
getting weak, so I put my foot on the pontoon about even with the driver's seat back and put some

down on one foot, the movement seems limited to that initial bit.  Additionally, the top is not jammed; it 
is free to rattle up and down a bit with full weight (about 220 pounds) applied.  The door opens and 
closes easily either with or without weight in the driver's seat, as long as the window is rolled down.  
The window hangs up on the rubber when rolled up more than halfway, with or without the driver's 
weight in the car, although it seems to be worse with the weight applied.

  There seems to be only slight surface rust around the jack point on the driver's side.  I have

there... no obvious structural problems.  The floor pan is in excellent shape.  There is virtually no rust 
on the outside; however there is some rust in the center tunnel on the inside.  I have yet to remove the 
driver's side outer rocker panel, however.  There is no observable rust in the area of the pontoons 
inside the rear wheel wells forward of the wheels, but the original undercoat and dirt prevents close 
inspection... there are no obvious gaps, holes, or other problems.
