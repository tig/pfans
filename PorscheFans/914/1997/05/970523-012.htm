Since I first posted a note here (about 3 weeks or so ago) about the
horizontle electric fan kit for the 914-4, many questions have been sent my
way.  I am probably as interrested/curious in its adaptability to street cars
and racers as any of you list members are.  Don Ramsey at ACR (Air Cooled
Racing, 423-671-4914) has built this system for several of his racing
customers and is successfully using it in the Knoxville, TN area.  He sells
the kit for $500.00 in glass and I believe it can be done in carbon fiber
also for an additional charge.
For those interrested, I have sent Richard Ellis
(http://www.dallas.net/rare/porschefans.html) several shots of this setup as
well as Allan McMichael (http://www.why.net/users/a914drvr/) to post on their
respective pages.  I would expect the posting setup to take a couple days, so
try looking at these sites early next week.  I am very interrested in
everones feedback.  
I plan on purchasing the ACR electric fan kit for my GT3 914 and have gotten
a oil cooler adapter/block-off plate from Kerry Hunter to help complete the
setup.  Kerry (khunter@baervan.nmt.edu) has produced a very nice CNC milled
piece at a very reasonable price.  He also sells headers for street/track
914-4s that are widely used in the PCA and SCCA  circles.
Discussions are underway at our local Skunk-Works (H&H Enterprises) about the
kits adaptability for street cars.  We've been through about three pots of
coffee, 11 Sprites and a pound and a half of jerkey at this point in the
discussion.  The overall consensis is we think it can be done with some
additional pieces and of coarse an external oil cooler.
