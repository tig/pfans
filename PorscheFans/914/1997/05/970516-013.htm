Take ALL the locks to a GOOD locksmith.  Tell him/her that you want the
keys to match.  (The one for the front hood is the worst to get out.)
This is called "re-pinning" a lock.

If you snag some locks from a VW in a junk yard (that use the same key
blank) he can get spare pins from them.

Cost me $20, but that included 4 keys; two Porsche-style, and 2 plain.
