As for R1 storage, consensus is:

Store inside in a dark, cool, dry spot.
Stack 'em.
Keep 'em inflated.
Put plastic or cardboard between the tires since they stick together
after a bit.
If they're outside, put them in dark garbage bags to protect from UV,
but don't seal tight because you want condensation to evaporate before
it could damage the finish on your rims.
Keep away from electric motors b/c they produce ozone.
