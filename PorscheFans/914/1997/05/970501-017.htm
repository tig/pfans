Ken Korn asks:

>Is anyone else experiencing this?
>Sometimes after I've been cruising along in 5th gear for a while (say 5-10
>miles) and I try to shift back to 4th, the tranny seems to be stuck there
>in 5th for a few seconds.

I've experienced this, and traced it to a loose rear engine [transmission]
mount. it allows just enough 'droop' in the assembly to bind the 4-5 shift,
which is a full extension of the shift rod. One the engine torque is
removed, and after a delay, the shift roid binding clears.
