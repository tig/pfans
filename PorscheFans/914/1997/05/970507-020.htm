I have played alot with sound systems.  I have put a small one in my
914.  I thought about an eight or 12" speaker.  The front trunk is out
because of the gas tank. And the required box is too big to put anywhere
else. I ended up taking four stout 5" woofers in a 12x12x4" sealed box
under the dash. I used a 100w/ch. rms amp w/ a built in crossover. I
wired pairs of speakers in paralell for a 2 ohm load which means the 100
watts is now theoreticaly 200.  It works pretty well.  Not super loud
but is enough to fill out the sound nicely.
