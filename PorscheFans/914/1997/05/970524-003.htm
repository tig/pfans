The distributor port that faces the left hand side of the car goes to
the port at the -front- of the throttle body (vac advance). And the
distrubutor port that faces right, connects to the port on teh -rear- of
the throttle body. A good way to remember it is that the shortest and
longest possible path lengths are the right ones to use. Go figure!
