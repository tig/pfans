If you don't know where the failure is, one of the easiest tests is to 
unscrew the cable from the speedo drive on the transmission.  Using your 
fingers (or a variable speed drill - SLOWLY), turn the square end of the 
cable while someone watches the speedo.

If the guage moves then your problem is in the angle drive and it will need 
to be replaced (common).

If this doesn't cause your guage to move, pull your speedo guage out and 
unscrew the cable from the back of it and again turn the cable at the drive 
end and have someone watch the other end of the cable.  If it is turning 
(but the gauge wasn't moving when it was attached) the problem is in the 
guage (not common).

If you turn one end of the cable and the other end doesn't move, then your 
cable is broken and needs to be replaced (common).

Good luck.
