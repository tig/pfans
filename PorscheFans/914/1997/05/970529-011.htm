	If I remember the original post correctly (no guarantees!), the
poster said that he liked the stock ride height and ride.
	If you want stock height, ride, and handling, get the stock
components.
	Some people may not find the ride with heavier springs offensive,
but you're pretty much guaranteed that the ride is *better* with the softer
ones.  (You also have good odds that the softer suspension doesn't handle
as well.)  It's a trade-off, and if someone has figured out what he or she
likes, that's what (s)he should go for.
