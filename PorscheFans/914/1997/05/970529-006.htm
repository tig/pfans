Check battery ground, check transmission ground strap, check 
voltage across ignition coil.  Times like these are when it's
good to get out the multi-meter and the wiring diagram...

The best way to get to the problem is to trace the circuit, if
you don't have a wiring diagram - I have a bunch (in color) and
could arrange to get you a set if you'd cover the shipping and
copying costs...

> not sure how to do a valve adjust right have never done one please give me some
> idiot proof directions on the whole process

Do you want the "official" procedure or a "cheat".  The standard
procedure is to roll the car in gear until the TDC mark is 
centered in the view hole of the fan housing - then adjust #1
valves.  Move car until distributor rotor moves couterclockwise
90 degress, adjust #2 valves....repeat for #3 and #4.

A friend had a novel way of doing his.  Jack rear end up and remove
rear tires - remove valve covers.  Zap starter until you get 
play on a clyinder for both valves(they are closed) adjust.  
Repeat until you get all 4 done.  Having the tires off and the
rear in the air makes it easier to get at the valves.
