On Wed, 28 May 1997, Bill K Logan wrote:

> Anyone have any advice on rear springs for my 914? 

For street use with sway bars, you need to keep your suspension 
compliance, so the stock replacements (100#/") should be fine. Some 
Porsche people go too far in using too stiff of a spring rate for street 
use. Streets are bumpy, you need the compliance. The sway bars will 
reduce body roll significantly without seriously effecting compliance. 

> Also, does
> anyone have any idea's about bilstein's with adjustable spring plates?
> Terry

I wouldn't bother. Get some Boges or maybe street Konis without 
adjustable ride height.
