Off which pins? There are five pins (four of them are used) on the TPS.
It sounds like you _may_ have been measuring the idle circuit. The
question is not if you get an open circuit, but _when_. And if you're
measuring off the idle circuit, it will always open as soon as the
throttle starts to move, what you need to do is look at it as the
throttle is closing. There is a directional switch that tell the
computer weather to use the acceleration circuit (the clicks) or the
idle circuit.
