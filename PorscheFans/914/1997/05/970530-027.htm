Since we are assuming a "stock" setup, the front is all we ave to worry about.

So.... Here's a "mere mortal" method.  It may not be perfect, but it should
work well enough.

(1) Find yourself a freight scale (I have one in my warehouse).
(2) Drive your car up to the scale so that only the right front tire is on
the scale.
(3) Notate the indicated weight. (with driver)
(4) Drive so that only the left front tire is on the scale.
(5) Notate the indicated weight. (with driver)
(6) If the right indicated weight is greater than the left, lower the right
and/or raise the left until the weights are even.
(7) If the left indicated wieght is greater than the right, lower the left
and/or raise the right until the weights are even.
