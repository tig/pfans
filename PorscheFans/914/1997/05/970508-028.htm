In response to Jeff L.: many people have told me to just unhook it from
each end and secure it with wire so it doesn't flop around. This way
it's easy to hook back up if oversteer becomes a problem. I just
unhooked mine but haven't autoxed like this yet. The other 914 autoxers
I've talked to unanimously agree to keep it unhooked with my current
setup. I do well in acceleration and slalom but tight 180's are where I
have problems with pushing through my front tires instead of turning.
This is where I lose the most time because I have to slow down so much.
I'll see if unhooking the rr bar corrects this understeer or if I also
need to tweak my alignment and get a fatter front bar. Good Luck,
