my recent exp w/ alum covers is that i have had em on & off about a
dozen times with same cork, still no leaks, but had constant leaking w/
oem covers & neoprene gskts... yes the alum cover is very close to sheet
metal at top... i had to whack the tin upward a bit in order to get a
fit..  vw builders like to silicon the cork into the oem cover & fasten
it in place overnight... but have heard no report on re-uses when one is
doing the inevitable multi adjustments on a fresh motor
