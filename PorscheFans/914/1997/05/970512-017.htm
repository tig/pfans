I bought a pair of Jensen 4x6 speakers at Walmart for $30 - they fit in the 
stock boxes just like the originals - no mods or anything.  By themselves, 
they don't deliver much bass (not much will when mounted in a tiny plastic 
box), but they sound decent and if you've got any kind of amp or high-power 
stereo unit you can here them while flying down the highway with the roof 
off.  And they're cheap enough to take a chance on.  Just my 2 cents.
