Ken, if you have aftermarket adjustable swaybars (with adjustable drop
links) you'll want to disconnect them prior to corner weighting. Just
removing or disconnecting one drop link renders the bar ineffective.  You do
this so any swaybar "preload" won't affect your corner weights.  Set your
corner weights, reconnect your bar/s and if your corner weights change,
you've got swaybar preload and you'll need to adjust the drop link length on
that bar.
