> 
> I am trying to remove the engine from 1973 914 but I cannot get the shifting
> rod to come off at the transmission.  I have gotten lose from the
> transmission itself but I cannot get it through the hole on the transmission
> and the piece the I need to remove, the one that connects with the shifting
> part of the transmission, is the thing I cannot get off.

There are two small hex screws (with little nipples) which attach the
shift linkage.  One is at the firewall and the other at the rear.  I
assume the rear one has been removed.  You also have to remove the 
retaining screw at the firewall. 

Move the shift linkage rearward first to free from the firewall, then
it will drop down so you can pull it forward to remove it from the 
tranny.
