The assembly is flexing when you hit it.

Brace it REAL well, and get the right king of drift punch,
and hit it REAL hard.  (Did I mention bracing it real well?
Brace the pedal, not the pedal holder.  And brace it right
near the pin.  And don't use soft wood to brace it.)
