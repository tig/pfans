	If you're doing it because of series rules, talk to your local rules-
folks.  If you're doing it just to be extra-sure, put them on the inside.  The
rear glass is held against part of the car's body on the outside part of the
glass.  There's metal between the window and the outside world, all the way
around the edge of the window.  If the window doesn't break, it will never
come out except into the cockpit.
