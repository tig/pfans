Josh,
At one time I had intermittent power loss on my 76 914 2.0 (D-jet).
Engine would die abruptly then refire just as abruptly a couple
of seconds later. Very intermittent but gradually got worse.
Turned out to be cylinder head temp. sensor so you might check that.

I believe there is a quick test where you can disconnect lead
from sensor and attach lead to ground - if problem goes away it's
the sensor. There is also a more scientific test one can make with
an ohm meter but I don't have the pointer handy. You should check
procedures for both tests before proceeding though.

Good luck,
