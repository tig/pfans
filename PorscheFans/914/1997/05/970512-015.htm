When I rebuilt a ch*vy motor way back when, I was told to pack the
new oil pump with a good amount of moly grease.  The grease fills the voids
between the gear teeth, and allows it to develop vacuum to pull oil into
the pump.  Maybe the same thing would've helped with your rebuild?  FWIW....
