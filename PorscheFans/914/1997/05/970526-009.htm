On Mon, 26 May 1997, Larry Moeller wrote:

> Can anyone give me a vacuum profile on a relatively stock engine & or
> comparisons from other built motors???

Before I took mine apart, I checked the vacuum, and it was around 25"Hg 
at idle, which is fairly normal. Vacuum goes down as the throttle opens 
wider. Also, check for a "beat" in the movement of the indicator. It 
shoudl be rock steady, but a periodic "beat" will indicate a leaking 
valve. You values are low - you shoudl have more vacuum than that, unless 
that cam is way different than stock. Does it idle smoothly? Does the 
plugs show a rich mixture?
