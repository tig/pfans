	The gauges are held into the instrument panel by their rubber
trim rings.  You may be able to reach up from under the dash and push
the speedo out.  If not, then CAREFULLY use a small flat-head screw-
driver to pry the gauge awy from the rubber ring.  When you have enough
of the gauge out to get ahold of with your fingers, just pull it toward
you.
	Why remove the instrument panel when you don't need to?  You do
not need to remove the steering wheel if you just pull the speedo out.
It is, however, not a bad idea to un-screw the nut that holds the odo
reset cable to the bottom of the dash before you pull too far.
