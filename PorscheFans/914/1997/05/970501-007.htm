Yes, it's excellent gear oil. In fact, all of thier products are first
rate. They claim to have some propritary chemical additives that protects
metal surfaces. The racers prefer Redline products, I think, but they are
both expensive compared to standard GL5 stuff. My opinion is that since 
Porsche recommends gear oil changes at every other engine oil change, 
then it's more economical to use regular GL5 stuff. The gear oil 
available these days is *much* better than what was available 25 years 
ago, so it should be plenty good enough.
