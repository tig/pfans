Steve Howitson wrote:

> I have a 72 914 with a tail shift.  The shifting is pretty sloppy.  I
> added a short shifter and its still all over the place.  Is there a
> better transmission option?  Is the swap a hard activity?  I'm up for
> the job if it will help the slop.

The obvious idea would be to swap out your tail shifter with a side
shift 901 transmission.  You'd have to replace the engine carrier
up front as it differs for the side-shifter (the linkage passes thru
the carrier with the side-shift).  Overall this could get expensive
as a used late-style engine carrier could run you $60-$100.

A tail-shifter with a factory short shift kit should get you about
the same action as a normal side shifter.  Have you replaced the 
nylon bushings of the shift linkage?  Wornout bushings would give
you extra play and they are cheap and easy to replace.  Could (and
usually is) the cause of extra play.

Don't remember where is bushings are on the tail shifter, but I'm
replacing them on my side-shifter (it's a $5 part) when the engine
goes back in.
