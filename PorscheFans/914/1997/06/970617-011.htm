Dion,
Sounds like the shift rod bushings, but make sure you positioned
the (loose) clutch tube such that it does not intefere with the shifter.
Note there's not much vertical clearance between the two.

Also, make sure you get the *914* bushings (oval I think) and not the
(square) 911 bushings for the joint back of the firewall where the two
rods connect, else there will still be some slop. (There seems
to be a tendency for some vendors to ship 911 bushings - worth double
checking).
