>So, how best to address a leaking oil filter flange seal is the
>question of the day!  Any answers?

Congrats on finding the problem. This is what I would do: remove the bracket
and check for flatness on the flange faces with a straight edge, file
accordingly to make it flat (hold the straight edge against the flange and
then put a light source behind it). Throughly degrease both faces with brake
cleen. Use permatex #2 on both surfaces and re-install. Let sit overnight
before starting the engine. Hope this helps.
