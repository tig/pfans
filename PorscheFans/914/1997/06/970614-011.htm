Fix everything.  Well put in new bushings available from performance or
automotion for about $35.  Make sure that the hole where the shift rod
goes into the box on the tranny is not worn out of round.  A little bit
of motion there 1/8" will really mess up your side to side adj. at the
shifter.  Get the cable tube welded, it makes a big difference on the
cable adjustment.  Most likely between those two things, niether of
which is much money or that much work, you will have a car that shifts
fine.  If you haven't done so check your tranny oil, I find teflon
additives make a nice difference. Good Luck, enjoy the road.
