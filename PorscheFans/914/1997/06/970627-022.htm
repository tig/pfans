It was mentioned the other day that the only models avaliable for the 914
was in 1:24 and 1:43, well here is a list.  It can be found at URL:
http://www.skurup.se/~carpet/914/modell.htm    or in the back of Das
Grossen VW und Porsche Buch.

1:1
914, Manufacturing 1970-1976 by Porsche AG, Germany. Length 3985, width
1650, height 1240, weight 970 kg. Engine 1,7/4 -2.0/6, 77-210 Horsepower.
Price 1970 about 23 000:- SEK in Sweden. Total Production 115 596 914/4,
3 360 914/6 of which 30 GT, 11 916 . About 80% of the car were exported
to the USA.

1:4,25

• 914/6, Handmade by Gunnar Öhrström, Sweden in middle of the 1970.
Length 970 mm, width 410 mm weight 25 kg. With working gearbox, brake
discs, shock absorbers, differential, headlights, door locks, windscreen
wipers and washers, and jack. The body is made of fiberglass after a
mould in wood. The Car was sold by Bukowski's auction May 7th 1995
Sweden. Estimate was 40-45 000:-SEK. Now it is at AAA Small Car World, in
Fort Worth, Texas. The scale 1:4.25 was dictated by the only tiers he
could get (ash tray tiers).
