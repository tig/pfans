	Your best bet is to hang out at your local race track and see if you
get ahold of some used race rubber from someone who is gonna toss em
anyway. If you want cheap _new_ race tires, look at the Hoosier. Last
years model ore on a fire sale right now for about $70-80 per tire. This
years model is still about $130 or more. Most of the DOT race tires out
there will cost about the same, $125-$150.
