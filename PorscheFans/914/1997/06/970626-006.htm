To follow up on my comments on tires, PCA rules as well as Vintage only 
allow a 3% change in revs/mile (which is directly linked to tire diameter. 
I am enclosing both the printout and the excel spreadsheet use to determine 
it.

Due to the original tires not carrying a % indicator, PCA has used 70% in 
that this is what a normal unrated tire is now.

Size	Width	%	Dia	Cir in inch	Revs / mile	Delta from stock	True Delta
175/80-15	175	80	26.02	534.11	118.63		
175/70-15	175	70	24.65	479.05	132.26	0.00%	-10.31%
195/55-15	195	55	23.44	433.51	146.16	10.51%	-18.84%
205/60-15	205	60	24.69	480.58	131.84	-0.32%	-10.02%
225/55-15	225	55	24.74	482.88	131.21	-0.79%	-9.59%
265/45-15	265	45	24.39	469.15	135.05	2.11%	-12.16%
