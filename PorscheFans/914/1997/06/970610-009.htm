On Mon, 9 Jun 1997, Jeff Lorentzen wrote:

> He also warned me, if you ever do pull the stub axles DO NOT ever roll the
> car around.  Something about the type of bearings the rears are.  Just
> rolling the car around without the stub axles in place can damage the
> bearings.

This is what I did. I don't think the bearings were ruined. The seals 
maybe, but I do know that I couldn't get the play out of them afterward 
when I put a stub axle back in and tightened the "big nut" real tight 
(but not up to spec). I don't know if the bearing was ruined, but since I 
couldn't inspect it with ruining it, I just decided to replace them. 
After I got them out, the races were fine. The grease was a little old 
and the seals had leaked, but the bearing was fine. Anyway, live and learn.
