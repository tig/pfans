>To follow up on my comments on tires, PCA rules as well as Vintage only

	It is important to remember that the formulas are only a
general guideline.  Many tires do not exactly conform to these
measurements, so in the end you will need to go and measure the
specific tire you are talking about.
	Since the rules are based on revs-per-mile, the easiest
way to find that info is to call the tire company and ask about
that particular model and size of tire.  Most tire manufacturers
keep that sort of info.
