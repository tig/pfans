Looks like 195/55 is not a good choice, let alone 195/60. Hmmm....

As you can see the best hit is a 205/60-15, you get the right revs/mile and 
the size is an easy one to find. 225/55-15 would be great but I have not 
been able to find in a race-street tire.

Remember that changing the revs/mile is the same as a linear gear change, 
(like a rear end gear change. If it was free you could give up corner exit 
speed for top end on long tracks or gain corner exit on short tracks. Which 
is why they set limits of the tires.
