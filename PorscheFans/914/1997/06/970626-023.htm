The factory design size was 175/80 but the 165/80 was an optional size for 
the bug's and as this was really a VW car they used the 165/80's they were 
buying in bulk and made the 175's the optional tire. On the -6 they used 
14" tires as the optional ones so for stock classes you have a better range 
of Rolling Dia.
