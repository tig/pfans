To summarize my "spring" project, I have acquired from list members and
installed:

(1) 19mm master cylinder
(2) front calipers
(3) front lines from the master cylinder to the calipers
(4) 15 x 7-inch "steelie" wheels

Now that everything is installed, I have spent the last 3 days attempting to
bleed the system (bringing up a whole other set of questions).  While doing
so, I have made the following observations:

(1) The Clymer manual says that the rear brakes have an "outer" and an
"inner" bleeder valve.
(2) The Haynes manual says that the rears have a single bleeder valve on the
inside near the top.

When checking MY rear calipers, the left rear caliper has 2 bleeders on the
inside (top and bottom), like a front caliper, and the right rear has a
single bleeder near the top.

Other than the bleeder locations, the rear calipers are identical.

Are any of these the correct calipers for my 75 2.0 ???
