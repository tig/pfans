>>
>> I am thinking about time trialing and autocrossing my 914.  I have a
>> tail shift transmission will it hold upto the strain.

	Like Josh2 said, the internals will be fine, but you have to be
careful to get the gear you want when you shift.  Check the local rules
about transmission swaps, though.  In some rule sets, that will bump
you up into a higher class.
	If it does, you may still be able to do it through update-
backdate rules.  The 73 1.7 came with a sideshifter, so hopefully you
can justify an earlier 1.7 sideshifter that way.
	Even if you can't, don't stress too hard on it.  I know several
stock Sixes with tail-shifters that autoX and time trial a lot with no
problems.  I even know of several tail-shifter fours that autoX without
worrying about it.
	Replace your bushings, adjust the linkage so that the shifting
is unambiguous, and then remember to be a little careful.
