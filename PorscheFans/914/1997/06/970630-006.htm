The front bolt heads on the pedal cluster should be welded to the pedal
cluster base.  You are right that you have to go underneath, remove the
cover under the m/c, and remove the nuts holding the m/c in place.

"While you're in there", check to make sure there are no leaks from the m/c.
Otherwise, you'll be rebuilding the cluster again soon!
