OK, everyone, I called NTW and they said that the tire size of 195/60 15 was
the most suitable. The tire rack has A509's in this size for 65$ each, hmmm....

The "195" refers to the width of the tread in mm and the number after the
slash "/" is the aspect ratio. I read somewhere that idealy the tire and rim
width should be roughly the same to insure that the sidewalls were vertical,
hence my questioning the wisdom of using relatively extremely wide tires on
these skinny rims. NTW does not recommend 205 width tires on these rims,
however.
