Have you tried adjusting the shifter with the littlle bolt (if I recall
10mm) found right under the shift boot? This allows you to "rotate" the
bar slightly either clockwise or counterclockwise and set it up for all
gears. Put the car in 4th and 5th to adjust, tighten it down, then try
all the other gears (before you try to drive off!)
Worked for me with bad bushings and all....
