> most of these parts will easily interchange. I'd like to know how
> true this is -- CAN I swap out steering racks, suspension A-arms
> and brakes ???

    Yes, David, BUT...

    All those pieces ae 30 years old; they'll probably need a fair
    amount of reconditioning or rebuilding.

    Also, the 1967 911 front suspension doesn't offer much that you
    don't already have; the aluminum crossmember and light alloy "S"
    calipers weren't introduced until years later.

    If I were you, I'd just put the 911 struts on the 914 and look
    for a pair of alloy "S" calipers to mount to them.  Keep in mind
    that the 911 brakes will require 5-lug 911 wheels.
