in the 4 cylinder engines? I'm curious to know what size rods to use (I

would be crazy not to try to use them. For example, you can get a set of 8
cnc-machined rods balanced and blueprinted for less than 400$. Hell, you
can get aluminum connecting rods for 600$! Or lighweight 560 gram rods for
less than 500$. These are all matched sets of 8. If two people ever wanted
to go in together on a set of rods (4 rods each), the savings could be
tremendous!
