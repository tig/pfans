At 8:22 AM -0700 6/5/97, Steve Howitson wrote:
>I'm new to the 914 world.  Is there some books that the garage wrench
>should have to reference.  I'm talking both general maintance and racing
>applications.

	General Maint:
John Muir's Idiot Book (esp. if you haven't done a lot of auto work)
Factory Wrokshop manual ($300+)
[OR Haynes manual; $20 but not quite as good]
Little Spec Book

	Racing:
Fred Puhn's "How to Make Your Car Handle"
	There's also a series of "XXX to Win" books (I think "Engineer
to Win" is one) but I can't remember the exact names or the author.

	Driving:
Hank Watts' "Secrets of Solo Racing" is very good for solo events.

	I'm sure everyone has their favorites, I'm also sure I've left
out a lot of good books.
