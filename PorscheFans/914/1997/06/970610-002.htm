> I just got my car back from George Wright who has been mentioned on the

> come up with a bolt on kit for adjustable proportioning valve.  While he
> had my car and was doing the rear bearing replacement and swapping my 4
> bolt hubs for some 5 bolt hubs I had him do something else he came up with.

> stub axles when replacing the boots or repacking CV joints.

> car around.  Something about the type of bearings the rears are.  Just
> rolling the car around without the stub axles in place can damage the
> bearings.
