One of my favorite Rust Killers is EXTEND, which is available as a spray
or brush on.
 As with most of these products, it converts rust (ferric Oxide) to
Ferrous oxide, essentially
stopping it's spread. The reason I like this stuff is that it looks like
milk going on
and turns the painted area a shiny, hard black. It doesn's seem to cause
any problems
when you paint over it. I used a bunch under the battery tray, engine
mount area 
(the 914 cancer hole!) and it is holding up great. I used some Hammerite
*over* the 
EXTEND to make it all a little prettier. Carefully follow the
instructions will all of the rust 
killers and it sure helps to wear rubber gloves!
     I get this stuff at a local auto supply (SHUCKS). It runs about $5
for 4 ounces. 
Well worth it.
