A word of caution: These fasteners are only about Grade 4 or so, so they
cannot be used in any high strength application. Exhaust nuts are only =

torqued to 15ft-lb so that would be a good application for ss. Totally
Stainless knows all about this, too.

About galling: Galling can easily be avoided by the use of anti-seize.
Totally Stainless sells a brand they recommend and they even include
a chart in the back of the catalog on the maximum recommended
torque for ss fasterners of various sizes. They also have a little
discussion about reducing the amount of torque applied as a result
of the use of anti-seize. Anti-seize is basically an EP lubricant so a =

reduction in applied torque of about 25% will achieve the same clamping =

force as the rated torque without anti-seize.
