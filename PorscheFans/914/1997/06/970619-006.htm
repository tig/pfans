Sam,

There are two brands available:  Earle and Weltmeister.

You can get the Earle's anywhere, (GPR, etc.) for $50 or so.

The Weltmeister's are (as far as I know) Automotion only,
and run $80.  The Welt's are DOT approved, but.......

Why did the DPO put on the SS lines?  Because they're cheaper.
OEM lines run $40 each.

......  but, OEM lines are STRONGLY (did I emphasise that enough?)
recommended for a street car.  Unless you're a certified motor-head
(check for the membership card in you wallet.) and inspect the
lines (you DO have a pressure tester, right?) regularly, stick
with OEM.
