If your car currently has no sway bar (ie, came from the factory with no
sway bar), then some body work is required in order to install one. You have
to weld in re-inforcements on the inside of the inner fender (in the gas
tank area) otherwise the force from the bar will rip the fender wall. You
also have to get the alignment of the bar correct, which may be tricky.
Unless you can weld, have a good body shop do it.
