I have a '73 911T with a worn out upper steering shaft bearing.  The 
problem is that the "quick fix" Automotion plastic sleeve and the 928 steel 
sleeve (P/N 928.347.739.02) do not work on 911's before 1974.  The steering 
shaft design changed from 1973 to 1974 and these parts do not work in the 
earlier design.  I know this because I ordered the 928 part and tried to 
install it and found out it does not work.  The 911 bearing I need is 
actually a 914 part number (914.347.711.00) but costs $154!  This 914 part 
was also used on '70 & '71 914's.  My question is has anyone out there gone 
through this?
