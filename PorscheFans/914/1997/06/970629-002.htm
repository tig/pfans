The splitting of the 914-4 rear calipers can be and has been done easily.
 The kits were available from several sources and included spacers, seals,
longer calipers bolts, a modified pad retainer and a 911 harware kit.  I have
been useing this setup for over 10 years and it works perfectly.  You will
need to use the 911 vented rear rotor and have it drilled for the 4 bolt
pattern.  
(Here comes another H&H plug) Wes Hildreth can do the rotor work if you need
some help there.
Try GPR for the spacer kits.  Ask for Jim or Dave.  Repost or send me an
e-mail if you can't find the spacer kits.
