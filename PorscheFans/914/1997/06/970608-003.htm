JJ Slowik  wrote:

> Is it possible to pull the computer and its harness without pulling
> the engine or its tin?  How (if at all) do I take the computer
> apart to investigate the connections?

JJ:

1.  Unbolt the computer from the firewall and GENTLY (remember, the
    wires are all 20 years old) pull it out a bit so you can work on 
    it.

2.  Open the strain-relief clamp that surrounds the wires where they
    enter the box.

3.  Once the clamp is open, remove the cover on the side of the box.

4.  Inside the box, the wires terminate in a long plug.  Slowly and 
    carefully unplug it.

Once you've removed the plug, you can clean it, check continuity,
reseat the wires in the plug, replace any wires that are broken,
etc.  If it were me and I wanted to make a big project out of it, I'd 
probably replace ALL the wires.
