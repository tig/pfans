Steve Howitson wrote:
>
> I am thinking about time trialing and autocrossing my 914.  I have a
> tail shift transmission will it hold upto the strain.  What
> modifications should I make to make it stronger.  Or should I put in a
> side shifter?

Steve, the transmission internals are pretty much identical. What really
differs is the linkage and accuracy of your shifts. If nothing else,
replace all of the linkage bushings. You _might_ be able to get away
with a tail shifter in much of autoxing as most of it will involve very
few shifts. But in TT, you'll be shifting alot, and one missed shift
could very well cost you an engine. So, if you can afford a new engine,
you certainly can afford a side shifter instead. It's no guarantee that
you won't miss a shift, but it certainly betters your chances. Also,
you'll be very pleased with how much better the shifting is with a side
shifter.
