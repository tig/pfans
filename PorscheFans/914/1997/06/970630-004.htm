Bernie,

I shopped around all the FLAPS and had a heckuva time.  Finally settled
on one that was _slightly_ smaller diameter, and _slightly_ thicker. 
Once it stretched to fit it was perfect.

I didn't have a turkey baster to refill the trans.  I took a laundry
detergent (liquid) pump, squeezed some tubing over both the sucking and
spitting ends, and viola!  The pump I have does about 1/4 cup per push,
works nicely.  If you have something heavy you can fix to the spitting
end it'll stay put in the trans.  I also thought about making a wire
hanger, which would work too.

Good luck (and don't forget, loosen the fill plug BEFORE the drain
plug)!
