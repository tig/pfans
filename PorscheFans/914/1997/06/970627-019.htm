> Are the trans fluid drain and fill plugs 19 or 17 mm hexes?

The advice for the re&re of the speedo-drive-O-ring is spot on, except for
the trans drain plug size.  It's *definately* a 17mm.  Just buy a 3/8" dr,
17mm allen-head socket from your FLAPS or Snap-On.  

They know it as a "VW trans drain socket."  I prefer the Snap-On one, just
because it's all shiny.  If you're buying from Snap-On, get a "long (3") VW
C.V. socket" too.  It's one of the best ones availible (ie easiest to use)
