> I have taken over production of the 914 combo Gauge from Don Haney.
> This is the gauge that was just recently featured in Pano and originally
> made by Stan Cooper.  I have tried to up hold his excellent product and
> craftsmanship.
> 
> The gauge face and back are made from .060 hot rolled steel.  Both items
> are machined to a tolerance within two thousands of an inch.  I then send
> the front gauge face out for powder coating, in a black that matches the
> current gauges.  Could do custom colors upon request.  Then the lettering
> is printed onto the face in the same font and size, in white.  The rear
> face is plated in cadidium to match, closely, the original gauge back
> color.  The plates are then boxed and ready for shipment along with the
> instructions for the procedure and gauge numbers and senders that work
> with those gauges.
> 
> This gauge is ideal for any 914-6 or conversion, or any 914-4 owner that
> wants to run a oil pressure and temperature gauge along with your fuel
> gauge and not have to add gauges out side the dash.
