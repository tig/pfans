If you have spark at the coil the only things between that and the plugs
are the rotor, cap and wires.  Do you have spark at the end of  a plug
wire?  If so either spark  is not your problem or it is out of timing or
the wires are not in order.  If you do not have spark try replacing your
rotor and cap.  Just some general ideas. Bill S.
