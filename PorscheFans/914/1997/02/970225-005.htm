O boy, you're in for some fun. When you replace the intermediate plate on 
your existing transmission, you disturb the ring and pinion interface. 
That means you have to go through the ring and pinion setup procedure, 
which means you need a 265$ tool to measure backlash, and a dial 
indicator. Basically, you have to determine the gasket thickness between 
the intermediate plate and the tranny housing. If you ask around, you can 
get a copy of the factory procedure from the factory manual (assuming you 
don't hvae this already).
