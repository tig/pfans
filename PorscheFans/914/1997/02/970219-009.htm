I'd like to toss in $.02 about Street vs/ Track pads.

Use both.

The cost of a set of race pads is a minor amount of the cost of
a "day at the races".

The cost of a set of street pads is way less than even the
smallest dent in your beast.

And they don't take that long to change.  Besides, you have to inspect
them before and after any track event.
