>Question that's been bugging me since I purchased my 75 2.0.  My title reads

	Don't know where "716" could have come from, but on a 10-key pad,
7 is in the same row as 9 but left instead of right, 6 and 4 are on the
same row, but swapped left-to-right.  Maybe they were entering "914" on
a keypad and got dyslexic for the first and last numbers?
	Just in case you are talking about the serial number:  For a 75,
the serial number should be "47529xxxxx", where 47 = 914/4, 5 = 1975
model year, 29 = Karmann factory, and xxxxx is which car that year.  You
can find the serial number on the left side windshield pillar, and on
the right side (passenger side) of the front trunk.
