I don't know about the failures (haven't had them on the road yet), but I use 
one 1/4" spacer on each front wheel for two reasons: the McGard wheel locks 
are so long they'd hit the strut otherwise (well, they'd hit something), and 
the fronts always look so undertired, they need to be pushed out.

All this is with 7" wheels with their own 5.25" backspace, so with the 
spacers, the fronts have an effective 5" backspace (compared to the stock 
4-7/8" backspace with 5.5" wheels).  Now if you used 911 style spacers (1" or 
more) . . . that's a different story.
