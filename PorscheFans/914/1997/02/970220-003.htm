All,
There are two types of Pedrini wheels.  One type will work only on the early 
cars as Don had stated.  The other will work on the late model 914. The 
difference being a portion on the inside of the wheel being machined to fit 
with the late model hubs.  If you have a copy of "The 914 Restoration Guide 
to Authenticity, there are photos of the wheels showing the difference.
