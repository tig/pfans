That is correct - converting a 914 to 5 lugs would render it illegal in CSP.

BTW - I don't agree that you have to go to a 5 lug wheel on a relatively light 
car like the 914 or risk cracking the rim.  I have _seriously_ stressed 14" 
four lug rims and haven't had any problems with them.  On a significantly heavy 
car, then you might have a problem.

YMMV.
