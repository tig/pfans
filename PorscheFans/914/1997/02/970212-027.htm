Have you considered the BMW 320i front caliper upgrade? It's detailed on 
Tim's 914 web page: http://www.estinc.com/porsche. The conversion 
requires a small amount of milling on the 320i caliper to center the pads 
on the disc. Also, you would have to bend your own hard line to the 
caliper. The advantage is that you get a considerably larger pad area 
over the stock brakes. I'm planning on doing the conversion someday.
