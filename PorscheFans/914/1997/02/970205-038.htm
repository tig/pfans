favorite ride to commute in, while on assignment.  I won't get into what it's

The point is his attitude about being a "true" sports car owner and driver.  He
preached that if a sports car is delivered to you with a removable top, that
means it is to be _removed_ as you drive off the showroom floor, and tossed

this chap didn't have his top even attached to his ride.  Rain, shine, snow,

figured that if you couldn't obey those rules, you were too much of a stuffed
shirt.
