If the rear wheel doesn't wiggle and doesn't get hot (as compared to the 
other side), then it's something else. It's a process of elimination. 
Check the front wheel bearing, the transmission bearings, and the CV 
joints. Also tires. Noises are often hard to locate (I had a sqweek in 
the bmw that took a long time to find - it was where the throttle shaft 
was journaled in the fire wall (no grease))
