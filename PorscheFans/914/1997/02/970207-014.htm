    "If you have to ask, stick with stock."

My '73 was shipped w/o factory bars.  I bought the pieces,
and paid for the welding, to put the factory bars in.

I even put the rear bar in so I could disconnect it.  (They
say disconnecting it improves handleing; how can I disconnect
it if it's not there?)

You will definately be amazed at the difference the front bar makes.
