The /6 conversion is well documented and off-the-shelf parts are readily 
available. I've seen re-built 2.2's for sale for about 2400$, I don't 
know if that's a great price, but the bigger the displacment, the more 
they cost, of course. Also, servicing a /6 is even harder then the 
regular /4, so I've heard. It's really just a question of money (like so 
many things in life). In terms of any return on your money, restoring a 
genuine 914/6 is better than any conversion. The automobile hobby is 
always a money loser, but you lose less restoring an original.
