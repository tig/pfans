>2) From looking at the Haynes manual, it appears that a funky 55mm wrench
>is needed to remove the shocks from the front strut assemblies.  At the
>moment, I'm leaning toward the idea of finding a shop with such a tool
>and taking the strut assemblies to them (after removing them from the
>car).  Is there a way of disassembling these parts at home without the
>special tool?

Follow the instructions on Tim's Web Page. Very easy to do. I used a pair
of 15 inch pipe wrenches to remove the cap on the strut to get the
cartridge out. 
 
>
>3) How necessary is the special tool to separate the lower ball joint on
>the front suspension?

With those instructions, you don't disconnect the lower ball joint or brake
line. Just come down through the inner fender opening and swing out to the
horizontal. This may sound confusing but check out the web article.
