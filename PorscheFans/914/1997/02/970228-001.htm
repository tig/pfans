Hmmm, my side shifter selector has a bronze bushing between the shaft and 
the holder. Also, the speedo drive is a separate unit (both inner and 
outer races), with the oring on the outside. 

What I've found to be a problem in these transmissions is the way the 
three shift rails slide in the mag case with no bushings. The clearances 
in the intermediate plate are sloppy now, so I bought some bronze 
bushings for a machinist to install for me (just haven't gotten a round 
toit!!)
