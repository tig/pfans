Jeff:

Tilton makes the world standard; you can reach them at 805 688-2353.

To test and set the proportioning valve, find a safe place (like a
big parking lot) and lock the wheels under braking from 40-50 mph
while an assistant watches.

What the assistant should be looking for is which wheels lock up 
FIRST; the idea is to adjust the valve so the front wheels lock up 
just before the rears.
