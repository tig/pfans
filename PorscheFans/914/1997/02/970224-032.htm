For Jeff Lorentzen and other interested parties:

  Jeff asks:

"   When I turn on the left turn signals which flash properly at fenders, 
the left/right arrow indicators in the tach both flash. Is this normal or a
problem? From the wiring diagram I expected just the left to flash.  "

  I don't know about anybody else's car, but both my arrows flash 
together for either turn signal; I have never looked at the diagram, 
but I assumed that there was just one bulb.

"  When I try the right turn signal I get no lights at the fenders, no 
arrow lights in the tach, and hear the relay buzzing.  Do these 
symptoms still sound correct for a bad wire/connection on the 
passenger side turn signal circuit?   "

  Yes they do...   no load on the flasher will cause it to buzz instead of 
operating at the correct speed.  I believe that there are seperate 
contacts for the tach indicator light, again because of the fact that 
that the indicator light is common to both left and right directions; 
hence, failure of the flasher to cycle properly will prevent indicator 
light contact closure.
