John, I have been using the POR15, or the product called WURTH RUST 
GUARD, which appears to be almost the same thing, for about 3-4 years.

I belive it is a good product and have seen positive results in repairs - it
has good adhesion and is easy to work with.  One note however is that if 
you use it on thin metal, perferated holes or with filler try to put some of
the
POR on the back side of the repair also to make sure it is completely 
sealed off from all sides.  It does not convert metal or anything from what
I can tell but it does make a very durable barrier.
