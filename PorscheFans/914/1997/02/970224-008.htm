Dan,

Try unbolting the four bolts that bolt the lid to the hinges (be careful of
the torsion bar loaded hinges and that they don't release suddenly), and you
should be able to raise the lid far enough  from the front to get a 10mm
socket on a long extension on the 10mm nuts that hold the mechanism in place.
You should be able to get to the hinge bolts with the engine lid open.  It
will be tedious, but I think you can get in this way without destroying
anything.
