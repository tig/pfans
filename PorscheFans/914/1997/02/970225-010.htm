the throttle bell crank!  Thanks Mike.

That's something else I didn't bother with doing....I couldn't be
bothered with buying the short 914-6 cable.  The last price I
saw was $55 a few years ago.

For my 1st conversion I simply used the 914 cable and made
it curve to the rear passenger side of the manifold for
the carb.  I made a small bracket where the cable was bolted
to and that went to the carb linkage.

My 2nd conversion used a different method...using a spare
bell crank for a carb.  I bolted the bell crank to the case
using one of the FI bolt holes.  You weld on a "socket" to
the end of the cable and that snaps onto the bell crank
and the other side of the bell crank snaps onto the
carb.  The final curve of the cable was reduced 90 degrees
using the 2nd method.  No binding of the throttle cable
has been noticed.
