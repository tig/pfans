First off, this car will be a rear mounted engine not mid-mounted. The
VW water pumpers are transverse and angled back to get the engine behind
the drive axles. So driving teh rears instead of the front will give you
a rear weight bias. It would be better to try and get a gear box from a
formula car, and then get a Kenedy Engineering package to mount whatever
to the gear box. Then we'll be talking.
