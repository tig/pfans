Just speculating, but could you run a hose from the trans vent to a 'breater 
box'?  (I use one for crankcase/head ventilation on my carbureted engine.) 
 Its just a little metal box with a foam filter in it and three 1/2" 
fittings for the hoses (you'd block off 2 of them).  You could mount the box 
to the sheet metal in the back of the engine compartment and route the hose 
through one of the holes in the back of the engine tin.  This way you could 
easily remove the foam filter for cleaning when it needed it.
