	"They all do that."

>Is there any trick to changing either of these seals.

	As I understand it, the main problem is that this often doesn't
stop the leaks.  Both of these areas have a steel shaft (HARD!) going
through a magnesium hole  (SOFT!).  The bores in the magnesium tend to
wear, and then the joint leaks.
	I've thought of having one of the machinists here at work make up
a steel collar that could be pressed into these places, but I'm afraid
that would just move the problem to the new mag/steel joint.  I don't want
to use up any favors with the guys with the good toys on a non-permanent
fix...
