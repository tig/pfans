When I restored my 74 2.0, I went back and forth on
whether or not to put in the engine pad. The car is
completely stock, and I did quite a bit of research to
make sure that it was restored to "stock," original
condition. But I did decide to leave the engine pad out,
and painted the inside panel body color, like the earlier
cars. To be honest, I did it mostly for looks. But, I
really did not notice any difference in the noise level. I
tried driving it back to back, with and without the pad,
without any real noticable difference in noise or
vibration.
