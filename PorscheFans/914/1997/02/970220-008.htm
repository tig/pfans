Just get a piece of open-cell foam and cut a slit in it.  Then slip the
slit part over the vent, and zip tie it in place.  The foam will catch any
dust, and whatnot, but still allow the trans to breathe.   It's not an
engine, you don't have to account for blow-by.  The vent is there so that
any moisture (condensation) in the transmission can boil itself away.  

Me?  I wouldn't even bother.  Porsche designed the thing quite well, and it
won't SUCK in anything.  Just wipe the thing clear every 3k miles or so,
and change the gear oil every year or two.

Just my 2cents.
