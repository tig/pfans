There were two differemt pedrinni wheels made for 914s. Loook at the back
of the wheel and read the part mumber. One ends with "00" and the other
with "01" The 00 has a domed hub mount and the 01 has a cylinder hub mount
to fit the centering disc on the 73-76 rotors. Both will easily fit the
rear.
