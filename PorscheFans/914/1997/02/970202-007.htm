Hey fellas,

Here a little suggestion for you. The stock VW type fuel filter isn't
adequate for a carb setup, let alone what your EFI injectors need. The 944
system operates at a like pressure and has a real slick filter. 

I'd adapt one of those on the output side of the fuel pump. Ovserve the
direction of flow. The big advantage is that this filter has much more area
and will catch much finer grunge that the stock, square filter. True, the
filter costs $28, but that's cheaper than a fuel injector. Even a GM
Multi-port injector fuel filter will work.

DO NOT use any in-line filters that use glass on the pressure side of the
fuel pump. If that sucker breaks, and they do, there is a BIG fire hazard.
