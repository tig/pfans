On Tue, 4 Feb 1997, Dave Darling wrote:

> 	With luck, I am going to make my rear window stop rattling
> this weekend.  Does anyone have a set of instructions for doing
> the R&R?

OK, here's what I did: First (obviously) remove the seats, backpad, and 
roll bar padding. Then I pulled out the window and pulled the old black 
cualk out. Then I removed the engine lid and the rubber seal at the base 
of the window. Then I cleaned and degreased the glass and the caulk area 
on the car. I ran a thick beadof clear RTV around the frame, and placed 
the window in there. Then I ran another beadaround the edge of the glass 
(it's real messy), and squished it into the recesses with my fingers 
(like caulking a house). Also, I ran another bead across the outside 
bottom edge, making sure all voids were filled. Finally, inspect the seal 
closely for voids and attempt to fill them. Let stand for a day or so, 
then go over the entire window with a razor scraper and solvent to get 
the window clean. The reason you dont' want any voids (especially where 
water can get in) is because if water gets in there and freezes, it'll 
expand and break the seal. I used acetone to throughly degrese the 
surfaces before applying the RTV. Hope this helps.
