Be VERRY carefull when touching the proportional unit. It will start to 
leak instantly ! and the cost to replace with a factory unit is unbelevable.

($45) from summit racing. What one on my friends did was to put his next 
to the Heater control (it has a red handle that can be adjusted through 
stops). and it is very effective. For racing you can use one , because 
the 14 had an adjustable one from the factory. You just have to lock it 
so it can't be adjusted while your racing.
