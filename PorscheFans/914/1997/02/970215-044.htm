The issue I ran into with the absence of the rain tray was water in the
carbs.  Three weeks of rainy weather and not driving the car allowed water to
enter the carbs.  The water reacts with the alloy carb body to create a white
residue that is impossible to remove without redrilling the carb body, I
understand.  I rigged a sloppy carb cover for each bank as I could not find
one for sale.  Of course, for a 911 a carb rain hat set is several hundred
dollars...

If anyone knows where I can buy a good set of rain hats I would love to know.
