Hey,
 Not trying to be a knowit-all or anything, but VW's and 914's originally
had Ceramic fuses not plastic ones! The cheap plastic ones have a
tendency to shrink or actually melt with age. The ceramic originals do
not have this problem (if that's what your trying to fix) The originals
do however tend to corrode if subjected to moisture and battery acid.
Insist on the original and you shouldnt have to subject your fine german
auto to US glass fuses that the concours judges simply do not like!
