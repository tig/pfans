>engine is cutting out.

	The tach needle swinging wildly is a big hint.  It is very likely
the ignition system--the points and/or condensor in particular.  The tach
takes its input from the same coil terminal that the points connect to.
	Bad points will cause cutting out and stumbling.  I am not aware
of any part of the engine that "knows" what gear the transmission is in,
so it is likely that the 4th/5th gear difference is coincidental.
	Regardless, checking the ignition system should always be the
first step in any FI diagnostic procedure.
	Have them examine the points for pitting.
