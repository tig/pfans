>I have a problem.  I want to sand blast the underside of the car. 

I have an opinoin on this, given to me by my bodyguy, the Porsche
restoration expert. He said don't ever sand blast a unibody car. The grit
gets into the spotwelded folds of metal and jams so tightly that you'll
never get it out. Then, when you've got the car all pretty and painted, the
flex of the car body will make those embedded grit bits rub the steel raw,
making for more rust! He used to blast car bodies. now no more. How do you
think he figured that out? When a customer came backwith a car he did that
was rusting again. duh.
