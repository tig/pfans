It is a problem, with the hose fittings and the barbs.  One solution
double clamp them.  A better mouse trap is to use a clamp retention
device.  I found some in a hardware store in town, but also see TIP
carries them now.  Idea is the hose clamp also clamps done a piece of
metal that attaches around the fitting, in this situations I would design
my own.  All it has to do is grab the clamp, to keep it from blowing out.
 Look for them in a well stocked pneumatics store.
