>	I have a problem.  I want to sand blast the underside of the car.  Does

	anyone out their have some plans for a rotissisuri (sp).  I want to remove

	the suspension and role it up on its side and blast away.  How high can I

	raise the car on one side with out running into really bad suspension

	deflection and wheel warpage.  Also should my torsion bars move side to

	side at the end where the adjusting screw is located.  It has a slight

	movement that concerns me.

I wouldn't recommend sand blasting.  It will get sand EVERYWHERE!!!  You can
do fine with a sharp wood chisel and a wire brush on a 4" grinder.  You need
to make a brace that will bolt between the door hinge bracket and the striker
plate bracket, this will help keep the car from sagging in the middle.  You
can always temporarily weld a brace between the roll bar and windshield frame
as well, or door posts too.

If your torsion bushings are a little worn, torsion bar will have some
deflection.  A little isn't too much to worry about on a street car, if your
doing any competition driving I'd replace the bushings with something less
compliant.
