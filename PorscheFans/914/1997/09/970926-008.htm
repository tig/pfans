The main advantage of the SC or Carrera front suspension will be the brakes.
There is no performance advantage to the 911 vs. 914 A arms, the only
difference is that they use different torsion bar splines. Aftermarket
torsion bars are available for either 914 or 911 up to 23mm. As mentioned,
steering racks are the same (you'll need to swap the steering shaft)
although if you are able to get a newer 911 front end we can reason that
that rack may be in better condition. Granted the 911 cross member is
lighter (aluminum) but stronger, I'm not so sure. On a budget, I'd just pick
up some stut/brake assemblies from a SC or Carrera and drop them on your
ball joint. Your 914 shock insert will fit, and as a matter of fact, if your
shopping for inserts, buy the 914 insert, the valving is different from the
911 version.
