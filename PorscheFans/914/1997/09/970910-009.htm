Mike asked about what size tire to buy. Previously, PCA rules did specify
tire circumferences and 205 50's are small enough to put you out of stock
and into improved class.  That said, however, the rules committee changed
that at this year's Parade. I would wait until the new rules are published
(should be in an upcoming issue of Pano) to see what tires are allowed for
the class you wish to compete in. They are changing to specifying tire
sizes rather than tire circumferences.
