I have a solution.  It isn't stock but it solves 2 problems.  I pulled
the front rotors and the rear flanges and had them drilled out and
pressed in standard studs from an autoparts store at about $1.50 each.
I used off the shelf lug nuts with them.  It is much easier to change a
tire with studs and the whole job should cost you less than $100., if
you can remove the parts yourself.   Bill S.
