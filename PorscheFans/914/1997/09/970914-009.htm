 Marshall,
    You need to check out the "Why a 914?" essay on Tim Jones' 914 Fan Page.
I think it says alot.  I bought a 914 1.8L two years ago and have
essentially re-created it.  Not to concours, but to my liking.  I have
touched every part of the car.  I bought and rebuilt a 2.0L engine and
stuffed it in.  New suspension, brakes, exhaust, interior, and paint.  I
will admit that toward the end of the project (as the aggregate expense was
approaching the cost of a 2-year old Z-28) I was wondering at my folly.
I put the car on the road this weekend.  It was a terrific time.  The first
time I carried speed into a corner, felt the car take a "set" and strafe the
apex....ahhhhhh.  I don't think you can find a more exquisitely balanced car
outside of a full-race set up.

   I think MR2's are nice cars, too.  I just don't like anti-freeze.   I
don't know if you have to fall in love with a 914 first in order to put one
back on the road properly...but it sure helped me.

Regards,
