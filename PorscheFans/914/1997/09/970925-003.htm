At 6:51 AM -0000 9/25/97, Marc Le Friant wrote:
>2. Use silicone (I use a GE tube) on the viton seals for the pushrod
>tubes.

	Silicone lube is fine.  Motor oil is fine, and cheaper.  Sili-
cone sealer (RTV, whatever) isn't.  It will frequently work for a week
or a month or more, but it will generally start to leak again pretty
soon.
	The tubes are supposed to slide in and out of the bores in the
case and the bores in the heads.  Half of everything in that motor ex-
pands and contracts at different rates when warmed, so things do move
around.  A good heavy bead of goop will prevent that, up until it fails.
Then your leak is back.
