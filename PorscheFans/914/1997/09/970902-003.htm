porsche914  wrote:

> Ive heard some people say that H4 headlights with 100 watt bulbs,
> will melt the plastic around the lights. Is this true?

    Yeah, usually... Although some manufacturers make bulbs with
    ceramic bases in an attempt to keep the heat out of the sockets.

    Even the ceramic-base bulbs don't completely solve the problem,
    though, and they're REAL expensive:  I've got a pair of
    ceramic-base PIAA bulbs (90W low-beam, 135W high-beam) that cost
    about $75.

    Also...

    High-wattage bulbs draw a lot of current; if you install them,
    you MUST rewire your headlights so they draw power directly from
    the battery through a relay (and a fuse) rather than through the
    headlight switch and the wimpy stock wiring.
