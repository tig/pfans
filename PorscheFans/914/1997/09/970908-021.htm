The Bosch coil does have a ballast risister inside itself. Although not
common, it does fail. When it fails it allows 12v. to run through the
points, quickly burning them. The ballast risister drops voltage, the
condensor "cushions" the blow. Your coil is most likely the problem. A new
Bosch "Blue" coil is only 
$22.15. Hope that all of us on the fan list help you get back on the road today.
