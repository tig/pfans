Brett,

I had the fibreglass replica 916 bumpers installed on my '70 914 and I
thought they looked and fit great.

Problems?  You'll be very careful in crowded city traffic!  They fit
perfectly, though I think a thin strip of black vinyl between the bumper
and the body would actually "unify" the look a bit. The rear bumper also
looked great, though it looked more complete when I added a Porsche
reflector strip between the taillights and retrofitted the 911 cookie
cutter wheels to the car.  Of course the whole project does tend to
change the simple roadster look of the car, but I liked it.  Looked
particularly good in Gulf orange.  Think I got my bumpers from Tweeks in
Indy.  I did not, however, add the 916 fender flares... something I
thought made it look less sleek.
