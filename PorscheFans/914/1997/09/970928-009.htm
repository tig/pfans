OK dudes, I was wrongo about those bolts. It turns out that they are metric
Grade 12.9 (150,000psi tensile strength), which is not the same as US Grade
12, and that the maximum torque is 31 ft#. The flange and stub axle must
also be made of the same strength material to withstand this tension, so
they must be forged and heat treated or something just like the bolts.
