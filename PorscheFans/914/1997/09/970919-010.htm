At 4:44 PM -0000 9/19/97, Mark Jaworek wrote:
>My 914's gas gauge never goes past 3/4 full.  When it is empty & the low
>gas light goes on, I fill it up & I can only put 10 gallons in.  How big
>is the tank in the 914? How can I fix it?

	Fill the tank all the way.  The 914 has a 16 gallon tank--that's
a *lot* of gas.  Unfortunately, many "modern" gas pumps (particularly
the vapor-recovering ones like we have in the Bay Area) won't completely
fill up a wide, flat tank on their own.  A tank like the 914 has.  If you
want to have 16 gallons in the tank, you have to pull the nozzle most of
the way out of the filler neck (requires pulling back the vapor-recovery
bit on those type of pumps) and fill the tank enough to actually see the
gasoline through the filler neck.
