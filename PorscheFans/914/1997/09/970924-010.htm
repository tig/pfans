     In answer to inquiry "can early 911 front calipers be used in front on 
     914 struts?"    Yes.
     
     
     Bolt pattern is same, spacer must be removed (new o-ring Must be used 
     between halves).  Also, some machining must be done.  Material must be 
     milled off the mounting flange on the 911 caliper so it properly 
     centers itself on the 914 rotor.  Also, the new 914 pads might need 
     some material sanded off to fit the 914 rotor into the opening.  The 
     amount of material removed from both caliper mount flange, and pads 
     needs to be determined by measuring your particular parts.
