Andy,

Before you start fly cutting, calc your expected compression ratio.  You
might not need them flycut, just cleaned up real good.  I took mine into my
mechanic telling him I wanted them flycut, and he refused.  I'm glad he
did.  But then again, if you want higher compression and are using an
aftermarket FI or carbs, you probably will want to take off a little.  But
still, calc the compression ratio and your clearances.
