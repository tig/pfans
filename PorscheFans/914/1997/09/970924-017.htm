Just a note to report that my 'bucking on acceleration' problem
appears to have been resolved by installation of new ignition
trigger points. On inspection, the old ones were pretty badly pitted.
I did notice some carbon build-up on the distributor cap contacts
which I cleaned when installing the new trigger points - this
could have contributed to the problem, but I suspect the trigger
points were the real cure.

After verifying the fix with the new points, I installed the new MPS,
but it hardly made a difference. Apparently the old MPS, in combination
with the 500 ohm resistor in the CHT lead, was doing the job even
though it would not hold a vacuum. I do suspect it was ready
to poop out anytime though.
