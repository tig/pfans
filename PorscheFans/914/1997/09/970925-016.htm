	There is a switch down under the red lever.  It grounds the wire
going to it when the red lever is pulled back all the way.
	That wire goes to one of the two switching inputs (dunno what the
electrician's term is) of the heater blower relay on the relay board.  I
think the other side gets 12V when the ignition is on.
	So, check the relay, then check for voltage int the sockets that
the relay plugs into.  Then start checking the wires.  The colors should
be listed on the wiring diagram.
