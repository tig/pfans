 You were right with the "uh oh"...  ;^^)    I'd suggest getting
the correct part for your year's FI.  If you think a richer mixture
will "save your heads", then put a (small) resistor in the head temp
circuit, or dial your fuel pressure up a couple of PSI.  Those changes
are easily (AND CHEAPLY!) reversible, unlike replacing the MPS again.
 BTW, the 74-76 MPSes are identical; same part number and every-
thing.  Only the 73 is different, and it used a different head temp
sensor and brain.
