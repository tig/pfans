John,

Buy the entire suspension...you can use it all!  All the body mounting
points have the same geometry, so its a direct bolt-on.

The 911 A-Arms are preferred because they accommodate larger torsion
bars.  The steering rack is the same part.  The '78 crossbar is
aluminum, and better (stiffer/lighter).  The only part you may not want
to use is the swaybar - underbody style.  The through-the-body is more
easily adjustable.  The A-Arm mounting tabs for the swaybar have to be
changed, but this is no big deal.

We see full front SC suspensions at Swap Meets for $250 to $500,
depending on condition.
