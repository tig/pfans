> resurrected 914.  Will the 914 tranny take a GL4 (like redline
> MT-90) or does it require a GL-5 (like Redline 75W/90NS)?

Rick:

It REQUIRES a GL-5.  There's some controversy as to whether 75W90NS 
or 75W90 is indicated for transaxles with limited-slip differentials 
(Redline's literature isn't exactly clear on that point), but if you 
have an open diff, either one should work fine.  I use 75W90.
