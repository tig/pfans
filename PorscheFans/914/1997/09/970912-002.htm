>My question is:  Where else do flexible rubber lines lurk on the 74 1.8?

Yes, there is hose between the rigid plastic lines and the tank. There are
also several bits by the fuel pump and wround the fuel filter (replace it).

The plastic/metal tubing in the shifter tunnel should be replaced also,
depending on how adventerous you are. I replaced mine with copper, some
people use steel, or rubber. The bad thing with the stock tubing is that the
plastic tubing is just slid onto the barbed end of the steel lines, no
clamp. The plastic gets brittle with age, so it can fail.
