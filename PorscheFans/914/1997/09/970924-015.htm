Porsches - boy, what a mistake!) now that I've got the new MPS
installed.  At idle, the CO fluctuates between 1.5 and 3.0, and the

rpm, the mixtures holds at a steady 2.5, and the hydrocarbons drop to a

so I've ordered a new Bosch Blue to replace it.  Anyone think this will
work?  The theory is that at lower RPM, the coil isn't making enough
