The job took less than 1.5 hrs. Rate manual for the job is 4.2. Basically you
need to of course remove the wheel and caliper. (Haynes says you need to
bleed the brakes) we didn't do this, just pulled the caliper and suspended
the caliper from the engine by a piece of wire. Remove the rotor and hub and
the driveshafts (both). Insert the tool as per directions and the bearing
will come right out. Installation is the reverse (copyright Haynes Manual).
Make sure that everything is torqued correctly and you are there. As to a
