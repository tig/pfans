Rick, I had a similiar situation with my 914 after it sat for along time. The
clutch disk froze itself to the flywheel or pressure plate or both. When I
pressed the clutch in it would not disengage. The fix? I put the car in 5th
grear and hit the starter. The starter torque was greater than that of the
frozen disk. it broke the bond instantly. The car started right up, I put it
in 1st gear and drove off. Try it. Good luck.
