If you are going to attempt to manufacture a swaybar, please remember that
you'll have to use heat treated, stress releived material.  Plain 4031 may
work to some extent without heat/stressing, but carbon steel won't, and I
wouldn't try either one without heat/stress treating.  Remember the swaybar
is also a spring of sorts.  Also you are gambling with spring rates too.
 Wouldn't it be easier to go to a few swap meets and find a good used one???
 Good luck though, and I hope you can do it! :  )
