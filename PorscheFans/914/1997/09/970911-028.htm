When I decided to go with longer lug bolts for my empi's, I ended up
getting some Audi 5000's at the local graveyard. They probably wont work
with alloys. They have about 1/4" more thread (give or take) than stock
and the bolt head is 17mm instead of 19mm. I have a star wrench with both
sizes. They have the same countour as the stock bolts to mate with the
wheels.The bonus over stock is that this set of 16 was $6! These are a
good option for anyone with steel wheels or other that don't need the
super longs. Aren't the factory alloy lug bolts longer than 1.5"?
