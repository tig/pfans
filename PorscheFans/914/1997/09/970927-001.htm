I would start at the beginning.  I would take out #1 plug get a stick,
something that will not hurt anything like a 1/4" dowel, and turn the
engine by hand till the piston is at TDC.  Mark this spot on the
flywheel.  Every 1" is roughly 10 deg.  so if you make another mark an
inch to the right (I think) that will be 10 deg BTDC.  Or do what I do
use a light with a built in advance and the TDC mark.  I have a V8 in
mine and did this but the process should be the same.   Good luck,  Bill
