The only safe place is possibly the center of the suspension, right under
the steering rack (*after* you remove the cover). I haven't tried it, so I
don't really know. I usually jack the car up at the "button" on each side
just aft of the fender well, and put a jackstand under the rocker. The car
is so light though that using a hydraulic jack in the center makes a lot of
sense.
