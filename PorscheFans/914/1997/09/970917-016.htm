Alan, the decel valve can be bought used from your local VW type2 wrecker. 
 Its a standard Bosch part, no need for the "Porsche 914" one, it's
availible from the FI type4 engines on the late "breadloaf" and early
vanagons.

Just ask them for the decel valve from a fuel-injected type4 engine.  Get
the one that looks the same, and it's the same piece (75-78 I think, not
sure though).  The ones that look different work too, just that they don't
look original--they are the "improved" version.

Of course, the alternative is your local VW dealer.  If you don't know what
the original piece looks like, have a look at your fuel pressure regulator.
 It is about the same size and shape, only it has 3 hoses going in/out of
it--a skinny one at one end, and fat one from the middle and at the other
end.  Try asking for the 76 vw van (2.0L) decel valve.
