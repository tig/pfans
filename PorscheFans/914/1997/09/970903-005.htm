> I'm running the Yoko 195-60 A509's right now on my '75 2.0, but since I
> have acquired some Fuchs, I'll probably go with 205-60's with a more
> agressive tire, maybe AVS intermediates, or something else extra sticky.
> Why not. I'm only putting on 3K to 5K miles annually, so even gumballs
> are good for two or three years.

That all depends on how you drive them. You'd be supprised how fast a
set of race tires can be consumed if improperly inflated or over-driven.
The AVS is a great tire, what I use for street tires on my 914, but they
do last longer than a set of R1s or A008s and they have tread that lasts
a good long time, and in the rain (real rain) I prefer to have a few
32nds of tread rather than none at all. While race tires may ultimtely
last 5k miles or so (and even that's a stretch) they won't have much if
any tread left on 'em when they get that far.
