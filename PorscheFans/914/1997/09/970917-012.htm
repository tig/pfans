I am having a problem with my 1976 914 2.0 ltr. It is a problem with 
the fuel injection. The fuel injection is stock.
The engine was flooding and throwing raw gas out the tail pipe 
when  I attempted to start the engine.
I have replaced the temp sensor II on the head. I have checked out 
the temp sensor and it shows 2500 ohms cold. I have also replaced the 
pressure sensor. I checked out the pressure sensor and pins 7 & 15 is 
100 ohms, pins 8 & 15 are 350 ohms. I tried blowing into the sensor 
to test the diaphragm and it held a vacuum.
I checked the pressure regulator and the pressure is 29 psi. 
I am going to change the oil and replace the oil filter, In case gas 
got into the oil. Is there any thing else I should checkout before I
