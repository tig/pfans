Once you have the bolts out, you usually have to drive a screwdriver or
something between the CV and the flange. They usually get stuck lke that.
There are 2 roll pins also that you need to keep track off, sometimes one
roll pin will stock in the flange and the other will be in the CV. You need
both of those roll pins, so don't loose them. You'll need a new gasket of
course, also.
