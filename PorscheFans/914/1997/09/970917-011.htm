Thanks from me also to DD.  I busted mine 10 years ago by resetting the
tripometer while in motion.  From experience, Dave warns of not letting
the rollers get loose.  From experience *I* warn of not bending the shaft
while driving it back through the rollers (I did manage to straighten it
out enough).
Think seriously before using a dab of glue.  I didn't use glue and my
roughened shaft gripped the gear just fine.  It gripped so strongly that
it took me a long time to push the shaft back in all the way. Glue would
proabably have caused a lot of trouble here.
