Just got the notice that SB42, the CA smog amendment to change it to exempt
cars 25 years and older just was voted on by the Assembly Appropriations
committee and passed 20 to 1.  This was after being put on suspension
earlier due to concerns that the state would lose money from smog fees (
not due to anything smog related).  Hopefully the bill will continue on.  I
don't know what the next step is.
