  As much as I apreciate the Star wars movies (and I do), I am 
compelled to point out their misuse of an astronomical term.  The 
parsec is a unit of distance... not a unit of time or velocity.  It is 
aproximately 3.26 light years, and is that distance from the earth at 
which a star exhibits exactly one arcsecond of parallelax at opposite 
ends of a diameter of the earth's orbit.  Hence the term "parsec"... PAR 
for parallelax, and SEC for one second of arc.
