Shrickumar:

If you have the late pilot bearing, it is threaded at the very bottom so that
you can insert a bolt and the bolt will force out the pilot bearing as you
tighten the bolt.  I believe the thread size is 10 x 1.5.  You will need a
bolt with a lot of threads, so a non shanked bolt of the correct lenght will
work the best.
