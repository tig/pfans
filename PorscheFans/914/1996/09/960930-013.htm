Thanks to everyone who responded to me about the best way to clean the
engine compartment.  The method I chose was to soak the he?l out of
everything, use a paint scraper to get the thick stuff off (including old
undercoating) then take a stainless wire wheel on my drill to what was left.
I then sprayed the exposed metal with a good anti-rust primer, then a coat
of Rustoleum black over that.  This weekend I plan to cover all that with
'new' undercoating.  We're just talking about the areas above the
transmission and the exhaust deflector heat shield for now.  The actual
engine compartment comes next, but now I have a plan.

Wow, I'm glad I didn't start sandblasting...
