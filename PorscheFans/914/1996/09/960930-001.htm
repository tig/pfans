Procrastinators, learn from my mistakes.  At the P.C.A. autocross I broke
the clutch cable on my 73 914.  Although I had always heard you should
carry spare clutch and accelerator cables, I had put off buying them.
Luckily for me, the cable broke just as I was parking after my first run and
a friendly, helpful P.C.A member had a spare cable.  It is also necessary to
carry the fittings (nuts, wedge) on the clutch end of the cable since they
can fall off and be lost.  I'm ordering spares today.
