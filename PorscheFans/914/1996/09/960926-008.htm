Yes, the _Hunter_ headers a good stuff, but they are not even close the
Pete Webers work. Yes, Kirbys stuff is cheaper, but the stuff Pete turns
out is far supeirior. Hunter headers are 4-1 with a flexible collector.
Weber headers are 4-2-1 with fixed collectors and are available in
various tube sizes and can be tuned for various RPM ranges. The flexible
collectorsa are nice for longevity but can leak after a time. Webers
work is expensive though, and custom. 
And I wouldn't supprised if there was something out there better than
Weber too.

Just don't say _no one beats x_, because inevitably someone can.
