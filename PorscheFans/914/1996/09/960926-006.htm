	If you're looking for cheap, it's probably cheaper to weld up
the old one.  Or, I have a used AutoMashin' unit that is slightly
damaged.  The pickup tube is crushed at the top (I guess I over-torqued
the retaining nut?) and the o-ring has squeezed out on one side.  The
bottom of the cylindrical trap is bowed up slightly in the middle.  I
think that was the only damage.  I was not really impressed with the
quality, but it kept the engine from starving in the corners...
	BTW, the one I'm using is an old (NOS?) Garretson piece, that
uses a stock cover plate as the bottom.  Welded to a cylinder to make
the trap.  The rest of it is identical; I think that Jerry Woods designed
the Garretson one, and AutoMashin' bought the rights to it.  If "bought"
is the right word; I heard a rumor that there was some sort of disagree-
ment on exactly what should be paid when...
