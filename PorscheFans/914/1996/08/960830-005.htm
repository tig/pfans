You can tee off the hose to the MP sensor.  To do it without a tee, you 
can remove the small hose where it attaches to the decel valve or 
remove the hose that attaches to the retard side of the dist.  If you 
hook to the dist retard hose, it will show full vacuum only with 
throttle closed.  All three places better give the same reading.
