Van,

There's an inner seal too.  It's really not that hard to get to.

You'll have to pull the injector assy off completely to get at it,
but it will probably be worth the effort.

GPR (or similar) will sell you a complete set of seals for $10 or so.

While you're in there, you can check the condition of the injectors
too.  Look for cracks, etc.

And I would very, very strongly advise *against* RTV (or any other
foreign substance) in that area.
