Jeffrey Canaday wrote:

> This is in response to Andy Lester's question about the pins in 914
> harness connections. If you are talking about the little female mini
> spade lugs about 1/8 inch wide. The factory versions have a little tab
> in the back that should be raised up a tad to catch on the connector
> block when inserted all the way. These get bent down when you remove the
> lugs from the connector block. Just pop them back out with a swiss army
> knife blade, SO's cutlery may work as well :-). I have found after
> market lugs have the tab positioned too far forward to seat properly and
> the lugs push back out. Be very careful. I have found the wires to be
> very brittle and/or corroded and easy to break right at the crimped
> joint.

Yes! Those wires are _very_ fragile. I learned this the hard way.
