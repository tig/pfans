Rick Eberle asks

>I just rebuilt the engine in my 914, and after putting in eight new 
>pushrod tubes and new seals, my engine is still leaking oil onto the heat 
>exchangers. I know the books all say never to use silicone etc, but is 
>there any reliable way to seal these things???

Did you install them dry? At least put a light coat of silicone GREASE (not
silicone gasket stuff) on them before installing. RTV-type silicone sealers
aren't designed to seal this sort of thing, which is why you're not supposed
to use it, but a thin coating of grease helps the o-rings seat better.
