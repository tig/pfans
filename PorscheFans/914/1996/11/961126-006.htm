First, thanks for all of the brake pad replacement advice. We changed the 
rear pads only yesterday. It went pretty quickly, we'd done it before, 
and we did not even need a tool to push back the rear caliper pistons -- 
I had forgotten but turning the nuts on the caliper moves the pistons; 
so moving in new pads was easy as can be. The usual sticky pistons always 
happens on the front; wait until spring for that.
