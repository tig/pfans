At 6:20 AM -0800 11/27/96, Thom Kuby wrote:
>Does anyone have any comments and /or experience with these "viton" pushrod
>tube seals that I keep hearing about?

	According to what I read, the Viton seals hold up to heat a whole
lot better than the factory seals.

>We just replaced the seals on a friend's 1.7, using the OEM factory seals,
>like two weeks ago and one tube is already leaking again...

	I don't know that Viton seals in particular would fix this.  It
sounds as if there's some other problem.  Check the hole in the head where
the tube goes, if there are rough or sharp edges (e.g., from the casting
seam) knock those down with some sandpaper (CLEAN UP VERY CAREFULLY!).
Maybe you forgot to lube the seals when you put them in?  Engine oil or
that silicon lubricant stuff--*NOT* silicon sealer (RTV, "goop", etc.).
	You can get Viton seals from your local Bug shop (same seals as the
other Type IVs) or from GPR.
