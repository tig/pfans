I have been using viton O rings since my Corvair days in the early 
70's.  They were needed for cars with AT, otherwise leaks would begin 
within 40K miles.  Leaks can begin even sooner in a 914 due to 
overheating making the originals brittle.

A leak in a week is surely a damaged O ring.  Care and feeding is 
required to avoid slicing a piece out of it in the head while 
inserting.  I use a socket on the end of an extension to insert in the 
end of the tube for pushing.  I then wiggle it around a bit to feed the 
head's O ring.  A small screwdriver to help feed the O ring will help.
