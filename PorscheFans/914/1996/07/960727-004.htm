I'm starting to race in SCCA IT where Kumho's are relatively popular. A
bit cheaper than R1's; not noticably slower and seem to last as long or
longer. Popular opinion still considers the Hoosiers "faster", but a
buddy actually turned faster times at a test day on his Kumhos than on
his Hoosiers.

For some reason, they don't seem to have caught on in PCA circles - not
sure why.
