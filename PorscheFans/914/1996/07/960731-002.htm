Frank....

My experience has been with all of them.....

Boge Turbo....very good replacement/good for street/ firm,controlled but
              not harsh ride.
KYB...........inexpensive for gas shocks/stiff ride and will raise car
              some....high pressure gas.
Bilsteins.....very good quality last for ever...high pressure gas...very
              firm controlled ride...pricey...adjustable rear spring
              perch...
Koni.(red).....low pressure gas...very good quality last for ever...have
              ajustable rear spring perch and all adjust for rebound
              from about like Bilsteins to rock hard....the Yellow Sport
              models are stiffer yet....

It amouts to what your butt and budget can take....
just so happens I have a front set of KYBs and a F/R set of Boge Turbos that I will
let go for a reasonable sum...each have less than 100mi on them....
My son's street 914 is running Bilsteins and my autox 914 is running KYBs....
