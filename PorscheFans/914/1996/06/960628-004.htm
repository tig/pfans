	I don't know much about it, but Bowlings automotive calcs has
a program for calculating the "proper" intake runner length. I think
it says that by tuning the intake to the proper length, you can take
advantage of the natural harmonics to set up air pulses which will
help force more air in. I think the caveat is that it only works at a
predetermined rpm which is part of the calculation and is usually
chosen as the RPM of max power.
