Good point Jeff. Also Dynomax. I've heard of people using simple turbo
mufflers as well. But as for back-pressure, sometimes more is better. It
all depends on where you want your power. Lowering the back-pressure
helps higher flow at the top end, but you can also lower it to the point
that the bottom end suffers, sometimes badly depending on the engine.
