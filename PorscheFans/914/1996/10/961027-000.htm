Thom,

You can just remove the rear springs/shocks as a unit
but you'll need new upper spring perches for your new
shocks if you don't want to bother with the spring 
compressor.  If you put the assembly on the ground and
just use a wrench, the spring top will FLY off with GREAT
velocity.  

Best way around that:  beg/borrow/steal a cheap
spring compressor, save the old upper spring perches.
You may want new bump stops at this time.

Brian "putting a motor in my new 914" Ng
