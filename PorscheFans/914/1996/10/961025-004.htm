Hello Bryon:

We also have semi-strict emissions testing in Ohio.  This doesn't answer
your question directly, but I have "gotten around" (legally) emmisions
testing by registering my 75 914 2.0 as a collector's vehicle.

By definition, a collectors vehicle is not your primary vehicle, and you are
in the process of restoring or maintaining the car.  That's all.

As a side benefit, my registration costs are less due to no testing required.
