Assuming you have a digital voltmeter, your battery is not 
getting/taking a full charge.  It should read about 13.8 volts while 
charging as it nears full charge.  It should read close to 12.6 volts 
after the surface charge has drifted off.  11.5 volts is a discharged 
battery.  

If it had a shorted cell, it would read about 10.5 volts after the 
surface charge dissipated.
