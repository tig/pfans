The main point behind getting a bursch are the tuned header collectors.
That is where most of the benefit comes from. It's just so far
downstream that it can only do so much. As for the muffler, I would
hazzard a guess and say just about anything you can fit in there will do
well. Go for a supertrapp. They're cheap and they're tunable.
