Beware of rust. I own a 1972 914 and rust is my problem. look for the rust in
the engine compartment under the battery and at the front of engine shelf.
Also I've been told that the floor of the rear trunk under the lights rusts.
One last real bad place is the floor under the rear window. Good luck finding
a car.
