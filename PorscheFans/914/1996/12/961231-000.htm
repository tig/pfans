IMHO....While your under there you should replace all of the studs...I know
may be an overkill but really they are not expensive and not too hard to get
out....when I replaced the stock headers on the 73 with SS I took the time
to put new studs in...bought the new studs and copper nuts that have the
flare from AA...all you have to do is take time use Liquid Wrench liberaly
and double nut the studs and they will come out...remember they have be in
there 20+ years and why take any chances with your time and $$$....
