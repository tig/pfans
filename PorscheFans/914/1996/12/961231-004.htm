Derek,

You probably have condensation under the relay board.  One quick fix is to
pull out the board and spray the back with a anti-moisture solvent (is that
what you'd call it?).  I've actually seen wd-40 do the trick.  The
long-term fix is to scrape all the gunk off of the bottom of the relay
board, check all the connections and reseal it back up.

Of course with a lot of condensation around, it could be elsewhere in the
electrical system (plug wires, cap and rotor, etc.). 

my .02.
