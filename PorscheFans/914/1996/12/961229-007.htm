To Jim T. and others. 

18 to 25ft-lbs using mechanical-type lock nuts. The factory used copper/brass
because they can be cut off the studs if you screw them up. At this late date
these would be almost non-existant on a 914 because most mechanics toss them
and use something other than stock. 
The muffler to exhaust manifold for a 1.7/1.8 prior to 1975 is the same. The
2.0 is 30 ft-lbs.

Beware of mechanics who think they know more than the factory. They have
ruined more Porsches than any other cause or defect.

Jim Pasha
