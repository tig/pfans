Note to everybody using this new list: notice where your responses go. The
return addresses for each post is the sender's private e-mail address. If you
reply automatically, your reply goes only to the original poster. If you want
to put your reply on the mail list, be sure to manually change the address to
PorscheFans-@ioio.com or cc (carbon copy) the
appropriate list, as in porschefans-911@ioio.com etc. Otherwise the rest of
us will lose the thread.

On the other hand, this avoids the embarassment of accidentally posting stuff
to the whole world when you just wanted to reply privately to the original
poster.

of Deep Thought at the moment and I don't have the software manual here...
