OK...
- rotate dist.  so rotor points to #1 mark on rim of dist.
- loosen 10mm long bolt and turn dist. counter clockwise
-unplug the ECU line (the aforementioned four prong line) from dist.
-undo (love that word...) 13mm bolt holding dist. onto engine.
- remove dist.
- triggers are at base of dist., this is the plate where the ECU line plugs in.
-there are (should be, anyway) two screws holding this plate to dist. body
-remove these two screws
-wiggle the plate out...these are your trigger points.  this is a horseshoe
arrangement.
-like any other set of points, check for wear, etc.  if they are old and
look beat to shit, I'd change em.  Best price I was able to find was @
Carlsens Porsche in Palo Alto...but look around.  Prices are all over the
place.

Keep me (us) posted...and be patient...you are eliminating problems one by
one. You'll get it eventually.
