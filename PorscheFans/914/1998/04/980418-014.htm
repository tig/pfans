Hi Mike.
For static timing, you would:
1. Hook up a 12 volt light bulb from the positive side of the coil to a
ground.
2. Rotate the engine until the distributor rotor is approaching #1
cylinder.
3. Take the cover off the timing hole, and move the engine until the 5
deg BTDC is    centered.
4. Ignition on, turn the distributor until the light just comes on.
5. Tighten the distributor clamp, and check it one more time by moving
the engine    back and forth.
