>Anyone use a product called "Correless" or something like that. It is a

I use Corroless extensively. It is great and very differnet from POR15.
Corroless is a combination rust converter and primer. You do not need to
topcoat it if you put on 2 coats of the stuff. It has glass flakes in it to
make it tougher. I read somewhere that POR 15 is not a converter at all, but
I have no details about it. I have scraped away portions of Corroless
painted areas to find black magnetite, so it does convert the rust. You want
to make sure when you go to treat a rusty spot that you remove the paint
around the spot enough to reveal a border of clean shiny metal. Rust creeps
under the paint so this is a must-do.
