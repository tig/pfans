Do you actually have it apart yet, or are you looking at pictures? This is
what I did: Get 32mm combination wrench from Sears (really huge!), with the
shaft out of the tranny and the clutch installed in the engine (which would
be laying around on a dolly) with flywheel lock in place, stick the shaft
into the clutch. Note that the shaft is now prevented from turning. Support
the shaft right about at where the nut is with a milk crate or something
sturdy. You don't want to bend the shaft when you go to undo the nut. Unbend
the securing washer flap and undo the nut with the huge wrench. Simple.
