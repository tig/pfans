Mike, I should also mention that I did all of my trim this way. All chrome and
aluminum trim was painted satin black. 10 years and it still looks like new. I
also painted my chrome bumpers body color, they were dented and pitted badly,
this way and they too look great. I had used DuPonts Vari-Prime self etch
primer, I don't think its available any longer due to the chemicals used in
it. There are newer self etch primers available now. The key is to clean the
chrome areas well, sand with 400 grit, corser if using a building primer, and
an etching primer.
