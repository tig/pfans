Gary:

After 25 years, most 914 windows don't crank as easily as they 
used to, so window cranks break a lot.

You can take your doors apart and clean, lubricate, and realign all
the pieces inside, or... You can take your broken window cranks to
your local VW dealer and ask him to find a replacement that'll fit.

I've got a pair of cranks from -- I think -- a VW Rabbit; they look
stock, they're MUCH stronger than the flimsy 914 cranks, and they
only cost about $12 or so.
