I learned a trick from a Diesel Mech friend of
mine. When troubleshooting a cutting out problem that
seems to be electrical in nature attach a jumper from 
the + terminal on the coil to the battery + terminal
and see the car still cuts out. Then attach the ground
via jumper to the chassis ground. I am planning on trying
this today. It seems my problem is starting to look like 
an electrical problem (suggested also, by Wayne at Pelican
Parts) the way the engine just cuts out while running even
though the fuel pressure is good. Does jumpering the coil
as a test seem to make sense??
