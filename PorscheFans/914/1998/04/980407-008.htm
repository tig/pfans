A fellow "Lister" posted regarding using a "Unisyn" for syncronising carbs.
After using a Unisyn for many years, and then getting a "Syncrometer", I would
strongly recommend the Syncrometer as it doesn't restrict the air flow.  The
Unisyn however must restrict air flow to get a reading and changes air flow.
If you can afford it, get two Syncrometers, one for each carb, this makes
balancing much easier.
