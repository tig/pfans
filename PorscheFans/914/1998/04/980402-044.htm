 to install the temp sensor II, try this method: get a cheap old 13mm
deep socket and saw a channel 1/3 to 1/2 the way down one wall of the
socket starting from the ratchet end, to accomodate the wire on the
sensor. I think this makes a tool not unlike an oxygen sensor tool.
that's really all it takes. If you have skinny fingers like I do you can
hand-start the threads in the hole by pulling out the #3 spark plug and
inserting a finger through the hole in the sheet metal to guide the
sensor in. Somehow this takes me less time than blindly aiming the
socket and sensor through the sheet metal. Good luck to you.
