Ben,
Thought I would join in. Good advice about the channel lock pliers.
I use two screwdrivers. Medium size. One on each side at the same time. Try
and work the end of the screwdriver blades in to where the dust cap meets
the metal at the back. Push both screwdrivers toward the 914 at the same
time and it should pop off. Getting it back on it tough too. Get it on
straight by hand and pop with a soft hammer once and then walk the hammer
around in 90 degree increments, giving it a pop.  Better word than pop is
tap, tap. Too hard and it will fall back off.
