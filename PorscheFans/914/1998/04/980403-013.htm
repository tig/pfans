At 11:47 PM -0000 4/2/98, Marc Le Friant wrote:
> to install the temp sensor II, try this method: get a cheap old 13mm
>deep socket and saw a channel 1/3 to 1/2 the way down one wall of the
>socket starting from the ratchet end, to accomodate the wire on the
>sensor.

	I did the same thing, but I started the channel from the bottom
of the socket, the part away from the ratchet.  That way, I don't have
to get the connector up through the square hole in the socket for the
ratchet.  It's probably not as strong as going the other way, but it has
worked for me (twice so far).
	I used a Craftsman socket, because it's not *that* expensive,
and if it dies I can always get another one.
