I have a general method for finding noises that locates them fairly
well.  I bought a stethescope from my local pharmacy for about $7 took
the head off it and put the tube from a long cheepie pen on it.  By
moving the end of the pen tube around I can get within an inch of the
noise.  Exhaust noises are even easier because the gas noise roars up
the tube.  Bill S.
