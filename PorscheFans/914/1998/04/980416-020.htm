One other thing you might want to try for that factory-fresh look is to
Gravitex it first, then paint it.  Gravitex is a stone/chip protector
like a hard undercoating.  It provides the perfect factory-like "bumpy"
look.  It comes in black and white and you'll need a special gun (cheap)
and a compressor to apply it.
