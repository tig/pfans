   Boy, this advice seems right on the money, I've already welded the
ears back on two of the pipes where they had started to crack away from
the pipe. I used a thick glass plate that I had to check for flatness,

lbs. of torque, so that must be the number, couldn't find it in my
microfiche manual. He also suggested loosening the pipes a little and
making sure that you can see copper  all around the pipe where it fits
