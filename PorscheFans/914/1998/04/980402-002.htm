For those of you who may be in the UK over the next month, the Design Museum
in London is going to be the place to be... Porsche AG have kindly loaned
Ferrys 914/8 for a static Exhibition celebrating 50 years of Porsche. The
914/8and several other rare Porsches turned up yesterday and the Porsche Club

hundred photos. The car is truely amazing with many weird and wonderful
additions... A full report is to be published in our club magazine, but for
those who are in the UK then I strongly recommend that you spend a day in
London, viewing this car.
