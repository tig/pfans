>4. Dash renewal, not the cover. Any advice on the difficulty and step by
>step mode of instruction of installing a NEW dash? Any pitfalls?
>

Have the Ben Gay ready when you're finished. It is a contortionists
nightmare. You'll need universal joints for the sockets, plus stubby
wrenches. You have to take the instrument cluster out, of course. There are
2 or 3 nuts behind it in addition to the several others that hold the dash
in. You have to remove the lower knee pad first, there are nuts behind it also.

work fine. Rub with a cloth before it sets.
