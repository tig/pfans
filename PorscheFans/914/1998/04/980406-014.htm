>5. Hanging things. Under the rear, the speedometer and clutch cable,

>cables. These hang down on most 914's I see around here. What is the

>tie points or whatever to keep them hidden but still functioning

I made a couple of clamps out of thin sheet metal.  Get some stips about
3/4 inch wide and about 3 inches long.  Drill a hole in one end so a
10mm or 13mm head bolt will fit through it easily (I made a couple of
each).  Then bend the other end into a "U" shape.  Go under the car and
find a few convenient spots to bolt these on (I have one on one of the
side bolts of my tranny, and one on a 10mm (I think) bolt that is
somewhere near the thermostat if I remember right.  Route your cables
through the "U" and then close the "U" a bit so the cables stay in
place.  Cheap, easy, not permanent, and effective.
