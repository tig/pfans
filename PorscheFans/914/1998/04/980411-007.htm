Hi Bill,
I bought that stuff about 10 yrs ago at a auto show here in Atlanta.  It
works great filling in holes in Coke cans, but  on anything heavier it
won't work well.  You need LOTS of heat and you have to "scrape" the rod
on the surface to catalize it.  I'm a pretty good welder/brazer/solderer
but I could never get it to work.  The Coke can demo is impressive to
others though!!
