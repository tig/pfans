Hi, thanks for the compliment.  I formed the flares by grafting on a set
of rear quarters from another car, this allowed additional metal and a
gradual flare starting just behind the door and ending just before the
tail light.  It is very subtle but is flared over 4inches out at the bottom.
Then I hand formed the "bump" above the tire by heating & stretching
the metal by hand w/ hammer/dolly etc.  

The baseball bat method can't go out far enough for what I wanted.
Painted in my garage addition/paint booth (I do bodywork on the side)
