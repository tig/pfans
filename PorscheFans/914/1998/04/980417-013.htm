> Jack up and crawl under your car, and block one of the wheels, engine off,
> trans in 5th.  When you turn the other wheel, it will rotate the engine.
> Watch the hole in the transmission bellhousing, and you will see the
> flywheel.
> 
> Turn until the flywheel mark lines up with the case seam under the car.
> This will give you BDC #1 or #3, which also happens to be TDC #2 and #4.
> Dab some paint on the impeller blade, and just put your timing light on #2
> or #4 plug wire.  To check for advance, either use an advance timing light,
> or ask someone for the distance in inches from TDC to 27 deg.
> 
> My flywheel has a file mark at 180 deg, courtesy of the machinist who
> re-surfaced it.
>
