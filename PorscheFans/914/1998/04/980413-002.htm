Thru bolts should not be reused; they stretch.  You can get new ones.  Order
bolts for the rear of a 911 with unvented brakes (69-71 T).

Don't bother with new O rings for the lever.  They are only a dust shield.
And new round covers are not available.  You'll have to braze up your old ones
or have new ones fabricated.
