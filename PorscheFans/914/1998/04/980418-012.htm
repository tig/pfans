If you have a Home Depot in your area, there you will find a 110 volt
Lincoln MIG welder for $299 + sales tax. I recommend it for MIG as you can
later buy gages and use gas as your shield which will greatly reduce the
splatter you will get if using without gas. The welder mentioned comes with
a held held eye shield, spool of flux coated wire which you use without gas.
A video is also included for a very short tutorial on getting started.
It meets your under $300 at $299.
If welding sheet metal, start on the lowest possible setting to keep from
blowing holes in your metal. Practice on scrap sheetmetal.

Hope this helps you.
