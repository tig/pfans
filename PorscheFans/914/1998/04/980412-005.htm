Well, thanks to the list, I got the rear brakes stripped down to a couple
of hunks of metal. Now they are ready to go to get blasted and then plated.
I'm going to clean up the heads of the bolts and re-blacken them with a kit
I got from Caswell Plating. These bolts are obsolete, to say the least (M7
grade 12.9!)

I used my new Dremel tool (what a great toy!) with a cutoff disc to slice
an "L" in that little metal cover opposite the parking brake lever. Then I
pried them out.

Are new covers available?
Are the O rings for the parking brake lever available? (16x25mm,
rectangular cross section)
I also need new bleeders, mine are not worth cleaning up.

Tip: the O rings that go between the caliper halves are the same as the
ones that are used in the stock VW front brakes.
