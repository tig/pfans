>Thanks for the reply, Jeffrey.  Where would they be crossed?

	When the clutch and accelerator cables get crossed, they
are getting wrapped around each other in the center tunnel.  It
happens sometimes when the clutch cable (especially) or sometimes
the throttle cable get replaced.  The new cable wraps around the
old one sometimes when it gets threaded in.
	The symptoms are as Jeff mentions--press the clutch in,
the motor revs up, even with your foot off the accelerator.
