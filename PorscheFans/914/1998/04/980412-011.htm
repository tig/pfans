yup.. there's a locktite product called form-a-thread that is for
putting into the holes & then running the bolt in & re-making the
threads.. i used it to pack into the inserts once they were back in
place & then put in the studs, & no problems since.. there is also a
stud with a fatter head-side thread that you can find at better porsche
shops, this will get you down to a single insert.. but i'd still use the
epoxy along with it

btw, my heads came back fom MSHP with this crappy loose, doubled insert
setup & I didn't discover it til hanging the pipes... lesson to be
learned about checking these things BEFORE we bolt the heads on!!
