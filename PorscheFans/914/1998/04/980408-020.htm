Back in March, Perry Kyle posted a message about his method of pressure
bleeding the 914 brake system.  He used an extra resevoir cap fitted with a
tire valve and clip-on air chuck. 10-15 lbs. of pressure from an air tank,
and voila, a one man brake bleeding operation is performed.  

Perry let me borrow the Tool, and it worked out great!  This is by far the
easiest way to bleed brakes I've ever seen.  Plus, my Wife is happy she
isn't pumping the pedal anymore.  See, I usually solicited her help right
after she got home from working out at night!

Thanks go out again to Perry who helped me in a jam.  Now I'm going to make
my own pressure bleeding tool, and actually enjoy bleeding the brakes!
