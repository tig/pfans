I just used a stack of brass fittings - two male to female NPT topped off by a
45-degree male to female (which aimed the sender away from the distributor).  So
far so good.

BTW, though, I learned something about Teflon tape the hard way.  I used to used a
single wrap with overlap (and sometimes got leaks), but I learned from my wife that
the plumbing standard is 2.5 wraps (she owns a rental condo and does her own
plumbing repairs).  That method has never leaked.
