If your car was produced before these became standard issue (as mine
was), it's another great use for 4" high rubber cove molding (pop rivet
the curved edge to the floor bottom) in your choice of designer colors
from the bathroom section of your local hardware store!  UpFixin had an
article on the dimensions, but from memory I'd say each one was about
12-15" long and they were about the same 12-15" apart from one another,
but if you want to "turbo" feed the impeller, you can make ones that go
clear out to the rocker panels in a giant NACA duct pattern!  Drives 'em
nuts in tech!
