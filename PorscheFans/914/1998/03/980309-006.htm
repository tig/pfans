Thanks to all those who responded to my question about rubber vs. solid
trans (and engine) mounts.  I think I'll spring for the extra bucks and
go with the rubber ones.  

The reason?  Even though the rubber ones cost nearly twice as much and
don't help with shifting as much as the solid ones, they are supposed to
be much QUIETER and transmit less vibration through to the body of the
car (and the driver).  And that's important to me.  I mean, after all, I
put 12k miles a year on this car and probably only 300 of those are on
the track.

So thanks for all the input.
