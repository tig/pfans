>I don't think powder coating would be a good idea for the fins, it would
>cut down the heat transfer which is the main thing you want.  Check out the
>Bob Hoover Sermons site for cylinder painting.  He recommends a thin coat

Yes, powder coating would not only be a very bad idea, it would be
impossible to get the coating down into the area between each fin because of
the faraday cage effect. Plating or painting the outsides are the best. If I
ever rebuild a motor, I will certainly plate them with something that
conducts heat better than iron, like copper or zinc.
