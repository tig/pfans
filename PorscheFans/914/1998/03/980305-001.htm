subject - cylinder head shims or gaskets

I've used the head gaskets or shims and I've gone without them too.  I
personally prefer not using them, and lapping the cylinders (barrels) to the
heads.  If you think about it, the shim adds an extra surface to possibly
leak.  You can take valve lapping compound, put a little around the top of the
barrel and set the barrel in the head (chamber facing upwards), then you
gently rotate the barrel back and forth, and turn aprox 1/4 a turn and do this
again.  John Muir recommends this in his "Complete Idiot" book, and I've found
it works pretty well.
