Jeff:

I was being lazy and waiting for someone else to pull out factory specs
or something. From the VW Idiot book, which to me has the best trouble
shooting guide for type IV FI, the connection is symmetrical, the only
thing that matters is the pairs of wires not be swapped when you re-wire
the plug. That means the two outside wires (1 and 4) should remain
outside and 2 and 3 should stay in the middle.

I uh, tested that theory and conirmed it one long weekend...

I agree with the advice to look for a broken wire (especialy at the
connections and the first 4 inches or so where the insulation has become
brittle) that failure mode has been much more common on my car than
actual component failure.

O2 Sensor power location: I wired in a T connection with the wire that
powers the aux. warm-up regulator.
