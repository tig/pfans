Ed,
Auto Atlanta has the part you ask about. It is called the engine shelf. Part
# 914 501 012 11S which my latest, but earlier catalog shows to be $135.

The firewall part is floor pan firewall repair kit. There is 2 parts. One is
called the inner firewall,  $75, and the outer firewall, $125. NO PART
NUMBERS. but I think all you will need is the inner since it mates up to the
engine shelf. The outer goes completely across the engine bay and is the
lower part below the engine rubber seal.

Buy it. You would have to be an artist in sheet metal to make anything near
as perfect to the easy fit of AA supplied sheetmetal. How do I know? I am
fluent in sheet metal design by oneself and buy my metal exclusively from
George when I have the money.
 
Otherwise I make it myself since I am a student from the school of hard
knocks and have learned alternative methods of craftmanships including
sheetmetal.
