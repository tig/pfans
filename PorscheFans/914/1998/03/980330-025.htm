The first thing I would look at is at the back of the Tranny, near the
exhaust on the passenger side. There is a right angle gear that the
speedo cable attaches to that drives the speedo cable. I had one come out
on the highway once. There is a small screw that holds it in rather
loosely so it can spin. I'd make dang sure that that gear is there cuz if
it ain't and you don't notice, you gots your tranny lube flying behind
you down the road.
