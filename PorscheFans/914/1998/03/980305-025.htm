Hi Brian;

I think I've run the gamut of all bushing types - from OEM to extreme
racing bushings...  On my track 911, I have the "lubricate often - and
squeak all the time" bushings.  Frankly - since this car is 99% track, I
don't really care - I can't hear the squeaks above the rest of the
noise...

On my street 911, I'm using Weltmeister on front suspension  & rear
trailing arms - they are a little noisy, but handling improvement is
only mediocre above the OEM.  When the current set wears out - I'll go
back to OEM..

On the 914 conversion (same as what you are planning) which I'm almost
done with - I'm staying OEM till I get the car (and me - the driver!)
sorted out for track.  But my guess is that I'll stay OEM (the 914 will
be street (90%) and track/AutoX)...  If you want to regain steering
precision - consider Turbo Tie rod ends (I installed them) and be sure
your ball joints have absolutely no play as well as the other front end
parts/bearings.

Just my $.02
