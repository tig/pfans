	Hmmmm...  That's odd.  As far as I know, the oil sender switch
should be NPT, tapered threads.  Same as any air-cooled VW motor.  If you
look in John Muir's Idiot Book, you'll see that he mentions this.  He also
says that you just tighten the sender a  bit past finger tight, then check
for leaks.  If it leaks, you tighten a little more.

timing.
