There is another alternative for replacing loose exhaust studs. My 1.7 had
several loose 8mm studs about 5 years ago. I went to the dealer (maybe the
VW dealer) and bought exhaust studs  that are 8mm on one end and 9mm on the
other. I drilled the stud hole with the engine still in the car, tapped
them, and have had no problems since. I don't remember the pitch of the
threads  If the stud is 9mm-1.25mm use a 5/16" drill. If it is 9mm-1.0mm
use a Letter O drill.
