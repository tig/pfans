What the quote means is that the pulses to the injectors is terminated (the
fuel is shut off conpletely) when the throttle position is closed and the
engine revs are higher than idle speed. It's a designed-in feature to
prevent backfires and to reduce exhaust emissions. It's in the ECU box.
There's nothing you can do about it except to remove the overrun bypass valve.
