I have them in 914 Lite and they do transmit a lot more vibration/noise
into the cockpit; on the plus side, the transaxle stays put when you're
shifting.  Worst case is that you buy them for $50 (or $40 during an
Automotion sale), don't like them, sell them on the list for $35, and get
something else (like used mounts).  I'd see it as worth a $15 net loss to
try them, but then I'm planning to win a state lottery soon.
