My local wrench about blew a gasket when he realized that I was dumping
fuel into my 911S's oil.  Apparently, mixing fuel with the oil
dramatically reduces cooling and lubricating capability (makes sense)
and he said sever damage to bearing is possible within very few miles.
I know very little about engines, but I took his advise and fixed the
problem immediately, changed the oil twice in short order after the fix,
and inspected the old oil for signs of metal.  He says I got lucky since
I had driven the car a couple of hundred miles with the problem.

I don't know if this is an issue you should be worried about with a 914.
 Perhaps others on this list would know better.
