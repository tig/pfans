Yeh you can.  Remove the head from one side, slowly remove a cylinder
untill you see the wrist pin.  Take out the retaining clip and slide the
wrist pin out.  Do the same for the other cylinder.  Split the case, remove
the old cam, etc....

The easiest side to do is cylinders 1,2.
