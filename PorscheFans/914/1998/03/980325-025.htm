	This includes checking to see if you've got a single-
element lightbulb in a dual-filament socket.  (Or vice-versa!)  If
I remember correctly, all four turn signals are part of a two-
element lightbulb.  (I know the fronts are on US-spec cars; I am
not quite so sure on the rears.)  The wrong type of bulb will
mess up the grounds and result in *really* screwy behavior.  BTDT.
	Another thing to check (not sure how, though!) is the
flasher relay that lives on top of the fuse panel.  It's (one of
the) rectangular can.  My turn signals did some screwy stuff when
I bought the car.  Replacing the relay fixed 'em.
