My brakes squeaked sometimes (and were worn out) so I replaced them a few weeks
back with OEM pads (front and rear).  I bought and installed the anti-squeal
liners from Automotion.  Lightly sanded the edges of the pads just to remove
any rough edges.  Didn't even turn the rotors because they were in good shape.
 No more squeaks!
The anti-squeal liners seem to work great. FYI, for anyone who is going to buy
these anti-squeal liners, the 914 pads are so small, that you only need to buy
the kit for front OR rear in order to do the whole car (save 5 bucks).
