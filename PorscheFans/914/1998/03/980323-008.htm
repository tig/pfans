The L-Jet injection system that first appeared on the 1.8 914 is a more
advanced system than the D-Jet.  Maybe not any "better" or worse, just more
sophisticated.  As Keith points out this what was put on the '76 912E (talk
about a car that can't get out of it's own way, or is it a "real" Porsche or
not - ha ha), and also the same type of system that Porsche and Bosch put on
much later cars.  The biggest problem with the L-Jet is the air box which is a
sensitive piece of equipment.
