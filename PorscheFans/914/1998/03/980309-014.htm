Dat's the stuff!!!

But for those whose access is limited, go to your FLAPS and pick up a
bottle of rubber cement. It's not _quite_ the same, but will do wonders
for keeping your VC gaskets in place where they should be. Apply the
cement _conservatively_ to the VC making sure to clean up any drips or
globs, and apply same to the gasket. Then just press the gasket in place
when the glue is ready. Works great. 

Why do I not have a problem with rubber cement and a big problem with
Teflon tape? Because rubber cement will get squished, torn appart and
dissolved if a little bit gets into the oil. Teflon however, will not.
