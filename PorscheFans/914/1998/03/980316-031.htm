Hi Dave.  The if the fuel pump is delivering 30 psi while you are cranking, 
then I doubt that the pump is the problem.  It sounds like a fuel injection 
problem.  Check all your wiring to start, very often the wiring harnesses 
fail, and then the problem is very hard to find.  I would check the 
continuity of the harness, in particular the head temp sensor.

Fuel injection problems are very hard to diagnose.  We have a couple of 
original Bosch diagnostic computers that we rent out for diagnosing 
problems.  Without that, there's the old method of switching and replacing 
components until it works...

There are a few documents on our site about troubleshooting the FI, plus a 
good one on Tim's site as well...

Hope this helps
