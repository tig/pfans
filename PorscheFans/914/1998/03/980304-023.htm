If you're really intending on being competetive with your autox toy,
then read the rules. Some rules don't allow poly engine/tranyy mounts.

> So, what are the advantages/disadvantages or gains/losses of the solid
> vs rubber mounts?

Stock:

Pro:	Stock, won't be DSQ'd from competition
Con:	Expensive
Pro:	Stock, will help keep that 'concourse' feeling
Con:	Shifting still a little sloppy
Pro:	Stock, smooth ride with minimal vibration

Poly:

Pro:	Firm, tighter shifting
Con:	Firm, more vibrations trasmitted to the driver
Pro:	Cheaper
Con:	Not Stock, may get you protested at an autox.
