Hi there.  The stock tranny can be used with anything up to a 3.0L with no 
problems.  You might want to gear your 1st up, but you don't need to.

The '74s are not smog exempt yet (I know, my conversion is a '74).  Using a 
'74 motor, though you fall into the category where all the 74 911s have 
been issued an exemption from SMOG II.  This is what I have heard, since 
the cars would not pass when new anyways...
