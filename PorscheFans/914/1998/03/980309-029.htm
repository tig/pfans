Yup. There is a fair amount of stress placed on the tube. And over 25 or
so years, that metal can fatigue. Why on some cars and not on others?
All 914s were hand-built and as such are all slightly different. The fix
is common as well, but must be custom fitted (because all 914s are
unique) by whomever is doing the welding work.
