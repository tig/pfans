Hi Wayne!

 You asked:

> Regarding the installation of a 96mm big bore kit in a 1.7L 914
Motor.
> An hints or tips on how to improve, maximize, the cylinder to head
seal
> considering the narrower, reduced, surface area with the larger
bore?

  Although the sealing area is reduced, the compressive force per
unit area is thereby raised, so I wouldn't think that you'd have to
do anything special.
  There are several authorities who recommend omitting the
aluminum-plated steel ring-type "head gaskets";  I think that I would
probably do so as well on my next re-build.   Of course, you will
need to re-calculate the compression ratio if you do, and possibly
shim the barrels to allow for the lost spacing due to the omitted
gaskets.

   Just my $.02 worth.
