Jeff Canady asked:

>My mechanic also supplied me with what looked like a modified
>bosch unit that had a brass adjusting screw added to adjust the idle
>pressure (anybody know who stocks these?)
Group 6 (A Volvo tuner, advertises regularly in _european_car_) makes
rising-rate, adjustable regulators that are based on Bosch fixed fuel
pressure regulators.  They modify a Bosch unit and attach their own
diaphragm "on top of it".  When I ordered mine (several years ago) they had
a couple of choices w/regard to the ratio. I use it on a '77 L-Jet BMW.
