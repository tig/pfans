Bill Sibner  wrote:
> I just put 73 doors on my 70 914.  I was wondering if there is a way
> to adjust the window angle.
Bill:
Along the bottom edge of the door, near the front, there are two
screws locked in place by nuts.  Loosen the nuts, then turn the
screws.  As I recall, the screw pointing UP adjusts the
forward-and-back angle and the screw pointing OUT adjusts the
left-to-right tilt of the window... But I could be wrong, since I
don't have my Haynes manual handy (it details the whole procedure
and has a lot of photos).
There's also a travel-limiting bolt on the regulator... With the nner
door skin off, you'll see it in the hole just to the rear of the
window crank.  To adjust it, you roll the window all the way up,
loosen the bolt until it stops turning, then tighten the locknut.
Again, I'm pretty sure that the Haynes manual has photos.
