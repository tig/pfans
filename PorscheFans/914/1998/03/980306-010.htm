My experience is similar to Dirk's - but I'll explain some more:

Synthetic tends to begin leaking at the major engine seals for older
engines that were once run on standard oil.  However, in all the "VW
type" fours/sixes with split engine cases (914/911) I've never
experienced any leaks except at the rubber seals - never between the
engine halves or with cork/paper based gaskets.  You can try a synthetic
and watch for leaks - if it leaks, go back to regular oil (gets
expensive on a 914-6 and 911 though if you have to dump all that good
stuff..) When I swapped over a 72K 911 engine - I had to only replace
the engine oil cooler seals to stop synthetics from leaking - 25K miles
later - still no leaks ...  BTW: I use Amsoil 20-50 racing and Mobil
1...
