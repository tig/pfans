Josh,
Are you sure that you don't have an obstruction in your fuel system,
(like a partially cloged filter or a pinched line)???  If the fuel system
was restricted and unable to keep up with the flow on extended WOT usage
then the fuel pressure could drop causing the mixture to go lean.  Then
after the usage decreases, the pressure builds back to normal.  This
could also be caused by a fuel pressure regulator that is not closing
enough to maintain fuel pressure under WOT.  If you can, put a fuel
pressure gauge on it and see if the pressure starts to bleed off on your
extended accelerations.
Just a thought. Hope it's helpful.
