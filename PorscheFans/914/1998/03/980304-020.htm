There is yet one other solution that I don't think anyone has mentioned.

You can get a stud with a BIGGER diameter one one end.  Like, if the 
normal exhaust stud is 8mm dia, this new one would be 8mm on one end and 
10mm on the other.  You simple drill the stripped-out hole in the head, 
re-tap it for the larger dia. stud, and screw it in.  I've done this on 
my car and haven't had any problems since (that was 10 years ago).

Just another alternative.
