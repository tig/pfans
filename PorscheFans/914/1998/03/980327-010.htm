	So we're back to basics.  Do you have a good spark?
Unplug the FI trigger points, remove a spark plug, then hook
the spark plug back up to the plug wire.  USING RUBBER HANDLED
PLIERS (leather gloves are *NOT* good enough--don't ask how I
know this!) hold the outer electrode of the plug against a
ground (like the head, fan shroud, etc).  Check to see that
you indeed do have a spark, and that it isn't orange and
pathetic-looking.  New components can be bad, so there is no
substitute for checking what you've got at the moment.
	Checking the ignition system is always the first step
of any FI (or even any carb) diagnotic procedure,.
