Given your requirements, I would use standard rubber mounts for the engine
bar and hard plastic mounts for the tranny.

** Depending on your autocross class, this may not be legal. **

The rubber mounts up front will absorb much of the vibration you would get
from solid mounts (this is a daily driver), but the hard plastic rears will
be worth it to improve your shifting.

I'm using this setup for my driver/CSP autocrosser.  It's not entirely
legal, but I'll deal with if I ever get to the point where I can beat those
darned Miatas, BMWs, RX7s, etc...
