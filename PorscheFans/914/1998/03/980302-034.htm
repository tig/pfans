  You asked:

> Does any one know if the windshield wiper motor is a 12V unit or if
it isnt,
> how do you test it.

 The unit is indeed 12 volt; it is also a two-speed motor, and also
contains a cam-operated switch system to facilitate self-parking.

  To test it, I'd recommend  applying 12 volts between the motor case
and each speed  wire (directly from the motor) in turn.   (Do this
with the motor disconnected from the car's electrical system.)  The
motor should run at two different speeds.   You can also check the
self-parking contacts if you can figure out which is which, by
powering the motor through them after first applying power briefly
directly to the motor to move it out of the "parked" position.   It
should cycle once at low speed and shut off in the "park" position.

  Hope this is of some help.
