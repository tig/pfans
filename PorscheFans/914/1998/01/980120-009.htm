At 3:50 AM -0000 1/20/98, Steve & Terri Malm wrote:
>Does anyone know when they changed from the glass faced gauges to the
>plastic faced gauges?  Just curious.  My later plastic face gauges also
>have a single blinker indicator, while my earlier gauges had two
>separate indicators.

	I don't know about glass vs. plastic, but the 73 and earlier
gauges have two turn signal indicators.  The later ones have a single
one.  Also, the early gauges have silver cones in the center, where
the pivot for the needle is.  The 74+ have a black center pivot.
	This is further complicated by Karmann's "use the parts in the
bin" method.  So a number of early 74s have 73-style gauges--with the
two blinker indicators flashing at the same time from the single input.
