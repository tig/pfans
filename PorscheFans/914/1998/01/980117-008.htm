I would think twice about using 'O'rings from the hardware store.  As
far a I know they are buna n rubber and will not stand up to break
fluid.  I put a hydraulic clutch in mine and went around and around with
compatibility problems bequest the master cylinder wanted break fluid
and the slave I was using wanted hydraulic oil.  I finally went to
silicone break fluid which is inert.  Befoire I used an unkown material
I would soak it in the fluid for a few days to see if it swells up.  Or
get ahold of a catalogue like McMaster Carr that describes what 'O' ring
will work with what fluid.
Just my .02  Bill S.
