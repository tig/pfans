You're going to have a cooling and possibly and oiling problem with such a
large engine. This is negatively affect your engine life. My opinion is that
you need the larger volume oil pump, higher pressure relief valves, an
external oil cooler, and coatings on the pistons and cylinder head. I've
heard something said about these motors having inadequate cooling of the
cylinder barrels, so that shoudl be addressed as well. The only solution I
can think of would be to copper plate the outside of the cylinders, both to
prevent rust and to enhance thermal conductivity. Hope this helps.
