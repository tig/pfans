I missed the original post, but my experiences with a Bursch
(steelpack?) for my 2.0 were similarl to those of Philip Wharton, except
that the paint quickly departed from the collector pipes. I finally
painted on that stainless-look header paint from Eastwood and had pretty
good results, considering I didn't sandblast. My advice is to sandblast
and have a really good high-temp coating applied before you install it.

The Bursch collector also makes it easy to install a single O2 sensor
(instead of two as required by the stock set up) for an air/fuel ratio
meter. My unit was the non-catalyst type and I have found that the
muffler portion can be easily upgraded to a number of standard
aftermarket 2.25" performance mufflers, like the Walker TurboMax or
whatever. In short, I like it.
