Sorry about your ordeal.
 
Borrowing from AA catalog, tech tip #45.
"914 oil coolers never leak, but oil cooler seals do leak. They CAN be
replaced with the engine in the car."

There are exceptions if a rock or some object has penetrated the cooler.

A lift will make this easier. The mechanic should be able to do this in
about 3 hours or less since I have done this laying under my, jack raised
and jack stand supported, 914 in just over 4 hours.

I cannot give you instructions. It will be explanatory as the person doing
this proceeds with getting it done. Getting the seal locally in Bilouxi may
raise most of your concern about the cooler. Your engine however is more
costly for repairs so if it takes the wrench longer than normal, It will
still be less cost than the engine repair if not done correctly.
