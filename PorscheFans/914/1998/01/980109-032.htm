> Dean,
> I think you can do this simple procedure with success to remove the lug bolts.
> Use a large ball peen hammer or a heavy shop hammer. Place a piece of oak
> wood or piece of heavy brass FLAT against the lug bolt and give a GOOD
> whack, straight on with the hammer. No more than 2 whacks per lug. This sets
> up a vibration that usually will allow the bolt to come loose with a socket
> fitted to a breaker bar. Worked for me, on more than three different cars. 
> This is a good cure for ANY frozen bolt. If it does not come off on your
> first try, forget I told you this as you don't want to break it off. Take it
> somewhere.
> Before retightening the lug bolts, coat them with a good grease for ease of
> removal in the future.
