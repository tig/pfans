A quicky version.  73 and earlier cars are exempt from the CA bi-annual
smog check and from the check when sold/bought.

The original equipment is still suppose to be on there though, the law was
not planned as a 'do anything you want to'.  So of course my 73 will be
totally stock ;-)

If you get caught with non-stock equipment I think they can require you to
correct it and get it checked.  There are suppose to be plans to set up
sniffer trucks to check the emissions of passing cars, etc.  I've also
heard of plans to have random checkpoints to check smog stuff.

Course the car would probably have to be a pretty heavy polluter to get
flagged by a sniffer truck. I'd guess they'd have a high threshold to trip.
But a random visual spot check would be a problem.
