starter problem and broken clutch cable tubing. Had to push start at

     Next it's down to Boulder to get my clutch tubing welded by

Have two problems with the clutch tubing that I know about: 1) there is

end of the tube interferes with
the shift lever inside the tunnel housing so I have to position the
shifter then push in the clutch and
shift. Crazy stuff but I felt vicariously like Magnum PI with my top
down and during the summer
speeding down the road. I can't wait.

        One other thing I know about for now is before I drove the car
up to Loveland it didn't appear
to lean hunt though I didn't really notice so I don't think it did. So
now it does and I guess I need
to look into my head temp sensor and maybe do the resistor fix? I have
Pertronix points replacement
but if the valves are not properly adjusted can this cause lean hunting?
Something seems to have
changed. It seems to run well otherwise.
