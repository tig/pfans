On a 75 (or any side-shifter), my opinion is that the best way to make the
shifting "better" is to install the hard plastic transmission mounts sold by
most 914 parts houses.

After 25 years of use, the original rubber mounts tend to become quite flexible.

I was amazed how "crisp" my shifting became after installing them on my 75.
