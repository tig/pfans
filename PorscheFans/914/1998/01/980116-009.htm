	Keith writes:
>Does anyone know if the engines had seals around the intake manifolds as they
>pass through the sheet metal?

	The intake runners bolt to the heads.  Between the two is a plastic
(phenolic?) insulator block with paper gaskets on each side.  The sheet
metal has a hole in it just about the size and shape of the block--but with
a small "tab" on the top side.  The tab gets sandwiched between part of the
block and part of the head, and I presume is there to keep the sheetmetal
from moving around at that place and letting the precious cooling air
escape.
	At least, that's how I remember putting my 2.0 together.  (Over and
over and over and...)
