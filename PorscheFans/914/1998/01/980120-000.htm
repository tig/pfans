hinges from wrecked cars were rather cheap.  If you have a local wrecking
yard with any 914s in it, check the hinges.  If they're OK (no big cracks
and little or no rust), drill out the four spot-welds, or cut the firewall
around them to get them out of the car.  Should only be a couple of bucks;
I think GPR sold me a pair for $10 (as part of a larger order).
