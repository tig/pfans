Just to clear up a point with the drill bits I mentioned:

They are the titanium-NITRIDE coated bits (I forgot NITRIDE),
the gold colored ones sitting beside the crappy silver-grey bits.

sells them as the "Excalibur" line.

as flimsey as you might think.  When attached, you can lean
