Perf Prod catalog. I have a set of these and can give you first hand =
knowledge about them. First and foremost they are DAMN LOUD and I mean LOUD. =
At most autoxes you will most likely not get buy with them unless you can =
convince the coordinators that you are just like a Mod car...The only people =
that like them are the 10 to 13 year old that come up and say Cool car =
man!... These are definately not for the street..think LOUD and =
ticket...They are very good for top end performance as the engine will rev =
much more freely and you will see and increase in rpms and top end....but as =
Josh noted the bottom end performance suffers...so for club racing ok but =
autox not really unless you do this modification to get back pressure... =
What I have done is to get a pair of Supertrap megaphone units....you have =
to cut the cookie cutter piece out of the megaphone end and then install the =
Supertrap unit...very straight forward job. This then gives you the need =
back pressure for low end performance....and as with most changes you have =
to experiment with the number of disks to add or take out depending on your =
intake system...they are still loud but not like the airplane engine noise =
of the open ones....the other thing is that they are very hot and if you =
don't look out you or another person may burn the crap out of a =
shin....really sucks in the summer with shorts on....
Bottom Line is: If you want top end RPMs that almost don't quit ....read =
this as you really can go until the engine blows then this is the thing for =
you.For Autox, you will need the Supertrap modification for bottom end.... =
For street...absolutly NOT !!! waste of money.
Just my $.02 worth of a real world experience.
