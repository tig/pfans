Bradley,

     I recently had a bunch of noise like that on my '73.  Some of it 
turned out to be loose cooling system sheetmetal underneath the heat 
exchangers and push rod tubes that "buzzed" like the valve train was 
flying apart, and scared the hell out of me when it would do it.  Some 
questions....Do you have a stock cam?  If not, your valve adjustments 
cannot necesarily be done by the book: .006 and .006. Sometimes getting 
the correct valve clearance can be troublesome if you're not running a 
stock cam, which is I understand common amongst us CARB fiends, yielding 
singer sewing machine sounds.  My '73 has the dreaded dual IDF 44 Webers.  
I don't know how long you've had the carbs, but especially with duals, you 
will automatically get more "valve-like" noise via the venturies because 
it is not as nearly muffled as it would be with either a single carb or 
FI.  That's my $.02.
