	I put together this list to help me respond to a question about
some D-jet part numbers.  All of this info comes from the Bosch "Gasoline
and Diesel Injection Products" catalog, 1996 edition.  In the future I
may add info on some of the other parts.
	Notes:  On some of the ECU VW part numbers, there were multiple
numbers listed for each Bosch part number.  The different ones are separ-
ated by commas, and after the first full part number, only the last group
is listed.  E.g., "022 906 021C, 021E" means that "022 906 021E" is also
a match for the particular Bosch part number.

	Extra note for 914 ECUs:
The Factory Parts manual shows only three D-jet ECU part numbers.  One
is listed for the early 1.7 (without idle mixture knob), one is listed
for 75-76 2.0 cars, and one is listed for all others.  That listing is
intended to show which replacement parts to buy, not to show what came
with the car originally.  But it does indicate that the changes from
72-74 were not that big.
