I think you either need to contact Pauter or Air cooled classics. I
personally do not have any experience with custom pistons or hotrodding the
VW motor, however, Probe Industries has a nice web page with some
downloadable piston-design software (http://www.probeindustries.com). Just
based on the pictures of the pistons on the page, I'd say you're probably OK
on the piston pin distance. The issue of the maximum stroke is more
complicated because you'll have to calculate the rod ratio. A longer stroke
motor requires longer rods for a long life motor. Ask Pauter what the
maximum stroke is advisable with thier rods. Hope this helps.
