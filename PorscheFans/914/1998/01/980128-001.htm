Gerold:  The best way to remove rusted bolts is to heat them to a cherry
red with a torch, and than remove them.  The rust is destroyed by the
heat. You can use a penetrant before and after this procedure which will
assist.  This works 96% of the time.  You do have to be careful of
surrounding items such as fuel line, brake lines and wires, when using
this method.  Also when turning a hot nut or bolt with a wrench or
socket, the tools become very hot so be careful.
