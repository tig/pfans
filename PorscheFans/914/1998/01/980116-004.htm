on 1/15/98 Brad Follett wrote:
> Ok, I'm still confused about the longitudinal support.  Is the inside rocker

panel the longitudinal support?  Is the outside rocker panel the chrome

piece that covers it all?  If this is correct, what do you call the area

that the brake handle is attached to?

The longitudinals are the "pontoons" on either side of the 914 Brad.  This is
the boxed section that runs down either side of the car, and is the outer
framework for the 914.  Yes, the handbrake lever is attached to the inner
longitudinal.  The outer suspension mounts and jack posts are attached to the
outboard side of these longitudinal sections.  These parts of the car are very
susceptable to corrosion from salt and road debris building up behind the
black rocker panels.
