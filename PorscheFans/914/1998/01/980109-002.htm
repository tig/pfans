According to the "how to hotrod your VW" book, VW doesn't have any numbers
on head temps, but Chevy has some for the Carvair. The book quotes Corvair
temps as something like 375F is normal, 450F for high speed cruising, and
500something sets off an alarm. The Corvairs had a warning switch in the
head set to something like 550F. This is all from memory, so I could be off
by a bit.
