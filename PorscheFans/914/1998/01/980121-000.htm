At 08:56 PM 1/20/99 -0500, Andy LeVesque wrote:

>I'm going to replace my leaking oil cooler seals, but it also appears that the
>crankshaft oil seal behind the impeller hub is leaking too.  How do I remove
>the impeller hub?  I tried gently prying behind with a screwdriver, but didn't
>get anywhere.  What is the correct way to remove the crankshaft oil seal?
>

You usually need a puller. They can become "bonded" over time. You also need
an O ring, as I recall. As far as re-installation goes, I put anti-seize on
the hub/crankshaft mating surfaces, but I think it's a tossup as to whether
you should use anit-seize or locktite on the threaded fasteners. I used
anti-seize, but on second thought, locktite may be more appropriate, since
it is a semi-permanent assembly.
