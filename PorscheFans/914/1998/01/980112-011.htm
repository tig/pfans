Ordered the rocker clamshell from Jay @ Parts Werks for $135. He sells two
kinds: Heavy duty (lighter weight for racers) and super heavy duty (street).
He charges more for the Heavy duty, believe it or not. This piece goes from
the front of the rocker inner all the way back to the rear suspension console
mount. Jay said that these add quite a bit more strength than stock and don't
add all that much weight. An frame alignment is only necessary in severe
cases. Remember that the factory racers all had welded in tops for strength
like the 916. These clamshells are advised for anyone who loses strength in
the longitudinals due to rust or stress, or anyone who never puts the top on.
