Brian,

	One of the best ones I have found to work with the FI is the WEB
Cam for 2.0L it is as good as you can get and still run FI.

The WEB cams are heat treated so you will need to run their lifters, the
standard ones are too soft. I have found that buying their CAM, LIFTERS,
and a new gear (ALWAYS GET THE NEW GEAR), comes in about what a good
regrind costs and less than stock cam.

The WED cam moves the power band a bit so you get a 2500 - 5400 pull, if
you add the Kerby Hunter headers you will still be pulling when you hit
6K, but the oil cooler seals blow at 6200 so watch yourself (The MSD
with soft rev limiter will bail you out here).

BTW: Check your rule book fro changes you can make if you plan to race
the car. The SCCA book shows the max lift so make sure your cam gets you
there and no more! WEB can help you there also.
