Joshua: My car has the VPC unit installed and the condensor is located in the
front trunk. A rectangular hole was cut in the pan, the condensor placed in
and above the hole and a three inch wide steel strap goes over the condensor
(for and aft). I presume that the strap is to provide stiffening for the
missing sheet metal. Also, the spare tire mounts on top of the strap. Trunk
space obviously is decreased. I have never had any problems with structural
integrity and the unit still works fine after 23 years although I did have to
replace the receiver/dryer and the hoses when I took the car out of its 13
year sleep.
