Mike, 
Back in my Microbus days, when we ordered shortblocks you got a case and it's
innards with the rods hanging out. No Pistons, cylinders or heads, pushrods,
etc.

Brian et al,
The 924 wiper will fit in a late style column and has an extra electrical
momentary contact for the wiper motor. You can use a 911 tank with the pump in
the bottom or do what i did--junkyard Audi pump! Mount it between the gas tank
and the wiper bottle and use the existing hoses to the squirters. Since my PO
had a mystery switch on the dash (power amp?), I replaced it with a flip
stlyle momentatry wiper washer switch from my FLAPS. Entire cost: about $6.
Concours: no! The boneyard usually has lots of VW and Audi parts for cheap
compared with Porsche (sometimes the same part!). I use lug bolts from an Audi
5000 cause they are the right shape at the head and about 1/4 longer. Set of
16: $4!
