I believe you are talking about the blueish headlamps such as on the
Benzs, Licolns and Bimmers which are high intensity descharge (HID)

The new headlamps are not  really blue in color as much as conventional
headlamps that we are all used to are just very yellow.  The purer light
just appear blue in comparison.
