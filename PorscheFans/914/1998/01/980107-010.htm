for what it's worth .. i used a web 91H with hyd lifters from Mark
Stephens (Eaton).. the thing revs easily to 6800 with the Rich zBontempi
9# flywheel, but the lifteres bleed down overa few days time.. it
doesn't work too good with the d-jet cuz it has so much overlap it has
low idle vacuum.. Pat Perkins has same cam from FAT, same specs.. his is
for sale new w/lifters..

more on d-jet problems.. low vac means full rich at idle & decel.. which
causes "trailer-hitching" other than that it goes like hell... stronger
than my 2.8 six!.. after much experimentation I have a set of late 2.0
injectors (blue) .. the green ones amplify the problems.. i tested thenm
at least 40% higher flow .. also tried 1.7 injectors but it then runs
too lean under full throttle, tho ok for street cruise.. pat & I believe
the answer is L-jet conversion since it cares not about vacuum.. that is
why Rich B says the 1.8 is the best SCCA motor.. it'll handle any cam
