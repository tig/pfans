Hi Nick;

Sorry to be the bearer of "bad news", but if both the inner and rocker
are rusted out, then they both need to be repaired.  You asked if just
the inner could be replaced (and I assume from your question, that both
your inners/outers are rusted through), if you do just the inner, then
it will hold for a short time, but after that, you will notice there
will be stress tears in the new metal taking on the load that should be
taken on by both.

However, the firewall is a double firewall (thanks Dirk for pointing
this one out :-) and if the rust is only confined to the firewall and
the inside of the interior longitudinal, then it can be replaced with a
good 20 gauge sheet. Cut far back enough to get into good metal and weld
in the new section...

Hope this helps and feel free to email me with more specifics,
