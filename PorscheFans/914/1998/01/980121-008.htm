I put a short shift kit from Automotion in my
'75 and it made a HUGE difference. There is a 
bit more force needed now to shift (which I like),
the gearbox doesn't feel as rubbery or vague 
either. I no longer worry about putting it 
into 1st. or reverse by mistake. On the whole 
it's a big improvment. The kit from Automotion 
is a bit more expensive but then I think it 
is probably the strongest one too. But it's 
worth it, it's not something I would like to 
have fail on me while driving.
