Andy, 
The lacquer thinner method works good. BEFORE using the thinner, cut a
circle from a piece of posterboard, about an inch larger than the intake
opening.
 
Set the circle on the intake opening. Keep a slight pressure on the center
of the posterboard circle with your hand. Using the other hand, Just press
edges around the perimeter on the posterboard and push into intake opening.
Just enough to keep it below intake opening. It won't be a perfect fit, but
will keep the junk from entering the intake. After you are satisfied the
surface is clean, vaccuum, then remove the cardboard. Vaccuum intake opening
to extract any small particals that may have gotten by.
This has always worked well for me..
