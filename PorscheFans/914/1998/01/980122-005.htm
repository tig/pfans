You have to take out the round plate at the top of that piece of the
caliper.
Under the plate is a snap ring that, when removed, will allow you to
remove
the lever and shaft.  You get the round plate out by drilling thru it
and
prying it out.  I resealed mine by brazing the hole back up before
putting the
plate back in.
