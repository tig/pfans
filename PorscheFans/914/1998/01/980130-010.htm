Pop the engine lid, and you should be able to reach in far enough to
loosen the bolts that hold the trunk lid to the hinges. You'll be able
to reach in far enough to get the first bolts and then shift the whole
lid enough to either get the other bolts or get the trunk lid off the
latch. Good luck!
