First question is, is this a Porsche type mechanic ?

On 914, when the rear bearing starts to go you get a grinding or
groaning sound from one side (the one that is failing). 

CV joints on the other hand start to click at gear shifts and
start/stops. (this could mean they are just dry and need grease).

Unless you have run the joints dry, or are running a "BIG 911" motor
they usually don't go bad quickly, in fact most 914's I have seen have
the same ones the factory put in, just repacked.

I would seriously question anybody who told me all four were bad !
