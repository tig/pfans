To the best that I can tell, #10 should also be a 25A fuse.  Although I did not
see a specific listing, two things lead me to belive this:

1. In Haynes (p173) illustrates all fuses except #8, #10, and #12 as plain
rectangles - #8, #10, and #12 are illustrated as filled rectangles.  This
indicates to me that #8, #10, #12 have the same rating, but no values are
specifically given.  This diagram also happens to be from a '74 but should be
the same as a '73.

2. An 8 amp fuse for both fog (aux) lights seems a little under-rated.  A 8A
fuse is only good for up to 96 watts, and each of the fog lights is rated at 55
watts (110 watts total).

Supposedly the correct fuse ratings are listed on the fuse cover, but I don't
remember seeing them there, and I didn't have a chance to look for it there.
 I'll check tonight and see what I find.

Anyway, if you don't have the fog lights, don't worry about it.  The only
reason I noticed this is because I just bought a fog light setup and was
studying the wiring diagram last night.
