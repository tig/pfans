For what it's worth, the 911 (and probably other recent models) has
cockpit-adjustable headlight height in its non-U.S. cars. It operates
off of vacuum (I am told) and is controlled by a large thumbwheel in the
left dash panel near the ignition. You will see a covered slot in U.S.
versions.

Anyone looking to upgrade should scour the Porsche parts bin first.
