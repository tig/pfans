Many on this list and elsewhere did not pay any more for their 914 than the
cost of having this rustoration done. Also to consider is that it probably

for having this done.

80% of the owners fall into this catagory. I easily fit into this notch.

getting around to the rustoration in patch mode.

funds to accomplish these means. Some do not have the money so it returns to
the marketplace to "maybe" re-coup the owners cost with rust intact.
