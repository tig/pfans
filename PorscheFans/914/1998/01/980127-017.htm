>Hey gang any recomondations for what to use to help
>loosen rusty bolts. ...
> Is there something better than liquid wrench?

	Yes.  Liquid Wrench, a hammer, and time.  Hose the bolt
down with Liquid Wrench (or the other stuff that people will
reccomend) on both ends of the bolt, if you can.  Then take a
hammer and THUMP the fastener sharply a couple of times.  (Don't
bend anything, though!)  Go have some lunch.  Then come back and
do it again.  Then come back the next day for one or two repeats.
After a couple of days, try the fastener again.  You may wish to
tap on the breaker bar as you turn (tap as if you were hitting
the bolt-head straight on, not as if you were using the hammer's
force to turn the bolt).
	If that fails, let the area dry out THOROUGHLY, then try
heat.  (Liquid Wrench is flammable, so wait for a while!)
