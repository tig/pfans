If it's already sagging, get the kit.  Either the AA clam shell, or the
Parts-Werks
"longitudinal support kit".  PW will sell one side at a time; they sell
more rights
than lefts.....  I've got them on mine; they're labor intensive to
install, but you
have a buddy that welds.  Installing a pair of "heavy duty" supports
will make a once
saggy car more rigid than stock.  George's kit looks better (in the
ads), and I've had
very good luck with his metal before.
