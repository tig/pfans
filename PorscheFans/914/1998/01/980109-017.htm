Yep, I have two of them!

Looks like a big puzzle piece in that it is a block with two knotches
cut in it to allow for different size positions, and has a strap with a
hook on it to tie it down with. It was covered in floor carpet. The
later cars had a foam insert under the carpet to level the foot rest
area, as well as a movable seat.

If you want one I will be glad to sell one.
