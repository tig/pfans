Re this whole thread on the (aggravating) rear calipers ...

Has anyone on the list checked out and/or had experience w/CB Performance
and their products?

http://www.cbperformance.com/

They are now offering a rear caliper upgrade for VWs that
_uses_a_914_rear_rotor_ (!).  This is a "sliding" OEM caliper (not fixed,
like the 914) with an integrated handbrake mechanism.  You might recognize
that such a rotor is perfect for a mechanical handbrake system because,
unlike a fixed caliper, where the hydraulics have to be part of the
handbrake mechanism, on these calipers the entire mechanism is external to
the (single) fluid cylinder.

IF CB were to offer a kit for the 914 rears (I would think just custom
cables and maybe an adpter would be required) I would be very interested. I
suspect other 914 listers might be, too.

Check out their "Rear Disk Brake Kit" with "Double Acting Caliper" under
PRODUCTS and "CB's Beautiful Binders" under ARTICLES AND REVIEWS on their
web pages. The latter is also also in Hot VW's January 1998.
