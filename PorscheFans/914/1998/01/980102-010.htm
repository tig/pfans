At 1:28 PM -0500 12/31/97, Dion Dunn wrote:
>Knowing the valve train is the weekest link in the system, what are the
>best ways to strengthen it?
Pauter Machine out on the left coast sells a really nice needle bearing,
roller tipped, aluminum rocker arm set for about 350$. I'm sorely tempted
to whip the 'ole VISA out and say "gimme!"
Basically, rockers are too heavy and there is too much friction in them.
Reducing valvetrian inertia is a time honored way to increase mechanical
effeciency. The stock rockers in my bmws are aluminum and they are very
light. The stock 914 rockers must be over twice/three times as heavy.
