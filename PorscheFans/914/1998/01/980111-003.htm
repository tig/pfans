When I bought my '86 951, she was wearing Goodyear GSC-S tires
(Goodyear's R-rated tire).  I drove around town like that for about two
weeks until my new street tires finally arrived.  Although the grip they
offered was absolutely astonishing (even in the wet -- standing water
aside), the GSC-S's *did* pick up every bit of gravel and debris on the
street and throw it into the wheel wells.  During this short period of
time, this never seemed to result in any paint chips, but the racket it
caused on some roads was pretty disconcerting.  If you are paranoid
about your car's cosmetics, the sound alone may drive you crazy -- but
boy, do those tires stick!
