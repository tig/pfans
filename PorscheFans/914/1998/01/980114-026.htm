Andy,

I have always had the best success for this kind of thing with lacquer
thinner.  Soak the gasket liberally and it will come right off.  Don't
let it sit on painted parts for any significant time, as it will lift
that off as well...a spray bottle works really well to do this.

Probably a good idea to stuff open holes with rags before you start.
