Hi Anand,

You will find that H4 lights throw out a different pattern than regular DOT
lights.      When they're properly aimed, they allow you to run brighter
bulbs without blinding on-coming traffic.

I run H4 lights in all of my vehicles.  Bosch, Hella, Cibie, all are good
brands.  I also run 90/100W bulbs for more light.  Keep your lights and
windows clean for best performance :))

If you run high wattages, you may wish to upgrade your wiring and install a
headlight relay to lessen the stress on your headlight switch.  Take a peek
at my web page for more info.
http://www.skyinternet.ca/~brianng/relays.html
