Mike, that is not how you do the conversion.  From the TWM graph I get
(20 in H2O or 1.5 in Hg pressure) 380 CFM air for a 48 mm throttle body,
360 CFM air for a 48 mm throttle body with a 43 mm venturi.

Multiply this by 4 and you get 1520 CFM and 1440 CFM respectively.
