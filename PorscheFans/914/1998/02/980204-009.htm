ordered some fuel injection parts. I ordered a vacum hose kit, George said
"the kit was $50", I said "I'll take one". I then asked for a set of 4 intake
boots, George said "it was $10 for the set", I said "I'll take one set". He
asked if I had their new catalog, I said no, he said no problem he would throw
one in. I was satisfied. The package arrived in a few days, I was impressed

catalog,the reciept said there was , but there wasn't. A few weeks later, I
called to check the status of the intake boots, still back ordered but will
ship as soon as they arrive. No mention of a price increase! So today the
intake boots show up. The reciept says "sorry Ed but the boots cost $8 each
and we have to charge you an additional $22". So I called right away and spoke
to Susan, I told her the situation and asked how to remedy it. Susan said the
price must have been misquoted, The price came from the owner of the company
for crying out loud!. So I pointed out that both of my reciepts show the price
