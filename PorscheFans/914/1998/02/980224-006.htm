I've had the same problem with door handles. I had one good leftover from my
last teener and just broke it using it to replace a broken passenger side.
After two epoxy attempts, I give up. Our graveyard charges $75 for them.
Anybody got some spares?
One tip: I used my same locks for consistency. Just unscrew the tumbler and it
slides out and you can use any key you want to align them with.  Take out the
spring when you screw the endpiece on--this makes them turn alot easier. I
also applied some lithium grease to the hinge and the tumblers. Boy, does my
driver side handle work good. Yep. Greatest handle out there. No doubt. Sure
would like another.....
