There was a discussion last week about fuses.  I found some of the answers on
the Pelican Parts page.

Basically, up through 1973, the fuses were as follows:

Fuse #  AMPS    Function
#1      8       Left headlight high beam.
#2      8       Right headlight high beam.
#3      8       Left headlight
#4      8       Right headlight
#5      8       Left parking lights
#6      8       Right parking lights
#7      8       License plate lights
#8      25      Windshield wipers, ventilator, horn, cigarette lighter
#9      8       Turn signals, stop lights, back-up lights
#10     8       Additional headlights
#11     8       Emergency flashers
#12     25      Headlight raising and lowering, interior lights

>From 1974 onward, there were some rating changes:

Fuse #  AMPS    Function
#1      8       Left headlight high beam.
#2      8       Right headlight high beam.
#3      8       Left headlight
#4      8       Right headlight
#5      8       Left parking lights
#6      8       Right parking lights
#7      8       License plate lights
#8      16      Windshield wipers, ventilator, horn, cigarette lighter
#9      8       Turn signals, stop lights, back-up lights
#10     16      Additional headlights
#11     8       Emergency flashers
#12     16      Headlight raising and lowering, interior lights

This info can be found at the following address:
http://www.pelicanparts.com/914/technical_specs/914_electrical_fuses.htm

Hope this clears up any confusion. Looks like I have some fuse changing to do
since it looks like the PO of my 914 shoved 25A fuses everywhere!
