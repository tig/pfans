>Regarding the installation of a 96mm big bore kit in a 1.7L 914 Motor.
>An hints or tips on how to improve, maximize, the cylinder to head seal
>considering the narrower, reduced, surface area with the larger bore?
>Thank You >Wayne

Cut the heads open a bit and use the 1.8 kit.  

Years ago I got about 40K miles on a set of 1.7 96mm before a head stud
pulled loose and I abandoned the engine in favor of a real 2.0.
When I pulled it down, the upper edges of the cyls that mate into the
head had cracks, but there was still a good head seal.

BTW, it felt like a 1.7/96 has about the same power as a stock 2.0.
