> > Does anyone know of a documented procedure, or have personal experience

> Here is what worked on mine while I had mine out (actually, it's still
> out - and I'm not sure my procedure is *documented* elsewhere:-)
> 
> Disassemble the box itself.  You'll notice that the box is clamped on
> with retaining circlips and the motor itself is on the bottom of the box
> assembly.  There are two things you'll want to check - the shaft
> bearings and the motor brushes.  I used white lithium grease to
> lubricate the shaft surfaces/bearings (which had gunked up over time)
> and cleaned the brushes.  When I reconnected the terminals again after
> the rebuild, the lowest speed setting started working again.  Also, you
> might want to check the contacts in the throw lever in the cockpit to be
> sure it's making contact at the lower settings.
> 
> Hope this helps...
