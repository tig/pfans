I put together a setup that I like.  I took a piece of masonite and
mounted a 4" midrange and a small 3/4" aluminum dome tweeter on it.  I
had to cut a good part of the plastic mounting plate on the tweeter to
get it close enough to the woofer for all to fit in the boxes.  It works
and sounds pretty good.  I also built a 12x12x4" box and stuffed 4) 5
1/4" woofers in it and mounted it under the dash.  I used a 100W amp
with a built in crossover to drive it as a subwoofer.  It is enough to
hear even with the top off at 80mph.  It is adequate for this
audiophile.  Hope this helps.  I can give you more details if needed.
