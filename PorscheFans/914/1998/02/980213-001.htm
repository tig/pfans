Regarding Bill Dugan's question about track wheels.  I just ordered a set of 
15x6 Panasports from Paul Spruell Inc. (800-552-2532).  They were $159 each.  I 
bought the lights rather than the ultra-lights because I need to be able to 
drive the car to the track.  The ultra-lights were also available in 15x7, but 
were more expensive.  The 15x6 are in stock, and you avoid the hassle of having 
custom wheels made.  I think they also look great, assuming you like the classic 
minilite look.  Good luck.
