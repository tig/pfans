Hi, I read your posting on the 914 list and wanted to let you know sometimes
parts don't move that quickly and you never to seem to get what you're asking.

including ac systems, running engines and weber carb set ups... I even had
spare hoods, doors, interiors etc... the parts filled a 25 foot uhaul to over
flowing with a car on the trailer behind and a car in the uhaul itself and
only got $1800 for everything. The 914 market seems to be falsely inflated as

lost my storage space. I eventually got out of 914s because I got sick of
seeing guys asking $10,000 for a car that wasn't worth $1500 and seeing the
OUTRAGEOUS prices I was having to pay for simple parts I didn't have, like
brake rotors. It seems ashame that the 914 carries the stigma of the bastard
Porsche but yet vendors will gladly ask 911 prices for everything.
