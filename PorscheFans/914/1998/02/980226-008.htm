As I recall, you can find this information in the back of the 914 Owners
Manual.  I'm not sure about the 1.7 and 1.8, or the 914/6 2.0, but from what
I recall the 1973 914 2.0 looked something like this:

95 HP
104 lb-ft
0-60 9.8 Secs
Top Speed: 118 mph

I think that I'm probably close... when I tried to measure the 0-60 time in
my 914 (1976 2.0) before my rebuild, I came up with something like 8.6 secs,
but there were a bunch of variables:  I didn't have the smog pump hooked up,
the heads were fly cut, and I've got Euro pistons and Total Seal rings.  I
would guess that in perfect condition my motor puts out around 105 HP, and
with the low-profile (205-50) tires probably puts in a 0-60 in the low 8's.
Nothing too impressive...
