I've been looking into these as well. They are quite expensive new. You
can see what's available on the espar and webasto web sites. The espar
web site is www.espar.com. I don't recall offhand the webasto site.
Espar sent me a nice brochure - their D1LC model puts out as much as
7500 BTU, weighs 7.7 lbs and is 14"x5"X5.3".  It would fit great behind
the stick shift in a custom console. To offset the weight of heater,
your wallet will be quite a bit lighter - I haven't checked the price,
but the equivalent Webasto is $1400!
