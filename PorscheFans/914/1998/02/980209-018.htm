I recently took the "Porterfields" off my 2.0L, in order to preserve my
sanity while driving it on the street (my blazer is getting an eng.
transplant). I'm having problems keeping air out of the brake system. I

HELP!!!

FYI- If anyone tells you that running anti-squeal pads on Porterfields
will quiet them down for the street they are WRONG. They will work until
you heat up the brakes 1 time and then you get your pedal feel and the
noise back. My anti-squeal shims lasted exactly 1 lap at Hallett. Don't
get me wrong, the porterfields are quiet as long as they are hot and boy
do they stop. But definitely not for the street.
