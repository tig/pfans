I can shed some light on the Master Clinder switch. I just pulled
three of them, at the junkyard.

  Part # 0 343 102 040 - This is a single pole switch, which can NOT be
                         reset from the outside of the switch.

  Part # 0 343 102 008 - This is a double pole switch, which can NOT be
                         reset from the outside of the switch.

  Part # 3 3525 9108 1 - This is a single pole switch, which CAN be
                         reset from the outside of the switch.This
                         switch has the white "nipple" which is clearly
                         visible. The brand says ATE. 
    This is the sort of switch that makes sense to have, because it is
    resettable. Dont know if its a stock piece.
