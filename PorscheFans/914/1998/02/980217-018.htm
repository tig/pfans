Mulitple sparks on the exhaust stroke??? Nah, the MSD allows you to run a
richer mixture and burn it all, and in high compression motors, it makes a
"miss" much less likely (which could be disasterious). Man, they make MSD
boxes for tow vehciles, even (the MSD5, which I used to have on my 2002).
They are great little things that noticably increased the power in my car.
To get even more bang, ditch that junky Bosch coil and get something decent,
like an MSD Blaster coil. Then open up your plug gap to 40 thousandths, then
the fun begins....
