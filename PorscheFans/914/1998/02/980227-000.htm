I think the 4th gear whine is characteristic of the 901 gearbox. As long as
the oil is changed regularly (every other engine oil change), the rest of
the tranny is quiet (carefully crawl under the car with the motor running
and the tranny in neutral and listen - be very careful to set the hand
brake) and you don't notice any difficulty shifting, then I wouldn't worry
about it. The whine while driving in 4th is a function of the gear teeth,
not any bearing.
