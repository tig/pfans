It's not the ECU that's really the problem in outfitting a 914 with a heftier
cam. The problem is in the very principle of the D-jet FI. The D-jet uses
manifold pressure/vacuum to moinitor intake conditions, and a tweaked cam will
create pressure waves in the intake tract that confuses the pressure sensor.
Yes, pressure waves exist all the time in an intake system, but D-jet can only
handle so much before the MPS starts giving screwy signals to the brain.

At least that's my understanding....
