	My most recent order from GPR included a "rear brake repair kit"
factory part.  It includes the "spring clips" that hold the brake pad
retaining pins, the covers for the outer adjusters, and the covers for
the inner adjusters.  Oh, and a couple of washers for the inner covers.
	Just in case you lose some of these pieces.

	On a totally unrelated note, didja know that the covers can fall
off pretty easily if they're only finger-tight?  And that, if you hammer
the retaining pins out without removing the spring clips, you have to hit
the punch REALLY HARD and then you shear the clips when the retaining pin
finally slides through the hole in the caliper?
	Totally unrelated to the rear brake repair kit mentioned in the
first part of the note.  Really, I swear.  (Whaddaya mean my nose is
getting longer?)
