Not to take up too much net time, but today was a great experience.  Our
car club (Akron Sports Car Club) took a trip over to Indianapolis and to
Stephan Johansson's Karting Center.  It was the most fun I've had on four
wheels since the last autocross in October of 1997.  We had 20 people and
raced in 10 teams of 2 for a 2 hour LeMans style endurance race.  What a
blast.  I would highly recommend it for club outings and such.  It's
specially nice to all of us in the great not-so-white North when our first
autocross isn't till April!

But jeez, am I sore!  Go there, you won't be dissapointed.
