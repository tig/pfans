Sam,
	I can only tell you about the MSD. I have yet to meet anyone using
the Crane system  the MSD has al sorts of add-ons timing adjustments rev
limit's etc.

	If you are using the factory tach you will need the tach adapter,
which is just a coil.

	As for replacing the coil , I tried that and basically it did
nothing. The stock coil when coupled with an MSD-6x throw out an
unbelievable spark.

	I have found that Summit Racing in Ohio, is by far the best place to
buy this product. Summit is a Chevy, Ford, Mopar race house and they carry
all our goodies at Chevy prices.

Their web is http://www.summitracing.com/msdigntn.htm

I use the 6A  which sells for $125, the soft rev limiter is nice but I like
my toe's limiter better. When you have to go over limit you can, and in 914
sometimes you have to do that.
