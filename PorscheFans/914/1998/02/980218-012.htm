At 09:54 AM 2/18/98 -0600, Alan Barrilleaux wrote:

>Good point! Is there anything I should do to the wiper motor/assembly while
>I have the blower and tank out?
>

Well yeah. Clean off the old grease and apply fresh grease. Actuate the
mechanism by hand until it moves nice and easy. It'll save you blowing up
the motor. Take the motor apart too and clean the commutator and lube the
bearings. Clean the contactor plate on the worm wheel also. Make sure the
fingers are clean and have good spring tension.
