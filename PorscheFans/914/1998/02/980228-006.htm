So I played hooky for a few days from the office and worked on the 914.
My web page is woefully behind the actual progress, so here's where I am
as of this evening (Saturday):

- The factory flares are welded on, faired and all 4 fenders are
primed... (THEY LOOK OUTSTANDING - but it takes about 8 - 10 hours per
flare/fender to do them right...)
- The 911 engine ('82 3.0) is rebuilt and ready including the 914-6
sheet metal, valves are adjusted, new chain ramps, updated tensioners,
etc.
- The 901 tranny is apart for a new 1st synro and dogteeth gear..
- The engine compartment is stripped to bare metal and is primed.
- New battery tray and mount is welded in.
- The outer GT Rockers have been fit and ready to go in.
- I'll weld in the engine mount this week (Vellious).
- The clutch, flywheel, TO bearing, etc have been trial fit (a 225mm
flywheel.)
- The entire body will be primed either this  week or next.
