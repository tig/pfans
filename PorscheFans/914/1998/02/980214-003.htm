Recently I decided that battery acid and fumes was something I wanted to
get rid of, so I looked at the Optima battery. I also gave our local
Exide distributor a call and ended up buying an Exide STR2000
recombinant battery. It is totally sealed like the Optima. Brochure says
it "can be safely mounted in passenger compartment or in trunk. If the
case is punctured, electrolyte will not leak."  It fits perfectly on the
battery shelf and normal clamp. The only problem is that the posts (+ -)
are opposite of the normal. The cost was $85.95 plus state tax. BTW, it
has an 84 month warranty.
