Why would you want to run steel wheels?  Why would you want to run a 7"
wheel?  I may be way off in this, but; steel wheels are heavy, 7" wheels
will be really heavy, I think it would cause handling problems on any
kind of rough road.  The momentum of the wheel will throw the suspension
around.  I would also be concerned about rolling resistance.  The wider
the wheel the higher the resistance given an otherwise similar tire.
I have a V8 in mine with 220/55/390's on aluminum 6" wheels.  I cannot
break them loose except jumping on it in a hard turn.  I have them all
the way around.  The steering is stiff, I will be replacing the fronts
with 195/70?/390's.  I know that wide wheels look cool, I am leaning the
other way these days. I will be in Solvang in may(?) see you there?
