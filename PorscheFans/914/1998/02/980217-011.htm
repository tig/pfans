Don't forget about the fastener behind the engine lid release knob.
Unscrew the knob from the cable, then unscrew the 1" diameter plastic
plate.  If it hasn't been off for a while it may be tough, but it does
unscrew.  Just be carefull not to rip your upholstery while working on
it.
