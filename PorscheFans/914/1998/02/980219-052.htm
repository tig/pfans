You already got good advice on the pressure and camber.  The next step is to
get a front sway bar.  I originally used my stock bar to time trial.  After
stepping up to a bigger bar, I had to get stiffen up the rear with 140lb
springs (spend more money) to offset the new bar in the front.  So plan
carefully so you don't spend foolishly.  The Automotion catalog has a pretty
good set-up guide for spring weights and sway bar set-up.
