John Snodgrass raised a question concerning the durability of duck tails. I
take exception to the "wisdom" provided by John's friends. I street a 72 RS
that has had a fiber glass duck tail since the mid 80s. The tail shows no
cracking, always locks, is not split, is, in one word, perfect.

There is, however, another problem. I have a sports muffler and exhaust
vapors always discolor (car is light yellow) the duck tail BIG TIME.
