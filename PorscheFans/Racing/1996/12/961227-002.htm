	After trashing a 944, I re-thought the deal and now have a 
dedicated 914 track car which is disposable. If you realy want to start

1/4, tires (R1's 3 races) Brakes (Cool Carbons ever Race), Rotors (3 
Races), Oil (Moble 1  2 changes a race weekend), food, lodging etc..)

Basicly its costly ! but fun (How do you make a million $'s racing ? 
Start with 5 and quit when you have one left..)
