Just a recommendation which I have not seen posted herein:

For those interested in understanding corner balancing and suspension tuning,
buy the Fred Puhn Book, How To make Your Car Handle.  It can be purchased from
a variety of locations, and has been a conerstone for many years.  It has all
of the equations for corner balancing, plus a lot of key reference material
for those who like to "read more about it."  Really a basic text which many
Porsche "racers" ought to have.
