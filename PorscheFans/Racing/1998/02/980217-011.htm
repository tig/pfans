I tow a simple dual-axle surge brakes trailer with my Explorer and have had
great success but only because we use a load leveler / torsion bar setup (from
U-Haul). It tows like it's on rails with very little bounce and no sway. I
will never tow without it again considering how awful it was the one time I
tried thinking it was "just a short trip and not worth the extra hassle".
