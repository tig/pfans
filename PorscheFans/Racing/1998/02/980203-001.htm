If you look below the driving lights you will see there is already a
colling inlet hole through the bumper. This was provided for the Turbo
Cup cars to provide brake ducting. You need to cut out the plastic in
the fender well to get it to go all the way through. I believe the Cool
Brake Kit from OG Racing can be used to finish the job. I am envisioning
doing he same thing to my car. It would be nice to hear from someone who
has used this kit in this fashion for installation details.
