Why would you think R compound tires would always be better in the rain 
than street tires?  I have found the opposite. Although I will admit to not 
having experience with the Kumho, if it is anywhere similar to the BFG R1,

how well the SZ50s worked as a track rain tire; how predictable they were.

20+ years.
