Hi Tim,

Racetech and OG Racing both have a design for 944's that mount
to the rails. I put an OG cage over the holidays and was very
happy with the design and fit. The cage was put in by Steve
Timmins of IG, Inc in Delaware. It definitely stiffened up the
car. I'm looking forward to see how it handles on the track. In my
opinion, attaching to the rails is the only way to go. I wouldn't want 
to be upside down at the track looking at the bottom of my 
rollcage poking up thru the holes in the floor pan
