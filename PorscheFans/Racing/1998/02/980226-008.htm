I can't help you on the tech inspection, but I can tell you about the
classing of the  car.

1.	You are right 194 2.0L with carbs puts you in I, were you don't have
a chance, unless no one else shows up.
2.	On the other hand if you are building a race car, you can go to the
GT class and build the car well and win. (The under 2.2L class is just right
for you, the /4 is less weight than the /6 and you can make a 914 less
weight than a 911 so it might be the hole to run in.
3.	The class is not related to skill level, I run in J and ran against
one of the best drivers I know (Dave Milton), and I have beaten GT-3, and I
cars driven by poor drivers.
4.	Class is only related to money not skill.

The first rule in racing is read the rule book and then build the car to the
top edge of a class, then finish the race and you have a chance.
