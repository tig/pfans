The new Nicholas Watts print "The Power and the Glory" signed by David
Piper, Derek Bell, Brian Redman, Richard Attwood and Jackie Ickx is now
available. The print is a tribute to driver/actor Steve McQueen and his
movie "Le Mans."

Fantasy and reality have merged over time. Newcomers to the world of
motorsports may believe that the #20 Porsche 917 won the 1970 race. It was
the Porsche 917 "short tail" Salzburg entry driven by Richard Attwood and
Hans Herrmann car #23 that actually won.

Regardless, the new print is a piece of beauty and a lasting tribute to
McQueen.
