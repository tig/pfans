I can only compare the sound of Joe Fabiani's work and the B&B.  I have
a B&B muffler only on my C4 and Joe's work sounds better to my ear.

resonance at 2000-3000.  My wife can't hear it but it bothers me.  I've

heavier, as well.  There is a slight increase in performance with the
B&B, but to really improve the breathing of a 964 you need to open the
intake up as well.  Something about matched components.;^)
