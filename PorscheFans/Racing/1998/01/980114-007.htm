My advice is simple and it is based upon reading and hearing about some
of the unfortunate incidents that have befallen other entrants over the
years. Be careful and be safe. Knowing when you have to slow to make
sharper corners is also encouraged.  Go to have fun. Do not go to set
any stupid record. I know two Porsche drivers who had a great time at
past events. Both did it for the same basic reason: to legally see what
the top end was like. Enjoy the experience and tell us all about it.
