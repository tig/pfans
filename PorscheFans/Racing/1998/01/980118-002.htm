There are about 120 open camp site that will be used at Laguna Seca for
the Historics.  To get one
just call Laguna Seca at 1-800-327-seca (7322).
The best alternative if they are full is the Marina Dunes campground in
Marina, Calif.  The trick at
Laguna if you can get in is to arrive on Wednesday.
Stay in the County campground wednesday night and
plan on getting up early (6:00am) to go over to the
open camping area.  It's first come first served over there and I
suspect it will fill up early.  There are 300 reserved camp sites and
try for one
of them when you call but I have heard they are all
taken.  Reservation for them started Jan 2nd.
Also if you are a Porsche Club member, Monterey Bay
Region puts on a corral and bar-b-que that is outstanding.  Contact the
Region president for details.
