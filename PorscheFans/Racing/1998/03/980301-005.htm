Tongue weight aplenty is what works for me and several of my friends. I
tried putting the 911 jack point between the axles (as theroy would suggest)
and it did not work well. The trailer would wander when it got up to good
cross country speeds. The lift point is now over the front axle. btw- the
tow veh is a cummins extra cab longbed, so it can take the tongue weight.
Others with lighter trucks are doing well with load leveling hitches to
achieve upper speed stability.
