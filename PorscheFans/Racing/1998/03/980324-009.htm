I am in the process of setting my car up for LRP on May 8-9. This
included the purchase of a Recaro SRD and _two_ back braces. I have an
email into Harry Hall asking for clarification that that SRD is OK for
next year. I haven't heard anything yet.

My problem is that I live in Manhattan so having two seats that I swap
at events would be a big pain. Since the car is a street/track hybrid,
going to a full-blown racing seat would be less than optimal for those
long hauls to Summit Point or Mid Ohio. I'd definitely prefer getting a
SRD-type seat, but I don't want to spend $800 this year only to find it
is no good next year...

Regarding seat back braces, this rule comes from the SCCA where the
problem of stock type seats going horizontal has been an issue for a
long time. There are those who think it doesn't do much good, but it is
now required. As I mentioned, it was my plan to use two braces to attach
the SRD to the roll cage.

Regarding this rule as it pertains to 914s in the SCCA, I'd be happy to
forward email to Richard Welty (Chief SCCA Tech/Scrutineer for the NE)
or to the wheel-to-wheel mailing list for clarification. I'm sure this
has been addressed since the 914 runs in IT.
