I want to add a correction to the comment on the weight of the new BFG
tire.  It is HEAVIER than the R1.  I asked the rep at techtactics and he
told us that the new tire is heavier even with the less amount of rubber on
it. The extra weight had to do with the new construction.  This new tire
sounds very much like the Hoosier tire, i.e., stiff sidewalls that do not
compensate for camber changes like the old R1.  I have switched to Hoosiers
last year and found them to be faster for autocross, but tricky at high
speed tracks.  They stick real good and without much warning, they just let
go.  And yes, they do not last as long as the old R1.  Another advantage is
that the Hoosier is significantly lighter than the R1 (I switched from 205
to 225 and the Hoosiers were lighter by a few pounds).
