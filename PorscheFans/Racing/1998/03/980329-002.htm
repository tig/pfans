Bill,

I've used 205/60, 195/60, and 205/50 Yokohama, TA, and RE71 R compound tires
on various vintage racers (66-68 911 and 912). Especially if your car is
lowered, you will have to experiment with things like flattening the the inner
lip of your fender openings, using spacers, and even rolling the fender well
out a bit. In my case, I've always used 6x15 Fuchs, but some of the cars I run
against actually squeeze in a late 7x15 Fuchs with 205/60 tires - so, it can
be done.

I still have some Yokohama A008 R from last year so I haven't tried to buy any
yet this year - hope you're wrong about no tires being available...
