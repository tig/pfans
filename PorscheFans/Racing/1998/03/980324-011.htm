22mm bars are pretty big, I don't know why people run them
unless you have a car with very high spring rates.

I have 19mm AJR bars on my "RS" and the end links are lousy and clunk
a lot. They are heim joints with bolts going through them, like
the Automotion "deluxe" adjustable drop links. Not a good
idea as threads going through the rod-end will wear, causing slop. 
I replaced the threaded bolts with bolts with threads on the end only
and they are better but still not great. 

I don't like the rear bar's mounting to the camber eccentic bolt.
I guess the newer cars are like that? I have enough trouble keeping
alignment without the damn swaybar trying to change it...
I have the stock swaybar mounts on my '73 banana arms that would seem 
better. Does anyone know of a link to attach my AJR slider-end bars 
to the banana arm?
