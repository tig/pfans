Farzaan, I have to disagree with you on this one. I personally think
that if you are really interested in competing in autocrossing that any
time you spend on street tires is almost completely wasted. Why? Because
you are radically changing the characteristics of the car. The
difference between the BFG R1 and _any_ street tire is night and day.
Besides, full tread street tires are very likely to be torn up due to
friction and the large tread blocks.  My advice to any but the most
casual autocrosser would be to get a second set of wheels and get the
R1s, or the Kumhos, or even the Toyo proxies as soon as you possibly
can. Another alternative is to simply drive all the time on the R1s. You
won't get more than 5000 miles from a set of unshaved tires, but it
alleviates the problems associated with changing and storing the other
set of wheels.

IMHO, of course,
