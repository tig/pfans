My street-legal SCCA T2 (non-P) car has a Recaro race seat.  The GCR has
since 1995 required all SCCA cars to have a seatback restraint against
the seatback and  permanently attached to the roll bar or otherwise to
the car.  In my case I used a rod attached to a brace which is bolted to
the seatback; it slides through a collar which can be released for fore
and aft seat movement and tightened once the seat is in place.  It uses
only a small Allen wrench which I keep in the car.  It complies with the
GCR, was easy to do and doesn't impair seat mobility in any way.
