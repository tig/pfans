Anyone using these lines needs to plan to replace at LEAST once a year. 
The innner lining expands & contracts just like a rubber line.  When it
does, it chafes against the SS outer lining.  Obviously this eventually
leads to leaks & failures.  

We race 3 cars with them.  All have had line failures. Fortunately never
at speed.  One failure was as we left tech inspection @ a Sebring Club
race. 
Are you willing to trust your life to a maintenance item you cannot
inspect directly therefore should change regularly?

That's my opinion, I could be wrong.
