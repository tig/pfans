Dan:

You can get it from Phil's Automotive. For their web listing, go to
http://platz.com/  then scroll down to "Mall Merchants" and click on
Phil's Automotive.

I've used the stuff a couple of times now. The black tire specks just
peel off after a quick hose down at the track. Cool. If removed after a
day or so, pressure washing rolls it off like wet saran wrap. I agree
with an earlier poster who said to mask your rubber trim, though. It
sticks to rubber (and unwaxed paint) a little too well, but comes off
with patient peeling. I left it on semi permanently on the underside of
my '93 aero mirrors. It seems to get harder as it ages, but softens up
again when wet. I will probalbly put it on my 964 rocker panels for the
winter next year and stop worrying about the mud.

Hope this helps.
