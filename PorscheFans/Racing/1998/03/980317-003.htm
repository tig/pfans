Hi Jeanne and Bob,
   The best answer to your question is that you will begin to feel 
weaknesses in the car.  Assuming you arrive at a point where you are 
sophisticated enough to maximize the car as it stands, you will feel 
the tires give up in a corner or the brakes or you will feel the 
suspension allowing too much uncontrolled movement.
   A practical person will tell you not to change anything that you 
cannot actually define a need for.  That way you and your car will 
progress at the same pace.
   These cars are much better than most of us will ever be anyway, 
but it is your car.  Do what you want.
