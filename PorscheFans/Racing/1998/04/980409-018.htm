on the questions about the 76 911S running in "stock" my set of the 1998
Rules say  under Prepared on page s 12 and 13 that carbs are allowed but
move up one class and the rest of the engine must remain stock.  Slope nose
conversion is allowed but moves up one class however wheels and tires remain
as for stock class.  I'd get a copy of the rules and read very very
carefully rather then relying upon advice from others. Bruce
