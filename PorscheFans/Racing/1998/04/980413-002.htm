"Sam Matthews"  asked :

>  Is it considered a bad idea to put  205/50's on a 15 X 5 1/2 inch rim??
>  The tires in question would be something like an R1 or Kumhos.

As Josh said, R1s will work perfectly in that configuration, not sure about
brand K but should be fine. Call Kumho, (C.U.T.) they will know the
recommended rim widths.
  
>  The reason I asked is that I ordered some Kumhos,
>  and basically I went with the size that was already on the car (205/60's). 
> I figured
>  the 205/50's would be a little smaller, but they look _much_ wider. 

Yes that seems to be generally true.

> The tire guy
>  said that mounting them on a narrow rim like that would cause them to
>  "balloon" and result in a small contact patch.

They will be squeezed a bit but I would not worry about it.
Even before the current R1 people were running that size
on older BFG, Yokos, etc
on stock 914s and going REALLY fast. Maybe it would be a bit faster
on a 6" or 7" rim but you should be OK. You could try running
a few PSI higher that normal to keep the tire in shape.
