Assuming you are a PCA member, call the National office and ask them to send
you the Club Racing packet.  It will contain the rule book and other info you
need.  Before you touch your car, read the rules carefully to make sure you
won't put yourself in a class you don't want to be in.  The stock classes are
a lot of fun, and don't have huge bills tied to them to make a competitive
car.
