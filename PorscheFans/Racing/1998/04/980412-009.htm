> Is it considered a bad idea to put
> 
> 205/50's on a 15 X 5 1/2 inch rim??

Not at all! As a matter of fact it's commonplace.

> The tires in question would be something like an R1 or Kumhos.

If you're going to pinch wide tires onto 5.5" Fuchs alloys, you should
go with R1s. The very stiff sidewalls have the added advantage of
enabling you to pinch the tire onto rather narrow rims without
excessively rounding the contact patch.
 
> The reason I asked is that I ordered some Kumhos,
> 
> and basically I went with the size that was already on the car (205/60's).  I figured
> the 205/50's would be a little smaller, but they look _much_ wider.  The tire guy
> said that mounting them on a narrow rim like that would cause them to "balloon" and
> result in a small contact patch.

I don't know about Kumhos on a narrow rims, I do know that people have
had good results with Hoosiers on the 5.5" rim, so maybe the Kumho would
work, but I do knwo that the R1 is just fine on a narrow rim.
