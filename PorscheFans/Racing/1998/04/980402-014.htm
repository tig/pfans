Ladies and Gentlemen.

Here is my $0.02,

Never, ever by leather shoes for racing. 

Why,  when leather is place within a heat source the leather will shrink! 
Now imagine your foot encapsulated in a shrinking leather shoe and being
crushed.  Fun thought,  NOT.

Ask yourself what is the value your health,  yes I know a flaming fireball
is highly unlikely but it is still remotely possible (Murphy is a friend of
mine), the cost of a good set of Nomex shoe is cheap insurance.

So as I stated,  this is my $0.02, and I wear a set of nomex shoes.  I've
only seen 1 flaming fireball after 2K mile of racing in SCCA IT and I still
consider these a prudent investment.

Good Luck
