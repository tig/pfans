Subject:Mini Enduro Racing Comes to PCA Club Racing
Date:Fri, 3 Apr 1998 11:01:30 -0800
Porsches were built for endurance racing. Now it's time for PCA Club racers
to get a taste of an endurance event:

August 8 and 9 PCA Club Racing will see its first ever "Mini Enduro" at
Portland International Raceway in Portland, Oregon. This 'taste' will last
for one hour with a mandatory five minute stop and an optional driver
change. The one hour race will be run on Saturday after the traditional
practice starts. Sunday will still include the normal sprint race schedule.
That makes it a two race weekend for each run group not counting the
practice starts.

Racers holding a PCA Club Racing license will automatically receive a
registration form for this special event. Feel free to send your questions
my way. See you in August!

Jim Coshow
