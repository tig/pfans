        Just a little bit on what we get up to downunder -
        
        One of the local Driver Ed schools organised an interclub 
        challenge day at the DOT Driver Training Facility, Gold Coast 
        Queensland, Australia.  Porsche, Fiat, Mazda MX5 and Subaru WRX 
        clubs fronted for the whole day, rotating between the skid pan, 
        2.5km circuit and large skid pad (motokana/autocross) for 
        basically a DE day.
        
        The challenge came at the end of the day in an Autocross style 
        event on the large skid pan.  Tight, wet and slippery!  Sad to 
        say the 4WD turbo ABS Subaru WRX's whipped our arses.  Sorry 
        there were no C4, C4S or 993 turbos present on the day.
        
        Chalk up one for the Rice Rockets!
