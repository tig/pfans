Steven - 
If you really want to mount your oil tank up front, and there are quite
a few good reasons to do so, I wouldn't build your own.  Coleman makes a
perfect tank for this application that will just fit in the A/C
compartment.  You can see a picture of it here:
http://www.drextec.com/ChrisBro/911rsr__the_buildup.htm .  Make sure you
use really large and smooth hose runs back to the engine, and use a good
breather box from the oil tank mounted in front of the tank.
