Jay, et al:

Go for it!. These multi-spark CD ignitions like the MSD 6-AL and the
Crane Hi-6
are just the ticket for keeping the plugs clean on a Weber-equipped 911.

These carbs have a somewhat crude idle and progression circuit that
requires a rich mixture to avoid flat spots. These ignition systems,
used with their respective coils, really make the car run much cleaner
with no plug fouling when idling
for periods of time.

The OEM Bosch and Permatune ignitions do not fare as well in these
applications.
