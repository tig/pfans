BONN (May 27, 1997 - 12:31 EST) - Italian ready-to-wear group Benetton will pull
out of formula one Grand Prix racing at the end of the current season, the economic
weekly Wirtschaftswoche reports in its Wednesday edition.

Group chairman Luciano Benetton has reportedly put the team up for sale because of 
mounting costs and difficulty in finding another engine after French firm Renault
cuts off its free supply next season.

Team manager Flavio Briatore is also reported to be on the move.

Porsche, BMW and Audi were reported to be in talks over the Benetton sale.
