I also have to mention - because my Porsche isnt ready for the race -
that I entered as Navigator in my Dads 86' Audi 5000 CS Turbo - we ran
the 110 mph class (the biggest class with 18 cars total) and received
3rd place for a -0.0651 variance from target (mph).
Unfortunately - because of a nasty accident involving a Mustang (no
fatalities I'm happy to say), 8 cars had to be stopped on the course,
and unfortunately because of that stop those 8 cars really didnt stand a
chance because the middle leg of the race was thrown out. One of those
cars was an 88' 928 S4 that was competing in the 150mph class. To let
you know how bad the middle leg being thrown out was - some of the
people we traveled up with in an 89 vette' had to be stopped - and their
time was so skewed as a direct result, they estimated they had completed
the race at a +6mph from their target! - But really they finished in 2nd
place in their class (140mph) with only a -0.3579.

Anyway - it was fun.
