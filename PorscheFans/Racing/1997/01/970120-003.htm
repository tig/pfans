This is all true, but the requirements for applying for a Club Racing license
are a little more clearly defined.  You have to have completed at *least* 8
days of PCA Drivers Ed, *and* be signed off by your regions Chief Instructor
verifying not only your 8 or more days, but the fact that you have acquired
enough track driving skill to take the next step.  This rule applies to
people who have no prior racing experience and who are applying for a "Rookie
License".
