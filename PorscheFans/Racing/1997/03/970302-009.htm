Hi Bill,

I have a 1987 Carrera and I have noticed similar happenings.  I attribute
the problem to leaking oil return tubes.  The oil drips a little (also from
the left side camshaft o-ring) and when the car is driven at highway speeds
I noticed these drops on the rear of the deck lid from the wind causing a
vortex around the tail and depositing the oil drops on the deck lid and
bumpers (it's not allot, but I sure notice them because my car is mint).

Anyway, your '96 may have a slight leak some where that is causing the same
problem - But I doubt it.  It could be attributed more to the condensation
coming out of the mufflers at early start-up.  Since you have a white car,
it's even more obvious!
