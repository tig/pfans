        A couple of days ago Ted asked about classing of the 944 Turbo Cup
Cars. Finally for 1997 they have a home in class D.  This is in the
addendum page to the rules that went out to license holders about a month
ago.  I think the weight limit is 2770pounds (but don't quote me on this)

atmospheric pressure).
