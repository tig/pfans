Sebring Octoberfest, October 17-19, 1997, a weekend filled with championship

Stars compete against America's top drivers. Feel the power of Ferrari,

See the American debut of the McLaren F1 GTR, Porsche GT1 and the Lotus GT1

makes its first stop on U.S. soil.
The FIA GT Championship is Europe's fastest growing racing series, second
only to Formula One. Professional SportsCar Racing is North America's
showcase for top drivers and sports cars. This event milestone in world
history of motorsports. Don't miss it!
