Well...

any change not specifically outlined for stock or prepared makes the car a
"super class" car.

Let's see: aftermarket FI - puts you into super class
           935 front - puts you into superclass
           coilovers instead of tortion bars - super class
           gutted interior - super class
           I think the rim width bumps you up to super class, depending on
what width is stock.
           brakes are free in prepared.

3.2 liter engine puts you in GT-3.

To run in prepared class all you need to do is: replace entire suspension
with stock, throw out the FI, re-install entire interior, and you're ready
to go. 

Looks pretty clean cut, your car is a GT-3 car until the day it dies.
