Barry Lenoble asked about roll bar bolts. 

Grade 8 (six lines on head) is stronger than Grade 5 (3 lines). Spend a few
bucks and get 3/8-24 Grade 8 bolts (fine thread). Buy them from a reputable
source because there have been problems with cheap Far Eastern forgeries on
the market. You won't find 8.8 or 10.8 bolts, those are metric designations
and you have SAE-size holes in your hardware. Unless the holes in your
hardware are slightly oversize, don't put a 10 mm bolt through a 3/8" hole,
it won't fit. 

Sources -- just about any good hardware store should carry these, I can buy
them locally at about 4 places within a 10 minute drive, but if all else
fails call Aircraft Spruce and Specialty in Fullerton, CA, they have an 800
number.
