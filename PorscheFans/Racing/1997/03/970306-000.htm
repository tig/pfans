For those of you in the Midwest who might be looking for a test and tune
day, or who are considering racing at our club race in September at
Indianapolis Raceway Park, Brian Stewart of AutoCar has rented the track
March 22, 1997. He has three or four openings. You will be able to run
you heart out all day. Give him a call at 317-750-4515 for particulars.

The track is on the west side of Indianapolis, just outside the 465
beltway and has easy access. Race gas is available just outside the
track.
