Michael,

I am pretty sure it would put you in one of the GT2 classes. If you ran
slicks, you would be in GT2R and if you ram DOT tires you would be in
GT2S.

Kevin Buckler runs in GT2R. He and his car are competitive in IMSA GTS3
races. GT2S, in the races I have seen, is a class that does not get many
entries (I have seen west coast races only). It appears that the people
that fall into the GT2 class like to put slicks on their cars.
