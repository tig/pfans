My '72T time trial/race toy has stock calipers & rotors with Pagid orange
pads and the Castrol $70/l brake fluid.  I've had no problems with not
having enough brakes or brake fade since upgrading.  I'd highly recommend
switching your fluid first before spending money on more expensive parts
to see if this is enough.
