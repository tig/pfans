Okay, for all you folks looking for an alternative to expensive racing I
suggest you check out:

http://www.muller.net/karting/index.html

You can race an entire season in a vehicle that pulls almost 3G's in a turn
for only a few thousand $/year.  Standard entry fees are between $20-$30
for a sprint race, slightly more for an enduro if you want to race at
Sumitt, Mid Ohio, Daytona, etc...  Even Carroll Smith recommends karting as
the best way to learn racing.
