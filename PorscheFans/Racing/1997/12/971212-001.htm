The Club Racing News is mailed to all licensed PCA Club Racers.

for a club race license. It is not necessary to submit a medical

to actually become a licensed racer. Just applt for the license
with a note that you will not be active, and you will be on the
mailing list each your that your 'license' fees are paid.
