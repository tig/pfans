Paul,

My advice for a place to stay is the Radisson in Corning.  It's about 20-25
minutes from the track but it's worth the trip.  

The Radisson is just one block from Market Street.  Market street has a
number of cafe type establishments.  I ate at 3 of them and was very
satisfied with the food.  There are also a couple of bars on market street,
everything from a coffee bar to a topless bar just around the corner.  

Have a good time.
