Went to the NNJR event at the Glen about three weeks ago and here are a
couple of answers to your questions:

>I'd appreciate some advice on places to stay, restaurants...

The Glen has their own web site at http://www.theglen.com. It has a
bunch of useful stuff including local hotels.  I used it to find a hotel
which was only about 4 miles from the track.  Just "Dial for Dollars"
from this list.....

A pretty good map of the track can be found at
http://theglen.com/m1.html.  "The Boot" is in the far right corner of
the map. The toe of the boot is a pretty wild  - just after the
"off-camber left".. Photographs can be found at
http://www.porschenet.com/the-glen.html.  This is PCA's NorthEast
Region's site and it's really well done.....

Hope this helps you out.
