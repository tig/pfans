On the 914-6 race car I crew for, we are switching from the
Performance Friction pads to Pagid Orange.  The primary reason we are
doing this is to get better rotor wear.  The PF pads stop very well,
but we were having to replace the rotors every couple of weekends.
The car has Willwood Superlite III calipers and Coleman 12" rotors.
We are switching to an all new brake system and Pagid pads based on
recommendations from a company that supplies brakes to many of the
Porsches racing in the Professional Sportscar series.  I'm planning to
run the Pagid pads on my car as well.
