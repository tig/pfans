As for the passing under yellow I can shed some light on the problem.
Normally I would be running in that race but I recently sold my E stock 944
Turbo because the racing is getting far too serious not to mention expensive,

As a result I was working in the timing and scoring tower at Pocono. Scoring

to publish the original standings, as the race finished. The reason that no

don't necessarily agree with the decision but the circumstances were unusual.
The scary part is that a bunch of mostly experienced racers, going at extreme
speeds, don't know the rules or chose to ignore them.
