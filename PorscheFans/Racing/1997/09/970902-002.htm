You drill holes in the middle of the steel control arms. Be very careful
to get them in exactly the same location on both arms or you will turn
one direction better than the other. ;)

You will find the 28mm bar is too big to balance the car without
replacing the rear bar.

A word of warning: I broke 3 control arms (2 autocrossing and one in an
accident) all of which broke in half right through the hole you drill.
Can you say 'stress riser'? You can update to the aluminum arms from an
'86 944 or you can reinforce the steel arms to help alleviate this
situation. But the bottom line is that the Weltmeister bar was designed
for the aluminum arm cars and the pre 85.5 arrangement is a bit of a
kludge.
