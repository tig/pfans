Barry,

On Track is the best motosports reporting magazine I have found. Keep in
mind that it reports on racing, not technology. If you want to find out
how pneumatic valves work, this is not the magazine you want. But if you
want reports on testing, qualifying, races, silly season, etc., this is
the magazine for you.
