Interested in driving Willow Springs with an amiable group of drivers?

The Touring Car Club has an event on November 22-23. Unlike Porsche-only
groups, your car can run  in time trial class pretty much as-is. You only
need a roll bar if you have a convertible. No fire suit is required for
time trial.

This is a great group for first-time track drivers. The driving course is
$225, and covers all the basics, plus you get plenty of track time. After
passing the course (or if you have a license from PCA or P.O.C. or another
recognized sanctioning body) you can time trial.

Several P.O.C. and PCA types show up, but you get to run against Corvettes,
M3s and an assortment of (surprisingly fast) Japanese pocket rockets.

There's also a race group, but the car prep and safety requirements are
comparable to other clubs.

Willow Springs is a fast, fun track. And TCC is a great group of people.
Join us.
