I bought some of that high-density padding for my roll bar. The surface of the
padding is slick so the problem of rotation is worse. I ended up using two
of the U-shaped sections on the overhead bar to give good coverage even if it
rotates during an accident. I think this is especially important on a street
driven car.

The U-shaped padding does not want bend much tighter than a 12" radius without
tearing so probably some of the conventional padding will be needed for a
full cage.
