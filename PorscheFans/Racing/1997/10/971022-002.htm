Depending on your material, hairspray *might* work (Clairol's "Final Net"
is the stickiest.) Bicycle shops use it to prevent handlebar grips from
rotating. It *is* flammable (more so before it dries :-), but then, so is
rubber cement. 

I use ties myself, with racer tape. A bit'o'tape on the inside and edges
prevents rotating, and tape on the foam before applying the zip ties
prevents the tie cutting into the foam.

Something new I'm going to try on the Porsche cage is the "U"-shaped
padding that Racer Wholesale has (for the incredible price of $9.49 per 3'
stick). It is supposed to be more flame retardant and more energy-absorbing
than the regular $3/stick stuff .
