Some hard learned 911 Oil Cooling Truths -
1)	Always use the radiator type cooler, the "tube type" just does not 
dissipate enough heat.
2)	More cooling is better, just keep the thermostat.
3)	Keep the stock cooler and add another cooler in the other 
fenderwell (if you can give up the washer bottle it is easier, but you 
can still do it)
4)	Use MOCAL coolers from BAT in Florida, call Eric at 813-355-0005, 
he is familiar with the Porsche applications and has all the parts to 
make life easier.
