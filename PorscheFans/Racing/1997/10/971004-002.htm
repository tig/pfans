Bob:
Two issues about powder coating fan:
1. Powder coating can bring the fan tips in contact with the housing due to
the tight tolerance (it did on mine anyway). So you may need to remove the
coating from the tips or premachine them before coating to open tolerance to
begin with - especially if you coat the housing as well. 
2. You should check fan balance after coating to be sure it is okay.
3. Consider polishing the fan and housing if you want that brite aluminum
show car look but be sure to seal it properly as it won't look nice for long
and is a real pain to keep nice.
