Ross:

As far as I know, cold-drawn-seamless tubing and DOM tubing differ
only in the steel from which they're drawn; they're both
manufactured by essentially the same process.  Cold-drawn-seamless
is strong, expensive, and drawn from 1018; DOM is just as strong,
cheaper, and drawn from 1020.
