SPECIALIZED PORSCHE FACTORY RACING TECHNICAL INFORMATION:

(1)  "INFORMATION REGARDING PORSCHE VEHICLES USED FOR SPORTS
     PURPOSES"  (W 351) 67 pages.
     For Porsche Models:      1968  911, 911T, 911L & 911S

This 1968 Porsche factory publication is about racing 911's and totals 67
pages.
The first section is titled:  "Porsche Production Competition Cars 1968".
Other
sections include:  Increasing engine performance. The rally engine and the
racing engine. Camshaft table. Gearbox, and suggestions for transmission
ratios. Limited slip differential. Wheels, tires and brakes. Adjustments to
running
gear. Shock absorbers and stabilizers. Lowering the Vehicle. Roll Bar. Parts
List.

This 67 page factory publication on racing 911's is reproduced in its
entirety 
and is available in a 3-ring binder with appropriate slip cover, postage
paid,
