Scott,
	There is an old quote that works here, "The way to go faster is to go 
faster".

	What that means is that before you start trying to get more bang out of 
the car, get all the bang that is in the car already. Most drivers without 
say 30-60 track days don't drive the car at 10/10's. My recommendation is 
if you have less than 30 track days is to buy more track days. Get the seat 
time in, then when you run out of car start with the suspension and brakes.

After you have used up all of that money, you just might look at more power
