For bleeding brakes I use a hand air pump with a 15' hose, I hook the
hose over the overflow hose outlet on the master cylinder reservoir. You
simply  pressurize the reservoir with the pump and crack the bleed screw
with your other hand.  I can then reach every corner of the car and
bleed the brakes without help. 

It doesn't take much pressure, I doubt if it even takes 10 psi.
Don't let the reservoir go dry or you will be injecting air into the
lines.
