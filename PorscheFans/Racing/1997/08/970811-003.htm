Joe: 
 
This car would be in a GT class under PCA rules, GT4 I believe.  The only 
gating factor for the GT classes is displacement and I believe GT4 captures 
all NA cars from 2.0 to 2.8.  There are no suspension limitiations or tire 
size limitations or weight restrictions so you can imagine what you may run 
against.  My guess is that you'd have a hard time being competitive.  Howeve=
r, 
if you like the car and the price is right and it's fresh and sound, GT4 is =
a 
very popular and competitive class and you'll have plenty of cars to dice 
with.  For reference, my experience has been that you can find a competitive=
