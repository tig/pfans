Hi Matthew!  2 options on seats:
a)  OMP brand.  Currently in my '87 Carrera, legal for Club Racing, very
comfy and allows for adjustment forward/back.  Excellent compromise for
street/track use.
b)  Pagid brand.  Currently in my SO's '73 race car.  Strictly a race seat,
metal, bolted in with minimal padding for the tush.
Both work well for we 2 drivers:  He's a "big guy", with broad shoulders.
(Your description fits him to a "t"...).  I'm tall but skinny.  For race
seat, I've had a special insert made to accommodate my less-padded rear.
Purchased both from Bob Johnson at Johnson Autosport in Northfield, MN
(507-663-1210) who is, himself, a Big Guy.  Hope this helps.  Teresa
