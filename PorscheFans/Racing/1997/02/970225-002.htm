Hi, Sheree,



We (the Hudson Valley Region PCA) used to run an autocross series as part
of a council of sports car clubs and we would use the SCCA Solo II rulebook
as the means of assigning classes...

Actually, we insisted that the entrants class themselves, and the other 
entrants in the class protest them out, if necessary. That kept us out of 
the classing business and shifted the responsibility elsewhere.

You can buy the SCCA rulebook (a new one each year) from SCCA in Colorado.


Good Luck!
