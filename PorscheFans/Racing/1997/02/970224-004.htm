Jay....I havn't beenable to follow the complete exchange on Reds vs Yellows
but I can pass this on.  The difference as Koni describes, is the Yellows
have twice the compression rate the Reds have.  The adjustablility is only on
rebound and service life of the Reds vs Yellow should be the same.  I have
use Red's on a 914-6 with 21 mm torsion bars and they are in year 7 of their
life, incident free.  The setting on 914s will certainly be different but for
refference they are set at about 1 full turn off soft.
If track is your intent go with the Yellows.  For a good place to discuss the
different applications try Paragon at 800-200-9366 or
paragon@interreconnect.net.  They have been very knowledgable and their
customer service is first rate.  Good luck!
