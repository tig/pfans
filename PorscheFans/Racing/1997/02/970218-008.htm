Regarding Red vs Yellow Konis, I recommend going to the Yellows if you
are going to do track or autocross.  I bought Reds for my 944 and then
bought Yellows a year later.  I know of many others that have done the
same.  There is a difference in performance on the track and you'll want
it later on in your track life.  They are guaranteed for life and KONI
replaced one from my 911 with absolutely no hassle.
