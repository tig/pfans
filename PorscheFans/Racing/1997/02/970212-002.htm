Jerry,
At the last drivers event at Road Atlanta last year, a friend and 
avid racer tried the Cool Carbon B2 compound on the fronts of his 
930. As you describe, lots of disconcerting wheel shake when braking. 
In fact it was so bad that he put back in a set of the used blue pads 
to get him through the weekend. Cool Carbon eventually took the B2 
pads back (I think, with lots of complaining). It would appear that 
they have a problem with the B2 compound. 
I race a Carerra wide body with 930 brakes. I had used the blue pads 
till mid season and changed the fronts over to Pagid oramge. I like 
the Pagid better because they seem to wear less (have 2 weekend of 
experience on them) and they seen to be more rotor friendly (via 
visual inspections of front rotorrs). Stopping and fade resistance is 
excellent with both the old blue Cool Carbons and the Pagids.
