Comment:  I have been time trialing my '86 944 Turbo with the GGR PCA for a
couple years now.  The pad used has always been Performance Friction in
compound 80.  Of course they squeal, but the pedal feel is good when cold and
only gets better as they heat up.  Fade has never been an issue, even up to
temperatures that warp the front rotors, which has been a continuing problem.
 For our last event, I replaced my cracked rotors with new slotted ones, and
installed a cool brake kit, to no avail.

Several fast Turbo and Turbo S drivers 'highly recommend' Pagid Orange pads to
help remedy this particular problem.  As Jerry has opened this door, I too
would like to hear any comments or opinions.  Thanks.
