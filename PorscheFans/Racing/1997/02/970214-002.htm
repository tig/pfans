PFans,

For many of us who tried the newest generation of Cool Carbon's brake 
pads (B2) last year, we found that, as the pad wore, residue adhered to the 
rotors creating the feeling of warped rotors under heavy braking. The 
fix was to clean the rotors and find a different brake pad. When I 
contacted  Cool Carbon I was treated appropriately and was sent a 
complete set of the previous generation Blues promptly. I was told to 
NOT use B2's and wait for whatever came after B2's. Rumor has it that 
a number of my peers were treated very poorly when they tried deal 
with Cool Carbon.  Thus, the demise of Cool Carbon the company.  

Pete
