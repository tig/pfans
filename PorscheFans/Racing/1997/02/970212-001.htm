I have been running CC Orange compound, which I get from Weston 
Motorsports (Paul Weston great guy). on my 914-4, I found that I could 
only get one race weekend out of a set, assume that I am breaking to 
often, or the fact that I brake very late. Anyway I found that the cool 
carbons work by sharing thgier compound with the rotor. That it they coat 
the rotor, so when you change pads the mix-match of the compounds gets 
real weird.

I am running the new B2.5 compound this weekend and Will report back, but 
I was told by Paul to either clean, turn, or replace the rotors before 
beading them in.

I plan to do this with #3 steel wool.
