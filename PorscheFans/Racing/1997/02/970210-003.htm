John,

Don't be tempted to go to the 255/50/16, I tried them on 9" FUCHs.  They
don't fit even with rolled lip.  The 911 chassis is slightly offset. 
The tire barely fits on the left but rubs on the right side.  I've used
245/45 YOKO 0008RS2s and they work fine.  Good luck.
