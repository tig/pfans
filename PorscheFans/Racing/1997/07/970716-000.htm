A good place to start!  Just remember that if you plan on doing track
events, the motorcycle (M) rated helmets will not pass tech... for
automotive use helmets must be rated SA90 and soon SA95 will be the
minimum requirements.   Why is this (M vs. SA)?  Because the SA rating
allows for fire retardance (a very big deal when you're strapped in a
burning car with only seconds to exit), multiple impacts (crashes or
roll-overs!), etc.
