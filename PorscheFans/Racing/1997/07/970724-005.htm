I've looked into harnesses just lately.
I found that MOMO was for me.  TRW is widely used by very capable and
knowledgeable drivers and was my second choice.

You will need to decide on having a brace or fitting a rollbar.

I don't think most drivers run the submarine (crotch) strap, so you're
looking at a four point harness.  But this is not so much an opinion as
just an observation.

Also, I've been advised that shoulder pads are worth the extra pennies for
the comfort.
