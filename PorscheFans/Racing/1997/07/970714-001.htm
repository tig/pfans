Air cooled VW hotrods often use straight-cut crank to cam gears.  Bruce
Anderson has a photo of a Porsche factory racing (962) straight cut
intermediate shaft gear in his book.  I want to reduce the thrust loads
helical gears impose on the crank and intermediate shaft bearings, and
don't think the extra noise in a racing engine will bother me (straight cut
gears in VW didn't add noticeably to noise).  I assume I am not going to
be able to find a used factory gear of this type, at least not at a price I
can afford for Club Racing.  Does anyone know of a machinist/gear
house who has made such gears for 911 engines?
