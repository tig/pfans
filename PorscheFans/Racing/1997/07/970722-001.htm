> So what else did I forget?  Any interest in forming a race team in

Driving gear. You need an SFI suit, nomex underwear (or a two layer
suit), gloves, SA 90 helmet, etc. You need steel lug nuts ($40 at
Automotion).  You need to get a catalog from Racer Wholesale. Call them
at 800-886-RACE, or http://www.racerwholesale.com. They're the best!

How are you going to get to the track? I drove my car to Lime Rock, and
I'll be driving to Pocono. I can fit a lot of stuff in it.  However,
I'm not comfortable doing that, so I'm thinking about what to get to
tow with. I keep moving from an old cargo van, to a motorhome, to a
pick up, and back again.

The last thing you need is a crew. While you can probably do without,
having at least 1 person there makes it a lot easier. I had a friend
meet me at the track. He timed laps, took tire temps and pressures,
helped me figure out how to adjust the car, gave advice and support,
and made the weekend go a lot easier.
