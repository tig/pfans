Barry, I'm no expert, for sure, but I attended the entire Parade Club Race
at TWS. I saw *alot* of practicing. I mean, a *lot*. I think you'd wear
the tires, brakes, suspension, engine, etc. out faster than you're
figuring, i.e. you should up your rental costs to cover it. Also, I've
seen a lot of spins and car to car contact. I know you can get insurance
for that sort of thing but it's bound to be expensive. I think you might
also attract some "risky" drivers. You know, people that don't want to
take risks with their car, but would be happy to take risks with someone
elses? Some food for thought...
