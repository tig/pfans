Hi Josh,
   Camberballs are a trade name for a particular product.  
Several companies make a ball type strut mount.
   In my experience, they make a significant difference 
especially if you are doing other suspension stuff, using 
sticky tires, and pushing your car.  We use Camberballs on my 
wife's 944 and Kelly-Moss on my C2.  Neither of us noticed any 
increase in ride harshness etc, but they do sharpen up the 
front end.
