Regarding 951 front springs, Chris' advice on front springs is right on the
mark. The 951/944 needs lots of help to reduce inherent understeer.  250lb
springs are really  street springs. I think anything more than stock T bars
in the back requires at least 350lb. front springs.  I run 550lb fronts.

With all this talk about springs, be sure to get some sort of camber
plate/spherical bearing for the upper strut mount. The camber loss from stock
strut mounts works against any improvements made with bars, springs or
struts. IMHO, This is the first step to getting the car to stick and making
front tire wear bearable.
