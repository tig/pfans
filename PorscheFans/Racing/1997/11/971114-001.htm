Hello P-Fans
	I have found Bridgestone RE-71's to be the best rain tire out there.
Bridgestones S0-2's are probably even better - but I haven't used them yet.
The Bridgestones give plenty of warning when they approach their limit. They
kind of feel like an eraser skipping on a piece of paper. The Yok
AVS-intermediates - Suck in the rain & snow! Ditto for the Yok A-509's. I do
Agree that the Dunlap D40--M2 are kind of scary in the rain. But are a decent
cheap-o tire overall.
