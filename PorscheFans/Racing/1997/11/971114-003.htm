I have a set of Yokohama R032A tires on my 993.   They work well in 
the rain as well as the dry in my experience.   Jan Mayo (our Zone 5 
Representative) and her husband Ed have them on an early 911 (72? 
Targa) and also report very good results.   They recently attended a 
Driver's Education event called "Coastal Classic" where it rained most 
of the first day and had a blast from what I heard while others flew 
off the track into the mud.   They also drove the car from Dallas to 
Corpus Christi and back on these tires in the rain.   

The tires might be worth checking out.   The prices on the tires were 
quite reasonable from our local Discount Tire Store.
