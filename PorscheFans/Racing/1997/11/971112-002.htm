     Re the recent posts about airbag deployment in Driver's Ed incidents-- 
     I'd recommend installation of an airbag bypass switch. This is a very 
     simple thing to wire up--so simple, that even NHTSA is going to permit 
     it soon in passenger cars. I removed my airbag-equipped steering wheel 
     for a Momo (technically, not so legal in a street car), but to protect 
     an instructor or passenger from an airbag deployment, I got the cutoff 
     switch installed. Now, instructors no longer have to stare at my Hot 
     Lap box, which is attached to a plate right over the passenger airbag 
     cover, and remark, "Uh, is that thing on?"
