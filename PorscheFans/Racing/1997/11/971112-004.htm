Contact the vintage club that you will join.  Each club has different eligability rule.  For instance, RMVR (Rocky Mountain Vinatage Racing) does not permit under 3 liter closed wheel after 1967.  So we have no 914s in the club.

If your not sure what club to join, buy a copy of Vintage Motorsports at a good magazine stand.  They have a list of all the clubs and the membership contact in each issue.
