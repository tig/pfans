>I am thinking about "big red"calipers for my 944TS. Will they take me
>out of my stock class? If I "run a chip", this will def'n change my
>classs, but would adding the calipers at that point make any difference?

A non - factory DME chip moves your 944 TurboS from "stock" to "prepared",
or from class E to D.  Once in prepared, "calipers, rotors, and master
cylinders are free, except the number of master cylinders must be as
supplied from the factory."
Hence either modification moves you up a class, but both are OK in "prepared".
