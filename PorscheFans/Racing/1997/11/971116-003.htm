I've heard many times that 15" wheels won't clear the front brake calipers
on ABS equipped 944'. But my spare tire from my /91 S2 is mounted on a
factory 15"x 5 wheel, so maybe they do? I would think on the racetrack the
15" wheel/tire combo should be lighter than a 16" or 17" combo. Plus you
could change your effective final drive ratio to a higher numerical ratio
with a smaller overall diameter. Anyone actually fitted 8"+ wide by 15"
wheels on /87+ 944 for racing?
