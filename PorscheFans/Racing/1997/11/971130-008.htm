Although not required, at least a harness bar is highly recommended.  A
harness that is just attached to the rear seat-belt mounting points is much
more likely to cause spine compression injuries.

If the harness goes from your shoulders directly DOWN to the mounting
points, that's the direction force will be applied in a collision.  Just
imagine all the energy that's generated in a collision being used to crush
your spine.  A harness bar will keep the harness at an angle where
compression injuries are far less likely.
