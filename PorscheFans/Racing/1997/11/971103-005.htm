Yes. However, what if you go all out, spend big bucks, then

Yeah, you most likely aren't going to win a race unless your car is
prepped to the max. However, that doesn't mean you aren't going to
have a great time! This club race stuff is all about having fun, and
less about winning. You don't the super prepped car to have fun!
Hell, I did my two races knowing that I would be a back marker, and
I had a blast.

I understand about safety, and there really can't be any compromise
made there. However, I also know about cost. If you make this so
expensive, you will cut out a lot of the little guys (me). 

Perhaps there can be one set of safety requirements for people
with rookie / provisional licenses, and another for fully licensed
drivers. This way, the first timers can get their feet wet without
too many modifications / expenses, and if you decide this is for
you then you have to get all the safety gear. 

Specifically, how about the roll bar rule for first two races,
then roll cage requirement?
