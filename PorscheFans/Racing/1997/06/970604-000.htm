In case you didn't know:

PCA's web site is operational:
http://www.pca.org

If you're not a member of PCA, you can locate your local region (zip code
directory to be added shortly after 1st of June) and join directly over the
internet-automated application form. You can also link to all regions with
web sites and contact their local membership chair.

If you're a Member of PCA you can use the web site to change your mailing
address/change your region affiliation, Order Goodies from the PCA Executive
Office, Read the latest issue of Club Racing News & link to all regions with
web sites.

Check it out and let the webmaster know what you think.
