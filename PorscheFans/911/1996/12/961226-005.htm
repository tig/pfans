Mario,

I bought fuel system pressure tester form NAPA about a year ago and it was
~$100.  I think the one thing you'll need to make sure of is that you get one
that allows you to flow through the gauge and still read pressure.  Of course
you also need to make sure it can handle CIS pressures.  I'm not sure if they
are different in the Porsche applications but for VW's that could be almost
100psi.  Hope that helps.
