Sounds like a typical switch failure.  

Procedure:  


        a.  Disconnect the battery ground.

        b.  Pop off the center cap on the switch; remove the 10mm nut
holding the knob
on.

        c.  Use a split ring pliers to spin off the plastic collar.


        d.  The switch will now come out forward and down.  Map the wires,
or replace one 
at a time.

        e.  Reverse the above.
