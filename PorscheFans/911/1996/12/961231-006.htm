This is just a data point but...
When I installed Bilstein strut inserts (Boge strut) in my '86 I received
the same advice i.e. put some antifreeze or oil in the strut for heat
transfer.
In my case, this damaged the seal at the top of the strut. I called to
Bilstein
to get new seals and was told _not_ to put anything in the strut. (Bilstein
was very helpful by the way.)
