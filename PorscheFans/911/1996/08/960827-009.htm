I neglected to mention that I have BOGE struts with Bilstein
inserts. These are probably different than the factort Bilstein struts.
The Bilstein Boge-strut inserts come with an instruction sheet which
states "Dispose of the rubber bump stop at the top of your
strut when installing Bilstein inserts, as these inserts have the
bump stops built-in". There is no room in the strut to add an external
bump stop when putting bilstein inserts in Boge struts.
Also there is no small shaft on these inserts.

Hope this clears it up.
