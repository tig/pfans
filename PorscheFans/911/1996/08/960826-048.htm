Bill:

Cause:  Old age/Battery acid/Rust.  Very Common.

Remedy:  Replace the front pan section.  There are aftermarket pieces made to
do this, but the factory stuff fits the best, looks original.  Most people
split the section about halfway back on the fuel tank.

By the way, this is not a fun job.  It is also not cheap.
