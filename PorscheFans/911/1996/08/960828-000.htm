Opinion:

Given the fact that I run a 3.4ltr pushing in the neighborhood of
300-325HP and my (70,000 mile plus) SC clutch doiewsn't slip, and that
930s use organic clutches, I think your plan to change to a puck type
clutch is overkill.

Puck and paddle clutches work like on-off switches.  NO slip or give EVER.

Not particularly good for your crank, trans or CVs as they create
fairly large and sharp shock pulses as they engage.  THis can lead to
varoius failures including crankshaft....

The only thing left to slip is the tire.
