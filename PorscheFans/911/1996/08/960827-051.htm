Tess,

My heart goes with the early 911, but the smart move would be a 914.  Most
of these are going to be 4 cylinder models.  If you can find one in good
shape, drive it with the 4 for awhile to get used to the 901 shift pattern
and the handling.  You also need to learn to double clutch, if you don't
know how already.    Then you can put a slightly modified 3.0ltr or highly
modified 2.7ltr.  The advantage of the 2.7 is that you can run the car in
GT3 PCA club racing, and have a car that can run up front.  Good luck on
your future.
