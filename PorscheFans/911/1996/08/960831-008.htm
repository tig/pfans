mjt@adc.com (Mike J Tietel) asked :

<< I'm a novice AutoX (oops- Driver's Ed) participant.  I'll be
attending my second low-speed (parking lot) event in a couple
weeks.

What should I be running for tire pressures on an 87 Targa with
Comp T/A VR4 205/55VR16 (front) and 225/50VR16 (rear)? >>

These are VERY soft sidewalled street tires. So you really should
run very high pressures to avoid rolling over and wearing out
the sidewalls and outer edge. I have been autocrossing seriously 
since before the "R" tires were available and used to use about 38 
front and 42 rear on those types of tires. Now I am about 13 pounds 
lower on BFG TA R1s.

Don't worry about "max inflation" warnings printed on the sidewall 
for low speed competition events. Hundreds of thousands of autox
runs have been taken at pressures in the 40s and 50s (FWD)
with no problems. Just bleed them down before going home!

Regards, Mike Piera   '72 911S Targa
