I did this last year as my right side mount broke.  There is now an 
updated factory mount, slightly wider, and with a welded in 
reinforcement rib.  It is available from almost every aftermarket 
Porsche parts outfit.  I bought mine from German Parts & Restoration. 
Replace both mounts while you are doing it.

I used a grinder to cut off the old mounts.  I then welded the new 
mounts into place.  I used some Wurth underbody coating to cover 
everything after I was done.  If you are not endowed with traditional 
fabrication and welding skills, have this work done by a 
professional.
