Forgot to mention this in previous post; I tried three different performance
chips in my '84 Carrera, and sent them all back.  In accelleration tests,
never got noticeable improvement (some made things worse, like
Autothority; another company's one had atrocious off-idle behavior - gas
pedal felt like an on-off switch).  Test was as follows, always on same 
stretch of road:

1) Place car in third gear, hold 2500 RPM
2) When pass certain road marker, floor accelerator
3) Start stopwatch when tach hits 3000 RPM, stop it when hit 6000 RPM
4) Return to same road point as (1), repeat test two more times

Mass flow sensor dropped times by about 1 second, as I remember.
