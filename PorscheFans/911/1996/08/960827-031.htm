In my continuing quest for grease I finally got some German CV joint   
grease trade marked MEISTERSATZ.  It probably means dumb American that   
didn't buy CV joint grease in local auto part store.  At any rate I got   
it from a local Porsche repair shop who ordered up a couple of extra   
tubes when I asked for it.  The stuff is distributed in the US by CRP   
Industries Inc. Carteret NJ.  Cost me about $7.00 for a 3 oz tube.   
 Outrageous but at the rate things have been going it's one of the least   
expensive things I've bought for my project as of late.
