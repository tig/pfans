Sorry Bob, I'm afraid you have the brake caliper seal in the wrong place.  The
seal sits in a groove in the caliper bore and does not move, so the condition
of the caliper bore matters little, as long the the seal's groove is cleaned
out and intact.

The piston moves past the seal, so it must be smooth to avoid leakage.  I used
the finest steel wool I could get (maybe 0000?) to clean the piston, which
seemed to do a fine job.  No problems.

BTW, keep us posted on the transmission rebuild.  I'm considering tackling mine
this winter too.
