Subject:CV Joint grease - My recomendation
Date:Wed, 28 Aug 1996 08:33:06 -0400

I have been using Valvolene Synthetic High Temp Bearing grease 
in my 911 CV joints for several years now with great sucess.

It costs about $6.00 per tube, something like 14oz. whcih loads in a grease gun.

It's kinda nasty black, and it says it's designed for wheel bearings
and CV joints.

I lost a CV boot at the Glen this year and continued to drive, fully
expecting to trash the axle.  I replaced the axle as a matter of course
when I returned home.  After an additional 6 run groups (we estimate)
the CV joint surfaces all look great. The grease stayed in the joint
(which is off an '86 carrera) and the axle looks great.  No play.
Beautiful surfaces.  Wish I hadn't replaced it.  Now it's my spare.

FYI,
	Steve
