>My question is that before I assemble the piston,
>caliper and new seals back together should I lightly polish the piston with
>SOLVOL AUTOSOL (Chrome cleaner) or AUTOGLYM super resin polish.
>Or should I just clean them with a clean rag to remove any slight bits
>of corrosion which appear on the piston.

Another opinion.  I don't think it much matters what you
clean the pistons with.  Clean pistons are important because
as Kevin Gross points out, the crud can ruin something else, but the
important part to have clean is the piston bores.  The pistons
themselves don't actually touch anything.  It's the rubber
seal on the piston that rides against the bore, not the
piston itself.  It's the bore that needs to be clean and
pit-free.
