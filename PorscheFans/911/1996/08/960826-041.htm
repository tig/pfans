Here's the info I have, but I know it's several months old.

There are two 14" and one 13", that won't apply to Porsche owners.  Other
then that all they have are 205/50R15, 225/50R15, and 245/45R17.  (Sure is
nice to have an old car with 15's)  They have listings for 205/55R16,
225/50R16, 245/45R16, and 255/50R16 as well as several 17" sizes.  But there
is no data listed for them.  The last time I spoke to my local rep, he said
those sizes are not available, and he did not know when they would be.

Tech specs:

Tire        UTOG       Overall   Overall      Tread     Tread
Size                   Width     Diameter     Width     Depth

205/50R15   60-A-A     8.1       23.2         8.0       6/32
225/50R15   60-A-A     8.9       24.0         8.3       6/32
245/45R17   60-A-A     9.5       25.7         9.4       6/32

All measurements are in inches.  I think the UTOG ratings translate to
treadwear 60, and traction and temperature ratings of A.
