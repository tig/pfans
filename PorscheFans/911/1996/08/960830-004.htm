HI Bob,

I don't know of any adjusted alignment specifications for the 993. I have
some preferred settings from Porsche for track use, but they are really aimed
at just making the cars go faster. The specifications that I was given for
track use are:
Front:
Toe +5' (total)
Camber -2 degrees 10"
Caster 5 degrees 20 minutes +15'/-0

Rear
Toe (per wheel) +10" +5'/-0
Camber -2 degrees 10'
Everything else is the standard settings. 
Note: The tire Wear will increase with these settings over the standard
settings.
