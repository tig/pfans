If this is a racing car then a racing clutch will be Okay, but if this is a
street car you had better approach this with moderation. The spring center
and marcel springs will allow you to drive this on the street. A puck clutch
wit not springs will be really tough for street use. 

A good street clutch wont slip, you must just have a bad clutch. 

The stiffer motor mounts will be a lot easier to live with.
