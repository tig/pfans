Mike et all:
You didn't say what car you have but if you have a close approximation of   
the Amp hr requirements (check your owners manual) you can get one that   
will fit the battery tray by looking at the physical sizes shown on a   
price list at your friendly auto parts store or Sears for that matter.  I   
did something a little different.  My cars is a 75 and the only body   
corrosion in the car is in the battery tray.  I mounted a plastic Marine   
battery box on the tray and got a battery that fit in the box.  The box   
is like $5 (cheaper than the CV joint grease I got yesterday) and comes   
with a strap down lid.
