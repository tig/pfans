Vic et all:
Glad to hear things went well and welcome to the land of fire.  My   
solution for headers was to use the SSI 74 style header/heat exchanger.   
 It gives you equal length headers along with heat.  Bruce shows a   
picture of these in his book.   I don't know about mounting it in an SC   
but in mine I needed a dual inlet muffler and changes to the oil lines to   
route them around the front of the engine.  An added benefit is the   
Stainless Steel and these things look so good you'll want to mount a   
mirror so you can show them off.  It cost me about $1k in total by   
sourcing the parts piece meal.
