Fake Fuchs are Junk.  They are made to look like fuchs, but they are cast not forged.
The problem is that the design id for the much stronger forged alloy, so the centers of the
cast fakes can actually rip out.

Also, used fakes are all but worthless.  Real fuchs can be concidered an investment as that always 
seem sellable.

If you shop you can find nice used fuchs (7+8") for about $1000, and expect to get $500 or so for your
6+7x16" fuchs.

You can use either 91 or 951 offset, though the 951 offset jobs seems to be slightly less expensive.
