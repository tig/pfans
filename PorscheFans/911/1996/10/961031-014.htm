Definition 1 
Old race car racing with a Vintage Racing organization.  Some SCCA chapters 
have vintage divisions, others on the West Coast include HARE, CSRG, HMSA, 
VARA, and SOVREN.  Each sets it's own definition of "Vintage".  In general, 
cars are 25years old or older, are supposed to be run in the same 
configuration they ran in when racing 25 or more years ago and the emphasis is 
on participating and having fun rather than winning. Races usally run under 
'no contact' rule. Some groups insist on cars being run on the same size and 
stiction tires they ran on 25 years ago.
