Wade,

   You didn't say if your wheels have been stripped or are still clear anodized.
If they are still anodized, you can remove it using drain cleaner. Might take
several applications, but it's cheap and it will do the job.
   After they are stripped you can use any good quality metal polish to remove
oxide and leave a wax surface. I use Mothers, but I think I will try some from
Griots when it's used up.
