Hi P- Fans - 

Saw a post on this topic recently.  Early model 911 sunvisors have 
a habit of  puffing up after the foam inside starts to deteriate.  You can
replace them for about $90.00 ea. or you could try this quick fix that I read

some place.  Buy a can of the foam insulation  at your local hardware
store (about 3 bucks).  Make a small, 1/4in, cut in the forward edge of the
visor
and insert the nozzle and shoot some foam in there.  How much?  Use your
best judgement, but it doesn't take too much.  Put the visor between two
boards
with some weight on it for about and hour.  I tried it and so far so good.
