I put Blaupunkt's on the rear deck of my Targa. I don't remember the
numbers, but I believe they are 5" x 7". The Blau's come with a plastic
tray that the speaker sits in, this surface mounts to the deck and a metal
grill covers the inner part of the tray. I like the look and the speakers
are great. Blaupunkt claims to have a superior sunlight resistant cone
material--a major factor on the Targa rear deck here in Florida.
