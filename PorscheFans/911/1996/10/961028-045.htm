   Scott wants more comments on running at Portland International Raceway. O.K.,
if it is raining or even damp, stay the H**l away from the inside line leading
onto the back straightaway. There is a transition in the pavement there, and if
you are REALLY getting it on, it will cause your inside rear wheel to lose
traction. The car may move QUICKLY to the left.
   The result may be a car pointed into the retaining wall at speed, or a bad
spin. Either way, this aint the place to do it.
   Yes, I did, and don't ask.
