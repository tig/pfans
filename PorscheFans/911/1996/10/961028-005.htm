Hi Jay:
Sorry to say but it doesn't sound good.  With both gauge dropping to 0   
and the light coming on I'd think you don't have oil pressure not a bad   
connection.  Were it to be me I'd pull and ground the coil wire and use   
just the starter to check for pressure on the scavenger side of the oil   
system at the tank return first.  From there I check the pressure side at   
the sensor or crack one of the cam tower lines.  You should be able to   
get the light out using just the starter of the pressure is OK.
