        Phil:

        If the other owner only received one plate from DMV, and swears to it, 
then you should be within the law.  If he got two but just gave you one, that's 
a different story, and not within CVC section 5200.  As for DMV providing for a 
replacement, if they only sent you one, it seems to me that you're under no 
obligation to go get another one, since CVC section 5200 specifically provides 
for the situation where you are only sent one - you put it on the rear of the 
vehicle.  This wouldn't be ignorance of the law - it maybe would be a little too
much knowledge of the law! :-)  Of course, a judge may think you're clever and 
find you not guilty, but he may also order you to contact DMV and get another 
one, in which case you would then be obligated to do so.
