Dennis,

The ducktail was Porsche's first attempt at improving the aerodynamics of
the 911. It was certainly effective and not unattractive. In fact, from an
asthetic standpoint, I like them the best on the pre-'74 narrow body cars.
However, the '75 Carrera whaletail is just plain beautiful on the
Carrera and SC bodies. I will take exception to your suggestion that the
ducktail is the only "real" Porsche spoiler. It was the first but not the
best, either functionally or asthetically. And, the whaletail is as
synonymous with Porsche as the ducktail and possibly more so.
