Steve Jagernauth wrote:

>Seeking feedback on Momo and Dino steering wheels - are they typically 
>smaller than the stock 3-spoke leather Porsche wheel in my car?  Do they 
>block viewing the gauges?   Any advantages other than looking cool?

Can't say for certain about the Momo or Dino, but I've got a Fittipaldi
"Personal" wheel on my SC and love it!  It's definitely smaller than the
stock 3-spokers, and has a significantly thicker rim.  Unfortunately, it
does block the gauges a little.  I can't see the speedo from 40-70mph -
who cares, that's too slow anyways - and checking the engine temp and 
pressure is a pain in the neck, literally.  It's worth it though, 'cause
it's sooooo smooooth!  You can also fix the gauge problem by turning
the gauges at different angles.   Just have to live with some funky
looks from your friends.

Incidentally, I always flash, wave and generally look like a
grinning ninny every time I drive the car!
