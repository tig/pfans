I'm getting ready to tackle the exact same project.  Bruce Anderson, in his
911 Performance Handbook, recommends delrin or poly-graphite for off road
(track) use only.  He gives the impression that anything harder than the
factory rubber will cost you your fillings!

The good news is that factory bushings for front sway bars are cheap-I paid
$8.41 each (x4).  The bad news is that Porsche doesn't make (offer)
replacement bushings for the A-arms.  You have to buy the whole unit at
around $280.00!

If anyone else has opinions, or better yet, experience in this area, I'd be
interested in your comments.
