You've come to the right place. I am in the same position as you except that
I am limiting my search to 911's and you couldn't find a more friendly bunch.
 First, go get the 911 Faq at
http://users.deltanet.com/users/guthrie/911.htm. This is a jumpstart which
includes a checklist when reviewing a potential car for purchase that is
invaluable. Second, get Bruce Anderson's book Porsche 911 Performance
Handbook, revision 1 or 2. The first version has a great section on buying a
used 911.  Contact your local Porsche Club of America organization, they can
be very helpful with your purchase.

Good Luck,
