Nothing!

I have another twist on the how do do pesky 911 valves.  Purists 
will have palpitations, but I simply adjust one valve with my 
trusty 0.004" feeler, the one that's easiest to get to, like #1 exhaust 
for example, and then grab the rocker and feel how it moves up 
and down.  I then simply feel all the rest.  If one doesn't feel right, 
I adjust it, but it's pretty rare for that to happen.  And you'd be 
amazed at how easy it is to feel the difference bewteen an adjusted 
and non-adjusted valve.
