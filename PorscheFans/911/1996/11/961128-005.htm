Tom, Ted & all,

Professionally Engineered Products (800/593-8787) sells back seat
2 & 3 point belt kits.  The 3pt is available with or without a
retractor (inertia-reel).  I've got a single non-retractable in
my 911 that works well for my 4 year old.

Installation is fairly simple except for drilling the hole in the
package shelf.  A right angle drill really helps with this job.
