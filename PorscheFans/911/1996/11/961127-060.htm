Hi Vince,

Try Al at Parts Heaven, he had one about a year ago, may still have it. The
Saratoga top is definitely aftermarket and looks it. The factory top is
factory and looks it. 

 The Targa had a one piece fiberglass hard top which is stored  in the trunk
and the folding roof was made available as an  option. The non folding roof
was only offered in 1974 and 1975.  The folding type top had the code M 436
and became standard  again in 1976.
