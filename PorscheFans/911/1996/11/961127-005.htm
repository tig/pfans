Have a funny speed freak story.

The other day, I was "testing" my 275hp 77 911s 3.6 on a deserted freeway
connector, when I decided to punch it.

I hadn't really tried "tests" in anything other than acceleration (0-60,
0-100, etc.) before, and wanted to see what would happen if I held the gas
down.

Well, 5500 rpm showed 145mph.... I looked up and saw a programmable
billboard.  

It said "You are going 145 miles per hour... SLOW DOWN!"

I nearly passed out from laughter and then relief that this radar gun
wasn't connected to a state cop.
