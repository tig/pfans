Mark,

If you needed two hands to move the washer after adjusting the bearings you 
are WAY to tight.  If at this tightness you still have play it doesn't 
sound like bearing adjustment is going to solve your problem.

I have to mount the wheel to feel any rocking movement in the bearings.  
Pulling and pushing on just the rotor only shows extremely lose bearings.  
I use the extra leverage from the tire to  fine tune the bearing 
adjustment.

I do not know what your problem is but I would recommend taking everything 
apart, cleaning and looking at the condition of the bearings, hub and the 
spindle.

Good luck,

Scott
