If you can't drill the little suckers out, I went another route -- although
not as professional as extraction, it definitely worked.

You'll find that the main reason these studs won't extract is fusion.
They've actually hardened and 'are one with' the threads. I had that one
mother that just wouldn't budge so I tapped the beast and put a beautiful
stainless bolt in the new hole. Of course, this bolt was a tad smaller, but
it worked beautifully and no helicoil or machine shop time. It's kind of
make-shift, but it got me up and on the road in minutes instead of days.
