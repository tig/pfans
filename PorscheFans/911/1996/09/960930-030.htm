Chris:
You need a new door stop.  They're easy to replace.  When I repaced mine a
few months ago, I also installed the reinforcement plates that Automotion
sells, since the metal area around the door stop appears to be atypically
under-engineered, for a Porsche, that is!  If you have an older car, you
can't use the hex head screws that come with the new door stop - you have
to save your allen head screws from the old door stop.
Good luck!
