A few years ago, I was lucky enough to visit the 'Big Dog Garage' as Leno's
facility is called. It is a plain concrete-walled industrial building
adjacent to the runways at the West end of Burbank-Glendale-Pasadena
Airport.

He has a truly odd collection of cars from a '56 Buick convertible to a
dual-cowl Duesenberg. A black Viper, replica 427 Cobra and lots of
Lamborghinis. One of the most interesting cars is a Hispano Suissa with an
18+ liter (!) aircraft engine. The whole car appears to be made from beaten
heavy-gauge aluminum. The motorcycle collection is even more impressive,
with Vincents and other classics. At the time, he and I both had a Norton
Commando John Player Special--a bike that was completely stock
performance-wise, but with a racing cowl, rear sets, etc. His comment on
the bike was hilarious. He said it was kinda like a transvestie: can look
great from a distance, but when you climb on it, something seems not quite
right...

Funny guy, and nice as can be.
