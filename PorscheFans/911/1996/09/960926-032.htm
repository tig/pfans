The 993 Carrera was introduced in 1/94 and sold in the US as a 1995 model.  
I believe it was called a 1994 model in some parts of the world.

The 993 Carrera 4 was introduced in the spring of '94.  The twin turbo was 
rolled out in early 95.  In the fall of 95 we saw the 4S and the new Targa.
