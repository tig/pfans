Hi Todd:
Don't know without looking it up about your springs but yes to oil (I use   
oil and assembly lube) on parts with metal to metal contact with one   
reservation.  I wouldn't lubricate the cylinder bores with engine oil.  A   
little oil on the rings and piston skirts to help them out of your ring   
compressor and into the bores but thats about it.  You want the rings to   
seat and not glaze the cylinder walls on start up.  Seating may not be as   
much of an issue with your engine but on my 2.7 I have Nicasil (sorry   
about the spelling)  which is pretty hard.  By doing the above mine   
seated fine.
