Hi Kevin, 

Helicoils don't work worth diddly, if he is forced to use an insert he should
use one of the one piece inserts such as Time Certs. 

The head studs are stretch bolts or studs and that is why the are narrower in
the middle. Better to break there than in the thread area. Usually the break
with enough stick out so that you can weld a nut on them to get them out. The
real problem lies in the fact that the Dilavar studs are brittle and love to
break. Any moisture will cause corrosion and exacerbate this breaking
problem.
