The subject line says it all.  After rebuilding the master cylinder,   
purging and refilling the brake system, cleaning and packing the wheel   
bearings ect, ect ect, I finally got my 75 project out and on the road.   
 So far so good and I have to admit I'm pretty pleased with the results.   
 Smooth and sounds great with plenty of low end after the overhaul and   
addition of the SSI equal length exhaust.   Nice boost when the cams come   
on at 4K.  Now I just have to keep my foot out of it until it brakes in.   
 Last thing needed is to get the CIS straightened out (surges and   
randomly doesn't idle down when warm) so I'm going to have a local shop   
pressure check it and set the CO to start.  At any rate its fun to drive   
and thats what its all about.
