This is known as a rising rate fuel regulator and was discussed
extensively on the Porschephiles list. The consensus was that on a stock
motor the thing is of little benefit and is definitely not worth the
money. The phrase "cash flow device" was used repeatedly. Now, if you
are building a race motor, that is a different story.
