HI Mike,

Your flywheel is an early 911S flywheel. They had both a copper surface on
the flywheel and pressure plate and a special clutch disc. They used that
style only in 1967 and part of 1968. In 1968-69 they switched to another
special pressure plate that was aluminum with a hard face that used a stock
flywheel, but also required a special disc.
