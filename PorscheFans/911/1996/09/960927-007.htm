I powder coated two fans (for my race cars) and had good experiences in both
cases, the powder coating lasts a WHOLE lot longer than paint or polish; the
leading edges of the fans get beat up pretty good (i.e. paint wears off
quickly due to abrasive effects of dirt/dust and water), but seven years on
my fan still looks good (matte black).
