Hi Terry:

I don't have a list of options but I owned one of these 1975 " Silver 
Anniversary" models in 1987.     This was a Euro 911S Targa.   It had Fuchs, 
dual flag-style electric mirrors,  electric windows and the funky 
hounds-tooth interior.    The front seats had been replaced with nice deep 
Recaros and it had an aftermarket Nardi steering wheel which I wish I'd 
kept.  I forgot what number it was but don't think there was anything too 
special about it though it was my first Porsche and got me hooked.    It 
only took finishing college, getting married, having 2 kids and another 5 
years before I was able to get another one.
