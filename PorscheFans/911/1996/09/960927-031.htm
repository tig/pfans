Chuck, 

993 is the "project", or "type" name for the 1995, 1996, and 1997 Porsche
911 Carrera.  Just as the 1990-1994 Carrera 2/4 is internally known as "type
964."  Of course, we speak of any of these cars as simply 911's.  So, when
you hear 993 Carrera, or 993 Turbo, or anything else "993", then what is
being referred to is a 1995-present 911.  Many people use these "type"
designations instead of the actual model name.  For instance, someone who
owns a 944 Turbo might call his car a "951", because that is the "type" name.
