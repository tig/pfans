Hi Ralf,

The studs are held in with Loctite. You have to heat the crankcase with a
torch to get the Loctite to release. When you heat the case you will feel the
Loctite let go. You just haven't heated them enough yet. When done correctly
this job is just a pain and not a total frustration. 

I wouldn't mess with your extractor on the broken stud, you will end up
breaking it off and could ruin your case. If you can't get these studs out
under optimum conditions what makes you think you are going to get one out
with a cobalt drill and an extractor set. You need professional help. Get
your case to a machinist who does this all of the time and get his opinion.
You may have to have the broken stud removed with an EDM (Electron Discharge
Machine) machine.
