Hi,

Radar detectors are illegal in most parts of Europe. I brought mine to
Germany anyway and it never went off. I even drove through photo radar
areas and it did't go off. I don't know if it was luck or the boxes were
empty at the time.

FWIW, a friend of mine commuted weekly from Berlin to the Czech
Republic, where it is also illegal, and he said that Radar detectors
worked just great. He would cruise at around 100 mph until his detector
went off and then slow down, see the radar trap, and the reaccelerate
until the next trap. He never got a ticket in the 2 years that he worked
there.
