Hi Bob,

I don't have a cross-reference or any way of comparing different manu-
acturers' heat designations.  But the best source of info re: spark plugs
is Bosch's Automotive Electronics book, the burgandy one you can buy from
SAE.

The tip needs to be in a particular range of operating temperatures.  You
pick the plug that gets you there.  Cylinders 2 and 5 are going to be
the hotest ones, but not by so much that, with the right plug, you'll
normally see a visual difference in coloration.  On the 72T, if you haven't
installed the late-style cooling baffles, maybe now is the time.
