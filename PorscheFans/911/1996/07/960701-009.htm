hi Steve,

I have an ugly solution for your transmission oil fill up. I had 
drained my transmission oil when I realized that my 1 liter bottles of 
Mobil 1 transmission fluid couldn't reach the filler. So I looked 
around my garage and found a 5 ft length of 3/4-1 inch sprinkler PVC 
piping and filed one end at an angle so that it would fit flush inside 
filler plughole. I then placed the spout of the bottle in the tube and 
started squeezing the bottle and the oil flowed through without making 
much of a mess. I probably spilled a few ounces, but it was pretty 
easy to do and definitely cheap.

If my explanation was unclear please e-mail me for a more detailed 
description,

Neil Kumpers
'73 911S targa

P.S. I forgot if you have to take off the left rear wheel, but you 
definitely need to jack the left side up in order to get a good angle 
and fit between the transmission and the filler pipe.
