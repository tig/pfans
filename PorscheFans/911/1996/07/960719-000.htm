	There are 3 books I can recommend. Bruce Anderson's tome is the bible if
you want to get into the real technical nitty gritty or do performance
enhancements, though as Bruce mentioned it may be difficult to find until
the next version hits the streets.

	Paul Frere and Chris Harvey have both written books about the 911 which 
trace the history and changes from year to year in detail, along with 
excerpts from contemporary test reports, etc.

Anderson, Bruce
Porsche 911 Performance Handbook
Motorbooks International
0-87938-269-4

Harvey, Chris
Porsche 911 In All Its Forms
Oxford Illustrated Press
0-946609-69-1

Frere, Paul
The Porsche 911 Story
Patrick Stephens Ltd
1-85260-128-0 (I *think* this is the latest edition)

	BTW, I have a (partial) bibliography of about 280 Porsche books which I'm
happy to send to anyone who'd like it (it's an Excel spreadsheet, so let
me know if you can handle it uuencoded, or if you want a .CSV format or
whatever).
