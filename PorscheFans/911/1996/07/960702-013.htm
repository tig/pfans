 I need to know if there are other ways to cool the heads
> down.  Please keep in mind that my income is quite limited.  How
> effective would things like taping the edges of the engine tin and
> plugging the holes to the spark plugs be?

One thing I'm trying is to block off the air flow from the main 
cooling fan to the heat exchnagers. There are 2 rectangular openings at 
the bottom fo the fan housing that connect to and intermediate merge pipe 
before entering the heat exchanger. Remove that part, block off the fan 
outlet with tape or foil, then put everything back. You'll still have 
heat because of the heater blower motor. Blocking this outlet shoudl 
increase the pressure in the rest of the cooling system, thus giving you 
more air flow over the heads and cylinders. Of course, your tin has to be 
in good, hole-free shape also. The plug holes should be blocked well.
