   After several years of getting hit in the head by sagging deck lids, I
converted to dual struts. There was a kit advertised in Pano to do the
conversion. I tried using three struts on the front, but eventually went to
four. Use your old ones together with one or two new struts.
   The kit consists mainly of longer pins and keepers with some washers thrown
in. Looks like if you can get to some clevis pins the proper length, you could
do it yourself.
