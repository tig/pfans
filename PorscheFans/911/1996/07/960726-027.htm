No, the whole engine shroud does not need to be changed to change the fan for
the 1978-79 SC's. The 1980 SC also used the larger 245 mm fan as well, it was
only 1978-79 that used the 226 mm fans. You will need the larger fan and the
cast shroud as well, but everything else remains the same.
