Taking up 2 spots only tends to draw attention to a vehicle
regardless of the marque.  You become an instant target because
everyone will think you are better than everyone else and your car
deserves better.  Not a good solution.

The best way to park is to find a spot with only one exposed side 
and park in as close in as possible.  That's how I park at work when I
drive
my 911.  Otherwise you are at the will of all the inconsiderate
individuals
out there.
