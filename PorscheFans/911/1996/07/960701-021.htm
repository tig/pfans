Motorcycles have tiny electric fans to go with their tiny radiators; I'd guess
around 5"-6" on sportbikes.  You can get them cheap from bike salvage yards; I
paid $5 for one not that long ago.  Also, I remove them for racing and put 'em
back on for street riding.  Go to the track during a m/c race, and you'll
probably find people with dedicated production race bikes willing to give them
away.
