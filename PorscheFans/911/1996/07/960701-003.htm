I concur with everything Andy said about shifting a 901 transmission.  I
don't know about for street use, but we developed a shifter with a
lock-out plate for our 914-6 race car.  This plate prevents the
transmission from "accidently" going into 1st or reverse.  My years of
crew service on this race car has allowed me the opportunity to drive this
beast at drivers eds.  I can attest personnally that the lock-out plate
works.

If the car will be a used in both arenas, you can choose not to
engage the lock-out while driving on the street.  If you plan to use this
car on the track, and would be interested in getting one of our units,
please send me mail personally.  Anyone else interested in this modified
shifter can contact me as well.  I will pass the information along to the 
designer, and have him contact you.
