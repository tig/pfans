this is done to provide the higher fuel delivery needed to feed (these)
turbo motors when desired output exceeds 350 to 370 hp.

the euro package has greater flow capacity.  this is quite desirable.

the differences in parts revolve around different:

airflow,
fuel regulator (?),
injector lines,
injectors,

w/o other mods one should see no real gain on a post 86 US 930 by just
bolting on a euro setup since stock hp output is well within the US CIS
delivery capabilities...

if you want more power from your 930 and don't want to pony up the big
$$$ for a more sophisticated fuel management system the euro cis is the
way to go...
