> They are 205/60 on
> 7" rims in the front and 225/50 mounted on 8" rims in the back.

Could have been better with 225/50/15 all around. Maybe try
it next time (or 205/50/15 in front if the 225's rub and you don't
want to roll your fender lips). 245/50/15 will fit on the rear
but may not be necessary on a 2350 pound car with under 200 HP.

>  -What are some good starting pressures for autocrossing?

Drew Spencer replied :

> Having driven both earlier ('82 SC) and later ('93 RS America) I'd start
out
> with 30 Front 34 Rear and adjust to your preference from there.   

IMHO these are about perfect for an SC, but you can run a few
pounds lower on an earlier car. My 2330 pound S is best with
28 ft 30 rear (my guage reads a bit high though, maybe try 
even a few PSI lower).
