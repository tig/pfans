My stock 1991 C4 is going under the wrench for a personality upgrade.  The

I'm grateful for past advice on this topic, and plan to have the car

Porsche's Euro RS or Cup cars won't work since my C4 will never slim down
to RS weight (i.e. still weigh in around 3200 pounds). I'm confused. What
sizes work best?

3) Wheels/Tires: Can someone with experience comment on using 8x17 front
