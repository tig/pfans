You can get a drill-powered rotary pump for about $30 (I got mine at a local
sports/auto store; GI Joe's) which works like a charm.  Most auto parts places
also see a hand pump; basically a larger version of the type used for liquid
hand soap.  Actually, looks about the same as the one on the "Fast Orange"
type hand cleaner.  If you had an old one that you *thoroughly* cleaned out,
it'd probably work.  But those things are a PITA, which is why I got the
rotary one.....
