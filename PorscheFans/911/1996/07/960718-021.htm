On August 24-25, Allegheny Region of the Porsche Club of America will be 
putting on a driver's education event at Nelson's Ledges race track outside
of Warren, Ohio. This event is open to all car types and drivers who meet
the following requirements:

     Safe car with equal seating and restraints for driver & passenger
     Minimum of 3-point harnesses for driver & passenger; equal restraints 
            for both
     Open cars require roll bars taller than driver & passenger
     Minimum of 3/32" tread depth
     Driver requires helmet with 1985 or newer SNELL rated helmet, long
sleeve
        shirt, long pants
     No open exhaust
     No more than 1 novice (i.e., no track experience) driver per car

Cost is $136 per driver.  Each driver will be teamed with a knowledgeable
instructor
for the duration of the event, and will have approx. 2 hrs. of track time per
day.
Also, classrooom instruction is part of the education.

If interested or want more information, e-mail me at porsray@aol.com with
your
name and address and I'll mail you an application.  Telephone no. is
412/824-5264
if you wish to discuss this.  Thanks.
