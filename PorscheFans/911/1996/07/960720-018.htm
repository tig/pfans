<< 
 I've noticed you have some experience in the subject of wheel sizes for
 911's. I posted this question to P-fans but got no response and thought you
 may know.
 Question is: I have a 83 911SC, stock suspension, I have 7" rims for the
 front with stock 125mm backspacing. Can I put 225-50-16 tires on this rim
 without hitting my fenders on every turn? >>

Probably not. They should rub the fender lip. If you roll the lips
and have lots of negative camber they MAY fit.

<< Also what's a good alignment
 setting for dual autocross/street use that will be competitive without
 making the car too darty on the highway?
  >>

About 1.5 negative camber ft, 2 rear. 0 toe front and a bit rear,
1/16" or so. Should be a good compromise, maybe a bit
towards autocross. Also car should be lowered and
corner balanced, front lower by 1 degree of rake.
