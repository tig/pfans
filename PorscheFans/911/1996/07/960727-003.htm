>Is there a simple way to stop and start mail while on vacation, etc. other

Unfortunately, no. If you suspect your mailbox is going to overflow, it's
best to unsubscribe yourself, and subscribe once you get back. It's simple
enough to get on and off this list. As for the missing mail, you can then go

get those after you come back.

To get the subscribe/unsubscribe instructions, again send an e-mail, any
subject, any body, to porschefans@ioio.com and you'll get the automatic
reply.
