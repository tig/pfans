The Phund was established to offer some relief for the accumulated
burden of running Porschephiles.  There was never a statement of
purpose for the Phund.  In fact, it was boldly stated that Stan
could/should do whatever he wanted with the funds.  The Phund was
basically a gesture of Thanks from the Philes participants to Stan.

However, there was an "implied" sense that the Phund would be used to
ensure the longevity of Phorschephiles.  Since Porschephiles has since
died, I can understand someone wondering about it.  But technically,
we have no right to question it.  The contributions were Donations
with NO terms or conditions.

------------------------------------------------------------------

I realize that he did make quite a sacrifice for a number of years 
keeping pfiles going.  The thing is, *no one* made him do it.  His 
actions for the past year or so (since it became a nightmare getting 
any administrative changes made on the list) were unacceptable.  Like 
Blaine said - at least he could shoot us a message and tell us about 
the new engine/paint job/gambling trip the $10k got him.
