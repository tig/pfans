While I agree with all the above, the fact is that there _are_ some
topics (discussions of factory tours, Daytona, LeMans, local dealers or
mechanics, industry trends, you name it) that span any individual
list. As a subscriber to all 5 lists (I'm a Porsche Fan, not just a 944
or 914 Fan), I _hate_ seeing these sorts of messages 5 times.  As good
as procmail is (the mail filtering agent I use to sort my mail), there's
no way for me, at the recieving end, to filter out duplicate messages.

If it's not possible for the Listmeister to filter these posts to
multiple lists so that those of us subscribed to more than one see them
only once, then _please_ add a new list for non-model-specific posts (a
previous poster suggested "PorscheFans-All" -- that sounds good to me).

BTW: where are we supposed to discuss the Boxter? In the 911 list (since
it will presumably share many parts with the 911), the 914 list (since
it's mid-engined), or perhaps the 928 or 944 lists (since it will be
water cooled)?
