Hi Steve,

Before you even think about drilling holes in your car, try putting one front tire on a curb or 
steep driveway in order to get some torsional flex (try the other side if it doesn't work). My 
trunk used to pop open at times after a little kerb surfing ;>, no seriously, sometimes after 
leaving a steep driveway my trunk would open. I had it adjusted and now it doesn't 
unintentionally open anymore, however, if you try to pull the lever while on a curb it might just 
do the trick.
