Swapping the boxes, and then having the original work correctly sounds
suspiciously like dirty connectors.  I'd spray out the connectors with
contact cleaner, check the wiring for breaks/bad insulation, and
then re-connect with some electrical grease.  You may also want to open
up the box, and if it isn't filled with that epoxy cr*p, check that
none of the wiring/components inside look dirty/corroded.
