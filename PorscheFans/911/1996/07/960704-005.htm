About engine cooling:
Be sure to remove anything sucked inside your cooling fan.  I was bit
by having 2 paper towels in there.  It would run for about 20 minutes then
die.  I didn't have an oil temp gauge then I do now.  

How hot is too hot.  This measures sump temperature andI assume the oil
fed to the bearing circuit is cooler.  Anyone ever measure this?
Sump temperature usually maxes out in the 220's and has peaked at 235 on
long uphill runs.  This sender/gauge setup is digital and read a perfect
212 in a boiling water bath and an almost perfect 33 in an ice water bath
before I installed it.
