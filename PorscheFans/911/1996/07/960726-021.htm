Greg Brown wrote:

>I hate to go against the flow, but I've never been able to tell, on the
>track, if a 911 has a Quaife in it or a open differential, so I don't have
>much use for them in high horsepower applications.  Anything with high
>horsepower will light off a Quaife faster than a M-80 with a 1/4" fuse.  I

Greg,

One of nice things about the Quaife is that you do not notice it when
you are driving. Unlike the factory LSD which is constantly reminding
you that it is there. You don't notice the Quaife until you see the lap
times that is. Clearly there is a difference between an open diff and a
Quaife.

Also, Martin Snow uses Quaifes in all of his cars (Melanie's too). These
cars aren't exactly down on horsepower. He does not 'light off a Quaife
faster than a M-80 with a 1/4" fuse'.
