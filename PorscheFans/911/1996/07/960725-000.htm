Hi, wondering if anyone else saw the episode of the Automobiles show on
the History Channel featuring the Porsche 911??  I was surprised not to
have read about it on the list already....our friend Bruce Anderson is now a
famous TV personality.

Anyway, it was a great show & nicely put together.  Although there were some
inaccuracies in the exact models described/shown in the footage - it was just
nice to see some of the early cars in use, and some of the factory test cars 
etc. Just keep in mind that the show is aimed at the "layperson" and trys to
fit a lot of info into a one hour show.  

Bruce did a great job exlpaining things and giving the historical
perspective.

The History Channel has a web site so you may want to check it out for future

air dates.  They seem to repeat the shows from time to time.
