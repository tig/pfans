The way the equal restraints requirement whether written, unwritten, or
required within the supplemental regulations for PCA drivers ed was
explained to me was:
 
There was an incident at a track (Potomac Region Summit Point's Drivers
Ed?), where a Green or Blue (Novice/Beginner) driver went off course and the
instructor received substantial injures, since he only had a 3-point seat
belt, where the driver walked away, since he was wearing a 5-point harness.
The reasoning for requiring at least equal restraints is that the driver
will not drive beyond the capabilities and feeling of comfort/confidence of
the restraint that he is using.
 
This was told to me second or third hand, so the facts may not be totally
correct.
