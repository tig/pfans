Jay,

The radiator fan switch will have no effect on your temps when the car is
at highway speed.  At speed, the fans only get in the way of cooling.  I
don't know about updates to the thermostat, but the fact that you installed
a new one is good.  I assume that the radiator has been bled, then rebled
properly.

I would check engine basic mix and make sure you're not running very lean,
which would heat things up, esp at speed.  Check your plugs, check the
injector flow patterns.  Scan fault codes from the DME as well, see whether
there is a fuel pressure problem.

Hope this helps.
