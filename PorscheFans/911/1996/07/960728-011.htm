Philip- I recently bought a Momo Montecarlo from German Parts and 
Restoration. With hub, their price was $164. I'm very happy with the 
feel of the wheel. The only drawback is that, due to its smaller size 
(350mm) and thicker padding, it partially obscures the temperature 
gauge and speedometer at 70+mph. I've learning to use the tach to set 
my speed (in 5th gear), and to periodically peer around to check the 
temperature gauge.
