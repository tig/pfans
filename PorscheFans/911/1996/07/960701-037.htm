Why not get clever....how about getting a container, possibly one gallon 
plastic jug of some sort, drill 2 hole in the cap, stick a couple of small 
tubes throug the cap (I'd use some scrap fittings around, or even drill 
throught the centre of a smallish bolt..head would seal inside, use the 
threads to seal the other.),  fit your air compressor to one tube along 
with a valve of some sort, set it at low pressure, fit a small hose to the 
other and stick it in the transmission. Note that the tube which supplies 
should go to the bottom or you need to turn the whole affair upside down. 
Feed a little air and voila....should move your oil!!
