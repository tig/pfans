>+-------
>Quick question ... how does one spot a Carrera tensioner ?
>Where should I look, and what should I look for ?
>+-------

Look for the oil lines feeding it.  There are lines on both sides of the
engine, at the back and visible to the left and right of the fan shroud.
Each line had a fitting into the case, next to the shroud, leading to a
rigid line with a flexible rubber hose centre section, leading to another
rigid line to the cam tower.  Before it reaches the cam tower, there is a
T with another rigid line which snakes down, through the skirt, and to
the tensioner via a banjo fitting in the middle of the chain cover.
