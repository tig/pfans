I saw a repost of a question from JJ Slowik asking about flames out his   
exhaust.  It has been my experience that this is from an over rich   
mixture being dumped into the exhaust on rapid throttle back.  I didn't   
see the original so didn't catch what the induction system was.  This is   
a pretty common occurrence on carburated motorcycles where the low end   
mixture has been enriched for better low end performance beyond factory   
EPA mandated settings.
