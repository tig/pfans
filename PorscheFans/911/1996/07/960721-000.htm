Hello all.  Make sure when replacing the sway bar mounts on the 
911 that you get the most up to date sway bar mounts (with the little H 
gusset welded in).  I was disappointed to see that Stoddard sent me one
with the H, and one without.  Some places may try to sell the older mounts
 - they are dimensionally different than the stock.
