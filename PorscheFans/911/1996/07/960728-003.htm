JHelgesen@aol.com writes:

>Can someone recomend rear alignment specs for a 76 911s.  I looked in my
>Haynes manual and it specs for 76 and up, 10 degrees of toe in per wheel.
> From 75 and back it says 0 degrees.  Anyone out there have suggestions.  I'm
>trying to cram a set of 7 x 15 alloys in the back, so I don't think the 10
>degrees would help at all.

John,

I am running zero toe in the rear on my '76 911.  BTW, I think you meant to say
10 *minutes* of toe in, not 10 degrees.  I think the zero toe is a good setting,
but if you must, run a little bit of toe in and avoid toe out like the plague.

I also am using 7x15s in the rear - 205/60s on the steet and 225/50s on the
track.  In addition to the zero toe, I am running about 1.3 degrees of negative
camber.  Seems to work nicely.

YMMV and all that,
