difference!)

I made my own short shifter here's how:

1.  Remove shifter.  Cut ball off end.  Weld in 1/2" extension.  Grind   
smooth.
   goes from this O-<= to O--<=
2.  Buy 1/2" plexiglass and cut out to space housing up.
3.  Buy 1/2" longer bolts to hold housing and shift rod guide.
4.  Reassemble using new bushings just to get rid of that slop.

Mine works great!  Shift is quicker, WAY cleaner, and not noticably   
harder.
You also don't need to take off the Nob!

When I first autocrossed with it I was miss-shifting but that is just   
because I was used to the old travel.

I have made this mod on both the old style shifter against a 901 and the   
new style shifter against a 915.  Both work great.
