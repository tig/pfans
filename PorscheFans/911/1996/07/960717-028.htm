Which plugs are you running? I had the best results with Bosch
silver plugs in the 6 range for the street, (WR6DS) they are hotter than
stock so they don't foul. Now I'm running the "real" Bosch
platinums W3DP0 plugs and they are not as good on the street,
but should be better on the track. Forget the K-Mart Bosch
platinums (WR5DP) that cost about $3, they won't work well.
