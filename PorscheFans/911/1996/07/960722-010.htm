Darrin:

It's much more likely that you simply had a corroded connection in 
the DME wiring-harness plug.  Unplugging and re-plugging the computer 
wiped the contacts clean and fixed the problem.

Bad solder joints generally show up as intermittent faults that
appear when the temperature changes or under vibration.  If, as you
say, your old computer works fine now, you've ALREADY fixed the
problem and don't have to worry about it any more.
