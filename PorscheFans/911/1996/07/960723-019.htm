There used to be a corrigated metal replacement for the plastic bushing
available from your local Porsche dealership. Porsche discontinued this piece
a couple of years ago and now there are some aftermarket solutions for this
bushing. We discussed this in Excellence magazine earlier this year and the
following is the most recent word on this subject:

In the April 1996 issue of Excellence we discussed installing the steering
column repair bushings. Well, I heard back from one of our readers, Steven
Grosekemper, at Dieter's Porsche Service in San Diego on this one. Steven,
says that even though the old wavey insert is no longer available for fixing
the 911 steering column bushing that you can use the 928 column spacer number
928 347 739 02 and it works like a dream. He says that the 928 column spacer
sells for just a few dollars and only takes a few minutes to install.
