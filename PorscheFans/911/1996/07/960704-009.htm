I have an 83 SC with the stock oil cooler. It used to run 250 or more in SF bay
area, where it is not that hot. Two years ago I finally had it looked at. My
friendly Porsche Mechanic did the following:
	Replace the Thermostat
	Replace warm up regualtor
	adjust fuel pressures
	replace oil switch
	remove dents from oil lines
	installed a newer model sump plate
	Consulted with Bruce A. and other Porsche Gurus

A $1000 later it now runs 220 to 250. I think some SC's are just hot blooded.
