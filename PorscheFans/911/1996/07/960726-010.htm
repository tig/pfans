With all the descriptions in recent postings, I began to wonder whether
everyone is clear about the proper positioning of the pop-off valve?  A
picture would be much easier, but let me give it a try.  It's simple:

You take off the rubber straps, adn lift off the air filter/snorkel housing.
Remove the air filter element.  In the box you have now uncovered, you will
see the valve in the far left corner... its little toilet seat cover will
"open" upward.  It should be oriented so that when it opens, it will not
foul the filter element.

The thing *has* to be easy to install... getting it on the wrong side of the
plenum would be *exceedingly* difficult!

Hope this helps.
