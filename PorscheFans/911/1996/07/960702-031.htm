The 911 comes with headroom which is acceptable for guys up to about 
six feet tall. For taller drivers the dealer can lower the seat. This 
adds about an inch. Usually they move the seat tracks rearward at the 
same time, this adds another inch. I have a number of 6'3 and 6'4 
inch friends driving 911's comfortably.  If you are visiting dealers, 
ask them they'll show you the details.
