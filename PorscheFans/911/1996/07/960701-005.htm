I have also inquired about the color of the engine lid letters, grille, and 
targa scripts on '72 - '73 911s.  What Ive learned is that ALL came with the 
targa scripts and METAL engine grilles anodized black.  They were also supposed 
to all have anodized black P O R S C H E and 911S/E/T letters.  But, my current 
'73 911S came with chrome P O R S C H E letters.  The 911S badge is faded but 
was anodized black.
