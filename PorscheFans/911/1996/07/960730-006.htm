My SC guage says I'm running hot, too. It's time for me to find out
if the guage is reading correctly. Someone posted that they checked
theirs at boiling temp. What is the recommended method?

My guess:
Assume the sender unit is a simple resistive device.
Put it in boiling water for a while.
Carefully remove from water and measure resistance between center
connector and sender body.
Build resistive network equal to measured amount.
Attach sender wire (in engine compartment) to resistive network.
Ground resistive network.
Switch on ignition and check reading (should be ~212 F).
If not accurate, pull needle off guage and reposition to 212.

Is there an easier method?
