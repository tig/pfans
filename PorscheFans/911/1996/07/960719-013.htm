The unanimous judgement (thnx Kyle, Andrew, David, and Barry) is that those
little metal valve stem supports are a cheap defense against possible
disaster at speeds even as low as 90 mph.  No one spoke against them.  

The force of a few gram valve cap and valve may exert as much as 5 lb force
on the valve stem at speed (100+).  Obviously the faster you go the more the
force.  

I'm going to carry some extras in the glove box and track bag.
