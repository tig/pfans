>Do I use Locktite on the bolts?

Rocker arm pinch bolts?  No.

>Will 15 ft/lbs of torque really hold the rockers in place?

I've never measured, but this figure sounds low.  I've been taught to make
them very tight.  I use a baby 1/4" breaker bar with a 5 mm stub allen on
one side, a cut-off 8 mm allen key on the other.  The breaker bar plus
stubby gives better leverage and really eliminates slipping.  You can put
some decent torque into it that way.

>Are these expansion bolts that can only be used once and have to be replaced?

No, they can be re-used indefinitely.
