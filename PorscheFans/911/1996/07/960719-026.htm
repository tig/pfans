Questions from a new 911 owner (worked my way up through 914s and 944T)

My new bl/bl '87 Targa, which was an executive's "weekend" car, has 31K miles
on it, sport suspension, sport seats. Ltd slip, etc...
1) Could it be possible that I have original battery?  It looks original (or
at least factory) and is huge and turns car over well even after a week...

2) I believe my car has the dreaded  needle bearing/rubber clutch set up.
 But, clutch feels pretty silky smooth still, makes a little of typical G50
gear noise coming off idle - should I expect catastrophic type failure some
day or will I know when needle bearing  starts to go?  (I expect the latter)
 Will rubber pieces jam the clutch like with original SCs?

3) The car needs new stereo/speakers.  I want to retain STOCK

those new under-the-seat type bass augmenters?  

4) A.C. blows air that is only marginally colder than outside air.  I fear

used A.C", although he SHOULD have used it one a month....   Question:  Even
with a good A.C system, will the 911 (w/ later vents) actually stay cool?

7) Can I add central locking?  I get conflicting advice on aftermarket

will see if I can get keys made.
