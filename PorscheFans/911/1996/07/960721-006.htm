My 90 C2 daily driver has been really hard to start when it (the car) is
cold. It cranks fine, but it takes 3 or 4 times before the engine catches
-- as if there isn't any fuel, it will fire then die immediately.

temp. If I let it sit for >8 hours in the garage, it gets hard start
disease again.

I had a local shop look at it as part of a tech inspection, and he adjusted
the mixture (which was lean), but that had no effect on the cold start. No
other problems came up at the tech.

Should I haul it into the dealer, or is there something I can gently
suggest to the local shop?
