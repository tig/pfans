Wayne,

Advice:
Just make sure you use the same pads all the way around.
When I bought my car it had a different kind front/back.
The front pads apparently had a higher coefficient of friction.
Anyway the front always locked up quickly. This was very dangerous 
on wet roads. Now I have the same brand all around and it brakes much
more evenly and w/o the quick lockup.
