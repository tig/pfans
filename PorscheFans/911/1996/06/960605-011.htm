Here's the method that works on the Acura NSX... I have no idea 
whether it'll turn off Porsche ABS:

    Car stopped, engine running.  Pull the hand brake up a click or
    two and drive forward about twenty feet or so.  The ABS light
    will come on and the ABS system will be disabled.  To turn ABS
    back on, switch the ignition off and on.
