Hi, Barry,

First of all, take a look at the PCA-Northeast Region Home Page on WWW.
I have a link to it from my home page (see .sig below).

Anyhow, you can drive your Porsche about 8-9 months of the year (although
I know a few who drive all year long - KG???) and can really appreciate
spring when it finally comes.

The Northeast US has several wonderful, challenging road racing courses,
among them Lime Rock Park, New Hampshire International Speedway, Watkins
Glen, Bridgehampton, Mont Tremblant, Mosport and Summit Point, all within
about 6 hours of Massachusetts.

PCA is very active, with Zone 1 representing about 40 percent of the entire
national membership.

Lots of great Porsche people, great cars, and wonderful places to drive,
what's keeping you in California???

Good Luck!
