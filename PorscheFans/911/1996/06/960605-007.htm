>The hose which goes from the engine sheet metal to the thermostat is,
>in fact, composed of two hoses. An inner hose, (part # 901.110.295.00) which
>is attached to the spigot on the sheet metal and the spigot on the injection
>pump by hose clamps, and the outer hose (part # 901.110.296.00) which is
>slightly shorter and floats between the hose clamps, acting as additional
>insulation.
