Subject:Cabs and new Targas - roll cage
Date:Tue, 25 Jun 1996 10:54:14 -0500
Our local Rennsport magazine has an interesting article this month.
Apparently PCA has ruled that the new Targa needs a roll cage (same
as a cab) for DEW events.  Offsetting this is a sidebar from a
Porsche staffer saying that the glass roofed targa meets the same
specs as the coupe for roll over etc.

Anyone know why? I met a couple of new targa and cab owners who are 
really keen to do track events. The prospect of a roll cage (as 
opposed to a simple bar) has discouraged them. A roll bar in a cab 
would look cool, a cage doesn't do much for the esthetics. Resale 
would suffer too I bet. Anyone know why the new Targa needs a cage?  
Wonder what PCA will rule for the Boxter? It has roll bars from the 
get-go, will it need a cage on top of that?
