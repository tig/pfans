I'm sorry Jeff but all SC's turn the opposite direction to your distributor
and the drive gear on the crank is different  to yours. 
The distributor will not work in your engine. Also the CDI box for an SC has
6 electrical pins and yours should only have 3. So that will not plug in
anyway. Also the tach is different and early and late tachs and boxes cannot
be mixed.
