          Omar, glad the suction cup trick worked for you.  I don't
          think that the problem is a vacuum.  I think the heat causes
          the plastic face to expand.  Because the diameter is pretty
          much held constant by the trim ring, the face becomes more
          concave.  Flipping the lens would make it convex (as it
          faces the driver), and would keep it from touching the
          needle when it expands.
