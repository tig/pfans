Dennis:

When I had a 75 911S I would get wind noise around the front edge of the
driver's door near the base of the windshield.  The automotive test reports
at the time it was "new" all mentioned excessive wind noise as a
"traditional" 911 characteristic.   It did help when I found that one of the
small rubber pieces that fit at the forward inside edge of the window frame
was missing.   Replacing it did reduce "but not eliminate" the wind noise.
 You might try Stoddard's or Tweeks among others to see if they have
replacement pieces.

I always preferred the 911 without a radio anyway....the mechanical "sounds"
were far better than any stereo (had a non stereo Blaupunkt Frankfurt in
originally) that I could buy at the time.
