A repeat of a P-philes message follows:

Recently someone asked about battery and battery box sizes for early 911s. 
Here are the results from my 1971 911E (C- type) .

The battery box is asymmetrical. The width at the top of the battery box is ~ 
8 3/4 in. The width at the bottom is ~81/4 ". The height of the battery box is 
~ 7 1/8 in. The depth at the front of the battery box is ~5 1/4 in. And the 
depth at the back is 6 1/4 deep.

When I purchased my car it had Interstate batteries - model number F8MA - 
installed. These batteries are ~ 71/4 in. by ~8 in.  at their base and ~6 7/8 
by ~ 7 7/8 in. At their top. The tallest height is ~ 6 7/8 in.
These are conventional lead - acid batteries.

I replaced these recently with two Deka batteries model CCA 245. These were 
$41.55 each.
