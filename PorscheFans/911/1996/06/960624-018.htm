Hi Colin-

My 2 cents....   I purchased my fully loaded (LSD, full leather, spoilers,
Fuchs, sunroof, cruise control, etc) '86 911 Coupe in December '93 for
$22K.  The car had 52K miles and had full (correct) service history -
excellent condition, but not concour, perfection quality.  The same car
today should sell for around $20K, a bit less for an '84, a bit more for an
'87 (which is what I'd hold out for if I had to do it again).  For $30K (or
less) you should be able to pick up a real nice low mileage turbo-look car,
if you're so inclined. 

Good luck, be patient, but do buy one,
