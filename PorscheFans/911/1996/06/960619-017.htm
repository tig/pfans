I don't usually tackle electrical questions but basic item I know from wiring 
mobile phones and cb type transmitters is go directly to the battery terminals 
or to pos. term. and a ground commmon to the -negative batt. term.  This 
bypasses all of your cars electronics.  Don't forget to fuse the line from the 
pos. term.  You can use wire lugs that go over the tightening bolt for the 
cable end.
