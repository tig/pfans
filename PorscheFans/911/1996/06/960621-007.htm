First a reply to Mark Gillis about 92 octane and ethanol.  I can't say if   
a 69 911s will act the same way as my 912 but the conditions are similar.   
 The 69 912 (and 911s I would imagine) came from the factory for   
California Emissions as lean as would allow them to run.  Many 912s   
suffered cracked heads because of the over heating they took.  Now for   
the Ethanol, any alcohol added to a fuel will increase octane BUT alcohol   
has 1/2 the BTU's!  So you are in effect leaning it out even more!  I had   
to re-jet the 912 to get it to run on 94octane 25%alcohol.
