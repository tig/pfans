In his last message Jay was commenting that his 69 911 T only had 110 hp. 
Well yes that is true, but if you look at the horsepower/torque curves in 
the back of your owners manuel, you will find alot of early torque in 
those engines. (Yes, I think those engines are almost identical to the 
914-6 engines) This makes a car fast off the line.
My 69 S is crappy off the line, the horses don't really kick in till 4500 
rpms or so. But it makes it really fun on the freeway :)
If you think you have S pistons, do a quick compression check. The 
compression will be much higher than stock.
