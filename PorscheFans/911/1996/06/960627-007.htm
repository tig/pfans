Dirk-

My opinion.  Not everyone will agree.  (But I'd
be interested in comments or other opinions.)

1) Timing light.  All you really need is one of those that
cost aroud $35.  The fancier ones allow you to "dial" in
the timing, but it's not really necessary to have
this feature.  Your 911 crankshaft pulley is very well
marked with the needed timing points.

2) Dwell Meter.  I don't think these are particularly
useful.  The dwell is determined by the point gap, which
is awfully simple to set with a feeler gauge.
