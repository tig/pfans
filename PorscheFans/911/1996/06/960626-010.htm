   Sounds like you have a broken accelerator link. It's a piece of rubber with a
metal disc bonded to each end. The throttle linkages screw into it. Look under
the car, and I'll bet you find the rubber part is broken.
   You can probably wrap some wire around it to get going temporarily. Replace
it with one of the later versions that has a sort of cage around it. Any of the
Vendors can provide one, Tweeks, Automotion, etc..
