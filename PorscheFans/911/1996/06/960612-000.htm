One slight problem that PorscheFans has run in to is that subscribers were
getting bumped off because the mail server couldn't deliver the mail.  After

times faster than a high-speed 28.8 modem) just one hop off of the backbone
of the Internet.

bumped.  Since when you get bumped, the notice I get doesn't tell me if you
got the messages in digest mode, or individual message, I'll resubscribe the
bump-ees in digest mode.
