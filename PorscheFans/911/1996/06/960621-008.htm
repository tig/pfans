Two potential solutions:

1. The one way valve going to that jet may be jammed.  I have gotten rid of
the originals and placed one generic one near the pump where it would be
easy to replace.  Since I have done that, I have not had to replace it. 
(Could this be the basis of a new scientific law?)

2. My wife's car had some dirt get into the nozzles.  I could only remove
it using an air gun blowing backwards through the nozzle.  After several
blows interspersed with several washings, they now work fine.
