You might want to consider just a single, simple, conventional,
lead-acid battery.  You can buy one that fits into the
battery box, sideways.  I forget what its number is, but
it's a very common battery, available at virtually any and all
automotive stores, like Schuck's or PepBoys or whatever you have 
in your area.  Costs abot $35, and works just fine.  Have one
in my '72E, and when the dual batteries in my '72T need
replacing, I'll do it this way.
