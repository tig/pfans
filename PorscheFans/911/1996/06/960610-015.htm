Thanks for all the advice re: running hot and the source of the oil in my air 
cleaner housing.  Just to clarify: this was a very hot day (90^+) with engine 
idling while waiting in line for my runs.  Typical runs were under a minute.

my car at Pocono Raceway and only saw temps go up to 210 on a cooler day.

headlight bucket but I think I'll try removing the parking light first.
