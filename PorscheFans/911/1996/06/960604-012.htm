Howdy,

On my '72 911S, the small hose that goes to the thermostat
came off once on a spirited drive. The car would not accelerate,
it ran terrible. It was VERY rich because it was warmed-up
but the thermostat then cooled down which made the MFI think
the engine was cool and richened it. This hose to the thermostat
MUST be in place to allow proper tuning.

The hot air hose into the air intake, however, should be
disconnected or plugged. Lots of rust and
road debris will come up this pipe and fill your air cleaner (could 
get in your engine if your air cleaner is not sealed well!).

Regards, Mike Piera  '72 911S
