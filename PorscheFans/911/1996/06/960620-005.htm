Make sure you get a chip designed for headers and supertrapps.

Also - don't let anyone talk you in to 1-3/4" headers.

I have a 3.4 and the break even point on 1-5/8 to 1-3/4" 
headers is 6700 RPM.

Make sure you DO NOT buy Bursch and that you buy the RSR
style (3 into 1 FLAT) collectors.

You may even want to just get SSI and keep your heat...

I recommend AutoTHORITY as they have chips specifically designed for
your application, headers.
