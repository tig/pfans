I think the 81 has the same sort of adjustments as most pre G50 911's, 
namely there is a threaded clevis at the pedal end, which you can get at by 
lifting up the carpets and looking in the tunnel, it is awkward to get at. 
The best option is to adjust at the transmission end, which will be on the 
bottom of the transmission (at the bell housing) where you ought to see a 
threaded adjuster on the clutch cable. There is a spec, but I tend to just 
adjust and try driving a bit as I am rather fussy on clutch engagement 
height. 

Good luck
