Making the adjustment at the bell housing is the easiest way to do it.  It
will require two 11mm wrenches to loosen the locking nuts.  Then one to
move the adjustment to where it feels comfortable.  What I use on my
street car and my friends 914-6 race car is 1.0-1.5 inches off the floor
for the pedal to start engaging.  After you have it adjusted, lock the
adjustment down using the two wrenches again.  Make the adjustment in 2-3
turn increments.  It does not take much of an adjusment at the tranny to
make a big difference at the pedal.
