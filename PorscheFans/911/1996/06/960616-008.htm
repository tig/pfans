Hi Geert,

If your bushing, yes the white/cream colored ones are tight you have a 
chance of adjusting your linkage and finding all of the gears.  

I think that I would start by finding neutral.  This is the clamp 
movement front to rear.  Mark it with some white out, paint or 
something.   Draw a line through the paint to index the clamp in rotation 
to the shifter rod of the transmission.  By this method you can place the 
shift pattern where you like it in relation to where you sit and reach.
