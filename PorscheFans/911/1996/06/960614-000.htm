I have replaced both front outer wheel bearings on my '72 911.  The old
bearings were oval shaped.  Maybe even worse that oval shaped, more like a
triangle than round.  Could this be caused by not having the front hub allen
bolt tight enough? Or, just normal use and abuse from track events
 
When I replaced the bearings, I tightened them up more that before.  I
remember someone a few months ago on Porschephiles said that the procedure
outlined in the Haynes manual would leave the front hubs too loose.  Not
sure I ever read what the factory manual said.
