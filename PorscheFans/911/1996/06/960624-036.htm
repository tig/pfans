Getrag G50 (/00 for Europe and /01for ROW, with lower 4th and 5th) is one of
the best transmission Porsche ever installed (according to many people the
best). It'a just a little noisy if you let it be. To avoid that  you just
need to drive yor car without letting her to drop under the torquy range of
rpm. Anyway, the rattle is absolutely  regular and harmless. My '89 3.2
Carrera has the improved G50/03 of C2 with a more silent 5th. Porsche tried
to fix that problem with the splitted Freudenberg two mass flywheel in the
C2-C4 engine,  more silent but weaker.
So, don't let your engine sit down and everything will be o.k.!
