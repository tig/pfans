I have a 1973 T that has been converted to a single battery.  I just bought 
the Optima sealed battery and am very happy with it.  The Optima is a small 
enough battery that it will fit into the battery trays of a '73 (at least 
mine left tray).  It cost me $100.  I also remember Stoddard having a sale 
on batteries a while ago.  Might be worth giving them a call.
