the anti-seize.  The torque specs are given for dry threads.  I think 
there are other reasons for not putting anti-seize on them as well.  I'm

personal opinion on this, but that of several experts and most mechanics 
I've talked to.  I must say, they do go in easier with anti-seize on 'em.
