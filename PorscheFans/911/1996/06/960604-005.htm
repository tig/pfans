Dennis-

I think they all are noisy.  I doubt your windshield seal is leaking 
enough to be a problem, but your door seal could be.  I had trouble 
with one on my '72 coupe.  While you're driving, listening to the 
noise, press your fingers into the door seal all around the front part of 
the door.  (On my 911's you can actually get your finger on the seal, 
even though the door is closed.)  You may find a spot where things 
suddenly get quite a bit quieter.  You can figure out what to do from 
there.
