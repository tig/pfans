Do a PCA driving school first....pick a good track with lots of run off... 
Not that you will need it, only that it is good to have. 
 
You can attend one of these schools for a 10th of the price. 
If you like it then try another track....then do the big $$$ 
school. 
 
I might just be lucky ...but we have some very good instructors in 
my region so I feel I'm getting more than my $$ worth. 
 
This will alos let you have an instructor who has the same 
type car as you do....big plus...experience counts here..... 
Only my $0.02......
