Kyle:

The engine was not a V-8 but the aircooled 3 litre 8 cylinder boxer type 908
racing engine.  The engine was originally designed with some consideration of
a possible family of four-camshaft eight and six cylinder Porsche production
engines.

Two were built in 1969...one for Ferry Porsche ("detuned to the extent
possible for a docile road machine") for his 60th birthday and the other for
Ferdinand Piech's "own amusement".  The later was a full racing version
putting out 300 hp at 7500 rpm.
