>>     2) I will never, ever, ever, EVER use Red Line MTL again!


I ran my '69 911 racecar in over 40 Time Trials between 1985 and 1992 using
Redline lubricants and never had a problem (traceable to lubrication failure,
that is). This was using a full bore 2.7 and 2.9 making between 250 and 290
dynoed HP and a 915 with Quaife LSD, and I'm not known for being kind to
trannies.

I also ran Redline MTL in the rear end and gearbox of my RX-7 ITA racer and had
no problems; ditto with my 914/4 and 930. However, I've used Swepco also and it
worked fine (but MTL reduced the gearbox temps by 5-10 deg. C and this is why I
finally settled on it). Plus they pay contingency funds for SCCA regionals!
