Jeff:

Crankfire ignitions are great things, but prospective buyers should
be aware that the proper mounting of the sensor/magnets is
CRITICAL... If and when the sensor slips in its mount, the timing
will go WAY off and/or the ignition will quit altogether.  I know a
number of people who, for whatever reasons, couldn't get the sensor
to stay in place under heavy-vibration conditions and were forced to
go back to a distributor.
