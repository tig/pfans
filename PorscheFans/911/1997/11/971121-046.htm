Good idea, but you would need a really high rez scanner to get an image
that you could digitally expand. The fiche are very high quality B/W photos
of the manual pages. My frau is a HS media specialist and can make prints
for me whenever I need hardcopy, your local library should have a printer.
The fiche are definitely worth the money if you can read them. I bought a
very nice used reader (in my mind, the wife pooh-poohed it as junk) for
about $75 at a used camera place. Sometimes you can get a reader cheap at
the local schoolboard surplus warehouse.
