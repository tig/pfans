Lately my 75 targa has been producing a kind of low pitched rattle sound
from the exhaust with medium to heavy acceleration. Almost like a tinny
sound. 

I heard the same thing from my car awhile back and the sound seemed to
change as the engine warmed up.  The mystery was cleared up when I took
the muffler off and shook out several pieces of the internal baffles
that had rotted and loosened.  Most of the sound is gone, but one large
piece (too large to get out the opening) still makes a slight tinny
rattle sound .

Perhaps you have the same problem.  It only takes 10 minutes to take the
muffler off and check.
