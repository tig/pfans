Re Greg's question on the noncanceling turn signal:  From my experience,
it's another manifestation of a failing turn signal-dimmer switch.  The
more common occurrence is where the detent that keeps the switch from
going into high beams fails.  There are those who've reported success at
repairing the switch, but the by-the-book method is to replace it--it
comes as a single unit.
