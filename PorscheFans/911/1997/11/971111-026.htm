Ron,

Fire up the BBQ.  I R&R ed the bushings on my '67 and on one side I had to use the propane torch.  It smelled so good, too.  (It didn't take much heat.  30 seconds or so).  

I hope you scratched a line on the body so you can get the spring plates back in the same place.  If you want to lower the body, make a template of an 18 degree angle and put the plates back on on the new line (as measured off the old baseline).  Pat Moyle taught me this and it worked like a charm.  Of course this won't work if your car has been previously lowered slightly.
