I tried to just install the bushings but discovered you have to machine-press out the
piece in the middle. It is steel but the outside part is aluminum and its real easy to
mess the thing up. 

I ended up having to replace the whole coupling...$^( 

Be real careful when you remove the old coupling. Put some marks on the linkage so
you can line it up just like it was or your shifting will get really bad (or not work at all).
