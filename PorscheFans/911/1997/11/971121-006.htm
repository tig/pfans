The fishe are definitely cheap and appear to be exact reprints of the 
manual in fishe format, no compromise there.  You might be able to find 
a reader where you work, assuming you do work.  They are more common in 
the era of digital imaging than many realize, I guess.  Used they are 
available, too.  Usually for $100 or less for view only.

If you want to be unique, I once saw a copier repair guy using a hand 
held fishe reader.  These are hard to find and cost new about as much 
as a whole reader used.
