I have a similar problem which I haven't corrected yet.  I am ordering new
struts for both the front and rear lids which I hope will solve the
problem.  Meanwhile, I learned from my mechanic how to open the lid myself,
without the aid of a second set of hands:  Pull on the release handle with
your left hand and use your right hand to gently pull the air intake grill
towards you.  It requires very little effort to "pop" the engine lid, at
least on my car, and doesn't do any damage.  Ever since I learned this
method, fixing the engine lid lost some of it's urgency.
