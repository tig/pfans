I installed a Monty muffler on my SC yesterday, and spent most of today
driving around listening to what I had been missing with the stock
muffler (for which the British term "silencer" is more appropriate). The
Monty allows just enough sound to be very enjoyable, without being
obtrusive, offensive, or illegal. The model I chose is the M31,
essentially the same shape as the stock muffler, with a somewhat larger
(100mm) tailpipe. Made entirely of stainless steel, even comes with two
new mounting bands.
