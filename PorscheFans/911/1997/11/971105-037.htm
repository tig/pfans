You could check your strut.  Open the engine cover and look inside left
almost out of sight under rear window.  (The rear hatch on mine has one
strut and the front luggage compartment has 2 struts.)  It could need
re-gassing.  If you have a turbo tail on the hatch then the weight of
that fighting a weakened or spent strut will yield a no result. Perhaps
someone could lift the tail up while you pull the engine cover release. 
If that works fine then probably the strut needs re-gassing or
replacing.  They aren't very costly at all.
