My 2 cents...

I recently put a one-piece fiberglass bumper on my Alfa 164, which I
sprayed myself with a HVLP Gravity-fed gun.  I used Glasurit 54 paint
(basecoat / satin clearcoat), and the results were pretty good,
especially considering the fact that I did this outside.  It's been only
5 months, but I've had no trouble at all with the paint peeling or
cracking. An extra measure of insurance (which I will use when I re-spray
some of the plastic parts), is to add a flex additive to the paint.
