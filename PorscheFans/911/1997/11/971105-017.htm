When I've had this problem it has been because the linkage was slight;y out
of adjustment, and the throttle plate on the offending cylinder was open a
bit more than the others. Check it by popping the linkage off #3 while
idling.  If the plate remains where it was, then that's not the problem.
If it closes slightly, adjust the linkage.
