Porsche was not "user friendly" in its plug access design.

Two tools should help (1) a magnetized plug socket and (2) a sidewinder
ratchet. Both are available from Performance Products. The ratchet costs
considerably less at J.C. Whitney.

You should not have to access the plugs from below by removing the
protective guard.

Whatever you do. 

PLEASE! PLEASE! DO NOT REMOVE THE OUTSIDE FENDERS TO GAIN ACCESS TO THE
PLUGS.

Good Luck!
