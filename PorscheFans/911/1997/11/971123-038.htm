I had one installed last year, as I found the long throw of the '85 annoying
after getting used to the shorter stock throw in my departed '86. It appeared
to be a well made piece, and accomplished what I wanted. Had to install a new
knob, as we couldn't get the old one off. I put on a MOMO, and am very happy
with it also. You can control the throw length somewhat by how deep you mount
the knob on the shaft.

I do need to adjust my linkage, as fast downshifting from 5th to 4th can
snick reverse.
