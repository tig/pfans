Your vent hoses from your tank are not connected properly or some of the
original evaporative components have been taken out. 

I had the same problem (yes, the smell and overflow from track events)
when the PO changed to a later style tank and by-passed several of the
earlier systems.  The fix I found is by installing a system similar to
that which Porsche used in the 87 Carrera - there is a small clear
plastic container (I'll get you the part # if you want) in line with the
overflow, vent and breather hoses.

Hope this helps you out.
