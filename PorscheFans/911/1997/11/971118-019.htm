The "pebbly" surface Targa tops tend to collect a coating of dirt over
time. Clean them first with a scrub brush (bristle, not wire) and a
detergent solution. After drying, I use Vinylex, again with a scrub brush,
to get off any residual dirt, then dry it again, and use Vinylex a final
time with a terry towel buffing to get a nice satin sheen to it.

Targa Tops also last longer if they're not folded and re-folded all the
time. Porsche did make a hard Targa panel back in the '70s for a couple of
years, but I don't know if one would fit an SC.
