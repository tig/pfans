I want to second the kudos for the Monty muffler. I am very happy with both
the sound and the quality of construction of my Monty. I can't comment on
any horsepower gains because I also installed SSI headers and I can't say
what percentage of the gain was contributed by the muffler. The model I
purchased was the M-22 for the '74 style headers with twin 75mm outlets. It
is kind of loud, but now I can hear the sound of the exhaust over the other
engine noises and I love it.
