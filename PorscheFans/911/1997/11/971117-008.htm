I have very good results with the Toyo F1S-R rain tires. R compound 
but DOT legal. Come with very deep tread, 10/16ths I think. Do not 
use in the dry unless you shave them, Squirmy as hell and they'll 
destroy themselves. These are genuine rain tires for wet conditions 
only. Shave them and they are similar to R1's.
