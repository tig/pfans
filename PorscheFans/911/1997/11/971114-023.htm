**Now to answer your question**
Go around your 911 carefully inventorying everything that you think needs
to be done (e.g., repainting)  Think through the process and list all of
the supplies (a repair manual will help).  For the body, you will need
sanding supplies, strippers, primers, fillers, tape, trim gaskets, paint,
thinners, tack clothes, degreasers, and more.  I think that there are many
quality products available in each category each of which may have a trade
off.  Personal preference certainly is a factor in you choice.

If you can't decide on a specific product (e.g., paint manufacturer) please
pose you question to the list for comments from others.

Good luck maintaining and rebuilding you 911.
