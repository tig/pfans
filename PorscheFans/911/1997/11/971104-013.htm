I had a similar problem, but mine was a gunky latch coupled with a weak =
spring.  I put a spacer above the spring (just spin the latch bolt out =
of the lid, add a washer between the spring and "spring cup", and screw =
it back in (make sure it is adjusted)), and all works fine now.
