I beleive the hammer works on any DME system. It is very expensive and can
be purchased with different attachment plugs. It connects in-between the
DME cable and the DME, thus intercepting and generating signals from/to the
engine. The DME on my '87 is fairly simplistic and all of the pinouts are
given in the shop manuals. Someone was trying to build a PC-based "hammer",
but without the details of the DME, that is a formidible task indeed.

BTW, it is called a "Hammer" because of the shape of the operators handset.
