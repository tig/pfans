You may be recalling my post of about a month ago.  Anyhow, I purchased
a replacement Porsche pump from Roger Beastly Porsche in Austin, TX in
late October. The part was $57 list.  (BTW, The next day, while
attending my first PCA region event, the "customer relations" manager
came out and handed out his business card with a note on the back of it
entitling all PCA members to 20% off parts.  Oh well....)

I prefer the OEM to the generic pump you can buy at Pep Boys etc...
because the cigarette lighter plug diameter is a bit larger on the 911
than on other cars (metric I assume) and the OEM part fits snugly
whereas other power plugs are a little loose. That seems like it would
be a problem as you stretch the cord to reach the corner of the car to
fill your tires.

I suppose there might be an adapter (does anyone know) to convert the
cigarette plug (metric male/non-metric female) and I could actually use
it for my cell phone charger and/or radar detector.
