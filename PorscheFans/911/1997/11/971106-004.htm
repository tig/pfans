I don't know if you mean to replace the actual steering column bearing, or 
just the little white plastic bushing at the top of the shaft, the demise of 
which makes the wheel wobble around like its going to come off (it won't!). My 
mechanic replace this little white ring in about 45 minutes or less, and the 
part cost like $3. He even got it from a VW dealer, apparently it was used on 
various Rabbits as well. Wobble now gone, mechanic charged me $38 total. Now 
this was not the actual bearing, but a little bushing at the top end of shaft. 
It is visible if you pull the wheel off the shaft. Machanic said the plastic 
gets old and dry and he has done lots of these little replacement jobs on 
older cars. Warren. '81 SC.
