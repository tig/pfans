David,  don't know what type of alarm you have, but the factory unit with the
door
key to me is adequate.  

This is on the key-lock type on my '95 (you lock the door manually by
inserting
key in door lock).  The newer cars apparently have a more sophisticated unit
available (option 530) with a remote locking/unlocking unit with an
immobilization
of the FI and starter and no apparent door switch for the key.  I read a tech
bulletin on this, and it scared the daylights out of me.  if the battery in
your remote unit
fails, you have to remember a 4-digit number, pry the plastic caps off the
door
locks, then go thru a 2-page procedure to open doors, insert key in ignition,
then 
flick key on and off in certain sequence to code in 4- digit number - all
done in
about a 2 minute window  or else the alarm will sound for 3 minutes and you
can't
shut it off!
