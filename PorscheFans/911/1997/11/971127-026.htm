After my freudian lapsus about 917 and 935, I'll try to regain self-esteem
reopening an old number-related thread. Sometime ago I referred to my 964
turbo as a 965. Bruce Anderson fulminated me asking "what a 965 is?". My
kiddish answer was: 964+1 K27=965. Now I discovered that 965 was the
factory number for the water cooled (a la 962C), 4WD, twin turbo, 959
styled that professor Bott was designing as substitute for the 930 in 1989.
Since she was to expensive and professor Bott retired, they decided to
change their mind and go for a 964 C2 with 3.3 lt , K27/2 and K-jetronic.
Do any of you know more about the 965 and her prototypes?
