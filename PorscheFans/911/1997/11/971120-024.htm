All right, I know there are many turbo owners out there that have seen
the ads for newer turbos to replace the factory K26.  They claim HP
gains of 20 or 30 by upgrading to a K27, or other, turbo.  If the turbos
are all boosting to the same .6 bar or 1.0 bar, how can the engine
realize another 20 or 30  HP?  If the air is pressurized to the same
level, how is there more HP?  I suppose more HP can be realized earlier
(at lower RPMs) with different propeller (is this the proper term for
the exhaust drive of the turbo?) designs, but can it make more peak HP?
