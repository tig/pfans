>By the way, I did this job on an SC and absolutely could not remove
>the allen plug on the side of the box (ended up adding the fluid through
>the top vent).  So, what does one do when the side allen plug won't come
out?
>What trick methods are there to get this baby loose?  Thanx!
Your '86 has the same transaxle as the '80 - in fact, '86 was the last
year this box was used on the 911.  There should be the same allen plug
on the side, just like your '80. I broke a cheap breaker bar the first
time I tried to remove mine - I now have an 18" breaker bar of much
superior quality which makes the job pretty easy.  There's about a
hundred recommendations on re-filling - I just use a suction pump and
it's done in about 15 minutes.  IMPORTANT - make sure the re-fill (top)
plug is removed BEFORE draining.  You don't want to drain unless you know
you will be able to re-fill!

Hope this helps,
