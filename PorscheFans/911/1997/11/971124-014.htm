The SC inherited the three liter displacement, but I believe that the
case, bearings, etc, were from the turbo.  Also, the SC got a very high
level of standard trim and amenities.  It was also a heavier car.
Maybe someone else knows, but I also think the flared fenders in the
rear were adopted from the turbo car.
It does not need leaded fuel.  It will run on standard grade unleaded,
and must do so in the US.
