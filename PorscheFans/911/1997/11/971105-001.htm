Further adventures in Targa Top repairland:

The creaks I can live with but the rattle was really starting to annoy
me.  Upon further inspection it was discoverd that on the passenger side
rearward the 2 stops on either side of the guide hole for the roof were
broken off.  I used Hillman Medium Duty Nylon anchors meant for hollow
walls, size 3/16x1.  They present about the same surface as the real
thing.  Most important, they came with a screw about the size of a small
finishing nail that was countersunk!  After inserting, aligning,
tightening, and applying a dab of silicone over it the problem was
solved.  Total parts cost about 30 cents/anchor.
