> changing the oil or do I need to rebuild the transmission?   

If you haven't gone too far, there may be even a far easier fix.
The shifter has a reverse lockout gate that prevents a 5th-to-
reverse shift.  If the shifter is adjusted so that the physical
shift pattern is too far to the rear of the cockpit, you can
"bump" into reverse before the lockout gate forces the shift
rod over to the 4th gear slider.  It's quite easy (OK, reasonably
easy) to fix by simply adjusting the gearshift rod to transmission
shift rod through the access panel on the rear floor.  There's 
a splined locking collar that can be loosened to allow the adjustment.
