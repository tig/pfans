Hi Mark,

You wrote that your engine was misfiring after a drivers' ed event. Well
the same thing happened to my '73 911S with carbs after an autocross. I
brought the car to my mechanic and he checked the ignition system and
then blew out the carbs. It ran fine afterwards, turned out that some
garbage plugged up one of the jets. You might want to get a bottle of
techron and see if that might clear out any obstruction in your FI
system before you spend hours looking at something else.
