Just how long have you been running BFG R1's and how good are you at
feeling the differences that a 1 or 2 PSI change makes? I know that when I
want to decrease understeer, adding a 1 or 2 psi to the rear makes a
noticable difference. If you add air to the rear tires on your car and you
feel more understeer, you are fooling yourself....R1's just don't work
that way! Adding air to an R1 decreases grip.....
