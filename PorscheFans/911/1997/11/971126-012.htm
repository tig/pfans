>Question:  Has anyone used any of the stop leak oil additives in a Porche
>engine?  What kind of results good / bad.  Any recommendations?

Don't waste your money.  First of all, this stuff is intended to
work on gaskets that have dried up, not on a metal-to-metal
seal like the 911 case halves.   It's a sort of light oil that in
theory seeps into the errant gasket, softening and expanding it.  I
say 'in theory' because it doesn't even do that.  I've never seen it
make a damn bit of difference.  Just another version of snake oil.
