I just replaced these tubes on my 86 Carrera.  Got the tubes from
Automotion.  I have no comment about the Viton seals.  ( I'm not even
sure if mine came with these).  Re: removal.  Take a pair of vice grips
and crush the existing (old) tube.  Then, cut with tin snips. Easy as
that.  Installing is another issue.  Passenger side is quite easy, but
the drivers side is a bit tougher.  I did not remove any existing parts
to replace the tubes.  Total job took me about 2 hours.  Remember to
remove the oil before you start.

This was my first time so check with an expert before you listen to
me...  Good luck..
