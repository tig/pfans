Thierry,

The fiche are Porsche parts that you should be able to get from a dealer.
I did get mine from Stoddards (www.stoddard.com) (1-216-951-1040).  They
suggested getting the 1972-1983 and the 1984-1989 repair fiche.  Here are
the part numbers that I have:
	1972-1983 PNA 000 106
	1984-1989 PNA 000 121
They cost about $40.

I use the fiche printer available at my local library.  The quality varies,
but they have a new machine that prints through an HP laser printer.  I can
only assume that the image gets digitized and then sent to the printer.
