     I don't know about access to the plugs on an SC with AC, but here's 
     what I've done.
     
     After dropping a plug on top of the cylinders after I had removed it 
     on my '73 911 and having to remove the intake valve cover to retrieve 
     it, I made up a set of tools to prevent that from happening again....
     
     I bought a Craftsman spark plug socket (with the neoprene "retainer" 
     inside) and used epoxy to permanently attach a 3" extension to it.  I 
     use a ratchet with a release button on the head so that the extension 
     only comes off when I'm ready - eliminates the need for pliers to get 
     the socket/extension assy off a plug when installing it.
     
     No problems since I made up the assy - much easier than removing the 
     intake valve cover.
     
     Good luck
