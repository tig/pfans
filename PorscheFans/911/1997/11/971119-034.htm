Hi Warren, 

Clutch chatter is usually not the rubber center failing. When the rubber
center fails the rubber gets caught between the clutch and the pressure plate
and the clutch will not disengage. Usually clutch chatter is wear on the
clutch disc itself, hot spots on either the flywheel or pressure plate, soft
motor mounts or inadequate bow in the clutch cable sheathe.
