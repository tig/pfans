Alfas come in many unique flavors, mine being a mid-eighties spider.
The sad truth about spiders is:

1. The chassis has the stiffness of a piece of overcooked linguini.
2. The engine, while a delight to rev, can't beat a Suburban across an
intersection.
3. If you are not a fanatic about top seal replacement, you will get wet
when it rains.
4. Handling is entertaining, but understeer and body lean are the rule.

However, when the sun comes out and the top goes down, the Alfa is the
only car I want to drive.

If anyone is truly interested in finding an Alfa which will entertain
the hardcore p-fan, I suggest an early 80's GTV-6 Turbo.  There is a
very nice GTV Turbo for sale a Cammissa Motors in Burlingame, CA (SF Bay
Area).
