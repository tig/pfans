After having problems with starting my 911 on thuesday I finaly found 
some time to investigate the problem. Remember that I got the car 
started with the help of startcables. Suddenly the tachometer behaved 
very strange.
Just one quick look under the hood solved the problem. One of the 
connections to the battery (+) was lose !!!
I think by removing the startcables it was pulled of the battery (it 
was dark, remember).

Boy what a relieve. No exchange of regulator, altenator etc. Just a 
recharge of the battery and I'm back on the road again.
The battery is charging right now, I hope no further damage will be 
found. 
Anyway thanks for all tips regarding exchanging the alternator, 
regulator.
