I finally figured out the problem I had with AM not working on my stereo.  The
PO had installed a booster under the dash.  I removed that booster after
finding out there is a factory booster in the trunk.  There was a small black
wire with a white connector on the end that came out of the same insulation as
the antenna lead.  That black wire needs 12v to power the factory booster. 
Once I hooked that up, AM and FM work great.
