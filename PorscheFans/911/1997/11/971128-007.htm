To my knowledge, the 904 had a unique platform that was not based on the
914 or any other Porsche.

"The chassis was a simple pressed steel frame formed from
rectangular-section longitudinal and cross-members.  Suspension at the
front was by unequal-length wishbones, with, at the rear, wishbones,
twin forward-facing radius rods and coil springs."
