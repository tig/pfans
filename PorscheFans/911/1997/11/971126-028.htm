I just purchased an 87 930 with 35,000 original miles.  The paint was
perfect, absolutely no dings, and the interior looked like the car
justed rolled out of the showroom.  The leather inside showed no wear at
all.  The car is straight and rattle free.  I paid $34,200 for this
absolutely stock, original car.  It also took me over 6 months to find
it.  Don't fall in lust at first site, and have a mechanic check it
out.  I ended up paying for two mechanical inspections on cars that I
turned down.  A real money saver in the long run.
