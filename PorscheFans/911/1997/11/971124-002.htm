Re Maureen Soar's question about braided ignition wires on her '76 S: 
I've often wondered the same myself.  My '75 (here he goes again) came
without the braided wires initially; then as part of a factory recall,
the braids were installed--no loom for the wires; they just lay across
the fan shroud.

In the process of tracking down ignition deterioration in '92, I had my
wires replaced.  An independent Porsche shop in Reno installed
custom-cut Bosch wires with no steel braids, and they've performed
perfectly.  There are various sets I've subsequently seen advertised,
and unless you're going concours I don't think you need the braided
wires.
