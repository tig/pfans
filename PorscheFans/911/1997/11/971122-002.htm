You say that fuel comes from the left wheel well, but the overflow hose
here comes from the boot below the filler cap and is designed to pick up
fuel that escapes during fill ups.   There is another vent hose that
exits under the car near the a-arm mounting point.  I suppose very hard
cornering could cause fuel to escape through this hose.  In any event I
doubt this is related to your problem.  I think johnd's got the answer.
The only thing I would add regards the short hose that connects the
filler pipe to the tank.  I've found that you can really only get these
trully tight using a 1/4" socket instead of a screwdriver.
