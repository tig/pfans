>How long does the car need to be "warmed up" in the mornings? I live

I usually start the car, check the mirrors, put on the seat belt, make
sure the seat is in the right position, pause and check guages for
weirdness then proceed.  By this time the oil pressure has come up and
oil is flowing over all the important stuff.  Then keep it under 4k RPM
until the temp guage indicates normal operating level.
