Depends on what year of 993, I think.  As of no later than '97, the
993 comes with the full-boat show . . . transmitter-driven auto 
doorlocks (which locks the glovebox and, if you have them instead
of the rear seats, the rear storage compartments!!, but, inexplicably,
not the trunk; the engine cover is presumed locked because the
door is locked, securing the handle), intrusion alarm (set the alarm
on a ragtop with the top down, then reach in an pull the trunk
lever and the horn/lights begin their show) and the dreaded motorblock
system from the twinTurbo.  I don't see how you'd possibly need more
than this.
