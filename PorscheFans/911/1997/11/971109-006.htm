Compared to regular aspirated 911s, I find the 930 heavy, and a dog at
lower RPM. Get the boost and indeed, feels like an airplane taking off.
Great on the highway, hard in city traffic.Only real danger I experienced
is when its wet and boost kicks in...provides some entertaining car moves.
Brakes and roadholding are great. Incidentally, the only 930 I witnessed
with minimal turbolag was a car with Motec setup, dynoed and prepped by
Jerry Woods...My $ 0.02.
