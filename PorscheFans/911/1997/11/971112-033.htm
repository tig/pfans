From my experience with my '76 911, the calipers should be fine.  However,
check the rubber dust seals around the caliper pistons; these definitely
wear.
Might also want to replace the pins holding the pads in place.

I put 150,000 miles on my '76, and the calipers never came apart. I don't 
think they need touching so long as you replace fluid regularly (once a
year).

I assume that you have considered repacking the front wheel bearings?  They
wore on my '76.
