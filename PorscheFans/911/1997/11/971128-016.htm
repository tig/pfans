Aldo-
	There is a picture, and a brief description of Project 965 in "Porsche 911,
Forever Young" by Tobias Aichele. It was indeed twin turbo, with Motronic
engine management. Output was 360hp. Bodywork was VERY 959, but what's
interesting is that it doesn't look like Porsche disguised the car too much,
instead, they put a decal on the back, something like "EPC Tuning". I guess
they figured people would think it was an aftermarket conversion instead of a
mule. Good book, too.
