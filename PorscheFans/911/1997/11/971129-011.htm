Tom, it's been awhile since I played with a 2.7, but first thing I would
check
is the rubber air duct at the top of the engine.  It is possible that you
popped
this off the airbox when the engine popped.

Next thought is to remove the cover over the airbox, remove the air filter,
and see if the air flow sensor is moving freely.  Also, you can turn on
key and see if fuel pump runs as you slightly move the air sensor 
plate/lever up.

Hope this is of some guidance.
