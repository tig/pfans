James,

I assume that you lifted BOTH levers.  If you did this, I think you are on
the right track checking the blowers and hoses.  There is one blower in
each kick panel.  You can probably narrow your search by checking if you
get heat without rotating the selector knob (e.g. 0 instead of 3).  You
should still get some hot air flow from the blower in the engine
compartment.  Check to ensure that you get the same amount of air flow from
each side.

Another possibility is to check that the flapper is closing the heater duct
under the car by the engine.  Perhaps one of the cables is loose preventing
it from closing and you are only getting cold air.
