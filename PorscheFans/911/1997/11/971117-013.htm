One suggested addition to Greg's excellent descrition of how to replace the
rear (and also the front) window seals (courtesy Jeff Shorey).  When you
place the string in the seal, go around the perimeter twice.  That way when
you pull it out, if for some reason it 'misses', you get a second shot at
it without having to remove the entire windshield.
