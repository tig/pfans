Yesterday I replaced the Pirelli P7s, the only tires I've ever had on my 
911SC, with Dunlop D40 M2s. I was, and am, running 205/55 and 225/50 16s. 
I've always followed the Tire Wars with interest, so here are my three 
immediate impressions of the change:

1) The ride is way smoother.
2) There is much less tendency for the wheels to follow grooves in the 
road.
3) Low speed steering effort is significantly reduced.

These observations are based on about 7 miles of city driving, so 
obviously I have no experience yet at speed or with broken-in grippiness, 
but so far it's all appendages up.
