Like Walt said I can't see where a dip stick heater would do much for a
911.  If whoever asked where interested enough they might check out some
sources for aircraft engine heater systems.  I had Tannis cylinder, oil
and case heaters on my Seneca (twin engine piston aircraft) that would
keep the oil and engine itself at 40f in sub zero weather.  If that
wasn't practical I've used portable propane fired heaters that blow hot
air.  Will heat an engine to a reasonable temp in about 5 min.
