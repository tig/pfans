I spoke to Dieter from Andial the other day about K & N filters - he said
that on the dyno, there was some increase at the top end, but that there
wasn't much practical benefit for street driving.

Because a K & N filters more poorly than the stock filter, I'm leaving the
stock filter in for the street, and putting the K & N in for track events. 

The stock filter is quite a bit quieter, also --that may or not be a benefit.
The troaty sound is cool and SOUNDS faster, but on a long trip, I'll take the
quiet.
