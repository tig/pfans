My experience with shifts from 4th to 5th is that you have to be pretty
deliberate--the same spring that locks out reverse also affects getting
into fifth gear, more or less.  Going from 5th to 4th is seldom a
problem.  The suggestion on the list that it's the driver is probably
correct.
