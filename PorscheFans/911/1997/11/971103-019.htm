Mike,

Your symptoms are very common with a bad DME relay.  The relay is located
under the driver seat (I think it holds true for '89) next to the DME
control box.  You can try cleaning the contacts in case it is corrosion
that is causing the problem, but you may also want to try replacing it.
You may want to do the same with the relay for the fuel pump.

Hope this helps,
