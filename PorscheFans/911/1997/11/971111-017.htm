Darrel,

I had a similar problem after putting new brake pads on the front of my
81 911. The problem was corrected by also replacing the rear pads with
the same brand.

My guess is that your front and rear pads have a different coefficient
of friction which is causing the front pads to lock up much sooner than
the rear.
