I've found it's pretty rare, but there are a few of us (Alfa-Porsche
owners).  In my case, I've done everything backwards...
The Porsche is red; the Alfa is white
The Porsche is nearly 100% stock; the Alfa is substantially modified
The Porsche stays in the garage; the Alfa sees daily duty (unless the
motor is pulled)

Although the motors have nearly the same HP (~200), they are completely
different animals.  The 911 is a street-legal race car which deserves to
be driven like one.  The 164 is a 4-door family cruiser with a race-car
motor under the hood (incidently, the Alfa easily wins the upper speed
race with a maximum speed in excess of 150 MPH) 
I wouldn't sell either one...
