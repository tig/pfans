Don't know if an '84 is the same (probably)... but for my '83 I just got
a big, expendable 1/2" drive socket... and a big grinding wheel... and
ground away for about a half hour until I had four teeth sticking up to
engage the nut on the ball joint.  Forget the original socket size but
it was thirtysomething millimeters.

When loosening the nut, I used a cheater on my 1/2" drive... and jacked
a block of wood lightly up under the joint to help hold my kludged tool
in place while I heaved away.
