Dearest Pfans:
	The discussion on "what was that chirping noise on the GT-1 cars" has
been interesting.  The national 930 tech guy "Doc" Emard has always told
me that the chirping is the result of the turbine compressor blades
changing speed and disturbing the ongoing flow of supersonic air.  This
happens as a result of extremely fast flow changes such as downshifting
as opposed to just decelerating.  The wastegate makes a "whoosing" noise
when open.  This is my best info.  Anyone got other ideas.
