Steve,

We run synthetics in our race car and most of the track guys in our
region run synthetic gear oil.  What we use is Redline MTL  (Manual
Transmission Lube).  This has a special additive to make the syncros
work better.  They also make a variety called MT 90.  That is a
straight 90 weight, and I don't recommend it for street cars.  I can
not comment about other brands, as I have never used them.
