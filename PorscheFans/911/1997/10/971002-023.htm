Andy,

I just replaced the speakers in my 87 911 Targa.  The door uses a 5 1/4"
speaker and the deck uses a 4"x6" Metric speaker.  From what I remember,
the depth for the door speakers weren't too important; however, the
depth of the rear speakers are.  If you bottom mount or flush mount the
speaker then you'll need to get a speaker with a depth of 2" or less.
Since my rear speaker grills were broken (and visually unappealing) I
bought a set of Kenwood 4x6 speakers that came with new grills.  These
speakers came with some sort of spacer that would allow you to use a
deeper speaker (although the speaker grill is no longer flush with the
deck).  Hope this helps!
