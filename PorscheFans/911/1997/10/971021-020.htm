Jeroen,

Both problems may be related to air leaks in you intake.  Un-metered air
entering the intake will lean you mixture which will cause the stutter and
frequently elevate the idle speed.  The idle speed may also "stick" because
the auxiliary air valve is not closing properly and "blipping" the throttle
gets it to close properly.  Try spraying it out with carburetor cleaner (it
is the cylindrical solenoid with hoses bypassing the throttle body).  I
removed mine, cleaned it, and fixed my idle problems.
