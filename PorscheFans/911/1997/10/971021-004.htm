I had the same thing. The fore-and-aft switch was broken, and would only
work in one direction. I switched the leads at the motor (under the front
of the seat) and got it to go in the other direction. Sounds like both
sides of your switch are shot, though. Could also be the motor itself. Find
a 12v power source and hook it directly to the motor leads. If it runs, the
trouble is in the switch.

Good luck.....
