One suggestion, Todd, is to get the version of the SRD that has a cutout 
in the seat cushion for a sub strap.  I had one of these seats.  It was 
a great seat, but over time I really regretted not being able to use a 
sub strap with it.  I got similar comments from instructors who used it.
