The door speakers in an '86 911 Carrera are 5.25" rounds. The rear deck
speakers are 4x6" ovals. Any aftermarket 5.25" will fit in the holes, but
it's a good idea to cut off the plastic baffle you'll find back there so
that larger magnets and frames won't rub. You can put a swatch of
fiberglass insulation in the door speaker pocket to cut down the rebound
noise.

The 4x6s can't be replaced with larger units in the existing holes becasue
the firewall is too close to the rear deck cover. You alternatives are to: 
a) build up an upholstered box to fit over the hole and give you more
vertical clearance;
b) pay +/-$315 for a Moch One speaker enclosure (which will hold 4--6"
round speakers); 
c) Replace with a top-end pair of 4x6s. ADS 200i's are the best, but are
very pricey. I used Pioneer TS-4602's for $60.

Good luck!
