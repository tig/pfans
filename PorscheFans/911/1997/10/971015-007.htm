I have an 86 911 Carrera that I purchased in Oct. 1993 (I remember
because I have exactly 12 payments left.). 2nd owner car with 39K
miles.  Triple black, 16" wheels, spoilers, all the goodies.  Excellent
condition with all service records.  Really a nice car.  I paid 22,800
then.

The asking price from the dealer sounds a bit high.  You should be able
to find a very nice example of that car for low 20's.  Find a recent
back issue of Excellence magazine.  I am not sure of the author but this
year they have done a comprehensive analysis of the value of used
Porsches.  The last issue covered 911 Turbos.

I love my 86 but if I had the chance I would prefer a 87.  The only
major change I know is the tranny, and the 87 unit is more user
friendly.  I am sure someone at this site can give you the details.
