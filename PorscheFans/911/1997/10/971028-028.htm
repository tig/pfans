I installed an Optima battery in my SC today. I had held off doing so
because all of the installation kits I saw required drilling holes.
AJ-USA in San Diego has a neat kit, which does not require drilling
holes. It is an aluminum plate (@ one-eigth inch thick) which bolts to
the bottom of the battery. The aft end of it extends past the battery
about one-half inch, and is bent upward slightly to engage the rear
mounting bracket in the car. The forward end is about two inches longer
than the battery, and has a hole for the allen-head anchor bolt. All in
all, a very neat installation. 

The only additional item needed was a longer ground cable. The stock
cable was about three inches too short.
