I drove a stock 1986 Carrera all winter last year in Chicago and the car
had high performance tires.  It is NO PROBLEM whatsoever.  You could not
get it stuck if you tried.  However, you can slide quite a bit and in deep
snow (over 4 inches) the front air dam serves as a snow plow.

I found it a BLAST and a good way to practice driving technique (DO NOT
LIFT on an icy corner).  I know some drivers poo poo driving in the snow
and salt, but the cars are build so well they will still last for years and
years without problem.

Happy plowing!!!!
