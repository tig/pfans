My experience ... I paid $800 for a rear lid and wing, used in good
condition from a guy advertising in Panorama - and in the right color! But
he packed it so poorly that I needed to uncurl the 3 corners that got
rolled in shipment and then repaint, which added another $400. Still ...
it's not $2-3000. I was checking the ads all around for 4 months. Somebody
somewhere is removing theirs in favor of a sexy bilevel wing; you just have
to be lucky. Note that the lid from an RSA is different than the 964 coupe
lid, and that there are different hinges also.
