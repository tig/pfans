Mark,

I had the same problem you just described, however it happened after
getting a new starter. It turned out that one of the teeth on the ring
gear (the toothed gear on the flywheel) had bent. That means taking the
clutch off to get to the ring gear. While you are there you might
consider changing the clutch.
