is noticable only at low speeds and the vibration gets more rapid as
speed increases. It seems to disappear above 30 mph. I notice it most
while driving on a smooth road. It really sounds and feels as if I am
driving over road joints spaced about 10 ft apart.

Could this be a problem with a drive shaft? I tugged on the shaft last
night and it moves freely left & right between the wheel and the gear
box. Any suggestions?
