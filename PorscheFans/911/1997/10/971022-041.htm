The reason you broke the bolts is that the windshield must be removed to
properly replace the dash.  It is best to wait until you need a new
windshield, and then take care of the dash then.  I think you will be able
to install the old bolts in the dash using a good grade of epoxy glue. 
Don't install the bolts unless you plan to take out the windshield.
