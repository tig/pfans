The Pano article that discusses how to disconnect the upshift indicator
light is in the October '96 issue, pg. 44, authored by Allan Caldwell. He
notes that removal of the bulb is non-trivial. He suggests either
disconnecting the black wire from pin 11 at the DME or at pin 1 of the
6-pin connector at the tachometer. Getting nervous? He has diagrams and
further discussion in the article.
