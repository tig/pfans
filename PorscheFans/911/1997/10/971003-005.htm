Larry Roberts wrote:

>Has anyone been successful in installing an engine sound/heat insulation
>pad without dropping the engine?  If so how--and how  bad was it?

Larry, I fixed my sagging insulation by cutting a 3" wide strip of thin
plexiglass, and slipping it under the metal clips at the top of the
engine - I drilled the center one, and secured it with one screw!

the result looks great, holds the insulation up against the firewall, and
now I'll wait to replace it when I upgrade my clutch release bushing,
in the near future...

hope this helps!
