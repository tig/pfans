I recently had the opportunity to take my 3.2 Carrera to the Dyno for an
hour.  I wish I had made some baseline runs before any mods were made but I
didn't.  The engine is stock except for a B  & B header, heat exchanger and
muffler system.  Bottom line was 197 at the rear wheel.  This includes 4 hp
gain with the O2 disconnected and 3 HP by removal of the AC drive belt.
With the customary 17 percent in power loss to the rear wheel this
translates to around 230 hp peak and over 200 foot-pounds from 4800 to 6000
rpm.  I am happy with the performance of the B & B.  The exhaust  still
shakes like a wet dog at low rpm and I despise the oil line set up.
