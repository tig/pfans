No need to be adding anything to the gas pedal to make it taller, all you
need to do is pull up the carpet, remove the wodden board underneath and
adjust the pedals. Both the Gas and Brake are adjustable, and very easily I
might add. I adjusted mine in literally about five minutes. Adjustment is
done by spinning the actuating rod clockwise or counter clockwise,
(depending one which way you want to move teh pedal) after loosenign the
jam nuts. I recomend trying the pedal out first before re-tightening the
jam nits, just to save a littel time if a bit more adjustment is necessary.
