Andy,

If your '86 is like my '83SC, the front doors take 5 1/4" (bottom mount) 
and the rear deck takes
4 x 6M (metric).  Don't get the standard 4 x 6 size unless you enjoy 
hacking and drilling.  These sizes are direct fits with no alterations 
required.

I updated with Blaupunkt coaxials in both positions and while I won't win 
any "sound-off" competitions, I like the results.
