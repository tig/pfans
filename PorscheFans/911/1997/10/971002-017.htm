Thought I'd add my $.02 worth. I agree with "Dennie G. Bourbeau"
. When you are using your brakes hard enough that a heel
and toe is necessary you'll find its just right. I've driven 911's for 16 years
and never had a problem heel and toeing but you MUST use the ball of your foot
on the brake and your heel on the gas. It's not like some cars that let you
just roll the ball of your foot onto the gas as well. Some of these pedal
add-ons try and make it work this way but if you do it the Porsche way ( you
gotta love these idiosyncrasies or you have the wrong car ) you'll be just
fine. Remember these cars have been raced in stock form forever and I've never
heard Hans Stuck complain :-)
