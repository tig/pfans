Brake pads are cheap, transmission rebuilds are bloody expensive. I would
use the brakes. Another factor against downshift braking is that one day
you might just downshift one gear too many, turn the engine past 8500,
valve kiss piston, valve stem snap, lots of bad noises, lots of smoke, oily
bits fall in road, owner curse when he realise that he tried to save $80 on
brake pads and have an engine rebuild costing $thousands. Make your choice.
