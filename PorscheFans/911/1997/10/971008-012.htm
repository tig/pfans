On front mounted coolers.  Seems that utilizing a pair in the fenders,
cutting out the headlight buckets providing adequate cooling for street
with headlights in and probably more than adequate at the track with the
headlights out.  

I query the engineers on our list about frictional loss in longer lines
and whether this could increase wear on the oil pump.  Maybe that and
the simplicity factor are why many racers run -16 lines to one big
cooler in the center.
