	Regarding your rough running 84... Some things to check:

+ Air leaks in the injection system. Check all hose clamps around intake
manifold. I had a split vac hose behind the throttle body. It was about
3" in length and could only be seen with a mirror.

+ Check idle control valve. Shinny cylindrical part with two hoses in front
of throttle body. Might be stuck.

+ Fuel injector seals. Just did they on my car, also an 84.

+ Head temp sensor. Controls initial enrichmment sequence to DME brain

I would look around for vac leaks... Our cars are over 13 yrs old and
rubber tends to deteriorate with age. 

Hope this help!
