Chris,

Minor correction of what I think is a common misperception:

	>From a human factors standpoint, I would think the 
	>higher up [further from the hinge] on the gas pedal you 
	>could press, the more "feel" you would have. 

While it's true that you need to hit the gas pedal enough distance
away from the hinge to be able to move it easily, "feel" is not
the issue here.  The stab at the gas during heel-toe (actually two
stabs if you're double clutching at really doing it right) is a ballistic
action.  The amount of reving you get is a function of how LONG you
press on the throttle, not how far down you press on it.  So it 
becomes a rhythm issue.

/hank
