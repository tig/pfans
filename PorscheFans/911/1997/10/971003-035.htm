Hi John:

Redline Gear Lubes in GL-4 work OK in the Porsche Gearboxes. Porsche
does recommend a GL-5 due to the extreme Hypoid angle on the Ring &
Pinion. Ring and Pinion wear is a problem, expecially for 915 and G50
transmisions. Being very expensive parts to buy and install, we would
strongly recommend a GL-5 Transmission Lube. We've had very good luck
with Swepco, Mobil 1 synthetic, and some others.

Users of Redline Gear Lubes in Porsche Transmissions should refer to the
factory data on which grade of gear oil to use. Redline's literature
makes no reference to which lubricants meet GL-5. Call them at (707)
745-6100 to find out which product meets this specification.

Remember how expensive that transmission is and who pays the bills!!

Redline MTL is   75W/80W GL-4
Redline MT-90 is 75W/90W GL-4 (Not a straight weight oil)
