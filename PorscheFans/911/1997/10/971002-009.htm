I just installed a Momo Competition wheel in my '87. It's a vast improvement
over the factory wheel. It
is slightly smaller in diameter than the factory wheel and therefore somewhat
obscures the speedometer. I
believe it's a 350mm wheel. Personally, I would not want it any smaller. If you
just follow the directions
you should have no trouble, *except* for possibly getting the hub nut off and
on. You need a shallow socket,
(it's big... 26 or 27 mm I think, but I'll double check), and a long extension
for your ratchet. On my factory
setup, a deep socket would NOT work due to the shape of the factory wheel at
the attach point. Just pull
off your horn button to see what I mean.
