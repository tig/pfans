The simplest test is to check the voltage at your battery terminals with
the car off.  Then start the car and check it again, have someone
throttle the gas a little to see what sort of differential you get
between idle and 3-4K RPMS.  You have a 12 volt battery so when the car
is off the voltage should be around 11.5 - 12.5 VDC.  With the car
running voltage should be in the 13.5 VDC neighborhood (You might get
someone else to confirm this since I am not positive on the number
particular to your car, but I believe it is a good estimate).  Good
luck.
