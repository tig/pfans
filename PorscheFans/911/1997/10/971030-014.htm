Yes, there is a compressor which plugs into your cigarette lighter and
allows you to fill your spare.  Don't leave home without it.  Mine had a
broken switch (stuck in on position) and was not putting out much air
pressure so I went down to the dealer expecting to pay a fortune for a
replacement but GOOD NEWS...  it only cost about $50.  I expected that
much of a premium just for the P crest!

Anyhow, since the size of the lighter plug is a little larger on the 911
than on US cars, in addition to getting a real P part, you also get one
that seats well in the lighter plug and won't come out as you stretch
the cord to inflate the spare while on the side of the road, at night,
in a downpour (There is some law that assures us is the only time you'll
get a flat)
