  Hi Warren ....
 Zymol is a high end wax and will indeed make your 911 fly like old
Chitty herself.  Like any good wax properly applied, you can expect an
immediate improvement in speed.  Your fuel mileage will, of course, see
a like improvement.  The drivers 'grin' factor, from having such a
beautiful car, will be worth the cost.  There is a downside to applying
this finish to your car that you should be aware of.  Tire wear can be
expected to increase in direct proportion to the cost of the wax.  Use
of a quality wax is a sure way to increase performance and enjoyment, 
two of many reasons I own a Porsche.
