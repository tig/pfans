According to my '97 manual, the LED will double-flash in the first 10 
seconds, as the system does a diagnostic.  If everything is OK, then the 
LED will revert to slow flashes (about 1 sec/flash).  If everything isn't 
OK, like the time I left my trunk ajar, then after 10 seconds the LED stays 
in double-flash mode, and alarm isn't armed.
