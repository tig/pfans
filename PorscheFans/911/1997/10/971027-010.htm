Just replaced the dash on my 80 SC.  It was relatively simple.  You have
to remove the glass.  Ther are some screws holding down the vinyl across
the front (near the seal).  Get a 10 mm socket and some long extensions,
along with a universal.  There are 5 bolts across the face of the dash,
and 2 just behind the tach and above (from the top).  It took me about 4
hours start to finish.  Get some dash clips from Porsche before you
re-install.
