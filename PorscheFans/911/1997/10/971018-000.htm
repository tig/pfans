This is in response to Glen's question about whether the insulation pad
is "necessary."  Of course it isn't, but it's desirable to have.  What's
undesirable is a pad that's disintegrating, which can, as he says, get
into the air intake.  You can scrape off the loose stuff and wait till
you have to drop the engine, but I'd start by getting the new pad and
being ready for the job.  Then you can find an excuse to do it ahead of
schedule.  In my case, I figured the clutch disk was close to being
worn, so that was my excuse.
