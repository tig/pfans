Robert,

The tumbler mechanism is hard to replace because of the steering wheel
interlock.  Two (or three?) screws hold the electrical switch part of the
ignition switch to the back of the tumbler mechanism.  You can see them if
you look up under the dash.  Remove the wires, remove the screws, replace
the electrical switch, replace the screws, replace the wires and you are
back in business.  That is the good news.......The bad news is you need a
size verrrrrry small hand to get the screws in and out.  My big ham fists
wouldn't fit so I had to cajole my bride of 37 years into doing the job.  It
took her all of 10 minutes.  That is the good news.........The bad news is
it cost me a dinner, a night on the town and a new outfit to convince her
she should waste her time with "that old thing in the garage that you are
always twiddling with...".
