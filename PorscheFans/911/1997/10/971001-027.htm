Stan-
	Your oil level guage is only accurate when the car is fully warm, and idling
on a level surface. When cold, or running at higher RPM, the level will read
low, this is normal. I'm sure you will get lots of responses telling you to
only use the dipstick, or only the guage, but it never hurts to check both.
The oil pressure rule is 10-15 psi per thousand RPM. 
	I'll defer to other turbo owners on oil choice, and I don't know what that
noise could be. Good luck.
