I've been complaining for months about how difficult it
it to toe and heel in my 911, and I *finally* heard about
the adjustment - its real simple, just look at it!  
The brake pedal can be adjusted just by turning the
turnbuckle!  
It seems true that the pedal should be higher for track
use, where you'll really mash the pedal, but I found that
even SLIGHTLY lower than the clutch makes t'n'h MUCH
easier for normal driving...
