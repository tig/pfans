Vincent,

I have a '78 911SC running basically the same tire sizes as you. I would
use 25 psi front, 28 psi rear. I would only adjust up from there. In other
words, if your car is understeering, put 1 psi more air in the rear. I
doubt very much that it will have an oversteer problem...
