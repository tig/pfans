> in the garage and inside the car.  Does anyone know what this could
> be?  I used the airconditioning the entire trip - could it be from

Randall:

The smell is from your catalytic converter... Some brands of
gasoline are made from high-sulfur-content crude oil; when they're
run through the catalytic converter, hydrogen sulfide ("rotten egg
gas") is created.

Now that you've returned home from your trip, the smell will probably 
fade away as you fill the tank with your usual brand of gasoline.
