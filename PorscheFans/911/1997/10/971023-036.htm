Michael, from your description of you hearing a "click" then a hum, 
the solenoid  is OK.  If you heard nothing, then it is likely the solenoid,
especially if the car starts fine when cold or after sitting for over an hour
but refuses to turn over during a hot start.

Suspect something in the motor; it might be something as minor as brushes,
if car starts OK cold.

To remove the starter/solenoid unit, you need a long 10mm allen wrench or
allen
socket.
