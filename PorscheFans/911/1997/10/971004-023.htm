Thanks to everyone who responded to my recent request for info on how to
improve the position of the gas pedal in my Carrera.  Some suggested
attaching a second porsche pedal to raise the pedal level, some suggested
using a pedal-shaped block of wood, some said the Third Foot, though
expensive, was the way to go.  Well, I finally actually looked at the
pedal, discovered that the throttle linkage that attaches to the pedal is
adjustable, and adjusted it.  I was able to bring the pedal rearward about
3/8 of an inch.  I can now brake with the left half of my right foot, and
blip the throttle with the right half.  It works great!  Thanks to everyone
for the input.  Cheers!
