In 1978 Porsche built cars for USA or ROW (Rest of World). 
1975-1979 VIN decoding:
Example 91183104891
911 = Model
8 = Last Digit of Model Year (1978)
3 = Country (3=ROW,   2=USA)
1 = Targa (0 = Coupe)
The remaining digits are the successive production #.
