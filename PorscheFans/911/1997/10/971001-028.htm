Chris,

Before you go out and spend the $$$, make sure that you really need the
pedal.  I initially had problems heel and toeing on the street, but once
I got to the track with the increased speed and braking effort, I found
that it only took a track day do get it down.  Several people I talked
to recommended against the pedal due to the fact it builds bad habits
early that are hard to correct later.  Also, remember you can adjust
your pedals if need be.
