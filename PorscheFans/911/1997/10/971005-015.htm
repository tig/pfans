What I found when I did this to my '84 Carrera:

1) much easier to adjust valves
2) slightly louder (more throaty) exhaust sound
3) probably reduced heat buildup in left rear of engine, saving exhaust
guides
4) test pipe is lighter than cat
5) don't have to worry about getting stranded somewhere due to cat getting
clogged (happened to a friend of mine - couldn't even get car up driveway, it
clogged the exhaust so bad)

As far as performance, in timed measured runs in third gear from 3000 - 6000
RPM acceleration, can't say I found an improvement.
