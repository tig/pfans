	 I have also experienced the centers of the rears wearing out much before the 
edges ( have not we all?). My Mechanic suggested running the rear tire 
pressure at around 44 psi. This has apparently improved the situation, but of 
course I have no control group in this one car experiment. His reasoning was 
that the sidewalls on the wide tires will be stiffened and thus the tire will
