Scott:
I just had this problem on my '79.  There is a bearing assembly behind the
steering wheel whose plastic center breaks.  The part costs about $20 at
the dealer.  After you remove the steering wheel ( held on by a
significantly-tight 27mm nut ), the hardest part is pulling the old bearing
out, then you just push the new one in and put the wheel back on, being
sure to tighten it down again!
