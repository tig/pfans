Merlin,

We just got back from the historic races and are thinking of heading
back for the GTs. The Laguna Seca site has information about times, etc.
but if you like I can give you some info on the track, viewing areas,
camping, etc.

All in all a great place to watch racing and I highly reccommend camping
as you get to experience so much more than just coming out for the day
without the hassle of parking, traffic, and other stuff. If you aren't
into camping you can ride the bus for free if you show your ticket. They
leave every hour on the hour (depending on traffic) and go to Salinas
and Monterey. It lets you off near the entrance (west of turn 5 or 6 I
think). 

This is the first time for these cars here and should be lots of fun! Be
sure to get tickets that include the paddock as it is where a lot of the
excitement is prior to the races.
