Todd:

Here's a few pieces of info:

Years of manufacture: [rare '65?], '66-69, '76

Range of 912s: '66-'69 Coupe, '67-'69 Targa (soft window and hard
window), '76 912E Coupe and Sunroof Coupe

Changes of body style: Essentially a 911 body, interior is close to a
base model 911 (i.e. 911T); '66-'68 early A model has shorter wheelbase;
'69 has slightly lengthened wheeelbase via longer trailing arms; '76 has
5mph bumpers, shorter trunk

Engine sizes: '66-'69 1600cc flat four with Solex carbs (origins from
356), 4 or 5 speed transmission from 911; '76 2000cc flat four with fuel
injection (origins from 914)

Spec changes: Significant changes include dual circuit brakes in '68,
larger generator '68-'69, smog equipment '68-'69, redesigned running
lights in '69, revised ventilation system '69, slightly flared fenders
for wider wheel/tire combination '69
