Hi All,
   Pagid Orange pads are great pads.  I hate to see a great 
product get a bad "rap" because people simply do not understand 
how to use it properly.
   Pagid orange pads, like many other really good high 
performance pads, MUST be bedded in properly before they will 
work.  The manufacturer, the vendor, or your tuner should be 
able to tell you how.  If not, shop elsewhere because it must be 
done properly or your expensive new pads will be worse than the 
cheapest street pad.
