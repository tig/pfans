Has anyone found a problem with Pagid brake pads depisiting their material
on the brake disc? I instructed a student at PCA's Zone I Watkins Glen
event in early July, and the car became progressively less and less
drivable due to increasing brake "judder". The owner (another instructor: I
was teaching his son) pointed to the build up of brake pad material on the
rotors as the cause.

Seems to me this would severly compromise their usefulness. I wouldn't want
to have to turn the rotors after every event in order to get drivable
brakes back.
