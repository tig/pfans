Hi Nill:
There is a spray paint for vinyl with the brand name Marhyde (sp ?).  I
know it sounds crazy, "what? paint my seats".  This stuff is pretty neat
and can definately make your vinyl look better.  You can blend it out or
paint an entire panel.  It dries very fast so you can see the results
right away.  If you are concerned that it might not look right, try it
on one of the back seats or a piece of scrap vinyl.  It will not rub off
on clothing and lasts a long time. 
  They also make other neat paints such as bumper black for restoring
the large rubber bumper guards, and cast iron paint for brake calipers. 
Have fun and hope these work for you.   Doug Dutton
