Another thing you might look at is the roundness of the filler neck.  
This may be more of a problem on the 924, 944, 968 series but it isn't 
common to find the filler neck out of round from the weight of the gas 
nozzle during fill up.  On the 911, it is more vertical thus leaving less 
room for the weight of the nozzle, your hand and any pressure being 
exerted on it to bend it out of round but may be worth a try.  If it is 
out of round, a pair of big pliers is all that is needed to give it a 
slight adjustmnent.
