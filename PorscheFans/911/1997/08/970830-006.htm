You did not state if the car had been repainted.  If so, was it due to
collision damage.  Rust bubbles under the paint are a come from two
sources:  1) panel perfroration (rust is comming through from the back),
or; 2) improper surface prep during a paint job.  In either case, you are
likely to see more rust appear over time.  A rusty Porsche is not worth
much.  The sort of rust you  are describing does not sound minor, since
more is on the way.  I would pass on the car unless you can convince
yourself that the spots are isolated and not are early symptom of a rust
bloom all over the car.  It they are isolated spots, I would get them
treated and recoated soon.  Corrosion under the paint film tends to spread
rapidly.
