I have an '85 Targa, black. The top, I find, when folded seems to wear over
time at the points where the top is folded. The material seems to deteriorate
where the stress of the fold occurs. I've found that whenever possible, I
leave the top at home in the garage on the pool table--unfolded, just the way
it comes off the car. This seems to be the best bet for wear-tho not very
practical when rain is a possibility. Other than that, I stow it away the way
you do-tho sometimes I put it in the back seat-a fact my standard poodle
Duffy resents the next time I take him out for a walk. I treat the top quite
often with a dressing to help keep it supple-I use a product called black
chrome-It works fine.If your top needs fixin' , I can suggest Dan Petchel in
Bordentown, NJ 609-298-7040. He advertises in Panorama and I've had him do
Targa top work in the past, with good results.
