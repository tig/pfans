My SC did a similar thing.  Turned out to be an oil leak from the thermostat
O-ring on the top of the engine leaking into the bell housing where the
clutch is.  When I took the clutch apart, it was soaked with oil.  I didn't
realize there was an oil leak until it got real bad and was leaking onto the
heat exchanger & making lots of smoke.
