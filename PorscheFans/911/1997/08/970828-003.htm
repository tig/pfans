I don't know about mods on the g50 but many '87s mine included have a
service bulletin on the clutch having to do with a shaft and sleeve related
to the throwout bearing. An older fix required modifying the bell housing of
the tranny but a newer kit is available which avoids this. If your mechanic
will let you buy parts you can save a considerable sum by buying from
vendors in Panorama or Excellence.
