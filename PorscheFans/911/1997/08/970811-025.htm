Chris -

I recently replaced the master cylinder on my '87 Carrera.  It wasn't too hard.
The Haynes manual actually was pretty good.  I learned that you do have to
unbolt the booster (thought I could avoid it, but not enough clearance at the
firewall).  I took a lot of extra time to remove duct tubes and hoses
to gain access, and recommend this as time well spent.  Remember that brake
fluid is very bad for paint.  A single drop spilled is big trouble.  And
as usual, patience pays...
