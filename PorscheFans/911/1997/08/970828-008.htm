#1 is normal. That gauge should only be observed when the car is warmed
up and sitting still on a level surface. Even then, don't trust it - the
dipstick is the only reliable measurement, to be checked with engine
running in the same conditions as above (especially sitting still!).

#2 could be do to a thorough brake bleeding/flushing. Try disconnecting
the battery for 30 seconds - 1 minute and see if it goes out. If so, you
should be fine.
