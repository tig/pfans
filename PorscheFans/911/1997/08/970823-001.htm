Unfortunately you've been subjected to the antiquated 911 ventilation
system a.k.a. VW Beetle).  You can only get fresh air coming through the
windshield area and the foot wells.  What is normally suggested is to turn
the A/C fan on to what ever speed you like, and just turn down the A/C
temperature to the first setting.  This is also suggested in the cold
months where you can blend in warm engine air with a little A/C fan air if
you don't have climate control.
