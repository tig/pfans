Paul Springer  wrote:

> Andrew Warren advise on chip repair was excellent, except I would
> not recommend the "Rust Avenger" pen for primer.  I have used the
> product, and had rust appear under the paint.  I have located a
> laboratory test report, which evaluated steel paint test panels
> treated with Rust Avenger and identical (rusty, but wire brushed
> clean) panels not treated.  The Rust Avenger panels rusted much more
> quickly in the salt fog test.  If you have rust in the chip, remove
> it, and use a good primer.  If you do not have rust in the chip, it
> is likely the factory primer is still sound, and you do not need to
> prime.  In any case, Rust Avenger is a water-based latex product,
> and should not be used on car bodies.

Paul:
