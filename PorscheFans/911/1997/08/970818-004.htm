The cloudiness results from a reaction between the cleaner and the anodized
aluminum. I don't think most Fuchs alloys were clear-coated, just anodized.

The commonest cause of the "cloudy" look is the use of the wrong type of
wheel cleaner. Be very careful what you use to clean the wheels. I'm not
sure of the chemistry issues, but the P21S wheel cleaner is safe for alloys.

Refinishing the wheels is the only way to fix the cloudiness. That's the
bad news. The good news is that is gives you the chance to customize them,
if you want. Full polish, body colored centers, you name it. Be sure to
have them clear-coated, though, to give you a chance to preserve the shine.
