Problem: The front A/C blower near battery doesn't work

If the motor is toast, just replace the motor.  The motor is a Bosch 
part which any Bosch dealer can get. (The part number is the longest 
number on the motor.) A local foreign parts place ordered one for
me, cost: under $25.
 
 The only tricky part is removing the "squirrel cage" fan from the 
shaft. I friend of mine figured out how to get it off without bending 
the delicate fan. He took off the "C" shaped clip that holds the motor 
shaft from moving in and out. Then he drove the shaft through the fan 
instead of trying to pry the fan off the shaft. (Of course you must
loosen that little screw that holds the fan on the shaft before you 
do any of this.)
