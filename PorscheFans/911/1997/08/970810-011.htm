I liked the feel of the new SS brake lines when I installed them. Because
they offer less expansion than the stock lines they have a more positivie
hydraulic feel. Be sure you get high quality size 3 lines to keep the flow vs
pressure correct in the hydraulic system. Troutman and AJRacing in California
have the correct lines.
