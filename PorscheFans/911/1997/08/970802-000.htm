Mike,

I recently replaced my stock Boges ('85 Carrera).  I put in Koni
Sports to be more competitive in autocross.  The old shocks didn't
exhibit any of the normal "old shock" bouncing, and in fact the 
car's ride seemed OK.  It was a bit of a handful in competition, 
and I had a bit of bump-steer feedback on the highway.

Putting in the Konis transformed the car.  The ride improved,
the bump steer went away, and the car is much more predictable
at speed.  I concluded that the Boges were toast, but I couldn't
really tell.  

Another data point...
