Hi Scott,

The bouncing oil gauge is par for the course.  The sender in
the oil tank looks like one of those float arms in a tank toilet, and
because the oils is rushing through the tank, sloshing around, etc., the
float on the sender is bouncing around.  It'll even bounce at idle, since
the oil is still pouring through, but it ought to be steadier.  It drops
when accelerating as the oil in the tank sloshes to the rear.

Don't worry about it.
