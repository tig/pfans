I don't think any oil-based solvent will hurt the finish on any
wheels.  Some use WD40, but it's expensive solvent.  Mineral spirits
or Vaseline ought to work, combined with elbow grease and a mild
abrasive, like a Tuffy pad or scrubber sponge.  

In fact, Vaseline is an old home remedy for white spots on clearcoated
alloys.
