The 1987 Porsche Tech Spec book quotes oil consumption at 1 liter per 1,000
km.
Mine uses about a third of that, and has 67 Kmiles.  I use 20w50 oil.

My 87 coupe uses about that amount.  I do however have a few drips from the
oil return tubes (when standing for a while) and a slight leak from the
right hand side cam seal.
I'm using 5w-50 Castrol Syntec Synthetic oil which might contribute a
little more to the leaking process.
