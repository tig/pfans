Hello,

I'm interested in purchasing a 1998 993 via European Delivery. I was
hoping someone who has actually done this can shed some light on the
process, and whether it's cost efficient or not.

I picked up my 96 BMW M3 last year in Munich through Euro Delivery, and
really enjoyed it. The process was very simple, and straight forward. I
took a month off, and drove around Europe with it, and really enjoyed
myself; and at the same time saved about 10% or so off the price of the
car. I would really recommend it to anyone...

Is the Porsche program similar? I've already talked to my local dealer
about it, and although he doesn't seem to have a lot of experience with
it, he has confirmed that EuroDel is available on the 98 993 from
September to December 1997.

Any comments or first hand experience would really be appreciated.

Thanks,

Alan Kamyab
