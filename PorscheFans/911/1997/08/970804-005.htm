The gas pedal is attached to the actuating rod with a ball and 
socket. The socket is on the back of the pedal. To remove, hold the 
rod and bend back the gas pedal and the ball will pop out. To 
reinstall, grasp the god with pliers (vise grip works well) and press 
the pedal onto the ball.  Takes about 15 seconds to remove or 
install. You need to remove it to get to the screw holding the floor 
board.
