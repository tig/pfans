August 15,1996 from Mark.J.Salvetti@usevs.mail.abb.com


I just replaced the plastic bearing collar at the top of my steering
column
with the 928 part Bruce A, recommended here and in Excellence (part
928.347.739.02).  It works great.

This repair looks like it's good for life.  The 928 part is metal, not
plastic
like my original broken part.  It's about an inch long bushing, the top
edge of
which is flanged to hold it against the bearing and keep it from falling
down
the column.  The part is slightly oversize, but it has a narrow slot cut
down
it's length so when you install it, it compresses slightly so it stays
in place.

I put some wheel bearing grease on it, and then easily pushed it into
place
using just finger presssure.

The part only cost me $4.86 at the local dealer.

Thanks Bruce!
