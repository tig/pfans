I have used them with no difficulties for a couple of years. My only 
nit is the squeal they make when they're cold. Break them in properly 
and they make less dust than stock OEM pads.  They come with 
instructions, follow them.  The rotors should be polished before 
installing new pads. Note, polished, doesn't mean they have to be 
turned on a lathe. Also possible is that you got a set of fakes. I've 
heard of this a couple of times.
