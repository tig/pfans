> A couple questions for you more experienced pcar pilots:
> To follow up on the oil tank level question-my tank level goes to the
> bottom of the red field while driving despite the level at temp stick
> reading being ok.  Should this be ignored ?

James,

This may be ignored. Nest time you're driving along at 70 mph and
everythings warmed up - put the car in neutral and coast for 30 seconds
or so. You will see the tank level gauge slowly creep up.

The level goes low because the oil is being pumped through your engine,
oil coller and filter and spends very little time in the tank. Just keep
checking the dip stick at normal operating tempertures.
