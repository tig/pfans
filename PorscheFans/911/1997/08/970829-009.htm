The never ending oil debate.  I use Castrol Syntec (Full Synthetic 5w-50)
and I'm really happy with it.  I previously used Castrol GTX 10W-40 and
found that the magnet on the drain plugs (when I changed my oil -
REGULARLY) had some tiny file like shavings on them.  These were very small
in quantity and not indicative of a major problem with the engine
internals.  Since I switched to Syntec (full synthetic) there never is any
build up of these things on the magnets...noticed that observation right
away.  And since I've gone synthetic, I've been doing track events and
longer drives with the club that I never did before.

Go for the synthetic that you bought (even if its partial synthetic) it's
still better oil than regular/conventional petroleum based stuff.
