I've installed Pagid RS4-4 (orange) pads on my '87 Carrera.  They are
fantastic!  Excellent stopping power, very rotor friendly, no sign of
any deposits on the rotors.  They squeak occasionally under light
braking in city traffic.

My car is not a daily driver.  I use it for fun and Autox, so nearly
all of the driving is agressive.  I "broke in" the pads with a half
dozen progressively more abrupt stops from very high speed.  My rotors
are new, and all wear seems uniform.  I cleaned and replaced seals in
calipers when I installed the pads.  I also replaced a faulty master cylinder
at that time.

Just another data point...
