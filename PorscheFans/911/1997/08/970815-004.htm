Scott & Charles,
	I just got off the Chevron Web page and found out some interesting
details.  Ultra lube iso 220 does not meet API-GL5, this is the spec. in
my 911 spec. book.  I then called Chevron and the tech mentioned they
recommended DELO SAE 80W-90 or Artic Lube 75W-90.  He said the Artic is
thinner so it would help shifting more than the DELO in the tranny.  He
aslo stated since the Ultra lube did not meet the API or Mil spec
chevron would not recommend it for use in a trans-axle set up.  I am
going to purchase a 5 gallon can of the Artic Lube(only way they will
sell it here in Bozeman) and I will let you know how it turns out.
