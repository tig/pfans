I found out the hard way that my '86 911 with a windshield antenna had a
booster already installed at the factory.

If your C2/4 has a windshield antenna, make sure it is working properly or
that someone who changed the radio failed to wire up the power lead to the
booster as it's not marked on the radio schematic label or the wiring diagram
in the Haynes manual. 

As I recall, it was a black lead from the booster, which is not what I
associated with a power wire from my experience with American, British &
Japanese cars.

I brought 2 radios back before I ran into a installer who told me about the
booster. 

This was pre-Porschefans, of course.
