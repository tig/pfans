At 7:10 PM +0800 8/22/97, Andy wrote:
>I have an 86 911 Carrera Targa with factory air conditioning.  I heard a rumor
>that you need to remove the AC to get access to the valves for adjusting....is
>this true?  Are there any special tricks to adjusting the valves in a 911
>(i've
>done them in a 914).
>

Actually you don't need to remove the AC.  You do need to remove the AC
compressor from its base and set the unit out of the way (typically on the
fender resting on a towel).  None of the AC lines need to be disconnected.
The only trick in adjusting the valves is to have lots of patience (and
don't forget to drain the oil first!).  Have fun :)
