Gene Holliker 930,
    The F 22mm, R 20mm sway bars for the USA 930's were reintroduced
with
the Turbo's in 1986. Originally the 930's came with 20mm Front and 18mm
Rear Sway bars. This is a very desireable upgrade but I would recommend
staying with the factory rubber bushings (they never squeak and don't
need lubricating) The P-Bushings are not expensive and you should take
the care to examine the SBar mounts to make sure they do not have be
reinforced before reinstalling new bushings.
