Regarding the suggestion to strip off the vinyl and paint the Targa top to
match body color, we seem to have a misunderstanding with reference
to how the 911 Targa top works.
A 911 Targa top is not a rigid panel such as is on the 914.  It is
essentially a removable convertible top that folds up sideways instead of
front-to-back.  If you strip off the vinyl, you have nothing left but a
collapsable aluminum frame covered with the cloth headliner.
The only exceptions to this are a few very early ('67?) 911 Targas that
supposedly did have a rigid panel, and of course the new retractable
'moon roof' style panel introduced this year.
