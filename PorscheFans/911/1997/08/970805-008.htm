The 'classic' Carrera problem.  I had both mounts go on me (not at the same
time but a few years after the first).  I just got both welded by a
competent welder (ask around and see who does roll-cage fabrication etc.
and get them to weld in a little supporting bracket).  I like the reweld
idea better since you dont have to be 'surgically invasive' and cut into
the car's underpan with an air-chissel to get the old plate out.

I haven't had any problems since the brackets have been rewelded, and I
have begun doing track events and driving a little more spiritedly.
