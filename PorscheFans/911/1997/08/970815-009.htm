Additionally, I do not know the reason you are seeking specifically a 912,
however, if price is the issue, you need to be aware that parts are getting
incredibly difficult to find for the 65-69 912s.  I know cuz I am constantly
looking for parts for the 68.  an example of this are tail lights.  One tail
light will generally cost around $500, because they aren't made any more and
suppplies are getting low.  Despite this, the 912 handles much like a 911,
some say better because of its lighter weight and better front/rear balance. 
I'd say our 68  912 handles pretty much the same as our 80 911SC.  Certainly
the 911 has more power.  Don't mistake me here, the 912 has enough power to
get you into your share of speeding tickets, but it is less than a 911.  Our
912 gets about the same mileage as the 911 does, although , we drive the 912 a
bit harder due to its less power, we attempt to make up for it with rpms.  
In the early years, there were a LOT of 912s made, and I personally have 4
friends that all own 68s, and love them.  Ours is a four speed, whereas, the
other ones I know of are all five speeds.
