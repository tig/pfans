For autocrosses, you want the Parade rule book, although each region is free
to make up their own rules for their series.  Most use the Parade rules as a
guide though.

The Club Racing rules are only used for wheel to wheel racing in PCA, not
autocrosses.

Both are available from the PCA national office; the number is in the back of
Pano.  I think there is a nominal cahrge for the Parade rule book.  Club Race
rules are free.  This assumes you are a PCA member.
