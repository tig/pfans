An update on the recent problems with the '68 911. It wasn't, as several
of you pointed out, a problem with the Techron. Dave Cooley diagnosed
the problem correctly: bad gas. We've been having a lot of flooding from
monsoon rains in Colorado lately, and apparently some found its way into
the tank of the service station I filled up at. Drained the tank,
cleaned the floats, refilled with good petrol, and everything is back to
normal. Even the weather.
Thanks to all for the responses.
