Lots of correct replies to why bottle nitrogen is different from average
compressed air but perhaps its the practical aspect you were questioning???

Running dry nitrogen will offer you much more stable / constant tire
pressure. In fact you can expect a 40-50% improvement. Therefore instead of a
10 - 12 psi rise from cold to full hot (as we typically see running out here
in the summer heat at Willow Springs) you may only get a significantly less 6
psi rise.  The benefits are many and the cost is low! Expect about $125 for
the initial bottle, valve and regulator with fill-ups costing $10.00.
