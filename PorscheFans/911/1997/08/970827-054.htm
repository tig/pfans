Sounds like you may need a new steering rack (rebuilts are cheaper). Install
the Turbo tie rod ends when you have it all apart. If your car is lowered,
now is also a good time to make sure that the spacers are installed under the
rack to preclude bump steer.

My '86 developed a big dead spot when traveling straight ahead, scary.
Nothing unusual happened that would have caused it. The new rack cured it.
