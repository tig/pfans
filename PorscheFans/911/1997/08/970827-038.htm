Scott-
I'm also an ex-944, current-911 owner, and this scared the hell out of
me at first..  The level does fluctuate and this is normal.  Here is
my amateur description:   As revs increase, more oil is being sprayed
into the engine, and the tank (sump?)  level falls.  As a rule, trust
the dipstick more than the guage, and only expect meaningful readings
from the guage  when the car is on level ground, warmed up fully, and
idling.  Mine is fairly accurate under these conditions.
