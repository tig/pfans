Gregg, if the 993 guages are at all like the other 911 guages, the painted
ring must first be removed then the glass will lift off the guage and you
can easily clean the glass. However, you will probably destroy the painted
rings trying to get them off. This is best done by a speedometer repair
shop like North Hollywood Speedometer but then you are paying an arm and a
leg, plus you must remove the steering wheel and column covers to get the
tach out of the dash.

Now, maybe you can live with whatever little smudge is driving you crazy.
