tensioners, do I need to lift the engine out, or can I do this with the =

You should probably check the valve timing while you are this far into the
engine.  There is no way you will change the timing without trying.  You
can replace the tensioners without jacking the engine.  I don't recall
having any problem doing mine, but a bad memory is necessary if you are to
keep your optimism about fixing your own Porsche.  Good luck.
