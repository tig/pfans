Hi Dean:

  As you well know, the CIS is very fussy about camshafts, not to 
mention the limitations imposed by the CIS-type pistons.
 We have had good luck with the 69-71 911 T cams in those 2.7 CIS 
motors. It is slightly more aggressive without having too much overlap 
that can send the sensor plate into convulsions. 
 The 2.7's seem to pull harder with those cams over the stock 2.7 S cam 
without any penalty below 3500 rpm.
