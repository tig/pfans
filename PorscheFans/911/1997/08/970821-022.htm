>>Major improvement:

>I'm curious Mark.  When I go home I'll look for this splitter but I'm
>surprized that such a simple change could dramatically improve the AC and
>yet was not done at the factory.
>
>What is the downside of this change?
>

There is no downside. The deflector under the dash on the '87 is a little
plastic piece held on by two hex-head screws. Take it off, put the
deflector and screws in a baggie and put the bag in the box in your garage
marked "911 parts". The open vent hole will blast cold air out into the
cabin and greatly facilitate the overall cooling.
