Many thanks to Jeff for pointing me to the Panorama issue which =
describes what I [and others] have been seeing. I checked the wiring =
diagram in my Haynes manual and, sure enuff, it shows a connection =
between the sensor side of the oil pressure warning light and a solid =
state module which drives the brake warning lights. I can't tell much =
about the ss module but, apparently, when the oil pressure switch  =
closes, it's meant to turn on the e-brake warning light, as well. I also =
note that this combo comes on when the ignition is first turned on, =
before starting.=20

Given the fact that this only occurs when the oil is at full operating =
temperature, and that the oil pressure gauge indicates normal pressure =
when the warning lights flicker, I've decided to trust the pressure =
gauge until I can get at the pressure switch & replace it.
