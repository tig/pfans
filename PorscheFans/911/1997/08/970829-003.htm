Re Edward Tyau's question about Optima battery in a 993, my
understanding is that all single battery 911s use the same size
battery.  The Optima battery requires an adapter tray--you bolt the
battery onto the tray, and then use the stock brackets to attach the
unit to your car.  This would be needed for any installation except
where there's an overhead mounting system.  Anyone who sells the
battery ought to have the adapter tray available.  The battery has the
shape, more or less, of a six-pack.
