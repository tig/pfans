Hi Bill,

I swapped the rear lid and motorized tail on my 964 C4 for an RS America
lid and tail. In the process I needed to convince the electronics that the
electrically operated tail, now gone, was up otherwise I'd have that
exclamation point and tail telltale illuminated in the gauges. Simple fix
was worked out by Brian Weathered at Midwest Eurosport (630.595.5577) and
involved stripping the wiring harness from the old lid, jumpering the pins
that sensed the tail position and plugging it back in near the firewall,
all folded over and taped to something solid. Stays there to this day.
