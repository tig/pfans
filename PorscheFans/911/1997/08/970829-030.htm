Edward,
    I have an Optima battery installed in my 1991 C2 964 and it
fits perfectly. I installed model #850S with 850 CA and highly recommend
them. On earlier 911's you need to use an adapter but
on the newer cars which are clamped on the side and use a battery size,
more or less, of 10"L X 6.5"W.
You do have to bend the angle of the negative clamp somewhat but
does not constitute a problem.
