There are several sources fore wheel straigfhtening.  One is wheel
collision center in Allentown PPA, another is Ye Olde Wheel Shoppe in
Glen Bernie MD.  The latter straightens wheels with a large hammer.

I have an affiliation with Sports Car Tire in Wilmington DE who is
the only shop authorized by Porsche to strighten wheels for dealers.  
The only such shop inthe country and is owned by a local club member.

They have a computerized machine that trues wheels to within .010",
similar to a large lathe.

If you are interested the price structure is as follows:

	15" wheels	$85
	16" wheels	$90
	17" wheels	$95
	18+" wheels	quoted individually.

This is for wheels which are bent but do not require welding.
Wheels which are torn or very badly damaged can be quoted individually.

This also does not include cosmetics.  Polishing, reanoodizing, machining, 
and repainting are also available.

Prices do NOT include return shipping, which is $8-17 per wheel
depending on desitination.
