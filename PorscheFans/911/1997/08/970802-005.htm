I don't believe that you will find that the Pro-Cooler does much to
increase the cooling capacity of your car's a/c. The idea is that if you
can cool the liquid in the drier the a/c will get colder but by the time
the liquid reaches the drier it is in fact a liquid and therefore about
as cool as it will get. The function of the condenser is to cool the
high pressure gas into a liquid state. Besides it takes six units of
cold to over come one of heat so the tank around the drier would have to
be the size of a small barrel to do much good.

I'm sure that simply recharging the a/c which is what you do after
installing this devise is when you most notice the improvement in
cooling.

Much ado about nothing !
