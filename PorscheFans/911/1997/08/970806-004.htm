>Pagid orange pads, like many other really good high 
>performance pads, MUST be bedded in properly before they will 
>work.  The manufacturer, the vendor, or your tuner should be 
>able to tell you how. 

Kevin at Racer's Group outlined the proper seasoning procedure
for brake pads -- it involves about half a dozen stops at
each of three stages. Install the pads, do about 6-10 mild
stops to get the pads warm. Then let them cool completely.
Then 6-10 medium stops, then let them cool completely.
Then, 6-10 panic stops, but don't let them lock up.
Then let them cool completely. See a pattern here?
COOL them off before proceeding to the next stage.
