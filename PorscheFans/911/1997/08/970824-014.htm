Congrats on your purchase. Check to see if you have the distributor cooling update kit 
was installed on the car. This will help prevent the drive belt from overheating and 
breaking. To check just look at your twin distributors there should be a a hollow tube 
attached to the bottom section to allow for air circulation.

If it has it great, if not it doesn't cost too much and will prevent a headache in the 
future.
