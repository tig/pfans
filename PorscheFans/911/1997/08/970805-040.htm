Hi Ken,
Yessir, those headlight trim rings can be real "character builders", to
use Dave Cooley's term. I wound up disassembling the headlight and trial
fitting the trim ring only. By rotating the rubber gasket, I was able to
find the point of best fit. Hook the top on first, and then press
down/back on the sides. They click into place with a distinct little
snap that is almost orgasmic.

It's probably not a good idea to use the screw to draw the ring in. The
screw is brass, and you may strip it, or worse, strip the threaded hole
in the mounting bracket inside the headlight bucket.
