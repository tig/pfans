Josh,

If your trombone "cooler" is hot than your external thermostat is opening.  
The thermostat you are referring to is just in front of your right rear 
wheel.  Take a peek under the fender and you will see it.  It is not 
connected in any way to your AC.  I would think turning your AC on would 
raise your oil temp. if anything.  If you are seeing 260 degrees it is time 
to pitch that trombone cooler and get a real one.  

Scott
