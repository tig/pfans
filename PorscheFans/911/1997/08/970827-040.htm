Denny Lindsay  wrote:

> What is the benefit if any or for that matter the reason for running
> Nitrogen N2 in tires instead of air ?

Denny:

The only reason is that air from your average trackside or 
gas-station compressor is likely to contain a significant amount of 
water vapor, which will make the cold-versus-hot tire pressures 
harder to predict.

ANY dry gas (well, any inert dry gas) would work just as well;
nitrogen's just the easiest to obtain.
