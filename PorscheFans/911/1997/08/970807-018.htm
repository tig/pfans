I have an '81 911SC with standard (non-power) seats.  I have a Kenwood =
CD changer under the driver's seat (I am tall enough to have the seat =
all the way back).  The Kenwood came with flat mounting brackets that =
attach the ones that bolt to the sides of the changer, so I attached =
those brackets to the floor pan with Silicone adhesive, then bolted the =
changer on to those brackets.  That way I didn't loose trunk space, and =
no holes were drilled in the car (a plus in my book).  The unit is tight =
with no skipping.
