with the projected flat top on the lens, but use a regular 9004 bulb that is
45Watts on low beam, and 65 W on high. On low, mine looked rather yellow (big
lites) and were pretty dim. 

I think it may be poor lense design, as the same bulbs on my '88 Olds are
entirely adequate. Replaced bulbs with 55/100W one which are much better.
