Yes, your symtoms are correct with a fuel tank developing a vacuum due
to insufficient venting. Therefore, the car can't get enough fuel and
you experienced a rough running engine. The black hoses and black box
was Porsche's attempt to provide some emmision control over the tank
vapors, venting, etc.  My hoses and box were missing when I got my 70
911T, so I had to plug the big vent on top of the tank.  ILO that vent,
I drilled two small hoses in the side of the gas cap for venting and to
prevent a vacuum build up.
