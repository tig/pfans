Does anybody know the correct Druck pressure at idle or highway speeds
(70-80 mph in 5 gear).
I have a 1984 Targa and I've never seen the Druck gauge read lower then 5
bars.  I'm begin to wonder if the oil pressure sensing unit is faulty.

Tony Saunders

I had my sending unit fail, and the needle stayed at mid position.  Finally
dawned on me when the engine stalled, and oil pressure stayed normal.  I
would think if the gage moved from zero to normal in a smooth fashion
during start-up, that would be a good indication that the system was
working OK.  Otherwise, stall the engine from idle (carefully), and watch
the gage.  It should drop smoothly to zero in 2 or 3 seconds after the
engine dies.

Hope this helps.
