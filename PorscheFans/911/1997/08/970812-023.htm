I had a tire guy tell me to turn the wheel so the glue is at the bottom. =
 Spray some Windex (ammonia cuts the glue) on it and let it sit a few =
minutes.  Then take a popsicle stick and "scrub" the glue off.  I tried =
this, and it seemed to work fine, although I did use a knife with a =
rounded point to get the bulk of the adhesive off first (no point makes =
it harder to gouge the wheels), and things look much better.  That's my =
story, hope it helps...
