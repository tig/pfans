Brad -

I just had the windshield on my '89 C4 replaced via Allstate insurance, who 
referred me to Windshields America, who's receipt says Vistar. The work was 
actually done by a local auto-glass shop, go figure.

My 911 has no marking aside from an A->S (I think) on the driver side edge 
center. I don't think it was replaced before. Tint is minimal. All other 
glass is Sekurit.

Make sure the glass shop knows to replace the gasket. When the repair van 
showed up at my house, he did some prying, then mentioned he did not have a 
replacement gasket. He left, ordered the gasked via the local P dealer. The 
next week, I brought the car to the shop, and he replaced glass and all. 
They even managed to replace my rearview mirror, inspection sticker and 
registration. All in all, the work was good.
