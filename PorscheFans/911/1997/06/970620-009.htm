Bruce Wm. Pigozzi wrote:
> 
> A little tip!
> For years now I've watched various folks use a piece of 2x4 to cushion their
> jacks as they raise their 944s and/or 911s. I too used this until I went
> with even lower profile tires; I found the 2x4 just a bit too big to get
> under my lowered 944 and searched around for a substitute.  I found a HOCKEY
> PUCK.  I now prefer it to the wood in virtually all circumstances.  It fits
> nicely on the jack and under the jack pads.  Give it a try.

And then...

Put a 6 mm short bolt in the hockey puck, so the bolt head protrudes.
This will fit nicely in the recess in the jack seat under the door. The
puck will then be less prone to slide. The puck is so soft  that a 5 mm
hole (please convert to your favourite measurement system, please!) will
be just about right. 

Happy jacking!
