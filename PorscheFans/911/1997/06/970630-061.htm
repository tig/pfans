Greetings,

Thank you all for your comments.  All is well with the car.

Dealer inspected  car and determined that the rough surface texture that is
applied to the lower end of the '97 dipstick is abraded slightly when
sliding through the dipstick tube, hence the particles on the stick when
wiping.  Several "trial" dips with the "noncoated '96 and earlier stick
(smooth lower end just like rest of stick) did not turn up any particles.
Checking other 97 993's in lot showed similar phenomena.  Moral of the
story: don't use your stick too often (yeah, I know - that's really bad).
