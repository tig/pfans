I'm looking for an alternative to the OEM (expensive) pads. I track 
about 7 times each summer and the porsche parts express doesn't seem 
to apply to pads for my C4S or 993 C2.  I don't need more stopping 
power, I'm happy with oem pads except for price.

I tried a set of Hawk Black racing pads. Worked well on the track but 
I have been told that like most racing pads they are rotor eaters if 
they are operated at low temps. In other words on track they are hot 
and won't eat the discs. Use them on the street and they run cool, 
need more pressure to stop and do eat the rotors.

The Hawke rep suggested that for my use the Hawke HP+ would be a good 
all round pad. Anyone tried these?
