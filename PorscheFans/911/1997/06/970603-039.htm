I painted mine and am only marginally satisfied with the results.  I
used a Rustoleum premium grade primer and enamel.  The paint seems to be
very resistant to chipping, but I am unhappy with the quality of the
finish.  I also spent far to much time preparing and painting. I had to
use a paint stripper to remove the old faded black paint.  It took
several applications, after which I had to wet sand. 

They look good from a distance, better than the old paint.  The price
was right, but in the end I got what I paid for.
