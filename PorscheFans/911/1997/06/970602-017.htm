I saw an excellent example of the difference between galvanized floors 
only (on the pre-'78 cars) and the post '78 fully galvanized bodies this 
past weekend at the Metro NY Bridgehampton Track event. One of the 
drivers had a '77 3.0 Litre Euro Carrera. The car was really nice except 
for two large rust through spots on each of the front fenders. The 
fenders appeared to be original (ie the rough finish on the inner fender 
lips). You never see rust on the post '78 SC and later versions of the 
911 with fully galvanized bodies.
