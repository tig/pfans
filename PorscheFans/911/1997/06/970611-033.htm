Hi guys,
Well finally I had the time and the inclination to get back to my 84 911
with the "no-start problem." Thanks to Dave, Scott, Barry and all others
that helped me figure this one out! As many suspected, it was indeed the
speedsensor. When I rechecked the ohms across the various plugs, one came
up with OL across all the prongs. That was the speedsensor, and from what
I was told by some of you, there should be a value across two prongs. I
removed it, replaced with a new one, and voila...instantaneous start up!
Seems like summer can now begin in earnest :) I did notice that my
ignition switch in the dash is starting to act up. Sometimes it lets me
turn the key all the way around without engaging the starter. I have read
about this problem before on the list and am afraid that this too can leave
one stranded. Is there a fix for this, short of ordering the $300 ignition
switch with steering lock etc? Any thoughts?
TIA, and thanks again to all that helped me with the speedsensor.
