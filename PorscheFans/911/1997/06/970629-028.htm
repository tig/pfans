Didn't catch the original post, but if it is a 911 fuel filler door that
won't open
due to a broken cable, there is possibly an easy way.

Get a length (approx. 2 ft) of strong fishing line.  Wrap each end around
each
hand, then feed the center under the back edge (facing the windshield)
of the lid.  Give a sharp tug forward parallel to the fender; should pop the 
lid catch.
