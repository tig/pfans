On dyno testing on it was determined that 1-5/8" headers were optimal
on a 3.4ltr motor (GE60 cams) with webbers making about 300ish HP.
Above 7000, the 1-3/4" headers made more power.

I would think that 1-3/4" headers would be the largest unless you have
a very happy 3.6ltr.  Remember what you gain at the top you'll lose at the 
bottom.

Here are the area (flow) percentage increases, for the calculator impared.

		1.5"		1.625"		1.75"

1.5"		100%

1.625		117%		100%

1.75		136%		116%		100%

1.875		156%		133%		115%
