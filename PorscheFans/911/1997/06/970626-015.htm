I just put a set of ROH 17" wheels on my '87 Targa and I love them.
They have Nittos mounted on them, and I was still able to lower the
front about .75" without compromising clearance. I *believe* the tire
sizes are 215/45-17 front, (7.5" wheel), and 235/45-17 rear, (8.5" wheel)

The only negative I have noticed is that they are somewhat heavier than
the original Fuchs, (of course my car came with 15" Fuchs so some of the
difference is due simply to the size difference).

Also, for those looking for a good source of mid-priced aftermarket 911
wheels, try Discount Tire Direct. (See Excellence or check in at
http://www.tires.com/).
