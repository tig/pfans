>Anyone have any experience in taking one of these gauges apart?

Yes.  Just pull it out of the dash and then, with a screwdriver, work
around the lip of the fascia gradually expanding it until it slips off.

Clean it.  Dry it with a hair dryer. Put the fascia back on and press it
tight with a screwdriver.

No sweat.
