I repainted the centers of my Fuchs myself using Krylon Glossy Black.  It is
a little time consuming as you have to take each wheel off separately.
 Taping off the wheel works alright, but you can coat the tire and the
aluminum lips with Vaseline and paint away.  When you're done, just wipe off
the Vaseline.
