You're right, Tony, on all accounts.  But don't forget the Targas that
ARE cabriolets.  Look at a 1966 factory spare parts catalog and you'll
see references to the "Cabrio (Targa)".  Unzip the back window of a
soft-window Targa and you've got a cabriolet, without the bulky boot and
mechanism.  The factory even made an optional tonneau cover, 356
cabriolet style.  I haven't regretted buying mine (except when I think
of the 911S version).
