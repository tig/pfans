Dennis,

This is very common, and is usually a failing speedo
sending unit.  The gismo is simply an inductive pickup
placed in the driver's side of the transmission just
above the rear wheel half shaft.  Replacement is
easy, but a little dirty (as any under car repair).
The sensor wire goes over the transmission and into
wire harness tunnel between the seats.  It clips into
the wire harness at a point accessible through an
access panel which is under the carpet between the
footwells of the rear passenger jump seats.

Keep Porsching,
