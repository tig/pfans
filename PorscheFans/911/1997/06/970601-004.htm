Also:  I have a leaking clutch hose.  My dealer showed me the hose when

car was on the rack.  It does not seem to be listed in any of the main

 The
hose is pretty much in a vertical position and located near the front 
of

would
run about 100.00.  Any ideas on what hose this is?

There are two hoses.  One is a braided blue line running from the 
reservoir to the clutch master cylinder, which Porsche sells by the 
meter, p/n 999.924.001.40.  The other is a two-part hose running 
between the master and slave cylinders.  The part numbers depend on the 
year and model, but for you should be 951.423.091.11 for the solid 
pipe, 951.423.189.01.
