My '86  would quit cooling after a hard run on the freeway at high rpms. The
temp control didn't seem to make much difference. It turned out to be a
expansion valve that was stuck (open or near open). Another symptom was the
inability to raise the temperature via the temp contol knob. You should be
able to make the dash vent temp ~60 degrees on the warm setting.
