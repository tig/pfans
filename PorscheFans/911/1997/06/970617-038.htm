I requested via Pirelli's Web page, tire pressures for my '90 C2 running
new 205x40x17" Pzero Directionals on the front
and  255x40x17s Assimetricals on the back.  I am experiencing "tracking" on
uneven pavement.
This was their response:

>Thanks for visiting Pirelli on the Web!!!!
>
>The 17" tire option for your vehicle calls for 36 psi in both the front
and the rear, >according to the 1997 Tire Guide.  The tracking feeling that

be fine.
>
>Thanks again!

I don't buy it.  Factory manual states pressures on 205 and 225x16" Dunlops
at 36 and 44lbs respectively.  I went thru a set of Pzero 235s last season,

35 and 42.
Any experience to suggest otherwise?   ChrisK.
