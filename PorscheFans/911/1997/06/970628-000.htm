Mr. Iverson,

  Regarding your question about an oil cooler for a 3.6 in your 914.

The Mazda cooler you are referring to is the RX3, RX-4 type cooler that 
must be modified to work on the front of your 914 (or 911). This cooler
is just marginal for a 3.6 that may be operated on the track. Other
manufacturers of Oil coolers that are very good for this application 
are: Earl's Performance
     Serck-Speed

Be sure to use a factory thermostat in your plumbing!!!!!. Minimum oil 
line size should be AN-16.
