Chris,  as I seemed to remember from working on the sunroof on my old '84 
Carrera (replaced broken cables twice), there is a tensioner on the big
pulley that drives the cable.  Unzip the access cover in the headliner
just in front of the rear window, then uncrew the cover.  You should see a 
large round pulley faciing you with a Phillips screw in the center. Just
tighten
it somewhat to increase the clutch tension.
