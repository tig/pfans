Hi Greg in NJ --

> Looking for help!
> The brake light on my dash, the one that comes on when the 
> parking brake is on, is now on all the time.  When I put the 
> parking brake up and down it does dim very slightly so I don't 
> think is the switch at the parking brake.
> The brakes work fine, stops well, firm pedel.

Check the brake fluid level in the reservoir.  Just recently, my 
brake light started coming on intermittently, though my brakes 
appeared to be working fine.  (Sounds sorta familiar, huh.)  When 
I checked my reservoir the fluid level was just a tad low.  So I 
added a bit of brand new brake fluid, and voila! the light went 
dark again.  8^)  (Yes, it still comes on with the parking brake, 
so it's not just a burned-out bulb.)

Hope this works for you too!
