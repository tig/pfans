after it was determined that the armature had failed.  The generator I
sent off was the type as desribed in the 912 Handbook with two external

generator is rotated right, the voltage regulator (B+ terminal) hits the
oil tank.  The numbers off of the replacement generator are as follows:

0104242006/005
7V45A27

    I can remove the voltage regulator and use my firewall mounted

somehow fasten.  Can anybody identify the generator that was sent to
me?  Is there anyway to rotate the internals of the generator so that I

run across this problem and found a solution that didn't require

regulator?  Should I contact the source of the generator for a more
original-style generator (if it's even available)?
