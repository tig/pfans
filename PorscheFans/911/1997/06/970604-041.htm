Eric,

The motor sound package essentially eliminates the 'muffler' effect of the airbox,
such that intake sound can be heard. This is not really noticeable outside the car,
but in the cockpit gives a throaty sound on wide open throttle. You can simulate the
same thing by removing the air box cover on your own car. Of course, you don't
really want to drive around much without the air filter in place, but I suppose you
could cut up your own air box so that the filter is still functional but you get
'better' sound.
