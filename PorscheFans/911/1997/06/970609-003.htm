PFans,
To get the pin back in position you can make
a holder from a piece of hanger and a large female
spade connector. Crimp the connecter to the end
of the wire and slip the female portion over the
head of the pin. You'll have to modify the connecter
some to fit over the pin. (Some clever soul posted 
this technique to the old Pfiles list.)

I'm not sure how I got the clip on but I suspect
I attached a small wire [I have a roll of "twistie
tie" wire] to the clip so it wouldn't go too far
if I dropped it. Then I would have used small hands
(mine) to position it and push it on with a long
screwdriver.
