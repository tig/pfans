Hello P-fans...  Some good press on the AJR Super starter.

I have a 77s with a 3.6.  The 915 trans it's connected to had a starter 
from the early 80's that was very reluctant about turning over the engine, 
especially when hot.

I bought the super starter for $320 (It's a Tilton, apparently adapted for 
the 911).  It's lighter, and it starts my 3.6 in a real hurry, sometimes 
with a "twitch" of the key.

Money well spent....
