Hi Richard,
I added a third brake light to my 83.  I considered the top-mounted
model, but some P-fans advised me that it obscures in the rear view
mirror vehicles that are directly behind you.  I installed the bottom
mount type.  It had the factory peel-and-stick adhesive, and immediately
after I installed it, I looked at it from outside the car and realized
that the WHITE adhesive stuck out like the proverbial sore thumb. (I can
just imagine the engineers at the factory discussing this--"Ja, it looks
bad but it sticks so good!"

I then got a putty knife and carefully peeled it off, taking care not to
damage the defroster wires.  I think if it has "cooked" onto the window
for several years, it might be more difficult to remove.  You might try
some adhesive remover (like 3M) and let it soak a bit before removal.

When I reinstalled mine, I used 3M adhesive trim tape.  It is black and
doesn't appear obtrusive.  Hope this helps.
