In case anyone on the list has a car with Daytime Running Lights 
(like all Canadian Porsches after 1990) here's a hint. Do not tape up 
the running lights for a track event. If you do you will:

    a) be lucky and simply have a lousy job to remove the melted glue
    b) be unlucky and have the lens crack. On a 993 that's $200 or so.

If you are in the 'a' category, WD-40 does a good job of removing the 
sticky residue caused by the heat of the lamp.  If you are in the 'b' 
group don't bother as you'll need one or two lens assemblies which 
are very costly.
