Blaupunkt is the best German radio/CD.  However, they have a different
philosophy than the American and Japanese.  The German's emphasize the
mid range of sound and sharply taper it off at both ends.  The Japanese
and American's tend to make their sound equiptment cover the broad
spectrum of say 40cps to 18-20k.  While the Blaupunkt is a high quality
product they would not please the audiophile.  However, if it is for a
Porsche, one might not hear the nuances due to high ambient noise level.
