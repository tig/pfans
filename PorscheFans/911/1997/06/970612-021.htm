Here's it what I did to my 87 911.

I removed the cover of the air outlet that is in the center down there
under the dash( kick panel?).
That alone gives you a good large hole where a jet of cold air can come
out. On top of that,
I attached a flex hose to the hole and ran it up to the edge of the dash.
This shoots cold air at either
passenger (whichever way the flex hose is aiming). and is a cheap way of
"somewhat" improving
the a/c flow in the cabin.
