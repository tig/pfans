Removal of radio: yes, get a wire coat hanger, cut two straight pieces
out of it approx. 6 inches each. Bend each into thirds, ('c' or 'u'
shaped), and insert one into the holes on each end of the unit. You
should be able to gently pull the unit out using the brackets as handles.
Toss 'em in your toolbox.

Puff of oil smoke = normal... I hope...      =8-)
