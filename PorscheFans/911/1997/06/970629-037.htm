It's not dumb if you don't know the secret, and thre are a lot of
secrets on a Porsche. This one I know: You have a standard DIN-type
unit. Behind the holes are four spring clips. You have to insert a stiff
wire into each hole simultaneously to release the four clips. Then,
simply slide out the unit.

My PO left me a present of pieces two coat hanger wire bent into the
shape of a U. They work great.
