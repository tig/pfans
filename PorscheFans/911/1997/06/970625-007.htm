To John Francis Duncan, 	I had a similar problem when trying to remove 
a flywheel. I stripped the head of the bolt in no time and it took a 
lot of patience to be able to remove. The key word is PATIENCE. A lot 
of loose nut spray and imagination. What I did is cut a groove in the 
head of the bolt and used an impact screwdriver to slowly release the 
bolt. It worked but it took a week untill	the bolt finally let go. I 
was trying once or twice a day, adding loose nut each time.	Good luck
