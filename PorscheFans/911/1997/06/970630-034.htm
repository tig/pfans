> For the guy with the metal bits in his new car's oil. Visible metal could
> be production residue in the oil tank, but the filter should pick it up
> immediately. 
> 
> Visible metal bits in the oil are nothing to fool around with. Change the
> oil and the filter immediately. Drive the car a while and recheck for the
> presence of metal in the oil.
> 
> Cut open the old filter (drain it first, then cut it open on something
> absorbent or tray-like) and inspect it for metal bits. Collect any you
find
> and get badck to your dealer with them. Ask to have the factory service
rep
> for the area look at them.
