Keep in mind that the solvents in the adhesives of decals can decompose the
shell of a helmet.  It's known to bike riders that 'stickers on your hat'
can be a bad idea.  FWIW, keep your black hat out of the sun when it's not
on your head or you're in for a warm day on the track.  Dehydration really
is a big threat to your performance on the day.
