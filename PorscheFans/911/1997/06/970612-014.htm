I have just received my own porsche (911 C2 "92) painted on Canvas (Oil
& Acrylic 200 cms x 140 cms) by Eric Jan Cremer. This is his first
Porsche painting and is fantistic !

Eric Jan Cremer, born on 17th february 1962 in Amsterdam (The
Netherlands), is undoubtedly one of the most sussesfull Formula 1 artist
at this moment (Jacques Villeneuve (world champion 1997 ?), Frensen and
Schumacher (world champion 94 and 95) bought his paintings).

Dynamics and expression are the distinguishing features of Eric Jan
Kremer's work, which sparks spectacular excitement.
