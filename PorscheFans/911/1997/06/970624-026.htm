Hi Peter:
This is kind of a never ending thread thats on every motor list I've ever   
seen.  In my case I change the oil every 3K or twice a year no matter   
what.  I don't use synthetic because I don't feel that the normal use of   
the car (short hop to work twice a day, engine rarely sees 180 degree oil   
temp) is enough to burn off impurities suspend in the oil which is part   
of the good and bad with synthetics and I change it to get these   
impurities out of the engine.  Given my personal use I'd change a   
synthetic just as often.   I use 10/30 as the winter storage oil and   
change to 20/50 in late spring.  Now having said this I'll bet that out   
of the some 1200+ people on this list there are as many opinions on what   
is best.
