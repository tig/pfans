Two words, 'speed sensor'.  I have thus far replaced two on my 87 coupe. 
They seem to go for some reason.  The last unit I purchased was a so called
'upgraded' unit that went on the turbo.  No problems since it had been
installed.  It is a simple fix, provided you can get the car up on stands
or a lift.  It goes from the tranny to the tunnel and connects with the
line going to the unit.  I doubt there is anything wrong with the speedo
unit itself.  But you may want to address this with the other list members
and they can tell you how to test the sensor etc.
