John Backstrom wrote:
>Have a shot at a 1968 911.  Been looking for newer, but this may be a 
>good place to start.  I will be looking at car soon, my questions:
>    1) What should I be on the lookout for with a '68 - Rust I know, but 
>what else?

A friend in California has one.  The biggest problem he had was passing
the smog test.  The '68 has a smog pump (air injection) that doesn't
work and can't be readily replaced.  If you must smog a car where you're
at, I'd check that out.

>    2) What is a reasonable price for a good quality car?

Depending on the condition, I've seen most in the $4000-$8000 range on
the left coast.  The sportomatic models seem to have lower values than
the 5-speeds.  YMMV.
