Last month I got a new set of wheels and tires for my 1989 Carrera.
It previously had 16x7 205/55 and 16x8 225/50 with gold centered
BBS RS wheels, with Yokohama AVS intermediates. I bought replica
911 turbo wheels, by Mille Migla they are 17x7.5 205/55 and 17x9
255/40, the tires are Bridgestone Potenza S02s. My car is lowered
and they fit perfectly with the front fender lips rolled. Judging from
the space left I doubt that you could fit on anything larger..

If anyone is interested in 1/18 scale cars, checkout my 1/18 scale car page
at http://www.geocities.com/motorcity/1703/diecast.html
The 911 GT1, GT2 and 550 Spyder (among others) will be available
soon in that scale.
