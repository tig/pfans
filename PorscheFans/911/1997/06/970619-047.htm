Pete asked about using Valvoline 10-40 for his new 911 ...

That oil would seem a bit thin to me. I have a 91, and here in Chicago I
use Castrol 20/50 from, say, May to November, i.e. when the temps are
generally above 50 deg. The owners manual says the same, suggesting 15w-40,
20w-50 or 40w for "mineral oil based engine oil" and other values for
synthetics.  (Do you have the owner's manual? If not, there are a number of
sources for a new one. Let me know and I'll dig up an advt for someone who
advertises in Panorama.) Be aware that there are differences between oil
brands; the quality is in the additive packages. You want one with the
highest ratings. I think they're now SG/SH.
