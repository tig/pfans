	Doug:

	The flywheel in my '90 C2 Cab went (it was bad when the car
had only 8800 miles on it when I purchased it in March, 1996) and I
replaced it with a 993 flywheel/clutch assembly.  These are much more
stable, and fit with no problem (I'm told by my mechanic).  I bought a
slightly used set from a 993 owner who was turning his car into a race
car and saved a bundle.  I paid $600 for the entire flywheel/clutch
assembly, and I understand it would have been about $1600 from a
dealer.  You might want to look for a similar situation.
