Eric:  I tried to avoid the stock Porsche muffler (because of clearance
problems on Porsche-powered VW van) but there's nothing else that works as
well.  If you think the resonance is bad with ->74 heat exchangers, you
should hear what the 75->77 exchangers sound like with anything but the
stock muffler.  I tried various combinations, even multiple mufflers in
series like a big American V8. Even if you get the noise level down, you
have a quiet resonance, rather than a loud one.  The huge stock muffler
is the only answer.  If you want a little more performance, buy or build
one of the dual-output variations, but get a muffler with that big volume.
(I finally gave up, used the stock Porsche muffler, and settled for the
reduced ground clearance needed to make it fit.)
