A large number of aftermarket speedo cables were installed in early 911s. 
You can try pulling the speedo out of the dash, removing the cable there,
and putting a few drops of oil in the drive cable.  Do not over-oil.  You
will make a big mess.  I that does not work, you need to replace the cable.
 This is not too difficult, but is time consuming because you have to
remove some interior pieces.  Do not use an aftermarket speedo cable.  I
have installed two of them, and both were junk.  Look for "VDO" imprints on
the part.  My VDO cable has lasted 15 years, after junking two aftermarket
cables.
