I'd like to thank everyone who responded to my plea for help regarding my C4's ABS & 4WD warning lights. It seems that a few other C4 owners have had or are having the same problem. I though I'd summarize the list of advice I received.

	Non-standard size road wheels may be out side the adjustment range for 		the ABS/4WD system. The manual states the the system can adjust for 		other sizes but only up to a certain limit.

	Brake fluid reservoir not filled to "Max" level. 

	ABS pump, pressure switch or pressure sensor failure. Only sure way to
	isolate is to use pressure gauge to determine fault. 
	
In my case adding a little more fluid (above the Max line) seemed to help a little. But only temporarily. The lights went off for two day (and ten miles) but came back on the next morning. They have been on ever since. The brake fluid level is fine (a little above Max). I've noticed my ABS pump seems to be operating all the time. I do not hear it build pressure and then switch off.
