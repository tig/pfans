The service manual on fiche is the small format and works fine on
American format fiche readers. The parts index, OTOH, is large format. I
had to cut my parts catalog in half using a razor blade.

I found the service manual on fiche somewhat frustrating to use because
the pictures are nearly illegible and it is hard to locate specific
sections. I found a used service manual and bought it and am very glad I
did. However, if the $350 or more that it would cost for a service
manual set is prohibitive for you, then the fiche is better than
nothing. I do find the parts index on fiche to be an invaluable asset,
though.
