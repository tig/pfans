Denny,

	Sounds like your shifter has gone out of adjustment. Take a look at the
coupler bushing. One side of mine cracked and fell out and gave the same
symptoms. Otherwise, check the mounting bolts of the shift assembly.  If
these have come loose, the adjustment would be lost.

	To check alignment of the shifter, put the transmission in first. 
Loosen the shift coupler clamp so that the shifter is free of the
tranny.  Move the shifter to where first should be and tighten the
clamp. Then, check all gears and go.

	Hope that helps.
