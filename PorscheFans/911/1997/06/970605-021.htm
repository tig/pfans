Chris:
	I solved the problem of attaching an anti-sub belt by
using 1" angle iron stock to fabricate a crossbar.

I cut the piece about 1.5" longer than the span of the original
seat rail support. I then cut the piece where
the two sides meet (corner). This allowed me to bend two ears back.
I removed the two ears that were not bent.

I then drilled a hole in the ears, and matching holes in the seat support
(u shaped, welded to the floor). I attached this fabricated piece with
bolts. The eyebolt for the belt attaches to it.

Kind of hard to picture without graphics.

Hope this helps...
