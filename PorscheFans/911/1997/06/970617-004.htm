Steve,

Your oil pressure varies by RPM, not MPH.  The rule of thumb is 1 bar of 
oil pressure for every 1000 RPM.  Idle, around 1K RPM, should give you 
around 1 bar of oil pressure.  5,000 RPM ought to give you somewhere 
between 4 and 5 bar.  What's a bar?  I think (but am not sure) that it's 
one atmosphere, 14.7 PSI.  If you've got a factory oil pressure guage, it 
should have increments of bar.
