Bruce's good advice (use ->74 heat exchangers on 911 SC) is actually pretty
straightforward.  The early heat exchangers (whether stock or stainless)
share the characteristic you want, which is a long pipe run before the
headers manifold together.  The SC heat exchangers actually aren't the 
worst: the 77 thermal-reactor heat exchangers bring the headers together
after just a few inches.  Anyway in addition to the aforementioned long
pipe run the early heat exchangers will require a dual-inlet muffler, so
you need to shop for all three pieces together.  You will also have to 
change some of your oil piping.

I tried a number of inexpensive dual inlet mufflers and finally came back
to the stock muffler.  The huge volume of it works fine for performance and
the smaller mufflers were completely unacceptable for noise level.

Good luck with yours!
