I've had this problem too.  Two or three times I've watched as the 
nozzle belched, and vomited gas all over the hood and fender (good thing 
I've got bad paint, or I'd have pitched a fit...).

I've found a passable solution:  don't insert the nozzle all the way into 
the filler neck, back it out half an inch or so.  This doesn't work on 
every pump (sometimes I still have to stand there like an idiot), but 
it's better than nothing.
