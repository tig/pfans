Bruce,
I am 6-4 with a long waist (32 in inseam ) equal to about 6-8 sitting.  I 
found a great solution.  The seat is a Corbeau Forza.  It has no shell using 
Parelli webbing instead.  This seat sits about 1.5 in lower than the sport 
seats in my Carrera.  I ordered their seat bracket with the seat and had it 
modified to gain another inch.  The total setup is 2.5 in lower than the sport 
seats.  I change seats for track events.  It only takes about 10 minutes and 
is part of my normal prep.  The Corbeau is not adjustable except for and aft 
with normal sliders.  The seat and bracket was only about $400.
