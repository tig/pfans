> Has anyone on the list had success (fabricating ignition wires sets)?

Yeah.  No sweat. I've done it a number of times on 911's and other cars.
It used to be a fairly common thing for people to do, before we entered the
"throw it away and buy a new one" age.

You might have trouble finding wire.  Here in Seattle none of the local
autosupply houses carry it, but most of the performance shops do--in
25 foot rolls.  Even in that quantity, the cost is much less than a set
of wires with connectors.
