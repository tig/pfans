Subject: How to remove door latch?


My driver's door latch is not working quite right on 
my '69 912 so I want to take it apart and check it out.

My question is, how to I remove the latching mechanism
from the door after I remove the door panel for access?

        Thanks,
        Craig

CRAIG,

Remove the door panel first.  Then its pretty straightforward.  Remove
the metal pin that holds the latch to the bracket in the door jam.  then
swing the door out so its about perpendicular to the side of your car. 
Take the proper size allen wrench and remove the two allen screws
holding the latch to your door.  Then you can reach inside the door and
pull out the latch.  Easy.  I'll bet your springs are broken and need 
replacing.  Dont buy those crappy new latches they sell now if you
decide to replace it.  You can find the correct springs for your latch
at a porsche salvage yard.  They are used on 914 latches.
