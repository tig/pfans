I'm no expert, but reading from "The Used 911 Story", page 37, Diagram 15,

The number should be of the form 6TYnnnn Where 'T' is the type (1=T, 2=E, 3=S),
'Y' is the last digit of the year, and 'nnnn' is a serial number.  For
example, my '73S engine number is 6331463.  Keep in mind that this format is
only valid for a US engine from '72 or '73.
