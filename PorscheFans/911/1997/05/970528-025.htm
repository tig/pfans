Hi Darrel

I had Bilstein shocks put on my 85 911 and I noticeed a big improvement.
I would not recomend Bilstein Sport shocks unless you have very smooth
roads or kidneys made of steel.  (assuming this is not a track car). 
I'm not sure how much new shocks will help an oversteer problem unless
you have unmatched shocks on the car now.  (Mine had Boge in the front
and KYB in the rear).  Beware of going too stiff on the shocks.  Regular
street bilsteins were much stiffer than the shocks they replaced (fronts
were original with about 150,000 reare had about 110,000.
