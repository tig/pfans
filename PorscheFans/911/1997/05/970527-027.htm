When I first bought my 84, I realized that either everybody else on the road was
driving 80 mph all the time without getting pulled over (not likely!), or my 
speedo was lying to me.  So I did some quick calculations of speed as a function
of engine rpm, and engine rpm needed for a particular speed, so I could do a 
rough check of my speedo's accuracy myself.  (Sure enough, it was reading about 
10% high.)  I based my calculations on the following:

Gear Ratios:             Tire size: 225/50-16
1      3.181818          Wheel/tire radius: 12.42913 inches
2      1.777778          Tire circumference: 78.09455 inches
3      1.260870
4      1.000000
5      0.789474
Final  3.875

The tables appear below.  Hope you find them useful!
