The wiper parking position is not difficult to change, at least on the
cars I have owned (69, 71, and 83).  It's just a matter of removing the
carpet and dash cover inside the trunk.  Then remove the fresh air
blower to gain access to the wiper motor.  Remove the nut that retains
the wiper actuating arm and gently pry the arm off the motor shaft. 
Rotate the arm 180 degrees, reinstall on the shaft, and replace the nut. 
Check the parking position of the wiper blades, and it should be at the
extreme right of the windshield.  If the wipers dither from the desired
position when turned on/off, rotate the arm on the wiper motor shaft
slightly.  

After verifying the parking position and replacing the items removed to
gain access, remove the straight wiper arm from the left side and put it
on the right.  Now bend the old right wiper arm at an angle
corresponding to the former angle, and put it on the left side.

Your wipers should now park out of the way on the right.
