Tony,

> Are the *galvanized* parts on a Porsche made by the hot dip route or by
> electroplate?

They're definitely not electroplated, and I believe I recall that the
sheet steel is coated by the steel factory (Thyssen) before it ever
reaches Porsche.

I believe the steel is coated (must be dipped, I guess?), then rolled
to produce the correct thickness of the zinc coating. I seem to recall
that the rolling process is also done in such a way that it changes
the characteristics of the zinc coating (perhaps done under high pressure
and temperature).

Sorry, I can't immediately find the reference I saw to this (I *think*
it must have been in "The Porsche 911 Story" by Paul Frere). If you can
read German, try the Thyssen home page at www.thyssen.com
