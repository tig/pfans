The Ansa exhaust systems are well made and sound real nice :^) but have
a few drawbacks that you should be aware of:

Less power than stock muffler.
More exhaust residue and "dirt" on rear of car.
Paint discoloration on rear panel that license plate attaches to.

They look nice, last a long time and definitely sound great. Just bear
in mind the items listed above. There are some stock appearing mufflers
with twin outlets that address all of these issues better than the Ansa,
unfortunately, at additional cost.
