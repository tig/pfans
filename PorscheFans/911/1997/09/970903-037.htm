I put in the kit and was very pleased with it. Certainly there is less
mechanical advantage due to the shorter lever, but if your shifting isn't
heavy now, it should be OK. Having come from an '86, I found the long throw
of my '85 to be really annoying and couldn't wait to change it.  I did have
to buy a new knob, as we couldn't get the old one off the stock shift lever.

BTW, the Weltmeister kit I bought from Automotion was not an epoxy-it
yourself  kit. The shift lever was all one piece as I remember. 

Try changing your trans oil to Swepco or Red Line to lighten up heavy
shifting. It basically gets better when it warms up.
