        Should SB 42 be signed into law, the exemption will not take
        effect until January 1, 1998.

        You may, however, choose to file a certificate of non-operation
        and not operate your vehicle on the road for the period between
        when your registration expires and 1/1/98.  Then, pay $10 to
        re-register the vehicle after the 1st of the year when the
        pre-'73 exemption is in force.
