This is a little belated but just a note to thank whoever it was that
posted the suggestion to construct a simple 4x4 engine dolly on casters
to set the engine down on during removal and then jack the now lightened
car up so the completely assembled engine can be rolled out from under
the car.  No valance removal, no injection disassembly, nothing.  Whole
process from the time I backed it onto the ramps took about 2 hrs
working alone which included the time it took me to find my misplaced
tools and build the dolly.  Now its into the transmission.
