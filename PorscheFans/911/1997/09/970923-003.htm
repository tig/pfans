My understanding is that the only 68 911 on which the later brakes 
will "bolt on" is the 911S with the larger aluminum "S" front brake
calipers.  This is because the caliper mounting bolt spacing is
different on the strut itself.  So for most early 911's, changing
to later brakes involves replacing the front strut housing with
a later one (911SC or later) which has the correct brake caliper
mounting bolt holes.  Hope this helps.
