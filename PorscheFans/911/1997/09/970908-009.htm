 In '85 the shaft changed to 108mm diameter flange and
the stub axle was friction welded to the outer CV joint
i.e. there are no bolts on the outer joint.  I just
replaced all the CV joint boots and one joint on my '86
and its no big deal if the joint comes off the shaft
easily. I had to have a friend press one of mine off 
for me and he wound up pressing the outer one off as
well which made things interesting...
