The September 22, 1997 edition of Autoweek quotes Fred Schwab, President of
Porsche Cars North America, in commenting about the new water-cooled 911, as
saying: "We can't go into the next century selling a brand-new old-fashioned
car."

Many of us believe (perhaps arguably) that certain Porsches, including the
911, have  timeless (rather than "old-fashioned") qualities.  Perhaps even a
bland "...enduring tradition..." type comment would have been less insulting
to long time owners.
