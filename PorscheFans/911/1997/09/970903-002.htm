 I had my '80 SC suddenly stumble & practically die while I was on my way to
my mechanic.  I barely made it there.  It turned out to be the lambda
control circuit relay (under the passenger front seat).  It was clicking in
and out all along and finally crapped out.  My car had similar symptoms.  I
don't know if this is your problem, but maybe it is just a coincidence that
you had gotten gas & washed the car.  It's worth a look if nothing else
works.
