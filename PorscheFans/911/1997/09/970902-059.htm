Clemson,

Go to the nearest auto parts or hardware store, and get a  tube of "rear
view mirror" adhesive.  It's made by Locktite;  the counterperson at the
auto parts store will know exactly what you are looking for, and in the
hardware store it will be found in the adhesives section.  Little tube,
about a half an inch long, with a little glass vial beside it.

Works very well;  follow the instructions.
