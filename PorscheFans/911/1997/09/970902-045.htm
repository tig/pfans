Porsche has recommended the 15,000 mile oil change interval for a number of
years now, even before they started using synthetic oils. They started in
August 1992 shipping all cars with SAE 5W - 40 synthetic oil. The 1979 911
Warranty & Maintenance Manual recommends a 15,000/24,000 km oil change
interval. They go on to say to provide good lubrication and to keep the
engine internally clean, the oil should be change and the filter replaced
regularly (at least once a year), so this is not a new recommendation.
