I just got some tubes from Porsche, and they came with green seals. 
From what I've been able to read, these are considered "the latest" 
although the brown seals work fine too. Definitely don't use 
silicone seal. The slicone paste is like a grease, and doesn't firm 
up like silicone seal. As soon as I finish updating the chain 
tensioners, replacing the leaking tubes is next.
