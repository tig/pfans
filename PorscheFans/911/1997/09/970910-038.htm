Hi Dan

I had the same problem with my 77 targa the car would be fine when I
first started it then it would break up after 3500 rpm.  It turns out
that my alternator was over charging and for some reason 911 motors
don't like voltage that is higher than 15 volts they start to sputter
and pop.  Get an A.V.R. test done to find out what your car is charging
at.
I got one done and found that my car was charging at 17 volts.  I
changed my external voltage regulator and Voila! problem solved.

Good luck
