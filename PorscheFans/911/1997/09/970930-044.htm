Steve,
   I just replaced the CV boots on my '87 911. It is necessary to remove the
stub axle nut to remove the halfshaft. From 85 - 89, the stub axle is
friction welded to the outer CV joint. There are no bolts on the outer joint;
only on the inner joint. Chris Bennet sent me excellent instructions on
removing the halfshaft and replacing the boots. If you are interested, I can
pass them on to you.
