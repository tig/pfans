Doug,

	I have a '72T Targa that I restored just in time to take my new son
home in. 
	At Pick-A-Part, I found that the rear belts from an old VW Bug had the
right look and the right length.
	Poking around through the carpet on either side of the rear seats, I
found that proper seat belt mounting holes were hidden under there. Some
careful work with an X-acto knife and some twists of a socket wrench,
and we have rear seat belts.
	While it is a lot easier to get the punk in and out with the top off,
it is do-able and actually works pretty well with it on.
