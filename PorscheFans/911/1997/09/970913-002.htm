have driven a 1997 993 Twin turbo on a daily basis and have logged in 9,000
miles.  It has been a fantastic experience and I always look forward to my
daily drives.  Recently, got the Automotion upgrade for it and according to
their ads, it adds 65 to 70 HP with the $3,000 upgrade.  The acceleration

There is no "fluff" in my car; it is all about getting down the road like a
Porsche should.
