Gabriel,

Your clutch is rubber centered.  You may want to stay with rubber 
though becuase the G50's received an improved version over the old SC 
rubber centers which tended to disintegrate.  Mine lasted over 100K and 
was going strong at replacement (I had a new spring centered one on 
hand).  If you go with springs, expect a slightly less forgiving 
drivetrain when you don't match revs right, especially on 16's/17's 
w/stiff sidewalls.
