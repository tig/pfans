Brad, funny you should ask.  Did just this last week.  Switches from
dealership cost $30 for pair.  Don't try to pull off door panel - no need.
Simply carefully pry old switches out using fabric wrapped putty knife or
similar flat bladed tool.  Be patient.  Then, unplug wires from old switch
and plug into new switch.  Press back into door panel and you're done.  teresa.
