>I have a 1980 911SC with an idle problem and would appreciate your help.

>The idle fluctuates between about 900 and 1200 RPM every 2 to 3 seconds.
>Doesn't seem to
>be missing or running rough, just dropping and rising.  Any suggestions on
>where to look?

If your engine has an idle regulator, then it could cause those symptoms.
The regulator is a small cylindrical tube with rubber hoses one each end
and an electrical connector with three wires comeing out of it. The unit
mounts vertically in front of the throttle body with the rubber tubes
providing an air bypass around the throttle. The regulator is a rotary
solenoid valve and is controlled by the DME in my '87.
