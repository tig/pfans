I had similar trouble with my '81 SC several years ago. Turned out to be the 
ignition switch contact for the AC blower circuit, which was shorting after 
burning out. The blower kept running when ignition was off. Created massive 
high amp drain which would kill battery in 2 hours. Strangely, did not blow 
fuse...?? Replaced switch and ok since then, though I still occasionally get a 
dead battery when I let the car sit for a couple of months, and I have never 
figured out why.... Good luck. Warren C.
