Garth -  Sounds to me like your 901 tranny shifts just like it was
designed to.  1st gear is really only meant to be used to get the car
going from a start.  You can down shift from 2nd to first while moving,
but it is it is mot mdesigned for a power shift. The $300 for a tune up
sounds a little high to me, but I do most of my own work in this area.
You could also.  The toughest part is the valve adjustment.  If you're
at all mechanically inclined, it is not difficult.  See the FAQ for
instructons. Tuning the carbs may be a different matter.  I had mine
done at a local shop and they only charged $60.  Once they're set, they
run fine for many miles.
