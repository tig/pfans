My experience has been with both H-1s and H-5s, that a fog develops on the
inside of the lenses caused by an imperfect seal of the gasket on the bulb. 

To clean, remove the headlight assembly from the car, remove the bulb, (being
careful not to touch the glass part with your hands), and clean the inside of
the lens with a rag, long stick, and windex, alcohol, etc. Let dry thoroughly
before reassembling.  It sometimes seems like the trim ring doesn't want to
go back on, but eventually it does.
