By Jove, you've got it Andy! Those mysterious red levers you discovered 
between the seats are the heater valve control levers. Pull 'em up to increase 
heat, one is for each side of car. I have older car, '81, so don't have round 
knob, but I think it is for automatic temp control. The red levers actually 
pull cables which open gates for the air to come forward from engine, as there 
are boxes around exhaust pipes to heat up the air, which flows through them. 
My levers were stuck when I bought my car in Florida-- never used I guess! 
(911's are not only air cooled, also air heated!) Have fun figuring out the 
dash knobs... those will really confuse you... RWC.
