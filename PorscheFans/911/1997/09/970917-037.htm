Steve:

I saw your posting on P-Fans about Metalmasters and I thought I'd toss
in our .02.

We have sold Metalmasters since 1984. Not anymore!. Their main redeeming
quality is that they leave minimal brake dust. Thats' it.

Metalmasters on Porsches:

Do not stop unless hot
Wear out rotors
Warp rotors prematurely

In short, you can do a lot better. The OEM pads are just fine except for
hard hard street use or track. Then, we use Pagid racing or Cool
Carbon-type pads. Hawks are great for racing only. PF are good for both
albeit at a high rotor wear rate.

There you have it, 20yrs of Brake pad experience for street or racing.

Hope this helps you to make an informed purschasing decision
