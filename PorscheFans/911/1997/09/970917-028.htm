Try pushing the pistons of the right caliper in and then using warn 
pads or pieces of wood thinner than your pads push the pistons out 
with the brake pedal. Try this a few times to "exercise" your pistons.
 Although the problem could be a loose right front wheel bearing. As 
the hub wobbles, it pushes the brake pistons into the caliper. It is 
also possible that you have air in that caliper. Check the bearings 
first, it is the easiest. Bruce
