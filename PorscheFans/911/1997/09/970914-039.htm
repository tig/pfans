Some months ago, just few days after purchasing my beloved 965, I asked the
list, and in particular Bruce Anderson, about a strange sensation felt when
I shift up without double clutching (although seldom I shift without d.c.).
Due to my poor english I wasn't able to explain that sensation and the
advice I received (try with a different grade oil etc.) confirmed I poorly
described my impressions (I can't say "problem").
Now, reading the "Excellence" introduction article for the 965 (2/91), I
found a very accurate description of the above phaenomenon:
"The down side is a strong jerk every time you lift off to shift up a
notch. Apparently this is due to the K-Jetronic throttle flap falling
closed. They are working on it". 
I'm now very happy to know I haven't  bent syncros, a clutch trouble or a
improper oil; I stand with pleasure with double clutching but, I'd like to
know, any of you is able to tell me if THEY worked on it, and how? Just to
know...
