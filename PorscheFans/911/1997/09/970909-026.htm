Adam Green  wrote:

> Is there a physical test to determine if the diff is open or limited
> slip?

    Yes, Adam... Lift the rear wheels off the ground and rotate one
    of them.  If the other one rotates in the opposite direction,
    you have an open differential; if it rotates in the SAME
    direction, you have a limited-slip or locked diff.
