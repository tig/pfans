Chris, 

In a word , NO. Ultimate adhesion and braking forces are ultimately dependent
on one thing, your tires. Only so much friction can be created between the tire
and the road and any brake system can exceed this. Only wider or stickier tires
will improve braking distance. 

That said, bigger brakes can change the feel of the system and make it appear
to stop better, much like the difference between boosted and non-boosted
brakes. They also will exhibit less fade with continued usage which is why they
are really needed ( and only needed )
