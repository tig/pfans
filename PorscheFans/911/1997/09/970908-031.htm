In yesterday's digest I read something about the reputability of a certain
Porsche parts supplier who has recently been advertising "complete rubber
kits" with "everything" you need to restore your 911 in a VERY modestly
priced kit.  Order one and you will find out that "the door kit is extra."
 Quite a bit extra.  So order that, too.  Then start looking at the enormous
number of rubber parts on your 911 and compare to the contents of the kit.
 You'll find this is nowhere near a complete rubber kit.  It does the windows
and the seams between body panels, the door handles, the doors if you order
the extra door kit, and not much more.  No engine compartment seals, no
sunroof seals, no rocker strips, no bumber strips, no accordions, etc., etc.
 I knew it sounded too good to be true.  Still, it's a reasonable price for
what you get, but come on, let's not call it a complete rubber kit for a 911
restoration!!
