Jeff Graham-
I have an '81 SC which had similar idle fluctuation problem several years ago. 
The fuel injector seals were leaking, allowing air to be sucked in and lean 
out the mixture. I guess the injection kept trying to adjust for this....New 
O-rings for the injectors did the trick on my car.   Vacuum leaks in general 
cause weirdness like this, I think.  Or maybe little Gremlins. Good luck,
