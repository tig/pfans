Except that the bigger brakes are going to shed heat much more 
efficiently (i.e. quicker).  You will be less likely to experience 
brake fade (which I do regularly with my stock SC brakes).  This may 
not be something you will experience on the street but it will 
definitely be there on the track.

> could someone offer a thoughtful answer to the question: Would

Chris:

If your existing brakes are sufficiently strong to lock up the
wheels, fitting larger rotors or calipers will do nothing to improve
your car's single-stop braking distance... Remember, it's the TIRES
that stop the car, not the brakes.
