While you're hunting for a BIG oil drain pan, be forewarned from personal and
messy experience not to get the kind with the top that slopes toward a 1 inch
hole in the center. The flow rate of the oil is too much for that little
hole, and you can guess the rest. I now use a 15 qt pan on which the entire
top opens. And spread some newspapers on the floor, because it will splash.
