Yesterday I posted a message about the Porsche 993 oil filter wrench I 
bought, part number 000-721-920-40. I tried it last night. It fits the end 
of the oil filters properly but there is not enough room for it to fit over 
the short filter because of the tube that partially obscures the filter. I 
don't know if that tube can be removed and did not want to experiment. 
However, excess metal can be trimmed from the wrench and it will then fit 
over the filter and will make removal of the filter fairly easy. I did it on 
 a friend's bandsaw. Otherwise, you would be better off looking elsewhere 
for a filter wrench .
