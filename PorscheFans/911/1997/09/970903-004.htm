Phillips Garland wrote:
 
>The steering wheel on my 81 SC has become loose lately. It can be moved
>up/down and left/right, but not in/out.

This is a very common problem, caused by the disintegration of a thin
plastic bushing in the upper steering column bearing. A good replacement is
avaialble from Perf Products, or Automotion.  Price is high at $20 (for what
it is), but it solves the problem quickly, and is effective.
