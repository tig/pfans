It is possible that your confusion regarding the dipstick<->oil level gauge
relationship arises from the fact that the oil level gauge reads only the
last quart or so of the total oil capacity.   So, if the car holds 12
quarts of oil, and you still have 11 quarts in it, the gauge may show "red"
or a low condition while the dipstick shows an "almost full" condition. 
It's probably set up that way so that you'll keep it topped up.
