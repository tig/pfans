About your tensioner concerns:

1. Could it be a blocked oil feed to the tensioner?  Is oil actually getting
there?

2. I cna't see how the tensioner could loose its prime by sitting around:
there's a check valve.

3. Maybe you have a broken chain guide? (That's no easier to explain ..)

A tensioner starting to go bad is pretty easy to spot.  First of all, its a
sound only on one side of the engine. Second, at some engine speeds, as you
rev the egnine up slowly, the sound will diminish, and go away, then come
back as the chain begins to flail again.
