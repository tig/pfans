  I have a Blaupunkt Portland, don't know if it is the same face as yours.
Here's what my manual says (Power On): If the clock does not appear in the
display, press and release the DISP Button until it appears. Press the DISP
button again for more than two seconds until the clock starts blinking. Press
the (upside down V here) and V controls to set the hour, and press the << and
>> controls to set the minutes. Press the DISP Button again to activate the
clock.
