Patrick,

I too was confused by last week's post that spoke of problems with the
Weltmeister short shift conversion.  I installed one in my car about 5 years
ago, and though it does increase the effort required to shift, it improves
things a lot.

I bought the Performance Products "conversion" first time around, and found
it to be a real joke ... the Weltmeister is much better designed, and I have
a hard time believing the ball end can fall off: it is machined from steel,
and welded on to a new shift lever when you get it.  (The Perf Products one
is glued onto your old shifter).

The only thing about the Weltmeister is the alloy spacer plate that comes
with it: it has a Weltmeister logo cast into it.  I found I had to machine
the logo off in order for the shifter to sit right.  Also, my kit came with
cheesy replacement bolts to hold the thing down ... I replaced them with
Allen bolts.

Hope this helps.
