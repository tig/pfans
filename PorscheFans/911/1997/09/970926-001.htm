>I have heard that SSI heat exchangers are the best...

Our street and dyno tests results showed that with 3.0 - 3.2 motors the 1
1/2" SSI's give you better throttle response but no top end Hp increase over
stock with cat bypass. Larger 1 5/8" gave good Hp increase but lost a little
of the zip.  The best we've seen is the B&B stainless with heat exchangers if
you want to keep the heat. They make an awesome custom header with 1 5/8"
primaries flaring to 1 3/4 at the collector to give the best of both worlds.
