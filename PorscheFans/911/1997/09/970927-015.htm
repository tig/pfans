I just replaced the wires on my '73.  Most say that the shielded wires weren't 
needed, but I wanted mine to look as original as possible. I found a new original 
Porsche set at a swap meet for about $40 bucks, and then removed, cleaned the old SS 
shields from the old set. It wasn't  difficult, and the set still looks original. 
You can slide any plug wire through the braid if the diameter isn't too much larger. 
With a little work, the rubber ends slide right off and easily go over the new 
wires. I wanted to change the plug connectors also, to get rid of the screw on ends. 
 Hope this helps !!!
