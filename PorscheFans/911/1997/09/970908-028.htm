First, do NOT start the engine until you have that nozzle in your hand!
One thing we would do is to remove the no#1 aluminum intake manifold
runner and vacuum out the nozzle with a good,strong shop vac. Use a new
gasket when reattaching manifold to head. Try this before you consider
removal of the entire airbox.
