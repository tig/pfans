Henk,

Believe what you will.   A spark plug lights the fire.   That is all.
If it takes an expensive special plug to light the fire, then something
else is wrong.   The ONLY thing one plug can do over another is
light it more reliably.    If it is lighting the fire more reliably then either
there was something wrong with the other plugs, or there is STILL 
something degraded in the fuel spray pattern from the injectors.
