I can't hazard a guess as to your problem, but I may know a source for
information that would be helpful.  Pick up a copy of the "Haynes"
repair manual #264.  It's approx $20.00 and is based on a complete
teardown of the motor and all working parts in the car, including
electrical.  It covers all 911 models from '65 to '89 except turbo
equipped models.  It comes with illustrations and step by step
instructions for all jobs.  It's by no means a complete service manual
like the one from Porsche but it is very informative to say the least.
I definately think it's a must for the do it yourselfer.  If you can't
find one try calling Haynes direct at (805) 498-6703.  Thier address is

Haynes North America
861 Lawrence Dr.
Newbury Park, CA
91320
