I have optima batteries in my 85 911, 72 911 and the 356B. I do not 
drive the 72 or 61 very often, so the optima is great for them. The 
optima looks like a six pack of large flashlight batteries. It may be 
mounted in any positon as there is no liquid to leak. I have 
experienced no corrosion with any of them. There may be some minor 
fabrication required in order to mount the battery. Good Luck
