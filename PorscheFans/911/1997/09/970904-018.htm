The best place to stay,  assuming that your budget is not too tight and you 
are not big on going out,  is in Carmel.    I'd suggest the LaPlaya Hotel in 
Carmel which is the only "real" hotel in Carmel.   It's close to the central 
district and the beach.   The rooms are nice and the staff is attentive.   
Try to get a room with an ocean view.   We were just there and we'd 
recommend it.
