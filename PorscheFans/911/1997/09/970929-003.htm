ladies?) fix to protect my fog lamps and headlights:  I cut out several
layers or corrogated cardboard to fit and tape securely with racers tape
that matches my body color.  Looks a bit goofy, to be sure, but goofy is not
so bad considering cost to replace any of those lenses!  Hope this helps.
