Doug,

I have the short shift kit installed in my '67 911 racer.  No problems with
the quality of the epoxy joint, etc., but then again, not a lot of miles on it. 

However,  when I installed it, I found that the increased length of the
shifter caused it to hit the heater cable tubes and other hardware in the
tunnel.  (Impossible to get into reverse and first gears, difficult for 4th &
5th).  Since I was converting to the track, I moved the offending metal
tubes.  If I had wanted to keep the heater, I probably would not have
installed the kit.  I don't know if this would be an issue with your vinatage
911.
