I ran into thew same thing with my 73 911 Targa.  While I drove around
the car charged the battery just fine but if I parked it for a long
period of time my battery would be dead.  I used a test light and found
that it was a diode in my alternator.  I however have an external
voltage regulator so I could pull the fuse that connected the circuit.
Yes I went through all my fuses like you did.  everything was working
fine but then I went to the engine compartment and found my problem.
Try taking the car in and asking them to check the diodes in your
alternator.  If one of them is shot it would answer your problem.
