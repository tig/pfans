>Question is: Do I need to pull the axle off the car?

No.

>Or can you just undo the 6 bolts that hold the axle to
>the transmission flange and work from there?

Yes.  It's probably possible to replace the boot by only
undoing the transmission end, but it will be a lot easier if
you also undo the end at the wheel and take the whole
shaft out from beneath the car.

You can also buy replacement boots that are split, and these
can be put on without removing the axle shaft or the CV
joint.  I've never tried one, because I doubt they are as lasting
as a solid boot, but I could be wrong.  They certainly are
easier to install.
