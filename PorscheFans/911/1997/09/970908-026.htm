 Really quite easy Eric..
  Remove the duct between the blower and engine fan housing by removing
lower clamp and the 10mm bolt on upper end.  Note that the lower clamp
has a locating pin and on the upper end on the forward side of the duct
at the blower housing, there is a tab that positions the duct on the
housing.  Slide the duct to the left and remove.
  Unclamp and disconnect both hoses attached to the 'y' at the heater
blower housing.  The 'y' will need to be removed by unscrewing two small
sheet metal screws on either side.  Loosen the clamp holding the heater
blower motor to its support bracket and voila! its out.  Well almost. 
You will need to juggle the fan and motor over the injection lines to
finish the job.  Hope this helps.  Good luck.......
