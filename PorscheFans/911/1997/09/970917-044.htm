Since you were referring to a 914, I'll assume your "reason for living"
can be talked out of a custom fit cover. Since the 914 is just a brick
with a half a brick on top, what's to custom fit anyway? I had very good
luck buying a generic cover based on vehicle length. The cover was tight
enough and even the mirror pockets fit properly. Now that my 914 is
sold, the same cover now fits my wife's Nissan Stanza. If you don't find
what you want locally, try the Summit catalog, that's where I found the
$59 blue cotton cover I use on the RS America :-). P.S. It lacks mirror
pockets.
