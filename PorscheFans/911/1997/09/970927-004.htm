I bought some of this stuff and used it the last time I ran MidOhio.  It
pains on easily with a brush or small roller.  Goes on like cheap white
paint, dries clear.
I do not know how many stone chips it prevented, but it came off easily
when I washed the car, the water making it peal off easily.  It is water
based...looks and smells like latex.
It did not mar the finish, tho some of my wax may have gone with it.
I'll probably use it again.
The only draw back is that the seller is a putz.  He does not tell you
what the stuff is, did not send any instructions with the stuff, and did
not answer my inquiry on how to use it, how not to use it...etc.

(A real caveat emptor seller.)

It also came crudely packaged in an unmarked plastic bottle with clear
tape wrapped around the top.
