I have an EZ Bleed system that I use regularly on all my cars.

I have used 15-20 psi on my 911's system with no problem.  I did crack a
seam in my MGB's 20 year old resevoir with 18-19 psi.  It did NOT burst,
only started to leak slowly under pressure.   I replaced it and now use
13-15 psi with no problems.
