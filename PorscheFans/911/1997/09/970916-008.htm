Replaced the sender on my 75 targa and did not run into any real serious
problems.  However...the unit is not so tough to get to it is having the
room to move a wrench to remove it.  In mine I could get less tha 1/16
of a turn and with a fine long thread this took a considerable amount of
time., being old it was not possible to hand turn it out untill it got
towords the end.  If you have long skinny fingers the new unit can be
started by hand but again I had a hard time because of restricted space
and bad angles coupled with fingers that are not the size of pencils.  I
just put a rag down to catch the little bit of oil that leaked out.  Not
much really to speak of. Have fun.
