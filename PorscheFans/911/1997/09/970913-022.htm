Bill,

If the cam sprocket moved in relationship to the timing chain,
when you replaced the chain tensioner, the sound you hear may be
the the valves hitting the pistons.

If that is what's happening, your engine will sustain serious
damage. You should remove the the timing cover and make sure
the timing marks are properly aligned.
