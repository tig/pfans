The alternator is behind the fan.

Remove the fanbelt using the special tool in your toolbox (2pins) and then 
remove the entire shroud assembly.  

Your car has a separate external voltage regulator.

You can get a rebuilt alternator fiarly inexpensively from Vertex, or you may be able to get yours done locally (replace bearings/brushes)
