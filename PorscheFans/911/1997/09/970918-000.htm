Here is my two cents on this subject.
Someone opined that Hawk pads are good for racing only.  Wrong.  I have
Hawk  HPS (High Performance Street) on my SC.  They are rotor friendly,
great for driver's ed events, make the car stop on a dime, and are just
fine for the street.  You will get a bit of squeal at very low speed
stops, like creeping up to a light, but not usually.
Metalmasters are ok for tame street driving.
I have no experience with kool karbons.
