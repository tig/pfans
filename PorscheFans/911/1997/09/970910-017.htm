On page 106 of the Porsche Parts manual they describe an update to the
speedometer's pulse sender.  The symptom was false or no reading below
30 mph.  It was a problem with early '87 G50's, and an updated sender was
used after production date 11/96.

My '87 probably has this problem.  The speedometer has lately been balky
at low speeds for the first few minutes of driving in the morning.
