I'm going with a 5000 mile interval on Mobil 1.    I have found in my BMW 
motorcycle (R1100RSL - a 2cylinder boxer) that the Mobil 1 (at least in the 
15W-50 grade) is still in excellent condition at 3000 miles.   Given the two 
filters to replace and the $200 cost for an oil change I'll try the 5000.    
 If you run the car real hard you might want to change it more frequently.   
 I think that trying to go 15,000 would be foolish.    There may be some 
theory that given the engine's burn and replacement rate that a longer 
interval is appropriate.   I don't buy it.
