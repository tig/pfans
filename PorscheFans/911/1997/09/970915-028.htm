Jeff Klein wrote:
>      Does anyone have a source for a replacement battery for an 89 Carrera?
>      
>      I have seen the Optima advertised but would like a direct drop in 
>      replacement.  The battery is large...Group 49, 850 CCA, 150RC.  Any 
>      help is appreciated.

Any reasonable battery shop should carry Group 49 because its also 
used in big Mercs and BMWs.  Western Auto and Pep Boys carry them 
for $80.  I just replaced the battery in my '88 and decided to go 
with the Optima.  As far as I'm concerned, its the only way to go.  
It weighs less, no acid fumes to corrode metal up front, and it was a 
breeze to install.  If your car isn't driven regularly, it is a much 
better solution than putting a trickle charger on the battery when 
it's parked.  Good luck.
