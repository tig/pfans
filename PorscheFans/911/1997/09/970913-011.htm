Robert-
	I have an '81 SC, and it had a bad washer pump as well. It seemed to have 
been killed by battery corrosion. Wrestled with old pump, and finally just got 
a $10 replacement and put it in line with the old one which is still there. 
This preserves "Originality", as I can just remove new one, and works better. 
Incidentally, you may also need to clean and/or replace little anti backflow 
ball check valve up at the cowling in the washer lines. This causes as much 
trouble as the pump. PS, my Dad was AF as well...Good Luck.... Warren C.
