I ordered a bottle of vinylex earlier this summer from Tweeks ($ 9.95; I
think I bought it on sale).  It does a good job of cleaning the vinyl and
it leaves a matte finish, not real shiny like Armor-all does (not that I
use Armor-all on the Porsche interior).  I hear that the Vinylex is not
silicone based, which probably explains this duller appearance.  This is
good though, as silicone can break down vinyl over long periods of time.

I'm even thinking of using it on the Cherokee's interior, but maybe just
once...
