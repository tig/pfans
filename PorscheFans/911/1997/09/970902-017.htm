 I have had this kit in my car for the last 2 years (about 35k miles) with
no trouble.  It was very easy to install and I like the way the car shifts
now.  I actually have the other kit (epoxy) but never installed it and
bought this one instead.  When I put the new shifter in I also put in a new
set of bushings.  I think I paid around $79.00 for the kit.  I don't know
what the factory kits costs, but I would recommend this kit.
