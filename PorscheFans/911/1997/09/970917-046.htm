I am running 23/31 bars on my car. I like the firm ride. I also have
22mm Weltmeister bars. Do you have the stock bars? If so, get Charlie
bars if you can afford them or Vision bars. I am not impressed with the
quality of the Weltmeister units. All of these units have adjustable
drop link and lever arm length. This adjustability allows you to set the
amount of torsion (with the lever arm length) so you can fine tune the
handling and to set zero preload after weight balancing (with the drop
link length).
