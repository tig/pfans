To Harley Myler's excellent exposition of the scientific basis for the
superior speed of Targas over cabriolets and coupes, I would add a
caution:  Since the Coriolis forces are reversed in the southern
hemisphere, Targas are at a disadvantage there.  Unfortunately, the
Targa top cannot just be installed backwards to counteract this. 
However, the factory offered at one time a special top for their Brazilian,
Argentinian, South African, Australian, and New Zealand customers with
the covering applied in reverse, which solved this problem.  I think it was
M473, but was generally known as the April 1 option.
