I installed a pair of 1992 C2 Electric seats in a 1982 930 and they fit
perfectly. The seats are around 3/4" higher so if you are tall you might
want to check it out first. Also you would need to make the electrical
instalation for the electric seats. Simply run a clean
electrical wire directly from the battery to the seats [with its own
fuse] then make a "Y" to divide the power for each seat.
