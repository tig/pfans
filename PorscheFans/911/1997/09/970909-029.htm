Bob,

If your 993 is similar to my '90 964 (I assume it should be similar), then
the top of the 'twist' section is BELOW the low-oil mark. On mine, above the
twist section, there's two notches in the dipstick. The oil level must
be between the two notches. Bottom notch to top notch is roughly 1.7 liters
according to the manual - the top of the twist section by extrapolation is
about 1/2 liter BELOW the minimum oil level!!!

Check out your owner's manual - mine explicitly mentions the notches (and
says nothing about the twisty section).
