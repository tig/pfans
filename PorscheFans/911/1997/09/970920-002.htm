Dear Eric,

The 911 and 912 were identical in terms of body dimensions.  My only
guess is that the windshield is from a very early 911, maybe a
prototype.  "Early 60s" would be very early indeed.  The first
production of the 912 as companion to the 911 was for the '66 model,
when it replaced the 356SC and used the latter's engine.

It's probably worth it to get a new windshield rather than try to make
this one fit.  I think the tolerances in the corners are pretty liberal,
so this one may be the right size, but the inside of the gasket ought to
fit, and if it doesn't, something's wrong.
