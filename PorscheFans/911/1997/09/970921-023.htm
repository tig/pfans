Regarding one wheel's worn out pads, compared to others'.:    I had same 
problem on my '81 SC last year, and my mechanic said it would be the old 
rubber brake lines refusing to allow the fluid to return and flow back out of 
caliper, thus keeping constant pressure on pads. He was right, as usual. He 
changed all four corners' lines, and I picked up quite a little bit of speed!  
Amazing how I had not noticed the gradual loss of power, as I drove around 
cheerfully with my brakes applied all the time...  (sheepishly)  RWC.
