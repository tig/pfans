Cesar:

The transparent faceplate over your speedo is made of plastic...
Over time, it can warp and bulge inward, pressing against the speedo
needle and impeding its progress.

Take a suction cup and pull the plastic forward, away from the face
of the speedo.  If that frees the needle, you can make the fix
permanent by opening up the speedo and inverting the faceplate so it 
bulges outward rather than inward.
