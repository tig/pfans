> Hi Brian:
> Can't help with an FAQ but FWIW from years of laying up cars, boats,
> airplanes, tractors and what have you here is what I do.  Note that my
> 911 is kept in a garage.  Fuel stabilizer, full tank of gas, change
> oil, park car on plastic sheet (you can also bag it but I've found
> that to be a real pain if the car has to be moved), remove the
> battery, check air pressure and put a cotton car cover on the car.
> You can fog the engine but I don't do that with the 911.  I pay some
> attention to the initial spring start up.  I remove the plugs shoot a
> little oil in the cylinders and pre-lube the engine by turning it over
> with the coil grounded until it gets some pressure.
