I don't have any Webers for you but I do have some advice.  Your engine has
small intake ports, mild cams, and low-compression pistons.  Unless you
make internal modifications to the engine, I don't think you will be
very happy with the results of changing from Zeniths to Webers, expecially
considering the expense.  A decent set of 40IDA's that aren't worn out
could easily go for $1000.  If you are planning on keeping the engine
stock, I would just rebuild the Zeniths and stay with those.
