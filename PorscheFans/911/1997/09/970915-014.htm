>> brake system problem or possibly just a bad pair of pads?

If the rubber brake hoses have not been changed I would start there. Older
hoses tend to rot on the inside and cause restriction of flow "back" to the
master cyl. This can cause the calipers to hang up and keep the pads in
harder contact with the rotor. If you have a weak caliper then could make
the problem worse.
