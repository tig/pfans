  Your symtoms are the classic MFI worn throttle bodies. The throttle 
shafts are leaking a little air at less than wide open throttle. This 
causes some leanness which is the hesitation at light throtle openings
and the popping in the exhaust. Depending how bad it is, you may be
 able to richen the pump a little bit to help compensate.
Otherwise, have the throtle stacks rebuilt. They will be good as new.
