Nill,

after many miles, I believe that the vibration of the stalk working the
switch "wears" the post that operates the switch,  Fix is easy;  either: 
1) Bend the switch contacts a little (you will be able to see this after
the steering wheeel is removed), or
2) Make a little sleeve of brass shim stock and slip it over and glue it to
the post that operates the switch and wears the weld build-up on the switch
operating plate.

To be different, I choose option 2 but both methods work.
Caution, when removing the steering wheel, don't use the ignition lock to
turn against - put the key in the lock (battery disconnected) and turn the
wheel all the way to the stop before "wrenching" the wheel loose.
