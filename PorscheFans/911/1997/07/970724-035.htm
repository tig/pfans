<< I'm trying to decide how to clean the aluminum VIN plate off the front
 of my car under the trunk lid.  Its a bit pitted and oxidized.  Should I
 use a wire brush and some GUNK?  

NO , no, no, unless yo want to ruin the plate!  Use a VERY mild polishing
compound (Simichrome or Blue Magic) and a soft clothe and gently polish it
out.  You'll be amazed at the results.
 
 Also, how do I remove the small Paint Code plate??  The rivets look
 tough to drill out since they are a rounded surface.

The rivets actually screw in when they are tapped in at the factory.  You MAY
be able to carefully unscrew them using a pair of needle nose pliers.  They
are not peened on the back like a normal rivet.  My best advise, having been
thru this, is to leave it it place and CAREFULLY mask it off.
