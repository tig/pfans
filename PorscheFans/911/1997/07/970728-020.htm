You have an accumulation of organic matter and water in the box containing
the condensor.  The soup is supporting some sort of biological growth, that
is giving off the stench.  Pull the condensate drain line from that box,
and see if the line or the drain opening is plugged.  The system will keep
itself flushed and stay clean if it drains properly.  Cleaning the drain
normally fixes this problem.  If not, you may need to open the system to
get the junk out.  Good luck.
