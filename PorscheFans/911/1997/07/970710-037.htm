Yours is a very common question. The easiest method is to send your wheels to
someone like Robert Wood (Wheel Enhancement). For the DIY'er, I'd direct them
to a library document submitted to the Porschestrasse Forum in Compuserve.
The gentlemen there had a pretty good method of refinishing his wheels.
Briefly here's how he did it:

1.	De-anodize wheels
2.	Polish to desired luster
3.	Re-anodize
4.	Paint centers (if desired)
5.	Bake

If you don't have access to Compuserve's forum, let me know and I will dig
into my archives for a copy of the text. Good luck.
