Nigel,

With any car A/C system, there is a mechanism for carrying away
condensation from the evaporator.  It is usually some sort of collection
plenum, which collects the water and lets it run out the underisde of the
car via a rubber tube.  If this tube gets blocked, water pools internally
somewhere.  It is possible that your drain tube is blocked, and there is a
puddle of "frog water" somewhere in your car.

Look for water under your car (up front ) after you've run the air
contitioner for a while.  If there is none, it is not draining properly.
