I just bought (about a month ago) an 86 Targa and have noticed similar sounds. 
One thing I notice is that if I leave the top on for a couple days and drive
about 50-100 miles I rarely hear anything except on *very* bumpy roads.  Either
I'm driving in a warm climate (80+ degrees F) and the rubber seals are
"swelling" slightly from the heat (which would make the top fit a bit tighter)
or it "settles" in after that amount of time and stops making noise.  It does
still make some noise, but only on very rough roads.

I've talked with one other 88 Targa owner and basically he said the noise is
just part of owning a Targa.
