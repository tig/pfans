There is probably nothing wrong with your Haynes manual if it is the 
911-specific one.  The 911 uses a separate drum brake for the 
emergency brake.  Procedure as I remember is to apply the hand brake
to the third notch, then adjust the cable at each wheel backing plate
until the wheel can barely be turned by hand.  You can see the adjuster 
star wheel thru the access hole in the brake disc around the wheel stud
area.
