For what it's worth, I replaced the 20 year old motor mounts in my 911
with new Club Sport mounts and have noticed a dramatic improvement in
the way my 915 shifts.

Before the new mounts, I would have problems shifting when driving the
car hard...especially from 3rd to 4th.  Now, all of these problems are
gone.

I guess these things have a shelf life...I had replaced everything else
on the car, I am surprised I didn't change these out sooner.
