When MFI units get old and tired, they almost always run way too rich.
Cold start circuit problems and/or leaks in the drivers side heat
exchanger and tubing conecting the MFI to the heat exchanger can also
cause running too rich.

Someone who knows the fuel system on MFI cars should check this car out.
A question - can you readily smell fuel in the oil tank?
