What model of Allen Wrench have you tried? My '71 was like this, almost
completely rounded out heads, and I just couldn't get them out, I was
using some craftsman allen sockets, and they just spun round in neet
tidy circles. A friend of mine asked why I bothered with Craftsman and
handed me a rail of his Snap-On allen keys, which worked no problem,
spun the bolts right out! You might try finding one to use. The other
technique is to drill the head off of them, pull the seat out and then
use a stud puller to get the remaining bolt out.
