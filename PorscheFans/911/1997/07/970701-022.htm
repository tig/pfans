Tom,

A VERY common problem in late 80's 911s is for the sway bar mounting
brackets to fatigue and crack thru.  When that happens, it sounds
exactly like you describe (I know).  Look under the car just ahead
of the rear wheels.  If the sway bar mounts are broken, you should
be able to see.  If this is the problem fret not, it is simply repaired
by a good welding shop.  Make sure they reinforce the repair with a
web piece or it will break again (BTDT).
