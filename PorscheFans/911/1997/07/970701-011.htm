Anyway, my '87 Carrera exhibited the same symptoms as yours about 4 years
ago and started exhibiting the symptoms again in late Jan.  The fix last
time was to replace the speed sensor.  The shop that did it for me told me
that these things were known for going bad.  I don't know whether that is
true or not, but after replacement I had no problems until recently.
