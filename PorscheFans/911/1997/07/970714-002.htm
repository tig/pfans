scott-
i think you will find a sheetmetal shroud over the top of the oil cooler that
is hiding those 2 nuts.  if you check out the british pub on restoring your
own 911, it has good pics of engine teardown, and shows this shroud.  good
luck finding the bolts that hold the tin in place!

you may want to consider leaking rocker shafts as the source of your leak.  a
bad #6 exhaust rocker will leak in the same area as a bad cooler seal.  pull
the exhaust rocker cover off, and see if the rocker shafts are centered and
tight in their bores.
