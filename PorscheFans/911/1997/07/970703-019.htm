No authoritative answers here... but I'll chip in that it must depend on
WHAT in your brakes is overheating.  If you're getting classic "brake
fade", i.e. pad frictioin going away due to high temps... OR if you're
warping rotors (happened to me at Laguna)... then I think it is a rotor
temperature issue, you're probably energy limited, and the braking duty
cycle won't have a big effect.  If you're boiling fluid (pedal to the
floor), then it is a fluid/caliper temperature issue... and I could be
persuaded, though I'm not prepared to argue, that
time-of-contact-with-hot-rotor would play a role.  Along with about 20
other variables!... I have to guess we're talking about a pretty
small-percentage effect here, regardless of the mechanism involved.

just my 2 cents..
