sounds like the cotter pin that secures the 10mm nut on the end of the cable
was omitted.  this would allow the cable to loosen, and then the cable guide
could flop around.  on my car, the nut fell off, and all the p-brake bits
went for a tumble in the rotor!

when i first got my well-used 77, someone had secured these guide tubes to
the hydraulic steel lines with wire.  since properly setting the p-brake
adjustment, i haven't seen a need for this type of "fix".
