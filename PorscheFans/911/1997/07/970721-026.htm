Andy -

I'm not too sure what "brake venting" means, but your description of the
mechanism that retracts the brake piston when you release the pedal is
correct.  The square cross-section in the "O-ring" does the trick.  Pretty
cool, I think.

Another interesting design feature is the shape of the piston where it
contacts the pad.  Its not symmetric.  There is about a 150 degree section
removed from what would otherwise be a circular contact patch.  And the
orientation of this section relative to the rotor is crucial to proper
brake operation.  I'd like to know more about this design...
