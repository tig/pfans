Hi Spiros.

Tyre size depends on what you would like to do.  For autocross or track
205/55 and 225/50 on 7's and 8's will give you a nice firm ride with
very little squirming and give you a nice crisp turn in.

245/45's will fit on the 8's but will shift a bit when you set the car
into a corner.
