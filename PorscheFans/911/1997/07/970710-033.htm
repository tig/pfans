I agree with Thwen's comments. I put a set on my '88 coupe several
thousand miles ago and am VERY impressed with both  wet and dry
performance.  The tire design and construction involves a bunch of new
tire technology that was most recently introduced in the SO2. 
(I believe they're built on the same line). Ride and noise
characteristics are excellent and the tires are very affordable through
outfits like Tire Rack.  All that and they look Great too! All and all,
an excellent choice.
