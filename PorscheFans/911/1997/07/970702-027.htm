The master cylinder will not cause a car to pull so I would wait on that.  
It sounds like you either have a front suspension problem or your rear left 
brake is not working correctly (assuming your fronts are at this point).  I 
would pull off your left rear wheel and give the caliper and rotor a good 
looking over.  Also jack up the front end and pull the front wheels off 
(yea again) and look and the struts, and tie-rod's comparing the 2 sides 
for anything that looks different.  If you didn't bleed correctly you would 
have a mushy pedal and should know it.  Did you bleed right rear, left rear,
 right front and then left front in that order?  I doubt this is where the 
problem is.

Good luck!
