I finished the radio amplifier relocation today. The dealer had put it
under the passenger side footboard, right in the intake for the AC. I
moved it under the driver's seat. Plenty of room there, and there is a
slight gap between the front and rear carpets that makes a nice mounting
point. The AC seems to flow more air--the low fan speed is now useful.
