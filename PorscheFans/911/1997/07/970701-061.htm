If you just want to check how far off you are, grab your stopwatch and head
to your nearest interstate.  Hold a steady indicated 60 MPH, start the watch
at a mile marker, stop it at the next one.  You should cover the mile in 60
seconds if the speedo is accurate; for every additional second over 60 you
take, speedo is off by 1 MPH (good for +/- 5 MPH)  If you like formulas:

MPH = 3600/(seconds/mile)
