Alex,

I'm not convinced that the oil is the problem.  You may want to check if
you overfilled the oil tank causing oil to enter the airbox.  Pull the
plugs, inspect and replace with a fresh set if necessary.

Other things to consider.  Did you knock off a vacuum line during the oil
change?  Did you get a bad tank of gas?  How old is you O2 sensor
(disconnect it or replace it)?

From your description, I might agree that additional leaks showed up from
the synthetic and perhaps some burning.  I'm not certain that the poor
running resulted from the oil.
