Jason,

>I have removed the engine from my 1975 2.7 911 targa so I can respray the
>engine bay at the same time as the rest of the car. Does anyone know a
>procedure for removing the rubber trim that seals round the engine bay. And
>more importantly will I be able to get it back in again.

It slides out and the new one slides in.  You may need to pry the channel
open a bit if it has been pounded on.  Lubricant (dishsoap) can be helpful,
but you need more of it in the seal channel than on your hands if you're 
going to make much progress.  Altogther not a tough job if the motor is
out and you have a little patience.
