I think I can cover this one.
There are several, numbers anybody ? bushings that control the shifting
function.
There is also a knuckle or rather a "U" joint in-line beneath the shifter. 
Be aware that installing a short shift kit as a fix to another problem is BAD
BAD BAD.
Have someone check out the entire shifter assembly linkages etc. BEFORE adding
anything it will
make diagnosing any future problems much easier not having to think about the
short shift kit possibly
being the culprit. My .02 Next.....
