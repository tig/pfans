Fellow list members,

All of the lists I belong to have had various connectivity problems in
the last couple of days.

Apparently, there have been some major problems with the rootDNS servers
that most internet traffic flows through. Many domain cache entries
became corrupt, especially .com and .net.

These DNS problems have had widespread consequences to internet traffic,
and probably go a long way towards explaining the recent connectivity
issues.

Follow the "root nameserver problems" thread on NANOG for more info.
