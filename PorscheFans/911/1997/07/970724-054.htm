Alex,
AC is not the strong suite of these cars.  Make sure that your system is
fully charged by checking to see that the ball floats in the sight glass
on the receiver-dryer, inside the LF fender.

You may want to install the improved bulkhead vent from Performance Aire
in Anaheim. It significantly improves air flow. To get an idea, just
remove the T-shaped diverter from the bulkhead below the dash, in front
of the console.

Also, I found that the radio amp on my car was installed under the pax
side footboard, directly in the AC cabin air intake. Relocating the amp
under the driver's seat seemed to help some.
