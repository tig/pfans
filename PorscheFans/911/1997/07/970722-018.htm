I have a 77 2.7S in my '73 911T.  I eneded up putting on an external oil 
cooler.  If your temps. are really never going over 220, then all I would 
recommend is going to synthetic oil (Mobil 1, etc..) and leave it at that.  
But unless you are fairly easy on the engine when you drive and live in a 
cold climate I would be suprised that you don't see much higher temps than 
220.  As far as your oil pressure you should see at least 50 at 5000 rpm 
with a warm/hot engine.  You should only see less than 20 when the engine 
is warm/hot and at idle or very low rpm.  If you are seeing less than 20 at 
5000rpm that's a very bad thing indeed.  I would verify your engine oil 
temp. gauge first off and then go from there.

Good luck!

Scott
