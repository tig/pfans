Yes, the bushings wear, and yes, they do affect throttle position.  There
are "bell-crank" bushings on the side of the trans, and a rubber element in
line with the pull bar.  On older cars, the rubber element breaks in two,
and leaves you with an engine at idle speed only.  The new design has a
steel capture feature, that gives you part throttle after the rubber
element breaks.
