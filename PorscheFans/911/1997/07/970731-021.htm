     Steve, PFans,
     
     
     Can't tell you anything about the other brand, but I installed a 
     Petronix breakerless ignitor in my '76 911S.  I found easier starts, 
     smoother idling, running, and a smoother top end.  "Feels like better 
     acceleration, but I never measured it.  However, my 911S has only 20K 
     miles on complete engine rebuild, so it probably isn't burning oil.  
     Installs in 15 minutes, with ordinary tools (including distributor 
     removal, replacement, & you will need a dynamic timing light).
     
     If your basic problem is oil fouling, A breakerless ignition, hotter 
     coil, etc. may give you a few more miles between plug changes, but the 
     basic problem will remain.  If a top-end rebuild is out of the 
     question for now, you might want to try an oil thickener (like STP) as 
     a temporary remedy.
     
     Good luck,
