While we are on the subject a way to check a 76 or later CIS for a leak =
down problem would be to jump the contacts for the air vane shut off =
switch at the fuel pump relay.  This will force the pump to run while =
the ignition is on and the engine isn't turning over.  If your hot start =
problem goes away you can figure that the problem is pressure dropping.  =
This is actually part of the pressure leak down test for the system.  On =
75 and older CIS systems the pump runs anytime the ignition is on.  =
There is a control piston in side the fuel distributor that meters fuel =
to the injectors and it will route incoming fuel back out a tank return =
line if the air vane is at rest and the piston is down.
