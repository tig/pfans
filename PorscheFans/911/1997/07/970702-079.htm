How old are the brake hoses? and when were they last replaced? A similar
problem occurred on another car list. The brake hoses sometimes collapse
on the inside; when the brake is pushed fluid is forced through the
hoses, pushing the pads out and braking, however, on release not all the
fluid can flow back to the master cylinder and thus release the brakes
properly. This causes them to bind, the fluid might eventually flow back
until till the brakes are pressed again.

This problem might be worsened as a result of track driving? but I am
not sure of that.
