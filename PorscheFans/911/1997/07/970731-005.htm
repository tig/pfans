Brad:

As long as your trailer is an "open" one, no problem!  For a short time, I
towed my 911 in an 18' enclosed trailer with a similar Explorer..... Not
good!  Ford shows a bit higher towing capacity than other makes because
they also specify a maximum frontal area of the trailer.

General advice regarding towing:
-An equalization hitch will make the whole package very stable if set up
properly.  Worth the $'s in my opinion.
-Get a good brake controller and take the time to adjust it to the load.
The  Explorer does NOT have sufficient brakes to adequately stop with a
loaded  trailer.
