If you install a tail that was an option for '85 (the Carrera tail),
then I don't see any non-originality issues. OTOH, putting a multi-wing
IMSA tail on the car certainly wouldn't look original. I recommend using
only factory parts for the wing. While aftermarket copies are less
money, all of those that I have seen are markedly inferior in quality
and appearance. My car's PO had an OEM wing installed by a good body
shop. It cost close to $1300 which included painting with Glausurit
paint to match the car
