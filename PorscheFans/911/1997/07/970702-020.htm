One trick I use to always ensure that I am re-filling the engine (any
engine)
with the correct amount of oil is before I add the new oil to the
engine, I
pour the used oil into 1 gallon milk jugs (our recycle folks take it in
these).
Using the milk jugs, I can determine within a 1/2 quart or so how much
oil came out of the engine.  I then add this much oil back.  Voila!
Right
on the mark.  I usually just have to add a little to make up for what
was
in the filter, etc.
