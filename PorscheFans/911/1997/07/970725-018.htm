I put new flywheel bolts in when I had the flywheel out for my clutch job.  I
didn't use any locktight.  You shouldn't need to with new bolts.  Just torque
them to the proper specs.  I think the reason for new bolts is that the bolts
are designed to strech a bit when they are torqued and after that, they are
no good (Is this the truth?)  In any event, I haven't had any problems and
it's been about 15k mi. and 3 track events since the job.
