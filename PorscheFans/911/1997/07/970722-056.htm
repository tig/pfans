   All Vehicles competing in the PCA Club Racing Program must be equipped=

with an approved roll bar. The roll bar must be securely mounted to the
floor ( metal to metal ) with the top of the main hoop two inches above t=
he
helmet of the seated driver.It must be full cockpit width and braced. An
inspection hole 3/16" must be drilled for verification of tube thickness.=

Any portion which may come in contact with the drivers' helmet must be
padded.
   The full text of the roll bar specs may be found in Appendix A of the
!997 PCA Club Racing Rules.
   For an '84 Carrera, the bar must be constructed of Mild steel 1.75"
diam. x .095 thickness or 1.50 diam. x .120 thickness. An Alloy steel bar=

requires a 1.50" dim. x .095 thickness.
   If you are contemplating going PCA racing, Check out the rules BEFORE
you install the roll bar. =
