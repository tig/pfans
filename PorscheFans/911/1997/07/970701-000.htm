Don't bother putting a mirror on the passenger side. You can't see out
of it, the door/ windscreen pillar is in the way. My '68 911T has
mirrors on both sides, cheap and nasty black square mirrors, which will
be replaced by one round chrome one on the drivers side when the car is
repainted and the holes can be covered. The reason there are holes in
the door for a mirror is for right hand drive cars (which is what mine
is).
