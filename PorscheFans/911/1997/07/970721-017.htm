The tape from Steve Maglione is from 3M.  I found out about this originally
after reading an article in AOPA which described the product and process.  
Again in my case I was working with 18 foot long sections for the leading
edges of my airplanes wings so lubrication was necessary to get the

and rear quarters for my 75 which became $220 when they sent me the
