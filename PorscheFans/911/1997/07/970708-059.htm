THBBB, nevertheless, the tail can be removed. Some on the list have done
this and they can give you the details of how. There are some cautions and
the lid will no doubt need repainting.

The tail is very effective in reducing lift when coupled with the front air
dam--it was not designed or applied for show. Second, it helps cool the
engine substantially and will assist your A/C condenser.

Is it ostentatious and "in your face"? You bet.
