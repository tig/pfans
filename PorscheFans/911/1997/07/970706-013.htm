In answer to posts on how to identify existence of limited slip, the
following info from the pocket size Porsche "tech" books might be of
interest. !987 Carreras had the G50, or type 950 transmissions. For the USA
cars, this should be 950/01 "type" numbers. The serial nunber, located on the
bottom of the case ( at least it is on my type 915 trans), would be 74H00001
to 20000 for USA spec 1987's.  If the car has limited slip, there is a "12"
between the "type" and "serial numbers".
Another way is to have the back of the car up on jacks , and hand spin one of
the rear tires. Th opposite wheel will spin in the same direction if limited
slip; opposite direction if not. 
Hope this helps!
