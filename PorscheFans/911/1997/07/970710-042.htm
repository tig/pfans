>I never saw any replies to the original poster's message.  Is there an
>easy way to repaint the center of the Fuchs alloys so that you get a
>nice crisp line that seperates the rim from the center painted section?
>(ie a masking template).  I don't want to even try to mask off a perfect
>circle with masking tape...

Believe it or not, it's easier to mask off than you think.  3M makes a
special masking tape (about .5 inches wide) that is made to conform
to curved surfaces unlike regular masking tape.  Most auto-refinishing
or auto paint supply stores sell it.

I've painted two sets of wheels, masking the centers like this, and
it wasn't difficult or time-consuming and to look at the wheels, you'd
never know it wasn't factory (other than the fact that I painted the
centers of one set orange...) :>)
