I am a lucky 914 owner, so can't comment 100% on the 70's 911 cousins,
but you might look inside the door where the threaded mirror
reinforcement should be. On 914s it was common on the passenger side
even if no mirror was mounted by the factory. If so blessed, you merely
have to punch the holes from the inside (gently, gently) and drill them
out. I forget who suggested this first.
