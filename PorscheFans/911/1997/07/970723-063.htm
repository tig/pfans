You should be able to pick up the whole car by the tow hooks.  Remember,
these cars have German engineering, and are very strong.  I would be
careful of pulling directly down on the tow hooks, because they are
designed for pulling the car horizontal to the ground.  Limit the downward
angle to 30 degrees from horizontal, and you will have not trouble.  Even
if they are pulled too hard, they will bend rather than break.  I you ever
find them bent, straighten them back, and find a better way.
