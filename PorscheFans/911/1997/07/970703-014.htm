I just installed SSI's and a Bursch 2-into-1 muffler on my '78 SC. I
definitely get mild backfiring upon lifting off the throttle. Actually its
not a backfire. More of a spitting noise. I don't mind that effect. My
problem is the sound of the muffler. It makes my car sound like a Japanese
super bike. I wonder if this is due to the Bursch 2-into-1 or the SSI's.
There is also a nasty rasp if you get particularly aggressive with the
throttle. Specially above 3000 RPM.  I just removed a newer Bursch 1-into-1
muffler and that had a much nicer deeeper V8ish sound. Also not as loud
unless you floored the throttle.  Anyone have any recommendations for a nice
sport muffler with a decent sound? Thanks
