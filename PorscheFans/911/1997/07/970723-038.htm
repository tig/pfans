Joe-

To change the mounts:

1.  Support the weight of the engine with a floor jack
2.  Remove the large bolt in the center of the mount 
3.  Remove the two smaller bolts that secure the mount
4.  Remove the old mounts
5.  Put everything back in reverse order

Repeat the same procedure for the transmission mounts.  To replace the
rear engine mounts, you don't even need to get under the car.

This is a very straight forward procedure...it only took me a half hour
to change all 4.
