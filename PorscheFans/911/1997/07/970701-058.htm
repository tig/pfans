The oil cooler is in front of the right wheel directly behind the
headlamp. That thing behind the license plate is part of the AC. Those
two pipes are carrying the oil to the cooler in the fender.

It doesn't sound like you have a problem. Remember that there are 13
quarts of oil. It takes a while to get it all warmed up.
