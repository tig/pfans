Terry, 
from my experience your problem likely will reappear because the old foam at
the fire wall will keep disintegating.  The solution is a new  pad. I chose
the Dynamat "Hoodliner" thermodynamic pad which has a reflective aluminum
layer at its rear facing surface (keeps heat away from the interior of the
car). I found that product reduced interior (cab) noise by about 2-3dB in the
100hz region over the stock Porsche replacement. I also installed a different
Dynamat product on the inside (cab side) engine wall, which reduced the noise
another db or two.
