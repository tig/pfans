Mike:

  The Monty muffler is a beautifully made Stainless Steel muffler made 
in Australia. Generally speaking, they add no power but they are still
a good upgrade due to their build quality, longevity, appearance and
sound.

  The SSI Stainless Steel heat exchanger systems is the most efficient
exhaust you can install on a street-type 911. This system, installed on
a 911SC or Carrera (USA versions) is usually good for a 15-18 HP 
increase over the stock single-inlet systems. They are however, NOT 
LEGAL due to the removal of the catastrophic converter. Some areas have 
smog testing every two years and some folks exchange systems, at that 
time, to pass the tests.
