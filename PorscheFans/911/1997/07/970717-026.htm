>Can somebody please describe how to adjust the rear camber on my '87 911?
>I've got a simple laser device to measure the setting, but from inspecting
>the various eccentric bolts can't figure out how to make an adjustment.
>My current setting is about 30 minutes negative, and I'd like to try some
>additional negative camber for AutoX and track events.

Loosen the 2 19mm nuts on the rear of the spring plate
Turn the camber eccentric nut (the rearmost one, the other changes toe)

Your toe setting will change when you change camber so you'll
need to set toe also and it becomes an iterative process setting
camber and toe until you get it where you want it.
Torque everything to spec when you're done.
