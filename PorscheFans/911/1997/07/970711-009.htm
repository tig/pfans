Scott:
The tube you see is a drain from the CIS air box under the air cleaner.   
 You'll get oil out of it if you overfill the engine.  You can get the   
CIS out off the car without pulling the engine ( I just did it, see my   
recent post).  There is a plastic cover over the top of the cooler which   
needs to come off to get to the top bolts.
Before you do anything else I'd suggest cleaning under the air cleaner   
and running the engine at mid dip stick.  Could be you don't have a leak.
