In my old car, the attachment for the belts were on the rear shelf.  A
large eye bolt and a suitable backing plate was used on each side.  I
was able, with help from a friend, to remove the eye bolts with the
engine in the car.  I then used clip in belts.  This worked good for
Drivers Ed, but I would not do this for racing.  One advantage this
has is in the event of an accident, the belts are level with the
shoulders.  When your body is forced forward, the belts wont try and
pull you down and compress the spine.
