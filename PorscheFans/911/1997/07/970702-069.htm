George:  The radiator at the front of the car is probably your aux. A/C
radiator.  The hoses for that go down the left side of the vehicle under the
rocker.  Your oil cooler is in the right front fender with the hoses running
down the right side rocker.  Don't worry, it takes awhile for 13 quarts of
oil to warm up.
