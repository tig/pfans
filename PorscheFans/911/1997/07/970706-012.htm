Hi P-Fans:
I seen some of the recent posts on rev limits of the 3.2 Carrera motor.
Certainly there is some valve train limit, but I think there should be more (
at least equal) concern of the rod bolt loading.  Earlier 911 engines had
stronger, 10 mm big-end rod bolts, and the 3.2's used a 9mm bolt, with less
"margin" of strength.  These motors have reputed long life "unless over
revved"- I wouldn't want to push things here unless there were some serious
bolt upgrades done- like Raceware bolts.
