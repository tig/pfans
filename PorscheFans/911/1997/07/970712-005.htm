Can't specifically help you with your SC, but since my '75 is very
similar...

Clutch cable is no big deal to replace. Basically there is a clevis at
the pedal end in the tunnel, which has a little clip holding it in
place. Disconnect there, take off the clevis, and pull out the back.
Installation is reverse (ie just slide it through the sleeve it runs
in). Refit the clevis (try and get it approximately in the same relative
position, refit the clip, adjust at transmission end (this is where your
car and mine vary a fair bit) and then its Labatt's time.....

Total should be under 2 hours....
