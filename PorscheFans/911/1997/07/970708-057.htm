Merlin....the accuracy of the stock electronic boost guage in the 930 is
questionable.  I have both the stock guage and a Ruf vacuum/boost guage in
place of the clock. The electronic guage reads significantly higher than the
mechanical one, ie .5 bar on the Ruf guage is 1 bar on the factory
guage....my mechanic says to use the Ruf reading.  SD
