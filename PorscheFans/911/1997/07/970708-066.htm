Nill,
  The way I use it, and I believe there may have been a service bulletin is
to wait until i'm in the later cycle of an oil change, then I put 20 oz. of
the real Techron (not the ProGuard w/Techron), which I buy in 6-Paks from
the back of Pano, used to get from Imparts, into a full tank of gas.  I only
use Chevron Premium which already has a little techron in it.  

Then when this full tank is down to 1/2, I re-fill the tank with gas.  I
think the service bulletin recommends only once or twice per oil change.

Years ago I had a car that died from valve deposits, and the mechanic
recommended Techron to prevent carbon and other buildups.
