Large Allen head nut, loosen filler nut first.

>3.  The engine seems to like a temp of 250 deg.  Upon starting and 
engine cool, oil pres builds up to 40 >or 60.  At 200 + temp, pres 
drops to 20 and then zero on the gauge.  What do I look for here or is 
this >"normal" ?

250 is too high, 180-210 is normal, 230 getting hot...  Rule of thumb for oil
pressure is 10-15psi for every 1000 rpms.   Change the oil first if you
haven't done so already.  With an unknown quantity like an auction car, I'd
do a complete tune-up of plugs, filters,  fluids, valve adjustment etc 
since I assume
you got no records with the car.
