Subject:Porsche wins Pikes Peak, again
Date:Sat, 5 Jul 1997 10:27:51 PDT
Jeff Zwart from Woodland Co, won his class for a 4th time at yesterday's
75th running of the Pikes Peak International Hillclimb. Zwart, driving a
'97 993 Turbo-S, won the High-performance 4wd division over more
traditional rally cars such as the Escort RS200, and the Subaru Impreza
WRX from Japan.

In the Open class, Steve Beddor drove his Ruf CTR to a second place
finish behind the factory backed Hyundai of Paul Choiniere. David Beddor
in the second Ruf CTR finished 4th.
