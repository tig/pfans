======================================================Per Spec book:
                                              max. diff left to right
Front   toe-in     0
           camber   0 +/- 20'                    20'
           caster      6 45' +/- 45'              30'

Rear    camber   -30' to -1 10'

Per 1968 Porsche factory guide for "Vehicles used for Sport Purposes"

          Front   toe-in     0
                    camber   -20' to -1 0'                20'
                    caster      6 45' +/- 45'              30'

     Rear    camber   -1 20' to -1 50'
                rear toe     0
