Nill Toulme  writes:

>Does anybody have the factory tech bulletin on proper use of Techron
>injector cleaner? Or any other lore on its use?

I think the official factory instructions are something like add a
bottle
of Techron when filling the tank and then run the car until the tank is
half empty (or half full - depending on your viewpoint) and then fill
it up with gas again.

I tend to skip the filling up the tank when it gets half empty part.

I had a fuel injector become clogged about a year ago at a time
trial.  Once we figured out that the problem was only a clogged
fuel injector and not some terminal 2.7 liter engine problem, I 
added a big bottle of Techron to about 10 gallons of gas and
drove the car for about 20 miles.  Problem solved!!!  Either I got
lucky and whatever was clogging the injector decided to give
up or the Techron really worked....
