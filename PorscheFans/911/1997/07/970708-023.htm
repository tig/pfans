Just a follow up note to my message on tires.

1) I have gotten in the habit of ordering my tires throught the mail. I use
Discount Tires at 1-800-385-3322. They have always had good availability and
some of the best prices I have found.

2) I have received some requests for the model of Dunlop tires I am running.

I am using the SP Sport 4000 on my 911 and am using D60 A2 tires on my
Infiniti and Saab. I have been really pleased with the performance of both
tires and find them much better than the POS I had from Goodyear.
