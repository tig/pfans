J Muller, sounds to me like the fan motor is drawing too much amperage
on high so it heats up the fuse or the circuit breaker if someone has
replaced the fuse. The c. b. should reset itself if this is the problem.

Porsche for many years put both source wires for the a/c on one fuse,
listed as no. 2 I believe on the fuse box cover. One these wires is for
the fan on the front condenser ( a red w/green ) the other covers the
evap. fan and the clutch (red w/ white ) If the fuse has been replaced
with a circuit breaker then this needs to be a 40 amp. c. b. 

The choice of compressor has nothing to do with a wire getting hot nor
does the level of charge in the a/c.
