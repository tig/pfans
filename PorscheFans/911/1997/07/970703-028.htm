Joe:

I humbly submit that the answer to your question is much similar than you
hypothesize.  Maximum rpms for a downshift are qhatever rpms put the
engine at or close to red line in the lower gear.  Minimum rpms for upshift,
if you're really driving the thing, should be at about 4500 rpms, although
it's really a shame to shift there because the engine is just coming into
the power band at that point..
