Just to add our 2 cents here, and my aplologies to Andy W. :^)

Heads: Larger ports but same valve sizes
Cams:  Way different!
Rods:   Nitrided surface treatment
Distributor: Different advance curve
FI:       Different pump, Throttle bodies and stacks are also larger
Pistons: Forged pistons with 8.5:1 CR (T had cast pistons and 7.5:1)

Mechanically speaking, all other engine pieces are the same besides the
fan shroud color. Not cheap to convert. FI alone can cost over $3000 to
rebuild and resize to "S" specs.
