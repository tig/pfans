For my other cars, I've managed to find sources for factory parts
that offer a substantial discount over typical dealer prices. For the
Z3 there is Quality Imports in Florida that sells genuine BMW parts 
at very near wholesale prices and will ship anywhere in the U.S. 
without tacking on an additional handling charge. For Toyota I had 
Cabe Toyota in Long Beach that basically did the same. What they lost 
in markup, they made up for in volume. Does anyone know of a similar 
source for Porsche 911 parts? There seem to be plenty of excellent 
sources for aftermarket parts but what do you do when you need the 
genuine article?
