The Porsche Technical Specifications book for the 78 through 81 models
specifies that these bolts be tightened to 42 NM in the 8mm size used in
the SC's and 83 NM in the 10 mm size that is used in the turbo's.  This is
on page 81, about half way up the page and called "axle shafts to
transmission and drive shaft.  It is termed a "filister head bolt" which I
am not sure about, but sometimes these translations get a little off the
path.
