Since my car was running hot, I ended up installing a MOCAL front oil
cooler from Florida. (Around $665 for complete kit with hoses and thermostat)

My (2.7 911 Targa) car was running at around 250 degrees normal (with the
thermal reactors).
Now, with the front  oil cooler.. The car is running at 210-220 degrees in
stop-n-go traffic.
The car is much more pleasant to drive now.

I'll keep everyone updated after I remove the thermal reactors. I hope to
bring it down even more.
