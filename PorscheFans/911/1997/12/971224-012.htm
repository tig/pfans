Seems like your tach problems, and some of the others are interelated.  =
The first thing that comes to mind is a poor ground serving the dash =
panel, or in your case possibly at the bus (looks like a fuse panel, and =
gangs the ground circuits).  Could also be at the bus (fuse panel) that =
serves 12V+ to these circuits.  Indications, when testing with VOM 12+ =
is indicated till you energize circuit (I.E. open door) then voltage =
drops.  The reason is resistance.  The circuit heats and acts like a =
inline resister.  The cause, corrodided connection.  You can prove this =
out by jumping a good ground and 12V+ to the item in question and =
observing the result.   Hope this helps you.
