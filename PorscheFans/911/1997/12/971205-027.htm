Brad.  It is very common to do this, especially for track applications
where hard use can cause the weights to go astray.
On the street, however, it is really unnecessary particularly if you do
not like the looks of it.  I put tape on the weights that are on the
inside of my wheels, and leave those on the outside rim alone.  The tape
gets a bit old looking after awhile.

But yes, "all the racers do it".
