The battery I got for my SC came charged. Also, AJ-USA in San Diego
makes a neat installation bracket that eliminates the need to drill any
additional holes. It is essentially a piece of aluminum plate, slightly
longer than the battery. It bolts to the bottom of the battery, and in
turn is held down by the stock battery retention clamp.
