"Clunk" could be something as simple as loose front insert.  On my car (72 911 
with Konis) the insert (that's what some call the shock 'cause it fits inside 
the strut tube) is screwed in via threads on the top of the strut tube.  These 
can loosen (its happened to me).  If one does, you will get a clunk that 
behaves as you describe.
