A bad head temp. sensor can give an intermittent problem like this. It
is the top (white) plug on the left side of the engine. Should be about
2000 to 3000 ohms cold & about 350 ohms hot, but the other problem is
the ohms can checkout OK and still give you the intermittent problem.
