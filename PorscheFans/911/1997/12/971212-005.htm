I hate to drag out an off topic thread but the transverse torsion 
bar/trailing arm suspension commonly found in armored vehicles was 
not developed nor perfected by Porsche. Walter(?) Christie, an 
American developed/perfected this system in the early '30s. When the 
US Army showed little interest in his design, he wound up selling it 
to the Soviets.
