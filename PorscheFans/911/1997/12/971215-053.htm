George:
I haven't had this problem, but according to the manual there is a relay
controling the blower.  Fuse #1 powers the coils to the relay and the
contacts powering the blower, so you are really dropping out the relay
when you pull the fuse.  The blower may be fine.  The relay is by the
blower on the side of the of the engine compartment., seems to be a dual
coil 8 conductor relay. The red wire from  fuse 1 end up as red wires
going to pins 2,3, and 8 (coils).  Hope this helps.
