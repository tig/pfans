Todd:

I have a '74 and use 15x7 cookie cutters (911 or early 944) front and
rear.  205/60R15 BFG R1 will bolt on with no mods.  225/50R15 will
require rolling the fender lips.  225/50 is tight and you should do a
trial fit first. 
With 225/50s, you may need to run at least 1.5 deg negative camber since
the
extra inch of 7" wheels is to the outside.  Running same tire size
front/rear and rotating between each event significantly improves tire
life. 

Hope this helps,
