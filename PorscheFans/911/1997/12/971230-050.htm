The Optima battery thread has resurfaced, and the product has my
enthusiastic support--I wouldn't use anything else in a 911.  One
caveat, however: the terminals are more exposed and closer together than
on a normal battery, and during installation it's entirely possible
that, as happened with me once, the handle of your wrench will connect
them!  In that event, kick it off pronto!
