<< Hello,
 I have tried to rotate the tachometer in my 87 911 coupe to get the 6 up
 at the 12:00 o'clock position.  Many helpful owners have said you just pry
 up the rubber circle around the face, pull the tach forward and make sure
 the wires in back are long enough to allow the rotation.  Then rotate the
 dial and push it back in.  I have tried using screwdrivers, butter knives
 and fingernails to pry it out without success.  Anyone have any idea how
 to accomplish this "little" fix. 
 TIA,
 
 Carl >>

Go in through the luggage compartment.  Unsnap the carpet and reach through
and carefully push the tachometer outward to "break" the rubber seal free from
the dash face.  You can also put some silicon spray or rubber lube around the
ring (gasket) to help removal.
