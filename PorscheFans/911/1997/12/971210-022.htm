It's impossible to NOT get water in the doors.  That's what the drain holes
in the bottom are for.  However, Porsche doesn't necessarily make the
optimum number of holes, so I have always added some on my cars, usually
drilling between the factory holes, as close to the seam as possible.  Then
I clean out the doors as well as possible, scrape down the metal/paint/rust,
and paint the inside, bottom and up 3"-4" on the sides, with POR 15 rust
preventative paint.  I've never had a door rust out after doing that to them.
