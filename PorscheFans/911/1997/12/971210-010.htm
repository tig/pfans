A good book from the standpoint of Ferry Porsche's view of events is a book
that I think is probably out of print.  Title is _We_at_Porsche_, and its
subtitle is " the autobiography of Dr. Ing.h.c. Ferry Porsche, with John
Bentley as co-author.  It was published in '76, and the first 2/3rds of the
book deal with WWII and before.  Its ISBN is 0-385-05309-6.  Good luck in
finding it.
