Thom,

Spokane is part of the great Inland Empire Porsche Club.  That chapter
covers a large geographic area and the leadership tends to move from place
to place over the years.  The current president and many of the leaders live
in Spokane and they are a most active, friendly and knowledgeable group. 
You will find everything from first rate Speedster restorations (as in first
time in the rain ever last summer during a club event in the Tri-Cities
about 150 miles south and west of Spokane.) to Boxters and everything in
between.  As I recall there were about 60 Porsches for our last summer
dinner at the Columbia Crest winery at Patterson, WA, about 30+- from
Spokane and a similar number from the Tri-Cities.  There are two road track
facilities and also several crazy formula "V" jocks in Spokane as well.  I
lived there for 19 years and doubt if you will find it as much of a
"paradigm shif" as you might imagine.  Welcome!
