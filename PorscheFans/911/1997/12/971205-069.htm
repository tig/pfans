If you do track events, you should consider taping the wheel weights.  But
don't use duct tape.  This stuff will turn to goo very quickly.  Get some
high-temp furnace tape.  It's basically an aluminum foil with high temp
adhesive on it.  Not very stick feeling, but I ran 4 events last year and
didn't lose any weights.

Note that the reason you lose wheel weights is more due to brake heat than to
forces generated by the wheel spinning.
