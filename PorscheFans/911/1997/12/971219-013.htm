     You may remember that a couple weeks ago I posted a memo looking for
advice on an oil leak in my 73 T.

     For what it is worth, here is what I have found (so far).

     Now that the car is on jack stands for the winter, I removed the MFI
air filter housing and found a good bit of oil on top of the engine.
Poking and probing revealed that the cold start thermo switch had vibrated
loose to only finger tight.  Having applied a bit of thread sealer it is
now securely in place and I am awaiting the spring season to see if that
solves the problem.  Incidentally, I have disconnected the wire because the
car is driven mainly in warm weather and I am apprehensive about the
thought of raw gasoline heading down the stack in the event that a backfire
drives flames up the stack.  Any thoughts from the group on this?
