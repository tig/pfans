Brad,

I have a 70 911 T with a 901 and 205/60 -15 tires on 15 x 6 Fuchs.  At
4000 RPM in 5th I am going about 88 mph (calculated, indicated is a few
mph faster).  My 5th gear is 29:22 and my ring and pinion is 7:31.  4000
RPM in 4th is about 72 mph.  My 4th gear is 27:25.

Porsche offers many different gear sets ,at least I know they did back
when our cars were built, so your gear ratios may be as delivered from
the factory.  If you pick up a copy of the technical specifications
booklet for your car, it will list the various gearsets which were
available.  I think if you get cardex info on your car it may reveal
whether the car came with optional gear ratios.

I hope this helps some.

Juergen
