Steve,

Last months European Car had an article with the first phase of a resto
on an old Alfa.  The guy rented a blasting booth and then ordered some
kind of plastic media.  This media did not eat metal, just paint, bondo,
and rust.  He did the outside and inside of a shell in about a day for
the grand cost of about $100 for the booth and $200 for the media.  That
will save you the estimated 25 hrs. x $65=$1625.  But, before you blast
it, remove all the stuff you want removed like lights, glass, bumpers,
etc....  when you are finished blasting everything tow it the paint
shop.  With luck you can remove about half of their labor costs.  When
you get it back you will have to reassemble everything yourself.  This
self prep will also allow you to inspect your car throughout and notice
any areas that might have gotten eaten etc. and clean anything you feel
like cleaning.

I will be going this route with my '82 SC as I have counted 2 different
paint jobs and they are both cracking.  Not to mention all the chips.
No dents though!
