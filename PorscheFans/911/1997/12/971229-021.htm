I replaced most of the components that wear out on my 76 911s.  It took a
while, but I did alot of other stuff also (new gas tank, repainted the trunk,

bars.  I also repainted the arms, installed a bump steer kit and a new master
cylinder.

Tie rod replacement was pretty easy, I upgraded to turbo tie rods and
recommend that others do the same.
