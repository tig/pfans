When I got my 71 911T back in 1982 it was full of water due to the rear
window having been smashed, It was replaced but the water still came in.It
took me some time to find out it was collecting  in the lower corners of the
window and running in under the trim and rear seats then collecting in the
foot wells.
If you think this is what is causing your problem I suggest you remove the
rear shelf trim and have someone run a hose on the rear window.

good luck
