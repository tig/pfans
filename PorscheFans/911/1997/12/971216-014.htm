Nill,
 If your car's toolkit is complete, changing the fan belt is an
easy job (in the daylight at least). Your toolkit should have 
the special spanner wrench, offset wrench and fan belt. These
belts (AC & fan) are so cheap there is no reason not to change 
them in the comfort of your garage every couple of years.
