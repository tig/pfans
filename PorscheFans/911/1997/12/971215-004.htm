Check the cable that controls the heater boxes. This has to be done from the bottom
of the car. There is just one cable that hooks through the heat leaver and connects to
each side of the car close to the heat exchangers. Also you will need to check the
hoses that connect the heat exchangers to the heater boxes. 

The cable requires that the hand brake be removed and that pretty much requires the
seat(s) to be taken out. Be sure to grease the cable when you put the new one in.
