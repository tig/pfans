Well as promised, here's the report on the new Targa top.

The old top from my '74 Traga was torn and tattered, leaking
heavily in the middle + edge seals.  The old top was giving me
great company playing wind songs and whistling thru' the gaps.

The options were to follow Poor Mans Porsche DIY or buy 
materials for my upholsterer or have him buy and complete the job.  

Went with option three, Almaden Upholsterey in San Jose purchased 
a great black canvas top material.  Took them just one day to 
complete and looks and performs like a factory fit.  No more music
from the top or raining inside.

And all this only for USD 300.
Now the grin is really painful :)
