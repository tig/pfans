Mark, I've done it both ways on my old '76 911S, '84 Turbo-Look, and now
my '95 993.  To jack up entire rear, put some padding on your jack pad
(I have used piece of old inner tube or block of 1/4 -1/2" pine), then
position
it under the engine approx. just forward of the drain plug. You can jack up
the whole back in this manner.
