Like any potential used Porsche purchase, make sure you get a pre-purchase
inspection by a qualified mechanic. And make sure that mechanic checks the
valve guides. There are known problems associated with premature valve guide
wear on 3.2L motors. Otherwise they are quite reliable. No, they don't have
power steering (think of it a good arm exercise) and no, they don't have
anti-lock brakes.
