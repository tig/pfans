>I recently purchased a bump steering kit from automotion. My car has been
>lowered. I was wondering if anyone has used the kits and if they really do
>make a difference. Also, do they change the camber-castor etc. and will I have
>to realign the car.

I assume you're talking about one of the steering rack spacer kits?
Installing these spacers won't affect your caster or camber , but can
affect your toe-in (it affected mine quite a bit, but I lowered the car at the
same time I installed the spacers, so I don't know to what extent the
spacers themselves will affect the toe).

If you're not going to mess with the ride height, I would hazard a
guess that the change in toe will be relatively minor and that you can
certainly drive the car without fear of chewing up tires or veering off
into ditches all the time...
