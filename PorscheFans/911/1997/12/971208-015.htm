To Denny Lindsay re oxygen sensor

I have replaced the sensor on my 86 Carrera twice (100,000 miles) and never
reset anything.  Also, I use the 2 wire cheaper Bosch sensor thus
eliminating the third wire which serves only to heat the sensor and allow
federal emision requirements to be satisfied.  The two wire is about
$40-60.00 versus $120.00 for the three wire porsche part.  Also, I run my 87
930 turbo with the oxygen sensor disconnected--does fine on the street and
on the track.  Neither car has a catalytic converter.  When you replace the
sensor, make sure you have a six point 7/8ths socket for the sensor--don't
rely only a 12 point socket to do the job.

Good luck

Lynn Whelchel
