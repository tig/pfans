An early 912 conceivably could have had a power antenna when new--they were
available in the early years.  If the antenna is on the left fender, it was
a manual.  If it was on the right side, it was a power antenna.

Blaupunkt (a division of Robert Bosch) made antennas back then, and I've
seen a 912 with a Bosch logo on the base of its antenna.  Of course, many
cars had Hirschman antennas.  I think Performance Products sells Hirschman
manual antennas for just over $ 20, and Automotion has a very similar
Harada antenna for around $ 14.
