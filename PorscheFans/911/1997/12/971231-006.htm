Two companies that make drop-in replacements are Pioneer (4x6s for the rear
deck) and a/d/s (a wide range of products which are costly but worth it). 

I just put a/d/s 525is speakers in the doors of my '87 Targa, and the 4x6
Pioneers in the rear deck. I haven't found a good spot yet for a
sub-woofer, though, and I'm not thrilled about body or interior panel
modifications.
