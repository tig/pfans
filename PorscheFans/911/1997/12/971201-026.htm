John:

This was a chronic problem with all earlier 911's.  It comes from the front
of the driver's door window frame.  Over the years, they added a small rubber
block gasket that goes at the forward edge of the base of the driver's window
frame where it joins the door.  (Oddly enough, the passenger side doesn't
seem to be as bad, it might be amplified by the driver's mirror).  You might
want to check to see if your's has it...presumably, you could get one from
Stoddard's or Tweeks since they usually carry body rubber replacements.
