Hi Kieth,
If the wire coming off the fuse block for the cruise is not making good
contact, the cruise will act dead. Yours could be and intermittent
contact. Check the CC wire coming off the bottom of the fuse box in the
trunk. If you don't know which fuse, let me know and I'll look it up for
you.

If you saved the old H/L flasher control, it is often repairable. Clean
it with some electrical contact cleaner, and then tweak the brass reed
contacts to get the correct function.
