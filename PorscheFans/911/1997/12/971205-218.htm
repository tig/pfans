I purchased the complete Eezi-Bleed system, but found a better answer
before I even used it once.  I replaced my stock bleeder screws with
Speed Bleeders.  These look just like regular bleeder screws, but they
have a check-valve built in.  This allows fluid to flow out on the down
stroke, but allows no air in on the up stroke.  They work as advertised -
both of my cars are now thusly equipped.  Contact Speed Bleeder at (630)
739-4620 or speedbleeder@super-highway.net.
