No, the engine doesn't have to come out, but you need a heck of a long
Allen wrench to fit the upper starter bolt.  I went up to Sears and bought
the longest 10mm Allen wrench they had, and used this with a ratchet
wrench with a 10mm socket to get at the upper mount.  Cutting the wrench 
was larely a trial & error procedure.  Things are tight up there!

I had similar symptoms as yours - cold starts OK, hot starts non-existent.
