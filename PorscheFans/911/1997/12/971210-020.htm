Hi Bryan:

Your injectors are held in place with rubber O-rings on the manifold
side and the fuel rail (with C-clips). In fact, if you disconnect the
fuel rail fittings, you would be able to pull them out (sometimes a
"hard pull"). Being able to turn your injectors in the manifold is
normal. If (when :-) you rebuild your injectors, the rebuild kit will
come with a number of new injector parts - including new O-ring's for
each injector.  

To remove them, disconnect the fuel rail fittings, and pull the
injectors out individually (3 per bank) or individually after removing
the rail itself from the injectors. Based on your description in your
post, you have nothing to worry about.
