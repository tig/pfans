With the recent quires on first time engine removal I thought I'd pass
on a tip I got from this list that worked great for me the 2nd time I
removed the engine.  I raised the car to a comfortable working height
and lowered the engine onto a dolly made up of 4x4's and casters.  With
this I could get the jack out from under the engine.  Then using a piece
of 4x6 as blocking I raised the back of the now lightened car such that
I could roll the intact engine and trans out from under the car.  Took a
total of 2hrs.
