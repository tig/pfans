Bill,

For your exhaust, give your car to a custom exhaust specialist, if it
doesn't already have dual exhaust, get it. Also, try putting a sound tip on
it.  In case there are people out there that don't know what it is, it is a
small piece of Chrome fitted to the end of your stock exhaust.  They run
about 8 dollars a piece, (US).  Also, go to a car parts store, like All-Car,
and pick up a Dyno-Max muffler for each pipe.  This puts a little rumble on
the street, but doesn't make it obnoxiously loud so they can hear you coming
for miles.
