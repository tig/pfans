The rev limiter is built into the rotor in the distributor.  It's pretty
ingenious -
basically a spring-loaded weight that stretches the spring the faster the
rotor spins and eventually contacts something that shorts the ignition (how's
that for a highly-technical explanation?).

I suppose it could fail.  Easy enough to check - just pull the rotor out and
play with it - it's pretty straighforward.  Or heck, put a new rotor/cap in and
be done with it.
