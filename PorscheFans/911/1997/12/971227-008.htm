Bruce, there is a possible solution.  I had similar problems with my old
'76 and '84 911s.  Found that in the parts manual/microfiche Porsche
has a special plug that fits these sockets.  So, you can buy a couple and 
replace all of the plugs on your accessories, or buy one and buy a standard
socket at a local auto parts store and wire up an adapter by joining the
two with wire.

The factory plug is an nice high quality unit; high quality fittings and 
two halves are bolted together.  I didn't have a problem with plug
wiggling out of socket with this unit.  Unfortunately, got rid of my manuals
 when sold above cars, so don't have part no. available.  A lister or the 
dealer should be able to help.
