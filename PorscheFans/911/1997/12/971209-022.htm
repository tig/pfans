Regarding lubricant for windshield seal:  I do not use any lubricant for
pressing aluminum trim into the seal prior to installation.  The thing
you want to avoid when installing the windshild into the car is the trim
popping out of the seal as you pull the rope.  Lubricant or soap on the
trip only makes this worse.  I do however use soap for the seal
conection to the car.
