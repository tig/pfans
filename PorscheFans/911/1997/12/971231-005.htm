 "John Rodriguez"  writes:

>I have a 1979 911SC. just removed the heat exchangers and installed a Bursch
>tuned header system, with and Ansa exhaust, 2 into one.
>Now my oil return line that runs towards the back under the engine, rubs
>against the new header pipes.
>Does anyone know how to correct this problem?

Yep.  Yank it off and throw it away.  You'll have to use two new oil lines -
one that comes out of the engine case on the driver's side and goes
under the engine/trans (rather than around the back) and another to
connect to this line and go up to the thermostat.  Replacing these lines
was harder than installing the headers, IMHO.

BTW, the part numbers *should* be:

911.107.739.10 (for the line that goes under the engine) and,
930.107.729.10 (for the line that connects to the thermostat)
