The 7" round Hella H-4 replacement light should have a light pattern nearly
identical to the Bosch-made Porsche specific H-4 light units.  Because of
the bigger reflector, either H-4 could be better than the unit with two H-1
bulbs.

However, Chris, if you are getting flashed by oncoming traffic when you use
your low beams, you may very well have badly aimed headlights.  These
headlights have a very sharp beam cutoff: the low beams should put LESS
light into oncoming drivers' eyes than sealed-beam headlights.  The sharp
beam pattern also makes them easy to aim.

Please aim your lights Chris.  Some friends of mine [that's it, friends of
mine--I'm almost sure that I would never do such a thing myself] have
well-aimed 55/100 Watt headlights and don't want any crackdowns.
