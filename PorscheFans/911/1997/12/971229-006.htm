Jon,

I would remove the oil filter and disect it with a pair of tim snips (Don't use a saw for obvious reasons).  Carefully un-fold the paper element and examine for bit of shiny  non-magnetic metal.  

When my engine ate its bearings, the metal flakes were relatively large.  About 1/16" sq.  There was plenty of finer metal in the filter, but that was magnetic and my mechanic told me that was not bearing material.  My bearings were far enough gone that the engine sounded like it had one or two valves out of adjustment.
