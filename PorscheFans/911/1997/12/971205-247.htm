Garth,

The '71 2.4 T engine weighed 176kg.

The '71 2.4 E and S engines weighed 182kg.

The '74 2.7 engines weighed 182kg.

The '75 - '77 911 2.7 engines weighed 195kg.

The '75 -'76 911S and '75 - '77 Carrera engines weighed 200kg.

Note: 2.204 pounds to a kg.
