After all this talk about going down through the gears I could not
resist putting in my .02.
Here it is.

There is no reason to downshift into any gear that you do not intend to
promptly use.  On the track or the street.

Using the engine to slow the car is nuts and a waste of clutch material.
Brake pads are cheaper.

If you need third gear, brake until the speed of the car matches a third
gear speed, then change gears into third.  Double clutch if you must,
but there is no reason to go through any other gears to get there if you
are more than one away, like 5th.

It may sound and feel racy to go through all those gears, but it is a
waste of time and puts unneeded wear on your gearbox and adds wear to
your clutch which you do not need.
