	My dad took a Bug towbar and modified the ends. We originally hooked it to the 
bottom of the car, I don't know where but it did not have enough ground 
clearance. We then drilled two holes in the bottom of the front bumper. We put 
the male end of our mount through the hole and put nuts on the bolts. To hook 
the male and female ends together we used pins from a trailer hitch. Look at 
the picture I have drawn to see what I'm talking about. Also I will mail a
