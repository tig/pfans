> Anyone have any idea of where to start out with tire pressures.  The newer
> P-cars with these tires run up to 44 psi in the rears.    Should I just
> stick with the pressures specified in the owners manual or are there better

I'd think that with the lower profile, you definitely want to err on 
the high side (too soft and you risk damaging the rim on bumps and 
potholes). If newer cars are running this rubber, I think I'd start 
with the new pressure specs, chalk the sidewall tread, and work my 
way down to the optimal pressure.
