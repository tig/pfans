Just a small coda to Tony's detailed description ... I've basically got the 
"no-holes/BK bar" setup in my 91 C4 except that I didn't want to drill any 
holes at all. As Tony says you can remove the seat, in which case the 
harness belt is bolted on the seat itself, on the mount point with the 
factory buckle. This results in a thickness of material between the tunnel 
and the seat but it doesn't inhibit forward/rear motion. The only negative 
is that this inner harness, not having an eyebolt, can not be removed. I 
generally leave it pulled forward and down along the tunnel and inner seat 
edge, out of the way when I'm not on the track.
