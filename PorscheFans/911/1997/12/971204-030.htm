Btcave68  wrote:

> With all of this discussion on limited slip differentials, I was
> wondering how you can tell if you have an LSD.  Isn't there a way of
> rotating the wheels while on jackstands to determine if you have an
> open or limited slip diff?

    Yes, Brian... Lift the rear wheels off the ground and rotate one
    of them.  If the other one rotates in the opposite direction, you
    have an open differential; if it rotates in the SAME direction,
    you have a limited-slip or locked diff.
