On my SC it turned out to be stiction between the Bilstein shock's rod
and seal.  I keep it at bay with a monthly squirt of HHS2000 on the
rods.  Prior to discovering that, I replaced ball joints (to no avail)
and A-arm bushings (with "Poly-Graphite", a definite mistake).  An
additional symptom in my case was very slightly "sticky" steering, most
noticeable on straight freeway stretches.
