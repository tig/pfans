Hi Don,

I used to have an 86 Carrera (really sorry I sold it) but I never
considered replacing the brakes because they are so good.  I am in the
process upgrading my 911 race car to 930 brakes but only because it is a
full race car.

In my 86 911, I did numerous track events (driver's ed) and always thought
I had more brakes than I needed.  I would recommend replacing the fluid
with ATE Super Blue and changing the pads for whatever application you use
your car (Pagid Oranges).  This will make a huge difference.  If your pedal
goes soft on the track after these upgrades, you could just add a larger
master cylindar.

If you do decide to go with the BIG BRAKES...I bought mine from Jeff at
Cool Rotors with a set of four cross drilled cool rotors.  These save about
10lbs a wheel of unsprung weight which should improve handling quite a bit.

Let me know if you need more info.
