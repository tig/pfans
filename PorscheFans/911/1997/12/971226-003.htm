Just one added bit of advice to Walt's excellent write-up on removing an engine without removing the tranmission.

If, for reasons of space, you do this outside on a sloped apron or your garage floor is not very level, make  sure that the nose of the car is pointed downhill!  Better you work hard pulling the engine out uphill than trying to align it with the tranny and be pushing it uphill at the same time.  

Another hint, don't do this anywhere close to an expansion joint or major crack in the concrete.

None of this actually happened to me you understand.  These are just worst case scenarios I thought up with while laying on my back, looking at clouds,  in my suddenly steep driveway.
