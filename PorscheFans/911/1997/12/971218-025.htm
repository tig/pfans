Jon, My 85 Carrera had a problem where the idle was somewhat lumpy (would
idle at 550 rpm, but was rough and unstable). After a year of putzing
around with idle control valves, O2 sensors etc, my mechanic retorqued the
intake manifold and flow meter. The difference is night and day. Now it
idles smoothly at 550, and pulls like a freight train from 550 all the way
up. Have your mechanic try it, it's about a 1 hour job and well worth the
$70 just to eliminate the possiblity.
