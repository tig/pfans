How many miles on your SC engine...sounds like valve guides, but every
SC engine I have been into, which is quite a few, has had either broken
rings, or broken rings and piston ring lands.  The distributors are
notorious for the timing not being set correctly due to sticking plates
or bad vacuum diaphrams, which leads to too much ignition advance.
