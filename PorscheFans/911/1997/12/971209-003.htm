Re Mark Selleck's question about replacement antennae, Imparts
advertises them regularly.  I think they sell the OEM Hirschmann as well
as a cheaper lookalike from Harada.  As I recall, the manual was under
$100--in general it's not hard to beat Automotion's prices, and Imparts
is very reasonable on shipping.  Phone is 800-325-9043; they're in St.
Louis, so they're on Central time. 

The downside is that to do the job right you have to pull the door, and
pulling the pins requires a special tool.
