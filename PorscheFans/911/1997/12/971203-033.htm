From memory, I believe that this is a 13mm hex drive. It is the same as that
used on air-cooled VWs. So, go to a VW parts place and ask them for a trans
drain-plug socket. I bought one in a 3/8" drive so that I can use a breaker
bar and/or ratchet.

Trans oil changing tip: loosen the filler plug on the side first. If you
can't get that one off, it is a real bummer to have already drained the
fluid by previously removing the bottom plug (e.g. no way to refill).
