If you pull the air pump you should also pull the air injection nozzles
which go into the exhaust port. These openings need to be plugged with a
10mm x .1 pitch threaded plug (if memory serves me correctly), which is
not exactly a standard size. I just found some bolts of the appropriate
size and pitch and cut them off  to form plugs...seems to work fine.
