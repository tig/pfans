	My '68 912 has the Talbot on the left only so I'm not absolutely
sure, but shouldn't the right side be symmetric with the left side?
Also, where did you get the Talbot reproductions?  I've seen the ones
for the 356, but I didn't know the 911/912 mirrors were available as a
reproduction.

	Also, a tip:  the original gasket around the perimeter of my
Talbot mirror was in sad shape and ready to fall out.  This is the only
thing holding the mirror glass in place.  A slick solution was to use
the readily available gasket used around the fog light cover.  You'll
need to cut it to length and slip the skinny side into place around the
perimeter of the mirror glass.  Cut it a little long so there is no gap
where the ends meet.  When finished, it looks like new.
