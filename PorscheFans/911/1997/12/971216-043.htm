Non braided wires fit just fine (at least they did on my '78). As for
the SSI's, you need to switch the oil line which runs around the back of
the engine for the earlier one which runs the oil between the engine and
transaxle. 2 parts are needed, a solid line which goes over the engine
and the line  (part solid, part flexible) which goes from the engine to
the thermostat in the wheel well.

All in all, not a difficult job, just a shame to hide those beautiful
stainless steel exchangers under the car...........
