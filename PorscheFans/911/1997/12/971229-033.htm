Hi Michael,

Here's what I know.  I've tried twice now unsuccessfully to rent Porsches
while travelling in Germany.  However, perhaps you will have better luck
than I.  The best price I received for a 911 was 300 DM with unlimited
miles from AVIS.  I was told that the unlimited mileage and the price was
discounted because I am a very frequent renter (sign up for their wizard
card).  Now, here's the catch.  What you are actually getting is a
reservation for that class of car which also happens to include a couple of
select BMW's and Mercedes.  Both times, all of the Porsches were rented.

Hertz rents Boxters for 249 DM per day, and I don't know if there is an
additional charge for mileage.  Once again, you are only getting a
reservation for that class of car.  I've turned down the Z3 a couple of
times now.

FYI, the current exchange is 1.77 DM = $1.

Good luck,
