Thanks to Neil and Joel for responding to my post. Glad to hear from
P-fans who have Pertronix Ignitors that work!  I like the idea of a magnetic pickup -- set the timing and forget it!  I ordered my Pertronix unit from Automotion, and it was supposed to be the correct one for my Porsche, (l975 911 S Targa with Permatune CD box and stock coil).  When it came the directions didn't seem to apply to my car, so that's why I took it to my local P-wrench.  The three units I sent back to Automotion did turn out to be defective, as did others they sold from the same batch -- it wasn't an issue of getting the wires crossed.  The customer service guy at Automotion says that Pertronix is a small company and they have had some quality control problems.  He also said that German Parts and Restoration (GPR) has a lot of experience with this product, so I may call them with my distributor # as Neil suggested and and give it another try!
