This is for John Richardson regarding the "clunking" sound on speed bumps.
Take a careful look at you shock ends, both front and rear, top and bottom
to see if the rubber bushings are still there, and in one piece.  There are
some other rubber bushings on the swaybars and trailing links.  If the
rubber is bad, or gone your metal against metal.  This stuff gets hard and
cracks every 20 yrs. or so! (G)
