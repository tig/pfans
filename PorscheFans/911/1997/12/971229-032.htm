This is for Juha, re: the High oil temp.
I am assuming you have a 911 series car.  If you do, as far as I know, all
oil  checking is done with the engine running.  I would think if you
checked it with the engine off it would indicate low, and cause you to
overfill.  I am not sure about our 911's, but on most other vehicles if the
oil is overfilled it will cause overheating.
So, have you added oil lately?  Try checking the oil with engine warm, and
running on level ground.  If you are over the mark, let some out of the
tank and go for a ride and see what happens.  Let us know.
