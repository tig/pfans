Hi Tom:

  At the stock ride height, as delivered by the factory, you may not
notice any difference. The factory setup is pretty darn close!
 The bump steer kits really do make a difference if the ride height has
been lowered from the factory settings. Its a very inexpensive and
worthwhile upgrade for any lowered 911.

Since the steering rack will be in a different place after the
installation of the spacers, you must re-align the front suspension. The
toe-in and camber will be most affected by this change.

 Hope this helps,  :^)
