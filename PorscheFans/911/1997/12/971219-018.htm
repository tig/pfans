I've heard several complaints about reproduction windshield seals.  My
0.02 on this based on using both OEM and repro seals: don't waste your
time (or money) on repro seals in this application or other "critical"
locations.  Repro deco base seals and other "simple" seals are fine, but
I would insist your source certify that the windshield seals are OEM.
