Hey Carl,

 If your 87 is like my older 911, then this is an easy one.  You don't actually have to pry anything.  You just grab the rim firmly around the perimeter and wiggle (the tach) back and forth until you get it loose.  It will pull straight out.  If you have trouble you could try a small amount of silicone spray around the edge of the rubber gasket.  It's set up like this:  The gauges have the rubber collar slipped onto them from the back, and then the whole thing slides into the dash.  Good luck!
