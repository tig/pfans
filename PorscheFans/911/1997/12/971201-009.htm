As for the performance claim, well I havn't raced every one out there, but

beat all the stock 911s.  Actually, the toughest competition was a 68 911S
that was stripped.  But this is all mini sprint and autocross stuff.  I do
admit that on the track (like that relates to country roads) some 911s will
prevail.  Depends on the track though!  Forget Laguna, it takes me 3
minutes to get to the top of the hill!  Seattle is tough too, any hilly
tracks are.  Portland is very 912 friendly with short straights and not too
much elevation change.  I guess the big advantage is late left foot
braking, right up to the apex.
