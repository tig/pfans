I had similar problems on my '86. The problem was bad dog teeth on the
1st/2nd slider (and worn syncros). It was over $500 just in parts. I did the
r'n'r and a retired p-club member did the trans for  a nominal labor fee.
This is a $1500 job if you take the car to a shop.

Or it could just be a linkage problem, which would be an order of magnitude
less expensive to fix.
