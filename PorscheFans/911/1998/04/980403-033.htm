Hi Andy,
Just use a floor jack under the engine, up in front of the oil screen
plate, padded with a piece of wood or plastic pad.  Put the jack stands
under the torsion bar housings (inside the fender well in front of the
wheel).  You can also pad the jack stands with a shop towel.  This
method is used by many shops.
