>lamp flickers when I hit large

this is normal behavior... the wheels are tracked rotatioally by the abs
sensors.. this system is used by the traction control brain to see which
tires are slipping or gripping..  if you hit a bump and a wheel spins and
the brain gets confused, the light will come on... when correct count is
regained, the light goes off.
