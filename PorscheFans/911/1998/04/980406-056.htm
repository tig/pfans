Thanks to all of you who added suggestions on what could be wrong with my
shift linkage. As it turns out, the 915 shift tower protrudes further into
the tunnel than the 901 shifter. Therefore, upon tightening the mounting
bolts it pinched the shift rod to the floor board. All I had to do was
space up the 915 shift tower a bit. Everything works fine except for some
minor adjustments at the coupler.

BTW, figuring out the clutch linkage on the newer 915 transmissions is a
pain if you have nothing to compare with or look at.

Thanks again, Chris
