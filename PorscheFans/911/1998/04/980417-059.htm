Actually the cables (asssuming you have a standard set up without auto
control) is not difficult, just tedious.

All you need to do is to lift up the carpet on the tunnel (interior),
remove the parking brake/heater lever assembly (3 bolts if I recall) and
weasel it out enough to get at the lever end where the wires attach to.
I would recommend that you loosen them at the heater valve end (found
over top of the rear axles) where you will find a little set screw
holding them in place, so that you get a bit more clearance.

Then just snip the old wires off, slide the new ones down the tube
(should be readily apparent.......about 1/4 inch diameter if I
recall)and then twist them on to the heater lever.

Adjust at the valve and all should be well. A bit of crawling around,
but not hard.

Good luck
