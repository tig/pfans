IF you drop the clutch and spin the wheels. But the C2 walks away from the

I have driven several 964-based C4 coupes and one 964-based C4 cab in

effectively. They are terrible cars for autocross. I don't think the
993-based C4 is much better in this regard. Yes, you can dial out some of
the understeer by changing springs and sway bars. You are wasting you time
playing with tire pressures. The all-wheel drive systems cause more
understeer. There is no way to get rid of that without geting rid of the
front drive.
