	You probably already know this, but just FYI, one very
	important point many people overlook when they are 
	installing new aerodynamic components on their cars is
	the fact that the primary role of these devices is not
	cosmetic but functional.

	The OEM GT2 wing is designed to generate considerable 
	downforce on the rear of the car, I am not certain, but 
	I could imagine that it will generate significantly more than
	the "stock" turbo wing. 

	Without "matched" aerodynamics on the front of the car 
	your *high* speed handling will change, increasing the
	downforce on the rear of the car, AND LIGHTENING THE FRONT.

	This change is unlikely to be catastrophic but it will make
	a difference, increasing the understeer of the vehicle.

	You could always fit the GT2 front splitters to increase
	the downforce on the front accordingly.

	BTW is the wing OEM or an aftermarket piece?
