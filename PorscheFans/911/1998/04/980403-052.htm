It probably wouldn't hurt to replace them if they are indeed the originals.

Don't needlessly lighten your wallet buying the stainless braided wires,
but *do* buy the factory wires (unshielded) originally used on the early
cars.  Many of the major mail order places (Zims, etc.) sell them for about
$70-$80.
