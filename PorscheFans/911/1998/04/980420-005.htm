I would not use these.  They will retain the heat in the wheel area and
can result in rotor warpage, yes even with a street car.
I put them on one of my earlier volvos, because I got tired of cleaning
the darned brake dust off the wheels.  After one trip through the
mountains on the way to the beach-car fully loaded with family and
getting hammered by yours truly through the twisties-an my rotors were
toast.

Go with a pad that creates less dust if that is your problem, but don't
mess with these things.
My .02.
