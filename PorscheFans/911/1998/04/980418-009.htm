Eric:

The climate control on my '84 Carrera has the same problem (full off or
full on) whenever the temperature sensor falls off.  The temperature sensor
is located in a rubber cone located on the headliner between the sunvisors.
 Check to make sure that 1) you have a temperature sensor, and 2) that it
is connected to the wiring in the headliner.
