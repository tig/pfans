Can't help you too much on the seat cover and brake questions, although
I'm interested in the responses you receive regarding the brakes. 

On the issue of car covers, I suggest you take a look at the Automotion
catalog, contact them at 800-777-8881 or refer to www.automotion.com.
Automotion tends to be a bit high on some items, but they offer 6
different types of car cover materials. Their catalog does a good job
detailing the different materials available and will give you a good
overview of which material works best for specific uses. The cover
prices range from $126.98 - $349.98. A wide range, but a small price to
protect the car. Automotion may be a good starting place to get the
possibilities. You may want to buy from them or go elsewhere for a lower
price. Other than pricey for some items, I've always had good
experiences dealing with them. Good luck.
