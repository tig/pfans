My '83 came with a B&B single outlet muffler (1,500 miles from new - ~5,000
miles now).  Its loud around town - will not please your neighbors late at
night. Conversely on the highway its quiter than you'd think.  I have heard
that there are problems with the baffles in the B&Bs breaking loose and that
B&B hasn't always been responsive to correcting the apparent defect. I've
also been told that the Borla is a better quality unit.  I'd like to off my
B&B for a lower profile exhaust more suitable for my small town - Newport,
RI. I may check out the Perf. Products SS dual outlet "cow horn" muffler.
Anyone have experience with it?.
