Hi Jeff,
The easiest way is to open one of the bleeder valves and depress the brake
pedal, wedging it down with a board.  With the brake pedal depressed the fluid
leak will be minimal, if any, as the master cylinder valving will not let
gravity act on the fluid in the system.
