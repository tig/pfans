Denny,

I have twin plugs in my 81 3.0 l engine. The most cost effective way to go is
with the Electromotive ignition. It is a good and reliable crankfire system.
You can also modify your distributor to hold the timing wheel as I have done.
The distributor route is easier  and probably cheaper but not ultimately as
accurate as the crank triggered wheel.
