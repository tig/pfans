Greg:

Redline makes great synthetic gear oils for your Porsche.  MTL,
however, isn't one of them.

MTL is a lightweight oil suitable for transmissions whose factory 
specs call for motor oil in the transmission; it's NOT suitable for 
transmissions like ours, which require GL-5 protection.

For your transmission, Redline's 75W90 or 75W90NS would be the right 
choice.
