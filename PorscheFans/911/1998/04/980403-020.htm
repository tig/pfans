Yes ! I did that myself 3 weeks ago on the recommendation of Terry, on
the list. Worked fine. The bushings on the A arm are not removable from
the arms, so yeah, either you replace the whole thing or you live with
the squeak. What I did was remove the center bushings, lube those, and
that gave the torsion bar enough play to squeeze in some grease in the A
arm bushings. They still squeaked when I was trying to lube them, but
once everything was bolted back on, the car was silent !  You may try
this yourself, it's not very difficult to do !  ;-)
