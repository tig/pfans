Hi Mike,
I put the Optima sealed battery in mine, and for the first time in five
P-cars, no worries about the battery spitting up and corroding the boot
floor.  As a bonus, it has 800 amps.  They are sold by advertisers in
Panorama and most of the parts catalogs--expensive, though.

AJ-USA in San Diego makes a neat mounting bracketfor the Optima.  Just
an aluminum plate, slightly longer than the battery, bent upward at the
back end, with a hole for the stock mounting bolt at the front end.  You
bolt it to the bottom of the battery, and then it mounts in the stock
location with no new holes needed.  You could make one yourself from a
piece of aluminum or stainless sheet.  You will also need a slightly
longer ground cable for the Optima.
