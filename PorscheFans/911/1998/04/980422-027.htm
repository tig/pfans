Vic,

I had the same experience with my oil level gauge.  I found a lose
ground wire on the oil sending unit on the oil tank.  I would check in
the area of your fuel tank sending unit, located on the top of the gas
tank, and look for a lose or corroded connection.

Hope this helps and good luck.
