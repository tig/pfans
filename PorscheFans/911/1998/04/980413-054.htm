     Todd,
     
     I just pulled my sunroof, cables and motor/gear drive unit to clean 
     and grease everything (PM work, as the car is not on the road yet).  I 
     don't know where your 1/8" went, but if you have to adjust the cable 
     it's pretty easy.  Unzip the zipper in the back of the headliner to 
     expose the motor/gear drive unit.  Remove only the two screws that 
     hold the gear drive in place.  It will slip off of the motor via a 
     rubber coupling.  At that point the cables are free.  Adjust the 
     sunroof the 1/8" you need and reinstall the gear drive.  Looking at my 
     cable, an 1/8" should not interfere with the gear position but you may 
     have to adjust either way for the gear to re-engage with the cable.  
     Worked well for me when correcting my cable travel.  
     Hope this helps
