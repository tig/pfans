Ernie wonders why the BMW M3s are beating the GT3 911s. Money, plain and
simple. The BMWs are a factory backed team. They are outspending the
privateer 911s probably by a huge factor. Having trucks full of spares, huge
amounts of test time, and professional, full-time mechanics is a great
advantage over teams that are run on a shoestring by volunteers. Porsche is
a much smaller company than BMW, so it is understandable that the factory
can only back cars in one class and that happens to be in GT1. I can't wait
to see the new, all carbon fibre 911 GT1s at Le Mans.
