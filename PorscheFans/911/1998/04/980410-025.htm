Bill:

As you discovered, Earl's does not manufacture any oil cooler kits for
911's. They supply most of the hardware to build your own if that is
what you want.
We have used their coolers for years with excellent results.

Good add-on or replacement oil cooler kits are available from a number
of sources. AJR, B&B and of course, us.  :^)

You may also find a good used factory cooler with fan, from a late-model
Carrera that is another good choice. Be sure to have ANY used oil cooler
ultrasonically cleaned before you install this on your car.

Good Luck!
