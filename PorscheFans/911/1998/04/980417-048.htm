Or... if you just want the information over the phone, you can call
Porsche's Customer Commitment Center at 1-800-545-8039. They'll tell
you everything that's on the Cardex over the phone, for free. You do not
need to provide documentation - all you need is to give a serial # over
the phone.

I used this when looking at used cars - they were able to tell me not
only Cardex information, but also what warranty service had been done,
whether recall work had been performed, etc etc.
