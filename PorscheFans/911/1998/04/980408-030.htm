I attended a tech session on valve reconditioning at a p-car specialty
garage. The mechanic drilled the guide with a bit sized to take out about
half the guide's thickness. He then used an air hammer with appropriate
mandrel to drive out the remaining guide. He pointed out that you could not
drill the entire guide out without damaging the head. Conversely, he said
that you don't want to try to drive the full-thickness guide out because
that would also degrade the head.
