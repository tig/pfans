I discovered a charming "design feature" yesterday!  It is when the poorly
designed crimping of the engine lid latch cable decided to let go of the
cable when trying to open the engine compartment lid!  I was pleased and
impressed to have the T-handle and 6 feet of cable in hand afterwards.  A
simple lead swedge crimped onto the end of the cable AFTER it has passed
through the latch assembly might be a cautious bit of preventative
maintenance.  Had Porsche done a bit better on this, my local dealer would
not be removing inner wheel well panels to spring the latch and re-thread
the cable, all at Porsche's warranty expense.

HUMBUG on cheesy, slipshod design!
