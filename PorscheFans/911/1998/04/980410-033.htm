Stoddard has this trim in the EARLY 911 parts catalog. Remember you 
have to remove the glass and rubber to install the bright trim strips.
 You may need fresh rubber to avoid leaks. You will put the 
weatherstripping rubber on the glass and then gently work the 
anodized aluminum trim in place. Then the whole unit is put into the 
car. If you bend the trim too much you can cause small "cracks" in 
the finish of the bright finish of the trim, usually only noticeable 
in bright light. You must be very patient.  Bruce
