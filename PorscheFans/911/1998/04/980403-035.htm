I don't know if there is a Porsche FAQ but if there is I think it
needs to have the following. When working on a Porsche 911 make
sure to have a wide array of 13mm sockets, wrenches etc.  You may
not be aware as all other cars you have previously wrenched  used
12 and 14 and 17 mm nuts. bolts. Well the Germans had to be a bit
different so the car is built with 13mm and big allen bolts. So a
must for any do it your selfer is to own a good set of 13mm
wrenches sockets etc and a nice supply of bigger then the norm
Allen wrenches..
