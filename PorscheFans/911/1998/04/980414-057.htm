There used to be both Silicone and non-silicone DOT 5.0 products - the two
types are incompatible with each other so a DOT 5.1 non-silicone base was
created. DOT 5 silicone must be tinted purple and be labeled SILICONE BASE,
and the DOT 5.1 keeps its amber color and is labeled NON-SILICONE. 

If the product you bought is not labeled silicone based, you are assured it is
not silicone.

Remember, the higher the DOT rating, the higher the performance.
