Hi Joukoh,

I'm responding to your question on the P-fans list about tire sizes.
I have an 87 carrera coupe with 245's on the rear wheels. My mechanic
resently suggested to go back to 225's when I was buying new ones.
Reason for this is that the 245's (on my 8x16" wheels) 'roll' more over the
wheel and therefore give less actual grip.
Perhaps other p-nuts on the list can second this (or not...).
I my self don't like the Pirelli's much and stick with Bridgestone's
