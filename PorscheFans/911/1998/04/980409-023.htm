There's a signal booster already built in (to the car, that is, not the
radio).  Sometimes people forget to reconnect it when they reinstall or
change the radio.  If I recall correctly, it's a white wire that's maybe
wrapped around the antenna lead.  You can use the power antenna connection
on most head units to connect it to.
