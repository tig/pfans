I have heard this space called the "package shelf." I am unsure of the
configuration on an early car. On the '86 and '83 I have had you need to
remove four screws on the vertical panel behind the folding seat backs. The
vertical panel and the shelf are really one piece. You then lift up on this
vertical part until a tang disengages (you have to lift it about an inch).
The assembly then pulls out. Check it out, your '68 may be similar.
