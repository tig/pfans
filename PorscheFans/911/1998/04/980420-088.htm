Hi,

The master cylinder circuit failure warning switch has probably failed. You could remove the switch and see if
cleaning it or other adjustments restore normalcy. Or,,, you could simply disconnect the wire from the switch
unit as well (the hand brake and oil function will still work correctly).

If you disconnect, you will get no warning if your master cylinder begins to leak. SInce this is not safe, I
certainly do not  endorse this solution (for others).
