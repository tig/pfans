I've used Dent Wizard in Bellevue WA twice. Once at Barrier Motors when they
put a door ding in my car and once on my own when *I* caused the door ding.

The first time the ding did not have any paint marring or scratching. It was
obviously caused by a "kiss". Came out flawlessly.

The 2nd time the ding was caused by something falling on the door in the
garage. I almost cried when I saw it and noticed that the paint was marred.
It wasn't scratched, just marred. I took it to Dent Wizard, the guy looked
at it and said "no problem". He was right. The repair was flawless. If I
remember correctly it cost about $170. Well worth it in my opinion. I
supposed I could find someone who would do it for less, but since Barrier
Motors choose these guys I figured they  were a quality outfit. And they
are.
