I recently bought a trickle charger for my car battery (part number
000.044.900.25 "Chargomat 240V UK-Ausfuhrung") from my Porsche dealers.
This gizmo plugs into the mains supply and into the car's cigarette
lighter socket and keeps the battery charged, avoiding the "flat battery"
problem when the car has been standing unused for a few weeks.

It is intelligent enough to know when the battery is fully charged and 
stops charging at that point.

Only slight complication is that the cigarette lighter socket on my 
Carrera 3.2 is only live when the key is in the accessory position. The guy 
in the dealership told me that I have to make the change to "permanently 
live" at the fuse board, *not* at the socket or it will affect the factory 
alarm. Instead, I'll probably install a separate socket on a flying lead in 
the trunk, directly wired to the battery (via an inline fuse), which he 
said is OK.

Question : the gizmo came without any instructions or specification. Does
anyone have an instruction manual/sheet or tech bulletin, etc for this
device?
