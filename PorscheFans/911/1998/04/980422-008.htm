intelligence of anybody driving (or worse, having bought) a C4, I would

ago) except that the "slow in, fast out" rule does not have to be adhered 
to as stringently as with the 2WD 2.7.
The fact is, thank God, we don't all think alike.  I suggest we quit this 
before it gets out of hand and go back to enjoying our (even if slightly 
different) cars !
