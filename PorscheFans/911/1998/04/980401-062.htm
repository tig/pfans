Hi again,

Seems we are going through very similar things with our C4's.  I just
changed the top six plugs with the Bosch tri-tip.  They are the recommended
plug from several sources.  Changing just the top six greatly improved the
feel.  Mine looked original with 55k on the car.  The gap had increased
from .34 to over .40.  Five of the top six were pretty easy.  Just remove
the heater duct for the drivers side, to gain access to those three.  The
passenger side has one tough one.  I took out the airbox and using the
Porsche tool from the kit and some patience was able to remove the front
most plug.  Thought things were going well, then I discovered (through this
list) that I would have to remove the exhaust.  It is VERY heavy.  About 60
lbs.  Since I needed a valve job, I took it in (today) to have the valves
done and the lower spark plugs replaced.  They suggested adding a 'power
tube' which replaces about 60 lbs of muffler, SLIGHTLY increases HP and in
their opinion improves the sound.

I will let you know how it goes.  I am figuring on $500 for everything.  By
the way, shop for the plugs.  Prices ranged from $12 to $3.50.  Buy 12,
that kind of money adds up.

Let me know if you have any questions.
