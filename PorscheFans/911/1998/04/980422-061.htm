On April 22, John D. writes:
>The black velvet-like trim piece on the leading edge of my sunroof
>cut-out needs replacing.  I have the spare, and have two questions:
>1. How is the old one best removed?
>2. What kind of adhesive is recommended for installation of the
>new one?

John,

When I did mine, I just pulled the old one off.  Then used 3M Adhesive,
Tar and Wax remover (liquid, black can, about $5) to take off old
adhesive.  For a small piece like this, I would use the small black tube
of 3M Black Weatherstripping Adhesive (about $4); you'll only need a
little but spread it evenly and thinly along the new seal.

Hope this helps, and good luck,
