I am contemplating making my '75 911S coupe look like a '74 Carrera RS.

There is a good lightweight interior available, my '73 has it.
It is lightweight "felt" and covers all the interior except the floor
under the seats. It replaces the rear seats with this felt which is glued
down everywhere.

You can also get the lightweight door panels which us a pull
strap for opening the door, they will match the lightweight
interior nicely.

GT racing makes some nice front and rear bumpers and spoilers,
the '74 RS spoiler (like '76 Turbo) is good.

For authenticity the early Carrera rear flares (metal!) should be
used instead of the SC style.

Not much more is needed to make your S into a good RS clone!
