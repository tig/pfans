     Andy,
     I just installed a complete OEM foglight system on my '77.  The 
     correct position for the switch is between the rear window defogger 
     switch and the cigarette lighter.  You need to cut away the dash 
     material which will expose the metal panel.  I then drilled a centered 
     7/16" hole through to the front luggage compartment.  The switch came 
     with a rubber boot that seals the luggage compartment from the 
     interior.  It's was real easy to do.  Ran the wires to the relay on 
     the fuse box and fuse(s) and then out to the lights.  Hope this helps.
