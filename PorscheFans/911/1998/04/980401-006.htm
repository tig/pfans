When you lower the car it is the stiffer torsion bars that help stop ( or at
least reduce ) bottoming. My car has both the larger torsion bars and the
Bilstein sport shocks ( 81 SC ). I would advise against the sport Bilstein's,
they are what really stiffen the ride and are not necessary unless you are
going for that last couple of tenths on the track. This is also Bilsteins
recommendation as was discussed in a previous thread on this subject.

As to the sway bars, I believe that 19 mm should be large enough.
