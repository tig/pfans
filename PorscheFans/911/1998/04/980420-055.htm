Without taking the valve covers off, the only way to tell is audibly. It
sounds like ticking that changes rate with RPMs. If you take the valve
covers off you can feel the valve stems move side-to-side in the guides. If
they move you've got wear.

See http://www.kindel.com/porsche/valves.asp. 

Charlie Kindel
'88 Venetian Blue Coupe that no longer has valve guide wear.
