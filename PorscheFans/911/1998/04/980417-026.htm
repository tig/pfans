Michel - 203 has a Moly additive whereas 201 does not. I believe the 203
doesn't have the "climbing" charateristics of the 201. The 201 literally
climbs gears and almost looks taffy like in use. I'd use the 201 in your
application because I think it would give you better synchro engagement.
