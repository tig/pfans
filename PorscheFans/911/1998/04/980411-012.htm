I have 7.5 and 9x17" C2t replica wheels on my '87 with 205-50's in front
and 255-40's in back.  They fit fine, but my car is not particularly low
and I did have to roll the front fender lips.  Just make sure you get the
right offset for your car.  Note that you will get a somewhat harsher ride
with 17" wheels, but it looks and handles and sticks great (Dunlop SP8000's).
