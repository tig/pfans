Can't give much advice on seats, but be VERY CAREFUL with the 
Allen head bolts mounting the seats.  They're very easy to strip, as
I found out this weekend when I was installing a cross-bracket to
hold my submarine belt.  Use a top-quality 6mm Allen wrench 
or socket, and if it won't break loose with a moderate amount of 
force, back off.  I stripped the heads of two bolts; had to drill them 
out.
