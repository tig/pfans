Porsche AG was aware of the problem  and published a service bulletin in
Dec/Jan 96. Problem occurred when rear third light was moved from the
'basket handle' and integrated into the recess above the rear window.
The adhesives used cause a short between the brake circuit and the rear
defrost grid.  The defroster does not have to be on to cause the damage.
The problem is suppose to be fixed, and the Porsche dealers can give you
the VINs of the suspect cars.
