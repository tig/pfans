        Jeroen 
        
        Take your mechanics advice - go back to 225's.  A 225 tyre will 
        not roll as much on an 8" rim. Why??
        
        The section width of a 225 tyre is about 8.85" - fairly close.
        
        The section width of a 245 tyre is about 9.64" - not so close!
        
        Porsche fitted 245's to 9" rims - unless I'm very much mistaken.
        
        I have 225/50 15 on 9" rims and they work ok.  They certainly 
        don't roll!
        
        However, check the recommended or design rim size for the tyre 
        that you are buying - it varies!  Don't forget, when chosing a 
        tyre, to select an aspect ratio that maintains the correct 
        gearing.  For standard gearing, the overall diameter of the tyre 
        should be about 632mm.  The diameter of a 225/50 16 tyre is 
        632mm.
