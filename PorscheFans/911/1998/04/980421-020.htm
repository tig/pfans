I have an 83SC.   I have done the battery shuffle twice.  
I would first recommend that whatever battery you buy that you get one
of the battery minders which maintains your battery at a full charge.
Letting the car sit and the battery run down will kill the battery.  Its
life will be very short.
Second, make sure the battery fits your car.  A lot of people will want
to sell you something that will not fit or will require the fabrication
of a new holder.
I have come to appreciate Interstate Batteries.  I have one that is
sized right for my car.  The price is much lower than an Optima and
should give 3-5 years of decent service.
My .02.
