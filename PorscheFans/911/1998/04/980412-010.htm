I'm not sure that the question about synthetic brake fluid was ever fully
answered.  It is very simple:  All brake fluid is synthetic and some
manufacturers simply take liberties with their advertising.  I think we all
know the warnings about DOT 5 fluids and should, in most cases, avoid those.
I notice a lot of you use the ATE Blue, as I have.  Be aware that it can
permanently stain the plastic parts of brake systems blue.  ATE also makes, as
most of us are aware, the same fluid without the blue dye which may be
preferable.
