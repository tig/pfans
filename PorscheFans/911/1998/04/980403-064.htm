It is not my intention to get this topic going again but, I receive a =

=20
I used a G-Tech accelerometer to measure HP at the rear wheels.  I made =
5 measurements with the old dirty paper filter and 5 with the K&N filter =
and calculated the average.  I did this over a small period of time so =

upgraded exhaust and Chip would experience that 10% increase I heard =
people talk about in more than one occasion.
