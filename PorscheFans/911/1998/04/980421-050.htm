Chris,
My '67 912 took one of those 15 mph $2500 hits to the rear end (not
much reinforcement back there).

The lady who hit me was insured by Allstate.

I took the car to my body shop and they sent the adjuster there. The
adjuster did not quibble at all. We replaced the rear panel (license plate
area) straightened the decklid, matched old off white paint and replaced
some rubber, new tailights, new chrome lettering, etc. 

Allstate gave me no problems, but it was their clients fault.
