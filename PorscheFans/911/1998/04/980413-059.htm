Hi Mike,

Door seals are easily replaced.  If they have not been over-heavily
glued into place, they should just pull off.  You may want to remove the
velvet door seals first for easier access at the top.  I replaced mine
with Porsche seals (about $50 per side); though there are cheaper
vendors, this (along with front and rear glass seals) I judged to be
worth the difference.  I used 3M Fast Tack adhesive brushed on; firm,
but not so tight as to rip the seal when removing.  Others may prefer a
true weatherstripping seal, which is a much stronger bond. I also used
3M Adhesive, Tar, and Wax remover along the door recess to remove old
adhesive prior to applying the new.

Hope this helps, and good luck.
