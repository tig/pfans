Euro taillights, right?  The posts are designed to snap off in your hand.
If you're really lucky, all three will snap, and you will have to use the
more effective "sheet metal screw & spacer" method for re-attachment that I
used.  I drilled a hole through the center of the lens, and used a section
of a bic pen as a spacer sleeve.  Through this I used a sheet metal screw.
Better than new!  :)
