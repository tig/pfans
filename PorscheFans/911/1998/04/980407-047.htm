It is said that Porsche made some revisions on 1996 model year rear brake disc
carriers and wheel bearings to withstand the extra "potential" stress produced
by the 18" wheels.  Porsche does not approve the installation of any 18" wheel
on any 993 chassis produced as a 1995 model year.
