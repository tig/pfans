Andy, I have placed the pad up under the center of the engine (on the 
flange) and jacked car up that way; used this method between approx. 
'77 and '96 on my '76 and '84 911s.  Just make sure you put some sort 
of pad on the jack plate.  I have used a peice of pine (cracked after short 
time) then a doubled-over section of inner tube.  Have recently switched to
a hard rubber hockey puck which works great.

Have tried it on the 993 as well; only problem is that it bent the engine
undercover screw mount (just a warning to you 964 and 993 owners).
