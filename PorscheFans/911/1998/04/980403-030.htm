The drain plug is maybe a third of the way up the side of the trans. It is
the same type of plug as the drain, meaning you need a giant allen wrench.
The VW parts places sell a socket that will work nicely with your 3/8" drive
tools (the hex recess is the same on p-cars and VWs). Important rule #1:
remove the filler plug first. If you first remove the drain plug and then
discover that the filler plug is stuck, well you have a problem.

I like Swepco 201 gear oil. Here in Seattle I can get it at the Porsche
dealer and at various parts houses.
