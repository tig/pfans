Mark your calender now for Sunday April 19 from 10:00 am to 4:00 pm!

- Find those elusive Porsche used and new parts in the Swap Meet.

Corral
- Get all the latest information on Upper Canada Region's 1998 Club

Store with lots of new items.
- Don't miss the Porsche Showcase with significant race-prepared
Porsches and new Boxters.
- Do you have questions about your Porsche's engine, suspension, fuel
injection, tires, electronics or maintenance ? Save them for Canada's
leading Porsche experts in the Technical Q&A
