Tim - the dreaded rubber spring plate bushings are not just glued on, they
are somehow bonded or vulcanized or otherwise firmly attached.  I don't
think there is any easy way to remove them.  I take my cutting torch and
go outside and sort of burn them off (carefully, but with lots of sooty
smoke), and then clean up the remaining mess with chisels and
eventually sandpaper.  However, you can make progress with a
hacksaw instead of a torch, or a knife, or sharp chisels, and sort of
whittle away.  Takes longer.  Ugly job no matter what.
