For Kevin Werner,
I had four small dents removed from my wife's Acura Legend using this
process with outstanding results.  One of the dents was in the roof
(golf ball?), which required loosening the edge of the headliner.  This
process is highly dependent on the technician's skill, so try to see
some examples of completed work--a black vehicle would be a good
example, as it shows every flaw.  If the paint itself is damaged, this
process doesn't repair that, of course.  It only straightens the metal.
