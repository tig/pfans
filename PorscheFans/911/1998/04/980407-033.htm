*********Here is the magic trick, forgive me if it's not obvious.  (It
wasn't to me...):
take a socket, preferably an impact socket, that barely, or almost fits over
the locking lug nut-hammer it onto the nut, then simply hook up a breaker
bar, impact wrench or whatever and crack it loose.

One warning, this can destroy your socket, or permanently attach a nut to
it.  You can then take the socket to your vise and knock the nut out of the
socket with some more handy tools.  If you use a "warranteed" socket, you
can't lose.
