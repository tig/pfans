i have found the best way to deal with that rubber strip on the rocker trim
panel is to remove the whole strip.  to re-install, take a pair of channel
locks and pry the upper "flange" or retaining strip of the rocker panel
upwards, being careful not to slip and scratch the paint.  now install the
rubber strip, being certain it is completely inserted under this upper
retaining strip.  When you have the entire strip in place, and it is centered
front-to-back, take your palm and push (hard) the upper edge of the rubber
strip along it's entire length.  By doing this you are reversing the bending
you did on the flange, and locking the strip in place. probably the most part
of removing rear torsion bars is messing with this strip!
