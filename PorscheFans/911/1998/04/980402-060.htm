> My 1989 C-4 has condensation in the tail light units.  This appears on all
> but hot summer days.  Does anyone know how to get rid of it?

Very early C-4s (and I think C-2s as well) had a problem with this - there
was a production change that added a vent tube coming off the engine
fan ducting to the tail light units to get rid of condensation.

Retrofitting these parts should be very simple and will not be terribly
expensive.
