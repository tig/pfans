There are two adjustments (low end and mid range/upper range). I 
remember someone trying to adjust these while the engine was running. 
Don't do this unless you need an excuse to buy a new pump. Engine off 
only when adjusting the pump. The Bosch book is really a must when 
making adjustments to MFI. If your heater boxes have holes or your 
valve guides are worn, you will never get the engine to run smoothly.
