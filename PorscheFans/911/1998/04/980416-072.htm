Is your wing window the fixed version or does it swivel in and out? If it
is the fixed version like a coupe's then you need to remove the whole
window assembly or at least raise it enough to get access to the bolts on
the underside...two allen bolts, if I remember correctly. If it is the
moveable wing window you will probably need to raise the whole mechanism
as well and you will probably see a nut on the underside where the window
swivels. From there you will probably be able to figure it out.
