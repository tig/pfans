Ron askes about broken spark plugs -

I think you can grab the top part with some of those four
prong remote *grabbers* if you don't have some of these, and
the magnetic grabbers, get some - you will save yourself hundreds
of curse words that could be put to better use elsewhere...

Worse comes to worse, you can remove the top valve cover to gain
a little more access to the plug areas...

Also, I like to remove the air box, and that heater pipe on my '89
to gain access to the sides of the engine more - these are usually
the first two things I do whenever I work on the engine...

It's been said before, but I only listened last time, now I'm a firm
believer - the tool kit spark plug wrench is the absolute best
spark plug wrench I have EVER used - less than 5 minutes to
remove and replace all the plugs!  Get one, use it; you'll love it!
