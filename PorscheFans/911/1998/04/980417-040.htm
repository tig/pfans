Mike,

I put cheaper partial gray sheepskins on my gray GMC Yukon leather seats. 
The covers were those kind where the seating area is sheepskin but the side is synthetic material.

In about 6 months, both the sheepskin and the fake stuff, had bled dye - permanently staining the  leather. 
Of course the stain doesn't match the leather's color so now I can't remove the skins until I'm ready to redye the entire seat(s).

I suggest not using seat covers as over time they can also cause damage to the leather -  especially if they ever get damp. 
If you do - use only natural color (not dyed) 100% sheepskins whatever their price.
