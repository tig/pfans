Don't, rep[eat DO NOT put locktite on any AN fittings.  They are designed with
tapered pipe threads and flared ends for proper sealing.  If installed
correctly (use the proper double wrench method) they will never leak.  Using
locktite will only make removing them practically IMPOSSIBLE!
If you feel that you must use something on the threads, use teflon tape.  Just
make sure that you wind the tape opposite the direction of the mating thread,
so that the end is facing counterclockwise (opposite on left hand threads of
course!)
Also be sure not to wrap the teflon all the way to the end, that way there
will be no way for any of it to get into the oil.
