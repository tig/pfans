Hi Don,
   Unless you have access to buffing/polishing equipment, you should 
just use a mild metal polish and alot of elbo grease.  Avoid any 
steel products like steelwool, brillo pads, etc.  They will transfer 
some of the steel to the stainless and rust.  For real serious work, 
use something like a scotch-brite pad.  Be careful to use a fine grit 
or you will never get the scratches out.
   If you need more help, e-mail me.  We do stainless all day every 
day.
