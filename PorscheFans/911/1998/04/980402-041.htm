Todd Sokolowski  asked :

> Does anybody know the difference in weight between a 2.0L 
> and a 3.0L 911 engine? 

If the 2.0 is a magnesium case ('69?) and the 3.0 has early style exhaust
then I'd say 50 pounds. If the 2.0 is aluminum then probably
less than 50 pounds (alum is heavier than mag). 
If the 3.0 has stock SC exhaust then probably more difference
in weight. One more option - if the 2.0 is a T engine it has
Cast Iron cylinders so it could be a bit heavier than an E or S.
