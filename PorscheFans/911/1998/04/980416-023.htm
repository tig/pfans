On April 15, Chris Morton writes:

>I'm trying to remove one of the rear seat fold-down backs on my 86
>911 Carrera.  Has anyone ever done this?  The hinge closest to the
>wheel well seems to be held in with a very large flat head screw
>but I can't really tell how the inside hinge is held in place. Thanks
>in advance.

Chris,

The rear seats are held on by the screw you reference (on the outer
side) and slip over a metal pin on the inner edge.  The carpet is also
secured at the back, just in front of the rear deck, with two allen head
screws. Once the screws are out, pull the seatback forward slightly and
wiggle it off the pin.  The inner hinge itself is not (easily)
removable.

Hope this helps,
