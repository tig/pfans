Just FYI - I posted to the list a week or two ago about the squeaking
bushings on my front suspension. My mechanic installed grease fittings on
the arms, and the squeak is gone ... one hour of labor, plus a couple bucks
for the fittings ... far cheaper than replacing the arms. It did, however,
take a LOT of grease ... about two guns worth! We'll see how long it holds
up.
