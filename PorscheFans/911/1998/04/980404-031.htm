Shirish:

Choosing ignition wires isn't too critical for most applications. The
OEM wires are quite satisfactory in this regard.

We have used the Magnecor brand wires with excellent results. Although
we have no attachment to their Company, these products work very well
and
cause no electrical interference whatsoever.

Indeed, they surpress a great deal of RF and EMI problems that are
present on other brands of wiring. Electromotive Crank-fired DIS systems
in particular, are quite susceptable to these type of problems and will
randomly mis-fire without high resistance wire.. The Magnecor wires are
pure magic in this sensitive application and I know they work just great
with OEM ignitions.

If one experiences noise with the OEM ignition wiring, I might suggest
checking each and every plug connector for continuity and make sure that
there is no arcing at any connection where the plug connector or
distributor nipples attach to the ignition wires.

Use what your budget will allow......... :^)
