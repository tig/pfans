Brian,

After the hex head is stripped, I usually "Vise-Grip" them out.  Some penetrating oil 
sprayed at the base of the CV joint, and left overnight, can help as well.  BTW, the 
replacement bolts for those are widely available at hardware stores which carry metric 
selections.
