My .02.
By splitting the end of the plug the manufacturer increases the surface
where the spark can jump...you would get the same effect by filing slits
in a regular plug.
According to Jacobsen (?) these plugs just enable a weak system to have
a decent spark, thus creating the "miracle" performance you hear on TV.
A lot of hype for this plug...just get decent Bosch plugs...
