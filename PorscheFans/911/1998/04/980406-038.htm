Tom,

A couple of days ago you posted a question asking for a description of
"Amazon Green".  I have a '93 C2 Cab in that color and I wouldn't trade
it for any other color.  It's a dark blue-green metallic that looks
terrific when it's shined up.  In the bright sunshine (like in San Diego
where I used to live) it gets more blueish; on overcast days (like
everyday here in San Francisco) it looks more green. At night it looks
almost black.

Overwhelmingly (probably 85%) of the compliments and remarks that I get
on my car are on how good the color looks.  Of course, the purists on
the list would probably never own a 911 in anything other than Guards
Red, black, white or silver, but that would make for a boring world if
everyone had only those colored cars.

Good luck on your new purchase if you go for it.
