Chris Dwight had some questions about 73 911's.

I had a 74 911 2.7 engine in my 73 911 and have upgraded to a 2.7 RS 
engine.  Both run fine on unleaded fuel.  I use high octane unleaded, 
though I have heard it said by some older 911 owners that their 
particular car seemed to run better on regular unleaded.  I race mine, 
so I'm not experimenting with low octane.

The 73 carrera flares are slightly different than the 74-89 flares, but 
you'd have to be a real afficianado to see the difference.  If you plan 
to do concours, you should use the proper flares, but if you just want 
to track the car, it would not make much difference.
