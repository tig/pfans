Don,
	I just installed the SSI/Burch combo on my 1977 911.  Major improvement!
Low-end torque and throttle response are enhanced significantly.
Installation proved tougher than expected.  Even using heat & penetrating
oil, I broke seven (%$*&#) studs.  Had to have them cut out of the head by
a laser shop.  But in the end, it was a very worthwhile change.
	The sound is enhanced by the "extractor" characteristics of the Burch.
Under acceleration, I would characterize it as a "ripping" sound.  At
cruise, the exhaust is no more noisy than stock.  Overall, it is rude
enough to turn heads, but not so uncivilized as to piss off the neighbors
(too much).
