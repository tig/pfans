I'm going to start off with the front of the car, front suspension. Caveat
emptor: My experience deals only with 911's (early ones at that); not 993's,
964's, 968's or 962's, and I haven't personally done everything there is to
do, but in this area of the 911 things haven't changed all that much. In
addition, the difficulty level numbers are relative. One person's 5 could be
another person's 3. Remember, this is to help initiate those who haven't done
these procedures yet. Time should not be as much of a factor compared to
difficulty.
