Regarding replacing the rubber rocker trim, I generally use Glycerine as a
lubricant to facilitate placement. Glycerine is what Porsche used to
recommend for preserving and protecting all of the rubber trim.  Don't know
if they still recommend it or not but I got 21 years out of the rubber on a
previous 912E and the rubber all around was still good when I sold the car
last November.
