> This being the season where we have the upper hand over the coupe owners,
> thought I'd pass along a tip I discovered a few years ago.
> 
> To significantly reduce wind and buffeting in the driver's seat at highway
> speeds, raise the passenger window all the way and the driver's side window
> about one third. Much smoother than both up all the way. Helps keep the
> dreaded "Targa hair" syndrome in check, and, for the hairly challenged,
> your toupee, God forbid, from landing on someone's windshield.
> 
> Don't know what the effect is on the passenger side, because I can't reach
> the pedals from over there, and the wife complains vehemently when the
> windows aren't all the way down.
