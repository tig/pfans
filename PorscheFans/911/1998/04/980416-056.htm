Thanks to everyone who commented on my electrical problem.  Replacing the
battery (which checked out as bad) did not solve the problem.  As many
suggested, I checked voltage at the battery and found an overcharging
situation.  I also checked the bulb at the idiot light, which was ok.  It
did turn out to be a bad alternator, which I've now replaced.  The
alternator light now "blinks" again when I start and stop the engine like
it used to.  The headlight bulb that recently stopped working when all this
happened is now working again also!(?)
