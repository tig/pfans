     hi , the best way i found to remove even rusted-solid ball joint nuts
     is to use a large pipe wrench that will fit on the out side of the
     ball joint 'nut' and then insert a iron pipe about 4 ft long into the
     pipe wrench handle , then get a friend 'whom you realy don't like a 
     whole lot' to kind'of keep the wrench on , while your putting some 
     REAL force on it , the above works great the only down fall is you 
     will have to buy some new ball joint nuts as they will get a little 
     mangled before they come off.
