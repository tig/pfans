Donald,
You may want to look into applying a ceramic coating to your heat exchangers.
After blasting to bare metal, they coat both the inside and outside of your
exhaust system. The companies claim corrosion protection and reduced heat
radiation. Here are some websites you can try.

http://www.hpcoatings.com/
http://www.cyberhost7.net/performa/
http://www.goracing.com/techline/index.html
JetHot Coatings (URL unknown, but easy enough to find)

I powder coated my heat exchangers some years ago and, although in storage
until recently, they seem to be holding up fine (at least for the 300 miles
the car's been running anyway).
