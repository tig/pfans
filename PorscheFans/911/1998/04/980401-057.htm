17-18% is the figure used for FWD cars, which is probably accurate enough
for a rear engine car. In reality, it really doesn't much matter what's
at the flywheel, it's what you put to the ground. Conceivably, you could
change trans fluid and pick up a few horses at the wheel and it would
have nothing to do with the flywheel HP.
