Hi Pete:

You won't have any problems using a good brand of injector/valve cleaner
in your
Carrera.

As a matter of fact, its good routine maintenance for keeping the
injectors and intake valves free of the carbon buildup caused by the
MTBE added to unleaded gasoline. This carbon buildup changes the
injector spray pattern as well as builds soft deposits on the backsides
of the intake valves. This tends to absorb the fuel being sprayed past
the valve and creates a lean 'stumble' and irregularity in idle and
throttle response.

Regular use of stuff like Techron and LubroMoly Jectron / Ventil Sauber
will maintain the crisp throttle response and good idle qualities that
these cars are capable of. It also seems to help valve sealing caused by
carbon buildup on the valve seats. We see improvements in leakdown %'s
when treating a "bad one".

Once a month is good maintenance  :^)
