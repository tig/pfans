A little over a year ago, I was looking for a 911.  I could have purchased
a 964, the price difference was not an issue for me at that time.  I decided
that I prefered the torsion bar suspension and the looks of the 84-89 911 to
the coil springs and different body of the 964.

The air conditioning is a joke. The ventilation is perfect--just drop the top!

I do not regret my choice...even though I have replaced the rubber-centered
clutch with a turbo clutch (that's expensive!). 

I prefer the more challenging handling and the more taught suspension, but
then
again I bought the car knowing I was going to autocross and perhaps someday
race
the car. I also figured that as one of the last few of the original
suspension 911s
the car would have a good chance of appreciating some day.  Relatively few
were
imported I think, or perhaps their owners just don't get rid of them,
because it 
wasn't easy to find one for sale at that time.

Tom Pickett
