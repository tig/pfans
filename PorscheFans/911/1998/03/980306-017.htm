One thing I would suggest is to make sure your particular tire sizes are
intended for use on a particular wheel size.  I know that for R1s you
can only use up to a 205/55/R16 on the front 6" wide rim, the 225s are
too wide to work properly. <<

Actually that is backwards - the R1s will work fine on rims that
are 1" narrower than standard. 225/50s will work OK on a 6"
rim, especially on the front which has light weight. 245s will
be OK on a 7" too. But wide street tires with soft sidewalls will not
function well on these narrow rims. I have found that 225/50/15
all around on 6 x 15" rims are the best on an early 911 and don't
rub much. (we have to use 6" to stay in SCCA stock class,
of course 7" would be better).
