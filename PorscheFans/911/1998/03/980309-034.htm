I think that is good advice.  When one considers the reliance we place
upon our brakes to bring these machines down from high speed the few
bucks saved by turning rotors does not make sense.
However, if the rotors have been warped because one has mistakenly held
the brake pedal down after some seriously hot brake work, which will
warp el rotors pretty quick if one sustains this behavior, and the
rotors were otherwise within spec, it might be worth saving them if the
process does not get them too close to the wear limit.
Just my .02.
