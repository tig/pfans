You will have a lots of "dirty" fun with the wheel bearings! We use the
Swepco #101 Moly grease in this application. Inexpensive and really
lasts a long time. Best stuff for CV's too as long as you are getting
greasy,.........

Spindle nuts use a allen wrench to loosen the clamp and uncrew off. Be
sure and inspect the outer races carefully and replace the rear hub
seals, too
