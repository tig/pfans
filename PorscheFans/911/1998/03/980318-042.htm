I've had 195/65-15 (Yoko's), 205/60-15 (Yoko's), and 205/55-15 (Dunlop's
& BFG's) on my '71 911S.  They all worked well, but all rubbed very
slightly on the inside for severe "parking lot" turns (so I just avoid
turning to a full lock and all seems to be OK).  I'd don't think I would
try a wider tire, unless I could try it on my car first.
