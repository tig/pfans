Hi John -

I have replaced several suspension parts on various cars, and
I HIGHLY recommend any of these leverage-type tools over the
picke-forks, which I only use as a last resort, and only then if
I'm replacing the parts, since they tend to destroy what ever
they come in contact with.

I think you'll find that the tie rods are on very-tightly, and you 
will have to hammer quite hard on the fork to remove the tie-rod,
something I don't think you will enjoy :-)
