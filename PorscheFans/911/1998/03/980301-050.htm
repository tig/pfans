Luis,
Although I've never tried it with my C4, I'm told they all do that
because the clutch can't handle the torque required to break  free all 4
wheels at once.  Since the tires won't spin, and engine won't stop
turning at 4000rpm full throttle the only thing left to give is the
drivetrain or the clutch.  The C4 drivetrain is solid, so clutch slips. 
BTW, I don't recommend this for repeated starts.  It gets expensive ;^)
So to answer your question, yes it was when you started.
