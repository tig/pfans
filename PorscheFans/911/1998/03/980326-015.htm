I disagree.  I have a '89 C4 that has almost no understeer.  I recently got
the car and expected the worse, but took it out on the track, with 36psi
front and 44 rear as recommended on the street tires, and it was easily
pitch around.  While the 993 AWD is better, the 964 is not good enough to
eliminate oversteer in the car.  Porsche dialed in understeer by using
softer springs.  Changing springs, shocks, etc should make the car handle
anyway you like.

This is just what I have heard from experts in my PCA region and from my
own experience.
