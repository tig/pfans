Hi Jad,

You should not under normal conditions (which includes driving the car very 
hard :-) need to bleed the AWD system. I think it's only necessary after 
servicing the system, and they aren't even a 30k or 60k checklist item. in 
fact, be careful, because even though there are nipples in various places 
you (1) need a special Bosch tool to cycle the interlocks for correct 
bleeding, and (2) some parts of the system have high pressure in em.

I wish i could tell you why your light went on, but i have no idea :-( 
  I've never illuminated the interlock warning on my c4 while driving 
except when I first got the car and was playing around before having read 
the owner's manual. I put the car in locked differential mode, but since I 
live on a cul-de-sac I needed to turn. Did you know that our cars have a 
warning horn? They do! The car screeched.
