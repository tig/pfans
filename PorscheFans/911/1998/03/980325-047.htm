I opened the bonnet (the trunk lid) and pushed the outermost two gauges out
from behind. I couldn't really get my hand through the maze of wires and
cables back there, so I used a stick to push. The stick was about 3/4"
square (20mm) or so, and about a foot long. I put a rag over the end of it
so I wouldn't bugger up the back of the gauges. You do have to be careful
that you are pushing on the gauge case and not on a wire. Use a flashlight
or a drop light so you can see what you are pushing on. Try to push at
different points around the circumference of the case back. Once you have it
out a small amount (a half inch or so), you can get in the car and pull the
rest of the way from the front.

Once the left-hand two gauges are out, you can reach through the larger hole
and push the next gauge out, etc.

The idea of using a screwdriver from the face side makes me shudder...
