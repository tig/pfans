The only real way short of unwinding or snipping the wires is to jack up
the car and you will find the other end of the cables on the flapper
boxes which direct heat either outside the car or to the heating system
over top of the rear axles, fastened to the body.. There is a small set
screw on each of the flappers (hard to miss) which you merely loosen and
there you are. Now I say merely as if the screws are corroded or
something nasty is wrong there it can be a bit of a pain......
