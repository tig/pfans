I did this last year.  I suggest that you take the hubs to a machine
shop and let them take out the inner races (cups in bearing-speak).  It
is a press fit and the hub is a nice alloy which you do not want to
bugger up.
I recommend you ask the parts folks for Timken bearings, an OEM to
Porsche.  Don't buy cheap bearings!
Any good wheel bearing grease will do.  I used Wolf brand the last time
I did this.
Bearings should at least be repacked and inspected every 30K.
Good luck
