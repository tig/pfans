I would suggest an alternate "test" to diagnose the clutch slippage.
Place the tranny in 3rd gear.
Do a heal-toe ( hold brke and apply gas at the same time )  and with out
"dumping"
the clutch, release the clutch and observe.
The engine should bog and die out.
If it continues to run depress the clutch peddle put it into first gear
and drive to the parts house and buy a new clutch and install it. You
may want to buy 2 clutches 
so you have one on hand as a back up for your drag race tactics.
( very fast wear, if you d not purchase one
that is designed for that type of use )
Just my .02 cents worth.
