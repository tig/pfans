One thing I would suggest is to make sure your particular tire sizes are
intended for use on a particular wheel size.  I know that for R1s you
can only use up to a 205/55/R16 on the front 6" wide rim, the 225s are
too wide to work properly.  Also, if you switch back to 205/55/R16 for
the front, you should also switch back to the 225/50/R16 for the rears,
to keep the proportion right.
