I don't have Vanetine One, but my detector came with a wire that allowed it
to be hard wired into the electrical system.  I wired it into a switch
circuit and ran the wire up the passenger side A pillar and across the top
of the windshield between the widshield seal & the windshield.  It comes
out at the left hand end of the passenger sunvisor.  I can clip my detector
on to the visor & plug it in.

This puts the detector as high as it can get (best theoretical position)
and eliminates the dangling cord that can be seen though the back window.
I keep the coiled cord in my wife's car so we can use the detector on
longer family trips as well.
