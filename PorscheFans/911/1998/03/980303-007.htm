Andy, I just did my calipers.  I marked the positions of the pistions with
a scribe before removing them.  Then I aligned them before putting them
back in.  I found that I was able to gently pry the pistions out of the
calipers with two large bladed screwdrivers as levers.  They came out very
easy.  If you can get them out a bit, you should be able to turn them.  I
used a channelock pliers with lots of electrical tape on the jaws as a
piston "turning pliers".
