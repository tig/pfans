To replace the sparkplugs on the lower side you need to remove the lower
pan. Then take the rt. side muffler off. to access the side panel. Take 
it off and there are plugs 4-6. To do the left side you have to remove
the cat. conv. to gain access. tocyls 1-3. Don't forget to unplug the 
o2 sensor lead on the top side. Be careful to get the plugwires properly
reconnected. You can check this by disconnecting the final stages for
the ing. systems one at a time and checking for a skip. The final stages
are located in front of each coil.
