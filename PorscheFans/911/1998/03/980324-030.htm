Hi all,
  If anyone is interested, I have just changed my oil to a synthetic
brand and like it alot.
  The 2 most significant things I noticed right away were the engine now
runs quieter and cooler. It has been 4 days since the change and as of
yet have not detected any oil leaks (my car has 90,000+ miles now).
  I recall some time ago when I tryed synthetic oil in my Datsun 280Z
that it began leaking the next day from the front crank seal.
  Anyway, Just thought I would pass this along. Thanks to all the list
for the info and advice, but spicifically to Andrew Warren and Ed
Yourkovich. Thanks Again
