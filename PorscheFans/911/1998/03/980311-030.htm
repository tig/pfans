Thanks to all who replied to my problem of the C4 tail
failing to raise and lower.  The problem seemed to come
and go and indicated an electrical problem or loose 
connection.

The solution was under the tail.  There are two connectors
there along the power wire that runs from the front of the
car along the passenger-side hinge for the tail and around
the rear of the tail.  After unplugging and reseating these
two connectors, the tail works perfectly!
