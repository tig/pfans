Luis,

> How can I tell if my car has the upgraded distributor kit and if it doesn't
> have it, where can I buy it.

Your local Porsche dealership should have the kit - it's cheap. If you
ask the service guys for a "distributor vent kit" for an early C2/C4, they
will know what you mean.

If you look at the bottom of the distributors, there are oval-shaped holes
in both distributors. The presence of metal plugs in both means you do not
have the kit installed. If you have a rubber/plastic hose going into one
of the holes, you DO have the kit installed (the hose will go up to the
heater ducting coming off the engine).
