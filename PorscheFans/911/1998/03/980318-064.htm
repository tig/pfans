Small payback here for some of the excellent tips i've benefitted from off
the list. Concerning synthetic oils 
Consumer Reports tested 20 popular oils and published the results in their
7/96 issue. They ran 75 New
York City taxi cabs a total of 4.5 million miles changing oil & filters at
6000 mile intervals. Then they 
dismantled the engines and measured wear on the crank main and con rod
journals,cam lobes, cylinders,pistons, rings, main and con rod bearings and
valve stems. Bottom line was that they found no
measureable differences between the engines that were run with synthetics
versus the engines using
the other oils. This publication accepts no advertising monies from anyone
so i would think they are 
unbiased . The report is available for viewing on thier web site at
www.consumerreports.org .
