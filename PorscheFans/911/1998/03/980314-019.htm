Hi,

I Have a 73 911 Targa,  My headlights did the same thing.  The car is a lot
older, but from what I know the switch is still the same type.  The
headlights you put in probably overloaded the switch and burned it to the
point that it only works in dropped position.  Either that or it just failed
due to age like mine did.    My switch only cost me $75 Canadian.  So I
figure it should cost you about $50 to $60 U.S.  Then you have to put it in.
You also might want to look into hooking up the lights through a relay
system so it doesn't happen again.   Hope this helps you out.
