Hi,

    I had my 97 993 lowered a few weeks ago with the H&R springs and
have the stock springs. The car looks good (I have JPEG's if you email
your address) but rides a little rough. I have noticed some squeaks &
and a clunck once in a while when I travel over bumps and uneven road
surfaces. My mechanic raised the car and we took a look to see if the
springs were rubbing, we could not find any signs of rubbing wear. One
word of caution, you must be aware of the lower ride around driveways
and sharp ramps, otherwise you will be replacing your front end lower
air works.
