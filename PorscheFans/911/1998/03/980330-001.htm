>Any opinions on this? Is the handling that dramatically better on the 993

I've driven, both on open roads and track, 964s(plain, turbo and C4) and
993s (plain and C4S) and I have found just the regular (maybe less)
differences you can find in cars with a 5 year gap. No miracles. I have too
say that in my experience you can say the same also comparing a late 3.2
Carrera with 205/16 - 225/16 (actually my own was very lowered and
stiffened) with a 964. Just a different feeling from a 964 but, torsion bars
or springs, you can drive a post '78 (and, maybe, earlier) 911 with the same
confidence. If handling is the only reason to switch for a 993 from a 964, a
better way to invest your money is attending some serious driving school.
That will be a great improvement in general performances. Please, don't
believe the blah-blah that car magazines make whenever a new model is
introduced; the biz must go on...
