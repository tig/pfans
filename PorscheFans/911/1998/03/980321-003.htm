if you mean the seal for the axle flange (the one that wears from mileage, not
just age), this is easy.  just undo one large (17mm/19mm) head bolt in the
center of the flange, and it will fall into your hand.  use s seal remover,
and you're set.  no worries on disturbing the diff.  
however, if you mean the large (8" diameter) diff side cover gasket, i can't
comment.
