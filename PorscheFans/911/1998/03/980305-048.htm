Andy, this simplest thing to do is to push the pistons out using the 
brake pedal.  You don't have to push them out of the bores, just extend 
them enough for the next step.  Get a large pair of pipe wrench pliers 
(aka channel locks), wrap the jaws with duct tape.  Then use them to 
gently grip and turn the piston into alignment.

BTW, for $5.88 you can buy a twenty-degree metal alignment template 
from Mercedes Benz, part number 000.589.35.23.00.
