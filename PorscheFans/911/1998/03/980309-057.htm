Brian,

My '88 Cab did this as well, when I first got it.  An alignment, and 
balancing the tires helped, but what the car really needed was new 
strut inserts.  I ended up with new Bilstein front struts (Green HD, 
not sports) and new Bilsteins shocks on the rear.  It totally cured 
my car of the shakes.  When the front dampers are totally shot, even 
the slightest little shimmy ends up being felt through the wheel.

Good Luck!
