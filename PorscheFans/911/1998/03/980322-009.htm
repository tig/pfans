Josh,

The VIN indicates a 911 (911) 1978 (8) SC with a US 3.0L engine (2) in a
coupe chassis (0), sequence number 0367. (Rf: Bruce Anderson's
Performance Handbook, pg 43).

Engine serial number is found to the right of the fan housing on a
vertical support member.  Should be in the range of 6280001-6289999
unless for California, in which case it should be in the range
6580001-6589999. (Rf: Stoddard's catalog, Body and Engine ID section, pg
114).

Hope this helps.
