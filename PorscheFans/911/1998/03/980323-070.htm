>Luis, a couple of ideas:

>1) you have worn pads, which now don't fit flat against the newly cut
rotors.
>Give it a couple of hundred miles or replace the pads, then see what

>
>2) rotors weren't cut evenly, leading to a slightly warped condition.  You

>If not, might not be the problem, although you can check by jacking up each
>wheel, pump brake once or twice, then slowly turn the wheel to see if it's
>dragging at any point in the rotation.  Very slight added resistance at one
>point
>is probably normal, but noticeable draggin at always one point is
indication
>of warped/badly cut rotor.
