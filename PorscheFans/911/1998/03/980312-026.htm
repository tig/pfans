> I want a Snell SA-95 rated helmet, and have not
> yet found a local dealer in Dallas/Fort Worth
> that has any I can try on.
> 
> Could I try on an M-rated helmet at a Cycle Shop
> and then assume a mail-order SA would fit ?

I wouldn't bet on it. Different models of helmets from the same company
can fit very differently. In some cases M rating versus SA rating doesn't
mean any difference in the helmet, but in some cases it does (for instance,
the viewport is often smaller on SA helmets).

Your best bet, if you CAN'T find a dealer in your area, is to order from
a mail-order company that will accept returns if the sizing is incorrect.
