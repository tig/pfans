I've had both a '85 and an '86. The ventilation systems in both are really
essentially useless for cooling or providing fresh air. (Heating is OK here in
So California.) Lot's of noise, not much air. Open the windows or sunroof.

The a/c is better on the '86, but adding the bulkhead vent on the '85 pretty
much evens things out. Neither are great, however, unless freshly charged. The
fresh charge doesn't last too long, unfortunately.
