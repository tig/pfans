Take out your spare, remove four plugs in the front panel. A socket 
wrench will take off the guards. You will note that the rubber of the 
front bumper deco is cut out for the guard studs, so you will need a 
new deco. There will also be two holes in the bumper below the deco. 
To change the deco strip, you must take off the bumper. Four bolts at 
the sides of the front compartment must removed (in front of the 
batteries) and there are two bolts, one at each side to take out. 
There is an angle bracket they bolt to. Bruce
