"Up-Fixin' der Porsche" is a compilation of all of the technical articles that
is in the Porsche Club magazine, the "Panorama".  Have to be a member to
order the Upfixin' series. 

If you own a Porsche and are not a member of the Porsche Club of America, 
you should have your head examined.  They are a great organization and
a wonderful bunch of people.  Monthly magazine is on eof the best car
publications going, plus you get to know the local Porsche nuts, meets,
and good (and bad) parts sources and fix-um-up places.
