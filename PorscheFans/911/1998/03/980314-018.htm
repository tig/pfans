I think the main reason why US 993s ride higher than Euro 993s is due to
US bumper height laws, just like on the BMW M3s.  You can order the
factory Euro shocks and springs here if you want to.  Several people
have complained that the euro springs don't install properly w/ US
shocks, leaving a "gap" between the spring and perch in the rear. 
Eibach and H&R springs have no problem fitting onto US shocks.  I will
be uploading pictures on the web of a friend's 993 w/ Eibach springs and
and my 993 w/ H&R springs in a few weeks.  I'll post the web address
once it's done.
