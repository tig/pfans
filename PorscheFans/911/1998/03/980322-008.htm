On March 22, Samer Hijazi writes:

>Can anyone validate for me if the engine serial number in a car I am
>looking at, is the actual motor for the model ?
>Car: Euro '72 911S
>The chassis number for the car is : 911 2 3 1 0867
>The engine number is: * 632 2251 *

According to Bruce Anderson's Performance Handbook, the car is a 1972
911S Targa.  This matches with the Body and Engine ID section shown in
the back of the Stoddard's catalog, which lists the engine numbers of
Targas for that year
as ranging from 6310001-6322586. Apparently, the engine is the correct
one for the year model.  However, the only way to tell if it is the
original engine (I think) is to get the cardex from Porsche Cars North
America (PCNA).  Call them at 800.PORSCHE and ask for the cardex; costs
$30, takes a few weeks, and requires a valid title.

Hope this helps.
