Tory,

From my experience and what I have read, an oil temperature of 210
degrees F is well within the normal operating limits for any 911 engine
(180-220 deg F). 200 degrees may be preferred, but actual engine
temperature will vary depending on driving conditions and ambient
temperature.  A rise in temperature in stop and go 'town' driving would
not be unexpected.  See if the problem persists or temps rise above
230-240 F before becoming too concerned.

Hope this helps.
