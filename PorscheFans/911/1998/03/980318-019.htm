Hi Chris,

Sometimes, depending on how tight the seals are, they can be
removed easily by screwing a drywall screw in it (they are sharp)
Now you can pull the seal out with pliers. Not so elegant, but work.
I've seen somekind of hooks for this purpose also.

If you open the side cover, no fancy stuff ahead. When you open the
side cover, the diff. can be removed and you can tap the seal out.
Actually the diff will fall out if you are not careful, I assume
transmission is still in the car? 
You need an O-ring 999.701.392.40 (195 x 3,5) for the cover.
Change bearings & such, then you should at least check the pattern on
ring & pinion.
