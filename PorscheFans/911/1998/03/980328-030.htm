Had an opportunity today, due to the beautiful Indiana afternoon, to install
my new 930S steering wheel from AJ USA into my 89 Speedster.  Installation
was a snap and what a nice steering wheel.  Full gauge visibility and a much
larger, better feel than the standard 4 spoke.  I had gotten used to the old
3 spoke on my former 912E and just could never adapt to the skinnier feel of
the 4 spoke.  150 miles latter and I'm much, much  happier now.
