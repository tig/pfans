Subject:Beware: Keep that Gas Tank Full.
Date:Sat, 21 Mar 1998 17:15:41 +0000
Our 73.5 T has been running perfectly for several months.  Last week, a few 
minutes after firing it up, it coughed and stumbled to a stop. The "need gas" 
light was on, so I concluded I'd run out and added a gallon. No luck, so we 
towed the car to the shop. 
The problem? I'd let the gas level get low in the tank and 25 years of #!&% was 
ingested by the fuel pump.
The answer? $650 to pull the tank, clean and refinish it with a paint-like 
substance, and replace the fuel pump, the filter, and a few gaskets. 
So clean that old tank out, or at least keep it near full.
