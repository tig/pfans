This is what Bruce Anderson ( Author of Porsche 911 Performance handbook)
had to say about the aftermarket chips available pg 148.  

After testing a bunch of chips with Jerry Woods, Mark Anderson wrote the
following:

"The largest horsepower increase that we saw was 9 percent, and at peak
horsepower the greatest improvement was 4 percent, or an increase from 217
DIN hp to 226 DIN hp.  The average improvement of the best of the best was
3.4 percent.  That is picking the best performance of the best chip for
each given rpm of the test."

He goes on to test exhaust systems and finds that far more performance can
be gained by simply using the older SSI heat exchangers with a sport
muffler.
