I know exactly what you are talking about.  I have a daily driver Renault Fuego with
bosch MPFI and an air/flow meter.  After checking for vacuum leaks in the system
and replacing the O2 sensor it still surged.  The fix was suprisingly easy.  There
are 2 adjustment screws on the intake side of the manifold.  1) altitude adjustment
and 2) idle enrichment.  Simply the adjusment of these screws takes care of the problem...

NOTE:(only adjust after the car has warmed up for at least a half hour of driving
and only make very small changes to the screws,note the position B4 adjustment.)

It took about 5 times of small adjustment to get it perfect so be patient.

This was on my renault but I assume the Bosch systems are all similar.
