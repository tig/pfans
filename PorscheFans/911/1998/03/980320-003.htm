This might be worth checking... Last year, while working in my the car, I
decided to lube the centrifugal advance mechanism inside the distributor on
my 84 Carrera. It rides on two plastic bushings and can be gotten to by
removing the distrib cap and rotor. Well, I decided to lube it up with some
white lithium grease. BIG MISTAKE! THe car developed a flat spot at about
3-4 k. It seems that by adding grease, I made them hang... After using
some brake cleaner and q-tips to completly flush the area, the car returned
to normal operation. If you think about it, at about 3.5K is where things
begin to happen, at least in my car. This coincides with the dist mech advance.

Might be worth to check yours out for gunk. Good luck!
