Having purchased my first Porsche in 1959, I recall the amazing camaraderie
among Porsche people in that era. We always high beamed each other on the
roadways and stopping to help someone who was stalled was very common. When I
bought my used 57 Coupe in a rural community, all the Chevy and Ford folks
said I would be stranded if ever a part failed.

Well it happened one Sunday but another Porsche driver stopped, spent around
an hour diagnosing the problem, got me and the Porsche to a motel within
walking distance of a Porsche dealer and all was well on Monday.

IMHO, I would be a fitting remembrance to Dr. Porsche to renew some of these
customs and attitudes.
