The other day, I had a problem with my washer pump. It was real simple 
to fix.
I disconnected the Hose's going to the washer pump, then remove the pump 
itself off the car. Remove the 2 screws at the bottom of the pump and 
the housing will come off. If it has some crud in it, then just tap it a 
little until it comes off. Inside of my pump was some crud that stopped 
the wheel on the pump to circulate. I cleaned all the crud off and lubed 
the wheel and the motor, and reinstalled the pump. No problems after 
that. It was that simple..and I saved around fifty bucks..
