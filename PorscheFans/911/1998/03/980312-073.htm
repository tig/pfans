PFans, 
I'm responding to Bob's question below about synthetic oil. I use castrol
syntec 5-50 and change it every 5000 miles. It doesn't burn or leak at all.
I've stretched it once to 6000 but changed the filter at 3000. Even at 6000
miles it's very conservative. IMHO, the extra expense for synthetic is well
worth it considering the extra protection your valuable investment gets.
Nothing beats synthetic. 
Also, what kind of oil are you buying for $7 per quart? I'm not convinced nor
have I seen any data that Redline or Lubromoly(both are over $7 per quart) are
any better than mobil 1 or castrol syntec. M1 and CS run less than $4/quart.
