This was the same BS story that Tweeks tried to sell me.  The Carrera 
tail seal and the Turbo tail seal are NOT the same.  There are little 
triangular cutouts at the corners to allow the seal to turn the corner.  
Since the base of the Turbo tail is so much larger than the base of the 
Carrera tail, the cutouts are not in the same place.  Frustrated, I just 
cleaned my old one up with 3M adhesive cleaner, reglued it and sent the 
Tweeks piece back.  It's an easy job, I used 3M weatherstrip adhesive 
(black) to hold the seal on.
