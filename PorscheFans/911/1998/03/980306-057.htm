Mine goes just under the rear view mrror.  As far as the power cord, I made
up one myself from a telephone line cord, cut off a large portion of the outer
insulation, cut out the two superfluous wires, then ran the cord all the way 
around the windshield grommet, down behind the dash, fed it thru the 
firewall, installed an in-line fuse, and then tapped into the fuse box at a 
switched source.   No hanging wires, and a lot neater.  Have done this on
my old '84 Carrera and on daily-driver Audi as well.
