For Steve Stomsky,
Pull the speedometer (it is easier with the steering wheel removed--the
horn pad just yanks off, unplug the horn wire, remove the 27mm nut, and
pull the wheel off).  Shine your flashlight around behind the
speedometer and you will see a black box about 1-1/2 inches square.  It
has a small button, push that in to reset it, and the light should go
out.
