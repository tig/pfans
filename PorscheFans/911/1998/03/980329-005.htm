Norm,

A couple days ago, you asked about the possibility of inspecting head studs on
the 2.7L engine.  After reviewing the Tech column in the November, '97
Excellence magazine (pg 32), I wanted to update a previous comment on how this
may be done.  Bruce Anderson notes that when the valve covers are off to inspect
valve guide wear, it is also possible to examine all 24 head stud nuts and
washers, as well as to look for corrosion.  It is not necessary to take the
engine apart further.

Hope this helps, and that the car is running well and ready for the driving
season.  Enjoy!
