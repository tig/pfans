I just overhauled my front calipers.  Several pistons were rusted and somewhat
frozen in the calipers.  the easy and safe way to remove them was to used an air
compressor.  You don't need much presssure (20 to 30 psi).  Put a block of wood
between the pistons, an O-ring on the air nozzle and they should both pop out.
