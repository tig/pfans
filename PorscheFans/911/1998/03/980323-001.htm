Have to post this, my P-enthusiasm grows every time I drive the car and
I want the world to know.

I am slowly approaching the danger zone now, exploring the physics of
Porsche handling. It is just amazing how good the car feels once at the
right speed, with the right amount of throttle and steering input. Once
it has settled into a corner, you can really feel the potential of
steering the car with the rear end. Walter Rorhl was right, cars are fun
when they have 200 HP+. 

Of course, I'm talking "potential" here. I'm not there yet and have a
long way to go before I master the specifics of this car, but I am
surely on my way to automotive heaven, driving a very well balanced
piece of German car engineering. And I am already anticipating the first
track time I have planned early April.
