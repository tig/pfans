  It sounds like you and your mechanic have covered all of the bases.
One thing does come to mind however,...........Is your 911SC lowered?

911's that are lowered and leveled without checking and adjusting the
corner weights will exhibit these characteristics. This is caused by
gross cross-weight problems that make the car rest on two diagonal
wheels. This will make the car quite unpredictable in hard braking and
behave erratically in right & left turns.

Not easy I know, but putting your car on a set of scales with the
swaybars disconnected will show any problems.
