Question:
I am looking at the 930/911 Turbo - Complete Kit With Heat Exchangers
from B&B Tri-Flo.

Can someone tell me if they are worth the money?

Answer:

The B&B system is very well made. However, the muffler produces a lot of 
resonance and the right header is routed a bit close to the Oil tank 
S-hose. The fit of the B&B replacement S-hose is a bit sloppy and care 
should be taken to ensure the clamps are tight.
