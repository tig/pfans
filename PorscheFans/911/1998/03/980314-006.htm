Drew:

The results indicate that your engine is in excellent shape.  There are other
tests (sounds like your Dr.) that might also be done and could tell more.
They may be reported on your results sheet.  They are fuel dilution,
viscosity, and (not relevant for 911's) glycol content.

You should continue with the tests at each oil change to look for any
increases in concentration of metals that would indicate degradation of
various engine components, such as bearings.
