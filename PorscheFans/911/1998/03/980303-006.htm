these things to check, though:

- Is there water in the tank ?
- Check the tubes to the nozles on the bumber. Mine needs to be
replaced. Any loose clamps which will cause a leak ?
- I don't know if there is a separate pump, but on many other cars there

water tank, and can get hard to reach.

tubes so you can see water comming out if not then the problem is closer
to the tank.
