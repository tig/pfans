Tony and Eric,
If the spots are really water hardness *relics,* just clean them off by
scrubbing using a paper towel soaking wet with vinegar.   Don't use sulfuric
acid (battery acid) since the calcium and magnesium water salts will form
insoluble gypsum.  Calcium and magnesium acetates, formed from the vinegar
reaction with the water spots, are very soluble.
Of course if the water spots are really slight corrosion/abrasion of the
glass, a
polish with a fine cerium oxide abrasive is just the thing.
