Recently I accidently tore my top.  The rip was 4 inches long!  My '88 
911 is a daily driver/autocrosser so its not a concours car.

I (with the help of my girlfriend) bought matching black thread and 
stiched the tear close.  Then we CAREFULLY ironed on a black patch used 
for clothing; one on the outside and one on the inside (over the liner). 

If you look closely, yes you can see the repair. My top is torn and 
repaired in several places(mostly seams) over the years but very 
functional(no leaks) and looks decent.
