You need to replace the tie rod if damaged.  Yes you can straighten it, 
but it is compromised.

You need to go over the suspension thoroughly.  In particular, on a 911 
I would be very concerned about the spindle/strut weld point. An 
alignment will tell you a lot about what else may have been tweaked in 
the accident.
