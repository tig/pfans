Daryl:

It depends entirely on the way the car was stored.  If the owner KNEW
that the car would hardly be driven, and treated it accordingly, it
may be in great shape and worth buying.  If he didn't, it may have
(among other things) a rusty gas tank, rusted-out muffler, cracked
and flat-spotted tires, sticking shocks, mildewed interior, brittle
rubber seals, corroded brake parts, AND heavy wear on the engine due
to improper break-in.
