Andy gave excellent advice on some of the things to do to a 2.7 that
will make more enjoyable for track use!

I just wanted to add one thing, the stock CIS pistons will strike the
valves if the "S" cams are used; too much lift for those puppies. You
must change the pistons to the RS ones or use a custom set of JE's with
valve reliefs.
Also, the Turbo oil pump is the best one, however the Carrera one will
work also.
Be sure to do the oil bypass mod if you change pumps provided this
hasn't been done. It depends upon what year the 2.7 bottom end is.

(Don't forget the nice big oil cooler!)
