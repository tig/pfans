Ernie, I can't speak about 3.2 Carreras, but my 1980 911SC has 213,500
miles on the original engine. I have added Carrera Tensioners (at
151,000 when I bought it from the original owner). Oil consumption
appears to be 1 quart (synthetic)/5,000 miles. I hope to make it to
300,000 miles before I consider a rebuild. The car is a daily driver so
it gets to temperature nearly every time I drive it. I suspect that
regular driving and routing maintainance is what keeps it alive.

I should point out that while the engine is strong, other things have
gone (fuel pump, distributor, starter, etc.). My mechanic says Porsches
tend to wear out from the outside in.
