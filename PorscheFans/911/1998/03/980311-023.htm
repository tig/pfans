Walt - You did what I would have done.  There are formulations which
say they are for this purpose and look like some sort of light oil lubricant
with some graphite flakes in suspension.  I used this recently - a little on
the key, move it in and out, turn, a little more - and it helped a lot.  Some
light lubrication on the tumblers is helpful.  On our '77s I think the key
mechanism is  separate from the electrical contacts, which is good as
this is less likely to mess them up.  I know the '84s are separate, as I had
to replace the switch part once.
