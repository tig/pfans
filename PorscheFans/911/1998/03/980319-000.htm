one of these bolts is used to adjust the relative angle of the spring plate to
the short arm that slips over the torsion bar end.  this bolt is in fact an
eccentric adjuster; ie., rotating it wil cause the angle to change.  the
second bolt is used to hold the adjustment and keep the two parts attached.
you need a special thin 39mm (?) wrench to get in there.  i have seen it
advertised in panaroma.
