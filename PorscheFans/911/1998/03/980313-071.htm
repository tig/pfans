Mike, I'd normally say buy a shop manual and read it, but on reviewing
mine, it doesn't cover getting at the lamp.  However, there is a Tech Bulletin
on converting an early 993 to the top-mounted brake light unit (Group 6, no.
9603).  Reading this, looks like the painted cover pries out, then there 
is a long mounting strip held in place with two nuts.  Unscrew this, and
you can then unscrew the lamp unit from underneath the strip.

You might be SOL on the bulb replacement; neither Tech Bulletin or parts
microfiche describe any individual lamp replacement.  Pictures show 
brake light unit as an (apparent) sealed unit.

If you are under warranty, I would say bring the sucker to the dealer and let
him play with it.  If he breaks anything, you still get a free repair; if it
looks
like you monkeyed with it, he might not honor the warranty.
