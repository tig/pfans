From the VIN you gave, the car should be a '75 911 Targa with a Carrera
2.7 mechanically injected engine.  Engine type '6' may be euro spec,
since '4' is specifically a US Carrera engine.  (From Bruce Anderson's
Performance Handbook).

The 'engine number' on the other hand is probably a part number
(901.106.100.15R) for the fan housing, since I think engine numbers for
that vintage start with '6'  (not sure about this one).
