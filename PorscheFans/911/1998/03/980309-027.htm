For 993 owners out there, based on a lot of local experience, a 993 
will use about a quart per 1200-2000 miles for the first 15,000 miles.

At around 15,000 miles something magic happens. The oil consumption 
drops to near zero. Conventional wisdom is that it takes longer to 
break in the engine with synthetic oil. 

Also, you will observe an increase in power as the engine finally gets 
all its parts seated correctly. My 96 C4S and 95 C2 both exhibited 
this behavior and a half dozen local 993 owners confirm it.
