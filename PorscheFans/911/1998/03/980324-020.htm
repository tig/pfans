As others have said, that's the way they are. BUT to add insult
to injury, Porsche put little 6" wide wheels on the fronts.
Running a 7" wide wheel with 225/50 tires will surely help.
Maybe you can get the same style wheel in 7" width.

A great upgrade I have seen on 964s is the stock 993 17"
cup wheels, 7" front and 9" rear. I don't know a good size for
the front in street tires however, but keep the front to rear
within 20-30mm (225/255 for example). The 993 wheels really
help the appearance of the 964 too, which I never liked much.
