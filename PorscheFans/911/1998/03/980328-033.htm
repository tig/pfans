You don't HAVE to do it; the car (probably?)won't blow up in your face or
 send a nasty message to Porsche AG over your cell phone saying it's 
being tortured.  However, if you are interested in postponing a turbocharger 
replacement and giving your engine the best chance of a long life, it is
highly recommmended to let it idle or (better yet) drive it gently for several
minutes.  I thing the gentle driving might be better to get a better air flow 
through the oil cooler (my old '84 Carrera always ran cooler above 10 MPH
 than it did at idle).  Oh yeah, turn off the A/C during this cool-down phase.
