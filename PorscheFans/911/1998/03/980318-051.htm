> my engine runs a compression ratio of   about 11.0:1  and the heads
> have all been rebuilt using hardened seats, bronze guides and all
> the other goodies. My question  is would my current setup safely
> allow me to run race fuel? such as VP RED or VP C12 which contains
> lead. I know that hardened valve seats were designed for the
> purpose of unleaded fuel's but would running a  leaded mixture
> cause harm to my engine?

Jeffrey:

Leaded gas hurts nothing except the environment and catalytic
converters.  Leaded race gas will do no harm to your engine... In
fact, with an 11:1 compression ratio, I'd never run anything BUT 
race gas.

Keep in mind that UNLEADED race fuel with octane ratings in the
100-110 range is pretty easy to find... You don't NEED to use leaded
gas unless you're looking for 115-120 octane.
