Hi Gary,
I had the clutch pedal break the weld and go loose on my  74' 911S. 
Being in  the san Francisco  area I went to Partsheaven and bought a 
used pedal for about $26. A rebuilt pedal assy (with bronze bearings) 
was just over $100. Call these guys, they  are a great help. Look in  
Excellence for their phone number, I don't have it near me right now. 
Just make sure you use a NEW pin to secure it, I didn't and had to pull 
my  pedal cluster out again to replace it when it broke. Good luck on  
your rebuild.
