Hi David:
The owner's manual for my car recommends using petroleum jelly on the
wheels periodically. I have done it several times, and it brings back
the nice dull luster on the black part. It also makes the polished parts
look nice. My wheels don't have any stains, so I can't say what it does
for that. The procedure is to wash the wheels, dry them, rub them
liberally with the PJ, then buff it off completely with a soft cloth. I
follow this with a coat of wax.
