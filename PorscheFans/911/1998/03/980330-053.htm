If you email Red Line ('redline@redlineoil.com'), they will send you
a list of distributors/dealers.

In fact, their web site (http://www.redlineoil.com) lists a Canadian
distributor in Concord, Ontario - Motorsport Technology at 905-660-6614

I sent them email and asked about proper oil weights, etc, for my car. Had
a fairly quick response, though they suggested their 10w40 weight which
seems odd as they have a 15w50 and 20w50 as well. Ask them which of their
gear lubes to use for your particular car, and tell them whether you've got
limited slip or not.
