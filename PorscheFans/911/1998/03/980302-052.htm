IMHO 238hp from a stock 2.7 RS is a distinct possibility. I rebuilt a stock
2.4S with SSI heat exchangers; other than that my 2.4 was completely stock. It
dyno'd at 210hp. Extrapolating up you'd get 236.5 hp from your 2.7 using the
same specific output I acheived. Taking in possibilities for tolerances and
polishing and balancing, etc that may have been done to your subject engine,
238 seems possible to  me.
