Don:

I can't give you any comparisons to the stock exhaust, since I never
ran the engine in my car without the headers and Bursch exhaust...
But I can tell you that a recent dyno test showed 204.5 HP @ 6200 RPM
and 182.6 pound-feet of torque @ 5100 RPM.

Those measurements were taken at the rear wheels; if we assume a 20%
loss through the drivetrain, the numbers work out to 245 HP and 219 
pound-feet.
