I have done this on my 930 and have been really pleased with the
outcome.  Buy the VDO that is made for the car, and spend the extra $50
on the install kit with the better connectors.  All the power comes from
the wires already there for your clock.  The boost part of the gauge
ties right into the hose that is already behind your dash for the vacuum
part of the process.  Very easy, very accurate, looks factory.  Bought
mine from Todd Zuccine BTW.  Try him for the parts.  Excellent prices nd
great knowledge about the car.  # is: 1-602-816-9911
