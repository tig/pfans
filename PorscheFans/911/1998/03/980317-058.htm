> What would be the normal operating indications for my oil gauges,
> such as oil temp, pressure.

    Andrew:

    Oil temperature should be 180 degrees F, oil pressure should read
    10-15 psi per 1000 RPM.

> Also, I don't have an english handbook, it's german which I no
> comprendo, what is the guage in the clock with the fuel guage?

    I'm not sure what you mean by "the gauge in the clock", but I
    suspect that you're talking about the oil-level gauge.  If it's
    ever accurate, it'll be when the oil is warm and the car's
    idling, parked on a level surface.

> In the engine compartment it says you should check your dip stick
> when the engines running idle , is that correct?

    Yes.  911s use a dry-sump oil system, so dipstick measurements
    are only accurate when the engine is running.  Make sure that
    the oil is warm and the car is parked on a level surface, too.
