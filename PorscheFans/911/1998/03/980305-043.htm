    I've got a 97 C2 that I just got lowered with H&R springs, rear
3.8GT wing, RS splitters and some new looking front air ducts with
integral fog lamps (the car looks and handles great, got some JPEG's if
anyone is interested). Anyway, I just use a rag with warm soapy water
for the rear window interior deck. Then apply Armoral or another similar
product to help keep the deck from cracking from the UV from sunlight.
If it's the rear plastic deck on the movable wing just use standard car
wash followed with Armoral for the same reason. I've had no problems
with this.
