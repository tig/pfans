How 'bout a loose or corroded battery terminal?  If it always starts when you
use jumper cables, that might be because you've jarred the battery terminal,
and it briefly makes a good connection.  Try disconnecting both battery
terminals, cleaning them (wire brush, and baking soda if there's any
corrosion), and reconnecting them.

It may sound improbable, but loose battery connections have been responsible
for several intermittent no-start conditions I've seen.

Just a thought,
