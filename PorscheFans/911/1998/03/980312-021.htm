The brake pads are very easy to change on a C2.  Do it yourself.  I don't know
a good source for street pads but shop around and make sure to ask for the PCA
or Excellence discount (you belong to PCA, right?).  I'd start at a Porsche
dealer to get their price first.

4 mm is actually a fair amount of pad.  I'd drive the car for a few months
before replacing them.
