I have the Toyo F1S's on an '85 911 and like  them.  For straight performance I prefer P7's but I found
the ride too harsh (I live in a neighborhood with brick streets).  The P7's
felt very responsive.  The toyos give up a little in the feel but for me the trade was worth it.  My 911
is also my daily driver.  I have also tried XGTV's and found them to have a sidewall that was much too
soft.
