Don't need a dyno at all, just a lonely stretch of back road and a good 
stopwatch
   -  Drive car in third at about 2000 RPM
   -  Floor accelerator at chosen landmark
   -  Start stopwatch when tach hits 3000 RPM
   -  Stop stopwatch when tach hits 6000 RPM
   -  Return to start, repeat 2-3 more times to eliminate errors

This should be approx. 9-10 second window,enough to evaluate any 
changes.  I used this when evaluating several chips iin my old ''84
Turbo-Look (switched between OE and purchased one).  Eventually 
kept original; all the aftermarket ones did
not improve times in above range, and some were worse.

Second option is a G-analyst, but to me have to drive consistently over
same stratch of road to get meaningful results.
