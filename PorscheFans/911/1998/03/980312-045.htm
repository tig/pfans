Bob - I change my oil - or more accurately have it changed - every 3,000 
miles and I use Mobil 1. But in my case that's about six months which is 
about as long as I think oil should go in a hot and humid climate.

If you want to look at it from an economic standpoint I would start out with 
the cost of rebuilding your engine.   I pay about $3.75 per quart for Mobil 
1 not $7.00 as you suggest.   Even with an hour of labor and a couple of oil 
filters I figure that $125.00 or so bucks every six months is cheap 
insurance when you look at the cost of a rebuild or any serious mechancial 
failure.  

Just my $.02.
