I wanted to mention a novel solution to the crossover placement problem for
separates in the door. There is a recess at the front of the door pocket
under the latch handle into which many crossovers will fit. This is handy if
you don't want to disassemble the door. The wires can be run under the door
panel if you don't want to drill it. The wires in this position can't really
be seen unless you are laying on your back on the ground (a position I don't
often find myself in since I left my misspent youth behind).
