The only performance upgrades regarding the 964 are to change the
intake/exhaust system.  The 3-1 exhaust header that came out on the 94-95
model cars increased hp by 22, to give 272 hp.  Then the induction system
was changed to the varioram.  Thus by changing only the intake and exhaust
system Porsche gained 35 hp out of the same engine.  Or that's what Bruce
Anderson said in "Porsche 911 Performance Handbook".

Nothing about chipping a 964.  A new exhaust and 46 IDA weber carbs will
give you about 310 HP and a nice flat torque curve around 290 if you can't
find a 993 car to get the varioram induction from.
