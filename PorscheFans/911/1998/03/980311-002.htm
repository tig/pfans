I originally installed a Triad muffler on my SSIs, but the resonance was
more than I could bear, so I installed a Monty. I have been very happy with
it. The construction is very good, comparable with the SSIs. It sounds great
and doesn't have any really noticeable resonances. It is loud, which may not
suit everyone's taste. BTW, mine is the twin 3" outlet model.

I didn't dyno either muffler so I can't testify as to the actual HP gain. It
does sound a lot faster though!
