Doug:

"E" or "S" cams work well with the Webers... For reliability, you may 
want to install a Turbo oil pump and Carrera tensioners (with the 
new-style chain ramps) in addition to the 11-blade fan and Dilavar or 
Raceware studs, and while you have the engine apart, you should make 
the middle air-deflector mod that Bruce describes in his book.
