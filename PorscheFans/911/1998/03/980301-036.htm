James:

It is commonly called helicopter tape as it is used to protect various parts
of copters.  It is available from most racing suppliers but is a bit pricy.
It comes in various widths.  Truechoice only lists 2 and 4 inch widths
(1-800-388-8783).  Racer Wholesale, Pegasus, Summit, and BSR don't list it.  I
know I found a roll of 8 inch wide stuff somewhere?

All 964's and maybe even earlier Porsches came with a layer of it on the
headlights.
