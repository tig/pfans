Before you attempt to saw the ends off of any aluminum lugs nuts, Porsche
sells, and I believe they were stock on early 911's, steel lug nuts that look
the same except they are already missing the closed end.  They are a few bucks
each, or less.  They are part number I have is N 020 112 1 and listed at $1.80
each (discount price was under $1) in 1992.  I don't know what the 'N' means
as it doesn't look like a normal Porsche part number.  

These are much stronger and required by PCA CR.
