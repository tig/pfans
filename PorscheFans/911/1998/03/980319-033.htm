According to the most recent market update I have for this year
(Excellence, August '97, pg. 96-97), the range of prices for an '84
Carrera coupe is $14200 at the low end, $16145 for good condition, and
$21600 for excellent condition.
Prices for Targas are similar, cabs run about $3000-4000 more.

As another point of reference, Excellence for February, 1998 (pg. 83)
lists an '85 Carrera Coupe (black/black leather, LSD, spoilers, climate
and cruise control, 76k miles) going for $23000.

Your price seems fair indeed.  Go for it!
