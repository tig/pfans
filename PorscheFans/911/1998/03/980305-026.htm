Check out Larry Reynolds car care tips at:

http://www.derporsche.net/tech.htm

The section on cabrio tops covers care of the plastic window. He recommends
Meguiar's #18 Plastic Polish for regular maintenance. Meguiar's #17 Clear
Plastic Cleaner followed by Meguiar's #10 Clear Plastic Pollish for removing
light scratches. This two step process works really well. I removed some
scratches from my window using this method.
