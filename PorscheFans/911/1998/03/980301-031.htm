Seeing a lot of the recent offerings for c2/c4 parts got me thinking about 
an obscure part requirement.

Anyone who might be considering putting the really big calipers on a 89-92 
964 should note that if you move up to the 17" rims because of the larger 
calipers you will also need to get a 17" space saver spare. Otherwise, when 
you may need a spare the most the early stock 16" version probably won't 
fit.
