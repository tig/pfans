In a November '95 issue of Excellence magazine Bruce Anderson addresses the
K&N filter by stating that it's a good filter but he can't substantiate any of
the h.p. claims.  On a 2.7RS engine it made less than one h.p.  He then ran a
3.2 Carrera engine with no filter or filter cover and gained only 2 h.p. I'd
love to see more dyno results from the subscibers using any of the after-
market products-the BMW and NSX lists have a number of people who have put
their cars to the test!
