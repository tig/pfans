The clicking noise you hear from your speedo could very well be the O2
sensor counter.  This is a mechanical counter which should trip your O2
sensor light at 30,000 miles.  Mine has clicked like this for several
years and I have just not gotten around to disconnecting the darn thing.
I know my speedo is ok since it was recalibrated and checked out by N.
Hollywood just last year.
