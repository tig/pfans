For Tory Blue,
IMHO, the best cooler for an SC is the '87 Carrera cooler, with the
thermostat and fan.  I tried the Terbatrol and the 28-tube brass cooler,
without much improvement.  If you get the Carrera cooler, I recommend
that you have it ultrasonically cleaned before installation.  The '87
Carrera fender brace allows you to relocate the horns for proper
clearance, so get it too.  Also, you may wish to install a manual switch
for the fan.  A rear window wiper switch in the stock position works
well.  If you need more info, including a wiring diagram, please contact

me off line @ btindel@gte.net.
