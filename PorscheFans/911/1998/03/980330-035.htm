I drove my car for the first time today with the Swepco 201 gear oil.  I
previously had RedLine synthetic in the gear box.  What a difference!  By
the time I got to work (44 miles) the transmission was shifting much
smoother.

Not that I don't think the RedLine was doing a good job, but I can't help
but notice the difference.  I bought the Swepco mainly because it was
cheaper and I had heard good things about it.  I will continue to use it.

Anybody else notice a difference with this oil?
