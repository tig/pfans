I have heard from many sources that this kit (exhaust tips and
air box mod) is the same as ordering the motor sound option
on a new car. But I have heard new cars with this option
and I swore they were different mufflers. Sure enough I asked
my local guru at the Porsche dealer and he confirmed that
the mufflers are different on the motor sound package.
So the kit that is available is IMHO totally bogus!!!
Unless you replace the mufflers too, you
might as well get your wheels chromed or some nomex
fuzzy dice.
