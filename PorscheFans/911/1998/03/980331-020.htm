After much talk and excellent input by Pfans (thanks Ezra, Lorenzo) and 
others not on the list and a long conversation with Louis Foubare (Ruf) who 
advised me of the differences in settings, springs and sway bar dimensions 
from car to car (i.e. don't use a C2 or Turbo kit on a C4 etc.), I took the 
plunge and had a new Ruf/ Bilstein kit installed on my '91 C4.
The job was very well done by Felix Amore at Gulf Performance (Pompano, 
Florida).  The kit included 4 Ruf valved Bilstein dampers, progressive rear 
springs and sway bars.  Tower brace was already in place.
One might say - crazy to spend this on a 7 year old car - why not simply 
install lowering hardware.  On the other hand, I now have a sublimely 
handling 911 at substantially less $$ than moving up to a newer model.
