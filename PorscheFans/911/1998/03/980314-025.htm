I tired SuperTrapps on my 1971 911S and had similar results to your
"race pipes".  The low end was worse, the mid-range had two bad flat
spots, although the high end seemed stronger.  I gave up and ended up
going to a sport muffler within a couple of months (and am very pleased
with the results).

During my experience, several people speculated that my car needed a
crossover pipe to function properly.  I didn't bother because it would
put the SuperTrapps sticking too far out the back.

BTW:  A friend put the SuperTraps on his 1979 SC with headers and it
worked great.
