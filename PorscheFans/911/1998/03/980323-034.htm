Robert:

I have a '90 C2 Cab that had the defective flywheel problem.  I replaced
the entire flywheel/clutch assembly with a 993 assembly, and have been
happy ever since.  If you want to put in a lighter racing flywheel, you can
do it, but you're going to need a new chip as well, to accommodate the
revs spinning up and down faster.
