A 205/60/15 street tire should be OK unless your car is very low, in which
case it might rub in back over bumps. 195/65 or 195/60 would be fine, or if
your car is not too low the 205/60s will look and work great.

For SCCA autocrossing with the stock 6" wheels I have used 205/50/15s
for a long time, and 225/50/15 last year (BFG R1s only will work in that
size). 
If you live in an area with nice
smooth roads the 205/50/15 will be a good street tire but your
engine will rev a lot on the highway and you may bottom out a bit
over bumps, driveways, etc.

The "RS" has flared rear fenders with 9" wide wheels and 7s on the
front. I hope to run 225/50/15 on the front (now has 205/55/16)
and 245/50/15 or 245/45/16 on the rear.
