Makes the case for using antiseize on stuff. The screws are pretty short
(probably less than 10 mm in depth) and really are not all that critical
to the operation. I suspect that they are there only to keep the disk
from falling off when the car has the wheel off for service.

Personally I would try an impact screwdriver if I was really determined
to try and keep the screws, otherwise I would drill the suckers
out.....just make sure you don't hit the disc too much........

The disc should just pull off, you will then see about 3 - 4 mm of it
exposed which you can then get a vise grip on  and probably take it
off....
