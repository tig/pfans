>Where do you autocrossers buy your helmets ?
>Could I try on an M-rated helmet at a Cycle Shop
>and then assume a mail-order SA would fit ?

I  would encourage you to try the thing on, or at least have return
privileges with whoever you order from.  When I bought mine, I found that
different models even from the same manufacturer fit differently,
especially with my glasses... so much so that I ended up buying the one
that felt the best rather than the one I thought was the coolest.

Bell has a website with a bunch of good fitting advice at
http://www.bellmotorsports.com/.  Racer Wholesale in Atlanta is where I
bought mine, and they also do mail order -- http://www.racerwholesale.com/.
