Al Cabrera stated a concern about 225s rubbing the front fender on an 86 911. 

I won autocross championships using 225x15s in the front on an 84 911 that was
lowered substantionally with no rubbing although we may have used the baseball
bat rolling technique on the inner lip of the fender.  Having heavier torsion
and anti-sway bars with better shocks helps in avoiding any rubbing.
