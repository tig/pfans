You may want to try to isolate the source of the power drain.  I would
hook up an ammeter between + terminal  and the battery
cable read the amperage drain.  Yours must be very high - anything
higher than .08 amp is excessive.  One at a time, remove
fuses, when you hit the hot drain, your amperage should return to the
normal range.  This isolates the problem circuit.
