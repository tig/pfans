Subject:'92 964 shift shaft seal leak at 39K miles?
Date:Thu, 19 Mar 1998 17:53:37 -0800 (PST)
Just last weekend I noticed a significant oil leak from under the transaxle.
The oil is dripping out around the panels, so it's difficult to identify it's
origin. My P-wrench identifies the oil as gear oil. Turns out the shift shaft
seal is leaking big time. This car has 39K miles on it. This seems really
unusual for a seal like this to fail with such low mileage. Could it be that
as a result of lack of use as a weekend driver? Has anyone else seen this same
leak on their C2/C4?

The fix was 2 hours of labor to replace a $16 seal. Not too terrible.
