For George Nedleman,
The brake fluid doesn't circulate, and it is hygroscopic (absorbs
moisture from the atmosphere), which can cause rust and other problems. 
IMHO, you want to completely flush the system about every two years. 
Cheap insurance against costly brake problems later on.
