At 02:46 PM 1/30/98 EST, you wrote:
>I am replacing the Blaupunkt Reno with a CD player in an 88 Cab.  Are there
>any tricks in removing the current radio that I should know about?  Thanks.

It pops right out using DIN removal tools or a couple of bent wire hangers.
 If you order your new unit from Crutchfield they give you everything you
need.  Some replacement units include the removal tools right in the box -
mine did.  You will need your Reno manual though in order to figure out
what wire goes where.  If you can't find it I can give you a pretty good idea.

I put a $249 Blau Miami CD from Crutchfield in my '87.  It was fairly easy
and I've been happy with it.  Looks OK, too.
