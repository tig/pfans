Here's my 2 cents about your wheel bearing question.  Try shaking a rear
wheel on your Rabbit, and compare the looseness there to that of the front
of your 911.  The front wheel bearings on a Rabbit are the cartridge type
and are pressed in, and require no adjustment.  The rear wheel bearings on
the Rabbit are the adjustable cone type, simular to the front of the 911. 
The other simularity I guess is that the rear wheels of the Rabbit carry
less weight and are not driven, like the front of the 911.
