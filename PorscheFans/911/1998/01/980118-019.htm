You should ask for a mechanics inspection, including leakdowns, and the
results in writing.

>  - Anything special I need to know about this year/model 911?
>    Were there problems with chain guards in 1987?

No problem with the chain guards. The G50 has some potential problems. The
first ones out the door had a problem with the clutch actuator that
required  machining on the housing to fix--might examine the records to see
if this was done. Also, the "rubber centered clutch" issue.

>  - I'm new to the USA and not very familiar with the DOT or
>    DMV rules and regs. Is it possible to get info on the car
>    from these sources via VIN? I'm in california.

Your biggest problem will be smog inspections--not just because it is a
911, but because you live in CA ;-)


>  - How hard is it to check the numbers on this car? Are the
