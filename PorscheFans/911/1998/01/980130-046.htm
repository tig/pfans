Rob,
Porsche made C2 5 spoke rims available in 17" with widths of 7", 7-1/2",
8" and 9".  The 7-1/2" were built for the 968 and have a different
offset than the others which are for C2/C4/RSA and C2T.  These 17" have
offsets corresponding to the same widths in 16"s.  If you want to go
aftermarket wheels you can get 7-1/2" (front) and 9" (rear). I've been
told since these wheels are cast and not forged they aren't better than
the aftermarket ones and we just pay more for the Porsche part number. 
Generally, that's 60% used and 100% new.  I've just purchased a pair of
used P-car C2T 17-9" for the back of my C4; moving the 17-8" to the
front. So I may have a pair of 17-7" with 205 P700Z's (5000miles) for
sale soon. Hope this helped.
