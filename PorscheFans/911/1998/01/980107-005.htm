The correct offset is 10.8mm for 6+7x16

When people run 7+8 or 8+9x16" the best offset seems to be 23mm FR
which is what the 930 rear wheels and most aftermarket wheels are.

As for the "technology wheels" these are 53mm /40mm which should work
with a 1" spacer in the front and NO spacer in the rear, since 930
wheels are 23mm and the additional 17mm is very close to putting all
the additional width (1" = 12.7mm*2) to the inside.  Given how much
variance there usually is with 930 bodywork, I would syspect that
fittment woul dbe optimized by mounting the wheels and then having
optimal spacers designed, the way I did on my 930-bodywork car with
335/35/17" on 12.5x17" wheels rear.
