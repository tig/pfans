No 911 experience with the TOYO RA1, but I did run a set of 235-45/17's
on 8" wide wheels  on my '97 M3.  Ran 4 track events at Lime Rock &
Pocono, and pushed the car hard.  I expect to get at least 2-3 more
events based on the camber wear pattern, after remounting them on the
opposite side of the car.  The tires were shaved, but not to the point
of being nearly slicks "out of the box".  I didn't feel that I was
giving anything away to the BF Goodrich's.  The car was stable in high
speed controlled drifts.
Hope this is useful information.
