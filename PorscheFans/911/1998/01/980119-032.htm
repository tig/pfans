Jim,

Is the front bar a "through-body" type?  If so, you can upgrade to 22mm
max. from Weltmeister, using the factory links.  Automotion sells the
bar alone for $89.  You will also need to get the proper bushings and a
chassis reinforcement kit.

Rears are a little tougher...you pretty much need the entire conversion
kit; again from Weltmeister.  I recommend a 19mm rear to go with the 22
front. 

The Automotion catalog goes into great detail and should cover all your
questions.
