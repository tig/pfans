Some time ago, while driving my 75 911S, I shifted and found myself
holding a shift lever not attached to anything. After getting over the
initial shock, I pulled over to the side and looked where I hoped the
problem would be, under the little cover on the tunnel, behind the front
seat. Shure enough, the rubber bushings in the linkage had pretty much
disintegrated and the link pin was hanging out one side of the coupling.
Pushed it back in , reaching back and re-centering the pin frequently
while driving to my destination. 

I ordered and installed new bushings in the coupling fork, re-installed
the pin(Very tight!!)

The pin does not stay centered, it keeps working its way out on one side
or the other???????? What am I doing wrong??

After every trip I now have to reach back and push the pin back towards
the center of the coupling. This is getting old fast!!!!!
