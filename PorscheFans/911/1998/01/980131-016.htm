If you're new to Porsche cars and parts it's worth knowing that you rarely 
need to pay 100% list on OEM parts even from Porsche dealers' parts desks. 
My local dealer will extend a 10% PCA club discount (thank you very much!) 
when asked. Dealers around the country can be even more aggressive on 
discounting. Personally I've had fast and helpful service from Don McGill 
Porsche in Texas (800-237-1698) with 20% off list if you mention their 
advertisements in Excellence.
