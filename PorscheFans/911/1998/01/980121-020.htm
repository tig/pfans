You've got a broken spring pivot for your window regulator.  The
regulator is what the window motor is attached to to raise/lower the
window.  It has a heavy spring, analagous to a garage door, that assists
the motor when raising the window.  At the center of this spring is the
pivot.  Over time these break; that's what the loud snap was.  Your
regulator will work, but over time w/o the spring your motor will pay
the price.  Replacement pivots are available from all the catelogs.
There are several styles of the pivots, so keep yours for ordering.
Replacement will require removing the regulator, pressing the new pivot
in, and reattaching the spring.
