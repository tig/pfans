I too have an 83 SC and it is a great car.  Admittedly mine has 1/3 the
mileage of yours, but you should still have  a lot of use out of what
you have acquired.
Recommendations:
Don't do things on the cheap.  Do them right the first time and put off
what you can't afford.
Buy a tool kit.  It will have the spanner which is made to hold the
pulley to get your belt off.
Investigate Stoddard's exchange program on transmissions.  Probably a
better way to go than having someone rebuild it who does not do it
regularly.
Good luck with your new wheels.  These are great cars.
