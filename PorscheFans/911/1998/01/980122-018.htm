In answer to Chris's question re his '72, a long time back, I remember a

hint from one of the experts in Pano (could have been Paterek, but I'm

not sure) about restoring color in faded turn signal and/or taillight

lenses.



The hint was to get translucent paint from an art supply

store--evidently it exists in orange and red--and coat the insides of

the lenses.  I've never seen the product, but supposedly this restores

color to like-new condition.



Has anyone out there done this?  I've never had the need, but I

distinctly remember reading the item.


I've done it.  I found the paint in a hobby supply store.  Translucent
orange.  Could have been Testor's but don't recall.  It worked beautifully.
I doubt anyone could have detected the 'repair'.
