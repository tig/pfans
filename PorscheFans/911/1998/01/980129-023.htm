These leaks can be very hard to track! I had the same symptoms on my '79
SC and on my'70 E. In both it ended up being the rear window seal. Just to
be same, replace both. If you shop around you can get them for about $40
each. I have heard of a wider seal that is made. This is especially nice
so you don't have to stress about equal gaps. Just a tip.

Good luck, Chris
