I've heard of a technique which I will call 'dipping':
Get a pan with a diameter larger than spokes but smaller than rim.  Place 
wheel face up on pan. Painted portion of wheel should be lower than top of 
pan.  Level the wheel.  Pour paint into pan thru holes between spokes until it 
covers the portion of the wheel you want painted.  Drain paint from pan 
(forgot to mention......drill hole in pan and plug it before you start this 
procedure).

I've never used this technique....but it sounds like it would produce nice 
straight edge.
