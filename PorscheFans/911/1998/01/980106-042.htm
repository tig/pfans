You might try the paint thinner sold for the enamel used on plastic models
(cars, airplanes, etc.)  The most common brand is Testors.  It's safe on
almost all plastics, so you needn't be especially careful about spilling
it.  It seems to take off most adhesive residues.

The only problem is that it's normally sold in very small quantities. 
Whatever you do, do NOT get the thinner for the "dope" used on wood model
airplanes.
