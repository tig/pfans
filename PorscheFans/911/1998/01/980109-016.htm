William-
For what it is worth, here is what I did.  Once you take off the outer
pulley half you will see a circle of about 6 mm holes, at least on some
models.  I tapped two these holes that are opposite each other to some
convenient size threads, and them screwed two bolts into the holes until
they contacted the alternator bearing part of the casting.  Once I could
see that they were not pushing against something critical like a bearing
seal, I just screwed them in farther until the bolts developed enough
force to push the fan off.  It worked well, and seemed more elegant than
hammering on something.
