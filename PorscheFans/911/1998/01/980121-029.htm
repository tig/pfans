Subject: window rattle

That round piece of pot metal you found is the anchor pin for the spiral
spring that assists the window mechanism. It's broken. The factory, in all
it's wisdom, made this out of soft pot metal. So eventually, all windows
should experience what you have. Without the spring helping the process,
there's more strain on the window motor to move the window up.

Various Porsche parts suppliers can sell you a replacement pin. They're
available in two lengths and the slot (to anchor the spring) comes in two
configurations (parallel and perpendicular) relative to the flats machined on
the pin. At about $25 a pop, it's cheaper than a new regulator assembly. You
can see a picture of it on page 65 of Automotion's catalog.
