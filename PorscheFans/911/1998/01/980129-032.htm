You will have to drill holes in you door in order to mount a passenger
mirror. Kind of a scary thought but really not too bad. Hint, don't put it
in the same spot as the drivers mirror otherwise you won't be able to see
anything but clouds. use another car to take measurements off from.

The wiring does not matter. You can just adjust them manually with a
gentle push on the mirror glass.
