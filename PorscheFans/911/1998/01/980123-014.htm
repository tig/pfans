There is another possibility here:

The large nut that holds the upper part of the mirror to the base may be
backing off. If so, the upper part of the mirror will pop up and hang at an
angle one day.

Tof ix this problem, you have to dismount the mirror base and pull the
mirror our far enough to reach the large nut around the collar of the upper
part 'flag' of the mirror. That nut can loosen up and it's not easy to
tighten it, so you might consider applying Loctite to the threads.
