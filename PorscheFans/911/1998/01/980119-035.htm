This is one of the least expensive blowers in the car. I bought my
replacement fan (and complete fresh-air housing) from a mail-order used
parts house for about $70. The fan/motor is located in a black plastic
housing under the "bug catcher", that black grill just under the wipers.
Very easy to remove, note the positions of the cabling when you pull it
out. Be sure you test the fan directly (12 VDC) lest it be a wiring, fuse
or relay problem.
