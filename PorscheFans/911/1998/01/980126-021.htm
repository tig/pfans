Actually, it's an interesting slant on taxation by various governments...

There's a 15% VAT (Value Added Tax) in Germany and no import duty in the
USA - thus the difference in USA vs. Germany prices.  Additionally, the
USA price does not include license/registration (cost depends upon which
state you live in) or sales tax (cost depends upon which state/city you
purchase the car in)...
