Bill suggested making up a jumper cable which works fine. But on my car 
when I replaced the stock tail with one from an RSA I unhooked the original 
wiring harness from the tail I was removing and reconnected it to the 
engine compartment factory wiring loom up behind the airbox where it 
normally connects. A bit of duct tape and wire wraps to hold it all 
together and I haven't had a problem since. I suppose I *am* carrying about 
a pound of extra wiring, but ... :-)
