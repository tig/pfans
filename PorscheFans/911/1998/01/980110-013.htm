If you get Autoweek, you see these raffles all the time.  The latest
edition had at least 3 of them, 2 Boxsters and a Vette.  They sell a
thousand or two tickets (chances) at $50 to $100 apiece.  The ads always
mention some charity or worthy cause as the beneficiary.  Given the number
of them, I would guess that some are legit.  They usually accept credit
cards and have a real address (vs. POBox).  BUT, it also seems like a great
front for a scam.  So, as always, buyer beware.
