Carmen and others:

There are also factory (Speedline made for Porsche, as are the others) 5 spoke
9.5 by 17 rims for the C2/C4.  They require a longer stud and a 25mm or so
spacer.  I have them on the rear of my C2.  They fit fine, even with 275x40x17
R1s (which I would never use on the road!).  Actually, there is very slight
rubbing, under hard cornering I suppose on the trailing arms.
