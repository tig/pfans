Somebody asked about Flat Six a couple of days ago (don't remember who, I was on the road when I read the mail).
 It is a French magazine on Porsches (mainly - but not only - 911 based), a bit in the same style as "911 & Porsche World". I like it and buy it on occasions when I find it.
I picked up a copy of the last issue (Jan 98) at the Paris airport last Wednesday and I know it is also available at newstands in Brussels (Belgium). Don't know about availability elsewhere.
You can get (most) back issues, they are listed in the back of the magazine.
