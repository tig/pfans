RE: The discussion of stone guards, You might try  little trick I've
been using for years on Motorcycle tanks and bodywork. Static cling
vinyl (you know....the stuff Taco Bell plasters all over their windows!)
is available VERY cheaply ($.25-.50 cents/sheet).  Simply cut your own
patterns, clean the body area to be covered, mix up a spray bottle of 1
pint water and 3 drops lemon Joy, and squeegee on after spraying the car
and the vinyl liberally. There is no secret here, just squeeze out the
water and the vinyl will stick but remains easy to peel off with NO
residue.  I swear you can't see it once installed and it makes great
protective film for headlights, hoods, fender lips, etc. Try looking for
this stuff at Ad. supply stores or signage shops.  Comes in a variety of
thicknesses and colors.  I think the primary manufacturer is Maalco. 
Cheap and effective!
