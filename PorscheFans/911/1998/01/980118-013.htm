William,

The fuel line I'm talking about is the line from the top of the fuel
filter, going under the air intake manifolds, rear towards the
alternator, then branching to each side to feed the fuel injector
rails.  The part consists of a 12" rubber line from the fuel filter into
an L-shaped brass tube running under the air intake and above the engine
case.  Just forward of the alternator, the brass is branched into a
T-section.  Each side then has about an 8" section of rubber line which
connect into each cylinder bank fuel rails.  The rubber sections dry out
and crack.

The Porsche Part Number:  930.110.595.05
