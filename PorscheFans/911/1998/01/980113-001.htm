My .02 on this.
I have my SC slightly dismantled.  The battery is disconnected, so I can
avoid frying myself or critical bits and pieces.  Since this is going to
take awhile I have hooked up a battery minder (from Performance
Products...made by Schumacher---but not Michael), not a trickle charger.
So far it seems to work fine.  Keeps the charge up. 
I seem to recall that lead acid batteries do not like to get fully
drained and then recharged, unlike their modern nickel cadmium cousins
who seem to thrive on that.  So, allowing it to sit and then recharge it
when it is down is not a good thing.
