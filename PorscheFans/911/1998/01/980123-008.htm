What is happening is that to do a proper color change you/they need to take
everything off which touches the old color. Door jambs, trunk, eng
compartment etc etc. All the trim front and rear window glass etc etc etc

Also the cost of material is escalating by the day with all the new EPA
rules. The prep work for a complete repaint is numerous hours.

The $4,000 figure is probably low for an upper end shop to do this. What
you get is a paint job similar to factory.
