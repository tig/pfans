  The "teatray" name seems to be a British name for the
Turbo spoiler.

>Can a whaletail spoiler come with or without a raised lip?
  There are two common flavors for the spoiler. The "Carrera"
spoiler with a lip on the trailing edge and the "Turbo"
spoiler which has a raised lips on the sides in addition to
the trailing edge one.  
  For this reason, the "Turbo" spoiler is preferred to the "Carrera" 
version when used as a baby changing table. The raised
side lips of the Turbo spoiler keep the baby from sliding off onto
the street if the owner forgets the baby and drives off by
accident.
