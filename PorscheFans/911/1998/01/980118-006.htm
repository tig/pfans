I have used a product called "Damp Rid" available from the local hardware
store. I have used this in various applications (boat, auto, home) and
seems to work fine. It is a moisture absorber in a container held in
place using a strainer to wick the water to the bottom. When full, empty
water. Also refills are available. About $4 initially.
