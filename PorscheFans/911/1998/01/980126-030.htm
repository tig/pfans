> Last but not least how do I decode the VIN number?? the number is

>From "The Used 911 Story", by Peter Zimmermann:

WP0 - The World Producer Code for "Porsche"
AA0 - The VDS Code:  The first "A" stands for the model:  911
                     The second "A" stands is the engine type:  172hp 2994cc
                                 6-cyl gas engine.
                     The "0" stands for the restraint system:  active
91 - First 2 digits of Porsche Type (91 = 911)
9 - Test digit
D - Model year:  1983
S - Manufacturer location:  Stuttgart
1 - Third digit of Porsche Type (1 = 911)
2 - 911 SC Coupe USA/Canada
2346 - Consecutive Chassis Number

Hope that helps!
