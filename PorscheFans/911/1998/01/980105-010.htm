IT IS DIFFICULT TO OBTAIN JUST THE BRACKET, HOWEVER, ITS NOT WORTH THE HASSLE, 
HAVING A WELDER MAKE A BRACKET THAT IS STRONGER THAN THE ORIGINAL WILL BE EASY 
AND WORTHWHILE.  IT HAS LONG BEEN A PROBLEM THAT THE STOCK BRACKETS FOR 
UNDER-CAR MOUNT SWAYS HAVE BROKEN OFF, OR BROKEN IN HALF.

YOU WILL NEED TO GRIND THE ENTIRE AREA WHERE THE ORIGINAL WAS, AND WIRE BRUSH 
THE SURROUNDINGS.

THEN, ANY COMPETENT WELDER CAN MAKE A NEW MOUNT.   MY BEST ADVICE TO YOU WOULD 
BE TO SAW-ZALL OFF THE OTHER MOUNT AND DO BOTH AT THE SAME TIME.   tHAT WAY 
THE SWAY BAR WILL BE BALANCED AND LAST.
