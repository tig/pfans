I can't comment on the grabbing or "crystalization," but When my C2 was
squealing like that it turned out one of the E-brake shoe retaining pins
had broken so the shoe could sometimes flop over and rub on the inside
of the rotor. I think you can inspect those pins from the back without
pulling the rotor.
