     Following up on the garage floor painting discussion:

     I painted my floor with some dark red outdoor tennis court paint I
picked up from the US Government as part of a base closing clearance sale.
It doesn't look bad and I never slip when it is wet.

     My advice would be to let the paint cure thoroughly before parking a
car with hot/warm tires on it.  I didn't.  When I drove away later I found
that the warm tires had lifted the new paint off the floor leaving a clear
tire pattern where the old surface showed through.

     On the positive side, it was a great way to measure/calculate the
dimensions of a tire contact patch.
