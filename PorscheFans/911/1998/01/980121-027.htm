I have a '79 SC which had the same problem of taking a while of
cranking before building (chugging) up to a decent idle. It's most
likely a fuel pressure problem (specifically a lack of). Cause is either
fuel pump check valve (40% chance. is about $30 worth of parts to update
to later type on pump or around $70 for new line which inludes one since
it's it's built into the banjo fitting on the fuel line which attatchs
to the pump), pressure accumulator(55% chance. cost is around $40. take
bottom fitting off of yours - if gas comes out, replace it), or (5%
chance)another misc leak. Of cours this is IMHO based on my experience
with CIS cars of various makes(VW, audi, pcars, etc). hope this helps
