All these recent headlight messages reminded me of the earlier mentions of
fogged-up headlights, from which my '87 also suffered.  I finally yanked
them out, pulled the bulbs, and cleaned them fairly easily using
Windex-soaked paper towels wrapped and taped around an appropriately
mangled wire coat hanger.  The reflectors were almost as fogged up as the
insides of the lenses.  While I was at it I put in some 55/100 H5 bulbs.
The improvement was worth the effort, both for lighting and appearance.
