Mike:

I have a '90 C2 Cab, which I bought about 2 years ago.  I immediately took
off the stock anti-sway bars and put on larger Weltmeister adjustable
bars, front and back (I'm not aware of the sizes - I let Steve Alarcon at
Johnson's Alignment in Torrance - long time Porsche racer and
suspension specialist extraordinaire- decide what to put on).  Huge
difference in the handling of the car - corners much flatter, and I am able
to dial in understeer and oversteer.  Highly recommended, and not terribly
expensive.
