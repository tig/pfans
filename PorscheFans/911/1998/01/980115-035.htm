Greg,
If what you are describing is "cupping" of the belt fabric across its
width, this is indicative that it has been severely stretched, as in a
collision.  Seat belts are designed to stretch in this manner once only,
as part of their occupant restraint/impact attenuation function. 
Replacement is recommended so you have that stretch available if you
need it.
