-----------------------------------
On a more serious note, has anybody had problems with a Bursch sport
muffler breaking the mounting point breaking? The result was that the
muffler was supported by the Catalytic Converter, further resulting in
the Converter tubing (exterior) breaking. It has been on the 911 for 2
1/2 years. 

Luckily, Express Muffler in San Diego was able to reweld the mounting
points (at a very reasonable price, I might add). I'd like to know if
this is an isolated instance or a (mounting) design flaw.
