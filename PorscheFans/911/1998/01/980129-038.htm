Gary,
If you are looking at 70's cars I suggest a '73 911SC the model prior to the
sprung bumpers. The reason behind this is because the weight to power ratio
makes for a far better performance vehicle and a more desirable purchase.
Also all 911 engines are basiclly the same, so the smaller capcity means
less stress on the engine itself. Ask any Porsche buff about these cars. I
have a 30 page artical on the 911 from 68 to 96 from an UK mag and they rate
this as the true 911 sports car.

If this is too old - go for the latest model you can afford and be prepaired
to spend some dollars.

Note that Whale tails are out of vouge.....

I hope this is of some help.
