The tandem hood shock kit has been around for a long time, and the
description on the list is accurate--it's just a set of long bolts and
washers that will accommodate two shocks in tandem on one side.  The kit
was advertised as recommended for whaletails and cars with a/c (this is
a hint as to how old the product is).  After some years, I installed
one, and it does have one advantage.  It's *not true*, despite claims to
the contrary, that you'll never need another hood shock again once you
install it.  But the outboard shock becomes very easy to replace,
indeed. No more clips to futz with -- just pull the nuts and slide it
on.

Just keep the original hardware for purism's sake.
