I think i do not qualify as early 911 owner, but a note of
these high watt lamps:
- the light get quite warm, definitely do not use in a car
  which have plastic headlights.
- Some times the caulk or whatever is used when quartz
  lamp is mounted in the metal base will come off and will
  destroy the reflector of headlight assy.
- Definitely a strain on wiring and switches, the Ampere
  is doubled.

To put this shortly; You are looking for some bright lights
and trouble.
