Now that somebody else has admitted to buying from Whitney, here's my $.02 on
what I bought. 
If you need sound insulation for your restoration project, check out their
"DynaMat" look insulation sheets (#12FX5514A). It's $24.99 for a sheet
(32x54"); enough to cover 1/2 a 911 floor or parts of 2 doors. Compare $$ with
what Automotion wants (albeit precut for doors). I also bought the Crane
pointless conversion kit for much less than what other suppliers are asking.
Here's one caveat:  I have not tested Whitney's customer service policies.
They will not refund money but will issue credit, and they seem to take their
time on deliveries.
