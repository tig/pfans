     The Bosch System tester Part # 9288 and the cable # 9288-1 is a
mandatory option! Maybe the price will go down because this tester is
used for the 911 964 engine & tiptronic trans diagnostics. The Boxster
and 993's used a lap-top tester which is different and probably more
expensive. 
     This tool would probably have to be ordered from your nearest and
friendly Bosch distributor. What a great idea to purchase one between
a group of friends! There is very little that can done to these 964's
without this tool.
