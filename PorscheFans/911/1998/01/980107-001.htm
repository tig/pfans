Re Carl Kaller's question and James Alton's response on residue from
window tint, I think the main ingredient in Testor's thinner is mineral
spirits, which you can buy as "paint thinner" at about $2 a gallon. 
It's messy and smells, but it's cheap.  It also won't hurt most plastics
(polystyrene is the only exception I know for sure) and will eventually
cut the adhesive.
