I just checked a reference and found that Eurostyle Extended was published
by "Digital Typeface" corp. It's not really the Porsche typeface (too
narrow). Other vendors have similar faces such as "Image Club Graphic's"
"Euro Technic Extended" and "QualiType's" "QTEuroType". These are all very
similar, but I think Eruo Tecnic Extended is the closest match.
