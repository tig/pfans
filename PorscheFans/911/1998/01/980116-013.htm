Recently one of you gave me excellent instructions on how to remove a C4 
steering wheel.  What I discovered the hard way, is that when you 
disconnect the airbag wires, you should NOT turn on the ignition (I went 
around the block after fixing the turn signal switch to make sure the wheel 
was straight) as it affects the airbag warning light which then has to be 
reset.
The other interesting thing I discovered was that Champion Porsche/Audi 
wanted $75 to reset it, while an independent (Gulf Performance) shop 
charged me $25.  Both are in Pompano Beach, Florida.
