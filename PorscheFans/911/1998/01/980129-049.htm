I have a '75 and have added the flag style mirrors.........it is not
difficult with the original Porsche parts (including wiring harnesses)
as it basically bolts on and plugs in. Only major issues I had were the
drilling of the mounting holes in the doors (they are about 3/4 inch
which requires either a unibit or a lot of filing to make a nice hole)
and the fact that you need to fill/repaint the area where the old mirror
went as the flag style mirrors do not cover the same area as the
original.

There are other fussy bits (like the mounting of the mirror switch on
the door) and threading the wires through to the body), but not really
difficult.....just tedious.

Up Fixin has a good article (Volume XIII, p 189) on how to do
it..........

Hope this helps
