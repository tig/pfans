Although I am catching this thread late, I removed window film off of an =
Acura Legend a while back, and I used Cheesecloth and Ammonia (straight, =
in a spray bottle).  The cheese cloth helps collect the goo, and the =
ammonia cuts the adhesive (Windex will also work to some extent, because =
it has Ammonia in it).  Just make sure you get fresh air on a regular =
basis, and save up on elbow grease, cause you'll need it :).
