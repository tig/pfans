Well, first, have you replaced the original lid shocks at all? Perhaps
they have gone bad.

Second, on mine I removed the latch on the car (not the stub on the lid)
and soaked it in 1" of WD-40 for about 10 min. then I sprayed it and all
kinds of road gunk/old grease came out.  I repeated this process and
used a bunch of q-tips and rags till the part was reasonably clean.
Applied a liberal dose of generic lube and reinstalled it.  I had to
fiddle with the cable length a few times but it works like a charm now.
I also replaced both of my lid shocks at the same time.  Buy the
lifetime guaranteed ones they work very well!

Enjoy!
