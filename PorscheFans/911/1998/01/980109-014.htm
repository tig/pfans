     The 964 C2 has 2 front fans. On the left front is the fan for the
A/C evaporator which turns on with the A/C. On the right side, as you
mentioned is the oil cooler fan which is activated when the oil reaches
233degrees.If your engine runs cool this fan should not be working and
your probably hearing the A/C fan working.
