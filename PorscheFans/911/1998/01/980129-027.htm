My $.02:

Try to use a 6 point deep socket on the valve after soaking it for 
several (3-4) days with penetrating oil.  This precludes putting a hose 
on the end of the valve, so it is messy, but I have never (knock wood) 
rounded a bleeder valve with a 6 point socket.  DO NOT use a wrench of 
any kind on it!

I have found that by allowing the oil to soak for several days 
(reapplying liberally and frequently), you GREATLY minimize the chance 
of rounding a fastener.  Have patience!
