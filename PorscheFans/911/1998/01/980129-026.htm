For autox 225/50/15 BFG R1s are great, not much taller
than the 205s and a lot wider. But street tires of this size
will not work, the R1 has a special sidewall which allows
fitting huge tires on narrow rims.

For a normal street tire probably 205/60 or 55/15 would
be biggest reasonable tire, or 205/50 for the low rider look
if you have better roads than we have in the NE.
