The thing with the leland west insurance is that these are limited mileage,
second car policies. These policies are not for daily drivers.

I have not a major claim but my windshield claim was handled quick and with
"porsche" glass plus a new seal. No questions no hassle. I have both of my
pcars insured with leland west.

There is NO TRACK coverage so if you do driver ed track events go forth
with caution.
