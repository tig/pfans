Most literature states that 1971 was the last year of the
(optional?) rear deck lid in aluminum. Also the rear license
plate panel was available in aluminum until 1971
on the S.

But I read the entire 1972/73 911 parts manual last
week checking for specs on options on a 73 RS.
(the parts manual is for all cars, not only US models).
I saw that they listed the rear deck lid as available
to the end of 1972 only, and no reference to the
license plate panel. So I don't think you need it
if you want your car original.
