Before you spend your money on a B&B or other aftermarket muffler, I suggest
you take your stock Carrera muffler to a welding/fabrication type shop and
have then cut your muffler in half around the seam then have them cut out
the inner baffles and remove all the stuff, reweld the muffler together
(much lighter) and reinstall. It sounds great, stock loudness at idle and
screams when floored, also find a European pre-muffler (10hp per bruce
anderson) or bypass pipe (5hp) and you will be happy. Stock appearance
(nice), raspy-superbike sound at full throttle (incredible), near-normal db
levels at crusing
speed, with no reverb. as with B&B twin outlet mufflers that will drive you
nuts. It should only cost you around $50-100 to complete! At track event at
Homestead Motor Speedway people couldn't believe the cool sounds my car was
making
as it was bouncing off of the side walls of the track.
Good Luck in your dilemma.
