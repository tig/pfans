> Is it safe to shift from a gear to neutral without using the clutch?
> Any reasons not to?

Johnny:

It CAN be safe, if the gears in the transmission are completely 
unloaded (i.e., the brakes aren't on and the engine is neither 
accelerating nor decelerating the car).

If the gears ARE loaded, however, it can be very UNSAFE... As you 
pull the transmission out of gear, all the engine torque can be 
concentrated on the very edge of the shift-gear teeth.

If you use the clutch, on the other hand, you can't do any damage.

Since you're presumably going to disengage the clutch for the shift 
from Neutral to the next gear, there's really nothing to be gained by 
omitting the clutch-push before shifting INTO Neutral.
