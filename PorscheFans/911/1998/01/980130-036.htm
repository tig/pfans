As I recollect, Porsche changed the rear (8by16) wheel offset for 1989, going
to less offset to give the rear wheels a deeper look (on the Carreras, not the
1989 C4's, of course).  As I recollect the two Fuchs offsets for 8x16's are
about 10.8 and about 24.4mm.  One of these was also used on the earlier 944
turbos (non-ABS).  (I always wondered if the factory was just trying to use up
excess 944 wheels when they did this???)  Either of these would work on any SC
or Carrera, I believe.  

Also, the rears should be fine on the front.

Someone correct me (I'm sure you will) if I am wrong.
