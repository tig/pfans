Dave,
You need to remove the steering wheel, then the top and bottom plastic covers 
on the column.  If you look at the switch, there are 3 parallel contacts.  What 
you want to do is get approximately equal spacing between all three.  I took a 
pair of flat nose pliers and just gently "tweaked" the center contact.  Don't 
put this off too long, or you'll burn up your switch.

HTH,
Alex
