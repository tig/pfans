Considering the possibilities of a leak, I would recommend replacement.
I think they are designed to crush once; after that, they don't have the 
original flexilbility.

I wonder, however, about the need for adjusting valves 3 times a year??
My experience in 150,000 miles on a '76 and 130,000 miles in an '84
911 was that in between the 15,000 mile valve adjustment periods, the
clearances hardly changed.  Don't know what mileage interval you are 
doing them, but it might be going overboard.  15K miles between oil
changes (by the book) might be absurd, but same mileage between
valve adjustments I think is reasonable.
