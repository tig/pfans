My .02.  You definately need to remove f&r glass, top of door seals,
peel back upholstery from the door and windshield posts, and remove the
rear deck.  I wouldn't bother with spray adhesive because of the
overspray and the fact that the only a small amount of glue is used at
the edges of the headliner, best applied with a small brush.  Take your
time and use the original headliner to determine how the factory did it.
