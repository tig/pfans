I'll admit I haven't given this alot of time, but checking the wiring
diagram, all those lights have a common ground located just under the
dash at the door post, drivers side.  It looks like a threaded stud with
some wires held to it by a nut.  One of the large brown wires comes from
the instrument panel warning lights, oil pressure and temp gauges.  If
the connection is weak, it's possible the gauges are flowing to ground
through one of the other none circuits.  I've seen stranger things. I'd
try the ground first.   Good Luck
