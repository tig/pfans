My 74 is updated to newer rectangular ones without electrics. No need for
the electrics as the adjustment doesn't change. I put a convex paste on the
passenger side. 
I had them put on and painted by my body shop that way they are in right
place and the paint matches. BE CAREFUL the passenger side mounts ahead of
vertical part of the wing window so the mirror part is visible behind the
vertical part.

The two are not mounted on the same part of the doors.
