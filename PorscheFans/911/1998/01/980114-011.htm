Bill had some good notions. Here are a few more I've collected"

Leave the doors closed to the first detent only so the door seals are 
not fully compressed.

Put blocks of wood, paper etc under the wiper arms so the blades are 
not resting on the glass.

A fully charged battery will be safe in a relatively warm (60 F) 
environment for 5 months without charging. Recharge before use.
