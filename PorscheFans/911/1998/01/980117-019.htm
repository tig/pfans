I need some advice and information on engine tuning. I have a 66 912.

I received a notice from the DMV saying that my car should have to pass

the smog check that day was probably the dumbest thing I have done in
the past year. You all have probably guessed by now that the car failed
the test. 
There are two parts to this CA smog check.  The first part of the test
checks emissions at idle RPM and the 2nd phase checks the emissions at
2500 RPM.  During this test my car failed the idle portion of the test
but passed the 2500 RPM part.  I do not have the emission results from
this test.  Needless to say, I was quite dissappointed but I thought
positively and decided that this had all happened for a reason.  This
was also a good time to give my motor a tune up.
I replaced the plugs, air cleaners, points, condenser, distributor cap,
and fuel filter. I set the timing to 3 degrees BTDC at 900 rpm which is
the set idle speed.  The carb linkage was adjusted and I was now ready
for a smog retest. 
I brought the car in for a retest this morning after driving it for

the car passed the idle portion of the test but failed the 2500 RPM part
