Van:

The solution's simple:  Call Eibach and tell them that you need
"tender" springs.  These are relatively low-rate springs that are
normally completely compressed and don't affect the suspension
spring rate... When the suspension droops, though, the tender
springs open up and keep your main springs from rattling loose from
their perches.
