Trust your instincts, the ball should fit snuggly into the plastic
socket. The socket part number is 911 424 139 00 and was used through
1986.  Ball diameter of your original stick should be the same as the
kit piece.  Check that the stick is assembled with the carrier and
buffer plates correctly. Good luck.
