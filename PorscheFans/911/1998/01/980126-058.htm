I had the same worry as I went to 100 watt high beams and looked at the
itty bitty switch contacts and as far as I recall all of the current
goes through the switch.....not a good deal in my mind. I added relays
to both high and low beams, Porsche sells the socket for the little
round black relays separately (p/n 901 612 333 00) and it is not a big
deal to wire them in.
Basically all you do is use the existing circuit to activate the relay
and then run power from an unused fuse or other circuit to your lights.

Takes about 2 hours with brain active, a whole day with a brain in need
of reboot (guess which path I took!!). I'd highly recommend it as you
then do not have another type of relay in the car and you can fit it
into the front relay panel with the rest of them (soak the relay socket
in hot water to soften the plastic so that you can pop it in).

Good luck
