For Joe Wilkerson:
The fact that all three of these malfunctioned at once seems to indicate
a minor electrical problem, rather than say, the "brain" for the cruise
contol, or some other expensive component.

On my 83, the "relais tempostat" relay is in the fourth position from
the rear of the car, on the electrical panel in the front trunk.  Try
substituting a known good relay that the car will run without (i.e. the
fog light relay).

On my car, the fuse for the cruise is also on this panel, seventh
position from the front.  Make sure that the wires entering and leaving
this fuse are clean and tight--sometimes they get loose.
