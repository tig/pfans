looks straightforward, but i'd hate to miss something.

wanna give me a reason not to?

i'm considering putting the battery in the "smuggler's boot" (the
compartment in the floor of the trunk, just aft of the gastank).  the car
is pretty modified, and it will be in a class that allows battery
relocation, so that's ok.  i've checked the dimensions, and it should fit.

battery in place.

pains to install weight (the cast-iron bumper weights).
