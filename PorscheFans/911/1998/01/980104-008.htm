Patrick:

There is a cure, but it involves the removal of the steering wheel and
turn signal/hi-low beam switch.   It seems that LUCAS gremlins have
invaded the Bosch electrics and caused excessive wear inside the switch
that controls the hi/low beam.  After removing the switch from the
housing around the steering wheel and looking inside, you can see the
two contacts that are too close together. Operate the hi/low switch to
see which ones make and break.  Very careful adjustment of the contacts
can cure the hi beam blues.   Be sure to disconnect the battery before
beginning.    There was another response some time ago that added
material to a spot on the switch.  You might check through the archives
from around six(?) months ago.   If all else fails, Tweeks, Automotion,
Stoddard etc. have the switch for about $100 US.
