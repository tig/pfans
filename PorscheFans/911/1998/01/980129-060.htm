I'm not a mechanic but I believe from my limited experience that the low
compression on one cylinder could be caused by a piece of carbon deposit
stuck on one of the valves of the cylinder in question.  The carbon
holds the valve slightly open which skews the reading.  But on the other
hand, if the engine does need rings, unfortunately $5k does not sound
too high. I've recently called around to several reputable Porsche shops
in the midwest and northwest to find out what a new top end costs and
been given the figure of $4-6k.
