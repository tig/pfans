>A few posts back I saw some information on vehicle storage.  Does anyone know

>is there to this as once the substance becomes saturated you're back to where

There is a product called Dry-Z-Air.  You can order it (Performance Products
sells it) or I've seen them in stores (Camping World, Orchard Supply, etc.). 
It has a bowl, with some chemical in a "cage" above the bowl.  That chemical
(I forget what it is) takes moisture out of the air and collects it in the
bowl below.  I use one in my Porche and a few other vehicles that aren't
driven much.  It works pretty well.
