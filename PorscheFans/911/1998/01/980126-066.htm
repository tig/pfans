Yarko,

You can do what you suggest, but you will have a massively understeering
pig. Here is what would work best:

225/50R15 (8" wheels) tires on the front
245/45R16 (9" wheels) tires on the rear
21mm torsion bars front
30mm torsion bars rear
22mm sway bar (#4 Charlie Bar) front
25mm sway bar (#6 Charlie Bar) rear
Bilstein shocks (non-adjustable) front and rear

I would run 1.75 to 2 degrees negative camber front and 1.5 to 1.75
degrees negative camber rear. I would consider using up to .5 degrees more
if you running anything but the '97 BFG R1 tires or the car is a track
only car.
