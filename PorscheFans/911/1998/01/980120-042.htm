Here's something you can do to adjust/remove the fan outer 
pulley (at least on my SC) if you don't have the wrench that 
came with the car:

Use an 8mm allen wrench to hold the shaft (it fits in the 
shaft tip) and pull the opposite way on the nut with a 24mm 
box end wrench..

I discovered this today while taking a break from my spark 
plug fiasco.  Hope this helps.
