One other way of dealing with the bleeder valve, but
particularly effective with brake line fittings that has
always seemed to work for me is to grab the fitting real
hard with a vice-grip. If you can't break it loose by hand,
whack the vice grip with a hammer.  A regular wrench will
quickly round the fitting, and the special brake fitting
wrenches aren't much better.  A good vice-grip will hold.
