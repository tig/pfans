Michael,

Typically, when oil and water are mixed (completely) you get the white stuff that you describe.  I would do a complete oil change (filter too) and look for the white scum on top of the oil after it is out of the car.  If there is none, the contamination probably occurred some time ago.

In any event, I think you just gained something to worry about on those nights you can't get to sleep.  I have no idea what running contaminated oil in an engine does to it.  I do know that when steel tubing is cut with a power hacksaw, the blade is lubricated with a mixture of oil & water at about a 50/50 ratio.  This prolongs the life of the blade and minimizes heat build-up at the cut.
