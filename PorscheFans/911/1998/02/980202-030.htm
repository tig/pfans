In order for a mag mount to work it must be "grounded". The mag mount
does not get a true ground off the body of the car even if it sits directly
on the metal. In most cases to obtain a good ground means running a
seperate wire from under the mag mount directly to part of the frame

I sometimes run a small amateur radio in the 911. The mount for the
antenna simply bolts to the frame and sits out behind the bumper about
6"-the antenna(about 4') bolts to that-looks ugly but it works-the trick is
getting it grounded to the car-not the location!
