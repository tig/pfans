On Tue, 24 Feb 1998, Mark Jennings wrote:

> I recently added a tail to my 1984 Targa, I then realized that I needed to
> add an additional strut on the right side to help hold it up.  I purchased
> a strut from Tweeks, but don't know how it is supposed to be mounted.
> There is a bracket hanging down for the left one to bolt to, but nothing on
> the right.  If anyone has ever done this before, I would appreciate your
> help on how to make this happen.  

Both struts go on the left side (mounted side by side). You'll need 
longer bolts and some spacers. You can buy these as a kit from 
various aftermarket P-car suppliers or just go to the hardware store 
and scrounge up the parts you need.
