The fog lights on my car are installed so the wire is left of the
bracket on both sides. That is, the driver's side fog light has the wire
outboard of the bracket, and the passenger side fog light has the wire
inboard of the bracket.

The amount of current flowing through the fog light relay should have no
effect on the fog light switch--that is what the the relay is there for.

If you want the fog lights on with high and low beams, just reconnect
the positive "signal" wire for the fog light relay. It is a small grey
wire, coming from the bottom of the one of the low beam headlight fuses,
going to the fog light relay. Move it to the bottom of the license plate
light fuse, and then the fogs will light whenever you have the
headlights on (or parking lights, if you have the fog light switch on).
