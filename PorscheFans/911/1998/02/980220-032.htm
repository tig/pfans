It is supposed to be located on the inside of your front hood.  It
should also be in your original service booklet, if you still have it. 
My father's 1983 911-SC coupe (bought used in 1994) does not have the
sticker on the inside of the front hood or in the service booklet.  It
seems that the dealership that originally sold the car had the
responsability of actually applying these labels.  Presumably some
dealerships didn't get the job done.  PCNA's Certificate's of
Authenticity should list most major options.
