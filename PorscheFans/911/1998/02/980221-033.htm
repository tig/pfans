I have a B&B four outlet on my 89 Speedster which I had painted black.
Looks are great, sound is fairly loud.  Don't set off any alarms in garages,
but definitely have to turn up the radio to hear over the car.  I did drive
the car last summer from Indianapolis to Lexington, KY and back and rather
enjoyed the sound.  Other Pfans have said that the car sounds great.
Valance modifications consisted of shaping driver's side valance to fit over
the outlet tubes.  Did this with a rasp, file and various grades of sand
paper.   Valance now sits directly on outlet tubes and I was intitially
concerned about the heat on the valance but this has not been a problem.
