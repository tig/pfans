A reply to Bill and any other owners of early (pre 75) brake/strut 
setups.

Several ways to go:

-pick up a set of oem used spindles, no brake upgrade

-used spindles and possible early bmw 320i calipers, we've done it on 
914s and i think the spacing is the same, they just need to be machined 
a little.

- S calipers, some people swear by them, others swear at them.
i suppose its a matter of the quality of the caliper and rebuild kit
used.

-later struts and brakes, 75 and later use the same type of caliper, you 
will need to update your master cylinder though, i would go for the 21 
or 23mm m/c, its a Mercedes part. made by ATE the oem manuf for Porsche

Of course the later the suspension in the 75-89 range the more likely it
will be in good shape, however anything past '68 is going to need, a 
complete front suspension as it is different. from the 65-68 cars.

Most of these parts are fairly pletiful in the used market, beware of 
suspension parts off wrecks though, they tend to be bent..
