Bruce wrote a book "911 Performance Handbook" and is a regular contributer 
to Excellence.

This book is a compulsory purchase for any 911 owner yet alone one who even 
contemplates doing work on his or her own car.

I was fortunate enough to meet Bruce when he visited NZ. As well as being an 
extremely approachable person  is very knowledgeable. He conducted a three 
hour plus technical seminar including talk and slide show on many of his 
race and Targa experiences ... everyone present was thoroughly entertained 
and would gladly have kept Bruce down here much longer, hopefully he will 
return soon.

I understand he runs similar sessions in the US and would recommend that you 
attend, what I cant understand is how anyone who lives and breathes Porsches 
hasnt heard of Bruce ...
