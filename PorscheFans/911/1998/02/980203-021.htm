Tired of a cord from your dash to cellular phone or laptop from dash
cigarette lighter???  I was,  so I installed a second power source down
on the passenger side of the center console.  Easy to do as there is a
"trap" door there held by two screws which opens up the center of the
console.  Install a Radio Shack lighter socket there and easily obtain
power from behind console switches ( they lift out ).  This also leaves
the dash plug for the radar detector!
