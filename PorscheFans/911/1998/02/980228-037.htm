I don't know about the Carrera 3.2 but I can tell you that the C4 is a great
car.  Specially the all wheel drive system is awesome.  Besides the obvious
advantages when driving in snow and rain (in rain you can drive like if the
road was dry) it improves handling tremendously including the understeer
when turning under acceleration and the oversteer when turning while laying
off the trottle.

There is a really good article about all wheel drive systems at
www.eskimo.com/~eliot/awd.html
They mention the C4 to be one of the better systems out there.

P.S. You can still get the rear wheel to break lose on the C4

Hope this helps
