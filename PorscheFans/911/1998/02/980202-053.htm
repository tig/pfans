Jim,

You can adjust the seats an inch(at least on my C2 you can), if it will
be in your favor or not depends on how the seats are currently mounted.

Forward the seats as much as possible. You should be able to see the
bolts in the rail. If you're lucky, just unscrew the 6 bolts(4 back+2 front)
and move the seat 1" backwards.

(I found this out when I put an amplifier under the drivers seat.)
