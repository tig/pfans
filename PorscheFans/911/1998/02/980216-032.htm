Obscure,

The "creaking" may be the front windshield seal or side window seals. If  you carefully pull back the front windshield seal
you will see that it is felt lined. The felt is to minimize the noise as the windshield "flexes". Try applying silicone to the felt 
(spray silicone on a rag and run the rag under the seal on the felt area, or apply a thin film from a tube using your fingers). 
Also, pull back the side window seals and do the same. It know it sounds funny but this may be your "creaking".

If you try it and it works let me know. This stopped the squeaking noises I was experiencing in my 993 C4.
