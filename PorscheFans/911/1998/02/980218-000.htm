Kevin H wrote
>As for a "quantum leap" between the 964 and 993, the 964 is
>80% newer than the Carrera, and the 993 is very similar
>mechanically to the 964 except for body and rear suspension
>(and intake/exhaust improvements, hydraulic valve adjusters).

Absolutely true! It's unbelievable how people stand on stereotypes more
than analyze true facts. 964 had a completely redesigned frame with a huge
improvement in strenght due, also, to the transmission tunnel, implemented
for the C4 but present in all 964 line. The introduction of springs instead
of torsion bars was another big revolution. Obviously the weight increased
but not so badly, considered the parallel increase of power. 993 was the
refinement (both mechanical and aesthetical) of a 964, excellent but not so
revolutionary as a 964 vs Carrera. I owned an '89 Carrera and, presently, a
964 turbo and I drove, track and road, 993s. Three beautiful cars!
