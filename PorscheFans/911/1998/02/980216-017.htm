Lee,

I think you may have a faulty windshield gasket. This was a fairly 
common problem on all 993's including my 96 C4S.  It caused all sorts 
of squeaks and rattles. Your dealer has a service bulletin on it and 
the fix is simple and covered by the warranty.  The repair can be 
done in a half day at the shop. Cured my squeaks and groans very 
nicely.

While you're thinking about this, check your windshield glass 
closely. If you see a milky discoloration at teh botton on the 
passenger side where the antenna wire joins the glass, you may get a 
new windshield out of the deal. Also a known condition on wide body 
cars.
