I use Mobil 1, and have done so for 8 years in my SC.  Expensive stuff,
but I think worth it.

and the "regular stuff" read Dennis Simanitis' article in Road @ Track
of a year or so ago.  Their index is available for review on AOL...if
you have access.  Great article.
The short version:   at temperature and stress extremes (do I hear track
time?) the synthetic will hold up better.
Your engine is worth it.  Leave cheap oil to the wankers.
