Okay, I couldn't stand not knowing.  I looked up the TSB on 18" wheels.
Porsche says any car not originally fitted with 18" wheels has the
potential to "result in loosened bolts and/or threaded connections on
the front wheel carriers.  This in turn, may cause fractures in these
components.  Fractures may also occur in the front strut mounts and in
the body in the area of the rear suspension as a result of the
installation of 18" wheels not already approved for installation for
this particular application."   They go on to say damage can occur
without warning, dealers should inform customers fitting 18" wheels of
this and report any cars retrofitted in this way to PCNA.  This is
TSB#9305 dated March 1993 for all cars.  I don't know if they've changed
since then.  I kinda doubt it.  

Good enough for me.  I'm keeping my 17's.
