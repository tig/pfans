Michael Rothenberg was having trouble starting his car.

His suspects were: 1. Starter
		   2. Starter Solenoid

He heard a clicking and a high pitched whine.

I think that both are working however the starter bendix is not being
activated.  If it is working then it is not making contact with the
starter ring gear teeth.  Either way you will be pulling the starting
motor.  Yes it is possible.  Start by getting some good hand cleaner.
