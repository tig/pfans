deep well
	socket of appropriate size (21?  23?) with a slot cut into its

	wire can stick out.  However, if you are removing an old one and
plan to
	throw it away, you can simply break off and cut off the wire and
	everything else that sticks out of metal hex, and use an
ordinary large
	socket.
