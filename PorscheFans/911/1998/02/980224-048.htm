Fred,

The seal kits are rather expensive (four pistons, etc.), but the calipers 
come apart beautifully, and are held together by 12mm socket head cap 
screws.  When you remove these and the caliper fluid lines, they 
dissassemble into three "easy-to-maitain" pieces.

If you need extra assembly bolts, I have about 10-12.
