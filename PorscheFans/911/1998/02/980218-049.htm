The welding is probably the best option, but you will cause a fair
amount of heat damage with a torch (much better with a mig or similar).
I would consider the rubber plug with appropriate rustproof paint or
failing that I would lead over the joint. It can be done with a very
small torch or heavy soldering iron and regular solder....takes a bit of
fussing, but it is rust proof and is sufficiently strong......
