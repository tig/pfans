Got my starter problem solved, thanks to the people who responded, I got
a number of good ideas as to how to diagnose it. Basically I checked the
wiring by running booster cables direct from the battery to the starter
(both ground and positive) and tested the solenoid operation by making a
jumper to bring power to the solenoid directly from the positve cable.
No change in performance.
Finally used both of my brain cells and tested the voltage across the
battery terminals......10.2 volts, means my battery was toast. Bought a
nice new Optima 800 and everything worked fine. Just have to get the
shift linkage adjusted and I am ready for the road.

Thanks to all......
