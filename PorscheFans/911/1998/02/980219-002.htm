Dear Terry,

Braze and body sheet metal arn't good combinations.  I many cases paint
won't stick to the brass.  Spot welding with a wire welder probably will
be the best for this repair.  However, making the hole round to a standard
diameter and fitting it with a rubber plug may be a good answer.  Another
idea would be to clean the undercoat back to good metal and prep with
anti-rust preparations.  Next use a short bolt and fender washers to plug
the hole. Re-undercoat with a paintable undercoat. Paint to match the
body.
