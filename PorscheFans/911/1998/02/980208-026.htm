When you download the Porsche screensaver (the German URL posted to
yesterday's list digest) you need to run Setup after you put the file in
Control Panel.  Then it works perfectly.  Against a dark background the
911 shapes morph into each other:  from late 60s/early 70s, to mid
70s/early 80s, to late 80s/early 90s, and finally to the 996.

My equipment is relatively primitive:  VGA with 1mb video card and
133mhz processor, in Windows 3.1.  So it ought to work with anything
higher, no?
