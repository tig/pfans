	Remove both stock rear mufflers and /or aftermarket exhaust system- if you
have the FABSPEED Motorsport 993 system you can change the lower plugs 
WITHOUT taking the system off SAVING 1-1.5hours additional R&R time. Lastly
the top right plug is a true pain in the ass as it is under the power
steering pump.

	Race harness belts 993 and 964s. There is a perfectly safe alternative to
drill a hole through the bottom of the car and carpet to install the 5 -
6th point for antisubmarine belt. On the front seat platform that are
welded to the floor chassis of the car are 2 triangular brackets on each
front seat. You drill a hole and slip in a AN bolt with 2 washers and a
locking nut in 1 side for a 5 point or both sides for 6 point. Also on the
transmission tunnel there should be a threaded BOSS that will accept a 7/16
bolt or you can have a shop weld a nut to a washer and weld this to a
fairly large factory made (air spce) captive hole under the rear carpet
area right near the floor pan.
