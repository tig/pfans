    I have a mini-CB w. cellular look-a-like antenna.  The small 2" base
   
    is attached to the rear glass with double-sided adhesive tape, no
   drilling required.  Works OK, probably not long range like the mag
   mount antenna, but I've received 'smokey' warnings 5-8 miles away.
   BTW, the antenna itself is quickly removed with 1 screw (the base
   remains) and it's track tested to 135-140 mph when I forgot to remove
   it last time.  Also,
   a bit more stealth than the 3-4' mag mount and not have to worry
   about scratching the paint.  CB is useful in avoiding traffic jams,
   construction etc and planning alternate routes even when commuting.
