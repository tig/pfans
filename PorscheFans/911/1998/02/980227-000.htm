Use the WRD5P plugs.  These are what are recommended for your car.
Some people say that copper plugs are better, but I have been using the
platinum ones for several years and like the service I got.
You should get a tech manual for your car.  They are available from most
aftermarket suppliers such as Performance Products.  This will help
answer a lot of your questions.

Swepco is better for your tranny.  Drain the old stuff, do your switch,
and then renew the fluid with Swepco.  Zims has good price on it.
