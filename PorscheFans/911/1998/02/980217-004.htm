Andy, I have just put new kits through my calipers and I just used an air
squirter/blower pushed against the caliper and about 30psi air pressure. I
also put a small block of wood in the middle of the caliper to stop one
side coming through too far. It also helps to have a rag over the lot to
stop any brake fluid squirting out. This job is much easier than it first
seemed. Hope this helps, regards Dean
