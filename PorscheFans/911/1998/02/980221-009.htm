Yes, I bought a '97 993 without Factory CD changer and added the correct
Sony to plug in to the Factory Becker radio. If you look at the trunk area
of a Factory-equipped 993, you will see that the changer mounts vertically
at the left front . I used a wire loom stud and the front 'frame rail'
ledge to mount my changer. No holes had to be drilled, except one in the
firewall to route the power and data leads to the back of the radio. Looks
right, works great.

E-mail me directly, and I'll give you more detail or see if I can send you
a photograph of it to make it more clear.
