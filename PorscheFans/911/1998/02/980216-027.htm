Jason,
On a 70 911S look for rust wherever there is metal ;)

Check the floorboards. They should be rock hard with no flex. Pull up the
carpets/mats from the inside.

Check the rocker panels (bottom of front fenders by doors).

Check the lip of the hood.

Check in the trunk. 

Look below the windows seals. No bubbles in paint.

Of course a 911S at the right price can need/undergo rust repair and still
be a good deal. On my old '67 912 (from which I learned the above) it
was cost prohibitive.

Good luck,
