Just done that one
1st check that its the sensor by manually shorting together the 2 wires
from sensor to the speedo (green & brown from memory), these are under the
little access cover in the centre of the car behind the seats, with the
ignition on this will cause the speed o needle to flick up if the speedo
itself is OK.
In which case its the sender (mine was)-  this is  easy to change if you
can get under the car, Even the wire clips go back easy - you will get very
mucky from the gung that collects on the top of the gear box.  About the
hardest part was getting the rubber bung that seals the hole to the tunnel
back - put washing up liquid on it 1st.
