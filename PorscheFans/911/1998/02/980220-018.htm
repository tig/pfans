Andrew:

I have a '90 C2 Cab and I had a 6-CD Changer put into the boot.  The
installer drilled holes in the passenger side portion of the boot wall and
put it there.  I've had very few problems with skipping - the only real
problem is that it's a pain in the ass when you need to take the spare tire
out, because you have to dismount the changer from the side wall.
