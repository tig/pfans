Art, I previously had a '84 Turbo-Look that I sold with 135,000 miles.
I intially used Castrol, then switched to Castrol synthetic then Mobil
1 synthetic.  

Engine had to come apart at 135,000 due to a seized/snapped spark plug.
Shop said heads were in good shape, onlyu problem were that valve
guides were at wear limit.  Bottom end they said was excellent.  This is on
a car that was at race track weekends prob. 6 times/year, plus 3-4 
autocrosses a year.

You should have no aftereffects due to switch.

Re the chips:  tried about 2-3 chips, incl. Weltmeister.  Did nothing
for performance, one of them caused driveability deterioration.  The mass flow
sensor change, though, does give you better high RPM kick., if 
you are looking for HP upgrade.
