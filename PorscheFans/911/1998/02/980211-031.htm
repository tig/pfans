>For all of you guys with late '80s 911s with Air Conditioning:  where is
>the "front condenser" located in these cars?

Rob, I'm working on memory here...

The condenser should be on the drivers side, behind the bumper about center
with the driver's seat. Lift up the carpet and you will see a conical black
plastic unit bolted to the decking there. That is the fan. If you remove it
you will see the fins of the condenser down below. That may help in your
search for the routing of the lines.
