Hi Doug.  Congrats on the new car.  I just finished putting a sound system in
my 86 Targa.  I didn't got all out or anything on it but it sounds pretty
good.  It has a couple Boston Acoustics speakers in the doors and some other
brand (I don't remember at the moment) plate style speakers on the rear deck
(the PO had these installed).  For subs I used a custom box built by Rod Birch
of Car Audio Innovations that fits in front of the rear seats, on the floor
(he just started a web site at http://bbs.uweb.ml.org/cain/default.html and
he's got an ad in Excellence).  If you want subs, I highly recommend his box. 
He'll match the carpet color of your interior too.  It looks factory.  I
didn't use a separate crossover, but the ones built in the amp I'm using.  It
won't win any of those sound competitons, but it sounds great with my favorite
backup singer ("flat 6") singing in the background.. :)  If you want to know
about my system in more detail let me know.
