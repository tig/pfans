As you probably observed, the door stay can be attached in two ways;
confirm that you've got it bolted in the correct way by comparing to the
other door.  Also, when I replaced mine the doors didn't close and like
you I wondered if they were installed correctly.  However, after
confirming that they were installed correctly and a deep breath, I
"cloooosed" the door they loosened up nicely. Good Luck.
