The 993 Targa is actually built on a Cabriolet chassis. The targa roof
structure is fitted separately, and the design does not carry uninterrupted
steel members up into the roof from the lower chassis the way the coupe
does.  As a result, many Porsche Club regions have required the 993 Targa
to use a roll bar to be eligible to run driver's education events.

I have no idea on the pricing, except that these were $75-90k cars when
new, depending on options, and this one sounds loaded.
