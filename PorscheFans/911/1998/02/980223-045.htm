I assume you're talking about a 901 tranny, if so I put the Weltmester shift
springs in several years ago.  Definitely reduces the tendency to nick rev on
a quick 1/2 shift  I don't recall having to drill any new holes but it was
several years ago.  They increase the amount of effort needed to get past the
lockout in 1st/reverse.  What really helped was a new shift guide and careful
adjusting of the rod between the shifter and the coupler.  This along with a
short shift kit made shifting much more positive and precise.
