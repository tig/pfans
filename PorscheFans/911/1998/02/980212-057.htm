Rick,

There are lots of 87-89 fans out there that support the idea
this is the best 'classic' 911 -- however
test drive a C2 for comparison and decide which is the
better car -- yes there is quite a difference.

There are a few things to be careful of with the early
C2/C4's -- the dual-mass flywheel, increased
costs, increased complexity of the C2/C4 make it less of a
DIY vehicle.  But the 3.6 feels a lot
stronger at low RPM's, and no one will mistake it for a '74
(good or bad, depending on your opinion).
