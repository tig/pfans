Dirk,

All the following assumes correctly adjusted suspension settings: ride
height, camber, etc.)

On the front, it's really easy.  Detach one droplink at the A-Arm, leave
the other side attached.  Settle the car out; place on a flat surface. 
Recheck ride height and equalize.  Settle the car out again.  Adjust the
detached droplink length so that it easily fits back on the A-Arm. 
Tighten down locknuts.  You're done with the front.

I presume rear is the same, although I've never had adjustable
droiplinks there, so don't have any firsthand experience.
