I have a K&N filter in my 78 SC, which works exactly as it was intended 
to: it prevents those little solid nasties from getting in the engine.

All this other nattering about horsepower gains and losses is beside the 
point...keep a clean air filter in your car, drive it, and be happy. The 
brand of air filter you use is not going to make any difference in a 
street car.
