be interested in:

-rebuilding drivers bucket.  PO was pretty heavy, and broke lower cushion side

those guys who make Madonna's costumes feel!  

A comment on two threads that were running recently.  Yes, dry chem fire
extinguishers (BC, ABC chem) is very nasty to aluminum and magnesium.  this is
why they are not allowed near aircraft, even for the ground support vehicles
at an airport.  these vehicles use purple K, suitable for soft metals.  you
could also get a halon extinguisher, or a halon alternative.

on targa tops, i had dan pechtel inspect my top and quote a price.  $725
