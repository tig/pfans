Your question might refer to Bruce Anderson's recommendation that you cut 
the cylinder cooling air deflectors on pre 76 cars about 1 inch. See his second 
edition, page 176 for a drawing and 184 for discussion. But he's talking about 
cutting engine temps, NOT cabin temps, by about 10 degrees.  Since these 
cars run hot, I'd definitely make sure this gets done. Hope this helps. Jim Taylor
