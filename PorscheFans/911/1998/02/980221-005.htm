VVVince, one data point:  I put a Bursch exhaust in my old '84 Carrera
Turbo-Look; running up & down thru the gears, at autocrosses, and at
track events sounded great.  However, the "growl" got tiring on long
trips.  This was standard Bursch; I believe they also have a "quiet" model
out there, with an added small muffler before the tailpipe.

I remember having poteintial corrosion problems, with rocks/sand/dust 
eventually wearing away paint and starting light surface rust.  Was never able
to find paint that lasted (buddy recommended black barbecue grille paint,
but didn't work).  Don't remember big problem with installation, but center
(main) mounting system was different than factory.
