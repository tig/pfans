Find a copy of the February issue of Excellence magazine, there's an article
in there about late model 911s (1989-1996).

They specifically cover the '94 Speedster:
 
"Another significant 964-based model is the Speedster, a bare-bones, cut-down
911 Cabriolet. The 1994 Speedster sold in low enough numbers (409 were
imported, 9 with the optional Tiptronic) that its market value can be
difficult to establish. The 964-based Speedsters range from $39,000 to
$52,000."
 
The article continues to point out some of the problems in the 964 models
(pre-'92 head seal problems, dual mass flywheel failure, distributor belt).

Also, in an old copy of the Dupont Registry (Nov '97), Prestige Imports (Miami
Bch., FL) lists a '94 Speedster, maritime blue/gray with 18K miles for $48,950.
 Hope this helps.
