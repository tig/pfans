>cap was removed.  On my '88, the idle drops some, but not as much as on the

84-89 3.2 engines are motronic and compensate with their idle stabilizers
when additional air, or A/C drag, another example, are present.  The aperture
in the stabilizer closes to compensate higher idle and visa versa.
