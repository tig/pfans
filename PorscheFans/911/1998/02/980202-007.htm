If you stick to the stock tire sizes for 16" rims (205/55 front; 225/50
rear) you don't need to roll the lip even if you lower the car 1". Any
larger tire sizes, though, and you will need to roll the lip. A friend has
done it for his '88 Carrera, and has promised to help me (I bought his old
wheels for track use). However, I can't comment on the technique yet.
