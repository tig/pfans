The Porsche workshop/repair manuals are currently available on microfiche for
cheap prices, especially compared to the hard copy ones.  I think I paid
$20-30 for each set, with PCA or Excellence discounts.

I picked up a cheap used euro format reader that works fine.  Others have cut
the fich in half and viewed them that way.  That may require a lens change but
that is doable.  I would think you'd lose quality and have trademark concerns
if you copied them?  I know, who cares on the trademark stuff but don't try to
advertise and sell them or the wraith of Porsche will be after you.
