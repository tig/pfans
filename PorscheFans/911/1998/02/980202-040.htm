Hi Daniel,
I just finished (almost) refurbing my steering wheel (85 Carrera).  The kit
from Agla (about $50 in Pano) is excellent and fits like a charm.  The
leather color (blue in my case) was also a perfect match.  Everything,
including needles, is included in the kit.  If you have spare time and
strong fingers the job is not too hard.  I do think that next time I will
send the wheel into them and for about $100 they will do the stitching.
