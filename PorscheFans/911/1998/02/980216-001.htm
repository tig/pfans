Hi Dan:

   First the fronts:

Near the bottom of the strut tube, you will see the ends (on both sides)
of a roll pin. This is what secures the insert into the strut body. Tap
this out with a suitable punch. Use new roll pins when reinstalling.

Now the rears:

This isn't easy, I usually hold the top of the strut with a very small
vice grip and remove the nylock nut with a short open-end wrench. Can't
move much so its a little at a time.

Hope this helps,
