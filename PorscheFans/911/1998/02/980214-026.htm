I'm a low temperature physicist who has heard about this multiple times.
All  I have heard in anecdotal in nature from multiple sources. One
example is the claim that cryogenic treatment of brake rotors ahve
significant benefits (they don't crack).

In essence, this can be thought of as another heat treatment cycle(s). I
suspect that there is something happening on a metallurgical level, but
don't have the time to pursue it. I'm not sure anyone has systematically
(academically) studied the process. The thermal cycling would be
somewhat different dempending on the alloy and appplication.
