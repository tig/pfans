Another thing to consider when looking at a magneticly mounted CB antenna is the speed you'll be traveling.

I had one on my Audi and it didn't scratch the paint until it came off at 75 mph which is legal in eastern Colorado.  I had to re-route the coax so that it approached the antenna from the front and held it on the car.
