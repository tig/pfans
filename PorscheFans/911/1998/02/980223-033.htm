Hi Stan:

  Two things come to mind about the clicking sound you hear.

First, you may have an exhaust leak somewhere in the system, or some
leakage at the juncture of cylinder head and barrel.

Second, you just might be hearing some detonation. Without hearing this
myself, its kinda hard to tell exactly,.....  :^)

Bottom line:   Inspect the entire exhaust system from heads to muffler
very carefully and do the same for the bottom of the engine. Pull the
spark plugs and, using a good magnifier, look at the porcelain around
the firing tip from very small balls of shiny metal. They will be
small!! If you see any, this can be aluminum from the pistons.

E-mail me with what you find.
