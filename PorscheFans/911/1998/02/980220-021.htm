For those of us that go without our Pcars during the winter months I
thought they might find a new aviation product being marketed by Shell
to be of interest.  Its called fluid 2F and is intended to protect
engines that are not run in less than ideal conditions from the effects
of humidity, rust and corrosion.  The idea is that you drain your oil,
replace it with 2F fluid run it for 15 min. wait till spring and replace
it with regular oil.  In the first report I saw Shell said it was not
for automotive applications but when I call there product support they
couldn't tell me why not other than its not recommended for road use.
If I find out any more I'll let you all know.
