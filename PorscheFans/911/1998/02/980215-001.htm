Mike,

I ran a 2.7 with CIS pistons and Webers for 8 years and they worked good. They
had also been flycut to allow me to run S cams (relief pockets for the valves).
It was a very economical way to approach a 2.7 RS spec engine. Granted my power
would have been a little lower (maybe 5 - 10 hp) than with the proper RS
pistons but the lower compression ratio made fuel quality problems nonexistent.
If you don't have $1500 burning a hole in your pocket ( the price of the RS
pistons and cylinders) I would recommend this approach. And if you stick with
the stock cam the power difference would be very small.
