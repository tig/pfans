To follow up on the thread from last week regarding the apparent wide 
range of vendor prices: don't forget to check your local dealer for 
maintenance parts!

Today I bought a shifter bushing from Circle Porsche for $5, which is 
listed at over ten from several others ($14.95 from Automotion, last I 
checked).  They also had factory 911 oil filters for $7.50...

The moral is that dealers may be very competitive on Porsche factory 
generated parts.  Call to compare if you live close enough to a good 
dealer.
