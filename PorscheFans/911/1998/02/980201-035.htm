Hi Steve,

I replaced the dual batteries in my 71E with the Optima and i'm very glad I
did. The car turns over so much better and no worry about battery acid. The
71E does not have the oval canister you mentioned unless you are referring
to the gas tank which is covered by the spare tire. Anyway the Optima
slides right into the existing battery compartment on driver's side (on
it's side). I first lined the compartment with battery mats so as not to
have any contact with terminals. I made an extension piece that allows me
to use the original hold down strap and it holds the battery nice and
secure. 

Hope this is of some help. Good luck.
