I have the factory kit installed, mine came with a new lever, springs
and the lockout plate (perhaps a few other small
parts).........basically the job is to unbolt the housing from the
centre tunnel, and then replace the old parts with the new. Only real
pain is the springs as they are under a fair tension and require a vise
or someone with strong fingers......

You also may want to see if you have the late housing.....you can tell
if you have a locknut as the front longitudinal pivot point for the
shift lever. That housing makes for nicer shifting as it lets you get
the fore/aft play right where it should be.......

I also replaced the cup on th bottom of the shift lever and the bushings
in the tunnel ......it helped.

Hope this helps
