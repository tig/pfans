Mark - 

On my turbo tail, I have two struts, BOTH mounted on the left side:
just bolt the extra one right next to the first one, on either side - it 
will be on the outside of the brackets, and you will need longer bolts,
but it works great for me...

BTW, mount the second strut backwards in relation to the first one -
this allows more clearance, since it puts rod next to tube, rather than
two tubes next to each other.

good luck...
