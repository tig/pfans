First do a leakdown test.  After you determine you have leakdown, pressurize
each cylinder and listen to where the air is escaping (exhaust for exhaust
valves, air intake for intake valves, and oil filler for rings.  This can tell
you quite a bit.  If you have about $64 for the tool and an air tank, you can
buy a inexpensive setup made by Tavia (part # TAV-08015) from Summit Racing
Equipment (800-230-3030) or elsewhere.
