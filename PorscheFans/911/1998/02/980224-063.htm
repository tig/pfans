I bought mine from AJ USA, basically you place the new shock beside the
original single shock and use the little aluminum spacers to space the
pivot points out far enough to allow the two struts room to sit side by
side. Your kit should have included longer bolts which go through the
original pivot bolts and give room to install the spacer and the second
strut. BTW, the second strut goes toward the centre line of the
car..............
