I have a '89 TurboLookTarga, and I have a *great* mount for my
CB radio - I removed the whale tail, and bolted a cb ant base
thru the louvers of the whale tail, and routed the antenna cable
into the interior - now I can remove the actual antenna, and 
all that's left is the small base, which I put a rubber cap over -
you can't even see it, since the louvers of the w.tail are black
also.

It took a little bit of work, but the visual result is worth it - much
better than a magnetic mount!

I get *great* reception, from 5-10 miles, with this wilson antennea
I have...

We need to encourage more people to get CB's in their cars  - 
it's the best way to drive safely at high-speed on our interstates!
