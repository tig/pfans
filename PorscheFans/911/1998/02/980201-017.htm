Lee:

These are, generally speaking, excellent handling and benign cars to
drive.

Your choice of tires is an excellent one, we have used and recommended
these to many people in that past 4 years with no problems.

Based upon our 993 experience, your car sounds like it is out of
alignment.
I think you'll find the toe setting out of spec when you have your 993
re-aligned. This will cause that sort of "nervousness" in the steering
when driving on uneven and bumpy roads. Excess toe-in or toe-out creates
the
wandering that you feel in the steering.
