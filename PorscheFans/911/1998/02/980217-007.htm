Here are the resistance values from the shop manual for the head temp.
sensor (911 Carrera):

	32 deg F	4.4 to 6.8 k-ohms
	59 - 86 deg F	1.4 to 3.6 k-ohms	
	104 deg F	1.0 to 1.3 k-ohms
	176 deg F	250 to 390 ohms
	212 deg F	160 to 210 ohms
	265 deg F	90 ohms
