I have a cordless BEL 945i.  If it does not pick up any activity for an
hour, it will shut off, but it will tell me beforehand.  It tells me when

picks up highway and local signals.  It senses road work, detours, etc.  It
picks up 3 frequencies.   I am not a reckless driver, but if I were one to
follow ALL the rules, I would have purchased something other than a turbo.

My detector has saved me from getting a ticket quite a few times.  (The
color of my car is not common.  My mufflers make it possible for people to
hear me a long time before they see me.  I am sure the local police have
