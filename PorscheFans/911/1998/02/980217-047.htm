Don't know if my method is approved by the factory, but I take a piece
of 2 x 6 and place it under the body right where the body curves up to
form the rear seats.....there is a seam there that seems pretty durable.
I then put the floor jack in from the side and jack from there. No
problems getting access to the torsion bars, but you need to make sure
that you don't go into the sheet metal floor pan as it is basically just
thin stuff and not too far back as you then get in the way as the
transmission moves downward.

Just put the engine in yesterday this way....not a problem.....
