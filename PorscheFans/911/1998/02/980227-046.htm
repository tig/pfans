Note that all dealers don't treat all customers the same.  I made a point
of stopping at Stoddard's on my last trip through Cleveland.  I picked up
several parts for my 911, thinking that I could avoid shipping and get the
PCA discount.  Stoddard's only grants the PCA discount to local region
members.  I understand their reluctance to offer a global discount on mail
orders.  I was surprised that they would differentiate based on home region
for counter sales.
