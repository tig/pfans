Andy,
I fixed a problem on my car this weekend with EXACTLY the same
symptoms you describe (wipers not parking, no intermittent operation). 
A guy on the list had pointed out to me the problem....the wiper switch
was not closing a contact when in the "off" position...gently pushing the
arm towards the dash closes the contact.  The fix suggested to me was
to build up the plastic piece that closes the contact.....however, it
seemed to work just as well to gently bend the two copper contacts
down slightly so the switch closed.  To access the switch in an "84
Carrera required pulling the wheel....the two plastic clamshells covering
the switch cannot be removed with the wheel in place.   Anyway, pull
the wheel (26mm socket I think), remove half a dozen small screws, look
inside the switch and bend the offending contacts, reassemble.  Took
about 30 minutes.  Good luck.
