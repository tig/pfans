Well...you're not going to want to hear this...but I think
there's a whole range of authentic scripts.  My very original '72 911E had
silver Porsche and silver 911E scripts.  My '72 911T has black Porsche script,
but silver 911 (not 911T!) script.  My '73 911T has black Porsche script
and black 911T script.

If I were you I'd pick whatever colors and combinations you liked best.
They all seem to be authentic.
