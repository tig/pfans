Walt wrote that getting the starter out while the engine/tranny is in
the car is possible.  In theory yes, but I want to tell you that if the
darned thing has been in there for a number of years that the bolts
holding it to the engine/tranny are a bitch to get out.  I had my SC
engine out and had to use a cheater bar on a power bar to get those two
nuts off...I do not think it would have been a lot of fun laying on my
back and trying to get this done with the starter in the car.
This might be a job best left to a professional wrench who is used to
this type of aggravation. 
 
My .02.
