You can obtain odometer rollback, odometer rollover and unreliable odometer
data from CarFax at http://www.carfax.com assuming that the title has been
transferred recently.  Additionally, the owner's manual should give you the
previous owner's name - call them.  The service records should
chronologically list mileage.  The servicing dealer should have computerized
records on the car.  Don't have the owner's manual, don't have the service
records, don't have enough data?  Don't buy the car.  Good Luck!
