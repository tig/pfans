John,
There is a Technical Service Bulletin about rear center reflector
condensation on early 964's.  The solution is similar to the one used
for the ozone (broken timing belt) in the second distributor.  A kit
that routes dry air into the back of the enclosure via clear plastic
Tygon tubing from just downstream of the electric fan in the engine
compartment.  There is also a gasket retrofit kit for the lens.  I'm not
sure of the exact serial numbers that require the gasketing.  You should
be able to get this all taken care of at your local dealer.  My car has
the tubing and stays dry.  Good Luck.
