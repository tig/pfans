The white scum under the oil tank cap and sometimes in the tube is the
result of condensation of the oil in the tank plus the breather hose coming
in at that top of tube. Usual cause is cold weather short trips. Remember
we have over twelve quarts of oil which need to get up to temp to get rid
of any of the water.
