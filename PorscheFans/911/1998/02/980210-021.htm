> Yousef:
> Lots of ways an engine can consume oil but a compression test is just
> that.  How much air pressure does the piston generate on the
> compression stroke.  Interestingly Porsche factory manuals don't list
> a specific pressure but rather the difference from one to the others.
> Leak down is a diagnostic method of seeing if and by how much pressure
> a cylinder will loose over a period of time.  This is usually given in
> a percentage of leak down from the fully compressed state.  With a
> leak down you can simply listen for the air escaping, but from
> experience depending on the engine intake and exhaust configuration
> this can be easier said than done.
