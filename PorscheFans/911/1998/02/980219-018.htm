Hi Al,

This isn't an answer to your question about 993 headers for a C2 but one 
easy way to "gain" horsepower is to drop weight so what you have has less 
to do it to :-)

Some ballast that you can do without is the extra weight of the first rear 
muffler that spans the back of the car. It can be replaced by a B&B (or 
other fabricator's) bypass pipe. The cost is around $200-$300 and the 
weight savings is at least 50 lbs, which is well worth it. The remaining 
muffler under the right side qtr panel is more than up to the job of 
keeping the car street quiet at reasonable RPM.
