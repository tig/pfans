I have been using K&Ns for years in my cars and cycles. I have run one in
my SHO for the last 125K miles or so (I have 158K on it now) and I only
burn 1 quart of oil every 2500 miles. This is with a 7500 rpm shift point
that I hit at least 2x a day . A very dusty construction sight is
rough on ANY filter, so unless your car lives in this environment, I
would not worry about the K&N. I keep to the 50K cleaning recommendation
and feel they are a great product.
