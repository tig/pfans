Boy are you going to get a lot of advice.

I have used Repco and they will not dust up and are "ok" for general street
use...but, if you are going to track the car you need a more agressive pad.

I use Hawke pads.  I do get a little squeal at low speeds when they are
cold, but the braking power of these pads is worth this minor irritant.

Go for the HPS version.  

Good luck.
