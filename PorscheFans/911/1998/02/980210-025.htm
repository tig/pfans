Hi Michael,

Your problem is caused by someone leaving out some of the shims that are used
to adjust the fan belt tension. When some of the shims are left out the fan
pulley cannot be properly tightened and you are trying to tighten the outer
pulley have against the rubber belt itself. 

When you adjust the belt you move the shims from one side of the outer pulley
to the other, you do not throw them away or save them in your tool box for
another day. I promise you that if you leave any of these shims out you will
have to buy a new fan and outer pulley half. 

Bruce Anderson
