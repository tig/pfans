You will need to remove the back rest of the seat to remove the quarter
panel.

When removing the panel, go slowly and make sure that you do not forget
any
screws, there are a lot of them around.

The thin portion around the window frame is held in place with
plastic snaps (4 or 5 of them)

Hope this helps.
