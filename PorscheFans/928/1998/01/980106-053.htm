To Phil & others like me wondering if the cooling fans will run after engine shut-down & ignition off:
Per PCNA Technical in Reno and a manual I saw this afternoon at Niello Porsche, yes the fans will run under the above scenario. They can run for up to 45 minutes after shut-down but they run at 1/2 speed. Two sources can cause this to occur.
1. temp exceeding 87.5 degrees C registered at the temp. sensor mounted on top of the intake manifold plenum.
2. coolant temp exceeding 85 degrees C registered at the radiator-mounted temp sensor. 
Opening the hood will obviously shut the fans down.
I was told the ign.-on fan activating temps are higher than the temps above which explains why the fans stop when the ign is turned off. Anyone is welcome to have at me on this, but I buy it.
