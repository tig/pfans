Option codes, twin exhaust pipes, shorter shifter. I am not sure of the option
code but someone will give it to you. Engine code is M28.47 for an '89.  If
you have that you have the GT engine and most likely sport seats and sport
shocks.  Also no indicator on the rear bumper, most GT's don't have one.  Also
5-speed, club sport wheels.  If you have all these you most likely have a GT.
