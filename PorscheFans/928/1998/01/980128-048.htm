miles, red/black, loaded with all options, stored winters, excellent =
condition.  $11900/obo.

The minute I saw the ad I picked a phone and inquired about it.  I asked =
most of the common questions one would ask,=20

Me: are you the original owner ?=20

Me: Has the car been in an accident ?=20
Him: No, it's in immaculate condition.

Me: Do you have all of the service records
Him: Yes

Me: Can I see it today?
Him: No, I'm very busy w/school. =20

Me: When can I see it ?=20
Him: Call me back next week.

Me: Ok, bye

Me: can I see the car?

phone.

Me: you told me to call you back at this time, day, and date...

Me: alright, bye.
Him: bye.

Him: Hi Sleu, if you're still interested in that Porsche it's here.  =

first person that looks at it will buy...
