Dan,

Tell the dealer to put a hot start circuit in your car.  My '86 S/Euro
was having the same problem.  A local Porsche mechanic friend and I
installed a little Bosch relay with three wires and an integral fuse.
This circuit shunts battery power directly to the starter when needed.
My car has been starting fine ever since.  The part is about $10.
Larry, the Porsche guy, says that most German alternators have the same
problem after a few years.  Their become a little weak and it takes more
to get them started.  Well, I'm a believer.  My car has been starting
fine ever since the small mod we made.

Good luck!
