Assuming that you have a 32 valve engine (2 distributors).  I got this
information from the
sticker on my radiator.

Passenger side distributor:  4-6-7-1
Driver side distributor: 5-2-3-8

Cylinder # on the engine:
            4        8
            3        7
            2        6
            1        5
              Front

I hope this help and that you learned a valuable lesson: Only change
1 wire at a time to eliminate the chance of confusion ;-)
