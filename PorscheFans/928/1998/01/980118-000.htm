Mark, 

The stubs going into the cover are not pressed in they are held in place
by Circlips in the case of two and a nut in the case of the other two.
note : only some have hoses (but you will have noticed that!!)
You can only take them out by removing the camshaft cover itself
unfortunatley. To replace the hose is also quite trickey as they go
under the inlet manifold and on to the bottom of the oil filler housing
and the inlet system if my memory serves me correctlybut the diagram is
in the manuals if you have them.
In any case good luck.
