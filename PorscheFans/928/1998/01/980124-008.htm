I cut 2 inches of  my 86 s4 shifter with a cut off wheel of a dremel tool
and 1 inch of the bottom of the knob with a band saw.  Take the center
console out, and the leather boot/knob comes out as a unit.  Turn the
leather boot inside out and you have access to the rubber knob to cut.
Rethread the shifter and the resulting 2 inch shorter shift is fun.

I had modified a previous ride, 76 280z, to a very short shift.  When I
bought
the 86, it felt like a ford f150 shift with the amount of travel.
Shortening
the shifter is an easy way to make the car just that much more fun.  I
initially had concerns about 'changing the stock' 928, but as soon as I
made the change there are no regrets.
