In California, it is illegal to tint the front windows; driver and
passenger.  Majority of the people (I included) uses 10-20% tint in the
front and able to get away with it.  In reality, yes, it depends on the
darkness of the tint and always, it depends on the cop.  I got stopped
on I-5 one day driving 63 in a 55 mph zone.  The CHP asked me if my
front windows tinted.  I said "Yes".  He said that there is a law
against tinting the front, etc. etc.  And I responded by saying that I
spend about 4 hours on the road daily and that my dermatologist said
that I need to protect my face while day-driving.  (pointing to my
freckles on the side of my face..)  He let me go with a warning.  No
citation on the window tinting.
