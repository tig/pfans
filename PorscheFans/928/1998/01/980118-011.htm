Nope. S4s of that era came (like mine) with a single oval outlet (which is
actually welded onto the stubs of two small pipes). Porsche points out that
the only dependable way to tell the GT apart from the S4 (apart from the
wheels) is spotting the two small circular pipes of the GT's exhaust.
