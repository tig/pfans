Barry,
I had this happen a few years ago on my '78.  The culprit was the clamp
bolt on the shift rod, just where it connects to the transmission.  The
bolt screws into the rod from the shifter and has a pointed end that ente=
rs
a conical depression in the transmission shift rod.  There is a universal
joint adjacent to the bolt, and then there is a rubber boot covering all
this. It is a bear to get to, since it is above the trans, but it can be
done.  Mine was almost falling out, by the time I located the trouble.  I
took it out, cleaned it, put on locktite and screwed it back in.  Tightly=
..=20
I think I had to use an open end crowfoot socket to get it done.  You can
slide the rubber boot away from the bolt for access.  Hope this helps.
Dave Nicholson
