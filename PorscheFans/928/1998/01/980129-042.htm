I stand by our original contention that weight has no relation to
aerodynamcis.  The amount of aerodynamic lift required to cause a car to
leave the ground will be in some part determined by the static mass of

greater import is the nature of the airflow around the vehicle.  I
further contend that the corvette is no lightweight, and moreover that
you consider a course in English Vocabulary.
