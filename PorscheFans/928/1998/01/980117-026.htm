Dear "Land Shark" tamers:

Those hoses (there are two) are oil return lines. At idle and low speeds,
they are under a vacuum. Over 1500 RPM they are no longer under a vacuum, 
but are under a bit of pressure. to the sump. The oil you see in the valley 
of your V8 is probably due to one or both hose leaking. Usually its' the 
front one. The oil returning to the sump is very hot and over time reacts 
with the rubber. Eventually, the rubber fails. The hoses is held by clams on

both ends. The top on is eazy to get at. The bottom one will require removal

of either the fuel rail or the intake manifold or both. It's Not an easy
job.

Good luck and happy motoring. Ed
