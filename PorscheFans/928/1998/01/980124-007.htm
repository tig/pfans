In regards to the color rauchquartzmetallic, I too have an '84S that is
this color. When I received my cardex from PCNA, it listed my car as
platinum. But, my door sticker said X5 rauchquartzmetallic. I was
confused until one of our Austin 928ers, Jim Stadtler, who is another
member of this list, loaned me a Porsche 928 brochure from 84 and it
listed both color options. In the brochure, X5 is listed as Quartz Gray
Metallic, and Platinum has a different color code. When I compared the
two color squares, my car was definitely Quartz Gary Metallic.  Even
though rauchquartz translates to smoke quartz, the US brochure calls it
Quartz Gray, (interesting). It makes me wonder about the accuracy of the
cardex...
