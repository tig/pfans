> I have some questions for the oracles of 928 and Porsche.
> What is the last year that 15 inch wheels will fit on a 928?

  Brakes were upgraded in '82 and these may preclude the use of
  15" wheels on any car from that year forward.  Double check the
  archives, but I recall that most owners that drove in snow just
  used factory 16" wheels.
  
> Do 911 wheels have the same offset and bolt pattern?

  Offset - no.  Bolt pattern - probably, but I'm not positive.  
  Anyway, they won't fit (properly).
  
> Is there a particular year that the factory lowered the car? some of the
> earlier ones seem higher.

  All cars came with rear ride height adjusters, and many came with front
  adjusters, so there's always the possibility that you've seen cars which
  were adjusted out of spec.  Aside from the S4->, I don't know of the 
  factory changing the ride height measurements.  The early cars without 
  the S spoiler in front give the illusion of sitting higher.
  
  Go buy a 928 and you'll want to start using that air cooled car as
  a snowblower!
