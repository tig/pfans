First, obvious guess is a dead battery. Probably puts out enough
current to light a few lights, etc (a few amps) but not enough to
run the starter (more like a hundred amps). The battery is rather
inconveniently located under the spare tire in back, but there is
a connection point under the hood, front right, little plastic
cover over it, that you can use to connect a jumper cable to.
