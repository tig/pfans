	I experienced the same events regarding the "Tail" warning light
coming on
	regularly. The fix is quick & simple. Just tighten all
electrical connections
	to the tail lights and clean all contacts including the lamp
bases and sockets
	with contact cleaner. No more problem.

On top of this, I also found that if you just bend the center tang
inside the bulb socket outward slightly, it will give you better
contact.
