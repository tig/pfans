When I had a mildew smell from my A/C, I turned on the vent with a low
fan setting and sprayed in Lysol Deodorizer/air freshener which kills
germs, etc.  I worked the system through all the settings, Vent, heater,
defroster, etc.  The smell of mildew was gone, replaced with Lysol,
yuck.  That cleared out within a few days with the windows down and roof
open.  Three years later, still no need to repeat the process.
