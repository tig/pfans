IF you have the factory alarm, and IF it is working, it is
armed every time you lock the door, and disarmed when you 
unlock. I'm not sure what the effect of locking or unlocking
the hatch should be on an '81. To test, roll down a window,
lock from the outside, unlock from the inside, and see
what happens (horns should sound, lights should flash).
Also car should refuse to start with alarm armed.
