> > Why change?  they should be the same except for the spindles.
> 
>   They're not the same.  The design of the upper and lower control
>   arms (as well as the spindles) changed on the S4.  The tie rods
>   may have also been changed.  The lower ball joint on an S4 faces
>   down instead of up as on the S.
> 
>   To change the S to S4 brakes, you'll need to change the entire
>   front suspension (unless you use a kit like what Devek offers).
>   S4 control arms will bolt in to the same mounting points as the
>   S control arms.
>
