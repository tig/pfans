I have over 120K total all weather miles in my previous '86 928S and my
current '91S4.
The S4 with Goodyear Eagle GW(winter) tires on the rear, Pirelli P7000
A\S on the front, and the PSD differential is far superior to the S.
I've been through some messy stuff-with some sweat- and only that low
front spoiler bothers me. I would never attempt winter use without at
least winter rears. Next year it's four snows or an early C4 with the
lockable differential. Note that Christophorus has a yearly winter tire
review
and Porsche's recommendation for chains.
