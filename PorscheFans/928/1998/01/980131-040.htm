To my knowledge, you will at least need to have your piston coated to
reduce
their friction coefficient (?).  The problem with aluminum sleeves is
that they
do not tolerate heat as good as steel.  To minimize this problem,
special pistons,
or specially coated one (I don't remember with what) must be used.

If you are looking for more power, you might be able to put steel
sleeves in
your block and then use regular piston.  I know it can be done with 944.
