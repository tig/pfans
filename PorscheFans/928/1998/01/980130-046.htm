At 03:47 PM 1/30/98 -0500, you wrote:
>Is there any real difference between an '87 and a '89 S4 auto?

Very little. Apart from the trip computer, the only other significant
difference is the final drive ratio (2.20 for '87-88, 2.538 for '89). 

>Besides the digital dash, what else should I be looking for?

Options! Check the build sticker under the mat in the trunk, write down all
the numbers and codes you see there, and proceed to Phil Tong's site
(http://www.928s4vr.com), specifically the "Option Codes" page, to decode
same. Code 220 means limited-slip differential, a very beneficial option.
