As been stated on the list, the VIN number does not distinguish between a GT
and an S4 (for Model Year '90, anyway).
However, according to the '90 Service Information Supplement and the small
Technical Specifications book (1990-1993), the engine serial number does. If
the engine serial number begins with an 85, then it's a GT. An 81 indicates
it's an S4. Again, this is for 1990 on up. The 85 prefix on a GTS indicates a
5-speed, 81 an automatic.
I'm not sure about 1989. Usually, that's more confusing because, of course,
there were 5 speed S4's and GT's in the same year
