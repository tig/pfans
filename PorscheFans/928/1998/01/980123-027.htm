>>Every 3-6K is pretty much within my comfort zone.

For most synthetics, I'd change at about 5000 miles.   Wide-range
synthetics (say 5W50) I'd change at 3000 miles.  Petroleums I
change at 3000 miles (I have an old truck that burns oil faster than
it burns gas).  With Red Line 10W40 and 20W50, I change oil at 
10,000 to 12,000 miles, with filter changes every 3000-4000 miles.
