Hi Mike,

Yes. According to the Service Information Technik (# WKD 496 521) and the
small Technical Specifications Book (# WKD 423 720), for MY 1990, the USA and
Canada engine numbers are: 81L00001-50000 for the S4 and 85L50001-60000 for
the GT.

No, I'm not sure why Phil's website says an 81... is for the GT. Since he has
one, he might have later/better information.

Yes, it does mention the 28.47 as being the engine type code for the 90 and 91
GT.

And, yes. As far as I know (which isn't very far) all 1990 cars came with the
electronically controlled Porsche limited slip differential (PSD).
