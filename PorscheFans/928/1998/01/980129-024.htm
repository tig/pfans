Wow! The tail of the 01/28/98 928 archive is FUBAR.

Tail light warning staying on, in spite of all bulbs working, is
an indication that there is a difference of resistance in the bulbs
that are installed. You _could_ try replacing all bulbs at the same
time with new bulbs. You  _could_ pull out the bulbs and use a DVM
to check and balance them. You _could_ say "phukit" and just drive
it as is (this is what I do).

The turn signal staying on after the car has been parked _is_ a 
feature. It makes your car more visible to the world which zips
closely by.
