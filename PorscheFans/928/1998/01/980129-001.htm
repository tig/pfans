Hey Steve,

That's not that nieve. Actually it's a fairly weak part of the design for
the heater control module. I think there are two or three tiny bulbs on the
back of the control module. One of the bulbs feeds some fiber optic cables
that tend to fall out of there sockets with use. I bought a used
controller, two slider knobs and some bulbs and rebuilt my existing unit. I
think I spent less than $75US. I super glued the fiber optic cables in so
they wouldn't fall out again. While you've got everything apart you should
check all the cables wires and vacuum hoses in the general vicinity for
wear and PLEASE disconnect your battery before going in there. Trust me.
