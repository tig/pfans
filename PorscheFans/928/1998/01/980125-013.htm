It's obviously a more regional colloquialism than I thought!

A hoon is generally a hoody, rough individual but not necessarily a criminal.  Someone
who buys an old V8,  soups it up and does smokey burnouts up the main street on a
Friday night is a hoon.  It's always a term used regarding cars and behaviour on the
road, and type of car driven.
