Dear "Land Shark" tamers:

If you use a high quality oil, additives are not needed or recommended. 
Take for example Mobil 1. Their chemical engineers have told us 
(PCA Potomac Region) that the product is a blend of many substances 
to provide many (some competing) characteristics required of engine oil. 
If the blend is altered by the addition of another substance, the balance 
of the blend may result in slight improvement in one characteristic at 
the expense of one or more attributes. Basically, change your filter and 
oil regularly (about 3k miles), even if it's synthetic.
