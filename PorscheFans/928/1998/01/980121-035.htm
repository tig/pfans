I have not done it myself but I heard that the bolts have a tendency
to strip if you do not have the right tool.  The recommendation was to 
clean the bolts as good as possible (use break or carburator cleaner if
necessary) then buy a high quality socket (I mean snap on or equivalent)
to remove them.
