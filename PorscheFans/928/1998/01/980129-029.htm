Aerodynamics & Weight....

There always seems to be some controversy over literal interpretation of posts
on this list.  I think that when "tarollins" said that weight and aerodynamics
were related, he didn't mean aerodynamics itself was related to weight, but
rather that the effect of aerodynamics was related...which it certainly is.
Weight counteracts the lift force (if any).  Therefore more weight equals more
traction, given equal aerodynamics.  Of course it is also true that
aerodynamics itself is not related to weight, as stated by several individual
posts to this list.
