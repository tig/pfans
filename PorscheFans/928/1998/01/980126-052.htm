I found the problem of my 90 S4 runnig rich.  There was two problems.

1.  There was a vaccum leak affecting the fuel pressure regulators/dampners.
2.  The Air temp sensor was barely outside tolerances...

Both issues have been resolved and the car is running great.  there are four
or five vaccum lines with rubber fittings under the intake.  My car is 7
years old, and I had just recently replaced the PCV hoses and several other
hoses under the intake.  The little rubber elbows, that connect the lines to
varoius connections felt flexible, at the time so I ignored them.  So, the
moral of the story is that if you ever remove the intake, replace every
rubber component located under it, because heat gets trapped between the air
dome and the intake, causing the rubber components to age prematurely.
