To Paul Barick, regarding the horn pad, it's a friction fit, so gentle
pressure on the back side, at 9 & 3, with your fingers will release
those two "fittings" (assuming a 3-spoke design).  The third will
readily release with the additional leverage.  Be aware that the wire to
the horn is still attached as you withdraw the pad.  Simple spade
connection.
