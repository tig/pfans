Dear Rick and all other "Land Shark" tamers:

Your oil leak is probably one of the rear cam seals. Your alternator wine
may be due to it or its belt rubbing against the lower shroud/cover or it
could indicate little or no air is being drawn to cool it. Check the fuse
for the power mirrors. Seatback switch probably needs cleaning. ABS light is
suppose to come on when the brakes are first applied. If the light stays on,
the fuse may be blown or something more serious needs to be checked.

Good luck and happy motoring. Ed
