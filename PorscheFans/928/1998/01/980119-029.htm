My '82 is doing the same thing occasionally. You can determine whether
it's a starter/starter connection problem or a battery by trying to
jumpstart
the car (use the under-hood terminal & cross-brace, not the battery).

If that works, then it's either the battery or the connections between
the
battery and the rest of the electrical system (and grounds).

In my case, it's a connection to the starter....
