I too had the strange acting tail light warning light. Initially it was
due
to the fact that I kept burning out a rear side maker light. After
continuously
replacing bulbs and then replacing all the bulbs at once, I finally
fixed the
continuing rear side marker light burnout.  But this did not fix the
tail light
warning light problem. It would still come on every once in a while,
even
with all lights working!

Mark Anderson of 928 International suggested I clean all the bulb socket

contacts. I did so a year and a half ago, and I have not had a problem
since.
Try it!
