With all this talk about winter driving, it just hit me that I may have
recently set some kind of record for low-temperature use of a 928.  I
had to move out of my place in the country by Jan 15 (I didn't pick
the timing) and the last thing I did was crank up my 928 ('82, all the
options, 162k km).  At -33 C it started fine (it has a block-heater). 
Although it took a while to warm up, it performed really well except
for creakier-than-usual doors and interior panels.
