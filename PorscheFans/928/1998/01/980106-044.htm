I can confirm that they are indeed the same car - the Sport and the SE.  Mine

has the "Sport" logo on the rear.  But I also have the ORIGINAL factory invoice

a lot of confusion when I bought the car and it wasn't until after I'd finally taken
the plunge and bought it that I confirmed what it was - when I cleaned out the
paperwork in the glove compartment all the original documentation was there.
