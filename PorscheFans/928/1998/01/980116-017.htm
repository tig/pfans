You can buy a gizmo called an Accusump, which connects to the 
pressurized part of your engine's oil system via a (manually or
electrically operated) shut-off valve. The idea is that it stores
pressurized oil - probably has an air chamber and a diaphragm
inside - so that you can pressurize your oil system before you
start the engine. Pretty common in racers, with horrifyingly
high compression, but I haven't seen it used in street autos.
