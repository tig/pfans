Mike...

Your battery should read higher than 12 v, at the battery, when the car is
first turned off.  I believe each cell is rated around 2.2 v or so, making a
6-cell battery nominally around 13.2 v.

It sounds like your charging system is OK.

If you buy a new battery, get a DieHard.  They have an exact fit replacement,
they work great, and a very good warranty (and they're half the price of a
Porsche battery, to boot).
