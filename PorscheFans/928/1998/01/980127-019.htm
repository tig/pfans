The starter solenoid is mounted on the starter.  What about voltage
measurement?  Any voltage drop at the starter B+ terminal in comparison
to the battery's positive terminal?  How's the condition of the ground
strap?  Any corrosion?  Don't know what year is your 928, but these are
the first things that I would check.  If automatic, did you check the
shifter lockout switch?  If manual, check relay socket XV (on both of my
928s..) for a tight connection.
