  You might verify that Temp Sensor II has the proper resistance values
  and that the connector to the sensor is making good contact.  L-Jetronic
  cars won't run without a signal from this sensor.  (What year is your
  car?)  My car behaved the same way and I traced part of the problem to a 
  deteriorated connector at this sensor.  (The other part of the problem 
  was an "enhancement" made along that same curcuit by the Porsche dealer 
  for the PO.)  On L-Jetronic models, the sensor is located on top of the 
  thermostat housing.
