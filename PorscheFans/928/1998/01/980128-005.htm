Within 30 minutes of fitting my K&N air filter, I had backed into a
parked car in a hardware store lot.  Not a very good start.  I hope you
have better luck.  One good point, they are reuseable, so there is a
cost, and environmental saving over time.  On the bad side they may
allow small paticles into the engine that a paper filter would trap.  As
far as price goes, I was paying $NZ 145 for paper filters, and the K&N
was about $NZ 180.
