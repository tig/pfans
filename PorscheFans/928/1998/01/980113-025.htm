	How about this approach:

	1) The most common symptoms of a defective master cylinder are?

I've experienced the pedal to the metal effect with no resistance on my
first MC.  On my second MC replacement, there was no clutch action.
Generally the brake pressure light will come on at the end of the
travel.  Not able to stop the car in time would be a dead giveaway.  :-)
Brake fluid lost... this would be visible.  I've seen one MC where the
fluid was leaking into the brake booster; destroying the seals.

	2) The most common symptoms of a defective set of pressure
regulators are?

My limited experience with pressure regulators is on my wife's Volvo.
Rear wheel.  The rear tend to fishtail a little on hard braking.  And
the brake squeal because of pressure loss after brake is applied.
