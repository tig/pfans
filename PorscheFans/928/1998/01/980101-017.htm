>Where did people get this info and where do I find the higher pressure
>brake bias valve?

 Definitely, YES.  This made a HUGE difference in braking distances at 
the track.  What I did was to remove both of the rear bias regulators 
(under the master-cylinder) and bend the lines up to bypass them.  I feel 
that there needs to be even more rear bias.  Probably the best way would 
be to upgrade the rear calipers/rotors.  Note: All my experiments were 
done on S-brakes, with a set of Cool Carbon pads, and a set of 
Porterfield full-race pads.
