Porsche (in publication WKD 495 921, "928 GT '89 Information Technik"
(thanks again, David), sez engine type M 28/47 carries number range 85 K
00001 - 10000, where the last five digits (optimistic!) are the serial
number within the year and type. However, it also points out that engine
serial numbers 1-50 are "reserved for research" and 51-60 "for special
engines", so production '89 GT engine numbers really start with 85K00061.
Similarly, production '89 GT transmission numbers begin with G28552K00501.

That same Swabian company, in publication WKD 423 220, the "Technical
Specifications" booklet for '85-'89 (not '88 as I said yesterday) cars,
repeat that GT engine numbers begin with 85 and S4 numbers with 81.
