Sounds like this guy has never done a 928 water pump before.  I don't think I
have ever seen it done for less than $1k.  This is a huge job but I do agree

before I would look for someone who has.  If they get the tension wrong on the

or more depending on internal damage.

Ask lots of Q's about what this guy knows about 928's and if he knows a lot
