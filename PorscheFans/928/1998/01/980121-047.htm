	> You definitely have to disconnect the battery first.  On my
'83 S,
	> remove pod and lever assembly.  Tilt pod forward to access the
	> instrumentation cluster.  Remove cluster from pod and
disconnect the
	> electrical edge connectors.  I found that the speedo wire was
loose but
	> this did not fully resolve the problem.  Turned out that the
speedo unit
	> uses a 40-pin IC, whereby the pins are tarnished.  They are
	> silver-plated.  Removed IC from socket and clean with an
	> oxidation/contact cleaner and reinstall.  This fixed the
problem.  Since
	> the cluster was off, I also checked the tightness of a few
rivets that
	> are used on the flex PCB.  I found that two of them was loose,
not
	> making a good electrical contact to the flex PCB.  I used a
flat punch
	> and tap lightly on the rivets to tighten.  Again, this is on a
'83 S.
	> Your mileage may vary.
