>could someone tell me were the fuel filter is located (87.928.S4).

Under the gas tank (under the car, behind the right rear wheel), behind a
protective cover, are the fuel pump and filter. Make sure you get the
connections back together in a leakproof way, and don't start up
(electricity, with spark potential, at the fuel pump with ignition on)
until whatever fuel leaked out has evaporated.
