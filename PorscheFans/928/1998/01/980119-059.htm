I also have a few recommendations regarding things I've done.  Someone was
asking about speakers.  I highly recommend Infinity as the sound clarity
and quality are unmatched, when the bass is augmented.  The highs are
unbelievable.  I've got  Infinity 6.5in 3-ways in the rear kick panels and
4in mids in the doors, a 6in Bazooka tube for bass, all powered by a
200watt JBL amp and an Alpine cd player.  The amp is actually too big, so
I've had to sneak it into the passenger footwell behind the driver's seat,
and I've just set the bass tube on the front passenger side footwell with
the speaker forward.  I've found that this actually gives the best sound as
it reflects the bass from the firewall to the back and through the center
to the driver's side.  The only problem is its just sitting there, but my
girlfriend does not think its in the way, as she is the passenger that's
normally there.
	I've also figured out how to wire keyless entry with a capable

anyone is interested, send a message, or maybe I'll just detail it with my
