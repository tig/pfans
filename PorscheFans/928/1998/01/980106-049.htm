Hi Phil,

I think I can add to the GT id confusion, as far as the tail pipes go.
I believe all 1989 S4's came with the single tailpipe. And, as mentioned the
'89 GT came with the duel tailpipes.

For 1990, according to the Service Information Technik, the S4 was honored
with various GT components for "commonality" as they put it.  These features,
if I'm reading this right, include the pistons, external oil cooler, cylinder
head (with machining on the intake side), intake system, and final muffler
with twin tailpipe.

Again, this is from the Porsche Service Information Technik (p/n WKD 496 521).
The booklette "...contains complete information on technical modifications,
which are being introduced on 1990 model 928S4 and 928 GT cars. ...".
