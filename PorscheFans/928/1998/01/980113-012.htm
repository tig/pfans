I had exactly the same issue after some maintenance on mine, James - the
main problem with mine was that the wear & tear on the undertray (those
things do take a beating) had ovalled out some of the holes and stressed
some threaded bits and it just basically couldn't handle being screwed into
place one more time.  The mechanic showed me - held it back in place,
drove the screw home and tugged on it and it seemed secure.  Then he
wiggled it around a bit and the screw fell out.

They drilled and created new mounting points for mine and that sorted it
out.  (Infortunately four weeks later I ran over a sledgehammer and destroyed
it completely!)
