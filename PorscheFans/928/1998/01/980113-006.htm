What year?  My '82 required much less work than that:

 - remove the hood (bungee the hood latch to the garage ceiling....TRUST
ME!)
 - loosen bolts on the 4' x 4" cowling strip that the wiper shafts poke
thru
 - two hard-to-see bolts on top, 1 hard-to see screw underneath the
glovebox
 - pull the rubber sleeve that hooks it to the AC unit back
 - wriggle & swear till it squeezes out

Figure on about an hour.  Got mine from 928 Int'l (they have used and
new
ones to choose from), was something in the $100 range, I think, for a
good
used one.
