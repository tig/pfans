I drive a 91 s4 A/T pretty stock and followed the posts regarding A/T
shifter managment with some interest. Last night out of curiousity I tried
starting with shifter in '2' and folded away a non-stock mustang blower.
Actually it was to impress the 968 behind us. I certainly was impressed. No
kickdown was required to start in 1st.

It seems I can push the shifter forward without the lock to '3' then 'D'
and influence shift-up as well. Also I can pull it back  to '3' without
lock (but not '2') and get a downshift. So I guess this is intentional. It
certainly acts more like a manual when I do this.

I have to admit, normally I just leave it in 'D' and cruise control
everywhere at either 30, 50, 80, or (on the rare sunny day) XXX.
