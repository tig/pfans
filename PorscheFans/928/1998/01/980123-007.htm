	Dead battery? First get a digital voltmeter, they are cheap.  Check the 
voltage at the battery with the ignition off, write down what you get.  
Now start her up and check the voltage again, with all electrical loads 
off, voltage should be about 1.5 more with it running.  Now sart turning 
things on, lights and stuff.  The voltage will drop as the load goes up 
but it should still be more than the battery.  If low voltage from just 
the battery ( should be just above 12) or charging voltage, clean all 
cable connections.  Disconnect the battery ground (wing bolt), clean the 
battey conections and the cable connections at the alternater.  Retest, 
if voltage still low, replace the alternater, it will come with the 
voltage regulater, so that will solve all the problems.  If you are 
charging OK, you might have a bad battery, go to sears.
