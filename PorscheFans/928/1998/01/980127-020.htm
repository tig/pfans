I don't agree that "most of the rest of the world" drives on the left
side of the road.  While it's true that the UK and most of their former
colonies drive on the left, all of Continental Europe, South and North
America, most of Africa and all the Middle East drive on the right side
of the road...except for a few independently-minded drivers..but they
don't last very long. In Asia China drives on the right side of the
road...which can be a problem for the people from Hong Kong when they
drive up to Guang Dong for a weekend.  Also, the people in the
Philippines drive on the right side of the road.
