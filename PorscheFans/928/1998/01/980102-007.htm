AutoThority chips advance the timing and adjust fuel mixture for the motor so
that you get a few more HP out of the car with a very easy change (ok,
installation of the chips is not that easy).  Borla is the exhaust

decrease back pressure on the motor and also slightly increase hp.  Both very
nice although I have only experienced the AutoThority in my car.  Helped tons
over their older version of the chip that was in my car.
