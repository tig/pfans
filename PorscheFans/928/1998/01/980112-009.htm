My '83 928S has 199K on the odo and it is still running strong as ever.
As to the longevity of the engine, it all depends on the maintenance.
Oil change interval, the driving habits of the owner(s) etc.  Major
things to look for are timing belt and water pump, ball joint condition,
(has it been updated to the steel one instead of the aluminum), wheel
bearing, torque tube, 2nd gear synchro if 5-speed.  Just to mention a
few.  I suggest that you take it to a competent Porsche mechanic and get
an expert opinion.
