Mike,

Though I'm not familiar with the S4 per say (my car's an "S"), I'd be 
willing to bet the answer is yes.

The door should definitely stop before hitting the fender.  The end of 
that metal bar (inside the door) has a pin thru it, so that it can 
only open so wide.  Maybe yours is broken off or something.

Check out the procedure I just posted earlier today for removing your 
door panels.  It should be similar for the S4.  You could take the 
panel off and see what's going on in there.  But before doing that, 
make sure the metal bar's hinge pin is connected (obvious, but easy to 
check first)...

You'll probably want to replace the metal bar and the rectangular 
friction piece it slides into to regain proper operation.
