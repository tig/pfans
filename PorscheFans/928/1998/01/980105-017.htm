Brian asked:

What is the best wax, car wash, and leather conditioner for my 928?
I have been told Zymol is the best wax and leather conditioner. Any truth?
What is recommended for the dashboard?

On another note: Has anyone had any luck with RainX?  I read the bottle and
like what it said, if it works.  (snip)

I use RainX and think it is a wonderful product, it both keeps the window
cleaner and extends wiper life.  With regard to the best wax, I spoke with a
couple of guys who repair/restore vintage cars and build rods, and they say
you should never ever use wax.  I have seen their shop and the work they do
(truly anal and as close to perfect as humanly possible) so I believe them.
They recommend either race glaze or hand glaze only.  BTW, they also recommend
that you never use a car cover - dust on the car is rubbed by the cover and
ruins the paint.  I told you they were picky!
