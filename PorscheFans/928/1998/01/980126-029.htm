Yes, the doors should stay open at two (or three, I forget) different 
"lock" positions.  One of my doors did and the other didn't.  The 
problem is that flat chrome piece in the door jam.  Inside the door is 
a rectangular part with plastic or teflon inside that "pinches" on 
this flat bar. There are two or three detents in the bar, and these 
are where the door "holds" open.  The plastic insert gets so worn, it 
just won't hold anymore.

You need to take the inner door panel off to gain access to the inside 
of the door.  Take out two bolts and one pin, and replace both items 
(sliding bar and "pincher").  A pretty easy fix.
