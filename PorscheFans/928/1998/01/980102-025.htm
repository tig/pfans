on it.  Great car.  But last night, I was surprised when it refused
to start.  It turned out that the battery had drained just low

loaded or not, the voltage jumps between the depressed value and
13V for no apparent reason.

I don't have any manuals yet, so I don't know anything about the
928's electrical system.  Is this a probably a short in the
alternator?  A problem with the voltage regulator?
