Cool cars for $10,000 and under to beat up while your 928 stays nice is
a great question.  Let me suggest 3 different Mercedes Benz.  
               1982-1985 300CD 2 door turbo diesel.  Quicker than you'd
think, great gas mileage, minimal maintainance, ultra dependable,
black/black is good color combo. 
     1970-1973 250c 2 door:  a killer one is only worth like $5000, with
Euro headlight conversion, new chrome fog lights, good chrome and paint
they look very stylish!  My current daily car.  
     1966-1973 250/280 S/SE:  Big authoritative tank, again killer ones
are not much more than the much more common trashy monsters.  Eariler
ones with bullet shaped blinkers rule.
     These are cheap cars that (the really nice ones) make you look good
at a fine restaurant.
     I've had three 280ZX Nissans (noticed that post) and that's a
durable machine too, just can't handle the icky icky plastic anymore
when nice Mercedes are available to the determined shopper.
