only are the speakers better than the 944's, but the front separates
were not blown as I had suspected. That Blau was just not up to par on
several levels, and appears to deserve the derision that so many of you
have forwarded. However, in yesterday's digest there was an explanation
that perhaps casts a fairer insight into the state of Blau-ness
currently(but one that I'll choose to avoid for the time being.)
