Another thing to keep in mind if you try Randy's method, is that you 
won't be able to use the loctite gasket goop.  First of all, it dries 
too fast (it takes long enough just to get all of those 30 bolts back 
in!); and secondly, you'd have the stuff all over your hand when you 
tried to re-attach the pick-up tube.  Too messy!

with your hand in there!
