At 10:48 AM 4/10/98 -0400, you wrote:
>isn't there an optimal tire width for a particular width of wheel?

Yes, although the optimum is pretty broad, and the same goes the other way
(wider tires want wider rims).

>If you have a tire that is "too wide" won't you experience excessive sidewall
>"roll" upon cornering?

If the tire is too wide for the rim, all sorts of unpleasant things can
happen. If you want to use really wide tires, expect to buy wheels with
really wide rims (e.g., 10" for 285s, 12" for 335) and find a way to fit
everything under the fenders.
