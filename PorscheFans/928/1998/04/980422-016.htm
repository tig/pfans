Theorically, it will be worse, due to efficency losses in the alternator, 
and the electric fan motor.  However, when your moving along, and you 
have a decent radiator, the fan does not come one, assuming it is 
thermostatically-controlled.  I merely wired mine to the existing 
temperature switch at the lower right side of the radiator.  Works great. 
The other thing is that at high RPMs the thermostatic fan is more of a 
heavy-load than the electric, even with all the electrical 
inefficiencies.
