No, something right. The molds for those taillight lenses were made in
1986, the calendar year the S4 went into production (for model year 1987),
and were not changed thereafter. You're used to cars which get restyled
every year or so, resulting in new optics and trim and making life harder
for parts suppliers and customers.

and get all the option codes (full instructions on Phil Tong's Web site at
http://www.928s4vr.com and look for the "Option Codes" page) to learn lots
more about the car and its equipment.
