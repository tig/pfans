Ahhh, the ole' worn shifter cup syndom (WSCS?). 
Afflicts/will afflict all manual sharks sooner or later. 
See the 928 FAQ for details, I have link to Stephane's site
off my main page.

For quick fix until then, drop exhaust, lower cat heat
shield, and tie large hose clamp(s) around shift rod and
central tube.   

The shift cup piece will run about $45 at Porsche.  The hose
clamps will facilitate install later as new cup is very
tight fit.  I run hose clamps full time as backup measure. 
Marc R. even re-engineered the pivot assembly - I bet he
could sell a bunch if he made them!

Good luck!
