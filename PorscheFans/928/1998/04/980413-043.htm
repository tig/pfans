This is the bearing the size of a quarter, located at the center of the flywheel.   One
end of the pilot shaft is mounted on this bearing.  The pilot bearing should be replaced
at every clutch job.  Cost about $30.  If it is worn sufficiently, the extra play will put
undue wear and tear on your clutch, torque tube and transaxle.  Before it seizes and die,
it'll usually give out a loud squeal noise.  R&R it would take just as long as a clutch
job.
