What he said, whoever it was, is right.  If you flip
open the amp cover on the floor starboard of the
passenger seat, the clicker should be a little
motor that has a plastic electrical plug.  Unplug it
as a test and see if the noise goes away when
you drive.  You can always plug it back in later.
