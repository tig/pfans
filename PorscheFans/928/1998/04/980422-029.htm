I like reading the postings on the page very informative.

Here are some dos and dont's my mechanic has suggested:

1. Early 32Valve engines 85+ can experience big carbon build ups in the top
end of the motor with a lot of short hauls around town or excessive idling,
can be costly to fix.

2. Storing of the 928 over an extended period of time should involve a full
gas tank.

3. The 928 should be warmed up by driving not idling.

4. The dual disk clutch can be changed over to the more reliable S-4 clutch,
remember when faced with a clutch repair.

5. The 84 and before 5 speed transmissions can be outfitted with the stronger
Borg-Warner syncros, keep in mind when opening up the tranny for any work
