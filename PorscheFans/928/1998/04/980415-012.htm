I just finished replacing the hood insulation on my 1982.

I got the material from 928 International.  It comes in a roll, and
I cut it with ordinary scissors.  Took me about 10 minutes of cutting
and fitting till it was right.  Luckily the metal frame of the hood has
about 1/4" of overlap, so you can cut it a little sloppy and tuck it in,
and it doesn't show.

Used 3M adhesive spray to glue it on.  Piece of cake, looks great now!
