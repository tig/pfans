Since I've been toying around with the same pump, I can tell you it is
much easier to get at from below the car.   Raise the right side (and
put on a jack stand!) until you can creep under the car.   Looking up
along the inner fender panel, it will be held onto a bracket with two
small screws.   It is a 'rubber arm' type job, but I would avoid
removing the coolant tank as it will cause a bit of stress to the tank
and all the fittings.
