From all info that I have seen for US cars, here is the simple formula.

4.5L = 928
4.7L = 928S

If you have the "S" spoilers on your 81, it most likely means some PO
installed them on the car. .  Notice I say "most likely", with Porsche you
never know, it is possible that some of the 81s had them factory installed,
however from most of the info it seems this was only an option in the late
82s.

In either case it would still be a 928 with the "Competition Option" and not
a 928S.
