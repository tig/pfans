John, 
Thanks for the help.  I have learned a trick to find vacuum leaks that
works pretty good.  Used it on my 81 euro.  If you suspect a leak, crank
the engine and let idle, then open the valve on a propane torch, (don't
light it!) and hold the torch nozzle close to suspected leak points.  If a
leak is encountered, the engine will smooth out because of the propane
going into the leak.  However, I haven't found any leaks on the 78 with
this.
