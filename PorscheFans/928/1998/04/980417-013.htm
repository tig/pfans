CAUTION: To anyone attempting this "shortcut":

Do not attempt this with a 32-valve engine, you will do extensive damage to
the cam covers from contact with the coils, damage A/C lines behind your
airbox,  possibly damage power steering hoses, wiring harnesses etc. You
will also put a nice arc in your upper crossbrace or crack your intake.


>Sounds easy, right?!?  It took me a full Saturday and half a Sunday to do
>this!

Most mechanics take about 5-7 hours labor for this procedure doing it the
non-shortcut way.
