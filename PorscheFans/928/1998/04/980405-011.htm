Glenn- I had a similar problem on my 91S4.  All of a sudden one bank
(one side) of the engine whould shut off, really rough idle, and I could
barely get it going above 25-30mph. I would disconnect the battery, and
it would start and run fine.  The po had it looked at a few times, and
had several ignition components changed (not cheap). When it started
happening to me, I originally thought water may be getting into the fuse
panel because it seemed to happen after it rained or after washing the
car. I found some water near the fuse panel , and had all gaskets and
drainage tubes replaced on sunroof. Inside was now bone dry but it
happened again.  After snooping around the engine, I found a split in
the ignition wire leading to the coil on the passenger side.  I guess 
this wire caused a miss, and there is a relay that can shut down either
side of the engine.  I had my technician replace the wire, and the relay
(it was a clear cube, that had a red and green light inside it) just in
case it was the relay itself.  No more problems, that was almost two
years ago.  Hope this helps, I know I got lucky.
