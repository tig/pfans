Today (two days ago, actually) I found a vaccuum leak, in a weird spot, and I have to fabricate a spacer to cure it!  The aircleaner on a 16v bolts to a device with a flap in it, I will hereby refer to this device as the air-sensor.  This air-sensor pushes into a elbow pipe, and seals with an o-ring (a big one!) The only thing that holds this air-sensor aligned and in place is the two rubber topped intake manifold bolts that the aircleaner is bolted down with.  My air-sensor only engages this elbow by 1/4" or so... enough to barely squish the o-ring.  I feel that this leak would be lessened by getting the air-sensor to more fully engage this elbow. It could go in another 1/2" almost! I took off the aircleaner and ran the car with the air-sensor crammed home in the elbow, and the idle smoothed up and the revs _seemed_ happier.  I have to fabricate a aluminum spacer to go between the aircleaner, and the air-sensor, to push the device into the elbow better.... Doing that in the next couple of days, I'll keep the list posted on how it goes!!
Maybe this will cure my stumble!
