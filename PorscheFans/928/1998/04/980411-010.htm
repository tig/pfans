Several years ago a reader posed a question to one of the Hot Rod
Magazine advice columns concerning axle breakage on his stock-class drag
car. The answer involved getting new axles and scribing a line from end
to end. Then you periodically check for twist, so you can replace them
before they break. The allowable twist on a stock american car axle
shaft was 180 degrees! No idea how much dynamic (temporary) wind up
there would be.

An axle shaft is somewhat shorter than a 928 driveshaft, so I assume
that wind-up will be greater on the 928 shaft.
