Euro S Fans,

	In the U.S. in '82' you could only get a 4.5ltr motor. In Europe you could
get a 4.7ltr motor. It had 300hp, due to cams, valves, compression, and
exhaust.You can tell if it is this motor by checking the engine number,
28/10 or 28/11. Another quick check is to look for CIS injection or
K-Jetronic( they are the same thing). This injection system only came on
the EuroS in '82'.
