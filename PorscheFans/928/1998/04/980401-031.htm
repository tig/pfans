The engine type for the 288 bhp US-spec '85-86 928 S engine for stick cars
is M 28.45 (auto M 28.46). The type for the 316 bhp engine in stick 928 S4
cars worldwide is M 28.41 (auto M 28.42). Look for the engine code in two
places:

- the build sticker (in the trunk, under the mat, on the right); a US '87
S4 stick should have a large-type line saying "M2841-81H G2813-81H" (the
"G" part describes the transmission); while you're there, write down all
the small-type code groups and go to Phil Tong's Web site
(http://www.928s4vr.com), then to the "Option Codes" page, and find out
what else you car has)

- the engine is stamped on a horizontal flat surface at the top front,
behind and a bit to the right of the dipstick; you'll need bright light to
read the stamp, which should begin "M28/41"
