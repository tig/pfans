     Bob,
     Sounds like your intake thermal switch is bad. It is located on top of
the
     intake just in front of the airbox.
     You can't miss it, it is the only thing on the top of your intake, it
has 2
     wires plugged into it and is a snap to replace.

This was the first thing that went wrong with my '87 928. 
Quick test: Turn the ignition key to ACC, then off again. 
Fan stays on for 45 minutes? Pull one of the leads off of the
(brass colored) thermal switch Dave mentions.  Fan stays off? 
Leave the wire off (saves your battery) and order part # 928.606.217.00
Should be < $30 US.
That switch just screws into the top of the manifold, you don't 
use any sealer or gaskets.
