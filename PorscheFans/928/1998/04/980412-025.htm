Lou,
Light bulbs of the same wattage must be installed on both sides. If not, the
indicator will be activated even when both bulbs are functioning. Could it be
the replacement bulb is a different wattage? You might try replacing the other
side with a new bulb anyway, as the resistance of a new same wattage bulb will
still be a little less than an old one. Hope this helps. Let me know what you
find out.
