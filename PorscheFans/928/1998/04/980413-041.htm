Gene,
Here is the fairly simple way to select between MIME and UUENCODE in
Outlook:
(requires The "Internet Enhancement Patch " which is a free download from
Microsoft)

On the main menu in Outlook select "Tools" then "Options"
Select the tab in the upper right hand corner of the options applet..
At this time you will see the choices of MIME or UUENCODE, chose your weapon
and "OK" your way back out.

Here is the same procedure for Outlook Express:

On the main menu in Outlook Express select "Tools" then "Options"
Select the "Send" tab, then under "Mail Sending Format" select "Plain Text"
and click the "Settings" tab.
At this time you will see the choices of MIME or UUENCODE, chose your weapon
and "OK" your way back out.
