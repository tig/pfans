Alex,

Off the top of my head, the fuel tank has 20 gallons
plus a 2-3 gallon reserve.

I can't speak for an automatic, but my 5-sp gets great
mileage - on rare occasions.  When I behave myself, I
can get an astonishing 26mpg at a constant 65-70 mph.
This has happened twice: the first time bringing the
car home last fall - I had the plates from my 944 on
the car and didn't really want to get stopped - and the
second time last weekend when my wife and I and our
3 year old were in no particular hurry to get where we
were going.  Most of the time I get about 15-16 mpg,
and have a heck of a lot more fun!
