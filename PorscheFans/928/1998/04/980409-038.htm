I had the same problem on my 91 GT.  I'd lose fire on four cylinders after
the car was running for awhile.  I could restart the car normally but the
problem would reoccur sporadically. 

The problem turned out to be the ignition relay located on the lower
right-hand side of the passenger footwell.  A new relay costs about $125 but
928 International has used ones for $60.  FWIW, It's a lot easier to replace
the relay than swap the coils.

Good luck,
