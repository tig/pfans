The coil takes 12 volts at high current (that external resistor limits 
current) on the primary side, and converts it to 20,000 volts on the 
secondary side (low current, but it will wake you up). It does this by 
having a proportionately larger number of loops (of thinner wire) on the 
secondary (high voltage) side, resulting in a much larger DC resistance.
