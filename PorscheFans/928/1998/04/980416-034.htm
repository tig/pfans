there is no reason you should need to retrofit your air conditioning
system.  The old refrigerant is still available although it is
expensive.
As long as it works and its not laking, I would not change a thing.  
If you needed to change your compressor anyway, then I would look at 
the cost of switching over.

Cruise control problems are normally created by a vacuum leak or a
prblem with rear brake lights (the cruise control is linked to the
brake light system so it de-activate when you step on the brake.  The
system automatically cancel itself if you have a bad brake light because
the electrical system is now open...)
