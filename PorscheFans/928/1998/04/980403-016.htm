>Anyone know the dial reading for the timing belt tension(1980).?. 

BTW, for those that are wondering, according to the shop manuals the
correct tension for the 16-valve engine is 4.5.

The spec for 32-valve is 5.0 +/- 0.3.

When we adjusted the tension on my '91 we discovered that the hack who
installed the t-belt in November had probably NOT tensioned it. It was
reading less than 3 on the tool.  We tightened the belt to 5.3 as we
expected the belt to stretch a little more since it was never
tensioned.  I'll be checking the tension again in a few hundred miles.
