I use Sears' heavy duty ramps but place the beginning of the ramp on 2 - 5/4
thick pieces of wood which are fastened together.  The wood is about 6 wide
inches by 24 inches long with a 30 degree bevel on one end.  This reduces
the angle of the incline and gets the front end up about 2 1/2 inches before
hitting the ramps.  This way I avoid scraping the front end of the car on
the ramp.  Works very well.
