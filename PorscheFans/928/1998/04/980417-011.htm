I'm just beginning to diagnose my own system.   You didn't mention if
you have the factory service manuals - because there is a section on the
Tempostat (cruise).   Not to duplicate the whole thing in here, but as I
recall, the steps are:
1.  Check fuse
2.  Inspect hoses for vacuum leaks, missed connections
3.  Perform vacuum test (test nipple on small hoses just under brake
booster)
4.  Check clutch switch operation (on pedal against firewall)
5.  Perform control/wiring function tests - too detailed to scribe in
here.
