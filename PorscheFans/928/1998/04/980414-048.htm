According to the Little Spec Book, if the car's VIN ends with
CS80nnnn or CS82nnnn, it was a plain 928. If the VIN ends with
CS84nnnn, it was a 928 S.

If it fits neither, it wasn't an '81 Porsche 928 or 928 S. And
there's no telling what it is now.
