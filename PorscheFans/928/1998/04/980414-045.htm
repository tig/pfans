Dave,
That is correct for the Euro cars, the Euro 928S was optionally available
starting in 1980, the US didn't get them until 83, (maybe late-82, only
Porsche knows for sure) .
Hence the comment I made:
> From all info that I have seen for US cars, here is the simple formula.
> 4.5L = 928
> 4.7L = 928S

Which stands true in either Euro or US versions.

Bottom line is, No 4.7 = No "S",

Of course it is possible to have a US 77-82  928 that a PO had dropped in a
4.7L  "S" engine.

In that case it would be a 928 little "s" or "928s"
