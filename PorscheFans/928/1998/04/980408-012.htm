So you want to access the bottom of the washed fluid reservoir?

You need to remove the fender linner.  Its held in place with about
8 nuts.  To make it easier, jack the car up  and remove the wheel.
When all the bolts are out, you just need to pop the ting out.
i find that getting the "lip" from around the wheel well first
makes removing the liner easier.
