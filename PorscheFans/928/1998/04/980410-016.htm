The cover is press fit and it is very hard to remove.  There is a 
special tool to do so but I do the following (kinda need a friend).

To minimize damage, I put some tape on the cap.
I hold the cap with a big pair of "hook" plier, pulling pretty hard.
Your friend then take a hammer and tap on the plier (giving a pulling
impact action)
Tap a few time, then rotate the wheel 180 degree and tap on the
other side.
Then move your pliers 90 degree and repeat.

This can take a while so be patient.

The large machined notch in the hub is there to enable you to screew
in or out the allen key that locks the nut in place.

Good luck,
