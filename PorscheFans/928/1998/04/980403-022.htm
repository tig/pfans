	Well, the gremlins have struck !  I have an intermittant driver side, power
	window.  Sometimes she works and other times NOT !  I have checked 
	the window switch and it checks out OK.

	If you checked the switch for continuity and its ok, that is not necessarily mean
that the switch is good.  The motor draw quite a bit of amperage and the early switches
are known to burn and pit rather quickly.  The amperage draw of the motor increases if you
have drag on the window pane.  What I would do first is to properly adjust the window
assembly for smooth operation, and then replace the switch with a new one.  They run about
$40.  I posted a R/R for the door panel awhile back.  I think Jim Stadter has a copy.
(Jim, are you out there?).
