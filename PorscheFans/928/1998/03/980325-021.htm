Jake,

  No 928's were delivered with the 215/60-15 and 225/50-16 configuration.
  The standard for your car was 225/50-16 at all four corners.  Some
  early 928's were delivered with 15" non-articulated phone dial rims,
  but these specified the same size wheel and tire at all four corners.
  
  I can't help but wonder if somehow your car ended up with a 911 tire
  pressure sticker on the fuel filler door.  You might take a look at
  the part number of the sticker.
