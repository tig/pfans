Jim,
Having owned one of the 91 300ZXs (bought it new put 117,000 miles on it
before I finally got rid of it), I will cast my vote that it is not even in
the same class.
The 300 was a fun car, quick, handled well, looked good etc.  However at the
end of the day it was still a mass produced Japanese car, lots of plastic,
an armrest/center console that would actually chafe the skin off of your
elbow, caused backaches on trips longer than 100 miles,  and believe it or
not they had to replace my front rotors at every oil change (3500) due to
rotors warping. Those are just some of the immediate things that pop into my
head.
