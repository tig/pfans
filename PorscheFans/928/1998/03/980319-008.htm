Hi Tony,
    With the selector in '2', my S4 would stay in 1st until around 5,000 rpm.  I
suggest you bury your boot, and see if it shifts once near the red line.  Perhaps be
ready to shift the selector if the trans doesn't shift.
    Another trick, was to start with the selector in '2', giving 1st.  Then move the
selector to '3', for a about half a second, then straight back to '2'.  This will give
2nd gear, a very versatile state of affairs.
    Good luck,
        Andy McIntyre
