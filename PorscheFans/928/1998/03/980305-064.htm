Anyone know what kind of plastic it is?

If it's nylon, you might try "Marine-Tex", sold at Boat repair centers.  I
used it to relocate a manifold on an intercooler with nylon end tanks and
it has held up very well.  It's a two-part deal that cures about as hard as
metal!

I've got some pictures of it up on the web of when I was using it, if
interested:
http://weber.u.washington.edu/~jaker/gtd/icmodify.htm

Marine-tex is probably be your next-best alternative to replacing it.
