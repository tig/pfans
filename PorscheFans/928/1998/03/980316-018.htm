  I've given up on letting anyone except me or my mechanic remove or
  install the wheels on any of my cars!  I've been burned one too many
  times by a grease monkey with an over zealous air wrench.  Whenever I
  need tire work, the car goes up on jackstands in my garage and the
  wheels get carted to the shop in the back of my truck.  The extra effort
  more than makes up for the time that would have to be spent repairing an 
  over-tightened or cross-threaded lug.
