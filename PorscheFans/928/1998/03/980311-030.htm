I probably went over board but first I flushed the car with BG radiator flush
then I pulled the radiator out of the car and brought it to a local radiator
shop that cleaned the cooling fins (lower third of whcih was pretty dirty) ,
flushed (again) and pressure tested the radiator (total cost $10!!!  the
beauty of living near Oildale, Ca).  I used Redline Water Wetter, antifreeze
(Porsche spec), and distilled water.   And as I mentioned earlier the temp
gauge now reads right at the lower white line. Prior to that it was reading
mid to upper white bar.
