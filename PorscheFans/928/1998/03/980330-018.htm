I have done this... and one of the most surefire ways, although a pain in the gills, is to stop at every port of entry in the state you are entering, and buy a 48hr travel voucher and affix to your rear glass. (usually $3-$5)  This has worked for me numerous times, and most cops, upon seeing that you are complying in SOME way, will ignore you.... Unless you are performing some super-shark maneuver....   :)
