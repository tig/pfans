One metric (e.g., DIN) horsepower is 735.5 W = 0.7355 kW, so 1 kW = 1.36
DIN HP, and the power numbers come out 462 and 408 DIN bhp (or, since one
good old Amurrican SAE-type horsepower = 746 W, 456 and 402 SAE bhp).
