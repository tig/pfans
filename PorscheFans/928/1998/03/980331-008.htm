Dan,
It sounds like you might have some slight front end wear & or the
toe setting off. Under heavy braking you may be getting some
" toe out " on the front end, this will cause the feeling your getting.
I have my 85 set up for auto-x, with some toe out dialed into my
front settings and I get the same thing braking hard. It's worse
on uneven pavement. It's been described as " hunts like a truffle pig "
by some writers. You get used to it.
