I painted mine bright red las summer, and they still look great. First, I
sanded lightly to remove grime and paint, then wiped them down. I masked
off the area, and painted them, with high heat paint in bright red. The
actual paint I used was bought at UAP/NAPA, and is actually Ford Red engine
block paint. After the paint dried, I sanded the PORSCHE script, so it
looks brushed silver, and the caliper is bright red. Someone recently asked
me if I had installe 993 turbo brakes on my car!
