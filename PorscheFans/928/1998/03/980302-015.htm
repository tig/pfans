> I assume that your '83 currently has speakers behind the door panels with
>  the little holes in it.  Unfortunately, if you want to maintain the stock
>  look of having bottom-mounted (behind the panel) speakers, your choices
>  aren't too great.  I recently replaced my stock speakers and didn't want to
>  cut the panels.  The only thing that will fit behind the panels without
>  having to cut any metal out of the door panels, are 3 1/2" speakers with no
>  tweeter protrusion and only 1 5/8" mounting depth.  I installed a pair of 3
>  1/2" Blaupunkts from Crutchfield which were a tight fit, but I got 'em in
>  there.  They sound pretty good and are rated pretty high for 3 1/2" but
>  they can't pump out much bass.  Your car probably has the stock amp as well
>  which is located under the compartment between the passenger seat and the
>  passenger door (where the hatch release knob is).  The amp may have been
>  useful with the stock equipment, but with the more powerful decks available
>  these days, it may not provide much benefit.  I installed a new deck and
>  just by-passed the amp.  I left the amp in place, however, in case I decide
>  to use it with a subwoofer.
