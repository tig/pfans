You're half right, Patrick. 928 cars through 1980 have the filter where you
say, but all later car's including your '84 and Adam's '87, have the filter
next to the fuel pump in a recess (behind a cover) under the fuel tank
(shop manual, page 20-2).

Adam also wanted to know how to check for a clogged filter. Apart from
replacing the filter, the only positive test is to disconnect the fuel
return line at the engine and measure the flow (shop manual, page 20-5 ff).

To track down venting issues, check the fuel system map (shop manual, page
20-17).
