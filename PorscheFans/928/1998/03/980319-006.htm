George,

After checking prices for replacement Porsche and Interstate batteries, I
got up off of the floor and went to my local Sam's Club.  They have a
Champion battery for around $57 that matches OEM (actually has greater
CCA).  It is white with the lock-down tabs at the bottom and fits the
battery compartment perfectly.  I've had it for about 6 months so can't
comment on its longevity but can say that it is a very strong battery.  My
old battery could not start the car if I left it for 2 weeks or more (due
to current drain).  The new one recently fired it up easily after the car
had been sitting for over 6 weeks.  I was impressed.
