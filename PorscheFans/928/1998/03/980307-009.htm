Hi Mike, My 85S used to make a lot of noise also. 
Open the hood and look at rear of engine compartment, you will find
a cheap plastic cover over the wiper linkage. It snaps & screws
in place. On my car the cover had been pushed down & was being hit
 by the wiper linkage.
