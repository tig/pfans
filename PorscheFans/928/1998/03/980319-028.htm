Yes you can just take the cats off, but you have to buy a test-pipe to
replace it with.  I have done this to my 928, and am very satisfied with
the results.  My pipe came from a place called EuroProducts and was made
by Bursch.  It is a very simple bolt on, bolt off process, and the pipe
even has the drilled holes for the oxygen sensors.  Price is less than
$200 if I remember correctly.  Europroducts number is: 1-800-962-0911
  

When I replaced my Cat's I just welded 2 1/2" pipe in place of the Cat's
and then welded a balance tube in and put the O2 sensor in the balance
tube. Works great and costs about $20 in parts + labor.
