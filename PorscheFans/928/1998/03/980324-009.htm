Right, the manual calls for a GL-5.  Note that you don't want anything
TOO 
slippery (the syncros won't be able to engage), so don't blindly put the
most
slippery synthetic you can find in there.  It would be different if the
diff did
NOT share its lube with the gearbox.

Red Line oil for the gearbox is the 75W90NS (not the plain 75W90).
