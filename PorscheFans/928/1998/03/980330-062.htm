 928 OWNERS:
There is nothing magic about blower motors jumping to high speed just a
electrical problem that can and will be solved. First lets cover several
scenarios. (1) if your fan jumps to high speed on its own and after 2 - 3
minutes returns to previous fan speed. you do not have aproblem. (2) If your
fan jumps to high speed and stays there pull relay xx11 out of fuse panel
(defrost relay) and  test drive again. if problem is gone then relay is bad
(unlikely) or terminal # 86 on relay is getting a 12 volt signal from heater
a/c controls on console. hook test ligt to this terminal # 86 on fuse panel
(bring test light up to pass. seat area so you can watch without crashing) Do
not jam test light in fuse panel use a male spade connector the same size as
relay terminal.
