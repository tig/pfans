Its Spring, and a 928 owner's thoughts turn to... car wax. With all the
wax postings, no one has mentioned the "Carnauba Wax" that Griot's
Garage sells. I've found it to be the deepest looking, longest lasting,
of any wax I'd previously used (on a 944). And $20 for 8 ounces! Griot's

workable (30% as I recall). Its not a cleaning wax, just wax. Has anyone
compared this to Zymol, which appears to be the early leader heading
into the clubhouse turn, by my informal count?
