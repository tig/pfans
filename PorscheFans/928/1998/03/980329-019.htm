I set out to add the factory lumbar supports to a non lumbar seat.  First of
all, if you are thinking about doing the same I have a few suggestions.

1.  Find a fully functional seat, and just swap over the leather.
2.  Set aside an entire day if you want to swap the parts.

I'm not going to get into the how to's on this subject until a later date.
The seat frames are identical, and the electric seats are prewired for all
optional accessories.  Basically you move over the mechanical portion of the
suppport along with the motors\switches & plug them in.  The most difficilt
portion was to figure out how to get the seat apart and seperate the top
portion from the bottom.  This is a must to remove the leather from the top
portion.  There are a ton of hog rings!  If you are curious as to the layout
of the parts, goto the local dealership and view ti on the parts
fische...... I suggest find a working seat and just swap over the
leather.....  Now I'm ready to take on a road trip!
