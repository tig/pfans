Jon, I took mine off my '81 last weekend, just by
coincidence.  There are two screws near the
bottom and one way up at the top.  Other than
that, it is head on by a little tar and some dead
grasshoppers.   I found that my aerial works ok,
but the the shaft is cracked so I have to locate
that part only.  One point of safety:  if you wash
it out under there, let it dry before turning the
key.  There is an exposed relay that made smoke
when I tested the aerial by turning on the radio.  I
didn't let it dry.  I dried it out and sprayed it with
plastic spray for distributors.

I also improved my cracked aerial operation and
made it easier for the motor by sanding it with
600 grit wet with WD40.
