As David Roberts mentioned the easiest way to the keep factory speaker look
and upgrade one's sound system, is to have the speaker cones replaced while
retaining the factory speaker basket & grill.  I have done this may times, and
much prefer it to trying to force fit new speakers into odd sized openings.
