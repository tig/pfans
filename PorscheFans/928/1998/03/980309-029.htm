	The real problem with aluminum lug nuts is making them to tight.  If 
they are to tight, they will break when you try to take them off, leaving 
a little ring that has to be cut out.  After having you 928 serviced 
check the lug nuts, even if you pick up your car in a suit.  Put a wrench 
on those nuts and then retorque them, if they used a air wrench to put 
them on and you get a flat on the way home, well good luck.

	When I was restoring the 914 it took a 3/4 inch drive air wrench, to get 
the wheel bolts off!

	The anti sieze goes on the threads, any brand will do!
