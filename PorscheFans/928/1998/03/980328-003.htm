Hi Gary,

We are refering to Loctite compound 518 which is an anerobic sealant. 
It cures only under pressure and the abscence of air.  It is used
commonly to seal the crankshaft halves on the air cooled thingys.  I
used a thread locker (Loctite 242) on one bolt that holds the timing
belt tensioner to the block, but it didn't hold because of the constant
flow of oil in the gally that the bolt is threaded into.  I redid the
job with 518, which is the current version of Loctite 574 (the compound
to use as stated by the manuals), and it stopped the leak.
