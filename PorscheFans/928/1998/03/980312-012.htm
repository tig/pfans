Yes. Autothority (http://www.autothority.com/), the leader in aftermarket
chip sets for Porsche cars, have a nice set for S4s. which they sell, at
least in the US on a money-back guarantee. Most users of these chips (you
need two, one for fuel and one for ignition) report significant
improvement, although a custom reprogramming (which needs the right
facility and know-how) can do better.
