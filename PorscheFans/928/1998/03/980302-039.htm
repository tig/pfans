At 12:42 PM 3/2/98 -0600, you wrote:
>I've been looking at tires but need some advice. There is a big difference
>in prices for tires and I'm wondering if the less expensive tires are OK.
>The Dunlop D40 M2 is priced right but I want to maintain a nice ride.

Ride is certainly not the D40 M2's strong point. I'd suggest you look at
the Bridgestone RE71, which was fitted as OEM by Porsche and is available
in 928 sizes in Porsche N1 spec. These are probably the best bargain in
performance tires for 928s right now, particularly when obtained from a
reputable discounter such as Tire Rack (http://www.tirerack.com/). For a
little more money, the Continental CZ 91 is another 928 OEM tire with
excellent performance and a smooth, quiet ride.
