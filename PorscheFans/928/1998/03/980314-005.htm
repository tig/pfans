Dear Warren and the rest of the "Land Shark" tamers:

That funny sound you hear (assuming the radio is off) is the thermostat fan
which is always on except when the A/C-Htr system is completely shut off
(the "O" on the console). If the sound is getting quite noticible, it is
probably stuck from dust accumulation or the bearings are shot. Try cleaning
it out with a long thin cotton swab. Apply End-Dust to the swab and see what
comes out. The fan has many small vanes each one requiring cleaning.

Good luck and happy motoring. Ed
