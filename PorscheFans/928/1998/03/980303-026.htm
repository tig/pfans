During the (certainly subjective; must add up to many hours over the years)
time I've spent behind such mobile roadblocks, I've speculated on the best
tool for this job, and realized that bullets are not enough, because they
would immobilize the target barge, worsening the very situation that caused
you to pull the trigger in the first place.

My conclusion is that a bazooka (or, given the advances in military
technology, examples of which I'm sure Adrian Canton can arrange to place
at our disposal, a small wire-guided missile) is what's called for here.
You need to blow the offending vehicle apart, quickly and forcefully enough
to blow the debris out of your path before you accelerate through the
recently cleared area.
