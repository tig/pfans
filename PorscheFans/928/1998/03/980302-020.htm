	On my '83 S, I removed the voltmeter from the instrumentation cluster.  Hook the
voltmeter directly to the cigarette lighter along with a digital voltmeter.  Adjust the
trimmer resistor until it matches the digital voltmeter.  I suggest that you clean the
trimmer resistor first.  I sprayed the trimmer with some contact cleaner and dry before I
make the adjustment.
