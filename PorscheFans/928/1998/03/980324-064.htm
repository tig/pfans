Has anyone thought of using Loctite 518 on the gasket and bolts?  The
old compound on the pan (assuming that the job may need redoing in
several years) is dissolved by the fresh stuff, so it doesn't need to be
completely removed.  The manuals recommend 574, and 518 is the new
version.  Automotion has it for $30, but most auto stores should carry
it for about $13.
