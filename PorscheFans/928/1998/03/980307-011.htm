In the recent past I see that people have asked about the
Eibach/Automotion springs.  

I installed the Eibach/Automotion spring set (front and rear) over
externally adjustable Koni shocks.  I also have the stiff Devek front
bar.  This seems to be a pretty hot setup.  The handling and feel is
vastly improved and is not too stiff or jiggly for the street.  I have
the shocks set full soft for street, I plan on cranking them down for
autocrossing and time trialing.

This setup should have been a Factory Sport Option from the start.
