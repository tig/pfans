Dave,
I am told after 86 Porsche stopped using the optional leather on the dash,
middle door armrest sections and main console areas (for obvious reasons)
unless it was special ordered that way. I have only seen one post 86 with
leather in these areas and that was on the last GTS sold at Jim Ellis (bet
it still has my drool marks on it ;-)

If your dash has stitches around the corners where it meets with the door
panels it is leather, likewise for your door armrest sections that go in the
middle of the door from the dash to rear of door.
Same story with the main console.
