Dear fellow "Land Shark" tamers:
    Here in the Potomac Region (Zone 2) fire extiguishers are not required
in stock cars. It is considered more important to get out of a burning car
than staying in it and trying to put out the fire. We have corner workers
with 50# extiguishers that are ready if there is a need.
    If your car is no longer stock, you will need a roll bar or cage. a fire
bottle can easily mounted to it within easy access for the driver.

Happy and safe motoring. Ed
