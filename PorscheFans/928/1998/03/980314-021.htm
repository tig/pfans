Curtis,

You are correct. The little wire (I used to think it was an additional
ground wire, hence very bad reception for years) is the Positive 12v feed
for the amplifier built into the base of the 89 and up rooftop antenna.

Make sure you attach it to the switched 12v + from your stereo (same one
that you would use for power ant, or external amp switch) because if you
just hook it to a 12v + that is not switched it will run down your battery
in a few weeks of non-use of your 928. (been there, done that.....thank
goodness for that Porsche battery 2-year replacement warranty).
