Here's the deal. I had a few headlight problems,
such as no high beams, lights sticking on, etc. I
replaced the relay, and voila! Problems fixed.
Anyhow, a week or two later, the lights stopped
working again. Assuming it was an unrelated problem
frying the relay, I purchased another one, and set
out to diagnose the system. Lo and behold, the
headlight bucket plastic cover was stuck, and when I
popped it loose, the lights popped on, all systems
working properly. So I have a new high beam/low beam
relay which cost $92.51 (USD) at the dealership for
sale. I have no use for it, and I'd rather someone
use it than to let it sit in my tool box. A reminder
in the lesson of looking for the simple things
first. Oh well...
