FWIW, I ran across an ad in "european car" back in December by a radiator
shop which perports to specialize in MBZ and Porsche radiator repair and
fabrication of "custom" radiators. I e-mailed the company to inquire of the
cost to "rebuild" a 928 radiator. I have yet to inquire about the processes
utilized to affect such a rebuild, but did receive the following reply:

    " we will rebuild your radiator for 150 $$ make like new 1 year
warranty
      call me at 800 306 9249 or just send to me and include info on you"  


                                    JIM

 Their e-mail address is: jgoldstein@mail.webtek.com

May be worth investigating. Beyond that, used units are available in the
$2-300 range. DEVEK sells their own custom, all metal, hi-capacity unit for
less than an OEM replacement. 

I'm told, that pressure testing can create havoc with some used units and
may not be adviseable. Be sure to get a replacement "waranty" with any such
item.
