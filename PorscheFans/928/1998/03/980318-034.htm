Jerry,

The resistor block controls the fan speeds.  If one or more of the 
speeds don't work, then those resistors are burned out.  If all of your 
speeds work, it's ok.

Noise is usually due to bad blower motor bearings, or could just be some 
leaves or debris caught in there somewhere.
