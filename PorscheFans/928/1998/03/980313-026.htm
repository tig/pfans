You have a drain somewhere.  A new battery should not be dead in two week.  Both my 928s
sits for three weeks with no problem starting.  Put a test lamp in series between the
positive cable and battery post.  If there is a drain, the lamp will glow.  Start pulling
fuses one at a time.  When the lamp is not lit, that is the circuit where the drain is at.
If all fuses are out and the lamp is still on, start checking the non-fused item, like the
alternator, starter etc.
