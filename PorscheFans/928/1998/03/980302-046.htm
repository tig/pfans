josh wrote:

> Is there a downside other than having to clean it a little more?

    The K & N air filter may let in some fine dust particles that a stock
filter will catch.  As the filter gets dirty, more of these particles will be
trapped.   I've no idea how much, if any extra engine war this will cause.
Some of the particles may end up in the engine oil, so changing that may
become more of a priority.    On the other hand, at least you don't have to
replace the K & N, and in NZ the price is less than double that of the stock
item.
