     Hey Derron!!!
     
     I wouldn't worry to much about the stops, unless the appear to be 
     crushed or the rubber O-ring is completely worn out, but I would 
     definitely replace the boots.  You don't want to put that nice new 
     rack into some old boots do you?  I would also look your ball joint

     over carefully.  You can put new ball joints and new boots on for
less 
     that $150.  At the least repack grease in the ball joints.  When I
did 
     mine, I replaced one ball joint/tie rode, mainly because I mangled
it 
     trying to get it off of the old rack.  Make sure that you have a 
     really good 22mm wrench and a friend to help you get those old ball

     joint/tie rodes off.  One was easy and the other was a nuckle
hater.
     
     Good Luck and have fun!!!
     
     Matt Butler
     1986 928S
