When I first got my shark, I was surprised to discover the hard way (I
ran out of gas and had to phone AAA for a sip) that filling an empty
tank with a closed vent nozzle (the kind that form a seal over the
filler neck to avoid releasing those nasty hydrocarbons into outr
atmosphere) caused a premature shutoff.  Empty tank only took 10-12
gals.  Solution was to use a slower fill rate at the gas station or pull
out and re-insert the nozzle.
