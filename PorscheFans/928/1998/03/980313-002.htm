Well it is on my news server (hurrah !) For those who are interested, here's
the news group's charter:


Charter for alt.autos.porsche928:

A new newsgroup for Porsche 928 owners to communicate about
repair/maintenance/insurance/etc. with others who own the same car and have
similar experiences to share.  These cars were manufactured from 1978-1995, and
stayed remarkably similar for the entire run.  Therefore,  there is much to be
gained by sharing information.

This retro-moderated newsgroup will provide a forum for discussing:
- Care and maintenance of the Porsche 928
- Performance enhancements
- Porsche 928 events and announcements
- The joys and experiences of driving the Porsche 928
- All other Porsche 928 related topics

Other guidelines:
-Private owner Porsche 928 For Sale posts will be allowed.
-Commercial advertising of Porsche 928 specific items will be allowed, in
moderation.
-Cross-posting will not be allowed
