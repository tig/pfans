Warren and Phil,

The speakers in the 89 GT Do Not Unscrew (unless someone has put older 928
speakers in your 89), there are 2 screwdriver slots on the grilles (look
closely around the perimeter edge closest to the interior panels) that you
use to POP them off.

Early 8 speaker system  928 speakers unscrew, later 89 and up 10-speaker
systems pop off.
