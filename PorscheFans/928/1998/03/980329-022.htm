Dave -

I mounted a 2.5 lb extinguisher with a Brey-Krause mount, which
goes right in front of the seat (and mounts to the seat rails).
The downside is that it cost around $70, and took some work with
a grinder to make it fit right; the upside is that it's a nice
neat installation, much cleaner than something I did myself.

A couple of other choices: I chose to mount it on the driver's
side, and I got a Halon extinguisher. The choice of mounting
is good for reachability and not inconveniencing a passenger,
but bad if it should come loose somehow. The Halon will do
less damage than dry powder, but is probably less effective
in actually extinguishing open-air fires. Take your choice.
