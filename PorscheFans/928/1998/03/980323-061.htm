Dear Dean, Jim, Dave and the rest of the "Land Shark" tamers:

The ball joint boot from an Audi 5000S will readily fit the Upper ball joint
on all 928s As  Dave pointed out, it is very important that the ball joint
is still clean and without rust. If it has become contaminated, you run a
serious risk of wheel seperation. Considering how many of us drive these
autobahn cruisers, such a loss could result in major body repairs for the
car and perhaps its occupants.
