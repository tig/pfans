At 08:14 PM 3/24/98 -0500, you wrote:
>I have an 86 928s with 225 50 16 tires on  the stock rims (I think 7" wide).
>
>Can I put 245 tires on these rims.  I know I wont get as much of a contact
>area on the ground as by putting 245 on an 8" wide rim, but am I better off
>with the 245 than the 225 on the 7" rim?

According to the tire makers, no. The minimum rim width for 245/45x16 tires
is 8.0 inches. On 7" rims, you may curve the tread or even prevent the bed
from seating and sealing. Stick with the 225/50x16 size, in which you can
get extremely good tires, or get S4 or other suitable (correct offsets, no
interference or rubbing) rear wheels in 8" or greater width.
