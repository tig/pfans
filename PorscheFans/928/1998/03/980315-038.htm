My '86 auto with 131k + miles shows 3+ bar at idle in Drive with the engine
hot. At RPMs above 1000, it shows 5 bar. That's with Redline 10W 40 oil. Do
you see the oil pressure change when the RPM goes above idle speed? I'd check
your oil pressure sender or the indicator.
