With all the discussion of exhaust, I thought I'd
add my comments on systems.
I had a couple of systems fabbed up on my car in the
search for perfect exhaust music.
First was stock. OK at idle, but just too quiet for
me.
Then a glasspack with a monza resonator tip.
WAY too loud at high RPM. Great at idle.
Then a Mercedes turbo muffler with same resonator
tip.
Whistley high end shriek.
Now, the system that sounds perfect:
Flowmaster with resonator tip. Totally kills all the
high RPM noise.
Just low rumble and roar. Grrrrrreat!
