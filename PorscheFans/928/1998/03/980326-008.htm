Tom,
Be very careful with the aftermarket hood shocks out there, I have seen a
few people bend their hoods in half from the aftermarket hood shocks that
are way too strong.

The original hood shocks from Porsche and a select few non-factory suppliers
(Vertex,GPR, etc.) is the safest way to go and the price will be very close
to what you buy at Pep Boys, etc. Cost from Porsche is around $20 each.
