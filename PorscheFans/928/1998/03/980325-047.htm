Remember, the U.S. 1985 - 1986 928 S was the first to make
the switch from the earlier 16-valve engine to the all new
32-valve 288 HP hemi.  It also sported a displacement
increase from 4.7 liters to 5-liters, as well as compression
increase dictating premium unleaded fuel instead of
regular.  Engine control was now fully computerized for both
ignition and fuel injection - making the 1985 928 S the
first 928 one could 'chip'.
