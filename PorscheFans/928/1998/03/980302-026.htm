Not to sound monomaniacal (I'm really a polymaniac), but your gauge may be
fine. It sounds as though you're not able to fill your tank, and this can
be caused by clogged or otherwise inop venting. The range you describe on
the fuel gauge (from 3/4 to roughly 1/4 tank) would, if the gauge is
accurate, only be about 10 gallons, since the official tank capacity is
"approximately 86 liters" (approximate because of the slightly free-form
shape of the plastic tank), which corresponds to about 22.718797703135 US
gallons.
