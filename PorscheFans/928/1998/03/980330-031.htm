A quick tip concerning the recent thread on stuck seats.  Stuck seats
usually result from  driving the seats all the way against the rear stop (all
the way back) and leaving  them in that position.  Whenever you hit the
rear stop move the seat slightly forward and you shouldn't have a
problem with the seat getting stuck.
