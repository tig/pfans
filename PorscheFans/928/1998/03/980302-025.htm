	Take the 9 volt battery and attach one of the left over wires.  Then proceed to
tap each of the left over wires to the other contact of the battery.  Once you hear an
audible pop from the a speaker you have found the leads for that particular speaker.
Disconnect the wires and label them accordingly.  The darker wire is usually the negative
side.  repeat this with the rest of the wires untill you have all your speakers
designated.  

I use a 1.5v AA battery to check the speaker.  It gives just as good of an audible pop and
lessen your chance of damage.
