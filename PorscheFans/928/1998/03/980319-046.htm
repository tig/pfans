OK, the piece with the pole attaches just behind the rear seats.  There are 2
holes to attach the twist clips to.   The part with the zip can go forward to
cover the rear seat, should they be folded flat.  The Zip allows one seat to be
covered independantly of the other.  Otherwise the zipped portion can clipped
to the other half.
    The unzipped portion attatches to the tailgate, 2 holes again, so the cover
lifts out of the way when the 'gate is opened.
