	If you want to adjust the shift lever farther forward, you can thread 
the ball cup farther down the shift rod.  The trick, the part to be 
careful about....  Is when you are done after you snap the cup onto the 
ball (easy if you use a hose clamp!) get the cup horizontal with the 
shift lever vertical (with the gear box in 2cnd or 3rd) then tighten the 
lock nut on the ball cup.  If the cup is tilted over to much it will make 
it difficult to move the gear lever over far enough to one side, until 
the cup is worn.   It is like a ball link, it will move in ever direction 
up to a limit..... You don't have to get it perfect, there is room for 
some error.  The tool they show in the book will hold the cup horizontal, 
but that tool requres the car to be removed!  If you want to make a 
adjustment, replace the ball cup, I wouldn't take off a old one and not 
replace it.
