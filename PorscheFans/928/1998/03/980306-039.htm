Hi all,
I've never had a factory rubber line fail, but I had a 2 year old
 SS line fail just outside the fitting. These were on a 4 wheel drive
truck at fairly slow speed so not much excitment factor. On this
application there was no difference in "feel" at the pedal.
We installed a set of Russell lines on a late model Camaro & could
not tell ANY diff. in braking feel. They have been on for 14 months
and are OK so far. I've got the stock lines on my 85S and if I
keep the brakes bled & use ATE Super Blue fluid they feel pretty
solid.
Dave P.  85S 5-speed
