|When I adjust the seats using the memory settings, the external 
|mirrors just keep whirring away even when I stop pressing the |button. 
|The only way to stop them is to use the mirror adjustment switches.
|
|Hardly on a par with broken timing belts, but I hate having anything 
|not working right.
|
|Any ideas?
The mirror has a variable resistor that tells the memory circuit its 
actual position. If this resistor breaks then the computer gets the 
wrong reading and sends the mirror all the way to one side. The resistor 
is soldered penperdicular to a circuit board and vibrations can break 
the connection. Sometimes the soldering part of the resistor will break, 
but you might have enough area to solder it back.
