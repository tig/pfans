Worse than smoke.  Horse pucky.  Any 928 that is not severly broken
should be amongst the cleanest running  cars out there.
I just smog tested my 1982 at the corner Chevron station.  It passed
California smog WAY below allowable:

2500rpm:
HC(ppm) 4 vs 220 allowed
CO(%) 0.00 vs 1.2 allowed

Idle test:
HC 4 vs 100 allowed
CO 0.00 vs 1.0 allowed

for comparison purposes, my 1993 Acura Legend w/110K miles
produced 5ppm HC at idle and 2ppm at 2500rpm. CO also 0.00 for
both.

If you are really concerned, have the smog tester run a pretest.  In CA
smog results are reported on-line directly to the Department of Motor
Vehicles.  You run the pretest without reporting to avoid being labelled

a "gross polluter".
