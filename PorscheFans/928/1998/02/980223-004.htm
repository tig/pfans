Dear "Land Shark" tamers:

If you think that having no rain land on your land shark's rear window while
it's in motion at over 50 MPH is "cool", open the sunroof. Not a drop gets
in. Now THAT is "way cool."

I found the intermittant problem of the low speed miss that I was having. I
turned out to be bad plugs and weak coils (a bad combination). The 928
manual calls for the plug gap to be 0.7 mm +0.1 mm. Most plugs come gapped
at 0.8 mm. Do not reset the gap, as the  plugs' electrodes may get damaged
while regapping. Also, my OEM 12 year old coils may not have been putting
out the boost they once had. For about $100 I replaced them and together,
the problem is gone.

Happy motoring. Ed
