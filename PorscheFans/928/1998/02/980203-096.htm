>>Left and right rotors? Have I missed something? How do you tell if you have
>>your rotors on the right sides?
>>
Look at the vanes inside the rotor. The inside of the vane, nearest
the hub, should _lead_ the outside part (when the car is moving
forward). That way, air is circulated from the center to the outside.

Or you can check the part number. For the left side, the third
group number is odd; for the right side, it's even.
