Brian wrote:

>Hey all.  I know this was beat to death a while back, but did we ever
>come to a consensus about how to tell an S4 from a GT?

Did I miss some great debate?  According to the "90-93 S4, GT & GTS
Technical Specifications" book, MY 90 & 91 S4 was an automatic with
engine type M28/42 , the manuals with engine type M28/27 produced in MY
89, 90 & 91 were the GT model.  (page 8)

>Specifically a 1990 model 928?  Does the VIN number tell?

No, but the engine and transmission sn should tell.
Manual trans code G2855 2 K 00001 and later is either an 89 S4 Club
Sport (non-US) or a GT.  (page 15 in the "85-89 928S & S4 Technical
Specifications" book)

Engine code 8 5 K 0001 and later is an '89 GT.  8 1 K 0001 is an '89 S4.
(page 10)
A 90 GT will have 8 5 L 0001+, a 91 will be 8 5 M 0001+.
