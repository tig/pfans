At 11:08 PM 2/26/98 -0800, you wrote:
>What can I do to take the swirls out? Should I just take the
>car to a reputable detailer or a paint shop?

Yes, with the paint shop preferred.

To get this surface smooth and swirl-free will require a lot of work,
leading to the removal of a non-trivial amount of material, and it's all
too easy to go right through the paint in places while doing that work. The
best detailers are as aware of the issues as a good paint shop, but most
detailers are too ready to try a wet sanding or compounding and let you
point out the shiny (as in bare metal) spots afterwards.

All this is complicated if your car has metallic paint, because there is a
clear coat on top.
