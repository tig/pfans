Here's how to calculate road speed from rpm generally:

road speed (in mph) = engine speed in rpm times 60 divided by (gear ratio x
final drive ratio x rear tire revs per mile)

Some numbers to put in here:

- all 928 transmissions, stick and auto, are direct (gear ratio 1) in top gear

- some typical final drive ratios:
		'85-88 US automatics, sticks		2.20
		'85-86 Euro automatics				2.357
		'87+ Euro, '89+ US automatics		2.538
		'85-'86 Euro sticks, '89+ GTs			2.7272
         '87+ Euro S4 sticks					2.6363

- some typical tire revolutions per mile:
		225/50 x 16 (through '86)			838
		245/45 x 16 ('87-92)				849
		255/40 x 17 ('93+)					823

So, making some assumptions about your car, you were doing:

3400 x 60 / (1 x 2.20 x 838) = 122
