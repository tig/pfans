>My recollection is that SAE hp was higher, because they measured it
>without driving things like fans, water pumps, and generators, but

John, you're thinking of the notorious SAE gross ratings, which were
popular in the 60s because they gave big numbers (remember the "horsepower
race"?), until the insurance companies started reading spec sheets and
raising premiums based on advertised horsepower. SAE net, which is what you
see these days, measures the engine with most of the real-world accessories
in place and driven.
