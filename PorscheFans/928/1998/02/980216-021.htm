[ David J. Masher writes: ]
> Mike, my syndrome is when the blower kicks in full for no apparent reason and then shuts off.  It also seems to blow very slowly when the knob is set to "0" and the selector is not set to off.  

To set the record straight, `0' is merely the lowest fan speed. One would
assume 0 means off, even in a 0-indexed world, but one has been known
to be wrong on occasion. The vent selection slider must be set to `0'
to turn the fan off. GTSs, however, replace their `off' position with
a recirculate setting. I.e. the blower is always running as long as the
car is on. This is merely what I've observed in every 928 I've been in.
