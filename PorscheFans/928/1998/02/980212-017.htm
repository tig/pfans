Does the motor stop running, or is the problem that the motor runs
but the blade stops? If the latter, you might want to be sure that
the triangular plate, that the blade drive engages with when you
close the hatch, is free to move up and down about 1/4 inch. They
have a tendency to get stuck in the down position, and therefore
to engage badly or not at all.
