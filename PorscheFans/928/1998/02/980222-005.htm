Nicholas, so you made the decision here is a little information that may help:
This info based on US vintage cars
1. The 1984 and before 928 used Porsche Syncros in the 5 speed prone to
problems, 85 + model years shifted to Borg Warner much better.
2. The 1984 and before 928 used the 16 Valve motor, 1985 + used the 32 valve
motor, much more power
3. The only difference in between the 85-86 928's as far as I know is the
availability of ABS.
4. The 87 S4 increased horsepower and top end, new spoiler and grounds
package. 
5. Big issue on any car US 85 forward, change the belts or disaster may
strike, age and/or mileage will make this necessary ie. 85 with 25,000 miles
probably needs belt changed due to age not so much mileage, cost me $750.00
US.
