To all with broken or soon-to-be-broken driver side door locks.  On my '85,
the hinged cover that keeps the outside elements out of the internals of the
lock broke (the spring) so that water, etc. destroyed the lock.  I bought a
new lock with a different key than the rest of the car (much cheaper than a
matching lock) and had a locksmith take it apart and key it to the original
key.  The lock didn't last long.  It never turned as smoothly as it should.
Anyway, here is the best fix:

Switch out the passenger side lock with the driver's side lock.

How many of us ever unlock the passenger side?  They are interchangeable at
least on my '85).

If the lock on my '93 breaks I will probably pony-up for the matching lock,
which is always an option too!
