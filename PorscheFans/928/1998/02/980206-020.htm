I'm sure you'd never over-react, but you almost certainly don't need to do
anything. The crank oiling problem comes with sustained (more than twenty
seconds or so) operation at 6500+ rpm and full throttle, a situation
unlikely to occur outside of the race track. However heavy your foot may be
(mine's not light), touching the red line briefly as you accelerate hard or
pass someone on the public road does not put you at risk. Driving the car
hard is good for it; keeps everything fresh and exercised.
