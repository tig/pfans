To the person with the 02 light glowing at idle.... I had a VW scirrocco that
did this and ran funny off idle.  The problem was a bad engine to chassis
ground.  Also check your battery to chassis ground.  

To the person with oil in the valley of your engine, I just replaced every
crank case vent hose on my 86 32v.  All hoses were brittle and in very poor
shape.  You will have to remove intale runners to do this right, and while in
there do all the vacuum lines.
