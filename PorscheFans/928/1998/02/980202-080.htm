Further to Wayne's post, the catalog number of the Solido model is #1525.  I found
a couple of these in a toy-shop here in Auckland just before Christmas and bought
them.  It's the same model as their earlier 928S model, with the wheels changed
and an S4 wing added.   (Look around the lower front of the car and it does look a
little different from an S4.)

I understood that these were run-out stock - Solido are no longer making them.
If this is not the case then, yes, it's a good model to go looking for.
