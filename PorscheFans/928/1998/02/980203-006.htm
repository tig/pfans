more than 520 cfm on the way out, because of expansion once the mixture
is ignited in the cylinder.

increase in volume, less the heat lost to work (pushing the piston), and
conducted away by the block etc.

of gasoline in 487 cubic feet of air.  At this point, I run dry - the
