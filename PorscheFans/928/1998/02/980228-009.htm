> My Saab runs on dino oil, but this is what I understand from my mechanic
> and experiences on The SAAB Network mailing list. Synthetic oil DOESN'T
> damage seals. However, over time gaskets shrink, crack, flex, and do
> other stuff. This leaves small gaps. Conventional oil gets into these
> gaps and dries up, thus closing small gaps. Synthetic oil breaks up the
> conventional oil seals in the gaskets. If you use synthetic from the
> start, the gaskets don't move, shrink or crack as much. That is the
> reason for no leaks. Mechanics on the Saab list recommend not going to
> synthetic at over 80,000 miles if you have any leaks. If you don't have
> any leaks they don't recommend changing to synthetic over 120,000 miles.
