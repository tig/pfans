According to the 928 tech at Niello Porsche quoting from his 928 shop
manuals, there are only 2 sources that will activate the cooling fans after
engine shut down with ignition off:
1.Intake air temp above 87.5 degrees centigrade (the temp sensor on top of
the intake  manifold)
2. coolant temp above 87 centigrade (sensor on radiator)

The fans may come on for up to 45 min after engine shut down @ variable
speeds.
