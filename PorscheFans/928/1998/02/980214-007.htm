Dear fellow "Land Shark" tamers:

In case you don't already know, the PCA Potomac Region has scheduled a
three-day Driver's Ed event at Mid Ohio for May 8-10, 1998. I plan on
participating and hope to see other 928's out there as well.

The event includes all instruction and corner workers. All participants do
is drive, attend a few classroom sessions, and keep their cars in running
condition. The cost for all this fun is $230. That's less than $80 per day.
This is a bargain considering that at our Region's "home track" (Summit
Point) we charge $85 for a one day event.

If you want a registration form or more information, please contact Marilyn
Hickson at:marilynh@iadb.org

Happy motoring, and I hope to see and meet lots of "Land Sharks" tamers
while I'm there. Ed
