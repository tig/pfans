I doubt there's much I can add to the subject at this point, but I've
used small amounts of Permatex Blue (the stuff guaranteed against oil
leaks) on bolt threads to accomplish the task of helping keep them in
place.   The effect is to fill in the teeny voids between threads with
this mildly adhesive stuff - and that minimizes the effects of vibration
and heat cycles.   Hard to guage effectiveness, but it's cheap, easy,
and doesn't hurt a thing.
