The ! light is part of the central warning system.  It is there to warn
you, the driver, of various car system status alarms.  There are
differing warning status levels ie. level 1, level 2 , which either can
be cleared off the ! button by pressing it once, or, if the warning is
more severe then the ! cannot be cleared which is to serve as a constant
visual warning for you to fix the problem.
