Top Fuel cars will launch at around 5G. The run logs will record
these numbers for most fuel funny cars & dragsters. ( something
to do with hot sticky tires, ie. burnouts)

I have about 60 1/4 mile passes on my 85S 5-speed. To avoid wheel
hop you must burn the living hell out of the tires ( slow ) or
settle for a slight bog ( also a little slow ). I usually go to around
3000 RPM & let the clutch drag very slightly ( preloads the drivetrain )
then drop it fairly quickly. I'm currantly using Yokohama A008RSII
autocross tires.
In damp track autocross situations it's possible to get very violent
hop off the corners. I usually just ease up & try to be very careful.
Different tires & or shocks did not help.
Regarding pan bolts- Try leaving the bolts dry & apply a small
glob ( tech term! ) of silicone over the head of the bolts. I
use this on some hardware on my aircraft & it works fine. It is easy
to remove for later service.
