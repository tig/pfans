According to the shop manual and spec book, Club Sport cars have the same
(different part numbers but interchangeable) camshafts, wheels, spacers,
and exhaust as the GT, all different from the S4.

From these same sources, the S4's red line (fuel cutoff by rev limiter) is
6600 rpm, GT 6800; S4 idle 675 rpm, GT 775.
