The torque tube is a tube that hold the driveshaft in our car (and in
the 944 and the
new corvette(?)).  If you look under your car, you will notice that you
cannot see the
driveshaft.  Porsche, because it puts the transmission in the back,
included a strong
mechanical link between the transmission and the engine.

The inside shaft is held in place by bearing that can go bad, leading to
some noise (never
