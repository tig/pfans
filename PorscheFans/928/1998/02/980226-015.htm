	I've seen posts mostly saying that the oxygen sensor will not affect
	performance, but I spoke to someone at the frenzy with an 87', and he said
	that when he replaced his, it made a big difference. 

It's best to get the exact replacement O2 sensor.  Alot of people think that all O2
sensors are the same.  Not true.  Some are more sensitive than others.  The LH/EZK system
is an adaptive system.  It learns and constantly adjust its internal lookup parameter
tables according to your driving environment.  The O2 sensor does play an important role
as part of this system.
