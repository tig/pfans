I have a black 928.
I have a cat, also black.
My cat has feet, generally brown and icky.
My cat likes the warmth of the hood of the black car.
My black car looks icky with catprints all over it.

So I simply bought a couple of lengths of dowel, five little pulleys,
some thick string and 5 metres of calico.  Now I have a simple cover
that hangs on the garage ceiling.  I back the Porsche into place and
release a hook on the wall which lowers the cover onto the car.  I 
simply pull that string and hook it to remove the cover from the car.

The cover is dirty and covered in cat hair and prints.  It's probably
more comfortable for Biggles than bare black paint, too.  

The Porsche looks best in black.
