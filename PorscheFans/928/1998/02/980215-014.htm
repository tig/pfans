Hi Howard,

I have a '90 S4, which I picked up in LA and drove home to the SF bay area. It
was during the fall of '96 (I was listening to the Cal vs. USC game), and a
nice warm day. I was heading up the grapevine (Gorman Pass?) with the A\C
running and I noticed the temp gage was in the middle of the last white tick
mark. Being unfamiliar with the car,  I turned off the A/C. Now, I know the
A/C will turn on the fans. After having the car in the SF bay area for a
while, I noticed the temp gage stayed pretty much parked at or right below
that tick mark. Later, I had the 75C thermostat installed and noticed the the
engine ran cooler.

Hope that helps,
