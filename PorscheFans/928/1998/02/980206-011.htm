I had considerable trouble with electrics including the turn signals and
headlights on my '85 after I bought it.  I removed the column switch and
soaked it heavily in WD-40.  Five years later, it still works like new
(it's as smooth as the switch on a new Camry I drove a while ago - that's
smooth).  I also removed every fuse and relay (one at a time) and coated
the terminals with conductive grease.  Several electrical gremlins
disappeared and have not returned.  I highly recommend it for all 928s.
