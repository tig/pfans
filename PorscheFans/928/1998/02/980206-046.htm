      What you described could be a number of problems. First, since you
mentioned you had a idle problem that would indicate to me that your engine
is out of tune. So I would start with checking the condition of all the
tune up items, i.e. plugs, wires, cap/rotor, fuel filter, ignition timing,
and idle. Then from there you should differentiate between whether you have
a fuel or ignition problem.
       You have to establish a starting point or you'll spent a lot of 
money swapping parts.
