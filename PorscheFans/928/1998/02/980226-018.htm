anything else.... 

928s are relatively common here in England - I can pick up many of the

grey metallic, with linen seats and, importantly, the limited slip diff.
Looking at two possiblities this weekend, (thanks to the knowledgeable

the one.....

Any recommendations from UK list members on specialists in London (or

who uses them for his racing 911- any thoughts from anyone else?

Just to add to the thread a few weeks ago on fuel prices...Here in
England (and in Europe in general) petrol is highly taxed (something like
70% of the pump price and rising every year) so filling up is always a
painful experience. Last time I filled up, I paid 65 pence a litre. If my maths
is correct, that is $4.00 per US gallon - 3 or 4 times the US price! That
hungry 928 V8 is going to make my wallet very lean :)
