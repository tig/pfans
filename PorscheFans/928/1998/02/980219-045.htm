As I found out today, the only thing that you can find out from Porsche
(1-800-PORSCHE) is if any recalls have been performed against your
vehicle by a Porsche authorized dealer. You can also find out which
options were originally installed be the factory(?). The only way to
find out the service history is get the records from the seller, or find
out which dealer serviced the car. The records stay with that dealer.
