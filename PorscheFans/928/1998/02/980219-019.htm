There were recently several posts on the belt tension light coming on with a
tight belt.  One possible solution:

Make sure that the connector between the tension sensor and the wiring
harness is not vibrating.  The connector is located close to the sensor and
if the connector vibrates it can set off the sensor.  In my '85 (32 valve) I
had the same problem you're having and it took about 5 trips to my mechanic
over a 2 week period to figure it out.
