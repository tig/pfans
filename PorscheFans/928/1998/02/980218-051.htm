I found that with the accelerator floored, the throttle was only opening
about 1/2 way.  I adjusted the cable and checked the WOT sensor
position, now working.  Also I adjusted the push rod that activates the
kickdown switch.  The manual spec for this rod adjustment is 48mm +/-1mm
between ball head centers.  This seemed way to short to me?  I adjusted
this to 68mm overall length which is 48mm in length excluding the ball
heads.  This activates the kickdown switch without excessive effort when
the accelerator pedal hits the stop.
