A few days ago I posted a  question about the location of the fuse for
the run-on function on the hatchback release motor.

Several people responded (Thanks!) that the fuse was in-line near the
motor. Mine wasn't.

After additional looking at the (really strange) wiring diagrams, I
discovered that Porsche had changed the fuse location. On the '86 (and
up, I presume) the fuse is No. 26 on the central electrical panel under
the passenger floor.

So, if you have to hold the hatch release button up until the hatch
release, check the in-line fuse on earlier models, and fuse No. 26 on
later models.

Someone alluded to a DIN wiring standard some time ago. This standard
tells what the numbers signify on the wiring diagrams. Does anyone know
of a source for this info?
