Is there a 'user' diagnostic procedure that can retrieve failure
codes from the engine controller? For example, on my Dodge van,
if you turn the ignition on/off three times in less than five
seconds, the 'check engine' light flashes codes that you can
look up. I used this to find an intermitant Hall Effect pickup
on my distributor.  

The procedure someone posted about tapping wires and checking
voltages seems pretty arcane for a car of the 928 caliber. Anyway,
a voltmeter is only good for slow DC signals, better than nothing,
but kind of like trying to see through a glass block wall. The
controller should know what the limits and timing are anyway.
