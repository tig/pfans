To quote the shop manual:

The wear limit has been reached for cars up to and including 1982
models when the front edge of [the release] lever just appears in
inspection hole. Beginning with 1983 models there were changes in
starter installation and inspection hole location. The wear limit
has been reached when front edge of lever reaches end of
inspection hole.

The Chilton manual, of course, only talks about pre-1982 cars.
