after performing the 'brain bridge' - at least it does not on my car. 
You might want to try reseating the RDK brain connector first, or, the
ground strap on the battery to see if that works before proceding into
the pod.  I'd hate to set off some other fail safe due to a missing
warning lamp bulb (no kidding - we have not yet fully delved into the
Porsche safety systems).
