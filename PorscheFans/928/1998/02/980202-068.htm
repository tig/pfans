Jason,

Having just finished upgrading my 79, herewith MHO on your predicament.

First question is, what symptoms lead you to exotica?  

Ball joints are certainly wear items and, as I guess you know from
previous posts, the upper ones are replacable only with the A-arm.  

If the uppers really are gone, buy the front suspension from a
dismantler, ideally from an 87 or newer (gets you the bigger brakes). 
You'll get newer ball joints, uprights, bearings, springs and shocks. 
928 Int'l is a good source.  Figure on at least $500 per side.  

The biggest non-$ problem you'll have, if you DIY, is getting the upper
A-arm nuts off the drivers side arm.  I gave up and pulled the whole
damn engine (other reasons made such an effort justifiable).

No experience with the steering rack.  Replaced both tie rods and ball
joints, however.  That's where the looseness was, in terms of alignment.

Anyway, if you've really got to replace all the stuff the shop listed,
get hold of 929 Int'l for used parts from a wreck.

Give me a call if you want more info.
