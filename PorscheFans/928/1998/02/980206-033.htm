  Just to expand beyond '88 a bit...
  
  The 8x16 was only fitted to the front of the GT in '89.  For '90-91
  the size on the fronts went down to 7.5x16 and construction went from 
  forged to cast.  I don't have the offsets handy for the 7.5x16.  The
  cast wheels are called Design 90 while the 8/9x16 combo are called Club
  Sport.
