>>I'm a new owner to a 928 S4 and have a question regarding the handbrake. 
>>Mine doesn't work at all, is it a way to adjust it without removing more 
>>than the wheel?
>>
Yes, when you remove the rear wheels, you will see a 1 cm hole in
the rotor. By rotating the rotor to the correct position, you
will have access to a cogged wheel, which can be turned with a
screwdriver tip to adjust. HOWEVER, chances are very good that
you actually have a broken cable, and adjustment won't do the
trick. Follow the cable from the wheel to the point behind the
transaxle, give a tug and see if there's lots of slack.
