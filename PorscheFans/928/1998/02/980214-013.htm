I had mine plastic welded, a 4" crack had formed on the bottom drivers
side (US drivers side) and was a steady dribble.  I have been
withholding this information because:
A.  I have not checked with the welder to find out if he is interested
in doing more.
B.  Wanted to give mine a chance to leak if the technique is not sound.

He did it just like one would weld w/metal,  routed a "V" in the tank
along the crack, hooked up his plastic "MIG" apparatus and buzzed
(squished) the crack shut.
2000 mi and no drips!
