The vented brake rotors on the front of my 85s have what the factory
calls " involutes ". These act as air pumps & have nothing to do with
the
gas slots on the rotor surface. The idea is that the rotor will pull
cooling air in from the brake ducts in the front air dam. I believe
the factory rotors have part #s stamped on then, if memory serves
( and it may not ) the # ending in an odd # ie. 3 or 5 or whatever,
installs on the right side. The factory manuals show correct
installation. It really does make a difference in temps.
