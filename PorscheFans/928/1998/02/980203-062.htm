> 2) The alternator cooling tube and attachment is
> missing on mine (as is the plastic wall that sits
> ahead of both front wheels).  Is this cooling
> needed?

You should replace the tube.  Mine is not the original type
but a tube from an aircraft (bought from Zimms in Texas).
The tube will limit the amount of water that might enter the
alternator (really important ??) but will also create a 
good flow of air over the temperature sender mounted inside
the front left fender (this is used by your interior temperature
controller).
