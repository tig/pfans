>David, could you elaborate on this, I had a heck of time compressing the
>springs on the shifter assembly and that was with a 'long-nose channel
>lock'...
Use 1 pair of needle nose vise grips to hold one connection closed while
using the needle nose channel locks etc. to compress the connection that you
are putting the spring clip on. I use a second pair of needle nose vise
grips to hold the clip secure and insert it into it's groove.
Of course I have done it over a dozen times and practice does make perfect.
;-)
