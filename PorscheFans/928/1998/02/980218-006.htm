At 12:46 PM 2/18/98 +0100, you wrote:
>The car was equipped with carrera cup wheels and quite big tires. I'm 
>about the change the rear tires what size should I pick. The current size 
>is, 265/40 - 17, should I go down to 255/40 or can I go for the 265?

The correct size is 255/40x17. The 265s will probably fit, but may rub
during bumps (even wider tires can fit, but only with trick wheel offsets).
They also make the already tall gearing even taller (823 v. 836 revs/mile)
and are a hair wide for 9" rims.
