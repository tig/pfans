Gary Tapp wrote:

> I use Mobil 1 synthetic oil. What weight of oil is recommended? I have been
> using 15W - 50. I have considered using the 40 weight (I believe it is 10W
> - 40). Any advantages or disadvantages?

The 15W-50 will cope better with extreme stresses, and provide better
protection to the bottom end during times of high temperature  / rpm.  There
was some discussion 2-3 weeks ago, but I'm unsure what the consensus was.  Some
contended that the lighter oil would provide better protection at startup,
while others (LL included) were concerned about adequate protection of the Big
End.
