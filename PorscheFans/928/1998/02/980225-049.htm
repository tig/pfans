Went thru entire car and cleaned all the electrical ground points....2 above
fuse panel, 1 under instrument pod,  2 coil strap to engine wires,  2 on the
right front engine bay, 1 on left front engine bay, 1 engine to body, and the
battery ground point. Others I missed?  Removed alternator and cleaned
connections on it and also starter connections.
Cleaned all misc. connections under the hood and also the positive connections
on the right and left front. (under hood)
Removed and cleaned every fuse, relay, and bottom row of connectors on the
fuse panel, along with the EZK and LH control unit connections and many, many
misc connectors under there and under the instrument pod. 
Everything really looked pretty good, with the exception of some of the ground
points being corroded and having some paint on the brass ground lugs. Ed
Croasdale had advised that cleaning the fuse panel connections was a good
preventative measure, and I just went a few steps further.....Thanks Ed.
Verified charging is well, however the guage still reads 13 to 13.5 volts when
it's really charging at 14 volts.
