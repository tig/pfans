> 4. Windshield and headlight washers don't work; how does this rate in
> terms of difficulty and expense to right.

If you try to activate the washer, do you either get a puddle of
washer fluid and a electrical motor noise, or no noise and no puddle?

If you have a puddle, you need to change the tubing going to the
nozzle (a lot of the rubber on my 85 is now dried out.  I would assume
your car is similar :(  )

If you do not hear the motor, check the fuse pannel, if that is OK,
you need to go after the motor itself (or its electrical connection)
