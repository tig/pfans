Anyway, regarding the windage tray, for $10, DEVEK will get one to you
pronto.  However, not sure it's worth the installation aggravation you'd
no doubt experience.  

It's a sheet-metal "plate" that mounts in the aft section of the deep
part of the pan.  Has a half moon cut-out so it can get right up the oil
pump pick-up.  The design doesn't strike me as preventing as much as
perhaps only slowing the flow of oil rear-ward under hard acceleration.
Seems to have no design feature limiting lateral fluid movement.  

Will the car see much track or AX time?  If not, may be worth spending
the time tightening your pan bolts. :)
