JP,
The shifter boots  and knobs from all years will fit on the GT shifter.

There are three different shifter boot/knob configurations
77-80 has plastic/rubber knob that contours down a couple of inches and then
the leather goes the rest of the way down to the baseplate.

81-89 ( some 90s) Have the completely leather wrapped knob with shift
pattern insert into the top.

90-95 Same as above but shifter knob size is about double, a really fat
shift knob that feels great in your hands.
