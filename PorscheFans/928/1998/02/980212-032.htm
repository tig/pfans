This works both ways. Stickier tires are good, but increasing tread width
is only one way to get more stick (better tires in the same size may be a
better choice). Meanwhile, bigger tires (which generally need wider wheels)
weigh more, and increasing unsprung weight invites wheel hop and other Bad
Things.
