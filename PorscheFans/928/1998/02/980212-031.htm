At 07:51 AM 2/12/98 -0500, you wrote:
>So is a possible fix better/stronger shocks?

Maybe, but only up to a point. Ultra stiff shocks, apart from punishing you
all the time (ultra hard ride) tend to make the suspension appear rigid for
rapid input and can make the skipping worse; this is known in off-road
stuff, where purpose built racers use fairly soft springs and LONG wheel
travel.

Vince Yu was right, however devilish he may have felt when he posted it, in
suggesting traction bars, but this is also inappropriate for every other
aspect of the car's operation.
