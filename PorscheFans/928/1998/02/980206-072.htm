There's a few more subtle differences in addition to those mentioned earlier.

The '89 has the roof mounted radio antenna, where the '87 has it mounted in
the windshield. The rear-seat seat belts are the three point style in the '89
and are two point style in '87. Also, I was told by a customer service rep at
PCNA-Reno that the '88 and later cars had an induction system modification,
but I haven't verified that one.

Hope that helps.
