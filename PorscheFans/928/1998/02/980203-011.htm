Several questions have appeared about the feature on Porsche's by which
the turn indicator can be illuminated even if the car is not switched
on.  I have been told that most german cars have this feature due to a
regulation (in Germany) that requires cars on narrow streets to jump the
curb with the curb side wheels when parked.  In this mode the driver is
required to leave his roadside turn indicator illuminated to warning
traffic that the road is restricted for their passage.
