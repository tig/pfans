	I had a similar problem with my '83 S and found that one of the
license plate light's hot wire was shorting to ground.  The wire was
mis-routed and got caught between the rear bumper cover and the aluminum
bumper member.  The vibration eventually chafe the insulation, thereby
exposing the wire.  Since I was restoring the S, removing the bumper
cover to access it was not a problem.  Maybe this is your problem.
