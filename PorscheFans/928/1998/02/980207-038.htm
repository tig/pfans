And PMJI, before you get them made make sure you check the type of
connector to your coils - post vs. pin type!  Not interchangeable. 
Magnecor should be able to handle this but not if they don't know about
your particular application.

Don't know about the deep wells to the 32-v spark plugs though.  I had
8.5kv competition wires on a 16-v 928 with no problems (but no long
reach to plugs either).  So it sounds like you may be breaking new
ground - and if at all possible should send them your old wires for
reference / review first, with the new set gratis for your
install/review until it can be determined they will work.

The routing of the 32-v wires take a much more circuitous (sp?) and
torturous path through / on the engine but I would agree the Magnecors
are robust and up to the task.

But then so are the OEM wires and connectors! Good to have options
though.
