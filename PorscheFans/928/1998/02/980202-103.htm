Pulling onto my street today I was greeted by about half a dozen
motorcycle police who motioned me over.  Oh great I thought - not
again!  Then I noticed the other half and more lining Columbus Avenue. 
Within a few moments the presidential but somber procession of limos had
glided past, replete with camera crews in tow.

Yes, San Francisco has lost it's most charismatic leader of the past,
Mayor Alioto, and North Beach will never be the same.  The skies rained
and it hasn't stopped yet.  I sat in retrospection for a moment,
listening only to the rain dancing on the hood and the deep idle of the
engine throbbing...
