If it's just general info, a Hall sensor often refers to a Hall affect sensor
switch. This is effectively a non-mechanical switch which is opened (or
closed) when exposed to a magnetic field. A typical application would be to
have a magnet mounted on a moving piece of the equipment and have the sensor
on a fixed portion. When the moving piece(I.E. magnet) comes close to the
sensor, the sensor triggers.

They're used in many industries for proximity switches, limit switches, etc.
