Matt - Here's some information about the US model, which seems to be
close but not quite the same:

30 - red - these are input voltage to the switch, always hot.
50 - yellow - to the starter relay, & a couple of other places
15 - black (2 wires) - feed a lot of things with ignition on.
P - grey - to parking light contact, hot with ignition off only.
R - red/black - radio
X - black/red - hot with switch anything but off, feeds light switch etc.

That still leaves a wire unaccounted for, but might get you close.
