The Steel panels are Hot-dip galvanized.  This deposites a layer of Zinc
over the top of the steel.  Zinc, like aluminium, doesn't rust.  The coating
can be worn away by scratching/sanding etc.  Thus we may also be concerned
over the reactions between Zn and Al.

The term Galvanizing comes from L. Galvani, an Italian Phisiologist who
died in 1798.  He used DC electricity for medical purposes.
