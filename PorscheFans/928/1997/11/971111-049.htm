Chris - I use 8" S4 rims on my car with no clearance problems, on the
track. My car does have the 21mm spacers. With 245/45-16 BFG R1 tires.
The clearance isn't much, though, and might even be non-xistent with
a different brand of tire, since they aren't totally consistent from
one brand to another. Be aware that if you remove the spacers, you
will have to either replace all the 72mm wheel bolts with 50mmm ones,
or use open-ended (typically steel) wheel nuts.
