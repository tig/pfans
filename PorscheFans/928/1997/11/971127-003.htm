Bad idea to get a basket case 928.  While for some cars basket cases make
sense, i.e., lots of donor vehicles, large aftermarket supplier base, lots of
repair specialists, lots of other people with similar situations and
knowledge, I don't think this appplies to 928s at all.

This is one of those vehicles where the difference in price between good/very
good condition and horrible condition is not very great...much, much less
than you'll end up sinking into the horrible condition 928 to bring it up to
very good condition.  Don't worry, even if you get a VG car, you'll still
have plenty of things to fix up over time, so you'll have the opportunity to
get to "know" your car.
