Eh? Are you getting non-zero readings on any gauge before you switch on?

>The drivers manual only says that the pressure should be at least 5-bar
>at 5,000 rpm.

You should see a pegged gauge (over 5 bar) just AFTER you start from cold.
As the oil warms up, oil pressure at idle will drop to 2-3 bar or so (exact
number affected by the particular oil you use), but should go up to 5+ bar
by 2500 rpm or so.
