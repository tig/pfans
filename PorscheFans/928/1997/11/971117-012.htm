Fine; this means you probably haven't disconnected anything important.

Meanwhile, the service bulletin you mentioned referred to the center stop
lamp. Porsche's recommended mounting for a cell phone antenna (see the shop
manual) puts it on the roof just ahead of the top of the rear window. It's
possible that the radio signal transmitted from that antenna was being
picked up by the wiring from the nearby center lamp, getting into the
control circuit for the warning, and causing that circuit to get confused
and raise the warning.
