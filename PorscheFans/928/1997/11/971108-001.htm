OK. I've got one. Have you pulled the fuel pump relay and bypassed it with
a wire jumper?

I seem to remember that the early injection system had a relay that has
additional electronics mounted in it (wired into the throttle sensors to
boost pump pressure during acceleration?). This might be causing your
problem.

Also, this is a bit rough, but have you tried measuring your fuel pressure
while running the engien to see if it drops away as the engine starts rough
idling?
