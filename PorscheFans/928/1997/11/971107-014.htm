If your rear wheels lockup before the front wheels you will likely get into
a spin unless you are going "dead straight" and have excellent control. Once
the rears lockup, they lose all ability to hold maximum grip. That can and
does usually mean that the front tires are holding the road better and
decelerating better as well. Consequently, the rear wants to go faster than
the front of the car. That is the recipe for a spin.

Happy controlled motoring. Ed
