Actually, a short is somewhat unlikely, since you aren't blowing any fuses. 
First thing to do is take a voltmeter and see if you have voltage at the
fuse (fuse 1 on my car). If so, there's a problem in the wiring from
there to the fog light. If not, find the Fog Light Relay on the relay
board. Pull it out, and see if you have battery voltage between pins
85 and 86. If so, the relay is probably bad; if not, the fog light 
switch is suspect. All this assumes that the wiring didn't change
significantly between your car and mine.
