Hi Mike,

As you probably know by now, the "turn signal noise is barely audible.
Most cars have the relay that "blinks" the turn signals mounted somewhere
behind the instrument panel, and its very audible.
The relay in the later S4's is mounted int central electronics area under the
passenger's feet. It has the wood support and the floor carpeting over it
and, as a consequence, it gets muffled.
