The "PORSCHE 928 CLUB HOLLAND" is born.

For anybody who's interested!!

Today we had our first meeting in WORMER, N. Holland and there were 21
of the 27 members in attendance. Membership growing all the time,
hopefully!!
Ten of those who have 928s brought them along, not bad considering the
time of year and weather conditions.
There were 928, 928S, and 928S4 models both auto and manual on display
although no SII, GT or GTS unfortunately, next time. Nice to see ten
928s together in one place.
I finally met our regular contributer Math Creemers, hello again Math
hope you got home OK.

Objectives of the club are of course to exchange information and
knowledge and to collectively enjoy these wonderful machines and where
possible to let the masses know that PORSCHE dont always mean 911.
AND of course to organise factory trips and most important of all TRACK
DAYS....

Vrrrooomm.

So here's to the future.
