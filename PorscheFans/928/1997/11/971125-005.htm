I have driven several cars with DRLs including my VW GTI in which the
running lights, head lights, tail lights, and dash lights are on _all_
the time.  The only difference between DRL-mode and "real" lights is a

to drive at night for a long time with only the DRLs on because the
only indication is the position of the light switch.
