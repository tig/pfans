>I always read about the problems that all you 928 owners have and it

Don't take things out of context. A lot of the traffic on this list
concerns repair or maintenance questions. 928s, like NSXs, Cadillac
Sevilles, or most other modern cars, are complex machines and need either
the full shop manual or direct prior experience for many procedures.

A lot of people who do their own work use this list as a "soft
encyclopedia", something which would probably better be served by a
fleshed-out FAQ of the sort the NSX people have put together
(http://www.public.usit.net/leng/nsxfaq00.htm) to deal with the problems of
and issues with that car.

Meanwhile, how about all the 928 list traffic exulting in tremendous,
exciting performance or long, enjoyable road trips at high average speed
and zero incidents?

>Can i feel comfortable with the 928s' reliability?

Yes. As with any other car, regular preventive maintenance is needed to get
best results.

>How much can i expect to spend on Maintenance annually?

Depends on the car's mileage and condition, on how much work you do
yourself, and how much you use the car.
