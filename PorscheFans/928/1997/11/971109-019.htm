One thing to check.  There's an Acceleration Switch that's hooked into the fuel injection
computer.  It's located near the Air Intake body on the front left side of the engine.
 If this switch is stuck in the closed position, the fuel injection computer sets
the pulses to the injectors for a richer mixture.  May not be your problem, but worth
checking.
