> Also, the car came with the 4 volume set of shop manuals for 79-81
928s.
> Are these worth anything?  I am considering getting the current 9

I don't think you really need the 9-vol set.  I wouldn't have purchased
mine except I couldn't find a 4-vol set.  The only advantage would be if
you felt you really needed the updates - not really very many or planned
on getting another, later 928 and keeping both.  As far as I know, the
1981 is identical to the 1982 in every respect.  1983, introduced the
4.7L engine.  BTW, the additional 5 vols. in the 9 vol set cover such
esoteric items as ABS, airbags, dual A/C, 32-valve engine, the newer
5-speed trans, the newer 4-speed automatic etc.  All this is stuff you
don't have on your car.
