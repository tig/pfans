This is fine until all the Zn has been oxidised.  I once came across
an old plough in a field.  It had been galvanized.  Where the Zinc oxide
was present, no problem. On the odd part where it wasn't there was --- Rust.

I have a small patch on the cowl where the plasiic panel that covers
the wiper mechanism was wrongly fitted, and wore through the paint.  
I appear to have surface rust there. I assume that the Zinc wore away 
too, and that once the electrons couldn't 'see' any more zinc (because 
it was covered in paint) they started on the Steel.
