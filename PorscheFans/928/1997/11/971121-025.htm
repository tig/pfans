Dear fellow "Land Shark" tamers:

I want to pass on something I learned recently about sunroofs.

Mine had started leaking near the right front corner whenever it rained for
any extended period of time. The problem was not the seals, but rather the
nearest drain hole was plugged. I had tried air pressure to blow out the
obstruction, but it would not budge. The repair was to simply "snake" the
"cable" from the rear brakes of my bicycle down the drain. The "cable" was:
thin, strong, manuverable, and long enough. It also permitted me to apply a
"roto-rooter" action that finally unblocked the drain.
