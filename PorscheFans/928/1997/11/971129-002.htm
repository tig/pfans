Dear fellow "Land Shark" tamers:

I am happy to report that I found the short in the heater/AC system. The
culprit was a burnt out temperature sensor fan motor.

Now here is the interesting/lucky part. It turns out that I bought a new
motor from "Dulles International Motorsport" (for $25) at a Potomac Region
Swap-meet about four years ago. Since the car's fan motor was still good, I
put the new one in storage "in case it's ever needed".

I still will replace the temp sensor (about $60), but that's all. I had
expected to spend about $500 or more. Needless to say, there will be more
gifts under the tree this year.

If the temperature control system on your heater/AC gives you lots of heat
no matter what you set the temperature at, you may want to check the
temperature sensor and motor.

Happy motoring. Ed
