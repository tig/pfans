Rebbe:
Is it the oil cooler leaking or is it one of the connections/fittings?  If
the former, contact DEVEK, since you may need a new radiator as well
(depending on the year of your 928).  Devek has a
better-than-stock/cheaper-than-replacement/probably-more-reliable-than-used
aluminum (not plastic) radiator for sale.  The unknown is whether or not you
have one of the combined rad-oil cooler-ac units.  I can't speak to the bad
connections issue.  A second opinion on possible fixes is worthwhile BEFORE
taking the expensive replacement route.
An experienced 928 (not just Porsche) mechanic is crucial to long-term
happiness!

Sorry, no one has yet found a source for new or used "luggage boot covers,"
let alone a cheaper one.  (Hey, aftermarket vendors, this is what we
capitalists call an "opportunity"!!!) The design, if you haven't seen one, is
nothing special: fabric, rods and bungee-style cords with plastic locknut
connectors.  There is no "window shade" type roller, which even cheap
hatchbacks have.

When you fixed the power steering line leak did this affect the weight/feel
of the steering?
