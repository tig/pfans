You might want to check out your friendly J.C. Whitney catalog. They have 7"
lights that have quartz lenses, and seem to have the same non-US beam pattern
as the Hella lamps. They are not DOT lamps. I have the Hella lights on my 83
944, and purchased the J.C. Whitney's for my 86 928. I can't tell the
difference in beam pattern. I ordered 90/130 bulbs for the J.C. Whitney's,
and they are white & bright. They cost less than $30 apiece including the
bulbs. They are made in Poland. The seal around one of the lenses had a pin
hole size leak (water entered when I drove in rain) which RTV fixed. I live
in one of the other Portlands (Oregon) where rain happens a lot.

Jacobs Electronics has a good setup for headlight relays. Quality stuff.
Pretty much a plug-in job, minimal wiring & drilling. Their phone is
1-800-627-8800.
