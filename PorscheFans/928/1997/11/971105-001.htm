"The Magazine About Porsche"
"excellence"

Available at most U.S. mega bookstores, therefore no auto club
membership required. I guess you could say it is the U.S. counterpart to
911 & Porscheworld, albeit with alot more technical details and racing
information (ok our steering wheels are on the wrong side, right?). 
Regular contributors include Bruce Anderson, Kim Crumb, Jim Pasha and
Pete Stout to name a few!

Published 8 times a year.

Foreign rates (including Canada) $27 one year, $49 two years, $69 three
years (payment in U.S. dollars)

All subscriptions...should be addressed to Excellence, Circulation
Dept., P.O. Box 1529, Ross, CA 94957

Editorial contributions...welcome and should be addressed to the Editor
at the same address.

There are tons of back issues I'm sure you would be interested getting
and there usually is an index in each magazine with ordering info...
