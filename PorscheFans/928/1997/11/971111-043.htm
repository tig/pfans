At 10:57 PM 11/11/97 -0000, you wrote:
>Has anybody fitted the larger 8"(rear) rims from an S4 to an S?
>I have a Euro spec 928S2(1985) with the standard 7" rims all round,and have
>just purchased some S4 rears The vehicle is currently fitted with wheel
>spacers at the rear and I need to know if these should be removed or can I
>go ahead and use them with the larger rims.

The S4 rear wheels should fit without the spacer. Their rims are an inch
wider and they are offset half an inch less (52.3 v 65 mm) than the S
rears, so the outer edge is an inch further out from the mounting surface.
If the spacer were an inch thick, removing it and installing the S4 rears
would put their outer edges and the tires' outer sidewalls just where your
stock items are now, while the inner surfaces would be an inch further in.
