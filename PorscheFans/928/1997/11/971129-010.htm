Hmmmm... a couple of things could verify whether it is an SE, but the year seems wrong.  As far
as we have been able to establish the SE was only produced in 1988.  Also, the seats should be
leather with velour inserts, black with red pin-striping.  Other things to look for that point in the
right direction are the '90 GT-style wheels with different offsets, the spacers on the rear wheels,
and, of course, it must be a five speed.  If it is a genuine car that someone has converted to
LHD (they were only produced with the steering wheel on the right, sorry!) then it could well be
a good buy.  There's mine and about 148 others out there to choose from!  (And mine ain't for
sale!)
