Subject:Seat Adjustment Switch - The parts just kept coming!
Date:Sun, 9 Nov 1997 19:37:53 -0500 (EST)
I read that the switch for the seats can be tricky to fix, but I had a hour
or so to commit to the task. What could be so hard. I would remove the cover
and spray some tuner tonic on the contacts and be back to full seat control,
or so I thought. Imagine my surprise when the springs and bearings shot out
of the switch. Re-assembly of that bugger was a challenge! While I am happy
to report a fully functional seat, I just lived through a relatively
inexpensive lesson this new owner will not forget - fixing this car must
start with a tech manual!
