If you want a clean install but dont want to go crazy with the
modifications/customization, I would recommend putting the amps upside down
under the spare tire cover. make sure they are not touching the tire. If I
was doing the job i would also hook up a little 12v fan (radio shack) and
have it blowing up through a hole that I cut in the spare tire cover. Make
sure the fan is switched with the ignition etc..
You might want to be careful if you put it anywhere else back there because
you realize your plastic gas tank is right there next to your tire
compartment.
