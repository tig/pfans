So here goes.....new calculations.......

The diameter of a 245 x 50 tire is 25.65"
So, the circumference is 80.58"
Which makes these tires turn at 786 revolutions per mile.
Therefore........

4550 / 2.20 = 2068 rpm (axle)
2068 / 786 = 2.63 miles per minute
2.63 x 60 = 158 mph
