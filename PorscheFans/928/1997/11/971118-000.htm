For the MY 1990 at least the Owner's Manual states:

"...If wheel slippage of either of the front wheels or the rear wheels
is sensed during braking, the brake pressure is adjusted automatically
until the wheel no longer slips.  The brake pressure is regulated for
each front wheel individually and for both rear wheels together."

I recall Kim's article whereby he completely removes the rear
proportioning valve on his ABS equipped 928 but note that he was running
the factory rims with speed sensors.
