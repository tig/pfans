Find the fuse associated with that headlight from your owner's manual -
wiggle it, check continuity, spin it with your fingers to check
corrosion, push down gently on the top of the fuse clip while the fuse
is in place and watch the light come on!   I'll bet money you have the
classic 'intermittant fuse conductivity' gremlin.
