Speaking purely hypothetically, well-aimed powerful lights (head plus
driving; I've got a total of 400 watts coming out of the four apertures on
high beam) on a 928 should show you anything retro-reflective (e.g., cars'

nighttime speeds should be reserved (again hypothetically) for
limited-access roads.

As to the "safe" part of your question, the trick with high speed is
watching and thinking continuously. Relaxing and chatting while doing 90+
down a dark country road adds considerably, maybe lethally, to the reaction
time component, as witness your story.
