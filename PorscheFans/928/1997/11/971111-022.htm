Let me help you out with the reliability issue.  I debated between a 928 and 
an NSX myself.  That was until I asked about the maintenence on the NSX.

always have at least one of them in the shop at all times, usually more.

always been for routine maintenance.  The NSX is overly complicated and
requires a lot more maintainence.  To do simple work, the engine has to
be dropped to get to the top of it.  The Porsche is much better.
