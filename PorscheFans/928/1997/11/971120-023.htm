*** The heater/AC control unit on the console is illuminated by a light
source deep inside the control unit.  The light source light pipes the
light by using several fiber optics bundles.  It turns out (after
talking to DEVEK and 928 International that the light bulb is NOT a
service replaceable part. ***

I can't speak about the later cars, but on my 78 Euro the bulb is easily
replaceable. It too is a single bulb which illuminates the heater/AC
control panel from the back. The two fibre optic bundles go to the backs
of the slider knobs and illuminate them.

It's not even particularly difficult to replace once the drivers side
console cover is removed (the carpet covered side piece). No need to
remove the heater/AC unit.

Can anyone confirm that the later cars are way different?
