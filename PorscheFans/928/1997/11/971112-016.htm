Dear "Land Shark" tamers:

Replacing springs and perches that have no adjustment to ones that do can
(and should) be done. All it takes is time and $$. However, do not be
tempted to lower a 928 too much. Your alignment will be thrown out of wack
and your tires will wear prematurely on the inside edge. Try to keep the
height to factory tolerances. Mine is set at 5mm above the minimum.

Happy motoring. Ed
