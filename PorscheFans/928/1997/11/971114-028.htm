If anybody cares, my schematic shows 31 grounded (that seems to be
a standard, by the way) and 16 as the green wire which leads to
the tach, the fuel pump relay, and the injection control unit. And
the ignition unit gets its signal from the distributor points. Of
course later models use a flywheel pickup.
