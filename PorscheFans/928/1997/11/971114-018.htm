>Since picking up the car about 6 weeks ago, the stop lamp warning light

This is correct behavior, even though it seems a bit silly. The test
circuit shows the warning until you apply the brakes and permit it to
evaluate the brake light bulbs.

>When I turned off the power to the cell phone, the problem went away!

But it's not a problem, so I'm concerned that you've done more than
de-power the cell phone. Please check that your brake lights work, and that
your cruise control disconnects when you tap the brake pedal. If you've got
an automatic transmission, the brake circuit also helps the transmission
decide when to downshift.

The service bulletin appears to describe a situation in which the warning
comes back on.
