To my amazement (why am I amazed? These are Germans, after all) there is
a DIN standard, 72552, for auto electrical system terminal designations.
As noted, 31 is ground. Some others:
15 - switched +
30 - battery +, direct
81 - NC switch input
81a - NC switch out
85 - ground side of relay winding
86 - start of relay winding
87, 87a - NC relay contacts
88, 88a - NO relay contacts
and on and on.

The above is from the Bosch Automotive Handbook, which is an impressive
collection of occasionally useful information.
