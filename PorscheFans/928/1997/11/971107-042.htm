	When pulling on the hand-brake here is a fair chance that
one wheel will be slowed more than the other. At speed, this can
become very interesting - no ABS on the park brake. I well remember
wrestling with a Ford Falcon in which I'd locked up one rear wheel
pulling this stunt.  Needless to say, I haven't been game to try it
in the Shark.
	By the by, another use of the Hand Brake.  Lightly applied,
it can retard a spinning wheel enough for the other wheel to bite.
Useful when reversing up gravel drive-ways, or other slippery slopes.
