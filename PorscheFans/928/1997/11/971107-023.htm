Yep, that's the point. A locked wheel doesn't care which direction it
goes; no preference for the usual fore/aft directions. So if you lock
the fronts, you will just go straight no matter what you do with the
steering; if you lock the rears, the first litle disturbance will send
them off in some undesired direction, over which you have no control.
That's why we all have proportioning valves in the rear brake circuit.
