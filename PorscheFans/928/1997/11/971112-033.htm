When I go to the track one of the things I take along is a box of
disposable rubber gloves. Use a pair to put my track wheels on at
the beginning of the day, another pair to remount the street ones
at the end of the day. Makes things a lot easier than trying to
scrub all that filthy brake dust off my hands. Costs about
$5 for a box of 50, as I recall. Amazing how much sweat
accumulates in the gloves after changing four tires, though.
