At 03:10 AM 11/21/97 +0000, you wrote:
>Will fitting the stiffer Weltmeister springs reduce the Weissach
>effect?  ie Will there be less toe-in under cornering loads?

The Weissach axle depends on another, separate compliant member working
laterally (no sport seats involved), not vertically like the springs you're
replacing, so there shouldn't be any change there.

>If so, am I likely to experience any problems? eg Instability
>during braking on corners.

Not for Weissach reasons, but stiffer springs can lead to bouncy wheels on
less-than-perfectly-smooth surfaces if the shocks don't control them
adequately.
