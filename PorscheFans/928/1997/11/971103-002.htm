The light switch doesn't usually go that soon, but it could happen.
Usually the Window/sunroof switches seem first to go.  

Try re-plugging it into the wiring harness, perhaps that will help if
it's just loose there.  It could be someone before you was rough with
it, twisted it too hard or otherwise caused some damage.  

The round disk you found seems like the one to adjust the instrument
lights. The wiper delay dial is on the other side of the pod, also on
the bottom.
