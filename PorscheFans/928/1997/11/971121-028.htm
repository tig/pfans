It's my impression that a bad O2 sensor is unlikely to be the cause
of running problems. If you look at the Probst book on Bosch fuel
injection, the _only_ problem for which a failed lambda sensor is
listed as the probable cause is "Failed emissions test." If you're
at all worried about the sensor, just disconnect it; that should
have very little effect on the way the ngine runs (until you go
for the emissions test, anyway).
