If there's a bulb in there somewhere, there has to
be a way to remove it. Once you do, contact an
automotive lighting manufacturer. Almost every
bulb is available. I've even found 12 volt bulbs
to fit 6 volt sockets in old cars. Once you get
the bulb out, you will eventually find a place
that can replace it for you. Or, If worse comes to
worse, you may have to add a bulb fitting to the
wires, and attach a similar sized bulb, and glue
the fitting in place. Any of those solutions is
cheaper than $400. What a rip.
