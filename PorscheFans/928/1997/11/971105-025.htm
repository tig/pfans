At 03:44 PM 11/5/97 -0500, you wrote:
>My 84 Euro has the Eruo 2.72 ration tranny, I'm not an expert on the
>subject, but does this mean that it has a lower top speed than the US
>gearboxes?

It works like this:

- Your engine is limited to about 6500 rpm (assuming it has power to turn
that fast in top gear).

- In top gear, the total speed reduction from crankshaft to axle is your
final drive ratio (because there is no overdrive in the transmission), here
2.72:1. This means that the axle spins no faster than 6500/2.72 = 2390 rpm.

- Your rear tires turn about 840 revolutions per mile traveled (most
Porsche sizes fall within the 835-850 range; also, tires can "grow" a
little at extreme speeds). At an axle speed of 2390 revolutions per minute,
this means the tires are moving the car 2390/840 = 2.845 miles per minute.
Since there are 60 minutes in an hour, this makes the calculated top speed
2.845 x 60 = 171 miles per hour.

I'll leave it to you to decide how limiting that is.
