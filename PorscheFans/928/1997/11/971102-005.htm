Paul........there are MUCH stranger things than a faulty headlight
switch at only 50k miles that could happen!   I don't know if you
removed it yet, but possible the wire harness plug vibrated loose on the
back end of the switch - very easy to check.   Another possibility is
that one of the wires in the harness has a break right at the connector,
and vibration is causing it to open momentarily.   This is tougher to
find, but is certainly possible.

The black rotor wheel on your left knee is the dimmer for the dash
lights.
