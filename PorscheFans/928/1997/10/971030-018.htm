> You're aware that the manual calls for 20W50, not 10W30?
I put 20W50 in with my Spring oil change before I start driving it
again. It's been awhile since I looked, but I seem to recall the graph
in the '84 owners manual recommending 10W30 for cold temps.  I'll check
it again tonight to verify.  

Since most engine wear occurs at start up, it does makes sense to go
lighter in winter in order to get the oil pumped through the engine as
quickly as possible.  My 928 is not driven much over the winter, so I'm
not terribly worried about it.  If I put more than 800 miles on the
10W30 over the winter before changing it in the spring I've driven it
allot.  I only use it when the daily driver is laid up for service or
it's an exceptionally nice day with clear roads and I'm not at work
(very rare).
