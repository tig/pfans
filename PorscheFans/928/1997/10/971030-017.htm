> My parking lights seem to be dead shorted, or on all 
> the time when the ignition key is in other than the off position.
Start with the turn signal stalk.  If the signal is left engaged when
you turn the car off, the parking lamps come on.  Could be the switch, a
relay or a bad wire/contact along the way. I once read there are over 9
(yes nine) miles of wire in a 928.   

> I'm not that good at tracing electrical stuff. Any body got a 
> good procedure for isolating a portion of the lighting system?
Get a test light/meter and wiring schematics and check the flow of
voltage in and out of every switch with the light on and off till you
find something not working as it should.
