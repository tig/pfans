The front fenders and hood are aluminum, I'm pretty sure the doors are
as well.  
The rear fenders are steel as is the roof for "safety" as I read in a
Porsche brochure years ago.  I would think an aluminum roof is safer
than the roof of a 911 cab or Boxster...

A brochure on the 928 from the early 80's made a big deal of all the
heavy galvanizing and special primer on the steel parts to prevent rust.
 Aside from the hatch area, I've never seen rust on a 928.  

I think the hatch rusts due to leaves, dirt, debris getting under the
window trim and the rubber "S" spoiler.  It's a good idea to remove the
spoiler if you have one and inspect/clean the area.  I found allot of
grunge under mine and a rust spot I took care of a few years back.
