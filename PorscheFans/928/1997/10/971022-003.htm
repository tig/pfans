about 160 miles or 200 on highway, plus maintance.

tune up will cost $200.  Keep in mine that you need at least $2,000 to do all
the maintance that a 928 needs in the next 6 months.  You probly think I'm
crazy, maybe I'm or just given you a waring!  PARTS for 928 are 3X more than
Hondas, Toyotas, Fords, Chev....

for....!
