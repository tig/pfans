(Rich wrote:)
> What is the multiplier to determine HP at the crank off a dyno report
> which calculates HP and torque at the wheels?

  HP_at_the_wheels * 1.2 = HP_at_the_crank
  
> Before: 202.9 @ 5100 RPM with 208.9 ft-lb.
  Before: 202.9 * 1.2 = 243.48 at the crank
    
> After: 236.3 @5100 RPM with 243.4 ft-lb.  
  After:  236.3 * 1.2 = 283.56 at the crank
  
  The before numbers seem a bit high for a stock '80 US motor.
