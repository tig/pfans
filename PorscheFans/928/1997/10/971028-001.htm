Last time I mounted a camera in my 928, I removed the electric sunroof
service hatch from the roof of the car and suspended the camera under it,
zip tied to the sunroof motor. Worked a treat. With a wide angle lens, you
can see the whole road (minus the bits obscured by the headrests, but also
the driver, steering wheel and pedal area. Makes for interesting footage.
