It's really in several places.  From memory (bad) I recall that there is
a section on removing the instrument panel (speedo etc.).  You first
remove steering wheel, then instrument cluster cover, then look in A/C
section 'cause you have to remove the console and the dash to get at all
of the A/C stuff .... UGH! Remember to look for hidden bolts if
something doesn't move easily.  928 interior parts are easy to break and
expensive to replace. .

BTW another question someone asked which I forgot to answer (not
yours).  The steering wheel just needs a 27mm(I think) deep socket or
standard socket w/short extension - no puller required.
