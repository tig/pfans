Prior to '86.5 928's were equipped with a single piston caliper at all 4
corners.  The 4 piston caliper was used on '86.5 and later cars.  The
single piston caliper is considerably smaller than the 4 piston caliper,
and does not have the PORSCHE lettering on it like the 4 piston does (it is
not painted either).  I wouldn't think you have after-market calipers.
