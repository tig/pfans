After all of the "Kill of the Week" posts, I figured I would put out 
a "Save of the Week".  This is an occurance where the 928s 
talents and abilities saved me from a nasty accident rather 
than defeating someone in performance.  I was cruising along 
at 71mph down a six lane interstate highway when suddenly a 
deer shot out from behind a retaining wall and charged across 
the road.  I hit the brakes and swerved around behind him.  Few 
other cars can maintain control of a swerve at those speeds 
with the brakes hit hard.  The deer ran on across the road and 
jumped over another wall to the opposing three lanes where 
a truck struck it.  I was very thankful to have a car with such 
wonderful abilities that allowed me to get my speed down 
quickly, swerve, and maintain complete control throughout.
