My '85 belt light came on all the time when I first bought the car
(including the drive home).  The belt was tensioned about 4 times by the
dealer (in a week) until they finally agreed that something else was wrong.
Eventually they told me that the connector between the belt sensor and the
wiring was vibrating just enough to set off the sensor.  I'm not sure what
the fix was; they probably just secured it better.
