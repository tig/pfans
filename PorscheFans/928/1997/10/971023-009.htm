 the 4wd, Cosworth turbo-powered Sierra (parent of our short lived Merkur
XR4Ti) and a 928 GT.

 Strangely, given England's moister-than-Aunt-Margie's-chocolate-cake

indeed, the 4WD Cosworth's traction advantages.
