I had similar problems with my '88 928, but much more intermittant.  I would
take it to the service centre and they would adjust it and roadtest it all
okay.  Within a day of picking it up it would suddenly show the belt tension
warning again.  From what I remember it turned out to be a faulty damper in
the tension warning sensor, which meant it would vibrate at certain revs
enough to trip the warning light.  It took weeks to sort out.
