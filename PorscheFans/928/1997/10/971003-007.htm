The torque tube *might* but your chasis won't. The automatic chasis has 
a bulge for the torque converter that the manual chasis lacks.

If you try it the other way around you'll have the unfun task of adding
a clutch petal to an automatic.

So the Thing To Do in this case is to find a good 5spd that has been
rear ended, and a good auto that has the front smashed. And pay a chop
shop to weld the good ends together. Then you really would have something
to honor Dr. Frankenstein.
