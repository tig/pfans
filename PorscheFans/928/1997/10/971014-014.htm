Welcome to PorscheFans!

For an easy way to join, leave, etc. a list, visit the PorscheFans web site at http://www.PorscheFans.com/.  All Porsche related questions should be posted the appropriate list.

If there are list errors or you have an administrative problem with a list, you can send an e-mail to listmaster@PorscheFans.com.  Please include the list you are having a problem with, and a detailed explanation.  DO NOT send Porsche related questions to the listmaster!  Please address them to the appropriate list.

    General     Anything relating to Porsches, but not a specific model
    356         Porsche 356 and 912 Engine
    911         Porsche 911 and 993
    914         Porsche 914

    Flamers     If you need to sound off, this is the place to do it

SUBSCRIPTION INFORMATION:
=========================

The easiest way to join, leave, etc. a list is via the PorscheFans web site at http://www.PorscheFans.com/.  To go directly to the subscription page, surf to http://www.PorscheFans.com/Subscribe.html

There are two ways to get the messages from PorscheFans: digest or non-digest.  We recommend DIGEST mode.  This sends you a daily collection (or "digest") of all messages for that list.  Not only does a digest keep your in-box from getting REALLY cluttered, and it drastically reduces the load on our mail server.  Digests are compiled and sent out at midnight, Pacific time.  Non-digest mode means you get an e-mail EVERY TIME someone posts a message to the list you are on.

If you are already a member of PorscheFans, and you want to switch your subscription from regular to digest (or vice versa), you don't have to quit the list.  Just send either a JOIN (to switch to regular mode) or a DIGEST (to switch to digest mode), and your subscription will be changed.

If you try joining a list that you are already a member of, you are politely notified that you are already a member.  So sending multiple join requests doesn't subscribe you multiple times.  Just one per customer (or e-mailbox).

It is NOT necessary to specify your e-mail address in the message to subscribe@PorscheFans.com -- the sender of the message is the one subscribed.  If you specify a different e-mail address than the sender, the address specified is subscribed, not the sender.

Messages sent to subscribe@PorscheFans.com are automatically processed.  So don't send any questions to this mail address, as they are never viewed by human eyes.  The subject of these messages is ignored.

None of the e-mail addresses or subscription commands are case-sensitive.  Sending a message to SUBSCRIBE@PorscheFans.com is exactly the same as sending a message to subscribe@porschefans.com.

NOTE: If you are joining multiple lists, please make sure that each request is on a line by itself, and that there are NO BLANK LINES between the requests.  The automatic subscription processor ignores everything after the first blank line.  We also recommend that you put at least one blank space at the beginning of the line, as some e-mail software word-wraps outgoing text.  Only the first subscription request on a line is processed.

SUBSCRIBING IN DIGEST MODE:

To get a daily digest of a list, send an email message to subscribe@PorscheFans.com with the body:
    digest 
For example:
    digest 356
To join all the lists, send an e-mail message to subscribe@PorscheFans.com with this in the body of the message:
    digest General
    digest 356
    digest 911
    digest 914
    digest 924-944
    digest 928
    digest Boxster
    digest Racing
    digest Flamers
    digest Tavern

-------------------------------
SUBSCRIBING IN NON-DIGEST MODE:

To join any of these lists, send an email message to subscribe@PorscheFans.com with the body:
    join 

For example:
    join 356

To join all the lists, send an e-mail message to subscribe@PorscheFans.com with this in the body of the message:
    join General
    join 356
    join 911
    join 914
    join 924-944
    join 928
    join Boxster
    join Racing
    join Flamers
    join Tavern

-----------------------------
CANCELLING YOUR SUBSCRIPTION:

To leave this list (either regular or digest mode), send an email message to subscribe@PorscheFans.com with the message:
    leave 
For example:
    leave 356

If your email address has changed since you joined, you can append your original email address:
    leave 356 
For example:
    leave 356 joeschmo@doe.com

------------------------------------------
CHANGING YOUR SUBSCRIPTION E-MAIL ADDRESS:

To change where your subscription is being sent (for example, if your e-mail address has changed), you must un-subscribe from the list with your old e-mail address and re-subscribe with the new one.

For example:
    leave  356 joeschmo@doe.com
    digest 356 joeschmo@test.com

This can easily be done from our web page at http://www.PorscheFans.com/Subscribe.html

=========================
SUBSCRIPTION SUSPENSIONS:
=========================

The PorscheFans list processor will suspend your account automatically if it was not able to deliver to your mailbox after three days.  To re-activate your subscription, you must go through the subscription process again.

===========================
POSTING A MESSAGE TO A LIST
===========================

To send a message to the list, send an email message to
    @PorscheFans.com
For example, send an e-mail message to:
    Boxster@PorscheFans.com

You will automatically receive a confirmation when your message has been received.

====================
LIST COMMAND SUMMARY
====================

JOIN  []
    Join or subscribe to the list. A copy of each message sent to the list
    will be forwarded to the email address given.

DIGEST  []
    Join or subscribe to the list. A single message will be sent each day
    containing an indexed summary of all the messages that have been sent to
    the list in the previous 24 hours.

LEAVE  []
    Leave or unsubscribe from the list. If your address has changed since
    you joined the list, append your original email address to cancel the
    subscription to that account.

HELP []
    Provide help about the list server or about the specified list.
