Bill,
According to the Factory Workshop Manuals, lower ball joints were changed to
steel as of build date "September 1983".

A magnet would help but you could get a false reading from the mounting hardware
and eccentrics.

A better way is measure the thickness of the ball joint where it is mounted to
the lower arm. The aluminum is 7/8" thick and the steel is about 1/2" thick.
The steel is a lot thinner obviously because the steel is stronger than the
aluminum.

Hope that helps,
