I see there are some new potential 928 owners posting to this group.  Well,
I just thought I'd throw in my "Two cents worth" about my love affair with
my 928.  I'm a commercial-instrument pilot with a flight instructor rating
for single and multi-engine aircraft.  Before I purchased my 928, if it
came to a choice of whether I could fly or drive to a particular
destination I'd almost always opt to fly.  Now, it's almost a toss-up on
shorter distance destinations.  When a pilot says that, it has to be one
hell of a car.  BTW, to those of you who either fly or know airplanes, I
own a 1971 Bellanca Super Viking 17-31A so it isn't the choice of a Porsche
Vs. a Cessna 172 although I own a 172, too.
