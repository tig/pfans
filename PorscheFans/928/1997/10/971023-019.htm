This is a 4.7L Euro car.

Translation per Technical Spec book:

M2821 = 85 or 86 Euro or R.o.W. (rest of world) car or 86 Sweden or
Switzerland, manual trans
-82G
  8 = 8 cyl eng
  2 = 4.7l. R.o.W.
  G = 1986

G2810 = 85 or 86 5-speed manual R.o.W.
-11G = 1986 R.o.W.

These are correct numbers for a 1986 Euro or R.o.W. car.  Chassis number
(VIN) should be in the range WPO ZZZ 92 Z GS 84 0001-5000
