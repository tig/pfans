Be careful !! The grilles on the 8 speaker system unscrew like a jar lid, except
for the large speaker just behind the front seats which obviously have 4 screws.
However if you have the optional 10-speaker system which is in most MY88 and up
the grills snap on to the speakers, there are 2 slight indention's around the
outside edge where the grille and speaker meets. Use a flat head screw driver to
carefully pop the grilles off.
