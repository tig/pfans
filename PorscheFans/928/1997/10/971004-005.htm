Autosport Designs (http://www.smarttrac.com/autosport/index.htm) put my
car on their chassis dyno, hooked up their computer to the car's fuel
and
ignition computers, and proceeded interactively to reprogram both
computers from the ground up to optimize their operation to my taste
(keep the broad torque curve but raise it; sharpen throttle response)
for
my specific engine (there are always unit-to-unit variations).


They found me about 50 bhp more without any loss of smoothness, fuel
economy, or any other desirable characteristic. This was done well over
a
year ago and I remain most pleased.
