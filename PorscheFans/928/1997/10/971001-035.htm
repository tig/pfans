Colin,

Not sure if this is your problem, but there is a service bulletin that
describes a problem with rivets in the torque converter coming loose in
85, 86 87 cars. My 87 makes sort of a rattling noise at idle - worse
when cold. The regional service rep said that it's the rivets. The fix
is to drill out the 8 rivets and replace with bolts and Loctite. The
dealer in Chicago estimates $1000 for the work, so I decided to wait
until I'm SURE that the rivets are making the noise before having it
fixed.
