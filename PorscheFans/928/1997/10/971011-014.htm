David, it shouldn't be necessary if you go to a quality 
928-experienced shop. However, there are shops who do it using a portable balancer 
which is pushed against the tire. Shops don't like to do it because there's 
no protection for the operator against weights which could fly off and 
hit something or somebody. In the past, it was supposed to be the 
ultimate in tire/wheel balancing. I've used it on my 928 with no superior 
success over the new off-car balancing equipment.
