only real use for them is at startup when the engine is cold.  I think people

government to easily intrude into and restrict out lives in the name of
"concern for our well being."  Heck, maybe they do care - if we all killed
ourselves driving non-catalyst equipped cars who would pick up the tab for
the ridiculous spending and criminal waste of money that occurs everyday?
 And ya know, the air in LA would be a lot cleaner if there were no people
