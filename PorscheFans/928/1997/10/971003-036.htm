Ed Ruiz wrote:

> This is from section 44 of the manual. Front height from level ground
> with a
> car that has not been raised should be between 170mm to 190mm. The
> difference from side to side should not be more than 10mm. Measure to
> the
> tab at the bottom of the rear control arm support. This only applies
> to cars
> with adjustable perches.
>
> For the rears the height shoul be between 173mm and 183mm.
>
> To adjust the height DO NOT raise the car. Turn the wheel as far over
> as
> possible to gain access to the large adjustment nut. With the spanner
> wrench
> (or with an adjustable clamp mentioned at the Frenzy) turn the nut
> counter-clockwise to go up and clockwise to lower. Depending on your
> spring
> rate you will change the height by about 2-3mms per full turn. If your
>
> height is way off, I recommend that you change the height by several
> turns.
> Stop after an equal number of turns have been applied to both sides.
> Drive
> the car a few miles/minutes, and  re-check the height. Repeat the
> process
> until the car is within specs. Now get it aligned by a shop that does
> Porsches. DO NOT go to any discount alignment shops!
>
> Happy motoring. Ed
