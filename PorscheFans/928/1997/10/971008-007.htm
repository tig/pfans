Here are two hints for door panel removal:
1.  Look inside the armrest compartment for a few bolts (three or four I
think) and take them out.  You will then see a couple more to remove.
2.  You also have to remove the door lock knob.  Take out the center
part of the knob (unscrews?) then you will see the nut that holds the
knob on. 

> I am also looking for suggestions for glue to re-attach a broken plastic
> mounting tab to the long arm rest / vent piece.

I have used a two-part product called "Plastic-Weld" from Ace hardware.
Although It looks and works like epoxy glue, it really seems to be more
like a catalized plastic.  I used it to repair/rebuild and make new
attachment tabs for the end of the console (near seats).  Lean on the
console too hard and you will feel these tabs break.  Ouch!
