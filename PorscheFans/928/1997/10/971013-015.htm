The names and numbers are all in your local phonebook.  BUT ... they are very
expensive.  I paid only $825 from Delaware to Portland, $725 from Detroit to
Portland, $550 from Dallas to Portland, and $350 from LA to Portland.  This is
about half to a third lower than the retail.  I did this through an account I
had to ship cars. Basically, anyone in the car industry can get a deep
discount by calling enough people.  I found my shippers at the local car
auction.  

Try calling on someone you know that sells cars to arrange the shipping, it
will save you a ton of money.

Also, go over the car VERY well when it arrives.  I had several problems with
cars, but they were all promptly fixed.  Mostly minor, and one major.  Pay on
delivery if possible, then if there are problems they don't already have all
of your money.
