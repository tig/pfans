  The biggest difference between Euro and USA versions is engine 
  configuration.  Here's the breakdown (from memory - resident pedantics
  will verify the numbers):
  
  Model 928   '80-82 USA: 16v, K-Jetronic, 220 HP
  Model 928 S '83-84 USA: 16v, L-Jetronic, 234 HP
  Model 928 S '85-86 USA: 32v, LH-Jetronic, 288 HP
  
  Model 928   '80-82 Euro: 16v, K-Jetronic, ~250 HP
  Model 928 S '80-84 Euro: 16v, K-Jetronic, 300 HP
  Model 928 S '85-86 Euro: 16v, LH-Jetronic, 310 HP
  
  Suspension between Euro and USA are almost identical; the exception being
  that spring rates were varied to account for optional equipment in Europe
  (e.g. A/C) that was usually standard in USA.  Also, late '86 USA cars with 
  serial number greater than 1000 have 4-pistion S4 brakes.
  
  Transmissions were improved on '85-> 5-speed USA cars to facilitate
  easier shifts.
