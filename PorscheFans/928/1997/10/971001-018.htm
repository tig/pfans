Actually, the reservoir has separate feeds for the clutch and the
brake system. So you can drain the clutch and still have fluid for
the brakes, though with very little reserve. Also, remember that
there's a little float in the reservoir, which should cause the
brake warning light to come on when the fluid gets low. So yes,
the belt and suspenders are still intact, and I don't think Phil
was in quite the danger he felt.
