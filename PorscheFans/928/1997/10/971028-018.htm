VERY simple solution:  petroleum oils thicken when cold, thin when hot.

Synthetics much less so.  Switch the diff and trans oil out to Red Line
synthetic 75W90NS (the NS is important!).

Tech info on all Red Line oils is at www.redlineoil.com.
