First, slide under the car and check the wear...there's a little
inspection hole under the clutch (look for the clutch slave cylinder to
get oriented).  On the early cars, like my '82, the wear limit is when
the end of the rod begins to show in the inspection hole.

It'll take you 2 minutes to check it.
