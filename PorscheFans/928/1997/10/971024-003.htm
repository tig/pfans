The plastic fingers do not transmit the torque of the engine to the torque 
tube.  When the clutch pedal is depressed, the the throw out bearing pushes on 
the fingers which separates the clutch from the flywheel.  I now have over 
122k (not sure of exact figure -- y'all know how VDO odometers are) on the 
original clutch on Nastassja (an '83 5spd).  Included in this 122k is a summer 
of drag racing where at least four times a night I dropped the clutch.
