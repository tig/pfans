A thought concerning to "Cat" or not to "Cat".
Due to Porsches extensive R & D and engine development, the 87 and up 928 32v
engines were the first engines to develop the same power with and without "Cats"
That is why they could use the same engine type world wide.
Example: 87-89 were type 41-stick and 42 auto in all countries. Also of note,
even the higher performance 89 GT engine type 47 was the same power with or
without cats and used in all countries.
