Will,

Regarding your need to determine if your car has and alarm for the new master
key.
You should be able to have PCNA cut you a new master key. 
To order it, you'll need a copy of the car's registration and your driver's
license. The dealer should take that info and have a key cut based upon the
VIN on the registration. The VIN will tell them if it had an alarm and cut
the key to fit. Of course, this assumes that the car has the original locks
and hasn't been rekeyed. 

Just another option.
