Now that it's September, I just wanted to remind all 928 fans about the "928
Frenzy".

Purpose of event: To discuss various aspects of the 928. Kim Crumb will be
our main guest speaker. Marc and Susan from "Devek Performance" as well and
Lucky Ekman (the EK in Devek) will be there. Door prizes will be provided
compliments of "928 International" and "Devek
Performance". Also, a highly modified 928GTS may be available for our
viewing and listening
pleasure. 928 related parts, shirts, boutique items, posters and literature
will be on sale at PCA Potomac Goody Store prices. Lastly, there will be a
short discussion on the viability of forming a "928 club" that would augment
our PCA membership.

Date & Time: 9/27/97 from 9:00 a.m.to 3:30 p.m. Location: "Auto Sportsystems
Group" (ASG) in Fairfax, VA. The cost (with lunch): $5 for PCA members, and
$10 for non-member.
For more information, send an E-mail to:eds928s@erols.com or call me
at:(703)481-9252 [before 10 p.m. eastern please]. Registration forms and
directions to "ASG" will be sent to respondents. I will need the completed
form with a check for the appropriate amount made out to "PCA Potomac" by
9/20/97.

Hope to see you there and happy motoring. Ed Ruiz (86'928S)
