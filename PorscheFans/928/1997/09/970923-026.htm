Diego Wrote:
>I have been trying to remove the cap that protects the wheel bearing without
>success.

>Does anyone know a "trick" to remove the cap?  I would like to inspect the
>wheel bearing and decide if they need to be packed or replaced.

 Diego,
Go to your local Auto Zone, Advance Auto etc., Most of these places have loaner
tools that you can take out on a daily basis by leaving a deposit. Ask for a
Slide Hammer with a 3 pronged "claw" type attachment. Tighten the claws around
the recessed area of the cap and then with a few firm slides with the slide
hammer it should come right off.
