One thing to check is whether your latch motor cycles around to the
correct position when you release the switch which activates it. If
you just pull the switch momentarily, you should hear the little
motor continue to run for a second or so. If it doesn't, it may be
stopped in a partially (or completely) unlatched position. There is
a little in-line fuse in one of the wires leading to the motor, just
a few inches from the motor; if it's blown, the motor won't cycle
to its home position.
