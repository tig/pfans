The "928 Frenzy" list of activities is expanding day by day. Today I spoke
with Dave Roberts  who will bring to the event parts from a dismantled 928.

that I'm beating my own drum, but I do not do so for any financial reward.

providers of the tables and chairs, the selected lunch supplier, and the
providers of the 928 items to be sold at the event. You as a participant
will gain knowledge that would otherwise take years and lots of money to
accumulate.
