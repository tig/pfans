Here is an update regarding service manuals.  

I've done some reading.  The US service manuals cover US, Canada, 
Japan and some European stuff (not enough, though.).   They aren't
the same as the UK or Japan (no right-hand drive stuff).  I didn't 
see any Euro specific stuff in the electricals (rear fog lamps, 
side lights, etc).  Furthermore, the pre-S4 head lamp
section only covered the old sealed beam units.  There were references 
to "unconnected" wires, probably due to one loom used for all the cars.

Only $170 - $130 more then here in the US (+ shipping????).

So, for you Euro owners, it looks like buying the English version
from Holland is the way to go ...as said by Math below.
