I too have a 1990 S4 that had a musty smell coming from the vents. I turned
out to be a leak in the high intensity washer resivour. There was some tubing
coming out of the resivour that was leaking some of the high intensity fluid.
The location (firewall in front of the driver) happened to be near the fresh
air inlet. The smell was that fluid. Patching the lines solved the smell.

Hope that helps!!
