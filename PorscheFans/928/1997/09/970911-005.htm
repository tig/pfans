David Ohlemacher wrote:
> 
> Three questions about brakes on an 86 928S(has the S4 upgrade):
> 
> 1) Is it as easy to change the brake rotors on the 928 as my VW Golf
> (two bolts and a screw)?

Yes.  All the brake job I did (Porsche , Acura and Honda) required
the same procedure.  Having seen the S4 brake at my mechanic, I would
say
that the 2 bolts and 2 screws is all that is required.

> 2) Do you have to change the pads when you change rotors?

You don't have too but you should.  If your pads are already grooved,
you
might start grooving your new disk.  At the very least, I would sand the
pads
flat to insure I do not groove the disk.

> 3) Can you replace the vented with drilled&vented? If so, from who?

Why do you want drilled disk?  I have been told that except if you go
all out racing, the cross drilling will not be very usefull.  Also, the
disk will wear out faster as you have less material to wear out...  If
it is for look, then go ahead.  It does look sharp to see the big
caliper
and the drilled brake through the wheel.
