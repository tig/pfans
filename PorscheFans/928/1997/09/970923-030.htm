A comment on the subject of dropping collision insurance.  Usually Ins.
Co's require a combo of collision and comprehensive, but I asked mine(
USAA) and they agreed to let me carry comprehensive w/o collision. I
strongly recommend that all carry comp if you drop collision. This
protects v. theft, non driving damage of all kinds, glass!! and all
manner of evil happenings.  Another effective way to lower insurance
cost is to raise your deductible way up....$1000 or 1500. It really
drops the premium.
