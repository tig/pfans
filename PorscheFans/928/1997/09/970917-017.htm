Jerry,
I believe this comes from an incorrectly performed alignment.  Like other
German cars, a Porsche does not immediately "settle" after being lifted into
the air (which alignment shops do).  Therefore, even though your car may be
aligned perfectly on the rack, once you drive it a few miles the suspension
will settle and the car will prematurely wear the inside front tires.  Don't
feel bad- my 87 does it too.
