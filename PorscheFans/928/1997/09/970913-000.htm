That's quite unfair! I have to read English instruments in my 928 (except for
the 24-hour clock), even though I (less and less) speak German... ;-)

Here are some translations:

Gas & Temp instruments:
	Schlusslicht -> rear light
	Bremslicht -> brake light
	Wa. Wasser -> (windshield) washer fluid
	Ku. Wasser -> coolant

Oil & Battery instruments:
	Bremsflussigkeit -> brake fluid
	Bremsbelag -> brake pad
	Bremskreis -> no idea what the English term is for this - I'd have to look
it up. Brake systems of modern cars usually have two "Bremskreise" -
independent hydraulic systems, that allow you to you brake (on two wheels),
if one of them fails.
	Oelstand -> oil level

Feel free to e-mail me, if you need further translations.
