Shoot!  Yeah, I wrote it in Word and then pasted into Netscape.  Should
be 180 [d(back)-d(front)] /(25x2x12xpi)  where d is in inches.  Here we
just approximating the sine of the angle as the angle in radians and
then converting to degrees.  To get toe measured in inches (for a 25"
tire diameter), just do 25x[d(back)-d(front)] /(25x2x12).
