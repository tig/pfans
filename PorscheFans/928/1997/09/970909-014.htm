> 2, Should I use an additive, Slick 50, T Plus.

DO NOT USE ADDITIVE IN YOUR OIL!!!  Any additive that contain teflon is 
even worse.  Dupont, the original maker of teflon, recommend AGAINST
using
it in motor oil.  The stuff can decompose and produce hydrofluoric
acid...  
enough said.

that point,
the pressure will go down to 2-3 bar at iddle and pegged at 5 bar at
about 3000 
rpm.
