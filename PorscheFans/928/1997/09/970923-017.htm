I've been enjoying the posts on "kills of the week" and it reminded me of
my first encounter with a 928 when I became the kill of the week.  In a
way, you may be contributing to increased future interest in the 928 when
you make your "kills"...

It was about 10 years ago as I was pulling out of a toll booth here in
Richmond that I noticed in my rear view mirror a light green S4 as it was
pulling up to the gate.  I had an '85 Mustang GT that I had recently
stomped a 911 with so I thought I was pretty fast.  I floored it just to
let the 928 driver hear the power of my Ford 5.0 liter, 4 barrel engine.  I
figured that'd be as close to me as he'd get.  I kept it floored thru first
and as I shifted into second gear, I checked my rear view mirror.  The 928
was way back, just getting out of the toll booth but I could see he was
leaving FAST.  This toll both was on an 8-lane expressway with little
traffic at the time so we had lots of room to run.  Well, the next time I
looked in my mirror, he was right behind me.  I still had it floored,
getting ready to shift into 3rd.   I knew I was toast and as he went by me
at 90+ mph, I looked over at him.  I think he was tuning his radio or
something - he just blew by me like I wasn't even there and he was just
driving routinely.

Right then and there, with my mouth still agape, I started formulating a
plan to one day own one of those things.
