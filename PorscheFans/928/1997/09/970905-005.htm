gaskets, valve job...).

could probably buy 2 Lingenfelter engines for the equivalent. There. Now I
can go 200K miles, just like a 928 motor! Oh yeah. All the while, if there
are any problems whatsoever, the cost to repair/replace is at least one
fifth and sometimes upwards of one tenth.
