Andy,

  Your euro has K-Jetronic, not L.  Euro models went from K directly to LH
  (in '85 I believe) whereas US models went K, L, then LH.  Rather than 
  Chilton's, I would recommend  "How to Tune and Modify Bosch Fuel 
  Injection" by Ben Watson.  This book is fairly easy to follow and has a
  lot of detail about each of the different Bosch systems.
  
  Leonard also recommends another Bosch FI book, but I don't recall the
  title at the moment.
