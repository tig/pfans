The previous response about the vacumn line near the brake booster 
being off is a possibility - without this vacumn source, the heater 
control valve will not close and hot water will be running through 
the heater core which is just in front of the AC evaporator core 
located just behind the upper center (bulkhead) vent - so hot air in 
front cold air in back.

But I really think the heater control valve (vaumn actuator) is 
probably broken and you need a new heater control valve ~$50.  To 
test, remove the air filter box and find the heater control valve 
just underneath the air filter box right-center (passenger side) - 
connect a vacumn pump to the heater control valve and pull a vacumn - 
if the actuator does not pull closed and stay closed this is your 
problem.

This is a common problem and an easy fix.
