I am no longer enamored with the Sport Contacts.  They go away MUCH too
quickly.  I believe I will lucky to get about 10-12 K out of the rears.  The
fronts are a different story, but if I switch brands on the rear, the fronts
fall to the same fate.

I also notice that it is exceptionally easy to spin the rears on fast starts.
 This, undoubtedly, contributes to the poor mileage.  If I paid $50 more,
each tire, could I reasonably expect to get 20-25 K out of them?  Anybody
else have any comments on tire mileages?
