Greg:

Only you know what your preferences are. Do you want show effects,
uncompromised handling/performance, or uncomplicated everyday use?

Just keep in mind that 18" wheels will not leave much rubber to soften your
ride - Whith those wheels and the wide tires, your 928 would most likely try
to follow every groove on the road, and especially when hitting a pothole you
will feel sorry for your your car (and your back).

Regarding lowering: The S4 is already quite low, and even though I'm careful
I feel I already bottom out too often. Check your front spoiler and belly
pan. No scratches? Then lower your car - it's time to get some... ;-)

Personally, if I wanted a stiffer ride, I'd go with maximum 17" wheels, and a
spring/shock combination that wouldn't lower the car much.

But that's just what I would do...
