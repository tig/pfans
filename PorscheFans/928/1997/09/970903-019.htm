Recently I have been treating my gearbox like
a fuel tank .  I have a serious leak somewhere
in it, an internal seal.  Every month I put through
about four litres of ATF.  So when it gets low in fluid, I
know when the car struggles from 1st to second,
especially when cold, and this does start to get a little
embrassing, I just refill it.
The best option is just to get the thing reconditioned.  What
this exercise does prove is that these gearboxes (3 stage auto)
are incredibly robust. I know its going to be exspensive and
I'm going to get the right people to do it.  But before i do this has
anybody expriemented with different gearboxes in an 928s, either
getting a 4-stage auto or a five- stage if you can, is it worth while.

I would be interested to know if anybody has done anything like this.
I really like autos and don't want to get a manual.
