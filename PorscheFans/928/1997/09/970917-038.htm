 Colin,
Look under the hood just behind and your power steering reservoir and inside
your inner fender liner. If there is a large black thing with brake lines going
into it you have ABS, if not, you do not. ABS was an option on your car and not
standard so most of the wiring and relay sockets are in place, but not used.
Some PO could have put a relay in the socket to try and get the ABS to work, you
never know.
