Hi Steve:  I just read your call for an automatic t-shift handle, and I'll
tell you what I  did when my shift handle in my '85S gave up the ghost last
spring:  A new shift handle from PCNA was about $500 Canadian, and I
thought that was a pretty stupid price to be paying for fifteen dollars
worth of plastic and one spring.  My local aftermarket shop had a leather
911-sized "Momo Anatomic" knob on sale for $49.00, so I decided it was
worth a shot.  We reamed the inside of the knob slightly, then disabled the
reverse lockout, and the knob fit on perfectly.   There's no lockout, but
then again, there's no lockout between reverse and first on an old 911/914
box either.  The upside is that you can manually shift though all four
forward gears without searching for that dumb button.  And it looks way
better than that old ugly t-handle, too.
