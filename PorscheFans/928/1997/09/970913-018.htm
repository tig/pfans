 Roy,
Did you just pour it in your reservoir?

If you did it will take a while before it will work it's way into the coolant
system.

The best way to get Water Wetter into the system is to drain a couple of quarts
from the radiator,  then pour in the water wetter and top of with water..

My guess is that the water wetter is still all in your
ervoir.;-( 

P. S . Water Wetter seems to work best with a 25% Anti-freeze / 75% water mix.
