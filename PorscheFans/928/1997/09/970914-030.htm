Not always. It varies by state. Often a personalized tag expires every 
year and will be replaced with a plain tag if the additional fee isn't
paid.

In Alabama the regular tags are replaced every 5 years. But it wasn't
15 or 20 years ago when all tags were replaced every year, and everybody
had to do it the same month. Now the tags expire during a particular
month based on the owner's last name, but only a sticker is issued for
4 of the 5 years.
