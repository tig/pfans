I have recently been all through mine.  The PO got a junkyard
replacement and it had a different key.  I'm kind of anal about
carrying loads of keys, so I took it apart and re-keyed it.  I don't
recommend the procedure, but that's just me. . .

The mechanism is made of die-cast metal and plastic.  My fear is that
you broke some plastic.  May be a spring.  You can EASILY take the
mechanism off and apart with little fear.  A junk yard replacement
can be used with your good lock cylinder. (Don't drill out the pin to
open the tumbler unless you're serious about re-keying!)  Two bolts
and a wire plug (alarm) secure it in my 1983 928S.

Good luck !
