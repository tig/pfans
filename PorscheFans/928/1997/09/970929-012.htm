>With Dunlop SP8000's the car was an absolute pig in the snow.

Part of this is a compromise made by the makers of top-performance tires,
the compounds of which tend to get hard and slippery at temperatures below
freezing. There are speed-rated snow tires available in standard 928 sizes
(I have a set of Michelins on my original rims).

The prospective 928 buyer would also be well advised to find a car with
limited-slip differential (option code 220); good traction enhancement
winter and summer.
