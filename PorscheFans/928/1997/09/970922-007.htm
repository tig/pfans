Gene,

It would be easier to pay someone $40-$50 and have them spend 30 min and get
your car back aligned, but that would ONLY happen if the car was already
straight.  I watched a guy (Porsche trained 928 mechanic) align and adjust
ride height on my car and it took about 5 hrs in total to do the whole job.
 It is not as simple as bolting on few devices and turning some screws...many
times as you adjust one setting it throws another setting off.  So there is a
lot of checking and rechecking that must take place and must be checked
thoughout the movement of the tire (turning movement).

Be careful with the quick and easy fixes out there...sometimes they cost lots
more in the long run.
