I have the same problem.  There is evidence of a bead of silicone around the
headlight.  I would guess that the glass and metal on the light are somehow
bonded and that bond can break down over time.  I would suggest that you
remove the light, remove the bulb, then let it dry out completely and seal it
up with silicone or similar.  If you get any other responses that explain the
problem, or have a better solution, please copy me in on them.
