In my post on the difficulty and expense of doing a rear S4 conversion on
an early 928, I left out an important point: the exhaust opening is in an
entirely different place on the later car.

I found the best solution was to adapt the S4 final muffler to the pipes
from the catalyst (I had previously updated to an '86 dual catalyst). To do
this required fabricating 's' shaped pipes, but the mounting points for the
original muffler work fine.
