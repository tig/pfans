I have used with great success, Ditzler's AcryliClean.   Can be
purchased in quart or gallon sizes from most any auto paint supply
house.  It's purpose is to remove grease and wax from body panels just
prior to painting.  It will not hurt paint,  rubber or plastics and will
not leave that chalky residue.  In short, it should do the trick for you
- you may need to use a fine brush (I like to use toothbrushes for this
size job) to insure that the cleaner gets into the crevices.

Good luck.
