First Steps:
During our initial conversation I will discuss with you exactly what you are
looking for in an automobile.  How you plan to use the car, your preferred

Locating Process:
After our initial conversation I will begin searching for your car based on
the criteria you have given me by utilizing various automobile publications
and the Internet, as well as several automobile dealers and wholesalers that
I have active business relationships with.  Usually it will take me from
three to seven days to find between five and fifteen cars that fall within
your specifications.  Before I am in touch with you again I will call the
owners of each of these automobiles and obtain all of the important
information on each of them.  Next comes your decision - which of the cars
that I have found interests you the most.

Final Steps:
After you decide which cars you have the most interest in you then must also
decide how involved you would like me to be in the purchasing process.
 Although I always try to locate automobiles that are within driving distance

have pictures or a video of the car sent to you, as well as have a
pre-purchase inspection performed on the automobile by an honest and
reputable third-party mechanic.  If time or finances limits you from
personally flying or driving to take possession of the car then door to door
delivery can also be arranged.
