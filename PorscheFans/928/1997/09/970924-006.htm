Stephane,
>1. We discussed changing the hose to the windshield nozzle a while back

>My question is how do you get the electrical wires through
>that little hole if you have a nozzke at one end of the wires and a big
>connector at the other end?

Take a small jewelers screwdriver and depress the tab on the metal connector
inside the plastic connector, while firmly pulling the wire w/metal connector
out of the plastic connector from the wire side. Do the same for the other wire.
Then run the loose wires through the holes on the hood service cover and
reinsert into plastic connector

>2. To remove the windshield washer reservoir, do you just remove that 1
>bolt on the bracket
>or is there some other bolt that is not visible to the naked eye?
Yes , just one bolt. However some years have a hose going into the top of the
reservoir which you need to remove in order pull the reservoir all the way out.
