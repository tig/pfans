 snooping around today I discovered the blades of one of the fans was 
jammed by the hose from the air pump filter. The hose is supposed to be 
routed around the fan housing thence back down under the engine on the 
passenger side. After re-routing it I was ecstatic to find the fan now 
operates normally, motor was not burned out. This explains my complaints 
re recent higher coolant temps. I'm sure this is a leftover perk from the 
recent cam belt change. Something to be aware of.
