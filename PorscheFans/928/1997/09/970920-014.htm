Steve,
Since our exalted expert Leonard seems to be quiet today, I will humbly try to
help you out. ;-)
In UP-FIXIN der PORSCHE, Vol. VII, page 123 it reads " ... use check valve part
number 810.906.093 and cap nut part number N011 0691"
This information was written a few years ago so check with your local Porsche
Parts Dude (or Dudette) to make sure it is the most recent part number.
BTW it is a replaceable part and simply screws into the output nozzle of your
fuel pump.
