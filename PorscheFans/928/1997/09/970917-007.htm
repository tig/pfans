Here's my experience:

1984 928 S (4.7 liter w/ automatic)  145k miles:  15.03 @ xx mph I
forgot.  low 90's?
1990 928 GT (5.0 liter w/ 5-speed)  81k miles:   14.25 @ 99.3mph (spun

top-ending you know :)

But like they say, how fast do you want to go?  A 6-liter 928 S4 would
do 12's easy and close to 200mph I'd say, all with stock gearing.
