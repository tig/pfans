Not possible (without slicing the body into pieces and trying to weld it back in
mirror image) , left and right drive chassis are totally different. They are
mirror imaged.  It is hard to explain , but if you can look at a stripped
chassis it will be painfully obvious, for example where all of the steering
column supports and pedals need to go on the right drive is where all of your
fuses,relays, fan blower, glovebox, etc.etc.etc. The sheetmetal and supports are
just not there or totally different.
