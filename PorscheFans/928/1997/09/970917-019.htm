In response to a question and an answer on 18" wheels, I would have to echo
Jorg's response. I have set up several 928's with 18". This includes first
year 78, 89S4, and GTS. The earlier the year, the harsher the ride. In
comparing the S4 back to back with 17", there was no appreciable handling
benefit, yet a punishing ride. The 18" on the front also caused a "bump
steer" like tendancy that was very anoying. 

Consider a 17" seriously before opting for the 18"
