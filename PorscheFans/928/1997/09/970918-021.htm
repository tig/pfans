From what I have seen that seems to be a common problem on 928s and 944s (I
assume 911s also). At first I thought it strange that the speedometer still
works but the odometer doesn't.
I have taken a few speedos apart when this happens and there is a small gear
that is driven by the odometer motor gear that looses some of its teeth or
breaks in half . I have yet figured out how to replace just the gear because of
the way it is installed. In all cases I have just replaced the speedos with good
used units.

Maybe someone out there has figured out how to replace that stupid gear with out
messing up the rest of the unit and can enlighten us to the procedure..
