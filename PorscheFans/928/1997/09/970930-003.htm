For what I know, Your brake can squeek for 2 reasons:

1. The compound used on the pad is noisy (especially some race pads)
2. Your pad does not sit perfectly right in the caliper and vibrate.

I am not sure how to fix #1 without changing the pad but #2 is normaly
resolved by putting some form of goo (Available at all autopart store)
that you apply to the BACK of the pad.  This stuff forms a rubbery
backing to the pad and eliminate (or greatly reduce) the brake noise.

You might want to check if the "goo" (Sorry, I forgot the technical 
term) has been applied and if not, try to add some and see if it helps.
