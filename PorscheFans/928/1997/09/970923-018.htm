>Can anything go out of adjustment with an automatic transmission?

If you have been reading the list for several weeks, you may have read about 
my recent tranny woes after the intercooler split pumping ATF into the 
coolant and vice-versa. After getting my car back, it was shifting into gear 
hard as well as hard into 2nd. Took it in and my mechanic adjusted the 
modulator which evidently controls the pressure on the clutches. It shifts 
fine now.

With that said, you might take your car in and have the fluid level checked, 
see if the filters and bands need replacement or see if there is adjustment 
left in the modulator.
