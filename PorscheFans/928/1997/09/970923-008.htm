clubs etc. He decided that he goes through so much rubber, that he could =

asked him if the higher levels of dust in the oil when using K&N was =

not important.

The synopsis is this: although there are higher levels of small dust =
particles in the oil, they are of such a minute nature that they are =

can see them does not mean that they will damage your engine.

He is very adamant about K&N - he talked about the benefits, not only =
for his two vettes (85 and 97', neither anywhere close to stock) but for =

without doing any of the inexpensive maintenence on the K&N, the K&N is =
STILL 12% better than a new paper filter, and 40% better than a paper =
filter with 30k miles on it.

He does take care of his vehicles, and he is quite a sharp guy, so if =
