Lowering a car ( or 928) can be good for you.  with few exceptions. 
UP SIDE  It looks good, handle better and cornering at ease, avoiding
accidents. fast tight turns.  JUST LOOK  DAME GOOD when
lowered!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!

DOWN SIDE toward lowering a 928, first thing to go is the front spoiler lip.
 then the underside of the hood accessaries, AC compresser, belt tension
holder, oil pan, sway bar, etc... anything below 3'' clearance will cause
headach if not careful.  Needs new aligment and look out for bumps and hills.

cost: about $400 to lowered with springs 

Then again, there is a BIG bill for fixing under the hood.if not careful.
