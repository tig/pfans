Hello Pfans,

Regarding Barry Gordon's synthetic oil question, I certainly recommend it. 

For the past several years, all new Porsches left the factory filled with
synthetic oil.  My synth-filled '93 GTS factory specified oil change
interval is once every 15K miles or once per year, my pre-synthetic '84 was
specified as every 7.5k miles (or every 6 months).  If you're driving in
extreme or dusty conditions, I'd recommend changes twice as often.

Regarding oil change intervals, given that the 928's oil capacity is about
twice the normal car, and even if a 3K mile interval "frame of mind" is set
the 928 could go at least 6K miles.  Why, the oil only gets dirty only half
as fast.
