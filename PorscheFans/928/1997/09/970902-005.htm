Steve,

  This will probably be unrelated, but may still be worth investigating.
  Up until Sept '83 (VIN 92 ES 860404), aluminum ball joints were used at 
  the lower end of the spindles.  These were apparently a very weak link and 
  have been superceeded by steel replacements.
  
  I'm not positive on how these ball joints act when they fail (although
  I assume it would be catastrophic), but at the very least they would
  probably make steering difficult.
