I know the feeling. I've had enduring cold start problems twice now in my 2
year 928 ownership.

Both times were traced to connectors on various fuel system components
having split rubber boots, allowing moisture to get in and subsequent
corrosion.

It would be worth your while popping the connectors off (one by one) and
checking for copper oxide! If you do find one, clean it up, then fill the
split boot up with sealant.
