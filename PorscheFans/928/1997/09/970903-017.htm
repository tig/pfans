Been there.  During an autox, lost P/S after a very tight left hairpin,
but only to the left, right was fine.  Checked fluids, belts, etc, all
OK.  My mech knew what it was immediately; a broken spring about one
inch long in the rack.
There are two of these springs in the unit, one was broken.  The shop
replaced the broken spring with a good one from a shelved unit with 
the same problem, and that wasn't too expensive.  I have autox'd since
with no problems.
