Nifty little trick for you:

Remove your locks.

Cut some holes in the top flap of a big cardboard box. Try to make these
holes approximate the holes in the doors where the locks insert.

Put the locks into these holes in the top of the box.

Place a nickle very carefully over the middle circle where the key inserts.

Spray away!

(yes, I know...defacing currency...but it's a perfect size masking!)
