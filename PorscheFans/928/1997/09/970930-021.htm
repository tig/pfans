> The brakes on my 87 S4 are very noisy...(snip) Is there any cure for this?

  Pads generally squeal because the back of the pad is vibrating against
  the piston.  I cured this on my wife's car by applying a paste to the
  back of each pad.  I bought the paste at a Mercedes dealer for
  25 cents/tube (each tube covered two pads).
