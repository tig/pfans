At 05:36 PM 9/3/97 -0400, you wrote:
>My car's leaking oil around the oil pan gasket.  I checked the bolts
>and they were loose.

I am reliably informed that this happens on its own, so you should check
these bolts whenever you change the oil or are otherwise in the vicinity.

>Does anyone know the proper torque for these?

Yes. Invest $9 in the factory Technical Specifications book (from any of
the mail order houses or http://www.my-porsche.com/link2.htm#tech), or $250
in the shop manual, and you will too. There are lots of specific torque
recommendations in these references, but these bolts fall into the "other"
category, for which the recommendations are:

	M6	6 lb-ft (8 Nm)
	M8	15 lb-ft (20 Nm)
	M10	30 lb-ft (40 Nm)
