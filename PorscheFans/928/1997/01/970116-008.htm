Phil Tong gave final drive ratios going back from the GTS to the 1987 S4.
Here's some more (only 5 speeds listed):

85/86 S =       2.20:1
83/84 S =       2.25:1
78-82 =          2.75:1

The Euro models retained the 2.75:1 final drive until at least 84, and I
believe they kept it there through 86.

All of these cars had a 1.00:1 (direct drive) fifth gear, but the 1st
through 4th ratios changed almost yearly.  1985 was the switch to the
Borg-Warner synchros (vs. the original Porsche style), the 1987 S4 saw the
switch to a single plate clutch, and 1983 was the first year of the reverse
gear block out.
