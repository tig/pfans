Has anybody installed the Devek big brake upgrade on an S4? I'm 
considering it and wonder if it makes much of an improvement over the 
already very good S4 brakes. 

If you haven't heard of this upgrade, it involves replacing the front 
rotors and calipers with the big rotors and red Brembo calipers used on 
the 928GTS and current 993 Turbo.  It is apparently an easy swap and 
they give you a $200 credit for your old calipers towards the $1195 cost 
of the upgrade.  You also need 17" wheels - I just got those Borbet GTS 
replica wheels - they look sweet and are relatively cheap as far as 
aftermarket wheels go.
