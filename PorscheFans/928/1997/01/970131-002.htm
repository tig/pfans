Skater44 asked on 31 Jan about under hood insulation.  My '86 928S had a
waffle urethane foam which desintigrated slowly. I was unable to patch or
glue any foam to it.  Factory replacement pad has the adhesive already
attached and is ready to install. This covers cars from '86 to '92.  I have
no knowledge of earlier years.  Part cast is about $95.   Dealer installed
mine.  Worst problem is getting the old pad off.
