I did it by the shop manual, and it was fairly easy, just time-
consuming. Make sure you do a good job of covering the fender, you
will spend a lot of time leaning over it, maybe spilling brake
fluid on it, etc. As I recall, one hose - probably the vacuum hose
to the brake booster - refused to come loose after ten or twelve
years of being baked in place. The replacement was actually one
of those rare jobs when there are no surprises.
