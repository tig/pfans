> I have an 89 S4 928. Last week the ABS warning indicator came on after I hit
> a bump. When I turn off the car are restart it the ABS system is OK,
> however, as soon as I hit a bump the light returns.

I had a similar problem with my '88. The problem was that enough gunk
had built up on the left front ABS magnetic pickup that it couldn't see
the trigger go by. Doesn't quite sound like your problem, but it's cheap
and easy to check. Couldn't hurt to clean them up anyway.

> I tested the breaks with the warning light on and the ABS are not working.

Once the ABS system detects a malfunction it shuts itself off so as not
to interfere with the power brake system. I'm not sure exactly what
electronics are involved.
