Re: what filter to use on a 928?

there was some good info on the bmw mail list a while 
ago on the difference between filters for the bmw m10
engine (the old SOHC four). One enterprising individual
cut the filters from three different manufacturer's
apart, measured the amount of filter material. Also 
noted any design and construction differences.

Bottom Line: Mahle was superior, Fram was a close second.
On this application, the Mahle has a pressure release
valve built into the filter, which makes it a superior
filter to the Fram. Also more expensive. (btw, the filter
pressure release valve was/is a precautionary backup for
a sticking pressure release valve in the oil pump.)

Bottom line:

Stick with OEM - and if your curious why, chop some 
filters up and see for yourself.
