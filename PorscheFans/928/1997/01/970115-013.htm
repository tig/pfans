I have a pressure bleeder I built out of a hand sprayer, a reservoir
cap from a junkyard (a VW cap, I think, you know the Germans would
standardize that sort of thing), and some hardware from a marine
store. Makes it really easy to bleed the clutch and brakes. Just
connect it, pump up a little pressure, open the bleeder (with a hose
on it), gently depress the pedal a couple of times, and close the
bleeder when bubble-free fluid starts coming out. I never had much
luck with the vacuum bleeders that suck fluid out of the bleeder
valves.
