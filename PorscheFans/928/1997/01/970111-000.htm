> What is your esthetic opinion on the 928 S's rear spoiler?  

I think the S spoiler integrates fairly well with the design, if a
little low-key.  Non-spoilered cars look too 'elemental' for me.  The
big craze (for those that want to modify) is the S4 spoiler, painted to
match or not.  I read that the first S4 spoiler was foldable but don't
know anymore about that.  I've also heard of aftermarket wings, with or
without the stock S spoiler, similar to the Mustang SVO bi-plane
spoiler.  The trick is to not make your car like any of those winged
Honda's running around.  While those have tons of enthusiasts (in the
US), they have failed to inspire me (home market also, where you'd be
considered nuts to mod a Honda).
