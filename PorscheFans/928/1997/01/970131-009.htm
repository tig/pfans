Walt:

Regarding the Automation Hood Thermo Mat, I bought one for my '88 S4 
about a month ago -- the original foam pad had begun to deteriorate 
(think crumble).  The Automation product has met my expectations.

The appearance is improved -- the shiny foil surface looks "sharp" when 
you show off whats under the hood.  Noise dampening is fine.  Thermal 
insulation... I haven't measured but seems to work.

Only trick here is installation.  I ultimately enlisted the help of my 
11-year daughter to achieve perfect positioning of the mat.  You 
probably only have one shot at getting it on straight -- the adhesive 
backing is very sticky.

Good product.
