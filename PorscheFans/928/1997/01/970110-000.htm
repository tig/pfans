The 928 rear ratio isn't really all that high (numerically low).  5th in the 
5sp is a direct 1:1 rather than an overdrive of .8:1 found in most other 
5spd's.  What you want to look at is the final drive ratio which is takes into 
account the transmission and rear ratio.  In this case, the 928's  rear of 
2.21 looks much better.
