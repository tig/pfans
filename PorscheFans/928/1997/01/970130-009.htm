Oops! I think I know that you mean your clutch hydraulics, not your
transmission. The answer to that is that the clutch uses the same
reservoir as the brakes; you should be able to see a hose leading
from it toward the left and down, to where the almost inaccessible
clutch master cylinder is. The brake master cylinder doesn't need
such a hose, since the reservoir is right on top of it.
