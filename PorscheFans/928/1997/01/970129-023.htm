As always there's two plus sides of any story but here's mine:

1.  Splitfires very expensive compared to others
2.  Degraded idle quality on my 928 (slight miss) and Miata (sometimes
stalled)
3.  Car recorded seemingly low dyno results.  
4.  Detonation detected at same test (89 oct gas)
5.  Replaced with Bosch platinums and  928 much better running all around
(92 oct)
6.  You might want to skip the standard Splitfires and go straight to the
platinum version?
7.  Free Splitfire decals with every box

Let us know what you find out!
