more delicate?  I have to be careful with pre-wax cleaner otherwise some red

The solid colors were non clear coated until about 1993-94.  All the metallic
paints were clear coated. When you use a cleaner or glaze on the paint some
of the color will come off in the form of oxidized paint.  We are talking
molecules in depth, so unless you are using a very abrasive cleaner, this is
normal.  When you clean/glaze a clear coated paint, you remove clear coat,
but you do not see color on your rag.  If you have further questions, I would
be glad to send you a couple of articles on cleaning and waxing your paint.
