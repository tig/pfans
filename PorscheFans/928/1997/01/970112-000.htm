Okay, there are 1.61 kilometers per mile.  To convert to mph, divide the
speed by 1.61.

272/1.61 = 168.944 mph (no pun intended).

That is not real fast, but maybe it gets there quickly.

According to the dictionary (my memory on this conversion was blank) one
horse power is equivalent to 746 watts.

So divide 340,000 by 746, or 340,000/746 = 455.764 hp.

Now, are you sure that the top speed wasn't 372 kph (231.056 mph)?  I do
not think 455 hp could get you to that speed, unless it is a free fall from
20,000 feet (nose first).
