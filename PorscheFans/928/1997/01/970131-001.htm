Regarding the rear wiper motor: on an '84, at least, it's
very easily accessed. Just take off the tool-kit panel at
the rear of the luggage area and there it is. Now, with
the rear hatch open, you can see the plate that the wiper
motor drives. It has a hole in it, which a peg attached to
the wiper arm must engage when the hatch is closed. The
plate can move up and down, so that nothing gets smashed
if everything isn't lined up right when you close the hatch,
and it can get stuck in the down position. In that case, the
motor will run, but the wiper won't go. If that's your
problem, a little lubricant and a little wiggling will
fix it right up.
