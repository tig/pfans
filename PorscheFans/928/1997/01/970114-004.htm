Re: recent thread on top speed of 928 - reported 272km/h...  This is
probably right.  The 928 (certainly 928 and 928S) never had very good
aerodynamics.  A lot of extra oomph probably isn't going to translate to a
very much higher max speed.

I seem to recall that in the 80's some guy (in Germany I think) made the
"ultimate" Q car by sitting a VW Golf (Rabbit in the US) body over a 928S
chassis and running gear.  The VW actually had better aerodynamics than the
928S (hard to believe but apparently true) so could actually get a higher
top speed!  Mind you, weight might have had something to do with it as well...

My Aussie $0.02.
