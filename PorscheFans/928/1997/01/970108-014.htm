83 (157K miles).  Any hints or ideas on what to use.  Are there different

back and forth 1/2 inch. I followed the play back to the rack where the
shaft enters. Lots of grit, even though the ruber boot was still good.
Change the power stearing fluid, just like the brake fluid!!!  The same
porblem on both sides of the car, yet not noticeable with the car on the
ground like the wheel bearing problem.
