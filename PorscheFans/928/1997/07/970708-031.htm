I would have to agree with Leonard... keep your 928.  It may have miles and
may not be a 1997 model, but look at the type of car you could buy for what
your car is worth.  Now consider, would this new car be built as well as a
928?  Will it turn as many heads?  Will it be as reliable?  Will it be as
fast?  Will it be as exclusive?  Etc ad nauseum.  My 928 is an 87 and I don't
foresee any reason in the near or distant future that I would ever get rid if


it, not even for a 95 or 96.  I love the car, it is in beautiful condition
both mechanically and cosmetically, and I still get a thrill out of driving
it.  Keep your 928, update it with the money you would spend on a newer car,
and enjoy!
Just on man's opinion though.
