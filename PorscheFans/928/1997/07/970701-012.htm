One thing to suspect is the reading itself. There seem to be enough
problems with poor connections in the dash wiring that you could be
seeing the effect of that. Get a voltmeter and see if the actual
voltage at the battery changes.

If it does, the exciter wire (blue) on an '84 goes to pin O8 (that's
connector O, pin 8) on the relay panel first. Your car may or may
not have the same wiring.
