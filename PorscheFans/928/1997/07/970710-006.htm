Vince,

A few days ago someone posted a message suggesting the use of a hair dryer.
I have used this method in the past (warranty labels....) and it works very
well.  

Just get the decal (and substrate) real warm and peel it up using an xacto
(craft) knife or equivalent.  The flat spade (putty knife shaped) blade
works the best.  Start on a corner working the knife under the decal and
pinching the decal between the blade and your finger.....  then slowly pull
while CONTINUING to heat the decal with the hair dryer. The continued
heating is important.


Regards
Frank Neuperger

PS. Don't use a heat gun unless it has a real low setting as this tends to
"Morph" your decals into something that would look best on a 928 caricature.
