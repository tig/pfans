 Valentine One Users,

To the 928ers who are having Full Alert, and Erratic Behavior with the Valentine
One.
Just got my V1 back from Warranty Repair. I also called to talk to the techinian
to find out what the problem was, it was as I suspected a bad solder connection
to one of the processors. Under certain vibrations, jolts, etc. it caused a lose
of connection to one of the processors and caused the unit to go to Full Alert
and other times caused it not to react to anything. All seems great for now,
will update after trip to NC this weekend.
