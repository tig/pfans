I've been looking for the past 10 years and haven't found much.

I've rarely looked for models that require assembly, (only once), but
have looked a lot for die cast or anything already assembled.


All I've found, (& I've bought all I've found), is a plush/stuffed 928S
made by coolux; spelled in a mirror image on the bottom of it (I don't
know why).  A "Pull Back Action" 1/39th scale 928S race version with
incorrect headlights.  A one inch Hot Wheels
looking (definitely NOT Hot Wheels though) but
much smaller 928 that has rolling tires.  A 928 shaped phone. A Herpa
928S4, looks to be about 1/42th scale model that is a Perfect replica
with great detail.  It even has the color correct hood crest and the
headlight squirters represented!  And finally a sterling silver key fob.
That's about all that I've found.
