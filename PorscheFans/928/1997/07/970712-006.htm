Stephane,
I have posted several times now my similar experience and the costly fix
that solved the problem.  In the case of my 1986 928S, I had to replace
the brake vacuum booster unit (ca. $400).  It appears that they can fail
in such a manner as to cause them to pull on the brake master cylinder
and engage the brakes while you are driving, without any foot pressure
being applied on the brake pedal.  This may not be your problem, but
since you have already reported to us that you completely rebuilt the
brake calipers, etc., I would give strong consideration to this as the
source of your problem.  Good luck!
