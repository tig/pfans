Stephane wrote:

>> Does anyone know how do reshape the cardboard/fiberglass material
that shapes the quarter pannel? <<

NOT cardboard/fiberglass.  This is a molded wood fibers product. 
Similar to hardboard or a medium density fiberboard (MDF) product. Wood
fibers mixed with a binder (some kind of glue or resin) and formed under
heat and pressure in a die.

>> Should I try to soak it with water and then bend them back into shape? <<

Be VERY careful, or you will probably dissolve the panel (ever seen MDF
that got wet - turns to mush).  You might try a portable steamer like
those sold to travellers.  I have one made by Oster.  Another method
might be to bend into position, weight or clamp it, and reinforce back
with strips of wood and fiberglass.
