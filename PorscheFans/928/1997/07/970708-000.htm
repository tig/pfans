Michael Jen wrote:

>> 3. What does the "Stop Lamp" mean, displayed on the upper left of the VDO/Dash. <<

When my car is started for the first time, the "Stop Lamp" indicator
comes on.  The first time I touch the brakes, it goes out.  Purpose is
to verify functional brake lights (per my trusty owners' manual).  My PO
told me it was a wierd light, obviously he hadn't RTFM in a while.... I
did - amazing stuff in there.

IF IT DOESN'T GO OUT.... you may have a burnt brake lamp, OR a new and
old lamp.  Sometimes these lighting indicators need a balanced circuit
to function properly.  I've seen this in cars other than my 928 (Acura,
Infiniti)
