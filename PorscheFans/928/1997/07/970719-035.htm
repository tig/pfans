In the newest issue of Panorama Kim Crumb does a whole article on the 928's.

to save weight and it did...about 200lbs if I am not mistaken.  Porsche
Performance Portfolio has an article on the CS 928 also.

Off the top of my head the things left out were:

Rear window wiper
sun roof
A/C
sound deadening hear and there

I am sure there were a few other things and maybe some things that were kept
but lightened.
