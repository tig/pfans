 Kurt,
From your description I think you are ready for new motor mounts. The mounts are
filled with a fluid and will rupture from heat and age. Worn motor mounts will
give  the exact vibrations that you describe. I have had the identical
vibrations and replaced motor mounts and the vibrations disappeared.
