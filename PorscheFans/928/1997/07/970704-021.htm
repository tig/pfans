Colin,
The Automatic Transmission in your 85 is a 4-Speed made by Mercedes-Benz.
The "go even faster" switch as you call it, is simply a "Shortcut" to the 
lowest available gear given your current RPM. It is not another gear, or 
nitro boost, or turbo boost, or special intake control as I have seen it 
called lately
