>>What are your opinions on pads for over all use ( street , track ,  road
>>racing ) ?

For me, stock pads (well, the pads the PO left on the car) faded
badly on the track. Performance Friction Carbon Metallic 80 pads
don't fade, though they do rattle and squeak some. I use them
on the street, they work OK even when cold. There are other
street/track pads available, but I have no basis for comparison.

>>What are the best tires to use for racing ( track , road racing ) ?
>>
BFG Comp T/A R1 is by far the most common on the track. Hoosier
has a fairly new DOT-legal tire also, which some prefer. I use
the R1's and think they're impressive.
