Stephane, it sounds like this device is just a "starter relay" that is
frequently installed by people who can't fix the real fault. The ignition
switch sends 12 volts to the starter solenoid which is a big relay (and
mechanical actuator). If the solenoid doesn't get full 12 volts, it
sometimes doesn't work too well and so a relay can be installed to bypass
this problem. My thoughts are to find out why the solenoid isn't getting 12
volts. Tne only time I would install a "starter relay" is if the ignition
switch was faulty and the customer couldn't afford a replacement.
