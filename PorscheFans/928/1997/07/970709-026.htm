>> My mechanic say's the 928 keys are very special. The OEM replacements are $2000?? <<

Must be some misplaced 0s here.  The 928 key is available from your
friendly Porsche dealer.  The key and lighted head are sold seperately. 
Combined cost is around $50-65.  You give the dealer your VIN and copy
of registration proving ownership, 2-3 days later, new key is in from
PCNA.

>> Or I can opt for a new cylinder and ignition key. <<

It would be about $275 according to my mechanic to replace the cylinder.
Mine was a little sticky, so I opted for the $2.95 fix...
Got a tube of white graphite powder from local hardware store and squirt
some into the cylinder, did the doors and hatch lock for good measure
also.
