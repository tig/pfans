Took out K&N filter to clean and lube today -- am somewhat concerned over
previous postings regarding relative ineffectiveness in filtering the small
stuff. Compared by looking at sun thru original paper filter and then K&N.
Pretty amazing that the paper filter just blurs the light completely while
the K&N has zillions of small holes all in it, and you can see thru it quite
easily. I guess this is why they provide so much more airflow! Cleaned
thoroughly and oiled heavily (Something"s got to catch the small stuff)
Clearly, the K&N filter has to have the oil to make it effective!
