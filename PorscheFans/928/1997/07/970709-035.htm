OK guys, my $.02.  I NEVER ever use my nozzles.  I have emptied both
reservoirs and don't even think about filling them up.  I'm sure that I'll
get a ton of mail telling me that my pumps will freeze up, etc., but the
solvent that is made for cleaning your windows also does a number on your
paint.  It's about the equivalent of waxing your car with a gas soaked rag.
 Have none of you ever noticed the streaks it leaves on your paint that DON'T
wash off? I'd rather replace a windshield washer pump (even though I won't
ever sell it) and have a beautiful factory paint job anyday.  Besides, if you
wash the car often enough and keep your windshield waxed (much cheaper and
easier to apply than Rain-X) the bugs and dirt won't stick.  Just a thought.
