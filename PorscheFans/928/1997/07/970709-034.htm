Hey, I get to answer a question from John White!
John asks what the mysterious threaded fittings are a few inches inboard
of the rear shock mounts.

John, they are the anchor points for a child restraint. When I imported my
928S from the UK to Australia, one of the things that the authorities were
hot on was the provision of child restraint mounting points. The engineer
who issued my certificate and affixed his little plate was confident that
that is what they are. I personally think that they are too small for an
adult race harness.
