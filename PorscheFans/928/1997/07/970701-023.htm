Check all the PC edge connectors on the instrumentation cluster; two on
the bottom and one on the right-side when facing cluster.  Pay
particular attention to the right-side one.  You will find a connection
with two yellow wires going into it.  (If I recollect, its the third one
from the top..) Mine was making an intermittent connection; hence the
bouncing of the voltage indicator.  I recrimped the finger connector and
solder it just in case.  Good luck.
