PCNA submits the VIN to Germany and gets back a listing of equipment. 
The standard option codes and standard equipment changes from year to
year so they just list the equipment.  If you have doubts about what is
listed, you can always discuss it with PCNA.  My first CoA was nor
complete, I discussed it with the signer, he researched further and
confirmed my thoughts, issued a revised (replacement) certificate and
all was well.  They do their best, but are not infallible.
