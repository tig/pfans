Do you ever smell coolant after the engine's hot?  The hose running from
the engine to the coolant reservoir on my car gave out right where it
connects to the engine.  It leaked under pressure and most of the water
evaporated off of the engine block hindering detection, but I knew I had an
external leak because I occasionally would get a whiff of coolant after
driving the car for a while.  The hose is easy to get to and replace (it
connects to the engine in front top center).  I priced the replacement hose
at $2.20 from NAPA, $21.00 from my local Porsche dealer and around $12.00
from various mail-order Porsche parts suppliers (plus shipping).  Hope this
helps.
