12 volts when the engine is iddling seems very low.  Some people have
noted that the gauge can be misleading so you should put a voltmeter
on the battery and check the reading.  If your reading still holds,
go to your local part shop and have them plug your car on their
charging system monitor.  They should be able to tell you if and where
you have a problem.

With this low a voltage, does the car even run when the headlight and
the A/C is on??

As for the rpm in neutral, the exact # for your car should be on the
sticker applied on the inside of the hood.  It is the most official
# you can find for your car and model year as this sticker has to
be applied there by law.
