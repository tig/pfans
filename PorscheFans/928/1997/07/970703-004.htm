It's my impression that it's difficult or requires special equipment,
so that you deal with it by buying a rebuilt torque tube. I believe
that 928 International is one source. The most likely symptom of
a bearing going bad would be noise; best way to diagnose it is to
get the car on a lift (with the rear wheels able to turn), run it
in gear, and go under there and listen carefully.
