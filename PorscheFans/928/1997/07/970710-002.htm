A while back we had some discussion about the 21mm wheel
spacers that some 928's (e.g., mine) have on the back and
their effect on tire clearance. I can report that with
8" rims and 245/45-16 tires, it's a close thing, but the
car got through a weekend on the track with no signs of
rubbing. I had an alternative prepared - a set of open
lug nuts - in case I had to take the spacers out, but
it wasn't necessary.
