> Sometimes, I'm showing 14+ volts, and other times, barely over 12 volts.

> for a couple of minutes (no RPM change).

  I've noticed a situation like this on my '83.  In my case, the drop to
  just over 12 volts occurs when I'm running the A/C and the aux electric
  fan switches on.  If the drop occurs when you're not running the A/C, 
  keep an eye on your temp gauge to see if the temp is high enough for 
  the fan to switch on and then note the voltage reading.
  
  I haven't noticed any problems from this situation, but then again, I
  keep my car on a trickle charger when parked, so I'm not sure I would
  notice a slightly discharged battery.
