A well-known culprit for coolant leaks is the heater valve. You can see
it, and change it if necessary, if you remove the air filter box (be
sure to cover the hole it connects to, you really don't want dirt or
anything else in there). Have you gone under the car with a light
to look for any telltale trails of evaporated coolant?
