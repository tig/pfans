Pietro,
Find a used GT engine($5000-7000) if you can, a used G28.12 or G28.55 Tranny($2500-3500) (close ratio), order the M474 Sport shocks ($1200), and Club
Sport springs ($2500) from Porsche (availible), then rip out your A/C system , remove your stereo, remove carpet and rip out all sound deadening, then
reinstall carpet,  order the factory "Sport" seats ($5000 ea), remove rear electric hatch release, remove rear wiper, order Club Sport Wheels form
Porsche(est $5000)  and rip out a few other creature comforts and you will have a FAKE Club Sport
Why not take the $30,000 or so plus your car and go by a nice GTS.
It would be a lot easier, a lot quieter, smoother and just as fast and you would not be butchering a nice S4. ;-)

Seriously there is a few articles in the "928 Performance Portfolio" book concerning the 88 Euro Club Sport.
