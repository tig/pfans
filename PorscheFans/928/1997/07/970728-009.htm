As an engineer, I have never heard of indexing a spark plug at anything except 
the intake valve.  The whole idea of indexing is to get the spark, and 
resulting flame kernel, and the air/fuel together.  Even with the varying 
combustion chamber geometries of different manufacturers affecting flow, it 
doesn't make sense to me that the spark plugs would be indexed to anything 
except the intake valve.  But, I could be wrong.
