I always inch over speed bumps....I have never bottomed my car out.  I have
had a large piece of tire tread in the road break out a few of the cooling
louvers from my S4's belly pan however.  If I take one bump in the
subdivision to fast the edge of the front "spoiler" (for lack of a better
word) on the front will scrape but it is no big deal.

When the stop lamp warning light is lit your central warning light should
also come on...put on your parking brake to be sure that the central light is
not burned out.  The stop lamp warning light indicates that you have a burned
bulb somewhere in the stop light circuit (obviously bulbs on the back of the
car) or there is some kind of malfunction in the warning system.  Step on the
brake and have someone tell you if all stop lights come on...R/R as
neccessary.
