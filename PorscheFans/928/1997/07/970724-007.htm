Hi Michael, although it doesn't sound exactly like what happened to mine
I thought I should still reply. 2 years ago I found I was losing coolant
without an obvious leak anywhere. It turned out to be a crack in the oil
cooler which communicated directly with the radiator. This was confirmed
by sticking my finger into the coolant overflow reservoir (when cold!)
and feeling lots of sludgy oil in there. I hope it's not that because it
was expensive to fix. Another clue in my case was that the oil level
also mysteriously dropped.
