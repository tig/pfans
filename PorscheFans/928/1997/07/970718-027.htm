It may well be extremely resistant to scratches, but let's hope it's not
extremeley resistant to flexing. Flexible paint is a good thing -
especially on a flexible surface.

Any competant body shop supplier should be able to provide you with a
flex agent additive for whatever paint you decide to use. Mix it as
directed with the paint you spray the bumper covers with.
