Nick,

I had this same problem. Also, my wipers would not work in intermittent
mode and the intensive washer would not work.. When I checked the
fuse/relay panel I found that both the intermittent wiper relay and the
intensive washer relay were missing. I replaced both with used ones from
928 International and everything worked fine.

78-79 928
Intermittent Washer Relay    928.615.101.00
Intensive Washer Relay        928.618.113.00 (optional equipment, bridge
otherwise)
