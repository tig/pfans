Something else to remember.  Someone else on the list can probably explain
this a little more precisely but here it goes.

If you park your car with keys left in the ignition, like inside your
locked garage for instance remember this...

When you turn the ignition off all the way to where you can remove the
keys, you must remove the keys until a click is heard and then reinsert
them to make sure everything electrical is shut off.  Otherwise there is
some drain of electricity through the ignition or accessory.
