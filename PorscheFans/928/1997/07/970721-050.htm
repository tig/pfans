It's really, really easy.  Just make sure the car is jacked up LEVEL.

>>Is the gearbox oil reservoir separate from the differential reservoir?
(It
> looks like there are two different drain plugs -- I'm assuming the one
> in
> the front is GB and the one to the rear is for the Diff)
> 
Nope, but you have to drain them separately.  I'd guess that there's
some
sort of baffling inside which prevents you from being able to completely

drain the front from the drain plug in the rear.  You fill both up via
the fill
hole in the rear.

>>If they are separate, is it OK to use GL-5 oil that says its not
> recommended for limited-slip diffs?
> 
See above...what is critical is to use GL-5 gear oil.  If you're using a

synthetic, use a less-slippery version such as Red Line's 75W90NS.
