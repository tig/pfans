This happened to me. The exact symptoms you describe.  Very
maddening....
My car had lost the bracket that holds the lower radiator hose away from
the power steering v-belt at some point in time (po) and it had been
replaced.  The point is:  The v-belt had "notched" the radiator hose and
it would only leak under very hot conditions when the hose would
expand.  My car only got hot enough to eject fluid when I was roaring
down the road or stuck in traffic, so I never got to see where this
liquid was going!
