I had the same thing happen to one of my 928s.  The seals had gone bad,
and were staying locked after every use.  I bought a new master-cylinder
from Mark Anderson at 928 International...I think I paid about $80.  
 Also, if anyone has ever "accidentally" put power steering fluid (or
other alien fluids) in the resorvoir, that will cause the seals to fail
within 2-3 days, and not allow the calipers to release.
