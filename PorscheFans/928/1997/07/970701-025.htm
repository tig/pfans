I don't have this problem on my 928S but experienced it on a Corvette,
Cadillac and a Pontiac.  All three of these instances leads to a bad
solenoid.  A solenoid is similar to a high-amp relay.  The contact(s)
can get pitted and corroded.  The high-temp would only aggravate the
problem more.
