I was poking around Kelley's blue book web site ( http://www.kbb.com ) to
get an opinion on the value of my car.  They have two sections:  one with
trade-in values, and one with prices you would expect to pay at a dealer.

Well, the trade-in value for my '83 928s was like $4300, but the dealer
price was $9000!!!!  A $4700 detail job is a bit steep, I'd say!

This just confirms my suspicions that KBB is a scam funded by car dealers
to depress trade-in prices and inflate dealer prices.
