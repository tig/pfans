>My most recent change I used valvaline 20w 30 racing

You probably meant so say "20W50", and a good thing, too. There are lots of
reputable brands of oil out there, but it's important that you put 20W50
weight oil in your 928. This is the weight the factory specifies, and it
really helps to keep metal away from metal under the high torques and high
speeds found inside a busy 928 engine. Don't forget to change your oil and
filter frequently (e.g., every 3000 miles)
