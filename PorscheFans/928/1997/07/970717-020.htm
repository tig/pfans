Since I bought my 928 0ver a year ago , I have changed the oil 3 times . At
first I used shell 10w 30  since the label on the car said the car uses shell
oils . I did not notice any change in the car's performance . At the second
change I used valvaline 5w30 , I used valavline duralube because my nearest
Porsche shop recommended it I used grade 5w30 because I remembered seeing it
in a DEVEK cataloge . My most recent change I used valvaline 20w 30 racing
oil which seems to make the car run smoother and increased performance some
what . 
I think we should all use what we like . We will never agree on one oil .
