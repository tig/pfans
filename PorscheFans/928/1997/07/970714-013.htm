Michael Nielsen asks about rear wheel spacers and says "My concern is the lack
of a middle raised centre "thing"."

Michael, it is crucial that the wheels are supported by the hub and NOT the
studs. The wheel studs are designed to be in tension and not shear. The
genuine Porsche 21mm spacers (Part No. 477 501 701) have a recess for the
original hub ring to fit into and a ring that fits into the wheel recess.


The Automotion spacers on page 19 of their catalogue (Part No. WS-9021) also
support the wheels correctly. You mentioned 6mm spacers, why bother?
