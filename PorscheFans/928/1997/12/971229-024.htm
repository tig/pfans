miles, but *IS* putting lots of engine hours and revolutions on it.  Go

> the oil every 3 mos regardless of miles or can I wait for 3,000 miles.

> currently use Mobil 1 15W-50.  Should I switch to Red Line?  
> 
Why use Mobil 1 rather than Red Line:

 - much cheaper than Red Line
 - easier to find dealer
 - still way superior to any petroleum

Why use Red Line rather than Mobil 1:

 - under very extreme conditions (heat/hp/racing etc.), 25% less
friction
 - slightly better protection
 - you can extend your drain intervals to 12,000 (or once per year)
 - extended drain intervals bring the annual oil cost down to the same
   as Mobil 1

I believe Red Line is the ONLY synthetic that doesn't break down at
the same (or greater) rate than petroleums...so don't extend your drain
intervals beyond 5000 miles or so with Mobil 1.

>>Also, is it worth switching to synthetic in my 85 Nissan Maxima
> with 128,000 on the clock or is that just asking for leaks?  
> 
You're basically going to stop the wear on the engine.  If it's 90%
of the way to needing a rebuild, it'll about stay there.  Problem is,
rubber, hoses, belts, vac lines, etc. are deteriorating, so you'll 
soon need engine parts anyway.  Questionable value in switching
at this point.
