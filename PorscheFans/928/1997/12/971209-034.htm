Guillermo - I had the identical failure with my '91GT. I believe the dealer is correct in that it is the LH fuel control unit. My car had been parked for 2 weeks & then would barely fire - no start. Flatbedded it to the dealer because the car was under the 5 yr emissions warranty (thank god). Porsche replaced the fuel unit. Voila! The car ran like it did before I parked it. Although Porsche paid for it, I was told the LH fuel control cost $1,800 + labor. I didn't watch the repair, so cannot speak to the labor you were quoted.
