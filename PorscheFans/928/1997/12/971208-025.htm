All this talk of intake castings reminded me:  I "refurbished" the
nearly-
disappeared crest on my intake casting (16V) by taking black BBQ
spray paint, spraying a bit into a little container, and taking a brush
and
filling in all the recesses (which were once black, many moons ago).

Once the paint dried, I used a light sandpaper and a flat sanding block
to sand away the "relief" portion....and it looks great!

And hey, don't flame me for using BBQ paint on my car, cause I 
painted myself with it afterwards and so I'm now flame-retardant.  Or
retarded.  Or something like that.
