Hi All,

I saw this subject mentioned recently.
To shift my 928 Auto, I start off in 1st gear by putting the shifter in 2.
Then, when I decide I want to shift to 2nd (whatever the RPM), I do a 
quick (3/4 second) shift up to 3, then back to 2.  This shifts the car 
smoothly into 2nd gear.  When 2nd is sufficiently wound up, I push 
the shifter up to 3 for 3rd, then when it's time, on up to D for 4th.

Forgive me if you already knew this.  I could've misunderstood the 
question.  When I was test driving 928's, before I bought mine in 1994,
one of the sellers showed me this method.  I took it for granted that 
everybody knew it.

Of course, once I shift out of 1st, the only way to drop it back down, 
is to floor it.  Or slow down to about 7MPH.
