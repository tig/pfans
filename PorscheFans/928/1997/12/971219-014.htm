	I would remove the factory alarm.  I fianally removed mine after it went 
off, making noise for no reason 3 nights in a row.  I gotta sleep!  The 
factory book shows where the alarm is, you also have to remove a relay 
and add a jumper wire.  You don't want the alarm to fail and prevent the 
car from starting!

	My new alarm is just a flashing light!  It turns off when you start the 
engine.



	The original stereo amp is under the cover between the passenger seat 
and the door,  my new amp is under the drivers seat.  All the speaker 
wires can be found under the drivers seat.  The large wire is positive, 
to determine which speaker the wires go to use a 1-1/2 volt AA battery.
