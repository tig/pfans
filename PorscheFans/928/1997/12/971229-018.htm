Here's where I am: removed the slave cylinder for the clutch (after a mildly
painful journey up the ramps) and disassembled: it appears the big rubber

return to scenic Steamboat and replace. Seems simple--the part is one of the 
few accessible things underneath and has a bleeder valve. 

My question: since the brakes and clutch share the same fluid reservoir,
