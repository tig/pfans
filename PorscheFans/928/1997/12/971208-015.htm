Sung Suh described the dome light coming on when hitting bumps in the road and
the lights not turning off as they should after the door is closed...

Although it might be the door switch as someone suggested, I think the time
delay relay is the culprit.  This would explain why a bump sets off the light
since the relay would be susceptible to these bumps based on its mounting
location and possible sensitivity of the relay contacts to vibration.  I find
it more plausible that the door jamb switch fails to make contact (hence not
turning the lights ON when the door is opened) rather than failing to break
contact (which would prevent the lights from being turned OFF after the door
is closed).
