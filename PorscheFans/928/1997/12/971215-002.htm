I may be a little late but, someone was asking about a leaking fuel
tank
after fill-up.  I would suspect it may be the vent tube at the top of
the tank.  This is a 90-degree fitting at the top of the tank piped
through the frame over to the top of the filler-neck.  This fitting may
be broken or the rubber hose may just be disconnected or cracked.  One
can access this fitting by dropping the fuel tank- not an ambitious
project.  If the fitting is broken I would try a repair with Marine-Tex
or some other epoxy-type repair putty.  Hope this helps.
