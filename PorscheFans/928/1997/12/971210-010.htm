> Doesn't the 911 use a Weissach (or very similar design) and doesn't some
> of the 911 have 345 section rear tires?  

The 911/930 from the 993 series, uses a so-called 'LSA' (lightweight stable
agile) double-wishbone rear geometry.  It's actually similar to a Detroit 
long-/short-arm setup.  Yes, it does provide toe-compensation, via compliance
in the bushings of the triangulated two-piece upper member.  The tire size
on the turbo is 285/30 ZR 18, however.  Not sure on the 996.
