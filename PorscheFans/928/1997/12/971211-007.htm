Yes, up to a point. The fuel pump is under the fuel tank and its normal
buzzing sound will be audible from outside an idling car. However, if the
buzz gets louder, particularly after you've been driving hard, there may be
a problem with fuel flow.

This was my tip that my car's air vent had stopped working; the pump was
working extra-hard truing to draw a vacuum in the tank. It had gotten loud
enough so I heard is (as a kind of grumbling sound) from inside the car,
then gout out and heard a much louder, angrier buzz than usual.

Don't say your car can't talk to you!
