At 07:32 PM 12/3/97 -0600, you wrote:
>Under the front hood, front right corner, is a terminal
>for the positive jump. Negative goes to the chassis.

That underhood terminal is a little sneaky. You should see a dull metallic
hexagonal object sticking out of a black plastic vertical panel. This is
not the terminal; it is the screw-on cap which covers the terminal. There
should be a little strap attached to the top of the hexagonal part to keep
it from getting lost when you unscrew it. Screw the cover back on when
you're done.
