>Does anyone know if there is a procedure for replacing the front crank
>oil seal without removing the crankshaft ? I have removed the dampner,
>crank pully, and crank sprocket...can the seal be extracted and pulled
>out of the block ?  Thanks for any help

 Easy.  Just pry out the old seal via the little notch (will require 
elbow grease).  You will most likely tear it up removing it, but who 
cares... just don't damage the faces on the crank/block getting too 
energetic.
 (One time I had very stubborn rear seal that I used the oxy-acetylene 
torch on--stunk up the garage for 1/2 hour, but came out in a jiffy.
