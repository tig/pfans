It depends how trashed your defoger elements are, I bought a kit from a
local auto parts store made by Loctite.  It comes with a conductive
paint, small brush and a stencil to place over any breaks in the grid. 
Just paint it on (3 coats) and wait 24 hours, your working again.  
I bought the kit 7 years ago and repaired a grid on my Jeep Cherokee,
Still working today.  Kit number is 15067.  The kit says it will repair
breaks up to 4 inches in length.
