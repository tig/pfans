	I use 10 w 30 in my 928 and 20 w 50 in my 914, why?  Well at operating 
temp they both need the same viscosity.  The air cooled 914 runs much 
hotter, so at operating temp both the oils are at the same viscosity, if 
you are using a 50 w in a water cooled engine, gotta ask WHY.  It's like 
the oil is never up to operating temp/operating viscosity.  OIl should 
not be to thick or to thin.

	Some racing engines hve the tolerances opened up and then use a 50 w 
oil, this is done to reduce friction, a motor set up like this won't last 
long.
