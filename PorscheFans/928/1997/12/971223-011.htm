Go ahead and switch; there should be no problem. There are stories
circulating about seals opening up and leaking when dino is replaced by
synth, but my car never dribbled.

Not all synthetic oils are equally good. The best easily available brew is
Mobil 1 15W-50 (http://www.mobil.com/consumer/mobil1/index.html ; this is
what Porsche now puts in new cars); better still, in terms of wear
protection and subtler issues, are Red Line 20W-50
(http://www.redlineoil.com/ ; vigorously promoted on this list by Michael
Cottam, a Red Line dealer) and Amsoil Series 2000 20W-50
(http://www.amsoil.com/ ; what I use, and I don't sell oil).
