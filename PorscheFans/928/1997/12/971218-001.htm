There is a company, here in the UK, that specialises in 1/43 Die Cast
White Metal kits, and they have an email address and a web site, at:

http://www.mmcars.demon.co.uk/

They should be able to help. 

Alternatively, if you can get hold of the 1/20 Tamiya 928S plastic kit,
it is well worth building it, as the detail is excellent, also consider
Fujimi, who do a 1/24 928 GT and a 928S, but supplies are very limited,
as both kits have been discontinued.
