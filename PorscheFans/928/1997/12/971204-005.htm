On the subject of stolen cars.  Thiefs steal the easiest targets.  I say
make them work for it!  The harder it is for them to get the car, the longer
it takes, the better chance ther is of them getting caught.  I personally
have tried to remove a club by sawing the steering wheel.  It is easy enough
to do, but if the club is on tight it takes some work to get it off.
Combine that with the factory alarm, and the  alarm I have added, my shark
is no easy target.  There is also a auto taser that you can install with
your factory arlarm that will disable the thief just like a normal taser!
It cost about $200.  if you want to keep your car, then work at it.  Owning
a 928 is not just the fun part of driving it......You have to protect it as
well.  Oh, I also moved the factory alarm module to a very obscure place
just in case the thief specializes in 928's! :o)
