The factory is located in:

7000 Stuttgart-Zuffenhausen

Factory Museum number is 41 711 8 27-56 85.  It is likely that
whomever answers that phone can tell you the number to call to 
arrange
a factory tour.

13-16:00 hours.  Factory tours are by appointment only.
