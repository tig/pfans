Porsche dealers don't in general seem to know or care much about 928s or
about QC of their own work. However, the same is true by and large of
dealers of other respectable European cars, and of a sadly large number of
independent repair shops.

Ultimately, even those who'd rather not work on their cars will benefit by
learning something about how they operate and where the various parts
actually are. Credit to Ed Ruiz for recognizing this and making swarming
over the engine bay and undercarriage the center of the program at the
recent Frenzy.
