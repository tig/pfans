This doesn' t answer your request for an 86 928 die cast but, I learned last
week from a local retailer of memorabilia that a company by the name of Anson
is coming out with a 1/18th scale 928.   

The description says it's a 1995 928 GTS but the drawing in the book is of an
S4.  According to their 1998 book, the 928 will have four opening parts, a
highly detailed engine, realistic soft driving seat with saftey belt and come
in three colors(silver, metallic green and red).  It's supposed to be out this
coming spring.

I will have to have a couple of these.  And will take one apart and repaint it
to match my 89(depending on whether it's really an S4 or a GTS).  Probably
wouldn't be a bad idea for those of us that are interested to call places like
Exoticar(1-800-348-9159) in the Boston area or EWA and inquire about it and
build the demand for it so they don't bag the idea.
