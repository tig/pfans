Sung, on two occasions my stereo has inexplicably stopped working
and displayed a blank LCD screen.  I just pulled the fuse for the stereo
amp (in my '89 I think it's fuse 12) and reinserted it.   That resets resets
the stereo to the "code" screen.  As for your other mystery electrical
problems I don't have a clue other than that 928s seem to be very
sensitive to having the proper battery.  Make sure that you have a decent
battery and not some KMart special from the PO.
