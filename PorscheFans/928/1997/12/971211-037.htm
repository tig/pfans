draw. Since no electric motor runs at 100% efficiency, there's even less
available to compress air. No mechanical supercharger delivers the goods
without consuming several horsepower; even your a/c compressor takes 3-5
bhp. Sounds miraculous.

Meanwhile, your stock alternator is not going to come up with this much
current continuously. If we're talking short bursts only, bring on the
nitrous (to the engine or, afterwards, the driver)!
