I'll quote the Devek catalog for those who don't have it.........
"The Weissach rear, which  provides toe in and toe out, works really well for
narrow tires, where a small amount of toe in or toe out results in small tire
contact patch displacements. However, the same changes in toe in / out, when
conveyed to the road through a wide tire, produce large lateral force changes
and a loss of predictability and control in the dynamic racetrack or high-
performance driving environment for which the wide tires are chosen."
