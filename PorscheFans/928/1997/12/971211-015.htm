Thanks for the tip about this movie, David.

I was aiming for an early night last night but Vivienne needed to go and get
some money out of a machine and it was a nice night so we took the Miata
for a little drive.  While she was getting money I wandered into the video
store and thought "What are the chances of an obscure 1987 Sci-fi movie..."

It was in the recommended section!  So we had to watch it last night, and David
is not wrong... it's worth spending 90 minutes in front of.  It's kinda a cross
between Starsky & Hutch, CHiPs and a B-grade version of the X-Files.  The
comments around the cop having a 928 are funny, and the total destruction of
the 308GTS is... well... sad, in a way!

Unfortunately it winds up with a big shoot-out and not a big car-chase like it
looks like it is going to, but it's still worth a look.
