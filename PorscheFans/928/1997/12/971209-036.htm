The factory is located in:

7000 Stuttgart-Zuffenhausen


I don't have the correct number for you to call, but the Porsche
Factory Museum number is 41 711 8 27-56 85.  It is likely that
whomever answers that phone can tell you the number to call to arrange
a factory tour.

You can visit the museum for free without appointment from 9-12:00,
13-16:00 hours.  Factory tours are by appointment only.

Other advise would be to contact PCNA.

Also, when you go make sure that a non-Porsche fanatic is
driving. When I was there I almost pranged the rental car when my head
went into automatic Exorcist mode while trying to follow a metallic
purple 959.
