Andy,
    I take it you mean Penzoil GT Performance 20W-50?  I use the same
shop(?) (Powerhaus?), and run Mobil-1 15W-50, which is now sold in New
Zealand as Mobil Racing 4T, a motorcycle oil,  Don't be fooled, it's
exactly the same stuff, just costs $1.00-$2.00 more per pack.  The
synthetic oil runs cleaner, and pumps more easily in the cold, or at
startup.  Also, oil pressure at idle is reduced by about 1 bar.
    I've experimented with the other Mobil-1's, and thought they seemed
fine, (especially the 0W-40).  However the wisdom from the garage is to
go with the 15W-50 (aka 4T) or the Penzoil.
    Mobol USA recommend the 15W-50 for High Performance applications,
and I like the cleanliness.
