One final note to give smug looks all round...I'm in the Porsche Owners 
Club here in the UK and recently in the newsletter "Porsche Post", a 944

three 944s (a Lux, a cabriolet and a standard S2) so I feel pretty well 
qualified to make a comparison myself.
  
Interestingly, the 928 has almost twice the engine size and cost me more

this to yourselves, I wouldn't want to encourage inter-marque conflict!!
