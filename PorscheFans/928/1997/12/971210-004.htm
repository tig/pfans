> In D or 3, my 928 always starts out in 2nd gear from a stop, unless I put my

Mine starts in 2nd, but then slots down to 1st with generous throttle (NOT kickdown).
This seems to happen with less throttle in selector position '3'. (My imagination?)

> When the selector is placed in 2, my 928 always starts out in 1st gear and
> stays in 1st until redline regardless of throttle pressure.  I can use light
> throttle pressure and cruise around in 1st, or keep my foot in it....but it
> stays in 1st until it hits the redline.  At that point, it shifts into 2nd and
> then I can let up on the throttle and it will stay in 2nd until I slow to
> around 10mph when it will downshift back into 1st.  If I engage the kickdown,
> it will shift into 1st at a higher rpm, of course.

Ditto, except mine changes to 2nd below the red-line, at maybe 5000 RPM.  
Kickdown takes it to 6000 or so.

> ... I might conclude ... that the transmission should start out in 1st gear (at
> least sometime[s]).

Well, yes.  I sometimes start in position '2', then flick the selector to '3' at
around 3500 RPM, then return to position '2' just as 2nd gear engages.  This
allows a snappy yet gentle takeoff in 1st, and once cruising in 2nd gear I can go 
either way -- mash the pedal, or flick up to 'D' and coast.  Useful around town, 
where traffic lights and other vehicles do unexpected things.

Hope this is some help.
