If you want better performance at all rpm's (especially 1000 - 3000) buy
the 309 transaxle. You will get a 40% improvement in performance at a cost
of 5% off your top end. This transaxle was designed for the 928. Check with
the factory. They will tell you that the 928 was designed for this
transaxle. The reason you see the 2:20 - 2:73 ratios is for EPA and top end
speed reasons.
