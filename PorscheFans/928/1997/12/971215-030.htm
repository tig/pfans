At a Diablo PCA tech session on CA emissions held April 12, 1997 at
Kahler's Porsche/Mercedes/BMW, 6117 Dougherty Rd, Bublin, CA
510/829-2050 I saw Denny Kahler's tripple rack tripple deck version
(holds 9 cars) which he uses out in back of his shop for car storage.
Why not give Denny Kahler a call and ask him about the system he uses.
I realize this might be a little large for your purposes, but just think
of all the additional space you would have for expanding your
collection....

> They're advertised in Hemmings by the half-dozen, and they look
intriguing.
> Some even work under an 8' ceiling. Prices range from $2,600- 3,000.

Be sure to get references.  A Porsche mechanic I use (Valhalla in
Lafayette, CA) was very uncomplimentary about one of his lifts which
needed repairs and the manufacturer was less than cooperative.
