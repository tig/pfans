If you've added the right amount (about a bottle's worth...doubling the
concentration is NOT better), you should expect to see a 10 to 20 degree
drop.  It seems to vary a bit from car to car, though.  I've seen only a
couple of degrees on a Toyota MR2, to over 20 degrees on a Lotus Esprit
Turbo.

There's a lot of technical details, including explanations of how it
works and a lot of comparison charts at:

http://www.redlineoil.com/wwti.htm
