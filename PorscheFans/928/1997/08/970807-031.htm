Is it just the instrument lights that flash from time to time? Or do you
have intermittant guage problems also?

  If everything is flaky, be sure to check that the instrument cluster
connectors (3 large edge-card connectors) are firmly seated on
corrosion-free card-edges.  You can reach up and see/feel/reseat the
connectors without removing the entire cluster and pod by laying on your
back with head under dash - helps if the drivers' seat is out.

  If all the lights (only - not guages) flash, obviously there is some
loose connection in the circuit supplying power to these lights. Time to
trace it out with a meter.

  If just one or two lights are strange, maybe just the bulbs are not
well seated.  Unfortunately, the entire instrument cluster and pod must
be loosened or partially removed for access to the instrument light
bulbs which are on the back of the cluster.
