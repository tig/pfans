These are small indeed. To address concerns like the one you express, I go
to the tire makers' published figures for revolutions per mile. Pretty much
all Porsches use front and rear sizes which give about 830-840 revs/mi. The
Michelin Pilot SX MXX3 listing gives the following figures:

	225/50 ZR 16 (S all around, S4 and GT front)	838
	245/45 ZR 16 (S4 and GT rear)			849
	225/45 ZR 17 (GTS front)			836
	255/40 ZR 17 (GTS rear)			836

There's no 225/50x15 in that line, but I did find a 225/50 ZR 15 in the
Yokohama AVS Intermediate line, shown at 868 rev/mi. Yes, your tires are
too damned small!
