My '85  928s had been running sporadically worse over the past six months and 
finally croaked, leaving us stranded on I-85 with a AAA 100 mile tow home on a 
flat bed.

Two months later, the car is finally fixed and running strong. After much 
diagnosis, including swapping the black box, the problem was isolated to the 
flywheel RPM sensor that sends data to the ignition and injection. This is a 
cheap part - $15 bucks -  but can play hell with the whole system .We also 
changed out all the major relays and cleaned up all the contacts and wiring in 
the fuse box near the passenger floor area.

Symptoms were:

Intermittent missing
Stalling when off the gas
Loss of power
Limited pull on acceleration  
.
