I have been reading with great interest the various messages on water 
temperature, my S4 varies unexplainably; however, never gets near the 
upper white mark.  If anything it stays on or near the next lower white 
mark.   But:  Here is something interesting......When it is running 
somewhat near the upper white mark,,,,,turning on the air conditioner 
lowers it significantly and quickly.....The only thing I can figure out 
is the AC automatically energizes the fans; therefore, I assume the temp 
at which the fans are operating varies somewhat for some reason....if 
your temp is runnning high, recommend turning on the AC to see if it 
comes down, if so check out the fan setpoint......just a thought!
