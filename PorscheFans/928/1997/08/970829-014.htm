My understanding is that the LH-Jetronic brain will alternate from
closed-loop to open-loop operation often through the driving cycle.
Open loop usually occurs most during cranking, warmup and wide open
throttle.  Closed-loop occurs most normally during warm cruise.  It is
possible that Colin's '85 is running closed-loop because the brain
senses that something is seriously wrong.

I would try disconnecting and reconnecting the battery to reset the
brain and see if that would clear up the problem.
