John & others,

At what tire pressures do people run R1's?  I was doing 30 psi cold and
got a lot of wear in the center.  (This last time @ LRP, the "squiggly"
tread appearence indicated I was generating a fair amount of tire
temperature.)  Next time, I'm guessing to start at 27 and probably drop
to 26, especially for the rear.  The "too low pressure groove-o'-doom"
was recently discussed on the 944 list - it looks the same as the
pressure is soo low the tire actually folds under itself in the middle
of the tire (imagine that!).  I'm pretty sure at 30 psi cold, I'm well
above the groove-o'-doom pressures.

(For those unfamiliar with the DOT approved road-racing BFG R1's,
they're funny tires with extremely stiff (inner) sidewalls and operate
well around 230 F.  All this leads to very low cold tire pressures.)
