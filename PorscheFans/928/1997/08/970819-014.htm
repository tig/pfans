I am sure many mechanic types know this trick, but here goes:  For
stubborn pilot bushings or bearings, simply find a dowel (wood or metal)
that fits the center hole really well.  Fill the end of the crank
completely with thick grease, keep pushing it in with the dowel until
there is no nore air in there.  Whack the dowel in, and the hydraulics
push the bearing/bushing out.  Add more grease, repeat.  Messy, but so
is a torch!! :)
