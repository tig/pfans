Earlier this spring I washed the engine compartment of my '84 and it didn't 
run well for over two weeks. About the time I'd think it was drying out, it 
would resume sputtering, hard starts, and missing. Of course, I thought 
maybe I had disconnected something with the water pressure although I was 
very careful and couldn't find any loose wires, etc. Took the car to my 
mechanic. According to him, when the water sitting on the manifold heats up 
and begins to steam, it will find any imperfection in the plug wires. The 
solution, buy new plug wires ($150 - $400) or be patient. Patience won. Like 
yours, though, I've never had any problems in the rain. Go figure.
