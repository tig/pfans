I just came across a very cool story concerning the longevity of the 928. It is
in the "928 12th Anniversary Sales brochure for the 89 928S4" (which BTW is
incredible).

The Story:

When a German 928S owner clocked 305,000 trouble free miles in 3 years as a
sales representative, he called the Porsche Museum suggesting it buy his car.

Porsche engineers replied, "ENJOY YOUR 928S FOR ANOTHER 300,000 MILES; SUCH
SERVICE IS NOT UNEXPECTED"
