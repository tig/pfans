Subject:Just found the HOT performance zone
Date:Tue, 12 Aug 1997 08:12:56 -0500
While driving this past saturday, I noticed that the Tachometer's
red zone extends from 6200 to 7000 but no red zone from 7000 to 7500.

Well, if Dr. Porsche did not put it red, it must be because its usable.
So I went pretty fast in 3rd gear and downshifted in second so that
the RPM would jump over the red line, above 7000 RPM.  Men, you should
have felt the power rush in that section!!  This is probably the best
hidden thing about the 928.

I must admit that it take some practice, and steel nerve the first time,
to pass the "red zone".  If you hit the red zone, the rev limiter
kick in and takes away the forbiden fruit....
