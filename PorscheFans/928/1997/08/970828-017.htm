A reminder to all 928 fans about the "928 Frenzy".

Purpose of event: To discuss various aspects of the 928. Kim Crumb
will be our main guest speaker. Marc and Susan from "Devek Performance"
as well and Lucky Ekman (the EK in Devek) will be there.
There will be door prizes compliments of "928 International" and "Devek

club"

Date & Time: 9/27/97 from 9:00 a.m.to 3:30 p.m.
Location: "Auto Sportsystems Group" (ASG) in Fairfax, VA.
The cost (with lunch): $5 for PCA members, and $10 for non-member.
