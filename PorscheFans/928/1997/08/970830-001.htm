James:

I had a similar problem.  It turned out that a small vaccume hose that
opens and closes the "flaps" that let the cold air into the front came
loose.  When you open the hood, there are 2 or 3 small, approximately
1/4" vaccume hoses on the inside left, up  relatively close to the
firewall.  Give them a check.  If it is one of them it will take you
less than 30 seconds to fix.  Hope it works for you.
