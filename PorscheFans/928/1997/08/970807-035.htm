Yes, my pump always ran.  They do make a faint whine when running. 
However, it would run only for a little bit (about 3 secs to pressurize
the system) and then quit.  This happens at the first ignition position
(before cranking starts).  Go to Barnes & Noble and read the book
"Modifying and Maintaining your Bosch Fuel Injection System".  This is
not a book by Bosche, rather it is a trouble-shooting guide about Bosch
FI complete with testing sequences etc.

Also, do you have a factory gascap?  Many FI systems need a pressurized
gastank to work correctly.
