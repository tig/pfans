Common problem with many cars.  Check the wire from the battery to the
starter solenoid.  As the resistance in this wire increases (corrosion,
bad contacts, broken wire strands), you'll get this problem.

Cable's a pain to get to though.  Left side of the car, jack it up, you
can see the wire going into the back of the starter.  From there, it
runs away and hides....
