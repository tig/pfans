> * Hi All:  I purchased an 84 928S with 71K in June. I fixed most of the little problems it had but two of them have got me. Can anyone tell me any information about the following:
> 
> 1. When I turn on the lights within 5 seconds I get a tail light warning light.  At first I had no tail lights on the right rear side.  I cleaned the fuse and holder and got the lights to work,  I have since replaced the fuse, but I still get the warning light.  Once and a while when I go over a bump the warning light goes out for a few seconds.
> 
> 2.      I've been having a problem with operation of the cruise control. It will seem to engage, sometimes but drop outs during operation.  I can accelerate using the control but it will not hold the speed I set it to.
> 

Check for vacuum leak.  The other possibility is that your rear light
problem disengage the cruise control.  The cruise control will not
work properly if your rear brake light work properly.  

My rear brake light had a wiring problem.  The cruise control did not
work.  I fixed the wire problem and now my cruise control works
perfectly.
