Before you rip out the switch, take a look at the  jump start connector
under the hood on the pass side. This is where the hot from the bat ties
into the rest of the system. the terminal has a screw slot unscrew the
terminal and clean all the terminals underneath.
I have a seen this on two other 928's and a good cleaning of these
terminals worked both times.
It would seem when these get corroded they will not pass enough current to
start the car.
