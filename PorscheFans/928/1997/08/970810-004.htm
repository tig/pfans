Are your brake light working properly or did you change one of the brake
light bulb?
If your brake light do not work properly or you bulb detector think one
of the bulb is burnt
out (a new bulb on one side and an old one on the other side can fool
the system).

Porsche did this as a safety measure.  The cruise control gets
deactivated when you use
the brakes.  This is accomplished with the same switch as the brake
light.  If the electric system for the brake light fails and this
feature was not on, you could get in trouble.
