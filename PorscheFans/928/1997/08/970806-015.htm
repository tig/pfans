Jetronic fuel injection system which is very different to the K Jetronic

the so called S3. The LH system does not have separate cold start valves 
and injectors. It changes the duration of the injection based on 
temperature measurements instead. Your message has given me another idea 
however. Perhaps I do have a fuel pump problem as you suggest. Most fuel

This is to prime the system presumably. This is certainly the case with

for oil pressure before the pump kicked in. Perhaps my assumption was a 
poor one. If I am in fact correct, I would assume the system should hold 
fuel pressure over night at least. Perhaps I have a leak down problem.

thoughts? Can anyone out there with an 85 or 86 (I suspect the S4 would
