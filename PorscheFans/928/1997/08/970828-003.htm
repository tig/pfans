I think the 16v cars in general and any A/T or high-mileage ones are
taking a beating in the marketplace.  Your local paper or the for sale

dyno testing section in Project 928, then to all the nice cars in the
gallery - especially Vince Yu's close to 200k mile 928.  Then ask the

move down the road like a 928 and look good doing so too.

I'd rather look at it this way, there's going to be alot of early bodied
projects shreding pavement in the future.  And Bob DeVore did get it
going with 16 valves...
