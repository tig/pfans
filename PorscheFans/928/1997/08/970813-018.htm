Well, after 3 tries, I finally was able to develop a backyard method to
check the corner balance of my S4 (with Weltmeister's, Koni's, and
R1's).  The final time, I could do all the measurements in less than 1
hour and the accuracy looks like it may be as good as about 15 lb. per
corner.  It involves measuring the deflection of the spare tire on each
corner plus some other contortions.  See

http://pantheon.yale.edu/~kgibble/bmw/corner_balancing.html

for the details.  (Leonard, please note the "improved" wallpaper.)
