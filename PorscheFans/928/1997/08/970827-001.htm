I am not trying to lessen the importance of this warning, but it could just mean the belt
needs re tensioning (my 89 S4 needed just that, about $250-300).  I am making a big
assumption: the belt has already been replaced, if not you should not drive it anymore
until you have it checked.

If your belt skips once (too loose) it could bend something...
If it snaps (does not really happen frequently on reasonably maintained cars), you are
looking at some serious money ($5,000-7,000 !)
