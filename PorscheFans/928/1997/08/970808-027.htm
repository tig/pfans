Hi all,
my '87 S4 is guards red, spent most of its life in Florida, and now lives
outside. Needless to say, the paint gets a bit oxidized and dull. Until 
recently, I had always washed/cleaned/polished/waxed it using Maguires 
products every 3 weeks or so (more washing between waxings of course) but
dull spots would still manifest after a bit over a week in the sun. I 
decided to experiment as follows: stripped all the wax off and cleaned up
any road tar etc. Next I covered all the painted surfaces with light 
mineral oil and let it soak like that for 3 to 4 hours. I washed the 
excess oil off and polished/waxed as usual. 2 months later, there is NO sign
of dulled/oxidized paint. Just an idea you may like to try if your 928 
is stuck outdoors in the sun..
