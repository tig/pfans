On a recent 200 mile trip @ elevations varying from 3k-6k, oat avg 82 
degrees coolant temp was mostly just below upper normal limit (2nd white 
line)to once just above same. When it was above 2nd white I didn't like 
it because it was not that hot outside, a/c off, following slower 
traffic up a mild grade in 4th @ 2200 rpm or so. On the way home, pushed the 
rig very hard (I love this car!), climbing a relatively steep segment of 
grade for 20 min w/ constant posted 25 mph curves taken much faster 
with a/c on, 2300-4000 rpm and the temp never strayed over 2nd white. a/c 
on apparently does cause it to run cooler.
