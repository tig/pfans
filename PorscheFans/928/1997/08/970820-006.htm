  Thought I'd post the part number for this electrical connector since
  quite a few dealers told me over the phone that it wasn't available.
  
    Plastic electrical connector: Porsche part # 037-906-240 (cost ~$5)
    Wire clip for the connector:               # 288126 (cost $.60)
    
  This connecter is what hooks up to Temp Sensor II and (I believe) the
  injectors on '80-'84 cars.  Mine had cracked with age.  The part is not
  listed in the P&TRM and my local dealer had to do quite a bit of searching
  to find it on the microfiche.
  
  The mail-order dealers I called told me to go to Pep Boys and find
  something suitable.  Ha!  Is that what they would have done if I had
  brought my car to them for service?!
