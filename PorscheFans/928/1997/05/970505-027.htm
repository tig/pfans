Stephane, you are right.  the locking mechanism have to come off in 
order to access the door handle assembly.  You first need to remove a 
shield that cover the locking mechanism.  Second, remove a linkage from 
the door latch to the lock mechanism's motorized arm.  There is also a 
linkage from the door handle to the door latch mechanism.

Believe me, you'll appreciate the Porsche over-engineering once you are 
finished... I personally never seen so many moving parts used in a door 
latch before!  Have fun!  :-)
