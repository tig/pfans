I spent hours working on that assembly to no avail.  The aluminum "cam"
was too worn for the adjustments to work.  What finally worked was a
3/4" by 1 1/2 " .050 shim stock carefully positioned (and shaped in a
slight arch) between the cam and the plastic part that actually releases
the hatch lock.  That took up enough clearance for the mechanism to
work.
