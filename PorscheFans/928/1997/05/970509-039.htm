        If your Tach is intermitant then I'd suggest to look under the hood
after you have looked in the dash, as you said you had.  Pay particular
attension to the wires leading from the case of the distributor, and of the
components inside of it.  Make shure the distributor contacts are clean and
that the wires leading in and out are solid.  "Solid" as in if you tug on
them, while holding BOTH side they don't feel like a rubber band.  Although
it is better if you peirce the wire with a pin on either side and measure
with a ohms meter wiith the car OFF!
