>   My understanding is that the front and rear spoilers weren't
> available
>   in '78.  They were equipped on S models in Europe starting in '80
> or
>   '81 and on an "S look-alike" package available in the US in '81 or
> '82.
>   The spoilers were standard on all US spec '83-86 928s since these
> cars
>   were true S models.
>
>   I can describe my factory rear spoiler to help you determine
> whether
>   yours is a Porsche part or aftermarket.  Mine is in 3 pieces - 2
> small
>   ones on either side of the hatch and a large one on the hatch.
> The
>   large piece has two long wind deflectors (for lack of a better
> word)
>   that attach about 3/4 of the way up the hatch.  There are clips
> with
>   screws going through them on the small pieces that are visible
> when
>   the hatch is opened.  The spoiler feels as though it is made of a
>   low density rubber since I can squeeze it fairly easily.
>
>   In case you're interested, the front spoiler is a single piece of
>   plastic.  I'm not sure it would fit your car since the spoiler has
>
>   cut outs for brake vents which weren't found on the earlier cars.
>   If you do end up buying a new nose piece, you may want to look for
>
>   an '83-86 nose so that you can install the brake vents and
> spoiler.
>
>   Hope this helps.
