Proceed immediately to call Wheel Enhancement
(http://www.wheelenhancement.com) and pose this question to Robert.
Air-cooled Porsches generally have different offsets from water-cooled
ones, so actual 930 wheels (which are generally 15" or 16" anyway) may not
fit, at least not without clearance problems or messing up the suspension
geometry.
