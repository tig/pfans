Option codes:

C03     California type car
CO3    California emissions package
158     Radio "Monterey" - 86 "Reno" - 87
418     Protective side mouldings
481     5 speed manual transmission
494     Amplifier system
570     High output air conditioner
650     Sunroof

This is from a list compiled by Kevin Gross and Bruce Anderson (among
others). If anybody wants the whole list, just ask. It's a little long
to post here I guess.
