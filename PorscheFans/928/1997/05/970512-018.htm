From time to time, the issue resurfaces. For those with queries 
regarding option codes, etc I found Porsche NA very helpful.

I was curious and called PCNA. I provided the VIN # and they kindly
provided me with all my codes as well as the inception date, recall
history and where my car was serviced. I found the service very helpful
and informative. I checked out several potential  928's this way before
I purchased my 86 928 S.  I found using this tactic,  I knew more about
the cars than the sellers.
