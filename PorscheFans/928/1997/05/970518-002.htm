Greetings,

Thanks to all who suggested causes for my sudden loss of power posting. In
the end it turned out to be the exhaust gas temperature sensors on the
left side of the engine were dirty. This was causing the computer to
mis-read the temperature and shut half the cylinders down into "limp home"
mode. 

Cleaning the sensors seems to have solved the problem.
