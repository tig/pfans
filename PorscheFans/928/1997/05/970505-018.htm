>2) The cruise control is intermittent and can sometimes work even with 
the
>brake warning light on. Could this be related to the above corruption?

This is possible since the cruise control disconnect occurs when you 
use the brake.

>4)The speedo  does not work sometimes below 30 mph. Could this be 
related
>to "3)" and can any one tell me where the speed sensor can be found?

Check speed sensor, instrumentation cluster electrical connection; edge 
connectors on bottom of cluster on each side; check for electrical 
contact of rivets on printed circuit "board".

>5)The starting seems to take alot of cranking but if I ensure the fuel
>system is charged by bridging the fuel pump relay the car starts 
instantly.
>I've noticed that the fuel pump does not cut in until I've cranked the 
car
>for sometime. Should this be the case?

You might need to replace the fuel pump since there should be residual 
fuel pressure in the fuel line even when engine is not running. Your 
check valve is leaking therefore you need to crank engine alot to build 
up fuel pressure prior to starting.  Unfortunately, the check valve is 
located within the fuel pump.  You can put a check valve inline with 
the fuel line and get the same result; cheaper.... Volvo uses a 
separate check valve in their fuel system.  That's an alternative.
