Stephane Chabot inquired about the remote hatch lower part.  For 1986 ( I
don't know what years it covers)  928 512 055 20  Rear hatch lower latch .
 List $76.88.

To remove old unit, take off shield held by one screw, disconnect the motor
crank arm, and unscrew socket head screws.  Also 2 10mm atf vetucak
adjustment screws in rear.
