The only information I have got (in brackets my source of information): 
Total 928: 61,000 (up to 1/7/96; 911 & Porsche World) 
1995: 312 (Christophorus 1/96) 
1996: 28 (Christophorus 1/97) 

From PNA the numbers are: Total 928's = 25101 (I think is US sales only).
GTS models = 301 (Same as above). BTW, there was one GTS Automatic and two
GTS 5-speeds sold in 1996, making them the last new 928's. 

In the December, 1996, "Market Report" article he wrote in "Excellence"
magazine, Porsche expert Bruce Anderson states the total 928 production run
was 66,000 vehicles, to include all variations. He adds that about 100 GTS
models were sold in the U.S. in each model year, 1992-1995.
