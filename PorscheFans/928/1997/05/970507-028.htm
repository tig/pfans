Stephane,  I repacked my bearing once but cannot comment on the race 
removal since mine is in good shape.  I just used a brand name 
high-temp bearing grease from your local auto supply house.  All I can 
say is that you do not want to over-pack.  In hot temp, the grease will 
release gas and if over-packed, the gas will blow out seals.  Also, I 
can almost guarantee you that after the repack and adjustment, you'll 
need a wheel alignment.
