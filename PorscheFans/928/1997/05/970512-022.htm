Howdy James,
Yes indeedy it has the handy dandy trip computer on it.  Its functions
are: (I just happen to have the owner's manual handy)
1. Tip Odometer 1 and 2
2. Stop watch
3. Instant Fuel Econonmy (Hah!)
4. Average Fuel Eco
5. Avg. Speed
6. Outside Temp
7. MPH display
8. Total Mileage
9. Cruising Range (mile remaining on tank)

It also has "PIS: Porsche Information System" that displays various
faults and Mechanical BooBoos:
Priority 1:
1Engine Temp too high (250F)
2. Oil Pressure
3. Check Brake Fluid
Priority2:
1. Engine Temp too High (246F)
2. Toothed Belt Service (does this sense the actual tension or does it
rely on the odometer?)
3. Check oil level  
4. Save Power (service)  Basically a dead battery
5. Coolant level
6. Call Range!  (Low on gas)
7. Release Park Brake
8. ABS Off

Priority 3:
1. Brake Pads Service
2. Stop Lamp failure
3. Tail lamp failure
4. Refill Washer Fluid


Sometimes it gets distracting at 120 playing with the gas mileage thing,
but hey, its entertaining...
