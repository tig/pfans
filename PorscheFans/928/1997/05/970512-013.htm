No, chilton only makes a book for the early 928 and 924 combined.  It
does have some relavent information that are nice to look at even for
my 85.  Well worth the $6 I paid for it.

The 928 factory book are on back order and have been for a long
time.  If you have access to a microfiche reader, the microfiche
are available now for ~ $40.  

Also, you might want to look at the Mitchell book in your public
librairy.  They are fairly complete in their procedure description
but cannot be relied to be as accurate as the factory book.
