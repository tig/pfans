I've had the pleasure of working on the windshield washer system 
before, and actually it's pretty easy.  All those check valves are used 
to keep the washer system pressurized so that when you turn it on, 
there will not be any delay in the washer fluid being delivered to the 
windshield.  The valves do wear out and its relatively cheap.. (I think 
I paid about $2 apiece.)  It does take patience to remove all the hoses 
within the hood.  (at least on my '83 anyway, but not sure how the 
other years are plumbed...)
