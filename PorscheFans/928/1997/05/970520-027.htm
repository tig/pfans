Hi everyone-  Just a little tip I thought I should share, you might be aware
of this already...  I have heard of this happening to someone before, they
were putting their headlights up and the two arms broke off and the big
powerfull headlight motor pushed the arm into and through the radiator and
into the engine... doing a lot of damage.  Check the link between the arms
and make sure there is a 'c' clamp on it.
