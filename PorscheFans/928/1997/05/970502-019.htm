Not a clue on the paint, but the door lock knob is a piece of cake.

The knurled knob has a center panel made of soft plastic. It's held on with
three small prongs. Use a skinny-bladed screwdriver to pop it off and you
will reveal a nut and washer. Remove these and the knob pops off. Push on
the dished bezel with your palm and unscrew it.
