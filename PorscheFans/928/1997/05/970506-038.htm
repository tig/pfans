Synthetic oils such as Mobil 1, Valvoline & (my favorite) Castrol do provide
enhanced lubricity over refined crude base oil.  They should be changed as 
often, and in all my cars, there is a slight increase in gas mileage, just about
enough to pay the difference between the cost.  The 928 goes from 17-18 mpg in
my normal driving averaged over 3000 miles between changes (and I only use
to go back & forth to work).

I have had really bad experience with Quaker State Gear lube.

Always use the weight of Lube per MFG Recommendation-not only are there gears,
but bearings, which seem to be less tolerant to viscosity changes than gears/
syncros.

Anyone know of a low mileage (>30k) 928 S for sale?  5-spd, any color OK
