20W50 is what Porsche specifies for the S4. I don't know specifically about
the S, but like the idea of maintaining viscosity and film strength at high
ambient temperatures for any engine. The higher the second number, the
better the oil will do that. My favorite repair shop uses 20W50 in all
Porsches, even those pesky rear-engined ones.

The only reason I know ever to depart from oil of this rating in this car
is for operation in extremely cold temperatures, where the "20" end of the
rating will lead to the oil congealing in the crankcase when the engine is off.
