The hatch lock is straight forward.  The door lock, you need to remove 
all panels to access the electrical connector to the alarm system.  
Then you need to disengage a linkage that goes from the cylinder arm 
lever to the door latch.  Remove a bolt that secure the cylinder from 


the side; located a few inches directly on top of the door latch.  Pull 
the cylinder out along with the electrical wiring.
