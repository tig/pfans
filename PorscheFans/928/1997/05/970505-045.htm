I just figured out a related problem, but it sounds a bit different. There
was corroded/loose wiring in my central electric panel that cut power to
the fuel pump.

To change the fuel filter, you have to remove a little plastic panel at the
rear of the car below the bumper cap. There will be two cylinder like
objects, one is the filter and one is the fuel pump.
