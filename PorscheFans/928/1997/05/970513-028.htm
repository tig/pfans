I've always had a personal view that when dealing with 'older' sports cars
its more likely that an auto hasn't been thrashed or tracked in the past and
thus the whole package is probably in better overall condition than the
manual gearbox equivalent. I find that auto vehicles tend to be treated much
kinder in general use and the wear and tear from improper maintenance habits
is lessened.
