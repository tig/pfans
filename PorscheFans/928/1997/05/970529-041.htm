I've seen posts regarding the difficulty of jacking up the 928 and thought
I'd share my solution with you all. Though I have a floor jack, I only have a
one car garage and space is limited. (I can only justify one 928 as I'd hate
to let the other one sit outside: Sad isn't it?) Anyway, what I did was go to
the local junkyard and pick up four spare tire jacks. They came from
different cars, but all are shaped like a hydraulic floor jack. Kinda (is
that a word?) like a bumper jack laid down. They were cheap (only 5 $ each)
and are very strong and stable. MUCH more stable than scissor jacks. I also
use my jackstands near the lift pads for safety. If jacking all four corners,
jack car evenly and slowly. Use your best judgement and PLEASE don't get hurt
or hurt your car on my account. (That's the disclaimer part :)
