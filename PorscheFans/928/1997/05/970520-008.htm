I looked into this a while back. Personally I wouldn't upgrade the lamps
without changing to Euro spec lenses. The dispersion pattern is *MUCH* better
for oncoming traffic that the stock US lights. But I would *MUCH* rather SEE
than hope I am seen by someone else. Having someone drive at you with excessive
lights can be annoying, but if some drivers are severly handicapped by this
then I would suggest they have second thought about driving at night. In the US
there is a tendency towards everyone having absolute rights to drive despite
problems that would invalidate them in most other countries.

Personally I feel very UNSAFE with any stock US lights. If you drive only as
far as is visible [i.e. safely] you WILL be driving about 40MPH on the
interstates let alone smaller roads.

Driving at night I REALLY miss my UK 944, put main beam on [brights] and the
world lit up in front of you. Over here you might as well wave a flashlight out
the window!
