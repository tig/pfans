To answer both of your questions, at least in part....I have replaced the
windshield in my 1982 928, but I did it with my checkbook--after asking for
advice from this list, and determining that the $400 I paid was relatively
cheap.  I have previously replaced a 911 windshield (in answer to the
second part of your question, there are some of us who have both) but the
928 was a totally different job--and members of the list convinced me that
it was one of those jobs best left to professionals.

Specifically regarding the 911/928 question, I have one of each currently,
and have driven both in many different types of conditions/events including
track events (the only thing I've done in a 911--while driving, that
is--that I haven't done in a 928 is enter a PCA Club Race), and I can't
think of any two cars I'd rather have--even though they are dramatically
different (moreso because my 928 is an automatic).  I hope I never have to
make a choice about which one to keep.....
