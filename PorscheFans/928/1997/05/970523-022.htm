I can say a kind word for Performance Friction Carbon Metallic,
80 compound pads. Plus a good-quality brake fluid, like Ate Blue.
Solved my problems with severe brake fade at track events, 
work OK on the street (real race pads don't work well until
they're hot, so they will scare the hell out of you when they
are cold). There must be other, comparable pads from other
manufacturers; just be sure they are appropriate for a street-
driven car.
