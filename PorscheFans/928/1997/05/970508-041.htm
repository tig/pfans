This is what I did:

I bought two 3" diameter, Borla XR-1 racing line mufflers. One 16" long,
and one 12" long. I put the longer one as the final muffler, and the 12"
long one as a replacement for the catalytic convertor (and middle muffler).


The car is definitely faster, no convertor, 3" free-flow exhaust, these
things contribute to a nice boost I can feel and use. Also, it sounds
really cool!
