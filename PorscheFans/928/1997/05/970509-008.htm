> Where is the air pump filter?  What is the procedure and cost?

The air pump filter is located in a small canister located on the
left wall between the radiator and the engine.  In my car, it is an all
metal can with a wingnut that holds the cover in place.  The new
filter cost me ~$11.  The time for the replacement is 5 minutes if you
include opening the hood ;-)
