Hi Folks,

Someone asked about the switching point of the electric fan, on my '87 S4
it comes on just as the needle gets to the upper "normal" limit on the temp.
gauge and this happens with the hood open or closed so watch those hands!
The temp. shown in the instrumentation is taken from the engine itself but 
the fan is switched by a seperate sensor on the bottom of the radiator in
front of the "out" hose so there may be some differences in switching points
depending on conditions but it should'nt be very noticable.
Problems could be Electrical (either sensor or wiring), water pump
efficiency or a radiator that needs flushing.
