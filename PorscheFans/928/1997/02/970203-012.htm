rear wing and interior components in good condition. He says that the PO said

chemical process. I guess that is a good reason not to use it. I purchased a
Zymol car care kit that contained a vinyl treatment and it works like a
charm! I can't advise of its long term performance but, being a Zymol
product, it can't be bad. In conclusion, many people say to keep all silicon
products away from your car. They might be easy to apply but they don't
protect in the long term.
