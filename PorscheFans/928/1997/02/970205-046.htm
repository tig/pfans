     Sorry for the long delay is getting back to you but as you will see 
     the list is long and it took some time to compile :-)  So here is the 
     list of mods to my little 89 GT:
     
     Wheels:  Porsche OEM Cup wheel 17 x 9.5 front and rear with Yokohama 
     AVS Intermediate tires 255/40 ZR-17 front; 275/35 ZR-17 rear.
     
     Brakes:  Upgraded to Porsche GTS brakes in front with 4 piston Brembo 
     calipers and GTS rotors.
     
     Shocks/springs:  Koni externally adjustable coilovers with Hypercoil 
     600lb front/400lb rear springs with adjustable spring perches.
     
     Sway bar:  DEVEK S4/race front sway bar, 6.0 mm wall thickness with 
     adjustable drop links.
     
     Clutch:  GTS pressure plate and clutch provides 23% greater holding 
     pressure for the increased output engine.
     
     The DynoTECH 6.0 liter engine. Here's a summary of what that includes:
     
     -  DEVEK stroker kit with Carrillo rods, Mahle pistons and DEVEK 
     stroker crank with modified pickup off mains
     -  baffled oil pan for improved oil pickup 
     -  Big valve heads, ported and polished
     -  high performance valve springs
     -  30 lb/hr fuel injectors
     -  rising rate fuel pressure regulator
     -  Modified headers and custom dual 3" exhaust system with dual 
     catalytic converters
     -  DEVEK radiator 
     -  engine blueprinted and balanced to very high tolerances
     
     Current figures:  335 hp and 387 lb-ft torque to the rear wheels.
     I hope this is going to go up just a little bit because we found a bad 
     vacuum diaphragm that let fuel enter the vacuum system. This has been 
     fixed but I have not had time to re-dyno the car since then.
     
     As for cost, look at your current catalog from Devek. You say you do

     that are not in the catalog are: 
        big valve heads. Those are kinda custom so call and get a current

        Engine building services from DynoTECH. these are quoted on a
            case-by-case basis. There are just too many variables for them
            to make a blanket price.
