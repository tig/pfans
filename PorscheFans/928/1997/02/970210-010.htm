The catalyst is not "a glorified filter".  It is a chemical product
that makes the reaction oxidizing the NOx and CO faster and more
complete.  This chemical can however be contaminated (ie, you cannot
use leaded fuel with a catalyst).

Just a few possible problem:

Have hou had a tune up lately?  Maybe you are running too rich or
your Lambda sensor needs replacement.
