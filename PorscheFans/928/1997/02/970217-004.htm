At 07:23 PM 1/25/97 -0500, you wrote:
>I need some views on synthetic oil. Recently purchased a 1987 S4 and the
>Previous owner used Castrol. Interested in all opinions.

The reading from here is 100% positive. I switched from conventional 20W50
to Amsoil Series 2000 20W50 about a year ago and have observed the following:

- quieter, smoother running, particularly at high engine speed

- freer revving, particularly under hard acceleration; this provides at
least the feeling of greater acceleration

- easier starting, particularly at low (0-20 F) temperatures

- better gas mileage (+1-2 mpg under all circumstances)

- less oil consumption and/or leakage (generally no top-ups between changes)

- no transient bad effects when first changing over

OK, it's true that each oil change costs more, because the synthetic oil's
more expensive. I can't think of any other drawback.
