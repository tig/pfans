The VIN says it's the 127th 928, 1984 model (that's the E). The
motor number says US 928S engine with auto trans, the trans
number says US 4-speed auto. That stuff is in the technical
spec book. Incidentally, the car is one of the last to have
the aluminum lower ball joint mounts, which some on this list
have had failures with.

According to an option list which may not be entirely correct:
CO3 - CA emissions
158 - some radio
415 - wider rear track (interesting, check it out)
438 - comfort seat right
468 - graduated tint glass
494 - 2 speakers in back
650 - sunroof
