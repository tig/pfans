I have a couple of problems with my car, which someone can hopefuly
enlighten me upon.

My first problem is the air con, or more accurately the centre vent. 
When I accelerate (even gently) the centre vent closes, and dosen't
open again.  If I turn the selector to the 'eco' position the vent
will open again.  I can then move it back to aircon and it will work

air).  I gather it must be vacuum driven somewhere along the line -
