> To figure out whether your car is an S, you could find the engine
> type designation. It is located on a horizontal surface, below and
> toward the centerline from the connection of the upper radiator
> hose to the block. If it's M28.19 or M28.20, it's an S, US model.
> If it's M28.11 or M28.12, it's an S, ROW model. Vanilla 928 for
> 1981 should be M28.15, M28.16, M28.09, or M28.10.
> 
> The above is from the technical spec book, which indicates that
> there were no 928S chassis numbers for US cars in 1981, but then
