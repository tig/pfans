Your friendly Porsche parts source sells a nice little parts catalog
for water-cooled Porsches up to about 1987, for around $10. It has
reprints of a few Porsche tech bulletins, including the one about
the aluminum vs. steel ball joints. Actually, first thing to do is
take a magnet and see if it sticks to the outermost piece of the 
lower control arm. Note that this is NOT a recall, just a product
improvement, and PCNA has apparently shown no interest in helping
pay for the upgrade.
