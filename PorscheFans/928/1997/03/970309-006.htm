Devek already has two families of HRE-built wheels for 928s, whose offsets
are VERY carefully worked out by Devek to permit putting 275-section tires
on front and rear of unmodified bodies without interference; this is quite
a trick.

Fikse basically builds wheels to order (as does HRE), so you tell them what
rim width, offset, finish, etc. you want. You'd better be right, because
they won't take the wheels back if there's a fitment problem. They have
some experience with 928s, and will make some conservative recommendations.
