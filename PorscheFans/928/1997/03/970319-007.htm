The '89 I have now replaced a 50K mile '85, and I wouldn't have done it if it
hadn't been run over by a semi.  The '89 has a bit more power, but with as
much HP as we are talking about, either car seemed to have all I needed.  I
chiped the '85, which would have added maybe 20-30 HP on the peak, getting it
to within a hair of the S4.  

 










I also chipped the '89 and added a K&N filter, who knows what the power is
now.  I would guess it went from 316 to somewhere around 385 to 400 on the
peak.  The Autothority claimed up to 20% on the peak on the S4, and K&N flows
45% more air to the engine.  Remember that this is not wheel power, but
engine power.  Reduce by about 15% to get wheel power.  

A couple of things that I do like on the '89 are the anti-lock brakes
(experiment in the rain so that you know how the car reacts to inputs when
the antilock is engaged), the trip computer, and the fog/driving lights (on
the '85 and '86 there are only fog lights in the front fascia). I also like
the wider stock wheels on the rear.  And in general I think the S4 and GT
have cleaner lines.
