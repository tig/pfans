Doug, I had a 6 disk cd changer mounted behind the passenger side rear seat.
It was attached to a mounting board that was below the deck carpet. (don't
screw into the metal here, it could penetrate gas tank). The board went to
about where the spare tire compartment was. we then added a pad over spare
tire compartment to elevate the carpet to the same level that the mounting
board had. CD cartridge was acsessable by folding down rear seat to expose
the front loading door.(changer faced the seat back) It worked fairly well.
Good luck with your iris blue shark looking thing!!
