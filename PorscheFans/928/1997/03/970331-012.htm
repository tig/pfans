Wrap the bleeder valve's THREAD with about 2" of teflon tape and 
re-insert.  I suggest doing this for all the valves before you start 
suction.  I've had a few situation where the bleeder valve was just 
tight enough to prevent brake fluid leak under pedal pressure, but 
allow air to seep into the line while under suction.
