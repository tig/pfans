At 01:17 PM 3/25/97 -1000, you wrote:
>Any thoughts on locking open the slats?

Porsche decided that the aerodynamic benefit of closing the slats when full
airflow through the radiator was not needed was itself not needed, so that
system was omitted from the 928 GTS.

The slats were part of an all-out campaign to reduce drag (a bugaboo of
pre-S4 928 cars) and made for a bit more dramatically reduced Cd as part of
the feature set of the 928 S4 at its introduction.
