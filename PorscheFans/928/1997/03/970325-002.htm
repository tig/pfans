At least on the '84, the fan is controlled by two switches: the coolant
tenperature switch, and the refrigerant temperature switch. They are in
parallel, so either one can make the fan run. And if one or the other
fails open, you'll probably never know it, since the fan will go 
ahead and run because the other switch is doing its job.
