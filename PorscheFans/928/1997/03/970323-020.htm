Man you've got long arms :)

Standing at the rear of the car, hatch open, I mounted my changer on the
right side so as not to interfere with getting at the spare/battery, and
hopefully avoid any tracking problems from vibrations on a rear seat back
installation.  Of course, the changer skips all on it's merry way if road
vibration is just right so go figure.

The trickest part of my install is mounting the amp, secondary amp fuse and
LF crossover on the parcel shelf - I even re-routed the globebox AC hose to
blow over the amp for WOT and also mounted a small red LED to illuminate
the fuse.  The light is triggered by the remote turn-on lead so it only
lights when the amp is on...

What's your system plan?
