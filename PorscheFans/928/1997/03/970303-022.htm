what you can do is buy a few resistors and install them into
the appropriate thermistor/sensor locations to functionally test the
output stage of the flap controls.  I had to drive with a resistor
in there last summer for a few days while I waited for a new main temp 
sensor to arrive after I found a bad one.

There's also a control box in the passengar side with test points
on it.  How the system works is in the factory manuals.
