>Well, while I am waiting for the parts to come in to change the 
>timming belt, I am looking around the engine bay and trying to figure
>out where all that **** oil came from.
>

When you get a chance, remove the driver-side cam gear housing, the one 
directly behind the cam year along with the distributor.  Behind  it, 
you'll find an oil galley about the size of a dime as well as a small 
neoprene washer.  This washer tends to shrink over time and therefore 
loses it's sealing property.  I bet that's where the majority of your 
oil leak comes from.  Reinstall using a good quality sealant.  
Apparently Porsche does not use any type of sealant on the mating 
surface.  It took me almost two years to figure this one out.
