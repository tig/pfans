Cardboard warped from heat, probably ruined, very common.  In the

>       2) One front speaker (the larger one of the two) for the door.

The four inch driver can be easily replaced.  UNSCREW the mesh cover
(thanks Leonard!).  Note that the smaller mid/tweet is wired in-line with a
capacitor.  Don't bypass it or you'll fry that smaller driver at high
volume.
