Rob, I can't remember what year they switched to 2 electric fans, 87 I think.
So your 86 should have 1 belt drive fan & 1 electric, anyway the electric fan
can be switched to come on with the ignition by bypassing the thermal switch
& plugging in into the incoming power lead. I do this on my 85 every summer
in Texas when the temp gets above 90 or so. it does help keep her a little
cooler in traffic.
