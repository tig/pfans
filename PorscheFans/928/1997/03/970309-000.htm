anyone know how to decode a Porsche VIN#
WPOJBO922DS8604777


WPO: West Germany - Porsche


JBO: called a VDS code, would be ZZZ for a Euro car; the chassis is
stamped ZZZ in any case


92: first two digits of 928


2: test digit, computed from the rest of the VIN


D: 1983 model year


S: manufactured in Stuttgart


8: third digit of 928


6: body and engine code; don't know specifically what this means


0477: consecutive serial number


7: excess number; not part of standard VIN (Porsche didn't make that many
928s in that or any other model year)
