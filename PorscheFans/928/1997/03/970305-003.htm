My guess from your experience is that the Anteras are chromed.

Chroming wheels reduces the oxidation protection.

--Which is not a problem as long as you put low-humidity air into your
tires. But add 'wet' air, and sooner or later, you will get corrosion. And
corrosion begets leakage.

I have seen people use pure nitrogen to prevent this problem, but avoiding
unfiltered, humid air will do it, too.

Once the oxidation has started, you need to see a specialty wheel dealer to
stop the leakage.
