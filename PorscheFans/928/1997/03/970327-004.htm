Not so!
All S4 and GT cars ('87-'91 in the US, '92 elsewhere) are supposed to run
36/44. The GTS ('92+) went to 17" wheels and 36/36 tire pressures. Don't
drop your rear tire pressures; they're supposed to be run at 44 psi!

===================================================

The manual for my late '86 also has 36 front & 44 rear, running 225/50-16's
on flat disc wheels.  I usually run 38 or 40 up front for sharper turn in &
somewhat lighter steering, with no ill effects on ride or, apparently, no
adverse wear characteristics on my relatively new Dunlop D40-M2's.
