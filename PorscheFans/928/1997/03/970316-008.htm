Reminds me of an old story...

Two fellows are sitting talking in a street in Calcutta. The first fellow
says "Its definitely Double 'U' Double 'O' Double 'M' - WOOMM!!!" His friend
then says (in his best Indian Accent - "No, No, No. Its Double 'U' Single
'O' and Double 'M' - WOMM!!!!"
At hearing this a helpful British gentleman strolled up and said to them
both. "I'm sorry chaps but I do believe you are both mistaken, the correct
spelling is Double 'U' Single 'O' Single 'M' and a 'B' - WOMB."
The two Indian fellows looked at him strangely and replied "With all respect
kind Sir, I believe that we are much better placed than yourself to spell
the sound of an elephant passing wind!!!"
