Use a valve stem puller to loosen the valve stem (assuming it like a regular
valve stem, you can determine this by comparing) to loosen the valve and let
the air run out freely.  You don't have to pull the whole stem, you will know
when it is out enough to let the air run out.  The owners manual says you
have to let it gradually reform to the collapsed shape for a while.  Couple
of days, several hours.

Valve stem puller looks somewhat like a regular screw driver with a notch cut
in it.  It fits over the valve and then you can unscrew the valve from the
stem.
