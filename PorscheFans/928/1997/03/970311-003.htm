Be careful using any oven cleaner on aluminum. Once Upon A Time there was a 
magazine known as "Cycle" who once printed a Tech Hints section where one 
reader advised using oven cleaner on aluminum motorcycle engines. When the 
lawsuits started Cycle quickly terminated the Tech Hints section.

The oven cleaner turned some engines brown (this was before black engines 
were popular). And ate the clear coat off most others.

Try the oven cleaner on the *inside* of the wheel before you do anything 
that would show. And do it at your own risk.
