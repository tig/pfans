In a message dated 3/6/97 1:44:38 AM, you wrote:

>I recently acquired one of these fine machines that is not a fine as it
>could be. I need some advice from a CIS expert for the '79 928. The car
>starts and idles fine, just below 1k, but when the accelerator is
>depressed the motor bogs, gasps, sputters up to 2k when it stalls
>completely. P.O. was not an enthusiast and has neglected our girl. Where
>should I start?


Possibly the boot on the injection unit ( I don't have any familiarity with
the 928 layout in order to tell you where to look, it should be fairly
obvious; but I have dealt with CIS on several other cars and one of them had
this problem) is torn, or cracked.  I would also expect that the injectors
are either dirty or worn out.  Finally, could be a vacuum leak, listen
closely for the sucking sounds, and see if any get louder as you open the
throttle a little.
