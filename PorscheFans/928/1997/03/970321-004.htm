That switch is either a rear hatch release of the fader for the stereo.  I
don't know about the older cars.  I think you probably have the fader there,

the instrument pod, top knob, turn to the right to operate.  If the Euro is
the same as the US, that is.  But there are plenty of Euro owners out there
to help.
