>>Need some help please, just got a 1979 928, RH power window FU, have 12VDC
>>to aft connection of motor with key on, switches to fwd connection when
>>switch depressed to down position, is this good?? Planning on motor RPL.

The schematic implies that you should have ground on both motor leads
with the switch in neutral. Then one side stays at ground, the other
goes to +12 when you depress the switch; obviously, opposite leads
get the +12 depending on whether you want up or down. The source of
the ground is the brown lead from the switch itself. So the switch
itself may be the culprit, try swapping leads with the switch for
the LH window.
