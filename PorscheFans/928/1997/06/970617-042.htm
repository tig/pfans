I can't recall a single 928 (and I've had six of them) that didn't flash the
"Belt Tension" warning from time to time.  After having the tension checked
a number of times on a few cars I finally just ignored it.  I have also had
false indications of brake pad wear, tail lights out, and several others
that I can't recall.  That electronic nightmare up above the passengers feet
should have been redesigned a long time ago.  Its' one of the 928s worst
weak spots in an otherwise superb automobile.
