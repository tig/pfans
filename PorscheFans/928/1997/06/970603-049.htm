Binding of rebuilt racks is quite common as pattern parts will have been used. 
This problem very unlikely to happen with genuine
factory replacement. Suggest you go back to mechanic who fitted your rack and 
get him to supply another but be prepared for
same fault if the rack is not the genuine article! I know the above from 
experience with two other 928S4's.
