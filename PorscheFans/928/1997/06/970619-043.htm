Speed Racer wrote:

>>I've got an '85 928S. When the steering wheel/instrument pod is all the
>>way down the pod rubs against the dash and squeaks annoyingly.

One of the bolts that holds the instrument cluster into the pod is
excentric.  It could be turned to allow centering the pod/cluster.

Another possibility that actually might be easier to adjust is the three
5mm socket-head bolts that hold the steering column.  You can loosen
these (careful not to strip them out as I did) and move the column for
adjustment about 1/8" up/down/left/right.  This might be enough to get
rid of the squeek.
