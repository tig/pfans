> Does the Porsche 928 use a timing belt or a timing chain?  Is one better

The 928 use a rubber belt which must be changed every 45,000 miles.

Which is better??  I guess they each have their pros and con.  I don't
think that people use chain for overhead cams very frequently (I think
Saab does).  The chain might not break but his heavier and will stretch
with time so you still have to change it.  The rubber belt is  lighter
and can be made to do more turns so they use them to drive other things
at the same time thus saving the addition of other belts.
