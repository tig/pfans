>>You are correct that Porsche calls for a 75W90 GL5 gear oil.
I would have recommended the 75W90NS in your application, but if it is
working OK...then I wouldn't change. Does the car have limmited slip? If   
so
you would probably need a more slippery fluid than the NS, to reduce
chatter. My usual "Porsche" recommendation is 75W90NS, except with   
limmited
slip, then I recommend adding some Friction Modifier to just eliminate   
the
chatter. The 75W90 has friction modifier added , and is a slippery fluid,
the "NS" is Not Slippery. I hope I havn't confused you with all the   
above,
I know of others that are using 75W90 in gear boxes also with no problem,
so it seems to be all right.
