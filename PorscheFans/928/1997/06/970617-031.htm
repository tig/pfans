you can tell by the serial #.  If you have any of the automotion or
other magazine, look at the brake pad section and they will tell
you what the number is.

If you are familiar with brakes, The "newer" 86 had the upgraded 4
pistons calipers (fairly large assembly which might be stamped
with Porsche on it(?)).  The older brakes are a floating calliper
type (large piston only on the inside)
