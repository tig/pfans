At 02:55 PM 6/19/97 -0400, you wrote:
>When the tension light comes on temporarily, does the [ ! ] light on the
>console come on and stay on or does it go out after the tension light
>goes out?

The "!" light comes on when any important warning light comes on. If a
transient event sets off the belt tension warning, the specific warning
light and the "!" both come on, and both will stay on until you stop and
restart the engine. Generally, If the "!" has gotten your attention for
something you don't care about (e.g., spurious brake light warnings), you
can cancel it by pushing the illuminated "!" button.
