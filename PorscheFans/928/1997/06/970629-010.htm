This is not correct behavior. Your car automatically adjusts the clutch, so
these symptoms probably mean that it's time to check out the clutch for
contamination or excess wear.

>[speculation that the clutch may be original with ~60k miles]

>Is it time to replace the clutch?  What is a "normal" replacement interval?

Because driving styles and circumstances vary so much, I don't think
there's a firm rule about miles for clutch replacements. When the clutch is
worn out or screwed up, replace it. However, make sure that the hydraulic
system which actuates the clutch is healthy; ditto the automatic adjustment
mechanism.
