Never did it but heard a lot about them when I was thinking of
getting a 944.  The most important thing they mentioned was to
get a high quality tool to pull the bolts (I think its either an
allen or 12 points):  This means to go talk to your local Snap on
guy.

Clean the bolts thouroughly before attempting to lowen them.  Then
insert the socket and tap it in with a hammer to ensure it goes all
the way in.
