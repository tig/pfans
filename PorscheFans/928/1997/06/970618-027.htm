Speed Racer,
Don't lower the pod all the way down. Just kidding.

Get out your flashlight, phillips screw driver and 6mm(I think) allen 
wrench.

Remove the black plastic trim piece from underneath your "Pod" (the one 
where your instrument illumination wheel. etc. is)
Look up inside your "Pod" on the front left and front right side you will 
see an allen head screw on each side. The one on your Left is in a 
"slotted" type hole. Loosen it, lower your pod and pull the pod away from 
dash(where it is rubbing), then tighten the allen bolt back up. If there is 
not enough slack with this "Pod fine tuning adjustment" your vinyl may be 
swelling from sunlight. Takes about 5 minutes to do.
