>I have personally never rebuilt an 928 MC and besides, the cost of a
>new/rebuilt is quite reasonable.  But I can relay my experience in
>rebuilding a Volvo MC.  I gather all MCs are about the same.  A major
>difference is whether it is a single or dual stage brake system.  When
>you disassemble the MC, remove the plunger and check the bore for wear.
> If you see any wear, don't bother to proceed further.  Replace all
>seals that comes with the rebuild kit.  Pre-lube the seals and bore
>with the proper brake fluid first.  Slide the plunger back in and check
>for play.  It should operate smoothly.  I recommend that you do the
>rebuild in a clean environment.  Dirt is a no-no, especially in the
>bore and seals.  Should take about 1 hour to do.  You MUST pressure
>bleed the system and prime the MC before the install.  Also, make sure
>you have a good set of flare nut wrenches before you start removing the
>lines.  Its cheaper to buy new flare nut wrenches than replacing a
>brake line!  I hope this help!
