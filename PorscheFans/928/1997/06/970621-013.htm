What is the condition of your alternator belt??

You might want to go to your local part store for them to look at
the condition of your charging system.  I know they are not expert
on Porsche, but the tool they use is pretty standard.

If the alternator belt is OK and not slipping and you are not
getting more than 12 V, either the alternator is dead, disconnected or
the voltage regulator is shot.

Again, if you can remove the alternator, you local part folks
can test it easily.
