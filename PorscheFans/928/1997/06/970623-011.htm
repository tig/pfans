Dear Mark,

The BMW 850CSi is a wonderful car for its purpose - high speed luxury
touring.  You can drive non-stop cross country and get out feeling like
you've had a good night's sleep at the Marriott.  The car is loaded with
electronics and hydraulic systems for everything one could imagine.  The
aluminum 322hp. V12 weighs within pounds of the medicore 282hp V8 but
pulls like a runaway locomotive throughout its rev band.

Unfortunately, it is heavier and much more expensive to maintain than the
928.  Also, they tend to hold their resale value far too high for most
us...
