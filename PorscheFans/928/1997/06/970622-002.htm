FWIW I read in a Porsche brochure that the 928 paint is 2 - 3 times thicker
than 'ordinary' production cars - and from the paint chip damage I've seen
on hoods, it does look thick...

Go easy, by hand, no buffers when using an aggressive cleaner/swirl
remover.  McGuire's has a full line-up rough to finish.  Note also that a
final wax does wonders to fill-in minor swirls (Jim I polished the GT today
and lots of swirls & scratches are gone/hidden).
