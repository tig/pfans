Frank, switch the trans and diff fluid to Red Line synthetic.  Note that
you'll want the higher-weight GL-5, *not* the MTL!  No more sluggish
or crunchy shifting when cold, plus you'll gain a couple hp at the rear
wheel through lower friction losses.  You'll need 4 quarts.
