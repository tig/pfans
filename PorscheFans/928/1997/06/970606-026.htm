A while back I posted a letter about the lack of improved performance when I
installed AutoThority chips in my 88 S4.  Well...things have changed and I'm
happy.  Here's the story.  I sent my chips back to AutoThority who confirmed
that the software was at least 5 years old and two revisions behind.  At no
cost to me, they replaced the chips with the latest software (mighty nice of
them, I think).  I installed these "new" chips and am satisfied.  The car
does perform better.  Now they don't turn Clark Kent into Superman, but it's
a difference I can feel and appreciate.  AND, gas mileage isn't bad.  I can
still get 19mpg around town if I'm easy on it.  If anyone is thinking about
chipping their car, email me for more specific info.  Thanks AutoThority!!
Your a good company.
