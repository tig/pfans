I was loosing freon in my 84 S to a point where a recharge would last about
3 months.  I took it to my mechanic (Matt at Targa in Austin Tx).  He used a 
sniffer and located the leak in the aluminum tube that
runs down the right side near the radiator.  He replaced the same and
recharged the system for about $450.  This was last summer.  The Air still 
works great now, so he obviously fixed it.
