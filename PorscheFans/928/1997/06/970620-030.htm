A factory Mustang eliminator device.   (ok Pony fans, just joking!)

Recall that while the earlier 2-speed auto shifters can select 1st gear
manually by the shift lever, the 4-speed auto shifters only have positions
for P R N D 3 2 - no 1st!  Thus the kickdown 'button'...

The kickdown switch is on the firewall directly under the gas pedal.  It's
purpose is to allow full-throttle kickdown of the autotrans for
acceleration, most notably selection of 1st gear (on 4-speed a/t cars) from
a standing start instead of the normal 2nd gear starts (concession to CAFE
requirements for economy).
