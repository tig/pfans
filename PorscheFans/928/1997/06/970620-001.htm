While we're at it, I hope you don't mind if I throw a plug in for
Flowmaster - why you might ask since I'm not really a fan of how they
sound?  Mainly because of their involvement with the 928 aftermarket
development.  For example, this goes way back before there was even a DEVEK
- when it was just Bob DeVore himself as featured in the Excellence
'Arizona Highwayman' issue (1990 - I just got a copy of that issue and

always welcome.  Remember the quote in 'Winning' - these performance shops
