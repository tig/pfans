This is INCORRECT, starting from year 1983 the 928S does *NOT* have valve
clearence, if you break the timing belt it will bend the valves. 
My mechanic said this to me and I confirmed it by calling Porsche of 
North America Customer Support center. I spoke with the technician 
myself (not the with the answering personel). When they went to the larger
displacement engine the clearence went away. Earlier years 82 and below
the valves will not bend.

BTW I prefer the 83 or 84 year, the engine is much simpler than the 32
valve one and relatively less costly to maintain. It is still expensive no
matter what year.
