(Adam wrote:)
> Well it's time for a major brake overhaul on my 83 euro.  Basically I need
> new rotors, pads and the calipers are due for a rebuild (drivers side is
> sticking a little).  What I was wondering is about the S4 brake upgrade.  I
> gather it does not require a rocket scientist to perform, and I remember
> reading somewhere on this list that you do not need to mess with the master
> cylinder.  Do you need anything apart from the rotor/caliper assembly?

  Looks like you won't need to update the master cylinder if you go with
  the Devek upgrade kit (based on the fact that they don't include one in
  their package).  Checking on whether a larger MC would provide added 
  benefit with an upgrade kit would probably be a good idea though (less
  pedal pressure(?), less PSI concentrated on stressed components(?) - I 
  don't know but bring these up for consideration).
  
  The Devek kit comes with adapters which will allow you to use the stock 
  spindles with the new calipers.  I think if you were to try the update 
  without such an adapter, you would need to upgrade the spindles and 
  everything that would encompass (ball joints, control arms, etc.).
  
  Note that you'll need an S4 alloy spare with the upgraded brakes.
