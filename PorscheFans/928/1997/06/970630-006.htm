I just put in the Autothority (performance) chips for my '88 S4 this
past weekend.  I felt like a teenager again anxiously wondering what the
results would be -- my first test drive was at about midnight Saturday,
with the "brains" still unmounted and screwdrivers rolling around the
passenger footwell.

From only two short outings this past weekend I can say there is a
difference in the characteristics of engine performance.  Here's what I
feel and observe:

	* no gain (or loss) in low-end torque
	* slight gain in midrange power
	* modest gain in high-end power

The tires are definitely "breaking loose" with greater ease, and I had
the sensation the engine could easily be over-rev'd.

I'm curious if anyone in the group has real dyno results (before/after),
and if these compare with my unmeasured observations.
