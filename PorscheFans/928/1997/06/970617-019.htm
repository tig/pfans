>a stange large dent in the anti sway bar that is on top of the engine.

That bar is not an anti-sway bar, but a brace to keep the front suspension
from moving sideways at the top under load. Any way I can imagine this bar
becoming bent is too gruesome to contemplate. You did well to leave this
"bargain".

By the way, anyone who is contemplating removing this bar for any reason
should first park the car on a flat, level surface and LEAVE IT THERE,
UNMOVED until you replace the bar. Without the bar, the front of the car
becomes flexible enough so that changing the load on any wheel may flex the
car and change the front suspension geometry.
