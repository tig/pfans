OK, I admit it, my dash *is* cracked.  But my antenna works fine (1982)!
It didn't retract all the way until I applied my super secret turbo ultra
amazing techo trick....

Coat the antenna with SYNTHETIC engine oil.  I use Red Line, partly   
because
I'm a huge Red Line fan, but more importantly the Red Line lubricants in
general resist running off of parts.  It's very slippery, stays on the   
parts, and
doesn't seem to collect too much dust.  Dip a small rag in a tablespoon   
or
so of the oil, and wipe the antenna a bunch of times, running it up and
down.
