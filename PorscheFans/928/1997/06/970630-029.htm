 Harry,

 Items needed for repair: small phillips screwdriver, clean cloth, putty knife, 2 part epoxy repair putty, suitable weights 
 
 Repair Procedure:

 1 - Remove console lid from hinge assembly - 7 small phillips screws around inside perimeter on lid

 2 - Gently remove the felt backing from inside the lid

 3 - Place lid top down on a cloth, press or knead internal cracks to make all cracks level (as if there were no cracks)

 4- Mix 2 part epoxy according to instructions, then use putty knife to spread even coat of the epoxy over entire plastic surface     
    (approx. 1/16" to 1/8" thick)

 5 - Place Felt back over plastic area in same position as original

 6 - Place suitable weights on top of felt to keep the entire lid flat and to keep the cracks from "popping" up.

 7-  Leave overnight, BE PATIENT

 8 - Reinstall lid next day, Console Lid should be stronger than it was originally.
