Fred,
Although you obviously have a serious problem and need some clutch work. 
Here is a tip for you when you go to replace your clutch.

There has always been a small amount of clutch chatter reported in most 
928s built after the change to a single plate clutch. That is why Porsche 
released an updated throw-out bearing, guide tube, and clutch fork release 
arm. YOU MUST USE ALL OF THESE PIECES TOGETHER. THEY WILL NOT FIT PROPERLY 
UNLESS ALL UPDATED PARTS ARE USED TOGETHER.

 If you do replace your clutch, Make sure that you get the updated 
Throw-out Bearing, guide tube, and Release arm. It will totally smooth up 
your clutch engagement.

Here are the Porsche part numbers for the latest updated parts.

Throw -out Bearing - 928.116.085.08 (lists for over $200, but worth it)
Guide Tube ----------- 928.116.087.16
Clutch Fork Arm----  928.116.832.09
