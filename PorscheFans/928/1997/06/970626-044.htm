Works great! Worth the effort.  Since the shifter lever is 2" shorter,
there is a little more effort required to shift (less leverage), however
the distance travelled is less (knob travels through a shorter arc).  A
major improvement is that the lever, at least for me "falls more readily
to hand".  I don't have to lift my arm way up in the air to hang onto
the shift knob.

The end result of this is that I am now VERY suspicious of ALL so-called
short shift kits -- you could easily claim a 10% reduction in shift
travel just by lopping a couple inches off of most any shifter.
