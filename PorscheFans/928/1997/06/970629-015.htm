I still propose that the BMW 850 holds its resale value better than my
'90 750iL did or your '87 928S4 does.  Of course, without hard numbers in
front of me, I can say that the 750 and 928 are affordable to me (I'm
pursuing purchase of my first 928) while the 850 has continued to be
elusive.  I don't consider Jaguars and Audi to be anywhere near the class
of a BMW, Mercedes or Porsche, however I do like my friend's Jaguar
Vanden Plas Majestic very much along with the new XJ8, I've owned a nice
Audi K70 and I really like the A8 Quattro - there may be one in my stable
some day.  I am not that excited by Mercedes, but really the main reason
is that my lousy lower back isn't properly supported by a Mercedes seat.

I'm not aware of a $140K version of an 850, however, BMW does have a
factory division that will customize any BMW you wish, any way you wish -
so I'm sure that is possible to order a $140K 850 from the factory (gold
plated shifters and Barney colored rugs with bullet proof glass are
possible for those with too much money).
