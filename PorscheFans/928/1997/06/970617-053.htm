>  Where can I acquire the scheduled maintenance handbook for my car
> and how much should I expect to pay for it?

Go to http://www.my-porsche.com
 There, you will find the owners manual and the warranty book for your
car.  They
will tell you all the service required for your car.  When ordering
these books,
you should consider the Porsche Technical Specification Book for your
car and
its model year.  It will provide you with the torque setting of all the
bolts on
your car, its performance rating, the explanation of the serial number,
all the
dimension and capacity and so on.  This is well worth the $9 it cost.
