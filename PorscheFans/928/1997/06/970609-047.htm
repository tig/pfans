I'm no expert, but here's my experience. 
 
I recently had my water pump replaced for $850 (350 part, 500 labor) ...
so 1500 sounds too steep.  You can find rebuilt waterpumps for as low as
100, but I recommend you  stay away from them.  You can also find
"non-Porsche" mechanics who'll do the work for a little less than 500,
but I recommend you stay away from them as well (you can't always trust
them to tension the belt right). 
 
In the course of replacing the waterpump (if that turns out to be the
problem), I also suggest you  get new timing belt, accessory belts,
thermostat, and belt tensioner (tensioner only if needed). 
 
Best of luck.
