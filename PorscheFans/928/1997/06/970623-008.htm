Hello All,

Spent the weekend polishing and waxing my black 81 928. I used Grand polish and
Blitz wax, both easy to put on and take off (used a buffer with a lambswool pad
on the hood and roof). Someone once said to try putting on the wax with your
bare fingers. I tried it but didn't like it because it goes on somewhat unevenly
and takes a lot longer than using a cotton cloth. It is however, a great way to
bond with your Porsche. Used "Black Again" on the spoilers and rubber trim. I
use the foam spray on the tires which I find also works very well on the wheels.
My car looks fantastic. There is nothing like a freshly polished black 928.....
or a red, white, blue, silver, etc.
