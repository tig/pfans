Check your engine mounts.  The clicking sound is probably coming from
the emission tripdometer/counter; located underneath a box on the right
side of the passenger seat.  On mine, the gear was slipping, causing a
clicking sound.  Took me hell of a long time to find it since you only
hear it when the car is in motion.  Didn't bother to replace it.  I just
unplugged the electrical connection that goes to the unit.
