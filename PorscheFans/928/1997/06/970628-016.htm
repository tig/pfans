At 12:23 PM 6/28/97 -0500, you wrote:
>I have recently purschased an 87 S4.  I am the 3rd owner.  The second 
>owner told me that when she purchased  the vehicle from the  original  
>owner he advised that the vehicle was equipped with a factory sport 
>suspension.  Could this be correct information  and is there any way to 
>verify this?  e.g. serial numbers etc. etc.

Lift the mat in your trunk, find the sticker on top of the gas tank (to the
right of the spare tire compartment), copy down all the numbers and codes
on that sticker, and decode them using the information at the "Option
Codes" page of Phil Tong's Web site (http://www.928s4vr.com/codes.htm). The
Sport Suspension group, if present, will be commemorated by code 030. Code
220 points to a limited slip differential.
