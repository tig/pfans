Re: Patrick with the door and window problem, think switch..., first,
disassemble the switch, careful with those little springs under the
contacts, clean it up, reassemble, check for voltage and ground at the
switch, then the motor, switch provides voltage and ground in one position,
and reverse, ground and voltage in the other. A swift hit with a flashlight
to the motor got my passenger door window moving after finding my switch
assembled incorrectly. Lastly, if volts and ground check out, sorry, get a
motor, around $100 928 INTL.
