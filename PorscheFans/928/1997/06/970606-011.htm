  There are some sheet metal heat shields around the junction of the exhaust
  manifolds and the Y-pipe that leads to the cat.  Double check to make sure
  these are tight.  Also check out the underbelly pan just behind the grill -
  sometimes the screw holes get enlarged to the point where the pan is no
  longer secure.  There is also a heat shield above that cat which could
  make the noise you describe, although that is obviously positioned at the
  center of the car (which isn't where you're hearing the noise?).

  I paid approx $1300 4 years ago.  This included an OEM (Sachs?)

  needed to help with alignment.
