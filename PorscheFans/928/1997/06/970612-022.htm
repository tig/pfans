Personally I would replace it, just to be safe.  I purchased my
second 928S with only 15,000 miles on it.  At the time it was eight
years old.  Just to be safe, due to the age of the belt obviously
sitting around a long time, I opted to replace it within the next
10,000 miles, well before the first recommended interval.  I chose 
to do this because there are a LOT of times that I drive in the 
triple digits and the last thing I need to worry about is the cam belt
breaking due to dry rotting or whatever.
