This is good advice. The fabric you want is felt.

I noticed that my ex's 450SL didn't squeak over bumps. Mercedes uses  a pad
of felt between any vinyl parts that might move.

My current 928 doesn't have the problem, but my 1985 did, and I used 3M
weatherstrip adhesive to attach a spot of felt to the lower inside corner
of the pod. Stopped the squeak for good.
The hardware store has stick-on felt pads for pots.
