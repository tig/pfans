>What are typical 0-60 and 1/4-mile times for stock '86 928's,
>both auto and manual?

Factory figures (generally conservative) for '86 928S (US figures in
parentheses):
	top speed:
		manual 158 (155) mph
		auto 155 (150)
	0-100 km/h (0-62.1 mph; takes noticeably longer than 0-60):
		manual 6.2 (6.3) sec
		auto 6.7 (6.8)
	1/4 mile
		manual (14.2) sec
		auto (14.9)

For those who are following along in the Spec Books, the non-US 0-100 km/h
line was printed offset one column to the left.
