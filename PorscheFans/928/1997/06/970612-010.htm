I put these springs on my '85 928, which was already a bit low. The
Automotion springs dropped it more than an inch (turns out it varies from
car to car). I liked the improvement in transient response. As with any
lowered Porsche, you have to be wary of driveways, speed bumps and other
steep transitions, or just keep a stock of front spoilers on hand...

BTW, I also put the Weltmeister front sway bar on the car, and I found the
stock sway bar mounts inadequate for the cornering loads I was able to
generate. Reinforce 'em if you're going to autocross!!!
