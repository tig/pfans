>>When using synthetic engine oil, at what interval do you replace it?
How frequently do you replace the filter?

Replace the filter at the same interval as you would with
petroleum...3000 to 5000 miles.  The filter is still going
to get crud in it.  Synthetic oil's strength is that it doesn't break
down with heat & use at anywhere near the rate
of a petroleum.  So, if you don't keep changing your filter, after
15,000 miles, your oil is great, but your filter
is clogged and your engine is starved!

Red Line recommends OIL change intervals of 12,000 to 25,000 miles,
depending on how severe the conditions
are.  Personally, I change at 12,000.  After all, filters are only $5
from 928 Int'l (special this month).

Every car I've had has been designed such that I could change the filter
without draining the oil, only losing maybe
1/2 quart or so.
