that a good lamp projects is well worth the price.  Also, from then
on when they burn out all you have to replace is the actual H-4 
element, not the whole lamp.  An additional benefit is if a foreign 
object breaks your lens, a sealed beam is done for whereas a H-4 
styled lamp will still work.  The same for H-5s'.
