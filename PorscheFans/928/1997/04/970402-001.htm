North America is fortunate to have a road course like Watkins Glen
International.  The new facility is the continuation of a road racing 
tradition dating back to 1948, when sports cars raced on the 6.6 mile 
course through the village of Watkins Glen. The permanent road course 
was built in 1956 and has been continually upgraded since that time, 
now hosting NASCAR, IMSA, SCCA and PCA events.  The Zone 1 48 Hours at 
Watkins Glen is not only a driving event, it is also a social event, 
bringing together members from the Zone and beyond.

The event will consist of 90 minutes per day of driving excitement for 
each entrant. The annual Saturday night dinner will be held at the 
Glen Club at the foot of the "esses". The cost of the event is $175 
per entrant (plus $20 per person for the Saturday dinner). To register 
for the event, send a completed application to George and Connie 
Beuselinck - Zone 1 Registrars, 172 Hardenburgh Road, Ulster Park, NY  12487.

The application is available on the World Wide Web at
http://www1.mhv.net/~georgeb/48hours.html
