Panorama is a monthly magazine published by none other than the Porsche
Club of America, PO Box 30100, Alexandria VA  22310  703-922-9300 - by
itself worth the cost of membership ($60? I forgot).  Give them a call and
they should have a listing of all the 'Up-Fixin' volumes available.  The
current volume is vol.X  (10) I think.

Porsche'.  One issue in particular comes to mind - Oct 1995, containing
featured article "A 928 in POC Racing"  (POC - Porsche Owners Club, a LA
based racing club).  Subscription info to excellence, Circulation Dept. PO
Box 1529, Ross CA  94957.
