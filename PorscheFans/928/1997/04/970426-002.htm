Hello Vince,

Just a thought as regards a seized nut; how about buying or borrowing a
nut-splitter? This will simply split the existing nut, allow you to remove
the mirror and then just replace the nut (Preferably with a little smear of
copper-grease to stop it ever seizing again!) You should find these
nut-splitters in most good tool shops/ hire centres (Well you can here in
England so I presume the US is the same!)

Good luck!
