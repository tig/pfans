For some reason, the spec book for '81 to '84 928s says nothing about
either wheel offsets or spacers. The shop manual barely mentions
offset, that I can find, and it doesn't talk about spacers except
for the 17 mm ones you mentioned. But my car is very stock, so it
seems unlikely to me that the spacers didn't come with it, and
there's nothing on the options it has like "Wide Rear Track".

By the way, I noticed that the shop manual does call for 44 psi in
the rear tires for all but US cars. In the US, it says, some tires
have a maximum allowable pressure of 36 psi. So clearly Porsche
wants us to run 36/44 F/R if the tires allow it, and the 36/36
is just CYA.

I would go look at the tire clearances on my car now, but it's
buried under two feet of snow. So much for spring.
