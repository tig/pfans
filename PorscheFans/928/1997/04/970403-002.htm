>Where do you see the oil leaking from; mixed in the coolant or seeing 
>it all over the engine?  This makes a difference.

You can try to re-torque the heads and see if that will do it.  But 
eventually the oil will get into the cooling system.  Worse yet, the 
coolant getting into the cylinder.  You can tell when your exhaust is 
smoking white.  By that time, the cylinder wall would be scored due to 
lack of lubrication.  I would at least do a compression check in an 
attempt to locate the trouble area.  $3000-3500 sounds about right to 
me.
