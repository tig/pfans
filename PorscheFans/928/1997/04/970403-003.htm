Randy asked about a black cargo cover to replace his rotted one.  The knitted
covers to rot due to UV.  My '86 cover did the same and since it was a rare
grey-green color I chose not to buy a new factory unit for $800plus. I had it
"recovered" using grey-green vinyl material.  The pvc was German to Porsche
specs and the upholstery man charge about $125 material and labor. It has
been in service over 5 years.

Re radiator question...don't second guess the factory suggestions to flush
every 2 years with the approve non-phosphate glycol.  Any other policy is an
invitation to the ".......pay me later "syndrome.
