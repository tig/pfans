John, Sorry it took so long for me to reply to you. Here is a 
synopsis of the book:

The book covers 928s from 1977 to 1994 and is a compilation of 
various articles that appeared in many different automobile magazines.
 Mostly British and American magazines. There are 38 articles in all 
and the book is 140 pages w/ black and white photos. I used the book 
when I began my search for a 928 over one year ago and it proved to 
be a great resource. There are no articles pertaining to performance 
enhancing one's 928 although there is an example of modifications 
done by German engineers that boast of the car reaching 
272km/hr(April 84). If you are a 928 freak as I am you must but this 
book. I believe I paid 22.95 for it. Here is a sampling of the 
articles which appear in the book:
