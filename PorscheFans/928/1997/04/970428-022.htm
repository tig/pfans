Stephane,

  Another Austin area 928 owner just returned my Wiring Diagrams volume
  of the factory service manual, so I happen to have it here at work.
  FWIW, the diagram for an '85 doesn't indicate that are markings on the
  back of the clock, but the picture looks like this:
  
           --- 0.5 brown --v
                        ---o-------------
                       |   o             |
                       |  (X)            |
                       |   o             |
                        ---o---o---------
      --- 0.5 black/blue --^   ^------ 0.5 red
      
  Based on the symbology, it looks like the brown and black/blue hook
  up to the clock reset button.  Brown is ground;  black/blue gets input
  from a junction box that includes the cigar lighter as well as the
  warning flasher switch.  Red hooks into a large circuit box on the
  diagram, but I don't understand what that box is.
  
  Hope this helps a bit.
