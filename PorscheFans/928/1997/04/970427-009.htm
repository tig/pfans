Regarding the bolt frozen in the mirror, I suggest drilling out the bolt
head to a selected large size and breaking off the head with that size pin
punch. Drill as large as practical for the space to allow the break to
occur. Then, with the mirror free of the car, invest in a Snap-On Easy-Out
kit, it has reverse drill bits as well, apply some heat and leverage. Hope
this is of some help.
