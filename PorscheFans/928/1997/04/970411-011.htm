  Speaking of rust, I found rust perforation in the bottom of the
  battery compartment on my car.  I guess I didn't see it when I bought my
  car because the undercoating hid it from view on the outside, and
  I didn't pull the battery to check the inside.  I took it to the
  dealer as a claim on the rust perforation warranty since I was still
  within the 7-year window and they told me that that location wasn't
  covered.  After a few letters to PCNA and an inspection by the
  regional warranty rep, they replaced the entire battery compartment
  under warranty - all of this within one month of the expiration date.
  
  Just something for owners of '90 or later cars to keep an eye on.
