  What do you mean be heat settings?  Being a Euro-spec car, the numerical
  temp values are likely to be in degrees Centigrade, so you'll have to
  do the conversion to get to the Farenheit scale.  The slider knob settings

  Porsche literature available.  Maybe they can order a Euro-spec manual
  for you.
