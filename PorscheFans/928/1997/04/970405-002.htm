>What can I do to increase the power of my 1985 928S (in addition to=20

It really depends on how much power you want and how much money you can
(or wish to) spend.  An easy answer would be "open your wallet very wide
and call Devek."

All the normal hot rod upgrades work on 928s just like they do on a Chevy
V-8 (that's also an option...).  Just be prepared to spend BIG money.
That means you can change the cams (potentially big power increase), add
headers, put on a low restriction exhaust system, etc, etc.  Of course if
you do this you end up with a somewhat bastardized car.  In the long run
it might be cheaper and easier to buy a later model S4 such as an '89,
which does 0-60 in 5.7 and 168 mph in 5-spd form.  This should be enough
power for most people... I'd take it!
