My O2 final results (less install).

Before I wrote that my O2 heater resistance was 5 ohms whereas all the
other 3 wires were around 3.5 ohms.  Well, the new one from the Porsche
dealer is 3.5 ohms.  So, electrically, everything's the same and so the
$40 universal 3 wire (from a '87 Mustang) might work.  However, the
openings in the tip is different for the '87-'95 (except '92) O2 sensor 
(Bosch # 258 003 048) than for most.  Porsche list is $156 and less 20%
PCA discount, it came out to $125 - $25 LESS than the NAPA, Pep Boys,
etc.'s.  So for a difference of $85, I figured I wouldn't introduce
another unknown.

Always check the dealer price,
