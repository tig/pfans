Hi Alan,

How I deal with that awful squeal from the brakes is pretty simple and very
effective; I simply smear the rear metal face of the pads (Where they
contact the pistons/ callipers) with a little bit of high temperature
grease such as "Copperslip". Obviously you need to be careful not to get it
on any braking surface but otherwise it works a treat for me!

Good luck!
