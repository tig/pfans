John wrote:

>>I've had my '79 928 for over 2 years now and the air coming from the side
>>window vents and the windshield vents has always warmed up the same time as
>>the engine coolant warmed up.

  There are a couple of things that can cause this, all of which have happened to me.

1) Bad heater valve.
2) No power on the A/C control head...check for bad relay or the ceramic fuse. It turned
   out to be the fuse oxidized to a paper thin thicness, it 'looked to be okay but
   it was actually not conducting. My mechanic suggested I replace all of them every 
   two years (they are cheap and this may avoid problems). BTW don't be fooled by the
   fact that the control head lights come on...this is not indicative of power to the
   control circuit.
