Matt,

Steer away from the vacuum pump system.  We discussed its use at 
lenght no so long ago and there was not much support from it.

From my own experience, it is hard to get a good seal either between
the tubing and the bleeder, or between the bleeder and the caliper.
This leads to you seeing air bubbles all the time.  The only way
to go is to have a partner push the pedal or purchase a pressure
bleeder system.
