Healthy S4 brakes are awfully strong, particularly if your tires are sticky
and your wheels are properly aligned. Do you really need more? If you feel
you do, you might want to look at changing the F/R braking balance, which
currently underutilizes the rears to avoid rear lockup and consequent
spins. Kim Crumb has found a Porsche part which strikes a less conservative
balance. Beyond this, race supply houses can furnish you with adjustable
balance valves.
