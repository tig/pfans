Recently there has been some talk about tires for 928s and what kinds to avoid.  Specific
comments were made about the undesirability of Goodyears.  I'm baffled by this and would

225/50 ZR16 tires on my stock '86 928S for three years and have had no troubles or

etc. as I have with other tires.

a set of S4 wheels and intend to put the Goodyear Eagle 245/45 ZR16 tires on the rear.
What am I missing here?  Any relevant information would be appreciated.
