I'm another 928 owner ('83' with 120k miles) who could use a lead on
where/how to get a key made. Through long and circuitous circumstances
we don't have the key to the rear hatch. A local firm (Palo Alto, Ca.)
can replace the lock for ~$150! We do have the code number for the key.
We love our 928 we just don't like buying parts for it. 

An entertaining description I have seen of the car showed up about a year
ago on the porschephiles mail list. Someone described it as a Barcolounger
on a  Rocket Sled. I'm impressed at how capably it handles on narrow,
twisty roads. We have a '90 Miata and the 928 impresses me in how favorably
it compares to the Miata on the terrain the Miata excels in. And there is
no comparison in
acceleration form ~45mph to 80 mph. In the 928 accelerating through that range
of speeds is like the Enterprise going to warp drive. You could say its an
engaging kind of car.
