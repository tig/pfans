If the inside of the front tires are wearing tremendously, it's the toe-in 
that is messed up -- or in this case toe-out. I don't think its possible to 
change the camber enough to wear the tires as you describe.  Usually, toe-out 
is used to improve handling.  Does your car wander on straight freeways?  This 
would be another sign of toe-out.
