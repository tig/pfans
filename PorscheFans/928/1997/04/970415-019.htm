It's all in the DME. Factory manual says all the distributor does is
distribute the spark - no timing.  (I know this is true for mine and I
believe it's for all 32 valvers at the very least.  The easy way to tell
is that on these, there is allegedly no adjustment possible of the
distributor timing.)
