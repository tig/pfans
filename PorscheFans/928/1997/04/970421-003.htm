Since there are few 928s around with the optional side molding, I may
finally be able to add some value to this list...

I replaced my door molding last year.  It is held in place by a clip in
the front and a integrated bolt in the back.  The nut for the bolt can
be accessed by pulling back the door rubber seal, just behind the end of
the molding.  In addition, you will find the required molding adhesive
all along the door/molding.  Once you get the nut off, you will be able
to slowly work the molding off, back to front.
