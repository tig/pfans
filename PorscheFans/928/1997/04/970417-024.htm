My 79 928 brakes seemed to be working fine even though the warning
was on until I let the car sit for a week, after which I found the master
cylinder was bad. It's now been relaced, but the warning light is still
on. (BTW- The master cylinder was $120 from Adirondack). The master
cylinder came equipped with both new sensors.
