I finally installed my new exhaust.  It is a single 3" pipe from the cat,
to a Borla 3" in/out XR-1 16" long muffler. Then out to a 4.5" slash-cut
tip. I had it installed by a friend of mine who is a welder, no leaks.

It is loud, but it sounds nice. And although I haven't installed headers,
there is a very noticeable performance gain. Goodbye to the PO's tiny Chevy
Cavalier exhaust, my 928 can finally breathe !

Total cost was:

A little under $500 (including 8 hrs @ $30 labor)

compare to $750 + installation for Devek's system (ok, so their's is a
3.5", big difference).
