sorry for my tardy response but my mail has been off-line for a few days
being re-configured to a new address (See above) First things first, knobs
by the handbrake.

On the European model there should be TWO knobs. The forward smaller one is
a fader switch for the rear speakers (Probably by-passed if the car is
fitted with a more modern stereo) The other larger switch with the picture
of a headlight is indeed an adjuster to allow the headlamp level to be
adjusted depending on load-state etc. though I have yet to meet or speak to
ANYONE whose switch actually works. Out of interest I believe the switch
works by some sort of self-contained liquid/ hydraulic system.
