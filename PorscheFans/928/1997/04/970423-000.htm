>Which other brand/type is quieter and goes well with D40s in the front (until

With respect to your desire to get full use from your current tires, I
strongly suggest that you replace all four (any car will be happier and
stabler with the same model tire all around), and consider doing so with
Continental CZ 91s, which are the least expensive true high performance
tires around.

Your car is too fast to be fully safe with the semi-sport tires, and you'll
be amazed at how much better it handles and stops with the good stuff on
the ground.

I suggest avoiding BFG Comp T/As, any of the Goodyear Eagles, and, frankly,
any of the Dunlop tires (more reports coming in of vibration and
un-align-ability of cars with SP 8000s).
