"Mystery 928 coolant leak

If your 928 seems to be losing coolant but you aren't seeing a puddle underneath 
the car, check around the
heater valve. (To see a heater valve, look at part #928.574.573.03 on page 173

   These valves are known for developing slow leaks, which are evaporated by the 
heat of the engine and
bellhousing before they form a tell-tale puddle.
