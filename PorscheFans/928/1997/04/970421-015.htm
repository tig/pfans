At 09:41 AM 4/21/97 -0700, you wrote:
>My 84 Euro also developed some kind of mood related 
>performance degradation. I have always suspected that
>is caused due to one or more clogged injectors.

If they're the original parts, they are due or overdue to be replaced.
Cleaning won't bring them al the way back, and can damage them. Get new
injectors every lotsa miles and enjoy your car again.

>I have tried various sort of fuel injection cleaner with no big help.

That's the common experience. Replace 'em. Meanwhile, make sure your fuel
pump is healthy, your fuel filter is not clogged (replace it), your gas
tank vent is not clogged or in backwards (happened to me, and the engine
starved on full throttle because the fuel pump was pulling a vacuum), etc.
All parts must work properly for best results.
