The S4 was introduced in model year 1987 with 316 SAE bhp and your choice
of stick or auto. Though this choice persisted for some time thereafter
outside the US, PCNA (according to its own Fact Book) stopped importing
stick S4s into the US after MY 1988.

For MY 1989, when the GT (stick only and 326 SAE bhp) was introduced, US
928 buyers had the choice of S4 automatics or GT sticks. This remained true
in MY '90 and '91.

For MY '92, PCNA did not import any 928 model. For '93, they brought in the
new GTS (345 SAE bhp, choice of stick or auto).
