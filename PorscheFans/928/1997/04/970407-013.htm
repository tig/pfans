The V12 engine in my BMW 750iL is 5.0 liters, weighs 249 lbs dry, and has
a stock hp of 300.  You can purchase the oldest model (1988 was the first
year) for ~ $10 - 12K which will give you lots of other nice parts

six-bolt main bearing caps, fully baffled cast aluminum structural oil
pan.  It even looks good...
