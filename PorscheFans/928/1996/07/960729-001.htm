Chuck-
I do not know what sort of fitting you fuel rail has on it for the
attachment of a pressure gauge.  If it is something you can buy
off-the-shelf, you can easily make your own gauge system, and pick any
specification for gauges you want.  Most of the fuel rails I have seen 
have a "Shrader" fitting.  This is a 1/4 inch flare fitting with a valve
core in it,  if this is your situation, you can get a refrigeration
service hose and build from there, it is really quite easy to do.
