Pete:

Your report about Le Mans, brought back a lot of memories of my days as a 
young air force recruit in Wiesbaden AB in '73-75.  Of course, one never 
really cherishes the moment as much until you look back on days gone by.

Cruising up and down the roads in a VW beetle conv. by the Rhine to check 
out winefests and admiring current model 911s tearing up the road comes 
to mind.

Camping and watching whatever races were going on at the time in 
Hockenheim and old Nurburgring, whether it's to cheer Lauda (my favorite 
at the time) or the 934s running the support races in F1 comes to mind. 
Still have pictures of those 934s.
 
Chasing a Fiat down the corniche into the town of Monte Carlo for the 74 
GP always come to mind when the GP broadcast is on ESPN. Obviously 
couldn't afford hotels in Monaco, loved those roads back and forth to San 
Remo. Still have pictures of Graham Hill signing autographs.
  
Just wish I had a Le Mans moment.
