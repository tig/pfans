Forgot to mention the HRE 525's are a TUV approved wheel. Agree with Lou
Krieg that you have to be careful about wheel quality. The TUV approval
should be a good standard. The German vehicle inspection process is far more
stingent than anywhere in the US. Here we modify our cars as we like. There,
any modification has to be shown not to have degraded the safety of the
vehicle as it was originally manufactured. Hence the TUV standards. The owner
paperwork in Germany lists any mods done to the car and they have to have
been done and used parts meeting the TUV standard for that area/part.

Hope I haven't misspoke too greatly for our German friends.
