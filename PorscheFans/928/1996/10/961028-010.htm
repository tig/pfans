Wayne Brewer asked:

>> Any tips on how to remove the inner door panel on an early (1982) 928
would be appreciated. <<

The bolts that hold the arm rest on are hidden under round plastic caps inside the armrest.
After the arm rest is removed, you will see a couple of bolts that hold the center (leather 
covered) part of the door panel. Then, remove the round door lock knob. After that is removed, 
there is only one or two screws at the bottom of the panel and then you could carefully pry away 
the whole inner panel.  You will also have to take off the plastic surround around the hard black 
plastic tube that protects wires going from the front rear of the door jamb to the front edge of 
the door held on by a couple of screws into the door panel.  Once you have the door panel loose 
take the oportunity to check the front speakers.  Mine were almost completely rotted around the 
edge of the paper cone.  No wonder the radio sounded so ragged.
