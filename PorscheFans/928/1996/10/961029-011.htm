Generally, you will ask for trouble on any 928 if it is in an unknown 
mechanical condition.  For the S4 just magnify that statement.

Unfortunately, the majority of cars I've seen are in this category (esp 
the relatively rare 5 spds for some reason - wild eyed sporty driving 
maniacs seem to buy em is my theory ;).

It's still worth it in the intermediate and long term to pay more
(ie book value or even more for some cars) and get a good known condition 
car than to try and save money up front.
