Welcome Chad,
I have an '83 with about the same mileage as the one you're looking at and can 
relate some of my experiences with it to you.  I hear the water pump is a 
problem, however, I still have the original pump on my car.  My car starts 
fine.  No roughnes on start up or any other time, so that would concern me.  
Roughness on startup could be simple stuff like cap, rotor, plugs, and wires.  
Replacement spark plug wires are relatively expensive.  The timing belt should 
have been changed a couple of times in 116k.  Check the brake rotors, they can 
be kinda expensive.  The exhaust system's mufflers are an absurd price too. 
Maintenance should have been done to the car during the 116k, but I can't 
think of anything that damn sure needs to be replaced.  If you look at the car 
and have other questions, please ask!
