The 9 vol. english language manual set available for just under $300 from PCNA is, I believe, 
intended to cover all years and versions of the 928.  The spec section describes all the 
differing versions: euro, US, and several other location-specific variants.  A lot of the info is 
for the later (more complicated) years.  If I had it to do over, I might be inclined to look for 
the earlier 6 vol. set.  The additions are mainly for 32 valve engine, airbags, power seats, ABS 
etc.
