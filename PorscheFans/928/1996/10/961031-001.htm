Hey Phil,
Check your battery.  As the battery gets older there isn't sufficient voltage. 
 Without enough voltage, the fuel injection control unit won't turn on the 
fuel pump.  Hence, your engine just cranks and cranks.  If the battery is ok, 
then it sounds to me like your fuel injection control unit is bad.  928 
International -- $900.00.  Been there, done that, bought the control unit.
