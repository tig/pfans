                Another factor that may need to be considered is that those very
clever and efficient Germans have 12 volt through the coil for starting, but 
after starting the voltage through the coil is reduced to about 6 for running. 
(by engaging a ballast resistor)  Maybe this switching circuit is not working 
correctly, and when first trying you are trying to start with 6V. Maybe the 
second time the 12 V is getting through...
