If you don't have a grounding problem, I speculate that
your sense point may be effected more by the state of
charge of you battery than you want.  Especially if you have 
modified the system, and run a heavy wire directly from
the alternator output to your battery.  Many guys then also
connect the sense lead to the same heavy wire to the battery.
Now, the battery is well charged, and regulated, but the
system voltage varies.  Such is not all bad, as most of the
electronics does not care too much about the voltage,
provided it is above 10-11V.  The battery; however, does
like to be charged.
