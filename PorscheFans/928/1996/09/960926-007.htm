Chuck,
 The intercoolers are mount at an extreme angle right next to one another 
slight below the last row of cooling fins to the left and right of the 
radiator. There are cutouts in the plastic shroud allowing the to fit as 
high as possible, of course the plumbing itself presents a difficult 
ground clearance problem. One of the retrofits on the new ones is a 
plumbing change to allow the connections all to be at the back of the 
intercoolers giving me back aboyt 2 inches of clearance.
