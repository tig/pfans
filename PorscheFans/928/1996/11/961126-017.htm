Jim Harriger asked:

>> -find a set of springs from a 928 that had gas shocks standard. anybody
know how to identify these? <<

My '82 928 has the "Sport Package" option which includes gas-filled Bilstein shocks.  
The Service manual says the gas-filled shocks are green in color and mine are.  I 
can't remember the color of the standard shocks.  I think some later cars and any S 
versions may have had gas shocks as standard.  I'm sure there are adapters/kits 
available.  The mail order catalog companies have special instructions and kits 
available for converting shocks.
