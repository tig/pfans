| The manual recommends 35 psi front and 44 psi rear for my 88 S4.  This seems

| My tires are Goodrich Comp T/A Z rated and are the factory sizes.  Wheels

I have the same Compt T/A tires on my '83.  All 928 owners probably have had, 
or still have, the problem with the center of the rears wearing faster than 
the outside.  For everyday driving, I run 32 in the fronts and 28 in the 
rears.  This seems to give pretty even tire wear.  I have 225's all around so 
I can also rotate them every 5k miles.
