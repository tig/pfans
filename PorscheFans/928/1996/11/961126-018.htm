Scott, do you still have the old lock?  On other Porsches it is possible to swap the 
tumbler set.  I've witnessed it done on a 911 doorlock.

Alt #2  When I needed to replace my locking gascap,  I bought a used one from a 
dismantler and they referred me to a locksmith who could swap out or rekey tumbler 
sets.  I'm sure there are other locksmiths who can also do this work.  

Alt #3  PCNA usually has two prices for replacement locks. One (very high price) with 
specific key, and one (lower) for non-specific key.  On the gascap, the differential 
was >$120 as I recall.
