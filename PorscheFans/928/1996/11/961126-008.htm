You should not exceed the recommended pressure as printed on the tire.  Other
than that, choose a pressure that provides the best ride for your purposes.
 For auto-cross, go with lower tire pressure (but not low enough that your tire
rolls past the scrub line), for highway driving, choose the higher tire
pressure for lower rolling resistance.
